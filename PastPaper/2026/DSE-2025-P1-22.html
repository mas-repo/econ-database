2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

堅尼系數 (Gini Coefficient): 堅尼系數是量度收入不均的指標，數值介乎 \(0\) 至 \(1\) 之間。數值越高，代表收入分佈越不平均。

The Gini coefficient is a measure of income inequality, ranging from \(0\) to \(1\). A higher value indicates a more unequal income distribution.

除稅及福利轉移前後 (Pre-tax/transfer vs. Post-tax/transfer):

  • 除稅前 (Pre-tax): 反映市場原本的收入分佈（未經政府干預）。
  • 除稅後 (Post-tax): 反映政府透過稅收（如累進稅）和福利轉移（如津貼）進行收入再分配後的結果。通常除稅後的堅尼系數會較低。
  • Pre-tax: Reflects the original market income distribution (without government intervention).
  • Post-tax: Reflects the result after government income redistribution through taxes (e.g., progressive tax) and transfers (e.g., subsidies). The post-tax Gini coefficient is usually lower.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 / Correct Option: A

數據分析 (Data Analysis):
除稅前的堅尼系數維持在 \(0.52\) 不變，這意味著市場原來的收入不均情況沒有改變。然而，除稅後的堅尼系數由 \(0.39\) 上升至 \(0.45\)，這表示政府在 2025 年的收入再分配政策（稅收及福利）減少了縮窄貧富懸殊的效用，導致除稅後的收入變得更不平均。

The pre-tax Gini coefficient remained unchanged at \(0.52\), meaning the original market income inequality did not change. However, the post-tax Gini coefficient increased from \(0.39\) to \(0.45\). This indicates that the government's income redistribution policies (taxes and transfers) in 2025 became less effective at narrowing the wealth gap, leading to a more unequal post-tax income distribution.

選項 (1) 分析: 正確
降低薪俸稅的累進程度，意味著高收入人士繳交的稅款比例相對減少，政府從富人轉移財富到窮人的力度減弱。這會導致除稅後的收入差距擴大，堅尼系數上升，符合題目數據。

Analysis of (1): Correct. Decreasing the progressivity of the salaries tax means high-income earners pay a relatively smaller proportion of tax, weakening the government's ability to transfer wealth from the rich to the poor. This widens the post-tax income gap, leading to a higher post-tax Gini coefficient, which matches the data.

選項 (2) 分析: 錯誤
以人工智能取代低技術工人會導致低技術工人失業或收入下降，從而令市場原來的收入差距擴大。這會導致「除稅前」的堅尼系數上升。但根據數據，除稅前堅尼系數維持在 \(0.52\)，因此這不是原因。

Analysis of (2): Incorrect. Replacing low-skilled workers with AI would cause them to lose jobs or income, widening the original market income gap. This would cause the "pre-tax" Gini coefficient to increase. However, the data shows the pre-tax Gini remained at \(0.52\), so this cannot be the reason.

選項 (3) 分析: 錯誤
向每個住戶派發 \(5,000\) 元現金。對於低收入家庭來說，\(5,000\) 元佔他們總收入的比例遠高於高收入家庭。這種定額轉移實際上具有累進效果，會縮窄除稅及福利轉移後的收入差距，令除稅後堅尼系數下降，這與題目中堅尼系數上升的數據矛盾。

Analysis of (3): Incorrect. Giving out $\(5,000\) cash to each household. For low-income families, $\(5,000\) represents a much larger percentage of their total income compared to high-income families. This lump-sum transfer actually has a progressive effect, which would narrow the post-tax income gap and lower the post-tax Gini coefficient, contradicting the data showing an increase.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point):
這條題目的關鍵在於區分「除稅前 (Pre-tax)」與「除稅後 (Post-tax)」的變動。當你看到除稅前數值不變 (\(0.52 \rightarrow 0.52\))，你就可以立刻排除所有影響市場原本收入的因素（例如科技改變、教育水平改變、最低工資等）。變化只可能來自政府的稅收或福利政策改變。

Pivot Point: The key to this question is distinguishing between "Pre-tax" and "Post-tax" changes. When you see the pre-tax value remains unchanged (\(0.52 \rightarrow 0.52\)), you can immediately eliminate any factors that affect the original market income (e.g., technological changes, education levels, minimum wage). The change must come purely from changes in government tax or transfer policies.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：誤以為「定額派錢」不會改變貧富懸殊
很多學生在看選項 (3) 時，認為每人派發相同金額（\(5,000\) 元），大家的收入差距絕對值沒有改變，因此堅尼系數不變。這是一個嚴重的誤解！堅尼系數看的是相對比例 (Relative proportion)。窮人增加 \(5,000\) 元可能是收入翻倍，富人增加 \(5,000\) 元只是九牛一毛，因此定額派錢會令收入分佈更平均。

Trap: Assuming "Lump-sum cash handouts" don't change inequality. Many students look at option (3) and think that since everyone gets the same absolute amount ($\(5,000\)), the absolute income gap remains the same, so the Gini coefficient shouldn't change. This is a critical misconception! The Gini coefficient looks at relative proportions. An extra $\(5,000\) might double a poor person's income, but it's a drop in the bucket for a rich person. Therefore, an equal lump-sum transfer makes the income distribution more equal.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼選項 (1) 降低累進程度會令堅尼系數上升？
A: 累進稅（Progressive tax）的意思是收入越高，稅率越高。這是政府「劫富濟貧」的主要工具。如果降低累進程度，富人交的稅相對變少了，政府能用來重新分配的資源也少了，因此除稅後的貧富懸殊就會比之前嚴重，堅尼系數自然上升。

Q: Why does decreasing progressivity in option (1) cause the Gini coefficient to rise?
A: A progressive tax means higher income earners face higher tax rates. It's the government's main tool for redistributing wealth. If progressivity is decreased, the rich pay relatively less tax, and the government has less to redistribute. Therefore, the post-tax wealth gap becomes more severe than before, naturally causing the Gini coefficient to rise.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧 (One-Second Kill):
在考場上，先看表格第一行：除稅前堅尼系數不變。這代表任何影響「市場收入」的選項都是錯的。選項 (2) 講述公司用 AI 取代工人，這明顯是市場行為，會改變除稅前收入。因此 (2) 必定錯誤。立刻刪除選項 C 和 D。剩下 A 和 B，再判斷 (3) 的派錢效應會令堅尼系數下降（與數據上升不符），即可迅速選出 A。

Shortcut: In the exam, look at the first row of the table first: Pre-tax Gini is unchanged. This means any option affecting "market income" is wrong. Option (2) is about companies replacing workers with AI, which is clearly a market behavior affecting pre-tax income. Thus, (2) must be wrong. Immediately eliminate options C and D. Between A and B, determine that the cash handout in (3) would lower the Gini (contradicting the data's increase), allowing you to quickly select A.
