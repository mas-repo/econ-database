HKDSE Economics: Question Analysis

Topic 廠商理論與利潤極大化 (Theory of the Firm and Profit Maximisation)

Difficulty ★★★☆☆ (3/5 Stars)

Key Concepts 受價者 (Price-taking firm), 利潤極大化 (Profit Maximisation), 邊際成本 (Marginal Cost), 固定成本 (Fixed Cost)

The Question (題目)

廠商 X 是一間受價廠商。假設市場價格和每單位產出的邊際成本皆上升了 $2，而固定成本則下降了 $1，廠商 X 利潤極大化的產量 ______ ，而其利潤會 ______ 。

A. 會維持不變 …… 上升
B. 會維持不變 …… 維持不變
C. 可能上升或下降 …… 上升
D. 可能上升或下降 …… 維持不變
Firm X is a price-taking firm. Suppose both the market price and the marginal cost of each unit of output rise by $2 while the fixed cost decreases by $1, the profit-maximising output of Firm X __________ and its profit will __________.

A. will remain unchanged ...... increase
B. will remain unchanged ...... remain unchanged
C. may increase or decrease ...... increase
D. may increase or decrease ...... remain unchanged

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 受價廠商的利潤極大化條件：
在完全競爭市場中，受價廠商面對水平的需求曲線，其邊際收益 (MR) 等於市場價格 (P)。利潤極大化的條件是邊際收益等於邊際成本。
1. Profit-maximising condition for a price-taking firm:
In a perfectly competitive market, a price-taking firm faces a horizontal demand curve, where Marginal Revenue (MR) equals Market Price (P). The profit-maximising condition is Marginal Revenue equals Marginal Cost.
$$ P = MR = MC $$
2. 利潤的計算：
總利潤 \( \pi \) 等於總收益 (TR) 減去總成本 (TC)。總成本又可分為總可變成本 (TVC) 和總固定成本 (TFC)。
2. Calculation of Profit:
Total Profit \( \pi \) equals Total Revenue (TR) minus Total Cost (TC). Total Cost can be divided into Total Variable Cost (TVC) and Total Fixed Cost (TFC).
$$ \pi = TR - TC $$ $$ \pi = (P \times Q) - (TVC + TFC) $$

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A

Option A: 會維持不變 …… 上升 (will remain unchanged ...... increase) - CORRECT

產量分析： 原本的利潤極大化產量 \( Q \) 發生在 \( P = MC \)。現在價格上升 $2，新的價格 \( P' = P + 2 \)。每單位的邊際成本也上升 $2，新的邊際成本 \( MC' = MC + 2 \)。由於等式兩邊同時加上 $2，新的均衡條件 \( P' = MC' \) 依然在原本的產量 \( Q \) 處成立。因此，產量維持不變。

利潤分析： 由於產量 \( Q \) 不變，總收益的增加量為 \( \Delta TR = \$2 \times Q \)。因為「每單位」的邊際成本上升 $2，意味著生產 \( Q \) 件產品的總可變成本也增加了 \( \$2 \times Q \)。所以，總收益和總可變成本的增加量互相抵消。然而，固定成本下降了 $1，這會直接使總成本減少 $1，從而令總利潤上升 $1。因此，利潤會上升。
Output Analysis: The original profit-maximising output \( Q \) occurs at \( P = MC \). Now the price increases by $2, so the new price is \( P' = P + 2 \). The marginal cost of each unit also increases by $2, so the new marginal cost is \( MC' = MC + 2 \). Since $2 is added to both sides of the equation, the new equilibrium condition \( P' = MC' \) holds at the exact same output level \( Q \). Thus, output remains unchanged.

Profit Analysis: Since output \( Q \) is unchanged, the increase in Total Revenue is \( \Delta TR = \$2 \times Q \). Because the marginal cost of "each unit" rises by $2, the Total Variable Cost for producing \( Q \) units also increases by \( \$2 \times Q \). Therefore, the increase in TR and TVC cancel each other out. However, the fixed cost decreases by $1, which directly reduces the Total Cost by $1, thereby increasing the total profit by $1. Thus, profit will increase.

Option B: 會維持不變 …… 維持不變 (will remain unchanged ...... remain unchanged) - INCORRECT

這個選項正確地指出了產量不變，但忽略了固定成本下降對利潤的正面影響。固定成本的減少會直接轉化為利潤的增加。
This option correctly identifies that output remains unchanged, but ignores the positive impact of the decrease in fixed cost on profit. A reduction in fixed cost directly translates to an increase in profit.

Option C & D: 可能上升或下降 (may increase or decrease) - INCORRECT

這兩個選項錯誤地認為產量會改變。由於價格 \( P \) 和邊際成本 \( MC \) 以相同的幅度（$2）向上移動，它們的相交點（即利潤極大化的產量）在橫軸上的位置絕對不會改變。
These two options incorrectly assume that the output might change. Since both price \( P \) and marginal cost \( MC \) shift upwards by the exact same amount ($2), their intersection point (the profit-maximising output) will absolutely not change its position on the horizontal axis.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解答這類題目的「關鍵點 (Pivot Point)」在於分開處理「邊際 (Marginal)」和「總量 (Total)」的概念：
1. 決定產量看邊際： 只有 \( P \) 和 \( MC \) 會影響產量 \( Q \)。固定成本 (FC) 的改變不會影響邊際成本，因此不會影響產量。
2. 決定利潤看總量： 利潤是看整體的 \( TR \) 和 \( TC \)。當你發現 \( \Delta TR = +2Q \) 且 \( \Delta TVC = +2Q \) 時，就知道這兩者對利潤的淨影響為零。最後只剩下固定成本減少 $1，這就是利潤上升的唯一原因。
The "Pivot Point" for solving this type of question is separating the concepts of "Marginal" and "Total":
1. Marginal determines Output: Only \( P \) and \( MC \) affect output \( Q \). Changes in Fixed Cost (FC) do not affect marginal cost, and thus do not affect output.
2. Totals determine Profit: Profit looks at the overall \( TR \) and \( TC \). When you realize that \( \Delta TR = +2Q \) and \( \Delta TVC = +2Q \), you know their net effect on profit is zero. The only thing left is the $1 decrease in fixed cost, which is the sole reason profit increases.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱 1：誤以為固定成本會影響產量。 很多學生看到「固定成本下降」，會直覺以為成本低了，廠商就會生產更多。記住：固定成本不影響邊際成本 (MC)，所以絕對不影響受價廠商的利潤極大化產量！
陷阱 2：忘記乘以產量 \( Q \)。 題目說「每單位」的邊際成本上升 $2。有些學生以為總成本只上升了 $2，這是錯的。總可變成本是上升了 \( 2 \times Q \)。
Trap 1: Assuming fixed cost affects output. Many students see "fixed cost decreases" and intuitively think lower costs mean the firm will produce more. Remember: Fixed cost does not affect Marginal Cost (MC), so it absolutely does not affect the profit-maximising output of a price-taking firm!
Trap 2: Forgetting to multiply by output \( Q \). The question states the marginal cost of "each unit" rises by $2. Some students mistakenly think total cost only rises by $2. Total variable cost actually rises by \( 2 \times Q \).

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果題目沒有說「每單位」的邊際成本上升 $2，而是說「總固定成本上升 $2」，產量會改變嗎？
A: 不會。只要是「固定成本」的改變，無論上升或下降多少，都不會改變邊際成本 (MC) 曲線的位置，因此產量 \( Q \) 永遠維持不變。只有利潤會受到影響。
Q: What if the question didn't say the marginal cost of "each unit" rises by $2, but instead said "total fixed cost rises by $2", would the output change?
A: No. Any change in "fixed cost", regardless of how much it increases or decreases, will not shift the Marginal Cost (MC) curve. Therefore, output \( Q \) will always remain unchanged. Only the profit will be affected.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

圖像平移法 (Graphical Shift Method)：
在腦海中畫一個標準的完全競爭市場圖。水平的 \( P \) 線向上平移 $2，同時向上傾斜的 \( MC \) 曲線也整條向上平移 $2。因為兩條線向上移動的垂直距離完全一樣，它們的新交點必然在原本交點的正上方。這意味著對應的橫軸數值（產量 \( Q \)）完全沒有向左或向右移動。一秒鐘就能確定產量「維持不變」，直接排除 C 和 D！
Graphical Shift Method:
Visualize a standard perfect competition graph in your mind. The horizontal \( P \) line shifts up by $2, and the upward-sloping \( MC \) curve also shifts up vertically by $2. Because both lines move up by the exact same vertical distance, their new intersection point must be directly above the original intersection point. This means the corresponding value on the horizontal axis (output \( Q \)) does not move left or right at all. You can determine output "remains unchanged" in one second, immediately eliminating C and D!
