HKDSE Economics Tutor

Topic (課題): Money and Banking - Three-Tier Banking System (貨幣與銀行 - 三級銀行體制)

Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆

Key Concepts (核心概念): Licensed Bank (持牌銀行), Restricted Licence Bank (有限制牌照銀行), Deposit-taking Company (接受存款公司), Demand Deposit (活期存款)

The Question (題目)

黎先生計劃將港幣 $500 000存入香港一家金融機構作兩個月的活期存款。下列哪類／些機構能接納該筆存款？

(1) 持牌銀行
(2) 有限制牌照銀行
(3) 接受存款公司

A. 只有 (1)
B. 只有 (3)
C. 只有 (1)、(2)
D. 只有 (2)、(3)

Mr Lai plans to deposit HK$500 000 to a financial institution in Hong Kong as demand deposit for two months. Which of the following type(s) of institution can receive this deposit?

(1) a licensed bank
(2) a restricted licence bank
(3) a deposit-taking company

A. (1) only
B. (3) only
C. (1) and (2) only
D. (2) and (3) only

Concept Review (概念重溫)

香港實行三級銀行體制 (Three-Tier Banking System)，不同級別的接受存款機構有嚴格的業務限制。要解答這類題目，必須熟記以下三大條件：存款種類、最低存款額、最短存款期。

Hong Kong implements a Three-Tier Banking System. Different tiers of deposit-taking institutions have strict business restrictions. To answer this type of question, you must memorize three main conditions: type of deposit, minimum deposit amount, and minimum maturity.
  • 持牌銀行 (Licensed Banks, LBs):
    可以接受任何金額、任何期限的存款。更重要的是，它們是唯一可以接受活期存款 (Demand Deposits) 及 儲蓄存款 (Savings Deposits) 的機構。
    Can accept deposits of any size and any maturity. More importantly, they are the ONLY institutions permitted to accept demand deposits and savings deposits.
  • 有限制牌照銀行 (Restricted Licence Banks, RLBs):
    只可接受定期存款 (Time Deposits)。最低存款額為 HK$500,000，沒有最短存款期限制。
    Can only accept time deposits. The minimum deposit amount is HK$500,000, with no restriction on minimum maturity.
  • 接受存款公司 (Deposit-taking Companies, DTCs):
    只可接受定期存款 (Time Deposits)。最低存款額為 HK$100,000，最短存款期為 3個月 (3 months)。
    Can only accept time deposits. The minimum deposit amount is HK$100,000, and the minimum maturity is 3 months.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A. 只有 (1) / (1) only

分析 (1) 持牌銀行: 正確。持牌銀行可以接受任何種類、任何金額及任何期限的存款，因此絕對可以接受黎先生這筆 $500,000 的活期存款。

Analysis for (1) Licensed bank: Correct. Licensed banks can accept deposits of any type, any amount, and any maturity. Therefore, they can absolutely accept Mr. Lai's demand deposit of $500,000.

分析 (2) 有限制牌照銀行: 錯誤。雖然黎先生的存款額達到 $500,000（符合有限制牌照銀行的最低金額要求），但題目指明這是活期存款 (Demand Deposit)。有限制牌照銀行不可接受活期存款。

Analysis for (2) Restricted licence bank: Incorrect. Although Mr. Lai's deposit amount reaches $500,000 (meeting the minimum amount requirement for RLBs), the question specifies this is a demand deposit. RLBs are not allowed to accept demand deposits.

分析 (3) 接受存款公司: 錯誤。接受存款公司同樣不可接受活期存款。此外，這筆存款的期限只有兩個月，亦不符合接受存款公司「最少三個月」的期限規定。

Analysis for (3) Deposit-taking company: Incorrect. DTCs are also not allowed to accept demand deposits. Furthermore, the maturity of this deposit is only two months, which fails to meet the "minimum 3 months" requirement for DTCs.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解答這類題目時，我們需要找出題目的「關鍵字 (Pivot Points)」。

When answering this type of question, we need to identify the "Pivot Points" in the question.
  • 關鍵字 1：活期存款 (Demand Deposit) → 最致命的條件！ 一看到「活期」或「儲蓄」，答案必定只有持牌銀行。
  • 干擾資訊：$500,000 → 考評局故意設定這個金額，因為它是有限制牌照銀行的最低存款額，企圖引誘沒有看清楚「活期」二字的同學選擇包含 (2) 的選項。
  • 干擾資訊：兩個月 (Two months) → 活期存款本來就沒有固定期限，這裡寫「兩個月」只是為了進一步擾亂考生的思緒。
  • Pivot Point 1: Demand Deposit → The most lethal condition! Once you see "demand" or "savings", the answer must be Licensed Banks only.
  • Distractor: $500,000 → The HKEAA intentionally set this amount because it is the minimum deposit for RLBs, trying to trick students who missed the word "demand" into choosing options containing (2).
  • Distractor: Two months → Demand deposits inherently do not have a fixed maturity. Stating "two months" here is just to further confuse candidates.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：只看數字，不看存款種類！

很多同學一看到 \( \$500,000 \)，腦海中立刻浮現「有限制牌照銀行 (RLB)」的定義，便急不及待地選擇了包含 (2) 的選項 (例如選項 C)。請記住，存款種類 (Type of deposit) 的優先級別最高。如果不是定期存款，後面所有的金額和期限都不用看了！

Trap: Only looking at the numbers, ignoring the deposit type!

Many students immediately think of "Restricted Licence Banks (RLB)" when they see \( \$500,000 \), and hastily choose options containing (2) (like Option C). Remember, the type of deposit has the highest priority. If it's not a time deposit, you can ignore all the amounts and maturity periods that follow!

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q1: 如果題目改成「定期存款 (Time Deposit)」，答案會是甚麼？

A1: 如果是 $500,000 的定期存款，期限為兩個月：
(1) 持牌銀行：可以接受。
(2) 有限制牌照銀行：可以接受（金額 \(\ge \$500,000\)，無期限限制）。
(3) 接受存款公司：不可以接受（雖然金額 \(\ge \$100,000\)，但期限少於 3 個月）。
所以答案會變成 (1) 和 (2)。

Q1: What if the question was changed to "Time Deposit"? What would the answer be?

A1: If it were a time deposit of $500,000 for two months:
(1) Licensed Bank: Can accept.
(2) Restricted Licence Bank: Can accept (Amount \(\ge \$500,000\), no maturity limit).
(3) Deposit-taking Company: CANNOT accept (Although amount \(\ge \$100,000\), the maturity is less than 3 months).
So the answer would become (1) and (2).

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

「秒殺」技巧 (One-second Kill Trick):

在 DSE 經濟科中，只要題目出現 「活期存款 (Demand Deposit) / 往來戶口 (Current Account)」 或 「儲蓄存款 (Savings Deposit)」，你完全不需要理會後面的金額和時間，直接選 「只有持牌銀行 (Licensed Banks only)」。這是一個 100% 準確的秒殺法則！

One-second Kill Trick:

In HKDSE Economics, whenever a question mentions "Demand Deposit / Current Account" or "Savings Deposit", you completely DO NOT need to look at the amount and time period that follow. Directly choose "Licensed Banks only". This is a 100% accurate shortcut!
