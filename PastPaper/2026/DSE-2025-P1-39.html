HKDSE Economics Tutor Guide

Topic: Fiscal Policy & Aggregate Demand (財政政策與總需求) Difficulty: ★★★★☆ Key Concepts: Aggregate Demand, Government Expenditure, Taxes

The Question (題目)

假設政府透過削減醫療服務支出以支付薪俸稅的退稅，而退稅的金額和削減支出的金額相同。
這對總需求的影響屬 ______。

A. 擴張性
B. 收縮性
C. 中性
D. 未能決定

Suppose a government reduces its expenditure on medical services to provide a tax rebate on salaries tax, and the amount of the tax rebate equals the amount of reduction in the expenditure.
The effect on the aggregate demand will be ______.

A. expansionary
B. contractionary
C. neutral
D. indeterminate

Concept Review (概念重溫)

總需求 (Aggregate Demand, AD) 是指在特定物價水平下，經濟體系內對本地生產物品和服務的總需求量。它的組成部分包括：

$$ AD = C + I + G + (X - M) $$

其中 \( C \) 是私人消費開支，\( I \) 是投資開支，\( G \) 是政府開支，\( X-M \) 是淨出口。

  • 政府開支 (\( G \)) 的直接影響： 政府開支是總需求的直接組成部分。政府開支減少 \( \$X \)，總需求會直接減少 \( \$X \)。
  • 稅收 (\( T \)) 的間接影響： 稅收並不直接出現在總需求的公式中。退稅（即稅收減少）會增加市民的可支配收入 (Disposable Income)。然而，市民不會把所有額外收入都花掉，他們會將部分額外收入儲蓄起來。因此，消費 \( C \) 的增加量會小於退稅的金額。

Aggregate Demand (AD) is the total demand for domestically produced goods and services in an economy at a given price level. Its components are:

$$ AD = C + I + G + (X - M) $$

Where \( C \) is private consumption expenditure, \( I \) is investment expenditure, \( G \) is government expenditure, and \( X-M \) is net exports.

  • Direct effect of Government Expenditure (\( G \)): \( G \) is a direct component of AD. A decrease in government expenditure by \( \$X \) directly reduces AD by \( \$X \).
  • Indirect effect of Taxes (\( T \)): Taxes do not appear directly in the AD formula. A tax rebate (decrease in taxes) increases citizens' Disposable Income. However, citizens will not spend all of this extra income; they will save a portion of it. Therefore, the increase in consumption \( C \) will be smaller than the tax rebate amount.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: Option B (收縮性 / contractionary)

選項 A (擴張性): 錯誤。
擴張性政策會導致總需求增加。但在這個情況下，政府開支的減幅大於私人消費的增幅，因此總需求實際上是減少的。
Option A (expansionary): Incorrect.
An expansionary policy leads to an increase in AD. In this case, the decrease in government expenditure is larger than the increase in private consumption, so AD actually decreases.
選項 B (收縮性): 正確。
假設政府削減開支 \( \$100 \)，同時退稅 \( \$100 \)。
1. 削減醫療支出會令政府開支 \( G \) 減少 \( \$100 \)。這會令總需求直接減少 \( \$100 \)。
2. 退稅 \( \$100 \) 會令市民的可支配收入增加 \( \$100 \)。由於市民會將部分退稅儲蓄起來，消費 \( C \) 的增加額必定小於 \( \$100 \)（例如只增加 \( \$80 \)）。
3. 淨影響：總需求減少 \( \$100 \) 但只增加 \( \$80 \)，淨效應是總需求下降（收縮性）。
Option B (contractionary): Correct.
Suppose the government cuts expenditure by \( \$100 \) and gives a tax rebate of \( \$100 \).
1. The cut in medical expenditure reduces government expenditure \( G \) by \( \$100 \). This directly reduces AD by \( \$100 \).
2. The \( \$100 \) tax rebate increases citizens' disposable income by \( \$100 \). Because citizens will save a portion of the tax rebate, the increase in consumption \( C \) must be less than \( \$100 \) (e.g., increases by only \( \$80 \)).
3. Net effect: AD drops by \( \$100 \) but only rises by \( \$80 \). The net effect is a decrease in AD (contractionary).
選項 C (中性): 錯誤。
中性意味著總需求沒有淨改變。只有當市民把退稅的全數金額都用於消費時，總需求才會維持不變。但在現實和經濟學假設中，市民總會儲蓄一部分收入。
Option C (neutral): Incorrect.
Neutral implies no net change in AD. This would only happen if citizens spent the entire amount of the tax rebate on consumption. However, in reality and economic assumptions, citizens always save a portion of their income.
選項 D (未能決定): 錯誤。
基於「市民會儲蓄部分收入」這個基本的宏觀經濟學假設，我們可以明確判斷出總需求必定會下降，因此並非未能決定。
Option D (indeterminate): Incorrect.
Based on the fundamental macroeconomic assumption that "citizens save a portion of their income", we can definitively conclude that AD will decrease. Therefore, it is not indeterminate.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題關鍵 (Pivot Point)：

這條題目的核心在於比較「政府開支」與「稅收」對總需求影響的不對稱性 (Asymmetry)。

  • 政府開支是 \( 1:1 \) 直接影響總需求。
  • 稅收是透過消費間接影響總需求，因為存在儲蓄 (Saving)，所以影響比例是 \( < 1:1 \)。

只要明白「減少 \( G \) 的殺傷力」大於「減少 \( T \) 的刺激力」，就能秒殺此題。

Pivot Point:

The core of this question lies in comparing the asymmetry of how "Government Expenditure" and "Taxes" affect Aggregate Demand.

  • Government expenditure affects AD directly at a \( 1:1 \) ratio.
  • Taxes affect AD indirectly through consumption. Because of saving, the impact ratio is \( < 1:1 \).

As long as you understand that the "destructive power of reducing \( G \)" is greater than the "stimulating power of reducing \( T \)", you can solve this question instantly.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：誤選 C (中性)

很多同學看到「退稅金額 = 削減支出金額」，大腦就會直覺認為一加一減，互相抵消（\( +100 - 100 = 0 \)）。這是因為他們忘記了稅收不會完全轉化為消費。記住：市民收到退稅後，一定會把一部分錢存入銀行！

Trap: Mistakenly choosing C (neutral)

Many students see "tax rebate amount = expenditure reduction amount" and intuitively think the plus and minus cancel each other out (\( +100 - 100 = 0 \)). This is because they forget that taxes do not fully translate into consumption. Remember: when citizens receive a tax rebate, they will definitely deposit a portion of it into the bank as savings!

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 題目提到「醫療服務支出」，這有特別含義嗎？

A: 沒有特別含義，這只是一個干擾資訊 (Distractor)。無論政府是削減醫療支出、教育支出還是基建支出，只要它屬於政府開支 \( G \)，對總需求的直接影響機制都是一樣的。

Q: The question mentions "expenditure on medical services". Does this have a special meaning?

A: No special meaning, it is just a distractor. Whether the government cuts medical, educational, or infrastructure expenditure, as long as it falls under government expenditure \( G \), the mechanism of its direct impact on AD is exactly the same.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解口訣：平衡預算乘數效應 (Balanced Budget Effect)

在 DSE 經濟科，當政府開支 \( G \) 和稅收 \( T \) 以相同金額、相同方向變動時，總需求 \( AD \) 的最終變動方向，永遠跟隨政府開支 \( G \) 的變動方向。

  • \( G \uparrow \) 和 \( T \uparrow \) (等額) \(\rightarrow\) AD 最終上升 (擴張性)
  • \( G \downarrow \) 和 \( T \downarrow \) (等額) \(\rightarrow\) AD 最終下降 (收縮性)

本題中，政府削減支出 (\( G \downarrow \))，所以閉著眼睛也能選出總需求下降 (收縮性)！

Shortcut Rule: Balanced Budget Effect

In HKDSE Economics, when government expenditure \( G \) and taxes \( T \) change by the same amount and in the same direction, the final direction of change in Aggregate Demand \( AD \) always follows the direction of government expenditure \( G \).

  • \( G \uparrow \) and \( T \uparrow \) (equal amount) \(\rightarrow\) AD ultimately increases (expansionary)
  • \( G \downarrow \) and \( T \downarrow \) (equal amount) \(\rightarrow\) AD ultimately decreases (contractionary)

In this question, the government cuts expenditure (\( G \downarrow \)), so you can confidently choose that AD decreases (contractionary) without a second thought!
