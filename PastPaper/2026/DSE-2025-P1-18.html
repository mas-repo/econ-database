HKDSE Economics Question Analysis

主題 (Topic): 市場干預與彈性 (Market Intervention & Elasticity)

難度 (Difficulty): ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5 Stars)

關鍵概念 (Key Concepts): 從量稅 (Per-unit Tax), 稅務負擔 (Tax Burden), 總收入 (Total Revenue), 弧彈性 (Arc Elasticity)

題目 (The Question)

參考下列物品 B 的需求表。

Refer to the following demand schedule of good B.

單位價格 Unit price ($) 14 16 18 20 22
需求量 Quantity demanded (units) 50 40 30 20 10

政府對物品 B 徵收從量稅。結果，市場價格由 $16 上升至 $20，而賣家的除稅後收入減少了 $360。下列哪些陳述是正確的？

The government imposes a per unit tax on good B. As a result, the market price increases from $16 to $20 and the sellers' revenue net of tax decreases by $360. Which of the following statements are correct?

(1) 政府對物品 B 徵收 $6 的從量稅。

(1) The government imposed a $6 per unit tax on good B.

(2) 物品 B 的供應較其需求更具彈性。

(2) The supply of good B is more elastic than its demand.

(3) 在新、舊市場價格範圍內，物品 B 屬高彈性需求。

(3) Within the range of the original and new market prices, the demand for good B is elastic.

A. 只有 (1)、(2)
A. (1) and (2) only

B. 只有 (1)、(3)
B. (1) and (3) only

C. 只有 (2)、(3)
C. (2) and (3) only

D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)
D. (1), (2) and (3)

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

1. 從量稅與賣家除稅後收入 (Per-unit Tax and Sellers' Revenue Net of Tax):
當政府徵收從量稅 \(t\) 時，消費者支付的市場價格 \(P_d\) 會上升，而賣家實際收取的淨價格 \(P_s\) 為 \(P_d - t\)。
賣家的除稅後收入 = \(P_s \times Q_1\) (新交易量)。

When the government imposes a per-unit tax \(t\), the market price paid by consumers \(P_d\) increases, while the net price received by sellers \(P_s\) is \(P_d - t\).
Sellers' revenue net of tax = \(P_s \times Q_1\) (new transacted quantity).

2. 稅務負擔與彈性的關係 (Relationship between Tax Burden and Elasticity):
稅務負擔的分佈取決於需求和供應的相對彈性。「彈性較低的一方，承擔較大份額的稅款」。
消費者稅務負擔 = 新價格 - 舊價格。
賣家稅務負擔 = 稅款 - 消費者稅務負擔。

The distribution of the tax burden depends on the relative elasticity of demand and supply. "The relatively inelastic party bears a larger share of the tax burden."
Consumers' tax burden = New Price - Old Price.
Sellers' tax burden = Tax - Consumers' tax burden.

3. 總收入測試與需求彈性 (Total Revenue Test and Demand Elasticity):
如果價格上升導致總收入 (Total Revenue, \(P \times Q\)) 下降，這表示需求量下跌的百分比大於價格上升的百分比，即需求屬高彈性 (Elastic)。我們也可以使用弧彈性 (Arc elasticity) 公式來計算。

If an increase in price leads to a decrease in Total Revenue (\(P \times Q\)), it means the percentage drop in quantity demanded is greater than the percentage rise in price, indicating elastic demand. We can also calculate this using the arc elasticity formula.

答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): D

逐步分析 (Step-by-Step Analysis):

分析陳述 (1)：
原本的市場價格為 $16，從需求表可見，原來的需求量為 40 單位。因此，原本的總收入 = \(16 \times 40 = \$640\)。
徵稅後，新市場價格為 $20，新需求量為 20 單位。
設從量稅為 \(t\)。賣家每單位實收 \((20 - t)\)。
題目指出賣家除稅後收入減少了 $360，即新收入為 \(640 - 360 = \$280\)。

Analyzing Statement (1):
The original market price was $16. From the demand schedule, the original quantity demanded was 40 units. Thus, the original total revenue = \(16 \times 40 = \$640\).
After tax, the new market price is $20, and the new quantity demanded is 20 units.
Let the per-unit tax be \(t\). Sellers receive \((20 - t)\) per unit.
The question states sellers' net revenue decreases by $360, meaning the new revenue is \(640 - 360 = \$280\).

$$ (20 - t) \times 20 = 280 $$ $$ 400 - 20t = 280 $$ $$ 20t = 120 \implies t = 6 $$

計算得出從量稅為 $6。因此，陳述 (1) 正確。

The calculated per-unit tax is $6. Therefore, Statement (1) is correct.

分析陳述 (2)：
總稅款為每單位 $6。
消費者承擔的部分 = 新價格 - 舊價格 = \(20 - 16 = \$4\)。
賣家承擔的部分 = 總稅款 - 消費者負擔 = \(6 - 4 = \$2\)。
因為消費者承擔的稅款 ($4) 大於賣家承擔的稅款 ($2)，這表示需求較供應缺乏彈性（即供應較需求更具彈性）。因此，陳述 (2) 正確。

Analyzing Statement (2):
The total tax is $6 per unit.
Consumers' burden = New Price - Old Price = \(20 - 16 = \$4\).
Sellers' burden = Total Tax - Consumers' burden = \(6 - 4 = \$2\).
Since the consumers' tax burden ($4) is greater than the sellers' tax burden ($2), it means demand is less elastic than supply (i.e., supply is more elastic than demand). Therefore, Statement (2) is correct.

分析陳述 (3)：
當價格由 $16 上升至 $20 時，消費者的總開支（即未扣稅前的總收入）由 \(\$16 \times 40 = \$640\) 變成 \(\$20 \times 20 = \$400\)。
價格上升導致總收入下降，根據總收入測試，這段價格範圍內的需求屬高彈性 (Elastic)。因此，陳述 (3) 正確。

Analyzing Statement (3):
When the price increases from $16 to $20, consumers' total expenditure (total revenue before tax deduction) changes from \(\$16 \times 40 = \$640\) to \(\$20 \times 20 = \$400\).
An increase in price leads to a decrease in total revenue. According to the total revenue test, the demand within this price range is elastic. Therefore, Statement (3) is correct.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

破題關鍵 (Pivot Point)：
本題的核心在於理解「賣家的除稅後收入減少了 $360」這句話。你需要懂得將文字轉化為數學等式：
舊總收入 - 新淨收入 = 360。
只要找出舊的 \(P\) 和 \(Q\)（$16 和 40），以及新的 \(P\) 和 \(Q\)（$20 和 20），就能輕易解開稅額 \(t\)。

Pivot Point:
The core of this question lies in understanding the phrase "sellers' revenue net of tax decreases by $360". You need to translate this text into a mathematical equation:
Old Total Revenue - New Net Revenue = 360.
By identifying the old \(P\) and \(Q\) ($16 and 40) and the new \(P\) and \(Q\) ($20 and 20), you can easily solve for the tax amount \(t\).

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱 1：混淆市場價格與賣家淨價格
很多學生會錯誤地用新市場價格 ($20) 去計算賣家的新收入（即 \(20 \times 20 = 400\)），忘記了賣家需要將部分收入上繳政府。賣家的實際單價是 \((20 - t)\)。

Trap 1: Confusing Market Price with Sellers' Net Price
Many students mistakenly use the new market price ($20) to calculate the sellers' new revenue (i.e., \(20 \times 20 = 400\)), forgetting that sellers must pay a portion to the government. The sellers' actual unit price is \((20 - t)\).

陷阱 2：錯誤計算彈性
在判斷陳述 (3) 時，有些學生可能會嘗試計算點彈性而得出錯誤結論。在一段價格區間內，我們應該使用弧彈性 (arc elasticity) 或直接使用總收入測試 (Total Revenue Test) 來判斷。

Trap 2: Calculating Elasticity Incorrectly
When evaluating Statement (3), some students might try to calculate point elasticity and reach the wrong conclusion. Over a price range, we should use arc elasticity or simply apply the Total Revenue Test.

學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 題目沒有給出供應曲線，我們怎麼知道新交易量是 20 單位？
A: 雖然我們沒有供應曲線，但我們知道「市場價格」是買賣雙方達成交易的價格。既然新的市場價格（消費者面對的價格）是 $20，我們只需查看需求表，當價格為 $20 時，需求量就是 20 單位。在均衡狀態下，交易量必然等於該價格下的需求量。

Q: The question doesn't provide a supply curve. How do we know the new transacted quantity is 20 units?
A: Even without a supply curve, we know the "market price" is the price at which buyers and sellers transact. Since the new market price (faced by consumers) is $20, we simply look at the demand schedule: at $20, the quantity demanded is 20 units. In equilibrium, the transacted quantity must equal the quantity demanded at that price.

考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

速解陳述 (3)：
在考試中，千萬不要浪費時間去代入複雜的弧彈性 (arc elasticity) 公式！直接看數據：價格由 16 升到 20（升幅約 25%），數量由 40 跌到 20（跌幅達 50%）。數量跌幅明顯大於價格升幅，一眼就能看出是高彈性 (Elastic)。或者直接心算 \(16 \times 4 = 64\)，\(2 \times 2 = 4\)，總收入下跌，秒殺此選項！

Shortcut for Statement (3):
In an exam, never waste time plugging numbers into the complex arc elasticity formula! Just look at the data: Price increases from 16 to 20 (approx. 25% increase), while quantity drops from 40 to 20 (a 50% drop). The percentage drop in quantity is clearly larger than the percentage rise in price, so it's instantly recognizable as elastic. Or just do mental math: \(16 \times 4 = 64\), \(2 \times 2 = 4\), total revenue drops, option solved instantly!
