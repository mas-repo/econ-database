HKDSE Economics Tutorial

Topic: Macroeconomics - Unemployment (宏觀經濟學 - 失業) Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆ (2/5) Key Concepts: Labour Force, Employed Population, Underemployed

The Question (題目)

下表是某經濟的就業資料。

15歲或以上的人口10 000 000
勞動人口參與率*80%
失業人口500 000
就業不足的人口1 000 000

*勞動人口參與率 = 15歲或以上的人口中勞動人口所佔比例

根據以上資料，該經濟的就業人口是 ______。

A. 6 500 000
B. 7 000 000
C. 7 500 000
D. 8 000 000

The following table shows the employment data of an economy.

Population aged 15 or above10 000 000
Labour force participation rate*80%
Unemployed population500 000
Underemployed population1 000 000

*Labour force participation rate = proportion of labour force in population aged 15 or above

Based on the above data, the employed population of the economy is ______.

A. 6 500 000
B. 7 000 000
C. 7 500 000
D. 8 000 000

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這條題目，我們必須清楚了解勞動人口（Labour Force）的組成部分以及各項定義。

To answer this question, we must clearly understand the components of the Labour Force and their definitions.

  • 勞動人口 (Labour Force): 包含所有就業人口和失業人口。
    Includes all employed and unemployed populations.
  • 就業人口 (Employed Population): 包含所有全職、兼職以及就業不足 (Underemployed) 的人士。只要有工作，就算就業。
    Includes all full-time, part-time, and underemployed persons. As long as they have a job, they are considered employed.
  • 失業人口 (Unemployed Population): 沒有工作，但隨時可以工作並正在尋找工作的人。
    People without a job, but who are available for work and actively seeking employment.
$$ \text{Labour Force} = \text{Employed Population} + \text{Unemployed Population} $$ $$ \text{Labour Force} = \text{Population aged } \ge 15 \times \text{Labour Force Participation Rate} $$

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): C

計算步驟 (Calculation Steps):

  1. 首先，計算總勞動人口： \( 10,000,000 \times 80\% = 8,000,000 \)
    First, calculate the total labour force: \( 10,000,000 \times 80\% = 8,000,000 \)
  2. 然後，利用公式找出就業人口： \( \text{就業人口} = \text{勞動人口} - \text{失業人口} \)
    Then, use the formula to find the employed population: \( \text{Employed} = \text{Labour Force} - \text{Unemployed} \)
  3. \( \text{就業人口} = 8,000,000 - 500,000 = 7,500,000 \)
    \( \text{Employed Population} = 8,000,000 - 500,000 = 7,500,000 \)

選項分析 (Option Breakdown):

Option A (6 500 000): 錯誤 (Wrong)
這是將勞動人口減去失業人口後，再錯誤地減去了就業不足人口 (\( 8M - 0.5M - 1M \))。就業不足人士本身已經是就業人口的一部分，不應被扣減。
This incorrectly subtracts the underemployed population after subtracting the unemployed from the labour force (\( 8M - 0.5M - 1M \)). Underemployed persons are already part of the employed population and should not be deducted.
Option B (7 000 000): 錯誤 (Wrong)
這是錯誤地將勞動人口直接減去就業不足人口 (\( 8M - 1M \))，完全忽略了正確的公式邏輯。
This incorrectly subtracts the underemployed population directly from the labour force (\( 8M - 1M \)), completely ignoring the correct formula logic.
Option C (7 500 000): 正確 (Correct)
正確運用公式：就業人口 = 勞動人口 - 失業人口 (\( 8M - 0.5M = 7.5M \))。
Correctly applies the formula: Employed Population = Labour Force - Unemployed Population (\( 8M - 0.5M = 7.5M \)).
Option D (8 000 000): 錯誤 (Wrong)
這是總勞動人口 (Labour Force) 的數字，而不是就業人口。
This is the figure for the total Labour Force, not the Employed Population.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point):
題目的核心在於先透過「15歲或以上人口」和「參與率」計算出勞動人口。這是解開整條問題的鑰匙。

The core of the question lies in first calculating the Labour Force using the "Population aged 15 or above" and the "Participation rate". This is the key to unlocking the whole problem.

干擾資訊 (Distractors):
表中的「就業不足的人口 1 000 000」是完全不需要使用的干擾數據（煙幕）。因為就業不足人士（例如兼職想轉全職的人）在統計上已經被歸類為「就業人口」。

The "Underemployed population 1 000 000" in the table is a distractor (smoke screen) that is not needed at all. This is because underemployed individuals (e.g., part-timers wanting full-time work) are statistically already classified under the "Employed population".

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：誤解「就業不足」的分類
很多同學看到「就業不足 (Underemployed)」帶有負面字眼，就誤以為他們屬於失業，或者認為需要從就業人口中剔除。記住：只要有做工（即使工時很少），就是就業人口。

Trap: Misunderstanding the classification of "Underemployed"
Many students see the negative connotation of "Underemployed" and mistakenly think they are unemployed, or that they need to be excluded from the employed population. Remember: As long as they are working (even with very few hours), they are part of the employed population.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼我們計算時完全不理會「就業不足的人口」？
Why do we completely ignore the "Underemployed population" in our calculation?

A: 因為「就業人口」是一個大集合，裡面包含了「充分就業的人」和「就業不足的人」。當我們用 \( \text{勞動人口} - \text{失業人口} \) 時，得出的 \( 7,500,000 \) 已經包含了那 \( 1,000,000 \) 的就業不足人士。如果再減去他們，就等於把他們當作失業人士了，這是不正確的。
Because the "Employed population" is a broad category that includes both "fully employed" and "underemployed" persons. When we use \( \text{Labour Force} - \text{Unemployed} \), the resulting \( 7,500,000 \) already includes those \( 1,000,000 \) underemployed individuals. If we subtract them again, it would be equivalent to treating them as unemployed, which is incorrect.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解技巧 (Shortcut): 刪除法 (Elimination Method)
在考場上，當你看到「就業不足 (Underemployed)」這個數據時，立刻在試卷上用筆將它劃掉。這是一個經典的 DSE 陷阱。一旦排除了這個干擾，題目就變成了極簡單的兩步計算：\( (10M \times 0.8) - 0.5M \)。

Shortcut: Elimination Method
In the exam room, when you see the data for "Underemployed", immediately cross it out with your pen on the question paper. This is a classic DSE trap. Once you eliminate this distractor, the question becomes an extremely simple two-step calculation: \( (10M \times 0.8) - 0.5M \).
