Topic (課題): 國際收支平衡 (Balance of Payments)

Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts (核心概念): 經常賬 (Current Account), 資本及金融賬 (Capital and Financial Account), 外匯儲備 (Foreign Exchange Reserves)

題目 (中文)

下表顯示某國有關國際收支平衡的資料。

項目百萬元
物品差額X
服務差額-40
資本流入100
資本流出140

假設該國的外匯儲備下降了 30 百萬元，X 的值是多少？

A. -110
B. -50
C. 50
D. 110

Question (English)

The following table shows the information about the balance of payments of a country.

Item$ million
Balance on goodsX
Balance on services-40
Capital inflow100
Capital outflow140

Suppose the foreign exchange reserves of the country decrease by $30 million. What is the value of X?

A. -110
B. -50
C. 50
D. 110

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解答這道題目，我們必須理解國際收支平衡表（Balance of Payments, BoP）的基本會計恆等式。國際收支平衡表主要由三個部分組成：經常賬、資本及金融賬（不包括儲備資產），以及儲備資產。

To solve this question, we must understand the basic accounting identity of the Balance of Payments (BoP). The BoP mainly consists of three parts: the Current Account, the Capital and Financial Account (excluding reserve assets), and Reserve Assets.

1. 綜合賬餘額 (Overall BoP):

$$ \text{Overall BoP} = \text{Current Account (CA)} + \text{Capital & Financial Account (CFA)} $$

Overall BoP equals the sum of the Current Account and the Capital & Financial Account (excluding reserve assets).

2. 外匯儲備的變動 (Change in Foreign Exchange Reserves):
綜合賬餘額與外匯儲備的變動息息相關。當綜合賬出現赤字（Deficit）時，國家需要動用外匯儲備來填補差額，導致外匯儲備下降。反之亦然。

The Overall BoP is closely related to the change in foreign exchange reserves. When there is an Overall BoP deficit, the country needs to use its foreign exchange reserves to cover the shortfall, leading to a decrease in reserves. Vice versa.

$$ \text{Overall BoP} = - (\text{Change in Reserve Assets}) $$

如果外匯儲備下降 \(30\) 百萬，這意味著綜合賬餘額為 \(-30\) 百萬（即赤字 \(30\) 百萬）。

If foreign exchange reserves decrease by \(30\) million, it means the Overall BoP is \(-30\) million (i.e., a deficit of \(30\) million).

答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): C

選項 A (-110) 及 B (-50): 錯誤。這些選項通常源於對外匯儲備變動符號的誤解，或者在計算資本及金融賬時將流入和流出的方向倒置。

Options A (-110) & B (-50): Incorrect. These options usually stem from a misunderstanding of the sign of the change in foreign exchange reserves, or reversing the direction of inflows and outflows when calculating the Capital and Financial Account.

選項 C (50): 正確。讓我逐步計算：
1. 經常賬 (CA) = 物品差額 + 服務差額 = \( X + (-40) = X - 40 \)
2. 資本及金融賬 (CFA) = 資本流入 - 資本流出 = \( 100 - 140 = -40 \)
3. 綜合賬餘額 (Overall BoP) = CA + CFA = \( (X - 40) + (-40) = X - 80 \)
4. 因為外匯儲備下降了 30 百萬，所以綜合賬餘額 = \(-30\)。
5. 設定方程式：\( X - 80 = -30 \)，解得 \( X = 50 \)。

Option C (50): Correct. Let's calculate step-by-step:
1. Current Account (CA) = Balance on goods + Balance on services = \( X + (-40) = X - 40 \)
2. Capital & Financial Account (CFA) = Capital inflow - Capital outflow = \( 100 - 140 = -40 \)
3. Overall BoP = CA + CFA = \( (X - 40) + (-40) = X - 80 \)
4. Since foreign exchange reserves decreased by 30 million, Overall BoP = \(-30\).
5. Set up the equation: \( X - 80 = -30 \), which gives \( X = 50 \).

選項 D (110): 錯誤。如果學生誤以為「外匯儲備下降 30」代表綜合賬餘額為 \(+30\)，就會得出 \( X - 80 = 30 \implies X = 110 \)。這是一個常見的陷阱。

Option D (110): Incorrect. If a student mistakenly thinks "reserves decrease by 30" means the Overall BoP is \(+30\), they would calculate \( X - 80 = 30 \implies X = 110 \). This is a common trap.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題關鍵 (Pivot Point):
這條題目的核心在於理解「外匯儲備下降」的經濟含義。當一個國家的資金流出多於資金流入（即經常賬與資本賬的總和為負數），中央銀行就必須賣出外匯儲備來填補這個缺口。因此，「儲備下降 30」直接等同於「綜合賬赤字 30」或「綜合賬餘額 = -30」。

Pivot Point:
The core of this question lies in understanding the economic meaning of a "decrease in foreign exchange reserves". When a country's total capital outflow exceeds its inflow (i.e., the sum of CA and CFA is negative), the central bank must sell foreign exchange reserves to cover the gap. Therefore, "reserves decrease by 30" is directly equivalent to "Overall BoP deficit of 30" or "Overall BoP = -30".

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

正負號混淆 (Sign Confusion):
很多學生會死記硬背「國際收支平衡表總和為零」，即 \( \text{CA} + \text{CFA} + \text{Reserve Assets Change} = 0 \)。在會計賬目上，儲備下降會被記錄為貸方（Credit，正數 \(+30\)），因為賣出外匯帶來資金。如果盲目套用公式 \( (X - 40) + (-40) + 30 = 0 \)，得出 \( X - 50 = 0 \implies X = 50 \)，雖然答案正確，但概念容易混亂。最穩妥的方法是記住：儲備下降 = 綜合賬赤字 (負數)。

Sign Confusion:
Many students memorize that "the sum of BoP is zero", i.e., \( \text{CA} + \text{CFA} + \text{Reserve Assets Change} = 0 \). In accounting entries, a decrease in reserves is recorded as a credit (positive \(+30\)) because selling foreign exchange brings in funds. If you blindly apply the formula \( (X - 40) + (-40) + 30 = 0 \), you get \( X - 50 = 0 \implies X = 50 \). While the answer is correct, the concept can be confusing. The safest way is to remember: Decrease in reserves = Overall BoP deficit (negative).

學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 題目沒有提供「初次收入 (Primary Income)」和「二次收入 (Secondary Income)」，我需要假設它們為零嗎？
A: 是的。在 DSE 經濟科中，如果題目沒有提供某些細項（如要素收益淨值或經常轉移淨值），我們應假設它們為零。因此，經常賬餘額就等於物品差額加服務差額。

Q: The question doesn't provide "Primary Income" and "Secondary Income". Should I assume they are zero?
A: Yes. In DSE Economics, if the question does not provide certain sub-items (like net factor income or net current transfers), we should assume they are zero. Therefore, the Current Account balance simply equals the balance on goods plus the balance on services.

考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

一線方程式速解法 (One-line Equation Shortcut):
在選擇題中，你可以直接寫出一條方程式來節省時間：
\( \text{物品差額} + \text{服務差額} + \text{資本淨流入} = -\text{儲備變動} \)
\( X - 40 + (100 - 140) = -30 \)
\( X - 80 = -30 \implies X = 50 \)
只需 10 秒即可得出答案！

One-line Equation Shortcut:
In multiple-choice questions, you can directly write out a single equation to save time:
\( \text{Balance on Goods} + \text{Balance on Services} + \text{Net Capital Inflow} = -\text{Change in Reserves} \)
\( X - 40 + (100 - 140) = -30 \)
\( X - 80 = -30 \implies X = 50 \)
You can get the answer in just 10 seconds!
