HKDSE Economics Tutor Guide

Topic (課題): Market Intervention - Quota (市場干預 - 配額)
Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts (核心概念): Effective Quota (有效配額), Market Equilibrium (市場均衡), Substitutes (替代品)

1. The Question (題目)

假設某經濟的政府對物品 A 實施一項有效配額。若物品 A 的 ______，其配額可能變得無效。

(1) 賣家數量減少
(2) 替代品的價格下降
(3) 配額減少

A. 只有 (2)
B. 只有 (1)、(2)
C. 只有 (1)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

Suppose the government of an economy imposes an effective quota on good A. The quota of good A may become ineffective if there is ________ of good A.

(1) a decrease in the number of sellers
(2) a decrease in the price of substitutes
(3) a decrease in the quota

A. (2) only
B. (1) and (2) only
C. (1) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

配額 (Quota): 政府對某物品在特定時間內的最高生產或進口數量限制。

有效配額 (Effective Quota): 當配額數量小於市場的均衡數量 \(Q_e\) 時，配額才會發揮作用，限制交易量。

無效配額 (Ineffective Quota): 當市場的均衡數量 \(Q_e\) 小於或等於配額數量時，配額不會對市場產生實際影響。

Quota: A government-imposed limit on the maximum quantity of a good that can be produced or imported over a specific period.

Effective Quota: A quota is effective when the quota quantity is less than the market equilibrium quantity \(Q_e\). It restricts the transacted quantity.

Ineffective Quota: A quota becomes ineffective when the market equilibrium quantity \(Q_e\) is less than or equal to the quota quantity. It has no actual impact on the market.

$$ \text{Effective Quota condition: } Q_{quota} < Q_e $$ $$ \text{Ineffective Quota condition: } Q_{quota} \ge Q_e $$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option B

(1) 賣家數量減少 (a decrease in the number of sellers): 正確。賣家數量減少會導致供應減少，供應曲線向左移。這會令均衡數量 \(Q_e\) 下降。如果 \(Q_e\) 跌至低於配額數量，該配額便會變得無效。

(1) a decrease in the number of sellers: Correct. A decrease in the number of sellers leads to a decrease in supply, shifting the supply curve to the left. This causes the equilibrium quantity \(Q_e\) to fall. If \(Q_e\) falls below the quota quantity, the quota becomes ineffective.

(2) 替代品的價格下降 (a decrease in the price of substitutes): 正確。替代品（例如物品 B）價格下降，消費者會轉買物品 B，導致對物品 A 的需求減少，需求曲線向左移。這同樣會令均衡數量 \(Q_e\) 下降。若跌至配額數量以下，配額便會失效。

(2) a decrease in the price of substitutes: Correct. If the price of a substitute (e.g., good B) decreases, consumers will switch to good B, causing a decrease in demand for good A. The demand curve shifts to the left. This also causes the equilibrium quantity \(Q_e\) to fall. If it drops below the quota, the quota becomes ineffective.

(3) 配額減少 (a decrease in the quota): 錯誤。配額減少意味著數量限制變得更嚴格（配額線進一步向左移）。由於原本的配額已經有效（\(Q_{quota} < Q_e\)），進一步減少配額只會令它「更加有效」，絕不會令其變得無效。

(3) a decrease in the quota: Incorrect. A decrease in the quota means the quantity limit becomes stricter (the quota line shifts further to the left). Since the original quota was already effective (\(Q_{quota} < Q_e\)), reducing it further will only make it "more effective". It will never make it ineffective.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的關鍵點 (Pivot Point) 在於理解「如何令一項有效的干預政策失效」。

  • 原本狀態：配額有效，即 \(Q_e > Q_{quota}\)。
  • 目標狀態：配額無效，即 \(Q_e \le Q_{quota}\)。
  • 解題路徑：我們需要找出能夠令均衡數量 \(Q_e\) 大幅下降的因素（即需求減少或供應減少），或者令配額數量 \(Q_{quota}\) 大幅上升的因素。

The Pivot Point of this type of question is understanding "how to make an effective intervention policy ineffective".

  • Initial state: Quota is effective, meaning \(Q_e > Q_{quota}\).
  • Target state: Quota is ineffective, meaning \(Q_e \le Q_{quota}\).
  • Solution path: We need to find factors that cause the equilibrium quantity \(Q_e\) to decrease significantly (i.e., a decrease in demand or supply), or factors that cause the quota quantity \(Q_{quota}\) to increase significantly.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱： 學生常將「配額減少」誤以為是「干預力度減弱」。

真相： 配額是一個「上限」。上限的數值越小，代表限制越嚴格。因此，配額減少會令政策約束力更強，而不是失效。

Trap: Students often mistakenly think that a "decrease in quota" means the "intervention becomes weaker".

Truth: A quota is a "maximum limit". The smaller the number of the limit, the stricter the restriction. Therefore, a decrease in the quota makes the policy more binding, not ineffective.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 供應減少會令價格上升，這不會令配額變得更有效嗎？

A: 判斷配額是否有效，我們只看數量 (Quantity)，不看價格 (Price)。配額的定義是限制數量。只要市場在沒有干預下的均衡數量 \(Q_e\) 跌穿了配額的界線，配額就無法發揮限制數量的作用，因此變得無效。

Q: A decrease in supply causes the price to rise. Wouldn't this make the quota more effective?

A: To determine if a quota is effective, we only look at the Quantity, not the Price. A quota is defined as a quantity restriction. As long as the free-market equilibrium quantity \(Q_e\) falls below the quota line, the quota can no longer restrict the quantity, thus becoming ineffective.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

畫圖速解法 (Graphical Shortcut):

在腦海中或草稿紙上畫一個基本的供需圖，並在均衡點左側畫一條垂直線代表有效配額。任何令供需交叉點（均衡點）向左移動的因素（需求減少、供應減少），都有機會令該交叉點越過垂直線，導致配額失效。選項 (1) 和 (2) 分別是供應和需求左移，因此正確。

Graphical Shortcut:

Draw a basic supply and demand diagram in your mind or on scratch paper, and draw a vertical line to the left of the equilibrium point to represent an effective quota. Any factor that shifts the intersection of supply and demand (the equilibrium point) to the left (a decrease in demand, a decrease in supply) has the potential to move the intersection past the vertical line, making the quota ineffective. Options (1) and (2) represent leftward shifts in supply and demand respectively, so they are correct.
