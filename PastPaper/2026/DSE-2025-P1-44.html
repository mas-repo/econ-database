📊 經濟學試題分析 | Economics Question Analysis

主題 (Topic): 匯率與國際貿易 (Exchange Rate and International Trade) / 聯繫匯率制度 (Linked Exchange Rate System)

難度 (Difficulty): ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5 Stars)

關鍵字 (Key Concepts): 聯繫匯率制度 (LERS), 升值 (Appreciation), 出口總值 (Total Export Value), 需求價格彈性 (Price Elasticity of Demand)

📝 題目 (The Question)

在香港聯繫匯率制度下，當美元兌英鎊升值，______。

A. 香港出口往美國的物品總值會上升（以港元計算）
B. 香港出口往英國的物品總值會下降（以港元計算）
C. 香港從英國的進口總值會上升（以港元計算）
D. 香港從英國的進口總值會下降（以港元計算）

Under the Linked Exchange Rate System of Hong Kong, when US dollar appreciates against British pound,

A. the total value (in terms of Hong Kong dollar) of Hong Kong exports to United States will increase
B. the total value (in terms of Hong Kong dollar) of Hong Kong exports to United Kingdom will decrease
C. the total value (in terms of Hong Kong dollar) of Hong Kong imports from United Kingdom will increase
D. the total value (in terms of Hong Kong dollar) of Hong Kong imports from United Kingdom will decrease

💡 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

1. 聯繫匯率制度 (Linked Exchange Rate System - LERS):
在香港，港元 (HKD) 與美元 (USD) 掛鈎。因此，當美元兌其他貨幣（如英鎊 GBP）升值時，港元亦會跟隨美元兌該貨幣升值。

In Hong Kong, the HKD is pegged to the USD. Therefore, when the USD appreciates against another currency (like the GBP), the HKD automatically appreciates against that currency as well.

2. 匯率變動對出口總值（以本國貨幣計算）的影響:
當港元升值，香港出口貨品在英國的售價（以英鎊計算）會上升，導致英國對香港貨品的需求量下降 \(Q_x \downarrow\)。
由於香港出口商的定價（以港元計算 \(P_{HKD}\)）不變，出口總值必定下降。

When HKD appreciates, the price of HK exports in the UK (in GBP) increases, leading to a decrease in quantity demanded \(Q_x \downarrow\). Since the price set by HK exporters (in HKD \(P_{HKD}\)) remains unchanged, the total export value must decrease.
$$ \text{Total Export Value (in HKD)} = P_{HKD} (\text{Constant}) \times Q_x (\text{Decreases}) \Rightarrow \text{Decreases} $$

3. 匯率變動對進口總值（以本國貨幣計算）的影響:
當港元升值，英國進口貨品在香港的售價（以港元計算 \(P_{HKD}\)）會下降，導致香港對英國貨品的需求量上升 \(Q_m \uparrow\)。
由於價格下降而數量上升，進口總值的變化取決於進口貨品的需求價格彈性 (Price Elasticity of Demand)。

When HKD appreciates, the price of UK imports in HK (in HKD \(P_{HKD}\)) decreases, leading to an increase in quantity demanded \(Q_m \uparrow\). Since price falls and quantity rises, the change in total import value depends on the price elasticity of demand for imports.
$$ \text{Total Import Value (in HKD)} = P_{HKD} (\text{Decreases}) \times Q_m (\text{Increases}) \Rightarrow \text{Uncertain} $$

✅ 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是 (The correct option is): Option B

選項 A (Option A): 錯誤 (Incorrect)
在聯繫匯率制度下，港元與美元的匯率保持穩定。美元兌英鎊升值，並不影響港元與美元之間的兌換率。因此，香港出口往美國的物品價格和數量在短期內不會受此直接影響，總值不變。

Under the LERS, the exchange rate between HKD and USD remains stable. The appreciation of USD against GBP does not affect the HKD/USD exchange rate. Therefore, the price and quantity of HK exports to the US are not directly affected, and the total value remains unchanged.

選項 B (Option B): 正確 (Correct)
美元兌英鎊升值，意味著港元也兌英鎊升值。香港出口到英國的貨物，對英國消費者來說變得更昂貴（需要支付更多英鎊）。因此，英國對香港貨物的需求量 \(Q\) 會減少。由於這些貨物以港元標價的價格 \(P\) 不變，總值 (\(P \times Q\)) 必然下降。

USD appreciating against GBP means HKD also appreciates against GBP. HK exports to the UK become more expensive for UK consumers (they pay more GBP). Thus, the quantity demanded \(Q\) for HK goods decreases. Since the price \(P\) in terms of HKD is unchanged, the total value (\(P \times Q\)) must decrease.

選項 C & D (Options C & D): 錯誤 (Incorrect)
港元兌英鎊升值，使得英國進口貨物在香港的售價（以港元計算）下降。這會導致進口數量增加。然而，進口總值 = 港元價格（下降）× 進口數量（上升）。因為題目沒有提供香港對英國貨物的需求價格彈性數據，我們無法確定總值是上升還是下降。

HKD appreciating against GBP makes UK imports cheaper in HK (in terms of HKD). This increases the quantity imported. However, Total Import Value = Price in HKD (decreases) × Quantity (increases). Because the question does not provide the price elasticity of demand for UK goods, we cannot determine whether the total value increases or decreases.

🔍 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point):
這類題目的核心在於區分「出口總值」和「進口總值」的計算。當以本國貨幣（港元）計算時：
1. 出口總值的變化方向必定與出口數量的變化方向一致（因為本幣定價不變）。
2. 進口總值的變化則同時受到本幣價格改變和數量改變的影響，必須依賴彈性 (Elasticity) 才能判斷。

The core of this type of question is distinguishing between "Total Export Value" and "Total Import Value". When calculated in domestic currency (HKD):
1. The direction of change in Total Export Value is always the same as the change in export quantity (since domestic price is constant).
2. The change in Total Import Value is affected by both the change in domestic price and quantity, so it requires Elasticity to determine.

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

學生最常犯的錯誤是直覺地認為「進口貨品變便宜了，所以進口總值會下降」（誤選 D）。這忽略了需求量會增加的事實。如果需求是富有彈性的 (elastic)，進口總值反而會上升！

The most common mistake is intuitively thinking "imported goods are cheaper, so total import value decreases" (wrongly choosing D). This ignores the fact that quantity demanded will increase. If the demand is elastic, the total import value will actually increase!

🙋‍♂️ 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼出口貨品的港元價格 \(P_{HKD}\) 會假設為不變？
A: 在 DSE 經濟學的標準假設中，本地生產商是以本地貨幣（港元）來支付成本和定價的。匯率變動影響的是外國買家兌換港元時所需的成本，而不是香港生產商的標價。

Q: Why is the HKD price of exports \(P_{HKD}\) assumed to be constant?
A: Under standard DSE Economics assumptions, local producers pay costs and set prices in the local currency (HKD). Exchange rate fluctuations affect the cost for foreign buyers to acquire HKD, not the price tag set by HK producers.

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

秒殺法 (The Elimination Method):
在選擇題中，只要看到問及「進口總值 (Total Import Value)」的改變，但題目完全沒有提及彈性 (Elasticity)，你可以立刻刪除這些選項（即 C 和 D）。因為沒有彈性數據，進口總值的變化永遠是「不確定 (Uncertain)」。

Shortcut: In MCQs, whenever you see options asking about the change in "Total Import Value" but the question mentions absolutely nothing about Elasticity, you can immediately eliminate those options (i.e., C and D). Without elasticity data, the change in import value is always "Uncertain".
