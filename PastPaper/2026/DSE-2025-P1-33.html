HKDSE Economics Tutorial

Topic: 貨幣與銀行 (Money and Banking) Difficulty: ⭐⭐ (2/5)
Key Concepts: 法定儲備比率 (Required Reserve Ratio), 銀行乘數 (Banking Multiplier), 貨幣基礎 (Monetary Base), 貨幣供應 (Money Supply)

題目 (The Question)

若銀行系統的法定儲備比率為100%，下列哪項陳述是正確的？

A. 中央銀行不能影響該經濟的貨幣供應。
B. 銀行仍可作為金融中介，將儲蓄引導作投資。
C. 貨幣供應不會等於貨幣基礎。
D. 最大銀行乘數會等於一。

Which of the following statements is correct if the required reserve ratio of a banking system is 100%?

A. The central bank cannot affect the money supply of the economy.
B. Banks can still serve as financial intermediaries that channel savings into investment.
C. The money supply will not be equal to the monetary base.
D. The maximum banking multiplier will be equal to one.

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解答這道題目，我們必須清楚理解在「100% 法定儲備比率」下，銀行系統的運作會發生什麼根本性的改變。

To answer this question, we must clearly understand the fundamental changes in the banking system's operation under a "100% required reserve ratio".

  • 法定儲備比率 (Required Reserve Ratio, \(RRR\)): 銀行必須保留作儲備的存款比例。若 \(RRR = 100\%\)，代表銀行必須將所有存款作為儲備，不能借出任何款項 (超額儲備為零)。
    The percentage of deposits that banks must keep as reserves. If \(RRR = 100\%\), it means banks must keep all deposits as reserves and cannot lend out any money (excess reserves are zero).
  • 最大銀行乘數 (Maximum Banking Multiplier): 衡量銀行系統創造存款的最大能力。
    Measures the maximum ability of the banking system to create deposits.
    $$ \text{Maximum Banking Multiplier} = \frac{1}{RRR} $$
  • 貨幣基礎 (Monetary Base, \(MB\)) 與 貨幣供應 (Money Supply, \(M_s\)):
    貨幣基礎是公眾持有的法定貨幣 (\(C_p\)) 加上銀行儲備 (\(R\))。貨幣供應是公眾持有的法定貨幣 (\(C_p\)) 加上存款 (\(D\))。
    Monetary Base is cash in public hands (\(C_p\)) plus bank reserves (\(R\)). Money Supply is cash in public hands (\(C_p\)) plus deposits (\(D\)).
    $$ MB = C_p + R $$ $$ M_s = C_p + D $$

答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): D

A. 中央銀行不能影響該經濟的貨幣供應。 (錯誤 / Incorrect)

即使銀行不能創造存款，中央銀行依然可以透過發行更多紙幣及硬幣（增加公眾持有的現金 \(C_p\)）來直接增加貨幣供應。因此，央行仍能影響貨幣供應。

Even if banks cannot create deposits, the central bank can still directly increase the money supply by issuing more notes and coins (increasing cash in public hands \(C_p\)). Therefore, the central bank can still affect the money supply.

B. 銀行仍可作為金融中介，將儲蓄引導作投資。 (錯誤 / Incorrect)

金融中介的作用是吸收存款並借出貸款。當 \(RRR = 100\%\) 時，銀行必須將 100% 的存款留在金庫作為儲備，無法借出任何貸款。因此，銀行失去了將儲蓄轉化為投資的金融中介功能。

The role of a financial intermediary is to take deposits and make loans. When \(RRR = 100\%\), banks must keep 100% of deposits in their vaults as reserves and cannot make any loans. Thus, banks lose their financial intermediary function of channeling savings into investment.

C. 貨幣供應不會等於貨幣基礎。 (錯誤 / Incorrect)

根據公式，\(MB = C_p + R\) 且 \(M_s = C_p + D\)。因為 \(RRR = 100\%\)，銀行沒有超額儲備，所以實際儲備等於存款 (\(R = D\))。將 \(R = D\) 代入公式，我們會發現 \(MB = C_p + D\)，這剛好等於 \(M_s\)。因此，貨幣供應必定等於貨幣基礎。

According to the formulas, \(MB = C_p + R\) and \(M_s = C_p + D\). Because \(RRR = 100\%\), banks have no excess reserves, so actual reserves equal deposits (\(R = D\)). Substituting \(R = D\) into the formula, we get \(MB = C_p + D\), which is exactly equal to \(M_s\). Therefore, the money supply will be equal to the monetary base.

D. 最大銀行乘數會等於一。 (正確 / Correct)

根據最大銀行乘數公式：\( \text{乘數} = \frac{1}{RRR} \)。當 \(RRR = 100\%\) (即 1) 時，最大銀行乘數 \( = \frac{1}{1} = 1 \)。這意味著銀行系統無法創造額外的存款，1元的儲備最多只能支持1元的存款。

According to the maximum banking multiplier formula: \( \text{Multiplier} = \frac{1}{RRR} \). When \(RRR = 100\%\) (which is 1), the maximum banking multiplier \( = \frac{1}{1} = 1 \). This means the banking system cannot create additional deposits; $1 of reserves can only support a maximum of $1 of deposits.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point):

這題的關鍵在於理解 「100% 法定儲備比率」 的物理意義。這代表 貸款 (Loans) = 0 且 儲備 (Reserves) = 存款 (Deposits)。

The key to this question is understanding the physical meaning of a "100% required reserve ratio". It means Loans = 0 and Reserves = Deposits.

一旦你確立了 \(R = D\) 這個等式，選項 C 的謬誤就不攻自破；而一旦你知道銀行不能借錢，選項 B 也會立刻被排除。

Once you establish the equation \(R = D\), the fallacy of Option C is immediately exposed; and once you know banks cannot lend money, Option B is instantly eliminated.

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱：混淆貨幣基礎 (MB) 與貨幣供應 (Ms)

很多同學死記「存款創造會令貨幣供應大於貨幣基礎」，卻忽略了這是在 \(RRR < 100\%\) 的前提下才成立。當 \(RRR = 100\%\) 時，沒有存款創造過程，兩者是完全相等的。

Trap: Confusing Monetary Base (MB) and Money Supply (Ms)
Many students memorize that "deposit creation makes the money supply greater than the monetary base", but they forget this is only true under the premise that \(RRR < 100\%\). When \(RRR = 100\%\), there is no deposit creation process, and the two are exactly equal.

學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 如果銀行不能借錢出去，那銀行靠什麼賺錢？
Q: If banks cannot lend money out, how do they make a profit?

A: 在現實世界中，法定儲備比率極少會是100%（通常只有百分之幾）。這題是一個極端的理論假設，用來測試同學對銀行乘數和貨幣定義的理解。如果真的實行100%儲備，銀行將無法透過「存貸息差」賺錢，只能靠收取保管費或手續費來維持營運。

A: In the real world, the required reserve ratio is rarely 100% (usually just a few percent). This question is an extreme theoretical assumption used to test students' understanding of the banking multiplier and money definitions. If a 100% reserve system were actually implemented, banks could not make money through the "interest rate spread" and would have to rely on charging custody or service fees to operate.

考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

秒殺技巧 (One-Second Kill):

看到「最大銀行乘數」和「法定儲備比率」，立刻在腦海中彈出公式：\( m = \frac{1}{RRR} \)。
題目給定 \( RRR = 100\% = 1 \)，所以 \( m = \frac{1}{1} = 1 \)。直接選 D，需時不到 5 秒！

When you see "maximum banking multiplier" and "required reserve ratio", immediately recall the formula: \( m = \frac{1}{RRR} \).
The question gives \( RRR = 100\% = 1 \), so \( m = \frac{1}{1} = 1 \). Choose D directly, taking less than 5 seconds!
