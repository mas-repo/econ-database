HKDSE Economics Tutor Analysis

Topic (課題): Market Structure - Perfect Competition (市場結構 - 完全競爭)

Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆

Key Concepts (核心概念): Price Taker (受價者) Homogeneous Goods (同質物品) Perfect Information (完全資訊)

The Question (題目)

廠商 X 是一間完全競爭市場的受價廠商。下列哪些有關廠商 X 的陳述是正確的？

(1) 廠商 X 進行價格競爭和非價格競爭。
(2) 若廠商 X 將其物品價格提高至高於市場價格，它將流失所有顧客。
(3) 顧客無法將廠商 X 銷售的物品和市場上其他廠商所銷售的物品作出區別。

A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

Firm X is a price-taking firm in a perfectly competitive market. Which of the following statements about Firm X are correct?

(1) Firm X engages in both price competition and non-price competition.
(2) If Firm X increases its price of goods above the market price, it will lose all of its customers.
(3) Customers cannot differentiate the goods sold by Firm X from those sold by other firms in the market.

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這道題目，我們必須清楚了解完全競爭市場 (Perfectly Competitive Market) 的四大特徵：

To answer this question, we must clearly understand the four main characteristics of a Perfectly Competitive Market:
  • 同質物品 (Homogeneous Goods): 市場上所有廠商生產的物品都是完全一樣的，顧客無法分辨。
    All firms in the market produce identical goods; customers cannot differentiate them.
  • 大量買家與賣家 (Many Buyers and Sellers): 每個廠商的產量佔市場總產量的比例極小，因此只能是受價者 (Price Taker)。
    Each firm's output is a tiny fraction of total market output, making them Price Takers.
  • 完全資訊 (Perfect Information): 買賣雙方對市場價格和產品質素瞭如指掌。
    Buyers and sellers have full knowledge of market prices and product quality.
  • 自由加入及退出 (Free Entry and Exit): 廠商可以自由進出市場，沒有任何障礙。
    Firms can freely enter or exit the market without any barriers.

對於受價者而言，其面對的需求曲線是完全彈性的 (Perfectly Elastic)。

For a price taker, the demand curve it faces is perfectly elastic.
$$ P = MR = AR $$

這意味著廠商在市場價格 \(P\) 下可以售出任何數量的物品，但若提價，需求量將跌至零。

This means the firm can sell any quantity at the market price \(P\), but if it raises the price, quantity demanded drops to zero.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是 (Correct Option): Option C

陳述 (1) 分析 / Analysis of Statement (1): 錯誤 (False)

在完全競爭市場中，廠商是受價者，只能按市場價格出售產品，因此不會進行價格競爭。同時，由於所有產品都是同質的（完全一樣），賣廣告或包裝等非價格競爭也是多此一舉。因此，廠商 X 不會進行這兩種競爭。

In a perfectly competitive market, firms are price takers and can only sell at the market price, so they do not engage in price competition. Also, since all products are homogeneous (identical), advertising or packaging (non-price competition) is pointless. Therefore, Firm X engages in neither.

陳述 (2) 分析 / Analysis of Statement (2): 正確 (True)

由於市場存在完全資訊，且產品是同質的，如果廠商 X 將價格提高至高於市場價格，理性的消費者會立刻轉向其他以市場價格出售相同產品的廠商購買。因此，廠商 X 會流失所有顧客（其面對的需求曲線是完全彈性的）。

Because there is perfect information and goods are homogeneous, if Firm X raises its price above the market price, rational consumers will immediately switch to other firms selling the exact same good at the market price. Thus, Firm X will lose all customers (its demand curve is perfectly elastic).

陳述 (3) 分析 / Analysis of Statement (3): 正確 (True)

這正是「同質物品」(Homogeneous goods) 的定義。市場上所有廠商提供的產品在物理特徵和消費者認知上都是完全相同的，顧客無法區分。

This is the exact definition of "Homogeneous goods". The products offered by all firms are identical in physical characteristics and consumer perception; customers cannot differentiate them.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point): 題目的關鍵字是「完全競爭市場」(perfectly competitive market) 和「受價廠商」(price-taking firm)。看到這兩個詞，你腦海中必須立刻浮現四大特徵，特別是「同質物品」和「無競爭行為」。

Pivot Point: The keywords are "perfectly competitive market" and "price-taking firm". Seeing these, the four characteristics should immediately pop into your mind, especially "homogeneous goods" and "no competitive behavior".

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

學生常犯的錯誤是將「完全競爭」與現實生活中的「激烈競爭」混淆。在日常用語中，「競爭激烈」意味著瘋狂減價或大量賣廣告（即價格與非價格競爭）。但在經濟學的定義中，「完全競爭」達到了一個極致，廠商反而不需要也不會作出任何競爭行為，因為他們能以市價賣出所有產出。

A common mistake is confusing "perfect competition" with "intense competition" in real life. In daily language, intense competition means crazy price cuts or massive advertising. But in economics, "perfect competition" is an extreme where firms actually do not need to and will not engage in any competitive behavior, as they can sell all their output at the market price.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 既然廠商 X 提價會流失所有顧客，那為什麼它不減價來吸引更多顧客呢？

Q: Since Firm X loses all customers if it raises the price, why doesn't it lower the price to attract more customers?

A: 因為廠商 X 在現時的市場價格下，已經可以售出它想要出售的任何數量（面對完全彈性的需求曲線）。減價雖然能賣出產品，但只會白白減少總收入和利潤，對廠商來說是不理性的行為。

A: Because at the current market price, Firm X can already sell any quantity it wants to (facing a perfectly elastic demand curve). Lowering the price would just unnecessarily reduce its total revenue and profit, which is irrational for the firm.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

消去法 (Elimination Method):
在多項選擇題中，一旦你確定陳述 (1) 是錯誤的（完全競爭沒有價格/非價格競爭），你可以立即剔除選項 A、B 和 D（因為它們都包含 (1)）。這樣你甚至不需要詳細分析 (2) 和 (3)，就能在一秒鐘內得出正確答案 C！

Elimination Method:
In MCQs, once you determine that statement (1) is false (no price/non-price competition in perfect competition), you can immediately eliminate options A, B, and D (since they all contain (1)). You don't even need to deeply analyze (2) and (3) to arrive at the correct answer C in one second!
