📍 Topic (課題): Market Failure - Externalities (市場失效 - 界外影響)
⭐ Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆
🔑 Key Concepts (關鍵概念): Negative Externality (負面界外影響), Overproduction (過度生產), Efficiency Loss (效率損失)

1. The Question (題目)

當有負面界外影響時，市場會出現 ______，而其有效率的產出水平會 ______ 其均衡產出水平。

When there is a negative externality, ________ occurs in the market and its efficient output level is ________ its equilibrium output level.

  • A. 過度生產 …… 高於
    A. overproduction ...... above
  • B. 過度生產 …… 低於
    B. overproduction ...... below
  • C. 生產不足 …… 高於
    C. underproduction ...... above
  • D. 生產不足 …… 低於
    D. underproduction ...... below

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

負面界外影響 (Negative Externality)： 指生產或消費活動對沒有參與交易的第三者造成了未被補償的成本。這意味著社會承擔的成本高於私人承擔的成本。

Negative Externality: Occurs when a production or consumption activity imposes an uncompensated cost on a third party not involved in the transaction. This means the cost borne by society is higher than the private cost.

$$ MSC = MPC + MEC $$

其中 \(MSC\) 是邊際社會成本，\(MPC\) 是邊際私人成本，\(MEC\) 是邊際外部成本（大於零）。因此，\(MSC > MPC\)。

Where \(MSC\) is Marginal Social Cost, \(MPC\) is Marginal Private Cost, and \(MEC\) is Marginal External Cost (greater than zero). Therefore, \(MSC > MPC\).

均衡產出 (Equilibrium Output, \(Q_m\))： 由私人市場決定，條件是邊際私人利益等於邊際私人成本 (\(MPB = MPC\))。

Equilibrium Output (\(Q_m\)): Determined by the private market, where Marginal Private Benefit equals Marginal Private Cost (\(MPB = MPC\)).

有效率產出 (Efficient Output, \(Q_e\))： 社會資源達到最佳分配的水平，條件是邊際社會利益等於邊際社會成本 (\(MSB = MSC\))。

Efficient Output (\(Q_e\)): The level where social resources are optimally allocated, where Marginal Social Benefit equals Marginal Social Cost (\(MSB = MSC\)).

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是：B

The correct option is: B

選項 A (錯誤)： 雖然負面界外影響確實會導致「過度生產」，但有效率的產出水平是「低於」而非「高於」均衡產出水平。

Option A (Incorrect): While a negative externality does lead to "overproduction", the efficient output level is "below", not "above", the equilibrium output level.

選項 B (正確)： 因為存在負面界外影響，\(MSC > MPC\)。假設沒有正面界外影響 (\(MSB = MPB\))。由於社會成本較高，社會最適宜的產量 \(Q_e\) 會少於私人市場決定的產量 \(Q_m\)。因此，有效率產出「低於」均衡產出 (\(Q_e < Q_m\))。既然實際生產量 \(Q_m\) 大於理想產量 \(Q_e\)，市場便出現了「過度生產」，並導致效率損失 (Efficiency Loss)。

Option B (Correct): Due to the negative externality, \(MSC > MPC\). Assuming no positive externality (\(MSB = MPB\)). Because the social cost is higher, the socially optimal output \(Q_e\) will be less than the private market output \(Q_m\). Thus, the efficient output is "below" the equilibrium output (\(Q_e < Q_m\)). Since the actual output \(Q_m\) is greater than the ideal output \(Q_e\), "overproduction" occurs in the market, leading to an efficiency loss.

選項 C & D (錯誤)： 「生產不足」(Underproduction) 是正面界外影響 (Positive Externality) 的特徵，而非負面界外影響。當有正面界外影響時，\(MSB > MPB\)，才會導致生產不足。

Options C & D (Incorrect): "Underproduction" is a characteristic of a positive externality, not a negative one. Underproduction occurs when there is a positive externality and \(MSB > MPB\).

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解答這類題目的關鍵樞紐 (Pivot Point) 在於將文字轉換為數學邏輯：

The Pivot Point to solving this type of question is translating the text into mathematical logic:

  • 第一步：看到「負面界外影響」，立刻聯想到 \(MSC > MPC\)。

    Step 1: Seeing "negative externality", immediately recall \(MSC > MPC\).

  • 第二步：因為成本被低估了（私人成本低於社會成本），自私的市場參與者會生產得「太多」。這就確定了第一個空格是「過度生產」。

    Step 2: Because costs are underestimated (private cost is lower than social cost), selfish market participants will produce "too much". This confirms the first blank is "overproduction".

  • 第三步：釐清主語和賓語。題目問的是「有效率的產出水平 (\(Q_e\))」相比於「均衡產出水平 (\(Q_m\))」是如何。既然生產過度 (\(Q_m > Q_e\))，反過來說，有效率產出 \(Q_e\) 就是「低於」均衡產出 \(Q_m\)。

    Step 3: Clarify the subject and object. The question asks how the "efficient output level (\(Q_e\))" compares to the "equilibrium output level (\(Q_m\))". Since there is overproduction (\(Q_m > Q_e\)), conversely, the efficient output \(Q_e\) is "below" the equilibrium output \(Q_m\).

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ 粗心陷阱：語意反轉
很多學生知道負面界外影響會導致過度生產 (\(Q_m > Q_e\))，但在閱讀第二個空格時太快，誤以為題目問「均衡產出」對比「有效率產出」，從而錯誤選擇了「高於」。請務必看清楚題目問的是「有效率產出」對比「均衡產出」，所以答案是「低於」。

⚠️ Careless Trap: Semantic Reversal
Many students know negative externalities lead to overproduction (\(Q_m > Q_e\)), but read the second blank too quickly, mistakenly thinking the question asks for "equilibrium output" compared to "efficient output", thus wrongly choosing "above". Always read carefully: the question asks for "efficient output" compared to "equilibrium output", so the answer is "below".

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

問：題目沒有提到利益 (Benefit)，我們怎麼知道 MSB 和 MPB 的關係？

Q: The question didn't mention benefits. How do we know the relationship between MSB and MPB?

答： 在經濟學選擇題中，我們通常採用「單一變數原則」。既然題目只指明了「負面界外影響」，我們就假設沒有正面界外影響的存在。因此，我們預設邊際社會利益等於邊際私人利益 (\(MSB = MPB\))，只需專注於成本面的分析即可。

A: In economics MCQs, we usually apply the ceteris paribus principle. Since the question only specifies a "negative externality", we assume no positive externality exists. Therefore, we presume Marginal Social Benefit equals Marginal Private Benefit (\(MSB = MPB\)) and solely focus on analyzing the cost side.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

邏輯連貫法 (Logical Consistency Method)：
這題其實可以秒殺！「過度生產」在字面上的意思就是「實際生產量 (均衡) > 理想生產量 (有效率)」。
換句話說：理想 (有效率) < 實際 (均衡)。
因此，只要第一個空格填了「過度生產」，第二個空格在邏輯上必須是「低於」。選項 A (過度生產 + 高於) 在邏輯上是自相矛盾的。利用這個語意邏輯，你可以直接排除 A 和 C。

Logical Consistency Method:
You can solve this instantly! "Overproduction" literally means "Actual output (Equilibrium) > Ideal output (Efficient)".
In other words: Ideal (Efficient) < Actual (Equilibrium).
Therefore, if the first blank is "overproduction", the second blank must logically be "below". Option A (overproduction + above) is logically self-contradictory. Using this semantic logic, you can immediately eliminate A and C.
