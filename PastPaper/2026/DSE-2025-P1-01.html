HKDSE Economics Tutor 解析

Topic (課題): Basic Economic Concepts (基本經濟概念) - Scarcity (稀少性)

Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆ (3/5)

Key Concepts (核心概念): Scarcity (稀少性) Opportunity Cost (機會成本) Interest (利息) Comparative Advantage (比較優勢)

The Question (題目)

沒有稀少性， ______ 並不存在。

(1) 利息
(2) 比較優勢
(3) 短缺

A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

In the absence of scarcity, _____ does NOT exist.

(1) interest
(2) comparative advantage
(3) shortage

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

Concept Review (概念重溫)

稀少性 (Scarcity) 是經濟學的核心。它指的是人類的慾望大於可用資源的數量。因為稀少性的存在，我們必須作出選擇 (Choice)，而選擇必然伴隨著機會成本 (Opportunity Cost)。

Scarcity is the core of economics. It refers to the situation where human wants exceed the available resources. Because of scarcity, we must make choices, and every choice inevitably involves an opportunity cost.

如果沒有稀少性 (In the absence of scarcity)：

  • 資源是無限的，所有人的所有慾望都能被完全滿足。
  • Resources are infinite, and all wants of everyone can be fully satisfied.
  • 由於不需要作出取捨，機會成本等於零 \( (Opportunity Cost = 0) \)。
  • Since no trade-offs are needed, opportunity cost equals zero \( (Opportunity Cost = 0) \).
  • 物品的價格為零 \( (Price = 0) \)，沒有競爭。
  • The price of goods is zero \( (Price = 0) \), and there is no competition.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是 (Correct Option): D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

分析 (1) 利息 (Interest) - 不存在 (Does NOT exist)

利息是提早獲取物品所需要支付的代價（或延遲消費的補償）。如果沒有稀少性，人們現在和未來的慾望都能瞬間被無限滿足，根本不需要為了「提早享受」而支付額外的代價。因此，利息不會存在。

Interest is the premium paid for the earlier availability of goods (or compensation for deferring consumption). If there is no scarcity, people's present and future wants can be infinitely satisfied instantly. There is absolutely no need to pay an extra premium for "earlier enjoyment". Therefore, interest would not exist.

分析 (2) 比較優勢 (Comparative Advantage) - 不存在 (Does NOT exist)

比較優勢是建立在機會成本的基礎上的。當一個生產者生產某物品的機會成本低於另一個生產者時，他便擁有比較優勢。如果沒有稀少性，所有生產的機會成本都是零。既然大家的機會成本都是零，就沒有所謂的「較低機會成本」，比較優勢自然不存在。

Comparative advantage is based on opportunity cost. A producer has a comparative advantage when their opportunity cost of producing a good is lower than another producer's. If there is no scarcity, the opportunity cost of all production is zero. Since everyone's opportunity cost is zero, there is no such thing as a "lower opportunity cost", and comparative advantage naturally does not exist.

分析 (3) 短缺 (Shortage) - 不存在 (Does NOT exist)

短缺是指在某一價格下，需求量大於供應量 \( (Q_d > Q_s) \)。如果沒有稀少性，即使價格為零 \( (P = 0) \)，供應量也足以滿足所有的需求量 \( (Q_s \ge Q_d) \)。因此，不可能出現供不應求的情況，短缺不會存在。

A shortage occurs when the quantity demanded is greater than the quantity supplied at a certain price \( (Q_d > Q_s) \). If there is no scarcity, even at a price of zero \( (P = 0) \), the quantity supplied is sufficient to meet all quantity demanded \( (Q_s \ge Q_d) \). Therefore, a situation of excess demand is impossible, and shortages would not exist.

Problem Decomposition & Common Traps (題目拆解與常犯錯誤)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point):
這類題目的核心在於將「沒有稀少性」翻譯為經濟學語言：「機會成本 = 0」以及「價格 = 0」。只要把這個條件代入各個選項，就能輕鬆破解。

Pivot Point: The core of this type of question is to translate "no scarcity" into economic language: "Opportunity Cost = 0" and "Price = 0". By applying this condition to each option, you can easily solve it.

常犯錯誤 (Common Mistakes):
部分同學會誤以為「時間」永遠是稀少的，所以認為利息 (1) 依然存在。但在 DSE 經濟學的嚴格假設下，「沒有稀少性」是一個烏托邦式的極端假設，意味著所有慾望（包括對時間的慾望）都能無代價地被滿足。既然不需要等待，提早消費的代價（利息）就是零。

Common Mistakes: Some students mistakenly believe that "time" is always scarce, so they think interest (1) still exists. However, under the strict assumptions of DSE Economics, "no scarcity" is a utopian extreme assumption, meaning all wants (including temporal ones) can be satisfied costlessly. Since there is no need to wait, the cost of earlier consumption (interest) is zero.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果沒有稀少性，那麼「絕對優勢 (Absolute Advantage)」還存在嗎？

A: 這是一個很好的問題！在沒有稀少性的情況下，討論絕對優勢（誰能用較少資源生產更多物品）變得沒有意義，因為資源是無限的。不過，嚴格來說，DSE 考試通常只會考「比較優勢」必然消失，因為它直接與機會成本掛鈎。只要記住：沒有稀少性 \(\rightarrow\) 沒有選擇 \(\rightarrow\) 沒有機會成本 \(\rightarrow\) 沒有比較優勢。

Q: If there is no scarcity, does "Absolute Advantage" still exist?

A: That's a great question! In the absence of scarcity, discussing absolute advantage (who can produce more goods with fewer resources) becomes meaningless because resources are infinite. However, strictly speaking, the DSE exam usually only tests the inevitable disappearance of "comparative advantage" because it is directly linked to opportunity cost. Just remember: No Scarcity \(\rightarrow\) No Choice \(\rightarrow\) No Opportunity Cost \(\rightarrow\) No Comparative Advantage.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

連鎖推論法 (Chain Deduction):
在選擇題中看到「沒有稀少性 (No Scarcity)」，立刻在腦海中閃過以下等式：
無稀少性 = 無需選擇 = 無機會成本 = 無價格 = 無競爭 = 無利息 = 無比較優勢 = 無短缺。
看到這三個選項都在這個「消滅名單」上，直接秒選 D，節省考試時間！

Chain Deduction: When you see "No Scarcity" in MCQs, immediately flash the following equation in your mind:
No Scarcity = No Choice = No Opportunity Cost = No Price = No Competition = No Interest = No Comparative Advantage = No Shortage.
Seeing that all three options are on this "elimination list", choose D instantly to save exam time!
