HKDSE Economics 經濟學試題分析

Topic / 課題 國民收入會計 (National Income Accounting)

Difficulty / 難度 ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts / 核心概念 GDP, GNI, Net Factor Income from Abroad (對外要素收益淨值), Resident (常住居民)

The Question (題目)

在計算香港的本地居民總收入時，下列哪項應在香港的本地生產總值加上？

  1. 一家英國投資公司從香港的商業物業投資賺取的租金收入
  2. 一位香港居民從美國大學獲取的獎學金
  3. 一位菲律賓家庭傭工為本港某家庭工作十八個月所收取的薪金
  4. 一家香港製造廠商從印度的工廠投資賺得的利潤

Which of the following should be added to the GDP of Hong Kong to calculate the gross national income of Hong Kong?

  1. the rental income earned by a UK investment corporation from its commercial property investment in Hong Kong
  2. the scholarship received by a Hong Kong resident from a university in the US
  3. the salary received by a Filipino domestic worker who worked for a Hong Kong family for 18 months
  4. the profit earned by a Hong Kong manufacturing firm from its investment in factories in India

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這條題目，我們必須清楚區分本地生產總值 (GDP) 與 本地居民總收入 (GNI) 的關係。

To answer this question, we must clearly distinguish the relationship between Gross Domestic Product (GDP) and Gross National Income (GNI).

1. 核心公式 (Core Formulas)

$$ \text{GNI} = \text{GDP} + \text{Net Factor Income from Abroad (NIA)} $$ $$ \text{本地居民總收入} = \text{本地生產總值} + \text{對外要素收益淨值} $$
$$ \text{NIA} = \text{Factor income from abroad} - \text{Factor income paid abroad} $$ $$ \text{對外要素收益淨值} = \text{從外地賺取的要素收益} - \text{支付予外地的要素收益} $$

2. 關鍵定義 (Key Definitions)

  • GDP (本地生產總值): 以「地域」為界線。只要在該經濟領域內進行生產，無論生產者是否本地居民，都會計入 GDP。
    GDP is "territory-based". As long as production takes place within the economic territory, it is included in GDP, regardless of whether the producer is a resident or not.
  • GNI (本地居民總收入): 以「居民」為界線。只要是該經濟體系的常住居民 (Resident) 所賺取的要素收益，無論在哪裡賺取，都會計入 GNI。
    GNI is "resident-based". As long as the factor income is earned by a resident of the economy, it is included in GNI, regardless of where it is earned.
  • Resident (常住居民): 在該經濟領域內逗留或打算逗留最少12個月的個人或機構。國籍 (Nationality) 並不重要！
    Individuals or organizations staying or intending to stay in the economic territory for at least 12 months. Nationality does not matter!
  • Transfer Payment (轉移支付): 沒有涉及生產或提供服務的收入（如獎學金、綜援）。這類收入不會計入 GDP 或 GNI。
    Income that does not involve production or provision of services (e.g., scholarships, social security). These are NOT included in GDP or GNI.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): Option D

選項 A (Option A): 錯誤 (Incorrect)

英國投資公司屬於「非本地居民」(Non-resident)。他們在香港賺取的租金屬於「支付予外地的要素收益」(Factor income paid abroad)。在計算 GNI 時，這筆款項需要從 GDP 中扣除，而不是加上。

The UK investment corporation is a "non-resident". The rental income they earn in Hong Kong is "factor income paid abroad". When calculating GNI, this amount should be deducted from GDP, not added.

選項 B (Option B): 錯誤 (Incorrect)

獎學金屬於「單向轉移支付」(Transfer payment)，因為學生並沒有提供任何生產要素（如勞力或資本）來換取這筆金錢。因此，它既不計入 GDP，也不計入 GNI，無需進行任何加減。

A scholarship is a "transfer payment" because the student did not provide any factor of production (like labor or capital) in exchange for the money. Therefore, it is excluded from both GDP and GNI, requiring no addition or deduction.

選項 C (Option C): 錯誤 (Incorrect)

這位菲律賓家庭傭工在香港工作了十八個月（超過12個月的界線），因此在經濟學上她被視為香港的「常住居民」(Resident)。她的薪金是在香港境內賺取的，已經包含在香港的 GDP 中；同時因為她是居民，這筆收入也屬於香港的 GNI。由於它同時存在於 GDP 和 GNI 中，我們不需要對 GDP 作出任何加減來計算 GNI。

The Filipino domestic worker has worked in Hong Kong for 18 months (exceeding the 12-month threshold), so in economics, she is considered a Hong Kong "resident". Her salary is earned within Hong Kong and is already included in Hong Kong's GDP; since she is a resident, this income also belongs to Hong Kong's GNI. Because it is present in both GDP and GNI, we do not need to add or subtract anything from GDP to calculate GNI.

選項 D (Option D): 正確 (Correct)

香港製造廠商是香港的「常住居民」(Resident)。他們從印度工廠賺取的利潤，屬於「從外地賺取的要素收益」(Factor income earned from abroad)。由於這筆利潤不是在香港境內產生，它沒有包含在香港的 GDP 中；但因為是由香港居民賺取，它必須計入香港的 GNI。因此，我們必須把這筆款項加上香港的 GDP 來計算 GNI。

The Hong Kong manufacturing firm is a Hong Kong "resident". The profit they earn from factories in India is "factor income earned from abroad". Since this profit is not generated within Hong Kong, it is not included in Hong Kong's GDP; however, because it is earned by a Hong Kong resident, it must be included in Hong Kong's GNI. Therefore, we must add this amount to Hong Kong's GDP to calculate GNI.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

面對這類題目，我們需要進行「三步過濾法」：

When facing this type of question, we need to use a "Three-Step Filtration Method":
  1. 是否要素收益？ (Is it factor income?)
    排除所有轉移支付（如選項 B 的獎學金）。
    Eliminate all transfer payments (e.g., the scholarship in Option B).
  2. 是誰賺取的？ (Who earned it?)
    判斷賺取者是「常住居民」還是「非本地居民」。選項 C 的「十八個月」是關鍵資訊 (Pivot Point)，用來確認外傭是常住居民。
    Determine if the earner is a "resident" or "non-resident". The "18 months" in Option C is the pivot point used to confirm the domestic worker is a resident.
  3. 在哪裡賺取的？ (Where was it earned?)
    題目要求「加上」(Added to GDP)，這意味著我們在尋找：本地居民 + 在外地賺取。只有選項 D 符合這個條件。
    The question asks what should be "added" to GDP, meaning we are looking for: Resident + Earned Abroad. Only Option D fits this condition.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 陷阱一：混淆國籍與常住居民身份 (Confusing Nationality with Residency)
    很多學生看到「菲律賓家庭傭工」或「英國投資公司」，就立刻以國籍來判斷他們是非本地居民。記住，經濟學上的 Resident 是看逗留時間（通常為12個月）及經濟利益中心。選項 C 故意寫出「十八個月」來測試你是否知道這個定義。
    Many students judge residency based on nationality when they see "Filipino worker" or "UK corporation". Remember, in economics, a Resident is defined by the duration of stay (usually 12 months) and center of economic interest. Option C deliberately states "18 months" to test if you know this definition.
  • 陷阱二：忘記轉移支付不計入國民收入 (Forgetting Transfer Payments are Excluded)
    學生可能會誤以為獎學金是從外國流入香港的資金，所以要加上。但只要沒有涉及生產，就不屬於 GDP 或 GNI 的計算範圍。
    Students might mistakenly think a scholarship is money flowing into Hong Kong from abroad and should be added. But as long as no production is involved, it falls outside the scope of GDP or GNI calculation.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果選項 C 的菲律賓傭工只在香港工作了 3 個月，答案會改變嗎？

A: 會的！如果她只工作了 3 個月，她會被視為「非本地居民」(Non-resident)。在這種情況下，她在香港賺取的薪金屬於「支付予外地的要素收益」(Factor income paid abroad)。這筆錢包含在香港的 GDP 中，但在計算 GNI 時必須扣除。不過，即使如此，她仍然不是這道題的正確答案，因為題目問的是哪項應該「加上」(added)。

Q: If the Filipino domestic worker in Option C only worked in Hong Kong for 3 months, would the answer change?

A: Yes! If she only worked for 3 months, she would be considered a "non-resident". In that case, the salary she earned in HK would be "factor income paid abroad". It is included in HK's GDP, but must be deducted when calculating GNI. However, even then, it wouldn't be the correct answer to this specific question, because the question asks what should be "added".

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺法 (The One-Second Kill Method):

看到題目問：如何從 GDP 計算 GNI 時需要「加上」(Add) 什麼？

你的大腦應該立刻浮現公式：\( \text{GNI} = \text{GDP} + \text{本地居民從外地賺取的收入} - \text{非本地居民在本地賺取的收入} \)。

既然是「加上」，目標只有一個：尋找選項中同時包含「香港居民/公司」以及「在外地/外國賺錢」的組合。掃視四個選項，只有 D（香港廠商 + 印度工廠）完美符合！

Shortcut: When the question asks what must be "added" to GDP to get GNI, your brain should immediately recall: \( \text{GNI} = \text{GDP} + \text{Income earned by residents abroad} - \text{Income earned by non-residents domestically} \).

Since it's "added", you are looking for ONE specific combination: "Hong Kong resident/firm" AND "earning money abroad". Scanning the options, only D (HK firm + Indian factories) perfectly matches!
