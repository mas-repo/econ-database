HKDSE Economics Question Analysis

Topic: Price Elasticity of Supply (供給價格彈性) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts: Price Elasticity of Supply (PES), Shift in Demand, Determinants of Elasticity

The Question (題目)

假設日本受到地震威脅。結果，日本旅遊套票的價格下跌 20%，而其交易量改變了 30%。下列哪項最能解釋日本旅遊套票的價格與交易量有上述的轉變？

A. 日本旅遊套票有很多接近的替代品。
B. 在勞工市場上能容易招聘或裁減提供日本旅遊套票服務的工人。
C. 進入和離開日本旅遊套票服務的市場受到限制。
D. 消費者在日本旅遊套票的開支佔其總開支的比例低。

Suppose there is a threat of earthquake in Japan. As a result, there is a 20% decrease in the price of Japan tour packages and a 30% decrease in its quantity transacted. Which of the following best explains the above changes in the price and quantity transacted of Japan tour packages?

A. There are many close substitutes for Japan tour packages.
B. The workers required in providing Japan tour package services can be easily recruited or dismissed in the labour market.
C. The entry into and exit from the market of Japan tour package services is restricted.
D. Consumers' expenditure on Japan tour packages takes up a small proportion of their total expenditure.

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這條問題，我們必須理解需求變動 (Shift in Demand)與供給價格彈性 (Price Elasticity of Supply, PES)之間的關係。當需求曲線移動時，新的均衡點會沿著原有的供給曲線移動。因此，價格和交易量的百分比變動，實際上反映了供給價格彈性。

To answer this question, we must understand the relationship between a Shift in Demand and the Price Elasticity of Supply (PES). When the demand curve shifts, the new equilibrium moves along the original supply curve. Therefore, the percentage changes in price and quantity transacted actually reflect the price elasticity of supply.
$$E_s = \frac{\%\Delta Q_s}{\%\Delta P}$$

供給價格彈性的決定因素 (Determinants of PES):
供給彈性取決於生產者對價格變動的反應能力。如果生產要素（如勞工）的流動性高，或者進入/退出市場很容易，供給就會是富有彈性 (Elastic, \(E_s > 1\))。

Determinants of PES:
Supply elasticity depends on producers' ability to respond to price changes. If factor mobility (e.g., labour) is high, or entry/exit into the market is easy, supply will be Elastic (\(E_s > 1\)).

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): B

首先，計算彈性數值：
First, calculate the elasticity value:

$$E_s = \frac{30\%}{20\%} = 1.5$$

因為 \(E_s > 1\)，這代表供給是富有彈性的 (Elastic supply)。
Since \(E_s > 1\), this indicates that the supply is elastic.

Option A (錯誤 / Incorrect):

替代品的多寡是決定「需求價格彈性」(PED) 的因素，而不是供給彈性。這無法解釋為何供給是富有彈性的。

The availability of substitutes is a determinant of Price Elasticity of Demand (PED), not supply elasticity. This cannot explain why supply is elastic.
Option B (正確 / Correct):

容易招聘或裁減工人代表生產要素的流動性高 (High factor mobility)。當價格下跌時，生產商可以輕易減少生產規模（裁員），導致供給量大幅下降。這正正解釋了為何供給富有彈性 (\(E_s = 1.5 > 1\))。

Easy recruitment or dismissal of workers implies high factor mobility. When the price falls, producers can easily scale down production (dismiss workers), leading to a proportionally larger decrease in quantity supplied. This perfectly explains why supply is elastic (\(E_s = 1.5 > 1\)).
Option C (錯誤 / Incorrect):

進入和離開市場受到限制會使供給變得「缺乏彈性」(Inelastic, \(E_s < 1\))，這與我們計算出 \(E_s = 1.5\) 的結果矛盾。

Restricted entry and exit would make supply "inelastic" (\(E_s < 1\)), which contradicts our calculated result of \(E_s = 1.5\).
Option D (錯誤 / Incorrect):

開支佔總開支的比例低是決定「需求價格彈性」(PED) 較低的因素，與供給彈性無關。

A small proportion of total expenditure is a determinant of a lower Price Elasticity of Demand (PED), which is irrelevant to supply elasticity.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (解題關鍵):
地震威脅會導致消費者對日本旅遊的需求減少（需求曲線向左移）。當需求移動時，均衡點會沿著供給曲線 (Supply Curve)移動。因此，題目提供的價格與交易量變動百分比，是用來計算供給彈性 (Elasticity of Supply)，而非需求彈性！

Pivot Point:
The earthquake threat causes a decrease in consumer demand for Japan tours (Demand curve shifts left). When demand shifts, the equilibrium moves along the Supply Curve. Therefore, the percentage changes in price and quantity provided in the question are used to calculate the Elasticity of Supply, NOT demand!

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：混淆需求彈性與供給彈性
很多學生看到價格和交易量的改變，就直覺以為要找「需求彈性」的決定因素，從而錯誤選擇 A 或 D。緊記口訣：「需求移動，測量供給彈性；供給移動，測量需求彈性」。

Trap: Confusing Demand Elasticity with Supply Elasticity
Many students see changes in price and quantity and intuitively think they need to find determinants of "Demand Elasticity", mistakenly choosing A or D. Remember the rule: "A shift in Demand measures Supply Elasticity; a shift in Supply measures Demand Elasticity."

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼地震威脅不是影響供給？
A: 地震「威脅」主要影響消費者的預期和偏好（怕危險所以不去），這直接導致需求下降。除非地震已經發生並摧毀了基建（如酒店、機場），那才會導致供給減少。

Q: Why doesn't the earthquake threat affect supply?
A: An earthquake "threat" primarily affects consumer expectations and preferences (fear of danger), which directly decreases demand. Unless the earthquake has already occurred and destroyed infrastructure (like hotels or airports), supply wouldn't necessarily decrease.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺法 (Elimination Method):
1. 判斷事件：「地震威脅」= 需求下跌。
2. 判斷彈性：需求變動 = 沿著供給曲線移動 = 考量供給彈性 (PES)。
3. 篩選選項：選項 A 和 D 都是影響需求彈性 (PED) 的因素，直接剔除！剩下 B 和 C。計算出彈性大於 1，選代表高彈性的 B。

Shortcut:
1. Identify the event: "Earthquake threat" = Demand decreases.
2. Identify the elasticity: Demand shifts = Movement along Supply = We are looking at Supply Elasticity (PES).
3. Filter options: Options A and D are determinants of Demand Elasticity (PED). Eliminate them immediately! Only B and C remain. Since calculated elasticity is > 1, choose B which represents elastic supply.
