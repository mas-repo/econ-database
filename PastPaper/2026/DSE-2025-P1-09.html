📚 經濟學題目分析 | Economics Problem Analysis

主題 (Topic): 企業所有權形式 (Forms of Business Ownership)

難度 (Difficulty): ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5 Stars)

關鍵字 (Key Concepts): 獨資經營 (Sole Proprietorship) 私人有限公司 (Private Limited Company) 利得稅 (Profits Tax) 延續性 (Continuity)

📝 題目 | The Question

在香港，若一家獨資經營變成了一家私人有限公司，______。

(1) 該廠商會有更多東主
(2) 在賺取相同利潤下，該廠商會面對較高的利得稅稅率
(3) 該廠商會享有延續性

A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

In Hong Kong, if a sole proprietorship is turned into a private limited company, __________.

(1) there will be more owners in the firm
(2) the firm will face a higher profits tax rate for the same amount of profit earned
(3) the firm will enjoy lasting continuity

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

💡 概念重溫 | Concept Review

1. 獨資經營 (Sole Proprietorship): 由一名東主擁有及控制的企業。它沒有獨立法定地位，東主需承擔無限責任，且企業缺乏延續性。

A business owned and controlled by one owner. It has no separate legal entity, the owner bears unlimited liability, and the business lacks continuity.

2. 私人有限公司 (Private Limited Company): 由 1 至 50 名股東組成的企業。它擁有獨立法定地位（Separate Legal Entity），股東只承擔有限責任，並且企業享有永久延續性（Perpetual Succession）。

A business formed by 1 to 50 shareholders. It has a separate legal entity, shareholders bear limited liability, and the business enjoys perpetual succession.

3. 香港利得稅率 (Hong Kong Profits Tax Rate): 法團（如有限公司）的稅率通常高於非法團業務（如獨資經營和合夥）。

The tax rate for corporations (like limited companies) is generally higher than that of unincorporated businesses (like sole proprietorships and partnerships).

稅款計算公式 / Tax Calculation Formula:

$$ \text{Tax Amount} = \text{Assessable Profits} \times \text{Tax Rate} $$

For any profit $P > 0$ :

$$ \text{Tax Rate}_{Corporation} > \text{Tax Rate}_{Unincorporated} $$

✅ 答案與詳細分析 | The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 (Correct Option)： Option C

分析句子 (1) | Analyzing Statement (1): 錯誤 (False)

獨資經營有 $1$ 名東主。雖然私人有限公司最多可以有 $50$ 名股東，但它的最低要求只是 $1$ 名股東（即一人公司）。因此，變成私人有限公司不一定會有更多東主。

A sole proprietorship has $1$ owner. While a private limited company can have up to $50$ shareholders, its minimum requirement is only $1$ shareholder (a one-person company). Therefore, converting into a private limited company does not necessarily mean there will be more owners.

分析句子 (2) | Analyzing Statement (2): 正確 (True)

在香港稅制下，法團（有限公司）的利得稅稅率（例如標準稅率 16.5%）高於非法團業務（如獨資經營，標準稅率 15%）。因此在賺取相同利潤 $P$ 的情況下，有限公司需繳交較高比例的稅款。

Under the Hong Kong tax system, the profits tax rate for corporations/limited companies (e.g., standard rate 16.5%) is higher than that for unincorporated businesses (like sole proprietorships, standard rate 15%). Therefore, for the same amount of profit $P$, a limited company pays a higher proportion in taxes.

分析句子 (3) | Analyzing Statement (3): 正確 (True)

私人有限公司是獨立法定實體，其存在獨立於其擁有者。即使股東破產或死亡，公司的運作也不會受影響，這稱為「永久延續性」。

A private limited company is a separate legal entity, existing independently of its owners. Even if a shareholder goes bankrupt or passes away, the company's operations remain unaffected. This is known as "perpetual succession" or lasting continuity.

🔍 題目拆解 | Problem Decomposition

這類題目的破題關鍵 (Pivot Point) 在於對不同企業形式的「極端值」的認知，以及香港特定的稅制知識：

  • 陷阱點： 很多同學一看到「公司」(Company)，就直覺以為一定是由「一群人」組成。但法律上，私人有限公司是可以由單一持股人成立的（即一人公司），所以人數不一定增加！
  • 知識點： 必須牢記香港的法團與非法團業務稅率是有差異的。政府賦予有限公司「有限責任」的優勢，代價之一就是較高的利得稅率。

The Pivot Point of this question lies in understanding the "extreme values" of business forms and specific HK tax knowledge:
- The Trap: Many students intuitively think a "company" must consist of a "group" of people. However, legally, a private limited company can be formed by a single shareholder (a one-person company), so the number of owners does not necessarily increase!
- Key Knowledge: You must remember that HK applies different tax rates to incorporated and unincorporated businesses. The government grants limited liability to companies, and one of the trade-offs is a higher profits tax rate.

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 | Common Mistakes & Traps

❌ 錯誤認知 (Misconception): 「有限公司 = 多個老闆」

✔️ 現實 (Reality): 根據香港《公司條例》，成立私人有限公司最少只需 $1$ 名創辦成員（股東）及 $1$ 名董事。一人完全可以同時兼任股東及董事。

❌ Misconception: "Limited Company = Multiple Owners"
✔️ Reality: Under the HK Companies Ordinance, forming a private limited company requires a minimum of only $1$ founding member (shareholder) and $1$ director. One person can act as both.

🙋‍♂️ 學生常見疑問 | Student Q&A

問 (Q): 既然有限公司的利得稅率較高，為什麼還有人要把獨資經營轉為有限公司？

答 (A): 主要是為了「有限責任」(Limited Liability) 的保障。在獨資經營中，東主需承擔無限責任，如果生意失敗，東主可能要變賣個人資產來還債。有限公司有獨立法定地位，股東的最大損失僅限於其投資本金（股份價值），個人資產不受牽連。此外，有限公司較容易向銀行融資。

Q: Since the profits tax rate is higher for limited companies, why would anyone convert a sole proprietorship into one?
A: Primarily for the protection of "Limited Liability." In a sole proprietorship, the owner bears unlimited liability; if the business fails, personal assets may be seized to pay off business debts. A limited company is a separate legal entity, meaning a shareholder's maximum loss is restricted to their invested capital (value of shares), keeping personal assets safe. Also, it is easier for companies to secure bank financing.

Definitive Elimination Method:
In DSE Economics MCs, if you are 100% sure about one statement, you can quickly eliminate options:
