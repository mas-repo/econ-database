HKDSE Economics Tutorial

Topic (課題): Public Finance - Taxation in Hong Kong (公共財政 - 香港的稅制)

Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆

Key Concepts (核心概念): Stamp Duty (印花稅), Direct vs Indirect Taxes (直接稅與間接稅), Hong Kong Tax System (香港稅制)

The Question (題目)

假設李先生在香港的股票市場購買了滙豐銀行的股票。數月後，滙豐銀行賺取利潤，並向其股東派發股息。其後，李先生出售他持有的滙豐銀行股票，並獲取資本增值。
根據以上資料，李先生要向香港政府支付 ______。

  • A. 股息稅
  • B. 利得稅
  • C. 資本增值稅
  • D. 印花稅

Suppose Mr Li purchased shares of HSBC in the stock market of Hong Kong. A few months later, HSBC earned profits and distributed dividends to its shareholders. Later, Mr Li sold his HSBC shares and earned a capital gain.
Based on the above information, Mr Li needs to pay ______ to the Hong Kong Government.

  • A. dividend tax
  • B. profits tax
  • C. capital gains tax
  • D. stamp duty

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這道題目，我們必須清楚了解香港的稅制特點。香港實行簡單及低稅率的稅制，並非所有類型的收入都需要徵稅。

To answer this question, we must clearly understand the characteristics of Hong Kong's tax system. Hong Kong implements a simple and low-tax system; not all types of income are subject to taxation.

  • 香港沒有的稅項 (Taxes NOT in Hong Kong): 香港沒有徵收資本增值稅 (Capital Gains Tax)、股息稅 (Dividend Tax) 及銷售稅 (Sales Tax)。
    Hong Kong does not levy Capital Gains Tax, Dividend Tax, and Sales Tax.
  • 利得稅 (Profits Tax): 僅向在香港經營任何行業、專業或業務，並從中獲得於香港產生或得自香港的利潤的法團或人士徵收。個人投資者買賣股票的利潤通常不屬於此類。
    Levied only on corporations or persons carrying on any trade, profession or business in Hong Kong, in respect of their assessable profits arising in or derived from Hong Kong. Profits from individual investors trading stocks generally do not fall under this category.
  • 印花稅 (Stamp Duty): 是一種間接稅，主要對特定文件徵收，例如樓宇買賣合約、租約以及香港股票的買賣單據。
    An indirect tax levied mainly on specific documents, such as property transaction contracts, tenancy agreements, and contract notes for the sale and purchase of Hong Kong stocks.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): D

選項分析 (Option Analysis):

選項 A (股息稅 / Dividend tax): 錯誤 (Incorrect)
雖然李先生收到了股息，但香港並沒有設立股息稅。因此，他不需要為此繳稅。
Although Mr. Li received dividends, Hong Kong does not have a dividend tax. Therefore, he does not need to pay tax on this.

選項 B (利得稅 / Profits tax): 錯誤 (Incorrect)
利得稅是針對在香港經營業務並獲取利潤的實體（如公司）。李先生作為個人投資者買賣股票，其資本增值不屬於應課利得稅的業務利潤。
Profits tax is aimed at entities (like companies) carrying on a business and earning profits in Hong Kong. As an individual investor, Mr. Li's capital gains from trading stocks do not constitute assessable business profits.

選項 C (資本增值稅 / Capital gains tax): 錯誤 (Incorrect)
雖然題目明確提到李先生獲取了「資本增值」，但香港的稅制中並沒有資本增值稅。
Even though the question explicitly states Mr. Li earned a "capital gain", there is no capital gains tax in Hong Kong's tax system.

選項 D (印花稅 / Stamp duty): 正確 (Correct)
李先生在香港股票市場購買及出售股票，這涉及股票買賣單據的轉讓。根據香港法律，買賣香港證券必須繳納印花稅。
Mr. Li purchased and sold shares in the HK stock market, which involves the transfer of contract notes for stock transactions. Under Hong Kong law, buying and selling Hong Kong securities is subject to stamp duty.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的核心在於測試學生是否能分辨「經濟行為」與「香港實際稅制」的關係。

The core of this type of question is to test whether students can distinguish the relationship between "economic behaviors" and the "actual Hong Kong tax system".

關鍵點 (Pivot Point):
題目描述了三個動作：買入股票、收取股息、賣出股票獲利。解題的關鍵在於知道香港政府對這三個動作的徵稅規定。
The question describes three actions: buying shares, receiving dividends, and selling shares for profit. The key to solving it is knowing the HK government's taxation rules for these three actions.

干擾資訊 (Distractors):
題目故意加入「派發股息 (distributed dividends)」和「資本增值 (capital gain)」這些字眼，誘使不熟悉香港稅制的學生直接選擇與之字面匹配的 A (股息稅) 或 C (資本增值稅)。這是一個典型的文字陷阱。
The question deliberately includes phrases like "distributed dividends" and "capital gain" to lure students unfamiliar with the HK tax system into directly choosing the literally matching options A (dividend tax) or C (capital gains tax). This is a classic word trap.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：望文生義 (Trap: Taking words too literally)
很多學生看到題目有甚麼字眼，就選甚麼稅。看到「利潤 (Profits)」就選利得稅，看到「資本增值 (Capital gain)」就選資本增值稅。在經濟學中，我們必須根據該地區的實際制度來判斷，不能單憑字面猜測。
Many students choose the tax that matches the words in the question. They see "Profits" and choose Profits Tax; they see "Capital gain" and choose Capital Gains Tax. In Economics, we must judge based on the actual system of the region, not just guess from literal meanings.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 滙豐銀行賺取了利潤，難道不需要交利得稅嗎？
Q: HSBC earned profits, doesn't it need to pay profits tax?

A: 滙豐銀行作為一間在香港經營的企業，其賺取的利潤需要繳交利得稅。但是，題目的問題是「李先生要向香港政府支付甚麼」，李先生是個人投資者，他收取的股息和買賣股票的差價不需要交利得稅。
A: As an enterprise operating in HK, HSBC does need to pay profits tax on its profits. However, the question asks what "Mr. Li needs to pay". Mr. Li is an individual investor; he does not pay profits tax on his dividends or the price difference from trading stocks.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

刪除法 (Elimination Method)
對於香港稅制的題目，請牢記「香港沒有的三大稅」：沒有資本增值稅、沒有股息稅、沒有銷售稅。一看到這三個選項，可以直接劃掉 (Cross out A and C)。
剩下 B (利得稅) 和 D (印花稅)。因為主角是「個人投資者 (Mr. Li)」而非「公司 (Corporation)」，所以不會是利得稅。瞬間就能鎖定答案 D！
For questions on the HK tax system, memorize the "Three Taxes HK Does NOT Have": No Capital Gains Tax, No Dividend Tax, No Sales Tax. Once you see these options, cross them out immediately (Cross out A and C).
You are left with B (Profits Tax) and D (Stamp Duty). Since the subject is an "individual investor (Mr. Li)" and not a "corporation", it won't be Profits Tax. You can instantly lock in answer D!
