HKDSE Economics Question Analysis

Topic: Opportunity Cost & Full Cost (機會成本與全部成本) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Opportunity Cost, Time Cost, Benefit vs Cost

The Question (題目)

深圳的私家牙醫收費一般較香港的私家牙醫低。儘管前往深圳需時較長，許多香港居民仍選擇前往深圳私家牙醫求診。

下列哪些有關上述現象的陳述是正確的？

  1. 當過境所需時間增加，香港居民前往深圳私家牙醫求診的成本會上升。
  2. 兩地私家牙醫的服務質素不會影響香港居民前往深圳私家牙醫求診的成本。
  3. 那些時間成本較高的香港居民傾向在本地私家牙醫求診，而非前往深圳私家牙醫求診。

A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

Private dentists in Shenzhen generally charge lower fees than those in Hong Kong. Despite the longer travel time to Shenzhen, many Hong Kong residents still choose to visit private dentists in Shenzhen.

Which of the following statements about the above phenomenon are correct?

  1. The cost of Hong Kong residents to visit private dentists in Shenzhen would increase if more time is required to cross the border.
  2. The service quality of private dentists in both places would not affect the cost of Hong Kong residents to visit private dentists in Shenzhen.
  3. Those Hong Kong residents with higher time costs tend to visit local private dentists in Hong Kong instead of private dentists in Shenzhen.

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

Concept Review (概念重溫)

機會成本 (Opportunity Cost)

機會成本是指在面臨選擇時，被放棄的選項中價值最高的一個（Highest-valued option forgone）。當我們考慮一項活動的成本時，必須考慮「全部成本」（Full Cost）。

Opportunity cost is the highest-valued option forgone when a choice is made. When considering the cost of an activity, we must look at the "Full Cost".

$$ \text{Full Cost} = \text{Explicit Cost (Money)} + \text{Implicit Cost (Time/Effort)} $$ $$ \text{全部成本} = \text{顯性成本（金錢）} + \text{隱性成本（時間/精力）} $$

成本與利益的區別 (Cost vs. Benefit)

成本 (Cost) 是你為了得到某事物而放棄的東西（例如：金錢、時間、其他選擇的價值）。
利益 (Benefit) 是你從該選擇中獲得的好處（例如：服務質素、滿足感）。

Cost is what you give up to get something (e.g., money, time, value of alternatives).
Benefit is what you gain from the choice (e.g., service quality, satisfaction).

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: B / 正確答案：B

分析陳述 (1) - 正確 (Correct)

「當過境所需時間增加，香港居民前往深圳私家牙醫求診的成本會上升。」
前往深圳的全部成本包括金錢成本（牙醫收費、車費）和時間成本。如果過境時間增加，市民需要放棄更多時間（這些時間本可用於工作賺錢或享受閒暇），因此隱性成本上升，導致總成本增加。

"The cost of Hong Kong residents to visit private dentists in Shenzhen would increase if more time is required to cross the border."
The full cost of going to Shenzhen includes money cost (dental fees, transport) and time cost. If border crossing takes more time, residents must give up more time (which could be used for working or leisure). Thus, the implicit cost rises, increasing the total cost.

分析陳述 (2) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

「兩地私家牙醫的服務質素不會影響香港居民前往深圳私家牙醫求診的成本。」
這是一個常見陷阱！前往深圳看牙醫的「被放棄的最高價值選項」通常是「在香港看牙醫」。如果香港私家牙醫的服務質素提升，留在香港看牙醫的價值就會增加。這意味著，當你選擇去深圳時，你放棄了一個「更好」的選項。因此，香港牙醫質素的改變會影響（增加）前往深圳的機會成本。另一方面，深圳牙醫的質素影響的是前往深圳的「利益」(Benefit)，而非成本。

"The service quality of private dentists in both places would not affect the cost of Hong Kong residents to visit private dentists in Shenzhen."
This is a classic trap! The highest-valued option forgone for visiting a Shenzhen dentist is often "visiting a Hong Kong dentist". If the service quality of Hong Kong dentists improves, the value of staying in HK increases. This means by choosing Shenzhen, you are giving up a "better" option. Therefore, the quality of HK dentists does affect (increase) the opportunity cost of going to Shenzhen. Meanwhile, the quality of Shenzhen dentists affects the "benefit" of going to Shenzhen, not the cost.

分析陳述 (3) - 正確 (Correct)

「那些時間成本較高的香港居民傾向在本地私家牙醫求診，而非前往深圳私家牙醫求診。」
高收入人士（如專業人士）的每小時工資較高，因此他們的時間成本（隱性成本）很高。對他們來說，花費數小時前往深圳的總成本，可能遠高於直接在香港支付較昂貴的牙醫費用。因此，他們更傾向留在香港以節省時間。

"Those Hong Kong residents with higher time costs tend to visit local private dentists in Hong Kong instead of private dentists in Shenzhen."
High-income earners (e.g., professionals) have a higher hourly wage, meaning their time cost (implicit cost) is very high. For them, the full cost of spending hours traveling to Shenzhen might be much higher than simply paying the expensive dental fee in Hong Kong. Thus, they tend to stay in HK to save time.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解答這類題目的關鍵樞紐 (Pivot Point) 在於釐清「成本」(Cost) 的定義，並找出「被放棄的選項」(Forgone Option)。

The Pivot Point to solving this type of question is clarifying the definition of "Cost" and identifying the "Forgone Option".

  • 拆解步驟 1： 看到「成本」，立刻寫下公式：\( \text{Cost} = \text{Money Cost} + \text{Time Cost} \)。這能幫你秒殺陳述 (1) 和 (3)。
  • Step 1: When you see "cost", immediately write down: \( \text{Cost} = \text{Money Cost} + \text{Time Cost} \). This helps you easily verify statements (1) and (3).
  • 拆解步驟 2： 處理陳述 (2) 時，問自己：「選擇去深圳，我放棄了什麼？」。放棄的是「留在香港看牙醫」。如果香港牙醫變好了（質素上升），我放棄的東西價值就變高了，成本自然上升。
  • Step 2: When tackling statement (2), ask yourself: "What do I give up by choosing Shenzhen?". You give up "visiting a dentist in HK". If HK dentists become better (quality rises), the value of what you give up increases, so the cost rises.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：混淆「物品自身的質素」與「成本」
很多學生誤以為「深圳牙醫質素變好，所以去深圳的成本降低了」。這是錯誤的！物品自身的質素只會影響你消費該物品時獲得的利益 (Benefit/Value)，不會改變你為了得到它而付出的成本 (Cost)。

Trap: Confusing "Quality of the good itself" with "Cost"
Many students mistakenly think, "If Shenzhen dentists' quality improves, the cost of going to Shenzhen decreases." This is wrong! The quality of the good itself only affects the Benefit/Value you receive from consuming it; it does not change the cost you pay to get it.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 既然深圳牙醫質素不影響成本，那為什麼題目陳述 (2) 還是錯的？

Q: If the quality of Shenzhen dentists doesn't affect the cost, why is statement (2) still wrong?

A: 因為陳述 (2) 說的是「兩地私家牙醫的服務質素都不會影響...」。雖然深圳牙醫的質素不影響成本，但香港牙醫的質素會！因為香港牙醫是你的「代替品」(Substitute) 也是你的「被放棄選項」。只要其中一地的質素會影響成本，這句「兩地都不會影響」的絕對陳述就是錯誤的。

A: Because statement (2) claims that the service quality of private dentists in BOTH places would not affect the cost. While Shenzhen dentists' quality doesn't affect the cost, Hong Kong dentists' quality DOES! HK dentists represent your "forgone option". Since the quality in one of the places affects the cost, the absolute statement that "both places would not affect" is false.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

消去法 (Elimination Method)：
在 DSE 經濟科中，看到包含「不會影響」(would not affect) 或「一定」(must) 等絕對字眼的選項時，要特別提高警覺。只要你能想到一個反例（例如：香港牙醫質素變好會增加去深圳的成本），就可以立刻判定 (2) 是錯的。排除了 (2) 之後，選項 A、C、D 都被淘汰了，答案必定是 B！你甚至不需要深入分析 (1) 和 (3) 就能得分，節省大量時間。

Elimination Method:
In DSE Economics, be on high alert when you see absolute terms like "would not affect" or "must". As long as you can think of one counter-example (e.g., HK dentists' quality improving increases the cost of going to Shenzhen), you can immediately determine that (2) is false. Once you eliminate (2), options A, C, and D are all crossed out. The answer MUST be B! You can score the mark without even deeply analyzing (1) and (3), saving precious exam time.
