核心概念 (Key Concepts): 支出計算法 (Expenditure Approach), 按市價計算的本地生產總值 (GDP at market price), 按要素成本計算的本地生產總值 (GDP at factor cost), 投資總額 (Gross Investment)

🧠 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解答這道題目，我們需要掌握支出計算法 (Expenditure Approach) 以及市價 (Market Price) 與要素成本 (Factor Cost) 之間的轉換。

To solve this question, we need to master the Expenditure Approach and the conversion between Market Price and Factor Cost.

1. 支出計算法公式 (Expenditure Approach Formula)

$$ \text{GDP at market price} = C + I + G + NX $$
  • \(C\) = 私人消費支出 (Private consumption expenditure)
  • \(I\) = 投資總額 (Gross investment) = 本地固定資本形成總額 (Gross domestic fixed capital formation) + 存貨改變 (Change in inventories)
  • \(G\) = 政府消費支出 (Government consumption expenditure)
  • \(NX\) = 淨出口 (Net exports) = 物品及服務出口總值 (Total exports) - 物品及服務進口總值 (Total imports)

2. 市價與要素成本的轉換 (Conversion between Market Price and Factor Cost)

按市價計算的本地生產總值包含了間接稅（這會推高價格）並扣除了補貼（這會降低價格）。因此，要計算生產要素的實際收入（要素成本），我們需要進行調整：

GDP at market price includes indirect taxes (which push up prices) and excludes subsidies (which lower prices). Therefore, to calculate the actual income received by factors of production (factor cost), we need to adjust it:
$$ \text{GDP at factor cost} = \text{GDP at market price} - \text{Indirect Taxes} + \text{Subsidies} $$

✅ 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是 (Correct Option): Option C

步驟一：計算按市價計算的本地生產總值 (Step 1: Calculate GDP at market price)

首先，我們將題目提供的數據代入支出計算法公式：

First, we substitute the given data into the expenditure approach formula:
  • \(C = 500\)
  • \(I = 220 + X\)
  • \(G = 500\)
  • \(NX = 280 - (100 + 60) = 280 - 160 = 120\)
$$ \text{GDP at market price} = 500 + (220 + X) + 500 + 120 = 1340 + X $$

步驟二：利用要素成本公式找出 X (Step 2: Use the factor cost formula to find X)

題目已知按要素成本計算的本地生產總值為 1400。我們將市價 GDP、間接稅 (60) 和補貼 (50) 代入公式：

The question states that GDP at factor cost is 1400. We substitute the market price GDP, indirect taxes (60), and subsidies (50) into the formula:
$$ 1400 = (1340 + X) - 60 + 50 $$ $$ 1400 = 1340 + X - 10 $$ $$ 1400 = 1330 + X $$ $$ X = 70 $$

因此，正確答案是 C。

Therefore, the correct answer is C.

選項分析 (Option Analysis):

  • A (-10): 錯誤。這可能是因為學生在計算淨出口或投資時出現了符號錯誤，或者錯誤地扣除了折舊。 (Incorrect. This might be due to a sign error when calculating net exports or investment, or incorrectly deducting depreciation.)
  • B (50): 錯誤。這可能是學生錯誤地處理了間接稅和補貼的加減關係。 (Incorrect. This might happen if the student incorrectly handled the addition/subtraction of indirect taxes and subsidies.)
  • C (70): 正確。 (Correct.)
  • D (100): 錯誤。這可能是學生將直接稅 (30) 也錯誤地加入了計算中。 (Incorrect. This might occur if the student mistakenly included direct taxes (30) in the calculation.)

🔍 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point): 本題的關鍵在於建立一條包含未知數 \(X\) 的方程式。你需要清楚知道哪些項目屬於 GDP 的計算範圍，以及如何從「市價」轉換為「要素成本」。

Pivot Point: The key to this question is setting up an equation containing the unknown \(X\). You need to know exactly which items are included in GDP calculation and how to convert from "market price" to "factor cost".

干擾資訊 (Distractors):

  • 直接稅 (Direct taxes): 30。直接稅是收入的轉移，不影響市價與要素成本之間的轉換，在計算 GDP 時應予忽略。 (Direct taxes are transfer of income and do not affect the conversion between market price and factor cost. They should be ignored in GDP calculation.)
  • 折舊 (Depreciation): 80。題目要求的是「本地生產總值 (Gross Domestic Product)」，而不是「本地生產淨值 (Net Domestic Product)」。因此，不需要扣除折舊。 (The question asks for "Gross Domestic Product", not "Net Domestic Product". Therefore, depreciation does not need to be deducted.)

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

1. 市價與要素成本的加減方向混淆： 很多學生會忘記是「減」間接稅、「加」補貼。記住，間接稅令市價變貴（所以要減去以還原成本），補貼令市價變平（所以要加回以還原成本）。

1. Confusing the signs for Market Price and Factor Cost: Many students forget that we "subtract" indirect taxes and "add" subsidies. Remember, indirect taxes make market prices higher (so subtract to find cost), and subsidies make market prices lower (so add back to find cost).

2. 誤墮「折舊」陷阱： 看到折舊就想減去它。只有在計算「淨值 (Net)」時才需要減去折舊。本題是計算「總值 (Gross)」，所以折舊是多餘資訊。

2. Falling into the "Depreciation" trap: Students often want to subtract depreciation whenever they see it. You only subtract depreciation when calculating "Net" values. This question asks for "Gross", so depreciation is redundant information.

🙋‍♂️ 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼進口分為「物品進口」和「服務進口」，計算時有什麼不同嗎？

A: 沒有不同。在計算總進口 \(M\) 時，我們必須將物品進口和服務進口加起來（100 + 60 = 160）。題目分開列出只是為了測試你是否知道兩者都屬於進口的一部分。

Q: Why are imports split into "Imports of goods" and "Imports of services"? Is there a difference in calculation?
A: No difference. When calculating total imports \(M\), we must add both goods and services together (100 + 60 = 160). The question lists them separately just to test if you know both are part of total imports.

Q: 如果題目問的是「按要素成本計算的本地生產淨值 (Net Domestic Product at factor cost)」，答案會是什麼？

A: 如果是計算淨值 (NDP)，我們就需要從 GDP 中扣除折舊 (80)。方程式會變成：\( \text{NDP at factor cost} = \text{GDP at factor cost} - \text{Depreciation} \)。

Q: What if the question asked for "Net Domestic Product at factor cost"?
A: If calculating Net value (NDP), we would need to deduct depreciation (80) from GDP. The equation would be: \( \text{NDP at factor cost} = \text{GDP at factor cost} - \text{Depreciation} \).

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

逆向推導法 (Working Backwards):

在選擇題中，為了節省時間，你可以先處理等式的一邊。既然 \( \text{GDP at FC} = 1400 \)，你可以立刻推算出 \( \text{GDP at MP} \)：

In multiple-choice questions, to save time, you can process one side of the equation first. Since \( \text{GDP at FC} = 1400 \), you can immediately deduce \( \text{GDP at MP} \):
\( \text{GDP at MP} = \text{GDP at FC} + \text{Indirect Taxes} - \text{Subsidies} \)
\( \text{GDP at MP} = 1400 + 60 - 50 = 1410 \)

然後，你知道 \( C + I + G + NX = 1410 \)。
\( 500 + (220 + X) + 500 + (280 - 160) = 1410 \)
\( 1340 + X = 1410 \)
\( X = 70 \)

這樣分兩步計算，可以大大減少將所有數字寫在同一條長方程式中出錯的機會。

Calculating in these two distinct steps greatly reduces the chance of making a careless arithmetic error in one long equation.
