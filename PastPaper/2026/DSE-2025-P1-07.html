📊 HKDSE Economics Question Analysis

Topic (課題): 廠商的生產與成本 - 規模經濟 (Production & Costs - Economies of Scale)

Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts (關鍵概念): 長期與短期 (Long Run vs Short Run), 規模經濟 (Economies of Scale), 平均成本 (Average Cost).

1. The Question (題目)

某廠商增加所有投入以將產出提高 50%。結果，總生產成本上升 20%。以下哪一項最能解釋上述情況？

  • A. 該廠商因可提供更多抵押品而享有較低的借貸成本。
  • B. 在用盡原料的所有廉價來源後，該廠商需要從更昂貴的來源購買原料。
  • C. 由於整個行業過度擴張，該廠商承擔了更高的市場推廣成本。
  • D. 邊際回報遞減定律適用於該廠商的生產。

A firm increases all its inputs to raise its output by 50%. As a result, the total cost of production rises by 20%. Which of the following best explains the above situation?

  • A. The firm benefits from lower cost of borrowing due to the availability of more collateral.
  • B. After exhausting all cheap sources of raw materials, the firm needs to purchase raw materials from more expensive sources.
  • C. The firm incurs higher marketing and promotion costs due to excessive expansion of the entire industry.
  • D. The law of diminishing marginal returns applies to the firm's production.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

短期 vs 長期 (Short Run vs Long Run)

在經濟學中，短期是指最少有一種生產要素（投入）是固定的時期。長期是指所有生產要素（投入）都是可變的時期。題目指出廠商增加「所有投入」，這意味著情況處於長期。

In economics, the short run is a period where at least one factor of production (input) is fixed. The long run is a period where all inputs are variable. The question states the firm increases "all its inputs", meaning we are looking at the long run.

規模經濟 (Economies of Scale)

當產量的百分比變動大於總成本的百分比變動時，長期平均成本會下降，這稱為規模經濟。這代表廠商因擴大規模而享有成本優勢。

When the percentage increase in output is greater than the percentage increase in total cost, the Long Run Average Cost (LRAC) falls. This is known as Economies of Scale. It represents cost advantages a firm enjoys due to expansion.

長期平均成本公式 (Long Run Average Cost Formula): $$AC = \frac{TC}{Q}$$ 如果 $\%\Delta Q > \%\Delta TC$，則 $AC$ 下降。
If $\%\Delta Q > \%\Delta TC$, then $AC$ falls.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option A

A 該廠商因可提供更多抵押品而享有較低的借貸成本。

這描述了「財務上的規模經濟」(Financial economies of scale)。當廠商規模擴大，它可以提供更多資產作為抵押，從而以較低的利率借款。這會降低平均成本，符合題目中總成本上升幅度(20%)小於產出上升幅度(50%)，導致平均成本下降的情況。

This describes "Financial economies of scale". As the firm expands, it can offer more collateral and borrow at a lower interest rate. This reduces the average cost, matching the scenario where the total cost increase (20%) is smaller than the output increase (50%), leading to a lower average cost.

B 在用盡原料的所有廉價來源後，該廠商需要從更昂貴的來源購買原料。

這會導致成本急劇上升，屬於「規模不經濟」(Diseconomies of scale)。如果發生這種情況，總成本的升幅會大於產出的升幅，與題目數據矛盾。

This would cause costs to rise sharply, representing "Diseconomies of scale". If this happened, the increase in total cost would be greater than the increase in output, which contradicts the data in the question.

C 由於整個行業過度擴張，該廠商承擔了更高的市場推廣成本。

這是「外部規模不經濟」(External diseconomies of scale) 的例子。行業過度擴張導致個別廠商成本上升。同樣地，這會使總成本的升幅大於產出的升幅，不符合題意。

This is an example of "External diseconomies of scale". Excessive industry expansion leads to higher costs for individual firms. Again, this would make the total cost rise faster than output, which is incorrect.

D 邊際回報遞減定律適用於該廠商的生產。

邊際回報遞減定律 (Law of diminishing marginal returns) 僅適用於短期，即假設最少有一種投入是固定的情況下。題目清楚說明廠商增加了「所有投入」，因此這是長期情況，該定律不適用。

The Law of diminishing marginal returns only applies in the short run, assuming at least one input is fixed. The question explicitly states the firm increases "all its inputs", making it a long-run scenario. Thus, this law does not apply.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

  • Pivot Point 1 (破題點 1): 「增加所有投入」(Increases all its inputs) ➔ 立即判定為長期 (Long Run)。立刻排除與短期相關的選項（如選項 D）。
  • Pivot Point 2 (破題點 2): 產出提高 50% $\%\Delta Q = 50\%$)，總成本上升 20% ($\%\Delta TC = 20\%$) ➔ 因為 $\%\Delta Q > \%\Delta TC$，平均成本 $AC$ 下降。這意味著廠商正經歷規模經濟 (Economies of Scale)。
  • Conclusion (結論): 我們需要在選項中尋找一個描述「規模經濟」（帶來好處、降低平均成本）的選項。B 和 C 都是成本上升的負面因素，只有 A 是降低成本的正面因素。
  • Pivot Point 1: "Increases all its inputs" ➔ Identifies this as the Long Run immediately. Rule out any short-run concepts (like Option D).
  • Pivot Point 2: Output up 50%, Total Cost up 20% ➔ Since $\%\Delta Q > \%\Delta TC$, the Average Cost ($AC$) is falling. The firm is experiencing Economies of Scale.
  • Conclusion: We need to find an option that describes an economy of scale (a cost-saving benefit). Options B and C describe negative, cost-increasing factors. Only A describes a positive, cost-saving factor.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

❌ Trap 1: 忽略了「所有投入」的關鍵字眼 (Ignoring the "all inputs" keyword)
學生常一看到成本或產量變動，就反射性選擇「邊際回報遞減定律」(選項 D)。記住，DSE 考評局經常使用「增加所有投入」來測試你是否分得清長短期！

Students often instinctively choose the "Law of diminishing marginal returns" (Option D) whenever they see changes in cost or output. Remember, the HKEAA loves using "increases all inputs" to test if you can distinguish between the short and long run!

❌ Trap 2: 誤以為總成本上升就是規模不經濟 (Assuming Rising Total Cost = Diseconomies of Scale)
很多同學看到「總生產成本上升 20%」，就覺得成本變貴了，於是選擇 B 或 C。事實上，只要廠商增產，總成本 $TC$ 一定會上升。關鍵在於它上升的「速度」是否快於產量。這裡成本升得比較慢，所以每件產品的平均成本 $AC$ 是變平了！

Many students see "total cost rises by 20%" and assume things are getting more expensive, picking B or C. In reality, whenever a firm produces more, Total Cost ($TC$) always rises. The key is whether it rises faster than output. Here, it rises slower, meaning the Average Cost ($AC$) per unit has actually decreased!

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

🙋 學生問 (Student): 「如果選項 D 改成『邊際回報遞減定律適用』，而且題目說『只增加勞力，機器數量不變』，那 D 會是答案嗎？」
"If the question said 'increases only labor while machines remain fixed', would D be the answer?"

👨‍🏫 老師答 (Tutor): 「這是一個很好的問題！如果題目說『固定某些投入』（即短期），那麼邊際回報遞減定律確實會發生。然而，即使在短期，如果產出升幅(50%)大於總成本升幅(20%)，這意味著邊際成本或平均成本仍在下降階段，尚未進入遞減定律導致成本急升的階段。所以必須非常小心閱讀數據！」
"Great question! If the question specified fixed inputs (Short Run), the law of diminishing returns would indeed apply eventually. However, since the output increase (50%) is greater than the cost increase (20%), the average cost is falling, meaning the firm hasn't reached the stage where diminishing returns cause costs to spike significantly yet. Always read the data carefully!"

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

⚡ 三秒速解法 (3-Second Elimination Method):

  1. 看到 "All inputs" (所有投入) 👉 立刻劃掉選項 D (短期專屬)。
  2. 比較數字：50% (產出) > 20% (成本) 👉 這是好事，代表平均成本下降，廠商賺到了好處。
  3. 看選項語氣：
    • A = 好事 (享有利率優惠)
    • B = 壞事 (買貴原料)
    • C = 壞事 (推廣費增加)
  4. 結論：只有 A 是好事，秒選 A！

Shortcut: 1. See "All inputs" 👉 Cross out D immediately. 2. Compare numbers: 50% (Output) > 20% (Cost) 👉 This is a good thing (Average cost is falling). 3. Check the tone of the options: A is a positive benefit, B and C are negative cost-drivers. Therefore, A must be the correct answer!
