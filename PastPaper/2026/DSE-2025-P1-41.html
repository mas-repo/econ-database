Topic / 課題 國際貿易：比較優勢 (International Trade: Comparative Advantage)
Difficulty / 難度 ★★☆☆☆
Key Concepts / 關鍵概念 比較優勢 (Comparative Advantage), 絕對優勢 (Absolute Advantage), 機會成本 (Opportunity Cost)

題目 (The Question)

某國擁有生產物品 X 的比較優勢。下列哪項有關該國的陳述是正確的？

A. 運用相同數量的資源，該國可生產的物品 X 數量較另一種物品多。
B. 該國物品 X 的產量較其他國家高。
C. 在生產一單位的物品 X 時，該國所需的資源較其他國家少。
D. 在生產一單位的物品 X 時，該國放棄另一種物品的數量較其他國家少。
A country has a comparative advantage in producing good X. Which of the following statements about the country is correct?

A. With the same amount of resources, it can produce more of good X than another good.
B. Its output of good X is higher than other countries.
C. It requires a smaller amount of resources to produce one unit of good X than other countries.
D. It gives up a smaller amount of another good than other countries in producing one unit of good X.

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

比較優勢 (Comparative Advantage)： 如果一個國家生產某物品的機會成本（即放棄生產其他物品的數量）低於其他國家，該國便擁有生產該物品的比較優勢。

絕對優勢 (Absolute Advantage)： 如果一個國家運用相同數量的資源能生產較多數量的某物品，或者生產相同數量的物品所需資源較少，該國便擁有絕對優勢。
Comparative Advantage: A country has a comparative advantage in producing a good if its opportunity cost (the amount of other goods given up) of producing that good is lower than that of other countries.

Absolute Advantage: A country has an absolute advantage if it can produce more of a good with the same amount of resources, or if it requires fewer resources to produce the same amount of a good compared to other countries.
生產 1 單位物品 X 的機會成本公式：
Formula for Opportunity Cost of 1 unit of Good X:
$$ \text{Opportunity Cost of 1X} = \frac{\text{Quantity of Good Y given up}}{\text{Quantity of Good X gained}} $$

答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): D
選項 A 分析：錯誤。 這個選項只是比較同一個國家內兩種物品的產量，並沒有與其他國家進行機會成本的比較。比較優勢必須涉及國家之間的機會成本比較。
Option A Analysis: Incorrect. This option merely compares the production volumes of two goods within the same country. It does not compare opportunity costs between countries. Comparative advantage must involve an inter-country comparison of opportunity costs.
選項 B 分析：錯誤。 產量較高只反映生產規模或總量，並不代表生產效率高，更不能反映機會成本的高低。
Option B Analysis: Incorrect. A higher output only reflects the scale of production or total volume. It does not indicate higher production efficiency, nor does it reflect the level of opportunity cost.
選項 C 分析：錯誤。 「所需資源較少」是絕對優勢 (Absolute Advantage) 的定義。絕對優勢關注的是投入的資源數量，而非放棄的其他物品數量（機會成本）。
Option C Analysis: Incorrect. "Requires a smaller amount of resources" is the definition of Absolute Advantage. Absolute advantage focuses on the amount of resources inputted, not the amount of other goods given up (opportunity cost).
選項 D 分析：正確。 「放棄另一種物品的數量較其他國家少」精確地描述了生產該物品的機會成本較低。這正是比較優勢 (Comparative Advantage) 的核心定義。
Option D Analysis: Correct. "Gives up a smaller amount of another good than other countries" accurately describes a lower opportunity cost of producing that good. This is the exact core definition of Comparative Advantage.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)： 看到「比較優勢 (Comparative Advantage)」，你的大腦必須立刻聯想到「機會成本 (Opportunity Cost)」或「放棄的數量 (Amount given up)」。
干擾資訊 (Distractors)： 題目中的選項 C 是一個經典的陷阱，它描述的是「絕對優勢」。考評局經常將這兩個概念放在一起來測試學生是否能清晰分辨「資源投入」與「機會成本」。
Pivot Point: When you see "Comparative Advantage", your brain must immediately link it to "Opportunity Cost" or "Amount given up".
Distractors: Option C is a classic trap; it describes "Absolute Advantage". The HKEAA frequently puts these two concepts together to test if students can clearly distinguish between "resource input" and "opportunity cost".

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

概念混淆： 學生最常犯的錯誤是混淆了「絕對優勢」和「比較優勢」。記住：
- 講「資源 (Resources)」或「產量 (Output)」 \(\rightarrow\) 絕對優勢
- 講「放棄 (Give up)」或「機會成本 (Opportunity Cost)」 \(\rightarrow\) 比較優勢
Concept Confusion: The most common mistake students make is confusing "Absolute Advantage" with "Comparative Advantage". Remember:
- Mentions "Resources" or "Output" \(\rightarrow\) Absolute Advantage
- Mentions "Give up" or "Opportunity Cost" \(\rightarrow\) Comparative Advantage

學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 一個國家可以同時擁有生產兩種物品的比較優勢嗎？ (Can a country have a comparative advantage in both goods?)
A: 不可以。 因為機會成本是互為倒數的 (reciprocals)。如果甲國生產物品 X 的機會成本低於乙國，那麼甲國生產物品 Y 的機會成本必然高於乙國。因此，一個國家最多只能擁有一種物品的比較優勢（除非兩國機會成本完全相同，則沒有比較優勢）。
A: No. Because opportunity costs are reciprocals. If Country A has a lower opportunity cost in producing Good X than Country B, Country A's opportunity cost in producing Good Y must be higher than Country B's. Therefore, a country can have a comparative advantage in at most one good (unless both countries have identical opportunity costs, in which case neither has a comparative advantage).

考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

關鍵字秒殺法 (Keyword Elimination Method)：
在多項選擇題中，當題目問及「比較優勢 (Comparative Advantage)」時，直接掃描選項尋找關鍵字「放棄 (give up)」或「機會成本 (opportunity cost)」。
- 看到「資源 (resources)」(選項 A 和 C) \(\rightarrow\) 立即刪除！
- 看到「產量 (output)」(選項 B) \(\rightarrow\) 立即刪除！
這樣不用 10 秒就能鎖定答案 D。
Keyword Elimination Method:
In MCQs, when the question asks about "Comparative Advantage", scan the options directly for the keywords "give up" or "opportunity cost".
- See "resources" (Options A and C) \(\rightarrow\) Eliminate immediately!
- See "output" (Option B) \(\rightarrow\) Eliminate immediately!
This way, you can lock in Option D in less than 10 seconds.
