HKDSE Economics 經濟學題解

Topic (課題): Business Cycle (經濟周期) / Macroeconomic Performance (宏觀經濟表現)

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐ (2/5 Stars)

Key Concepts (核心概念): Business Cycle 經濟周期 Unemployment Rate 失業率 Inflation 通貨膨脹

The Question (題目)

某經濟正經歷下列情況：

  • 貨幣的購買力正在下跌。
  • 生產者的存貨正在減少。
  • 政府在轉移支付上支出的金額正在減少。
  • 失業率正在下降。

該經濟最有可能正處於經濟周期的 ______ 階段。

A. 復甦
B. 高峰
C. 衰退
D. 低谷

An economy is experiencing the following situations:

  • The purchasing power of money is decreasing.
  • The inventories of producers are decreasing.
  • The amount of government expenditure on transfer payments is decreasing.
  • The unemployment rate is decreasing.

The economy is most likely in the ______ phase of a business cycle.

A. recovery
B. peak
C. recession
D. trough

Concept Review (概念重溫)

經濟周期（Business Cycle）分為四個階段：復甦（Recovery）、高峰（Peak）、衰退（Recession）和低谷（Trough）。

The Business Cycle consists of four phases: Recovery, Peak, Recession, and Trough.

  • 復甦 (Recovery): 總需求增加，廠商銷量上升導致存貨減少（Inventories decrease）。廠商增聘人手，失業率下降（Unemployment rate decreases）。物價水平上升，導致貨幣購買力下降。
  • 轉移支付 (Transfer Payments): 政府無償支付給市民的款項，例如失業救濟金。當經濟好轉，失業人數減少，這項支出自然下降。
  • Recovery: Aggregate demand increases, leading to higher sales and decreasing inventories. Firms hire more workers, so the unemployment rate decreases. The general price level rises, reducing the purchasing power of money.
  • Transfer Payments: Payments by the government without receiving goods or services in return, e.g., unemployment benefits. As the economy improves and unemployment falls, this expenditure decreases.

貨幣購買力與物價水平的關係：

$$ \text{Purchasing Power of Money} \propto \frac{1}{\text{Price Level}} $$

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是 / The correct option is: A. 復甦 (Recovery)

選項分析 (Option Analysis):

Option A (正確 Correct): 題目中所有的「正在...」都顯示經濟活動正在擴張。總需求上升令存貨消耗加快（存貨減少）；廠商增聘人手令失業率下降；就業改善令政府減少發放失業救濟金（轉移支付減少）；需求拉動的通脹令物價上升，貨幣購買力下跌。這些都是典型的復甦特徵。

All the "-ing" (decreasing) trends indicate an expanding economy. Rising aggregate demand depletes inventories; firms hiring more workers lowers unemployment; better employment reduces the need for unemployment benefits (lower transfer payments); and demand-pull inflation lowers the purchasing power of money. These are classic signs of a recovery.

Option B (錯誤 Incorrect): 高峰是經濟周期的轉折點（Turning point），此時失業率處於最低水平，但未必「正在下降」。同時，在高峰期末段，增長放緩，存貨可能會開始累積，而不是持續減少。

A peak is a turning point where the unemployment rate is at its minimum, not necessarily "decreasing" further. Also, towards the end of a peak, growth slows and inventories might start to accumulate rather than decrease.

Option C (錯誤 Incorrect): 在衰退期間，失業率會上升，存貨會增加，政府的轉移支付（如失業救濟金）會增加。

During a recession, the unemployment rate increases, inventories accumulate, and government transfer payments (like unemployment benefits) increase.

Option D (錯誤 Incorrect): 低谷是另一個轉折點，失業率處於最高水平，經濟活動停滯。

A trough is another turning point where the unemployment rate is at its maximum and economic activities are stagnant.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (解題關鍵): 留意題目中的動態詞語「正在下跌/減少 (decreasing)」。這暗示經濟處於一個「過程 (Phase)」而非「轉折點 (Turning point)」。

Pay attention to the dynamic word "decreasing". This implies the economy is in a "Phase" (a process) rather than a "Turning point".

既然是過程，答案只能是復甦（Recovery）或衰退（Recession）。由於失業率下降代表經濟正在好轉，因此必定是復甦。

Since it's a process, the answer must be either Recovery or Recession. Because a decreasing unemployment rate means the economy is improving, it must be Recovery.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

混淆高峰與復甦 (Confusing Peak and Recovery): 很多學生看到經濟表現良好，就誤以為是「高峰」。記住，高峰是一個點（Point），失業率已經到達谷底；而復甦是一個持續改善的過程（Process），失業率「正在」下降。

Many students mistake good economic performance for a "Peak". Remember, a peak is a point where unemployment has bottomed out; recovery is an ongoing process where unemployment is actively "decreasing".

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼貨幣購買力下跌代表經濟好轉？

A: 貨幣購買力下跌意味著物價水平（Price Level）正在上升，即出現通貨膨脹（Inflation）。在經濟復甦期間，市民收入增加，消費意欲上升（總需求增加），這通常會拉高物價，導致輕微至溫和的通脹。

Q: Why does a decrease in the purchasing power of money indicate an improving economy?

A: A decrease in purchasing power means the general price level is rising (Inflation). During a recovery, people's incomes rise and consumption increases (higher aggregate demand). This typically pulls prices up, causing mild to moderate inflation.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

Elimination Method (排除法): 看到「正在... (decreasing/increasing)」，立刻排除 B (高峰) 和 D (低谷)，因為它們是靜態的轉折點。剩下 A 和 C。看到「失業率下降 (unemployment decreasing)」，這是好現象，立刻選 A (復甦)。5秒內即可找出答案！

When you see continuous actions like "decreasing", immediately eliminate B (Peak) and D (Trough) as they are static turning points. You are left with A and C. "Unemployment decreasing" is a positive sign, so choose A (Recovery). You can solve this in 5 seconds!
