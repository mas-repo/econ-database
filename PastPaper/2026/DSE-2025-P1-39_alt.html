主題 (Topic): 財政政策與乘數效應 (Fiscal Policy and Multiplier Effect)
難度 (Difficulty): ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)
關鍵字 (Key Concepts): 總需求 (Aggregate Demand) 政府支出 (Government Expenditure) 乘數效應 (Multiplier Effect) 稅收 (Taxes)

題目 (The Question)

假設政府透過削減醫療服務支出以支付薪俸稅的退稅，而退稅的金額和削減支出的金額相同。
這對總需求的影響屬 ______。

A. 擴張性
B. 收縮性
C. 中性
D. 未能決定

Suppose a government reduces its expenditure on medical services to provide a tax rebate on salaries tax, and the amount of the tax rebate equals the amount of reduction in the expenditure.
The effect on the aggregate demand will be ______.

A. expansionary
B. contractionary
C. neutral
D. indeterminate

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解答這條題目，我們需要理解政府支出乘數 (Government Expenditure Multiplier) 和 稅收乘數 (Tax Multiplier) 的分別。

To solve this question, we need to understand the difference between the Government Expenditure Multiplier and the Tax Multiplier.

  • 總需求 (Aggregate Demand, AD): \( AD = C + I + G + NX \)
  • 政府支出乘數 (Government Expenditure Multiplier, \(k_G\)): 量度政府支出 \(G\) 變動對總收入/總需求的影響。公式為：
$$ k_G = \frac{1}{1 - MPC} $$
  • 稅收乘數 (Tax Multiplier, \(k_T\)): 量度稅收 \(T\) 變動對總收入/總需求的影響。由於減稅只會增加可支配收入，市民會將部分收入儲蓄起來（取決於邊際消費傾向 MPC），因此減稅的刺激作用較小。公式為：
$$ k_T = \frac{-MPC}{1 - MPC} $$

關鍵結論 (Key Conclusion): 在絕對值上，政府支出乘數永遠大於稅收乘數，即 \( |k_G| > |k_T| \)。

In absolute terms, the government expenditure multiplier is always greater than the tax multiplier, i.e., \( |k_G| > |k_T| \).

答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): B. 收縮性 (contractionary)

A. 擴張性 (expansionary) - 錯誤 (Wrong)

雖然退稅（減稅）具有擴張性作用，但由於稅收乘數的絕對值小於政府支出乘數，退稅帶來的總需求增加幅度，不足以彌補削減政府支出所造成的總需求跌幅。

Although a tax rebate (tax cut) has an expansionary effect, since the absolute value of the tax multiplier is smaller than the government expenditure multiplier, the increase in aggregate demand from the tax rebate is not enough to offset the decrease in aggregate demand caused by the cut in government expenditure.

B. 收縮性 (contractionary) - 正確 (Correct)

假設削減的政府支出和退稅金額均為 \( \Delta X \)。
削減政府支出導致總需求下跌：\( \Delta AD_1 = -\Delta X \times \frac{1}{1-MPC} \)
退稅導致總需求上升：\( \Delta AD_2 = \Delta X \times \frac{MPC}{1-MPC} \)
淨影響 (Net Effect) = \( \Delta AD_1 + \Delta AD_2 = -\Delta X \)。
因為淨影響為負數，所以整體效應是收縮性的。

Suppose the reduction in government expenditure and the tax rebate amount are both \( \Delta X \).
The cut in government expenditure decreases AD by: \( \Delta AD_1 = -\Delta X \times \frac{1}{1-MPC} \)
The tax rebate increases AD by: \( \Delta AD_2 = \Delta X \times \frac{MPC}{1-MPC} \)
Net Effect = \( \Delta AD_1 + \Delta AD_2 = -\Delta X \).
Since the net effect is negative, the overall effect is contractionary.

C. 中性 (neutral) - 錯誤 (Wrong)

只有當擴張性效應和收縮性效應完全抵消時，結果才會是中性。如上所述，兩者的乘數效應並不相等。

The result would only be neutral if the expansionary and contractionary effects perfectly offset each other. As explained above, their multiplier effects are not equal.

D. 未能決定 (indeterminate) - 錯誤 (Wrong)

只要知道邊際消費傾向 \( MPC < 1 \)，我們就能確定政府支出的影響必定大於稅收的影響，因此方向是確定的。

As long as we know the marginal propensity to consume \( MPC < 1 \), we can be certain that the impact of government expenditure is greater than the impact of taxes, so the direction is determinate.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point): 認清「直接支出」與「轉移/稅收」的分別。
政府支出 \(G\) 是總需求 \(AD\) 的直接組成部分。政府每減少 $100 支出，總需求立刻減少 $100（然後再產生乘數效應）。相反，退稅 $100 給市民，市民不會把 $100 全部花掉，他們會儲蓄一部分（例如儲蓄 $20，只消費 $80）。因此，退稅帶來的第一波消費刺激只有 $80，威力自然不及削減 $100 政府支出。

The Pivot Point: Recognize the difference between "direct expenditure" and "transfers/taxes".
Government expenditure \(G\) is a direct component of aggregate demand \(AD\). A $100 cut in government spending immediately reduces AD by $100 (before the multiplier kicks in). Conversely, if the government gives a $100 tax rebate, citizens won't spend the entire $100; they will save a portion (e.g., save $20, spend $80). Therefore, the initial consumption stimulus from the tax rebate is only $80, which is naturally weaker than the $100 cut in government spending.

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

⚠️ 直覺陷阱 (Intuition Trap): 很多學生看到「退稅金額 = 削減支出金額」，就直覺以為一加一減會互相抵消，從而錯誤選擇 C. 中性 (neutral)。在宏觀經濟學中，金額相同不代表對經濟的最終影響相同，必須考慮乘數 (Multiplier) 的差異。

⚠️ Intuition Trap: Many students see "tax rebate amount = expenditure reduction amount" and intuitively think the plus and minus will cancel each other out, wrongly choosing C. neutral. In macroeconomics, equal amounts do not mean equal final impacts on the economy; one must consider the difference in Multipliers.

學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼市民不把退稅的錢全部花光？

A: 根據凱恩斯經濟學，人們的邊際消費傾向 (MPC) 通常大於 0 但小於 1，即 \( 0 < MPC < 1 \)。這意味著每增加一元可支配收入，人們只會消費當中的一部分，剩餘的會成為儲蓄 (漏出 leakage)。這就是為什麼稅收乘數較弱的原因。

Q: Why don't citizens spend all the money from the tax rebate?

A: According to Keynesian economics, people's marginal propensity to consume (MPC) is typically greater than 0 but less than 1, i.e., \( 0 < MPC < 1 \). This means for every extra dollar of disposable income, people will only spend a fraction of it, and the rest becomes saving (a leakage). This is why the tax multiplier is weaker.

考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

💡 速記法則：平衡預算乘數 (Balanced Budget Multiplier)
在簡單凱恩斯模型中，如果政府同時且同等金額地增加政府支出 (\(G\)) 和稅收 (\(T\))，總需求的淨增加額將剛好等於該筆金額（即平衡預算乘數 = 1）。
反之亦然（本題情況）：如果政府同時且同等金額地減少政府支出 (\(G\)) 和稅收 (\(T\))，總需求將會下跌該筆金額。既然總需求下跌，答案必定是收縮性 (contractionary)。秒殺！

💡 Shortcut: Balanced Budget Multiplier
In a simple Keynesian model, if the government increases government expenditure (\(G\)) and taxes (\(T\)) simultaneously by the same amount, the net increase in aggregate demand will be exactly equal to that amount (i.e., balanced budget multiplier = 1).
The reverse is also true (the scenario in this question): if the government decreases \(G\) and \(T\) simultaneously by the same amount, aggregate demand will decrease by that exact amount. Since aggregate demand decreases, the answer must be contractionary. Solved in seconds!
