📝 HKDSE Economics Tutor Analysis

📚 Topic (課題): Money and Banking - Credit Creation (貨幣與銀行 - 信用創造)

⭐ Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆ (3/5 Stars)

🔑 Key Concepts (核心概念): Required Reserve Ratio (法定儲備比率), Banking Multiplier (銀行乘數), Monetary Base (貨幣基礎), Money Supply (貨幣供應)

題目 (The Question)

若銀行系統的法定儲備比率為100%，下列哪項陳述是正確的？

A. 中央銀行不能影響該經濟的貨幣供應。
B. 銀行仍可作為金融中介，將儲蓄引導作投資。
C. 貨幣供應不會等於貨幣基礎。
D. 最大銀行乘數會等於一。

Which of the following statements is correct if the required reserve ratio of a banking system is 100%?

A. The central bank cannot affect the money supply of the economy.
B. Banks can still serve as financial intermediaries that channel savings into investment.
C. The money supply will not be equal to the monetary base.
D. The maximum banking multiplier will be equal to one.

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解答這道題目，我們必須清楚理解「法定儲備比率」為 100% 的含義及其對銀行系統的影響。

To answer this question, we must clearly understand the implications of a 100% "Required Reserve Ratio" on the banking system.

  • 法定儲備比率 (Required Reserve Ratio, \(RRR\)): 銀行必須將存款的某個百分比作為儲備保留，不能借出。當 \(RRR = 100\%\) 時，銀行必須保留所有存款作為儲備。
    The percentage of deposits that banks must keep as reserves and cannot lend out. When \(RRR = 100\%\), banks must keep all deposits as reserves.
  • 最大銀行乘數 (Maximum Banking Multiplier): 衡量銀行系統創造存款的最大能力。
    Measures the maximum ability of the banking system to create deposits.
    $$ \text{Maximum Banking Multiplier} = \frac{1}{RRR} $$
  • 貨幣基礎 (Monetary Base, \(M_b\)) 與 貨幣供應 (Money Supply, \(M_s\)):
    $$ M_b = C_p + R $$ $$ M_s = C_p + D $$
    其中 \(C_p\) 是公眾持有的法定貨幣，\(R\) 是銀行儲備，\(D\) 是存款。
    Where \(C_p\) is cash in public hands, \(R\) is bank reserves, and \(D\) is deposits.

答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): Option D

❌ Option A: 中央銀行不能影響該經濟的貨幣供應。

即使銀行不能創造額外存款，中央銀行仍然可以透過發行更多紙幣或硬幣（增加 \(C_p\)）來直接增加貨幣供應。因此，央行仍能影響貨幣供應。

Even if banks cannot create additional deposits, the central bank can still directly increase the money supply by issuing more notes and coins (increasing \(C_p\)). Therefore, the central bank can still affect the money supply.

❌ Option B: 銀行仍可作為金融中介，將儲蓄引導作投資。

當 \(RRR = 100\%\) 時，銀行必須將所有吸收回來的存款作為儲備，完全不能借出任何款項。既然不能發放貸款，銀行就失去了將儲蓄轉化為投資的金融中介功能。

When \(RRR = 100\%\), banks must keep all collected deposits as reserves and cannot lend out any money at all. Since they cannot grant loans, banks lose their financial intermediary function of channeling savings into investment.

❌ Option C: 貨幣供應不會等於貨幣基礎。

由於 \(RRR = 100\%\)，銀行的儲備將完全等於存款（即 \(R = D\)）。根據公式，\(M_b = C_p + R\) 而 \(M_s = C_p + D\)。因為 \(R = D\)，所以 \(M_b\) 必定等於 \(M_s\)。這選項說「不會等於」是錯誤的。

Since \(RRR = 100\%\), bank reserves will be exactly equal to deposits (i.e., \(R = D\)). According to the formulas, \(M_b = C_p + R\) and \(M_s = C_p + D\). Because \(R = D\), \(M_b\) must be equal to \(M_s\). This option stating they "will not be equal" is incorrect.

✅ Option D: 最大銀行乘數會等於一。

根據最大銀行乘數公式：\(\frac{1}{RRR}\)。當 \(RRR = 100\%\)（即 \(1\)）時，最大銀行乘數 = \(\frac{1}{1} = 1\)。這意味著銀行系統無法創造多倍的存款，最初的存款就是最終的總存款。

According to the maximum banking multiplier formula: \(\frac{1}{RRR}\). When \(RRR = 100\%\) (which is \(1\)), the maximum banking multiplier = \(\frac{1}{1} = 1\). This means the banking system cannot create multiple deposits; the initial deposit is the final total deposit.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point):

本題的核心在於理解 100% 法定儲備比率的極端情況。當你看到 \(RRR = 100\%\) 時，你的大腦應該立刻聯想到兩個直接後果：

  1. 銀行不能借錢 (No lending allowed) \(\rightarrow\) 失去中介功能。
  2. 儲備等於存款 (\(R = D\)) \(\rightarrow\) 貨幣供應等於貨幣基礎 (\(M_s = M_b\))。

The core of this question lies in understanding the extreme case of a 100% required reserve ratio. When you see \(RRR = 100\%\), your brain should immediately associate it with two direct consequences: 1) Banks cannot lend money, thus losing their intermediary function. 2) Reserves equal deposits (\(R = D\)), meaning Money Supply equals Monetary Base (\(M_s = M_b\)).

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

⚠️ 混淆貨幣基礎與貨幣供應 (Confusing Monetary Base and Money Supply)

很多學生忘記了 \(M_b\) 和 \(M_s\) 的公式。只要寫出 \(M_b = C_p + R\) 和 \(M_s = C_p + D\)，再代入 \(R = D\) 的條件，就能輕易排除 Option C。

Many students forget the formulas for \(M_b\) and \(M_s\). By simply writing down \(M_b = C_p + R\) and \(M_s = C_p + D\), and substituting the condition \(R = D\), Option C can be easily eliminated.

學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

🙋‍♂️ 學生問 (Student): 如果銀行不能借錢出去（Option B 錯），那銀行靠什麼賺錢？銀行還會存在嗎？
If banks cannot lend money out (Option B is wrong), how do they make a profit? Will banks still exist?

👨‍🏫 導師答 (Tutor): 在 100% 儲備制度下，銀行確實無法透過「存貸息差 (interest rate spread)」來賺錢。但銀行依然可以作為「保管箱」存在，透過向存款人收取保管費或手續費 (service fees) 來維持營運。這是一個理論上的極端情況，現實中極少出現。
Under a 100% reserve system, banks indeed cannot make money through the "interest rate spread" between deposits and loans. However, banks can still exist as "safes," maintaining operations by charging depositors safekeeping or service fees. This is a theoretical extreme case and rarely happens in reality.

考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

⚡ 秒殺技巧 (One-Second Kill Trick):

在 DSE 選擇題中，看到「最大銀行乘數」和「法定儲備比率」，第一時間寫下公式：\( \text{Multiplier} = \frac{1}{RRR} \)。
題目給定 \(RRR = 100\% = 1\)。
直接計算：\(\frac{1}{1} = 1\)。
Option D 直接命中，無需猶豫！

In DSE MC questions, when you see "maximum banking multiplier" and "required reserve ratio", immediately write down the formula: \( \text{Multiplier} = \frac{1}{RRR} \). The question gives \(RRR = 100\% = 1\). Calculate directly: \(\frac{1}{1} = 1\). Option D is a direct hit, no hesitation needed!
