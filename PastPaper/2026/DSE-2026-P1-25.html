Topic (課題): National Income Accounting 國民收入會計

Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts (核心概念): Nominal GDP (名義本地生產總值), Real GDP (實質本地生產總值), GDP Deflator (本地生產總值平減物價指數)

The Question (題目)

假設某封閉經濟只生產玩具和成衣。細閱以下有關該經濟的資料。

Suppose a closed economy produces toys and clothing only. Study the following data of the economy.

玩具的單位價格		 Quantity of clothing
成衣產量		 Unit price of clothing ($)
成衣的單位價格
Year 1 (第一年) 20 5 50 6

下列哪些有關該經濟的陳述是正確的？
(1) 第二年的名義本地生產總值增長率是 25%。
(2) 第二年的實質本地生產總值上升。
(3) 第二年的本地生產總值平減物價指數下降。

Which of the following statements about the economy are correct?
(1) The growth rate of nominal GDP is 25% in Year 2.
(2) There is an increase in real GDP in Year 2.
(3) There is a decrease in GDP deflator in Year 2.

A. 只有 (1)、(2) / (1) and (2) only
B. 只有 (1)、(3) / (1) and (3) only
C. 只有 (2)、(3) / (2) and (3) only
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3) / (1), (2) and (3)

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這條題目，我們需要掌握以下三個核心公式：

To solve this question, we need to master the following three core formulas:

  • Nominal GDP (名義本地生產總值): 以當年價格計算的產值。
    The value of output calculated at current year prices.
    $$ \text{Nominal GDP} = \sum (P_{\text{current}} \times Q_{\text{current}}) $$
  • Real GDP (實質本地生產總值): 以基年（Base Year）價格計算的產值，反映實際產量的變化。
    The value of output calculated at base year prices, reflecting the actual change in quantity.
    $$ \text{Real GDP} = \sum (P_{\text{base}} \times Q_{\text{current}}) $$
  • GDP Deflator (本地生產總值平減物價指數): 反映整體物價水平的指標。基年的指數必定為 100。
    An indicator reflecting the overall price level. The index in the base year is always 100.
    $$ \text{GDP Deflator} = \frac{\text{Nominal GDP}}{\text{Real GDP}} \times 100 $$

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是 / The correct option is: Option D

Step-by-Step Analysis (逐步分析):

陳述 (1) 分析 / Statement (1) Analysis:

首先計算兩年的名義本地生產總值 (Nominal GDP)。
第一年名義 GDP = \( (20 \times 5) + (50 \times 6) = 100 + 300 = 400 \)
第二年名義 GDP = \( (30 \times 8) + (65 \times 4) = 240 + 260 = 500 \)
增長率 = \( \frac{500 - 400}{400} \times 100\% = 25\% \)。陳述 (1) 正確。

First, calculate the Nominal GDP for both years.
Year 1 Nominal GDP = \( (20 \times 5) + (50 \times 6) = 100 + 300 = 400 \)
Year 2 Nominal GDP = \( (30 \times 8) + (65 \times 4) = 240 + 260 = 500 \)
Growth rate = \( \frac{500 - 400}{400} \times 100\% = 25\% \). Statement (1) is correct.

陳述 (2) 分析 / Statement (2) Analysis:

計算實質本地生產總值 (Real GDP) 需要固定價格在基年（第一年）。
第一年實質 GDP = 第一年名義 GDP = \( 400 \)
第二年實質 GDP = \( (30 \times 5) + (65 \times 6) = 150 + 390 = 540 \)
因為 \( 540 > 400 \)，實質 GDP 上升了。陳述 (2) 正確。

Calculating Real GDP requires fixing the prices at the base year (Year 1).
Year 1 Real GDP = Year 1 Nominal GDP = \( 400 \)
Year 2 Real GDP = \( (30 \times 5) + (65 \times 6) = 150 + 390 = 540 \)
Since \( 540 > 400 \), real GDP increased. Statement (2) is correct.

陳述 (3) 分析 / Statement (3) Analysis:

計算第二年的 GDP 平減物價指數。
第二年 GDP Deflator = \( \frac{500}{540} \times 100 \approx 92.59 \)
基年（第一年）的指數必定是 \( 100 \)。因為 \( 92.59 < 100 \)，指數下降了。陳述 (3) 正確。

Calculate the GDP deflator for Year 2.
Year 2 GDP Deflator = \( \frac{500}{540} \times 100 \approx 92.59 \)
The index for the base year (Year 1) is always \( 100 \). Since \( 92.59 < 100 \), the deflator decreased. Statement (3) is correct.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：忘記基年的平減物價指數是 100。
很多學生計算出第二年的平減物價指數是 92.59 後，不知道如何判斷它是上升還是下降，因為他們沒有計算第一年的指數。記住：基年（Base Year）的 GDP Deflator 永遠是 100！

Trap: Forgetting the base year GDP deflator is 100.
Many students calculate the Year 2 deflator as 92.59 but don't know how to judge if it increased or decreased because they didn't calculate Year 1. Remember: The base year GDP Deflator is ALWAYS 100!

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺陳述 (2)： 觀察題目數據，玩具產量由 20 升至 30，成衣產量由 50 升至 65。既然所有物品的產量都上升了，實質 GDP（反映總產量）必定上升！完全不需要計算！

Instant kill for Statement (2): Observe the data. Toy quantity increased from 20 to 30, and clothing quantity increased from 50 to 65. Since the quantities of ALL goods increased, Real GDP (which reflects total output) MUST increase! No calculation needed!

秒殺陳述 (3)： 我們知道 \( \text{Deflator} = \frac{\text{Nominal GDP}}{\text{Real GDP}} \times 100 \)。在第二年，名義 GDP (500) 小於 實質 GDP (540)。這意味著分數 \( \frac{500}{540} < 1 \)，所以 Deflator 必定小於 100。既然基年是 100，它必定是下降了。

Instant kill for Statement (3): We know \( \text{Deflator} = \frac{\text{Nominal GDP}}{\text{Real GDP}} \times 100 \). In Year 2, Nominal GDP (500) is less than Real GDP (540). This means the fraction \( \frac{500}{540} < 1 \), so the Deflator must be less than 100. Since the base year is 100, it must have decreased.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 題目沒有明示哪一年是基年 (Base Year)，我怎麼知道要用第一年？
A: 在 DSE 經濟科中，除非題目特別指明，否則我們一律預設較早的年份（即第一年）為基年。

Q: The question didn't specify which year is the base year. How do I know to use Year 1?
A: In DSE Economics, unless specified otherwise, we always assume the earlier year (i.e., Year 1) is the base year.
