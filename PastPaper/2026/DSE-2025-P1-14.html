HKDSE Economics Question Analysis

Topic (課題): 供求關係、彈性與總收入 (Supply & Demand, Elasticity and Total Revenue)

Difficulty (難度): ★★★★☆

Key Concepts (核心概念): 競爭性供應 (Competitive Supply), 聯合供應 (Joint Supply), 需求價格彈性 (Price Elasticity of Demand), 總收入 (Total Revenue)

The Question (題目)

若物品 X 和物品 Y 是 ______ ，而物品 Y 的需求屬 ______ ，物品 X 的需求下跌會令物品 Y 的總收入下跌。

(1) 競爭性供應 …… 高彈性
(2) 競爭性供應 …… 低彈性
(3) 聯合供應 …… 高彈性
(4) 聯合供應 …… 低彈性

A. 只有 (1)、(3)
B. 只有 (1)、(4)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. 只有 (2)、(4)

If good X and good Y are in ________ and the demand for good Y is ________, a decrease in the demand for good X will lead to a decrease in the total revenue of good Y.

(1) competitive supply ...... elastic
(2) competitive supply ...... inelastic
(3) joint supply ...... elastic
(4) joint supply ...... inelastic

A. (1) and (3) only
B. (1) and (4) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (2) and (4) only

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這條題目，我們需要結合兩個核心概念：供應關係以及彈性與總收入的關係。

To solve this question, we need to combine two core concepts: supply relationships and the relationship between elasticity and total revenue.
  • 競爭性供應 (Competitive Supply): 生產者使用相同的資源來生產兩種物品。當物品 X 的產量減少時，生產者可以將資源轉移去生產物品 Y，導致物品 Y 的供應增加。
    Producers use the same resources to produce two goods. When the production of X decreases, resources can be shifted to produce Y, leading to an increase in the supply of Y.
  • 聯合供應 (Joint Supply): 兩種物品是一起被生產出來的（例如牛肉和牛皮）。當物品 X 的產量減少時，作為副產品的物品 Y 的產量也會隨之減少，導致物品 Y 的供應減少。
    Two goods are produced together as by-products (e.g., beef and leather). When the production of X decreases, the production of Y also decreases, leading to a decrease in the supply of Y.

總收入 (Total Revenue, TR):

$$ TR = P \times Q $$

彈性與總收入的關係 (Relationship between Elasticity and TR):

  • 若需求屬高彈性 (Elastic, \(E_d > 1\))：價格與總收入呈反向變動。價格上升，總收入下跌；價格下跌，總收入上升。
    If demand is elastic: Price and TR move in opposite directions. \(P \uparrow \Rightarrow TR \downarrow\) and \(P \downarrow \Rightarrow TR \uparrow\).
  • 若需求屬低彈性 (Inelastic, \(E_d < 1\))：價格與總收入呈同向變動。價格上升，總收入上升；價格下跌，總收入下跌。
    If demand is inelastic: Price and TR move in the same direction. \(P \uparrow \Rightarrow TR \uparrow\) and \(P \downarrow \Rightarrow TR \downarrow\).

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是 (Correct Option): Option C

第一步：分析物品 X 需求下跌的影響
當物品 X 的需求下跌 \(D_X \downarrow\)，物品 X 的價格和交易量都會下跌 \(P_X \downarrow, Q_X \downarrow\)。

Step 1: Analyze the effect of a decrease in demand for Good X
When the demand for Good X decreases \(D_X \downarrow\), both its price and quantity transacted will decrease \(P_X \downarrow, Q_X \downarrow\).

第二步：分析不同情況下的組合

Step 2: Analyze the combinations under different scenarios

情況一：競爭性供應 (Competitive Supply)
因為 \(Q_X \downarrow\)，生產者釋放了資源，導致物品 Y 的供應增加 \(S_Y \uparrow\)。這會令物品 Y 的價格下跌、交易量上升 (\(P_Y \downarrow, Q_Y \uparrow\))。
題目要求物品 Y 的總收入下跌 (\(TR_Y \downarrow\))。當價格下跌時，要令總收入下跌，需求必須是低彈性 (Inelastic)。因此，(2) 是正確的，(1) 是錯誤的。

Scenario 1: Competitive Supply
Since \(Q_X \downarrow\), resources are freed up, leading to an increase in the supply of Good Y (\(S_Y \uparrow\)). This causes the price of Y to fall and quantity to rise (\(P_Y \downarrow, Q_Y \uparrow\)).
The question requires the total revenue of Y to decrease (\(TR_Y \downarrow\)). When price falls, for TR to decrease, the demand must be inelastic. Therefore, (2) is correct and (1) is incorrect.

情況二：聯合供應 (Joint Supply)
因為 \(Q_X \downarrow\)，物品 Y 作為副產品的產量也會減少，導致物品 Y 的供應減少 \(S_Y \downarrow\)。這會令物品 Y 的價格上升、交易量下跌 (\(P_Y \uparrow, Q_Y \downarrow\))。
題目要求物品 Y 的總收入下跌 (\(TR_Y \downarrow\))。當價格上升時，要令總收入下跌，需求必須是高彈性 (Elastic)。因此，(3) 是正確的，(4) 是錯誤的。

Scenario 2: Joint Supply
Since \(Q_X \downarrow\), the production of Y as a by-product also decreases, leading to a decrease in the supply of Good Y (\(S_Y \downarrow\)). This causes the price of Y to rise and quantity to fall (\(P_Y \uparrow, Q_Y \downarrow\)).
The question requires the total revenue of Y to decrease (\(TR_Y \downarrow\)). When price rises, for TR to decrease, the demand must be elastic. Therefore, (3) is correct and (4) is incorrect.

結論：(2) 和 (3) 正確，故答案為 C。

Conclusion: (2) and (3) are correct, so the answer is C.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目是典型的「連鎖反應」題 (Chain Reaction Question)。解題的「樞紐 (Pivot Point)」在於準確推導出物品 Y 的價格變化。

This is a typical "Chain Reaction" question. The "Pivot Point" of solving it lies in accurately deducing the price change of Good Y.
  1. 先找出 X 的產量變化 (\(Q_X \downarrow\))。
  2. 根據供應關係，判斷 Y 的供應變化 (\(S_Y\))。
  3. 畫圖或在腦海中推演，找出 Y 的價格變化 (\(P_Y\))。
  4. 最後，利用 \(P_Y\) 的變化方向，配合目標 (\(TR_Y \downarrow\))，反推所需的彈性。
  1. First, find the change in output of X (\(Q_X \downarrow\)).
  2. Based on the supply relationship, determine the change in supply of Y (\(S_Y\)).
  3. Deduce the price change of Y (\(P_Y\)).
  4. Finally, use the direction of \(P_Y\) change and the goal (\(TR_Y \downarrow\)) to deduce the required elasticity.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：混淆需求關係與供應關係
很多同學看到「X 需求下跌」，就立刻聯想到替代品 (Substitutes) 或互補品 (Complements)。但請注意，題目問的是競爭性供應和聯合供應，這是生產者層面 (Producer side) 的關係，影響的是物品 Y 的供應 (Supply)，而不是需求！

Trap: Confusing Demand Relationships with Supply Relationships
Many students see "demand for X decreases" and immediately think of Substitutes or Complements. But note that the question specifies competitive supply and joint supply. This is a relationship on the producer side, which affects the supply of Good Y, not its demand!

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼 X 的需求下跌，會影響 Y 的供應？

A: 因為 X 需求下跌會導致 X 的價格下跌，生產者為了減少利潤損失，會減少生產 X（即 \(Q_X\) 下跌）。在生產過程中，X 和 Y 共享資源（競爭性供應）或一起被製造（聯合供應），所以 X 產量的改變會直接改變 Y 的供應量。

Q: Why does a decrease in demand for X affect the supply of Y?

A: Because a decrease in demand for X leads to a lower price for X. To minimize profit loss, producers will produce less X (i.e., \(Q_X\) decreases). Since X and Y share resources (competitive supply) or are produced together (joint supply), the change in the production quantity of X directly alters the supply of Y.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法：畫箭頭筆記 (Arrow Notation Shortcut)
在試卷旁邊快速寫下這兩行：
1. 競爭：\(D_X \downarrow \rightarrow Q_X \downarrow \rightarrow S_Y \uparrow \rightarrow P_Y \downarrow\)。要 \(TR \downarrow\)，\(P\) 和 \(TR\) 同向，所以是低彈性 (Inelastic)。
2. 聯合：\(D_X \downarrow \rightarrow Q_X \downarrow \rightarrow S_Y \downarrow \rightarrow P_Y \uparrow\)。要 \(TR \downarrow\)，\(P\) 和 \(TR\) 反向，所以是高彈性 (Elastic)。

Shortcut: Arrow Notation
Quickly jot down these two lines on the margin of your exam paper:
1. Competitive: \(D_X \downarrow \rightarrow Q_X \downarrow \rightarrow S_Y \uparrow \rightarrow P_Y \downarrow\). To get \(TR \downarrow\), \(P\) and \(TR\) move in the same direction, so it's inelastic.
2. Joint: \(D_X \downarrow \rightarrow Q_X \downarrow \rightarrow S_Y \downarrow \rightarrow P_Y \uparrow\). To get \(TR \downarrow\), \(P\) and \(TR\) move in opposite directions, so it's elastic.
