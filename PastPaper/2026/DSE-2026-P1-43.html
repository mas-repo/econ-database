HKDSE Economics Tutor Analysis

Topic (課題): International Trade - Import Quota (國際貿易 - 進口配額)

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5 Stars)

Key Concepts (核心概念): Small Open Economy (小型開放經濟), Effective Import Quota (有效進口配額), Domestic Supply (本地供應), World Price (世界價格)

The Question (題目)

某小型開放經濟對小麥實施一項有效進口配額。若小麥的本地生產成本下降，______。

(1) 小麥的本地生產量會上升
(2) 小麥的進口量會上升
(3) 小麥的本地價格會下降
(4) 小麥的世界價格會下降

A. 只有 (1)、(3)
B. 只有 (1)、(4)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. 只有 (2)、(4)

A small open economy has imposed an effective import quota on wheat. If the domestic production cost of wheat decreases, ______.

(1) the quantity of wheat produced domestically will increase
(2) the import quantity of wheat will increase
(3) the domestic price of wheat will decrease
(4) the world price of wheat will decrease

A. (1) and (3) only
B. (1) and (4) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (2) and (4) only

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這道題目，我們需要清楚理解以下幾個國際貿易的核心概念：

To solve this question, we need to clearly understand the following core concepts in international trade:
  • 小型開放經濟 (Small Open Economy): 該經濟體在世界市場中只佔極小部分，是「價格接受者」(Price taker)。其本土政策或供求改變不會影響世界價格 \(P_w\)。
    The economy accounts for a very small portion of the world market and is a "price taker". Its domestic policies or changes in supply/demand will not affect the world price \(P_w\).
  • 有效進口配額 (Effective Import Quota): 政府對進口數量設定上限，且該上限小於自由貿易下的進口量。這會導致本地價格 \(P_d\) 高於世界價格 \(P_w\)。
    The government sets a physical limit on the import quantity, and this limit is smaller than the free-trade import quantity. This causes the domestic price \(P_d\) to be higher than the world price \(P_w\).
  • 實施配額後的總供應 (Total Supply under Quota):
    Total supply after imposing a quota:
    $$ S_{total} = S_{domestic} + Quota $$
    當本地生產成本下降，本地供應曲線 \(S_{domestic}\) 會向右移，帶動總供應曲線 \(S_{total}\) 同樣向右移。
    When domestic production cost decreases, the domestic supply curve \(S_{domestic}\) shifts to the right, which in turn shifts the total supply curve \(S_{total}\) to the right.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): A

分析 (1)：小麥的本地生產量會上升

正確。本地生產成本下降會令本地供應增加（供應曲線向右移）。因為總供應增加，本地價格 \(P_d\) 會下降。在較低的價格下，總需求量會增加。由於進口量被配額限制在固定水平，為了滿足增加的總需求量，本地生產量必須上升。

Correct. A decrease in production cost increases domestic supply (supply curve shifts right). As total supply increases, the domestic price \(P_d\) decreases. At a lower price, the total quantity demanded increases. Since the import quantity is fixed by the quota, domestic production must increase to satisfy the higher total quantity demanded.

分析 (2)：小麥的進口量會上升

錯誤。「有效進口配額」的意思是進口量已經達到了政府規定的上限。只要配額仍然有效，進口量就會被鎖定在配額數量，不會上升。

Incorrect. An "effective import quota" means the import quantity has already reached the maximum limit set by the government. As long as the quota remains effective, the import quantity is locked at the quota level and will not increase.

分析 (3)：小麥的本地價格會下降

正確。如前所述，本地供應增加帶動總供應增加（總供應曲線向右移）。在需求不變的情況下，總供應增加必然導致均衡的本地價格 \(P_d\) 下降。

Correct. As mentioned earlier, an increase in domestic supply leads to an increase in total supply (total supply curve shifts right). Assuming demand remains constant, an increase in total supply will inevitably lead to a decrease in the equilibrium domestic price \(P_d\).

分析 (4)：小麥的世界價格會下降

錯誤。題目特別註明這是一個「小型開放經濟」。小型經濟體的任何內部變動（如生產成本下降）都微不足道，絕對無法影響龐大的世界市場價格 \(P_w\)。

Incorrect. The question specifically states this is a "small open economy". Any internal changes in a small economy (like a decrease in production cost) are insignificant and absolutely cannot affect the vast world market price \(P_w\).

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的「破題關鍵」(Pivot Point) 在於題目給出的兩個前設條件：

The "Pivot Point" of this type of question lies in the two prerequisites given:
  1. 「小型開放經濟」： 看到這六個字，立刻知道世界價格 \(P_w\) 是常數（不會變）。這直接幫我們排除了選項 (4)。
    "Small open economy": Seeing this immediately tells you the world price \(P_w\) is constant. This directly eliminates option (4).
  2. 「有效進口配額」： 看到這幾個字，立刻知道進口量已經「封頂」（Maxed out）。除非本地價格跌穿世界價格令配額失效，否則進口量絕不可能增加。這直接幫我們排除了選項 (2)。
    "Effective import quota": Seeing this immediately tells you imports are "maxed out". Unless the domestic price falls below the world price making the quota ineffective, imports can never increase. This directly eliminates option (2).

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：混淆配額 (Quota) 與關稅 (Tariff) 的效果

很多同學會把配額和關稅混為一談。如果是實施關稅，當本地生產成本下降（本地供應增加）時，本地生產量會上升，而進口量是會下降的（因為本地貨更平，取代了部分進口）。但本題是配額，只要配額仍然有效，進口量就是固定的上限，不會改變（更不會上升）。

Trap: Confusing the effects of Quota and Tariff
Many students confuse quotas with tariffs. If a tariff were imposed, a decrease in domestic production cost (increase in domestic supply) would increase domestic production, and imports would decrease (because cheaper domestic goods replace some imports). But this question is about a quota. As long as it's effective, the import quantity is a fixed maximum limit and will not change (and certainly not increase).

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 題目說本地生產成本下降，有沒有可能成本跌得太多，令本地價格 \(P_d\) 跌穿世界價格 \(P_w\)？

A: 理論上是有可能的！如果成本大幅下降，本地均衡價格跌至低於 \(P_w\)，該國就會由進口國變成出口國。在這種情況下，進口量會變成 0（即進口量下降）。所以無論如何，進口量都不可能上升，選項 (2) 始終是錯的。

Q: The question says domestic production cost decreases. Is it possible that the cost drops so much that the domestic price \(P_d\) falls below the world price \(P_w\)?
A: Theoretically, yes! If the cost drops drastically and the domestic equilibrium price falls below \(P_w\), the country would turn from an importer into an exporter. In this case, the import quantity would become 0 (i.e., imports decrease). Therefore, no matter what, the import quantity cannot increase, making option (2) incorrect in all scenarios.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

這題可以使用「秒殺排除法」(Elimination Method)：

You can use the Elimination Method to solve this in seconds:
  • Step 1: 看到「小型經濟」，即時知道世界價格不變。-> (4) 錯。
    Step 1: See "small economy", immediately know world price is unchanged. -> (4) is wrong.
  • Step 2: 看看選項，B 和 D 都包含 (4)，直接劃掉 B 和 D。
    Step 2: Look at the options, B and D contain (4). Cross out B and D.
  • Step 3: 剩下 A (1,3) 和 C (2,3)。我們只需要判斷 (1) 或 (2) 哪個對。
    Step 3: Left with A (1,3) and C (2,3). We only need to judge whether (1) or (2) is correct.
  • Step 4: 看到「有效配額」，知道進口量已達上限，不可能再升。-> (2) 錯。
    Step 4: See "effective quota", know imports are at maximum limit, cannot increase. -> (2) is wrong.
  • 結論：排除 C，答案肯定是 A！完全不需要畫圖或深入思考 (1) 和 (3) 的複雜邏輯。
    Conclusion: Eliminate C, the answer must be A! You don't even need to draw a graph or think deeply about the complex logic of (1) and (3).
