HKDSE Economics Analysis (經濟學題目分析)

Topic (課題): Labour Market - Labour Mobility (勞工市場 - 勞工流動性)
Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆
Key Concepts (核心概念): Geographical Mobility (地域流動性) Occupational Mobility (職業流動性) Labour (勞力)

1. The Question (題目)

下列哪項／些會令某國勞工的地域流動性上升？

(1) 政府增加給予工人的交通津貼金額。
(2) 政府增加再培訓計劃的津貼。
(3) 外國工人需要申請額外簽證才可在該國工作。

A. 只有 (1)
B. 只有 (2)
C. 只有 (1)、(3)
D. 只有 (2)、(3)

Which of the following will increase the geographical mobility of labour in a country?

(1) The government increases the amount of travelling subsidies to workers.
(2) The government increases the subsidies on retraining programmes.
(3) Foreign workers are required to apply for an additional visa to work in that country.

A. (1) only
B. (2) only
C. (1) and (3) only
D. (2) and (3) only

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

勞工流動性 (Labour Mobility) 是指工人改變其工作地點或職業的容易程度。它主要分為兩類：

  • 地域流動性 (Geographical Mobility): 工人從一個地區（或國家）轉移到另一個地區工作的容易程度。影響因素包括交通成本、住屋成本、簽證限制及文化/語言差異。
  • 職業流動性 (Occupational Mobility): 工人從一種職業轉移到另一種職業的容易程度。影響因素包括技能要求、教育水平及培訓機會。

Labour Mobility refers to the ease with which workers can change their work location or occupation. It is mainly classified into two types:

  • Geographical Mobility: The ease with which workers can move from one region (or country) to another for work. Factors affecting this include transportation costs, housing costs, visa restrictions, and cultural/language barriers.
  • Occupational Mobility: The ease with which workers can switch from one occupation to another. Factors affecting this include skill requirements, education levels, and training opportunities.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option A

(1) 政府增加給予工人的交通津貼金額：正確。
交通津貼降低了工人跨區通勤或搬遷的成本，使他們更容易在不同地區之間移動以尋找或從事工作。因此，這會提高地域流動性。

(1) The government increases the amount of travelling subsidies to workers: CORRECT.
Travelling subsidies lower the cost of commuting or relocating across districts, making it easier for workers to move between regions for work. Therefore, this increases geographical mobility.

(2) 政府增加再培訓計劃的津貼：錯誤。
再培訓計劃幫助工人獲得新技能，使他們更容易轉換行業或職業。這會提高職業流動性 (Occupational Mobility)，而非地域流動性。

(2) The government increases the subsidies on retraining programmes: INCORRECT.
Retraining programmes help workers acquire new skills, making it easier for them to switch industries or occupations. This increases occupational mobility, not geographical mobility.

(3) 外國工人需要申請額外簽證才可在該國工作：錯誤。
額外的簽證要求增加了外國工人進入該國勞動市場的難度、時間和成本。這構成了勞工跨國移動的障礙，因此會降低（而非提高）地域流動性。

(3) Foreign workers are required to apply for an additional visa to work in that country: INCORRECT.
Additional visa requirements increase the difficulty, time, and cost for foreign workers to enter the country's labour market. This creates a barrier to the cross-border movement of labour, thus decreasing (rather than increasing) geographical mobility.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解答這類題目的關鍵點 (Pivot Point) 在於嚴格區分「地點改變」與「技能/職業改變」。

  • 看到「交通 (Transport)」、「房屋 (Housing)」、「簽證 (Visa)」、「移民 (Immigration)」等字眼，應聯想到地域流動性。
  • 看到「技能 (Skills)」、「教育 (Education)」、「培訓 (Training)」、「發牌制度 (Licensing)」等字眼，應聯想到職業流動性。

The Pivot Point for solving this type of question is to strictly distinguish between a "change in location" and a "change in skills/occupation".

  • When you see keywords like "Transport", "Housing", "Visa", or "Immigration", you should link them to Geographical Mobility.
  • When you see keywords like "Skills", "Education", "Training", or "Licensing", you should link them to Occupational Mobility.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：混淆兩種流動性
學生常犯的錯誤是認為「任何對工人有利的政策都會提高所有流動性」。例如，誤以為再培訓津貼（選項2）能全面提升工人的適應力，從而連帶提升地域流動性。在經濟學考試中，我們必須將政策的直接影響對號入座。

Trap: Confusing the two types of mobility
A common mistake is assuming that "any policy beneficial to workers increases all types of mobility." For example, mistakenly thinking that retraining subsidies (Statement 2) comprehensively improve workers' adaptability and thus indirectly increase geographical mobility. In Economics exams, you must match the direct impact of a policy to the specific concept.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼選項 (3) 不是增加本地工人的流動性？
A: 題目問及的是「某國勞工的地域流動性 (geographical mobility of labour in a country)」，這包含了勞工流入和流出該國的容易程度。簽證限制增加了勞工跨越邊境的摩擦力 (friction)，整體上降低了勞工作為生產要素的地域流動性，而不會提升本地工人的地域流動性。

Q: Why doesn't statement (3) increase the mobility of local workers?
A: The question asks about the "geographical mobility of labour in a country", which includes the ease of labour flowing into and out of the country. Visa restrictions increase the friction of labour moving across borders, thereby decreasing the overall geographical mobility of labour as a factor of production. It does not increase the geographical mobility of local workers.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法：關鍵字排除法
一看到題目問「地域流動性 (Geographical)」，立即掃視選項尋找與「地點/距離」相關的詞彙。選項 (2) 的「再培訓 (retraining)」明顯屬於「職業 (Occupational)」範疇，可直接剔除包含 (2) 的選項 (B 和 D)。接著看 (3)，「額外簽證 (additional visa)」是增加限制，必然降低流動性，剔除 (C)。答案秒選 A。

Shortcut: Keyword Elimination
As soon as you see the question asking for "Geographical mobility", scan the options for words related to "location/distance". "Retraining" in statement (2) clearly belongs to the "Occupational" category, so you can immediately eliminate options containing (2) (Options B and D). Next, look at (3); an "additional visa" is an added restriction, which inevitably decreases mobility, so eliminate (C). The answer is instantly A.
