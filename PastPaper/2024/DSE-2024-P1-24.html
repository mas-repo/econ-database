HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic (課題): National Income Accounting (國民收入會計)
Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts (核心概念): Expenditure Approach (支出面), Gross vs Net (總值與淨值), Net Exports (淨出口)

The Question (題目)

參考下列有關某經濟的資料。Refer to the following data of an economy.

Components (成分) $ million (百萬元)
Private consumption expenditure (私人消費開支)100
Net domestic fixed capital formation (本地固定資本形成淨值)20
Government consumption expenditure (政府消費開支)30
Change in inventories (存貨改變)-5
Net exports of goods (物品出口淨值)60
Re-exports (轉口)27
Import of services (服務進口)80
Export of services (服務出口)40
Depreciation (折舊)11
Indirect tax (間接稅)18

以市價計算的本地生產總值是 ____________ 百萬元。The gross domestic product (GDP) at market price is $ ____________ million.

A. 165
B. 176
C. 194
D. 203

Concept Review (概念重溫)

本題考核使用支出面 (Expenditure Approach) 計算 GDP。 This question tests the calculation of GDP using the Expenditure Approach.

\( \text{GDP} = C + I + G + (X - M) \)
  • C (Private Consumption Expenditure / 私人消費開支): Spending by households on goods and services.
  • I (Gross Investment / 總投資): 這是關鍵！公式中的 \(I\) 是「本地固定資本形成總值」加上「存貨改變」。
    \( \text{Gross Investment} = \text{Net Fixed Capital Formation} + \text{Depreciation} + \text{Change in Inventories} \) This is key! \(I\) in the formula is "Gross domestic fixed capital formation" plus "Change in inventories". Note that Gross = Net + Depreciation.
  • G (Government Consumption Expenditure / 政府消費開支): Spending by the government.
  • NX (Net Exports / 淨出口): \( (X - M) = (\text{Goods Exports} + \text{Service Exports}) - (\text{Goods Imports} + \text{Service Imports}) \) Total exports minus total imports (including both goods and services).

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Answer: B

Step-by-Step Calculation (逐步計算)

Step 1: Calculate Investment (I) / 計算投資 (I)

題目給出的是「本地固定資本形成淨值」。我們需要將其轉換為「總值」並加上存貨改變。 The question gives "Net domestic fixed capital formation". We need to convert it to "Gross" and add change in inventories.

$$ \begin{align*} I &= (\text{Net Fixed Capital} + \text{Depreciation}) + \text{Change in Inventories} \\ I &= (20 + 11) + (-5) \\ I &= 31 - 5 = 26 \end{align*} $$

Step 2: Calculate Net Exports (NX) / 計算淨出口 (NX)

題目直接給出了「物品出口淨值」(Net exports of goods)，即 \( (X_{goods} - M_{goods}) = 60 \)。我們還需要計算服務的淨出口。 The question directly gives "Net exports of goods", which is \( (X_{goods} - M_{goods}) = 60 \). We still need to calculate net exports of services.

$$ \begin{align*} NX &= \text{Net exports of goods} + (\text{Export of services} - \text{Import of services}) \\ NX &= 60 + (40 - 80) \\ NX &= 60 - 40 = 20 \end{align*} $$

Step 3: Summing up GDP / 匯總 GDP

$$ \begin{align*} \text{GDP} &= C + I + G + NX \\ \text{GDP} &= 100 + 26 + 30 + 20 \\ \text{GDP} &= 176 \end{align*} $$

因此，以市價計算的本地生產總值為 176 百萬元。 Therefore, the GDP at market price is $176 million.

Distractor Analysis (干擾項分析)

Option A (165)

錯誤原因： 忘記加上折舊。如果計算投資時只用「淨值」(20) 加「存貨改變」(-5)，得出 \(I = 15\)。
\( 100 + 15 + 30 + 20 = 165 \)。 Error: Forgot to add Depreciation. If Investment is calculated using only "Net value" (20) + "Change in inventories" (-5), resulting in \(I = 15\).

Option C (194)

錯誤原因： 錯誤處理間接稅。GDP (市價) 不需要減去間接稅，也不需要加上間接稅（因為市價已包含）。如果錯誤地加上間接稅 (18)：\( 176 + 18 = 194 \)。 Error: Mishandled Indirect Tax. GDP (at market price) does not need indirect tax added or subtracted (it's already included in market prices). If you wrongly add indirect tax (18): \( 176 + 18 = 194 \).

Option D (203)

錯誤原因： 錯誤地加上了「轉口」(27)。轉口已經包含在總出口數字中，或者在計算淨出口時已經處理，不應單獨再加一次。
\( 176 + 27 = 203 \)。 Error: Wrongly added "Re-exports" (27). Re-exports are already part of total export figures or accounted for in net exports; they should not be added separately.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

1. 尋找「總」與「淨」 (Find "Gross" vs "Net"):
題目要求 GDP (Gross Domestic Product)。
數據給出的是「本地固定資本形成淨值」(Net domestic fixed capital formation)。
Pivot Point (關鍵點): 必須加上折舊 (Depreciation) 才能從 Net 變 Gross。 The question asks for GDP (Gross). The data gives "Net domestic fixed capital formation". You MUST add Depreciation to convert Net to Gross.

2. 處理淨出口 (Handle Net Exports):
題目混合了「物品出口淨值」和分開的「服務進/出口」。
Pivot Point (關鍵點): 物品部分已經淨額結算 (Net-off)，服務部分需要自己計算 (出口 - 進口)。 The question mixes "Net exports of goods" with separate "Import/Export of services". Goods are already netted off; Services must be calculated manually (Export - Import).

3. 識別多餘資訊 (Identify Redundant Info):
- 轉口 (Re-exports): 這是干擾項。轉口包含在物品出口中，題目已給出「物品出口淨值」，無需理會轉口。
- 間接稅 (Indirect Tax): 這是干擾項。題目求「市價」(Market Price)，支出面的數據本身就是市價，無需調整。只有求「要素成本」(Factor Cost) 時才需要減去間接稅。 - Re-exports: Distractor. Included in goods exports; ignore it as "Net exports of goods" is given.
- Indirect Tax: Distractor. The question asks for Market Price. Expenditure data is already at market price. Only subtract indirect tax if calculating at "Factor Cost".

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 陷阱 1：忽略折舊 (Ignoring Depreciation)

    學生常直接把「本地固定資本形成淨值」當作 \(I\) 的一部分，忘記 \( \text{Gross} = \text{Net} + \text{Depreciation} \)。 Students often treat "Net domestic fixed capital formation" directly as part of \(I\), forgetting that \( \text{Gross} = \text{Net} + \text{Depreciation} \).

  • 陷阱 2：服務進出口方向搞反 (Reversing Service Trade)

    公式是 \( (X - M) \)。服務出口是 40，進口是 80。結果應該是 \( 40 - 80 = -40 \)。很多學生會算成 \( 80 - 40 = +40 \)。 The formula is \( (X - M) \). Service Export is 40, Import is 80. Result should be \( 40 - 80 = -40 \). Many students calculate it as \( 80 - 40 = +40 \).

  • 陷阱 3：重複計算轉口 (Double Counting Re-exports)

    看到數據表上有數字就想加進去。記住，轉口是出口的一部分，如果題目給了總出口或淨出口，通常不需要額外處理轉口。 Adding numbers just because they are in the table. Remember, re-exports are part of exports. If Total/Net Exports are given, usually you don't need to add re-exports separately.
