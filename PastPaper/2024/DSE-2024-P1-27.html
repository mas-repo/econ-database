課題 (Topic): 貨幣需求與利率決定 (Money Demand & Interest Rate Determination)
難度 (Difficulty): ★★★☆☆
核心概念 (Key Concepts):
免稅額 Tax Allowance 可支配收入 Disposable Income 交易性貨幣需求 Transactions Demand for Money 名義利率 Nominal Interest Rate

📝 題目 (The Question)

假如薪俸稅的免稅額提高，交易性貨幣需求會 __________ ，而均衡名義利率會__________。

A. 上升 …… 上升
B. 上升 …… 下降
C. 下降 …… 上升
D. 下降 …… 下降
If there is an increase in the tax allowance of salaries tax, transactions demand for money will __________ and the equilibrium nominal interest rate will __________.

A. increase …… increase
B. increase …… decrease
C. decrease …… increase
D. decrease …… decrease

🧠 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

1. 免稅額與可支配收入 (Tax Allowance & Disposable Income)
免稅額是政府允許市民在計算稅款前扣除的入息部分。免稅額提高，市民繳交的薪俸稅減少，從而令可支配收入增加。
Tax allowance is the portion of income that is exempt from taxation. An increase in tax allowance reduces the salaries tax burden, thereby increasing disposable income.
$$Y_d = Y - T$$
(\(Y_d\) = Disposable Income, \(Y\) = Gross Income, \(T\) = Net Taxes)
2. 交易性貨幣需求 (Transactions Demand for Money)
市民持有金錢以應付日常交易的需求。它與國民收入或可支配收入呈正向關係。當可支配收入上升，市民的消費增加，對交易性貨幣的需求也會相應增加。
The demand for holding money to carry out everyday transactions. It is positively related to national or disposable income. When disposable income rises, consumption increases, leading to a higher transactions demand for money.
3. 貨幣市場均衡與利率決定 (Money Market Equilibrium)
在流動性偏好理論中，利率是由貨幣供給 \(M_s\) 和貨幣需求 \(M_d\) 的均衡點決定的。當貨幣需求增加而貨幣供給不變時，會出現超額貨幣需求，導致利率上升。
According to the Liquidity Preference Theory, the interest rate is determined by the equilibrium of money supply \(M_s\) and money demand \(M_d\). If money demand increases while money supply remains constant, an excess demand for money occurs, pushing the interest rate up.
$$M_s = M_d$$ $$M_d = M_t + M_a$$
(\(M_t\) = Transactions Demand, \(M_a\) = Asset Demand)

✅ 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): A
選項 A (正確):
當薪俸稅免稅額提高，個人的稅務負擔減輕，可支配收入 \(Y_d\) 增加。隨着收入增加，人們的消費活動變得頻繁，因此交易性貨幣需求（Transactions demand for money）會上升。在貨幣供應量不變的假設下，總貨幣需求曲線向右移，在原有的利率水平下會出現超額貨幣需求（Excess demand for money）。為獲取更多流動資金，人們會出售債券，導致債券價格下跌。因為債券價格與利率呈反向關係，均衡名義利率會上升。
Option A (Correct): When the tax allowance increases, the tax burden lightens, causing disposable income \(Y_d\) to rise. With higher income, people consume more, so the transactions demand for money increases. Assuming money supply is constant, the total money demand curve shifts to the right, creating an excess demand for money at the original interest rate. To obtain liquidity, people sell bonds, causing bond prices to fall. Since bond prices and interest rates are inversely related, the equilibrium nominal interest rate increases.
選項 B (錯誤):
前半部正確（交易性貨幣需求上升），但後半部錯誤。貨幣需求增加會推高利率，而非降低利率。
Option B (Incorrect): The first half is correct (transactions demand increases), but the second half is wrong. An increase in money demand pushes the interest rate up, not down.
選項 C & D (錯誤):
這兩個選項都假設交易性貨幣需求下降。但免稅額提高會增加可支配收入，這必定會帶動交易性貨幣需求上升，因此 C 和 D 皆可直接排除。
Options C & D (Incorrect): Both options assume the transactions demand for money decreases. However, a higher tax allowance increases disposable income, which definitively drives up the transactions demand for money. Thus, C and D can be eliminated immediately.

🔍 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

這類題目的核心在於掌握「連鎖反應」。我們需要將題目的情境拆解為三個轉折點 (Pivot Points)：
The core of this type of question is mastering the "chain reaction". We need to break down the scenario into three Pivot Points:
  1. 轉折點 1: 財政政策對收入的影響。 題目中的「免稅額提高」是一個干擾詞，你只需將它翻譯為「市民交少咗稅 $\rightarrow$ 可支配收入增加」。
    Pivot 1: Fiscal policy's impact on income. "Increase in tax allowance" is just a trigger phrase. Translate it mentally to: "People pay less tax $\rightarrow$ Disposable income increases."
  2. 轉折點 2: 收入與貨幣需求的關聯。 緊記：交易性貨幣需求只受收入水平影響。收入上升，交易性貨幣需求必然上升。
    Pivot 2: Link between income and money demand. Remember: Transactions demand for money is strictly a function of income. Income goes up, transactions demand goes up.
  3. 轉折點 3: 貨幣市場的供求機制。 貨幣需求增加 $\rightarrow$ 市民沽售債券套現 $\rightarrow$ 債券價格跌 $\rightarrow$ 利率升。
    Pivot 3: Money market mechanism. Money demand increases $\rightarrow$ People sell bonds for cash $\rightarrow$ Bond prices drop $\rightarrow$ Interest rate rises.

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱 1：混淆貨幣需求與貨幣供應
部分學生會誤以為市民手上的錢多了（可支配收入增加），代表「貨幣供應（Money Supply）」增加，從而錯誤推論出利率會下降。注意：免稅額改變只影響財富分配和收入，不會改變中央銀行發行的貨幣總量（即貨幣供應 \(M_s\) 不變）。
Trap 1: Confusing Money Demand with Money Supply
Some students mistakenly think that because people have more money on hand (higher disposable income), the "Money Supply" has increased, leading to the wrong conclusion that interest rates will fall. Note: Changes in tax allowance only affect income distribution; they do not change the total money base issued by the central bank (i.e., Money Supply \(M_s\) remains constant).
陷阱 2：忘記資產需求（Asset Demand）的移動特徵
不要以為利率上升是因為資產性貨幣需求曲線移動。資產性貨幣需求與利率呈反向關係，利率變動只會造成「沿著曲線移動（Movement along the curve）」，而導致整體貨幣需求曲線（整體 \(M_d\)）向右平移的元兇是交易性貨幣需求的增加。
Trap 2: Forgetting the characteristics of Asset Demand
Do not think the interest rate rises because the asset demand curve shifts. Asset demand is inversely related to the interest rate; a change in the interest rate only causes a "movement along the curve". The culprit that shifts the entire money demand curve to the right is the increase in transactions demand.

🙋 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 既然利率上升了，這會不會反過來令投資減少，甚至影響整體經濟？
Q: Since the interest rate increases, won't this in turn decrease investment and affect the overall economy?
A: 你的思路很宏觀，非常好！這被稱為「排擠效應 (Crowding-out effect)」。利率上升確實會增加借貸成本，從而令私人投資 (Investment) 下降。但在解答這條多項選擇題時，我們只需要聚焦於貨幣市場 (Money Market) 的即時反應，不需要過度推演至商品市場的後續影響，否則容易選錯答案。
A: Your macroeconomic thinking is excellent! This is related to the "Crowding-out effect". A higher interest rate indeed increases borrowing costs, leading to a decrease in private investment. However, for this specific MCQ, we only need to focus on the immediate reaction in the Money Market. Overthinking the subsequent effects on the goods market can lead to choosing the wrong distractor.

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

技巧：背誦「收入—利率」連鎖公式
在考場上，為了節省時間，你可以直接記住這個宏觀經濟學的經典連鎖反應（假設貨幣供給不變）：
Trick: Memorize the "Income-Interest Rate" Chain
To save time during the exam, you can memorize this classic macroeconomic chain reaction (assuming money supply is constant):
$$Y \uparrow \implies M_d \uparrow \implies R \uparrow$$
只要題目中任何政策導致市民整體收入 \(Y\) 上升（如減稅、派發消費券），必然帶動貨幣需求 \(M_d\) 上升，最終必定推高利率 \(R\)。秒殺這類題目！
As long as any policy in the question leads to an increase in people's overall income \(Y\) (e.g., tax cuts, distribution of consumption vouchers), it will inevitably drive up money demand \(M_d\), which ultimately pushes up the interest rate \(R\). You can solve these questions in seconds!
