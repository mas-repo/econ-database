HKDSE Economics Tutorial 經濟學導修

Topic (課題): Macroeconomics - AS-AD Model (宏觀經濟學 - 總供應及總需求模型)

Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts (核心概念): Aggregate Demand (總需求), Long-Run Aggregate Supply (長期總供應), Labor Force (勞動人口)

1. The Question (題目)

假設大量高技術境外人才獲批來港定居及工作。下列哪幅圖最能說明其對香港經濟的影響？

Suppose a large number of highly skilled foreign talents have been admitted to live and work in Hong Kong. Which of the following diagrams best illustrates the effects on Hong Kong’s economy?

  • A. Only AD shifts right (只有總需求向右移)
  • B. Both AD and LRAS shift right (總需求及長期總供應均向右移)
  • C. Only SRAS shifts right (只有短期總供應向右移)
  • D. Only LRAS shifts right (只有長期總供應向右移)

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這條問題，我們需要理解總需求 \(AD\) 和長期總供應 \(LRAS\) 的決定因素：

To answer this question, we need to understand the determinants of Aggregate Demand \(AD\) and Long-Run Aggregate Supply \(LRAS\):

1. 總需求 (Aggregate Demand, AD): 代表經濟體系內對物品和服務的總需求量。當人口增加（例如新移民定居），整體消費開支 \(C\) 會上升，導致 \(AD\) 向右移。

1. Aggregate Demand (AD): Represents the total demand for goods and services in the economy. When the population increases (e.g., new immigrants settling), overall consumption expenditure \(C\) rises, causing \(AD\) to shift to the right.

$$ AD = C + I + G + NX $$

2. 長期總供應 (Long-Run Aggregate Supply, LRAS): 取決於生產要素（土地、勞力、資本、企業家精神）的數量和質量，以及技術水平。「高技術人才」的加入同時增加了勞動力的數量和質量，提升了經濟的潛在產出，導致 \(LRAS\) 向右移。

2. Long-Run Aggregate Supply (LRAS): Depends on the quantity and quality of factors of production (land, labor, capital, entrepreneurship) and the level of technology. The influx of "highly skilled talents" increases both the quantity and quality of the labor force, raising the economy's potential output and causing \(LRAS\) to shift to the right.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: B (正確答案：B)

詳細分析 (Step-by-Step Analysis):

  • Option A: 錯誤。只顯示了 \(AD\) 增加，忽略了人才對生產力 ( \(LRAS\) ) 的正面影響。
    Incorrect. It only shows an increase in \(AD\), ignoring the positive impact of talents on productivity ( \(LRAS\) ).
  • Option B: 正確。人才「定居」增加了人口，帶動消費，使 \(AD\) 右移；「高技術」及「工作」增加了勞動力的數量和質量，提升潛在產出，使 \(LRAS\) 右移。這幅圖最全面地反映了雙重影響。
    Correct. Talents "living" increases the population, boosting consumption and shifting \(AD\) right. "Highly skilled" and "working" increase labor quantity and quality, raising potential output and shifting \(LRAS\) right. This diagram best illustrates the dual effect.
  • Option C: 錯誤。只顯示了短期總供應 \(SRAS\) 的變動，未能反映長期生產容量的擴張及總需求的增加。
    Incorrect. It only shows a shift in Short-Run Aggregate Supply \(SRAS\), failing to reflect the expansion in long-run productive capacity and the increase in aggregate demand.
  • Option D: 錯誤。只顯示了 \(LRAS\) 增加，忽略了人口增加帶來的消費需求 ( \(AD\) ) 上升。
    Incorrect. It only shows an increase in \(LRAS\), ignoring the rise in consumption demand ( \(AD\) ) brought by population growth.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

關鍵字眼 (Pivot Points):

  1. 「定居 (live)」: 意味著香港總人口增加。人口增加必然導致整體消費支出 \(C\) 上升，因此 \(AD\) 會向右移。
  2. 「高技術...工作 (highly skilled... work)」: 意味著勞動人口數量增加，且平均技術水平（勞動質量）上升。這會擴大經濟的生產容量，因此 \(LRAS\) 會向右移。

Keywords (Pivot Points):

  1. "live": Implies an increase in HK's total population. A larger population inevitably leads to higher overall consumption expenditure \(C\), so \(AD\) shifts right.
  2. "highly skilled... work": Implies an increase in the size of the labor force and an improvement in average skill level (labor quality). This expands the economy's productive capacity, so \(LRAS\) shifts right.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

單向思維陷阱： 學生常犯的錯誤是只看到「人才」就立刻聯想到生產力（選擇 D），或者只看到「人口增加」就只聯想到消費（選擇 A）。在宏觀經濟學中，移民政策通常會同時產生需求面 (Demand-side) 和 供應面 (Supply-side) 的影響。

One-track mind trap: A common mistake is seeing "talents" and immediately thinking only of productivity (choosing D), or seeing "population growth" and thinking only of consumption (choosing A). In macroeconomics, immigration policies usually have both Demand-side and Supply-side effects simultaneously.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼圖 B 中沒有顯示 SRAS (短期總供應) 的移動？

A: 實際上，當 \(LRAS\) 向右移時，\(SRAS\) 通常也會向右移。但在 DSE 的多項選擇題中，圖表往往會簡化以突出最重要的長期結構性改變。圖 B 已經是四個選項中「最能說明 (best illustrates)」整體經濟影響的圖表。

Q: Why doesn't diagram B show a shift in SRAS (Short-Run Aggregate Supply)?

A: In reality, when \(LRAS\) shifts right, \(SRAS\) typically shifts right as well. However, in DSE MC questions, diagrams are often simplified to highlight the most important long-term structural changes. Diagram B is already the one that "best illustrates" the overall economic impact among the four choices.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法： 遇到「移民 / 專才輸入」的題目，記住口訣：「有人就有消費 (\(AD \uparrow\))，有工做就有產出 (\(LRAS \uparrow\))」。直接尋找 \(AD\) 和 \(LRAS\) 同時向右移的選項，秒殺題目！

Shortcut: When encountering "immigration / talent scheme" questions, remember this rule of thumb: "People bring consumption (\(AD \uparrow\)), workers bring output (\(LRAS \uparrow\))". Directly look for the option where both \(AD\) and \(LRAS\) shift to the right to solve it instantly!
