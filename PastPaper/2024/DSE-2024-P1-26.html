DSE Economics: Unemployment Rate Analysis

Topic: Measurement of Economic Performance Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Unemployment Rate, Labour Force, Underemployment
題目： 假設某經濟的失業率是5%。下列哪些情況會令該經濟的失業率下降？
Question: Suppose the unemployment rate of an economy is 5%. Which of the following situations would lead to a decrease in the unemployment rate of the economy?
(1) 輸入外勞以填補不同行業的職位空缺。
(1) Foreign workers are imported to fill the vacancies in various industries.
(2) 加入勞動市場的應屆大學畢業生中，96%成功獲得聘用。
(2) 96% of the new university graduates who enter the labour market are employed.
(3) 一些就業不足的工人的工時增加。
(3) Some underemployed workers work for more hours.
  • A. 只有 (1)、(2)   /   (1) and (2) only
  • B. 只有 (1)、(3)   /   (1) and (3) only
  • C. 只有 (2)、(3)   /   (2) and (3) only
  • D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)   /   (1), (2) and (3)
概念重溫 Concept Review

1. 失業率公式 (Unemployment Rate Formula):

$$ \text{Unemployment Rate} (u) = \frac{\text{Unemployed Population} (U)}{\text{Labour Force} (L)} \times 100\% $$

其中，勞動人口 \( L = \text{就業人口} (E) + \text{失業人口} (U) \)。
Where Labour Force \( L = \text{Employed} (E) + \text{Unemployed} (U) \).

2. 就業不足 (Underemployment):

就業不足的人士（例如兼職想轉全職但找不到工作的人）在統計上被視為就業人口 (Employed)。
Underemployed persons (e.g., part-timers seeking full-time jobs involuntarily) are statistically counted as Employed.

3. 邊際與平均的關係 (Marginal vs. Average):

如果新加入群體的失業率低於原有的平均失業率，整體失業率會下降。
If the unemployment rate of the new group is lower than the original average rate, the overall rate will decrease.

答案與詳細分析 Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確選項 Correct Option: A

Correct 正確

(1) 輸入外勞以填補職位空缺 / Importing foreign workers to fill vacancies

分析： 輸入外勞意味著就業人口 \(E\) 增加，因此勞動人口 \(L\) 亦增加（因為 \(L = E + U\)）。由於外勞是填補「空缺」，原本的失業人口 \(U\) 數量不變。
根據公式 \( u = \frac{U}{L} \)，分子 \(U\) 不變，分母 \(L\) 增加，數值必然下降。

Analysis: Importing workers means the Employed population \(E\) increases, so the Labour Force \(L\) also increases (since \(L = E + U\)). Since they fill "vacancies", the number of Unemployed \(U\) remains constant.
According to the formula \( u = \frac{U}{L} \), if the numerator \(U\) is constant and the denominator \(L\) increases, the rate must decrease.

Correct 正確

(2) 應屆畢業生 96% 獲聘用 / 96% of new graduates are employed

分析： 這批新加入勞動市場的人士中，96% 就業，即只有 4% 失業。這批新人的「邊際失業率」是 4%。
由於新人的失業率 (4%) 低於 原本經濟體的平均失業率 (5%)，這會拉低整體的平均數。

Analysis: Among this new group entering the labour market, 96% are employed, meaning only 4% are unemployed. The "marginal unemployment rate" of this group is 4%.
Since the new group's rate (4%) is lower than the economy's original average (5%), it will pull down the overall average.

Incorrect 錯誤

(3) 就業不足工人工時增加 / Underemployed workers work more hours

分析： 就業不足人士本身已經被界定為「就業人口」。當他們增加工時，只是由「就業不足」變為「充分就業」，但他們的身份依然是就業人口 (Employed)。
因此，失業人口 \(U\) 和勞動人口 \(L\) 的總數都沒有改變，失業率維持不變。

Analysis: Underemployed workers are already defined as "Employed". When they work more hours, they shift from "underemployed" to "fully employed", but their status remains Employed.
Therefore, neither the total Unemployed \(U\) nor the Labour Force \(L\) changes. The unemployment rate remains unchanged.

題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

關鍵點 (Pivot Point): 必須區分「人數變動」與「身份變動」。
Pivot Point: Distinguish between "change in numbers" and "change in status".

  • 選項 (1) 是分母增大效應 (Denominator Effect)。
  • 選項 (2) 是平均數拉動效應 (Weighted Average Effect)。關鍵數字是 \(100\% - 96\% = 4\%\)，這個 \(4\%\) 必須與題目開頭的 \(5\%\) 作比較。
  • 選項 (3) 是定義陷阱。就業不足 \(\neq\) 失業。
學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

Q: 為什麼選項 (2) 如果只有 94% 獲聘用，失業率就會上升？

A: 如果只有 94% 獲聘，代表這批新人的失業率是 \(6\%\)。因為 \(6\% > 5\%\)（原有平均），新加入的高失業率群體會把整體平均數拉高。
A: If only 94% were employed, the unemployment rate of this new group would be \(6\%\). Since \(6\% > 5\%\) (original average), adding a group with a higher rate pulls the overall average up.

Q: 職位空缺 (Vacancies) 減少會直接影響失業率嗎？

A: 不會直接影響。失業率只計算「人」(就業或失業)，不計算「職位」。除非空缺被填補（就業增加）或被裁員（失業增加），否則單純空缺數量的變化不進入公式計算。
A: Not directly. The unemployment rate counts "people" (employed or unemployed), not "jobs". Unless vacancies are filled (employment rises) or people are fired (unemployment rises), changes in vacancy numbers alone do not enter the formula.

考試技巧 Exam Tricks

快速排除法 (Elimination Method):

一看到「就業不足 (Underemployed)」，立即聯想它屬於「就業 (Employed)」。
只要題目問的是失業率變化，而情境僅涉及就業不足人士的工時改變，該選項必定錯誤。
排除選項 (3) 後，答案只剩下 A。你甚至不需要詳細分析 (1) 和 (2) 就能得分！

Logic Check: As soon as you see "Underemployed", immediately link it to "Employed". If the scenario only involves changing hours for underemployed workers, it cannot affect the unemployment rate. Eliminate option (3), and the only remaining choice is A. You can solve this without even analyzing (1) and (2)!
