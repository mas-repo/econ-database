HKDSE Economics Question Analysis

Topic: International Trade & Finance (國際貿易與金融) Difficulty: ★★★★☆ Key Concepts: Exchange Rate (匯率), Competitiveness (競爭力), Causality (因果關係)

The Question (題目)

下表顯示港元（HKD）兌人民幣（RMB）的匯率變動。 The following table shows the change in the exchange rate of Hong Kong dollar (HKD) against Renminbi (RMB).

2022年8月11日 (11 August 2022): HKD 1 = RMB 0.86

2023年8月11日 (11 August 2023): HKD 1 = RMB 0.92

上述的匯率變動會導致 ________________。 The above change in the exchange rate would lead to ________________.

  • A. 美元兌人民幣升值
    an appreciation of US dollar against Renminbi
  • B. 以港元計算，香港從內地的進口總值上升
    an increase in the total value of imports from the Mainland to Hong Kong in terms of Hong Kong dollar
  • C. 內地出口往香港的貨物競爭力上升
    an increase in the competitiveness of the Mainland’s goods exported to Hong Kong
  • D. 從內地進口建材的香港建築公司的生產成本上升
    an increase in the production cost of construction companies in Hong Kong which import construction materials from the Mainland
Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 匯率變動 (Exchange Rate Changes): When 1 unit of HKD exchanges for more RMB (0.86 $\to$ 0.92), HKD has appreciated (升值), and RMB has depreciated (貶值) against HKD.

2. 價格競爭力 (Price Competitiveness): Competitiveness often refers to price. If a country's currency depreciates, its goods become cheaper for foreigners to buy.

$$ P_{\text{HKD}} = P_{\text{RMB}} \times \text{Exchange Rate (RMB/HKD)} $$ $$ \text{If RMB depreciates, Mainland goods become cheaper in HKD.} $$

3. 因果關係 (Causality in Linked Exchange Rate): Under the peg, HKD follows USD. A change in HKD/RMB is usually the result of a change in USD/RMB, not the cause.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: C
Option C: 正確。 題目顯示 1 港元能兌換更多人民幣（0.86 $\to$ 0.92），即港元升值，人民幣相對港元貶值。這意味著內地貨品以港元計算的價格會下降。當內地貨品在香港變得更便宜，其價格競爭力便會上升（相對於其他國家的進口貨或香港本地貨）。 Correct. The data shows HKD appreciating against RMB (and RMB depreciating against HKD). This means Mainland goods become cheaper in terms of HKD. When the price of Mainland exports decreases in the Hong Kong market, their price competitiveness increases.
Option A: 錯誤（因果倒置）。 這是最危險的陷阱。雖然在聯繫匯率下，港元升值通常伴隨著美元升值，但題目問的是「導致」(lead to)。港元只是美元的掛鈎貨幣（影子），港元兌人民幣的變動是結果，而非原因。是美元兌人民幣升值，才導致港元兌人民幣升值。港元的變動不會「導致」美元變動。 Incorrect (Reversed Causality). This is a major trap. While HKD and USD move together due to the peg, the question asks what the HKD change "leads to" (consequence). The HKD is a passive follower of the USD. The change in HKD/RMB is the result of USD strength, not the cause. HKD appreciation does not "cause" USD appreciation.
Option B: 錯誤。 港元升值令內地進口貨品價格（以港元計）下降。根據需求定律，進口量（Quantity）會上升。但進口總值（Total Value = Price $\times$ Quantity）的變動取決於需求的價格彈性（Price Elasticity）。題目未提供彈性數據，因此無法確定總值是上升還是下降。 Incorrect. HKD appreciation lowers the price of imports in HKD. Quantity demanded will rise. However, the change in Total Value (Price $\times$ Quantity) depends on Price Elasticity of Demand. Without this data, the outcome is uncertain.
Option D: 錯誤。 港元升值，購買內地建材的港元價格下降。這應該會導致建築公司的生產成本下降，而不是上升。 Incorrect. HKD appreciation means imported materials from the Mainland become cheaper in HKD. This would lead to a decrease in production costs, not an increase.
Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

1. 判斷匯率方向 (Determine Direction):
HKD 1 = RMB 0.86 $\to$ 0.92. HKD buys more RMB.
結論：HKD 升值 (Appreciates) / RMB 貶值 (Depreciates)。

2. 分析貿易影響 (Analyze Trade Impact):
HKD stronger = Imports cheaper.
內地貨品在香港變平 $\to$ 競爭力上升 (Option C)。

3. 排除因果謬誤 (Eliminate Causality Errors):
Does HKD move the USD? No.
港元是被動的，不能選 A。

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)
  • 因果關係混亂 (Causality Confusion): Students often see that HKD and USD move together and select Option A. Remember: In the Linked Exchange Rate System, USD is the driver, HKD is the passenger. The passenger's movement doesn't cause the car to move.
  • 混淆「競爭力」的主語 (Subject of Competitiveness): "Competitiveness increases" usually means the goods become cheaper to buy. Since RMB depreciated against HKD, Mainland goods are cheaper for HK buyers, hence more competitive.
