HKDSE Economics: Market Equilibrium & Derived Demand

Topic: Demand & Supply (需求與供應) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Derived Demand, Factor Market

The Question (題目)

假設一項活動成功吸引很多來自內地城市的機師來香港工作。與此同時，越來越多香港人外遊消遣。下列哪一幅圖最能說明上述事件的影響？
Suppose a campaign successfully attracted many pilots in Mainland cities to work in Hong Kong. Meanwhile, more and more Hong Kong people travel abroad for leisure. Which of the following diagrams best illustrates the effects of the above events?
  • A. Market: Pilots working in Hong Kong (在香港工作的機師) - Supply shifts right \( (S_0 \to S_1) \), Demand shifts right \( (D_0 \to D_1) \).
  • B. Market: Pilots working in Hong Kong (在香港工作的機師) - Supply is constant, Demand shifts right \( (D_0 \to D_1) \).
  • C. Market: Flight Services in Hong Kong (香港的航空服務) - Supply shifts right \( (S_0 \to S_1) \), Demand is constant.
  • D. Market: Flight Services in Hong Kong (香港的航空服務) - Supply shifts left \( (S_1 \leftarrow S_0) \), Demand shifts right \( (D_0 \to D_1) \).

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 生產要素市場 (Factor Market)： 機師是提供航空服務的勞工（生產要素）。當更多機師來港，勞工供應增加。
1. Factor Market: Pilots are labor (factors of production) used to provide flight services. When more pilots come to HK, the labor supply increases.
2. 引申需求 (Derived Demand)： 對生產要素（機師）的需求，是源自對其產品（航空服務）的需求。當航空服務需求上升，對機師的需求亦會隨之上升。
2. Derived Demand: The demand for a factor of production (pilots) is derived from the demand for the good/service it produces (flight services). When demand for flights rises, demand for pilots also rises.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A
事件一： 「吸引很多來自內地城市的機師來香港工作」。這直接增加了香港機師的供應。因此，在「在香港工作的機師」市場中，供應曲線向右移 \( (S_0 \to S_1) \)。
Event 1: "Attracted many pilots... to work in Hong Kong". This directly increases the supply of pilots in HK. Therefore, in the market for "Pilots working in Hong Kong", the supply curve shifts to the right \( (S_0 \to S_1) \).
事件二： 「越來越多香港人外遊消遣」。這增加了對「香港的航空服務」的需求。由於對機師的需求是引申需求，航空服務需求增加會導致航空公司聘請更多機師。因此，在「在香港工作的機師」市場中，需求曲線向右移 \( (D_0 \to D_1) \)。
Event 2: "More and more Hong Kong people travel abroad". This increases the demand for "Flight Services in Hong Kong". Since the demand for pilots is a derived demand, an increase in demand for flights leads airlines to hire more pilots. Thus, the demand curve for pilots shifts to the right \( (D_0 \to D_1) \).
選項分析：
A: 正確顯示了機師市場中供應和需求同時增加。
B: 錯誤，因為忽略了機師供應的增加。
C & D: 錯誤。這兩個圖表顯示的是「航空服務」市場。在航空服務市場中，需求應該增加（向右移），而供應也應該因為機師增加（成本下降/產能增加）而增加（向右移）。圖C和D都沒有正確顯示這兩個變動。
Option Analysis:
A: Correctly shows both Supply and Demand increasing in the Pilots market.
B: Incorrect because it ignores the increase in the supply of pilots.
C & D: Incorrect. These diagrams represent the "Flight Services" market. In this market, Demand should increase (shift right), and Supply should also increase (shift right) due to more pilots (lower costs/higher capacity). Neither C nor D shows both correct shifts.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題關鍵 (Pivot Point)： 認清圖表的「市場主體」(Market Subject)。選項 A 和 B 是關於「機師」(要素市場)，而 C 和 D 是關於「航空服務」(產品市場)。
Pivot Point: Identify the "Market Subject" of the diagrams. Options A and B are about "Pilots" (factor market), while C and D are about "Flight Services" (product market).

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

學生常犯的錯誤是沒有仔細閱讀圖表的 X 軸和標題，將「要素市場」和「產品市場」混淆。此外，容易忘記「引申需求」的概念，只看到市民外遊就以為只影響航班需求，而忽略了對機師需求的連帶影響。
A common mistake is failing to read the axes and titles of the diagrams carefully, confusing the "factor market" with the "product market". Additionally, students often forget the concept of "derived demand", assuming that people traveling only affects the demand for flights, ignoring the knock-on effect on the demand for pilots.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼事件二不會導致機師的供應減少？
A: 市民外遊是消費者的行為，影響的是對航空服務的「需求」。機師的「供應」是由願意且能夠工作的機師數量決定的，與消費者是否去旅行無關。
Q: Why doesn't Event 2 cause a decrease in the supply of pilots?
A: People traveling is consumer behavior, which affects the "demand" for flight services. The "supply" of pilots is determined by the number of pilots willing and able to work, which is independent of whether consumers go on trips.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法： 看到題目有兩個獨立事件，通常意味著需求和供應都會發生移動。事件一明顯增加機師供應 \( (S \uparrow) \)，事件二明顯增加航班需求進而增加機師需求 \( (D \uparrow) \)。直接尋找一個 S 和 D 都向右移的圖表，即選項 A。
Shortcut: Seeing two independent events usually means both Demand and Supply will shift. Event 1 clearly increases pilot supply \( (S \uparrow) \), and Event 2 clearly increases flight demand, hence pilot demand \( (D \uparrow) \). Look directly for a diagram where both S and D shift to the right, which is Option A.
