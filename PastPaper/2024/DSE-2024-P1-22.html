市場干預與外部性 (Market Intervention & Externalities)

負界外效應 (Negative Externality) 社會成本 (Social Cost) 效率損失 (Deadweight Loss) 經濟效率 (Economic Efficiency)

題目 (Question)

近年，越來越多人在公共交通工具上用智能電話大聲地播放影片，對其他乘客造成滋擾。

In recent years, more and more people play videos loudly with their smartphones on public transport, disturbing other passengers.

根據上述資料，下列哪些陳述是正確的？

Based on the above information, which of the following statements are correct?

(1) 假如政府禁止在公共交通工具上用智能電話大聲地播放影片，總社會盈餘可能會下降。

(1) Total social surplus may decrease if playing videos loudly with smartphones on public transport is banned by the government.

(2) 假如公共交通服務的供應者在其交通工具上設立靜音區，不准乘客在靜音區內用智能電話大聲地播放影片，經濟效率可能會有所改善。

(2) Economic efficiency may improve if suppliers of public transportation services designate quiet zones in their vehicles where passengers are not allowed to play videos loudly with their smartphones.

(3) 假如在公共交通工具上用智能電話大聲地播放影片會被罰款，私人成本與社會成本的分歧可能會下降。

(3) The divergence between private and social costs may decrease if people playing videos loudly with their smartphones on public transport are fined.

A. 只有 (1)、(2) (1) and (2) only
B. 只有 (1)、(3) (1) and (3) only
C. 只有 (2)、(3) (2) and (3) only
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3) (1), (2) and (3)

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

1. 負生產/消費界外效應 (Negative Externality)

當個人的行為對第三方造成負面影響，而該個人無需為此賠償時，便會出現負界外效應。在本題中，乘客大聲播放影片對他人造成滋擾，這是一種負消費界外效應 (Negative Consumption Externality)。

A negative externality occurs when an individual's action imposes a cost on a third party without compensation. In this question, passengers playing loud videos disturb others, representing a Negative Consumption Externality.

關鍵公式 (Key Formulas):

$$ MSC = MPC + MEC $$ $$ MSB = MPB + MEB $$
  • \(MSC\): 邊際社會成本 (Marginal Social Cost)
  • \(MPC\): 邊際私人成本 (Marginal Private Cost)
  • \(MEC\): 邊際界外成本 (Marginal External Cost) - 即滋擾 (The disturbance)

2. 市場失效與效率 (Market Failure & Efficiency)

在沒有干預的情況下，個人只考慮 \(MPC\) 而忽略 \(MEC\)，導致過度消費 (\(Q_{actual} > Q_{efficient}\))，產生效率損失 (Deadweight Loss)。

Without intervention, individuals only consider \(MPC\) and ignore \(MEC\), leading to over-consumption (\(Q_{actual} > Q_{efficient}\)) and creating Deadweight Loss.

答案與詳細分析 (Answer & Analysis)

正確答案：選項 D (Correct Option: D)

分析陳述 (1): 正確 (Correct)

「假如政府禁止...總社會盈餘可能會下降。」

雖然大聲播放影片有界外成本，但這並不代表「最優數量 (Optimal Quantity)」一定是零。如果播放者從中獲得的邊際利益極大，大於其造成的滋擾成本，那麼完全禁止（將數量強制降為 0）可能會導致消費不足 (Under-consumption)。這會消除原本存在的潛在社會盈餘，導致總社會盈餘下降。

"Total social surplus may decrease if... banned..."
Although loud videos create external costs, the "Optimal Quantity" is not necessarily zero. If the marginal benefit to the user is extremely high (exceeding the disturbance cost), a total ban (forcing quantity to 0) causes under-consumption. This eliminates potential social surplus, causing total social surplus to decrease.

分析陳述 (2): 正確 (Correct)

「設立靜音區...經濟效率可能會有所改善。」

這是一種界定產權 (Defining Property Rights) 或市場分割的方法。通過設立靜音區，供應商將乘客分流。想安靜的乘客去靜音區，想喧嘩的去非靜音區。這減少了雙方的衝突（減少了界外成本），使得資源配置更符合乘客的偏好，從而提升經濟效率。

"Designate quiet zones... efficiency may improve."
This is a method of defining property rights or market segmentation. By creating quiet zones, suppliers segregate passengers. Those wanting silence go to the quiet zone; others go elsewhere. This reduces conflict (lowers external costs) and allocates resources better according to preferences, improving economic efficiency.

分析陳述 (3): 正確 (Correct)

「罰款...私人成本與社會成本的分歧可能會下降。」

罰款是一種將外部性內部化 (Internalizing Externality) 的手段。原本播放者的私人成本 (\(MPC\)) 很低。罰款令 \(MPC\) 上升（\(MPC_{new} = MPC + \text{Fine}\)）。這使得私人成本更接近社會成本 (\(MSC\))，兩者之間的差距（分歧）因此縮小。

"Fined... divergence between private and social costs may decrease."
Fines are a way to internalize externalities. Originally, the user's \(MPC\) is low. The fine increases the \(MPC\) (\(MPC_{new} = MPC + \text{Fine}\)). This moves the private cost closer to the social cost (\(MSC\)), thereby reducing the divergence (gap) between them.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題關鍵點 (Pivot Point)

  • 關鍵詞「可能 (May)」： 題目中的三個選項都使用了「可能」一詞。在經濟學MC中，這通常意味著只要存在一種合理的情況支持該陳述，該陳述即為正確。
    Keyword "May": All options use "may". In Econ MC, this implies if there is at least one logical scenario supporting the statement, it is correct.
  • 完全禁止 vs 最優數量： 學生必須明白，消除界外效應（零污染/零噪音）並不等於達到經濟效率。效率要求 \(MSB = MSC\)，而不是 \(Q=0\)。
    Total Ban vs. Optimal Quantity: Eliminating externalities (zero pollution/noise) is not the same as efficiency. Efficiency requires \(MSB = MSC\), not \(Q=0\).

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

誤解 1：界外效應必定要完全消除

很多同學認為只要有噪音就是無效率，所以禁止（數量變為0）一定好。這是錯的。如果聽音樂帶來的快樂大於對他人的滋擾，完全禁止反而是無效率。

Misconception 1: Externalities must be eliminated completely. Students often think any noise is inefficient, so a ban is always good. This is wrong. If the joy of music exceeds the disturbance, a ban is inefficient.

誤解 2：混淆「分歧」與「總成本」

選項 (3) 提到「分歧 (Divergence)」下降。分歧是指 \(MSC - MPC\) 的垂直距離。罰款令 \(MPC\) 增加，使其向上移動靠近 \(MSC\)，所以分歧減少。

Misconception 2: Confusing "Divergence" with "Total Cost". Option (3) mentions divergence decreases. Divergence is the vertical gap \(MSC - MPC\). A fine raises \(MPC\) closer to \(MSC\), reducing the gap.

考試技巧 (Exam Tricks)

💡 技巧：注意極端字眼與溫和字眼 (Watch for Extreme vs. Moderate Words)

當題目出現「禁止 (Ban)」這種極端措施時，通常會伴隨效率損失的風險（除非該活動百害而無一利）。而「罰款 (Fine)」或「產權界定 (Quiet Zones)」通常是經濟學推崇的解決方案，往往能改善效率。

When "Ban" appears, it often risks efficiency loss (unless the activity is purely harmful). "Fines" or "Defining Rights (Quiet Zones)" are usually preferred economic solutions that tend to improve efficiency.
