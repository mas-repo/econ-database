2. 題目 / The Question

香港政府推出「高端人才通行證計劃」，以吸引境外人才來港工作。成功申請者會獲發為期兩年的許可證，讓他們在香港探索工作機遇。推出上述計劃的結果是 __________________。
The Hong Kong Government has implemented the ‘Top Talent Pass Scheme’ to attract foreign talents to work in Hong Kong. Successful applicants will be issued a two-year pass for exploring job opportunities in Hong Kong. As a result of the implementation of the above scheme, _______________.
(1) 香港的勞力供應會上升
(2) 境外人才的地域流動性會上升
(3) 在香港的工人的平均勞工生產力會上升
(1) Hong Kong’s labour supply will increase
(2) geographical mobility of foreign talents will increase
(3) average labour productivity of workers in Hong Kong will increase
A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)
A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

3. 概念重溫 / Concept Review

勞力供應 (Labour Supply): 指在特定工資率下，勞工願意並能夠提供的工作時數總和。
Labour Supply: The total amount of working hours that workers are willing and able to offer at a given wage rate.
$$\text{Labour Supply} = \text{Number of Workers} \times \text{Average Working Hours}$$
地域流動性 (Geographical Mobility): 勞工改變工作地點的難易程度。政府政策、簽證要求等制度性因素會直接影響它。
Geographical Mobility: The ease with which workers can change their geographical working locations. Institutional factors like government policies and visa requirements directly affect it.
平均勞工生產力 (Average Labour Productivity): 每名勞工（或每工時）的平均產出量。
Average Labour Productivity: The average output produced per worker (or per man-hour).
$$\text{Average Labour Productivity} = \frac{\text{Total Output (Volume)}}{\text{Total Labour Input (Man-hours or Number of Workers)}}$$

4. 答案與詳細分析 / The Answer & Detailed Analysis

Correct Option (正確答案): Option D

分析 (1) - 正確: 「高端人才通行證」吸引了更多境外人才來港。由於這批人「願意並能夠」在香港工作，香港的潛在勞動人口增加，這直接導致勞力供應（Labour Supply）上升。
Analysis (1) - Correct: The pass scheme attracts more foreign talents to HK. Since these individuals are "willing and able" to work in Hong Kong, the size of the working population increases, which directly leads to an increase in labour supply.
分析 (2) - 正確: 地域流動性受制度性障礙（Institutional barriers）影響。簽證計劃放寬了入境和工作限制，大大降低了境外人才來港工作的法律及制度門檻，因此其地域流動性上升。
Analysis (2) - Correct: Geographical mobility is affected by institutional barriers. The visa scheme relaxes immigration and employment restrictions, significantly lowering the legal and institutional hurdles for foreign talents to work in HK, thus increasing their geographical mobility.
分析 (3) - 正確: 題目明確指出這是一項針對「高端人才（Top Talents）」的計劃。高端人才通常具備較高的人力資本和技術水平，他們的個人生產力高於市場平均值。當高生產力的勞工加入勞動市場時，會從數學上拉高整體的平均勞工生產力。
Analysis (3) - Correct: The question specifically mentions that the scheme targets "Top Talents". Top talents possess higher human capital and skill levels, meaning their individual productivity is higher than the market average. Introducing high-productivity workers into the labour force will mathematically pull up the overall average labour productivity.

5. 題目拆解 / Problem Decomposition

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point): 本題的關鍵在於準確解讀「高端 (Top)」這個字眼，以及理解「簽證 (Pass)」所代表的經濟意義。
Pivot Point: The key to solving this question lies in accurately interpreting the word "Top" and understanding the economic implications of a "Pass / Visa".
  • 簽證/許可證 = 降低制度障礙 $\rightarrow$ 流動性上升。
    Visa/Pass = Lowering institutional barriers $\rightarrow$ Mobility increases.
  • 「高端」= 高於平均的人力資本 $\rightarrow$ 拉高平均生產力。
    "Top" = Above-average human capital $\rightarrow$ Pulls up average productivity.

6. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 / Common Mistakes & Traps

陷阱一：混淆流動性種類
學生經常將「地域流動性 (Geographical Mobility)」與「職業流動性 (Occupational Mobility)」混淆。簽證政策改變的是跨地域工作的難度，與跨行業工作的難度無關。
Trap 1: Confusing Types of Mobility
Students often confuse geographical mobility with occupational mobility. Visa policies change the difficulty of working across borders/regions, which is unrelated to the difficulty of switching between different industries.
陷阱二：忽略「平均」的數學性質
部分學生會錯誤以為增加工人總數會「攤薄」資源，從而降低生產力。但在這裡，新加入的單位（高端人才）其數值高於原本的平均數，因此必定會令新的平均數上升。
Trap 2: Ignoring the Mathematical Property of "Averages"
Some students mistakenly think that adding more workers will "dilute" resources and lower productivity. However, in this case, the newly added units (top talents) have a value higher than the original average, which mathematically guarantees an increase in the new average.

7. 學生常見疑問 / Student Q&A

問：題目說他們只是來香港「探索工作機遇 (exploring job opportunities)」，還沒真正就業，這也算勞力供應增加嗎？

Q: The question says they are just "exploring job opportunities" and might not be employed yet. Does this still count as an increase in labour supply?

答： 算的！在經濟學中，勞力供應包含所有「願意並能夠 (willing and able)」工作的人，這等同於「勞動人口 (Labour Force)」。即使他們暫時處於失業或找工作狀態，只要他們正在積極尋找工作，就已經是勞力供應的一部分。

A: Yes, it does! In economics, labour supply includes everyone who is "willing and able" to work, which is equivalent to the Labour Force. Even if they are temporarily unemployed or job-hunting, as long as they are actively seeking jobs, they are already part of the labour supply.

8. 考試技巧與速解 / Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

極端形容詞法 (Extreme Adjectives Shortcut):
在DSE經濟科中，當考評局特意加上如「高端 (Top)」、「優良 (Excellent)」、「新科技 (New Technology)」等極端或正面的字眼時，通常強烈暗示著勞工生產力 (Labour Productivity) 或 人力資本 (Human Capital) 的提升。看到這些字眼，可以直接判斷 (3) 是正確的，這能幫你快速排除錯誤選項。
Extreme Adjectives Shortcut:
In DSE Economics, when the HKEAA deliberately uses extreme or positive adjectives like "Top", "Excellent", or "New Technology", it strongly implies an improvement in Labour Productivity or Human Capital. Spotting these keywords allows you to immediately deduce that statement (3) is correct, helping you quickly eliminate wrong options.
