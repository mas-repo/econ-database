2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

在香港，貨幣供應（Money Supply）根據流動性（Liquidity）分為三個層次：M1、M2 和 M3。要解答這題，我們必須清楚記住它們的定義，特別是它們與香港三級銀行體系（持牌銀行、有限制牌照銀行、接受存款公司）的對應關係。

In Hong Kong, the money supply is classified into three tiers based on liquidity: M1, M2, and M3. To answer this question, we must clearly remember their definitions, especially their correspondence with Hong Kong's three-tier banking system (Licensed Banks, Restricted Licence Banks, Deposit-taking Companies).

$$M1 = \text{公眾持有的法定貨幣 (Legal tender in public circulation)} + \text{持牌銀行的活期存款 (Demand deposits with licensed banks)}$$
$$M2 = M1 + \text{持牌銀行的儲蓄及定期存款 (Savings & time deposits with licensed banks)} + \text{非銀行公眾持有的持牌銀行發行可轉讓存款證 (NCDs issued by licensed banks)}$$
$$M3 = M2 + \text{有限制牌照銀行及接受存款公司的客戶存款 (Customer deposits with RLBs & DTCs)} + \text{非銀行公眾持有的RLBs及DTCs發行可轉讓存款證 (NCDs issued by RLBs & DTCs)}$$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是：C

Correct Option: C

選項 A (錯誤): 這個選項包含了所有三類接受存款機構的所有存款。這不僅涵蓋了M2的成分，還涵蓋了M1（活期存款）和M3（有限制牌照銀行及接受存款公司的存款）的成分，因此不等於 \(K\)。

Option A (Incorrect): This option includes all types of deposits across all three types of deposit-taking institutions. This covers not just M2 components, but also M1 (demand deposits) and M3 (deposits with RLBs and DTCs) components. Therefore, it does not equal \(K\).

選項 B (錯誤): 這些項目是從 M2 計算 M3 時需要加入的成分（即 \(M3 - M2\)），而不是 \(M2 - M1\)。

Option B (Incorrect): These items are the components added to M2 to calculate M3 (i.e., \(M3 - M2\)), not \(M2 - M1\).

選項 C (正確): 根據定義，\(M2 = M1 + K\)，所以 \(K = M2 - M1\)。這正是持牌銀行的儲蓄存款、定期存款，以及由持牌銀行發行並由非銀行公眾持有的可轉讓存款證。這完全符合選項 C 的描述。

Option C (Correct): By definition, \(M2 = M1 + K\), so \(K = M2 - M1\). This is exactly the savings deposits and time deposits with licensed banks, plus negotiable certificates of deposit issued by licensed banks and held by the non-bank public. This perfectly matches the description in Option C.

選項 D (錯誤): 銀行儲備金（Reserves）絕對不計算在任何貨幣供應（M1/M2/M3）之內。貨幣供應只計算「公眾」持有的貨幣，而儲備金是由銀行持有的。

Option D (Incorrect): Bank reserves are NEVER included in any definition of money supply (M1/M2/M3). Money supply only measures money held by the "public", whereas reserves are held by banks.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)： 題目給出 \(M2 = M1 + K\)，這是一個簡單的代數關係，意味著 \(K\) 就是 M1 和 M2 之間的「差額」。你只需要在腦海中提取 M2 的公式，然後減去 M1 的部分。

Pivot Point: The question provides \(M2 = M1 + K\), a simple algebraic relationship meaning \(K\) is the "difference" between M1 and M2. You just need to recall the formula for M2 and subtract the M1 portion.

關鍵詞配對：
1. 看到 M1 / M2 \(\rightarrow\) 只能聯想到「持牌銀行 (Licensed Banks)」。
2. 看到 M3 \(\rightarrow\) 才會聯想到「有限制牌照銀行 (RLBs)」和「接受存款公司 (DTCs)」。

Keyword Matching:
1. Seeing M1 / M2 \(\rightarrow\) Only associate with "Licensed Banks".
2. Seeing M3 \(\rightarrow\) Then associate with "Restricted Licence Banks (RLBs)" and "Deposit-taking Companies (DTCs)".

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱一：混淆銀行體系層次
很多學生會忘記 M2 只涉及「持牌銀行」。選項 B 故意放入「有限制牌照銀行」來迷惑你，那是屬於 M3 的範疇。

Trap 1: Confusing Banking Tiers
Many students forget that M2 ONLY involves "Licensed Banks". Option B deliberately includes "Restricted Licence Banks" to confuse you; that belongs to the M3 category.

陷阱二：將儲備金計入貨幣供應
選項 D 是一個經典陷阱。記住：貨幣供應 = 公眾持有的購買力。存在銀行金庫或金管局的儲備金，並不流通於市面，因此絕對不計入貨幣供應中。

Trap 2: Including Reserves in Money Supply
Option D is a classic trap. Remember: Money Supply = Purchasing power held by the public. Reserves kept in bank vaults or at the HKMA are not circulating in the market, so they are strictly excluded from the money supply.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼選項 C 要強調「非銀行公眾持有 (held by non-bank public)」？

Q: Why does Option C emphasize "held by non-bank public"?

A: 因為如果可轉讓存款證（NCDs）是由另一家銀行持有，那就屬於銀行同業之間的資產（Inter-bank transactions），並不代表一般市民或企業的購買力。為了避免重複計算（Double counting），我們只計算由「非銀行公眾」持有的部分。

A: Because if Negotiable Certificates of Deposit (NCDs) are held by another bank, they are considered inter-bank assets and do not represent the purchasing power of the general public or firms. To avoid double counting, we only include those held by the "non-bank public".
