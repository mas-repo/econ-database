📝 HKDSE Economics Topic Analysis

Topic (課題): 市場非均衡：短缺與交易量 (Market Disequilibrium: Shortage & Quantity Transacted)
Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)
Key Concepts (關鍵概念): 短缺 (Shortage), 需求量 (Quantity Demanded), 供應量 (Quantity Supplied), 交易量 (Quantity Transacted)

1. 題目 (The Question)

作為「開心香港」活動的一部分，「全港戲院日」在2023年4月舉行，參與活動的戲院的電影票價一律為$30。有報道指，很多人即使排隊輪候數小時，仍未能購得優惠戲票。

從以上資料可以推斷，該些優惠戲票的交易量 __________。

A. 等於其供應量而小於其需求量
B. 等於其供應量而大於其需求量
C. 等於其需求量而小於其供應量
D. 等於其需求量而大於其供應量

As part of the ‘Happy Hong Kong’ campaign, the ‘Hong Kong Cinema Day’ was held in April 2023, with tickets priced at HK$30 across all participating cinemas. It was reported that many people failed to buy any discounted film ticket even after queuing up for hours.

From the information above, we can infer that the quantity transacted of the discounted film tickets is __________.

A. equal to the quantity supplied and smaller than the quantity demanded
B. equal to the quantity supplied and larger than the quantity demanded
C. equal to the quantity demanded and smaller than the quantity supplied
D. equal to the quantity demanded and larger than the quantity supplied

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

短缺 (Shortage / Excess Demand):
當市場價格低於均衡價格時，消費者願意購買的數量（需求量）超過了生產者願意提供的數量（供應量）。

When the market price is below the equilibrium price, the quantity that consumers are willing and able to buy (quantity demanded) exceeds the quantity that producers are willing to provide (quantity supplied).

$$\text{Shortage} \implies Q_d > Q_s$$

交易量 (Quantity Transacted, \(Q_t\)):
在任何市場情況下，實際成交的數量取決於需求量 \(Q_d\) 和供應量 \(Q_s\) 之間較小的一個。這被稱為「短邊原則」(Short-side Rule)。在短缺的情況下，因為供應有限，所以交易量會受制於供應量。

In any market situation, the actual quantity transacted is determined by the smaller of quantity demanded \(Q_d\) and quantity supplied \(Q_s\). This is known as the Short-side Rule. In a shortage, because supply is limited, the quantity transacted is constrained by the quantity supplied.

$$Q_t = \min(Q_d, Q_s)$$

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): A

選項 A (Option A):
題目提到「很多人排隊數小時仍未能購得戲票」，這明確指出市場出現了短缺 (Shortage)。在短缺的情況下，需求量大於供應量，即 \(Q_d > Q_s\)。由於戲院只提供了 \(Q_s\) 數量的戲票，即使有更多的需求，實際成交的數量也只能等於戲院提供的數量。因此，交易量等於供應量，並且小於需求量 (\(Q_t = Q_s < Q_d\))。這是正確的。
The question states that "many people failed to buy... even after queuing," which clearly indicates a Shortage. In a shortage, quantity demanded is greater than quantity supplied (\(Q_d > Q_s\)). Since cinemas only provide \(Q_s\) tickets, the actual transacted quantity is limited by what is supplied, regardless of the higher demand. Therefore, quantity transacted equals quantity supplied, and is smaller than quantity demanded (\(Q_t = Q_s < Q_d\)). This is correct.
選項 B (Option B):
雖然交易量等於供應量 (\(Q_t = Q_s\)) 是對的，但這個選項說供應量大於需求量 (\(Q_s > Q_d\))，這描述的是「過剩 (Surplus)」，與題目中排隊買不到票的「短缺」情況矛盾。
While stating that quantity transacted equals quantity supplied (\(Q_t = Q_s\)) is correct, this option claims that quantity supplied is larger than quantity demanded (\(Q_s > Q_d\)). This describes a "Surplus", which contradicts the "Shortage" situation of people queuing and failing to buy tickets.
選項 C (Option C):
如果交易量等於需求量 (\(Q_t = Q_d\))，且需求量小於供應量 (\(Q_d < Q_s\))，這同樣是在描述「過剩 (Surplus)」，即戲院有剩餘的票賣不出。這不符合題目情境。
If the quantity transacted equals quantity demanded (\(Q_t = Q_d\)) and is smaller than quantity supplied (\(Q_d < Q_s\)), this also describes a "Surplus" where cinemas have unsold tickets left. This does not fit the scenario.
選項 D (Option D):
這個選項在邏輯上是不可能的。如果需求量大於供應量 (\(Q_d > Q_s\))，交易量不可能等於較大的需求量，因為市場上根本沒有足夠的貨品（戲票）來滿足所有的需求。我們不能賣出不存在的東西！
This option is logically impossible. If quantity demanded is larger than quantity supplied (\(Q_d > Q_s\)), the quantity transacted cannot equal the larger quantity demanded because there simply aren't enough goods (tickets) in the market to satisfy all the demand. You cannot sell what doesn't exist!

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

1. 尋找「破題點」(Identify the Pivot Point):
題目的核心眼袋在於這句話：「很多人即使排隊輪候數小時，仍未能購得優惠戲票」。這是一個標準的經濟學情境描述，暗示市場處於短缺 (Shortage / Excess Demand) 狀態。這意味著在 $30 這個價格下，\( Q_d > Q_s \)。

The core clue is the phrase: "many people failed to buy... even after queuing up for hours". This is a standard economic description implying the market is experiencing a Shortage (Excess Demand). It means at the price of $30, \( Q_d > Q_s \).

2. 應用規則 (Apply the Rule):
一旦確認了 \( Q_d > Q_s \)，立刻應用「短邊原則 (Short-side Rule)」：實際交易量 \( Q_t \) 永遠受限於較小的那一方。既然 \( Q_s \) 較小，所以 \( Q_t = Q_s \)。

Once you establish \( Q_d > Q_s \), immediately apply the Short-side Rule: the actual quantity transacted \( Q_t \) is always limited by the smaller side. Since \( Q_s \) is smaller, \( Q_t = Q_s \).

3. 干擾資訊 (Distractors):
「$30」和「2023年4月」只是提供背景，對於推導這條題目的邏輯而言，具體數字並不重要，重要的是它作為一個低於均衡點的「有效價格上限 (Effective Price Ceiling)」的結果。

"$30" and "April 2023" are just background context. The specific numbers are not required for the logical deduction; what matters is the result of it acting as a price below equilibrium (like an effective price ceiling).

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

⚠️ 陷阱一：混淆「需求量」與「交易量」
學生常常以為「很多人想買」就代表「交易量很高」。在經濟學中，「需求量 (Quantity Demanded)」是你想買且有能力買的數量，而「交易量 (Quantity Transacted)」是實際成功買賣的數量。有需求不代表有交易！

⚠️ Trap 1: Confusing "Quantity Demanded" with "Quantity Transacted"
Students often think "many people want to buy" means "high quantity transacted". In Economics, Quantity Demanded is what you are willing and able to buy, while Quantity Transacted is what is actually bought and sold. Demand does not guarantee a transaction!

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

學生問 (Q): 既然這是「全港戲院日」，戲院應該準備了很多戲票啊，為甚麼交易量不直接等於需求量呢？
Since it's "Cinema Day", shouldn't cinemas have prepared a lot of tickets? Why doesn't the quantity transacted just equal the quantity demanded?

導師答 (A): 戲院的座位數量是固定的（短期內供應缺乏彈性）。當票價大幅下降至 $30，想看電影的人數（需求量）會急劇增加。即使戲院「全院滿座」（達到了最大的供應量），也無法滿足所有想買特價票的人。因此，實際能賣出的票數（交易量）就被戲院的座位總數（供應量）限制住了。

Tutor's Answer (A): The number of cinema seats is fixed (supply is highly inelastic in the short run). When the price drops drastically to $30, the number of people wanting to watch a movie (quantity demanded) skyrockets. Even if cinemas operate at "full house" (reaching maximum quantity supplied), they cannot satisfy everyone who wants a discounted ticket. Therefore, the actual tickets sold (quantity transacted) is constrained by the total number of seats (quantity supplied).

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

💡 秒殺口訣：
看到「排隊 (Queueing) / 搶購 / 買不到」 👉 立刻寫下：短缺，\(Q_d > Q_s\)。
然後找選項裡包含「\(Q_t = Q_s\)」和「\(Q_s < Q_d\)」的答案。直接選 A，不用猶豫！

💡 Speed-solving Trick:
When you see keywords like "Queueing / Rushing to buy / Failed to buy" 👉 immediately write down: Shortage, \(Q_d > Q_s\).
Then look for the option that says "\(Q_t = Q_s\)" and "\(Q_s < Q_d\)". Pick A directly without hesitation!
