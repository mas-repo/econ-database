HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: International Trade Difficulty: ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts: Opportunity Cost (機會成本), Terms of Trade (貿易比率), Gains from Trade (貿易得益)

The Question (題目)

下表顯示Y國的書本及智能電話的產出。

The following table shows the outputs of books and smartphones of Country Y.
書本 (單位)
Books (units)		 智能電話 (單位)
Smartphones (units)
專門化和貿易前的產出
Outputs before specialisation and trade		 60 60
專門化後的產出
Outputs after specialisation		 0 180

假設Y國從每單位智能電話的交易中的得益是0.2單位書本。貿易比率是1單位智能電話可換 __________ 單位書本。

Suppose for each unit of smartphones traded, the gain to Country Y is 0.2 units of books. The terms of trade for 1 unit of smartphones is ______ units of books.
A. 0.3
B. 0.7
C. 1.2
D. 2.2

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. Opportunity Cost (機會成本)

在生產可能性邊界（PPF）上，生產多一單位某物品所放棄的另一種物品的數量。
The quantity of another good given up to produce one more unit of a specific good along the Production Possibility Frontier (PPF).

$$ \text{OC of Good X} = \frac{\text{Decrease in Good Y}}{\text{Increase in Good X}} $$

2. Per Unit Gain from Trade (每單位貿易得益)

對於出口國而言，每單位的得益等於「出售價格」（貿易比率）減去「生產成本」（機會成本）。
For an exporting country, the gain per unit is equal to the "Selling Price" (Terms of Trade) minus the "Production Cost" (Opportunity Cost).

$$ \text{Gain per unit} = \text{Terms of Trade (TOT)} - \text{Opportunity Cost (OC)} $$

*注意：這公式適用於出口國。如果是進口國，公式則是 \( OC - TOT \)。
*Note: This formula applies to the exporting country. If importing, the formula is \( OC - TOT \).

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: B

Step-by-Step Solution (逐步題解):

Step 1: Determine the Opportunity Cost (計算機會成本)

Y國由「專門化前」轉變為「專門化後」：
書本 (Books): \( 60 \to 0 \) (減少 60)
智能電話 (Smartphones): \( 60 \to 180 \) (增加 120)

Y國為了生產額外 120 單位智能電話，放棄了 60 單位書本。
Country Y gave up 60 units of books to produce an additional 120 units of smartphones.

$$ \text{OC of 1 Smartphone} = \frac{60 \text{ Books}}{120 \text{ Smartphones}} = 0.5 \text{ Books} $$

Step 2: Identify Export vs. Import (確認出口與進口)

由於Y國專門生產智能電話（產量由60增至180），Y國會出口智能電話。
Since Country Y specializes in producing smartphones (output increased from 60 to 180), Country Y will export smartphones.

Step 3: Calculate Terms of Trade (計算貿易比率)

題目給定：每單位智能電話的得益 = 0.2 書本。
設貿易比率 (TOT) 為 \( 1 \text{ Smartphone} = T \text{ Books} \)。
Given: Gain per unit of smartphone = 0.2 Books. Let Terms of Trade (TOT) be \( 1 \text{ Smartphone} = T \text{ Books} \).

$$ \text{Gain} = \text{TOT} - \text{OC} $$ $$ 0.2 = T - 0.5 $$ $$ T = 0.2 + 0.5 $$ $$ T = 0.7 $$

所以，貿易比率是 1 單位智能電話可換 0.7 單位書本。
Therefore, the terms of trade is 1 unit of smartphone for 0.7 units of books.

Why other options are wrong (為何其他選項錯誤):

  • A (0.3): 這是誤將得益從成本中扣除 (\(0.5 - 0.2\))，這只有在Y國是智能電話的進口國時才合理，但Y國是出口國。
    This implies subtracting gain from cost (\(0.5 - 0.2\)), which would only be valid if Y were an importer of smartphones.
  • C (1.2): 計算錯誤，可能是誤用了書本的機會成本 (\(2.0\)) 進行計算。
    Calculation error, possibly confusing the OC of books (\(2.0\)) in the calculation.
  • D (2.2): 這是誤將書本的機會成本 (\(2.0\)) 加上得益 (\(0.2\))。
    This implies adding the gain (\(0.2\)) to the OC of books (\(2.0\)).

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

The Pivot Point (關鍵點)

這題的關鍵在於理解「每單位得益」(Per Unit Gain) 的定義。
把它想像成做生意：
利潤 (Gain) = 售價 (TOT) - 成本 (OC)

1. 數據篩選 (Data Filtering):

題目中的「60, 60」和「0, 180」這兩組數字，唯一的用途是用來計算機會成本 (OC)。一旦算出 OC = 0.5，這些數字就不再需要了。
The numbers "60, 60" and "0, 180" are solely for calculating the Opportunity Cost (OC). Once OC = 0.5 is found, these numbers are no longer needed.

2. 單位確認 (Unit Check):

題目問的是「1單位智能電話」，所以所有計算都要以「每單位智能電話」為基準。
The question asks about "1 unit of smartphone", so all calculations must be based on "per unit of smartphone".

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

商業思維法 (Business Logic Method):

不要死記公式，用常識思考：
1. Y國自己生產一部電話要放棄 0.5 本書 (這是成本 Cost)。
2. Y國透過貿易賺了 0.2 本書 (這是利潤 Profit)。
3. 那麼，Y國賣出電話的價格 (Price) 必須是多少？

$$ \text{Price} = \text{Cost} + \text{Profit} $$ $$ \text{TOT} = 0.5 + 0.2 = 0.7 $$

這樣想就不會搞錯是用加法還是減法了！
Thinking this way prevents you from confusing whether to add or subtract!
