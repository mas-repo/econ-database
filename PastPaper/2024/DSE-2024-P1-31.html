HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic Money & Banking (貨幣與銀行)
Difficulty ★★★☆☆ (3/5)
Concepts Properties of Money (貨幣特質), Functions of Money (貨幣功能)

The Question (題目)

9999金比鑽石更能發揮貨幣的 __________ 功能，因為前者的 __________ 較高。

9999 gold is better than diamond in performing the __________ function of money because the former has a higher __________.

(1) 交易媒介 …… 可分割性
(1) medium of exchange …… divisibility

(2) 記帳單位 …… 同質性
(2) unit of account …… homogeneity

(3) 價值儲藏 …… 可攜性
(3) store of value …… portability

  • A. 只有 (1) (1) only
  • B. 只有 (3) (3) only
  • C. 只有 (1)、(2) (1) and (2) only
  • D. 只有 (2)、(3) (2) and (3) only

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答此題，我們必須比較黃金 (Gold) 與 鑽石 (Diamond) 在物理特質上的差異，以及這些特質如何影響貨幣功能。

To solve this, we must compare the physical properties of Gold and Diamond and how these properties affect the functions of money.

1. 可分割性 (Divisibility)

貨幣應該容易被分割成較小的單位，且分割後總價值不變。

Money should be easily divided into smaller units without losing its total value.

數學表達：\( V_{total} = \sum V_{parts} \)

2. 同質性 (Homogeneity)

每一單位的貨幣品質必須相同，這樣大眾才能輕易判斷其價值，無須每次交易都進行檢驗。

Every unit of money must be of the same quality so that people can easily judge its value without inspecting it every time.

3. 可攜性 (Portability)

貨幣應該輕便，容易攜帶。即「價值/重量」比率 (Value-to-weight ratio) 要高。

Money should be light and easy to carry. The "value-to-weight ratio" should be high.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確選項): C

(1) 交易媒介 …… 可分割性 (Medium of exchange ... divisibility) ✔ CORRECT

分析：黃金具有極高的可分割性。如果你將一塊 10 安士的金條切成 10 塊 1 安士的金塊，其總價值不變。這使得黃金適合作為交易媒介，因為它可以找續 (make change)。

Analysis: Gold has high divisibility. If you cut a 10oz gold bar into ten 1oz pieces, the total value remains unchanged. This makes gold suitable as a medium of exchange because it allows for making change.

對比鑽石：鑽石若被分割，價值會大幅下跌（一顆 5 卡的鑽石價值遠高於 5 顆 1 卡的鑽石總和）。因此，黃金在這方面確實優勝。

Vs Diamond: If a diamond is divided, its value drops significantly (one 5-carat diamond is worth much more than five 1-carat diamonds combined). Thus, gold is indeed superior here.

(2) 記帳單位 …… 同質性 (Unit of account ... homogeneity) ✔ CORRECT

分析：記帳單位要求貨幣必須標準化。9999 黃金是標準的，品質均一 (Homogeneous)。我們可以輕易說某物品價值「5 安士黃金」。

Analysis: A unit of account requires money to be standardized. 9999 Gold is standard and uniform (Homogeneous). We can easily say an item is worth "5 oz of gold".

對比鑽石：鑽石具有異質性 (Heterogeneous)。每一顆鑽石的顏色、淨度、切工都不同。如果標價寫「1 顆鑽石」，沒有人知道具體價值是多少。因此，黃金因同質性較高，更適合作為記帳單位。

Vs Diamond: Diamonds are heterogeneous. Every diamond differs in color, clarity, and cut. If a price tag says "1 diamond", no one knows the specific value. Therefore, gold is better as a unit of account due to higher homogeneity.

(3) 價值儲藏 …… 可攜性 (Store of value ... portability) ✘ INCORRECT

分析：雖然黃金可以儲藏價值，但題目聲稱黃金優勝是因為「可攜性較高」，這是錯誤的。

Analysis: While gold is a store of value, the statement claims gold is better because it has "higher portability", which is FALSE.

事實：鑽石的「價值/重量」比率極高。一小袋鑽石可能價值數千萬，可以輕鬆放入口袋帶走；同樣價值的黃金則非常沉重，難以攜帶。因此，鑽石的可攜性其實比黃金高。

Fact: Diamonds have an extremely high "value-to-weight" ratio. A small bag of diamonds can be worth millions and fits in a pocket; the same value in gold would be very heavy and hard to carry. Therefore, diamonds actually have higher portability than gold.

🚀 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

關鍵點 (Pivot Point)： 必須同時驗證「功能 (Function)」與「特質 (Property)」之間的因果關係。

Pivot Point: You must verify the causal relationship between the "Function" and the "Property".
  • 第一步：比較特質。黃金比鑽石更可分割嗎？(是)。黃金比鑽石更同質嗎？(是)。黃金比鑽石更易攜帶嗎？(否)。
  • 第二步：如果特質比較正確，再看該特質是否支持該功能。

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 誤解「可攜性」(Misunderstanding Portability):

    學生常以為黃金做成金幣所以很易攜帶。但在經濟學比較中，我們看的是同等價值下的重量。鑽石濃縮了極高價值，所以可攜性勝過黃金。

    Students often think gold is portable because of gold coins. But in economics, we compare weight for equal value. Diamonds concentrate immense value, so they beat gold in portability.
  • 混淆「耐用性」與「同質性」 (Confusing Durability with Homogeneity):

    兩者都很耐用 (Durable)，這不是區分它們的關鍵。關鍵在於每一塊黃金都是一樣的 (同質)，但每一顆鑽石都是獨一無二的 (異質)。

    Both are durable. The key distinction is that every piece of gold is the same (homogeneous), while every diamond is unique (heterogeneous).

🙋 Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼鑽石不能分割？切開不就變小鑽石了嗎？ (Why can't diamonds be divided?)

A: 經濟學上的「可分割性」是指分割後總價值不變。一顆 10 卡的鑽石價值連城，如果切成 10 顆 1 卡的鑽石，總價值會暴跌 (Loss of value)。相反，10 安士金條切開後，價值剛好等於原本的總和。

A: In economics, "divisibility" means the total value remains unchanged after division. A 10-carat diamond is worth a fortune; if cut into ten 1-carat stones, the total value plummets. In contrast, cut gold retains its proportional value.
Q: 為什麼選項 (3) 是錯的？黃金不是很好的價值儲藏嗎？ (Why is option 3 wrong? Isn't gold a good store of value?)

A: 這是邏輯陷阱。黃金是好的價值儲藏，但題目說它是因為「可攜性較高」。這個理由 (Reason) 是錯的。因為鑽石比黃金更易攜帶。如果題目改為「因為黃金的流動性 (Liquidity) 較高」，那可能就成立了。

A: This is a logic trap. Gold is a good store of value, but the question claims it is because of "higher portability". This reason is false. Diamonds are more portable. If the question said "because of higher liquidity", it might have been correct.

⚡ Exam Tricks (考試速解)

"The Cutting Test" (切割測試):

遇到 Commodity Money (商品貨幣) 的題目，想像你手上有把刀：

  • 切下去會心痛 (價值大跌) \(\rightarrow\) 缺乏可分割性 (如：鑽石、牛皮)。
  • 切下去沒感覺 (價值按比例) \(\rightarrow\) 具備可分割性 (如：黃金、白銀)。
Imagine you have a knife. If cutting it makes you cry (value loss) \(\rightarrow\) lacks divisibility. If cutting it feels fine (proportional value) \(\rightarrow\) has divisibility.
