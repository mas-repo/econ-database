HKDSE Economics Tutor 經濟學導師解析

Topic (課題): Demand and Supply - Changes in Equilibrium (需求與供應 - 均衡點的改變)

Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆ (2/5)

Key Concepts (關鍵概念): Substitutes (替代品) Shift in Demand (需求移動) Movement along Supply Curve (沿供應曲線移動)

1. The Question (題目)

[Diagram shows an upward-sloping supply curve \(S\). Point X is higher up on the curve, and Point Y is lower down on the same curve. A movement from X to Y indicates a decrease in both Price and Quantity.]

下圖顯示的士服務的供應曲線。下列哪項是均衡點由X點改變至Y點的最佳解釋。

The following diagram shows the supply curve of taxi services. Which of the following best explains the change in the equilibrium position from point X to point Y?

  1. 政府降低的士收費。
    The government lowers the taxi fares.
  2. 政府補貼市民使用鐵路服務。
    The government provides subsidies for citizens to use railway services.
  3. 政府減少的士牌照的配額。
    The government reduces the quota on taxi licences.
  4. 政府修訂法例，懲罰乘車共享服務的提供者，例如優步(Uber)司機。
    The government amends the law to punish providers of ride-sharing services such as Uber drivers.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這類題目，我們必須清楚區分「沿曲線移動」(Movement along a curve) 和 「曲線的移動」(Shift of a curve)。

To answer this type of question, we must clearly distinguish between a "Movement along a curve" and a "Shift of a curve".

  • 沿供應曲線移動 (Movement along the supply curve): 當只有該物品的價格 (Price) 改變時發生。在圖中，X 點和 Y 點都在同一條供應曲線 \(S\) 上，這意味著供應 (Supply) 本身沒有改變，而是需求 (Demand) 發生了改變，帶動了價格和交易量的變化。
    Occurs when only the Price of the good changes. In the diagram, both points X and Y are on the same supply curve \(S\). This means Supply itself has not changed; instead, Demand has changed, driving the change in price and quantity.
  • 替代品 (Substitutes): 兩種可以互相替代滿足同一需求的物品（例如的士和鐵路）。當物品 A 的價格下降時，消費者會多買 A 而少買 B，導致物品 B 的需求下降。
    Two goods that can replace each other to satisfy the same want (e.g., taxis and railways). When the price of Good A decreases, consumers buy more A and less B, leading to a decrease in demand for Good B.
Equilibrium Condition (均衡條件): $$ Q_d = Q_s $$

If Demand decreases (\(D \downarrow\)), then Equilibrium Price (\(P_e \downarrow\)) and Equilibrium Quantity (\(Q_e \downarrow\)).

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option B

A. 政府降低的士收費 (The government lowers the taxi fares.)

這屬於價格管制（價格上限）。雖然價格下降會導致供應量減少（沿供應曲線向下移動），但這會造成供不應求（短缺），而不是形成一個新的「均衡點」。題目明確指出 Y 點是一個新的均衡點。此外，政府直接定價並非由市場供求力量決定的均衡改變。

This is a price control (price ceiling). Although a lower price leads to a decrease in quantity supplied (movement down the supply curve), it creates a shortage, not a new "equilibrium position". The question specifically states Y is a new equilibrium. Furthermore, direct government pricing is not an equilibrium change determined by market forces.

B. 政府補貼市民使用鐵路服務 (The government provides subsidies for citizens to use railway services.)

鐵路是的士的替代品 (Substitute)。政府補貼鐵路會令市民乘搭鐵路的成本下降。市民會轉而多乘搭鐵路，少搭的士。這會導致的士的需求下降 (Demand decreases)，需求曲線向左移。在同一條供應曲線上，這會令均衡價格 \(P\) 下降，均衡數量 \(Q\) 減少，完全符合圖中由 X 點移至 Y 點的情況。

Railways are a Substitute for taxis. Government subsidies lower the cost of using railways for citizens. Citizens will switch to taking more railways and fewer taxis. This leads to a decrease in Demand for taxis, shifting the demand curve to the left. Along the same supply curve, this causes the equilibrium price \(P\) and quantity \(Q\) to fall, perfectly matching the movement from point X to point Y in the diagram.

C. 政府減少的士牌照的配額 (The government reduces the quota on taxi licences.)

減少牌照配額會直接減少的士服務的供應 (Supply)。這會導致整條供應曲線向左移動，結果是均衡價格上升，均衡數量下降。這與圖中供應曲線維持不變的情況不符。

Reducing the quota on licences directly reduces the Supply of taxi services. This would shift the entire supply curve to the left, resulting in a higher equilibrium price and lower equilibrium quantity. This contradicts the diagram where the supply curve remains unchanged.

D. 政府修訂法例，懲罰乘車共享服務的提供者 (The government amends the law to punish providers of ride-sharing services...)

優步(Uber)是的士的替代品。懲罰 Uber 司機會減少 Uber 的供應，令其價格上升或更難預約。市民會轉而多搭的士，導致的士的需求上升 (Demand increases)。這會令的士的均衡價格和數量同時上升（沿供應曲線向上移動），與圖中價格和數量下降的情況相反。

Uber is a substitute for taxis. Punishing Uber drivers reduces the supply of Uber, making it more expensive or harder to book. Citizens will switch to taxis, leading to an increase in Demand for taxis. This would cause both equilibrium price and quantity of taxis to rise (movement up along the supply curve), which is the opposite of the decrease shown in the diagram.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (破題關鍵): 觀察圖表，X 和 Y 都在同一條供應曲線 (Same Supply Curve) 上！

Pivot Point: Observe the diagram carefully. Both X and Y are on the Same Supply Curve!

  1. Step 1: 圖中顯示的是沿著供應曲線向下移動 (Movement down along the supply curve)。這代表供應 (Supply) 沒有改變。
    The diagram shows a movement down along the supply curve. This means Supply has not changed.
  2. Step 2: 比較 X 和 Y，價格 \(P\) 下降了，數量 \(Q\) 也下降了。
    Comparing X and Y, the price \(P\) has decreased, and the quantity \(Q\) has also decreased.
  3. Step 3: 甚麼因素會導致在固定供應下，\(P\) 和 \(Q\) 同時下降？答案是：需求下降 (Decrease in Demand)。
    What factor causes both \(P\) and \(Q\) to fall given a fixed supply? The answer is: a Decrease in Demand.
  4. Step 4: 在四個選項中，尋找哪一個會導致的士「需求下降」。只有選項 B 符合。
    Among the four options, look for the one that causes a "decrease in demand" for taxis. Only Option B fits.

5. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼不能選 A？圖中的價格明明下降了，選項 A 說政府降低收費，不是剛好吻合嗎？

Q: Why can't I choose A? The price in the diagram clearly dropped, and Option A says the government lowers fares. Doesn't that match perfectly?

A: 這是一個常見的陷阱！選項 A 描述的是政府實施「價格管制」(Price Control)。當政府強行把價格定在均衡點以下，市場會出現短缺 (Shortage, \(Q_d > Q_s\))，這時候的交易量受制於供應量，但這不是一個新的均衡點 (Not a new equilibrium)。題目明確指出 Y 點是新的「均衡點」(Equilibrium position)，這意味著必須是市場供求力量自然達到的新平衡（即需求曲線左移與原供應曲線的相交點）。

A: This is a common trap! Option A describes a government "Price Control". When the government forces the price below the equilibrium, a shortage occurs (\(Q_d > Q_s\)). The transacted quantity is restricted by the quantity supplied, but this is not a new equilibrium. The question specifically states Y is a new "equilibrium position", which means it must be a new balance naturally reached by market forces (i.e., the intersection of a leftward-shifted demand curve and the original supply curve).

6. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

「同一曲線法則」(The Same Curve Rule):

在多項選擇題中，如果你看到點的移動是發生在同一條曲線上（例如本題的供應曲線），你可以立刻刪除所有會導致該曲線移動的選項！

In MCQs, if you see the movement of points occurs on the same curve (like the supply curve in this question), you can immediately eliminate all options that would cause that specific curve to shift!

  • 本題供應曲線沒變，所以任何改變的士「供應」的因素都是錯的。
    The supply curve didn't change here, so any factor changing the "supply" of taxis is wrong.
  • 選項 C (牌照配額) 直接改變供應，秒殺刪除！
    Option C (licence quota) directly changes supply. Eliminate it instantly!
