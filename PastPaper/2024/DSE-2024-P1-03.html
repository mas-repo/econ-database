The Question (題目)

下列哪項關於海水的陳述是正確的？

Which of the following statements about seawater is correct?

A. 當漁民用海水保持漁獲鮮活時，海水是資本物品。
Seawater is a capital good when fishermen use it to keep their catches alive.
B. 因為政府提供海水予公眾作沖廁之用，所以海水是共用品。
Seawater is a public good as the government provides it to the public for flushing the toilets.
C. 因為人們可以免費獲得海水，所以海水是免費物品。
Seawater is a free good as people can obtain seawater free of charge.
D. 因為沒有人飲用海水，所以海水是劣等物品。
Seawater is an inferior good as no one drinks seawater.

Concept Review (概念重溫)

Free Good vs. Economic Good (免費物品 vs 經濟物品)

  • Free Good (免費物品): Quantity available is greater than quantity desired at zero price. No scarcity, no opportunity cost.
    Formula \( Q_s > Q_d \text{ at } P=0 \)
  • Economic Good (經濟物品): Scarce relative to wants. Has an opportunity cost.
  • Trap: "Free of charge" (Price = 0) does not mean "Free Good".

Public Good (共用品)

  • Non-rival (非競爭性): One person's consumption does not reduce the amount available for others.
  • Non-excludable (非排他性): Impossible or too costly to prevent non-payers from consuming it.
  • Trap: "Provided by the public (government)" does not mean "Public Good".

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A
Option A (Correct) Analysis

當漁民用海水保持漁獲鮮活時，海水是資本物品。

Chinese: 雖然海水本質上是自然資源（土地），但當它被提取並儲存在漁船的水箱中，用於協助生產過程（保持魚類存活以便出售）時，它被視為人造資源或生產投入的一部分（類似於存貨或流動資本）。

English: While seawater is naturally a natural resource (Land), once it is extracted and contained in a tank on a fishing boat to aid the production process (keeping fish alive for sale), it functions as a man-made resource or production input (similar to inventory or working capital).

Option B (Wrong) Concept Trap

因為政府提供海水予公眾作沖廁之用，所以海水是共用品。

Chinese: 這是典型的概念混淆。「由政府提供」是指「公營物品」(Publicly provided good)，而非「共用品」(Public good)。沖廁水具有競爭性（你用了我就不能用）和排他性（可以截斷供應），所以它是私用品 (Private Good)。

English: This is a classic confusion. "Provided by the government" makes it a "Publicly provided good," not a "Public good." Flushing water is rival (if you use it, I cannot) and excludable (supply can be cut off), so it is a Private Good.

Option C (Wrong) Logic Trap

因為人們可以免費獲得海水，所以海水是免費物品。

Chinese: 雖然海裏的海水通常被視為免費物品，但這個選項的推論邏輯是錯誤的。「免費獲得」(Price = 0) 不等於「免費物品」(Opportunity Cost = 0)。例如，免費報紙是經濟物品，因為它依然稀缺。

English: Although seawater in the ocean is often considered a free good, the reasoning here is flawed. "Obtained free of charge" (Price = 0) does not imply "Free Good" (Opportunity Cost = 0). For example, a free newspaper is an economic good because it is still scarce.

Option D (Wrong) Definition Error

因為沒有人飲用海水，所以海水是劣等物品。

Chinese: 劣等物品 (Inferior Good) 的定義是當收入增加時，需求會下降。這與是否有人飲用無關。沒有人飲用只是表示對作為飲品的海水需求為零。

English: An Inferior Good is defined as a good where demand decreases when income increases. This has nothing to do with whether people drink it. The fact that no one drinks it simply means the demand for seawater as a beverage is zero.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

The Pivot Point (關鍵點)

The key is to distinguish between the nature of the good (scarcity) and the monetary price.

關鍵在於區分物品的本質（稀缺性）與金錢價格。

Distractor Identification (干擾項識別)

  • Option B: Confuses "Public Good" (economic definition) with "Public Service" (everyday language).
  • Option C: Uses a false causal link. "Price = 0" is the distractor. The definition of a Free Good relies on Supply vs. Demand, not Price.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: Isn't seawater a "Gift of Nature"? Why is it Capital in Option A?

A: Excellent question. In its natural state (in the ocean), yes, it is Land. However, Economics focuses on context. Once the fisherman expends effort to pump it into a tank, it becomes an input for production. Just like crude oil is "Land" underground, but once extracted and stored in barrels, it becomes "Inventory/Capital" for the refinery.

中文： 很好的問題。在海洋中的自然狀態下，它是土地。但經濟學看重情境。一旦漁民花費力氣將其泵入水箱，它就變成了生產投入。就像地下的原油是「土地」，但一旦開採並儲存在桶中，它就變成了煉油廠的「存貨/資本」。

Q: Why is Option C wrong? Seawater IS a free good, right?

A: Seawater in the ocean is usually a free good. However, Option C is wrong because of the word "as" (因為). It claims it is a free good because it is free of charge. This logic is false. Air in a diving tank is free of charge to the diver (included in the fee), but it is an Economic Good.

中文： 海洋中的海水通常是免費物品。但選項 C 錯誤在於「因為」二字。它聲稱海水是免費物品是因為它免費。這邏輯是錯的。潛水氣瓶裡的空氣對潛水員來說可能不另外收費，但它是經濟物品。
