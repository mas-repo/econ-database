HKDSE Economics MCQ Analysis

Topic: 市場干預與價格彈性 / Market Intervention & Price Elasticity

Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ (3/5)

需求價格彈性 (Price Elasticity of Demand) 補貼 (Subsidy) 曲線移動 (Curve Shift)

1. 題目 / The Question

假設某經濟的政府補貼空氣淨化機的生產。結果，空氣淨化機的價格由$1 200下降至$1 000，而交易量則由5 000單位上升至8 000單位。這顯示了在該價格範圖內，__________ 價格彈性（以絕對值計算）的數值 __________。

A. 需求 …… 大於1
B. 需求 …… 小於1
C. 供應 …… 大於1
D. 供應 …… 小於1

Suppose the government of an economy subsidises the manufacturing of air purifiers. As a result, the price of air purifiers decreases from $1 200 to $1 000 and the quantity transacted increases from 5 000 to 8 000 units. This indicates that within the price range, the price elasticity (in absolute value) of __________ is _________.

A. demand …… greater than 1
B. demand …… smaller than 1
C. supply …… greater than 1
D. supply …… smaller than 1

2. 概念重溫 / Concept Review

解答此題需要理解兩個核心經濟學概念：市場干預引致的曲線移動 與 價格彈性的計算。

Solving this question requires understanding two core economic concepts: Curve shifts due to market intervention and the calculation of price elasticity.

  • 政府補貼 (Government Subsidy): 當政府補貼生產者時，生產成本下降，導致供應曲線向右移 (向下移)。需求曲線在此過程中保持不變。

    Government Subsidy: When the government subsidizes producers, the cost of production decreases, causing the supply curve to shift to the right (or downwards). The demand curve remains stationary during this process.

  • 計算哪一種彈性？(Which elasticity to calculate?): 由於新的均衡點是沿著固定不變的需求曲線移動，這段價格與數量的變化反映的是消費者的反應程度。因此，我們只能計算需求價格彈性 (Price Elasticity of Demand, PED)。

    Which elasticity to calculate?: Since the new equilibrium point moves along the stationary demand curve, the change in price and quantity reflects consumers' responsiveness. Therefore, we can only calculate the Price Elasticity of Demand (PED).

需求價格彈性公式 (Price Elasticity of Demand Formula): $$ E_d = \left| \frac{\% \Delta Q_d}{\% \Delta P} \right| = \left| \frac{\frac{Q_2 - Q_1}{Q_1}}{\frac{P_2 - P_1}{P_1}} \right| $$

如果 $E_d > 1$，代表需求屬彈性 (Elastic)。如果 $E_d < 1$，代表需求屬低彈性 (Inelastic)。

If $E_d > 1$, demand is elastic. If $E_d < 1$, demand is inelastic.

3. 答案與詳細分析 / The Answer & Detailed Analysis

選項 A (Option A) Correct 正確

分析： 首先，補貼導致供應增加，均衡點沿著需求曲線移動，因此我們計算的是需求價格彈性。接著計算變化的百分比：
價格變動百分比 ($\% \Delta P$) = $\frac{1000 - 1200}{1200} = -16.67\%$
數量變動百分比 ($\% \Delta Q$) = $\frac{8000 - 5000}{5000} = +60\%$
由於數量改變的百分比 (60%) 大於價格改變的百分比 (16.67%)，即 $\left| \% \Delta Q \right| > \left| \% \Delta P \right|$，彈性數值大於 1。

Analysis: First, the subsidy increases supply, and the equilibrium moves along the demand curve, so we are calculating the price elasticity of demand. Next, calculate the percentage changes:
$\% \Delta P = \frac{1000 - 1200}{1200} = -16.67\%$
$\% \Delta Q = \frac{8000 - 5000}{5000} = +60\%$
Since the percentage change in quantity (60%) is greater than the percentage change in price (16.67%), i.e., $\left| \% \Delta Q \right| > \left| \% \Delta P \right|$, the elasticity value is greater than 1.

選項 B (Option B) Wrong 錯誤

分析： 雖然正確指出了這是「需求」的彈性，但計算結果錯誤。因為 60% 明顯大於 16.67%，彈性數值不可能小於 1。

Analysis: Although it correctly identifies this as elasticity of "demand", the calculation is wrong. Since 60% is clearly greater than 16.67%, the elasticity value cannot be smaller than 1.

選項 C & D (Options C & D) Wrong 錯誤

分析： 這是概念上的根本錯誤。補貼導致整條供應曲線移動 (Shift in Supply)。我們只有舊供應曲線上的一點和新供應曲線上的一點，無法計算供應價格彈性 (因為兩點不在同一條供應曲線上)。

Analysis: This is a fundamental conceptual error. The subsidy causes the entire supply curve to shift. We only have one point on the old supply curve and one point on the new supply curve, making it impossible to calculate the price elasticity of supply (because the two points do not lie on the same supply curve).

4. 題目拆解 / Problem Decomposition

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point): 題目的第一句話「政府補貼空氣淨化機的生產」是破題的關鍵。你必須立刻聯想到：生產受影響 $\rightarrow$ 供應曲線移動 $\rightarrow$ 必須計算「需求」彈性。這一步直接幫你刪去 C 和 D 選項。

Pivot Point: The first sentence "government subsidises the manufacturing" is the key to solving the problem. You must immediately connect the dots: Production is affected $\rightarrow$ Supply curve shifts $\rightarrow$ We MUST calculate "Demand" elasticity. This step instantly eliminates options C and D.

關鍵資訊與干擾項 (Crucial Info vs Distractors): 題目中所有的數字 ($1200, $1000, 5000, 8000) 都是關鍵資訊，沒有廢話。真正的干擾在於文字選項中對「供應」與「需求」的混淆。

Crucial Info vs Distractors: All numbers in the question ($1200, $1000, 5000, 8000) are crucial for the calculation; there is no irrelevant data. The real distractor is the conceptual confusion between "supply" and "demand" in the options.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 / Common Mistakes & Traps

陷阱一：因果倒置。 學生常常看到「補貼生產」，就直覺以為題目在考「供應彈性」。記住，一條曲線移動，我們量度的是另一條固定曲線的彈性。

Trap 1: Reversing Cause and Effect. Students often read "subsidises manufacturing" and instinctively think the question tests "supply elasticity". Remember, when one curve shifts, we measure the elasticity of the OTHER stationary curve.

陷阱二：計算粗心。 學生可能會把公式倒轉，變成 $\frac{\% \Delta P}{\% \Delta Q}$。永遠記住：數量 ($Q$) 永遠在分子 (上面)。

Trap 2: Careless Calculation. Students might invert the formula, calculating $\frac{\% \Delta P}{\% \Delta Q}$ instead. Always remember: Quantity ($Q$) is always the numerator (on top).

6. 學生常見疑問 / Student Q&A

問 (Q): 為什麼我們不能使用弧彈性 (中點公式 / Mid-point formula) 來計算？

Q: Why don't we use the arc elasticity (mid-point formula) for calculation?

答 (A): 在 HKDSE 經濟科的選擇題中，通常使用簡單的百分比變動（以原來的數值為分母）已經足夠判斷大於 1 還是小於 1。即使你使用中點公式，$\% \Delta P$ 約為 $18.18\%$，$\% \Delta Q$ 約為 $46.15\%$，結果仍然是 $E_d > 1$。為了爭取考試時間，使用簡單百分比法（甚至只估算分數比例）是最明智的。

A: In HKDSE Economics MCQs, using the simple percentage change (with the original value as the denominator) is usually sufficient to determine if it's greater or smaller than 1. Even if you use the mid-point formula ($\% \Delta P \approx 18.18\%$, $\% \Delta Q \approx 46.15\%$), the result is still $E_d > 1$. To save time during the exam, using the simple percentage method (or just estimating the fractions) is the smartest approach.

7. 考試技巧與速解 / Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

秒殺技巧 (The "Shift = The Other" Rule):

Shortcut (The "Shift = The Other" Rule):

  • 供應移動 $\rightarrow$ 找需求彈性

    Supply shifts $\rightarrow$ Find Demand elasticity

  • 需求移動 $\rightarrow$ 找供應彈性

    Demand shifts $\rightarrow$ Find Supply elasticity

速算技巧 (Quick Estimation):
不要浪費時間精確計算小數點！
看價格：跌了 $200，佔 $1200 的一小部分 ($\frac{1}{6}$)。
看數量：升了 $3000，佔 $5000 的一大半 ($\frac{3}{5}$ 或 60%)。
很明顯，數量的變動幅度遠大於價格的變動幅度 ($Q$ 變動大 $\rightarrow$ 彈性高)，所以絕對值必然大於 1。十秒鐘即可選出 A！

Quick Estimation:
Don't waste time calculating exact decimals!
Look at Price: Dropped by $200, a small fraction of $1200 ($\frac{1}{6}$).
Look at Quantity: Increased by 3000, more than half of 5000 ($\frac{3}{5}$ or 60%).
Clearly, the magnitude of change in quantity is much larger than that of price (Large change in $Q \rightarrow$ Highly elastic), so the absolute value must be greater than 1. You can choose A in 10 seconds!
