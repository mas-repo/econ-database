HKDSE Economics Tutor Analysis

Topic (課題): Business Ownership - Private Limited Company (企業所有權 - 私人有限公司)

Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆ (2/5)

Key Concepts (關鍵概念):

Separate Legal Entity (獨立法定實體) Private Limited Company (私人有限公司) Financial Disclosure (財務披露)

1. The Question (題目)

ABC工作室有限公司是一間香港的影片製作公司。法例上，它毋須向公眾披露其財務狀況。下列哪項關於該公司的陳述是正確的？

A. 新股東的加入毋須得到現有股東同意。
B. 該公司可以其名義擁有資產。
C. 該公司可透過向公眾發行債券集資。
D. 股東人數沒有上限。

ABC Studio Limited is a video production company in Hong Kong. Its financial status is not required to be disclosed to the general public by law. Which of the following statements about the company is correct?

A. The admission of new shareholders does not require a consent from existing shareholders.
B. The company can own assets under its name.
C. The company may raise capital by issuing bonds to the general public.
D. There is no upper limit on the number of shareholders.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這條題目，我們必須了解**私人有限公司 (Private Limited Company)** 的特徵。題目的關鍵在於「毋須向公眾披露其財務狀況」，這確認了該公司是一間私人有限公司，而非公眾有限公司 (Public Limited Company)。

To answer this question, we must understand the characteristics of a **Private Limited Company**. The key phrase "not required to be disclosed to the general public" confirms that this is a Private Limited Company, not a Public Limited Company.

Key Definitions (關鍵定義)

  • 獨立法定實體 (Separate Legal Entity): 在法律上，公司被視為一個獨立的「法人」。它可以擁有資產、簽訂合約、起訴及被起訴。
  • 有限債務 (Limited Liability): 股東的最大損失僅限於其投資額。如果我們設 \(L\) 為最大潛在損失，那麼：
  • Separate Legal Entity: In the eyes of the law, the company is treated as an independent "legal person". It can own assets, enter into contracts, sue, and be sued.
  • Limited Liability: Shareholders' maximum loss is limited to their investment amount. If we let \(L\) be the maximum potential loss, then:
$$L_{max} = \text{Total Value of Fully Paid-up Shares}$$
  • 私人有限公司特徵: 股份轉讓受限制（通常需現有股東同意），股東人數限制為 \(1 \le n \le 50\)（其中 \(n\) 為股東人數），且不能向公眾發行股票或債券集資。
  • Features of Private Limited Company: Restriction on the transfer of shares (usually requires consent of existing shareholders), shareholder limit of \(1 \le n \le 50\) (where \(n\) is the number of shareholders), and cannot issue shares or debentures to the general public.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是：選項 B (Option B)

The correct answer is: Option B

選項 A 分析：錯誤。
作為私人有限公司，股份轉讓是受限制的。新股東的加入或現有股份的轉讓，通常必須得到現有股東或董事局的同意。這防止了外人輕易控制公司。

Analysis for Option A: Incorrect.
As a private limited company, the transfer of shares is restricted. The admission of new shareholders or transfer of existing shares usually requires the consent of existing shareholders or the board of directors. This prevents outsiders from easily gaining control of the company.

選項 B 分析：正確。
所有有限公司（無論是私人還是公眾）都是獨立的法定實體 (Separate Legal Entity)。這意味著公司在法律上擁有自己的身份，可以以公司自己的名義購買和擁有資產，而不是以股東的個人名義。

Analysis for Option B: Correct.
All limited companies (whether private or public) are separate legal entities. This means the company has its own identity in the eyes of the law and can purchase and own assets under its own name, rather than the personal names of the shareholders.

選項 C 分析：錯誤。
私人有限公司被嚴格禁止向普羅大眾發行股票 (Shares) 或債券 (Bonds/Debentures) 來集資。只有公眾有限公司才可以這樣做。

Analysis for Option C: Incorrect.
Private limited companies are strictly prohibited from issuing shares or bonds/debentures to the general public to raise capital. Only public limited companies can do this.

選項 D 分析：錯誤。
私人有限公司的股東人數有明確的法律上限，即最多 50 人（即 \(n \le 50\)）。公眾有限公司才沒有股東人數上限。

Analysis for Option D: Incorrect.
Private limited companies have a clear legal upper limit on the number of shareholders, which is a maximum of 50 (i.e., \(n \le 50\)). Only public limited companies have no upper limit on shareholders.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point)：
題目中的 「毋須向公眾披露其財務狀況」 是破題關鍵。這句說話直接告訴你這是一間「私人有限公司」。一旦確立了這一點，你只需要運用私人有限公司的特徵來篩選選項。

The Pivot Point:
The phrase "not required to be disclosed to the general public" is the key to solving the problem. This statement directly tells you it is a "Private Limited Company". Once established, you simply apply the characteristics of a private limited company to filter the options.

干擾資訊 (Distractors)：
「影片製作公司」完全是多餘的資訊。公司的行業性質（如影片製作、餐飲、科技）不會影響其法律架構的特徵。

Distractors:
"Video production company" is completely redundant information. The industry nature of the company (e.g., video production, catering, tech) does not affect the characteristics of its legal structure.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

同學最常犯的錯誤是看到公司名稱有「有限公司 (Limited)」一詞，就自動聯想到可以在香港交易所上市的大型企業（即公眾有限公司），從而錯誤選擇 C 或 D。請記住，香港絕大部份的「有限公司」其實都是「私人有限公司」。

The most common mistake students make is seeing the word "Limited" in the company name and automatically associating it with large corporations listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange (i.e., Public Limited Companies), thereby wrongly choosing C or D. Remember that the vast majority of "Limited" companies in Hong Kong are actually "Private Limited Companies".

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 既然不能向公眾發行債券或股票，那這間公司如何集資擴充？
A: 它可以透過向現有股東發行新股、向銀行借貸 (Bank loans)、或者尋找少數願意加入的私人投資者（且總人數保持在50人或以下，並獲得現有股東同意）來集資。

Q: Since it cannot issue bonds or shares to the public, how does this company raise capital for expansion?
A: It can raise capital by issuing new shares to existing shareholders, taking out bank loans, or finding a few private investors willing to join (provided the total number remains 50 or below, and existing shareholders consent).

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧：
當你看到題目問及任何「有限公司」(無論是私人還是公眾)，**「獨立法定實體」(擁有資產、簽訂合約、起訴權)** 以及 **「有限債務」(Limited Liability)** 這兩個選項幾乎永遠是絕對正確的真理 (Universal Truth)。如果你在選項中看到「可以其名義擁有資產」，你可以大膽假設它是正確答案，然後快速驗證其他選項是否有明顯錯誤即可節省時間。

Speed-Solving Trick:
Whenever a question asks about ANY "Limited Company" (whether private or public), options stating **"Separate Legal Entity" (owning assets, making contracts, right to sue)** and **"Limited Liability"** are almost always universal truths. If you see "can own assets under its name" in the options, you can safely assume it is the correct answer. Simply do a quick verification of the other options for obvious errors to save time.
