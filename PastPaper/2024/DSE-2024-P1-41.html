Macroeconomics: Fiscal Policy and National Income Determination

難度 (Difficulty): ★★★★☆

關鍵字 (Key Concepts): 入息稅 (Income Tax) 國民收入 (National Income) 乘數效應 (Multiplier Effect) 進口函數 (Import Function)

📝 題目 (The Question)

在一個開放經濟中，假如政府提高入息稅稅率，入息稅收入 _________ ，而進口總值 ___________。

In an open economy, if the government raises the income tax rate, the income tax revenue _________ and the total import value ___________.

A. 會上升 …… 可能上升或下降
will increase …… may increase or decrease

B. 會下降 …… 可能上升或下降
will decrease …… may increase or decrease

C. 可能上升或下降 …… 會上升
may increase or decrease …… will increase

D. 可能上升或下降 …… 會下降
may increase or decrease …… will decrease

🧠 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要拆解這道題目，我們需要明白稅率 (Tax Rate)的改變如何透過乘數效應 (Multiplier Effect)影響整體經濟。

To solve this problem, we need to understand how changes in the tax rate affect the overall economy through the multiplier effect.

1. 入息稅收入函數 (Income Tax Revenue Function)

$$T = t \times Y$$

當中 $T$ 是總稅收，$t$ 是稅率，$Y$ 是國民收入。當政府提高稅率 ($t$ 上升) 時，市民的可支配收入會減少，導致消費減少，最終令國民收入 ($Y$) 下降。因為 $t$ 上升但 $Y$ 下降，兩股力量相反，所以 $T$ 的最終改變方向是不確定的。

Where $T$ is total tax revenue, $t$ is the tax rate, and $Y$ is national income. When the government raises the tax rate ($t$ increases), citizens' disposable income falls, leading to a decrease in consumption, which ultimately decreases national income ($Y$). Because $t$ increases but $Y$ decreases (opposing forces), the final change in $T$ is uncertain.

2. 進口函數 (Import Function)

$$M = M_0 + mY$$

當中 $M$ 是進口總值，$M_0$ 是自發性進口，$m$ 是邊際進口傾向。由於整體國民收入 ($Y$) 必定下跌，市民購買外國貨品的能力減少，因此誘發性進口 ($mY$) 會下跌，導致整體進口總值 ($M$) 必定下降。

Where $M$ is total import value, $M_0$ is autonomous imports, and $m$ is the marginal propensity to import. Since overall national income ($Y$) definitely falls, citizens' purchasing power for foreign goods decreases. Therefore, induced imports ($mY$) will drop, meaning the total import value ($M$) will definitely decrease.

✅ 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): Option D

A 及 B (錯誤 Wrong):
這兩個選項誤以為稅收一定會隨著稅率上升或下降。正如上述公式 $T = t \times Y$ 所示，我們無法單憑稅率 ($t$) 的上升來斷定總稅收 ($T$) 的去向，因為稅基 ($Y$) 正在縮小。

These two options mistakenly assume that tax revenue must directly increase or decrease with the tax rate. As shown in the formula $T = t \times Y$, we cannot determine the final direction of total tax revenue ($T$) solely based on the increase in the tax rate ($t$) because the tax base ($Y$) is shrinking.

C (錯誤 Wrong):
雖然這個選項正確指出稅收「可能上升或下降」，但它錯誤地指出進口會「會上升」。國民收入下跌必定導致進口減少，而非增加。

Although this option correctly identifies that tax revenue "may increase or decrease", it incorrectly states that imports "will increase". A drop in national income will definitely lead to a decrease in imports, not an increase.

D (正確 Correct):
完美符合我們的推論：由於 $t$ 升 $Y$ 跌，稅收 $T$ 的改變不確定 (可能上升或下降)；由於 $Y$ 跌，進口 $M$ 必定下跌。

Perfectly matches our deduction: Because $t$ rises and $Y$ falls, the change in tax revenue $T$ is uncertain (may increase or decrease); Because $Y$ falls, imports $M$ will definitely decrease.

🔍 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題關鍵 (The Pivot Point):
這條題目的核心在於區分「確定性」與「不確定性」。當遇到兩個變數的問題時，通常其中一個變數的結果是絕對確定的，而另一個則是模稜兩可的。

The core of this question lies in distinguishing between "certainty" and "uncertainty". In problems with two variables, usually, the outcome of one variable is absolutely certain, while the other is ambiguous.

  • 迷惑點 (Distractor): 「提高入息稅稅率」很容易讓人直覺認為「政府收多了錢」(稅收上升)。這是一個常見的直覺陷阱。
  • "Raising the income tax rate" easily misleads intuition into thinking "the government collects more money" (revenue increases). This is a common intuitive trap.
  • 破局點 (Breakthrough): 緊抓國民收入 ($Y$) 的變化。只要你知道緊縮性財政政策必定拉低 $Y$，你就能立刻推斷出依賴 $Y$ 的進口 ($M$) 必定下跌。
  • Focus on the change in national income ($Y$). As long as you know contractionary fiscal policy definitely lowers $Y$, you can immediately deduce that imports ($M$), which depend on $Y$, must fall.

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

混淆「稅率」(Tax Rate) 與「稅收」(Tax Revenue)

同學經常忘記稅收是由「稅率」和「稅基」(即整體國民收入) 共同決定的。以為 $t$ 增加，$T$ 就一定增加，是忽略了宏觀經濟中的乘數效應 (加稅會冷卻經濟，令市民整體賺少了錢)。

Students often forget that tax revenue is jointly determined by the "tax rate" and the "tax base" (i.e., overall national income). Assuming that an increase in $t$ strictly leads to an increase in $T$ ignores the multiplier effect in macroeconomics (raising taxes cools down the economy, leaving citizens earning less overall).

🙋‍♂️ 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 什麼情況下，提高稅率會令稅收真的上升呢？

Q: Under what circumstances would raising the tax rate actually increase tax revenue?

A: 如果國民收入 ($Y$) 下跌的百分比，小於稅率 ($t$) 上升的百分比，總稅收 ($T$) 就會上升。這通常發生在稅率原本較低的時候；相反，如果稅率已經極高，再加稅會令經濟大幅萎縮 ($Y$ 大跌)，總稅收反而可能下跌。

A: If the percentage drop in national income ($Y$) is smaller than the percentage increase in the tax rate ($t$), total tax revenue ($T$) will increase. This usually happens when the initial tax rate is low; conversely, if the tax rate is already extremely high, further tax hikes will cause the economy to shrink drastically ($Y$ drops significantly), and total tax revenue might actually fall.

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

「捨難取易」淘汰法 (The Elimination Method)

在考場上，如果你忘記了稅收的複雜推導，請先看進口總值 (Imports)！

  1. 加稅 = 經濟變差 ($Y \downarrow$)。
  2. 經濟變差 = 市民沒錢買外國貨 = 進口必定下跌 ($M \downarrow$)。
  3. 觀察選項：只有 Option D 指出進口「會下降」。

結論： 只需 3 秒鐘判斷進口的走勢，連稅收的部分都不用思考，就能直接秒殺選出 D！

In the exam room, if you forget the complex derivation of tax revenue, look at Total Imports first!
1. Tax hike = Economy cools down ($Y \downarrow$).
2. Economy cools down = Citizens have less money to buy foreign goods = Imports definitely fall ($M \downarrow$).
3. Look at the options: Only Option D states that imports "will decrease".
Conclusion: By taking just 3 seconds to determine the trend of imports, you can skip analyzing the tax revenue part entirely and instantly choose D!
