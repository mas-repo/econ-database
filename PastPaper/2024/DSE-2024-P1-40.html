Topic: Macroeconomics - AD & BoP Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐ Key Concepts: Aggregate Output, Balance of Payments, Exports, FDI

1. The Question (題目)

下列哪些措施可能令總產出上升，並同時令國際收支平均逆差降低？

(1) 設立夜市以吸引外國遊客
(2) 從外國進口原材料以興建基礎建設
(3) 舉辦一項宣傳活動以吸引更多外國投資

A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

Which of the following measures may increase the aggregate output and reduce the balance of payments deficits at the same time?

(1) to set up night markets to attract foreign tourists
(2) to construct infrastructure by importing raw materials from foreign countries
(3) to launch a promotion campaign to attract more investment from foreign countries

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 總產出 (Aggregate Output, \(Y\))
總產出由總需求 (Aggregate Demand, \(AD\)) 決定。公式為：
1. Aggregate Output (\(Y\))
Aggregate output is determined by Aggregate Demand (\(AD\)). The formula is:
$$ Y = C + I + G + X - M $$
任何令消費 (\(C\))、投資 (\(I\))、政府支出 (\(G\)) 或出口 (\(X\)) 上升的因素，都會令 \(AD\) 增加，從而令總產出 \(Y\) 上升。相反，進口 (\(M\)) 上升會令淨出口 (\(NX\)) 下降，從而令 \(AD\) 下降。
Any factor that increases consumption (\(C\)), investment (\(I\)), government expenditure (\(G\)), or exports (\(X\)) will increase \(AD\), thereby increasing aggregate output \(Y\). Conversely, an increase in imports (\(M\)) decreases net exports (\(NX\)), which decreases \(AD\).
2. 國際收支平衡 (Balance of Payments, BoP)
國際收支平衡記錄了一國與世界其他地區之間的所有經濟交易。它主要分為兩部分：
  • 經常帳 (Current Account)：包括貨物及服務的進出口。出口增加會改善經常帳；進口增加會惡化經常帳。
  • 資本及金融帳 (Capital and Financial Account)：包括外國直接投資 (FDI) 及證券投資。外資流入會改善此帳戶。
要降低國際收支逆差 (BoP deficit)，我們需要資金流入增加（例如：出口上升、外資流入）。
2. Balance of Payments (BoP)
BoP records all economic transactions between a country and the rest of the world. It consists mainly of two parts:
  • Current Account: Includes imports and exports of goods and services. An increase in exports improves it; an increase in imports worsens it.
  • Capital and Financial Account: Includes Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) and portfolio investment. Capital inflows improve this account.
To reduce a BoP deficit, we need more capital inflows (e.g., higher exports, more foreign investment).

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): B

分析 (1) 設立夜市以吸引外國遊客：正確
外國遊客來港消費屬於「服務輸出」(Export of services, \(X\))。當 \(X\) 上升時，總需求 (\(AD\)) 增加，帶動總產出 (\(Y\)) 上升。同時，服務輸出增加會帶來資金流入，改善經常帳，從而降低國際收支逆差。
Analysis of (1) Set up night markets to attract foreign tourists: Correct
Spending by foreign tourists is considered an "export of services" (\(X\)). When \(X\) increases, Aggregate Demand (\(AD\)) increases, leading to a rise in aggregate output (\(Y\)). Simultaneously, the increase in service exports brings capital inflows, improving the current account and thereby reducing the BoP deficit.
分析 (2) 從外國進口原材料以興建基礎建設：錯誤
興建基建會增加投資 (\(I\)) 或政府支出 (\(G\))，可能令總產出 (\(Y\)) 上升。然而，從外國進口原材料會增加進口 (\(M\))。進口增加會導致資金流出，惡化經常帳，從而擴大（而非降低）國際收支逆差。因此不符合題目要求。
Analysis of (2) Construct infrastructure by importing raw materials: Incorrect
Constructing infrastructure increases investment (\(I\)) or government expenditure (\(G\)), which may increase aggregate output (\(Y\)). However, importing raw materials increases imports (\(M\)). Higher imports lead to capital outflows, worsening the current account and thereby increasing (not reducing) the BoP deficit. Thus, it does not meet the question's requirement.
分析 (3) 舉辦宣傳活動以吸引更多外國投資：正確
外國投資增加會直接提升本地的投資支出 (\(I\))，令總需求 (\(AD\)) 增加，總產出 (\(Y\)) 上升。同時，外國資金流入會改善資本及金融帳 (Capital and Financial Account)，從而降低國際收支逆差。
Analysis of (3) Launch a promotion campaign to attract more foreign investment: Correct
An increase in foreign investment directly boosts local investment expenditure (\(I\)), increasing Aggregate Demand (\(AD\)) and aggregate output (\(Y\)). At the same time, the inflow of foreign capital improves the Capital and Financial Account, thereby reducing the BoP deficit.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這條題目的「關鍵點」(Pivot Point) 在於題目要求同時滿足兩個條件：
  1. 總產出上升 (\(Y \uparrow\))
  2. 國際收支逆差降低 (BoP deficit \(\downarrow\)，即需要資金淨流入)
很多同學只留意到總產出上升，而忽略了第二個條件。
The "Pivot Point" of this question is that it requires satisfying two conditions simultaneously:
  1. Aggregate output increases (\(Y \uparrow\))
  2. BoP deficit reduces (requires net capital inflow)
Many students only notice the increase in aggregate output and ignore the second condition.

5. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

問：外國投資不是會導致未來的利潤流出嗎？為什麼會改善國際收支？
答：在短期內，吸引外國投資意味著大量的初始資金流入（記錄在資本及金融帳中），這會立即改善當期的國際收支平衡，降低逆差。雖然長遠來看可能會有股息或利潤流出（記錄在經常帳的初級收入中），但題目問的是該措施的直接/短期影響。
Q: Doesn't foreign investment lead to future profit outflows? Why does it improve the BoP?
A: In the short run, attracting foreign investment means a large initial inflow of capital (recorded in the Capital and Financial Account), which immediately improves the current BoP and reduces the deficit. While there may be dividend or profit outflows in the long run (recorded as primary income in the Current Account), the question focuses on the direct/short-term impact of the measure.

6. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧 (Elimination Method)：
在考試中，你可以先檢查最容易判斷的條件。題目要求「降低國際收支逆差」（即需要賺外匯/資金流入）。
看第 (2) 項：「從外國進口原材料」。進口必然需要支付外匯，導致資金流出，絕對會惡化國際收支。因此 (2) 必定錯誤。
刪去包含 (2) 的選項：A、C、D 全部出局。答案立刻鎖定為 B！完全不需要花時間詳細分析 (1) 和 (3)。
Shortcut (Elimination Method):
In the exam, check the easiest condition first. The question requires "reducing BoP deficits" (meaning we need foreign exchange/capital inflows).
Look at item (2): "importing raw materials". Importing inherently requires paying foreign exchange, leading to capital outflow, which absolutely worsens the BoP. Therefore, (2) must be wrong.
Eliminate options containing (2): A, C, and D are out. The answer is instantly locked as B! You don't even need to spend time analyzing (1) and (3) in detail.
