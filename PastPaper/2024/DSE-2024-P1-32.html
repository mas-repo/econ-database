Topic: 貨幣與銀行 Money & Banking Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆
Key Concepts: HKMA (香港金融管理局), Lender of Last Resort (最後貸款人), Prime Rate (最優惠利率)

題目 The Question

下列哪些是香港金融管理局的職能？
(1) 作為商業銀行的最後貸款人
(2) 釐定銀行貸款的最優惠利率
(3) 發行港元硬幣
Which of the following are functions of the Hong Kong Monetary Authority?
(1) acting as the lender of last resort for commercial banks
(2) setting the prime rate (最優惠利率) of bank loans
(3) issuing Hong Kong dollar coins
A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)
A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

概念重溫 Concept Review

香港金融管理局 (HKMA) 的主要職能：
香港金融管理局（金管局）扮演類似中央銀行的角色，負責維持貨幣及銀行體系穩定。其主要職能包括：
  • 最後貸款人 (Lender of Last Resort): 當商業銀行面臨流動性危機時，金管局會提供資金援助。
  • 發行硬幣及十元紙幣: 香港的硬幣及十元紙幣由金管局發行；而其他面額的紙幣則由三間發鈔銀行（匯豐、渣打、中銀）發行。
  • 管理外匯基金 (Managing Exchange Fund): 維持聯繫匯率制度。
利率的釐定：
金管局只負責釐定貼現窗基本利率 (Base Rate)。而商業銀行向顧客貸款的最優惠利率 (Prime Rate)，是由各商業銀行自行決定的。
Main Functions of the HKMA:
The HKMA acts as Hong Kong's de facto central bank, maintaining monetary and banking stability. Key functions include:
  • Lender of Last Resort: Providing liquidity support to commercial banks facing financial distress.
  • Issuing coins and $10 notes: The HKMA issues all coins and $10 notes, while other banknotes are issued by the three note-issuing banks (HSBC, Standard Chartered, BOCHK).
  • Managing the Exchange Fund: To maintain the Linked Exchange Rate System.
Setting Interest Rates:
The HKMA only sets the Base Rate (貼現窗基本利率). The Prime Rate (最優惠利率) for bank loans is determined by individual commercial banks based on market conditions.

答案與詳細分析 The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Option: B

✓ 聲明 (1) 正確： 金管局作為香港的中央銀行機構，會在商業銀行出現流動性短缺時充當「最後貸款人」，以維持銀行體系穩定。
✓ Statement (1) is Correct: As the de facto central bank, the HKMA acts as the "lender of last resort" to provide liquidity to commercial banks in times of crisis, ensuring banking stability.
✗ 聲明 (2) 錯誤： 「最優惠利率」是由個別商業銀行（如匯豐銀行）根據資金成本和市場競爭自行釐定的，並非由金管局決定。金管局釐定的是「基本利率」。
✗ Statement (2) is Incorrect: The "prime rate" is determined by individual commercial banks (like HSBC) based on their cost of funds and market competition, not by the HKMA. The HKMA sets the "Base Rate".
✓ 聲明 (3) 正確： 在香港，所有硬幣以及十元塑膠/紙幣均由香港特區政府（透過金管局）發行。
✓ Statement (3) is Correct: In Hong Kong, all coins and ten-dollar notes are issued by the HKSAR Government (through the HKMA).

題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

解題關鍵 (Pivot Point)： 本題的關鍵在於區分「中央銀行（金管局）」與「商業銀行」的職責。考評局經常利用「利率釐定」和「貨幣發行」這兩個概念來設置陷阱。只要你清楚記得 最優惠利率 = 商業銀行決定，就能立即破解此題。
Pivot Point: The key to solving this question is distinguishing the roles of the "central bank (HKMA)" from "commercial banks". The HKEAA often uses "interest rate setting" and "currency issuance" as traps. As long as you remember that Prime Rate = determined by commercial banks, you can crack this question instantly.

常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

陷阱 1：混淆各種利率
學生常以為金管局控制所有利率。記住：金管局只控制貼現窗基本利率 (Base Rate)。

陷阱 2：發鈔權的誤解
學生常忘記香港的貨幣發行是「混合制」。金管局發行硬幣及 $10，而三間發鈔銀行發行 $20、$50、$100、$500 及 $1000 紙幣。
Trap 1: Confusing different interest rates
Students often think the HKMA controls all interest rates. Remember: HKMA only controls the Base Rate.

Trap 2: Misunderstanding currency issuance
Students forget Hong Kong has a "mixed" issuance system. HKMA issues coins and $10 notes, while the three note-issuing banks issue $20, $50, $100, $500, and $1000 notes.

學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

Q: 為什麼金管局不直接發行所有紙幣？
A: 這是歷史原因。在金管局成立之前，香港一直依賴具規模的商業銀行發行紙幣。為了維持市場穩定及延續歷史傳統，香港保留了由三間商業銀行發行主要紙幣的制度，但它們必須向金管局提交等值的美元以換取發鈔權（負債證明書）。
Q: Why doesn't the HKMA issue all banknotes directly?
A: This is due to historical reasons. Before the HKMA was established, Hong Kong relied on major commercial banks to issue notes. To maintain market stability and tradition, Hong Kong kept the system where three commercial banks issue the bulk of banknotes, though they must submit equivalent US dollars to the HKMA to gain the right to issue notes (Certificates of Indebtedness).
