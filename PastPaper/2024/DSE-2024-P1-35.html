HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Money & Banking (貨幣與銀行) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts: Money Supply (貨幣供應), Monetary Base (貨幣基礎), Banking Multiplier (銀行乘數), Reserves (儲備)

The Question (題目)

假設某經濟體系的數據如下：
Suppose an economy has the following data:
貨幣供應 (Money Supply) $95 million
公眾持有的現金 (Cash held by public) $15 million
實際儲備比率 (Actual Reserve Ratio) 25%
超額儲備 (Excess Reserves) $4 million
根據上述數據，下列哪項或哪些陳述是正確的？
Based on the above data, which of the following statements is/are correct?
  1. 貨幣基礎是 35 百萬元。
    The monetary base is $35 million.
  2. 最大銀行乘數是 5。
    The maximum banking multiplier is 5.
  3. 最大貨幣供應是 115 百萬元。
    The maximum money supply is $115 million.
A. (1) only
B. (1) and (2) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 貨幣供應與貨幣基礎的關係
The relationship between Money Supply and Monetary Base

貨幣供應 \(M_s\) 是公眾持有的現金 \(C_p\) 加上存款 \(D\)。
Money Supply \(M_s\) is cash held by public \(C_p\) plus deposits \(D\).

$$ M_s = C_p + D $$

貨幣基礎 \(MB\) (或稱高能貨幣) 是公眾持有的現金 \(C_p\) 加上銀行儲備 \(R\)。
Monetary Base \(MB\) (or High-powered Money) is cash held by public \(C_p\) plus reserves \(R\).

$$ MB = C_p + R $$
2. 儲備的構成
Composition of Reserves

實際儲備 \(R\) = 法定儲備 \(RR\) + 超額儲備 \(ER\)。
Actual Reserves \(R\) = Required Reserves \(RR\) + Excess Reserves \(ER\).

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: D
在分析選項之前，我們必須先計算出隱藏的數值：存款 \(D\) 和 法定儲備比率 \(rrr\)。
Before analyzing the options, we must first calculate the hidden values: Deposits \(D\) and Required Reserve Ratio \(rrr\).
Step 0: Preparation (準備工作)

Find Deposits (\(D\)):

$$ D = M_s - C_p = 95 - 15 = 80 $$

Find Actual Reserves (\(R\)):

$$ R = D \times 25\% = 80 \times 0.25 = 20 $$

Find Required Reserves (\(RR\)):

$$ RR = R - ER = 20 - 4 = 16 $$

Find Required Reserve Ratio (\(rrr\)): (Crucial Step!)

$$ rrr = \frac{RR}{D} = \frac{16}{80} = 20\% $$
Analysis of Statement (1)
「貨幣基礎是 35 百萬元。」
"The monetary base is $35 million."

貨幣基礎等於公眾持有現金加上實際儲備。
Monetary Base equals cash held by public plus actual reserves.

$$ MB = C_p + R = 15 + 20 = 35 $$

Statement (1) is CORRECT.

Analysis of Statement (2)
「最大銀行乘數是 5。」
"The maximum banking multiplier is 5."

最大銀行乘數取決於法定儲備比率，而不是實際儲備比率。
The maximum banking multiplier depends on the required reserve ratio, not the actual reserve ratio.

$$ \text{Max Multiplier} = \frac{1}{rrr} = \frac{1}{20\%} = 5 $$

Statement (2) is CORRECT.

Analysis of Statement (3)
「最大貨幣供應是 115 百萬元。」
"The maximum money supply is $115 million."

要達到最大貨幣供應，銀行會借出所有超額儲備，使實際儲備 \(R\) 全部變為法定儲備。
To reach maximum money supply, banks lend out all excess reserves, so Actual Reserves \(R\) become fully Required Reserves.

Max Deposits (\(D_{max}\)):

$$ D_{max} = \frac{R}{rrr} = \frac{20}{20\%} = 100 $$

Max Money Supply (\(M_{s,max}\)):

$$ M_{s,max} = C_p + D_{max} = 15 + 100 = 115 $$

Statement (3) is CORRECT.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

The Pivot Point (關鍵轉折點)

這道題目的成敗關鍵在於計算 法定儲備比率 (rrr)。
The pivot point of this question is calculating the Required Reserve Ratio (rrr).

題目給了「實際儲備比率」(25%)，這是一個陷阱。你不能直接用 25% 來計算最大乘數。你必須先扣除超額儲備，算出真正的法定要求。
The question gives the "Actual Reserve Ratio" (25%), which is a distractor. You cannot use 25% to calculate the max multiplier. You must deduct excess reserves first to find the true legal requirement.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • Mistake 1: 誤用實際儲備比率計算乘數。 Using the Actual Reserve Ratio (25%) to calculate the multiplier.
    Incorrect: \(1 / 0.25 = 4\).
  • Mistake 2: 計算最大貨幣供應時忘記加上現金 \(C_p\)。 Forgetting to add Cash \(C_p\) when calculating Max Money Supply.
    Incorrect: Max \(M_s\) = Max Deposits = 100.
  • Mistake 3: 混淆貨幣基礎與貨幣供應。 Confusing Monetary Base with Money Supply.
    Remember: Base = Cash + Reserves; Supply = Cash + Deposits.
