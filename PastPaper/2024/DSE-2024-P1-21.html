HKDSE Economics 經濟學題解

Topic (課題): Market Intervention & Efficiency (市場干預與效率)

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4/5)

Key Concepts (關鍵概念): Consumer Surplus (消費者盈餘) Deadweight Loss (淨損失) Price Control (價格管制)

The Question (題目)

以下是物品X的供需圖。原初，物品X的價格是 \(P_0\)。
The following is the demand-supply diagram of Good X. Initially the price of Good X is \(P_0\).

下列哪些情況可能令物品X市場上的消費者盈餘下降，同時造成淨損失？
Which of the following situations may reduce the consumer surplus and create a deadweight loss in the market of Good X at the same time?

  1. 物品X的需求下降
    a decrease in the demand for Good X
  2. 對物品X實施一有效的價格上限
    the imposition of an effective price ceiling on Good X
  3. 對物品X實施一有效的價格下限
    the imposition of an effective price floor on Good X

A. 只有 (1)、(2) / (1) and (2) only
B. 只有 (1)、(3) / (1) and (3) only
C. 只有 (2)、(3) / (2) and (3) only
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3) / (1), (2) and (3)

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這條問題，我們必須清楚理解以下幾個核心概念：

To answer this question, we must clearly understand the following core concepts:

  • Consumer Surplus (消費者盈餘, CS): 消費者願意支付的最高價格與實際支付價格之間的差額。在圖表中，它是需求曲線以下、價格線以上的面積。
    The difference between the maximum price consumers are willing to pay and the actual price they pay. Graphically, it is the area below the demand curve and above the price line.
  • Deadweight Loss (淨損失, DWL): 由於市場未能達到資源分配的最高效率（例如受到政府干預），導致總社會盈餘（Total Social Surplus）減少的部分。
    The loss of total social surplus that occurs when the market is not operating at the efficient equilibrium (e.g., due to government intervention).
$$ \text{Total Social Surplus} = \text{Consumer Surplus} + \text{Producer Surplus} $$

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是 / The correct option is: Option C

分析 (1): 物品X的需求下降 / Decrease in demand

當需求下降（需求曲線向左移），新的均衡價格和交易量都會下降。雖然消費者盈餘確實會改變（通常是下降），但這是一個自然市場調節的過程。新的均衡點仍然是有效率的（Marginal Benefit = Marginal Cost）。因此，不會產生淨損失 (No Deadweight Loss)。

When demand decreases (demand curve shifts left), both the new equilibrium price and quantity fall. While consumer surplus does change (usually decreases), this is a natural market adjustment. The new equilibrium is still efficient (Marginal Benefit = Marginal Cost). Therefore, it does NOT create a deadweight loss.

分析 (2): 有效的價格上限 / Effective price ceiling

有效的價格上限設定在均衡價格 \(P_0\) 之下。這會導致交易量減少（由 \(Q_0\) 跌至供應量 \(Q_s\)），從而必定產生淨損失 (Creates DWL)。至於消費者盈餘，由於價格下降，買到的單位享有更多盈餘；但同時交易量減少，失去了部分盈餘。整體消費者盈餘可能上升或下降。因為題目問「可能 (may)」，所以這項成立。

An effective price ceiling is set below the equilibrium price \(P_0\). This reduces the transacted quantity (from \(Q_0\) to quantity supplied \(Q_s\)), which definitely creates a deadweight loss. Regarding consumer surplus, the lower price increases CS for the units bought, but the reduced quantity decreases CS. The net effect means CS may increase or decrease. Since the question asks what "may" happen, this statement is correct.

分析 (3): 有效的價格下限 / Effective price floor

有效的價格下限設定在均衡價格 \(P_0\) 之上。交易量會減少（由 \(Q_0\) 跌至需求量 \(Q_d\)），必定產生淨損失 (Creates DWL)。由於消費者需要支付更高的價格，而且能買到的數量減少了，消費者盈餘必定下降。這完全符合題目的要求。

An effective price floor is set above the equilibrium price \(P_0\). The transacted quantity falls (from \(Q_0\) to quantity demanded \(Q_d\)), which definitely creates a deadweight loss. Because consumers pay a higher price and buy a smaller quantity, consumer surplus definitely decreases. This perfectly fits the question's criteria.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這條題目的「破局點 (Pivot Point)」在於理解「淨損失 (Deadweight Loss)」的成因。

The "Pivot Point" of this question lies in understanding the cause of Deadweight Loss (DWL).

  • 市場自身供求改變（如偏好改變、成本改變）只會帶來新的均衡點，不會導致無效率，因此沒有 DWL。
  • 人為干預（如價格管制、稅收、配額）阻止了市場達到均衡，才會產生 DWL。

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱： 很多學生誤以為「價格上限 (Price Ceiling)」一定會增加消費者盈餘，因為「東西變便宜了」。

Trap: Many students mistakenly believe a Price Ceiling ALWAYS increases consumer surplus because "things are cheaper".

真相： 雖然價格下降了，但供應量也減少了 (Shortage)。消費者可能根本買不到物品！如果供應曲線非常缺乏彈性 (inelastic)，因數量減少而損失的消費者盈餘，可能會大於因價格下降而增加的盈餘，導致總體消費者盈餘下降。

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼選項 (1) 需求下降沒有 Deadweight Loss？盈餘明明減少了啊！
Why doesn't option (1) have a Deadweight Loss? The surplus clearly decreased!

A: 盈餘減少不等於有淨損失 (DWL)。DWL 是指「本來可以實現，但因為市場被扭曲而未能實現的潛在利益」。需求下降代表消費者對該物品的評價 (Marginal Benefit) 真正降低了，社會不生產那麼多是正確且有效率的決定。沒有扭曲，就沒有 DWL。
A decrease in surplus does not equal DWL. DWL refers to "potential gains from trade that are lost because the market is distorted". A decrease in demand means consumers' valuation (Marginal Benefit) has genuinely dropped; producing less is the correct and efficient outcome. No distortion means no DWL.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧 (5-Second Kill):

一看到題目要求「造成淨損失 (create a deadweight loss)」，你應該立刻反應：只有市場干預才會產生 DWL，單純的供求移動不會！

When you see the requirement "create a deadweight loss", your immediate reaction should be: Only market interventions create DWL, simple shifts in supply/demand do NOT!

因此，(1) 需求下降絕對錯。立刻刪去包含 (1) 的選項：
❌ A. (1), (2)
❌ B. (1), (3)
❌ D. (1), (2), (3)
答案只可能是 C！連 (2) 和 (3) 的詳細圖表都不用畫就能得分。
