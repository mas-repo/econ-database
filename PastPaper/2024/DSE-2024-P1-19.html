HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Efficiency & Market Failure (效率與市場失靈) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Allocative Efficiency, Externalities, Equilibrium
Question / 題目:
下列哪項／些關於過度生產的陳述是正確的？
Which of the following statements about overproduction is/are correct?
(1) 當某物品有超額供應時，該物品會出現過度生產。
Overproduction of a good occurs when there is an excess supply of the good.

(2) 每當市場處於均衡狀態時，便不會出現過度生產。
Overproduction does not occur whenever the market is at equilibrium.

(3) 當過度生產出現時，最後一單位的邊際社會成本大於邊際社會利益。
When overproduction occurs, the marginal social cost is greater than the marginal social benefit at the last unit.
  • A. 只有 (2) (2) only
  • B. 只有 (3) (3) only
  • C. 只有 (2)、(3) (2) and (3) only
  • D. (1)、(2) 及 (3) (1), (2) and (3)
Concept Review / 概念重溫

1. 配置效率 (Allocative Efficiency):
當資源分配達到社會最佳水平時，即邊際社會利益等於邊際社會成本。
Achieved when resources are allocated at the socially optimal level, where Marginal Social Benefit (MSB) equals Marginal Social Cost (MSC). $$ MSB = MSC $$

2. 過度生產 (Overproduction):
指生產數量超過了社會最佳水平 \(Q^*\) (即 \(Q > Q^*\))。在此情況下，額外生產單位的社會成本高於其帶來的社會利益，導致無謂損失 (Deadweight Loss)。
Refers to a quantity produced that exceeds the socially optimal level \(Q^*\). In this case, the social cost of the extra units exceeds their social benefit, leading to Deadweight Loss. $$ MSC > MSB $$

3. 超額供應 (Excess Supply):
這是市場不均衡的一種狀態。指在特定價格下，供應量 \(Q_s\) 大於需求量 \(Q_d\)。
A state of market disequilibrium. It occurs when Quantity Supplied \(Q_s\) is greater than Quantity Demanded \(Q_d\) at a specific price.

Correct Option: B (Only 3)
正确選項：B (只有 3)
Statement (1): Incorrect / 錯誤

分析：「超額供應」(Excess Supply) 與「過度生產」(Overproduction) 是兩個完全不同的概念。

  • 超額供應是指市場價格高於均衡價格，導致 \(Q_s > Q_d\)（賣剩貨）。這是一個市場不均衡的現象。
  • 過度生產是指實際產量 \(Q\) 大於社會最優產量 \(Q^*\)。這是一個資源配置低效的現象。
  • 即使市場有超額供應，實際交易量通常由較小的需求量 \(Q_d\) 決定，這可能導致產量過少（生產不足），而非過度生產。

Analysis: "Excess Supply" and "Overproduction" are distinct concepts. Excess supply is a market disequilibrium where \(Q_s > Q_d\) because the price is too high. Overproduction is an inefficiency where actual quantity \(Q\) exceeds the socially optimal quantity \(Q^*\). Even with excess supply, the quantity traded is usually determined by the smaller \(Q_d\), which might actually represent underproduction, not overproduction.

Statement (2): Incorrect / 錯誤

分析：市場均衡 (Market Equilibrium) 不一定代表有效率。

  • 市場均衡發生在私人邊際利益等於私人邊際成本時 (\(MPB = MPC\))。
  • 如果存在生產的負外部性（例如污染），社會成本會大於私人成本 (\(MSC > MPC\))。
  • 此時，市場均衡產量 \(Q_e\) 會大於社會最優產量 \(Q^*\)，這就是過度生產。因此，即使在市場均衡點，過度生產依然可能發生。

Analysis: Market Equilibrium does not necessarily imply efficiency. Equilibrium occurs where \(MPB = MPC\). However, if there are negative externalities in production (e.g., pollution), then \(MSC > MPC\). In this case, the market equilibrium quantity \(Q_e\) will be greater than the socially optimal quantity \(Q^*\), resulting in overproduction. Thus, overproduction can occur even when the market is at equilibrium.

Statement (3): Correct / 正確

分析：這是過度生產的定義。

  • 當產量超過社會最優水平 (\(Q > Q^*\)) 時，對於那些額外生產的單位（由 \(Q^*\) 到 \(Q\)），其對社會造成的成本 (\(MSC\)) 必然高於其帶來的利益 (\(MSB\))。
  • 正是因為 \(MSC > MSB\)，社會才會蒙受福利損失（無謂損失）。

Analysis: This is the definition of overproduction. When quantity exceeds the socially optimal level (\(Q > Q^*\)), for those extra units produced, the cost to society (\(MSC\)) is strictly higher than the benefit to society (\(MSB\)). It is precisely because \(MSC > MSB\) that a Deadweight Loss exists.

Problem Decomposition / 題目拆解

這道題目的核心在於區分「市場狀態」與「效率狀態」。
The core of this question lies in distinguishing between "Market State" and "Efficiency State".

  • Pivot Point (關鍵點):
    市場均衡 (\(D=S\)) 只考慮私人成本與利益。效率 (\(MSC=MSB\)) 則考慮整個社會。兩者只有在沒有外部性 (Externalities) 時才相等。
    Market Equilibrium (\(D=S\)) only considers private costs and benefits. Efficiency (\(MSC=MSB\)) considers the whole society. They are only equal if there are no externalities.
  • Distractor (干擾項):
    選項 (1) 利用「多」(Excess/Over) 這個字眼的相似性來混淆學生。記住：超額供應是「價格定錯了」，過度生產是「資源配錯了」。
    Option (1) uses the semantic similarity of "Excess/Over" to confuse students. Remember: Excess Supply means "Price is wrong"; Overproduction means "Resource allocation is wrong".
Common Mistakes & Traps / 常犯錯誤與陷阱

陷阱：認為「均衡」就是「好」/「有效率」

學生常誤以為只要供需平衡 (Equilibrium)，市場就達到了最優狀態。這是錯誤的。如果不考慮外部性（如污染、二手煙），自由市場的均衡往往會導致資源配置缺乏效率（過度生產或生產不足）。

Trap: Assuming "Equilibrium" means "Good" / "Efficient"
Students often mistakenly believe that as long as Supply meets Demand (Equilibrium), the market is optimal. This is false. Without accounting for externalities (like pollution, second-hand smoke), free market equilibrium often leads to inefficient resource allocation (overproduction or underproduction).
