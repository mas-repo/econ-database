2. The Question (題目)

參考上圖。P和Q分別代表什麼？
Refer to the above diagram. What do P and Q stand for respectively?
  • A. 消費者物品；服務 (consumer goods; services)
  • B. 消費者物品；原料 (consumer goods; raw materials)
  • C. 生產者物品；服務 (producer goods; services)
  • D. 生產者物品；原料 (producer goods; raw materials)

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

產出種類 (Types of Output)

1. 消費者物品 (Consumer Goods): 直接滿足消費者慾望的物品。它們被售予最終使用者（住戶）。

Goods that directly satisfy consumer wants. They are sold to final users (households).

2. 生產者物品 / 資本物品 (Producer Goods / Capital Goods): 用作生產其他物品或服務的物品。它們被售予其他廠商（例如：機器）。

Goods used to produce other goods or services. They are sold to other firms (e.g., machinery).

3. 服務 (Services): 無形的產出（例如：保險、物流、零售）。

Intangible outputs (e.g., insurance, logistics, retail).

生產階段 (Stages of Production)

各個生產階段的產值貢獻可以用以下公式計算：

The value contribution of each production stage can be calculated as:

$$Value\ Added = Value\ of\ Output - Value\ of\ Intermediate\ Goods$$

初級生產（採掘）、次級生產（加工製造）和三級生產（服務）的附加價值總和，相等於本地生產總值 ($GDP$)。

The sum of value added from primary (extraction), secondary (manufacturing), and tertiary (services) production equals the Gross Domestic Product ($GDP$).

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是：選項 A (Option A)

分析箭咀 P (Analyzing Arrow P):
箭咀 P 從「次級生產」指向「住戶」。次級生產負責製造實體物品。由於這些物品直接流向住戶作最終消費用途，因此它們必定是消費者物品。

Arrow P points from "Secondary Production" to "Households". Secondary production manufactures tangible goods. Since these goods flow directly to households for final consumption, they must be consumer goods.

分析箭咀 Q (Analyzing Arrow Q):
箭咀 Q 從「三級生產」指向「初級生產」。三級生產的定義是提供無形的產出，因此其輸出的必定是服務（例如為農場提供銀行貸款或運輸服務）。

Arrow Q points from "Tertiary Production" to "Primary Production". Tertiary production is defined by providing intangible outputs, therefore its output must be services (e.g., providing bank loans or transport services for a farm).

為何其他選項錯誤 (Why other options are wrong):
B & D: 原料 (Raw materials) 是由「初級生產」提供的，而不是從「三級生產」輸出，因此 Q 不可能是原料。
C & D: 雖然次級生產也可以製造「生產者物品」（例如工廠用的機器），但箭咀 P 明確地指向「住戶」，住戶購買的是消費者物品，而非生產者物品。

B & D: Raw materials are provided by "Primary Production", not exported from "Tertiary Production", so Q cannot be raw materials.
C & D: Although secondary production can also create "producer goods" (like factory machines), Arrow P specifically points to "Households". Households purchase consumer goods, not producer goods.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

要破解這類流程圖題目，我們需要找到兩個「關鍵點」(Pivot Points)：

To crack this type of flowchart question, we need to locate two "Pivot Points":

  • 關鍵點 1：箭頭的起點 (Origin of the arrow)
    起點決定了產出的「廣泛性質」。源自次級生產 = 實體物品；源自三級生產 = 服務。
    Pivot Point 1: Origin of the arrow.
    The origin dictates the "broad nature" of the output. From secondary = tangible goods; from tertiary = services.
  • 關鍵點 2：箭頭的終點 (Destination of the arrow)
    終點決定了實體物品是「消費者物品」還是「生產者物品」。流向住戶 = 消費者物品；流向其他廠商 = 生產者物品。
    Pivot Point 2: Destination of the arrow.
    The destination decides if a physical good is a "consumer good" or "producer good". To households = consumer goods; to other firms = producer goods.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：死背「次級生產 = 消費者物品」
很多同學誤以為所有由工廠（次級生產）製造出來的東西都是消費者物品。這是錯的！如果次級生產製造了一部起重機並賣給建築公司，這件產出就是生產者物品（箭頭會指向另一個生產階段，而非住戶）。必須看清楚箭頭的終點！

Trap: Memorizing "Secondary Production = Consumer Goods"
Many students mistakenly believe that everything made in a factory (secondary production) is a consumer good. This is wrong! If secondary production builds a crane and sells it to a construction firm, that output is a producer good (the arrow would point to another production stage, not households). Always check the destination of the arrow!

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺法 (The 5-Second Kill):
直接看 Q。Q 從「三級生產」出發。三級生產只有一個產出：服務。看選項，只有 A 和 C 的 Q 是「服務」。
接著看 P。P 指向「住戶」。住戶只會買「消費者物品」。答案立刻鎖定為 A。完全不需要考慮其他複雜的流向！

The 5-Second Kill:
Look directly at Q. Q originates from "Tertiary Production". Tertiary production has ONLY one output: Services. Look at the options, only A and C have "services" for Q.
Next, look at P. P points to "Households". Households only buy "consumer goods". The answer is instantly locked to A. No need to overthink the other flows!
