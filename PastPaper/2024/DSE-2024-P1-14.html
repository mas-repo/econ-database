Mathematical Proof (æ•¸å­¸è­‰æ˜)

Let \( t_1 \) be the original tax and \( Q_1 \) be the original quantity.
Let \( t_2 \) be the new tax and \( Q_2 \) be the new quantity.

Given:
1. Tax is halved: \( t_2 = 0.5 t_1 \)
2. Revenue is halved: \( TR_2 = 0.5 TR_1 \)

Using the formula \( TR = t \times Q \):
$$ t_2 \times Q_2 = 0.5 (t_1 \times Q_1) $$ Substitute \( t_2 = 0.5 t_1 \):
$$ (0.5 t_1) \times Q_2 = 0.5 t_1 \times Q_1 $$ Divide both sides by \( 0.5 t_1 \):
$$ Q_2 = Q_1 $$
