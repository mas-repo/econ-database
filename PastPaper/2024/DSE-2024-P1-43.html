HKDSE Economics Tutor

Topic: 國際貿易與市場干預 (International Trade & Market Intervention) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: 關稅 (Tariff), 世界價格 (World Price), 進口量 (Import Quantity)

📝 The Question (題目)

[中文] 下表顯示某小型開放經濟中物品X的市場。該經濟向物品X徵收從量關稅。
假如物品X的世界價格上升， ___________。

  • A. 物品X的本地價格會維持不變
  • B. 物品X除稅後的進口總值會上升
  • C. 物品X的本地生產量會上升
  • D. 物品X的關稅收入可能上升

[English] The diagram below shows the market of Good X in a small open economy. A per-unit tariff is imposed on Good X in the economy.
If the world price of Good X increases, ___________.

  • A. the domestic price of Good X will remain unchanged
  • B. the import value net of tariff of Good X will increase
  • C. the quantity of Good X produced domestically will increase
  • D. the tariff revenue of Good X may increase

💡 Concept Review (概念重溫)

在小型開放經濟中，實施從量關稅（Per-unit tariff）後，本地市場的價格和數量由以下公式決定：

In a small open economy, after imposing a per-unit tariff, the domestic market price and quantities are determined by the following formulas:

$$ P_{domestic} = P_w + t $$ $$ \text{Import Quantity (進口量)} = Q_d - Q_s $$ $$ \text{Tariff Revenue (關稅收入)} = t \times (Q_d - Q_s) $$
  • 本地價格 (Domestic Price): 等於世界價格 \(P_w\) 加上從量關稅 \(t\)。
  • Domestic Price: Equals the world price \(P_w\) plus the per-unit tariff \(t\).
  • 本地生產量 (Domestic Quantity Supplied, \(Q_s\)): 由本地價格 \(P_w + t\) 與本地供應曲線 (S) 的相交點決定。價格上升，\(Q_s\) 增加。
  • Domestic Quantity Supplied (\(Q_s\)): Determined by the intersection of the domestic price \(P_w + t\) and the domestic supply curve (S). As price increases, \(Q_s\) increases.
  • 本地需求量 (Domestic Quantity Demanded, \(Q_d\)): 由本地價格 \(P_w + t\) 與本地需求曲線 (D) 的相交點決定。價格上升，\(Q_d\) 減少。
  • Domestic Quantity Demanded (\(Q_d\)): Determined by the intersection of the domestic price \(P_w + t\) and the domestic demand curve (D). As price increases, \(Q_d\) decreases.

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: C

Option A (Incorrect 錯誤)

由於本地價格 \(P_{domestic} = P_w + t\)，當世界價格 \(P_w\) 上升而關稅 \(t\) 不變時，本地價格必然會上升，而非維持不變。

Since the domestic price \(P_{domestic} = P_w + t\), when the world price \(P_w\) increases and tariff \(t\) is constant, the domestic price must increase, not remain unchanged.

Option B (Incorrect 錯誤)

除稅後的進口總值公式為 \(P_w \times (Q_d - Q_s)\)。當 \(P_w\) 上升時，本地價格上升，導致需求量 \(Q_d\) 下降及供應量 \(Q_s\) 上升，因此進口量 \((Q_d - Q_s)\) 必然下降。由於價格上升但數量下降，總值的變化是不確定的（取決於進口需求的彈性），因此它不一定會上升。

The import value net of tariff is \(P_w \times (Q_d - Q_s)\). When \(P_w\) increases, the domestic price increases, causing quantity demanded \(Q_d\) to fall and quantity supplied \(Q_s\) to rise, so the import quantity \((Q_d - Q_s)\) must fall. Because price increases but quantity falls, the change in total value is uncertain (depends on the elasticity of import demand). Therefore, it will not necessarily increase.

Option C (Correct 正確)

當世界價格 \(P_w\) 上升，包含關稅的本地價格 \(P_w + t\) 亦會向上移。沿著向上傾斜的本地供應曲線 (S)，較高的價格會鼓勵本地生產商增加產量，因此本地生產量 \(Q_s\) 必然上升。

When the world price \(P_w\) increases, the domestic price inclusive of tariff \(P_w + t\) shifts upwards. Moving along the upward-sloping domestic supply curve (S), a higher price encourages domestic producers to increase output. Thus, the quantity produced domestically \(Q_s\) will increase.

Option D (Incorrect 錯誤)

關稅收入公式為 \(t \times (Q_d - Q_s)\)。由於從量關稅 \(t\) 維持不變，而進口量 \((Q_d - Q_s)\) 因本地價格上升而減少，關稅收入必然下降，絕對不可能上升。

Tariff revenue is \(t \times (Q_d - Q_s)\). Since the per-unit tariff \(t\) is constant and the import quantity \((Q_d - Q_s)\) decreases due to the higher domestic price, the tariff revenue must decrease. It cannot "may increase".

🔍 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point): 本題的關鍵在於理解「世界價格上升」如何牽動整條水平線 \(P_w + t\) 向上平移。只要畫出新的、較高的 \(P_w' + t\) 水平線，就能直觀地看到它與 S 曲線和 D 曲線的新交點。

Pivot Point: The key is understanding how an "increase in world price" shifts the entire horizontal line \(P_w + t\) upwards. By drawing a new, higher horizontal line \(P_w' + t\), you can visually identify its new intersections with the S and D curves.

干擾資訊 (Distractors): 選項 B 和 D 試圖用「總值 (Value)」和「收入 (Revenue)」來混淆考生。考生必須清楚區分「除稅後進口總值」(\(P_w \times \text{Imports}\)) 和「關稅收入」(\(t \times \text{Imports}\)) 的不同公式。

Distractors: Options B and D attempt to confuse students using "Value" and "Revenue". Students must clearly distinguish between the formulas for "import value net of tariff" (\(P_w \times \text{Imports}\)) and "tariff revenue" (\(t \times \text{Imports}\)).

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱 1：誤以為進口總值必定上升
很多學生看到價格 \(P_w\) 上升，就直覺認為總值 (Price \(\times\) Quantity) 會上升。他們忘記了根據需求定律，價格上升會導致進口數量大幅減少。如果需求屬彈性 (Elastic)，總值反而會下降！

Trap 1: Assuming import value must increase
Many students see price \(P_w\) increase and intuitively think total value (Price \(\times\) Quantity) will rise. They forget that according to the law of demand, a price increase leads to a drop in import quantity. If demand is elastic, the total value will actually decrease!

🙋 Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼選項 D 是錯的？題目說「可能 (may) 上升」，難道完全沒有可能嗎？
Q: Why is Option D wrong? The question says it "may increase", is there absolutely no possibility?

A: 完全沒有可能。關稅收入 = \(t \times \text{進口量}\)。題目中 \(t\) 是固定的從量稅（常數）。當世界價格上升，本地價格隨之上升，導致本地需求減少、本地供應增加，進口量必定減少。常數乘以一個變小的數字，結果必定變小。因此關稅收入必定下降，連「可能上升」的機會都沒有。

A: Absolutely no possibility. Tariff revenue = \(t \times \text{Import Quantity}\). Here, \(t\) is a fixed per-unit tax (a constant). When world price rises, domestic price rises, causing domestic demand to fall and domestic supply to rise, so import quantity must decrease. A constant multiplied by a smaller number must yield a smaller result. Thus, tariff revenue must strictly decrease; there is zero chance it "may increase".

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

圖像速解法 (Graphical Shortcut):
在考試時，直接在試卷的圖表上畫一條比原來 \(P_w + t\) 更高的水平線。你會立刻看到：
1. 新線與 S 曲線的交點向右移 \(\rightarrow\) 本地生產量上升 (秒殺選 C)。
2. 新線與 D 曲線的交點向左移 \(\rightarrow\) 本地需求量下降。
3. 兩點之間的距離 (進口量) 明顯縮短 \(\rightarrow\) 關稅收入必然下降 (排除 D)。

Graphical Shortcut:
During the exam, simply draw a new horizontal line higher than the original \(P_w + t\) directly on the question paper. You will immediately see:
1. The intersection with S moves right \(\rightarrow\) domestic production increases (Instantly choose C).
2. The intersection with D moves left \(\rightarrow\) domestic quantity demanded decreases.
3. The gap between the two points (imports) clearly narrows \(\rightarrow\) tariff revenue must decrease (Eliminate D).
