📚 主題 (Topic): 宏觀經濟學 - 貨幣政策與總需求 (Macroeconomics - Monetary Policy & Aggregate Demand)
⭐ 難度 (Difficulty): ★★★★☆ (4/5 Stars)
🔑 核心概念 (Key Concepts): Discount Rate 貼現率 Transmission Mechanism 傳導機制 Aggregate Demand 總需求

1. 題目 (The Question)

假設甲國的中央銀行下調貼現率。下列哪項情況會削弱上述政策對甲國在短期的總產出的影響？

Suppose the central bank of Country A lowers the discount rate. Which of the following situations would weaken the effect of the above policy on the aggregate output of Country A in the short run?

  • A. 數碼貨幣的使用在甲國漸趨流行。
    The use of digital money becomes more popular in Country A.
  • B. 甲國的一些主要貿易伙伴經歷經濟衰退。
    Some major trading partners of Country A experience an economic recession.
  • C. 甲國政府放寬輸入外勞的限制。
    The government of Country A relaxes the restrictions on the importation of foreign workers.
  • D. 甲國政府增加對本地基建的投資。
    The government of Country A increases its investment on local infrastructure.

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

擴張性貨幣政策的傳導機制 (Transmission Mechanism of Expansionary Monetary Policy)

當中央銀行下調貼現率 (Discount Rate)，這屬於擴張性貨幣政策。貼現率是商業銀行向中央銀行借款的利率。貼現率下降會降低銀行的借貸成本，促使它們借入更多儲備，從而增加貨幣基礎和貨幣供應。

When the central bank lowers the discount rate, it is adopting an expansionary monetary policy. The discount rate is the interest rate commercial banks pay to borrow from the central bank. A lower discount rate reduces the cost of borrowing for banks, encouraging them to borrow more reserves, which increases the monetary base and money supply.

$$ \downarrow \text{Discount Rate} \rightarrow \uparrow \text{Money Supply} (M_s) \rightarrow \downarrow \text{Interest Rate} (r) \rightarrow \uparrow \text{Investment} (I) \ \& \ \uparrow \text{Consumption} (C) \rightarrow \uparrow \text{Aggregate Demand} (AD) \rightarrow \uparrow \text{Aggregate Output} (Y) $$

總需求公式 (Aggregate Demand Formula):

$$ AD = C + I + G + (X - M) $$

題目要求找出會「削弱 (weaken)」這項政策對總產出 (\(Y\)) 影響的因素，即尋找會抵消總需求增加或降低貨幣政策效力的選項。

The question asks for a situation that would "weaken" the policy's effect on aggregate output (\(Y\)). We need to find an option that offsets the increase in Aggregate Demand or reduces the effectiveness of the monetary transmission mechanism.

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): B

正確 Correct選項 B (Option B)

分析：貿易伙伴經歷經濟衰退，意味著他們的國民收入下降。這會導致他們對甲國出口商品的需求減少，甲國的出口額 (\(X\)) 下降。由於 \(AD = C + I + G + (X - M)\)，出口下降會導致總需求 (\(AD\)) 下降（向左移）。這剛好抵消了擴張性貨幣政策帶來的總需求增加（向右移），從而削弱了政策對提升總產出的影響。此外，這也削弱了貨幣政策中透過匯率貶值來刺激出口的傳導效果。

Analysis: If trading partners experience a recession, their national income falls. This leads to a decrease in their demand for Country A's exports (\(X \downarrow\)). Since \(AD = C + I + G + (X - M)\), a drop in exports causes Aggregate Demand (\(AD\)) to fall (shift left). This directly offsets the increase in AD (shift right) brought by the expansionary monetary policy, thereby weakening the policy's overall effect on raising aggregate output. Furthermore, it weakens the exchange rate transmission channel of monetary policy (where currency depreciation usually boosts exports).

錯誤 Wrong選項 A (Option A)

分析：數碼貨幣變得流行，公眾會減少持有實體現金，導致現金流失率 (cash leakage ratio) 下降。這會擴大貨幣乘數 (money multiplier)。在相同的貨幣基礎增加下，貨幣供應量 (\(M_s\)) 的增幅會更大，利率下降幅度更大，反而會加強 (strengthen) 該政策的影響。

Analysis: If digital money becomes more popular, the public will hold less physical cash, leading to a lower cash leakage ratio. This increases the money multiplier. For a given increase in the monetary base, the resulting increase in money supply (\(M_s\)) will be larger, leading to a larger drop in interest rates. This would actually strengthen the policy's effect.

錯誤 Wrong選項 C (Option C)

分析：放寬輸入外勞會增加勞動人口，從而增加短期總供應 (SRAS，曲線向右移)。當總需求 (AD) 因貨幣政策向右移時，配合總供應的增加，總產出 (\(Y\)) 的實際增幅會比原本更大，物價升幅則較溫和。因此，這會加強政策對總產出的正面影響。

Analysis: Relaxing restrictions on foreign workers increases the labor supply, which increases Short-Run Aggregate Supply (SRAS shifts right). When AD shifts right due to the monetary policy, an increased SRAS means the actual increase in aggregate output (\(Y\)) will be larger than otherwise, while the price increase will be milder. Therefore, this strengthens the positive effect on aggregate output.

錯誤 Wrong選項 D (Option D)

分析：增加本地基建投資屬於擴張性財政政策，會直接增加政府開支 (\(G \uparrow\))。這會進一步推高總需求 (AD 向右移)。財政與貨幣政策雙管齊下，會加強對總產出的影響，而非削弱。

Analysis: Increasing local infrastructure investment is an expansionary fiscal policy that directly increases government expenditure (\(G \uparrow\)). This further pushes up Aggregate Demand (AD shifts right). Combining expansionary fiscal and monetary policies will strengthen the effect on aggregate output, not weaken it.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

  • 解題支點 (The Pivot Point): 題目的核心字眼是「削弱 (weaken)」。中央銀行的政策是「擴張性 (Expansionary)」的，目的是增加 \(Y\)。你要找的選項，必須是一個能產生相反力量（減少 \(Y\)）或阻礙傳導機制的事件。
  • 方向判定:
    • 政策目標：\(AD \uparrow \rightarrow Y \uparrow\)
    • A: 乘數效應變大 $\rightarrow$ 效果加強 (Strengthen)
    • B: 出口 \(X \downarrow \rightarrow AD \downarrow\) $\rightarrow$ 抵消/削弱效果 (Weaken)
    • C: \(SRAS \uparrow \rightarrow Y \uparrow\) $\rightarrow$ 效果加強 (Strengthen)
    • D: 政府開支 \(G \uparrow \rightarrow AD \uparrow\) $\rightarrow$ 效果加強 (Strengthen)

Pivot Point: The core keyword is "weaken". The central bank's policy is expansionary, aiming to increase \(Y\). You must look for an option that creates an opposing force (decreasing \(Y\)) or hinders the transmission mechanism. By evaluating the directional impact of each option on \(AD\) or \(AS\), Option B clearly provides a counteracting force.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

⚠️ 誤解「數碼貨幣」的影響 (Misunderstanding the impact of Digital Money):
很多學生會誤以為數碼貨幣流行代表「不需要央行貨幣」，從而削弱央行政策。事實上，在 DSE 課程框架中，電子支付/數碼貨幣的普及通常意味著公眾對實體現金的交易需求減少，降低了現金流失率 (cash leakage ratio, \(c\))，這會使銀行體系的存款創造能力上升（貨幣乘數變大）。因此，央行的貨幣政策威力反而會被放大。

Many students mistakenly think digital money means "central bank money is no longer needed," thus weakening the policy. In the DSE syllabus, the popularization of electronic payments/digital money usually means the public has less transaction demand for physical cash, lowering the cash leakage ratio (\(c\)). This increases the banking system's deposit creation capacity (larger money multiplier), thereby amplifying the power of the central bank's monetary policy.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

🙋‍♂️ 學生問：為什麼選項 C (增加外勞) 不算是削弱貨幣政策？貨幣政策不是只影響需求面 (AD) 嗎？

Student: Why doesn't Option C (importing foreign workers) count as weakening monetary policy? Doesn't monetary policy only affect Aggregate Demand (AD)?

👨‍🏫 導師解答：好問題！雖然貨幣政策主要推動總需求 (AD) 向右移，但題目問的是對「總產出 (Aggregate Output, \(Y\))」的最終影響。如果增加外勞，短期總供應 (SRAS) 會向右移。當 AD 向右移，同時 SRAS 也向右移時，兩者疊加會令總產出 (\(Y\)) 上升得比單純 AD 移動更多。既然 \(Y\) 上升得更多了，政策對總產出的影響是被「加強」了，而不是被「削弱」。

Tutor's Answer: Great question! While monetary policy primarily shifts Aggregate Demand (AD) to the right, the question specifically asks about the final effect on "aggregate output (\(Y\))". Importing foreign workers shifts Short-Run Aggregate Supply (SRAS) to the right. When AD shifts right and SRAS simultaneously shifts right, the combined effect causes aggregate output (\(Y\)) to increase even more than if only AD moved. Since \(Y\) increases by a larger margin, the policy's effect on aggregate output is "strengthened", not "weakened".

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

💡 秒殺法 (The Elimination Shortcut):
在選擇題中遇到「甚麼會削弱擴張性政策」這類題目，你可以直接為每個選項標註方向符號：
A: 貨幣乘數 \(\uparrow\) (同向助攻)
B: 出口 \(\downarrow\) (反向抵消) 🎯 即刻選定！
C: 總供應 \(\uparrow\) (同向助攻產出)
D: 政府開支 \(\uparrow\) (同向助攻需求)
只有 B 是產生「反方向」拉力的因素，考試時只需 15 秒就能鎖定答案！

For questions asking "what weakens an expansionary policy", simply annotate each option with a directional arrow. Options A, C, and D all provide complementary forces (pushing output up or amplifying the mechanism). Only Option B provides a counteracting force (pulling output down). You can lock in the answer in 15 seconds using this logic check!
