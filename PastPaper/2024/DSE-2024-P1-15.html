HKDSE Economics: Market Intervention Analysis

Topic / 課題： Market Intervention - Quotas / 市場干預：配額

Difficulty / 難度： ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts / 核心概念： Quota (配額), Marginal Benefit (邊際利益), Total Expenditure (總開支), Price Elasticity of Demand (需求價格彈性)

The Question (題目)

假設某經濟的政府對太平洋藍鰭金槍魚實施一項有效的捕撈限制（配額）。如果降低該捕撈限制，最後一單位的太平洋藍鰭金槍魚的邊際利益會 ___________，而消費者對太平洋藍鰭金槍魚的總支出 ___________。
Suppose the government of an economy imposes an effective catch limit (quota) on Pacific bluefin tuna. If the catch limit is lowered, the marginal benefit of the last unit of Pacific bluefin tuna will __________ and consumers’ total expenditure on Pacific bluefin tuna __________.
  • A. 上升 …… 會上升
    A. increase …… will increase
  • B. 下降 …… 會下降
    B. decrease …… will decrease
  • C. 上升 …… 可能下降或上升
    C. increase …… may decrease or increase
  • D. 下降 …… 可能下降或上升
    D. decrease …… may decrease or increase

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 有效配額 (Effective Quota)： 政府設定的數量上限。若配額「有效」，即表示該上限低於市場均衡數量 \(Q_e\)，會造成市場短缺，從而推高價格。
1. Effective Quota: A maximum quantity limit set by the government. If it is "effective", the limit is set below the market equilibrium quantity \(Q_e\), creating a shortage and pushing the market price up.
2. 邊際利益 (Marginal Benefit, MB)： 消費者願意為最後一單位物品支付的最高價格，在圖表上由需求曲線 (Demand Curve) 反映。根據需求定律，數量越少，邊際利益越高。
2. Marginal Benefit (MB): The maximum price consumers are willing to pay for the last unit of a good, reflected by the Demand Curve. Due to the Law of Demand, as quantity decreases, marginal benefit increases.
3. 總開支 (Total Expenditure, TE)： 總開支等於價格乘以交易數量。其變動取決於需求價格彈性 \(E_d\)。
3. Total Expenditure (TE): TE equals price multiplied by quantity transacted. Its change depends on the Price Elasticity of Demand \(E_d\).
$$ TE = P \times Q $$ $$ E_d = \frac{\%\Delta Q_d}{\%\Delta P} $$

Graph: Lowering the quota from \(Q_1\) to \(Q_2\) moves the market along the Demand (MB) curve, increasing the price from \(P_1\) to \(P_2\).

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option)： C

第一部分：邊際利益 (Part 1: Marginal Benefit)

當政府降低捕撈限制（即配額減少），市場上金槍魚的供應量 \(Q\) 會進一步下跌。因為邊際利益（MB）由向下的需求曲線反映，沿着需求曲線向左上方移動，最後一單位的邊際利益 \(MB\) 必然上升。因此，我們可以剔除選項 B 和 D。
When the government lowers the catch limit (quota decreases), the supply of tuna \(Q\) in the market falls further. Since Marginal Benefit (MB) is represented by the downward-sloping demand curve, moving up and to the left along the curve means the marginal benefit of the last unit must increase. Thus, we can eliminate options B and D.

第二部分：總開支 (Part 2: Total Expenditure)

總開支 \(TE = P \times Q\)。隨着配額降低，數量 \(Q\) 下降，而價格 \(P\) 上升。總開支的最終變化取決於金槍魚的需求價格彈性：
  • 如果需求是彈性 (Elastic, \(E_d > 1\))：數量的百分比跌幅大於價格的百分比升幅，總支出會下降。
  • 如果需求是低彈性 (Inelastic, \(E_d < 1\))：價格的百分比升幅大於數量的百分比跌幅，總支出會上升。
由於題目沒有提供太平洋藍鰭金槍魚的需求彈性資料，總支出可能下降或上升。因此，正確答案是 C。
Total Expenditure \(TE = P \times Q\). As the quota is lowered, quantity \(Q\) decreases, and price \(P\) increases. The final change in TE depends on the price elasticity of demand for tuna:
  • If demand is Elastic (\(E_d > 1\)): The % drop in \(Q\) outweighs the % rise in \(P\), so TE decreases.
  • If demand is Inelastic (\(E_d < 1\)): The % rise in \(P\) outweighs the % drop in \(Q\), so TE increases.
Because the question does not provide information on the demand elasticity of Pacific bluefin tuna, total expenditure may decrease or increase. Therefore, the correct answer is C.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)： 本題的關鍵在於辨識「邊際利益 = 需求價格」這個概念。很多學生忘記了 MB 曲線就是 D 曲線。只要知道 \(Q\) 跌，沿著 D 曲線 \(P\) 就會升，第一部分就解決了。
Pivot Point: The key is recognizing the concept that "Marginal Benefit = Demand Price". Many students forget that the MB curve is essentially the D curve. Once you know \(Q\) drops, \(P\) rises along the D curve, solving the first part.
干擾資訊 (Distractors)： 題目故意不提及彈性（Elasticity）。這是一個典型的 DSE 陷阱，考驗學生是否會因為缺乏資訊而「自動腦補」某種彈性（例如認為奢侈品一定是高彈性）。在經濟學科中，除非題目明示，否則不能隨意假設彈性數值。
Distractors: The question deliberately omits elasticity. This is a classic DSE trap testing whether students will make unfounded assumptions (e.g., assuming a luxury good is definitely elastic). In Economics, unless stated, you cannot casually assume an elasticity value.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

混淆邊際利益 (Marginal Benefit) 與 總利益 (Total Benefit)：
當配額減少時，消費總量下跌，消費者獲得的總利益 (Total Benefit) 必然下降。但題目問的是邊際利益 (Marginal Benefit)！因為物品變得更稀缺，消費者對「剩下那最後一條魚」的估值反而會提高。
Confusing Marginal Benefit with Total Benefit:
When the quota decreases, the total quantity consumed falls, so the consumers' Total Benefit must decrease. However, the question asks for Marginal Benefit! Because the good becomes scarcer, consumers value "that last remaining fish" even more, so MB increases.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

問：為什麼我們不需要考慮金槍魚的供應彈性 (Elasticity of Supply)？
Q: Why don't we need to consider the elasticity of supply for tuna?
答：因為這是一個「有效配額」。在有效配額下，供應量被政府硬性規定在一條垂直線上。不管原本的供應曲線彈性如何，市場交易量已經被鎖定在配額數量。價格完全由該數量對應的需求曲線決定。
A: Because this is an "effective quota". Under an effective quota, the quantity supplied is rigidly fixed by the government at a vertical line. Regardless of the original supply curve's elasticity, the transacted quantity is locked at the quota limit. The price is determined entirely by the demand curve at that fixed quantity.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧： 在 DSE MC 題中，只要看到題目同時問及「總收入/總開支 (Total Revenue / Total Expenditure)」，而整條題目完全沒有提供、暗示或計算出需求彈性（如沒有提到「價格大幅上升但數量微跌」等字眼），那麼關於總開支的答案有 90% 以上機率是「不確定」或「可能升或跌」。這能讓你瞬間將選項縮窄至 C 和 D！
Speed Trick: In DSE MC questions, whenever you see a question asking about "Total Revenue / Total Expenditure", and the prompt provides absolutely zero information or hints about demand elasticity, there is a 90%+ chance that the answer for TE is "uncertain" or "may increase or decrease". This allows you to instantly narrow down the choices to C and D!
