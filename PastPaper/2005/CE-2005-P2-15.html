📝 HKDSE Economics Tutorial

Topic / 課題 Factors of Production (生產要素) - Land (土地)

Difficulty / 難度 ★★☆☆☆ (2/5)

Key Concepts / 核心概念 Land (土地), Capital (資本), Gifts of Nature (大自然恩賜), Human Effort (人類努力)

📍 The Question (題目)

下列哪一項在經濟學上被歸類為土地？

Which of the following is classified as land in economics?

  • A. 加了化學肥料的泥土

    A. soil with chemical fertilizers added to it

  • B. 植林區裏的樹木

    B. trees in a plantation area

  • C. 蘊藏於地下的煤礦

    C. underground coal reserves

  • D. 經開墾的土地

    D. a piece of cultivated land

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

在經濟學中，我們將生產要素 (Factors of Production) 分為四大類：勞力、土地、資本和企業家職能。要準確區分「土地」和「資本」，關鍵在於是否涉及人類的努力 (Human Effort)。

In economics, we classify factors of production into four categories: labor, land, capital, and entrepreneurship. To accurately distinguish between "Land" and "Capital", the key lies in whether human effort is involved.

  • 土地 (Land)： 指所有未經人類努力而產生的大自然恩賜 (Gifts of nature)。例子：陽光、海水、地下礦物、原始森林。

    Land: Refers to all gifts of nature that are produced without human effort. Examples: sunlight, seawater, underground minerals, virgin forests.

  • 資本 (Capital)： 指所有由人類製造出來，並用於協助生產的人造資源 (Man-made resources)。例子：機器、工具、已開發的農地。

    Capital: Refers to all man-made resources used to assist in further production. Examples: machines, tools, cultivated farmland.

在宏觀經濟學的生產函數中，總產量 $Q$ 往往由多種生產要素決定：

In the macroeconomic production function, total output $Q$ is determined by various factors of production:

$$Q = f(L, K, N, E)$$

其中 $L$ 是勞力 (Labor)，$K$ 是資本 (Capital)，$N$ 是土地/自然資源 (Land/Natural Resources)，$E$ 是企業家職能 (Entrepreneurship)。

Where $L$ is Labor, $K$ is Capital, $N$ is Land/Natural Resources, and $E$ is Entrepreneurship.

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是：選項 C (Option C)

The correct answer is: Option C

選項 A 分析 (錯誤)：「加了化學肥料的泥土」涉及了人類的加工（加入肥料以提升土壤肥力）。因為有人類努力的介入，它被歸類為資本（人造資源）。

Option A Analysis (Incorrect): "Soil with chemical fertilizers added to it" involves human processing (adding fertilizers to improve fertility). Because human effort is involved, it is classified as capital (a man-made resource).

選項 B 分析 (錯誤)：「植林區裏的樹木」是由人類為了商業或環保目的而刻意種植的。既然是人工種植，它就屬於資本，而不是純粹的大自然恩賜。

Option B Analysis (Incorrect): "Trees in a plantation area" are deliberately planted by humans for commercial or environmental purposes. Since they are artificially planted, they are capital, not pure gifts of nature.

選項 C 分析 (正確)：「蘊藏於地下的煤礦」是天然存在的資源，在尚未被人類開採或加工之前，它完全是大自然的產物，不包含任何人類努力。因此，它是標準的「土地」。

Option C Analysis (Correct): "Underground coal reserves" are naturally occurring resources. Before being extracted or processed by humans, they are purely gifts of nature containing no human effort. Therefore, they perfectly fit the definition of "Land".

選項 D 分析 (錯誤)：「經開墾的土地」意味著人類已經投入了勞力去除雜草、翻土等。這些改良使它成為了協助生產的資本。

Option D Analysis (Incorrect): "A piece of cultivated land" implies that humans have invested labor to clear weeds, plough the soil, etc. These improvements make it capital used to assist production.

🔍 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)： 面對這類題目，你只需要問自己一個問題：「這件物品有沒有經過人類的加工或努力？」

Pivot Point: When facing this type of question, you only need to ask yourself one question: "Has this item undergone human processing or effort?"

題目中充滿了干擾字眼 (Distractors)。選項 A、B、D 都有動詞或形容詞暗示了人類活動：「加了 (added)」、「植林 (plantation)」、「經開墾 (cultivated)」。只要看到這些字眼，就可以立刻排除它們屬於「土地」的可能性。

The question is full of distractors. Options A, B, and D all contain verbs or adjectives implying human activity: "added", "plantation", "cultivated". As soon as you spot these words, you can immediately rule out the possibility of them being "Land".

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

日常用語 vs 經濟學定義： 這是最常見的陷阱！在日常生活中，我們習慣把所有泥土和農田都叫作「土地」。但在經濟學中，「土地」是一個嚴格的專有名詞，僅限於大自然恩賜。一旦農民對泥土施肥或翻土，那塊泥土在經濟學上就變成了「資本」。

Daily Language vs. Economic Definition: This is the most common trap! In everyday life, we are used to calling all soil and farmland "land". But in economics, "Land" is a strict technical term limited ONLY to gifts of nature. Once a farmer fertilizes or ploughs the soil, that soil economically transforms into "Capital".

💬 Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

學生問： 如果選項 C 的煤礦已經被工人挖出來，並放在工廠裡準備發電，它還算是「土地」嗎？

Student Asks: If the coal in Option C has already been extracted by workers and placed in a factory for power generation, is it still considered "Land"?

老師答： 不算！當煤礦被挖掘出來，這個過程已經投入了工人的「勞力」(Labor) 以及採礦機器的「資本」(Capital)。因此，已經開採出來的煤礦成為了原料 (Raw materials)，在經濟學上會被歸類為「資本」(Capital)。題目特別強調「蘊藏於地下 (underground)」，就是要確保它處於未經人類干預的自然狀態。

Teacher Answers: No! When the coal is extracted, the process involves the workers' "Labor" and mining machinery's "Capital". Therefore, extracted coal becomes a raw material, which is classified as "Capital" in economics. The question specifically emphasized "underground" to ensure it remains in its natural state, untouched by human intervention.

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

「找動作」排除法： 在 HKDSE 考試中，分辨土地和資本的秒殺技巧是尋找「人類動作」。

"Action Verb" Elimination Method: In the HKDSE exam, the instant-kill technique for distinguishing between land and capital is to look for "human actions".

  • 看到「人造」、「種植」、「開墾」、「加了」、「飼養」 $\rightarrow$ 秒選 資本 (Capital)。
    Spot "man-made", "planted", "cultivated", "added", "reared" $\rightarrow$ Immediately choose Capital.
  • 看到「野生」、「天然」、「地下」、「海洋中」 $\rightarrow$ 秒選 土地 (Land)。
    Spot "wild", "natural", "underground", "in the ocean" $\rightarrow$ Immediately choose Land.
