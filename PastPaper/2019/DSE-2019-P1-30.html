課題 (Topic): 貨幣的功能 (Functions of Money)
Topic: Functions of Money
難度 (Difficulty): ★★★☆☆
核心概念 (Key Concepts): 交易媒介、記帳單位、價值儲藏
Key Concepts: Medium of Exchange, Unit of Account, Store of Value

2. 題目 (The Question)

參考以下的假設個案：
自2018年初美國公佈一系列針對T國的貿易制裁措施後，T國的貨幣「T元」貶值了40%。結果，T國的一些店舖以美元而非T元訂價。黑市的店舖拒絕接受T元。一些市民購買外幣以保障他們的資產。

T元的哪些貨幣功能被削弱了？
(1) 交易媒介
(2) 記帳單位
(3) 價值儲藏
  • 只有 (1)、(2)
  • 只有 (1)、(3)
  • 只有 (2)、(3)
  • (1)、(2) 及 (3)
Refer to the following hypothetical case:
The currency of Country T, T-Dollar, has depreciated by 40 percent from the beginning of 2018 after the US announced a series of trade sanctions against Country T. As a result, some shops in Country T quoted prices in US Dollar instead of T-Dollar. Shops in black markets refused to accept T-Dollar. Some citizens bought foreign currencies to safeguard their assets.

Which functions of money of the T-Dollar were weakened?
(1) medium of exchange
(2) unit of account
(3) store of value
  • (1) and (2) only
  • (1) and (3) only
  • (2) and (3) only
  • (1), (2) and (3)

3. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

貨幣的購買力 (Purchasing Power of Money):
當物價水平 \( P \) 上升，或者貨幣出現嚴重貶值時，貨幣的購買力就會下降。我們可以用以下公式表示貨幣的價值：
Purchasing Power of Money:
When the price level \( P \) increases, or the currency depreciates significantly, the purchasing power of money decreases. We can represent the value of money with the following formula:
$$ Value\ of\ Money\ (V) = \frac{1}{General\ Price\ Level\ (P)} $$
貨幣的三大主要功能 (Three Main Functions of Money):
Three Main Functions of Money:
  • 交易媒介 (Medium of Exchange): 貨幣被普遍接受作為購買財貨和服務的支付工具。如果人們拒絕接受某種貨幣進行交易，這項功能即被削弱。
    Medium of Exchange: Money is generally accepted as a payment tool for buying goods and services. If people refuse to accept a currency for transactions, this function is weakened.
  • 記帳單位 (Unit of Account): 貨幣作為衡量和標示物品價值的標準。當商店不再使用該貨幣來標示價格（訂價）時，這項功能便失效。
    Unit of Account: Money serves as a standard to measure and quote the value of items. When shops stop using the currency to quote prices, this function is impaired.
  • 價值儲藏 (Store of Value): 貨幣可以在未來保持其購買力。如果貨幣急速貶值，市民為了保障資產而轉向持有外幣或實物資產，這代表其儲藏價值的功能已被削弱。
    Store of Value: Money can retain its purchasing power for the future. If a currency depreciates rapidly and citizens switch to holding foreign currencies or physical assets to protect their wealth, its function as a store of value is weakened.

4. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 / Correct Option: D
分析選項 (1) 交易媒介：
題目提到「黑市的店舖拒絕接受T元」。這直接指出T元已不再被普遍接受作為交易的支付工具，因此其「交易媒介」的功能被削弱了。
Analysis of (1) Medium of Exchange:
The question states that "Shops in black markets refused to accept T-Dollar." This directly indicates that the T-Dollar is no longer generally accepted as a tool of payment for transactions. Therefore, its "medium of exchange" function is weakened.
分析選項 (2) 記帳單位：
題目提到「店舖以美元而非T元訂價」。標示價格（訂價）正是記帳單位的定義。既然商店改用美元標價，T元的「記帳單位」功能便被削弱了。
Analysis of (2) Unit of Account:
The question states that shops "quoted prices in US Dollar instead of T-Dollar." Quoting prices is the exact definition of a unit of account. Since shops switched to pricing in USD, the T-Dollar's "unit of account" function is weakened.
分析選項 (3) 價值儲藏：
題目提到T元貶值了40%，且「市民購買外幣以保障他們的資產」。這意味著持有T元會導致財富縮水，它無法有效將購買力保留到未來，因此「價值儲藏」功能被削弱了。
Analysis of (3) Store of Value:
The text notes a 40% depreciation and that "citizens bought foreign currencies to safeguard their assets." This means holding T-Dollars leads to a loss of wealth; it cannot effectively retain purchasing power for the future. Thus, the "store of value" function is weakened.
結論： (1)、(2) 和 (3) 都正確，故選 D。
Conclusion: (1), (2), and (3) are all correct. Therefore, the answer is D.

5. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解答這類題目的樞紐點 (Pivot Point) 在於「關鍵字配對 (Keyword Matching)」。考評局喜歡在情境題中加入具體行為，你必須將這些「行為」與經濟學中的「定義」精確連繫起來。
  • 行為：「拒絕接受 (Refused to accept)」 ➡️ 對應功能： 交易媒介 (Medium of exchange)
  • 行為：「訂價 / 標價 (Quoted prices)」 ➡️ 對應功能： 記帳單位 (Unit of account)
  • 行為：「保障資產 / 貨幣貶值 (Safeguard assets / Depreciated)」 ➡️ 對應功能： 價值儲藏 (Store of value)
題目中沒有任何干擾資訊 (Distractors)，每一句話都精準對應了一個貨幣功能。
The Pivot Point for solving this type of question is "Keyword Matching". The HKEAA loves inserting specific behaviors into scenario-based questions. You must explicitly link these "behaviors" to their economic "definitions".
  • Behavior: "Refused to accept" ➡️ Corresponding Function: Medium of exchange
  • Behavior: "Quoted prices" ➡️ Corresponding Function: Unit of account
  • Behavior: "Safeguard assets / Depreciated" ➡️ Corresponding Function: Store of value
There is no distractor information in this question; every sentence strictly corresponds to one function of money.

6. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱：混淆「交易媒介」與「記帳單位」
同學常以為「用美金標價 (訂價)」就等同於「用美金交易」，從而把兩者混為一談。請記住：
- 標示價格 (Price tag) 只涉及記帳單位。
- 實際付錢的動作 (Handing over cash) 才涉及交易媒介。
本題中，考評局非常清晰地將兩者分開描述（「以美元訂價」對應 (2)；「黑市拒絕接受T元」對應 (1)），避免了含糊不清。
Trap: Confusing "Medium of Exchange" with "Unit of Account"
Students often assume that "pricing in USD" automatically means "trading in USD", confusing the two functions. Remember:
- Putting a price tag only involves the Unit of Account.
- The actual act of paying (handing over cash) involves the Medium of Exchange.
In this question, the examiner clearly separated the two ("quoted prices" points to (2); "refused to accept" points to (1)) to avoid ambiguity.

7. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q1: 只要貨幣貶值，它的三個功能就一定會同時被削弱嗎？
A: 不一定。輕微的貶值（例如每年 2% 的通脹）只會輕微影響「價值儲藏」的功能，人們仍然會用該貨幣標價和買賣。只有在嚴重貶值（如惡性通脹或劇烈貶值40%）時，市民才會徹底對貨幣失去信心，導致「記帳單位」和「交易媒介」功能相繼崩潰。本題因為列出了具體行為（改標價、拒絕接受），我們才能確定三個功能都受損。
Q1: Does a currency depreciation automatically weaken all three functions at the same time?
A: Not necessarily. A mild depreciation (like a 2% annual inflation) mainly affects the "store of value" function slightly; people will still use the currency to quote prices and trade. It is only during severe depreciation (like hyperinflation or a drastic 40% drop) that public confidence collapses, leading to the failure of the "unit of account" and "medium of exchange" functions. In this question, we know all three are weakened because the text explicitly lists the behaviors (changing price tags, refusing acceptance).

8. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

速解法：掃描動詞 (Verb Scanning)
在DSE考試中遇到長篇幅的個案題，無需逐字閱讀。直接「掃描」關鍵動詞：
1. 看到「訂價 (quote)」 ➔ 勾選 (2) 記帳單位。
2. 看到「接受/拒絕接受 (accept/refuse)」 ➔ 勾選 (1) 交易媒介。
3. 看到「保障資產/儲蓄 (safeguard/save)」 ➔ 勾選 (3) 價值儲藏。
一旦確認三個動詞都出現了，直接選 D，節省時間。
Shortcut: Verb Scanning
When facing long scenario-based questions in the DSE, do not read word-for-word immediately. Scan for key verbs:
1. See "quote (prices)" ➔ Tick (2) Unit of Account.
2. See "accept/refuse" ➔ Tick (1) Medium of Exchange.
3. See "safeguard (assets) / save" ➔ Tick (3) Store of Value.
Once you confirm all three verbs are present, select option D immediately to save time.
