主題 (Topic): 宏觀經濟 - 香港金融管理局的功能 (Macroeconomics - Functions of the HKMA)

難度 (Difficulty): ⭐⭐ (2/5)

關鍵字 (Key Concepts): HKMA (金管局) Exchange Fund (外匯基金) Monetary Policy (貨幣政策)

📝 題目 (The Question)

下列哪項／些是香港金融管理局的功能？

(1) 管理外匯基金
(2) 監察財政政策的實施
(3) 決定香港的市場利率

A. 只有 (1)
B. 只有 (2)
C. 只有 (3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

Which of the following is/are the function(s) of the Hong Kong Monetary Authority?

(1) Managing the Exchange Fund
(2) Supervising the implementation of fiscal policy
(3) Determining Hong Kong’s market interest rate

A. (1) only
B. (2) only
C. (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

🧠 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

香港金融管理局 (HKMA) 的四大主要職能：

  1. 維持貨幣穩定 (Maintaining Currency Stability): 透過聯繫匯率制度支持港元。
  2. 促進金融體系穩定 (Promoting Financial System Stability): 監管銀行業務。
  3. 管理外匯基金 (Managing the Exchange Fund): 投資及管理香港的外匯儲備。
  4. 發展金融基建 (Developing Financial Infrastructure): 維護支付及結算系統。

Four main functions of the HKMA:

  1. Maintaining Currency Stability: Supporting the HKD through the Linked Exchange Rate System.
  2. Promoting Financial System Stability: Regulating banking operations.
  3. Managing the Exchange Fund: Investing and managing Hong Kong's foreign exchange reserves.
  4. Developing Financial Infrastructure: Maintaining payment and settlement systems.

財政政策 vs 貨幣政策 (Fiscal vs Monetary Policy)

財政政策 (Fiscal Policy): 涉及政府開支 \(G\) 和稅收 \(T\)，由財政司司長負責。
貨幣政策 (Monetary Policy): 涉及貨幣供應 \(M_s\) 和利率 \(r\)，由金管局負責。

Fiscal Policy: Involves government spending \(G\) and taxation \(T\), managed by the Financial Secretary.
Monetary Policy: Involves money supply \(M_s\) and interest rates \(r\), managed by the HKMA.

✅ 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): A

(1) 管理外匯基金 (Managing the Exchange Fund) - 正確 (True)

外匯基金由金管局負責日常管理，主要目的是為了維持香港的貨幣及金融體系穩定。

The Exchange Fund is managed daily by the HKMA. Its primary purpose is to maintain the stability of Hong Kong's monetary and financial systems.

(2) 監察財政政策的實施 (Supervising the implementation of fiscal policy) - 錯誤 (False)

財政政策（如稅收和政府開支）是由香港政府的財政司司長及庫務局負責，而非金管局（金管局負責貨幣政策）。

Fiscal policy (such as taxation and government spending) is the responsibility of the Financial Secretary and the Treasury Bureau of the HK Government, not the HKMA (which handles monetary policy).

(3) 決定香港的市場利率 (Determining Hong Kong’s market interest rate) - 錯誤 (False)

香港的市場利率（如銀行同業拆息 HIBOR 或最優惠利率 Prime Rate）是由市場資金供求決定的。雖然金管局會設定「基本利率」(Base Rate)，但它不能直接「決定」市場上的零售利率。

Hong Kong's market interest rates (like HIBOR or the Prime Rate) are determined by the market supply and demand for funds. Although the HKMA sets the "Base Rate", it does not directly "determine" retail market interest rates.

🧩 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

這類題目的關鍵點 (Pivot Point) 在於區分「政府部門的職責」。

  • 看到「財政政策」(Fiscal Policy) 👉 聯想到政府/財政司 (Government/Financial Secretary)。
  • 看到「市場利率」(Market Interest Rate) 👉 聯想到市場供求 (Market Supply & Demand)，特別是在聯繫匯率制度下，香港利率很大程度受美國息率影響，而非金管局單方面決定。

The Pivot Point of this question is distinguishing the "responsibilities of government departments".

  • When you see "Fiscal Policy" 👉 Associate it with the Government/Financial Secretary.
  • When you see "Market Interest Rate" 👉 Associate it with Market Supply & Demand. Especially under the Linked Exchange Rate System, HK interest rates are largely influenced by US interest rates, not unilaterally determined by the HKMA.

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱：混淆「基本利率」與「市場利率」
很多學生知道金管局會調整貼現窗基本利率 (Base Rate)，就誤以為金管局決定了所有市場利率。記住，商業銀行有權自行決定其存貸利率。

Trap: Confusing "Base Rate" with "Market Interest Rate"
Many students know the HKMA adjusts the Discount Window Base Rate, mistakenly thinking the HKMA determines all market rates. Remember, commercial banks have the right to set their own deposit and lending rates.

🙋 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 既然香港實行聯繫匯率，金管局不是要跟隨美國加息嗎？為什麼說它不決定利率？

A: 金管局確實會跟隨美國聯儲局調整「基本利率」，但這只影響銀行向金管局借錢的成本。市民在銀行遇到的「市場利率」（如按揭利率），是由個別商業銀行根據自身資金充裕程度和市場競爭來決定的。

Q: Since HK has a Linked Exchange Rate, doesn't the HKMA have to follow US rate hikes? Why say it doesn't determine interest rates?

A: The HKMA does follow the US Fed in adjusting the "Base Rate", but this only affects the cost for banks borrowing from the HKMA. The "market interest rates" (like mortgage rates) faced by the public are determined by individual commercial banks based on their own liquidity and market competition.

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

消去法 (Elimination Method):
在 DSE 經濟科中，只要你確定「財政政策」絕對不屬於金管局（它是財政司司長的工作），你就可以立即判定 (2) 是錯的。這樣一來，選項 B 和 D 就被淘汰了。接著，只要你知道市場利率由市場決定，(3) 也是錯的，淘汰 C。答案秒選 A！

Elimination Method:
In DSE Economics, once you are certain that "fiscal policy" is definitely not the HKMA's job (it's the Financial Secretary's), you can immediately determine (2) is wrong. This eliminates options B and D. Next, knowing that market rates are determined by the market makes (3) wrong, eliminating C. The answer is instantly A!
