📊 HKDSE Economics Topic Analysis

Topic (課題): 存款創造與貨幣供應 (Deposit Creation and Money Supply)

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)

Key Concepts (關鍵概念):
貨幣供應 (Money Supply), 貨幣基礎 (Monetary Base), 法定儲備率 (Required Reserve Ratio), 存款創造 (Deposit Creation)

📝 The Question (題目)

下表顯示甲國銀行系統的資產負債表。甲國的貨幣供應是$2 800，而所有銀行並不持有超額儲備。

The following table shows the balance sheet of Country A’s banking system. The money supply in Country A is $2 800 and all banks do not hold excess reserves.

資產 (Assets) $ 負債 (Liabilities) $
儲備 (Reserves) 500 存款 (Deposits) 2 500
貸款 (Loans) 2 000

假設公眾將$200現金存入該銀行系統。在存款創造的過程完成後，下列哪項有關該銀行系統的陳述是正確的？

Suppose the public deposits $200 cash into the banking system. Which of the following statements about the banking system is correct after the deposit creation process is completed?

  • A. 存款會增加$3 500。 (The deposits would increase by $3 500.)
  • B. 貨幣供應會增加$1 000。 (The money supply would increase by $1 000.)
  • C. 貨幣基礎會增加 $200。 (The monetary base would increase by $200.)
  • D. 貸款會增加$800。 (The loans will increase by $800.)

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解決這類題目，我們必須熟記宏觀經濟學中關於貨幣定義的核心公式。由於銀行沒有持有超額儲備 (No Excess Reserves)，代表實際儲備等於法定儲備，銀行已將存款創造發揮到最大極限。

To solve this type of question, we must memorize the core formulas regarding the definition of money in macroeconomics. Since banks hold no excess reserves, actual reserves equal required reserves, meaning banks have maximized the deposit creation process.

1. 法定儲備率 (Required Reserve Ratio, $rrr$):
$$rrr = \frac{\text{Reserves (R)}}{\text{Deposits (D)}}$$
2. 貨幣供應 (Money Supply, $Ms$):
$$Ms = C_p + D$$ ($C_p$ = 公眾持有的現金 Cash in public hands)
3. 貨幣基礎 (Monetary Base, $Mb$):
$$Mb = C_p + R$$
4. 最大存款 / 存款乘數 (Max Deposits / Banking Multiplier, $m$):
$$D_{max} = R \times \frac{1}{rrr}$$

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: D

Step 0: 尋找初始狀態 (Find the initial state)

首先，找出法例規定的儲備率 ($rrr$) 及公眾手上的現金 ($C_p$)。
$rrr = \frac{500}{2500} = 0.2$ (即 20%)
因為 $Ms = C_p + D$，所以 $2800 = C_p + 2500 \implies C_p = 300$。

First, find the required reserve ratio ($rrr$) and cash in public hands ($C_p$).
$rrr = \frac{500}{2500} = 0.2$ (or 20%).
Since $Ms = C_p + D$, we have $2800 = C_p + 2500 \implies C_p = 300$.

Option A: 存款 (Deposits) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

公眾存入$200現金，銀行新儲備 $R_{new} = 500 + 200 = 700$。
最大新存款 $D_{new} = \frac{700}{0.2} = 3500$。
存款的變化 $\Delta D = 3500 - 2500 = 1000$。
存款是增加至$3 500，而不是增加$3 500。

The public deposits $200 cash, so new reserves $R_{new} = 500 + 200 = 700$.
Max new deposits $D_{new} = \frac{700}{0.2} = 3500$.
The change in deposits $\Delta D = 3500 - 2500 = 1000$.
Deposits increase to $3 500, not by $3 500. This is a classic trap!

Option B: 貨幣供應 (Money Supply) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

存入現金後，公眾手上的現金減少了$200，新 $C_{p(new)} = 300 - 200 = 100$。
新貨幣供應 $Ms_{new} = C_{p(new)} + D_{new} = 100 + 3500 = 3600$。
貨幣供應的變化 $\Delta Ms = 3600 - 2800 = 800$。

After depositing cash, cash in public hands decreases by $200. New $C_{p(new)} = 300 - 200 = 100$.
New Money Supply $Ms_{new} = C_{p(new)} + D_{new} = 100 + 3500 = 3600$.
Change in Money Supply $\Delta Ms = 3600 - 2800 = 800$.

Option C: 貨幣基礎 (Monetary Base) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

貨幣基礎 $Mb = C_p + R$。
初始 $Mb = 300 + 500 = 800$。新的 $Mb = 100 + 700 = 800$。
貨幣基礎沒有改變 (改變為 $0)。

Monetary Base $Mb = C_p + R$.
Initial $Mb = 300 + 500 = 800$. New $Mb = 100 + 700 = 800$.
The monetary base remains unchanged (change is $0).

Option D: 貸款 (Loans) - 正確 (Correct)

資產負債表必須平衡。新存款為 $3500，新儲備為 $700。
新貸款 $L_{new} = D_{new} - R_{new} = 3500 - 700 = 2800$。
貸款的變化 $\Delta L = 2800 - 2000 = 800$。完全正確！

The balance sheet must balance. New deposits are $3500, new reserves are $700.
New Loans $L_{new} = D_{new} - R_{new} = 3500 - 700 = 2800$.
Change in Loans $\Delta L = 2800 - 2000 = 800$. This is absolutely correct!

🔍 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

突破點 (Pivot Point)：
解決這題的關鍵是利用題目的「隱藏資訊」。題目給了總貨幣供應 ($2800) 和存款 ($2500)，你必須第一時間反應過來：這兩個數字的差額就是「公眾手持現金」(Cash in public hands, $C_p$)。沒有算出 $C_p = 300$，你就無法準確計算之後的變化。

Pivot Point:
The key to cracking this question lies in utilizing the "hidden information". The question provides the total money supply ($2800) and deposits ($2500). You must immediately realize that the difference between these two numbers is the "Cash in public hands" ($C_p$). Without calculating $C_p = 300$, you cannot accurately determine the subsequent changes.

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 陷阱 1：文字遊戲 (增加 vs 增加至)
    考生很容易算出新存款為 $3500，然後直接選 A。請仔細閱讀：選項 A 說的是「增加」(increase by)，而不是「增加至」(increase to)。

    Trap 1: Word Play (Increase by vs. Increase to)
    Students often calculate the new deposits as $3500 and immediately choose Option A. Read carefully: Option A says "increase BY", not "increase TO".

  • 陷阱 2：忘記扣減公眾現金
    在計算新貨幣供應時，很多學生只加上了新創造的存款，卻忘記市民將錢存入銀行後，公眾手上的現金 ($C_p$) 其實減少了。

    Trap 2: Forgetting to deduct public cash
    When calculating the new money supply, many students simply add the newly created deposits, forgetting that when citizens deposit money, cash in public hands ($C_p$) actually decreases.

🙋 Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼市民存入現金，貨幣基礎 (Monetary Base) 完全沒有改變？

Q: Why does the Monetary Base remain completely unchanged when citizens deposit cash?

A: 記住公式 $Mb = C_p + R$。貨幣基礎是由「公眾手上的現金」和「銀行系統的儲備」組成的。當市民把 $200 存入銀行，這筆錢只是從市民的口袋 ($C_p$ 減少 $200) 轉移到了銀行的金庫 ($R$ 增加 $200)。左手交右手，整體的總和完全沒有變！

A: Remember the formula $Mb = C_p + R$. The monetary base consists of "cash in public hands" and "reserves in the banking system". When citizens deposit $200 into the bank, the money simply moves from people's pockets ($C_p$ decreases by $200) to the bank's vault ($R$ increases by $200). It's a transfer within the system, so the total sum remains exactly the same!

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

在選擇題 (MCQ) 中，時間就是分數。這裡有一些「秒殺」技巧：

In Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ), time is points. Here are some "one-shot" shortcuts:

  1. 秒殺 C： 只要沒有中央銀行的干預 (例如買賣債券)，單純的公眾存款/提款行為絕對不會改變貨幣基礎。一眼就可以剔除 C！

    Insta-kill C: As long as there is no central bank intervention (like buying/selling bonds), simple public deposit/withdrawal behaviors NEVER change the monetary base. Eliminate C at a glance!

  2. 公式速算貸款： $\Delta L = \Delta D - \Delta R$。
    我們知道 $\Delta R = +200$，並且 $\Delta D = +200 \times \frac{1}{0.2} = +1000$。
    所以 $\Delta L = 1000 - 200 = 800$。直接得出答案 D，無需計算龐大的最終數值！

    Formula shortcut for Loans: $\Delta L = \Delta D - \Delta R$.
    We know $\Delta R = +200$, and $\Delta D = +200 \times \frac{1}{0.2} = +1000$.
    Therefore, $\Delta L = 1000 - 200 = 800$. We arrive at D directly without calculating the large final values!
