📚 Topic (課題): Property Rights / Market Intervention (私有產權 / 市場干預)

⭐ Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆ (3/5)

🔑 Key Concepts (核心概念): Right to Use Right to Receive Income Right to Transfer

The Question (題目)

參考以下解決房屋問題的建議：
政府應該興建一種新類型的房屋「居住易」，其業主能以一受管制的租金出租其單位。若業主想出售其單位，他們只能將單位售回政府。

與擁有一個私人樓宇單位相比，擁有一個「居住易」單位的業主的 ___________會被削弱了。
(1) 使用權
(2) 獲取收入權
(3) 轉讓權

A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

Refer to the following suggestion for tackling the housing problem.
The government should build a new type of housing, called Easy Living, for which the owners can lease their flats at a controlled rent. If the owners want to sell their flats, they can only sell them back to the government.

Compared with owning a private housing unit, ___________ will be weakened for those owning an Easy Living housing unit.
(1) the right to use
(2) the right to receive income
(3) the right to transfer

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

Concept Review (概念重溫)

私有產權 (Private Property Rights) 包含三個不可或缺的部分。如果任何一項受到政府法例限制，該產權便會被視為「被削弱」 (weakened)。

Private Property Rights consist of three indispensable components. If any of these are restricted by government regulations, the property right is considered "weakened".

  • 使用權 (Right to Use): 業主擁有決定如何使用該資產的獨家權利（例如：自住、空置或租出）。
    The exclusive right to decide how the asset is used (e.g., self-occupation, leave vacant, or lease).
  • 獲取收入權 (Right to Receive Income): 業主擁有從資產中獲取金錢回報的獨家權利。在房屋租賃市場中，總收入 $TR$ 的計算如下：
    $$TR = P \times Q$$
    其中 $P$ 是租金價格，$Q$ 是出租單位數量。如果政府實施租金管制限制了 $P$，業主的總收入就會受損。
    The exclusive right to earn monetary returns from the asset. In the housing rental market, total revenue $TR$ is calculated as above, where $P$ is the rental price and $Q$ is the quantity. If the government imposes rent controls restricting $P$, the owner's income is compromised.
  • 轉讓權 (Right to Transfer): 業主擁有在市場上自由買賣或轉讓資產給任何人的權利。
    The right to freely buy, sell, or transfer the asset to anyone in the market.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 / Correct Option: C. 只有 (2)、(3) / (2) and (3) only

(1) 使用權 / Right to use: 沒有被削弱 (Not weakened)
題目指出業主「能以一受管制的租金出租其單位」。這代表業主依然可以選擇將單位租出，或者選擇自住。他們決定如何使用單位的權利並沒有被剝奪。
The question states owners "can lease their flats". This means owners still have the choice to lease the unit out or live in it themselves. Their right to decide how to use the flat is not taken away.

(2) 獲取收入權 / Right to receive income: 被削弱了 (Weakened)
題目提到租金是「受管制的 (controlled rent)」。與私人樓宇可以自由按照市場最高價格定租金相比，業主無法賺取自由市場下的最高潛在收入，因此獲取收入權受損。
The text mentions a "controlled rent". Compared to private housing where rent is set freely at market rates, owners cannot earn the maximum potential income the free market would offer. Thus, the right to receive income is impaired.

(3) 轉讓權 / Right to transfer: 被削弱了 (Weakened)
題目表明「只能將單位售回政府 (only sell them back to the government)」。業主失去了在自由市場上尋找買家的權利，交易對象受到嚴格限制，因此轉讓權被大幅削弱。
The text specifies they can "only sell them back to the government". Owners lose the right to find buyers in the open market, and their trading partners are strictly limited. Therefore, the right to transfer is significantly weakened.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解答這類產權題目的關鍵在於「尋找關鍵字 (Identifying Pivot Points)」：

The key to solving property rights questions is identifying the "Pivot Points" (keywords):

  • 關鍵字 1: "受管制的租金 (controlled rent)" $\rightarrow$ 直接指向金錢回報受限 $\rightarrow$ (2) 獲取收入權受影響。
  • 關鍵字 2: "只能...售回 (only sell... back)" $\rightarrow$ 直接指向買賣自由受限 $\rightarrow$ (3) 轉讓權受影響。
  • 干擾資訊 (Distractor): 提到「出租 (lease)」一詞可能會讓學生誤以為使用權受影響，但實際上「能夠出租」正正證明了他們仍保有一定的使用權。

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ 混淆「租金管制」與「使用權」 (Confusing Rent Control with Right to Use)
很多學生會認為「政府干預了租賃，所以使用權被削弱」。請記住：限制你賺多少錢是削弱「獲取收入權」；限制你能不能租給別人/如何裝修才是削弱「使用權」。

Many students think "the government intervened in leasing, so the right to use is weakened." Remember: restricting how much money you make weakens the right to receive income; restricting whether you can lease it out / how you renovate it weakens the right to use.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果題目改成「業主必須自住，不可出租」，答案會有甚麼不同？
Q: If the question changed to "owners must occupy the flat and cannot lease it out", how would the answer change?

A: 這樣的話，(1) 使用權 和 (2) 獲取收入權 都會被削弱！因為業主失去了選擇用途的權利（不能出租），同時也完全失去了從租賃中獲取收入的途徑。
A: In that case, BOTH (1) right to use and (2) right to receive income would be weakened! The owner loses the choice of use (cannot lease) and completely loses the avenue to generate rental income.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

對於 DSE 經濟科的多項選擇題，善用「配對排除法 (Matching & Elimination)」：
當你讀到「售回政府 (sell back to government)」時，你非常肯定這影響了「轉讓權 (3)」。
因此，你可以立刻排除沒有 (3) 的選項，即 選項 A。
接著讀到「受管制的租金 (controlled rent)」，肯定影響「收入 (2)」。
此時只剩下 選項 C 和 D。最後確認「使用權」沒有被明確限制，即可鎖定 C。

For DSE Econ MCQs, use Matching & Elimination. When you read "sell back to government", you are 100% sure "transfer right (3)" is affected. Eliminate Option A. Reading "controlled rent" secures "income (2)". Now it's between C and D. Since the right to use isn't explicitly restricted, you lock in C.
