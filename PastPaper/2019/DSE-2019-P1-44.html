HKDSE Economics Tutorial

Topic (課題): Balance of Payments (國際收支平衡)

Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆ (3/5)

Key Concepts (核心概念): Balance of Payments Residents vs Non-residents Current Account Financial Account

2. The Question (題目)

一名本港億萬富翁到俄羅斯旅遊，欣賞世界盃賽事。下列哪些會包括在本港國際收支平衡的計算之內？

(1) 他向一家本港代理為他的俄國之旅購買旅遊保險。

(2) 他在莫斯科購買一件俄羅斯國家隊球衣做紀念品。

(3) 他投資於俄羅斯一家球會，成為大股東。


A. 只有 (1)、(2)

B. 只有 (1)、(3)

C. 只有 (2)、(3)

D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

A Hong Kong billionaire travelled to Russia to watch matches in the World Cup. Which of the following would be included in the calculation of Hong Kong’s balance of payments?

(1) He bought a travel insurance for his Russia trip from a Hong Kong agency.

(2) He bought a jersey of the Russia national team in Moscow as a souvenir.

(3) He invested in a Russia football club and became the major owner.


A. (1) and (2) only

B. (1) and (3) only

C. (2) and (3) only

D. (1), (2) and (3)

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

國際收支平衡 (Balance of Payments, BOP) 是一個統計表，有系統地記錄在一個特定期間內，一個經濟體系與世界各地之間的所有經濟交易。最核心的原則是：該交易必須發生在常住居民 (Resident) 與 非常住居民 (Non-resident) 之間。

Balance of Payments (BOP) is a statistical statement that systematically summarizes, for a specific time period, the economic transactions of an economy with the rest of the world. The core principle is: the transaction must occur between a Resident and a Non-resident.

$$ BOP = \text{Current Account} + \text{Capital Account} + \text{Financial Account} + \text{Errors & Omissions} $$

如果交易雙方都是香港常住居民，這屬於本地交易，不會計入香港的 BOP。如果交易涉及香港居民與外國居民（例如俄羅斯實體），則會計入 BOP。

If both parties are HK residents, it is a domestic transaction and is NOT included in HK's BOP. If the transaction involves a HK resident and a foreign resident (e.g., a Russian entity), it IS included in the BOP.

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option C

分析 (1)：他向一家本港代理購買旅遊保險。
這項交易發生在「香港居民（富翁）」與「香港居民（本港保險代理）」之間。由於雙方都是香港常住居民，這是一項本地交易，不涉及對外交易，因此不包括在國際收支平衡內。

Analysis of (1): He bought travel insurance from a HK agency.
This transaction occurs between a "HK resident (the billionaire)" and another "HK resident (the HK insurance agency)". Since both parties are residents, it is a domestic transaction, not an external one. Therefore, it is NOT included in the BOP.

分析 (2)：他在莫斯科購買俄羅斯國家隊球衣。
這項交易發生在「香港居民（富翁）」與「俄羅斯居民（莫斯科的商店）」之間。香港居民在外地旅遊時的消費，屬於服務輸入（旅遊），因此包括在國際收支平衡的經常賬 (Current Account) 內。

Analysis of (2): He bought a jersey in Moscow.
This transaction occurs between a "HK resident" and a "Russian resident (the shop in Moscow)". Expenditure by HK residents travelling abroad is classified as an import of services (Travel), so it IS included in the Current Account of the BOP.

分析 (3)：他投資於俄羅斯一家球會，成為大股東。
這項交易發生在「香港居民」與「俄羅斯實體」之間。因為他成為了大股東（擁有對企業的持久利益和控制權），這屬於對外直接投資 (Outward Direct Investment)，因此包括在國際收支平衡的金融賬 (Financial Account) 內。

Analysis of (3): He invested in a Russian football club and became the major owner.
This transaction is between a "HK resident" and a "Russian entity". Because he became a major owner (acquiring a lasting interest and control), this is classified as Outward Direct Investment. Thus, it IS included in the Financial Account of the BOP.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的「解題樞紐」(Pivot Point) 只有一個：辨認交易雙方的身份。你不需要過度關注他們買了什麼（保險、球衣、還是股票），你只需要問自己：這筆錢是從香港人流向香港人，還是從香港人流向外國人？

The "Pivot Point" for this type of question is singular: Identify the residency of the transacting parties. You don't need to overthink what they bought (insurance, jersey, or shares); you only need to ask: is the money flowing between two HK residents, or between a HK resident and a foreigner?

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：被「俄國之旅」字眼誤導
很多學生看到 (1) 購買「俄國之旅」的保險，就以為這是一項國際交易。記住，產品的用途（用於外國旅行）並不重要，向誰購買才是決定性因素。向本港代理買，就是本地經濟活動。

Trap: Misled by the phrase "Russia trip"
Many students see the insurance is for a "Russia trip" in (1) and assume it's an international transaction. Remember, the purpose of the product doesn't matter; who you buy it from is the determining factor. Buying from a local agency makes it a domestic economic activity.

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 在外國買實體紀念品（球衣），為什麼計入「服務輸入（旅遊）」而不是「貨物輸入」？

A: 根據國際收支平衡的會計準則，旅客在外地購買並在當地消耗或帶走的絕大部分商品及服務，都會統一起來記錄在經常賬內的「服務：旅遊 (Services: Travel)」項目下。不過，無論歸類為貨物還是服務，它都屬於經常賬，必然計算在 BOP 內。


Q: Why is buying a physical souvenir (jersey) abroad counted as "Import of Services (Travel)" instead of "Import of Goods"?

A: Under BOP accounting standards, most goods and services acquired by travelers abroad for their own use are collectively recorded under "Services: Travel" in the Current Account. However, regardless of whether it's classified as goods or services, it falls under the Current Account and is definitely included in the BOP.

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧 (Elimination Method)：
在 DSE 選擇題中，尋找最明顯的錯誤選項來剔除。當你看到 (1) 是「本港富翁」向「本港代理」買東西，立刻知道這絕對不是 BOP 項目。因此，任何包含 (1) 的選項都是錯的。劃掉 A、B 和 D，答案瞬間鎖定為 C！完全不需要花時間去詳細分析 (2) 和 (3)。

Shortcut (Elimination Method):
In DSE MC questions, look for the most obvious wrong statement to eliminate options. When you see (1) is a "HK billionaire" buying from a "HK agency", you instantly know it's NOT a BOP item. Therefore, any option containing (1) is wrong. Cross out A, B, and D. The answer is instantly locked as C! You don't even need to spend time analyzing (2) and (3).
