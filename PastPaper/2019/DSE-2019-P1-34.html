Public Finance & Taxation (公共財政與稅收)

HKDSE Economics / DSE 經濟科

Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts: Fiscal Drag (財政拖累), Tax Net (稅網), Real Income (實質收入)

The Question (題目)

某年的工資率和一般物價水平上升 8%。若政府增加薪俸稅免稅額 5%，

Both the wage rate and general price level increase by 8% in a certain year. If the government increases the salaries tax allowance by 5%,

A. 更多人會跌入稅網。
more people will fall into the tax net.
B. 收入分佈會變得更平均。
income distribution will become more even.
C. 政府的稅收會減少。
government’s tax revenue will decrease.
D. 人們的稅後實質收入會增加。
people’s real income after tax will increase.

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. Fiscal Drag (財政拖累)

當通脹發生時，若免稅額 (Tax Allowance) 或 稅階 (Tax Brackets) 的調整幅度低於名義收入的增長幅度，納稅人會被迫繳納更高比例的稅款，或原本不用交稅的人跌入稅網。

Occurs when inflation pushes income into higher tax brackets or into the tax net, because tax allowances or brackets do not increase as fast as nominal income.

2. Tax Net (稅網)

只有當 名義收入 \( > \) 免稅額 時，市民才需要繳交薪俸稅（跌入稅網）。

People only pay salaries tax (fall into the tax net) when their Nominal Income \( > \) Tax Allowance.

3. Real Income (實質收入)

\[ \text{Real Income} = \frac{\text{Nominal Income}}{\text{Price Level}} \] \[ \text{實質收入} = \frac{\text{名義收入}}{\text{物價水平}} \]

若名義收入上升幅度 \( = \) 物價上升幅度，則稅前實質收入不變。

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Answer: Option A

Option A: More people will fall into the tax net (Correct)

分析： 名義工資上升了 \(8\%\)，但免稅額只上升了 \(5\%\)。這意味著收入增長快於免稅額的調整。
假設某人原本的收入剛好等於舊的免稅額（未跌入稅網）。現在他的收入增加了 \(8\%\)，但保護傘（免稅額）只高了 \(5\%\)。因此，他的新收入會高於新免稅額，從而跌入稅網。

Analysis: Nominal wages increased by \(8\%\), while the tax allowance only increased by \(5\%\). Income grew faster than the allowance.
Consider a person whose income was previously exactly equal to the old allowance (paying no tax). Now, their income is \(8\%\) higher, but the threshold is only \(5\%\) higher. Their new income exceeds the new allowance, causing them to fall into the tax net.

Option B: Income distribution will become more even

分析： 雖然高收入人士可能繳納更多稅款，但低收入人士（原本不用交稅的）現在可能被迫交稅（跌入稅網）。這種「財政拖累」通常對剛跌入稅網的低收入階層打擊較大，未必能改善收入分佈，甚至可能惡化貧富差距（視乎具體稅階結構）。因此不能確定收入分佈會變得更平均。

Analysis: While high-income earners pay more, low-income earners (previously tax-exempt) are now dragged into the tax net. This "fiscal drag" often hurts the lower-income group entering the system significantly. It does not necessarily make the distribution more even; it might even worsen it depending on the specific tax brackets.

Option C: Government’s tax revenue will decrease

分析： 由於收入增長 (\(8\%\)) 大於免稅額增長 (\(5\%\))，應課稅收入 (Taxable Income) 增加了。加上更多人跌入稅網，政府的稅基擴大了，稅收總額必然增加而非減少。

Analysis: Since income growth (\(8\%\)) exceeds allowance growth (\(5\%\)), the taxable income increases. Additionally, more people fall into the tax net, expanding the tax base. Therefore, total tax revenue will definitely increase, not decrease.

Option D: People’s real income after tax will increase

分析： 1. 稅前：名義工資 \( \uparrow 8\% \)，物價 \( \uparrow 8\% \) \(\rightarrow\) 稅前實質工資不變。
2. 稅後：由於免稅額調整滯後（只加 \(5\%\)），市民需繳交的實質稅款增加了（財政拖累）。
3. 結論：賺的一樣多（實質），但交的稅多了（實質），所以落袋的錢（稅後實質收入）減少了。

Analysis: 1. Pre-tax: Nominal wages \( \uparrow 8\% \), Prices \( \uparrow 8\% \) \(\rightarrow\) Pre-tax real wages remain constant.
2. After-tax: Because the allowance adjustment lagged (\(5\%\)), the real tax burden increased (fiscal drag).
3. Conclusion: You earn the same in real terms, but pay more real tax. Therefore, real income after tax decreases.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

The Pivot Point (關鍵點)

這題的勝負關鍵在於比較兩個百分比：
Income \(\%\Delta\) vs Allowance \(\%\Delta\)

  • Income: \(+8\%\)
  • Allowance: \(+5\%\)
  • Result: Income wins. The "Tax Shield" shrinks relative to income.

Distractors (干擾項)

"Price Level increases by 8%" (物價上升 8%)

這個資訊對於判斷 Option A (跌入稅網) 是無關的。稅網只看「名義收入」和「免稅額」。
但這個資訊對於排除 Option D (實質收入) 是至關重要的，因為它鎖定了通脹率。

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

Numerical Substitution Method (代數字法)

如果覺得百分比很抽象，直接代入數字：

假設舊收入 = $100, 舊免稅額 = $100 (不用交稅)

--- 變化後 ---

新收入 = $100 \times 1.08 = $108

新免稅額 = $100 \times 1.05 = $105

結果：收入 ($108) > 免稅額 ($105)。跌入稅網！

Use simple numbers ($100) to verify logic instantly.
