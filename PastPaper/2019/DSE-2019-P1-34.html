HKDSE Economics - Fiscal Drag Analysis

Public Finance & Taxation DSE 2019 P1 Q34 Difficulty: ★★★☆☆

某年的工資率和一般物價水平上升 8%。若政府增加薪俸稅免稅額 5%，

Both the wage rate and general price level increase by 8% in a certain year. If the government increases the salaries tax allowance by 5%,

  • A. 更多人會跌入稅網。 (more people will fall into the tax net.)
  • B. 收入分佈會變得更平均。 (income distribution will become more even.)
  • C. 政府的稅收會減少。 (government’s tax revenue will decrease.)
  • D. 人們的稅後實質收入會增加。 (people’s real income after tax will increase.)

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. Fiscal Drag (財政拖累):
當通脹發生時，若 免稅額 (Tax Allowance) 的調整幅度低於名義收入的增長幅度，納稅人會被迫繳納更高比例的稅款，或原本不用交稅的人跌入稅網。
Occurs when inflation pushes income into higher tax brackets or into the tax net because tax allowances do not increase as fast as nominal income.

2. Tax Net (稅網):
只有當 \( \text{Nominal Income} > \text{Tax Allowance} \) 時，市民才需要繳交薪俸稅（跌入稅網）。
People only pay salaries tax (fall into the tax net) when their Nominal Income exceeds the Tax Allowance.

3. Real Income (實質收入):

$$ \text{Real Income} = \frac{\text{Nominal Income}}{\text{Price Level}} $$

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Option A (Correct): 更多人會跌入稅網。 (More people will fall into the tax net.)

分析：名義工資上升了 \(8\%\)，但免稅額只上升了 \(5\%\)。這意味著收入增長快於免稅額的調整。假設某人原本的收入剛好等於舊的免稅額（未跌入稅網）。現在他的收入增加了 \(8\%\)，但保護傘（免稅額）只高了 \(5\%\)。因此，他的新收入會高於新免稅額，從而跌入稅網。

Analysis: Nominal wages increased by \(8\%\), while the tax allowance only increased by \(5\%\). Income grew faster than the allowance. Consider a person whose income was previously exactly equal to the old allowance. Now, their income is \(8\%\) higher, but the threshold is only \(5\%\) higher. Their new income exceeds the new allowance, causing them to fall into the tax net.

Option B: 收入分佈會變得更平均。 (Income distribution will become more even.)

分析：雖然高收入人士可能繳納更多稅款，但低收入人士（原本不用交稅的）現在可能被迫交稅。這種「財政拖累」通常對剛跌入稅網的低收入階層打擊較大，未必能改善收入分佈，甚至可能惡化貧富差距。

Analysis: While high-income earners pay more, low-income earners (previously tax-exempt) are now dragged into the tax net. This often hurts the lower-income group significantly and does not necessarily make the distribution more even.

Option C: 政府的稅收會減少。 (Government’s tax revenue will decrease.)

分析：由於收入增長 (\(8\%\)) 大於免稅額增長 (\(5\%\))，應課稅收入 (Taxable Income) 增加了。加上更多人跌入稅網，政府的稅基擴大了，稅收總額必然增加。

Analysis: Since income growth (\(8\%\)) exceeds allowance growth (\(5\%\)), taxable income increases. Additionally, more people fall into the tax net, expanding the tax base. Tax revenue will increase.

Option D: 人們的稅後實質收入會增加。 (People’s real income after tax will increase.)

分析： 1. 稅前：名義工資 \( \uparrow 8\% \)，物價 \( \uparrow 8\% \) \(\rightarrow\) 稅前實質工資不變。
2. 稅後：由於免稅額調整滯後（只加 \(5\%\)），市民需繳交的實質稅款增加了（財政拖累）。
3. 結論：賺的一樣多（實質），但交的稅多了（實質），所以落袋的錢（稅後實質收入）減少了。

Analysis: Pre-tax real wages remain constant (both wage and prices \( \uparrow 8\% \)). However, because the tax allowance lagged (\(5\%\)), the real tax burden increased. Therefore, real income after tax decreases.

Exam Tricks: Numerical Substitution (代數字法)

如果覺得百分比很抽象，直接代入數字 (Use simple numbers to verify logic)：

假設 (Assume):
舊收入 (Old Income) = $100
舊免稅額 (Old Allowance) = $100 (不用交稅 / No Tax)

變化後 (After Change):
新收入 (New Income) = \( 100 \times 1.08 = \$108 \)
新免稅額 (New Allowance) = \( 100 \times 1.05 = \$105 \)

結果 (Result): 收入 ($108) > 免稅額 ($105)。
跌入稅網！ (Fall into the tax net!)

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q1: 為什麼判斷 Option A (跌入稅網) 時，不需要考慮「物價上升 8%」？
Why don't we consider the "8% price level increase" when analyzing Option A?

A: 稅務局決定你是否需要交稅，只看你的「名義收入」(Nominal Income) 是否高於「免稅額」。物價水平只影響你的購買力（實質收入），但不影響你是否跌入稅網。稅網的門檻是名義數值 (Nominal value)。
The tax department determines if you pay tax based solely on whether your Nominal Income exceeds the Allowance. The price level affects your purchasing power (Real Income) but has no bearing on the legal threshold for the tax net, which is a nominal figure.


Q2: 如果免稅額增加了 10% (高於通脹和工資增長)，結果會怎樣？
What if the tax allowance increased by 10% (higher than inflation and wage growth)?

A: 那就會出現相反的情況。因為免稅額的增長 (\(10\%\)) 快於收入增長 (\(8\%\))，保護傘變大了。原本剛好在稅網邊緣的人可能會跌出稅網 (fall out of the tax net)，而納稅人的實質稅務負擔會減輕。
The opposite would happen. Since the allowance growth (\(10\%\)) outpaces income growth (\(8\%\)), the "shield" gets larger. People on the margin might fall out of the tax net, and the real tax burden for taxpayers would decrease.
