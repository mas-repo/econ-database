HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Fiscal Policy & Business Cycle Difficulty: ★★★★☆ Keywords: Inflationary Gap, Fiscal Surplus, AD/AS

The Question (題目)

假設某經濟出現通脹（產出）差距和財政盈餘。下列哪項措施有助於縮小通脹（產出）差距，同時達至財政收支平衡？ Suppose an inflationary (output) gap and a fiscal surplus exist in an economy. Which of the following measures would help narrow the inflation (output) gap and attain a balanced fiscal condition at the same time?
A. 減少入境旅客的配額
A. reducing quota for inbound tourists
B. 提高入息稅率
B. raising income tax rate
C. 為全部65歲或以上長者推行全民退休保障計劃
C. adopting a universal retirement protection scheme for all elderly aged 65 or above
D. 增加基建方面的政府開支
D. increasing government spending on infrastructure

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 通脹差距 (Inflationary Gap):
當實際產出 (\(Y\)) 高於潛在產出 (\(Y_f\)) 時出現。這意味著總需求 (AD) 過高。要消除差距，我們需要減少總需求 (Decrease AD)。 Occurs when actual output (\(Y\)) is greater than potential output (\(Y_f\)). This implies Aggregate Demand (AD) is too high. To eliminate the gap, we need to decrease Aggregate Demand.
\( \text{Goal 1: } AD \downarrow \)
2. 財政盈餘 (Fiscal Surplus):
當政府收入 (\(T\)) 大於政府支出 (\(G\)) 時出現。要達至收支平衡，我們需要減少收入或增加支出。 Occurs when Government Revenue (\(T\)) is greater than Government Expenditure (\(G\)). To attain a balanced budget, we need to decrease Revenue or increase Expenditure.
\( \text{Current: } T > G \quad \Rightarrow \quad \text{Goal 2: } T \downarrow \text{ or } G \uparrow \)

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

CORRECT (正確) Option A (選項 A)
分析：減少入境旅客配額會減少服務輸出 (\(X\))，從而導致總需求 (\(AD\)) 下降。這有助於縮小通脹差距。 同時，由於經濟活動減少（旅客消費減少），政府的稅收（如利得稅、銷售稅等）會隨之下降。在政府支出 (\(G\)) 不變的情況下，稅收 (\(T\)) 減少會使財政盈餘 (\(T-G\)) 減少，從而趨向財政收支平衡。 Analysis: Reducing the quota for inbound tourists decreases exports of services (\(X\)), leading to a decrease in Aggregate Demand (\(AD\)). This helps narrow the inflationary gap. At the same time, due to reduced economic activity (less tourist spending), government tax revenue (e.g., profits tax, sales tax) will fall. Assuming Government Expenditure (\(G\)) remains constant, a decrease in Tax Revenue (\(T\)) reduces the fiscal surplus (\(T-G\)), moving towards a balanced fiscal condition.
INCORRECT (錯誤) Option B (選項 B)
分析：提高入息稅率會減少可支配收入，導致私人消費 (\(C\)) 下降，總需求 (\(AD\)) 下降。這雖然能縮小通脹差距，但提高稅率會增加政府收入 (\(T\))，這會使現有的財政盈餘進一步擴大，而非達至平衡。 Analysis: Raising the income tax rate reduces disposable income, leading to a fall in Private Consumption (\(C\)) and Aggregate Demand (\(AD\)). While this narrows the inflationary gap, raising taxes increases Government Revenue (\(T\)). This would widen the existing fiscal surplus instead of balancing it.
INCORRECT (錯誤) Option C (選項 C)
分析：推行全民退休保障計劃通常涉及增加轉移支付（政府支出的一種）。這會增加長者的可支配收入，從而增加私人消費 (\(C\)) 和總需求 (\(AD\))。這會使通脹差距擴大（變得更嚴重），與目標背道而馳。 Analysis: Adopting a universal retirement protection scheme typically involves increasing transfer payments (a form of government spending). This increases the disposable income of the elderly, leading to higher Private Consumption (\(C\)) and Aggregate Demand (\(AD\)). This would widen the inflationary gap (make it worse), contradicting the goal.
INCORRECT (錯誤) Option D (選項 D)
分析：增加基建開支直接增加了政府支出 (\(G\))。這雖然能減少財政盈餘，但同時會增加總需求 (\(AD\))，導致通脹差距擴大。 Analysis: Increasing infrastructure spending directly increases Government Expenditure (\(G\)). While this would reduce the fiscal surplus, it simultaneously increases Aggregate Demand (\(AD\)), causing the inflationary gap to widen.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

🔑 The Pivot Point (關鍵轉折點)

這道題目的難點在於「雙重目標的衝突」(Conflict of Dual Objectives)。
通常，要解決通脹差距（需要緊縮政策），我們會增加稅收或減少開支，但這會增加盈餘。 要解決盈餘（需要擴張性財政調整），我們會減少稅收或增加開支，但這會惡化通脹。

The difficulty lies in the "Conflict of Dual Objectives".
Usually, to fix an inflationary gap (requiring contractionary policy), we raise taxes or cut spending, but this increases the surplus. To fix a surplus (requiring expansionary fiscal adjustment), we cut taxes or raise spending, but this worsens inflation.

唯一的出路 (The only way out): 尋找一個能減少總需求 (\(AD\))，但其副作用會減少政府稅收 (\(T\)) 的選項。這就是選項 A（非財政政策導致的經濟收縮）。

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼選項 A 會影響財政收支？題目沒有說政府向旅客徵稅啊？
A: 這是經濟學中的內生變量 (Endogenous Variables) 概念。雖然政府可能沒有直接向旅客徵收「人頭稅」，但旅客消費會產生銷售稅，依賴旅遊業的企業利潤會產生利得稅。當旅客減少，經濟活動放緩，政府的整體稅收收入 (\(T\)) 自然會下降。 Q: Why does Option A affect the fiscal balance? The question didn't say the government taxes tourists directly.
A: This relates to Endogenous Variables in economics. Even if there isn't a direct "head tax" on tourists, tourist spending generates sales tax, and profits of tourism-related firms generate profits tax. When tourists decrease, economic activity slows down, and total Government Revenue (\(T\)) naturally falls.
Q: 選項 C 和 D 為什麼一定錯？
A: 因為它們都是擴張性 (Expansionary) 的措施。在通脹差距（經濟過熱）時，任何增加 \(AD\) 的措施（如派錢、建橋）都會火上加油，使通脹問題更嚴重。即便它們能解決財政盈餘問題，也無法滿足題目「縮小通脹差距」的要求。 Q: Why are Options C and D definitely wrong?
A: Because they are both Expansionary measures. During an inflationary gap (overheating economy), any measure that increases \(AD\) (like handing out money or building bridges) adds fuel to the fire, worsening the inflation. Even if they solve the surplus issue, they fail the requirement to "narrow the inflation gap".
