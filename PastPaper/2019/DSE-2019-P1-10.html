3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

Perfect Competition & Price-Takers (完美競爭與受價者)

在完美競爭市場中，廠商是受價者（Price-taker），意味著它們無法改變市場價格，只能接受市場供需決定的價格。因此，其邊際收入（Marginal Revenue, MR）等於市場價格（Price, P）。

In a perfectly competitive market, firms are price-takers. This means they cannot influence the market price and must accept the price determined by market supply and demand. Therefore, their Marginal Revenue (MR) equals the market Price (P).

$$\text{P} = \text{MR} = \text{AR}$$

Profit Maximization Condition (利潤極大化條件)

所有追求利潤極大化的廠商，都會在邊際收入（MR）等於邊際成本（MC）時進行生產。結合上述概念，受價者廠商的利潤極大化條件為：價格等於邊際成本。

All profit-maximizing firms produce at the quantity where Marginal Revenue (MR) equals Marginal Cost (MC). Combining this with the concept above, the profit-maximizing condition for a price-taking firm is when Price equals Marginal Cost.

$$\text{MR} = \text{MC}$$ $$\text{P} = \text{MC}$$

Cost Formulas (成本公式)

要計算邊際成本，我們必須先從平均成本（AC）找出總成本（TC）。

To calculate Marginal Cost, we must first find Total Cost (TC) from Average Cost (AC).

$$\text{TC} = \text{AC} \times \text{Q}$$ $$\text{MC} = \frac{\Delta \text{TC}}{\Delta \text{Q}}$$

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

破題關鍵 (Pivot Point):
題目中出現「受價者」(Price-taking firm) 和「利潤極大化」(Profit-maximising) 兩個關鍵字。這就像是一個解碼指令，告訴你必須立刻寫下公式：\(\text{P} = \text{MC}\)。因此，你真正的任務只是「找出產量為 6 時的 MC」。

Pivot Point:
The keywords "Price-taking firm" and "Profit-maximising" appear in the question. This is a code instructing you to immediately write down the formula: \(\text{P} = \text{MC}\). Therefore, your actual task is simply to "find the MC when output is 6."

干擾資訊 (Irrelevant Info):
整個表格提供的 AC (平均成本) 都是用來迷惑你的干擾項。同學常常直接從表格中找答案，而忘記必須將 AC 轉換為 TC，再轉換為 MC。

Irrelevant Info:
The AC (Average Cost) provided in the entire table acts as a distractor. Students often try to find the answer directly from the table, forgetting they must convert AC to TC, and then to MC.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱 1：直接讀取平均成本 (AC)
很多同學一看到「產量是 6」，就直接對應表格中 6 單位的平均成本 $25 (選項 B)。請記住，決定利潤極大化的是「邊際」概念 (Marginal)，絕對不是「平均」概念 (Average)。

Trap 1: Reading Average Cost (AC) directly
Many students see "output is 6" and directly pick the corresponding average cost $25 (Option B). Remember, profit maximization is determined by "Marginal" concepts, never by "Average" concepts.

陷阱 2：錯誤計算邊際成本
部分同學會嘗試計算「邊際平均成本」(例如 $25 - $20 = $5，選項 A)。這在經濟學中是完全錯誤的計算方式。MC 必須由總成本 (TC) 計算得出。

Trap 2: Calculating Marginal Cost incorrectly
Some students try to calculate "Marginal Average Cost" (e.g., $25 - $20 = $5, Option A). This is a completely invalid mathematical approach in economics. MC must be derived from Total Cost (TC).

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q1: 為什麼這間廠商在 \(\text{P} = \text{MC} = \$50\) 時，它的平均成本 (AC) 只有 $25？這代表什麼？

A: 這代表廠商正在賺取「超額利潤」(Supernormal Profit)。每賣出一件產品，廠商的平均成本是 $25，但市場價格是 $50。因此，每件產品賺 $25，總利潤為 \(\$25 \times 6 = \$150\)。

A: This means the firm is earning a "Supernormal Profit." For every unit sold, the firm's average cost is $25, but the market price is $50. Thus, it makes $25 per unit, making a total profit of \(\$25 \times 6 = \$150\).

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法 (Shortcut Calculation):
在選擇題中，時間就是分數。你不需要填滿整個 TC 和 MC 表格。既然題目只問產量為 6 的情況，你只需要計算第 5 單位和第 6 單位的總成本即可：

Shortcut Calculation:
In MCQs, time is marks. You do NOT need to fill out the entire TC and MC table. Since the question only asks about output 6, you only need to calculate the total cost for the 5th and 6th units:

1. \(\text{TC}_5 = 5 \times \$20 = \$100\)
2. \(\text{TC}_6 = 6 \times \$25 = \$150\)
3. \(\text{MC}_6 = \$150 - \$100 = \$50\)

三步計算，十秒內即可選出 D！

Three steps, and you can confidently choose D in under ten seconds!
