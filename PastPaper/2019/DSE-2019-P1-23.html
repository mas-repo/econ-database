經濟學多項選擇題分析

Topic: Macroeconomics - National Income Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
Real vs Nominal GDP Per Capita GDP Growth Rates

1. The Question (題目)

[繁體中文]
下表顯示某經濟於某年度的本地生產總值資料：

實質本地生產總值的轉變： -2.6%
名義本地生產總值的轉變： -1.3%
人口的轉變： -1.8%

根據以上資料，我們可以得出結論如下：在該年度，物價水平 _________ ，而人均名義本地生產總值 ___________。
A. 上升了 …… 增加了
B. 下降了 …… 減少了
C. 上升了 …… 減少了
D. 下降了 …… 增加了
[English]
The following table shows the gross domestic product (GDP) data of an economy in a certain year.

Change in real GDP: -2.6%
Change in nominal GDP: -1.3%
Change in population size: -1.8%

According to the information above, we can conclude that in that year, the price level _________ and the per capita nominal GDP ___________.
A. increased …… increased
B. decreased …… decreased
C. increased …… decreased
D. decreased …… increased

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這類題目，我們必須掌握宏觀經濟學中關於增長率（百分比轉變）的近似值公式。當數值變化較小時（通常小於10%），我們可以使用加減法來代替複雜的乘除法。
To solve this type of question, we must master the approximation formulas for growth rates (percentage changes) in macroeconomics. When the changes are small (usually less than 10%), we can use simple addition and subtraction instead of complex multiplication and division.

Key Formulas (關鍵公式):

$$ \%\Delta \text{Nominal GDP} \approx \%\Delta \text{Real GDP} + \%\Delta \text{Price Level} $$
$$ \%\Delta \text{Per Capita GDP} \approx \%\Delta \text{GDP} - \%\Delta \text{Population} $$
Definitions (定義):
  • 名義本地生產總值 (Nominal GDP): 以當年價格計算的總產值。
  • 實質本地生產總值 (Real GDP): 以基年價格計算的總產值，排除了物價變動的影響。
  • 人均本地生產總值 (Per Capita GDP): 總產值除以人口，反映平均每人攤分的產值。
  • Nominal GDP: The total value of output calculated at current year prices.
  • Real GDP: The total value of output calculated at base year prices, eliminating the effect of price changes.
  • Per Capita GDP: Total output divided by population, reflecting the average output per person.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A (上升了 …… 增加了 / increased …… increased)

Step-by-Step Analysis (逐步分析):

Part 1: Price Level (物價水平)

根據公式：名義GDP增長率 \(\approx\) 實質GDP增長率 \(+\) 物價水平增長率。
代入數據：\(-1.3\% = -2.6\% + \%\Delta \text{Price Level}\)
\(\%\Delta \text{Price Level} = -1.3\% - (-2.6\%) = +1.3\%\)
因為結果是正數（\(+1.3\%\)），所以物價水平上升了。
According to the formula: \(\%\Delta \text{Nominal GDP} \approx \%\Delta \text{Real GDP} + \%\Delta \text{Price Level}\).
Substituting the data: \(-1.3\% = -2.6\% + \%\Delta \text{Price Level}\)
\(\%\Delta \text{Price Level} = -1.3\% - (-2.6\%) = +1.3\%\)
Since the result is positive (\(+1.3\%\)), the price level increased.

Part 2: Per Capita Nominal GDP (人均名義本地生產總值)

根據公式：人均名義GDP增長率 \(\approx\) 名義GDP增長率 \(-\) 人口增長率。
代入數據：\(\%\Delta \text{Per Capita Nominal GDP} = -1.3\% - (-1.8\%) = +0.5\%\)
因為結果是正數（\(+0.5\%\)），所以人均名義本地生產總值增加了。
According to the formula: \(\%\Delta \text{Per Capita Nominal GDP} \approx \%\Delta \text{Nominal GDP} - \%\Delta \text{Population}\).
Substituting the data: \(\%\Delta \text{Per Capita Nominal GDP} = -1.3\% - (-1.8\%) = +0.5\%\)
Since the result is positive (\(+0.5\%\)), the per capita nominal GDP increased.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (解題關鍵):
這題的關鍵在於能否準確運用百分比變化的近似值公式，並小心處理負數（負負得正）。題目要求計算「人均名義本地生產總值」，因此在計算第二部分時，必須使用「名義GDP的轉變」而非「實質GDP的轉變」。
Pivot Point:
The key to this question is accurately applying the approximation formulas for percentage changes and carefully handling negative numbers (double negatives become positive). The question asks for the "per capita nominal GDP", so you must use the "change in nominal GDP" rather than the "change in real GDP" for the second calculation.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 陷阱一：用錯數據。 學生在計算「人均名義GDP」時，可能會錯誤地使用了「實質GDP」的數據（\(-2.6\%\)），得出 \(-2.6\% - (-1.8\%) = -0.8\%\)（減少了），從而誤選答案 C。
  • 陷阱二：正負號計算錯誤。 在處理 \(-1.3\% - (-1.8\%)\) 時，忘記了負負得正，錯誤計算成 \(-3.1\%\)。
  • Trap 1: Using the wrong data. When calculating "per capita nominal GDP", students might mistakenly use the "real GDP" data (\(-2.6\%\)), resulting in \(-2.6\% - (-1.8\%) = -0.8\%\) (decreased), leading them to incorrectly choose Option C.
  • Trap 2: Sign errors. When processing \(-1.3\% - (-1.8\%)\), students might forget that a double negative makes a positive, mistakenly calculating it as \(-3.1\%\).

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼我們可以使用加減法（近似值公式）而不是精確的乘除法公式？ / Why can we use addition and subtraction (approximation formulas) instead of exact multiplication and division?
A: 嚴格來說，精確公式是 \((1 + \%\Delta \text{Nominal}) = (1 + \%\Delta \text{Real}) \times (1 + \%\Delta \text{Price})\)。但當百分比變化很小（通常小於 5%）時，兩者相乘的交叉項非常微小，可以忽略不計。在 DSE 考試的選擇題中，使用近似值公式加減法已經足夠準確且速度更快。
A: Strictly speaking, the exact formula is \((1 + \%\Delta \text{Nominal}) = (1 + \%\Delta \text{Real}) \times (1 + \%\Delta \text{Price})\). However, when percentage changes are small (usually < 5%), the cross-product term is negligible. For DSE MCQs, the approximation formula using addition/subtraction is accurate enough and much faster.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

邏輯速解法 (Logic Shortcut):
1. 實質GDP跌了 \(2.6\%\)，但名義GDP只跌了 \(1.3\%\)。名義跌得比實質少，這意味著「價格」一定在背後支撐著（即物價上升了），立刻排除 B 和 D。
2. 總產值（名義GDP）跌了 \(1.3\%\)，但分餅的人（人口）跌得更多（跌了 \(1.8\%\)）。既然人數減少的幅度大於餅縮小的幅度，每人分到的餅（人均名義GDP）自然就變大了（增加了），選 A。完全不需要動筆計算！
Logic Shortcut:
1. Real GDP fell by \(2.6\%\), but Nominal GDP only fell by \(1.3\%\). Since nominal fell less than real, "prices" must be propping it up (i.e., price level increased). Eliminate B and D immediately.
2. The total pie (Nominal GDP) shrank by \(1.3\%\), but the number of people sharing it (Population) shrank even more (by \(1.8\%\)). Since the number of people decreased faster than the pie shrank, the average slice per person (Per Capita Nominal GDP) naturally got bigger (increased). Choose A. No written calculations needed!
