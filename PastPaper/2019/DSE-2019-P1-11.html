HKDSE Economics Expert Guide

Topic: Elasticity & Market Equilibrium (彈性與市場均衡) Difficulty: ★★★★☆ Key Concepts: Price Elasticity of Supply, Demand Shift

1. The Question (題目)

細閱以下報章撮要：
登革熱恐慌襲港——上星期本港有19人確診在本地感染該病毒。登革熱是由白紋伊蚊傳播給人類，而白紋伊蚊是在香港常見的蚊子。
假設上述新聞發布後，本港售賣的蚊怕水價格和數量隨即分別轉變了5%和120%。這顯示了在該段時間的價格範圍內，本港蚊怕水的 _________ 。

A. 供應屬高彈性
B. 供應屬低彈性
C. 需求屬高彈性
D. 需求屬低彈性

Study the following news abstract:
A Dengue fever scare has hit Hong Kong after 19 people in the past week were confirmed to have contracted the virus locally. Dengue fever is transmitted to humans by Aedes albopictus, a type of mosquito commonly found in Hong Kong.
Suppose the price and quantity of mosquito repellents sold in Hong Kong changed by 5% and 120% respectively shortly after the release of the above news. This indicates that during that period and within the price range, there was an _________ the mosquito repellents in Hong Kong.

A. elastic supply of
B. inelastic supply of
C. elastic demand for
D. inelastic demand for

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 需求移動與供應彈性 (Shift in Demand and Elasticity of Supply): 當影響需求的非價格因素（如對疾病的恐慌）發生變化時，需求曲線會移動。這會導致新的均衡點沿著原有且不變的供應曲線移動。因此，均衡價格與數量的變化百分比，反映的是供應彈性，而不是需求彈性。

When a non-price determinant of demand (like fear of disease) changes, the demand curve shifts. This causes the new equilibrium point to move along the original, unchanged supply curve. Therefore, the percentage changes in equilibrium price and quantity reflect the elasticity of supply, not demand.

2. 供應的價格彈性 (Price Elasticity of Supply, \(E_s\)): 量度供應量對價格變動的反應程度。

Measures the responsiveness of quantity supplied to a change in price.

$$ E_s = \frac{\%\Delta Q_s}{\%\Delta P} $$

如果 \(E_s > 1\)，屬高彈性 (Elastic)。如果 \(E_s < 1\)，屬低彈性 (Inelastic)。

If \(E_s > 1\), it is elastic. If \(E_s < 1\), it is inelastic.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A

Option A (正確 Correct): 登革熱爆發導致市民對蚊怕水的需求增加（需求曲線向右移）。均衡點沿著供應曲線向上移動。我們可以使用題目提供的數據計算供應彈性：\(E_s = \frac{120\%}{5\%} = 24\)。因為 \(24 > 1\)，所以供應屬高彈性。

The Dengue fever outbreak increases the demand for mosquito repellents (demand curve shifts right). The equilibrium moves upwards along the supply curve. We calculate the elasticity of supply: \(E_s = \frac{120\%}{5\%} = 24\). Since \(24 > 1\), the supply is highly elastic.

Option B (錯誤 Incorrect): 雖然我們是在計算供應彈性，但計算結果為 24，大於 1，這代表高彈性而非低彈性。

Although we are measuring supply elasticity, the calculated value is 24, which is greater than 1, meaning it is elastic, not inelastic.

Options C & D (錯誤 Incorrect): 這是最常見的陷阱！新聞事件改變了消費者的偏好，導致「需求曲線移動」。當需求曲線移動時，我們無法利用兩個均衡點來計算需求彈性（因為它們位於兩條不同的需求曲線上）。

This is the most common trap! The news event changed consumer preferences, causing a "shift in the demand curve". When the demand curve shifts, we cannot use the two equilibrium points to calculate demand elasticity (because they lie on two different demand curves).

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

關鍵點 (Pivot Point): 辨識哪一條曲線發生了移動。新聞內容（登革熱恐慌）明顯影響消費者的購買意欲，即需求增加。這意味著我們只能量度另一條沒有移動的曲線（即供應）的彈性。

Pivot Point: Identify which curve shifted. The news (Dengue scare) clearly affects consumers' willingness to buy, meaning demand increases. This implies we can only measure the elasticity of the curve that did NOT shift (i.e., supply).

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱： 學生看到「需求增加」，就直覺以為題目在考「需求彈性」，直接選擇 C 或 D。記住，計算彈性需要同一條曲線上的兩個點！

Trap: Students see "increase in demand" and intuitively think the question is testing "elasticity of demand," jumping to C or D. Remember, calculating elasticity requires two points on the SAME curve!

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼不能計算需求彈性？ (Why can't we calculate demand elasticity?)

A: 因為需求曲線移動了！舊的均衡點在舊的需求曲線上，新的均衡點在新的需求曲線上。用這兩個點算出來的數字是沒有經濟學意義的。只有供應曲線保持不變，所以這兩個點都在同一條供應曲線上。

A: Because the demand curve shifted! The old equilibrium is on the old demand curve, and the new equilibrium is on the new demand curve. A number calculated using these two points has no economic meaning. Only the supply curve remained unchanged, so both points lie on the same supply curve.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

「對家法則」 (The Opposite Curve Rule):
在MC題中，如果題目描述的事件令需求移動，答案必定是關於供應彈性。
如果事件令供應移動（例如：成本上升），答案必定是關於需求彈性。
利用這個法則，你可以立刻刪除 C 和 D，然後只需心算 \(120 / 5 > 1\)，秒殺此題選 A！

The Opposite Curve Rule:
In MC questions, if the event shifts Demand, the answer MUST be about Supply elasticity.
If the event shifts Supply (e.g., cost increase), the answer MUST be about Demand elasticity.
Using this rule, you can immediately eliminate C and D, mentally calculate \(120 / 5 > 1\), and quickly choose A!
