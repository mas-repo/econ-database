2. The Question (題目)

下列哪些是一家咖啡店的資本？
Which of the following are capital of a café?
(1) 咖啡機
(1) coffee machines
(2) 咖啡豆
(2) coffee beans
(3) 顧客帶來開派對用的遊戲機
(3) video game consoles brought by its customers for party
(4) 可供顧客免費借閱的漫畫書
(4) comic books which can be borrowed by its customers free of charge
A. 只有 (1)、(2)
A. (1) and (2) only
B. 只有 (1)、(2) 及 (4)
B. (1), (2) and (4) only
C. 只有 (1)、(3) 及 (4)
C. (1), (3) and (4) only
D. 只有 (2)、(3) 及 (4)
D. (2), (3) and (4) only

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

資本 (Capital)： 是指由人類製造，並用作協助生產其他物品及服務的資源。它屬於生產者物品 (Producer Goods) 的一種。
Capital: Refers to man-made resources used to assist in the production of other goods and services. It is a type of producer good.
判斷一件物品是否為某企業的「資本」，必須符合兩大條件：
To determine whether an item is the "capital" of a firm, it must fulfill two main conditions:
  1. 人造的： 不是自然賦予的（若是自然賦予的，則屬於「土地」）。
    Man-made: Not a gift of nature (if it is naturally endowed, it is classified as "Land").
  2. 用於協助生產： 該物品必須被生產者（企業）使用，以提供服務或製造產品，而非供物主直接消費。
    Used in production: The item must be used by the producer (the firm) to provide services or manufacture products, rather than for the direct consumption of the owner.
在經濟學中，我們經常使用生產函數 (Production Function) 來表達產出與各種生產要素的關係：
In economics, we often use the Production Function to express the relationship between output and various factors of production:
$$Q = f(L, K, N, E)$$
公式中，產出量 \(Q\) 取決於投入的勞力 \(L\)、資本 \(K\)、土地 \(N\) 以及企業家職能 \(E\)。本題考查的正是如何辨識投入中的資本 \(K\)。
In the formula, the output quantity \(Q\) depends on the inputs of labor \(L\), capital \(K\), land \(N\), and entrepreneurship \(E\). This question specifically tests how to identify the capital input \(K\).

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 / Correct Answer: Option B
(1) 咖啡機 (Coffee machines)
這是人造資源，咖啡店利用它來沖泡咖啡（生產產品），因此它是資本（固定資本）。
(1) Coffee machines
These are man-made resources used by the café to brew coffee (produce goods), so they are capital (fixed capital).
(2) 咖啡豆 (Coffee beans)
咖啡豆已經過人類收割及加工（人造資源），並作為原料被咖啡店用來生產咖啡。因此它屬於資本（流動資本）。
(2) Coffee beans
Coffee beans have been harvested and processed by human effort (man-made resources) and are used as raw materials by the café to produce coffee. Therefore, they belong to capital (working/circulating capital).
(3) 顧客帶來開派對用的遊戲機 (Video game consoles brought by customers)
雖然遊戲機是人造的，但它是顧客帶來作自己直接娛樂（消費）用的，並不是咖啡店用來生產服務的資源。因此，對顧客而言它是消費者物品，不是咖啡店的資本。
(3) Video game consoles brought by its customers for party
Although game consoles are man-made, they are brought by customers for their own direct entertainment (consumption). They are not resources used by the café to produce services. Thus, they are consumer goods to the customers, not the café's capital.
(4) 可供顧客免費借閱的漫畫書 (Comic books borrowed by customers free of charge)
這些漫畫書是人造的，雖然不收費，但它們是咖啡店用來提供更佳環境、吸引顧客的資源（生產「休閒服務」的一部分）。因此，它們是咖啡店的資本。
(4) Comic books which can be borrowed by its customers free of charge
These comic books are man-made. Even though they are free of charge, they are resources used by the café to provide a better environment and attract customers (part of producing "leisure services"). Therefore, they are the café's capital.
綜合以上分析，只有 (1)、(2) 及 (4) 是資本，故選 B。
Based on the above analysis, only (1), (2), and (4) are capital, hence option B is correct.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point):
這道題的核心在於判斷資源的「用途」與「使用者」。不要被物品擺放的地點（在咖啡店內）所迷惑。判斷的唯一標準是：「這件人造物品是否由生產者（咖啡店）用來提供貨品或服務？」
The Pivot Point:
The core of this question lies in determining the "usage" and "user" of the resources. Do not be distracted by the location of the items (inside the café). The only standard for judgment is: "Is this man-made item used by the producer (the café) to provide goods or services?"
干擾資訊 (Distractors):
選項 (3) 的「遊戲機」是一個典型的陷阱。它出現在營業場所，但擁有權和使用目的皆屬於消費者自己，與生產者的生產過程無關。
選項 (4) 中的「免費 (free of charge)」也是干擾字眼。物品是否收費並不影響其作為「資本」的性質，只要它有助於企業的業務運作即可。
Distractors:
The "video game consoles" in option (3) is a typical trap. It appears in the business premises, but its ownership and purpose of use belong entirely to the consumers, completely unrelated to the producer's production process.
The term "free of charge" in option (4) is also a distractor. Whether an item is charged or not does not affect its nature as "capital," as long as it facilitates the firm's business operations.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

錯誤概念一：將「原材料」誤認為「土地」
部分同學會認為咖啡豆源自大自然，所以屬於「土地」。然而，咖啡店購入的咖啡豆已經過農夫的種植、收割及烘焙等加工過程。只要經過人類加工並用於進一步生產的資源，在經濟學上均視為資本（流動資本）。
Misconception 1: Confusing "Raw Materials" with "Land"
Some students might think coffee beans originate from nature and thus belong to "Land". However, the coffee beans purchased by the café have undergone processing such as planting, harvesting, and roasting by farmers. Any resource that has been processed by human effort and used for further production is considered capital (working capital) in economics.
錯誤概念二：認為「免費」提供的東西不是資本
同學容易混淆物品的收費模式與其經濟學屬性。漫畫書雖然不另外收費，但它為咖啡店的整體服務增值（吸引客源）。因此它絕對是協助生產的資本。
Misconception 2: Believing items provided for "Free" are not capital
Students often confuse the pricing model of an item with its economic nature. Even though comic books are not charged separately, they add value to the café's overall service (attracting customers). Therefore, they are absolutely capital assisting in production.

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

問：如果顧客帶來的遊戲機借了給咖啡店老闆自己玩，那遊戲機算是資本嗎？
Q: If the game console brought by the customer is lent to the café owner for their own enjoyment, does it become capital?
答： 不算。如果老闆只是為了自己的私人娛樂而玩這部遊戲機，這並不是用來協助咖啡店的生產或提供服務。在這種情況下，遊戲機對老闆而言是一件「消費者物品」。
A: No. If the owner plays the game console purely for personal entertainment, it is not being used to assist in the production or service provision of the café. In this scenario, the game console acts as a "consumer good" to the owner.
問：咖啡店內負責沖咖啡的員工算是資本嗎？
Q: Are the employees brewing coffee in the café considered capital?
答： 不是。雖然員工協助生產，但人類付出的體力或腦力勞動屬於另一種生產要素，稱為「勞力 (Labor)」，資本僅指「人造的非人類資源」。
A: No. Although employees assist in production, the physical or mental effort exerted by humans belongs to another factor of production called "Labor". Capital only refers to "man-made, non-human resources".

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

消去法 (Elimination Method)：
這類多項選擇題最快的解法是找出「最明顯不對」的選項。在閱讀題目時，(3)「顧客帶來...」明顯不屬於店舖用來生產的物品。一旦確認 (3) 錯誤，你可以立刻在選項中劃掉包含 (3) 的 C 和 D。
剩下的 A 和 B 都包含 (1) 和 (2)，這意味著考場上你根本不需要花時間思考咖啡機和咖啡豆。你只需集中判斷 (4) 漫畫書是否為資本即可，從而大幅節省答題時間。
Elimination Method:
The fastest way to solve this type of multiple-choice question is to find the most "obviously wrong" item. While reading, (3) "brought by its customers..." clearly does not belong to items used by the shop for production. Once you confirm (3) is wrong, you can immediately cross out options C and D which contain (3).
The remaining options A and B both contain (1) and (2), which means you don't even need to spend time analyzing the coffee machines and beans during the exam. You only need to focus on evaluating whether (4) the comic books are capital, thereby saving a significant amount of time.
