2. The Question (題目)

參閱以下物品X的供需表。

Refer to the following supply and demand schedules of Good X.

單位價格 Unit price ($) 181614121084
需求量 Quantity demanded (units) 100150200250300350400
供應量 Quantity supplied (units) 400350300250200150100

若政府把物品X的配額由200單位增加至350單位，市場價格會 _____________。

If the government increases the quota for Good X from 200 units to 350 units, the market price will _____________.

  • A. 上升$2 (increase by $2)
  • B. 上升$6 (increase by $6)
  • C. 下降$2 (decrease by $2)
  • D. 下降$6 (decrease by $6)

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

配額 (Quota): 政府對某物品在特定時間內的最高生產或進口數量所設定的限制。

Quota: A limit set by the government on the maximum quantity of a good that can be produced or imported during a specified period.

要判斷配額的影響，必須比較配額數量 \(Q_q\) 與自由市場均衡數量 \(Q_e\)：

To determine the impact of a quota, we must compare the quota quantity \(Q_q\) with the free market equilibrium quantity \(Q_e\):

  • 有效配額 (Effective/Binding Quota): 當 \(Q_q < Q_e\) 時，配額會限制交易量，導致市場價格上升。價格由需求曲線決定。
  • Effective/Binding Quota: When \(Q_q < Q_e\), the quota restricts the traded quantity, causing the market price to rise. The price is determined by the demand curve.
  • 無效配額 (Ineffective/Non-binding Quota): 當 \(Q_q \ge Q_e\) 時，配額沒有實際約束力，市場維持在自由均衡狀態。
  • Ineffective/Non-binding Quota: When \(Q_q \ge Q_e\), the quota has no actual binding effect, and the market remains at the free equilibrium state.

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: C

第一步：尋找自由市場均衡 (Step 1: Find the free market equilibrium)
在沒有政府干預的情況下，需求量等於供應量（\(Q_d = Q_s\)）。從表中可見，當價格為 $12 時，\(Q_d = Q_s = 250\)。因此，均衡價格 \(P_e = \$12\)，均衡數量 \(Q_e = 250\)。

Without government intervention, quantity demanded equals quantity supplied (\(Q_d = Q_s\)). From the table, when the price is $12, \(Q_d = Q_s = 250\). Thus, the equilibrium price \(P_e = \$12\) and equilibrium quantity \(Q_e = 250\).

第二步：分析原有的配額 (Step 2: Analyze the initial quota)
原來的配額是 200 單位。因為 \(200 < 250\)，這是一個有效配額。市場交易量被限制在 200 單位。消費者願意為 200 單位支付的最高價格（根據需求表）是 $14。因此，原來的市場價格是 $14。

The initial quota is 200 units. Since \(200 < 250\), this is an effective quota. The market traded quantity is restricted to 200 units. The maximum price consumers are willing to pay for 200 units (according to the demand schedule) is $14. Therefore, the initial market price is $14.

第三步：分析新的配額 (Step 3: Analyze the new quota)
新的配額增加至 350 單位。因為 \(350 > 250\)，這是一個無效配額。市場不會交易到 350 單位，而是會回到自由市場的均衡點。因此，新的市場價格是 $12。

The new quota increases to 350 units. Since \(350 > 250\), this is an ineffective quota. The market will not trade up to 350 units but will return to the free market equilibrium. Therefore, the new market price is $12.

第四步：計算價格變動 (Step 4: Calculate the price change)
價格由 $14 變為 $12，即下降了 $2。

The price changes from $14 to $12, which is a decrease of $2.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的關鍵樞紐 (Pivot Point)是判斷配額是否「有效」。

The Pivot Point of this type of question is determining whether the quota is "effective".

  • 干擾資訊 (Distractors): 供應表上對應 200 單位的價格 ($10) 是一個陷阱。在有效配額下，價格由消費者的最高願付價格（需求曲線）決定，而不是生產者的最低要求價格。
  • Distractors: The price corresponding to 200 units on the supply schedule ($10) is a trap. Under an effective quota, the price is determined by the consumers' maximum willingness to pay (demand curve), not the producers' minimum required price.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱一：誤以為無效配額會強迫市場交易量達到配額上限。
學生常以為配額設為 350，交易量就是 350，從而錯誤地找對應的價格。記住，配額只是「上限」，如果市場本身只想交易 250 單位，配額 350 是沒有影響的。

Trap 1: Assuming an ineffective quota forces the market quantity to reach the quota limit.
Students often think if the quota is 350, the traded quantity is 350, and wrongly find the corresponding price. Remember, a quota is just a "maximum limit". If the market naturally only wants to trade 250 units, a quota of 350 has no effect.

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼當配額是 200 時，價格是 $14 而不是 $10？

Q: Why is the price $14 instead of $10 when the quota is 200?

A: 因為在只有 200 件物品供應的情況下，消費者之間的競爭會把價格推高。根據需求表，消費者願意為第 200 件物品支付高達 $14。賣家當然會以最高可能價格出售，所以價格會停在 $14。

A: Because with only 200 units available, competition among consumers will drive the price up. According to the demand schedule, consumers are willing to pay up to $14 for the 200th unit. Sellers will naturally sell at the highest possible price, so the price settles at $14.

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法 (Shortcut): 看到配額題目，第一時間找出 \(Q_e\) (250)。
舊配額 200 < 250 (有效) ➔ 看需求表找價格 ➔ $14。
新配額 350 > 250 (無效) ➔ 價格變回均衡價 ➔ $12。
$14 - $12 = 下降 $2。只需 10 秒即可解題！

Shortcut: When seeing a quota question, immediately find \(Q_e\) (250).
Old quota 200 < 250 (Effective) ➔ Look at demand schedule for price ➔ $14.
New quota 350 > 250 (Ineffective) ➔ Price returns to equilibrium ➔ $12.
$14 - $12 = Decrease by $2. Solved in 10 seconds!
