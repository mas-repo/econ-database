Topic: National Income Accounting 國民收入會計 Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts: GDP (本地生產總值), GNI (本地居民總收入), Resident (常住居民), Factor Income (要素收益)

2. The Question (題目)

要計算香港的本地居民總收入，我們須在香港的本地生產總值加上：

  1. 一位日本廚師在本港某餐廳工作兩個月所賺到的收入。
  2. 一位本港居民將他在日本的房屋出租予一位日本人所收到的租金。
  3. 一為本港居民在一家澳洲公司的本港分行任職秘書所賺到的薪金。
  4. 一為本港居民出售其在澳洲的物業所收取的款項。

To calculate the gross national income of Hong Kong, which of the following should be added to the GDP of Hong Kong?

  1. the income earned by a Japanese chef who has worked in a Hong Kong restaurant for two months
  2. the rental received by a Hong Kong resident from leasing his house in Japan to a Japanese
  3. the salary earned by a Hong Kong resident from working as a secretary at the Hong Kong branch office of an Australian company
  4. the money received from selling an apartment in Australia by a Hong Kong resident

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這題，我們必須清楚區分 GDP (本地生產總值) 和 GNI (本地居民總收入) 的概念。

To answer this question, we must clearly distinguish between the concepts of GDP (Gross Domestic Product) and GNI (Gross National Income).

  • GDP: 以「地域」為本。只要在香港境內生產，無論生產者是否香港居民，都計算在內。
    Territory-based. Includes all production within Hong Kong's economic territory, regardless of the producer's resident status.
  • GNI: 以「居民」為本。只要是香港常住居民賺取的要素收益，無論在哪個國家賺取，都計算在內。
    Resident-based. Includes all factor incomes earned by Hong Kong residents, regardless of where they are earned.
$$ GNI = GDP + \text{Net Factor Income from Abroad (NFIA)} $$ $$ GNI = GDP + \text{Factor income from abroad} - \text{Factor income paid abroad} $$

因此，要在 GDP 加上某個項目來得出 GNI，該項目必須是「本港居民從外地賺取的要素收益」 (Factor income from abroad)。

Therefore, for an item to be added to GDP to calculate GNI, it must be "Factor income earned by residents from abroad".

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option B

Option A: Incorrect (錯誤)

日本廚師只工作了兩個月（少於一年），屬於非本港居民。他在香港賺取的收入屬於「支付予外地的要素收益」。在計算 GNI 時，這筆款項應該從 GDP 中減去，而不是加上。

The Japanese chef worked for only two months (less than a year) and is a non-resident. His income earned in HK is "factor income paid abroad". When calculating GNI, this amount should be deducted from GDP, not added.

Option B: Correct (正確)

本港居民在日本（外地）賺取租金（要素收益）。這屬於「從外地賺取的要素收益」。因此，我們必須將其加上香港的 GDP 以計算 GNI。

A HK resident earns rental income (factor income) in Japan (abroad). This is "factor income from abroad". Therefore, we must add it to HK's GDP to calculate GNI.

Option C: Incorrect (錯誤)

本港居民在香港境內工作，其薪金已經包含在香港的 GDP 內。由於他也是本港居民，這筆收入也屬於 GNI。既然已經在 GDP 內，就不需要再額外加上去，否則會重複計算。

The HK resident works within HK territory, so his salary is already included in HK's GDP. Since he is a resident, it is also part of GNI. As it's already in the GDP figure, adding it again would result in double counting.

Option D: Incorrect (錯誤)

出售物業屬於資產所有權的轉移（二手交易），並不涉及當期的生產活動，因此不屬於要素收益。這筆款項既不計入 GDP，也不計入 GNI。

Selling an apartment is a transfer of asset ownership (second-hand transaction) and does not involve current production activities. It is not a factor income. This amount is included in neither GDP nor GNI.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的關鍵點 (Pivot Point) 在於理解公式：\( GNI - GDP = \text{從外地賺取的要素收益} - \text{支付予外地的要素收益} \)。

The Pivot Point of this type of question lies in understanding the formula: \( GNI - GDP = \text{Factor income from abroad} - \text{Factor income paid abroad} \).

題目問「加上」什麼，即尋找「從外地賺取的要素收益」。你需要檢查每個選項的三個條件：

  1. 誰賺的？ 必須是「本港常住居民」。(排除 A)
  2. 在哪裡賺？ 必須是「香港以外的地方」。(排除 C)
  3. 賺什麼？ 必須是「要素收益」（工資、租金、利息、利潤），不能是資產轉移。(排除 D)

The question asks what to "add", meaning we are looking for "factor income from abroad". You need to check three conditions for each option: 1. Earned by whom? (Must be a HK resident). 2. Earned where? (Must be outside HK). 3. What is earned? (Must be factor income, not asset transfer).

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 陷阱一：混淆「國籍」與「常住居民」。 經濟學上，只要在該地居住或打算居住超過 12 個月，就是常住居民。選項 A 強調「兩個月」，就是為了告訴你他不是居民。
    Trap 1: Confusing "Nationality" with "Resident status". In economics, a resident is someone who stays or intends to stay for more than 12 months. Option A specifies "two months" to indicate he is a non-resident.
  • 陷阱二：忘記檢查是否為「生產活動」。 看到外地賺錢就選，忘記了選項 D 是賣樓（資產轉移），根本不計入國民收入。
    Trap 2: Forgetting to check if it's a "productive activity". Option D is selling an apartment (asset transfer), which is never included in national income.

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼選項 C 不用加？他是在「澳洲公司」工作啊？
Q: Why don't we add Option C? He works for an "Australian company"!

A: 因為他是在該公司的「本港分行」工作！生產活動發生在香港境內，所以這筆薪金已經被計算在香港的 GDP 裡面了。如果我們再把它加上去，就會變成重複計算 (Double counting)。
A: Because he works at the "Hong Kong branch"! The production activity takes place within HK territory, so this salary is already calculated in HK's GDP. If we add it again, it would be double counting.

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

「三步秒殺法」 (3-Step Quick Kill):

題目問：\( GDP \rightarrow GNI \) (要加什麼？)

  1. 找主角：必須是「本港居民」 \(\rightarrow\) 劃走 A。
  2. 找地點：必須在「外地」發生 \(\rightarrow\) 劃走 C (本港分行)。
  3. 找性質：必須是「要素收益 (薪/租/息/潤)」 \(\rightarrow\) 劃走 D (出售物業是資產轉移)。

答案 B 瞬間浮現！

Shortcut: Question asks what to add to GDP to get GNI. 1. Must be "HK resident" (Eliminate A). 2. Must happen "abroad" (Eliminate C). 3. Must be "Factor Income" (Eliminate D). Option B is the only one left!
