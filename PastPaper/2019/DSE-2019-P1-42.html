📍 Topic: Comparative Advantage & Terms of Trade (比較優勢與貿易比率)
⭐ Difficulty: 4 / 5
🔑 Keywords: Opportunity Cost, Terms of Trade, Transport Cost

1. 題目 (The Question)

下表顯示在甲、乙兩國各以1單位資源可生產物品的數量：

The following table shows the amount of goods that can be produced with 1 unit of resources in Country A and B respectively.

物品X / Good X (單位/units) 物品Y / Good Y (單位/units)
甲國 / Country A 8 或 / or 12
乙國 / Country B 2 或 / or 4

假設一單位物品X的運輸成本是0.2單位物品Y，並由出口國承擔。下列哪項是甲、乙兩國互惠互利的貿易比率？

Suppose the transportation cost for 1 unit of Good X is 0.2 units of Good Y and it is borne by the exporting country. Which of the following is a mutually beneficial terms of trade between Country A and Country B?

  • A. 1單位物品X = 1.3單位物品Y (1 unit of Good X = 1.3 units of Good Y)
  • B. 1單位物品X = 1.7單位物品Y (1 unit of Good X = 1.7 units of Good Y)
  • C. 1單位物品X = 1.8單位物品Y (1 unit of Good X = 1.8 units of Good Y)
  • D. 1單位物品X = 2.0單位物品Y (1 unit of Good X = 2.0 units of Good Y)

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

機會成本 (Opportunity Cost): 放棄了的其他選擇中，價值最高的一項。在生產可能性表（產量表）中，生產1單位某物品的機會成本是放棄另一物品的數量。

Opportunity Cost: The highest-valued option forgone. In a production table (output approach), the opportunity cost of producing 1 unit of a good is the amount of the other good given up.

$$ \text{Opportunity Cost of X} = \frac{\text{Max. Output of Y}}{\text{Max. Output of X}} $$

比較優勢 (Comparative Advantage): 如果一個國家生產某物品的機會成本低於另一個國家，該國便擁有生產該物品的比較優勢，並會出口該物品。

Comparative Advantage: If a country can produce a good at a lower opportunity cost than another country, it has a comparative advantage in producing that good and will export it.

貿易比率與運輸成本 (Terms of Trade & Transport Cost): 互惠互利的貿易比率必須介乎於兩國的機會成本之間。如果涉及運輸成本，承擔運輸成本的一方，其利潤空間會被壓縮（即出口國要求更高的最低價格，或進口國願意支付的最高價格降低）。

Terms of Trade (TOT) & Transport Cost: A mutually beneficial TOT must fall between the domestic opportunity costs of the two countries. If transport costs are involved, the party bearing the cost will face a squeezed profit margin (the exporter requires a higher minimum price, or the importer's maximum willingness to pay decreases).

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案：C

Correct Option: C

選項 A 錯誤 (Option A is Incorrect): $1.3Y$ 低於甲國的生產成本。甲國不會願意出口。

$1.3Y$ is lower than Country A's cost of production. Country A will not be willing to export.

選項 B 錯誤 (Option B is Incorrect): $1.7Y$ 剛好等於甲國的總成本（生產 $1.5Y$ + 運輸 $0.2Y$）。在此比率下，甲國只能做到「收支平衡」，並沒有在貿易中獲得好處。互惠互利意味著雙方都必須獲益。

$1.7Y$ is exactly equal to Country A's total cost (Production $1.5Y$ + Transport $0.2Y$). At this rate, Country A only breaks even and gains nothing from trade. "Mutually beneficial" means both parties must gain strictly.

選項 C 正確 (Option C is Correct): $1.8Y$ 介乎於甲國的總成本 ($1.7Y$) 與乙國的最高願付價格 ($2.0Y$) 之間。甲國賺取 $0.1Y$ 的利潤，乙國節省 $0.2Y$ 的成本，雙方互惠互利。

$1.8Y$ falls between Country A's total cost ($1.7Y$) and Country B's maximum willing-to-pay price ($2.0Y$). Country A earns a profit of $0.1Y$, and Country B saves $0.2Y$. Both parties benefit.

選項 D 錯誤 (Option D is Incorrect): $2.0Y$ 剛好等於乙國自行生產 X 的機會成本。在此比率下，乙國只能「打和」，無法從貿易中獲益。

$2.0Y$ is exactly equal to Country B's domestic opportunity cost of producing X. At this rate, Country B only breaks even and gains nothing from trade.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

這類題目的「關鍵點」在於先找出誰出口物品X，然後正確地將運輸成本加到相應的邊界上。步驟如下：

The "Pivot Point" of this type of question is to first identify who exports Good X, and then correctly add the transportation cost to the appropriate boundary. The steps are:

  1. 計算機會成本 (Calculate Opportunity Cost):
    甲國生產 1X 的成本 = $12 / 8 = 1.5Y$
    乙國生產 1X 的成本 = $4 / 2 = 2.0Y$

    Cost of 1X in Country A = $12 / 8 = 1.5Y$
    Cost of 1X in Country B = $4 / 2 = 2.0Y$

  2. 決定出口國 (Determine the Exporter):
    因為甲國生產X的成本較低 ($1.5Y < 2.0Y$)，所以甲國擁有比較優勢，甲國出口X。

    Since Country A has a lower cost for X ($1.5Y < 2.0Y$), it has a comparative advantage. Country A exports X.

  3. 處理運輸成本 (Handle Transportation Cost):
    題目指明運輸成本由「出口國」(即甲國) 承擔。因此，甲國賣出1X的「底價」必須涵蓋其生產成本與運輸成本。
    甲國最低要求 = 生產成本 ($1.5Y$) + 運輸成本 ($0.2Y$) = $1.7Y$

    The question states the "exporting country" (Country A) bears the transport cost. Therefore, Country A's minimum acceptable price must cover both production and transport costs.
    Country A's min. requirement = Production ($1.5Y$) + Transport ($0.2Y$) = $1.7Y$

  4. 找出互惠互利區間 (Find the Mutually Beneficial Range):
    乙國作為進口國，其最高願付價格就是自己生產的成本 ($2.0Y$)。
    因此，貿易比率 (TOT) 必須滿足：

    As the importer, Country B's maximum willing-to-pay price is its domestic cost ($2.0Y$).
    Thus, the Terms of Trade (TOT) must satisfy:

    $$ 1.7Y < \text{TOT of 1X} < 2.0Y $$

    在此區間內唯一符合的選項是 1.8Y (選項 C)。

    The only option that strictly falls within this range is 1.8Y (Option C).

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

🚨 陷阱一：扣錯運輸成本
學生常死記硬背「將運輸成本除二」或「隨便加減」。必須看清楚題目要求！如果運輸成本由進口國承擔，進口國願意付給出口國的最高價格就會下降（因為進口國要預留資源自己付運費）。

🚨 Trap 1: Misallocating Transport Cost
Students often blindly memorize "divide transport cost by two" or add/subtract randomly. Read carefully! If the transport cost was borne by the importer, the maximum price the importer is willing to pay the exporter would decrease (as they must reserve resources to pay for shipping themselves).

🚨 陷阱二：忽略「互惠互利」的嚴格定義
很多同學會選 B 或 D，以為邊界值也算。在經濟學考試中，「互惠互利」(Mutually beneficial) 通常暗示雙方都有淨得益 (Strictly positive gain)。邊界值只能算打和 (Break-even)。

🚨 Trap 2: Ignoring the Strict Definition of "Mutually Beneficial"
Many students might choose B or D, thinking boundary values count. In economics exams, "mutually beneficial" typically implies both parties have a strictly positive gain. Boundary values represent a break-even point where there is no incentive to trade.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 如果題目說運輸成本由「進口國」承擔，答案會變成什麼？

Q: What if the question said the transportation cost is borne by the "importing country"?

A: 這樣的話，甲國（出口國）的最低要求維持在 $1.5Y$。但乙國（進口國）要自己付 $0.2Y$ 的運費，所以乙國最多只願意付 $2.0Y - 0.2Y = 1.8Y$ 給甲國購買物品X。互惠區間會變成 $1.5Y < \text{TOT} < 1.8Y$。這時 1.7Y 就會是正確答案！

A: In that case, Country A's (exporter) minimum requirement remains at $1.5Y$. However, Country B (importer) has to pay the $0.2Y$ transport cost itself, so it is only willing to pay a maximum of $2.0Y - 0.2Y = 1.8Y$ to Country A for Good X. The mutually beneficial range would shift to $1.5Y < \text{TOT} < 1.8Y$. In this scenario, 1.7Y would be the correct answer!

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

⚡ 數線畫圖法 (The Number Line Method)

When you encounter these questions, don't keep the numbers in your head. Draw a simple number line during the exam!

  • 左端點：出口國的機會成本 ($1.5$)
  • 右端點：進口國的機會成本 ($2.0$)
  • 如果是出口國付運費，把左端點向右推 (+0.2 $\rightarrow$ 1.7)。區間變窄為 $[1.7, 2.0]$。
  • 一眼就能看出，只有 1.8 落在這個被壓縮的區間中間。
  • Left boundary: Exporter's OC ($1.5$)
  • Right boundary: Importer's OC ($2.0$)
  • If Exporter pays transport: Push the left boundary to the right (+0.2 $\rightarrow$ 1.7). The range narrows to $[1.7, 2.0]$.
  • At a glance, only 1.8 falls comfortably within this squeezed range.
