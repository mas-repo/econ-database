主題 (Topic): 收入分配與洛倫茨曲線 (Income Distribution and Lorenz Curve)

難度 (Difficulty): ★★★☆☆

關鍵字 (Key Concepts): 洛倫茨曲線 (Lorenz Curve), 收入不均 (Income Inequality), 累進稅 (Progressive Tax), 收入重分配 (Income Redistribution)

📝 題目 (The Question)

下列哪項政策會使香港的洛倫茨曲線有以上的轉變？

A. 減低利得稅率
B. 減少薪俸稅免稅額
C. 提高薪俸稅的累進程度
D. 增加機場離境稅

Which of the following policies would result in the above change in the Lorenz curve in Hong Kong?

A. lowering the profits tax rates
B. reducing the salaries tax allowance
C. increasing the progressivity of the salaries tax
D. raising the air passenger departure tax

🧠 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

1. 洛倫茨曲線 (Lorenz Curve)

洛倫茨曲線用作量度一個經濟體內的收入分佈狀況。圖中的對角線稱為絕對平均線 (Line of Perfect Equality)。當洛倫茨曲線越靠近絕對平均線，代表收入分佈越平均；相反，越遠離對角線，代表收入越不均。

The Lorenz curve is used to measure the income distribution within an economy. The diagonal line in the graph is called the Line of Perfect Equality. When the Lorenz curve shifts closer to the diagonal line, it means income distribution has become more equal; conversely, shifting away means higher income inequality.

2. 稅制與收入分配 (Taxation and Income Distribution)

  • 累進稅 (Progressive Tax): 高收入人士的平均稅率較高。這有助縮窄貧富懸殊，使洛倫茨曲線向內移（靠近對角線）。
    Higher-income individuals face a higher average tax rate. This helps narrow the wealth gap, shifting the Lorenz curve inwards (closer to the diagonal).
  • 累退稅 (Regressive Tax): 低收入人士的平均稅率較高（通常是定額的間接稅，如銷售稅或離境稅）。這會加劇貧富懸殊，使洛倫茨曲線向外移。
    Lower-income individuals face a higher average tax rate (usually flat-rate indirect taxes, like sales tax or departure tax). This worsens the wealth gap, shifting the Lorenz curve outwards.

3. 數學表達 (Mathematical Expression)

堅尼系數 (Gini Coefficient) 亦可從洛倫茨曲線推導出來。若 \( A \) 為對角線與洛倫茨曲線之間的面積，\( B \) 為洛倫茨曲線下方的面積：

The Gini Coefficient can also be derived from the Lorenz curve. If \( A \) is the area between the line of perfect equality and the Lorenz curve, and \( B \) is the area under the Lorenz curve:
$$ \text{Gini Coefficient} = \frac{A}{A + B} $$

圖中新洛倫茨曲線的面積 \( A \) 減少了，即堅尼系數下降，收入更平均。

In the graph, the area \( A \) for the new Lorenz curve has decreased, meaning the Gini coefficient has dropped, and income is more equal.

✅ 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): C

A. 錯誤 (Incorrect) 減低利得稅率 (lowering the profits tax rates)

利得稅主要由企業擁有人（通常為較高收入群體）繳交。減低利得稅率會增加高收入人士的稅後收入，擴大貧富懸殊，令洛倫茨曲線向外移（遠離對角線）。

Profits tax is primarily paid by business owners (usually higher-income groups). Lowering profits tax rates increases the post-tax income of the rich, widening the wealth gap and shifting the Lorenz curve outwards (away from the diagonal).

B. 錯誤 (Incorrect) 減少薪俸稅免稅額 (reducing the salaries tax allowance)

免稅額是指納稅人不用繳稅的部分。減少免稅額會令原本不用交稅的低收入人士跌入稅網，同時加重中低收入人士的稅務負擔（相對於其總收入的比例）。這具有累退效應，會令洛倫茨曲線向外移。

Tax allowance is the portion of income exempt from taxation. Reducing the tax allowance drags low-income earners into the tax net and increases the tax burden (as a proportion of total income) for lower-middle income earners. This has a regressive effect, shifting the Lorenz curve outwards.

C. 正確 (Correct) 提高薪俸稅的累進程度 (increasing the progressivity of the salaries tax)

提高累進程度意味著高收入人士需要繳交佔其收入更高比例的稅款。這能有效地將財富從高收入階層重新分配，縮窄稅後收入差距，令洛倫茨曲線向內移（靠近對角線），與題目圖表吻合。

Increasing progressivity means high-income earners must pay a larger proportion of their income as tax. This effectively redistributes wealth from higher-income groups, narrowing the post-tax income gap and shifting the Lorenz curve inwards (closer to the diagonal), matching the graph.

D. 錯誤 (Incorrect) 增加機場離境稅 (raising the air passenger departure tax)

機場離境稅是定額的間接稅。對於低收入人士來說，這筆定額稅款佔其收入的百分比，比高收入人士的更高。因此，這是一種累退稅 (Regressive tax)，會令收入分配更不均，洛倫茨曲線向外移。

Air passenger departure tax is a flat-rate indirect tax. For low-income earners, this fixed amount takes up a larger percentage of their income compared to high-income earners. Therefore, it is a regressive tax, making income distribution more unequal and shifting the Lorenz curve outwards.

🔍 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

破題關鍵 (Pivot Point): 第一步必須觀察圖表的變化方向。圖中的「新洛倫茨曲線」比「舊洛倫茨曲線」更貼近 45 度角的對角線。這代表「稅後收入差距縮窄」或「收入變得更平均」。

Pivot Point: The first step is to observe the direction of the shift. The "New Lorenz curve" is closer to the 45-degree diagonal line than the "Old Lorenz curve". This indicates that the "post-tax income gap has narrowed" or "income has become more equal".

一旦確定了目標是尋找「令收入更平均」的政策，我們只需逐個檢驗選項的「劫富濟貧」效果：

  • 幫富人省錢的政策 ➔ 不均 (A錯)
  • 向所有人收相同定額稅 ➔ 窮人負擔重 ➔ 不均 (D錯)
  • 向富人收更高比例的稅 ➔ 平均 (C對)
Once we established the goal is to find a policy that "equalizes income", we simply evaluate each option's redistributive effect:
  • Policy saving money for the rich ➔ Unequal (A is wrong)
  • Flat tax for everyone ➔ Heavier burden on the poor ➔ Unequal (D is wrong)
  • Taxing the rich at a higher proportion ➔ Equal (C is correct)

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱：誤解「免稅額 (Tax Allowance)」的作用

很多同學會誤以為「減少免稅額」是針對富人，因為富人交多了稅。但實際上，免稅額是保護基層市民免跌入稅網的重要防線！

假設免稅額由 \( \$130,000 \) 減至 \( \$100,000 \)：
原本賺 \( \$120,000 \) 的基層市民本來交 \( \$0 \) 稅，現在卻要開始交稅，其稅後收入顯著減少（相對其總收入）。相反，對於百萬富翁來說，這幾萬元的免稅額減少對其龐大的稅後收入影響微乎其微。因此，減少免稅額具有強烈的累退效應，會加劇貧富懸殊！

Trap: Misunderstanding the role of "Tax Allowance"

Many students mistakenly think "reducing tax allowance" targets the rich because the rich pay more absolute tax. In reality, the tax allowance is a crucial defense line preventing grassroots citizens from falling into the tax net!

Suppose the allowance drops from \( \$130,000 \) to \( \$100,000 \):
A low-income worker earning \( \$120,000 \), who originally paid \( \$0 \), now has to pay tax, significantly reducing their post-tax income (relative to their total income). Conversely, for a millionaire, this small reduction in allowance has a negligible impact on their massive post-tax income. Therefore, reducing the tax allowance has a strong regressive effect and worsens wealth inequality!

🙋‍♂️ 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

學生問 (Student): 「為何『增加機場離境稅 (D)』不是正確答案？富人才會經常坐飛機去旅行，這不是應該向富人抽更多稅嗎？」

Question: "Why is 'raising the air passenger departure tax (D)' not correct? Only rich people travel frequently by air, shouldn't this tax the rich more?"

老師答 (Teacher): 這是一個非常經典的 DSE 謬誤！在經濟學中評估稅制的累進/累退性時，我們看的是「稅款佔總收入的百分比」，而不是絕對交稅的次數或金額。
假設離境稅加至每次 \( \$200 \)。一個月入 \( \$10,000 \) 的基層市民如果因急事坐飛機，這 \( \$200 \) 佔他收入的 \( 2\% \)。但對於一個月入 \( \$100,000 \) 的富人，這 \( \$200 \) 只佔他收入的 \( 0.2\% \)。既然低收入人士要承受較高的平均稅率（佔收入比例），這種定額間接稅就被定義為累退稅 (Regressive tax)，它會令貧富懸殊惡化。

Answer: This is a classic DSE fallacy! In economics, when assessing the progressivity/regressivity of a tax, we look at "the tax paid as a percentage of total income", not the absolute amount or frequency.
Assume the departure tax is raised to \( \$200 \). If a low-income citizen earning \( \$10,000 \)/month has to fly for an emergency, this \( \$200 \) is \( 2\% \) of their income. But for a rich person earning \( \$100,000 \)/month, it's only \( 0.2\% \) of their income. Since the low-income earner bears a higher average tax rate (as a proportion of income), this flat-rate indirect tax is defined as a Regressive tax, which worsens income inequality.

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

速解法 (Shortcut): 當看到洛倫茨曲線向內移（靠近絕對平均線），你的大腦只需馬上浮現四個字：「劫富濟貧」。

  1. 掃描選項尋找「對富人不利、對窮人有利」或「對富人徵收更高比例」的字眼。
  2. 所有「定額稅」(如牌照費、離境稅、銷售稅) 都是累退的，會令曲線外移 (即加劇不均) ➔ 秒殺 D。
  3. 「減少免稅額」等於把窮人拉入稅網 ➔ 秒殺 B。
  4. 利得稅減低代表老闆賺更多 ➔ 秒殺 A。
  5. 只剩下 C 是明顯向高收入開刀的政策，直接選出答案。

Shortcut: When you see the Lorenz curve shifting inwards (closer to the line of perfect equality), your brain should immediately think of one concept: "Robin Hood (Taxing the rich)".

  1. Scan options for policies that disproportionately target the rich or benefit the poor.
  2. All "flat-rate taxes" (e.g., license fees, departure tax, sales tax) are regressive and shift the curve outwards ➔ Eliminate D instantly.
  3. "Reducing tax allowance" drags the poor into the tax net ➔ Eliminate B instantly.
  4. Lowering profits tax means business owners earn more ➔ Eliminate A instantly.
  5. Only C remains as an explicit policy targeting high earners. Choose C directly.
