HKDSE Economics Question Analysis

Topic: 宏觀經濟學基本概念 (Basic Macroeconomic Concepts) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: 投資 (Investment), 出口 (Exports), 貨幣供應 (Money Supply)

題目 (The Question)

下列哪項的答案有可能是負數？

A. 投資總額減投資淨額
B. 總出口減轉口
C. 貨幣供應定義三減貨幣供應定義二
D. 就業人口減失業人口

Which of the following results can be a negative number?

A. gross investment minus net investment
B. total exports minus re-exports
C. money supply (M3) minus money supply (M2)
D. employed population minus unemployed population

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解答這道題目，我們需要清楚各個宏觀經濟學變量的定義與公式：

To solve this question, we need to clearly understand the definitions and formulas of various macroeconomic variables:
$$ \text{Gross Investment} - \text{Net Investment} = \text{Depreciation (折舊)} $$ $$ \text{Total Exports} - \text{Re-exports} = \text{Domestic Exports (本地產品出口)} $$ $$ M3 - M2 = \text{Deposits at RLBs & DTCs (有限制牌照銀行及接受存款公司的存款)} $$

折舊、本地產品出口和存款餘額在定義上都必須是大於或等於零的實數，不可能為負數。而勞動人口分為就業人口與失業人口，兩者皆為人數，雖然通常就業人數大於失業人數，但在極端情況下（例如失業率高於 \(50\%\)），失業人數可以大於就業人數。

By definition, depreciation, domestic exports, and deposit balances must be real numbers greater than or equal to zero; they cannot be negative. The labour force is divided into employed and unemployed populations. While the employed population is usually larger, in extreme cases (e.g., an unemployment rate above \(50\%\)), the unemployed population can exceed the employed population.

答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): D

Option A (錯誤 Incorrect)

投資總額減投資淨額等於折舊 (Depreciation)。折舊是指固定資產的耗損，其數值必定大於或等於零 \( (\ge 0) \)。

Gross investment minus net investment equals depreciation. Depreciation refers to the wear and tear of fixed assets, and its value must be greater than or equal to zero \( (\ge 0) \).

Option B (錯誤 Incorrect)

總出口減轉口等於本地產品出口 (Domestic Exports)。出口貨值不可能為負數 \( (\ge 0) \)。

Total exports minus re-exports equals domestic exports. The value of exported goods cannot be negative \( (\ge 0) \).

Option C (錯誤 Incorrect)

貨幣供應 \(M3\) 包含 \(M2\) 以及有限制牌照銀行 (RLBs) 和接受存款公司 (DTCs) 的客戶存款。因此，\(M3 - M2\) 等於這些機構的存款總額，存款金額不可能為負數 \( (\ge 0) \)。

Money supply \(M3\) includes \(M2\) plus customer deposits at Restricted Licence Banks (RLBs) and Deposit-taking Companies (DTCs). Therefore, \(M3 - M2\) equals the total deposits in these institutions, which cannot be negative \( (\ge 0) \).

Option D (正確 Correct)

就業人口減失業人口。如果一個經濟體經歷極度嚴重的經濟衰退，導致失業率超過 \(50\%\)，那麼失業人口就會多於就業人口，相減的結果就會是負數。這在數學和邏輯上是完全可能的。

Employed population minus unemployed population. If an economy experiences an extremely severe depression causing the unemployment rate to exceed \(50\%\), the unemployed population will be larger than the employed population. The difference will then be negative. This is mathematically and logically possible.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題關鍵 (Pivot Point): 辨識出 A、B、C 三個選項實際上都是某個特定經濟變量的恆等式變形，且這些變量（折舊、本地出口、存款）在現實中具有「非負數 (Non-negative)」的物理或會計限制。而 D 選項純粹是兩個獨立群體人數的比較。

Pivot Point: Recognize that options A, B, and C are algebraic rearrangements of specific economic identities, and these resulting variables (depreciation, domestic exports, deposits) have a "non-negative" physical or accounting constraint. Option D is simply a comparison between the sizes of two independent demographic groups.

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

學生常犯的錯誤是依賴「常理」而非「定義」。在現實生活中，失業率很少超過 \(50\%\)，所以學生直覺認為「就業人口一定多於失業人口」。然而，經濟學選擇題考驗的是邏輯上的可能性 (Logical possibility)，而非日常的普遍性。

A common mistake is relying on "common sense" rather than "definitions." In reality, unemployment rarely exceeds \(50\%\), so students intuitively think "employed must always outnumber unemployed." However, Economics MCQs test logical possibility, not just everyday prevalence.

學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 投資淨額 (Net Investment) 有可能是負數嗎？
Can Net Investment be negative?

A: 可以！如果一年的折舊大於投資總額（即社會沒有補足損耗的資本），投資淨額就會是負數。但題目問的是「投資總額 - 投資淨額」，這等於折舊，折舊本身不能是負數。
Yes! If depreciation in a year is greater than gross investment, net investment will be negative. However, the question asks for "Gross Investment - Net Investment", which equals depreciation, and depreciation itself cannot be negative.

考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

消去法 (Elimination Method): 當你看到 A、B、C 都可以轉換成一個單一的經濟名詞（折舊、本地產品出口、特定存款），而這些名詞代表的是實質的數量或金額（不能小於零），你就可以迅速刪去這三個選項，直接選 D，無需過度糾結失業率是否真的能超過一半。

Elimination Method: When you see that A, B, and C can be converted into single economic terms (depreciation, domestic exports, specific deposits) representing physical quantities or amounts (which cannot be less than zero), you can quickly eliminate them and choose D. You don't need to overthink whether the unemployment rate can actually exceed half in reality.
