HKDSE Economics Analysis Guide

Topic: Factors of Production - Labour (生產要素：勞工) Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆ Labour Productivity Occupational Mobility

2. The Question (題目)

在2018-19年度財政預算案，香港政府將持續進修基金的資助上限由每位申請人$10 000提高至$20 000，以鼓勵市民繼續進修。
該政策引致將來 _________ 和 _________ 上升。
In the 2018-19 Budget, the Hong Kong Government raised the subsidy ceiling of the Continuing Education Fund from $10 000 to $20 000 per applicant to encourage members of the public to pursue further education.
This policy will lead to a rise in _________ and _________ in the future.
  • A. 勞力供應 …… 地域流動性 / labour supply …… geographical mobility
  • B. 勞力供應 …… 職業流動性 / labour supply …… occupational mobility
  • C. 勞工生產力 …… 地域流動性 / labour productivity …… geographical mobility
  • D. 勞工生產力 …… 職業流動性 / labour productivity …… occupational mobility

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

勞力供應 (Labour Supply): 指在特定時間內，所有工人願意並能夠提供的總工作時數。它受人口、勞動人口參與率及工作時數影響，但不受教育水平直接影響。
Labour Supply: The total number of man-hours all workers are willing and able to provide in a given period. It depends on population size, labour force participation rate, and working hours, but is not directly affected by education level.
$$Labour\ Supply = Number\ of\ Workers \times Working\ Hours$$
勞工生產力 (Labour Productivity): 指每單位勞力（如每工時）的平均產量。教育和培訓能提升工人的技能，從而提高生產力。
Labour Productivity: The average output per unit of labour (e.g., per man-hour). Education and training improve workers' skills, thereby increasing productivity.
$$Labour\ Productivity = \frac{Total\ Output}{Total\ Man-hours}$$
職業流動性 (Occupational Mobility): 工人轉換行業或職位的能力。擁有更多技能或更高學歷的工人，更容易轉行。
Occupational Mobility: The ability of workers to change their jobs or professions. Workers with more skills or higher education levels find it easier to switch occupations.
地域流動性 (Geographical Mobility): 工人跨地區或跨國工作的能力。這通常受交通、房屋、簽證及文化差異影響。
Geographical Mobility: The ability of workers to move across regions or countries for work. This is typically affected by transportation, housing, visas, and cultural differences.

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是：選項 D
The correct option is: Option D
❌ Option A 錯誤 (Incorrect)
進修並不會增加可用的人手或工作時數（勞力供應），亦不會改善交通網絡或降低跨區工作的成本（地域流動性）。
Further education does not increase the available workforce or working hours (labour supply). It also does not improve transportation networks or lower the cost of working across districts (geographical mobility).
❌ Option B 錯誤 (Incorrect)
雖然進修能提升「職業流動性」，但前段的「勞力供應」是錯誤的。如上所述，教育改變的是勞工的「質」，而非「量」。
Although further education improves "occupational mobility," the first part, "labour supply," is incorrect. As mentioned, education changes the "quality" of labour, not the "quantity".
❌ Option C 錯誤 (Incorrect)
進修確實能提升技能和「勞工生產力」，但後段的「地域流動性」與教育資助無關。
Further education indeed enhances skills and "labour productivity," but the second part, "geographical mobility," is unrelated to education subsidies.
✅ Option D 正確 (Correct)
進修基金鼓勵市民學習新知識。當工人的知識和技能上升，他們每工時的產出便會增加（勞工生產力上升）。同時，擁有多元技能的工人不再局限於單一行業，轉行變得更容易（職業流動性上升）。
The fund encourages learning. When workers' knowledge and skills improve, their output per man-hour increases (rise in labour productivity). Simultaneously, workers with diverse skills are no longer restricted to a single industry, making it easier to switch jobs (rise in occupational mobility).

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

  • 破題關鍵 (Pivot Point): 留意「繼續進修 (further education)」這個詞。在經濟學中，教育與培訓永遠對應人力資本 (Human Capital) 的提升，從而直接聯繫到勞工質素 (Quality of labour)。
    Pivot Point: Focus on the term "further education." In economics, education and training always correspond to an increase in human capital, directly linking to the quality of labour.
  • 干擾資訊 (Distractors): 題目中的金額「$10 000提高至$20 000」及「2018-19年度」是完全無關的細節。它們只為了說明政府提供了更多誘因，不用作任何數學計算。
    Distractors: The figures "$10 000 to $20 000" and the year "2018-19" are entirely irrelevant details. They simply illustrate that the government provided stronger incentives and are not meant for calculation.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

混淆「勞力供應」與「勞工生產力」
學生常誤以為工人變得更「能幹」，就等同於勞力供應增加。記住：勞力供應量度的是時間（工時），勞工生產力量度的是效率（每工時產出）。即使全香港工人都變成了博士，如果總工作時數不變，勞力供應量依然不變！
Confusing "Labour Supply" and "Labour Productivity"
Students often mistakenly believe that "more capable" workers mean an increase in labour supply. Remember: Labour supply measures time (man-hours), while labour productivity measures efficiency (output per man-hour). Even if all workers in HK became PhDs, if total working hours remain unchanged, the labour supply remains exactly the same!

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 既然市民去進修，這會否反而令短期的勞力供應下降？
Q: Since people are going to study, wouldn't this actually decrease the labour supply in the short term?
A: 這是一個極佳的問題！如果市民辭去全職工作去進修，短期的勞力參與率（及勞力供應）的確會下降。但請留意題目的字眼：「引致將來 (in the future)」。當他們完成進修重返職場，勞力供應會回復正常，但他們具備了更高的生產力與流動性。因此，長遠來看，政策提升的必然是生產力。
A: Excellent question! If people quit their full-time jobs to study, the short-term labour participation rate (and thus labour supply) would indeed fall. However, note the specific wording in the question: "lead to... in the future". Once they finish studying and return to the workforce, the labour supply normalizes, but they now possess higher productivity and mobility. Therefore, in the long run, productivity is the guaranteed increase.

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

MC題秒殺法 (The "Instant Kill" Method):
在HKDSE經濟科中，有些關鍵詞是存在「死板對應」的：
1. 看到 教育 / 培訓 (Education / Training) ➔ 立即尋找 生產力 (Productivity) 或 職業流動性 (Occupational Mobility)。
2. 看到 交通基建 / 高鐵 / 跨區津貼 (Transport / Infrastructure) ➔ 立即尋找 地域流動性 (Geographical Mobility)。
利用這個技巧，一看到「持續進修」，直接刪除包含地域流動性的 A 和 C，以及包含勞力供應的 B，3秒內即可鎖定 D！
The "Instant Kill" Method for MCQs:
In HKDSE Economics, certain keywords have strict, rigid pairings:
1. See Education / Training ➔ Look for Productivity or Occupational Mobility immediately.
2. See Transport infrastructure / MTR / Cross-district subsidies ➔ Look for Geographical Mobility immediately.
Using this trick, once you see "further education", instantly cross out A and C (geographical mobility) and B (labour supply). You can lock in D within 3 seconds!
