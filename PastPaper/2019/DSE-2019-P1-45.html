HKDSE Economics Analysis

Exchange Rates & Arbitrage Correction | 匯率與套戥修正

Topic: International Finance / Exchange Rates (國際金融 / 匯率)
Difficulty: ★★★★☆
Key Concepts: Law of One Price (單一價格定律), Transaction Costs (交易成本)

The Question (題目)

細閱iPhone X在香港的價格（以港元計算）與在內地的價格（以人民幣計算）。 Study the prices of iPhone X in Hong Kong in terms of Hong Kong dollar (HKD) and in the Mainland in terms of Renminbi (RMB).

香港 (Hong Kong)
$8,588 HKD
內地 (The Mainland)
¥8,316 RMB

已知匯率是 1港元兌0.875元人民幣。下列哪項陳述是正確的？ Given the exchange rate of HKD 1 = RMB 0.875, which of the following statements is correct?

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解決此類問題，我們必須將所有價格轉換為同一種貨幣進行比較。 To solve this problem, we must convert all prices into a common currency for comparison.

$$ \text{Price in HKD} = \frac{\text{Price in RMB}}{\text{Exchange Rate (RMB per HKD)}} $$

套戥條件 (Arbitrage Condition):
若 \( \text{Price Gap} > \text{Transaction Costs} \)，有利可圖。
If \( \text{Price Gap} > \text{Transaction Costs} \), profit can be made.

Step-by-Step Analysis (逐步分析)

第一步：統一貨幣 (Step 1: Unify Currency)

將內地的價格轉換為港元。 Convert the Mainland price into HKD.

$$ \text{Mainland Price (HKD)} = \frac{8316}{0.875} = 9,504 \text{ HKD} $$

第二步：比較價格 (Step 2: Compare Prices)

  • 香港價格 (HK Price): $8,588
  • 內地價格 (Mainland Price): $9,504
  • 價格差距 (Price Gap): \( 9504 - 8588 = 916 \text{ HKD} \)

結論：香港較便宜，差價為 916 港元。 Conclusion: Hong Kong is cheaper. The gap is 916 HKD.

Option Analysis (選項分析)

Option A

錯誤 (Incorrect)。 遊客傾向於在價格較低的地方購買。香港 ($8,588) 比內地 ($9,504) 便宜，遊客應在香港購買。 Travellers tend to buy where the price is lower. Since HK ($8,588) is cheaper than the Mainland ($9,504), they should buy in HK.

Option B

錯誤 (Incorrect)。 陳述指「內地價格比香港便宜」，這與計算結果相反（內地其實較貴）。 The statement claims "price in the Mainland is lower", which contradicts our calculation (Mainland is actually more expensive).

Option C (Answer)

正確 (Correct)。 目前的潛在毛利 (Price Gap) 是 916 港元。如果運費是 950 港元： The current gross profit (Price Gap) is 916 HKD. If transport cost is 950 HKD:

$$ \text{Net Profit} = 916 - 950 = -34 \text{ HKD} $$

因為成本高於潛在收益，所以在香港購買並運往內地轉售確實無法獲利。 Since the cost exceeds the potential gain, buying in HK and reselling in the Mainland will indeed yield no gain.

Option D (Trap!)

陷阱 (Logic Trap)。 這是一個邏輯陷阱。如果香港價格高於 9,504 港元（例如 10,000 港元），雖然「在香港買、內地賣」無利可圖，但交易方向會逆轉。 This is a logic trap. If the HK price rises above 9,504 HKD (e.g., to 10,000), while "buy in HK, sell in Mainland" is no longer profitable, the direction of trade reverses.

人們會改為「在內地買（9,504）、在香港賣（10,000）」。因此，「低買高賣」的潛在利益並沒有消失，只是方向改變了。 People would simply "buy in Mainland (9,504) and sell in HK (10,000)". The potential gain from "buy low, sell high" does not disappear; it just shifts direction.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ 邏輯陷阱：交易方向逆轉 (Logic Trap: Reversal of Trade)

很多學生誤選 Option D，因為他們只關注目前的交易方向（由香港賣往內地）。

記住：套戥 (Arbitrage) 是雙向的。只要兩個市場的價格不相等，就存在獲利機會。

  • \( P_{HK} < P_{Mainland} \): 買港貨，賣內地。
  • \( P_{HK} > P_{Mainland} \): 買國貨，賣香港。
  • 只有當 \( P_{HK} = P_{Mainland} \) 時，獲利機會才真正消失。

Many students mistakenly choose Option D because they focus only on the current direction of trade (HK to Mainland).
Remember: Arbitrage is bidirectional. As long as prices are not equal, an opportunity exists.
Only when \( P_{HK} = P_{Mainland} \) does the opportunity truly disappear.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼我們要將人民幣轉為港元，而不是將港元轉為人民幣？ (Why convert RMB to HKD instead of HKD to RMB?)

A: 數學上兩者皆可。但題目中的運費 (Transaction Cost) 是以港元 (HKD 950) 給出的。 為了直接比較利潤與成本，將所有價格統一為港元是最快的方法。如果你將所有價格轉為人民幣，你還必須將運費也轉為人民幣才能比較，這會多一個計算步驟。

Mathematically, both work. However, the transaction cost in the question is given in HKD (HKD 950). To directly compare profit vs. cost, converting everything to HKD is the fastest method. If you converted prices to RMB, you would also have to convert the shipping cost to RMB to compare, adding an extra step.

Q: 在 D 選項中，如果香港價格剛好等於 $9,504 港元，會發生什麼事？ (In Option D, what happens if the HK price is exactly $9,504 HKD?)

A: 如果 \( P_{HK} = P_{Mainland} = 9504 \)，那麼價格差距 (Price Gap) 為 0。 這時候，無論向哪個方向交易都無利可圖。這才是「潛在利益消失」的真正時刻。但 D 選項說的是價格「高於」9504，這意味著差價再次出現（只是內地變便宜了），所以利益依然存在。

If \( P_{HK} = P_{Mainland} = 9504 \), the Price Gap is 0. At this exact point, no profit can be made in either direction. This is when the potential gain truly disappears. However, Option D says the price is "higher than" 9504, which means a gap re-emerges (Mainland becomes cheaper), so the profit opportunity still exists.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

  • 第一步 (Step 1): 統一貨幣單位。計算出內地手機的港元價值 ($9,504)。
    Unify currency. Calculate the HKD value of the Mainland phone ($9,504).
  • 第二步 (Step 2): 計算差價 (Gap)。\( 9504 - 8588 = 916 \)。
    Calculate the Gap ($916).
  • 第三步 (Step 3): 驗證 C 選項。比較差價與運費。\( 916 < 950 \)，無利可圖。正確。
    Verify Option C. Compare Gap vs. Freight. 916 < 950, no profit. Correct.
  • 第四步 (Step 4): 驗證 D 選項。思考「如果香港價格變貴會怎樣？」。會變成由內地倒賣回香港，利潤依然存在。
    Verify Option D. Ask "What if HK gets expensive?". Trade reverses to Mainland -> HK. Profit still exists.
