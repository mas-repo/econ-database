HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: International Finance / Exchange Rates Difficulty: ★★★★☆ Key Concepts: Law of One Price, Transaction Costs

The Question (題目)

細閱iPhone X在香港的價格（以港元計算）與在內地的價格（以人民幣計算）。
Study the prices of iPhone X in Hong Kong in terms of Hong Kong dollar (HKD) and in the Mainland in terms of Renminbi (RMB).

香港 (Hong Kong) 內地 (The Mainland)
8 588 港元 (HKD) 8 316 人民幣 (RMB)

已知匯率是1港元兌0.875元人民幣，下列哪項陳述是正確的？
Given the exchange rate of HKD 1 = RMB 0.875, which of the following statements is correct?

  • A. 遊客傾向於在內地而非香港購買iPhone X。
    Travellers tend to buy an iPhone X in the Mainland instead of in Hong Kong.
  • B. 人民幣的幣值被低估，因為iPhone X在內地的價格比在香港的便宜。
    RMB is under-valued as the price of an iPhone X in the Mainland is lower than that in Hong Kong.
  • C. 若運費是950港元，在香港購買一部iPhone X並在內地轉售便再不能獲利。
    If the transportation cost is HKD 950, buying an iPhone X in Hong Kong and re-selling it in the Mainland will no longer gain.
  • D. 當本港iPhone X的價格高於9 504港元，「低買高賣」的潛在利益便會消失。
    The potential gain from ‘buy low, sell high’ will disappear when the price of iPhone X in Hong Kong is higher than HKD 9 504.

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解決此類問題，我們必須將所有價格轉換為同一種貨幣進行比較。

To solve this problem, we must convert all prices into a common currency for comparison.

1. 匯率轉換公式 (Exchange Rate Conversion):

$$ \text{Price in HKD} = \frac{\text{Price in RMB}}{\text{Exchange Rate (RMB per HKD)}} $$

2. 交易成本與套戥 (Transaction Costs & Arbitrage):

套戥利潤 = 價格差距 - 交易成本 (如運費)。
若 交易成本 > 價格差距，則無法獲利。

Arbitrage Profit = Price Gap - Transaction Costs (e.g., shipping).
If Transaction Costs > Price Gap, no profit can be made.

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案：C (Correct Option: C)

關鍵計算 (Key Calculation):
首先，將內地的價格轉換為港元。
First, convert the Mainland price into HKD.

$$ \text{Mainland Price (HKD)} = \frac{8316}{0.875} = 9504 \text{ HKD} $$

現狀比較 (Current Comparison):
香港價格 (HK Price): $8,588
內地價格 (Mainland Price): $9,504
價格差距 (Price Gap): \( 9504 - 8588 = 916 \text{ HKD} \)。
Conclusion: Hong Kong is cheaper. The gap is 916 HKD.

Option A: 錯誤 (Incorrect)

遊客會傾向在價格較低的地方購買。香港 ($8,588) 比內地 ($9,504) 便宜，遊客應在香港購買。

Travellers tend to buy where the price is lower. Since HK ($8,588) is cheaper than the Mainland ($9,504), they should buy in HK.
Option B: 錯誤 (Incorrect)

陳述指「內地價格比香港便宜」，這與計算結果相反（內地其實較貴）。

The statement claims "price in the Mainland is lower", which contradicts our calculation (Mainland is actually more expensive).
Option C: 正確 (Correct)

目前的潛在毛利 (Gross Profit) 是 \( 9504 - 8588 = 916 \) 港元。
如果運費是 950 港元，淨利潤 = \( 916 - 950 = -34 \) 港元。
因為成本高於潛在收益，所以在香港購買並運往內地轉售確實無法獲利。

The current gross profit is 916 HKD. If transport cost is 950 HKD, Net Profit = 916 - 950 = -34 HKD. Since the cost exceeds the potential gain, buying in HK and reselling in the Mainland will indeed yield no gain.
Option D: 陷阱 / 錯誤 (Trap / Incorrect)

這是一個邏輯陷阱。如果香港價格高於 9,504 港元（例如 10,000 港元），雖然「在香港買、內地賣」無利可圖，但交易方向會逆轉。
人們會改為「在內地買（9,504）、在香港賣（10,000）」。
因此，「低買高賣」的潛在利益並沒有消失，只是方向改變了。

This is a logic trap. If the HK price rises above 9,504 HKD (e.g., to 10,000), while "buy in HK, sell in Mainland" is no longer profitable, the direction of trade reverses. People would simply "buy in Mainland (9,504) and sell in HK (10,000)". The potential gain from "buy low, sell high" does not disappear; it just shifts direction.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ 邏輯陷阱：交易方向逆轉 (Logic Trap: Reversal of Trade)

很多學生誤選 Option D，因為他們只關注目前的交易方向（由香港賣往內地）。

記住：套戥 (Arbitrage) 是雙向的。只要兩個市場的價格不相等，就存在獲利機會。

  • \( P_{HK} < P_{Mainland} \): 買港貨，賣內地。
  • \( P_{HK} > P_{Mainland} \): 買國貨，賣香港。
  • 只有當 \( P_{HK} = P_{Mainland} \) 時，獲利機會才真正消失。

Many students mistakenly choose Option D because they focus only on the current direction of trade (HK to Mainland).
Remember: Arbitrage is bidirectional. As long as prices are not equal, an opportunity exists.
Only when \( P_{HK} = P_{Mainland} \) does the opportunity truly disappear.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

  • 第一步 (Step 1): 統一貨幣單位。計算出內地手機的港元價值 ($9,504)。
    Unify currency. Calculate the HKD value of the Mainland phone ($9,504).
  • 第二步 (Step 2): 計算差價 (Gap)。\( 9504 - 8588 = 916 \)。
    Calculate the Gap ($916).
  • 第三步 (Step 3): 驗證 C 選項。比較差價與運費。\( 916 < 950 \)，無利可圖。正確。
    Verify Option C. Compare Gap vs. Freight. 916 < 950, no profit. Correct.
  • 第四步 (Step 4): 驗證 D 選項。思考「如果香港價格變貴會怎樣？」。會變成由內地倒賣回香港，利潤依然存在。
    Verify Option D. Ask "What if HK gets expensive?". Trade reverses to Mainland -> HK. Profit still exists.
