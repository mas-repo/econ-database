3. 概念重溫 Concept Review

要解答這條題目，我們必須區分擴張性（Expansionary）和緊縮性（Contractionary）的宏觀經濟政策。擴張性政策的目的是刺激總需求 \( AD \)，從而增加實質本地生產總值 \( Real\ GDP \)。
To solve this question, we must distinguish between expansionary and contractionary macroeconomic policies. The goal of an expansionary policy is to stimulate Aggregate Demand \( AD \), thereby increasing Real GDP.
$$ AD = C + I + G + (X - M) $$
1. 擴張性貨幣政策 (Expansionary Monetary Policy):
中央銀行透過增加貨幣供應 \( M_s \) 來壓低利率 \( r \)，從而刺激投資 \( I \) 和消費 \( C \)。
常見工具包括：在公開市場購入債券（Open market purchase of bonds）、降低貼現率（Lowering the discount rate）或降低法定儲備比率。
1. Expansionary Monetary Policy:
The central bank increases the money supply \( M_s \) to lower the interest rate \( r \), thereby stimulating investment \( I \) and consumption \( C \).
Common tools include: Open market purchase of bonds, lowering the discount rate, or lowering the required reserve ratio.
2. 擴張性財政政策 (Expansionary Fiscal Policy):
政府透過減少稅收 \( T \) 或增加政府開支 \( G \) 來刺激經濟。增加薪俸稅免稅額會減少市民需要繳交的稅款，增加可支配收入 \( Y_d \)，從而帶動消費 \( C \) 上升。
2. Expansionary Fiscal Policy:
The government stimulates the economy by decreasing taxes \( T \) or increasing government spending \( G \). Increasing the salaries tax allowance reduces the tax burden on citizens, increasing their disposable income \( Y_d \), which drives up consumption \( C \).

4. 答案與詳細分析 The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Option: B. 只有政策 II (Policy II only)
分析 政策 I：
「購入政府債券」會增加貨幣供應，屬於擴張性 (+)；但「提高貼現率」會增加商業銀行向央行借款的成本，導致貨幣供應減少，屬於緊縮性 (-)。一加一減之下，我們無法確定淨效應，因此政策 I 整體而言不一定屬擴張性。
Analysis of Policy I:
"Purchasing government bonds" increases money supply and is expansionary (+). However, "a rise in the discount rate" increases the borrowing cost for commercial banks from the central bank, decreasing money supply, which is contractionary (-). With one positive and one negative effect, the net effect is ambiguous, so Policy I is not necessarily expansionary overall.
分析 政策 II：
「購入政府債券」是擴張性貨幣政策 (+)。「增加薪俸稅免稅額」會增加市民的可支配收入，刺激消費，屬於擴張性財政政策 (+)。兩項措施皆為擴張性，因此政策 II 必定是擴張性政策。
Analysis of Policy II:
"Purchasing government bonds" is an expansionary monetary policy (+). "A rise in the salaries tax allowance" increases citizens' disposable income and stimulates consumption, which is an expansionary fiscal policy (+). Since both measures are expansionary, Policy II is definitively expansionary.
分析 政策 III：
「增加薪俸稅免稅額」為擴張性 (+)，但「提高貼現率」為緊縮性 (-)。與政策 I 相同，一加一減令淨效應變得不確定。因此政策 III 不一定屬擴張性。
Analysis of Policy III:
"A rise in the salaries tax allowance" is expansionary (+), but "a rise in the discount rate" is contractionary (-). Similar to Policy I, the mix of expansionary and contractionary tools creates an ambiguous net effect. Therefore, Policy III is not necessarily expansionary.

5. 題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

這類題目的「破題點」（Pivot Point）在於判斷政策組合的「方向一致性」。要確認一個組合為「擴張性」，該組合內的所有政策工具必須都是擴張性的（或者題目提供了具體數據證明擴張大於緊縮）。
The Pivot Point of this type of question lies in determining the "directional consistency" of the policy combinations. To confirm a combination as "expansionary", all policy tools within that combination must be expansionary (unless specific data is provided to prove the expansion outweighs the contraction).
注意事項： 英文版本的題目只寫了 "open market operation"（公開市場操作），沒有指明是買入還是賣出；但中文版本明確指出是「購入」（purchase）。在 DSE 考試中，如果中英文版本在細節上有落差，通常以較具體的那一個（此處為中文版）作為推論基礎。購入債券即為放水，屬擴張性。
Important Note: The English version of the question ambiguously states "open market operation" without specifying buy or sell; however, the Chinese version explicitly states "purchase" (購入). In DSE exams, if there's a discrepancy in specificity between the two languages, you should base your deduction on the more specific one (the Chinese version here). Purchasing bonds injects money into the economy and is expansionary.

6. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

❌ 誤解貼現率 (Misunderstanding the Discount Rate):
很多同學會把「貼現率」與一般銀行的「存款利率」混淆，或者直覺以為「提高 = 擴張」。事實上，貼現率 \( r_d \) 是商業銀行向中央銀行借錢的利息。當 \( r_d \) 上升，商業銀行借貸成本增加，它們的貸款能力（創造存款的能力）就會下降，導致貨幣供應減少，這是緊縮性的。
❌ Misunderstanding the Discount Rate:
Many students confuse the "discount rate" with standard retail bank "deposit interest rates", or intuitively think "increase = expansion". In fact, the discount rate \( r_d \) is the interest rate central banks charge commercial banks for short-term loans. When \( r_d \) rises, borrowing costs for commercial banks increase, reducing their ability to lend (and create deposits), which decreases the money supply. This is contractionary.

7. 學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

問：為什麼政策 I 和 III 不能算作擴張性？它們明明包含了一項擴張性措施呀？
Q: Why can't Policy I and III be considered expansionary? They clearly contain one expansionary measure!
答： 因為它們同時包含了緊縮性措施。在沒有提供具體金額（例如：買入債券放水100億，但提高貼現率抽走200億）的情況下，我們無法得知最後的「淨效應」是正是負。在經濟學多項選擇題中，如果結果是「不確定 (ambiguous)」，我們就不能肯定地稱之為擴張性。
A: Because they simultaneously contain a contractionary measure. Without specific figures (e.g., injecting $10 billion via bond purchases, but draining $20 billion via a higher discount rate), we cannot determine if the final "net effect" is positive or negative. In Economics MCQs, if the outcome is "ambiguous", we cannot definitively label it as expansionary.

8. 考試技巧與速解 Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

正負號標記法 (+/- Tagging Method)：
在考試時，不需要寫一大段文字，直接在考卷上為每項措施標上 (+) 代表擴張，(-) 代表緊縮。
  • 政策 I：(+) 和 (-) ➔ ❌ 淘汰
  • 政策 II：(+) 和 (+) ➔ ✅ 保留
  • 政策 III：(+) 和 (-) ➔ ❌ 淘汰
只需 10 秒鐘就能找出只有政策 II 是純粹的正數組合，瞬間選出答案 B！
+/- Tagging Method:
During the exam, you don't need long explanations. Simply tag each measure on your question paper with a (+) for expansionary and a (-) for contractionary.
  • Policy I: (+) and (-) ➔ ❌ Eliminate
  • Policy II: (+) and (+) ➔ ✅ Keep
  • Policy III: (+) and (-) ➔ ❌ Eliminate
It takes only 10 seconds to see that only Policy II is a purely positive combination. Instantly select B!
