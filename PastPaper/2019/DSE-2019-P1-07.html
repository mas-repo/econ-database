Difficulty ★★★☆☆

題目 / The Question

新世界中國地產有限公司（股票編號：0917）宣布把公司私有化，並取消其在香港交易所的上市地位。
下列哪些是上述改變的可能好處？

New World China Land Limited (Stock Code: 0917) announced privatisation of the company and cancelled its listing status at the Hong Kong Stock Exchange.
Which of the following are the possible advantages of the above change?

(1) 該公司可以選擇不向公眾披露其財政帳目。

(1) The company can choose not to disclose its financial accounts to the public.

(2) 該公司可以減低被收購的風險。

(2) The company can reduce the risk of being taken over.

(3) 該公司可享有較低的利得稅率。

(3) The company can enjoy a lower profits tax rate.

A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

概念重溫 / Concept Review

1. 上市地位與披露要求： 根據香港交易所的上市規則，上市公司必須定期向公眾公開其財務報表（如中期及年度報告）。取消上市地位後，公司可免除這些嚴格的公眾披露責任。

1. Listing Status and Disclosure: Under HKEX rules, listed companies must regularly publish their financial statements to the public. Cancelling the listing status relieves the company of these strict public disclosure obligations.

2. 企業私有化的性質： 這裡的「私有化」是指將公司的股份從股票市場上撤回，不再讓公眾在交易所自由買賣。值得留意的是，私有化後，公司可能會變成非上市公眾有限公司 (unlisted public limited company)，而不一定是私人有限公司 (private limited company)。公眾有限公司不等於上市公司，它可能沒有上市，但仍然受到《公司條例》中對公眾公司的規範。

2. Nature of Corporate Privatisation: Here, "privatisation" means withdrawing the company's shares from the stock market so they are no longer freely traded by the public on an exchange. It is crucial to note that after privatisation, the company might become an unlisted public limited company rather than a private limited company. A public limited company is not equivalent to a listed company; it can be unlisted but is still subject to public company regulations under the Companies Ordinance.

3. 敵意收購 (Hostile Takeover)： 上市公司的股份在公開市場自由轉讓，外人可以透過大量買入股票來奪取控制權。退市後，股份轉讓受到限制，大大降低了被收購的風險。

3. Hostile Takeover: Shares of a listed company are freely transferable on the open market, allowing outsiders to seize control by buying massive amounts of shares. After delisting, share transfers are restricted, greatly reducing takeover risks.

答案與詳細分析 / The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 / Correct Option: A

分析 (1) - 正確： 上市公司受嚴格監管，必須向公眾披露財務狀況。取消上市地位（無論最終是成為私人有限公司還是非上市公眾有限公司）後，公司不再受港交所的上市規則約束，不需要向「公眾」發佈詳細的財務報表，這增加了商業機密性。

Analysis (1) - Correct: Listed companies are strictly regulated and must disclose financials to the public. After delisting (whether becoming a private limited company or an unlisted public limited company), it is no longer bound by HKEX listing rules. It does not need to publish detailed financial reports to the "general public", increasing commercial confidentiality.

分析 (2) - 正確： 上市公司的股票在交易所自由買賣，競爭對手可以透過在市場上吸納大量股票來進行敵意收購。私有化退市後，股份無法在公開市場買賣，股權變得集中，這有效地防止了公司被外來者收購的風險。

Analysis (2) - Correct: Shares of listed companies are freely traded. Competitors can launch hostile takeovers by absorbing shares in the market. After privatisation and delisting, shares cannot be traded openly, effectively preventing the risk of being taken over by outsiders.

分析 (3) - 錯誤： 企業的法律地位（例如是否上市）並不影響其適用的利得稅率。在香港，只要是有限公司（法團），其適用的標準利得稅率都是劃一的。因此，退市並不會帶來較低利得稅率的好處。

Analysis (3) - Incorrect: A company's listing status does not affect its applicable profits tax rate. In Hong Kong, the standard profits tax rate is uniform for all limited companies (corporations). Therefore, delisting does not bring the advantage of a lower tax rate.

題目拆解 / Problem Decomposition

解題關鍵 (Pivot Point)： 本題的關鍵在於區分「公司形式帶來的商業特徵」與「政府稅收政策」。學生必須清楚知道，香港的稅制對待所有法團（Corporations）都是一視同仁的。只要辨認出稅率與上市地位無關，就能迅速破解這道題。

Pivot Point: The key is to distinguish between "commercial features of business forms" and "government tax policies". Students must know that Hong Kong's tax system treats all corporations equally. Recognizing that tax rates are independent of listing status breaks the question wide open.

常犯錯誤與陷阱 / Common Mistakes & Traps

⚠️ 概念混淆陷阱： 許多學生誤以為「私有化 (Privatisation)」就等同於公司必然變成「私人有限公司 (Private Limited Company)」。實際上，即使取消了上市地位，公司仍可能是「非上市公眾有限公司 (Unlisted Public Limited Company)」。不過，無論是哪種情況，它們都不再受交易所的公眾披露規則約束，因此 (1) 依然是正確的。

⚠️ Conceptual Trap: Many students mistakenly assume that "Privatisation" means the company must become a "Private Limited Company". In reality, even after cancelling its listing status, it might remain an "Unlisted Public Limited Company". Regardless of which one it becomes, they are no longer bound by the exchange's public disclosure rules, so statement (1) remains correct.

學生常見疑問 / Student Q&A

問：既然退市有這麼多好處，為什麼當初公司要上市呢？
答：上市最大的好處是籌集資金（集資）的能力。透過發行股票給公眾，公司可以籌集大量無須償還的資金 \(C\) 用於擴張。退市後，公司只能依賴銀行貸款、保留發行利潤或向現有股東集資，集資能力會大幅下降。

Q: If delisting has so many advantages, why did the company list in the first place?
A: The biggest advantage of listing is the ability to raise capital. By issuing shares to the public, the company can raise massive amounts of non-repayable capital \(C\) for expansion. After delisting, the company must rely on bank loans, retained profits, or raising funds from existing shareholders, which significantly weakens its capital-raising ability.

考試技巧與速解 / Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

💡 排除法 (Elimination Method)：
在處理這類多項選擇題時，尋找最明顯錯誤的選項。在 DSE 經濟科中，我們知道法團利得稅率是不會因為「上市」或「非上市」而改變的。既然確定 (3) 是絕對錯誤的，我們就可以立刻排除選項 B、C 和 D。連 (1) 和 (2) 都不用深究，答案必然是 A！

💡 Elimination Method:
When tackling these MCQs, look for the most obviously incorrect statement. In DSE Economics, we know corporate profits tax rates do not change based on listing status. Once you are certain (3) is completely wrong, you can immediately eliminate options B, C, and D. You don't even need to analyze (1) and (2) deeply—the answer must be A!
