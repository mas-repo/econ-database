HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Basic Economic Problems (基本經濟問題) Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆ Key Concepts: Resource Allocation, For whom to produce
下列哪些陳述與「為誰生產」的問題有關？ Which of the following statements are related to the problem of ‘for whom to produce’?

(1) 只有長者和合資格的傷殘人士才可以享有「公共交通票價優惠計劃」的每程$2票價優惠。
(1) Only the elderly and eligible persons with disabilities can enjoy a concessionary fare of $2 per trip under the Public Transport Fare Concession Scheme.

(2) 若公共租住房屋（公屋）的住戶其家庭入息超過公屋入息限額五倍，便須遷出其公屋單位。
(2) Public rental housing (PRH) households whose family income exceeds 5 times the PRH income limits should vacate their PRH flats.

(3) 電競音樂節由香港會議展覽中心轉移到香港體育館舉行，以容納更多來自世界各地的電競迷。
(3) The e-Sports & Music Festival moved from Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre to Hong Kong Coliseum to house more fans from all over the world.

  • A. 只有 (1)、(2) (1) and (2) only
  • B. 只有 (1)、(3) (1) and (3) only
  • C. 只有 (2)、(3) (2) and (3) only
  • D. (1)、(2) 及 (3) (1), (2) and (3)
Concept Review (概念重溫)

三個基本經濟問題 (Three Basic Economic Problems):

  1. 生產甚麼 (What to produce): 涉及產品的種類和數量。 Concerns the types and quantities of goods/services to be produced.
  2. 如何生產 (How to produce): 涉及生產方法、技術或投入要素的組合（例如：勞力密集 vs 資本密集，或生產地點的選擇）。 Concerns the method of production, technology, or mix of inputs (e.g., labor-intensive vs. capital-intensive, or choice of production location).
  3. 為誰生產 (For whom to produce): 涉及產品的分配 (Distribution)。即是誰可以獲得該產品？通常取決於收入分配、價格機制或非價格分配準則（如排隊、抽籤、資格審查）。 Concerns the distribution of output. Who gets the goods? It depends on income distribution, price mechanism, or non-price allocation criteria (e.g., queuing, lucky draw, eligibility checks).

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A
Statement (1): $2 Fare Concession (2元乘車優惠) YES

分析：這項政策規定只有「長者」和「合資格傷殘人士」才能享有優惠。這是一個關於誰能獲得產品/服務（或誰能以特定價格獲得服務）的問題。這是根據年齡和身體狀況來分配資源，屬於「為誰生產」。

Analysis: This policy stipulates that only "elderly" and "eligible persons with disabilities" can enjoy the concession. This is a question of who gets the good/service (or who gets it at a specific price). It allocates resources based on age and physical status, which falls under "For whom to produce".

Statement (2): Public Housing Income Limit (公屋入息限額) YES

分析：這項政策規定若收入過高（\( \text{Income} > 5 \times \text{Limit} \)），就必須遷出。這是利用「收入水平」作為準則，來決定誰有資格居住在公屋單位內。這直接涉及最終產品（公屋單位）的分配，屬於「為誰生產」。

Analysis: This policy states that if income is too high (\( \text{Income} > 5 \times \text{Limit} \)), tenants must move out. This uses "income level" as a criterion to decide who is eligible to live in the public housing units. This directly concerns the distribution of the final good (housing), falling under "For whom to produce".

Statement (3): Venue Change (更換場地) NO

分析：主辦單位決定將地點由「會展」轉到「紅館」。這改變了生產的地點和配套，屬於生產方法的改變。雖然結果是「容納更多觀眾」（產量增加），但這主要是關於「如何生產」（地點選擇）或「生產甚麼」（產量增加）。它沒有提及門票是如何分配給觀眾的（例如是否價高者得或抽籤），因此與「為誰生產」無關。

Analysis: The organizer decided to move the venue from "HKCEC" to "HK Coliseum". This changes the location and logistics of production, which is a change in production method. Although the result is "housing more fans" (increased quantity), this is primarily about "How to produce" (choice of location) or "What to produce" (quantity). It does not mention how tickets are distributed to fans (e.g., via price mechanism or lottery), so it is unrelated to "For whom to produce".

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題關鍵點 (Pivot Point): 尋找「分配準則」(Allocation Criteria)。
Key Strategy: Look for "Allocation Criteria".

問自己：這項陳述是否在定義誰有資格獲得該物品？
Ask yourself: Does this statement define WHO is eligible to receive the good?

  • 陳述 (1) 的準則：年齡 / 傷殘狀況。 (Yes)
    Statement (1) criterion: Age / Disability status. (Yes)
  • 陳述 (2) 的準則：家庭入息水平。 (Yes)
    Statement (2) criterion: Family income level. (Yes)
  • 陳述 (3) 的重點：地點轉移 / 產量增加。 (No - 這是生產決策)
    Statement (3) focus: Location shift / Capacity increase. (No - This is a production decision)

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：混淆「目的」與「問題性質」
Trap: Confusing the "Purpose" with the "Nature of the Problem"

在陳述 (3) 中，學生看到「容納更多來自世界各地的電競迷」，可能會誤以為這是指「為這些電競迷生產」。
In statement (3), students see "to house more fans from all over the world" and might mistakenly think this means "producing FOR these fans".

糾正：只要涉及「地點」、「技術」或「投入要素」的改變，通常歸類為「如何生產」。單純增加座位數量（產量）而不涉及分配規則，則不屬於「為誰生產」。
Correction: Changes involving "location", "technology", or "inputs" are usually classified as "How to produce". Simply increasing seat capacity (quantity) without specifying allocation rules does not constitute "For whom to produce".
