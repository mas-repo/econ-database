HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Money & Banking (貨幣與銀行) Sub-topic: Money Creation (貨幣創造) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts: Money Multiplier (貨幣乘數), Money Supply (貨幣供應), Balance Sheet (資產負債表)

題目 / Question:

假設整個銀行系統都不持有超額儲備，而法定儲蓄比率是20%。若某甲從一家銀行提取$100，則：

Assume the whole banking system does not hold excess reserves and the required reserve ratio is 20%. If a person withdraws $100 from a bank,

  • A. 該銀行系統的放貸最多減少$400。
    the amount of loans made by the banks to the public will fall by at most $400.
  • B. 貨幣供應量最少減少$400。
    money supply will fall by at least $400.
  • C. 該銀行系統的存款最少減少$500。
    the amount of deposits in the banking system will fall by at least $500.
  • D. 該銀行系統的現金儲備最多減少$80。
    the amount of each cash reserve of the banks will fall by at most $80.
概念重溫 / Concept Review

1. 銀行乘數效應 (Banking Multiplier Effect):

當現金流出銀行體系（提取存款）時，會引發存款收縮。最大收縮量取決於法定儲備率 (\(r\))。

When cash leaks out of the banking system (withdrawal), it triggers a contraction in deposits. The maximum contraction depends on the required reserve ratio (\(r\)).

$$ \text{Max. Change in Deposits} (\Delta D) = \Delta \text{Reserves} (\Delta R) \times \frac{1}{r} $$

2. 貨幣供應定義 (Money Supply Definition):

貨幣供應 (\(M_s\)) = 公眾持有的現金 (\(C_p\)) + 存款 (\(D\))。

Money Supply (\(M_s\)) = Cash held by public (\(C_p\)) + Deposits (\(D\)).

$$ \Delta M_s = \Delta C_p + \Delta D $$

答案與詳細分析 / Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 / Correct Option: A

計算步驟 / Calculation Steps:

  • 法定儲備率 (Required Reserve Ratio, \(r\)): 20% = 0.2
  • 銀行乘數 (Banking Multiplier, \(k\)): \(1 / 0.2 = 5\)
  • 初始變動 (Initial Change):
    • 公眾持有現金 (\(C_p\)): +$100 (提取 / withdrawal)
    • 銀行儲備 (\(R\)): -$100
  • 最大存款變動 (Max. Change in Deposits, \(\Delta D\)): $$ \Delta D = -100 \times 5 = -500 $$
  • 最大放貸變動 (Max. Change in Loans, \(\Delta L\)): $$ \Delta L = \Delta D - \Delta R = -500 - (-100) = -400 $$
  • 最大貨幣供應變動 (Max. Change in \(M_s\)): $$ \Delta M_s = \Delta C_p + \Delta D = +100 - 500 = -400 $$
Option A: 正確 / Correct

該銀行系統的放貸最多減少$400。 / The amount of loans made by the banks to the public will fall by at most $400.

由於存款最大減少 $500，而儲備減少了 $100，根據資產負債表平衡原則，放貸必然減少 $400（這是最大收縮幅度）。如果過程未完成，減少量會少於 $400。因此，「最多減少 $400」是正確的描述。

Since deposits fall by a maximum of $500 and reserves fall by $100, loans must fall by $400 to balance the books (this is the maximum contraction). If the process is incomplete, loans fall by less than $400. Thus, "fall by at most $400" is correct.

Option B: 錯誤 / Incorrect

貨幣供應量最少減少$400。 / Money supply will fall by at least $400.

貨幣供應量的最大跌幅是 $400（計算：\(+100 - 500\)）。如果收縮過程未完成，跌幅會小於 $400（例如只跌 $200）。因此應該是「最多」減少 $400，而非「最少」。

The maximum fall in money supply is $400 (Calc: \(+100 - 500\)). If the contraction is partial, the fall would be smaller than $400 (e.g., only drops by $200). So it should be "at most", not "at least".

Option C: 錯誤 / Incorrect

該銀行系統的存款最少減少$500。 / The amount of deposits in the banking system will fall by at least $500.

存款的最大潛在跌幅是 $500。實際跌幅介乎 $100 至 $500 之間。因此應該是「最多」減少 $500。

The maximum potential fall in deposits is $500. The actual fall is between $100 and $500. Therefore, it should be "at most" $500.

Option D: 錯誤 / Incorrect

該銀行系統的現金儲備最多減少$80。 / The amount of each cash reserve of the banks will fall by at most $80.

當客戶提取 $100 現金時，這 $100 實實在在地離開了銀行金庫。銀行系統的實際儲備剛好減少了 $100，而不是 $80。

When the customer withdraws $100, that $100 physically leaves the bank vault. The banking system's actual reserves fall by exactly $100, not $80.

題目拆解 / Problem Decomposition

關鍵點 (The Pivot Point): 不要忘記 \(M_s\) 的定義！

很多學生只計算了存款 (\(D\)) 的變動，而忘記了公眾持有的現金 (\(C_p\)) 增加了。提取現金是一個「資產轉移」的過程：現金從銀行轉到公眾手中。

Key Insight: Don't forget the definition of \(M_s\)! Many students calculate the change in Deposits (\(D\)) but forget that Cash held by the public (\(C_p\)) has increased. Withdrawal is an asset transfer: cash moves from the bank to the public.

文字陷阱 (Word Trap): 「最多 (At most)」 vs 「最少 (At least)」

在收縮過程中，乘數效應計算出來的數值通常是上限 (Upper Bound)。因此，正確的描述通常涉及「最多減少...」。

In the contraction process, the calculated multiplier value is usually the Upper Bound. Therefore, the correct description usually involves "falls by at most...".

學生常見疑問 / Student Q&A

Q: 為什麼放貸減少不是 $500？ (Why isn't the fall in loans $500?)

A: 存款減少了 $500，這代表銀行少了 $500 的資金來源。但是，銀行不需要減少 $500 的放貸，因為這 $500 裡面，有 $100 本來就是銀行不再需要持有的法定儲備（因為存款少了，需要的儲備也少了）。銀行只需要追回 $400 的貸款來填補資金缺口。

Deposits fell by $500, meaning the bank lost $500 in funding. However, the bank doesn't need to cut loans by $500. Out of that $500 drop, $100 represents required reserves the bank no longer needs to hold (since deposits are lower). The bank only needs to recall $400 in loans to balance the books.

Q: 題目說「不持有超額儲備」，這有什麼重要性？ (Why is "no excess reserves" important?)

A: 這意味著銀行一開始是「借盡」的。當儲備減少 $100 時，銀行立刻出現儲備不足 (Reserve shortage)，必須馬上收回貸款，從而啟動乘數收縮過程。

This means the bank is fully loaned out initially. When reserves fall by $100, the bank immediately faces a reserve shortage and must recall loans, triggering the multiplier contraction process.

考試技巧與速解 / Exam Tricks

速算法 (Quick Check):

看到「提取現金 (Withdrawal)」，直接記住以下規律：

  1. 存款 (\(D\))： 大幅減少 (乘數效應)。
  2. 貨幣供應 (\(M_s\))： 減少，但幅度小於存款 (因為 \(+\text{Cash}\) 抵銷了部分跌幅)。
  3. 公式： \(\Delta M_s = \Delta \text{Loans}\)。

在這個題目中：放貸減少 $400 \rightarrow M_s$ 減少 $400。這是一個恆等式 (Identity)。

Shortcut: When you see "Withdrawal", remember:

  1. Deposits (\(D\)): Drop significantly (Multiplier effect).
  2. Money Supply (\(M_s\)): Drops, but less than deposits (because \(+\text{Cash}\) offsets some drop).
  3. Formula: \(\Delta M_s = \Delta \text{Loans}\).

In this question: Loans drop $400 \rightarrow M_s$ drops $400. This is an identity.
