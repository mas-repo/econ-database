HKDSE Economics Tutorial

Topic: 廠商的生產 (Production) - 規模經濟 Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆
Key Concepts: 內部規模經濟 (Internal Economies of Scale), 外部規模經濟 (External Economies of Scale), 聚集經濟 (Agglomeration Economies)

2. 題目 (The Question)

港島東的太安樓是著名的「為食天堂」。那裡很多小店售賣各式街頭小吃，如車仔麵、雞蛋仔、餃子和芒果糯米糍。來自不同地區的顧客均在太安樓大飽口福。

根據上述資料，太安樓的街頭小吃店能 ___________。

  • A. 享有內部規模經濟
  • B. 享有外部規模經濟
  • C. 承受內部規模負經濟
  • D. 承受外部規模負經濟

Tai On Building in eastern Hong Kong Island is well-known as a 'foodie paradise'. A variety of street snacks such as cart noodles, egg waffles, dumplings and mango mochi are sold by numerous small vendors. Customers from different districts fill their stomachs at Tai On Building.

Based on the above information, street snack vendors at Tai On Building can ___________.

  • A. enjoy internal economies of scale
  • B. enjoy external economies of scale
  • C. suffer internal diseconomies of scale
  • D. suffer external diseconomies of scale

3. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

規模經濟 (Economies of Scale)

規模經濟是指當產量 \(Q\) 增加時，長期平均成本 \(LRAC\) 下降的現象。

Economies of scale refer to the phenomenon where long-run average cost \(LRAC\) decreases as output \(Q\) increases.

$$LRAC = \frac{LRTC}{Q}$$ (LRTC = 長期總成本 Long Run Total Cost, Q = 產量 Quantity)

1. 內部規模經濟 (Internal Economies of Scale)

由個別廠商自身的生產規模擴大而引起的平均成本下降（例如：大量採購獲得折扣、引入大型專業機器）。

A decrease in average costs resulting from the expansion of the individual firm's own scale of production (e.g., bulk buying discounts, using specialized machinery).

2. 外部規模經濟 (External Economies of Scale)

由整個行業或地區的擴張（廠商聚集）而引起的平均成本下降，即使個別廠商的規模沒有擴大也能受惠。例如太安樓的食店聚集，建立了「為食天堂」的良好聲譽，吸引大量跨區顧客，為所有小店帶來額外客源，無形中降低了每位顧客的平均宣傳成本。

A decrease in average costs resulting from the expansion of the entire industry or region (agglomeration of firms), benefiting individual firms even if their own scale hasn't grown. For example, the cluster of food stalls at Tai On Building builds a "foodie paradise" reputation, attracting cross-district customers. This brings extra foot traffic to all small vendors, implicitly lowering the average marketing cost per customer.

4. 答案與詳細分析 (Answer & Detailed Analysis)

CORRECT 正確 選項 B (Option B): 享有外部規模經濟 (enjoy external economies of scale)

分析： 題目指出太安樓有「很多小店」聚集，並形成了「為食天堂」的著名地標。這種同類廠商集中在同一地點的現象（聚集經濟），吸引了「來自不同地區的顧客」。這意味著即使個別食店的規模很小，它們也能夠從整個地區建立的聲譽和龐大的客流量中獲益，從而降低平均成本或增加利潤空間。因此，這是外部規模經濟的標準例子。

Analysis: The question states there are "numerous small vendors" clustered together, forming a well-known "foodie paradise". This concentration of similar firms in one location (agglomeration economies) attracts "customers from different districts". This implies that even though individual stalls are small, they benefit from the regional reputation and massive foot traffic generated by the group, lowering average costs or increasing profitability. Thus, this is a classic example of external economies of scale.

WRONG 錯誤 選項 A (Option A): 享有內部規模經濟 (enjoy internal economies of scale)

分析： 內部規模經濟的前提是「個別廠商自身」擴充規模（例如小店變成大型連鎖集團）。但題目特別強調它們是「很多小店」(small vendors)，並沒有提及任何一間店鋪擴大了自身的生產規模，因此無法得出它們享有內部規模經濟的結論。

Analysis: The prerequisite for internal economies of scale is the expansion of the "individual firm's own" scale (e.g., a small shop growing into a large restaurant chain). However, the question specifically highlights them as "numerous small vendors" and mentions nothing about any single shop expanding its own production scale. Hence, we cannot conclude they enjoy internal economies of scale.

WRONG 錯誤 選項 C & D (Options C & D): 承受內部/外部規模負經濟 (suffer internal/external diseconomies of scale)

分析： 「負經濟」(Diseconomies) 意味著規模擴張導致了效率下降或平均成本上升（例如管理困難、交通擠塞、租金暴漲）。題目描述的是一個非常正面的情況——「為食天堂」吸引了大量顧客來「大飽口福」，這是帶來好處的現象，並沒有提及任何負面影響或成本上升的跡象。

Analysis: "Diseconomies" imply that expansion leads to decreased efficiency or increased average costs (e.g., management difficulties, traffic congestion, skyrocketing rent). The situation described is highly positive—a "foodie paradise" attracting crowds to "fill their stomachs." This is a beneficial phenomenon, and there are no signs of negative impacts or rising costs mentioned in the text.

5. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

  • 關鍵字 / 破題點 (The Pivot Point):
    「很多小店」(numerous small vendors) + 「著名的為食天堂」(well-known foodie paradise) + 「來自不同地區的顧客」(customers from different districts)。
    這三個線索結合起來，告訴你：主角沒有變大（排除內部），但他們因為「聚集在一起」而獲得了整體名氣帶來的龐大客源好處（確認外部）。
  • 干擾資訊 (Distractors):
    「車仔麵、雞蛋仔、餃子和芒果糯米糍」這些具體的小吃名稱只是為了豐富題目背景，對解答經濟學概念毫無影響，考試時可以直接略過。

6. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

誤區：看到「客量多 / 賺大錢」就選「內部規模經濟」。

很多同學以為只要廠商生意好、顧客多，就是「內部規模經濟」。這是一個極大的誤解！
必須緊記：內部 (Internal) 看的是廠商自身的體積/產能有沒有變大（例如：一間店擴張成五間分店，買了大型機器）。如果廠商維持「小店」規模，但因為身處的地點好、同業多而受惠，這必定是外部 (External)。

Trap: Choosing "Internal Economies of Scale" just because "business is good / there are many customers".
Many students mistakenly believe that as long as a firm is doing well with many customers, it implies "internal economies of scale." This is a huge misconception!
Remember: Internal focuses on whether the firm's own physical size/capacity has expanded (e.g., one shop expanding to five branches, buying large machines). If the firm remains a "small vendor" but benefits from a great location and a cluster of peers, it must be External.

7. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 如果題目加上一句「太安樓的業主因為該處人流增加，大幅加租」，答案會改變嗎？

A: 會的！如果因為廠商過度聚集，導致對該區資源（如舖位）的競爭加劇，從而推高了租金（生產成本），這就成為了外部規模負經濟 (External Diseconomies of Scale)。這時選項 D 就可能成為正確答案。

Q: If the question added, "The landlord of Tai On Building significantly raised the rent due to the increased foot traffic," would the answer change?
A: Yes! If the over-concentration of firms leads to intensified competition for local resources (like shop spaces), thereby driving up rent (production costs), this becomes External Diseconomies of Scale. In that case, Option D might become the correct answer.

8. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

在 DSE 經濟科 MC 卷中，處理「規模經濟」題目時，請使用「主體排除法」：

  1. 先看主體： 題目描述的是「一間大公司 (One large firm)」還是「一群小公司/整個地區 (A group of small firms / a region)」？
    • 若是前者 ➔ 鎖定內部 (Internal)。
    • 若是後者 ➔ 鎖定外部 (External)。
  2. 再看結果： 情況是「正面 / 成本下降」還是「負面 / 成本上升」？
    • 若是正面 ➔ 鎖定經濟 (Economies)。
    • 若是負面 ➔ 鎖定負經濟 (Diseconomies)。

套用在本題：主體是「很多小店 (一群)」➔ 外部；結果是「大飽口福 (正面)」➔ 經濟。秒殺答案：外部規模經濟。

In DSE Economics MC papers, when tackling "Economies of Scale" questions, use the "Subject Elimination Method":
1. Identify the Subject: Is the question about "One large firm" or "A group of small firms/a region"? (Former -> Internal; Latter -> External).
2. Identify the Outcome: Is the situation positive (cost down) or negative (cost up)? (Positive -> Economies; Negative -> Diseconomies).
Applied here: Subject is "numerous small vendors" (External); Outcome is "foodie paradise/beneficial" (Economies). Instant answer: External economies of scale.
