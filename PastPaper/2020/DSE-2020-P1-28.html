📝 DSE Economics Tutor Analysis

Topic (課題): Money Demand / 貨幣需求

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)

Key Concepts (核心概念): Asset Demand for Money (資產貨幣需求), Transaction Demand for Money (交易貨幣需求), Opportunity Cost (機會成本), Shifts vs. Movements (整條曲線移動與沿線移動)

The Question (題目)

下圖顯示某經濟的資產貨幣需求。下列哪項會引致以上的資產貨幣需求轉變？

The following diagram shows the asset demand for money of an economy. Which of the following will cause the above change in asset demand for money?

  • A. 名義利率增加。
    The nominal interest rate increases.
  • B. 貨幣基礎收縮。
    There is a contraction of monetary base.
  • C. 電子錢包（如支付寶Alipay）的使用日趨普及。
    The use of electronic wallets such as Alipay becomes more popular.
  • D. 持有債券的預期回報率上升。
    The expected rate of return of holding bonds increases.

📚 Concept Review (概念重溫)

在宏觀經濟學中，總貨幣需求 \(M_d\) 由兩部分組成：交易貨幣需求 \(M_t\) 和資產貨幣需求 \(M_a\)。

In macroeconomics, the total demand for money \(M_d\) consists of two parts: transaction demand for money \(M_t\) and asset demand for money \(M_a\).

$$M_d = M_t + M_a$$
  • 資產貨幣需求 (Asset Demand for Money, \(M_a\)): 人們為了儲存財富而持有貨幣的需求。持有貨幣的機會成本是放棄了的利息（例如債券的回報）。因此，\(M_a\) 與名義利率呈反向關係。
    The demand for holding money as a store of wealth. The opportunity cost of holding money is the forgone interest (e.g., return from bonds). Therefore, \(M_a\) is inversely related to the nominal interest rate.
  • 沿線移動 vs. 整條曲線移動 (Movement vs. Shift):
    - 如果是Y軸的變數（名義利率）改變，只會導致沿著曲線移動 (movement along the curve)。
    - 如果是其他因素（如其他資產的預期回報、風險等）改變，則會導致整條曲線移動 (shift of the curve)。
    - A change in the y-axis variable (nominal interest rate) causes a movement along the curve.
    - A change in other factors (like expected returns of other assets, risks) causes a shift of the curve.

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是 / The correct option is: Option D

Option A: 名義利率增加 / The nominal interest rate increases. (❌ 錯誤 / Incorrect)

名義利率是圖表中的 Y 軸。Y 軸數值的改變只會導致沿著資產貨幣需求曲線向上移動，而不會令整條曲線向左平移。

The nominal interest rate is on the Y-axis of the diagram. A change in the Y-axis value will only cause an upward movement along the asset demand curve, not a leftward shift of the entire curve.

Option B: 貨幣基礎收縮 / There is a contraction of monetary base. (❌ 錯誤 / Incorrect)

貨幣基礎收縮會影響「貨幣供應 (Money Supply)」，而題目問的是「貨幣需求 (Money Demand)」。兩者是不同的概念。

A contraction of the monetary base affects the "Money Supply", while the question is asking about "Money Demand". These are different concepts.

Option C: 電子錢包的使用日趨普及 / The use of electronic wallets becomes more popular. (❌ 錯誤 / Incorrect)

電子錢包普及會減少人們為了日常交易而持有實體現金的需要，這會令「交易貨幣需求 \(M_t\)」下降，但不會直接影響作為儲存財富用途的「資產貨幣需求 \(M_a\)」。

The popularization of electronic wallets reduces the need for people to hold physical cash for daily transactions. This decreases the "Transaction Demand for Money \(M_t\)", but does not directly affect the "Asset Demand for Money \(M_a\)" which is used as a store of wealth.

Option D: 持有債券的預期回報率上升 / The expected rate of return of holding bonds increases. (✅ 正確 / Correct)

債券是持有貨幣的替代資產。當債券的預期回報率上升，人們會傾向將財富由貨幣轉移至債券以賺取更高回報。因此，在任何一個給定的名義利率下，人們對資產貨幣的需求都會減少，導致資產貨幣需求曲線向左平移（由 \(M_{A0}\) 移至 \(M_{A1}\)）。

Bonds are alternative assets to holding money. When the expected rate of return of holding bonds increases, people will tend to transfer their wealth from money to bonds to earn higher returns. Therefore, at any given nominal interest rate, the demand for asset money will decrease, causing the asset demand curve to shift to the left (from \(M_{A0}\) to \(M_{A1}\)).

🧩 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

面對這類圖表題，你需要遵循以下步驟拆解：

When facing this type of diagram question, you should follow these steps to decompose it:

  1. 看清標題 (Read the title): 題目明確指出這是「資產貨幣需求 (Asset demand for money)」，所以任何影響「交易貨幣需求 (Transaction demand)」的選項（如選項 C）都是陷阱。
  2. 看清坐標軸 (Read the axes): Y軸是名義利率。圖中顯示的是整條曲線向左移 (Leftward shift)，這意味著必定是 Y軸以外的因素發生了改變。因此，直接秒殺選項 A。
  3. 區分供求 (Distinguish Supply and Demand): 圖表顯示的是需求曲線 \(M_a\)，所以選項 B（影響貨幣供應）不相關。

🙋‍♂️ Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼電子錢包 (Alipay) 不會影響資產貨幣需求？我平時把錢放在支付寶裡不也是一種資產嗎？

A: 在 DSE 經濟學的嚴格定義中，我們假設人們持有貨幣的動機主要分為兩種。使用 Alipay 主要是為了方便日常購物和支付，這屬於「交易動機 (Transaction motive)」。而「資產動機 (Asset motive)」是指將貨幣視為一種投資組合的一部分（與債券、股票等比較）來儲存財富。因此，支付方式的進步只會影響交易貨幣需求。

Q: Why doesn't Alipay affect asset demand? Isn't keeping money in Alipay also a form of asset?

A: In the strict definition of DSE Economics, we assume people hold money for two main motives. Using Alipay is primarily for the convenience of daily shopping and payments, which falls under the "Transaction motive". The "Asset motive" refers to holding money as part of an investment portfolio (compared to bonds, stocks, etc.) to store wealth. Therefore, advancements in payment methods only affect the transaction demand for money.

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

「看軸秒殺法」 (The Axis Elimination Method):
在 MC 卷中，只要看到圖表是「整條曲線平移 (Shift)」，第一時間檢查選項中有沒有提到 Y 軸或 X 軸的變數。這題 Y 軸是「名義利率」，選項 A 說「名義利率增加」，這絕對只會造成「沿線移動 (Movement)」。考試時看到 A 可以連想都不用想，直接劃掉，節省時間！

The Axis Elimination Method:
In Paper 1 (MC), whenever you see a diagram showing a "Shift" of the entire curve, immediately check if any option mentions the variables on the Y-axis or X-axis. Here, the Y-axis is "Nominal interest rate", and Option A says "Nominal interest rate increases". This will absolutely only cause a "Movement". In the exam, you can cross out A immediately without a second thought to save time!
