📚 經濟學題目解析 / Economics Problem Analysis

Topic (課題): Theory of the Firm (廠商理論) Profit Maximization (利潤極大化)

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐⭐

Key Concepts (核心概念): Total Cost (TC) Marginal Cost (MC) Marginal Revenue (MR)

題目 / The Question

參考以下某利潤極大化廠商的成本與產出關係表。若每單位產量的平均成本降低$1，而市場價格是$8：
Refer to the following table about the cost-output relationship of a profit-maximising firm. If the average cost of every unit of output decreases by $1 and the market price is $8:
數量 (Quantity) 1 2 3 4 5
原平均成本 (Old Average Cost) $5 $6 $7 $8 $9
A. 最低生產成本為$18。
B. 利潤極大化產量是3單位。
C. 最大利潤是$10。
D. 每單位產量的邊際成本會維持不變。
A. the minimum cost of production is $18.
B. the profit maximising output is 3 units.
C. the maximum profit is $10.
D. the marginal cost of every unit of output will remain unchanged.

📖 概念重溫 / Concept Review

要解決這類題目，我們必須先掌握不同成本之間的換算，以及利潤極大化的黃金法則。
To solve this type of question, we must first understand the conversion between different costs and the golden rule of profit maximization.
  • 總成本 (Total Cost, TC): 生產某一數量的總開支。
    Total Cost (TC): The total expense incurred in producing a certain quantity.
    $$ TC = AC \times Q $$
  • 邊際成本 (Marginal Cost, MC): 額外生產一單位產品所增加的總成本。
    Marginal Cost (MC): The increase in total cost resulting from producing one additional unit of output.
    $$ MC = \frac{\Delta TC}{\Delta Q} $$
  • 利潤極大化條件 (Profit Maximizing Condition): 當市場為受價者（如本題給定固定市價 $P = \$8$），邊際收益等於價格。廠商會生產直到邊際收益等於邊際成本。
    Profit Maximizing Condition: In a price-taking market (as given by the fixed market price $P = \$8$), Marginal Revenue equals Price. The firm will produce up to the point where Marginal Revenue equals Marginal Cost.
    $$ MR = MC $$

✅ 答案與詳細分析 / The Answer & Detailed Analysis

Correct Option (正確答案): Option B

❌ A 的錯誤原因： 雖然在產量為3時，總成本確實是$18，但這並不是廠商的「最低生產成本」。從表中可見，生產1單位的成本最低，僅為$4（或不生產時成本為$0）。
❌ Why A is wrong: Although the total cost at $Q=3$ is indeed $18$, this is not the "minimum cost of production". As seen in the table, the lowest production cost is $4$ at 1 unit (or $0$ if producing nothing).
✅ B 的正確原因： 在完全競爭市場中，廠商面對的邊際收益 $MR = P = \$8$。根據利潤極大化條件 $MR = MC$，當產量 $Q = 3$ 時，新的 $MC = \$8$。因此，廠商會生產3單位來極大化利潤。
✅ Why B is correct: In a perfectly competitive market, Marginal Revenue $MR = P = \$8$. According to the profit maximizing condition $MR = MC$, at output $Q = 3$, the new $MC = \$8$. Therefore, the firm maximizes profit by producing 3 units.
❌ C 的錯誤原因： 在產量為3時，總收益 $TR = 8 \times 3 = 24$，總成本 $TC = 18$。最大利潤 = $TR - TC = \$24 - \$18 = \$6$，而不是$10。
❌ Why C is wrong: At output 3, Total Revenue $TR = 8 \times 3 = 24$, and Total Cost $TC = 18$. Maximum profit = $TR - TC = \$24 - \$18 = \$6$, not $10.
❌ D 的錯誤原因： 當平均成本(AC)每個產量都下降$1時，代表總成本(TC)下降了 $1 \times Q$。這意味著變動成本改變了，因此邊際成本(MC)也會改變（實際上每個產量的MC都下降了$1）。
❌ Why D is wrong: When Average Cost (AC) falls by $1 at every output level, it means Total Cost (TC) falls by $1 \times Q$. This implies a change in variable costs, which in turn means Marginal Cost (MC) will also change (specifically, MC decreases by $1 at every unit).

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 / Common Mistakes & Traps

陷阱：誤以為「成本下降 = 邊際成本不變」
Trap: Assuming "Cost decrease = Marginal Cost remains unchanged"
很多同學會將這題與「固定成本 (Fixed Cost) 改變」混淆。如果題目說「總固定成本下降」，那麼邊際成本 (MC) 確實會維持不變。但本題說的是「每單位的平均成本」都下降$1，這等同於每生產一件產品，就省下$1。因此，這屬於變動成本的改變，MC 必定會下降。
Many students confuse this scenario with a "change in Fixed Cost". If the question stated "Total Fixed Cost decreases", then Marginal Cost (MC) would indeed remain unchanged. However, this question states the "average cost of every unit" decreases by $1, meaning saving $1 for every unit produced. This is a change in variable cost, so MC must decrease.

🙋 學生常見疑問 / Student Q&A

Q: 從表格可見，產量為 2 和 3 時的利潤都是 $6。為什麼利潤極大化的產量是 3 單位而不是 2 單位？

Q: From the table, the profit is $6 at both output levels 2 and 3. Why is the profit-maximizing output 3 units instead of 2?

A: 根據邊際分析法 (Marginal Analysis)，只要生產下一單位的邊際收益大於或等於邊際成本 ($MR \ge MC$)，廠商就會繼續生產。在 $Q=3$ 時，$MR = MC = \$8$。由於生產第3單位的額外收益完全涵蓋了額外成本，它並不會減少總利潤。在經濟學常規中，我們假定廠商會擴張產量直到 $MR = MC$ 的那一點，因此答案是 3 單位。
A: According to Marginal Analysis, a firm will continue to produce as long as the marginal revenue of the next unit is greater than or equal to its marginal cost ($MR \ge MC$). At $Q=3$, $MR = MC = \$8$. Since the extra revenue from the 3rd unit fully covers its extra cost, it does not reduce the total profit. By economic convention, we assume firms expand output up to the exact point where $MR = MC$, hence the answer is 3 units.

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 / Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

如果想在考試中省下畫完整個表格的時間，你可以運用代數邏輯速解：
If you want to save time plotting the entire table during an exam, you can use algebraic logic for a shortcut:
既然 $AC$ 統一減少 $1，代表每個單位的變動成本直接減少 $1。所以，新 $MC = \text{舊 } MC - \$1$。已知 $MR = \$8$，我們只需尋找哪一個產量的舊 $MC$ 剛好等於 $9$ 即可 (因為 $9 - 1 = 8$)。計算舊 $MC$：產量3的舊 $TC = 21$，產量2的舊 $TC = 12$，舊 $MC = 21 - 12 = 9$。因此，產量3就是答案！
Since $AC$ decreases uniformly by $1, it means the variable cost per unit directly drops by $1. Therefore, New $MC = \text{Old } MC - \$1$. Given $MR = \$8$, we simply need to find which output level had an Old $MC$ exactly equal to $9$ (because $9 - 1 = 8$). Calculating Old $MC$: Old $TC$ at $Q=3$ is $21$, Old $TC$ at $Q=2$ is $12$, Old $MC = 21 - 12 = 9$. Thus, output 3 is the answer instantly!
