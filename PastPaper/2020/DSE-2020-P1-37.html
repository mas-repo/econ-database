HKDSE Economics Question Analysis (Correction)

📚 課題 (Topic): 總供求模型與貨幣市場 (AD-AS Model & Money Market)

⭐ 難度 (Difficulty): ★★★★★

🔑 關鍵概念 (Key Concepts): 資產貨幣需求 Asset Demand for Money 貨幣政策 Monetary Policy 財富效應 Wealth Effect

📝 題目 (The Question)

一位經濟學家指出：「我國股票價格持續下挫，已經影響消費支出及貨幣需求。我們應該推出政策以紓緩對產出水平和利率的影響。」下列哪項政策能於短期同時穩定該國的產出水平和利率？
（假設在這情況下，利率主要受資產貨幣需求的影響。）

  1. 政府增加對貧困人士的現金津貼。
  2. 政府提供進口貨品的關稅豁免。
  3. 中央銀行指示商業銀行收緊私人貸款條件。
  4. 中央銀行從公眾購入政府債券。

An economist said, 'The continuous fall in stock prices in our country has already affected the consumption expenditure as well as the money demand. We should implement a policy to relieve the effects on the output level and the interest rate.' Which of the following policies can stabilise the output level and the interest rate in the country at the same time in the short run?
(Assume the interest rate is mainly affected by the asset demand for money in this case.)

  1. The government increases cash subsidy to the poor.
  2. The government gives tariff exemption to the imports of goods.
  3. The central bank instructs the commercial banks to tighten the requirement of personal loans.
  4. The central bank buys government bonds from the public.

🧠 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

我們必須嚴格遵守題目中的假設：「利率主要受資產貨幣需求的影響」，這意味著我們需要忽略交易性貨幣需求 (Transaction Demand for Money) 的變動。讓我們重新分析「股票價格下挫」的初始衝擊 (Initial Shock)：

  • 產出水平 (Output Level, \(Y\)): 股票價格下跌導致財富減少，消費支出 \(C\) 下降，總需求 \(AD\) 減少，導致產出水平 \(Y\) 下降。
  • 利率 (Interest Rate, \(r\)): 當股票價格持續下挫（預期回報下跌/風險增加），人們會傾向拋售股票並持有貨幣以求保本（流動性偏好增加）。這導致資產貨幣需求 (Asset Demand for Money, \(M_a\)) 上升。在貨幣供應不變下，貨幣需求 \(M_d\) 增加會令利率 \(r\) 上升。

目標 (The Goal): 「穩定」意味著政策必須抵消上述衝擊，即需要提高產出水平 \(Y\) 並降低利率 \(r\)。

We must strictly follow the assumption: "interest rate is mainly affected by the asset demand for money", which means we ignore changes in transaction demand. Let's re-analyze the initial shock:

  • Output Level (\(Y\)): A fall in stock prices reduces wealth. Consumption \(C\) decreases, Aggregate Demand \(AD\) decreases, leading to a fall in output \(Y\).
  • Interest Rate (\(r\)): When stock prices crash (lower expected returns/higher risk), people sell stocks and hold money to avoid capital loss (flight to liquidity). This increases the asset demand for money (\(M_a\)). With money supply constant, higher money demand \(M_d\) increases the interest rate \(r\).

The Goal: To "stabilise" means the policy must offset these shocks: increase output \(Y\) and decrease interest rate \(r\).

$$ \text{Initial Shock: } Y \downarrow, \ r \uparrow $$ $$ \text{Required Policy Effect: } Y \uparrow, \ r \downarrow $$

✅ 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): D

Option D (正確 Correct)

中央銀行從公眾購入政府債券屬於擴張性貨幣政策 (Expansionary Monetary Policy)。這會增加貨幣供應 \(M_s\)。在貨幣市場中，貨幣供應增加會令利率 \(r\) 下降（成功穩定利率）。同時，較低的利率會刺激投資 \(I\) 和消費 \(C\)，令總需求 \(AD\) 增加，從而令產出水平 \(Y\) 上升（成功穩定產出）。

The central bank buying government bonds is an expansionary monetary policy. It increases the money supply \(M_s\). In the money market, an increase in money supply lowers the interest rate \(r\) (stabilising \(r\)). Meanwhile, a lower interest rate stimulates investment \(I\) and consumption \(C\), increasing Aggregate Demand \(AD\) and thus increasing output \(Y\) (stabilising \(Y\)).

Option A (錯誤 Incorrect)

政府增加現金津貼屬於擴張性財政政策。這會增加市民的可支配收入，令消費 \(C\) 和產出水平 \(Y\) 上升。但是，由於題目假設利率主要受資產貨幣需求影響（忽略交易貨幣需求），產出 \(Y\) 的上升不會帶動貨幣需求增加。因此，利率 \(r\) 會維持在高位，無法被「穩定」（即無法降低利率）。

Increasing cash subsidies is an expansionary fiscal policy. It increases disposable income, consumption \(C\), and output \(Y\). However, because the question assumes the interest rate is mainly affected by asset demand (ignoring transaction demand), the increase in \(Y\) does not increase money demand. Therefore, the interest rate \(r\) remains high and is not "stabilised" (i.e., it fails to lower \(r\)).

Option B (錯誤 Incorrect)

豁免進口關稅會令進口 \(M\) 增加，淨出口 \(NX\) 下降，導致總需求 \(AD\) 和產出水平 \(Y\) 進一步下跌，無法穩定產出。

Tariff exemption increases imports \(M\) and decreases net exports \(NX\), leading to a further fall in \(AD\) and output \(Y\), failing to stabilise output.

Option C (錯誤 Incorrect)

收緊私人貸款屬於緊縮性貨幣政策。這會減少貨幣供應 \(M_s\)，令利率 \(r\) 進一步上升，並令產出水平 \(Y\) 進一步下跌，兩者皆無法穩定。

Tightening personal loans is a contractionary monetary policy. It reduces money supply \(M_s\), pushing the interest rate \(r\) even higher and output \(Y\) even lower. It fails to stabilise both.

🔍 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

這題的「陷阱與關鍵」在於括號內的假設：（假設在這情況下，利率主要受資產貨幣需求的影響。）

  1. 釐清初始衝擊： 股票大跌 \(\rightarrow\) 避險情緒升溫 \(\rightarrow\) 拋售股票換取現金 \(\rightarrow\) 資產貨幣需求 (\(M_a\)) 上升 \(\rightarrow\) 利率 \(r\) 上升。同時，財富減少 \(\rightarrow\) 產出 \(Y\) 下降。
  2. 鎖定目標： 需要一個能令 \(r \downarrow\) 及 \(Y \uparrow\) 的政策。
  3. 排除干擾： 括號內的假設強迫我們切斷「\(Y\) 改變 \(\rightarrow\) 交易貨幣需求改變 \(\rightarrow\) \(r\) 改變」這條傳導路徑。因此，純粹的財政政策 (Option A) 無法改變利率。
The trap and key lie in the assumption: (Assume the interest rate is mainly affected by the asset demand for money...) 1. Clarify initial shock: Stock crash \(\rightarrow\) risk aversion \(\rightarrow\) sell stocks for cash \(\rightarrow\) asset demand for money (\(M_a\)) rises \(\rightarrow\) interest rate \(r\) rises. Wealth falls \(\rightarrow\) output \(Y\) falls. 2. Set target: Need a policy that causes \(r \downarrow\) and \(Y \uparrow\). 3. Eliminate distractors: The assumption forces us to cut off the transmission mechanism of "\(Y\) changes \(\rightarrow\) transaction demand changes \(\rightarrow\) \(r\) changes". Thus, pure fiscal policy (Option A) cannot change the interest rate here.

🙋 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼股票價格下跌會令「資產貨幣需求」上升而不是下降？

A: 在宏觀經濟學中，人們將財富分配在「貨幣」和「非貨幣資產（如股票/債券）」上。當股票價格持續下挫，持有股票的風險極高且預期回報為負。為了避免資產進一步縮水，投資者會選擇將股票套現，轉為持有流動性最高且名義價值不變的「貨幣」。這種「逃向流動性 (Flight to liquidity)」的行為，直接導致對貨幣的資產需求急升。

Q: Why does a fall in stock prices INCREASE rather than decrease the "asset demand for money"?
A: In macroeconomics, wealth is allocated between "money" and "non-money assets (like stocks/bonds)". When stock prices crash, holding stocks becomes highly risky with negative expected returns. To avoid further losses, investors liquidate stocks into "money", which is highly liquid and holds its nominal value. This "flight to liquidity" directly causes a surge in the asset demand for money.
