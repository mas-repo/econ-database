📊 經濟學概念解析：匯率轉變
Economics Concept: Exchange Rate Changes

Topic: 宏觀經濟學 - 匯率與國際貿易 (Macroeconomics - Exchange Rates & International Trade)

Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)

Key Concepts: 貨幣貶值 (Depreciation), 貨幣升值 (Appreciation), 出口與進口 (Exports & Imports)

📝 題目 (The Question)

下表顯示歐元兌美元的匯率轉變。

日期 匯率
2018年9月22日 1歐元 = 1.176美元
2019年8月22日 1歐元 = 1.109美元

上述匯率的轉變會引致 ____________ 下降。

  1. 到訪美國的歐洲遊客人數
  2. 從歐洲出口至美國的物品數量
  3. 美國出口至歐洲的貨物總值（以歐元計算）
  4. 歐洲出口至美國的貨物總值（以美元計算）

The following table shows the change in the exchange rate of Euro against the US dollar.

Date Exchange rate
22 September 2018 1 EUR = US$ 1.176
22 August 2019 1 EUR = US$ 1.109

The above change in the exchange rate will lead to a decrease in

  1. the number of European tourists visiting the US.
  2. the volume of goods exported from Europe to the US.
  3. the total value of US exports to Europe (in terms of Euro).
  4. the total value of European exports to the US (in term of the US dollar).

💡 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解答這條問題，我們必須了解匯率 (Exchange Rate)的變動如何影響進出口價格。從題目可見，1歐元能兌換的美元由 1.176 減少至 1.109。這代表：
1. 歐元貶值 (Euro Depreciation)： 歐元相對美元變得便宜。
2. 美元升值 (US Dollar Appreciation)： 美元相對歐元變得昂貴。

To answer this question, we must understand how changes in the Exchange Rate affect import and export prices. As seen in the question, the amount of US dollars that 1 Euro can exchange for decreased from 1.176 to 1.109. This means:
1. Euro Depreciation: The Euro has become cheaper relative to the USD.
2. US Dollar Appreciation: The USD has become more expensive relative to the Euro.

價格效應 (Price Effect)： 當外幣（美元）升值時，以外幣定價的商品（如美國貨品、美國旅遊）對本地人（歐洲人）來說會變得更昂貴，從而導致需求量下降。

Price Effect: When the foreign currency (USD) appreciates, goods priced in that foreign currency (e.g., US goods, US tourism) become more expensive for locals (Europeans), leading to a decrease in quantity demanded.

總值公式 (Total Value Formula): $$TV = P \times Q$$

如果價格 $P$ 與數量 $Q$ 呈反方向變動，總值的變化取決於需求價格彈性 (Price Elasticity of Demand, $E_d$)。

If price $P$ and quantity $Q$ move in opposite directions, the change in total value depends on the Price Elasticity of Demand, $E_d$.

✅ 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是 (Correct Option)： A

選項 A 分析： 歐元貶值意味著美元升值。對歐洲人而言，前往美國旅遊（購買美國的服務出口）的成本以歐元計算變得更加昂貴。根據需求定律，價格上升會導致需求量下降，因此到訪美國的歐洲遊客人數肯定會減少。

Analysis of Option A: Euro depreciation means the USD has appreciated. For Europeans, traveling to the US (purchasing US service exports) becomes more expensive in terms of Euros. According to the law of demand, a higher price leads to a lower quantity demanded, so the number of European tourists visiting the US will definitely decrease.

選項 B 分析： 由於歐元貶值，歐洲出口到美國的商品以美元計算會變得更便宜。這會刺激美國人對歐洲商品的需求，因此從歐洲出口至美國的物品數量將會上升，而非下降。

Analysis of Option B: Due to Euro depreciation, European goods exported to the US become cheaper in terms of USD. This stimulates Americans' demand for European goods, so the volume of goods exported from Europe to the US will increase, not decrease.

選項 C 分析： 對歐洲人來說，美國貨物變貴了（$P$ 上升），所以歐洲進口的數量會減少（$Q$ 下降）。總值 $TV = P \times Q$。由於價格和數量的變動方向相反，除非我們知道歐洲對美國貨物的需求彈性，否則無法確定總值（以歐元計算）是上升還是下降。

Analysis of Option C: For Europeans, US goods become more expensive ($P$ increases), so the quantity of imports to Europe decreases ($Q$ decreases). Total value is $TV = P \times Q$. Since price and quantity move in opposite directions, unless we know the elasticity of demand for US goods in Europe, we cannot determine whether the total value (in Euros) increases or decreases.

選項 D 分析： 對美國人來說，歐洲貨物變便宜了（以美元計算的 $P$ 下降），因此需求量會增加（$Q$ 上升）。同樣地，$TV = P \times Q$，價格下降但數量上升，我們必須知道美國對歐洲貨物的需求彈性才能確定總值（以美元計算）的變化方向。因此，它不一定會下降。

Analysis of Option D: For Americans, European goods become cheaper ($P$ in USD decreases), so quantity demanded increases ($Q$ increases). Again, $TV = P \times Q$. With price falling and quantity rising, we must know the elasticity of demand for European goods in the US to determine the direction of change in total value (in USD). Therefore, it will not necessarily decrease.

🛠️ 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

關鍵支點 (The Pivot Point)： 解題的核心在於準確判斷「誰升值、誰貶值」。把數字簡化：以前 1 歐元能換大概 1.17 美元，現在只能換 1.10 美元。歐元購買力下降（貶值），美元購買力上升（升值）。

The Pivot Point: The core of solving this is accurately determining "which currency appreciated and which depreciated". Simplify the numbers: Before, 1 EUR bought about 1.17 USD; now it buys only 1.10 USD. The purchasing power of the Euro fell (depreciation), and the USD rose (appreciation).

干擾資訊 (Distractors)： 題目中的具體日期（2018年、2019年）是完全無關的干擾資訊。我們只需要對比兩組匯率數字的方向，甚至不需要精確計算兩者的百分比差異。

Distractors: The specific dates (2018, 2019) in the question are completely irrelevant. We only need to compare the direction of the two exchange rate numbers, and we don't even need to accurately calculate the percentage difference between them.

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

1. 死記硬背，沒有代入情境： 很多學生會背誦「本幣貶值有利出口」。但在這題中，你必須分清楚是以「歐洲」還是「美國」的角度來看。如果題目問的是美國，那答案的方向就會完全不同。

1. Rote memorization without context: Many students memorize "depreciation favors exports." But in this question, you must distinguish whether you are looking from the perspective of "Europe" or the "US". If the question were asking about the US, the direction of the answers would be entirely different.

2. 忽略「總值」(Total Value) 與彈性 (Elasticity) 的關係： 這是 DSE 最常見的陷阱！看到選項 C 和 D 出現「總值」字眼，如果題目沒有提供「彈性大於 1 ($E_d > 1$)」或「彈性小於 1 ($E_d < 1$)」的資訊，通常該選項都是無法確定的（Uncertain），因此不能選為必然發生的結果。

2. Ignoring the relationship between Total Value and Elasticity: This is the most common DSE trap! When seeing "total value" in options C and D, if the question does not provide information like "elasticity is greater than 1 ($E_d > 1$)" or "less than 1 ($E_d < 1$)", the outcome for that option is usually uncertain and cannot be chosen as a definite result.

🙋 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

問 (Q): 為什麼「遊客」會和匯率扯上關係？這不是關於進出口的題目嗎？
Why are "tourists" related to exchange rates? Isn't this a question about imports and exports?

答 (A): 在經濟學中，旅遊業屬於「服務輸出與輸入」。當歐洲人去美國旅遊，他們在美國消費（住酒店、吃飯），這對美國來說是「服務出口」(Service Exports)，對歐洲來說是「服務進口」。美元升值等於美國的「服務」變貴了，所以歐洲人會減少去美國消費。

In economics, tourism is classified as "exports and imports of services". When Europeans travel to the US and spend money (hotels, dining), this is a "service export" for the US and a "service import" for Europe. The appreciation of the USD means US "services" become more expensive, so Europeans will reduce their consumption in the US.

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

「彈性排除法」(The Elasticity Elimination Method):
在 DSE 選擇題中，當題目問及「必定引致哪一項上升/下降」，只要選項中帶有「總值 (Total Value) / 總收入 (Total Revenue) / 總開支 (Total Expenditure)」字眼，而題目沒有提及需求彈性 (Elasticity)，你可以立即將這些選項圈起來並視為「不確定 (Uncertain)」，優先排除掉。在此題中，你可以秒速排除 C 和 D，只需在 A 和 B 之間用邏輯判斷，節省大量考試時間！

The Elasticity Elimination Method:
In DSE MCQs, when asked "which of the following will definitely lead to an increase/decrease", if an option contains the words "Total Value / Total Revenue / Total Expenditure", and the question does not mention demand elasticity, you can immediately circle these options and treat them as "Uncertain", eliminating them first. In this question, you can instantly eliminate C and D, leaving you to logically judge only between A and B, saving a lot of exam time!
