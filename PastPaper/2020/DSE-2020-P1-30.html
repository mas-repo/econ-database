Topic (課題): 貨幣與銀行：存款創造 (Money and Banking: Deposit Creation)

Difficulty (難度): ★★★★☆

Key Concepts (核心概念): 貨幣基礎 Monetary Base 貨幣供應 Money Supply 銀行乘數 Banking Multiplier

2. The Question (題目)

參考以下有關某經濟的資料。假設所有銀行均沒持有超額儲備。

Refer to the following information about an economy. Suppose all banks do not hold any excess reserves.

百萬元 ($ million)
公眾流通的貨幣 (Currency in public circulation)10
貨幣基礎 (Monetary base)30
貨幣供應 (Money supply)110

若公眾把所有現金存入銀行系統，
If the public deposits all the cash into the banking system,

A. 實際銀行乘數會是4。
A. the actual banking multiplier will be 4.

B. 最大貸款額會是120百萬元。
B. the maximum amount of loans will be $120 million.

C. 最大存款額會是140百萬元。
C. the maximum amount of deposits will be $140 million.

D. 新的貨幣供應會是160百萬元。
D. the new money supply will be $160 million.

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要拆解這類題目，我們必須熟記宏觀經濟學中關於貨幣系統的三個核心等式：

To solve this type of question, we must memorize the three core equations regarding the monetary system in macroeconomics:

$$MB = C + R$$ $$M_s = C + D$$ $$D = R + L$$
  • $MB$ (Monetary Base 貨幣基礎): 由公眾持有的現金 $C$ 及銀行儲備 $R$ 組成。
  • Consists of cash held by the public $C$ and bank reserves $R$.
  • $M_s$ (Money Supply 貨幣供應): 由公眾持有的現金 $C$ 及銀行存款 $D$ 組成。
  • Consists of cash held by the public $C$ and bank deposits $D$.
  • $L$ (Loans 貸款): 銀行將存款 $D$ 扣除必須保留的儲備 $R$ 後，借出的款項。
  • The amount banks lend out after deducting reserves $R$ from deposits $D$.

法定儲備率 (Required Reserve Ratio, $rrr$): 當銀行沒有超額儲備 (No excess reserves) 時，實際儲備等於法定儲備。公式為：

When banks hold no excess reserves, actual reserves equal required reserves. The formula is:

$$rrr = \frac{R}{D}$$ $$m = \frac{1}{rrr}$$

這裡的 $m$ 代表最大銀行乘數 (Maximum Banking Multiplier)。

Here, $m$ represents the Maximum Banking Multiplier.

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 Correct Option: B

第一步：計算初始狀態 (Step 1: Calculate Initial State)

根據題目提供的數據，我們可以找出隱藏的變量（儲備 $R$ 和 存款 $D$）：

Based on the provided data, we can find the hidden variables (Reserves $R$ and Deposits $D$):

  • $R = MB - C = 30 - 10 = 20$
  • $D = M_s - C = 110 - 10 = 100$

因為沒有超額儲備，我們可以計算出儲備率 $rrr$ 和銀行乘數 $m$：

Since there are no excess reserves, we can calculate the reserve ratio $rrr$ and the banking multiplier $m$:

$$rrr = \frac{20}{100} = 0.2 = 20\%$$ $$m = \frac{1}{0.2} = 5$$

第二步：計算存款後的新狀態 (Step 2: Calculate New State After Deposit)

公眾存入所有現金，代表新的公眾流通貨幣 $C' = 0$。現金流入銀行成為新的儲備，但貨幣基礎 $MB$ 不變（依然是 30）。

The public deposits all cash, meaning the new currency in circulation $C' = 0$. Cash flows into banks as new reserves, but the monetary base $MB$ remains unchanged (still 30).

  • 新儲備 New Reserves ($R'$) = $30$
  • 最大存款 Maximum Deposits ($D'$) = $R' \times m = 30 \times 5 = 150$
  • 最大貸款 Maximum Loans ($L'$) = $D' - R' = 150 - 30 = 120$
  • 新貨幣供應 New Money Supply ($M_s'$) = $C' + D' = 0 + 150 = 150$

選項分析 (Option Breakdown)

A. 錯誤 (Incorrect): 銀行乘數由法定儲備率決定，$m = 5$，並非 4。

The banking multiplier is determined by the required reserve ratio, $m = 5$, not 4.

B. 正確 (Correct): 根據計算，最大貸款額 $L' = 120$ 百萬元。

Based on calculations, the maximum loans $L' = $120 million.

C. 錯誤 (Incorrect): 最大存款額 $D'$ 是 150 百萬元，而非 140。

The maximum deposits $D'$ is 150 million, not 140.

D. 錯誤 (Incorrect): 新的貨幣供應 $M_s'$ 是 150 百萬元，而非 160。

The new money supply $M_s'$ is 150 million, not 160.

5. Problem Decomposition & Common Mistakes (題目拆解與常犯錯誤)

題目樞紐 (Pivot Point): 破題的關鍵在於利用初始數據找出 $rrr$（20%）。沒有這個隱藏數據，後續的存款創造計算便無法進行。

The key to cracking this problem is using the initial data to find $rrr$ (20%). Without this hidden data, subsequent deposit creation calculations are impossible.

常犯錯誤 (Common Traps):

  • 誤以為貨幣基礎會改變 (Thinking Monetary Base changes): 很多學生以為現金存入銀行會增加貨幣基礎。事實上，現金只是從公眾手中轉移到銀行金庫，總量 $MB$ 依然是 30。
  • Many students think depositing cash increases the monetary base. In fact, cash simply moves from the public's hands to bank vaults; the total $MB$ remains 30.
  • 忘記扣除儲備計算貸款 (Forgetting to deduct reserves for loans): 計算出存款 $D' = 150$ 後，有些學生會誤以為這就是貸款額。必須記住 $L = D - R$。
  • After calculating deposits $D' = 150$, some mistakenly think this is the loan amount. Must remember $L = D - R$.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼題目要特別強調「所有銀行均沒持有超額儲備」？

Q: Why does the question emphasize "all banks do not hold any excess reserves"?

A: 這是一個重要的假設。如果銀行持有超額儲備（即保留多於法定要求的資金不借出），實際的存款創造過程就不會達到數學上的最大值。有了這個假設，我們才能確定 實際儲備 = 法定儲備，並使用 $m = 1/rrr$ 的公式計算「最大」貸款和存款額。

A: This is a crucial assumption. If banks hold excess reserves (keeping more funds than legally required instead of lending them out), the actual deposit creation process won't reach its mathematical maximum. With this assumption, we can equate Actual Reserves to Required Reserves, allowing us to use the $m = 1/rrr$ formula to calculate "maximum" loans and deposits.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

在考試中，你可以使用「終極狀態法」秒殺這類題目：

In an exam, you can use the "Ultimate State Method" to kill this question quickly:

如果公眾存入所有現金 ($C' = 0$)，並且銀行借出所有可借資金（無超額儲備），那麼整個貨幣基礎 $MB$ 將會全部變成銀行的法定儲備 $R'$。
這代表新的存款將會是貨幣基礎的乘數倍：

If the public deposits ALL cash ($C' = 0$) and banks lend out all available funds (no excess reserves), the entire monetary base $MB$ becomes the banks' required reserves $R'$. This means new deposits will simply be the monetary base times the multiplier:

$$M_s' = D' = MB \times m$$ $$D' = 30 \times 5 = 150$$

知道 $D' = 150$ 且 $R' = MB = 30$ 後，貸款 $L' = 150 - 30 = 120$。一步到位，無需逐個步驟推導！

Knowing $D' = 150$ and $R' = MB = 30$, Loans $L' = 150 - 30 = 120$. Done in one step without tedious derivation!
