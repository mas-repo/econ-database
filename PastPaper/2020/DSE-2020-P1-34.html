📊 HKDSE 經濟學題解 (Economics Tutorial)

Topic: 宏觀經濟學 - 通脹的影響 (Macroeconomics: Effects of Inflation) Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆
Key Concepts: 非預期通脹 (Unexpected Inflation) 實質價值 (Real Value) 財富重分配 (Wealth Redistribution)

1. 題目 (The Question)

當非預期通脹出現，下列哪位人士會得益？

When there is unexpected inflation, which of the following persons will gain?

A. 一位根據物價指數調整其租金的業主
A. a landlord who adjusts his rental according to the price index
B. 一位以浮動利率借出一筆款項的債主
B. a creditor who has loaned out a sum of money at a floating interest rate
C. 一位與僱員簽訂固定名義薪酬合約的僱主
C. an employer who signs a fixed nominal wage contract with his employees
D. 一位領取每月定額長俸的退休公務員
D. a retired civil servant who receives a fixed monthly pension

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

💡 核心概念：通脹與財富重分配 (Inflation & Wealth Redistribution)

通脹會令金錢的購買力下降。當通脹是非預期 (Unexpected) 時，合約雙方未能及時調整合約金額，這會導致財富的重新分配。

Inflation reduces the purchasing power of money. When inflation is unexpected, contracting parties cannot adjust the contract amounts in time, leading to a redistribution of wealth.

要計算任何名義價值（如薪水、利息）的實質購買力，我們使用以下公式：

To calculate the real purchasing power of any nominal value (like wages or interest), we use the following formula:

$$ \text{實質價值 (Real Value)} = \frac{\text{名義價值 (Nominal Value)}}{\text{物價指數 (Price Index)}} \times 100 $$

  • 固定名義收入接收者 (Fixed Income Receivers)： 受損 (Lose)。因為他們收取的固定金額 \(N\) 在物價 \(P\) 上升時，實質購買力會下降。

    Fixed Income Receivers: Lose. Because the real purchasing power of the fixed nominal amount \(N\) they receive drops when the price level \(P\) increases.

  • 固定名義支出支付者 (Fixed Payment Payers)： 得益 (Gain)。因為他們支付的固定金額 \(N\) 在物價 \(P\) 上升時，實質負擔減輕了。

    Fixed Payment Payers: Gain. Because the real burden of the fixed nominal amount \(N\) they pay decreases when the price level \(P\) increases.

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

✅ 正確答案 (Correct Option)：C

C. 一位與僱員簽訂固定名義薪酬合約的僱主

分析： 僱主是「支付方」。合約規定了固定 (Fixed) 的名義薪酬。由於非預期通脹，物價上升，貨幣購買力下降。僱主支付的同一筆金錢，其實質價值（購買力）下跌了。這意味著僱主在實質上支付了較少的工資，因此得益。相反，收取工資的僱員則受損。

C. an employer who signs a fixed nominal wage contract with his employees

Analysis: The employer is the "payer". The contract specifies a fixed nominal wage. Due to unexpected inflation, prices rise and the purchasing power of money falls. The real value of the money paid by the employer decreases. This means the employer pays less in real terms, and thus gains. Conversely, the employee receiving the wage loses.

❌ 錯誤選項分析 (Distractor Analysis)

A. 一位根據物價指數調整其租金的業主

分析： 關鍵字是「調整 (adjusts)」。既然租金會跟隨通脹調整，這代表業主收取的實質租金（購買力）保持不變。沒有得益也沒有受損。

A. a landlord who adjusts his rental according to the price index

Analysis: The key word is "adjusts". Since the rental income adjusts with inflation, the real rental income (purchasing power) collected by the landlord remains unchanged. They neither gain nor lose.

B. 一位以浮動利率借出一筆款項的債主

分析： 關鍵字是「浮動 (floating)」。債主收取的利率會根據市場情況（包括通脹）向上調整，以補償通脹帶來的購買力流失。因此，其實質回報受到保護，不受影響。

B. a creditor who has loaned out a sum of money at a floating interest rate

Analysis: The key word is "floating". The interest rate collected by the creditor will adjust upwards according to market conditions (including inflation) to compensate for the loss of purchasing power. Therefore, their real return is protected and unaffected.

D. 一位領取每月定額長俸的退休公務員

分析： 退休公務員是「接收方」，領取的是「定額 (fixed)」收入。當非預期通脹出現，物價上升，他們收取的固定退休金能買到的東西減少了（實質價值下降）。因此，他是受損者 (Loser)，而非得益者。

D. a retired civil servant who receives a fixed monthly pension

Analysis: The retired civil servant is the "receiver" of a "fixed" income. When unexpected inflation occurs, prices rise, and the fixed pension they receive can buy fewer goods (real value decreases). Therefore, they are a loser, not a gainer.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

如何破解此類題型？ (How to tackle this type of question?)

  1. 尋找「支點」(Find the Pivot Point): 找出選項中的合約性質。是「固定 (Fixed)」還是「浮動/調整 (Floating/Adjustable)」？凡是會隨物價調整的（如選項 A 和 B），在非預期通脹下通常不受影響，可以直接剔除。

    Find the nature of the contract in the options. Is it "Fixed" or "Floating/Adjustable"? Anything that adjusts with prices (like Options A and B) is generally unaffected by unexpected inflation and can be eliminated immediately.

  2. 辨別身分：支付方 vs 接收方 (Identify the role: Payer vs Receiver): 剩下的選項（C 和 D），誰在付錢？誰在收錢？

    For the remaining options (C and D), who is paying? Who is receiving?

  3. 應用法則 (Apply the Rule): 非預期通脹對「支付固定金額」的人有利（因為付出的錢不值錢了）；對「收取固定金額」的人不利。

    Unexpected inflation benefits those who "pay a fixed amount" (because the money paid is worth less); it harms those who "receive a fixed amount".

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • ⚠️ 混淆預期 (Expected) 與非預期 (Unexpected) 通脹： 只有「非預期」的通脹才會導致財富重分配。如果通脹是預期之內的，合約雙方（例如勞資雙方、借貸雙方）早就在簽約時將通脹率計算在內（例如要求加薪或提高名義利率 \(R\)），就不會有人無故得益或受損。

    Confusing Expected and Unexpected Inflation: Only "unexpected" inflation leads to wealth redistribution. If inflation is expected, contracting parties would have already factored it into their contracts (e.g., demanding higher wages or higher nominal interest rate \(R\)), so no one unexpectedly gains or loses.

  • ⚠️ 盲目記誦「債主必敗」： 很多同學死記「通脹對債主不利」。這只適用於固定利率 (Fixed Interest Rate) 的情況。選項 B 故意寫出「浮動利率 (Floating Interest Rate)」來設下陷阱，同學切勿大意！

    Blindly memorizing "Creditors always lose": Many students memorize that "inflation harms creditors". This ONLY applies to Fixed Interest Rate scenarios. Option B sets a trap by explicitly stating "Floating Interest Rate". Don't be careless!

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

🙋‍♂️ 學生問： 為什麼選項 C 的僱主要簽訂固定薪酬合約？難道他預知會有通脹嗎？

Student: Why did the employer in Option C sign a fixed wage contract? Did they foresee the inflation?

👨‍🏫 導師答： 不是的。在現實生活中，大部分的薪酬合約（例如月薪制）都是短暫固定的（例如一年檢討一次）。題目的重點是通脹發生得太突然（非預期），合約還未到期更改。僱主得益只是因為合約機制導致的客觀結果，而不是因為他有預知能力。

Tutor: No. In reality, most wage contracts (like monthly salaries) are fixed for a certain period (e.g., reviewed annually). The point of the question is that the inflation happened too suddenly (unexpectedly) before the contract could be renewed. The employer gains merely as an objective consequence of the contract mechanism, not because of foresight.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

⚡ 秒殺技巧 (The 10-Second Kill)

在多項選擇題中，尋找對立面。題目問「誰會得益 (Gain)」：

  • 看到「調整 (Adjusts)」、「浮動 (Floating)」 ➔ 即刻劃掉 (Cross out A & B)。
  • 剩下 C (付錢) 和 D (收錢)。
  • 通脹 = 錢變薄了。
  • 付薄了的錢 = 賺了 (Gain) ➔ 答案必定是 C！

In MCQs, look for contrasts. The question asks who will "Gain":

  • See "Adjusts" or "Floating" ➔ Cross them out immediately (Eliminate A & B).
  • Left with C (Paying) and D (Receiving).
  • Inflation = Money becomes less valuable.
  • Paying less valuable money = Gain ➔ Answer must be C!
