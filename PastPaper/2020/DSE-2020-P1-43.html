DSE Economics 經濟學名師解析

Topic (課題): 國際貿易與市場干預 (International Trade and Market Intervention)

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5 Stars)

Key Concepts (核心概念): World Price 世界價格 Imports 進口 Total Expenditure 總開支

The Question (題目)

下圖顯示在某小型開放經濟中物品X的市場情況。\(P_0\) 是貿易前的價格，而 \(P_1\) 是貿易後的價格。在該經濟開放國際貿易後，下列哪些有關物品X的陳述是正確的？

The diagram below shows the market situation of Good X in a small open economy. \(P_0\) is the price before trade and \(P_1\) is the price after trade. Which of the following statements about Good X are correct after the economy opens up for international trade?

  • (1) 本地消費者對物品X的總支出會是 \(P_1 \times Q_d\)。
    The total expenditure on Good X of domestic consumers would be \(P_1 \times Q_d\).
  • (2) 本地生產物品X的價值會是 \(P_1 \times Q_s\)。
    The value of domestically produced Good X would be \(P_1 \times Q_s\).
  • (3) 物品X的短缺會是 \(Q_d - Q_s\)。
    The shortage of Good X would be \(Q_d - Q_s\).

A. 只有 (1)、(2) / (1) and (2) only

B. 只有 (1)、(3) / (1) and (3) only

C. 只有 (2)、(3) / (2) and (3) only

D. (1)、(2) 及 (3) / (1), (2) and (3)

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這道題目，我們需要理解小型開放經濟 (Small Open Economy) 在面對低於本地均衡價格的世界價格 (World Price) 時的市場反應。

To solve this question, we need to understand how a Small Open Economy reacts when facing a World Price that is lower than the domestic equilibrium price.

  • 進口 (Imports): 當世界價格 \(P_w\) 低於本地貿易前價格 \(P_0\) 時，本地需求量 \(Q_d\) 會大於本地供應量 \(Q_s\)。這個差額不會造成短缺，而是會透過進口來填補。
    When the world price \(P_w\) is lower than the domestic autarky price \(P_0\), domestic quantity demanded \(Q_d\) exceeds domestic quantity supplied \(Q_s\). This gap does not create a shortage; it is filled by imports.
$$ \text{Imports (進口量)} = Q_d - Q_s $$
  • 總開支 (Total Expenditure): 消費者購買物品的總花費。
    The total amount spent by consumers to purchase the good.
$$ \text{Total Expenditure} = P \times Q_d $$
  • 本地生產價值 (Value of Domestic Production): 本地生產者出售物品獲得的總收入。
    The total revenue received by domestic producers for selling the good.
$$ \text{Value of Domestic Production} = P \times Q_s $$

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A

Step-by-Step Breakdown (逐項分析):

陳述 (1): 正確。 在貿易後，市場價格變為 \(P_1\)。在這個價格下，本地消費者的需求量是 \(Q_d\)。因此，消費者的總支出是價格乘以需求量，即 \(P_1 \times Q_d\)。

Statement (1): Correct. After trade, the market price becomes \(P_1\). At this price, the quantity demanded by domestic consumers is \(Q_d\). Therefore, the total expenditure of consumers is price times quantity demanded, which is \(P_1 \times Q_d\).

陳述 (2): 正確。 面對世界價格 \(P_1\)，本地生產者只能以這個價格出售物品。根據供應曲線，他們願意且能夠提供的數量是 \(Q_s\)。因此，本地生產的總價值（即本地生產者的總收入）是 \(P_1 \times Q_s\)。

Statement (2): Correct. Facing the world price \(P_1\), domestic producers can only sell at this price. According to the supply curve, the quantity they are willing and able to supply is \(Q_s\). Thus, the total value of domestic production (total revenue of domestic producers) is \(P_1 \times Q_s\).

陳述 (3): 錯誤。 這是本題最大的陷阱！在封閉經濟中，如果政府實施有效價格上限 (Price Ceiling) 於 \(P_1\)，確實會產生 \(Q_d - Q_s\) 的短缺 (Shortage)。但是，這是一個開放經濟。當本地供應不足以應付本地需求時，差額 \(Q_d - Q_s\) 會由外國進口 (Imports) 填補。因為市場需求完全被滿足，所以沒有短缺。

Statement (3): Incorrect. This is the biggest trap of the question! In a closed economy, if the government imposed an effective price ceiling at \(P_1\), it would indeed create a shortage of \(Q_d - Q_s\). However, this is an open economy. When domestic supply is insufficient to meet domestic demand, the gap \(Q_d - Q_s\) is filled by foreign imports. Since the market demand is fully satisfied, there is no shortage.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point): 留意題目中的關鍵字「開放國際貿易」(opens up for international trade)。這句話改變了整條供需曲線的解讀方式。一旦開放貿易，低於均衡點的橫線不再代表「價格上限」(Price Ceiling)，而是代表「世界價格」(World Price)。

Pivot Point: Notice the keyword "opens up for international trade". This phrase changes how we interpret the supply and demand graph. Once trade is opened, a horizontal line below the equilibrium no longer represents a "Price Ceiling", but rather the "World Price".

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

概念混淆：短缺 (Shortage) vs 進口 (Imports)
很多學生看到 \(Q_d > Q_s\) 就會條件反射地認為是短缺 (Shortage)。記住：短缺是指在某價格下，消費者想買但買不到。在自由貿易下，消費者想買的數量 \(Q_d\) 是可以完全買到的（一部分向本地買，一部分向外國買），所以不會有短缺。

Concept Confusion: Shortage vs Imports
Many students reflexively think of a "Shortage" whenever they see \(Q_d > Q_s\). Remember: a shortage means consumers want to buy but cannot. Under free trade, the quantity consumers want to buy (\(Q_d\)) is fully available (partly from domestic producers, partly from foreign countries), so there is no shortage.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果題目中的 \(P_1\) 是高於 \(P_0\)，情況會有什麼不同？

Q: What if \(P_1\) in the question was higher than \(P_0\)?

A: 如果世界價格高於本地貿易前價格，本地供應量 \(Q_s\) 會大於本地需求量 \(Q_d\)。這時多出的數量 \(Q_s - Q_d\) 不會變成過剩 (Surplus)，而是會變成出口 (Exports) 賣到外國去。

A: If the world price was higher than the domestic autarky price, domestic quantity supplied \(Q_s\) would exceed domestic quantity demanded \(Q_d\). The excess quantity \(Q_s - Q_d\) would not become a surplus; instead, it would become Exports sold to foreign countries.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧： 在做 DSE MC 題時，只要看到「國際貿易 (International Trade)」加上一條橫線，請立即在圖表上把 \(Q_d\) 和 \(Q_s\) 之間的差距標記為 Imports (進口) 或 Exports (出口)。如果在選項中看到「短缺 (Shortage)」或「過剩 (Surplus)」字眼，通常可以直接剔除該選項 (Cross it out)！

Speed Trick: When doing DSE MC questions, whenever you see "International Trade" with a horizontal price line, immediately label the gap between \(Q_d\) and \(Q_s\) on the graph as either Imports or Exports. If you see the words "Shortage" or "Surplus" in the options, you can usually cross them out immediately!
