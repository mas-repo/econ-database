📝 HKDSE Economics MC Analysis / 經濟學選擇題分析

Topic 市場干預：配額 (Market Intervention: Quota)
Difficulty ⭐⭐⭐½ (3.5/5)
Key Concepts Quota, Price Elasticity of Demand, Total Revenue

1. The Question (題目)

參考以下中國稀土出口的供需圖。
若中國取消對稀土的出口配額，

Refer to the following supply-demand diagram for the exported Chinese rare earth minerals.
If China abolished the quota on the export of rare earth minerals,

  • (1) 稀土的出口價格會下降。
    the export price of the rare earth minerals would drop.
  • (2) 稀土的出口數量會上升。
    the export quantity of the rare earth minerals would rise.
  • (3) 出口稀土的總交易價值會下降。
    the total exchange value of the exported rare earth minerals would drop.
  • A. 只有 (1)、(2)
    (1) and (2) only
  • B. 只有 (1)、(3)
    (1) and (3) only
  • C. 只有 (2)、(3)
    (2) and (3) only
  • D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)
    (1), (2) and (3)

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 配額 (Quota)

配額是政府對某物品的生產量或進出口量設定的法定上限。在圖表中，有效的配額會令供應曲線在配額數量處變成垂直線（完全缺乏彈性）。

A quota is a legal limit imposed by the government on the quantity of a good that can be produced or traded. In a diagram, an effective quota turns the supply curve into a vertical line (perfectly inelastic) at the quota quantity.

2. 總交易價值 (Total Exchange Value / Total Revenue)

總交易價值（或總收入）等於價格乘以交易數量。在圖表中，它代表均衡點下方的矩形面積。

Total Exchange Value (or Total Revenue) is equal to price multiplied by the transacted quantity. On a graph, it represents the area of the rectangle under the equilibrium point.

$$ TR = P \times Q $$

3. 需求價格彈性與總收入的關係 (Price Elasticity of Demand and TR)

沿著一條向右下傾斜的直線需求曲線，中點的彈性為單位彈性 (\(E_d = 1\))，此時總收入最高。中點以上的上半段為**富有彈性 (Elastic, \(E_d > 1\))**，中點以下的下半段為**缺乏彈性 (Inelastic, \(E_d < 1\))**。

Along a downward-sloping linear demand curve, the midpoint is unit elastic (\(E_d = 1\)), where Total Revenue is maximized. The segment above the midpoint is **elastic (\(E_d > 1\))**, and the segment below is **inelastic (\(E_d < 1\))**.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option / 正確答案： A. 只有 (1)、(2)

Step-by-Step Breakdown (逐項分析)：

陳述 (1)：稀土的出口價格會下降 (True)

取消配額前，供應曲線為 \(S_0\)（在「原初配額」處變成垂直）。均衡點位於 \(D_0\) 與 \(S_0\) 垂直部分的相交點，價格較高。取消配額後，供應曲線回復為原本向上傾斜的 \(S_1\)。新的均衡點是 \(D_0\) 與 \(S_1\) 的相交點。由於新均衡點位於舊均衡點的右下方，價格必然下降。

Before abolishing the quota, the supply curve is \(S_0\) (vertical at "Original quota"). The equilibrium is at the intersection of \(D_0\) and the vertical part of \(S_0\), resulting in a high price. When the quota is abolished, the supply curve reverts to the upward-sloping \(S_1\). The new equilibrium is the intersection of \(D_0\) and \(S_1\). Since the new equilibrium is to the lower right of the old one, the price definitively drops.

陳述 (2)：稀土的出口數量會上升 (True)

承上題，取消配額後，市場不再受制於垂直的配額上限。新的均衡數量由 \(D_0\) 和 \(S_1\) 的相交點決定，該點在圖上明顯位於「原初配額」線的右方，因此數量上升。

Following the previous point, after abolishing the quota, the market is no longer constrained by the vertical limit. The new equilibrium quantity is determined by the intersection of \(D_0\) and \(S_1\), which is visibly to the right of the "Original quota" line. Hence, quantity rises.

陳述 (3)：出口稀土的總交易價值會下降 (False) ❌

這是本題的關鍵。總交易價值的變化取決於價格下降時的需求彈性。觀察圖表，直線需求曲線 \(D_0\) 的中點大約在 \(S_1\) 與 \(D_0\) 相交點的附近。而「原初配額」的垂直線明顯位於該中點的左方（即需求曲線的上半段）。
在需求曲線的上半段，需求是**富有彈性 (Elastic, \(E_d > 1\))** 的。當價格在富有彈性的區間下降時，數量增加的百分比會大於價格下降的百分比，導致**總交易價值（總收入）上升**，而非下降。因此，陳述(3)錯誤。

This is the crux of the question. The change in total exchange value depends on the price elasticity of demand when price falls. Observing the graph, the midpoint of the linear demand curve \(D_0\) is roughly around the intersection of \(S_1\) and \(D_0\). The vertical "Original quota" line is clearly to the left of this midpoint (i.e., on the upper half of the demand curve).
On the upper half of a demand curve, demand is **elastic (\(E_d > 1\))**. When price falls in an elastic range, the percentage increase in quantity demanded is greater than the percentage decrease in price, leading to an **increase** in Total Exchange Value (Total Revenue), not a drop. Therefore, statement (3) is false.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

The Pivot Point (解題樞紐):

這題的陷阱在於(3)。很多同學知道 \(P\) 跌 \(Q\) 升，但不知道 \(P \times Q\) 到底升還是跌。解答的關鍵在於「目測需求曲線的彈性」。

The trap is in statement (3). Many students know \(P\) drops and \(Q\) rises, but aren't sure if \(P \times Q\) goes up or down. The key is to **"visually estimate the elasticity of the demand curve"**.

  • Step 1: Find the midpoint of \(D_0\). (找出 \(D_0\) 的中點)
  • Step 2: Check where the original quota is. It is on the left side (Elastic Region). (檢查原配額位置，它在左側富有彈性區間)
  • Step 3: Apply the rule: \(P \downarrow\) in Elastic region \(\rightarrow\) \(TR \uparrow\). (應用法則：在彈性區間降價，總收入增加)

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • Trap 1: Reality vs. Graph (現實與圖表的混淆)
    在現實中，稀土是必需的工業原料，需求可能非常缺乏彈性 (highly inelastic)。如果憑現實知識作答，同學可能會誤以為價格下跌會令總收入減少。但考評局的題目必須嚴格根據提供的圖表作答。圖中的配額線明顯畫在需求曲線的左半部（富有彈性區間）。
    In reality, rare earth is essential, so its demand might be highly inelastic. If students use real-world knowledge, they might mistakenly think a price drop reduces total revenue. However, HKDSE questions must be answered **strictly based on the provided graph**. The quota line is clearly drawn on the left half of the demand curve (elastic region).

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果題目沒有畫出整條需求曲線，我看不到中點，該怎麼判斷總收入的變化？

Q: If the question didn't draw the full demand curve and I couldn't see the midpoint, how would I determine the change in total revenue?

A: 好問題！如果圖表給的資訊不足以判斷彈性（例如兩條線交叉在一個看不出比例的位置），那麼「總交易價值會下降」這個陳述就處於「不確定 (Uncertain)」的狀態。在 MC 題中，只要一個陳述不是「必然正確 (necessarily true)」，我們就不能選它。因此，即使你無法判斷它是升還是跌，只要它不是「必然下降」，陳述(3)依然是不選的。

A: Great question! If the graph doesn't provide enough visual information to determine elasticity, then the statement "total exchange value would drop" becomes **"Uncertain"**. In MC questions, if a statement is not "necessarily true", we cannot select it. Therefore, even if you can't tell if it rises or drops, as long as it's not *definitively* dropping, you still wouldn't choose statement (3).

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

The "Rectangle Drawing" Trick (畫格仔大法):

在考場上，如果你忘記了中點彈性定律，可以直接在試卷上用筆畫出兩個矩形（如上方 SVG 圖所示）。

  1. 畫出原本的 \(P \times Q\) 面積（窄而高的矩形）。
  2. 畫出新的 \(P \times Q\) 面積（較闊而稍矮的矩形）。
  3. 比較失去的頂部面積（Price Loss）和獲得的右側面積（Quantity Gain）。因為原配額線很靠近左邊（Y軸），向右擴展的面積明顯大於頂部削去的面積，因此 TR 必然增加。

During the exam, if you forget the midpoint elasticity rule, just draw two rectangles directly on the question paper. Compare the area lost at the top (due to price drop) with the area gained on the right (due to quantity increase). Since the original quota is very close to the Y-axis, the gained area on the right visually dwarfs the lost area on top. Hence, TR increases.
