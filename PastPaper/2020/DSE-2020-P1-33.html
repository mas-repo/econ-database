Topic / 課題: Money & Banking / 貨幣與銀行
Difficulty / 難度: ★★★★☆
Key Concepts / 關鍵概念: Actual Banking Multiplier, Excess Reserves, Cash Leakage

The Question / 題目

下列哪項會令實際銀行乘數上升？

Which of the following will increase the actual banking multiplier?

  • A. 公眾傾向持有更多的現金。
    The public tends to hold more cash.
  • B. 信用卡的使用日漸普及。
    The use of credit card becomes more popular.
  • C. 中央銀行調高法定儲備比率。
    The central bank increases the required reserve ratio.
  • D. 銀行存款的利率下降。
    The interest rate of bank deposit decreases.

Concept Review / 概念重溫

實際銀行乘數 (Actual Banking Multiplier)

這是指每一元儲備實際上能支持多少存款。它取決於銀行實際持有的儲備比率。

This refers to how much deposit can be supported by one dollar of reserves. It depends on the actual reserve ratio held by banks.
$$ k_a = \frac{1}{r_a} $$
Where:
\( k_a \) = Actual Banking Multiplier (實際銀行乘數)
\( r_a \) = Actual Reserve Ratio (實際儲備比率)
$$ r_a = \frac{\text{Actual Reserves (實際儲備)}}{\text{Deposits (存款)}} $$

實際儲備 (Actual Reserves) 的組成：

$$ \text{Actual Reserves} = \text{Required Reserves} + \text{Excess Reserves} $$

$$ \text{實際儲備} = \text{法定儲備} + \text{超額儲備} $$

如果銀行持有超額儲備，實際儲備比率 \(r_a\) 會高於法定儲備比率，導致實際乘數 \(k_a\) 小於最大乘數。

Answer & Detailed Analysis / 答案與詳細分析

✅ Correct Option: B

信用卡的使用日漸普及 / The use of credit card becomes more popular.

詳細分析：

當信用卡普及，公眾對現金作為交易媒介的需求下降。這產生兩個效果：

  1. 公眾將手上的現金存入銀行，增加了銀行體系的儲備基礎（這影響貨幣供應量，但不直接改變乘數）。
  2. 關鍵點：由於公眾提款需求減少（因為多用卡少用現金），銀行預期流動性風險降低，因此願意減少持有的超額儲備 (Excess Reserves)。

當超額儲備減少，實際儲備比率 \(r_a\) 下降。根據公式 \( k_a = 1/r_a \)，實際銀行乘數上升。

Detailed Analysis: When credit cards become popular, the public's demand for cash as a medium of exchange drops. This has two effects: 1. Public deposits cash into banks (increasing the reserve base). 2. Pivot Point: Since withdrawal demand drops, banks face lower liquidity risk and are willing to reduce their Excess Reserves. When excess reserves drop, the actual reserve ratio \(r_a\) decreases. As \( k_a = 1/r_a \), the actual banking multiplier increases.

❌ Option A

公眾傾向持有更多的現金 / The public tends to hold more cash.

修正分析 (Revised Analysis)：

當公眾持有更多現金，他們會從銀行提取存款。這導致：

  1. 存款 (Deposits, D) 減少。
  2. 實際儲備 (Reserves, R) 減少（因為現金流出銀行體系）。

雖然 D 和 R 同時減少，但我們必須考慮銀行行為。面對公眾提款增加（現金流失），銀行為了應付流動性風險，往往會增加超額儲備的持有比例，以防擠提。這會導致實際儲備比率 \(r_a\) 上升，因此實際銀行乘數下降。

Revised Analysis: When the public holds more cash, they withdraw deposits. This causes both Deposits (D) and Reserves (R) to fall. However, facing higher withdrawal demands (cash leakage), banks will likely increase their Excess Reserves ratio to manage liquidity risk. This increases the actual reserve ratio \(r_a\), causing the actual banking multiplier to decrease.

❌ Option C

中央銀行調高法定儲備比率 / The central bank increases the required reserve ratio.

修正分析 (Revised Analysis)：

實際儲備比率 \(r_a\) 不會低於法定儲備比率 \(r_r\)。

  • 情況 1 (無超額儲備)：如果銀行原本沒有超額儲備 (\(r_a = r_r\))，調高 \(r_r\) 會直接迫使 \(r_a\) 上升，乘數下降。
  • 情況 2 (有超額儲備)：如果銀行持有大量超額儲備，調高 \(r_r\) 可能只是將部分「超額儲備」轉性為「法定儲備」，\(r_a\) 未必立刻改變。

然而，在一般情況下，調高法定要求會限制銀行的放貸能力，傾向於令實際儲備比率上升（或保持不變），絕不會令乘數上升。

Revised Analysis: The actual reserve ratio \(r_a\) cannot be lower than the required ratio \(r_r\). If \(r_r\) increases, it pushes the floor of \(r_a\) up. Even if banks hold excess reserves, increasing the requirement tightens the constraint. This leads to an increase (or at best, no change) in the actual reserve ratio, meaning the multiplier will decrease or stay constant, but never increase.

❌ Option D

銀行存款的利率下降 / The interest rate of bank deposit decreases.

分析：存款利率下降降低了公眾持有存款的誘因，可能導致提款（類似選項 A 的效果）。銀行面對資金流失風險，可能需要維持或增加超額儲備比率，導致乘數不變或下降。

Analysis: Lower deposit rates reduce the incentive to hold deposits, potentially leading to withdrawals (similar to Option A). Facing liquidity risk, banks may maintain or increase their excess reserve ratio, causing the multiplier to stay constant or decrease.

Problem Decomposition / 題目拆解

Logic Chain (邏輯鏈):

要令 \( \text{Multiplier} \uparrow \)，必須令 \( \text{Actual Reserve Ratio} \downarrow \)。

$$ \text{Actual Reserve Ratio} = \frac{\text{Required} + \text{Excess}}{\text{Deposits}} $$

  • 選項 A (現金流失): 銀行怕沒錢給人提款 \(\rightarrow\) 增加超額儲備 \(\rightarrow\) Ratio \(\uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) Multiplier \(\downarrow\)
  • 選項 B (信用卡普及): 銀行不怕人提款 \(\rightarrow\) 減少超額儲備 \(\rightarrow\) Ratio \(\downarrow\) \(\rightarrow\) Multiplier \(\uparrow\)
