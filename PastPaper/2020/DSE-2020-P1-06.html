Step 1: Analyze Captain Cook's Contribution (åˆ†ææ›²èˆ¹é•·çš„è²¢ç»)

Contribution: Cook provides two things:

  1. He works as the captain (Human effort \(\rightarrow\) Labour).
  2. He brings his own fishing boat (Man-made resource \(\rightarrow\) Capital).

Conclusion: His income consists of Wage (for labor) and Interest (for capital).
