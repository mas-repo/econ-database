📚 Topic: Market Intervention (市場干預 - 價格下限)

Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5 Stars)

Key Concepts: Price Floor (價格下限), Effective vs Ineffective (有效與無效), Efficiency Loss (效率損失)

📝 The Question (題目)

參閱物品 X 的供需圖。
若物品 X 的價格下限由 \(P_1\) 轉變至 \(P_2\)，

  1. 生產者盈餘會上升。
  2. 物品 X 的交易量會維持在 \(Q_1\)。
  3. 效率損失會增加。
  4. 物品 X 的超額需求會轉變成超額供應。

Refer to the supply-demand diagram for Good X.
If the price floor of Good X changes from \(P_1\) to \(P_2\),

  1. producer surplus will increase.
  2. quantity transacted of Good X will remain at \(Q_1\).
  3. efficiency loss will increase.
  4. excess demand of Good X will change to excess supply.

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

價格下限 (Price Floor)： 政府法例規定的最低交易價格。目的是保障生產者（如最低工資保障工人）。

Price Floor: The minimum legally allowable price set by the government. It aims to protect producers (e.g., minimum wage for workers).

  • 無效的價格下限 (Ineffective Price Floor): 設於均衡價格之下。市場力量會將價格推回均衡點，因此對市場沒有影響，沒有效率損失。
    Set below the equilibrium price. Market forces will push the price back to equilibrium, so it has no effect on the market and causes no efficiency loss.
  • 有效的價格下限 (Effective Price Floor): 設於均衡價格之上。價格被迫提高，導致需求量減少、供應量增加，出現超額供應 (Excess Supply)，並產生效率損失 (Efficiency Loss)。
    Set above the equilibrium price. The price is forced up, leading to a decrease in quantity demanded and an increase in quantity supplied, creating excess supply and an efficiency loss.

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): C

分析 A (Analysis A): 錯誤 (Incorrect)
當價格下限由無效的 \(P_1\)（市場處於均衡點）升至有效的 \(P_2\) 時，價格上升但交易量下跌至 \(Q_1\)。生產者盈餘的改變是不確定的（取決於得益於高價的面積與損失於交易量減少的面積之差）。我們無法肯定它一定會上升。
When the price floor changes from the ineffective \(P_1\) (market is at equilibrium) to the effective \(P_2\), price increases but quantity transacted falls to \(Q_1\). The change in producer surplus is uncertain (it depends on the gain from the higher price versus the loss from the lower quantity). We cannot be sure it will definitely increase.

分析 B (Analysis B): 錯誤 (Incorrect)
在 \(P_1\) 時，價格下限無效，交易量處於較高的均衡數量（兩線相交對落的數量）。當價格下限升至 \(P_2\) 時，交易量才下跌至 \(Q_1\)。因此，交易量不是「維持」在 \(Q_1\)。
At \(P_1\), the price floor is ineffective, and the quantity transacted is at the higher equilibrium quantity (the quantity directly below the intersection). When the price floor rises to \(P_2\), the quantity transacted falls to \(Q_1\). Therefore, the quantity does not "remain" at \(Q_1\).

分析 C (Analysis C): 正確 (Correct)
在 \(P_1\) 時（無效），市場處於均衡狀態，資源分配達致最高效率，效率損失為零。在 \(P_2\) 時（有效），交易量減少至 \(Q_1\)，導致總社會盈餘減少，產生了效率損失（圖中左側的三角形面積）。因此，效率損失增加了。
At \(P_1\) (ineffective), the market is at equilibrium, resource allocation is efficient, and efficiency loss is zero. At \(P_2\) (effective), quantity transacted falls to \(Q_1\), reducing total social surplus and creating an efficiency loss (the triangle area on the left). Therefore, efficiency loss increases.

分析 D (Analysis D): 錯誤 (Incorrect)
在 \(P_1\) 時，由於價格下限無效，市場會自動調節至均衡點，此時沒有超額需求或超額供應（市場結清）。因此，情況是從「沒有超額供求」轉變為「超額供應」，而不是從「超額需求」轉變而來。
At \(P_1\), because the price floor is ineffective, the market automatically adjusts to equilibrium, where there is no excess demand or excess supply (market clears). Therefore, the situation changes from "no excess demand/supply" to "excess supply", not from "excess demand".

🔍 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point):
這題的關鍵在於辨認 \(P_1\) 是一個無效的價格下限。很多同學看到 \(P_1\) 在均衡點下方，就立刻聯想到「超額需求」，但這是將「價格下限 (Floor)」與「價格上限 (Ceiling)」混淆了！

Pivot Point: The key to this question is recognizing that \(P_1\) is an ineffective price floor. Many students see \(P_1\) below the equilibrium and immediately think of "excess demand", but this is confusing a "Price Floor" with a "Price Ceiling"!

步驟 (Steps):

  1. 標示原來的狀態：\(P_1\) 是下限，但低於均衡價。結果：無效，市場在均衡點運作。
  2. 標示新的狀態：\(P_2\) 是下限，高於均衡價。結果：有效，價格升至 \(P_2\)，交易量跌至 \(Q_1\)。
  3. 比較兩者差異：從「完美均衡」變成「有干預的扭曲狀態」，必然產生效率損失。

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：死背圖表位置 (Trap: Memorizing diagram positions blindly)
學生常誤以為「在均衡點下方的橫線就是價格上限 (Price Ceiling)，會造成超額需求」。
糾正： 必須先看清楚題目寫的是「價格下限」還是「價格上限」。價格下限設在均衡點下方是無效的 (Ineffective)，不會造成超額需求，市場會停留在均衡點！

Students often mistakenly assume "a horizontal line below equilibrium is a Price Ceiling and causes excess demand".
Correction: You must read carefully whether the question states "Price Floor" or "Price Ceiling". A price floor set below equilibrium is ineffective; it does not cause excess demand, and the market stays at equilibrium!

🙋 Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼選項 A 的生產者盈餘 (Producer Surplus) 不一定上升？價格不是變高了嗎？
Q: Why isn't Producer Surplus (Option A) guaranteed to increase? Didn't the price go up?

A: 雖然生產者能以更高的價格 \(P_2\) 售出物品（這會增加盈餘），但同時交易量由均衡數量大幅下跌至 \(Q_1\)（這會減少盈餘）。除非我們知道供求曲線的實際彈性 (elasticity)，否則無法確定增加的面積是否大於減少的面積。
A: Although producers can sell the good at a higher price \(P_2\) (which increases surplus), the quantity transacted simultaneously drops significantly from the equilibrium quantity to \(Q_1\) (which decreases surplus). Unless we know the actual elasticities of the supply and demand curves, we cannot be certain if the area gained is larger than the area lost.

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法 (Shortcut):
在 DSE 選擇題中，當你看到市場從「無干預 / 無效干預」轉變為「有效干預」（例如徵稅、配額、有效價格管制）時，「效率損失會增加」 (Efficiency loss will increase) 幾乎 99% 是正確答案。因為任何將市場推離自由均衡點的有效干預，必定會產生效率損失。
In DSE MC questions, when you see a market transition from "no intervention / ineffective intervention" to "effective intervention" (e.g., tax, quota, effective price control), "efficiency loss will increase" is almost 99% the correct answer. This is because any effective intervention that pushes the market away from the free-market equilibrium will inevitably create an efficiency loss.
