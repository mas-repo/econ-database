📚 Topic (課題): 價格差異與不同物品 (Price Differences and Differentiated Goods)
⭐ Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆
🔑 Key Concepts (核心概念): 需求變動 (Shift in Demand), 品質差異 (Quality Difference), 溢價 (Premium)

1. 題目 (The Question)

小明在一家渡假酒店預訂了一間房。後來他發現小欣在同家酒店以較低價格預訂了一間房。下列哪些是這價格差異的可能原因？

(1) 小明的預訂屬不可退款，而小欣的預訂可免費取消。
(2) 小明預訂了週末的房間，而小欣預訂了平日的房間。
(3) 小明預訂了海景房，而小欣預訂了街景房。

A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

Alfred booked a room in a resort hotel. Later he found that Betty had booked a room in the same hotel at a lower price. Which of the following are possible reasons for such a price difference?

(1) Alfred's booking was non-refundable while Betty's booking was with free cancellation.
(2) Alfred booked the room in a weekend while Betty booked the room in a weekday.
(3) Alfred booked the room with sea view while Betty booked the room with street view.

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

價格差異 (Price Differences)

同一家酒店的房間價格不同，通常源於以下幾個原因：

  • 產品特徵/品質不同 (Different Quality/Features): 較高的品質或附加服務（如海景、免費取消）會帶來較高的邊際成本或較高的消費者支付意願，因此價格較高。
  • 需求變動 (Shift in Demand): 在不同時間（如週末與平日），市場需求 \(D\) 不同。週末需求較高，均衡價格 \(P\) 亦會較高。

Price Differences

Different room prices in the same hotel usually stem from:

  • Different Quality/Features: Higher quality or add-on services (e.g., sea view, free cancellation) lead to higher marginal costs or higher willingness to pay, resulting in higher prices.
  • Shift in Demand: Market demand \(D\) varies at different times (e.g., weekends vs. weekdays). Higher demand during weekends leads to a higher equilibrium price \(P\).

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): C

分析 (1): 錯誤 (Incorrect)

「免費取消」是一項附加價值（彈性較高），通常酒店會對此收取較高的價格（溢價）。如果小明的預訂是「不可退款」，他應該享有折扣，支付較低的價格。但題目指小明支付的價格較高，因此這不能解釋價格差異。

"Free cancellation" is an added value (higher flexibility), for which hotels usually charge a premium. If Alfred's booking was "non-refundable", he should have received a discount and paid a lower price. However, the question states Alfred paid more, so this cannot explain the price difference.

分析 (2): 正確 (Correct)

週末對渡假酒店的需求通常遠高於平日。需求增加導致價格上升。這能合理釋釋為何小明（週末）支付的價格高於小欣（平日）。

The demand for resort hotels is usually much higher on weekends than on weekdays. An increase in demand leads to a higher price. This perfectly explains why Alfred (weekend) paid more than Betty (weekday).

分析 (3): 正確 (Correct)

海景房的質素較高，消費者的支付意願較高，酒店的定價自然較高。這能解釋為何小明（海景房）支付的價格較高。

A sea view room is of higher quality and consumers have a higher willingness to pay for it, so the hotel sets a higher price. This explains why Alfred (sea view) paid more.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解答此題的「關鍵點」(Pivot Point) 在於釐清「誰付了較高的價錢」。題目明確指出小欣以較低價格預訂，即小明付了較高的價格。

我們只需逐一檢驗選項，看看哪個情況會導致小明面臨較高的價格：

  • (1) 小明不可退款 \(\rightarrow\) 價格應較低 (與題目不符)
  • (2) 小明週末入住 \(\rightarrow\) 價格應較高 (符合)
  • (3) 小明住海景房 \(\rightarrow\) 價格應較高 (符合)

The "Pivot Point" of this question is identifying who paid the higher price. The question states Betty paid a lower price, meaning Alfred paid a higher price.

We just need to check which scenario leads to a higher price for Alfred:

  • (1) Alfred is non-refundable \(\rightarrow\) Price should be lower (Contradicts the question)
  • (2) Alfred stays on a weekend \(\rightarrow\) Price should be higher (Matches)
  • (3) Alfred has a sea view \(\rightarrow\) Price should be higher (Matches)

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱：粗心大意 (Careless Error)

很多同學看到 (1) 知道「退款政策不同會導致價格不同」，就立刻認為它是正確的原因。但忽略了方向性！不可退款的房間風險由消費者承擔，因此價格必然較便宜。做題時必須將條件對應到特定人物（小明）身上。

Trap: Careless Error
Many students see (1) and think "different refund policies lead to different prices", assuming it's a valid reason. But they ignore the direction! Non-refundable rooms shift the risk to the consumer, so they are always cheaper. You must map the condition to the specific person (Alfred) when answering.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 這題是在考「價格分歧」(Price Discrimination) 嗎？

A: 嚴格來說，這題更多是考「不同物品」(Differentiated Goods)。價格分歧是指「相同成本的同一物品，以不同價格售予不同顧客」。但在選項 (2) 和 (3) 中，週末與平日的房間（時間不同）、海景與街景（質素不同）在經濟學上被視為不同的物品，因此價格不同是正常的市場現象，不一定屬於純粹的價格分歧。

Q: Is this question testing "Price Discrimination"?

A: Strictly speaking, it tests "Differentiated Goods" more. Price discrimination is selling the "same good with the same cost at different prices to different customers". In options (2) and (3), rooms on weekends vs. weekdays (different time) and sea view vs. street view (different quality) are considered different goods in economics. Therefore, different prices are normal market phenomena and not necessarily pure price discrimination.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

秒殺法 (The 10-Second Shortcut):

常識判斷：「免費取消」是一項服務升級 (Upgrade)。升級要加錢。小欣有升級，小明沒有。如果只有這個差異，小明應該比較便宜。但題目說小明比較貴，所以 (1) 絕對錯。刪去包含 (1) 的 A、B、D，直接得出答案 C！

The 10-Second Shortcut:
Common sense: "Free cancellation" is an upgrade. Upgrades cost extra. Betty has the upgrade, Alfred doesn't. If this were the only difference, Alfred should be cheaper. But the question says Alfred is more expensive, so (1) is definitely wrong. Eliminate A, B, and D which contain (1). The answer is instantly C!
