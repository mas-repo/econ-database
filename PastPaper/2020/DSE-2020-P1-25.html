Topic 國民收入會計 / National Income Accounting
Difficulty ★★★★☆
Key Concepts GDP, GNI, Net Factor Income from Abroad

HKDSE Economics: GDP vs GNI Adjustment

📝 題目 / The Question

在計算香港的本地居民總收入時，下列哪項應從香港的本地生產總值中扣除？

Which of the following should be deducted from the gross domestic product of Hong Kong to calculate the gross national income of Hong Kong?

  • A. 一家香港足球會付給一支日本球隊來港進行一場表演賽的費用
    the fee paid by a Hong Kong football club to a Japanese football team for an exhibition match in Hong Kong
  • B. 一家香港地產發展商從其於日本的住宅物業賺得的租金
    the rent earned by a Hong Kong property developer from its residential property in Japan
  • C. 一家日本保險公司從香港的金融投資賺得的資本增益
    the capital gain earned by a Japanese insurance corporation from its financial investment in Hong Kong
  • D. 一家日本上市公司付給一名香港居民的股息
    the dividend paid by a listed company in Japan to a Hong Kong resident

🧠 概念重溫 / Concept Review

要解答這條問題，我們必須清楚理解本地生產總值 (GDP)與本地居民總收入 (GNI)之間的關係。

To answer this question, we must clearly understand the relationship between Gross Domestic Product (GDP) and Gross National Income (GNI).

$$ \text{GNI} = \text{GDP} + \text{Net Factor Income from Abroad (NIA)} $$ $$ \text{本地居民總收入} = \text{本地生產總值} + \text{從外地賺取的淨要素收益} $$

其中，淨要素收益 (NIA) 的計算方法為：

Where Net Factor Income from Abroad (NIA) is calculated as:

$$ \text{NIA} = \text{Factor income earned by residents from abroad} - \text{Factor income earned by non-residents in domestic territory} $$ $$ \text{NIA} = \text{本地居民從外地賺取的要素收益} - \text{非本地居民在本地賺取的要素收益} $$

因此，要從 GDP 計算 GNI，我們需要加上本地居民在外地賺取的收入，並扣除非本地居民在本地賺取的收入。

Therefore, to calculate GNI from GDP, we need to add the income earned by residents from abroad, and deduct the income earned by non-residents in the domestic territory.

✅ 答案與詳細分析 / The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 / Correct Option: A

選項 A (正確 Correct): 日本球隊（非本地居民）在香港進行表演賽，他們在香港境內提供了生產要素（勞力），創造了產值。這筆費用包含在香港的 GDP 中。但在計算 GNI 時，因為這筆收入屬於非本地居民，所以必須從 GDP 中扣除（作為支付予外地的要素收益）。

The Japanese football team (non-residents) played a match in HK, providing factors of production (labor) within HK's territory. This fee is included in HK's GDP. However, when calculating GNI, since this income belongs to non-residents, it must be deducted from GDP (as factor income paid abroad).

選項 B (錯誤 Incorrect): 香港地產商（本地居民）從日本（外地）賺取租金。這是本地居民從外地賺取的要素收益，在計算 GNI 時應該加上，而不是扣除。

A HK developer (resident) earns rent from Japan (abroad). This is factor income earned by a resident from abroad. It should be added to GDP when calculating GNI, not deducted.

選項 C (錯誤 Incorrect): 資本增益（Capital gain）並不涉及當期的生產活動，因此它一開始就沒有被計入 GDP 中。既然不在 GDP 內，自然不需要在計算 GNI 時作任何扣除。

Capital gains do not involve current production activities, so they are not included in GDP in the first place. Since it's not in GDP, there is no need to deduct it when calculating GNI.

選項 D (錯誤 Incorrect): 香港居民從日本公司收取股息。這是本地居民從外地賺取的要素收益，在計算 GNI 時應該加上，而不是扣除。

A HK resident receives dividends from a Japanese company. This is factor income earned by a resident from abroad. It should be added to GDP when calculating GNI, not deducted.

🔍 題目拆解 / Problem Decomposition

這類題目的「破題關鍵」(Pivot Point) 在於留意題目要求的是「扣除」(deducted)。根據公式 \( \text{GNI} = \text{GDP} + \text{Resident income from abroad} - \text{Non-resident income in HK} \)，需要扣除的必然是「非本地居民在香港的收入」。

The "Pivot Point" of this question lies in the word "deducted". According to the formula \( \text{GNI} = \text{GDP} + \text{Resident income from abroad} - \text{Non-resident income in HK} \), what needs to be deducted must be "income earned by non-residents in Hong Kong".

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 / Common Mistakes & Traps

陷阱：資本增益 (Capital Gains)

很多學生看到「日本保險公司在香港賺錢」(選項C)，就直覺認為要扣除。但請記住，資本增益（如炒賣股票、樓宇賺取的差價）不屬於生產活動，根本不計入 GDP。如果一開始就不在 GDP 裡面，就沒有「扣除」的需要！

Trap: Capital Gains. Many students see "Japanese company earning in HK" (Option C) and instinctively think it should be deducted. Remember, capital gains (e.g., profit from trading stocks or property) do not result from current production and are excluded from GDP. If it's never in GDP, you don't deduct it!

🙋 學生常見疑問 / Student Q&A

Q: 股息 (Dividends) 和租金 (Rent) 算是要素收益 (Factor Income) 嗎？

A: 是的！在國民收入會計中，要素收益包括僱員補償（工資）以及營業盈餘（包括租金、利息、股息和利潤）。因此選項 B 和 D 都是要素收益的流動。

A: Yes! In national income accounting, factor income includes compensation of employees (wages) and operating surplus (which includes rent, interest, dividends, and profits). Thus, Options B and D are flows of factor income.

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 / Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

在考試中遇到「從 GDP 計算 GNI 需扣除什麼」的題目，你可以使用「三步秒殺法」：

When facing "what to deduct from GDP to get GNI" questions in exams, use the "3-Step Quick Kill" method:

  1. 尋找關鍵字「扣除」(Deduct) \(\rightarrow\) 目標是尋找外國人 (Non-residents)。
  2. 尋找地點 \(\rightarrow\) 目標是尋找他們在本地 (in HK) 賺錢。
  3. 排除非生產性收入 \(\rightarrow\) 立即劃走所有「資本增益」(Capital gains) 或「轉移支付」(Transfer payments)。

套用此法：選項 B 和 D 是香港人賺錢（錯）；選項 C 是資本增益（錯）；只剩下選項 A（外國人在香港賺錢），秒殺！

Applying this: Options B & D are HK residents earning (Wrong); Option C is capital gain (Wrong); Only Option A remains (Foreigners earning in HK). Solved instantly!
