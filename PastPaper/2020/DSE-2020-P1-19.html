📚 Topic (課題): Market Intervention - Price Ceiling (市場干預 - 價格上限)

⭐ Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆ (3/5)

🔑 Key Concepts (核心概念): Price Ceiling (價格上限), Deadweight Loss (效率損失), Shortage (短缺)

📝 The Question (題目)

細閱下列的新聞撮要：
近十年來，由於需求持續上升，柏林住宅單位的租金已上升一倍。為了應付該問題，柏林政府實施「租金凍結法」，限制住宅單位的租金最少5年內不能增加。

假設香港推行類似的法例。下列哪項陳述是正確的？

Study the following news extract.
In the last decade, the rent of residential units in Berlin was doubled due to continuous increase in demand. To deal with the problem, the Berlin government imposed a 'Rent Frozen Bill' under which the rent of residential units was not allowed to increase for at least 5 years.

Suppose a similar bill is implemented in Hong Kong. Which of the following statements is correct?

  • A. 堅尼系數會上升。 (The Gini coefficient will increase.)
  • B. 政府的物業稅收入會上升。 (The property tax revenue of the government will increase.)
  • C. 若住宅單位的需求持續上升，效率損失會出現。 (There will be a deadweight loss if the demand for residential units keeps on increasing.)
  • D. 物業擁有人會有較高的意欲裝修其單位。 (The property owners will have higher incentives to renovate their flats.)

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

Price Ceiling (價格上限)

價格上限是政府規定的最高合法價格。當價格上限設定在均衡價格之下時，它才是有效 (effective) 的。
A price ceiling is a legal maximum on the price at which a good can be sold. It is effective only when it is set below the equilibrium price.

$$ P_c < P_e $$

Where \(P_c\) is Price Ceiling (價格上限) and \(P_e\) is Equilibrium Price (均衡價格)

Consequences of an Effective Price Ceiling (有效價格上限的後果):

  • Shortage (短缺): Quantity demanded exceeds quantity supplied \( (Q_d > Q_s) \).
  • Deadweight Loss (效率損失 / 淨損失): Total social surplus is reduced because the quantity transacted falls below the efficient market equilibrium quantity.
  • Quality Deterioration (質素下降): Sellers cannot raise prices, so they cut costs by reducing quality or maintenance.

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): C

Option A: 堅尼系數會上升 (The Gini coefficient will increase) - ❌ WRONG

租金管制通常會將財富從較富有的業主轉移到較低收入的租客身上，這可能會使收入分佈變得更平均，導致堅尼系數下降或影響不明確，而非必然上升。
Rent control typically transfers wealth from wealthier landlords to lower-income tenants. This might make income distribution more even, potentially decreasing the Gini coefficient or making the effect ambiguous. It will not necessarily increase.

Option B: 政府的物業稅收入會上升 (The property tax revenue will increase) - ❌ WRONG

物業稅通常基於租值計算。如果租金被凍結，租值無法隨市場上升，政府的物業稅收入將會停滯甚至減少，而不會上升。
Property tax is usually based on rental value. If rents are frozen, the rental value cannot rise with the market, meaning property tax revenue will stagnate or decrease, not increase.

Option C: 若需求持續上升，效率損失會出現 (DWL will occur if demand increases) - ✅ CORRECT

當需求持續上升，市場的均衡價格 \(P_e\) 會隨之上升。由於租金被凍結（即價格上限 \(P_c\) 固定），當 \(P_e\) 升穿 \(P_c\) 時，該「租金凍結」便成為了有效的價格上限 (effective price ceiling)。這會導致供應量減少，交易量低於市場均衡水平，從而產生效率損失 (Deadweight Loss)。
When demand keeps increasing, the market equilibrium price \(P_e\) rises. Since the rent is frozen (fixed price ceiling \(P_c\)), once \(P_e\) rises above \(P_c\), the rent freeze becomes an effective price ceiling. This reduces the quantity transacted below the efficient market level, resulting in a deadweight loss.

Option D: 業主有較高意欲裝修單位 (Higher incentives to renovate) - ❌ WRONG

由於租金被凍結，業主無法透過提高租金來彌補裝修成本。此外，市場出現短缺 \( (Q_d > Q_s) \)，業主不愁沒有租客，因此他們缺乏意欲去維修或裝修單位。
Because rents are frozen, landlords cannot raise rents to cover renovation costs. Furthermore, due to the shortage \( (Q_d > Q_s) \), landlords have no trouble finding tenants, giving them lower incentives to maintain or renovate their flats.

🔍 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (解題關鍵):
題目提到「需求持續上升 (demand keeps on increasing)」。這句是全題的核心！在沒有管制下，需求上升會令均衡租金上升。法例「凍結租金」等同設立了價格上限 (Price Ceiling)。當均衡租金高於凍結的租金時，這個管制就會生效 (effective)，從而引發一系列經濟後果（短缺、效率損失等）。

The phrase "demand keeps on increasing" is the core of the question! Without intervention, rising demand increases equilibrium rent. The "rent freeze" acts as a Price Ceiling. When the equilibrium rent rises above the frozen rent, the control becomes effective, triggering economic consequences like shortages and deadweight loss.

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • Trap 1: 誤以為價格管制「一定」會導致效率損失。記住，只有當管制是有效 (effective) 時（即 \(P_c < P_e\)），才會出現效率損失。題目特意加入「需求持續上升」來確保均衡價格會超過凍結租金，使管制生效。
    Assuming price controls "always" cause DWL. Remember, DWL only occurs if the control is effective (\(P_c < P_e\)). The question specifically added "demand keeps on increasing" to ensure the equilibrium price surpasses the frozen rent, making the ceiling effective.

🙋 Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果題目改為「需求持續下降」，答案會有什麼不同？ (What if the question said "demand keeps decreasing"?)

A: 如果需求下降，均衡租金 \(P_e\) 會下跌。如果 \(P_e\) 跌至低於凍結租金 \(P_c\) 的水平，這個價格上限就會變得無效 (ineffective)。在這種情況下，市場會如常運作，不會出現效率損失 (No Deadweight Loss)。
If demand decreases, equilibrium rent \(P_e\) falls. If \(P_e\) falls below the frozen rent \(P_c\), the price ceiling becomes ineffective. The market would clear normally, and there would be no deadweight loss.

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

Elimination Method (排除法):
租金管制 (Rent Control) 是一項對業主極度不利的政策。知道這一點後，你可以秒殺選項 B 和 D：業主收不到更多租金，政府自然收不到更多稅 (B錯)；業主無利可圖，自然不會花錢裝修 (D錯)。剩下 A 和 C，而 C 是教科書中價格上限的標準後果，直接選 C。
Rent control is notoriously bad for landlords. Knowing this, you can instantly eliminate B and D: landlords can't earn more, so the government can't tax more (B is wrong); landlords can't make a profit, so they won't spend on renovations (D is wrong). This leaves A and C, and C is the standard textbook consequence of a price ceiling. Choose C.
