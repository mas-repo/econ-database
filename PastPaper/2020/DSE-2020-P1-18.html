HKDSE Economics Tutorial

Topic (課題): Types of Business Expansion / 企業擴張的種類 (Firm and Production / 廠商與生產)

Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆ (2/5 Stars)

Key Concepts (核心概念): Forward Expansion (前向擴張) Conglomerate Expansion (集團擴張) Stages of Production (生產階段)

The Question (題目)

ABC鑽石切割工廠的業務擴張如下：

ABC Diamond Cutting Factories expand its business as follows:

[ABC鑽石切割工廠 / ABC Diamond Cutting Factories]

↓ (X)            ↓ (Y)

[ABC珠寶店 / ABC Jewellery Shops]      [ABC酒店 / ABC Hotel]

對 ABC 鑽石切割工廠來說，X 和 Y 所示的擴張類型分別是 ______ 擴張和 ______ 擴張。

To ABC Diamond Cutting Factories, the types of expansion shown by X and Y are ______ expansion and ______ expansion respectively.

  • A. 後向 ...... 集團
    A. backward ...... conglomerate
  • B. 後向 ...... 側向
    B. backward ...... lateral
  • C. 前向 ...... 集團
    C. forward ...... conglomerate
  • D. 前向 ...... 側向
    D. forward ...... lateral

Concept Review (概念重溫)

在廠商與生產的課題中，企業擴張（或合併）主要分為以下幾類：

In the topic of Firm and Production, business expansion (or integration) is mainly classified into the following types:

  • 垂直擴張 (Vertical Expansion): 同一行業，但處於不同的生產階段。
    Vertical Expansion: Same industry, but at different stages of production.
    • 前向 (Forward): 向產品的最終消費者方向擴張（例如：製造商開設零售店）。
      Forward: Expanding towards the final consumers of the product (e.g., a manufacturer opening a retail shop).
    • 後向 (Backward): 向原材料供應的方向擴張（例如：製造商收購原材料農場）。
      Backward: Expanding towards the supply of raw materials (e.g., a manufacturer acquiring a raw material farm).
  • 橫向擴張 (Horizontal Expansion): 同一行業，且處於相同的生產階段（例如：兩間超級市場合併）。
    Horizontal Expansion: Same industry, and at the same stage of production (e.g., two supermarkets merging).
  • 側向擴張 (Lateral Expansion): 不同行業，但彼此有關聯（例如產品有互補關係，或擁有共同的目標客群）。
    Lateral Expansion: Different industries, but related to each other (e.g., complementary goods, or sharing the same target market).
  • 集團擴張 (Conglomerate Expansion): 完全不同且毫無關聯的行業。
    Conglomerate Expansion: Completely different and unrelated industries.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Answer: Option C (選項 C)

分析 X (擴張至珠寶店) / Analysis of X (Expansion to Jewellery Shops):

ABC原本是「鑽石切割工廠」（屬於製造/加工階段）。擴張至「珠寶店」（屬於零售階段）意味著企業向著最終消費者的方向邁進。這屬於同一行業的下一個生產階段，因此是前向擴張 (Forward Expansion)。

ABC was originally a "Diamond Cutting Factory" (manufacturing/processing stage). Expanding into "Jewellery Shops" (retail stage) means the firm is moving closer to the final consumers. This is the next stage of production within the same industry, hence it is Forward Expansion.

分析 Y (擴張至酒店) / Analysis of Y (Expansion to Hotel):

「鑽石切割」與「酒店」業務性質完全不同，兩者在生產鏈上沒有交集，產品之間也沒有直接的關聯（不是互補品或替代品）。這種跨足完全無關行業的行為屬於集團擴張 (Conglomerate Expansion)。

"Diamond cutting" and "Hotel" businesses are completely different in nature. They do not intersect in the production chain, nor are their products directly related (not complements or substitutes). Venturing into a completely unrelated industry is Conglomerate Expansion.

選項排除 / Eliminating Options:

A & B 錯誤：因為 X 是向零售端發展，不是向原材料端（如鑽石礦場）發展，所以不是後向 (Backward)。

A & B are incorrect: Because X is developing towards the retail end, not the raw material end (like a diamond mine), it is not Backward.

D 錯誤：因為 Y（酒店）與鑽石切割毫無關聯，不能算作側向 (Lateral)。側向擴張需要兩者有一定關聯，例如鑽石切割廠擴張去製造高級手錶。

D is incorrect: Because Y (hotel) is completely unrelated to diamond cutting, it cannot be considered Lateral. Lateral expansion requires some connection, for example, a diamond cutting factory expanding into manufacturing luxury watches.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解答這類題目的關鍵樞紐 (Pivot Point) 在於判斷新舊業務之間的「行業關聯度」與「生產階段」。

The Pivot Point for solving this type of question lies in determining the "industry relationship" and "stage of production" between the old and new businesses.

  1. 步驟一：判斷是否同行業。 鑽石切割與珠寶店同屬珠寶首飾行業；鑽石切割與酒店屬於完全不同的行業。
    Step 1: Determine if they are in the same industry. Diamond cutting and jewellery shops belong to the same jewellery industry; diamond cutting and hotels belong to completely different industries.
  2. 步驟二：同行業看方向。 既然 X 是同行業，問自己：是更接近買家（前向）還是更接近原料（後向）？工廠到商店，顯然是接近買家。
    Step 2: Check direction for the same industry. Since X is in the same industry, ask yourself: is it closer to the buyer (forward) or closer to raw materials (backward)? Factory to shop is obviously closer to the buyer.
  3. 步驟三：不同行業看關聯。 既然 Y 是不同行業，問自己：兩者有關聯嗎？鑽石和酒店沒有直接關聯，所以是集團擴張。
    Step 3: Check relationship for different industries. Since Y is a different industry, ask yourself: are they related? Diamonds and hotels have no direct relation, so it's a conglomerate expansion.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

混淆「側向 (Lateral)」與「集團 (Conglomerate)」：

Confusing "Lateral" and "Conglomerate":

很多學生一看到「不同行業」就隨便猜一個。記住，側向擴張必須有「關聯」。例如，一家報社收購一家雜誌社，或者一家生產網球拍的公司開始生產網球。而集團擴張則是八竿子打不著的關係，例如做鑽石的去開酒店、做地產的去賣豬肉。

Many students just guess one when they see "different industries". Remember, Lateral Expansion must have a "connection". For example, a newspaper acquiring a magazine, or a tennis racket manufacturer starting to produce tennis balls. Conglomerate Expansion involves completely unrelated businesses, like a diamond company opening a hotel, or a real estate developer selling pork.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 對於 ABC 鑽石切割工廠來說，怎樣才算是「後向擴張 (Backward Expansion)」？

Q: For ABC Diamond Cutting Factories, what would be considered "Backward Expansion"?

A: 如果 ABC 工廠收購了一個「鑽石礦場 (Diamond Mine)」，這就是後向擴張。因為礦場是提供未經切割的原石（原材料）給切割工廠的，這代表企業向生產鏈的上游（原材料端）發展。

A: If ABC Factories acquired a "Diamond Mine", that would be backward expansion. Because a mine provides uncut rough stones (raw materials) to the cutting factory, meaning the firm is moving upstream in the production chain (towards raw materials).

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

逆向淘汰法 (Reverse Elimination)：

Reverse Elimination:

在考試中，你可以先看最明顯、最容易判斷的那一個。在這題中，Y（鑽石廠開酒店）顯然是完全無關的行業，必定是「集團擴張 (Conglomerate)」。
一旦確定 Y 是「集團」，你就可以立刻刪除選項 B 和 D。剩下的 A 和 C 中，只需判斷 X 是前向還是後向即可，這大大節省了思考時間並提高了準確率。

In an exam, you can look at the most obvious one first. In this question, Y (diamond factory opening a hotel) is clearly a completely unrelated industry, which must be "Conglomerate".
Once you confirm Y is "Conglomerate", you can immediately eliminate options B and D. Between the remaining A and C, you only need to determine if X is forward or backward, which saves thinking time and increases accuracy.
