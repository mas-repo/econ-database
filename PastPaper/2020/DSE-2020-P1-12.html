Topic: Supply Curve & Ceteris Paribus
課題：供應曲線與其他因素不變

Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆ Law of Supply (供應定律) Ceteris Paribus (其他因素不變)
The Question (題目)

當導出某物品的市場供應曲線時，下列哪項／些會假設為不變？

When the market supply curve of a good is derived, which of the following is/are assumed to be constant?

(1) 該物品的市場價格
the market price of the good

(2) 該物品的生產要素價格
the prices of factors of production of the good

(3) 預期該物品在將來的價格
the expected future price of the good

  • A. 只有 (1) (1) only
  • B. 只有 (2) (2) only
  • C. 只有 (3) (3) only
  • D. 只有 (2)、(3) (2) and (3) only
Concept Review (概念重溫)
1. 二維圖表法則 1. The 2D Graph Rule 試想像一個標準的供應圖表，它只有兩條軸：
  • Y 軸 (縱軸)： 價格 (\(P\))
  • X 軸 (橫軸)： 供應量 (\(Q_s\))
曲線顯示當 \(P\) 改變時，\(Q_s\) 如何改變。因此，價格和數量容許改變（它們是沿著線移動的）。 Think of a standard Supply Graph. It only has two axes:
  • Y-axis: Price (\(P\))
  • X-axis: Quantity Supplied (\(Q_s\))
The curve shows how \(Q_s\) changes when \(P\) changes. Therefore, Price and Quantity are allowed to change (they move along the line).
2. 其他因素不變 (Ceteris Paribus) 2. Ceteris Paribus 任何影響供應但不在坐標軸上的因素，在繪畫這條線時都必須保持不變。如果這些「背景因素」改變，整條線就會移動。

常見的「背景因素」（必須不變）：
  • 生產成本（工資、原材料）
  • 技術水平
  • 賣家數量
  • 預期（未來價格）
  • 天氣 / 政府政策
 Any factor that affects supply but is NOT on the axes must be held constant to draw the line. If these "background factors" change, the line itself will shift.

Common "Background Factors" (Must be constant):
  • Cost of production (wages, raw materials)
  • Technology
  • Number of sellers
  • Expectations (future price)
  • Weather / Government policies
Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: D

(1) 該物品的市場價格 — 錯誤 (1) The market price of the good — False 價格位於 Y 軸。供應曲線的目的就是觀察價格變動時供應量如何反應。因此，價格是可變的，並非固定。 Price is on the Y-axis. The whole point of a supply curve is to see how quantity responds when price changes. Therefore, price is variable (not constant).
(2) 該物品的生產要素價格 — 正確 (2) The prices of factors of production — True 這指生產成本（如工資、租金）。這不在坐標軸上。若成本改變，生產者的計劃會完全改變（曲線移動）。為了畫出一條特定的曲線，我們必須假設成本固定。 This refers to costs (e.g., wages, rent). This is not on the axis. If costs change, producers change their plans completely (the curve shifts). To draw one specific curve, we must assume costs are fixed.
(3) 預期該物品在將來的價格 — 正確 (3) The expected future price — True 這是關於未來的心理因素。它不在坐標軸上（坐標軸顯示的是「現時」價格）。若賣家預期下週價格上升，他們今天可能會減少出售。分析今天的供應時，此因素必須假設不變。 This is a psychological factor regarding the future. It is not on the axis (the axis shows the current price). If sellers expect prices to rise next week, they might sell less today. This factor must be held constant to analyze today's supply.
Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)
「軸上」測試法 The "On the Axis" Test 對每一個選項使用這個簡單的邏輯檢查： Use this simple logic check for every option:

該因素是否在 X 軸或 Y 軸上？

Is the factor on the X or Y axis?

  • 是 (價格、數量)： 它會改變。它不會保持不變。
    YES (Price, Quantity): It changes. It is NOT held constant.
  • 否 (成本、未來、天氣)： 它是背景因素。它必須保持不變。
    NO (Costs, Future, Weather): It is a background factor. It MUST be held constant.
Common Mistakes (常犯錯誤)
混淆「現時價格」與「預期價格」 Confusing "Current Price" with "Expected Price" 學生常以為凡是「價格」都不變，或者凡是「價格」都可變。 Students often get confused by the word "price", thinking either all prices are constant or all are variable.
  • 現時價格 (Current Price, P)： 在軸上 → 可變 (Variable)。
    Current Price (P): On the axis → Variable.
  • 預期未來價格 (Expected Future Price, \(P^e\))： 不在軸上 → 不變 (Constant)。
    Expected Future Price (\(P^e\)): Not on the axis → Constant.
