HKDSE Economics Guide: Supply Curve Assumptions

Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆

Topic: Supply Curve & Ceteris Paribus
課題：供應曲線與其他因素不變

The Question (題目)

當導出某物品的市場供應曲線時，下列哪項／些會假設為不變？

When the market supply curve of a good is derived, which of the following is/are assumed to be constant?
  1. 該物品的市場價格
    the market price of the good
  2. 該物品的生產要素價格
    the prices of factors of production of the good
  3. 預期該物品在將來的價格
    the expected future price of the good
Options:
  • A. 只有 (1) (1) only
  • B. 只有 (2) (2) only
  • C. 只有 (3) (3) only
  • D. 只有 (2)、(3) (2) and (3) only

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 二維圖表法則 (The 2D Graph Rule)

試想像一個標準的供應圖表，它只有兩條軸：

Think of a standard Supply Graph. It only has two axes:
  • Y 軸 (縱軸)： 價格 \(P\)
    Y-axis: Price \(P\)
  • X 軸 (橫軸)： 供應量 \(Q_s\)
    X-axis: Quantity Supplied \(Q_s\)

曲線顯示當 \(P\) 改變時，\(Q_s\) 如何改變。因此，價格和數量容許改變（它們是沿著線移動的）。

The curve shows how \(Q_s\) changes when \(P\) changes. Therefore, Price and Quantity are allowed to change (they move along the line).

2. 其他因素不變 (Ceteris Paribus)

任何影響供應但不在坐標軸上的因素，在繪畫這條線時都必須保持不變。如果這些「背景因素」改變，整條線就會移動。

Any factor that affects supply but is NOT on the axes must be held constant to draw the line. If these "background factors" change, the line itself will shift.

常見的「背景因素」（必須不變）：

Common "Background Factors" (Must be constant):
  • 生產成本（工資、原材料） Cost of production (wages, raw materials)
  • 技術水平 Technology
  • 賣家數量 Number of sellers
  • 預期（未來價格） Expectations (future price)
  • 天氣 / 政府政策 Weather / Government policies

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: D

  • (1) 該物品的市場價格 — 錯誤 (False)

    價格位於 Y 軸。供應曲線的目的就是觀察價格變動時供應量如何反應。因此，價格是可變的，並非固定。

    Price is on the Y-axis. The whole point of a supply curve is to see how quantity responds when price changes. Therefore, price is variable (not constant).
  • (2) 該物品的生產要素價格 — 正確 (True)

    這指生產成本（如工資、租金）。這不在坐標軸上。若成本改變，生產者的計劃會完全改變（曲線移動）。為了畫出一條特定的曲線，我們必須假設成本固定。

    This refers to costs (e.g., wages, rent). This is not on the axis. If costs change, producers change their plans completely (the curve shifts). To draw one specific curve, we must assume costs are fixed.
  • (3) 預期該物品在將來的價格 — 正確 (True)

    這是關於未來的心理因素。它不在坐標軸上（坐標軸顯示的是「現時」價格）。若賣家預期下週價格上升，他們今天可能會減少出售。分析今天的供應時，此因素必須假設不變。

    This is a psychological factor regarding the future. It is not on the axis (the axis shows the current price). If sellers expect prices to rise next week, they might sell less today. This factor must be held constant to analyze today's supply.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

「軸上」測試法 (The "On the Axis" Test)

對每一個選項使用這個簡單的邏輯檢查：

Use this simple logic check for every option:

該因素是否在 X 軸或 Y 軸上？

Is the factor on the X or Y axis?
  • 是 (價格、數量)： 它會改變。它不會保持不變。
    YES (Price, Quantity): It changes. It is NOT held constant.
  • 否 (成本、未來、天氣)： 它是背景因素。它必須保持不變。
    NO (Costs, Future, Weather): It is a background factor. It MUST be held constant.

Common Mistakes (常犯錯誤)

混淆「現時價格」與「預期價格」

Confusing "Current Price" with "Expected Price"

學生常以為凡是「價格」都不變，或者凡是「價格」都可變。

Students often get confused by the word "price", thinking either all prices are constant or all are variable.
  • 現時價格 (Current Price, \(P\))： 在軸上 → 可變 (Variable)。
    On the axis → Variable.
  • 預期未來價格 (Expected Future Price, \(P^e\))： 不在軸上 → 不變 (Constant)。
    Not on the axis → Constant.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼選項中沒有提到「供應量」(Quantity Supplied)？如果有的話，它應該保持不變嗎？
Why isn't "Quantity Supplied" mentioned in the options? If it were, should it be held constant?

A: 這是個很好的問題！供應量位於 X 軸 (\(Q_s\))。根據「軸上測試法」，既然它在軸上，它就是可變的。當 Y 軸的價格改變時，X 軸的供應量會隨之改變（這叫做沿著曲線移動）。因此，供應量不假設為不變。

That's a great question! Quantity Supplied is on the X-axis (\(Q_s\)). According to the "On the Axis Test", since it is on the axis, it is variable. When Price on the Y-axis changes, Quantity on the X-axis changes along with it (this is called a movement along the curve). Therefore, Quantity Supplied is NOT assumed to be constant.

Q: 如果題目問的是「導致供應曲線移動」(Shift in supply curve)，答案會一樣嗎？
Q: If the question asked about what causes a "Shift in supply curve", would the answer be the same?

A: 剛好相反！如果要讓曲線移動 (Shift)，我們需要改變那些原本被假設為不變的「背景因素」。所以，如果題目問什麼會導致移動，答案會是 (2) 和 (3) 的改變，而 (1) 價格的改變只會導致沿線移動 (Movement along)，不會導致整條線移動。

Exactly the opposite! To make the curve shift, we need to change those "background factors" that were originally assumed to be constant. So, if the question asked what causes a shift, the answer would be a change in (2) and (3). A change in (1) Price would only cause a movement along the curve, not a shift of the curve itself.
