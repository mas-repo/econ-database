If the government provides a $6 per-unit subsidy for the producers of the good, the total subsidy provided by the government will be _________ and the ratio of consumers' share to producers' share of the subsidy will be ________.

A. $300 …… 1:1
B. $300 …… 1:2

概念重溫 / Concept Review

1. 尋找補貼後的新均衡 (Finding the New Equilibrium after Subsidy):
當政府提供從量補貼時，生產者實收的價格 \( P_p \) 會高於消費者支付的市場價格 \( P_c \)，兩者的差距正是每單位補貼額 (Unit Subsidy)。
When a per-unit subsidy is provided, the price received by producers \( P_p \) is higher than the market price paid by consumers \( P_c \). The difference is exactly the unit subsidy.

$$ P_p - P_c = \text{Unit Subsidy} $$

2. 總補貼 (Total Subsidy):
總補貼等於每單位補貼乘以新的交易量 \( Q_1 \)。
Total subsidy equals the per-unit subsidy multiplied by the new transacted quantity \( Q_1 \).

$$ \text{Total Subsidy} = \text{Unit Subsidy} \times Q_1 $$

3. 補貼份額 (Subsidy Shares):
消費者享受的補貼是原有價格 \( P_0 \) 減去新價格 \( P_c \)。生產者享受的補貼是新實收價格 \( P_p \) 減去原有價格 \( P_0 \)。
The consumer's share is the original price \( P_0 \) minus the new price \( P_c \). The producer's share is the newly received price \( P_p \) minus the original price \( P_0 \).

$$ \text{Consumer's Unit Benefit} = P_0 - P_c $$ $$ \text{Producer's Unit Benefit} = P_p - P_0 $$

答案與詳細分析 / Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Option: D

第一步：找出原有均衡 (Step 1: Find Initial Equilibrium)
在政府干預前，當價格為 $24 時，需求量 (50) 等於供應量 (50)。因此，原均衡價格 \( P_0 = \$24 \)，原均衡數量 \( Q_0 = 50 \)。
Before intervention, at $24, Quantity Demanded (50) equals Quantity Supplied (50). Thus, initial equilibrium price \( P_0 = \$24 \), and quantity \( Q_0 = 50 \).

第二步：找出新均衡 (Step 2: Find New Equilibrium)
政府提供 $6 補貼。我們需要從表中找到一個交易量，使得生產者要求的價格 (供應表) 與消費者願意支付的價格 (需求表) 相差恰好 $6。
觀察圖表：當交易量為 60 時，消費者願意支付的價格為 $22 (\( P_c \))，而生產者願意供應的價格為 $28 (\( P_p \))。差距為 \( \$28 - \$22 = \$6 \)，完全吻合補貼額！因此新交易量 \( Q_1 = 60 \)。
With a $6 subsidy, we need to find a quantity where the supply price and demand price differ by exactly $6. Looking at the table: at quantity 60, consumers are willing to pay $22 (\( P_c \)), and producers require $28 to supply (\( P_p \)). The difference is \( \$28 - \$22 = \$6 \). This matches the unit subsidy! Thus, new quantity \( Q_1 = 60 \).

第三步：計算總補貼與份額 (Step 3: Calculate Total Subsidy and Shares)
總補貼 = 每單位補貼 × 新數量 = \( \$6 \times 60 = \$360 \)。
消費者份額 = 原價 - 新市價 = \( \$24 - \$22 = \$2 \)。
生產者份額 = 實收新價 - 原價 = \( \$28 - \$24 = \$4 \)。
比率 (消費者 : 生產者) = 2 : 4 = 1 : 2。
Total Subsidy = Unit Subsidy × New Quantity = \( \$6 \times 60 = \$360 \).
Consumer's share = Old Price - New Market Price = \( \$24 - \$22 = \$2 \).
Producer's share = New Received Price - Old Price = \( \$28 - \$24 = \$4 \).
Ratio (Consumer : Producer) = 2 : 4 = 1 : 2.

選項分析 / Option Breakdown:

A 錯誤 (Incorrect): 錯誤地使用了原有數量 (\( Q_0 = 50 \)) 來計算總補貼 (\( 50 \times \$6 = \$300 \))，並錯誤假設雙方平均分享補貼 (1:1)。
Incorrectly uses the old quantity (\( Q_0 = 50 \)) to calculate total subsidy and falsely assumes equal sharing of the subsidy (1:1).

B 錯誤 (Incorrect): 份額比率正確 (1:2)，但同樣錯誤地使用了原有數量來計算總補貼 ($300)。
The share ratio is correct (1:2), but it incorrectly uses the old quantity to calculate the total subsidy ($300).

C 錯誤 (Incorrect): 總補貼計算正確 ($360)，但錯誤計算了補貼份額比率 (1:1)。
Correct total subsidy ($360), but incorrectly calculates the subsidy share ratio (1:1).

D 正確 (Correct): 總補貼 ($360) 與份額比率 (1:2) 均計算無誤。
Both the total subsidy ($360) and the share ratio (1:2) are correct.

題目拆解 / Problem Decomposition

解答這類題目的「關鍵支點 (Pivot Point)」是明白補貼會令生產價格與消費價格分離。不要盲目把表格裡的所有價格加減 $6。你應該尋找一個特定的數量 (\( Q \))，在這個數量下：
\( \text{供應表上的價格 } (P_s) - \text{需求表上的價格 } (P_d) = \text{補貼額} \)
找對了這個新的 \( Q \)，所有問題都會迎刃而解。

The "Pivot Point" for this type of question is understanding that a subsidy separates the producer price from the consumer price. Do not just blindly add/subtract $6 from the table prices. You must look for a specific quantity (\( Q \)) where:
\( \text{Price on Supply Schedule } (P_s) - \text{Price on Demand Schedule } (P_d) = \text{Subsidy Amount} \)
Once you find this new \( Q \), the rest is simple arithmetic.

常犯錯誤與陷阱 / Common Mistakes & Traps

陷阱 1：使用舊均衡量計算總額 (Using Old Q for Total Amount)
很多同學會直接把 $6 乘以原來的交易量 50 (得出 $300)。請緊記：補貼會使供應增加，均衡交易量必然會上升。計算總額必須使用新的交易量。
Many students multiply $6 by the old quantity 50 (getting $300). Remember: a subsidy increases supply, so the equilibrium quantity must rise. Always use the new quantity to calculate total amounts.

陷阱 2：搞混稅收與補貼的公式 (Confusing Tax and Subsidy Conditions)
對於稅收 (Tax)：\( P_d - P_s = \text{Tax} \) (消費者付更多)
對於補貼 (Subsidy)：\( P_s - P_d = \text{Subsidy} \) (生產者收更多)
如果弄反了，你可能會跑到 \( Q = 40 \) 去尋找答案！
For a Tax: \( P_d - P_s = \text{Tax} \) (Consumers pay more)
For a Subsidy: \( P_s - P_d = \text{Subsidy} \) (Producers receive more)
If you mix them up, you might look at \( Q = 40 \) by mistake!

學生常見疑問 / Student Q&A

Q: 題目說補貼是「給生產商的」，這代表生產者會得到全部好處嗎？ / The question says the subsidy is for "producers". Does this mean producers get all the benefits?

A: 絕對不是 (Absolutely not)。 在經濟學中，法定歸宿 (Statutory Incidence，即政府把錢交給誰) 並不影響經濟歸宿 (Economic Incidence，即最終誰受益)。最終雙方如何瓜分這 $6 的好處，完全取決於供需的相對彈性 (Elasticity)。因為供應曲線比需求曲線更缺乏彈性 (Inelastic)，所以生產者獲得了較大份額 (佔 $4)。就算政府把 $6 補貼直接發給消費者，最終市價也會變成 $28，生產者依然多賺 $4，消費者依然省下 $2！

A: Absolutely not. In economics, statutory incidence (who receives the cheque) does not affect economic incidence (who actually benefits). How the $6 benefit is shared depends entirely on the relative elasticity of demand and supply. Because supply is more inelastic than demand here, producers get a larger share ($4). Even if the government gave the $6 directly to consumers, the market price would rise to $28, meaning producers still gain $4 and consumers still save $2!

考試技巧與速解 / Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

秒殺法 (The 5-Second Kill Method):

在 MC 卷中，你可以不用計算就刪去一半選項！

1. 觀察彈性 (Observe Elasticity): 從表中可見，價格每變動 $2，需求量只改變 10 單位，但供應量改變了 5 單位。這意味著供應 (Supply) 對價格的反應較小（較缺乏彈性 Inelastic）。
2. 彈性與負擔法則 (Rule of Incidence): 較缺乏彈性的一方，會獲得較大比例的補貼！因此，生產者享受的補貼必定大於消費者 (Producer Share > Consumer Share)。比率絕不可能是 1:1，立刻刪除 A 和 C！
3. 交易量方向 (Quantity Direction): 補貼必令交易量增加 (\( Q > 50 \))。總補貼 = \( Q_{new} \times \$6 \)。既然 \( Q_{new} > 50 \)，總補貼必定大於 \( 50 \times 6 = 300 \)。刪除 B。
答案只剩下 D！完全不用硬算！

The 5-Second Kill Method:
In MCQs, you can eliminate options without full calculation!
1. Observe Elasticity: For every $2 change in price, Qd changes by 10, but Qs changes by only 5. This means Supply is more inelastic (steeper).
2. Rule of Incidence: The more inelastic side gets the larger share of the subsidy! Thus, Producer Share > Consumer Share. The ratio cannot be 1:1. Eliminate A and C instantly!
3. Quantity Direction: Subsidy increases quantity (\( Q > 50 \)). Total Subsidy = \( Q_{new} \times \$6 \). Since \( Q_{new} > 50 \), the total subsidy MUST be greater than \( 50 \times 6 = 300 \). Eliminate B.
Only D remains! No complex math required!
