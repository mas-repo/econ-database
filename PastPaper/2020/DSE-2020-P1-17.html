HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: 價格彈性與市場干預 (Price Elasticity & Market Intervention) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts: 需求價格彈性 (Price Elasticity of Demand), 總開支 (Total Expenditure), 負界外效應 (Negative Externality), 累退稅 (Regressive Tax)

題目 (The Question)

某非牟利組織支持增加香煙稅，該組織透露，香煙價格每增加10%，香煙總成交量則下降4%。下列哪項關於香煙稅的陳述是不正確的？

A. 上述個案說明了需求定律。
B. 在增加香煙稅後，對香煙的總支出會增加。
C. 增加香煙稅可能導致收入不均加劇。
D. 香煙稅有助於減輕香煙生產不足的問題。

A non-profit organisation supports the increase in cigarette tax. The organisation reveals that every 10% increase in cigarette prices reduces the total cigarette quantity transacted by 4%. Which of the following statements about cigarette tax is INCORRECT?

A. Law of demand is illustrated in the above case.
B. The total expenditure on cigarette will increase after the increase of the cigarette tax.
C. The increase in cigarette tax may lead to a greater income inequality.
D. The cigarette tax could help reduce the problem of under-production of cigarette.

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

1. 需求價格彈性 (Price Elasticity of Demand, \(E_d\)):
量度需求量對價格變動的反應程度。當價格上升的百分比大於需求量下降的百分比時，需求屬低彈性 (Inelastic)。
Measures the responsiveness of quantity demanded to a change in price. When the percentage increase in price is greater than the percentage decrease in quantity demanded, demand is inelastic.
$$ E_d = \frac{\%\Delta Q_d}{\%\Delta P} $$
2. 總開支 (Total Expenditure, TE):
總開支等於價格乘以數量 \( TE = P \times Q \)。在低彈性需求下，價格上升會導致總開支增加。
Total expenditure equals price times quantity \( TE = P \times Q \). Under inelastic demand, an increase in price leads to an increase in total expenditure.

3. 負界外效應與過度生產 (Negative Externality & Over-production):
香煙消費會產生二手煙等負界外效應，導致社會邊際成本大於私人邊際成本。在自由市場下，這會引致過度生產 (Over-production)，而非生產不足。
Cigarette consumption generates negative externalities (e.g., second-hand smoke), making marginal social cost greater than marginal private cost. In a free market, this leads to over-production, not under-production.

答案與詳細分析 (Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): D (因為題目要求選出不正確的選項 / Because the question asks for the INCORRECT statement)

選項 A (正確陳述): 價格上升10%，數量下降4%。這顯示了價格與需求量之間的逆向關係，完全符合需求定律。
Option A (True Statement): Price increases by 10%, quantity decreases by 4%. This shows an inverse relationship between price and quantity demanded, which perfectly illustrates the law of demand.
選項 B (正確陳述): 根據題目，\( E_d = \frac{4\%}{10\%} = 0.4 < 1 \)，需求屬低彈性。由於價格上升的幅度 (10%) 大於數量下跌的幅度 (4%)，總開支 (\( P \times Q \)) 必然會增加。
Option B (True Statement): According to the question, \( E_d = \frac{4\%}{10\%} = 0.4 < 1 \), so demand is inelastic. Since the percentage increase in price (10%) outweighs the percentage decrease in quantity (4%), total expenditure (\( P \times Q \)) must increase.
選項 C (正確陳述): 香煙稅是一種間接稅 (Indirect tax)。間接稅通常具有累退 (Regressive) 的性質，即低收入人士需將其收入的較大比例用於繳稅，因此會加劇收入不均。
Option C (True Statement): Cigarette tax is an indirect tax. Indirect taxes are generally regressive in nature, meaning lower-income individuals spend a larger proportion of their income on the tax, thereby worsening income inequality.
選項 D (不正確陳述 - 本題答案): 香煙會產生負界外效應（如二手煙影響他人健康）。自由市場會導致香煙過度生產 (over-production)。徵收香煙稅是為了減少過度生產的問題，而不是減輕「生產不足」。
Option D (Incorrect Statement - The Answer): Cigarettes generate negative externalities (e.g., second-hand smoke affecting others). The free market leads to an over-production of cigarettes. Imposing a tax helps reduce the problem of over-production, not "under-production".

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point): 本題的關鍵在於看清題目要求選出「不正確」的選項，並將微觀經濟學中的三個不同課題（彈性、稅收公平性、界外效應）結合起來分析。

干擾資訊 (Distractors): 題目提供的數字（10% 和 4%）是用來驗證選項 B 的。如果你只專注於計算，可能會忽略了選項 D 中明顯的概念錯誤（香煙是過度生產而非生產不足）。

Pivot Point: The key is to notice the word "INCORRECT" and to synthesize three different microeconomic topics (Elasticity, Tax Equity, Externalities).

Distractors: The numbers provided (10% and 4%) are used to verify Option B. If you focus too much on calculation, you might miss the glaring conceptual error in Option D (cigarettes are over-produced, not under-produced).

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • 忽略「不正確」字眼： 很多學生看到 A 或 B 是對的，就直接選擇了，忘記了題目要求選錯的陳述。
  • 混淆界外效應的結果： 記住：負界外效應 (Negative Externality) = 社會成本 > 私人成本 = 過度生產 (Over-production)。正界外效應 (Positive Externality) 才會導致生產不足。
  • Ignoring the word "INCORRECT": Many students see that A or B is true and immediately select it, forgetting the question asks for the false statement.
  • Confusing Externality Outcomes: Remember: Negative Externality = Social Cost > Private Cost = Over-production. Positive externalities lead to under-production.

學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼間接稅（如香煙稅）會導致收入不均加劇？
A: 假設香煙稅是每包 $50。對於月入 $10,000 的窮人和月入 $100,000 的富人來說，如果他們都抽同樣數量的煙，窮人交的稅佔其總收入的比例遠高於富人。這被稱為累退稅效應 (Regressive effect)，會擴大貧富差距。
Q: Why do indirect taxes (like cigarette tax) worsen income inequality?
A: Suppose the cigarette tax is $50 per pack. For a poor person earning $10,000 a month and a rich person earning $100,000, if they smoke the same amount, the tax takes up a much larger percentage of the poor person's income. This is known as a regressive effect, which widens the wealth gap.

考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

秒殺技巧： 在處理這類「下列何者不正確」的長題目時，建議先快速掃視所有選項。當你看到選項 D 提到香煙「生產不足」時，你的經濟學直覺應該立刻響起警號——香煙是教科書中「負界外效應」和「過度生產」的經典例子！發現這個明顯的錯誤後，你甚至不需要花時間去計算選項 B 的彈性。

Shortcut: For "Which of the following is INCORRECT" questions, scan all options first. When you see Option D mentioning the "under-production" of cigarettes, your economics intuition should immediately ring alarm bells—cigarettes are the textbook example of "negative externalities" and "over-production"! Spotting this glaring error saves you from even needing to calculate the elasticity for Option B.
