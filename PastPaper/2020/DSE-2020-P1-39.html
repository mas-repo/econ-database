HKDSE Economics 經濟學詳解

Topic (課題): Public Finance - Principles of Taxation (公共財政 - 課稅原則)

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐ (2/5)

Key Concepts (核心概念): Adam Smith (亞當史密夫), Canons of Taxation (課稅原則), Economy (經濟)

The Question (題目)

以下為稅務局使命的撮要。
我們的使命
我們致力
- 以有效率及有成本效益的方法收取稅款
- 協助員工具備應有的知識、技巧和態度，從而竭盡所能，實踐我們的抱負

上述使命符合亞當史密夫所提出的徵稅 _______ 原則。

A. 公平
B. 確定
C. 方便
D. 經濟

The following is an extract of the mission of the Inland Revenue Department.
Our Mission
We are committed to
- collecting revenue efficiently and cost-effectively
- enabling staff to acquire the necessary knowledge, skills and attitude so that they can contribute their best to the achievement of our vision

The above mission is in line with the _______ principle of taxation put forward by Adam Smith.

A. equality
B. certainty
C. convenience
D. economy

Concept Review (概念重溫)

亞當史密夫（Adam Smith）提出了四大課稅原則（Canons of Taxation），用以評估一個稅制是否優良：

Adam Smith proposed four Canons of Taxation to evaluate whether a tax system is good:
  • 公平 (Equality): 稅款應按納稅人的負擔能力來徵收。
    Taxes should be levied according to the taxpayer's ability to pay.
  • 確定 (Certainty): 繳稅的時間、方式和金額必須清晰明確，不可隨意更改。
    The time, manner, and amount of payment must be clear and plain to the contributor, not arbitrary.
  • 方便 (Convenience): 繳稅的時間和方式應盡量方便納稅人。
    Every tax ought to be levied at the time, or in the manner, in which it is most likely to be convenient for the contributor to pay it.
  • 經濟 (Economy): 徵稅的行政成本應盡量減低，以確保政府獲得最大淨收益。
    The administrative cost of tax collection should be kept to a minimum to ensure maximum net revenue for the government.
$$ \text{Net Tax Revenue} = \text{Total Tax Collected} - \text{Administrative Costs} $$

根據「經濟」原則，行政成本（Administrative Costs）佔總稅收的比例應盡可能低，即 \( \frac{\text{Administrative Costs}}{\text{Total Tax Collected}} \) 的數值越小越好。

According to the "Economy" principle, the proportion of administrative costs to total tax revenue should be as low as possible, meaning the value of \( \frac{\text{Administrative Costs}}{\text{Total Tax Collected}} \) should be minimized.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 Correct Option: D. 經濟 (Economy)

Step-by-Step Analysis (逐項分析):

Option A (公平 / Equality): 錯誤 (Incorrect)
題目內容沒有提及納稅人的收入水平或負擔能力（例如累進稅率等）。
The extract does not mention taxpayers' income levels or their ability to pay (e.g., progressive tax rates).

Option B (確定 / Certainty): 錯誤 (Incorrect)
題目沒有提及稅例是否清晰、有沒有隱藏收費或隨意更改稅款。
The extract does not mention whether tax rules are clear, or if there are hidden charges or arbitrary changes to tax amounts.

Option C (方便 / Convenience): 錯誤 (Incorrect)
題目沒有提及為市民提供便利的繳費方式（如網上繳費、分期付款等）。
The extract does not mention providing convenient payment methods for citizens (such as online payment, installments, etc.).

Option D (經濟 / Economy): 正確 (Correct)
題目明確指出「以有效率及有成本效益的方法收取稅款」（collecting revenue efficiently and cost-effectively）。這正正對應了「經濟」原則中，盡量降低徵稅行政成本的核心概念。
The extract explicitly states "collecting revenue efficiently and cost-effectively". This perfectly corresponds to the core concept of the "Economy" principle, which is to minimize the administrative costs of tax collection.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (破題關鍵):
關鍵字眼是「成本效益 (cost-effectively)」。在經濟學的課稅原則中，只要見到與「政府行政成本」、「徵稅開支」或「效率」相關的字眼，就必定是指「經濟 (Economy)」原則。

Pivot Point: The key phrase is "cost-effectively". In the economic principles of taxation, whenever you see terms related to "government administrative costs", "collection expenses", or "efficiency", it always refers to the "Economy" principle.

Distractors (干擾資訊):
第二點「協助員工具備應有的知識...」是一個輕微的干擾項。但仔細思考，培訓員工的目的同樣是為了提升工作效率，從而降低長遠的行政成本，這依然歸結於「經濟」原則。

Distractors: The second point about "enabling staff to acquire necessary knowledge..." is a minor distractor. However, thinking deeply, the purpose of training staff is also to improve work efficiency, thereby reducing long-term administrative costs, which still boils down to the "Economy" principle.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

混淆「方便」與「經濟」 (Confusing Convenience and Economy)
學生常誤以為「有效率」是指對市民方便。記住：
- 方便 (Convenience) 是從納稅人 (Taxpayers) 的角度出發（例如：可以用支付寶交稅）。
- 經濟 (Economy) 是從政府 (Government) 的角度出發（例如：稅務局全面電腦化以節省人手）。

Students often mistakenly think "efficient" means convenient for citizens. Remember:
- Convenience is from the perspective of the taxpayers (e.g., being able to pay taxes via Alipay).
- Economy is from the perspective of the government (e.g., the IRD becoming fully computerized to save manpower).

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼「經濟 (Economy)」這個詞在這裡不是指宏觀經濟學的「經濟體系」？
Why doesn't the word "Economy" here refer to the macroeconomic "economic system"?

A: 在亞當史密夫的四大原則中，"Economy" 取其「節儉、節約、符合成本效益」的字義，而不是指「經濟體系 (Economic system)」。這是一個專有名詞的特定用法，必須死記。
In Adam Smith's four canons, "Economy" takes its literal meaning of "thrift, frugality, or cost-effectiveness", rather than referring to an "economic system". This is a specific usage of terminology that must be memorized.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

Keyword Matching (關鍵字秒殺法): 處理這類題目，只需認準關鍵字：

  • 見到「能力 (Ability) / 收入比例 (Income proportion)」 ➔ 公平 (Equality)
  • 見到「清晰 (Clear) / 不隨意 (Not arbitrary) / 逃稅漏洞 (Loopholes)」 ➔ 確定 (Certainty)
  • 見到「時間地點 (Time & Place) / 繳費方式 (Payment methods)」 ➔ 方便 (Convenience)
  • 見到「成本 (Cost) / 效率 (Efficiency) / 行政費 (Administrative fee)」 ➔ 經濟 (Economy)
Keyword Matching: For these types of questions, just spot the keywords:
  • "Ability / Income proportion" ➔ Equality
  • "Clear / Not arbitrary / Loopholes" ➔ Certainty
  • "Time & Place / Payment methods" ➔ Convenience
  • "Cost / Efficiency / Administrative fee" ➔ Economy
