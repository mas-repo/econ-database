📝 DSE Economics Tutor Guide

Topic (課題): National Income Accounting 國民收入會計

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)

Key Concepts (核心概念): Nominal GDP (名義 GDP), Real GDP (實質 GDP), GDP Deflator (本地生產總值平減物價指數), Price Level (物價水平)

The Question (題目)

下圖顯示甲國的本地生產總值(GDP)。
下列哪項有關甲國的陳述是正確的？

A. 2016至2018年間，價格水平上升。
B. 2017年之前出現通貨收縮。
C. 2017年實際通脹率相等於預期通脹率。
D. 自2016年起，名義GDP一直上升。

The following diagram shows the gross domestic product (GDP) of Country A.
Which of the following statements about Country A is correct?

A. The price level increased between 2016 and 2018.
B. Deflation occurred before 2017.
C. The actual inflation rate was equal to the expected inflation rate in 2017.
D. The nominal GDP has been increasing since 2016.

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這道題目，我們必須理解名義 GDP、實質 GDP與物價水平 (Price Level)之間的關係。這三者透過「本地生產總值平減物價指數 (GDP Deflator)」連結起來。

To solve this question, we must understand the relationship between Nominal GDP, Real GDP, and the Price Level. These three are connected through the "GDP Deflator".

$$ \text{GDP Deflator} = \frac{\text{Nominal GDP}}{\text{Real GDP}} \times 100 $$
  • 物價水平上升 (Inflation 通脹): 當名義 GDP 的增長速度快於實質 GDP 時，兩者的比率會上升，代表物價水平正在上升。在圖表中，這表現為名義 GDP 曲線相對於實質 GDP 曲線向上攀升（差距收窄或擴大）。
  • 基年 (Base Year): 當名義 GDP = 實質 GDP 時（圖中兩線相交的 2017 年），GDP 平減物價指數為 100，這通常是基年。
  • Rising Price Level (Inflation): When Nominal GDP grows faster than Real GDP, their ratio increases, indicating a rising price level. On a graph, this is shown by the Nominal GDP curve rising relative to the Real GDP curve.
  • Base Year: When Nominal GDP = Real GDP (the intersection in 2017 on the graph), the GDP Deflator is 100. This is typically the base year.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option A

Option A (Correct 正確)

分析： 從圖表可見，在 2016 至 2018 年間，名義 GDP（虛線）相對於實質 GDP（實線）的比例不斷增加。在 2017 年之前，虛線從下方靠近實線；2017 年之後，虛線在實線上方並持續拉開距離。這意味著 \(\frac{\text{Nominal GDP}}{\text{Real GDP}}\) 的數值一直在上升，因此價格水平（物價水平）在這段期間是上升的（即存在通脹）。

Analysis: From the graph, between 2016 and 2018, the ratio of Nominal GDP (dashed line) to Real GDP (solid line) is constantly increasing. Before 2017, the dashed line approaches the solid line from below; after 2017, it goes above and widens the gap. This means the value of \(\frac{\text{Nominal GDP}}{\text{Real GDP}}\) is continuously rising. Therefore, the price level increased during this period (i.e., there was inflation).

Option B (Incorrect 錯誤)

分析： 2017 年之前，名義 GDP 低於實質 GDP，這只代表當時的物價水平低於基年（2017年）的物價水平（即 GDP Deflator < 100）。然而，由於名義 GDP 追近實質 GDP 的速度很快，兩者的比率實際上是在上升的。物價水平上升代表通脹（Inflation），而非通貨收縮（Deflation）。

Analysis: Before 2017, Nominal GDP was lower than Real GDP. This only means the price level was lower than that of the base year 2017 (i.e., GDP Deflator < 100). However, because Nominal GDP was catching up to Real GDP, their ratio was actually increasing. A rising price level indicates inflation, not deflation.

Option C (Incorrect 錯誤)

分析： 圖表只提供了名義和實質 GDP 的數據，我們無法從中得知任何關於「預期通脹率 (Expected inflation rate)」的資訊。這是一個完全無關的干擾項。

Analysis: The graph only provides data on nominal and real GDP. We cannot deduce any information about the "expected inflation rate" from it. This is an irrelevant distractor.

Option D (Incorrect 錯誤)

分析： 觀察名義 GDP（虛線）的走勢，它在 2019 年左右達到頂峰後開始向下彎曲（下跌）。因此，「一直上升 (has been increasing)」的說法是錯誤的。

Analysis: Observing the trend of Nominal GDP (dashed line), it peaks around 2019 and then starts to slope downward (decrease). Therefore, the statement that it "has been increasing" continuously is false.

Problem Decomposition & Exam Tricks (題目拆解與考試技巧)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point): 認清圖表斜率與差距的意義。不要把「物價水平低於 100」和「物價水平正在下跌 (通縮)」混淆。

速解技巧 (Shortcut): 只要看到名義 GDP 曲線的增長斜率（上升幅度）比實質 GDP 曲線更陡峭，就代表物價水平在上升（通脹）。在圖中，虛線明顯比實線升得快，直接秒殺選 A。

Pivot Point: Recognize the meaning of the slopes and gaps in the graph. Do not confuse "price level below 100" with "price level is falling (deflation)".

Shortcut: Whenever you see the Nominal GDP curve growing at a steeper slope than the Real GDP curve, it means the price level is rising (inflation). In the graph, the dashed line is clearly rising faster than the solid line, making A the obvious choice instantly.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼 2017 年之前不是通縮 (Deflation)？名義 GDP 明明比較低啊！
Why wasn't there deflation before 2017? Nominal GDP is clearly lower!

A: 這是一個非常經典的陷阱！「名義 GDP < 實質 GDP」只代表那一年的物價比「基年 (Base year)」平。通縮的定義是物價水平持續下跌。在 2015 到 2017 年間，雖然物價比 2017 年低，但它是一直在上升向 2017 年的水平邁進的。既然物價在上升，那就是通脹 (Inflation)。
This is a classic trap! "Nominal GDP < Real GDP" only means the prices in that year were cheaper than the "Base year". Deflation is defined as a continuous fall in the price level. Between 2015 and 2017, although prices were lower than in 2017, they were constantly rising towards the 2017 level. Since prices were rising, it was inflation.
