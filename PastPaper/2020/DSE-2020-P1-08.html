Concept Review / 概念重溫

1. 邊際回報遞減定律 (Law of Diminishing Marginal Returns):
在短期內，當可變要素（如勞力）不斷增加到固定要素（如機器）時，邊際產量 (MP) 最終會下降。 1. Law of Diminishing Marginal Returns:
In the short run, as variable factors (e.g., labour) are continuously added to fixed factors (e.g., machinery), the Marginal Product (MP) will eventually decrease.
2. 重要公式 (Key Formulas): To solve this, we must convert Average Product (AP) to Total Product (TP), and then to Marginal Product (MP).
$$ \text{Total Product (TP)} = \text{AP} \times \text{Labour (L)} $$ $$ \text{Marginal Product (MP)}_n = \text{TP}_n - \text{TP}_{n-1} $$

Answer & Detailed Analysis / 答案與詳細分析

Correct Answer: Option B

Step 1: Calculate Total Product (TP) and Marginal Product (MP) / 計算總產量與邊際產量

我們不能只看平均產量 (AP)，必須計算出邊際產量 (MP) 才能應用定律。 We cannot rely solely on Average Product (AP); we must calculate Marginal Product (MP) to apply the law.

L AP (Given) TP (\(AP \times L\)) MP (\(TP_n - TP_{n-1}\))
1 20 \(20 \times 1 = 20\) 20
2 21 \(21 \times 2 = 42\) \(42 - 20 = 22\)
3 22 \(22 \times 3 = 66\) \(66 - 42 = \mathbf{24}\)
4 X \(X \times 4 = 4X\) \(4X - 66\)

Step 2: Apply the Law / 應用定律

題目指出資料「說明」(illustrate) 了邊際回報遞減定律。觀察上表，MP 從 22 上升到 24。為了在第 4 單位「說明」此定律，MP 必須開始下降。 The question states the data "illustrate" the law. Observing the table, MP rises from 22 to 24. To "illustrate" the law at the 4th unit, MP must start to fall.
$$ MP_4 < MP_3 $$ $$ 4X - 66 < 24 $$ $$ 4X < 90 $$ $$ X < 22.5 $$
結論： 只要 \(X < 22.5\)，邊際回報遞減定律就成立。 Conclusion: As long as \(X < 22.5\), the Law of Diminishing Marginal Returns holds.

Step 3: Evaluate Options / 評估選項

A. Can be 23 / 可以是 23
錯誤。23 大於 22.5。如果 X=23，MP 會變成 \(4(23)-66 = 26\)，MP 仍在上升 (24 -> 26)。 Incorrect. 23 is greater than 22.5. If X=23, MP becomes \(4(23)-66 = 26\), meaning MP is still rising (24 -> 26).
B. Can be smaller than 22 / 可以小於 22
正確。因為 \(X\) 必須小於 22.5，所以它「可以」是 21、20 等數值（這些都小於 22）。這是一個可能的陳述。 Correct. Since \(X\) must be less than 22.5, it "can" be values like 21 or 20 (which are smaller than 22). This is a valid possibility.
C. Must be larger than 22 / 必然大於 22
錯誤。X 可以是 21（滿足 \(X < 22.5\)），所以不必大於 22。 Incorrect. X could be 21 (which satisfies \(X < 22.5\)), so it doesn't have to be larger than 22.
D. Must be smaller than 22 / 必然小於 22
錯誤。X 可以是 22.4（滿足 \(X < 22.5\)），但 22.4 並不小於 22。 Incorrect. X could be 22.4 (which satisfies \(X < 22.5\)), but 22.4 is not smaller than 22.

Problem Decomposition / 題目拆解

關鍵轉折點 (The Pivot Point): 學生必須意識到題目給的是 AP，但定律關於 MP。 The Pivot Point: Students must realize the data provides AP, but the law is about MP.
  • 陷阱數據: 看到 AP 上升 (20, 21, 22)，直覺可能認為 X 也要上升或下降，但這與 MP 的轉折點不同步。 Distractor Data: Seeing AP rise (20, 21, 22) might lead to intuition about X rising or falling, but AP trends do not perfectly sync with MP turning points.
  • 邏輯檢查: 題目問的是「X 的值」，但其實是在問「MP 下跌的條件」。 Logic Check: The question asks for "the value of X", but it is mathematically asking for "the condition where MP falls".

Student Q&A / 學生常見疑問

Q: Why isn't the answer "Must be smaller than 22" (Option D)? / 為什麼答案不是「必然小於 22」(選項 D)？

A: 這是數學邏輯問題。我們計算出的條件是 \(X < 22.5\)。這意味著 X 可以是 22.1, 22.2, 22.3 等等。這些數字都大於 22。因此，我們不能說 X 必然(Must) 小於 22。 A: This is a logic issue. Our calculated condition is \(X < 22.5\). This means X could be 22.1, 22.2, 22.3, etc. These numbers are larger than 22. Therefore, we cannot say X must be smaller than 22.

Q: Does "illustrate the law" mean MP must fall immediately? / 「說明定律」是指 MP 必須立刻下降嗎？

A: 是的。在這個表格的語境下，如果 MP 繼續上升（例如 MP=26），那麼這組數據就顯示了「邊際回報遞增」，而不是遞減。要讓這組數據符合定律，MP 必須在最後一個數據點顯示下降。 A: Yes. In the context of this table, if MP continued to rise (e.g., MP=26), the data would illustrate "increasing marginal returns". For the data to fit the law, MP must show a decrease at the final data point provided.

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts / 考試技巧與速解

AP 與 MP 的關係速記： AP vs MP Relationship Shortcut:
  • 當 AP 上升時，MP 必定大於 AP (MP > AP)。 When AP is rising, MP must be greater than AP (MP > AP).
  • 在 L=3 時，AP=22 且正在上升，所以 MP (24) > AP (22)。這是合理的。 At L=3, AP=22 and rising, so MP (24) > AP (22). This checks out.
  • 速解思路： 如果 X=22 (AP持平)，MP 會等於 AP (即 MP=22)。從 24 跌到 22，符合定律！所以 X=22 是可能的。既然 X=22 可能，選項 D (必然小於22) 和 C (必然大於22) 立刻刪除。剩下 A 和 B，稍微計算一下即可。 Quick Logic: If X=22 (AP is constant), then MP equals AP (MP=22). Falling from 24 to 22 fits the law! So X=22 is possible. Since X=22 is possible, Option D (Must be < 22) and C (Must be > 22) are eliminated immediately. Left with A and B, a quick calc confirms B.
