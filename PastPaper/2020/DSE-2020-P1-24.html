Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解決這類題目，我們必須清楚了解勞動市場各項數據的定義以及它們之間的關係。

To solve this type of question, we must clearly understand the definitions of various labor market statistics and their relationships.

  • 勞動人口 (Labor Force): 所有15歲或以上，並且符合就業或失業條件的人士的總和。
    The sum of all persons aged 15 or above who meet the conditions of being employed or unemployed.
  • 就業不足人口 (Underemployed Persons): 屬於「就業人口」的一部分，指在統計期間非自願地工作少於指定時數（通常為35小時），並正在尋找更多工作的人。
    A subset of "employed persons". It refers to those who involuntarily worked less than a specified number of hours (usually 35 hours) during the statistical period and are seeking more work.
$$ \text{Labor Force} = \text{Employed} + \text{Unemployed} $$ $$ \text{勞動人口} = \text{就業人口} + \text{失業人口} $$
$$ \text{Underemployment Rate} = \left( \frac{\text{Underemployed}}{\text{Labor Force}} \right) \times 100\% $$ $$ \text{就業不足率} = \left( \frac{\text{就業不足人口}}{\text{勞動人口}} \right) \times 100\% $$

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: C

首先，我們需要計算勞動人口 (Labor Force)，它是計算失業率和就業不足率的分母。
勞動人口 = 就業人口 + 失業人口 = \( 3871 + 114 = 3985 \)（千人）。

First, we need to calculate the Labor Force, which is the denominator for calculating both the unemployment rate and the underemployment rate.
Labor Force = Employed + Unemployed = \( 3871 + 114 = 3985 \) ('000).

然後，代入就業不足率公式：
就業不足率 = \( \left( \frac{41}{3985} \right) \times 100\% \approx 1.0288\% \)，四捨五入為 \( 1.03\% \)。

Then, plug it into the underemployment rate formula:
Underemployment Rate = \( \left( \frac{41}{3985} \right) \times 100\% \approx 1.0288\% \), rounded to \( 1.03\% \).

Breakdown of Options (選項拆解):

  • Option A (0.55%): 錯誤。這是將「總人口 (Population)」作為分母計算得出：\( \left( \frac{41}{7524} \right) \times 100\% \approx 0.545\% \)。
    Incorrect. This uses "Total Population" as the denominator: \( \left( \frac{41}{7524} \right) \times 100\% \approx 0.545\% \).
  • Option B (0.62%): 錯誤。這是將「15歲或以上人士 (Aged 15 or above)」作為分母計算得出：\( \left( \frac{41}{6590} \right) \times 100\% \approx 0.622\% \)。
    Incorrect. This uses "Aged 15 or above" as the denominator: \( \left( \frac{41}{6590} \right) \times 100\% \approx 0.622\% \).
  • Option C (1.03%): 正確 (Correct)。正確使用了「勞動人口 (Labor Force)」作為分母。
    Correct. It correctly uses "Labor Force" as the denominator.
  • Option D (1.06%): 錯誤。這是將「就業人口 (Employed persons)」作為分母計算得出：\( \left( \frac{41}{3871} \right) \times 100\% \approx 1.059\% \)。
    Incorrect. This uses "Employed persons" as the denominator: \( \left( \frac{41}{3871} \right) \times 100\% \approx 1.059\% \).

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (破題關鍵): 題目的核心考驗在於你是否知道「勞動人口 (Labor Force)」在表格中並沒有直接提供，你必須主動把「就業」和「失業」人口相加來找出這個隱藏的關鍵數據（分母）。

Pivot Point: The core test of this question is whether you know that the "Labor Force" is not directly provided in the table. You must proactively add the "Employed" and "Unemployed" figures to find this hidden crucial data (the denominator).

Distractors (干擾資訊): 題目提供了「總人口」和「15歲或以上人士」的數據。這些數字在這條計算中完全是多餘的，目的是又騙你去用錯誤的分母。

Distractors: The question provides data for "Population" and "Aged 15 or above". These figures are completely redundant for this calculation and serve only to trick you into using the wrong denominator.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

很多學生會誤以為「就業人口」就等於「勞動人口」，從而直接用 \( 3871 \) 作為分母（得出 Option D）。記住，失業人士雖然沒有工作，但他們仍在市場上積極尋找工作，因此他們絕對是勞動人口的一部分！

Many students mistakenly assume that "Employed persons" equals the "Labor Force", and directly use \( 3871 \) as the denominator (leading to Option D). Remember, although unemployed people do not have jobs, they are actively seeking work in the market, so they are definitely part of the labor force!

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼我們不需要把「就業不足人口」加進勞動人口的計算中？ (e.g., Labor Force = Employed + Unemployed + Underemployed ?)

Q: Why don't we add the "Underemployed persons" into the calculation of the Labor Force?

A: 這是一個極常見的誤區！在官方統計中，「就業不足人口」本身已經是「就業人口」裡面的一個子集（Subset）。這意味著那 41 千人已經包含在 3871 千人的就業數據內了。如果你再把它加一次，就會造成重複計算 (Double Counting)。

A: This is a very common misconception! In official statistics, "Underemployed persons" is already a subset of "Employed persons". This means those 41,000 people are already included in the 3,871,000 employed figure. If you add it again, you will be making a Double Counting error.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

在考場上處理這類滿佈數字的圖表題，最好的方法是「物理消除法」。一看到題目問有關勞動人口比率的問題（失業率 / 就業不足率），立刻拿起筆，在「總人口」和「15歲或以上人口」這兩行上畫一個大交叉。然後在旁邊寫上 \( LF = E + U \)，先算出分母。這樣就能保證你在緊張的考試環境下不會拿錯數字。

When dealing with number-heavy table questions in an exam, the best method is the "Physical Elimination Method". As soon as you see a question asking about labor force ratios (unemployment rate / underemployment rate), immediately take your pen and cross out the rows for "Population" and "Aged 15 or above". Then, write \( LF = E + U \) next to it and calculate the denominator first. This guarantees you won't grab the wrong numbers under exam pressure.
