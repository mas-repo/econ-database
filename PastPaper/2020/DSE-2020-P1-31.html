HKDSE Economics Tutorial 經濟學導修

Topic: 貨幣市場與公開市場操作 (Money Market & Open Market Operations) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: 貨幣需求 (Money Demand), 貨幣供應 (Money Supply)

The Question (題目)

下圖顯示某經濟的貨幣供應曲線 (\(M_s\)) 和貨幣需求曲線 (\(M_d\))。假設原初均衡點是 E。

若公眾的收入增加，而中央銀行向公眾發售政府債券，則利率 ______ ，貨幣數量 ______。

A. 會減少 …… 會增加
B. 會增加 …… 可能增加或減少
C. 可能增加或減少 …… 會減少
D. 會增加 …… 會減少

The following diagram indicates the money supply curve (\(M_s\)) and money demand curve (\(M_d\)) of a certain economy. Suppose the initial equilibrium point is at E.

If the income of the public increases and the central bank sells government bonds to the public, the interest rate ______ and the quantity of money ______.

A. will decrease …… will increase
B. will increase …… may increase or decrease
C. may increase or decrease …… will decrease
D. will increase …… will decrease

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這條題目，我們需要理解貨幣市場中兩個主要因素的變動：

To solve this question, we need to understand the shifts of two main factors in the money market:

  • 貨幣需求 (Money Demand, \(M_d\)): 公眾的收入增加會導致交易性貨幣需求上升。在任何一個利率水平下，人們需要持有更多貨幣來應付日常交易，因此 \(M_d\) 曲線會向右移動。
    An increase in public income leads to a higher transaction demand for money. At any given interest rate, people need to hold more money for daily transactions, causing the \(M_d\) curve to shift to the right.
  • 貨幣供應 (Money Supply, \(M_s\)): 中央銀行向公眾發售政府債券（公開市場操作）時，公眾需要用貨幣支付給中央銀行。這會減少銀行體系的儲備，導致貨幣基礎收縮，最終令貨幣供應減少。因此，垂直的 \(M_s\) 曲線會向左移動。
    When the central bank sells government bonds to the public (Open Market Operations), the public pays the central bank with money. This drains reserves from the banking system, contracting the monetary base and ultimately decreasing the money supply. Thus, the vertical \(M_s\) curve shifts to the left.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): D

選項 A (Option A): 錯誤 (Incorrect)

如分析所述，利率必定上升，貨幣數量必定下降。此選項方向完全相反。

As analyzed, the interest rate must increase and the quantity of money must decrease. This option states the exact opposite directions.

選項 B (Option B): 錯誤 (Incorrect)

雖然利率會增加，但貨幣數量並非「可能增加或減少」。因為 \(M_s\) 是一條垂直線，貨幣的均衡數量完全由 \(M_s\) 決定。\(M_s\) 向左移，貨幣數量必定減少。

Although the interest rate will increase, the quantity of money is not "may increase or decrease". Since \(M_s\) is a vertical line, the equilibrium quantity of money is entirely determined by \(M_s\). A leftward shift of \(M_s\) guarantees a decrease in the quantity of money.

選項 C (Option C): 錯誤 (Incorrect)

貨幣數量會減少是正確的，但利率並非「可能增加或減少」。\(M_d\) 向右移（推高利率）和 \(M_s\) 向左移（同樣推高利率），兩股力量同時推高利率，因此利率必定增加。

It is correct that the quantity of money will decrease, but the interest rate is not "may increase or decrease". The rightward shift of \(M_d\) (pushes interest rate up) and the leftward shift of \(M_s\) (also pushes interest rate up) both work together to raise the interest rate. Therefore, the interest rate must increase.

選項 D (Option D): 正確 (Correct)

綜合以上兩點：
1. 收入增加 \(\rightarrow\) \(M_d\) 右移 \(\rightarrow\) 利率上升。
2. 央行賣出債券 \(\rightarrow\) \(M_s\) 左移 \(\rightarrow\) 利率上升，貨幣數量減少。
兩者結合，利率必定增加，而貨幣數量必定減少。

Combining the two points above:
1. Income increases \(\rightarrow\) \(M_d\) shifts right \(\rightarrow\) Interest rate increases.
2. Central bank sells bonds \(\rightarrow\) \(M_s\) shifts left \(\rightarrow\) Interest rate increases, quantity of money decreases.
Combined, the interest rate will definitely increase, and the quantity of money will definitely decrease.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解決這類「雙重移動 (Double Shifts)」題目的關鍵在於逐一分析每個事件的影響，然後將結果疊加。

The key to solving these "Double Shifts" questions is to analyze the impact of each event individually and then superimpose the results.

關鍵點 (Pivot Point): 留意 \(M_s\) 是一條垂直線（完全缺乏彈性）。這意味著貨幣的均衡數量只受貨幣供應影響，不受貨幣需求影響。只要知道貨幣供應的方向，就能 100% 確定貨幣數量的變化。

Pivot Point: Notice that \(M_s\) is a vertical line (perfectly inelastic). This means the equilibrium quantity of money is solely determined by the money supply, not by money demand. As long as you know the direction of the money supply, you can be 100% sure about the change in the quantity of money.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱 1：混淆買入與賣出債券的效果
很多學生會死記硬背，結果把「央行發售 (sell) 債券」當作增加貨幣供應。記住一個簡單邏輯：央行賣債券給市民，市民就要付錢給央行。市場上的錢被央行收走了，所以貨幣供應會減少。

Trap 1: Confusing the effects of buying vs. selling bonds
Many students rely on rote memorization and mistakenly think "selling bonds" increases money supply. Remember a simple logic: when the central bank sells bonds to the public, the public has to pay the central bank. Money in the market is taken away by the central bank, so the money supply decreases.

陷阱 2：以為需求增加會令數量增加
在一般的商品市場 (如蘋果)，需求增加會令價格和交易量同時上升。但在貨幣市場中，由於 \(M_s\) 是垂直的，\(M_d\) 的移動只會改變利率，不會改變均衡貨幣數量。

Trap 2: Assuming an increase in demand increases quantity
In a normal goods market (like apples), an increase in demand raises both price and quantity transacted. However, in the money market, because \(M_s\) is vertical, a shift in \(M_d\) only changes the interest rate and does not change the equilibrium quantity of money.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼貨幣供應曲線 (\(M_s\)) 是一條垂直線？

A: 在 DSE 經濟學的基礎模型中，我們假設貨幣供應量是由中央銀行（如香港金管局）外生決定的 (exogenously determined)，它是一個固定的數值，不會因為市場利率的升降而自動改變。因此，無論利率 \(r\) 是多少，貨幣數量 \(M\) 都保持不變，形成一條垂直線。

Q: Why is the money supply curve (\(M_s\)) a vertical line?

A: In the basic HKDSE Economics model, we assume the money supply is exogenously determined by the central bank (like the HKMA). It is a fixed amount that does not automatically change when the market interest rate goes up or down. Therefore, regardless of the interest rate \(r\), the quantity of money \(M\) remains constant, forming a vertical line.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺法 (The One-Second Kill Method):

在考場上，看到這類題目可以運用「排除法」。
第一步：看到「央行發售債券」，立刻知道 \(M_s\) 左移，貨幣數量必定減少。馬上刪除選項 A 和 B。
第二步：剩下 C 和 D。因為 \(M_d\) 右移推高利率，\(M_s\) 左移也推高利率，兩者沒有衝突，所以利率必定增加，不可能「可能增加或減少」。刪除 C，答案選 D！

Shortcut in the Exam:
In the exam, use the "elimination method" for such questions.
Step 1: Seeing "central bank sells bonds", you immediately know \(M_s\) shifts left, so the quantity of money MUST decrease. Cross out options A and B instantly.
Step 2: You are left with C and D. Since \(M_d\) shifting right pushes the interest rate up, and \(M_s\) shifting left also pushes the interest rate up, there is no conflict. The interest rate MUST increase, it cannot be "may increase or decrease". Cross out C, and the answer is D!
