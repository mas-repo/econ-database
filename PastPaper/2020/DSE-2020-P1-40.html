HKDSE Economics Tutor Guide

Topic (課題): Taxation in Hong Kong (香港稅制)

Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts (核心概念): Territorial Source Principle (地域來源原則) Stamp Duty (印花稅) Property Tax (物業稅)

2. The Question (題目)

陳先生在內地營運一家工廠並賺取豐厚的利潤。最近，他在香港買入了一所住宅和一輛二手電動車，並從其持有的匯豐銀行股票獲得股息。

根據以上資料，他要向香港政府繳交下列哪種／些稅項？
(1) 物業稅
(2) 印花稅
(3) 利得稅
(4) 首次登記稅

A. 只有 (2)
B. 只有 (1)、(4)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. 只有 (1)、(3) 及 (4)

Mr. Chan runs a factory in mainland China and earns a huge profit. Recently he has bought a flat and a second hand electric car in Hong Kong, and received dividends from his shares of HSBC.

According to the above information, which type(s) of tax does he need to pay to the Hong Kong government?
(1) Property tax
(2) Stamp duty
(3) Profits tax
(4) First registration tax

A. (2) only
B. (1) and (4) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (3) and (4) only

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這道題目，我們必須清楚了解香港的主要稅項及其徵收條件：

To answer this question, we must clearly understand the major taxes in Hong Kong and their charging conditions:
  • Territorial Source Principle (地域來源原則): 香港實行地域來源徵稅原則，即只有源自香港的利潤或收入才須在香港課稅。
    Hong Kong adopts a territorial source principle of taxation, meaning only profits or income arising in or derived from Hong Kong are taxable.
  • Property Tax (物業稅): 向香港土地及建築物的擁有人徵收，按物業的租金收入計算。單純擁有物業而沒有出租，不需繳交物業稅。
    Charged on the owners of land and buildings in Hong Kong, based on the rental income of the property. Simply owning a property without renting it out does not incur property tax.
  • Stamp Duty (印花稅): 對某些文件（如樓宇買賣合約、租約、股票轉讓等）徵收的稅項。
    A tax imposed on certain documents (e.g., property transaction contracts, leases, stock transfers).
  • First Registration Tax (首次登記稅): 對在香港首次登記的汽車徵收的稅項。
    A tax levied on vehicles registered for the first time in Hong Kong.

一般稅款計算公式 (General Tax Calculation Formula):

$$ \text{Tax Payable} = \text{Net Chargeable Income} \times \text{Tax Rate} $$

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A

(1) 物業稅 / Property Tax: ❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect)

陳先生雖然在香港買入了一所住宅，但題目沒有提及他將物業出租以賺取租金收入。因此，他不需要繳交物業稅。

Although Mr. Chan bought a flat in Hong Kong, the question does not mention that he rented it out to earn rental income. Therefore, he does not need to pay property tax.

(2) 印花稅 / Stamp Duty: ✅ 正確 (Correct)

在香港買賣住宅物業必須繳付印花稅。因此，陳先生買入住宅需要繳交此稅項。

Buying or selling residential properties in Hong Kong requires the payment of stamp duty. Therefore, Mr. Chan needs to pay this tax for buying the flat.

(3) 利得稅 / Profits Tax: ❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect)

陳先生的工廠位於內地，利潤並非源自香港（不符合地域來源原則）。此外，從股票獲得的股息在香港一般不需繳交利得稅。因此，他不需要繳交利得稅。

Mr. Chan's factory is in mainland China, so the profits are not derived from Hong Kong (does not meet the territorial source principle). Also, dividends received from shares are generally not subject to profits tax in Hong Kong. Therefore, he does not need to pay profits tax.

(4) 首次登記稅 / First Registration Tax: ❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect)

陳先生買入的是「二手」電動車，這意味著該車輛之前已經在香港登記過。首次登記稅只適用於全新或首次在港登記的車輛。

Mr. Chan bought a "second hand" electric car, which means the vehicle has already been registered in Hong Kong previously. The first registration tax only applies to brand new vehicles or those registered in HK for the first time.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的核心在於找出題目中的「關鍵字 (Pivot Points)」並與稅制定義作配對：

The core of this type of question is to identify the "Pivot Points" in the text and match them with tax definitions:
  • 「內地營運」 (Mainland China) ➔ 提示利潤屬離岸性質，避開了香港的利得稅 (Profits Tax)。
  • 「買入住宅」 (Bought a flat) ➔ 觸發了印花稅 (Stamp Duty)。沒有提及「出租 (rented out)」，所以排除了物業稅 (Property Tax)。
  • 「二手」 (Second hand) ➔ 這是最大的陷阱！二手車不需要再繳交首次登記稅 (First Registration Tax)。
  • 「股息」 (Dividends) ➔ 香港不對股息徵收個人入息稅或利得稅。

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 買樓不是要交差餉 (Rates) 和地租 (Government Rent) 嗎？為什麼不選物業稅？

A: 差餉和地租確實是業主需要繳交的費用，但它們在經濟學分類中屬於間接稅或政府收費，而物業稅 (Property Tax) 是專指對「租金收入」徵收的直接稅。兩者概念完全不同，切勿混淆！

Q: Don't property owners have to pay Rates and Government Rent? Why not choose Property Tax?
A: Rates and Government Rent are indeed paid by owners, but they are different concepts. Property Tax is a direct tax specifically levied on "rental income". Do not confuse them!

In the exam, as soon as you see the word "second hand", you can immediately determine that (4) First Registration Tax is wrong. Look at the options:

  • D. 包含 (4) ➔ 淘汰
