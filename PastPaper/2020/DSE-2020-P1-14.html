📝 The Question (題目)

參閱以下物品Y的供需圖。
假設物品Y是劣等物品，而原初市場均衡點是E。若消費者的平均收入水平下降，新的市場均衡點會是 ________。

Refer to the following supply-demand diagram for Good Y.
Suppose Good Y is an inferior good and the initial market equilibrium point is E. If there is a fall in the average income level of consumers, the new market equilibrium point will be ________.

A. A
B. B
C. C
D. D

💡 Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這條題目，我們需要釐清「收入」與「物品性質」之間的關係：

To answer this question, we must clarify the relationship between "Income" and "Types of Goods":
  • 正常物品 (Normal Good): 當消費者收入上升時，需求增加；收入下降時，需求減少。
    When consumer income increases, demand increases; when income decreases, demand decreases.
  • 劣等物品 (Inferior Good): 當消費者收入上升時，需求減少；收入下降時，需求增加。
    When consumer income increases, demand decreases; when income decreases, demand increases.
劣等物品的關係式 (Relationship for Inferior Goods): $$ \text{Income} \downarrow \implies \text{Demand} \uparrow $$

市場均衡 (Market Equilibrium): 均衡點是需求曲線 (Demand Curve, $D$) 與供應曲線 (Supply Curve, $S$) 的相交點。需求增加會令需求曲線向右移動。

The equilibrium point is the intersection of the Demand Curve ($D$) and the Supply Curve ($S$). An increase in demand shifts the demand curve to the right.

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 Correct Option: A

Option A (正確 Correct):

題目指出物品Y是劣等物品，而消費者的平均收入下降。根據定義，消費者對劣等物品的需求會增加。因此，需求曲線會由 $D_0$ 向右移動至 $D_1$。由於供應沒有改變（維持在 $S_0$），新的均衡點是 $D_1$ 與 $S_0$ 的相交點，即是點 A。

The question states that Good Y is an inferior good and average income falls. By definition, the demand for an inferior good increases when income falls. Therefore, the demand curve shifts to the right from $D_0$ to $D_1$. Since supply remains unchanged (at $S_0$), the new equilibrium is the intersection of $D_1$ and $S_0$, which is point A.
Option B (錯誤 Incorrect):

點 B 代表需求不變 ($D_0$)，但供應減少 ($S_0 \rightarrow S_2$)。收入改變只會影響消費者的需求，不會直接影響生產者的供應。

Point B represents unchanged demand ($D_0$) but a decrease in supply ($S_0 \rightarrow S_2$). A change in income affects consumer demand, not producer supply.
Option C (錯誤 Incorrect):

點 C 代表供應不變 ($S_0$)，但需求減少 ($D_0 \rightarrow D_2$)。如果物品Y是「正常物品」(Normal Good)，收入下降才會導致這個結果。

Point C represents unchanged supply ($S_0$) but a decrease in demand ($D_0 \rightarrow D_2$). This would be the correct answer only if Good Y were a "Normal Good".
Option D (錯誤 Incorrect):

點 D 代表需求不變 ($D_0$)，但供應增加 ($S_0 \rightarrow S_1$)。如前所述，收入改變不會引致供應曲線移動。

Point D represents unchanged demand ($D_0$) but an increase in supply ($S_0 \rightarrow S_1$). As mentioned, a change in income does not shift the supply curve.

🔍 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解答此類圖像題，可遵循以下三個步驟：

To solve this type of graphical question, follow these three steps:
  1. 找出「受影響的主體」 (Identify the affected party): 題目提到「消費者收入」(Consumer income)。這會影響需求 (Demand)，因此我們可以排除所有供應曲線移動的選項 (B 和 D)。
  2. 尋找「關鍵字」 (Find the Pivot Point): 關鍵字是「劣等物品」(Inferior good) 和「收入下降」(Income falls)。
  3. 判斷「方向」 (Determine the direction): 負負得正（劣等 + 收入跌），結論是需求上升，曲線向右移至 $D_1$。答案鎖定在點 A。

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

混淆物品性質 (Confusing Types of Goods):
很多同學一看到「收入下降」，就直覺認為「買少啲嘢」，立刻選擇需求減少的點 C。這是一個致命陷阱！考試時必須圈起「劣等物品」或「正常物品」字眼，這決定了曲線移動的方向是順向還是逆向。

Many students intuitively think "buy less" when they see "income falls" and immediately choose point C (decrease in demand). This is a fatal trap! In exams, always circle the terms "inferior good" or "normal good" as they dictate whether the curve shifts in the same or opposite direction to income.

🙋‍♂️ Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 點解供應曲線 $S_0$ 唔會郁？ (Why doesn't the supply curve $S_0$ shift?)

A: 影響供應曲線移動的因素通常是生產成本（如工資、租金）、技術進步、政府稅收或補貼等。「消費者收入」只會影響消費者的購買意欲和能力，即是需求層面。除非題目提到生產成本改變，否則供應曲線維持不變。
Factors that shift the supply curve are typically production costs (e.g., wages, rent), technological advancement, government taxes, or subsidies. "Consumer income" only affects consumers' willingness and ability to buy, which is the demand side. Unless the question mentions a change in production costs, the supply curve remains unchanged.

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

「一秒刪除法」 (The One-Second Elimination Method):
一看到「收入改變」，立刻知道是需求線 (D) 移動。原點在 E ($D_0, S_0$)。既然供應 (S) 不變，新答案一定在 $S_0$ 這條線上！看圖可見，$S_0$ 線上只有 E, A, C 三點。因此，B 和 D 絕對是錯的，可以立刻劃掉，將答對機率瞬間提升至 50%！

When you see "change in income", you immediately know the Demand curve (D) shifts. The initial point is E ($D_0, S_0$). Since Supply (S) is unchanged, the new answer MUST lie on the $S_0$ curve! Looking at the graph, only points E, A, and C are on the $S_0$ line. Therefore, B and D are absolutely wrong and can be eliminated instantly, increasing your odds to 50% right away!
