貨幣需求與利率 (Money Demand & Interest Rate)

Topic: Money & Banking Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Asset Demand, Speculative Motive

題目 (The Question)

參考上圖。當 ______________ ，資產貨幣需求會由 \(M_{a0}\) 移至 \(M_{a1}\)。 Refer to the diagram above. The asset demand for money will shift from \(M_{a0}\) to \(M_{a1}\) when ______________.
  • A. 收入上升
    A. income increases.
  • B. 利率下降
    B. the interest rate decreases.
  • C. 一般物價水平上升
    C. the general price level increases.
  • D. 人們預期利率上升
    D. the interest rate is expected to increase.

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

資產性貨幣需求 (Asset Demand for Money, \(M_a\))： 這源於凱恩斯的流動性偏好理論 (Liquidity Preference Theory)。人們持有貨幣作為價值儲存的一種形式，主要與持有債券 (Bonds) 作比較。 Based on Keynes' Liquidity Preference Theory. People hold money as a store of value, primarily weighing it against holding bonds.
$$ \text{Bond Price (債券價格)} \propto \frac{1}{\text{Interest Rate (利率)}} $$
關鍵關係：
  • 利率與債券價格成反比： 當利率上升，債券價格下跌。
  • 投機動機 (Speculative Motive)： 如果人們預期利率會上升，即預期債券價格會下跌 (資本虧損 Capital Loss)。為了避免虧損，人們會賣出債券，持有現金。
  • Inverse Relationship: When interest rates rise, bond prices fall.
  • Speculative Motive: If people expect interest rates to rise, they expect bond prices to fall (Capital Loss). To avoid this loss, they sell bonds and hold cash.

答案與詳細分析 (Answer & Analysis)

正確答案：選項 D (Correct Option: D)
分析選項 D (預期利率上升)：正確

圖中顯示 \(M_a\) 曲線向右移動（在同一利率水平下，人們想持有更多貨幣）。當人們預期利率上升，意味著他們預期債券價格將會下跌。為了避免持有債券帶來的資本虧損 (Capital Loss)，人們會現在賣出債券並轉為持有現金。因此，對資產貨幣的需求增加，曲線向右移動。

Analysis of D (Expected interest rate increase): Correct. The diagram shows a rightward shift of the \(M_a\) curve (people want to hold more money at the same interest rate). When people expect the interest rate to increase, they expect bond prices to fall. To avoid capital loss from holding bonds, they sell bonds now and hold cash instead. Thus, the asset demand for money increases, shifting the curve to the right.

分析選項 A (收入上升)：錯誤

收入 (Income, \(Y\)) 主要影響交易性貨幣需求 (Transactions Demand, \(M_t\))，而不是資產性需求 (\(M_a\))。雖然總貨幣需求會上升，但題目特定指明是 \(M_a\) 曲線。在簡單凱恩斯模型中，\(M_a\) 是利率的函數，不隨收入改變。

Analysis of A (Income increases): Incorrect. Income (\(Y\)) primarily affects Transactions Demand (\(M_t\)), not Asset Demand (\(M_a\)). While total money demand would rise, the question specifically labels the curve \(M_a\). In the simple Keynesian model, \(M_a\) is a function of interest rates, not income.

分析選項 B (利率下降)：錯誤

利率 (Interest Rate) 是縱軸 (Y-axis) 的變量。利率的改變只會導致沿著曲線移動 (Movement along the curve)，而不會導致整條曲線移動 (Shift)。利率下降會導致沿著 \(M_{a0}\) 向右下方移動。

Analysis of B (Interest rate decreases): Incorrect. The interest rate is the variable on the Y-axis. A change in interest rate causes a movement along the curve, not a shift of the curve. A decrease in interest rate leads to a downward movement along \(M_{a0}\).

分析選項 C (一般物價水平上升)：錯誤

與收入類似，物價水平 (Price Level, \(P\)) 上升意味著人們需要更多現金進行日常交易，這主要影響交易性貨幣需求 (\(M_t\))。它不直接導致人們為了投機或資產配置目的而改變對貨幣的需求。

Analysis of C (General price level increases): Incorrect. Similar to income, an increase in the Price Level (\(P\)) means people need more cash for daily transactions, primarily affecting Transactions Demand (\(M_t\)). It does not directly cause a shift in the asset demand for money for speculative purposes.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

1. 識別變量 (Identify Variables)： 縱軸是利率 (\(R\))，橫軸是貨幣量 (\(M\))。曲線標記為 \(M_a\) (Asset Demand)。
2. 區分「移動」與「沿線移動」 (Shift vs. Movement)： 題目顯示的是整條曲線向右移動 (Shift)。這意味著除了現行利率以外的因素發生了變化。這直接排除了選項 B (利率改變)。
3. 鎖定決定因素 (Locate Determinants)： 資產性貨幣需求 (\(M_a\)) 的主要移動因素是預期 (Expectations) 或風險 (Risk)。收入和物價主要影響交易性需求。因此，只有選項 D 涉及「預期」，且邏輯正確。

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • 混淆 \(M_t\) 與 \(M_a\)： 學生常誤以為所有「對錢的需求增加」都會移動同一條線。必須分清：
    - \(M_t\) (交易用) 受收入 \(Y\) 和物價 \(P\) 影響。
    - \(M_a\) (資產/投機用) 受利率 \(r\) (沿線移動) 和 預期利率 \(r^e\) (整線移動) 影響。
  • 搞錯利率與債券價格的關係： 記住口訣：利率升，債價跌 (Inverse relationship)。如果你預期利率升，你就是預期債券會虧錢，所以你會賣債券、拿現金 (需求曲線右移)。

學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

問：為什麼收入上升 (Option A) 不會增加資產貨幣需求？有錢人不是會有更多資產嗎？

Q: Why doesn't an increase in income (Option A) increase asset demand? Don't rich people have more assets?

答：在經濟學定義中，「資產性貨幣需求」特指持有現金作為資產，而不是持有股票或房地產。當收入上升，人們確實更有錢，但通常會增加消費（交易需求 \(M_t\)）或購買生息資產（如債券）。單純的收入增加並不直接導致人們想在同一利率下持有更多「零回報的現金」作為價值儲存。

A: In economics, "Asset Demand for Money" specifically refers to holding cash as an asset, not stocks or real estate. When income rises, people do get richer, but they usually increase consumption (Transactions Demand \(M_t\)) or buy interest-bearing assets (like bonds). An increase in income alone doesn't directly cause people to want to hold more "zero-return cash" as a store of value at the same interest rate.
