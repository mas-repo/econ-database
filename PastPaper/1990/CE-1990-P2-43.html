HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Demand & Supply / Exchange Rates (需求與供應 / 匯率) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Substitutes (替代品), Change in Demand vs Quantity Demanded (需求改變 vs 需求量改變)

題目 (Question):

假設日元兌港元升值而意大利里拉兌港元貶值。香港對意大利汽車的需求會

Suppose that the Japanese yen appreciates against the Hong Kong dollar and the Italian lira depreciates against the Hong Kong dollar. The demand for Italian cars in Hong Kong will
  • A. 增加。
    increase.
  • B. 減少。
    decrease.
  • C. 不變，因為只是需求量改變。
    remain unchanged because there is only a change in the quantity demanded.
  • D. 增加或減少是取決於價格變動的大小。
    either increase depending on the extent of price changes.

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

1. 需求量改變 vs 需求改變 (Change in Quantity Demanded vs Change in Demand)
  • 需求量改變 (Change in Qd): 由物品本身價格 (Own Price) 改變引起。圖表上表現為沿著同一條需求曲線移動 (Movement along the curve)。
  • 需求改變 (Change in Demand): 由非價格因素 (Non-price factors) 引起（如收入、相關物品價格）。圖表上表現為整條需求曲線移動 (Shift of the curve)。
  • Change in Quantity Demanded: Caused by a change in the good's own price. Represented by a movement along the demand curve.
  • Change in Demand: Caused by non-price factors (e.g., income, price of related goods). Represented by a shift of the demand curve.
2. 替代品 (Substitutes)
若物品 X 和 Y 是替代品（例如日本車和意大利車）：
\( P_X \uparrow \implies D_Y \uparrow \)
當 X 的價格上升，消費者會轉而購買 Y，導致 Y 的需求 (Demand) 增加（曲線向右移）。 If goods X and Y are substitutes (e.g., Japanese cars and Italian cars): When the price of X increases, consumers switch to Y, causing the Demand for Y to increase (curve shifts right).

答案與詳細分析 (Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): A

步驟 1：分析日元升值的影響 (Step 1: Analyzing the JPY appreciation)

日元兌港元升值 \(\rightarrow\) 日本汽車（以港元計算）的價格上升。
日本汽車與意大利汽車是替代品 (Substitutes)。
由於日本汽車變貴，香港消費者會轉買意大利汽車。
結果：意大利汽車的需求 (Demand) 增加（需求曲線向右移）。

JPY appreciates against HKD \(\rightarrow\) Price of Japanese cars (in HKD) increases.
Japanese cars and Italian cars are substitutes.
Since Japanese cars become more expensive, HK consumers switch to Italian cars.
Result: The Demand for Italian cars increases (Demand curve shifts to the right).

步驟 2：分析意大利里拉貶值的影響 (Step 2: Analyzing the ITL depreciation)

意大利里拉兌港元貶值 \(\rightarrow\) 意大利汽車（以港元計算）的本身價格下降。
物品本身價格下降，只會導致需求量 (Quantity Demanded) 上升。
注意：這會導致沿著需求曲線的移動 (Movement)，而不是需求曲線的移動 (Shift)。因此，這部分不會改變「需求 (Demand)」。

ITL depreciates against HKD \(\rightarrow\) The own price of Italian cars (in HKD) decreases.
A decrease in the own price leads to an increase in Quantity Demanded.
Note: This causes a movement along the curve, NOT a shift of the curve. Therefore, this part does not change "Demand".

綜合結論 (Conclusion)

題目問的是「需求 (Demand)」的變化（即曲線是否移動）。
1. 里拉貶值 \(\rightarrow\) 價格變，需求量變，需求不變。
2. 日元升值 \(\rightarrow\) 替代品價格升，需求增加。
因此，整體效果是需求增加。

The question asks about the change in "Demand" (shift of the curve).
1. ITL depreciation \(\rightarrow\) Price changes, Quantity Demanded changes, Demand remains unchanged.
2. JPY appreciation \(\rightarrow\) Substitute price rises, Demand increases.
Therefore, the net effect is an increase in Demand.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

這類題目通常包含兩個變數，你需要分辨哪個變數影響「需求 (Demand)」，哪個影響「需求量 (Quantity Demanded)」。

These questions usually contain two variables. You need to distinguish which variable affects "Demand" and which affects "Quantity Demanded".
  • 關鍵點 (Pivot Point): 題目問的是 "Demand" (需求) 還是 "Quantity Demanded" (需求量)？如果是問 "Demand"，你只需要尋找非價格因素 (Non-price factors)，例如替代品價格的變動。
  • 干擾項 (Distractor): 「意大利里拉貶值」是干擾項。雖然它讓意大利車變便宜，但这屬於「本身價格」變動，不符合經濟學上「需求改變 (Change in Demand)」的定義。
  • Pivot Point: Does the question ask about "Demand" or "Quantity Demanded"? Since it asks about "Demand", you only look for non-price factors, such as the change in the price of substitutes.
  • Distractor: "Italian lira depreciates" is a distractor regarding the Demand curve shift. While it makes Italian cars cheaper, this is an "own price" change, which does not fit the definition of a "Change in Demand" in Economics.

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

錯誤觀念：「意大利車便宜了，所以大家更想買，需求 (Demand) 增加。」

糾正：在經濟學中，物品變便宜導致購買量增加，稱為需求量增加 (Increase in Quantity Demanded)。只有當外在因素（如日本車變貴）導致你在同樣價格下想買更多意大利車，才叫需求增加 (Increase in Demand)。

Misconception: "Italian cars are cheaper, so people want to buy them more, thus Demand increases."
Correction: In Economics, buying more because the good is cheaper is called an Increase in Quantity Demanded. It is only called an Increase in Demand if external factors (like Japanese cars becoming expensive) make you want to buy more Italian cars at the same price.

學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼選項 C 不對？里拉貶值不是真的只改變了需求量嗎？
(Why is Option C wrong? Doesn't the depreciation of the lira only change the quantity demanded?)

A: 如果題目只有提到里拉貶值，選項 C 就會是正確的。但題目同時提到了日元升值。日元升值改變了替代品（日本車）的價格，這是一個外在因素，它會直接導致意大利車的需求曲線向右移動（需求增加）。我們不能忽略日元升值的影響。
If the question only mentioned the depreciation of the lira, Option C would be correct. However, the question also mentions the appreciation of the Yen. The appreciation of the Yen changes the price of a substitute (Japanese cars), which is an external factor that shifts the demand curve for Italian cars to the right (Increase in Demand). We cannot ignore the effect of the Yen.
