HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Money & Banking (貨幣與銀行) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Keywords: Money Supply M1, Deposit Creation, Banking Multiplier

題目 / Question:

某人存入$1 000至銀行系統。法定儲備比率是0.25。結果，最大創造的存款為_______________，而最大創造的貨幣供應為________________。 A person deposits $1 000 with the banking system. The required reserve ratio is 0.25. As a result of the deposit, the maximum amount of deposit created would be _______________ and the maximum amount of money supply created would be ____________.
  • A. $4 000；$4 000
  • B. $4 000；$3 000
  • C. $3 000；$4 000
  • D. $3 000；$3 000
💡 概念重溫 / Concept Review

1. 銀行乘數 (Banking Multiplier):

$$ \text{Multiplier (乘數)} = \frac{1}{\text{Required Reserve Ratio (法定儲備比率)}} = \frac{1}{r} $$

2. 存款最大變動 (Max. Change in Deposits):

當現金存入銀行時，這筆錢成為「初始存款」。 When cash is deposited into a bank, this becomes the "Initial Deposit".
$$ \Delta \text{Deposits} = \text{Initial Deposit} \times \frac{1}{r} $$

3. 貨幣供應定義 (Money Supply Definition - M1):

$$ M1 = C_p + D $$
  • \( C_p \): 公眾持有的現金 (Cash held by public)
  • \( D \): 客戶存款 (Customer Deposits)

✅ 答案與詳細分析 / Answer & Analysis

Correct Option: B ($4 000; $3 000)

步驟 1：計算最大存款創造 (Step 1: Calculate Max Deposit Created)

題目問的是「最大創造的存款」(Maximum amount of deposit created)。這通常指銀行體系內的存款總變動 (Total Change in Deposits)。
The question asks for the "maximum amount of deposit created". This refers to the Total Change in Deposits within the banking system.

初始存款 (Initial Deposit) = $1,000
法定儲備比率 (RRR) = 0.25

$$ \Delta \text{Deposits} = \$1,000 \times \frac{1}{0.25} = \$1,000 \times 4 = \$4,000 $$

銀行體系最終會擁有 $4,000 的存款。由於最初這筆錢是現金（存款為0），所以存款增加了 $4,000。
The banking system will end up with $4,000 in deposits. Since the money was originally cash (deposits were 0), deposits increased by $4,000.

步驟 2：計算最大貨幣供應創造 (Step 2: Calculate Max Money Supply Created)

這是最關鍵的一步。貨幣供應 (M1) = 公眾持有現金 (\(C_p\)) + 存款 (\(D\))。
This is the critical step. Money Supply (M1) = Cash held by public (\(C_p\)) + Deposits (\(D\)).

變動分析 (Change Analysis):

  • \(\Delta C_p\) (現金變動): 市民把 $1,000 現金存入銀行，所以公眾手上的現金減少了 $1,000。
    The person deposited $1,000 cash into the bank, so cash held by the public decreased by $1,000.
    \(\Delta C_p = -\$1,000\)
  • \(\Delta D\) (存款變動): 如步驟 1 計算，存款總額增加了 $4,000。
    As calculated in Step 1, total deposits increased by $4,000.
    \(\Delta D = +\$4,000\)
$$ \Delta M1 = \Delta C_p + \Delta D $$ $$ \Delta M1 = -\$1,000 + \$4,000 = +\$3,000 $$

結論 (Conclusion):
存款創造 = $4,000
貨幣供應創造 = $3,000
因此，答案是 B。

🔍 題目拆解 / Problem Decomposition

這類題目的「決勝點」(Pivot Point) 在於分辨資金來源。
The "Pivot Point" of this question is identifying the source of funds.

  • 情況 A：現金存入 (Cash Deposit)
    如果題目說「某人存入現金」(A person deposits...)，這意味著現金從公眾手中消失，進入銀行。計算貨幣供應時，必須扣除這筆現金。
    If the question implies a cash deposit, cash disappears from the public hands. You MUST subtract this cash when calculating the change in Money Supply.
  • 情況 B：央行注入 (Central Bank Injection)
    如果題目說「中央銀行購買債券」(Central bank buys bonds...)，這筆錢是憑空創造的新錢，公眾沒有損失現金。那樣的話，\(\Delta MS = \Delta Deposits\)。
    If the central bank injects money, the public didn't lose any cash. In that case, \(\Delta MS = \Delta Deposits\).

本題屬於情況 A。

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 / Common Mistakes & Traps
  • 陷阱 1：忘記扣除現金 (Forgetting to subtract cash)
    學生常誤以為貨幣供應增加量等於存款增加量 ($4,000)，從而誤選 A。記住：存款增加了，但你手上的現金減少了！
    Students often mistakenly think the increase in Money Supply equals the increase in Deposits ($4,000), leading to Option A. Remember: Deposits went up, but the cash in your hand went down!
  • 陷阱 2：混淆「存款創造」與「信貸創造」 (Confusing Deposit Creation vs Credit Creation)
    有時學生會計算「信貸創造」(Loans Created = $4,000 - $1,000 = $3,000) 並將其填入第一格。雖然邏輯上沒錯，但在 DSE 考試慣例中，「最大創造的存款」通常指最終存款總額 (Total Deposits)。
    Sometimes students calculate "Credit Creation" (Loans = $3,000) for the first blank. While logically sound, in DSE convention, "Maximum deposit created" usually refers to the Total Deposits generated.
🙋 學生常見疑問 / Student Q&A
Q: 為什麼「存款創造」不是 $3,000 (即是貸款額)？ (Why is "Deposit Created" not $3,000, i.e., the loan amount?)

A: 這是一個定義問題。銀行資產負債表上的「存款」一欄最終變成了 $4,000。雖然其中 $1,000 是原本的現金，但作為「存款」這個項目，它確實從 $0 變成了 $4,000。如果題目問「最大信貸創造」(Maximum credit creation)，答案才是 $3,000。

A: This is a definition issue. The "Deposits" column on the bank's balance sheet ends up at $4,000. Even though $1,000 was the original cash, as a "Deposit" item, it went from $0 to $4,000. If the question asked for "Maximum credit creation", the answer would be $3,000.

Q: 如果題目沒有說「最大」(Maximum)，答案會不同嗎？ (If the question didn't say "Maximum", would the answer differ?)

A: 會的。如果銀行持有超額儲備 (Excess Reserves) 或者公眾持有現金流失 (Cash Leakage)，實際創造的存款會少於 $4,000。「最大」假設了銀行借出所有錢，且公眾不持有額外現金。

A: Yes. If banks hold Excess Reserves or if there is Cash Leakage held by the public, the actual deposit created would be less than $4,000. "Maximum" assumes banks lend out everything and the public holds no extra cash.

⚡ 考試技巧 / Exam Tricks

「減一法則」 (The "Minus One" Rule)

當題目涉及現金存入 (Cash Deposit) 時：

  • 存款變動倍數 = \( \frac{1}{r} \) (這裡是 4倍)
  • 貨幣供應變動倍數 = \( \frac{1}{r} - 1 \) (這裡是 3倍)

直接用 $1,000 乘以 3，即可秒殺第二部分答案 ($3,000)。
Multiply $1,000 directly by 3 to instantly get the answer for the second part ($3,000).
