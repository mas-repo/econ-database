宏觀經濟學 Macroeconomics 財政政策 Fiscal Policy 總需求 Aggregate Demand
難度 (Difficulty): ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)
關鍵概念 (Key Concepts): 通縮差距 (Deflationary Gap), 財政赤字 (Fiscal Deficit), 誘發性稅收 (Induced Tax)

1. 題目 (The Question)

假設某國出現通縮（產出）差距和財政赤字。下列哪項會同時縮小通縮（產出）差距及減低財政赤字？
Suppose a deflationary (output) gap and a fiscal deficit exist in a country. Which of the following would narrow the deflationary (output) gap and reduce the fiscal deficit at the same time?
  • A. 增加政府的基建支出
    increasing government expenditure on infrastructure
  • B. 調低利得稅率
    reducing the profits tax rate
  • C. 增加薪俸稅的退稅
    increasing the tax rebate on salaries tax
  • D. 容許更多旅客免簽證到該國旅遊
    allowing more tourists to travel to the country visa-free

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

通縮（產出）差距 Deflationary (Output) Gap:
當均衡產出（實質 GDP）低於潛在產出（充分就業產出水平）時出現。要縮小差距，必須增加總需求 (Aggregate Demand, $AD$)。
Occurs when the equilibrium output (Real GDP) is below the potential output (full-employment output level). To narrow the gap, Aggregate Demand ($AD$) must increase.
$$ AD = C + I + G + (X - M) $$
財政赤字 Fiscal Deficit:
當政府支出 ($G$) 大於稅收 ($T$) 時出現。要減少赤字，必須增加政府收入或減少政府支出。
Occurs when government expenditure ($G$) is greater than tax revenue ($T$). To reduce the deficit, the government must increase revenue or decrease expenditure.
$$ \text{Fiscal Deficit} = G - T > 0 $$
誘發性稅收 Induced Tax:
國民收入 ($Y$) 上升時，稅收 ($T$) 會自動增加（例如薪俸稅和利得稅），這有助於改善財政狀況。
When national income ($Y$) rises, tax revenue ($T$) automatically increases (e.g., salaries tax and profits tax), which helps improve the fiscal balance.

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): D

A (錯誤 Wrong)
增加政府基建支出會令 $G$ 上升，總需求 ($AD$) 增加，這的確能縮小通縮差距。但是，因為 $G$ 增加，政府開支變大，這會令財政赤字惡化，無法滿足題目的第二個要求。
Increasing government expenditure on infrastructure raises $G$, which increases $AD$. This does narrow the deflationary gap. However, because $G$ increases, government spending becomes larger, which worsens the fiscal deficit. It fails the second condition of the question.
B (錯誤 Wrong)
調低利得稅率會鼓勵投資 ($I$ 上升)，總需求 ($AD$) 增加，能縮小通縮差距。然而，稅率下降會直接減少政府稅收 ($T$ 下降)，導致財政赤字惡化。
Reducing the profits tax rate encourages investment ($I$ increases), increasing $AD$ and narrowing the deflationary gap. However, a lower tax rate directly reduces government tax revenue ($T$ decreases), causing the fiscal deficit to worsen.
C (錯誤 Wrong)
增加退稅等於變相減稅 ($T$ 下降)，市民可支配收入增加，帶動消費 ($C$ 上升)，總需求 ($AD$) 增加，能縮小通縮差距。但同樣地，退稅會減少政府淨收入，令財政赤字惡化。
Increasing the tax rebate is effectively a tax cut ($T$ decreases). Disposable income rises, stimulating consumption ($C$ increases). $AD$ increases, narrowing the deflationary gap. But similarly, the rebate reduces net government revenue, causing the fiscal deficit to worsen.
D (正確 Correct)
容許更多旅客免簽證旅遊會增加旅遊收益，這屬於服務輸出 ($X$ 上升)。出口增加帶動總需求 ($AD$) 上升，成功縮小通縮差距。

同時，總需求上升帶動國民收入 ($Y$) 增加。在稅率不變的情況下，更高的收入會帶來更多的稅收（誘發性稅收，$T$ 上升）。由於政府開支 ($G$) 沒有因此改變，而稅收 ($T$) 增加了，財政赤字得以減少。完美符合兩個條件！
Allowing more visa-free tourists increases tourism revenue, which is an export of services ($X$ increases). An increase in exports raises $AD$, successfully narrowing the deflationary gap.

Meanwhile, higher $AD$ leads to an increase in national income ($Y$). With tax rates unchanged, a higher income generates more tax revenue (induced tax, $T$ increases). Since government expenditure ($G$) remains unchanged but tax revenue ($T$) has increased, the fiscal deficit is reduced. It satisfies both conditions perfectly!

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題關鍵 (The Pivot Point):
題目要求「同時」達成兩個目標。你要明白擴張性財政政策（增加開支、減稅）是一把雙面刃：它能救經濟（縮小通縮差距），但必定會傷政府荷包（擴大赤字）。因此，答案必定不能是傳統的擴張性財政政策。
The question requires achieving two goals "at the same time." You must understand that expansionary fiscal policy (increasing spending, cutting taxes) is a double-edged sword: it saves the economy (narrows the deflationary gap) but hurts the government's wallet (widens the deficit). Therefore, the answer cannot be a traditional expansionary fiscal policy.
尋找外部注入 (Looking for an External Injection):
你需要找一個能帶動 $AD$ 上升，但又不需要政府自掏腰包的選項。選項 D（出口增加）正正就是這種「外部注入」，它是破局的關鍵。
You need to find an option that stimulates $AD$ without the government paying for it out of pocket. Option D (an increase in exports) is exactly this kind of "external injection," which is the key to solving the problem.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • 陷阱一：只看到「通縮差距」而忽略「財政赤字」。很多同學一看到需要刺激經濟，就條件反射地選擇 A、B 或 C，忘記了這些政策會令政府變窮。
    Trap 1: Seeing only "deflationary gap" and ignoring "fiscal deficit". Many students instinctively choose A, B, or C to stimulate the economy, forgetting that these policies make the government poorer.
  • 陷阱二：忘記「旅遊業是出口」。部分同學不知道外國遊客在本地消費屬於「服務輸出 ($X$)」，導致無法判斷 D 選項對 $AD$ 的影響。
    Trap 2: Forgetting that "tourism is an export". Some students don't realize that spending by foreign tourists locally is an "export of services ($X$)", making them unable to assess Option D's effect on $AD$.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q1: 國民收入 ($Y$) 增加，政府不需要增建基建或增加服務開支嗎？這不會令 $G$ 也上升嗎？
Q1: When national income ($Y$) increases, doesn't the government need to build more infrastructure or increase service spending? Wouldn't this cause $G$ to rise too?
A1: 在宏觀經濟學的基礎模型中，政府支出 ($G$) 通常被假設為自發性 (Autonomous) 的，即它由政府政策決定，不會隨國民收入的短期波動而自動改變。相對地，稅收 ($T$) 是誘發性 (Induced) 的，會隨收入增加而自動上升。因此，我們只考慮 $T$ 的上升。
A1: In basic macroeconomic models, government expenditure ($G$) is generally assumed to be autonomous, meaning it is determined by policy and does not automatically change with short-term fluctuations in national income. In contrast, taxes ($T$) are induced and automatically rise with income. Therefore, we only consider the increase in $T$.
Q2: 如果選項 B 是「調高利得稅率」，能符合條件嗎？
Q2: What if Option B was "increasing the profits tax rate", would it satisfy the conditions?
A2: 不能。調高稅率會增加政府收入 ($T$ 上升)，確實能減少財政赤字。但它會打擊投資 ($I$ 下降)，導致總需求 ($AD$) 下降，這會令通縮差距擴大，而不是縮小。
A2: No. Raising the tax rate increases government revenue ($T$ increases), which indeed reduces the fiscal deficit. However, it hurts investment ($I$ decreases), leading to a fall in $AD$. This would widen the deflationary gap, not narrow it.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

同類排除法 (The Grouping Elimination Method):
這是一個非常有用的選擇題技巧。仔細看看選項 A、B 和 C：
  • A = 擴張性財政政策 (Expansionary Fiscal Policy)
  • B = 擴張性財政政策 (Expansionary Fiscal Policy)
  • C = 擴張性財政政策 (Expansionary Fiscal Policy)
這三個選項的經濟機制和對政府財政的最終影響方向是完全相同的！在單項選擇題中，如果三個選項的本質是一樣的，它們不可能同時是正確答案。因此，你甚至不需要深入分析，就可以直接選出與眾不同的 D 選項（外部需求增加）！
The Grouping Elimination Method:
This is a highly effective MCQ trick. Look closely at options A, B, and C:
A, B, and C are all Expansionary Fiscal Policies. Their economic mechanisms and final impacts on the government budget move in the exact same direction! In a single-choice question, if three options are fundamentally identical, they cannot all be correct. Therefore, even without deep analysis, you can instantly choose the odd one out: Option D (Increase in external demand)!
