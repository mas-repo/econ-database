📝 題目 (The Question)

下列哪些會包括在本港本地生產總值的計算之內？

(1) 本港某自住物業的估算租金價值
(2) 某地產經紀賣出二手物業所收取的佣金
(3) 某大學生收取的獎學金

A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

Which of the following are included in the calculation of the gross domestic product of Hong Kong?

(1) the estimated rental value of an owner-occupied property in Hong Kong
(2) the commission received by a real estate agent from selling second-hand property
(3) the scholarship received by a university student

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

🧠 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

什麼是本地生產總值？ (What is GDP?)

本地生產總值 \( (GDP) \) 是指一個國家或地區在特定時間內（通常為一年），在其常住生產單位內所生產的所有最終物品及服務的總市場價值。

Gross Domestic Product \( (GDP) \) is the total market value of all final goods and services currently produced within the domestic boundary of a country or territory in a specified period (usually a year).
$$ \text{GDP} = C + I + G + (X - M) $$

註解 (Note)：\( C \) = 私人消費 (Private Consumption), \( I \) = 投資 (Investment), \( G \) = 政府開支 (Government Expenditure), \( X \) = 出口 (Exports), \( M \) = 進口 (Imports)。

計入 GDP 的三大原則 (Three Golden Rules for GDP Calculation):

  1. 必須涉及當前的生產 (Current Production): 過去生產的物品（如二手貨）不計算在內，但為二手交易所提供的新服務（如經紀佣金）則計算在內。
    Must involve current production: Previously produced goods (like second-hand goods) are excluded, but new services provided for the second-hand transaction (like agent commissions) are included.
  2. 必須具有市場價值 (Market Value): 非市場活動（如自己做家務）通常不計。但自住物業的估算租金 (Imputed rent) 是特例，必須計入，以反映房屋提供的服務價值。
    Must have market value: Non-market activities (like doing your own housework) are generally excluded. However, imputed rent for owner-occupied properties is a special exception and is included to reflect the value of housing services.
  3. 不能是轉移支付 (No Transfer Payments): 沒有涉及生產新物品或服務的資金轉移（如獎學金、綜援、利是、養老金）不計算在內。
    Must not be transfer payments: Financial transfers where no new goods or services are produced in exchange (e.g., scholarships, welfare, red packets, pensions) are excluded.

✅ 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option)： A. 只有 (1)、(2) / (1) and (2) only

項目 (1)：本港某自住物業的估算租金價值 (Include)

計入。 雖然業主沒有實際繳交租金，但該物業正在提供「居住服務」。為了準確量度社會總產出，政府會估算該物業如果在市場上出租可獲得的租金，並將其計入 \( GDP \)（消費開支 \( C \) 的一部分）。

Included. Even though the owner does not pay actual rent, the property is currently providing "housing services". To accurately measure total output, the government estimates the rent the property would fetch in the market and includes it in \( GDP \) (as part of Consumption \( C \)).

項目 (2)：某地產經紀賣出二手物業所收取的佣金 (Include)

計入。 二手物業本身的價值不計入今年的 \( GDP \)，因為它在建成當年已經計算過。但是，地產經紀為了促成這宗二手交易所提供的「中介服務」是在今年發生的，屬於當前的生產，因此其佣金必須計入 \( GDP \)。

Included. The value of the second-hand property itself is excluded because it was counted in the year it was built. However, the "intermediary service" provided by the real estate agent to facilitate the sale happened this year. It is a current productive service, so the commission is included in \( GDP \).

項目 (3)：某大學生收取的獎學金 (Exclude)

不計入。 獎學金屬於「轉移支付」(Transfer Payment)。大學生獲取獎學金時，並沒有向社會提供任何等價的新物品或服務，這純粹是資金的轉移，因此不計入 \( GDP \)。

Excluded. A scholarship is a "Transfer Payment". When the university student receives the scholarship, they do not provide any equivalent new goods or services to society in return. It is purely a financial transfer, thus excluded from \( GDP \).

🔍 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解答此類題目的樞紐點 (Pivot Point) 在於分辨「是否有新的物品或服務被生產出來」。

  • 解題關鍵一： 看到「二手」(Second-hand)，立刻要區分是「物品本身的價值」還是「交易過程產生的服務費（如佣金）」。
  • 解題關鍵二： 看到「無償獲得的錢」（獎學金、津貼、禮物），立刻將其歸類為轉移支付並剔除。
  • 解題關鍵三： 牢記「估算租金」(Imputed rent) 是 \( GDP \) 計算中最重要的特例之一，逢見必入。
The Pivot Point for answering this type of question is determining "whether a new good or service was produced."
  • Key 1: When you see "second-hand", immediately distinguish between "the value of the good itself" (exclude) and "the service fee generated by the transaction, like commission" (include).
  • Key 2: When you see "money received without providing a service" (scholarships, allowances, gifts), classify it as a transfer payment and exclude it.
  • Key 3: Memorize that "imputed rent" is one of the most important exceptions in \( GDP \) calculation; it is always included.

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱一：看見「二手」就全盤否定 (The "Second-hand" Trap)
很多同學一看到「二手物業」四個字，就立刻判定不計入 \( GDP \)，而忽略了後面「收取的佣金」幾個字。請記住，舊物品的交易往往會衍生出新的服務（如經紀費、運輸費），這些新服務是計入 \( GDP \) 的。

陷阱二：誤將獎學金當作生產收入 (Confusing Scholarships with Production Income)
有些學生會認為「學生讀書也是一種付出」，所以獎學金是收入。但在經濟學上，讀書並不是向市場提供可供消費的物品或服務，因此不構成 \( GDP \) 定義下的生產活動。

Trap 1: Rejecting everything with the word "Second-hand"
Many students immediately rule out an option when they see "second-hand property," ignoring the words "commission received." Remember, the trading of old goods often generates new services (e.g., agent fees, transportation fees). These new services are included in \( GDP \).

Trap 2: Confusing Scholarships with Production Income
Some students think "studying is hard work," so a scholarship is earned income. However, in economics, studying does not provide a consumable good or service to the market, so it does not constitute a productive activity under the definition of \( GDP \).

🙋 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼政府一定要「估算」自住物業的租金？不計算不行嗎？

A: 假設社會上有 100 間屋，原本全部出租，這 100 間屋的租金會計入 \( GDP \)。如果第二年，所有租客把房屋買下來自住，房屋提供的居住服務沒有減少，但如果我們不計算自住租金，該年的 \( GDP \) 就會突然大幅下跌。為了避免 \( GDP \) 因為「租」與「買」的產權變化而產生扭曲，經濟學家必須估算這筆租金。

Q: Why must the government "estimate" rent for owner-occupied properties? Can't we just ignore it?

A: Imagine a society with 100 houses, all rented out. The rent of these 100 houses is included in \( GDP \). If next year, all tenants buy the houses they live in, the actual housing services provided haven't decreased. But if we don't count owner-occupied rent, \( GDP \) would suddenly drop massively. To prevent \( GDP \) from being distorted purely by the change in ownership status (renting vs. owning), economists must impute this rent.

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

「關鍵字秒殺法」(Keyword Elimination Method)：

在 DSE 經濟科 MC 卷中，處理 \( GDP \) 題目時可以依賴幾個高頻關鍵字來迅速篩選：

  • 見到 「佣金」 (Commission) 👉 秒速打勾 (✓)，必定計入。
  • 見到 「估算租金」 (Imputed rent) 👉 秒速打勾 (✓)，必定計入。
  • 見到 「綜援 / 獎學金 / 退休金 / 禮物」 (CSSA / Scholarship / Pension / Gift) 👉 屬於轉移支付，秒速打交叉 (✗)，必定不計入。

在本題中，(3) 是獎學金 (✗)，立刻可以刪除選項 B、C、D，直接得出答案 A！

Keyword Elimination Method:

In DSE Economics MC papers, you can rely on high-frequency keywords to quickly solve \( GDP \) questions:

  • See "Commission" 👉 Instantly tick (✓), it is always included.
  • See "Imputed rent" 👉 Instantly tick (✓), it is always included.
  • See "CSSA / Scholarship / Pension / Gift" 👉 These are transfer payments, instantly cross out (✗), never included.

In this question, since (3) is a scholarship (✗), you can immediately eliminate options B, C, and D, landing directly on Answer A!
