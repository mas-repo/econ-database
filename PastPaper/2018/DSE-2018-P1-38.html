1. The Question (題目)

假設某經濟中薪俸稅的基本免稅額下降了。下列哪項可以抵銷其對總需求的影響？

Suppose there is a reduction in the basic allowance of the salaries tax in an economy. Which of the following can offset its effect on aggregate demand?

A. 人們的儲蓄意慾增加。

A. People’s desire to save increases.

B. 物價水平下降。

B. The price level decreases.

C. 中央銀行於公開市場出售政府債券。

C. The central bank carries out an open market sale of government bonds.

D. 銀行體系的法定儲備比率下降。

D. The required reserve ratio in the banking system decreases.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 總需求 (Aggregate Demand, AD)

總需求是一個經濟體內對本地生產物品及服務的需求總和。其公式為：

Aggregate demand is the total demand for domestically produced goods and services in an economy. The formula is:

$$AD = C + I + G + NX$$

當中 \(C\) 是私人消費開支，\(I\) 是投資開支，\(G\) 是政府消費開支，\(NX\) 是淨出口。

Where \(C\) is private consumption expenditure, \(I\) is investment expenditure, \(G\) is government consumption expenditure, and \(NX\) is net exports.

2. 薪俸稅與可支配所得 (Salaries Tax and Disposable Income)

基本免稅額下降意味著市民需要繳交更多的薪俸稅。這會導致「可支配所得」(\(Y_d\)) 下降，從而令私人消費開支 (\(C\)) 減少，最終導致總需求 (\(AD\)) 下降（向左移）。

A reduction in the basic tax allowance means citizens pay more salaries tax. This lowers "disposable income" (\(Y_d\)), which reduces private consumption expenditure (\(C\)) and eventually leads to a decrease (leftward shift) in Aggregate Demand (\(AD\)).

3. 擴張性貨幣政策 (Expansionary Monetary Policy)

為了「抵銷」(\(AD\) 下降) 的影響，我們需要一項能令總需求「增加」的政策。降低法定儲備比率 (Required Reserve Ratio, \(RRR\)) 是一種擴張性貨幣政策，它會增加貨幣供應 (\(M_s\))，導致利率 (\(r\)) 下降，從而刺激投資 (\(I\)) 及消費 (\(C\))，令 \(AD\) 增加。

To "offset" the effect (a fall in \(AD\)), we need a policy that "increases" Aggregate Demand. Decreasing the Required Reserve Ratio (\(RRR\)) is an expansionary monetary policy. It increases the money supply (\(M_s\)), which lowers the interest rate (\(r\)), thereby stimulating investment (\(I\)) and consumption (\(C\)), causing \(AD\) to increase.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案：Option D / 選項 D

選項 A (錯誤 / Incorrect)

如果人們的儲蓄意慾增加，邊際儲蓄傾向 (MPS) 會上升，這代表他們會減少消費 (\(C\) 下降)。這會進一步令總需求 (\(AD\)) 減少，加劇了原來的影響，而不是抵銷它。

If people's desire to save increases, the marginal propensity to save (MPS) rises, meaning they will spend less (\(C\) decreases). This will further reduce Aggregate Demand (\(AD\)), worsening the original effect rather than offsetting it.

選項 B (錯誤 / Incorrect)

物價水平下降只會導致沿著同一條總需求曲線的移動 (Movement along the AD curve)，使需求量增加，但並不會使整條總需求曲線向右移動 (Shift in AD curve)。因此，它不能抵銷導致曲線左移的外部衝擊。

A decrease in the price level only causes a movement along the same AD curve (increasing the quantity of real GDP demanded), but it does not cause the entire AD curve to shift to the right. Therefore, it cannot offset the exogenous shock that shifted the curve to the left.

選項 C (錯誤 / Incorrect)

中央銀行在公開市場出售政府債券是一種緊縮性貨幣政策。這會減少貨幣供應，推高利率，從而減少投資 (\(I\)) 和消費 (\(C\))。這同樣會令 \(AD\) 進一步下降，無法達到抵銷的效果。

The central bank selling government bonds in the open market is a contractionary monetary policy. This reduces the money supply and pushes up interest rates, thereby reducing investment (\(I\)) and consumption (\(C\)). This would also cause \(AD\) to decrease further, failing to offset the effect.

選項 D (正確 / Correct)

降低法定儲備比率會擴大貨幣乘數效應，令貨幣供應增加。當貨幣供應增加，利率會下跌，借貸成本降低，從而刺激私人消費 (\(C\)) 和投資 (\(I\))。這會使總需求曲線向右移，完美抵銷了因加稅而導致 \(AD\) 下降的影響。

Decreasing the required reserve ratio expands the money multiplier effect, increasing the money supply. When money supply increases, interest rates fall, lowering borrowing costs, which stimulates private consumption (\(C\)) and investment (\(I\)). This shifts the AD curve to the right, perfectly offsetting the negative impact of the tax increase on \(AD\).

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

破題關鍵 (The Pivot Point): 題目的核心字眼是「抵銷」(Offset)。解題需分兩步：

The Pivot Point: The core keyword is "offset". Solving this requires two steps:

  1. 第一步：釐清原事件的影響。免稅額下降 = 稅款增加 = 消費 (\(C\)) 下降 = 總需求 (\(AD\)) 下跌。
    Step 1: Clarify the initial event. Lower allowance = higher tax = lower consumption (\(C\)) = Aggregate Demand (\(AD\)) DECREASES.
  2. 第二步：尋找相反的影響。「抵銷」代表我們需要在選項中找尋一個能令總需求 (\(AD\)) 上升的因素。
    Step 2: Find the opposite effect. "Offset" means we must find an option that causes Aggregate Demand (\(AD\)) to INCREASE.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 混淆「移動 (Shift)」與「沿曲線移動 (Movement along)」： 很多學生會誤選 B。記住，任何價格水平 (\(P\)) 的改變只會導致沿著曲線移動；要抵銷外在因素導致的整條曲線移動（Shift），必須依賴另一個能導致曲線移動的外在因素（如政府政策改變）。

    Confusing "Shift" with "Movement along": Many students wrongly choose B. Remember, a change in the price level (\(P\)) only causes a movement along the curve; to offset a shift in the curve caused by an exogenous factor, you must rely on another exogenous factor (like a policy change) that also shifts the curve.

  • 買賣債券方向錯誤： 在公開市場操作中，央行「買入 (Purchase)」債券是放水（增加貨幣供應），「賣出 (Sale)」債券是抽資（減少貨幣供應）。選項 C 是賣出，所以是緊縮政策。

    Confusing the direction of bond trading: In Open Market Operations, when the central bank "purchases" bonds, it injects liquidity (increases money supply). When it "sells" bonds, it drains liquidity (decreases money supply). Option C is a sale, so it's contractionary.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼薪俸稅免稅額下降，不直接看作政府收入增加，從而帶動政府開支 (G) 上升呢？

Q: Why don't we assume that a decrease in the tax allowance leads to higher government revenue, which then increases government spending (G)?

A: 在經濟學假設中，除非題目特別指明政府實行「平衡預算」並將新增稅收全數用於開支，否則我們必須獨立分析單一政策的直接影響。稅收變動直接影響的是市民的「可支配所得」和私人消費 (\(C\))。

A: In economic assumptions, unless the question explicitly states the government runs a "balanced budget" and spends all the extra tax revenue, we must analyze the direct impact of the single policy independently. A change in tax directly impacts citizens' "disposable income" and private consumption (\(C\)).

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

「箭頭消除法」(The Arrow Method):

The Arrow Method:

在試卷旁邊，用箭頭標示題目的最終效果，然後為每個選項標示箭頭。尋找方向相反的選項：

In the margin of your exam paper, draw an arrow for the ultimate effect of the question, and then draw an arrow for each option. Look for the opposite direction:

  • 題目：免稅額下降 \(\rightarrow\) 稅上升 \(\rightarrow\) \(AD \downarrow\) (目標：找 \(AD \uparrow\)) / Question: Tax \(\uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) \(AD \downarrow\) (Goal: Find \(AD \uparrow\))
  • A: 儲蓄 \(\uparrow \rightarrow C \downarrow \rightarrow\) \(AD \downarrow\) ❌
  • B: 物價下降 \(\rightarrow\) \(AD\) 曲線不變 (No shift) ❌
  • C: 賣債券 \(\rightarrow M_s \downarrow \rightarrow\) 利率 \(\uparrow \rightarrow\) \(AD \downarrow\) ❌
  • D: 法定儲備比率 \(\downarrow \rightarrow M_s \uparrow \rightarrow\) 利率 \(\downarrow \rightarrow\) \(AD \uparrow\) ✅
