1. 題目 (The Question)

索尼的PS4的銷售額為178億元，在環球遊戲機硬件市場的佔有率為51%。與其相比，微軟的Xbox One的銷售額為91億元，市場佔有率為26%。任天堂的3DS平台（及其他型號如2DS）則是遠遠落後的第三名，銷售額26億元，市場佔有率為8%。

上述環球遊戲機硬件市場的市場結構屬寡頭壟斷。這陳述

A. 是正確的，因為各公司在市場售賣異質物品。
B. 是正確的，市場有數家具支配力的公司。
C. 是不正確的，因為PS4只佔市場的51%。
D. 是不正確的，因為環球市場有眾多賣家。

Sony’s PS4 was responsible for $17.8 billion of sales value, or 51% share of the global console hardware market, compared with Microsoft’s Xbox One at $9.1 billion of sales value or a 26% market share. Nintendo’s 3DS platform (and its variants, such as the 2DS) was a distant third on the list with $2.6 billion of sales value or 8% share of the market.

The market structure of the above global console hardware market is oligopoly. This statement is

A. correct because the companies sell heterogeneous goods in the market.
B. correct because there are a few dominating companies in the market.
C. incorrect because the PS4 was only responsible for 51% of the market.
D. incorrect because there are a lot of sellers in the global market.

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

寡頭壟斷 (Oligopoly)

寡頭壟斷是指市場上只有少數幾個具支配力的賣家 (a few dominant sellers) 的市場結構。這些主要企業的市場佔有率加起來非常高（即高市場集中度）。因為賣家數量少，他們的決策會互相牽制 (Interdependence)。

Oligopoly is a market structure characterized by a few dominant sellers. The combined market share of these major firms is very high (i.e., high market concentration). Because there are only a few sellers, their decisions are highly interdependent.

特徵 (Characteristics):

  • 少數具支配力的賣家 (A few dominant sellers)
  • 物品可以是同質 (homogeneous) 或 異質 (heterogeneous)
  • 進入市場存在巨大障礙 (High barriers to entry)
  • 不完全資訊 (Imperfect information)

市場集中度 (Market Concentration)

經濟學上，我們會用集中率 (Concentration Ratio, \(CR\)) 來量度市場結構。它計算了市場上最大的 \(n\) 家公司的市場佔有率總和。

In economics, we use the Concentration Ratio (\(CR\)) to measure market structure. It calculates the sum of the market shares of the top \(n\) firms in the market.
$$ CR_n = \sum_{i=1}^{n} S_i $$

Where \(S_i\) is the market share of the \(i\)-th largest firm. In this question, the 3-firm concentration ratio \(CR_3\) is:
$$ CR_3 = 51\% + 26\% + 8\% = 85\% $$

這顯示極高的市場集中度，印證了這是寡頭壟斷市場。

This shows a very high market concentration, proving it is an oligopolistic market.

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): B

❌ 選項 A 分析 (Option A Analysis):

「各公司售賣異質物品」不是寡頭壟斷的決定性特徵。雖然遊戲機是異質物品，但壟斷性競爭 (Monopolistic Competition) 同樣售賣異質物品。寡頭壟斷也可以售賣同質物品（如石油）。因此，不能單憑「異質物品」來推斷市場是寡頭壟斷。

"Selling heterogeneous goods" is not the defining characteristic of an oligopoly. Although consoles are heterogeneous, monopolistic competition also features heterogeneous goods. Oligopolies can also sell homogeneous goods (e.g., crude oil). Therefore, you cannot deduce an oligopoly solely based on "heterogeneous goods".

✅ 選項 B 分析 (Option B Analysis):

這是正確的。題目數據顯示，首三間公司（索尼、微軟、任天堂）已經佔據了全球市場高達 85% (\(51\% + 26\% + 8\%\)) 的份額。這完美符合了寡頭壟斷的定義：市場由「數家具支配力的公司」控制。

This is correct. The data shows that the top three companies (Sony, Microsoft, Nintendo) account for a massive 85% (\(51\% + 26\% + 8\%\)) of the global market. This perfectly matches the definition of an oligopoly: the market is controlled by "a few dominating companies".

❌ 選項 C 分析 (Option C Analysis):

51% 的市場佔有率確實不足以構成絕對的獨佔 (Monopoly)，但這並不會使「寡頭壟斷」這個陳述變得不正確。作為市場領導者擁有51%份額，加上其他幾個主要競爭對手，正正構成了寡頭壟斷的局面。

A 51% market share is indeed not enough to constitute an absolute Monopoly, but this does not make the statement of "oligopoly" incorrect. Having a 51% share as a market leader, alongside a few other major competitors, forms exactly an oligopoly scenario.

❌ 選項 D 分析 (Option D Analysis):

即使環球市場上可能還有其他許多小賣家，但只要大部分的市場份額集中在「少數幾個」巨頭手中，這個市場依然會被歸類為寡頭壟斷，而不是完全競爭或壟斷性競爭。

Even if there might be many other small sellers in the global market, as long as the vast majority of the market share is concentrated in the hands of a "few" giants, the market is still classified as an oligopoly, not perfect or monopolistic competition.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

遇到市場結構的題目，我們需要像偵探一樣尋找線索：

  • 轉捩點 (Pivot Point): 留意題目提供的百分比 (Percentages)。這是解題的最關鍵資訊。當你看到三個數字加起來超過 80% 時，你就可以立刻鎖定這是「少數支配市場」的格局。
  • 干擾資訊 (Distractors): 題目中給出的具體銷售額金額（如 178億元、91億元）其實是干擾項。在判斷市場結構時，我們關心的是相對的市場力量（佔有率），而不是絕對的銷售金額。
When facing market structure questions, we need to look for clues like a detective:
  • Pivot Point: Pay attention to the percentages. This is the crucial key. When you see three numbers adding up to over 80%, you can immediately lock in that this is a "few dominating the market" scenario.
  • Distractors: The specific sales values given (like $17.8B, $9.1B) are actually distractors. When determining market structure, we care about relative market power (market share), not absolute sales figures.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • 誤解壟斷性競爭與寡頭壟斷的區別： 很多同學看到品牌不同（異質物品）就急著選「A」，這是非常危險的。異質物品不是區分這兩者的核心，賣家數量及其支配力才是。
  • 誤以為全球市場就是「眾多賣家」： 雖然是「全球市場」，這聽起來很大，似乎應該有無限個賣家（選項D的陷阱）。但實際上，生產遊戲主機的門檻極高，真正能在全球競爭的只有幾家巨頭。
  • Confusing Monopolistic Competition with Oligopoly: Many students rush to choose "A" when they see different brands (heterogeneous goods), which is very dangerous. Heterogeneous goods are not the core differentiator; the number of sellers and their dominance are.
  • Assuming global markets always mean "many sellers": "Global market" sounds huge, implying countless sellers (the trap in Option D). But in reality, the barrier to entry for producing consoles is extremely high, leaving only a few giants capable of competing globally.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

問 (Q)：索尼的市佔率超過了一半 (51%)，為什麼它不是「獨佔」(Monopoly)？
Q: Sony's market share is over half (51%). Why isn't it considered a Monopoly?

答 (A)：在經濟學中，獨佔 (Monopoly) 通常指的是市場上只有一個賣家，或者該賣家擁有壓倒性的市佔率（例如接近 90-100%），並且沒有相近的替代品。雖然 51% 很高，但微軟 (26%) 仍然是一個極具威脅的競爭對手。這兩家公司必須互相牽制，這正是寡頭壟斷的特徵。
A: In economics, a Monopoly typically refers to a market with only one seller, or a seller with an overwhelming market share (e.g., near 90-100%) and no close substitutes. While 51% is high, Microsoft (26%) remains a highly threatening competitor. These two firms must continuously react to each other (interdependence), which is the exact hallmark of an oligopoly.
