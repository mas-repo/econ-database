2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

商業擁有權類型 (Types of Business Ownership)

要解答這道題目，我們必須從特徵中辨認出廠商的擁有權類型：

To solve this question, we must identify the types of business ownership from their features:

  • 公眾有限公司 (Public Limited Company): 股份可自由轉讓（毋須同意），必須向公眾披露財務狀況，屬於獨立法人（Separate Legal Entity），股東享有有限責任。
  • Shares are freely transferable (no consent needed), mandatory public disclosure of financial accounts, is a separate legal entity, and shareholders enjoy limited liability.
  • 私人有限公司 (Private Limited Company): 股份轉讓受限制（通常需要其他股東同意），不需要向公眾披露財務狀況，屬於獨立法人，股東享有有限責任。
  • Transfer of shares is restricted (usually requires consent), no public disclosure required, is a separate legal entity, and shareholders enjoy limited liability.
  • 合夥 (Partnership): 擁有權轉讓需要其他合夥人同意，不需向公眾披露財務，不是獨立法人，普通合夥人須承擔無限債務責任。
  • Transfer of ownership requires consent of other partners, no public disclosure required, is NOT a separate legal entity, and general partners bear unlimited liability.

利潤與資本的經濟學定義 (Economic Definitions of Profit and Capital)

企業的利潤 \( \pi \) 與其擁有權結構沒有直接的數學關係。利潤取決於總收入 \( TR \) 和總成本 \( TC \)：

A firm's profit \( \pi \) has no direct mathematical relationship with its ownership structure. Profit depends on Total Revenue \( TR \) and Total Cost \( TC \):

$$ \pi = TR - TC = (P \times Q) - TC $$

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): Option D

錯誤 Wrong A. 廠商Y比廠商X賺取更高利潤。 (Firm Y earns a higher profit than Firm X.)

擁有權類型、擁有者數量或財務披露規定並不能決定一間公司的利潤。廠商X雖然只有2個擁有者，但可能是賺取暴利的大企業；而廠商Y可能是正在虧損的公司。

The type of ownership, number of owners, or financial disclosure requirements do not determine a company's profit. Firm X could be a highly profitable enterprise despite having only 2 owners, while Firm Y might be making a loss.

錯誤 Wrong B. 廠商Y所籌集的資本比廠商X多。 (Firm Y raises more capital than Firm X.)

雖然公眾有限公司（廠商Y）在市場上籌集資本的「能力」通常較高，但這不是絕對的。廠商X的兩位擁有者可能是極度富有的投資者，投入的資本絕對有機會多於由10名普通散戶組成的廠商Y。

Although public limited companies (Firm Y) generally have a higher "capacity" to raise capital, this is not an absolute rule. The two owners of Firm X could be extremely wealthy investors who injected more capital than the 10 retail investors in Firm Y combined.

錯誤 Wrong C. 兩家廠商的擁有者均享有有限債務責任。 (Owners of both firms enjoy limited liability.)

廠商Y是公眾有限公司，股東享有有限責任。但是，廠商X的特徵符合「私人有限公司」或「合夥」。如果廠商X是合夥（Partnership），其擁有者（合夥人）需承擔「無限債務責任」。因此，我們不能肯定兩者均享有有限責任。

Firm Y is a public limited company, so its shareholders enjoy limited liability. However, Firm X's features match either a "Private Limited Company" or a "Partnership". If Firm X is a partnership, its owners face "unlimited liability". Thus, we cannot be certain that both enjoy limited liability.

正確 Correct D. 廠商Y是獨立法人而廠商X可能不是。 (Firm Y is a legal entity while Firm X may not be.)

廠商Y毋須同意即可轉讓擁有權，並須公開財務，100%確定是「公眾有限公司」，因此必然是獨立法人（Legal Entity）。而廠商X轉讓需同意且不公開財務，它可能是「私人有限公司」（是獨立法人）或「合夥」（不是獨立法人）。因此，「廠商X可能不是獨立法人」這句描述是完美正確的。

Firm Y allows transfer without consent and requires public financial disclosure, making it 100% a "Public Limited Company", which is definitely a separate legal entity. Firm X restricts transfer and requires no public disclosure, meaning it could be a "Private Limited Company" (which is a legal entity) OR a "Partnership" (which is NOT a legal entity). Therefore, the statement "Firm X may not be a legal entity" is perfectly accurate.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point):
做這類題目的第一步，是立刻在草稿紙上寫下廠商X和Y可能對應的企業類型。
The first step in solving this type of question is to immediately write down the possible business types for Firm X and Firm Y on your scratchpad.

  • 廠商Y的關鍵詞：「毋須同意」、「需要披露」。這強烈指向公眾有限公司。
  • 廠商X的關鍵詞：「需要同意」、「不需要披露」。這可以對應私人有限公司，也可以對應合夥。

干擾資訊 (Distractors):
「擁有者數目」 (2 vs 10) 在這裡其實是干擾項。合夥可以是 2-20 人，私人有限公司可以是 1-50 人，公眾有限公司是 1 人或以上。所以 2 和 10 這個數字不能幫你精確排除任何類型！
The "Number of owners" (2 vs 10) is actually a distractor here. Partnerships can have 2-20 partners, private limited companies 1-50, and public limited companies 1 or more. So the numbers 2 and 10 do not help you eliminate any specific type accurately!

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • 過度推論 (Over-inferring): 學生常誤以為「上市公司（公眾有限公司）一定賺比較多錢」或「一定資本較大」。記住，經濟學選擇題中，除非有確實數據支持，否則不能假設「必然如此」。
    Students often mistakenly assume "listed companies (public limited companies) always earn more money" or "always have larger capital." Remember, in Economics MCQs, you cannot assume an "absolute truth" unless backed by hard data.
  • 忘記合夥的特徵 (Forgetting Partnership Features): 很多學生看到「需要同意」和「不公開財務」就立刻認定廠商X是私人有限公司，而忘記了「合夥」也完全符合這兩個條件。
    Many students see "consent required" and "no public disclosure" and instantly assume Firm X is a private limited company, completely forgetting that a "Partnership" also perfectly matches these two criteria.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 廠商 X 可以是獨資企業 (Sole Proprietorship) 嗎？

A: 不可以。因為表格清楚顯示廠商 X 有 2 個擁有者，而獨資企業顧名思義只有 1 個擁有者。

Q: Could Firm X be a Sole Proprietorship?
A: No. The table clearly shows Firm X has 2 owners, whereas a sole proprietorship, by definition, has exactly 1 owner.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

字眼測試法 (The Word Test Method):

在應付 DSE 經濟科多項選擇題時，留意選項中的「絕對字眼」與「可能字眼」：

When tackling DSE Economics MCQs, pay attention to "absolute words" versus "probability words" in the options:

  • 選項 A 和 B 使用了「更高」、「更多」(higher, more)。這類絕對的比較，在缺乏實際財務數據（如資產負債表）的情況下，通常是錯誤的陷阱。
  • 選項 D 使用了「可能不是」(may not be)。在資料不足以 100% 確定廠商 X 的具體類型時，這種保留可能性的陳述往往就是正確答案！
