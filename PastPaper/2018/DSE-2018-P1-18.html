1. 題目 (The Question)

參閱以下物品X的供需表。

Refer to the following supply and demand schedules for Good X.

單位價格 Unit price ($) 13121110987
需求量 Quantity demanded 708090100110120130
供應量 Quantity supplied 130120110100908070

假設政府將價格上限由$8提高至$12。下列哪項陳述是正確的？

Suppose the government increases the price ceiling from $8 to $12. Which of the following statements is correct?

A. 物品X會出現40單位的短缺。
There would be a shortage of 40 units of Good X.

B. 物品X會出現40單位的盈餘。
There would be a surplus of 40 units of Good X.

C. 對物品X的總支出會上升$360。
The total expenditure on Good X would increase by $360.

D. 對物品X的交易量會維持不變。
The quantity transacted of Good X would remain unchanged.

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

市場均衡 (Market Equilibrium)

當需求量等於供應量時，市場達到均衡。圖表中 \(\text{Q}_d = \text{Q}_s = 100\) 時，對應的均衡價格為 \(\text{P}_e = \$10\)。

Market equilibrium occurs when quantity demanded equals quantity supplied. In the table, when \(\text{Q}_d = \text{Q}_s = 100\), the equilibrium price is \(\text{P}_e = \$10\).

價格上限 (Price Ceiling)

政府法定的最高交易價格。只有設定在均衡價格（$10）之下才有效 (Effective)。若設定在均衡價格之上，則無效 (Ineffective)，市場會以均衡價格交易。

A legal maximum price. It is only effective if set BELOW the equilibrium price ($10). If set above, it is ineffective, and the market operates at the equilibrium price.

核心公式 (Key Formulas)

交易量 (Quantity Transacted): $$Q_t = \min(Q_d, Q_s)$$ 總開支 (Total Expenditure): $$TE = P \times Q_t$$ 短缺 (Shortage): $$\text{Shortage} = Q_d - Q_s \quad (\text{when } P < P_e)$$

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option)：Option C

選項 A (Option A) ✗ 錯誤 (Incorrect)

當價格上限為$8時，\(\text{Q}_d = 120\)，\(\text{Q}_s = 80\)，短缺確實是 \(120 - 80 = 40\) 單位。但是，當價格上限提高到$12時，由於$12高於均衡價格$10，上限變得無效。市場價格回復到$10，此時 \(\text{Q}_d = \text{Q}_s = 100\)，短缺為0單位。

When the price ceiling was $8, \(\text{Q}_d = 120\) and \(\text{Q}_s = 80\), so the shortage was indeed \(120 - 80 = 40\) units. However, when raised to $12 (which is above the $10 equilibrium), the ceiling becomes ineffective. The market price settles at $10, where \(\text{Q}_d = \text{Q}_s = 100\). The shortage is 0 units.

選項 B (Option B) ✗ 錯誤 (Incorrect)

價格上限只是限制「最高」收費，不會強迫市場一定要以$12交易。由於均衡價格$10沒有超過$12的限制，市場會以$10交易，因此不會出現盈餘。若這是價格下限 (Price Floor)，才會出現盈餘。

A price ceiling only dictates the maximum price; it does not force the market to trade at $12. Since the equilibrium price of $10 does not violate the $12 limit, the market trades at $10. Thus, there is no surplus. A surplus would only occur if this were a price floor.

選項 C (Option C) ✓ 正確 (Correct)

原本情況 ($8 上限)： 價格被壓在$8，此時 \(\text{Q}_d=120\)，\(\text{Q}_s=80\)。交易量 \(Q_{t1} = \min(120, 80) = 80\)。
初始總支出 \(TE_1 = \$8 \times 80 = \$640\)。
新情況 ($12 上限)： 上限無效，市場以均衡價$10交易，交易量 \(Q_{t2} = 100\)。
新總支出 \(TE_2 = \$10 \times 100 = \$1000\)。
總支出改變 = \(\$1000 - \$640 = +\$360\)。

Original state ($8 ceiling): Price is constrained at $8. \(\text{Q}_d=120\), \(\text{Q}_s=80\). Transacted quantity \(Q_{t1} = \min(120, 80) = 80\).
Initial TE \(= \$8 \times 80 = \$640\).
New state ($12 ceiling): Ceiling is ineffective. Market trades at equilibrium $10. Transacted quantity \(Q_{t2} = 100\).
New TE \(= \$10 \times 100 = \$1000\).
Change in TE \(= \$1000 - \$640 = +\$360\).

選項 D (Option D) ✗ 錯誤 (Incorrect)

如上述計算，交易量由原本的80單位，上升至均衡狀態下的100單位，因此交易量改變了。

As calculated above, the quantity transacted increases from 80 units to the equilibrium quantity of 100 units. It does not remain unchanged.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

這類題目的破題關鍵 (Pivot Point) 在於「找出市場均衡點」並判斷「管制是否有效」。

  • 找出隱藏基準： 題目沒有直接給出均衡價格，你要從表格中找出 \(\text{Q}_d = \text{Q}_s\) 的行數（即 \(P=\$10\)）。這是所有推論的基礎。
  • 干擾資訊 (Distractors)： 選項A的40單位短缺是舊管制($8)下的數據，目的是騙那些不重新計算新管制狀態的考生。表格中$13、$12、$11、$9、$7的供需數據也只是用來讓你判斷短缺或盈餘的干擾項。

The Pivot Point of this question type is to "identify the market equilibrium" and "determine if the intervention is effective". You must first find where \(\text{Q}_d = \text{Q}_s\) (\(P=\$10\)). Option A's shortage of 40 is a trap based on the old ceiling.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

❌ 陷阱 1：誤以為市場價格一定等於法定價格
許多同學看到「價格上限提高至$12」，就直接假設市場價格變成$12，從而計算出 $12 \(\times 80 = 960\) 的總開支，或得出40單位盈餘（誤選B）。記住：上限只是「天花板」，如果均衡價在天花板之下，天花板就毫無作用！

❌ Trap 1: Assuming Market Price = Legal Price. Many students see the ceiling raised to $12 and assume the transaction price becomes $12. Remember: a ceiling is just a maximum. If the equilibrium price is lower, the ceiling has no effect!

❌ 陷阱 2：用「需求量」計算總開支
在$8時，同學可能會用 \(\$8 \times 120 \text{ (需求量)}\)。緊記：買賣必須雙方同意，當供應只有80時，最多只能成交80件。公式必須是 \(P \times \min(Q_d, Q_s)\)。

❌ Trap 2: Using Quantity Demanded for TE. At $8, some calculate TE as \(\$8 \times 120\). Remember: trades require both a buyer and a seller. If supply is only 80, only 80 units are transacted. Always use \(\min(Q_d, Q_s)\).

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 如果政府設定的是「價格下限 (Price Floor)」$12，答案會變成什麼？
A: 價格下限規定「最低」收費。由於$12高於均衡價格$10，此下限會有效。價格將被迫停留在$12，此時 \(\text{Q}_d = 80\)，\(\text{Q}_s = 120\)。市場會出現 \(120 - 80 = 40\) 單位的盈餘 (Surplus)。那選項B就會是正確答案了！
Q: What if the government set a "Price Floor" at $12 instead?
A: A price floor sets a "minimum" price. Since $12 is above the equilibrium $10, it would be effective. The price would be forced to $12, where \(\text{Q}_d = 80\) and \(\text{Q}_s = 120\). There would be a surplus of 40 units. In that case, Option B would be correct!

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

「先找均衡，再判斷」法則 (Find Equilibrium First Rule):

  1. 做任何市場干預題目，第一秒先在表格/圖上圈出均衡點 (\(P_e = 10, Q_e = 100\))。
  2. 比較干預價格與均衡價格。
  3. 速記口訣：
    「上限在下才有效」 (Ceiling must be below to bite).
    「下限在上才有效」 (Floor must be above to bite).
  4. 這題 $12 上限 > $10 均衡，秒速判斷「無效，直接用$10計算」。這樣就不會跌入選項A和B的陷阱。

Shortcut: Always circle the equilibrium first (\(P_e=10\)). Memorize: "Ceilings bite below, Floors bite above." Since the $12 ceiling is above $10, you instantly know it's ineffective and the market uses the $10 price. This immediately eliminates options A and B.
