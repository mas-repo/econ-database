Topic: Basic Economic Questions (基本經濟問題) Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆
Key Concepts: What to produce (生產什麼), How to produce (如何生產), For whom to produce (為誰生產), Resource Allocation (資源分配)

2. The Question (題目)

[中文] 行政長官提議增加教育經常開支五十億元。其中一項提案是向修讀自資課程的合資格中學畢業生提供30,000元補貼。以上提案與經濟學中的 _______ 問題有關。

A. 「如何生產」
B. 「為誰生產」
C. 「生產什麼」和「如何生產」
D. 「生產什麼」和「為誰生產」

[English] The Chief Executive proposed to raise the recurrent education spending by $5 billion. One of her proposals was to provide a $30,000 subsidy to eligible secondary school graduates studying self-financing programmes. The above proposal is related to the __________ question(s) in Economics.

A. ‘how to produce’
B. ‘for whom to produce’
C. ‘what to produce’ and ‘how to produce’
D. ‘what to produce’ and ‘for whom to produce’

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

由於資源存在稀少性 (Scarcity)，社會必須做出選擇，從而衍生出三個基本經濟問題。這三個問題決定了社會如何分配資源。

Due to scarcity, society must make choices, which gives rise to three basic economic questions. These questions determine how a society allocates its resources.

  • 1. 生產什麼 (What to produce): 決定生產哪種物品和服務，以及生產的數量。例如：政府決定將資金投入教育還是醫療。
    Determines the types and quantities of goods and services to be produced. E.g., The government deciding whether to allocate funds to education or healthcare.
  • 2. 如何生產 (How to produce): 決定使用哪種生產方法和資源組合。這涉及勞力與資本的比例。在經濟學中，我們可以用生產函數來表達產量 \( Q \) 與勞力 \( L \) 和資本 \( K \) 的關係：
    Determines the methods of production and the combination of resources used. This involves the ratio of labor to capital. In economics, we can express the relationship between output \( Q \), labor \( L \), and capital \( K \) using a production function:
    $$ Q = f(L, K) $$
  • 3. 為誰生產 (For whom to produce): 決定誰能獲得這些生產出來的物品和服務，即資源的分配準則（例如：按價格分配、先到先得、或按特定資格分配）。
    Determines who will receive the produced goods and services; this is the criteria for distributing resources (e.g., price mechanism, first-come-first-served, or specific eligibility).

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Answer: Option D / 正確答案：選項 D

分析： 題目包含兩個重要部分。第一，「增加教育經常開支五十億元」代表政府決定將更多社會資源分配給「教育服務」這種產品，因此這涉及「生產什麼」。第二，「向合資格中學畢業生提供補貼」設立了獲得該資源的標準（特定資格的學生才能受惠），這決定了誰能獲得這些教育服務，因此涉及「為誰生產」。

Analysis: The premise contains two crucial parts. First, "raise recurrent education spending by $5 billion" means the government has decided to allocate more social resources to the "product" of education services, which addresses 'what to produce'. Second, "provide a subsidy to eligible secondary school graduates" establishes the criteria for receiving this resource (only students with specific qualifications benefit), which determines who gets these educational services, thus addressing 'for whom to produce'.

選項 A (Option A): 錯誤。題目沒有提及用什麼方法來提供教育（例如：聘請更多老師還是使用更多電腦教學），因此不涉及「如何生產」。
Incorrect. The question does not mention the method of providing education (e.g., hiring more teachers vs. using more computer-aided teaching), so it does not involve 'how to produce'.
選項 B (Option B): 錯誤。雖然包含了「為誰生產」，但忽略了政府決定把50億元投入教育這一「生產什麼」的決定。答案不完整。
Incorrect. Although it includes 'for whom to produce', it ignores the government's decision to allocate $5 billion to education, which is a 'what to produce' decision. The answer is incomplete.
選項 C (Option C): 錯誤。如同選項A的分析，題目完全沒有提及「如何生產」的具體方法。
Incorrect. As analyzed in Option A, the question does not mention any specific methods regarding 'how to produce'.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

處理這類題目時，我們需要將題目的情境拆解成獨立的經濟行動，並逐一對應三個基本問題：

When tackling this type of question, we need to break down the scenario into independent economic actions and map them to the three basic questions:

  • Pivot Point 1 (關鍵點 1): "raise recurrent education spending" (增加教育開支) = 決定生產物品的種類 (Types of goods) \(\rightarrow\) What to produce.
  • Pivot Point 2 (關鍵點 2): "eligible secondary school graduates" (合資格中學畢業生) = 決定分配準則/受惠對象 (Criteria / Target group) \(\rightarrow\) For whom to produce.
  • Distractors (干擾資訊): "$5 billion" (五十億元) 和 "$30,000" (30,000元)。這些具體數字只是為了讓題目看起來更真實，對判斷屬於哪一個經濟問題沒有實質影響。
    The specific numbers "$5 billion" and "$30,000" are just distractor information to make the scenario look realistic; they have no actual impact on determining which economic question is involved.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：誤解「如何生產」的定義
很多學生會誤以為「政府透過提供補貼來支持教育」是一種「方法」，因此錯誤選擇「如何生產」。請記住，經濟學中的「如何生產」嚴格指生產要素的組合（勞力與資本）。派發金錢或補貼是資源分配的機制，不是工廠或學校裡實際製造產品或服務的技術方法。

Trap: Misunderstanding the definition of 'how to produce'
Many students mistakenly think that "the government supporting education by providing a subsidy" is a "method," leading them to wrongly choose 'how to produce'. Remember, 'how to produce' in economics strictly refers to the combination of factors of production (labor vs. capital). Distributing money or subsidies is a mechanism of resource allocation, not a technical method of actually creating the product or service in a factory or school.

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果題目改成「政府決定增加撥款，讓學校能購買更多人工智能教學軟件，以減少對教師的依賴」，答案會是什麼？

Q: If the question changed to "The government decided to increase funding so schools can buy more AI teaching software to reduce reliance on teachers", what would the answer be?

A: 這樣答案就會包含「如何生產」！因為「減少教師（勞力）」並「增加AI軟件（資本）」正正改變了生產教育服務的資源組合方法。

A: In that case, the answer WOULD include 'how to produce'! This is because "reducing teachers (labor)" and "increasing AI software (capital)" exactly changes the combination of resources used to produce educational services.

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

在DSE考試中，你可以用以下關鍵字快速鎖定答案 (Keyword Matching)：

In the DSE exam, you can use the following keyword matching to lock in your answers quickly:

  • 看到 「預算分配給某行業 / 生產某種新產品」 \(\rightarrow\) 秒選 「生產什麼」 (What)
  • 看到 「勞工 intensive / 機器 intensive / 新技術」 \(\rightarrow\) 秒選 「如何生產」 (How)
  • 看到 「合資格 / 低收入人士 / 抽籤 / 派發給特定人群」 \(\rightarrow\) 秒選 「為誰生產」 (For Whom)
