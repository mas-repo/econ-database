HKDSE Economics Tutor Analysis

主題 (Topic): 國際貿易與國際收支平衡 (International Trade and Balance of Payments)

難度 (Difficulty): ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5 - 包含數學陷阱 Contains mathematical traps)

關鍵字 (Key Concepts): 貿易盈餘 (Trade Surplus), 增長率 (Growth Rate), 價值與數量 (Value vs. Volume), 百分比與絕對值 (Percentage vs. Absolute Change)

題目 (The Question)

下圖顯示甲國去年第二季的一些貿易數據。甲國在該期間錄得貿易盈餘。根據已有的資料，我們可得出結論：去年第二季，

A. 貿易差額出現赤字。
B. 進口物品的數量的增長比出口物品的快。
C. 有形貿易盈餘下跌。
D. 經常帳的赤字有所增加。

The diagram below shows some trade data of Country A in the second quarter last year. Country A reported a trade surplus during that period. Based on the given information, we can conclude that during the second quarter last year,

A. the trade balance was in deficit.
B. the volume of imported goods increased faster than that of exported goods.
C. the visible trade surplus decreased.
D. the current account deficit increased.

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

1. 貿易餘額 (Trade Balance)

有形貿易差額是指一個國家出口貨物總值與進口貨物總值的差額。

The visible trade balance is the difference between a country's total value of exported goods and total value of imported goods.
$$ \text{Trade Balance} = \text{Value of Exports } (X) - \text{Value of Imports } (M) $$

如果 \(X > M\)，則存在貿易盈餘 (Trade Surplus)。如果 \(X < M\)，則存在貿易赤字 (Trade Deficit)。

If \(X > M\), there is a Trade Surplus. If \(X < M\), there is a Trade Deficit.

2. 價值與數量 (Value vs. Volume)

總值是價格與數量的乘積。我們不能單憑總值的變動來推斷數量的變動，除非我們知道價格的變動。

Value is the product of price and quantity (volume). We cannot deduce the change in volume solely from the change in value unless we know the change in price.
$$ \text{Value } (V) = \text{Price } (P) \times \text{Quantity } (Q) $$

答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

⚠️ 注意 (Attention): 無正確答案 (No Correct Option)Flawed Question

這是一條被廢棄的「問題題目」。出題者原意是 選項 C，但他犯了一個常見的數學邏輯錯誤：混淆了「增長百分率」與「絕對增長額」。以下將為你詳細拆解。

This is a discarded "flawed question." The setter's intended answer was Option C, but they made a common mathematical logic error: confusing "percentage growth rate" with "absolute growth amount". We will break this down below.
選項 A: 貿易差額出現赤字 / the trade balance was in deficit. (❌ 錯誤 Incorrect)

題目已經明確指出「甲國在該期間錄得貿易盈餘」。這與赤字是互相矛盾的。即使盈餘減少，只要 \(X > M\)，它仍然是盈餘。

The question explicitly states "Country A reported a trade surplus during that period." This directly contradicts a deficit. Even if the surplus shrinks, as long as \(X > M\), it remains a surplus.
選項 B: 進口物品的數量的增長比出口物品的快 / the volume of imported goods increased faster than that of exported goods. (❌ 錯誤 Incorrect)

圖表顯示的是「總值 (Value)」的增長率，而不是「數量 (Volume/Quantity)」。由於我們缺乏進出口價格 \(P\) 變化的數據，我們無法透過 \(V = P \times Q\) 來推斷 \(Q\) 的變化。

The graph shows the growth rate of "Value", not "Volume/Quantity". Since we lack data on the changes in import and export prices \(P\), we cannot deduce the change in \(Q\) using \(V = P \times Q\).
選項 C: 有形貿易盈餘下跌 / the visible trade surplus decreased. (❌ 出題者本意，但邏輯錯誤 Setter's intended answer, but logically flawed)

圖表顯示進口總值增長率（約 20%-28%）高於出口總值增長率（約 8%-11%）。出題者錯誤地認為：進口增長得「快」，所以盈餘必然「縮窄」。

錯誤在哪裡？ 我們只知道增長百分比，不知道基數 (Base value)！

$$ \Delta \text{Surplus} = \Delta X - \Delta M = (X \times \%\Delta X) - (M \times \%\Delta M) $$

假設出口基數極大：出口 \(X = 1000\)，進口 \(M = 10\)（存在龐大盈餘）。
如果出口增加 10% (\(\Delta X = 100\))，進口增加 20% (\(\Delta M = 2\))。
雖然進口增長率(20%) > 出口增長率(10%)，但絕對增長額 \(\Delta X\) (100) > \(\Delta M\) (2)。
結果：貿易盈餘實際上增加了 98！

因為我們不知道 \(X\) 和 \(M\) 的確實數字，我們不能斷言盈餘是上升還是下跌。所以此選項無法被確定為絕對正確。

The graph shows the import value growth rate (~20-28%) is higher than the export value growth rate (~8-11%). The setter mistakenly thought: since imports are growing "faster", the surplus must "shrink".
Where is the flaw? We only know the percentage growth, not the base values!
Suppose the export base is huge: Exports \(X = 1000\), Imports \(M = 10\) (huge surplus).
If exports increase by 10% (\(\Delta X = 100\)) and imports increase by 20% (\(\Delta M = 2\)).
Although import growth rate (20%) > export growth rate (10%), the absolute increase \(\Delta X\) (100) > \(\Delta M\) (2).
Result: The trade surplus actually INCREASES by 98!
Because we do not know the actual figures of \(X\) and \(M\), we cannot conclude whether the surplus increased or decreased. Thus, this option cannot be definitively true.
選項 D: 經常帳的赤字有所增加 / the current account deficit increased. (❌ 錯誤 Incorrect)

首先，題目指明有「貿易盈餘」。第二，經常帳除了有形貿易，還包括無形貿易（服務）、初級收入和次級收入。我們完全沒有這些項目的資料，無法推斷整個經常帳的狀況。

First, the question states there is a "trade surplus". Second, the current account includes invisible trade (services), primary income, and secondary income, alongside visible trade. We have absolutely no data on these other components, making it impossible to deduce the state of the current account.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

這類題目的「核心考點 (Pivot Point)」通常是測試學生能否區分不同經濟變量。圖表看似複雜，但你只需要提取兩個資訊：1. 兩條線都是正數（都在增長）。 2. 進口增長率線高於出口增長率線。

然而，這題的最大陷阱在於考評局出題者的盲點。遇到這種題目，如果必須硬選一個「最佳（或最符合出題者思路）」的答案，學生過往只能被迫選擇 C，但現今的 DSE 審題已較嚴格，這類數學不嚴謹的題目多會被直接剔除。

The "Pivot Point" of such questions is usually testing if students can distinguish between different economic variables. The graph looks complex, but you only need to extract two pieces of info: 1. Both lines are positive (both are growing). 2. The import growth rate line is above the export growth rate line.
However, the biggest trap here is the setter's blind spot. If forced to choose the "best (or setter's intended)" answer in the past, students would have to pick C. But modern DSE moderation is stricter, and mathematically unrigorous questions like this are usually discarded.

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • 混淆總值與數量 (Confusing Value and Volume): 見到「總值 (Value)」上升，就以為「數量 (Volume/Quantity)」一定上升。忘記了價格 \(P\) 的因素。
  • 混淆百分比與絕對值 (Confusing Percentages and Absolute Values): 以為「增長率高」就等於「增加的數額大」。必須時刻記住：\(\text{絕對改變} = \text{基數} \times \text{百分比改變}\)。
  • Confusing Percentages and Absolute Values: Assuming a "higher growth rate" means a "larger absolute increase amount". Always remember: \(\text{Absolute Change} = \text{Base Value} \times \text{Percentage Change}\).

學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 如果題目修改成「已知去年的出口與進口總值相若」，那選項 C 就可以選了嗎？

A: 可以。 如果 \(X\) 和 \(M\) 的基數相若 (即 \(X \approx M\))，那麼較高的進口增長率 (\(\%\Delta M > \%\Delta X\)) 就會無可避免地導致進口的絕對增長額大於出口的絕對增長額 (\(\Delta M > \Delta X\))。在這種情況下，貿易盈餘 (\(X - M\)) 就必定會下跌。這正是出題者腦海中假設了（但忘記寫出來）的隱藏條件！

Q: If the question was modified to "Given the total value of exports and imports were similar last year", would Option C be correct?

A: Yes. If the bases of \(X\) and \(M\) are similar (\(X \approx M\)), then a higher import growth rate (\(\%\Delta M > \%\Delta X\)) would inevitably lead to a larger absolute increase in imports than in exports (\(\Delta M > \Delta X\)). In this specific case, the trade surplus (\(X - M\)) would definitely decrease. This is exactly the hidden assumption the setter had in mind but forgot to explicitly state!

考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

在 DSE 經濟科中，當你看到百分率 (Percentage) / 增長率 (Growth Rate) 的圖表時，你的腦海應該立刻響起警號：「我有沒有基數 (Base)？」 如果沒有提供基數，所有關於「絕對差額 (Absolute difference)」(如盈餘縮窄、赤字擴大) 的推論通常都是錯誤或不確定的。用這個秒殺技巧，你可以瞬間排除很多選項！

In DSE Economics, whenever you see a graph showing Percentages or Growth Rates, alarm bells should immediately ring in your head: "Do I have the Base values?" If no base values are provided, any deduction regarding "Absolute differences" (like shrinking surplus or expanding deficit) is usually wrong or uncertain. Using this shortcut, you can instantly eliminate many distractor options!
