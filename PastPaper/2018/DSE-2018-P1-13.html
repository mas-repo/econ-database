Economics MCQ Guide: Types of Demand

經濟學選擇題指南：需求種類
Topic: Demand & Supply (需求與供應) Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆ (2/5) Key Concept: Derived Demand (引伸需求)

📝 The Question (題目)

參考上圖，農具的價格會

  1. 上升，因為農作物和農具屬聯合需求。
  2. 下降，因為農作物和農具屬聯合需求。
  3. 上升，因為農作物的需求衍生出對農具的引伸需求。
  4. 下降，因為農作物的需求衍生出對農具的引伸需求。

Refer to the above diagram. The price of farming tools will

  1. rise because farm products and farming tools are in joint demand.
  2. fall because farm products and farming tools are in joint demand.
  3. rise because demand for farm products creates a derived demand for farming tools.
  4. fall because demand for farm products creates a derived demand for farming tools.

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

To solve this question, we must distinguish between two key relationships in Economics: Joint Demand and Derived Demand.

要解答這道題目，我們必須區分經濟學中兩個重要的關係：聯合需求和引伸需求。

1. Derived Demand (引伸需求)

當我們對某種產品（最終物品）有需求時，會因此帶動對生產該產品的「生產要素」（例如勞力、機器、原料）的需求。農具是用來生產農作物的資本（Capital），因此對農具的需求是由對農作物的需求「引伸」出來的。

When there is a demand for a final good, it creates a demand for the "factors of production" (e.g., labor, machinery, raw materials) used to produce it. Farming tools are capital used to produce farm products, so the demand for farming tools is "derived" from the demand for farm products.

2. Joint Demand (聯合需求 / 輔助品)

兩種物品必須一起「消費」才能滿足同一個慾望，例如汽車與汽油、咖啡與糖。這適用於消費品（Consumer goods），而非生產要素。

Two goods that must be "consumed" together to satisfy a single want, such as cars and petrol, or coffee and sugar. This applies to consumer goods, not factors of production.

3. Market Equilibrium Shift (市場均衡的變動)

根據題目圖表，農作物的需求曲線向右移（$D_1 \rightarrow D_2$），代表農作物的需求增加。這會導致對農具的引伸需求同時增加。當需求增加，價格就會上升。

According to the graph, the demand curve for farm products shifts to the right ($D_1 \rightarrow D_2$), representing an increase in demand. This leads to an increase in the derived demand for farming tools. When demand increases, price rises.
$$ \text{Demand for Farm Products} \uparrow \implies \text{Derived Demand for Farming Tools} \uparrow \implies \text{Price of Farming Tools} \uparrow $$

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

The correct option is Option C (選項 C).

❌ Option A & B (選項 A 及 B)

錯誤。農作物和農具並不是一起被消費者消費的物品（不是輔助品）。農具是生產農作物的工具，因此它們之間不是聯合需求關係。

Incorrect. Farm products and farming tools are not consumed together by consumers (they are not complements). Farming tools are instruments used to produce farm products, so they are not in joint demand.
✅ Option C (選項 C)

正確。圖中顯示農作物的需求增加。因為農具是生產農作物的要素，農作物需求的增加會衍生出對農具的引伸需求（引伸需求增加）。在供應不變的情況下，農具需求增加會導致其價格上升。

Correct. The diagram shows an increase in demand for farm products. Since farming tools are factors of production for crops, an increase in demand for farm products creates a higher derived demand for farming tools. Assuming supply is constant, an increase in demand leads to a higher price for farming tools.
❌ Option D (選項 D)

錯誤。雖然前半部的概念（引伸需求）正確，但當對農具的引伸需求增加時，農具的價格應該是「上升」而非「下降」。

Incorrect. Although the concept in the second half (derived demand) is correct, an increase in derived demand for farming tools will cause their price to "rise", not "fall".

🧩 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目通常包含兩個判斷點（Pivot Points）：

This type of question usually contains two Pivot Points for judgment:
  1. 物品之間的關係 (Relationship between goods):
    問自己：這兩件物品是「一起用來享受」的（聯合需求），還是「用 A 來製造 B」的（引伸需求）？判斷出是「生產關係」後，立刻可以剔除選項 A 和 B。
    Ask yourself: Are these two goods "consumed together for enjoyment" (Joint Demand), or is one "used to produce the other" (Derived Demand)? Once you identify it's a "production relationship", you can immediately eliminate options A and B.
  2. 價格的變動方向 (Direction of Price Change):
    觀察圖表，箭頭向右代表需求增加（$D \uparrow$）。需求增加必定導致價格上升（$P \uparrow$）。因此，可以剔除說價格會下降的選項 D。
    Observe the graph. The rightward arrow indicates an increase in demand ($D \uparrow$). An increase in demand necessarily leads to an increase in price ($P \uparrow$). Therefore, you can eliminate option D, which states the price will fall.

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • Confusing Complements with Factors of Production (混淆輔助品與生產要素): 學生常以為「農夫需要農具來處理農作物」，就誤以為這是一種「聯合需求」。緊記，聯合需求專指消費者層面的消耗（如牙膏與牙刷），而生產層面的投入必定是引伸需求。

🙋 Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果圖表顯示的是農作物的「供應」向右移（供應增加），農具的價格會怎樣改變？

Q: What if the diagram showed a rightward shift in the "Supply" of farm products (increase in supply)? How would the price of farming tools change?

A: 好問題！如果農作物供應增加，通常意味著生產規模擴大了（例如科技進步或良好天氣）。為了生產更多的農作物，農夫在生產過程中依然需要使用更多的農具，這同樣會增加對農具的引伸需求，使農具價格有上升壓力。不過，DSE 考題通常會像本題一樣，用最終物品的「需求」變動來直接連繫引伸需求，邏輯最為直接。

A: Great question! If the supply of farm products increases, it usually means production has expanded. To produce more, farmers would still require more farming tools, which would also increase the derived demand for tools, putting upward pressure on their price. However, DSE questions usually link the change in "demand" of the final good directly to derived demand, as the logic is most straightforward.

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

2-Second Rule (兩秒法則):

在 DSE 經濟科中，只要看到「原材料 (Raw materials)」、「勞工 (Labour)」或「機器/工具 (Machinery/Tools)」，立刻在腦海中關聯 Derived Demand (引伸需求)。這能讓你瞬間淘汰一半的錯誤選項！然後看圖表箭頭，向右 = 升，向左 = 跌。答案呼之欲出。

In DSE Economics, whenever you see "Raw materials", "Labour", or "Machinery/Tools", immediately associate them with Derived Demand in your mind. This lets you instantly eliminate half of the wrong options! Then, look at the arrow in the diagram: Right = Rise, Left = Fall. The answer becomes obvious.
