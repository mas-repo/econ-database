課題 (Topic): 貨幣與銀行：香港的三級銀行體制 (Money and Banking: Three-tier Banking System in Hong Kong)
難度 (Difficulty): ★★★☆☆
核心概念 (Key Concepts): 認可機構 (Authorized Institutions), 存款最低限額 (Minimum Deposit Requirement), 存款期 (Maturity Term)

1. 題目 (The Question)

拜仁想在香港的認可機構以可轉讓存款證的方式存入80 000元，為期兩個月。下列哪項陳述是正確的？

Brian wants to deposit $80 000 for two months in an authorised institution in Hong Kong in the form of a negotiable certificate of deposit. Which of the following statements is correct?

A. 由於存款期長於一個月，接受存款公司可以接納其存款。

A. As the term of maturity is longer than one month, a deposit-taking company can accept his deposit.

B. 由於存款期短於三個月，有限制牌照銀行可以接納其存款。

B. As the term of maturity is shorter than three months, a restricted licence bank can accept his deposit.

C. 由於存款額少於500 000元，有限制牌照銀行不可以接納其存款。

C. As the amount of deposit is less than $500 000, a restricted licence bank cannot accept his deposit.

D. 由於持牌銀行沒有發售可轉讓存款證，持牌銀行不可以接納其存款。

D. As negotiable certificate of deposits are not available in a licensed bank, a licensed bank cannot accept his deposit.

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

香港實行三級銀行體制，所有接受存款的機構統稱為「認可機構」(Authorized Institutions, AIs)。它們在可接受的最低存款額和最短存款期上有不同的法定限制：

Hong Kong operates a Three-tier Banking System. All institutions accepting deposits are collectively known as Authorized Institutions (AIs). They have different legal restrictions regarding minimum deposit amounts and maturity terms:

機構類別
(Institution Type)		 最低存款額
(Min. Deposit)		 最短存款期
(Min. Maturity)
持牌銀行 (Licensed Banks, LB) 沒有限制 (No restriction) 沒有限制 (No restriction)
有限制牌照銀行 (Restricted Licence Banks, RLB) $500,000 沒有限制 (No restriction)
接受存款公司 (Deposit-Taking Companies, DTC) $100,000 3個月 (3 months)

定義：可轉讓存款證 (Negotiable Certificate of Deposit, NCD)
這是一種由銀行或金融機構發行的存款證明。與一般定期存款不同，它可以在到期前於二手市場買賣。持牌銀行 (LB) 絕對可以發行此類金融工具。認可機構發行的 NCD 亦是計算香港貨幣供應量 (Money Supply) 的重要部分：

Definition: Negotiable Certificate of Deposit (NCD)
A certificate issued by a bank or financial institution indicating a specific deposit. Unlike a normal time deposit, it can be traded in the secondary market before maturity. Licensed Banks (LBs) can absolutely issue these instruments. NCDs issued by AIs are also a crucial part of calculating Hong Kong's Money Supply:

$$ M2 = M1 + \text{Savings & Time Deposits in LBs} + \text{NCDs issued by LBs held by public} $$ $$ M3 = M2 + \text{Time Deposits in RLBs & DTCs} + \text{NCDs issued by RLBs & DTCs} $$

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option)：C

分析 A (錯誤)：接受存款公司 (DTC) 要求的最低存款額為 $100,000，且存款期最少為 3 個月。拜仁的存款只有 $80,000 且為期 2 個月，兩項條件均不符合。

Analysis A (Incorrect): A Deposit-Taking Company (DTC) requires a minimum deposit of $100,000 and a minimum maturity of 3 months. Brian's deposit is only $80,000 for 2 months, failing both conditions.

分析 B (錯誤)：有限制牌照銀行 (RLB) 雖然沒有存款期的限制（短於三個月也沒有問題），但其最低存款額高達 $500,000。8萬元的存款太少，因此不能接納。

Analysis B (Incorrect): Although a Restricted Licence Bank (RLB) has no restrictions on maturity (under 3 months is fine), its minimum deposit requirement is $500,000. A deposit of $80,000 is too small to be accepted.

分析 C (正確)：正如上述，有限制牌照銀行 (RLB) 的最低存款要求是 $500,000。因為 $80,000 少於法定下限，所以 RLB 不可以接納這筆存款。此陳述完全正確。

Analysis C (Correct): As mentioned above, the minimum deposit for an RLB is $500,000. Since $80,000 is strictly less than this legal limit, an RLB cannot accept this deposit. This statement is perfectly correct.

分析 D (錯誤)：持牌銀行 (LB) 是香港金融體系中服務最全面的機構，它們不僅可以接受任何金額和期限的存款，亦會發行可轉讓存款證 (NCDs)。

Analysis D (Incorrect): Licensed Banks (LBs) are the most comprehensive institutions in HK's financial system. They accept deposits of any amount and maturity, and they definitely issue NCDs.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

尋找「解題樞紐」(The Pivot Point)：
解決這類題目的關鍵在於對比「題目給予的數字」與「法定要求數字」。題目提供了兩個關鍵數據：金額 ($80,000) 及 期限 (2個月)。

Finding the Pivot Point:
The key to solving this type of question is comparing the "numbers given in the prompt" with the "statutory limits". The question provides two critical data points: Amount ($80,000) and Maturity (2 months).

干擾資訊 (Distractors)：
「可轉讓存款證 (NCD)」這個詞語其實是一個干擾項（除了用來排除選項D之外）。在判斷機構能否接納存款時，我們只需要關注金額和期限，存款形式（定存或NCD）不會改變最低限額的規則。

Distractors:
The term "Negotiable Certificate of Deposit (NCD)" is mostly a distractor (except for eliminating Option D). When determining if an institution can accept a deposit, we only care about the amount and maturity. The form of deposit does not change the minimum limit rules.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱 1：混淆 RLB 和 DTC 的限制條件
很多同學記得「50萬」和「10萬」，但容易將它們與對應的機構配錯。緊記：
- RLB 的英文首字母 R 可以聯想為 "Rich" (富有)，因此需要最高的門檻 ($500,000)。
- DTC 的要求是「10萬」加「3個月」。

Trap 1: Confusing RLB and DTC limitations
Many students memorize "$500k" and "$100k" but mismatch them with the institutions. Remember:
- The 'R' in RLB can stand for "Rich", hence the highest threshold ($500,000).
- DTC requires "$100k" PLUS "3 months".

陷阱 2：見到「不可以」就覺得是錯的陳述
在多項選擇題中，學生心理上傾向選擇描述「可以」發生的正面陳述。這題的正確答案 (C) 偏偏是一個否定句（不可以接納），考驗考生能否客觀根據數據判斷。

Trap 2: Assuming "cannot" implies a false statement
Psychologically, students tend to look for affirmative statements ("can happen") in MCQs. The correct answer (C) here is a negative statement ("cannot accept"), testing if you can judge objectively based on facts.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q1: 如果題目把金額改為 $200,000，期限改為 4 個月，答案會是甚麼？

Q1: If the question changed the amount to $200,000 and maturity to 4 months, what would be the answer?

回答：如果這樣改，接受存款公司 (DTC) 就可以接納這筆存款（因為大於10萬且超過3個月）。但有限制牌照銀行 (RLB) 依然不可以，因為 $200,000 仍未達到 50萬的門檻。

Answer: If changed, a Deposit-Taking Company (DTC) could accept it (since it's > $100k and > 3 months). However, an RLB still couldn't, because $200,000 has not reached the $500,000 threshold.


Q2: 為什麼香港要設立三種不同的銀行牌照？

Q2: Why does Hong Kong have three different types of bank licences?

回答：這是為了平衡競爭與風險管理。持牌銀行 (LB) 服務一般大眾（小額存戶），因此受金管局最嚴格的監管。而 RLB 和 DTC 主要服務大額投資者、企業或進行特定融資，因涉及金額大、參與者風險承受能力較高，其監管重點與 LB 有所不同。

Answer: It's to balance competition and risk management. LBs serve the general public (small depositors) and face the strictest HKMA regulations. RLBs and DTCs focus on corporate finance or large investors. Since they deal with larger amounts and participants with higher risk tolerance, their regulatory focus differs from LBs.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

一秒刪除法 (The 1-Second Elimination Method)：
做這種題目時，永遠先看金額！
題目的金額是 $80,000。請立刻在腦海喚出三級制的門檻：LB(無), DTC(10萬), RLB(50萬)。
因為 $80,000 是所有門檻中最低的，意味著除了持牌銀行 (LB) 之外，沒有其他機構有資格接納這筆存款！
因此，只要選項說 DTC 或 RLB 「可以」接納，就必定是錯的（秒殺 A 和 B）。

The 1-Second Elimination Method:
For these questions, always check the amount first!
The amount is $80,000. Immediately recall the thresholds: LB(none), DTC(100k), RLB(500k).
Since $80,000 is lower than all thresholds, it means NO institution except a Licensed Bank (LB) is qualified to take this deposit!
Therefore, any option claiming a DTC or RLB "can" accept it is automatically wrong (instantly eliminating A and B).
