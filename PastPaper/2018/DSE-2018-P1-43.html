Economics Concept Analysis

Topic: 國際貿易 International Trade Difficulty: ★★★★☆
Opportunity Cost Comparative Advantage Gains from Trade

📍 題目 The Question

下表顯示甲、乙兩國每單位資源的產出：

The following table shows the output per unit of resources in Countries A and B:
電腦 Computer (單位 units) 稻米 Rice (單位 units)
甲國 Country A 16 或 OR 16
乙國 Country B 12 或 OR 3

假設電腦生產國出口 10 單位的電腦以換取 5 單位的稻米。電腦進口國的貿易總得益是多少？

Suppose the computer-producing country exports 10 units of computer in exchange for 5 units of rice. What is the total gain from trade for computer-importing country?
  • A. 0.5 單位的電腦 (0.5 units of computer)
  • B. 1 單位的電腦 (1 unit of computer)
  • C. 2.5 單位的電腦 (2.5 units of computer)
  • D. 5 單位的電腦 (5 units of computer)

📚 概念重溫 Concept Review

要解答這道題目，我們必須掌握以下幾個核心概念：

To solve this question, we must master the following core concepts:

1. 機會成本 (Opportunity Cost)

這是一個「產出表」(Output Table)。在產出表中，生產物品 X 的機會成本等於放棄的物品 Y 數量除以物品 X 的數量。

This is an "Output Table". In an output table, the opportunity cost of producing Good X is equal to the quantity of Good Y given up divided by the quantity of Good X.
$$ \text{Opportunity Cost of X} = \frac{\text{Maximum Output of Y}}{\text{Maximum Output of X}} $$

2. 比較優勢 (Comparative Advantage)

若一個國家生產某物品的機會成本低於另一個國家，該國便擁有該物品的比較優勢，並會專門生產及出口該物品。

If a country can produce a good at a lower opportunity cost than another country, it has a comparative advantage in that good, and will specialize in producing and exporting it.

3. 貿易得益 (Gains from Trade)

貿易得益等於「貿易後的成本（或收益）」與「沒有貿易時的國內成本」之間的差額。總得益 = 單位得益 × 交易數量。

Gains from trade equal the difference between the "cost (or revenue) with trade" and the "domestic cost without trade". Total gain = per unit gain × volume of trade.

📝 答案與詳細分析 The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Option: D

第一步：計算機會成本 Step 1: Calculate Opportunity Costs

根據公式，我們計算兩國生產電腦 (C) 和稻米 (R) 的機會成本：

Using the formula, we calculate the opportunity costs for producing computers (C) and rice (R) for both countries:
  • 甲國 Country A: 1C = 1R ; 1R = 1C
  • 乙國 Country B: 1C = 0.25R ; 1R = 4C

第二步：找出比較優勢 Step 2: Identify Comparative Advantage

乙國生產 1 單位電腦的機會成本 (0.25R) 低於甲國 (1R)。因此，乙國是電腦生產國（出口電腦）。相反，甲國生產 1 單位稻米的機會成本 (1C) 低於乙國 (4C)，所以甲國是電腦進口國（出口稻米換取電腦）。

Country B's opportunity cost of producing 1 unit of computer (0.25R) is lower than Country A's (1R). Therefore, Country B is the computer-producing country (exports computers). Conversely, Country A's opportunity cost of producing 1 unit of rice (1C) is lower than Country B's (4C), so Country A is the computer-importing country (exports rice for computers).

第三步：計算貿易得益 Step 3: Calculate Total Gain from Trade

題目問的是「電腦進口國」（即甲國）的貿易總得益。
交易條件：甲國出口 5 單位稻米，換取 10 單位電腦。
在沒有貿易的情況下，甲國要自己生產 10 單位電腦，必須放棄 10 單位稻米 (\( 10 \times 1R = 10R \))。
現在有貿易，甲國只需付出 5 單位稻米，就能獲得 10 單位電腦。
甲國節省了：10R - 5R = 5R。
因為題目選項要求的單位是「電腦」，我們需要將 5 單位稻米轉換回電腦。根據甲國的國內成本 (1R = 1C)，5 單位稻米等同於 5 單位的電腦。

The question asks for the total gain of the "computer-importing country" (Country A).
Terms of trade: Country A exports 5 units of rice to get 10 units of computers.
Without trade, to produce 10 units of computers domestically, Country A must give up 10 units of rice (\( 10 \times 1R = 10R \)).
With trade, Country A only pays 5 units of rice to get 10 units of computers.
Country A saves: 10R - 5R = 5R.
Since the options are in "units of computer", we must convert 5 units of rice into computers. Based on Country A's domestic cost (1R = 1C), 5 units of rice equals 5 units of computer.

選項分析 Options Breakdown:

❌ Option A (0.5 單位): 這是陷阱。甲國每進口 1 單位電腦的「單位得益」是 0.5 單位稻米 (國內成本 1R 減去 貿易價格 0.5R)。如果學生忘記乘以總交易量 (10 單位) 或搞錯單位，就會誤選此項。

This is a trap. The "per unit gain" for Country A for each imported computer is 0.5 units of rice (Domestic cost 1R minus Trade price 0.5R). If a student forgets to multiply by the total volume (10 units) or mixes up the units, they will pick this.

❌ Option B (1 單位): 隨機干擾數字，或者學生錯誤使用了其他不相關的比例進行扣減。

A random distractor, or the student mistakenly used irrelevant ratios for subtraction.

❌ Option C (2.5 單位): 學生可能錯誤地將總得益 5 單位稻米，錯誤地用「貿易比率」(1C = 0.5R) 去轉換為電腦（即 5 × 0.5 = 2.5），忘記了轉換本國得益必須使用「本國的機會成本」。

A student might mistakenly take the total gain of 5 units of rice and convert it using the "Terms of Trade ratio" (1C = 0.5R) instead of Country A's domestic opportunity cost, resulting in 5 × 0.5 = 2.5.

✅ Option D (5 單位): 正確計算了總得益，並正確使用了甲國的機會成本 (1R = 1C) 將稻米轉換為電腦單位。

Correctly calculated the total gain and correctly used Country A's opportunity cost (1R = 1C) to convert rice into computer units.

🔍 題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

這類題目通常有三個致命的「轉折點 (Pivot Points)」：

  1. 誰是進口國？ 題目故意不直接說「甲國得益多少」，而是說「電腦進口國」。你必須先透過比較優勢算出甲國進口電腦，否則整題都會算錯。
  2. 總得益 vs 單位得益： 題目問的是「貿易總得益 (Total gain)」，所以必須用單位得益乘以交易量。
  3. 單位的轉換： 你算出來的得益原本是以「稻米」為單位（節省了 5 單位稻米），但所有選項都是「電腦」。你必須用甲國自己的機會成本把稻米換算成電腦！
Questions like this usually have three crucial "Pivot Points":
1. Who is the importer? The question intentionally hides "Country A" behind the phrase "computer-importing country". You must find out who has the comparative advantage first.
2. Total vs Per Unit Gain: The question asks for "Total gain", so you must multiply per unit gain by the trade volume.
3. Unit Conversion: Your initial calculated gain is in terms of "Rice" (saved 5 units of rice). But all options are in "Computers". You must convert it using Country A's domestic opportunity cost!

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

錯用「貿易價格 (Terms of Trade)」去計算得益的單位轉換！
很多同學算出甲國賺了 5 單位稻米後，會用市場上的匯率（10C = 5R）把稻米換成電腦，得出 2.5C（選C）。這是大錯特錯！
請記住：評估國內的真實得益時，必須使用「國內沒有貿易時的機會成本」來進行轉換，因為這代表了該國在自己家裡生產需要付出的真實代價。

Using the "Terms of Trade" to convert units of gains!
Many students, after calculating a gain of 5 units of rice, use the market exchange rate (10C = 5R) to convert rice to computers, getting 2.5C (Option C). This is completely wrong!
Remember: When evaluating real domestic gains, you MUST use the "domestic opportunity cost without trade" for conversion, because it represents the actual sacrifice the country would have made to produce it domestically.

🙋 學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

Q: 為什麼甲國明明出口 5 單位稻米，我們卻說它「賺了 5 單位稻米」？

A: 換個角度想：甲國想要 10 部電腦。如果不貿易，它要自己生產，就必須放棄 10 單位稻米。現在有了貿易，它只需要拿出 5 單位稻米跟別人換，就能得到 10 部電腦。原本要花 10 碗飯，現在只花了 5 碗飯，所以它「節省了」5 碗飯，這節省下來的成本就是它的「得益」。

Q: Why do we say Country A "gained 5 units of rice" when it actually exported 5 units of rice?
A: Think of it this way: Country A wants 10 computers. Without trade, it must give up 10 units of rice to make them. With trade, it only needs to hand over 5 units of rice to get the 10 computers. It used to cost 10 bowls of rice, now it only costs 5. The 5 bowls of rice it "saved" is its "gain".

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

速解法 (Direct Output Comparison):
既然要找甲國以「電腦」為單位的總得益，我們直接用「電腦」的進出口數量來算：
甲國為了出口 5 單位稻米，在國內需要放棄多少電腦？因為 1R = 1C，所以放棄了 5 單位電腦的產量。
甲國用這 5 單位稻米換回了多少電腦？換回了 10 單位電腦。
得益 = 換回來的數量 - 放棄的數量 = 10 - 5 = 5 單位的電腦。直接秒殺！

Shortcut Method (Direct Output Comparison):
Since we need Country A's total gain in terms of "computers", let's calculate directly using computer quantities:
To export 5 units of rice, how many computers must Country A sacrifice domestically? Since 1R = 1C, it sacrifices the production of 5 computers.
How many computers did Country A get back in exchange? It got 10 computers.
Gain = Quantity received - Quantity sacrificed = 10 - 5 = 5 units of computer. Solved instantly!
