HKDSE Economics: Question Analysis

Topic (課題): 外部性與市場失效 / Externalities and Market Failure

Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆

Key Concepts (關鍵概念): 負面外部性 Negative Externality 社會成本 Social Cost 政府干預 Government Intervention

1. The Question (題目)

近年，無人飛行系統變得十分普及，但在市區使用這些飛行器引起人們對它在噪音和安全方面的關注。民航處正考慮對生產、進口和銷售無人飛行系統作出監管。

這是私人和社會 _______ 分歧的一個例子，而 _______ 的方法正被考慮用作減少該分歧。

A. 成本 …… 市場
B. 成本 …… 政府
C. 利益 …… 市場
D. 利益 …… 政府

Recently, unmanned aircraft systems have become very popular but using those aircrafts in urban areas has caused noise and safety concerns. The Civil Aviation Department is considering regulating the manufacture, import and sale of unmanned aircraft systems.

It is an example of divergence between private and social ____________ and the _________ solution is being considered to reduce the divergence.

A. costs …… market
B. costs …… government
C. benefits …… market
D. benefits …… government

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

外部性 (Externalities): 當生產或消費活動對沒有參與交易的第三者造成影響，而沒有作出補償或收取報酬時，便會出現外部性。

Externalities: Occur when a production or consumption activity affects third parties who are not involved in the transaction, without any compensation paid or received.

負面外部性 (Negative Externality): 活動對第三者造成損害（例如噪音、污染）。這會導致邊際社會成本 \(MSC\) 大於邊際私人成本 \(MPC\)。兩者的差距稱為邊際外部成本 \(MEC\)。

Negative Externality: The activity imposes harm (e.g., noise, pollution) on third parties. This causes the Marginal Social Cost \(MSC\) to be greater than the Marginal Private Cost \(MPC\). The difference is the Marginal External Cost \(MEC\).

$$ MSC = MPC + MEC $$

解決方法 (Solutions):
1. 市場方法 (Market Solution): 透過界定產權（高斯定理 Coase Theorem），讓雙方自行談判解決。
2. 政府方法 (Government Solution): 透過立法監管、徵收庇古稅 (Pigouvian tax) 或發放補貼來糾正市場失效。

Solutions:
1. Market Solution: Defining property rights (Coase Theorem) and allowing private negotiation.
2. Government Solution: Correcting market failure through legislation/regulation, Pigouvian taxes, or subsidies.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option B

Option A: 成本 …… 市場 (costs …… market) - ❌ Incorrect
雖然「成本」是正確的，但「民航處監管」屬於政府行為，並非市場方法（如界定產權）。
While "costs" is correct, "regulation by the Civil Aviation Department" is a government action, not a market solution (like defining property rights).

Option B: 成本 …… 政府 (costs …… government) - ✅ Correct
無人機造成的「噪音和安全關注」是對第三者造成的負面影響，屬於外部成本，因此是私人與社會「成本」的分歧。民航處（政府部門）考慮「監管」，這顯然是「政府」的方法。
The "noise and safety concerns" caused by drones are negative impacts on third parties, which are external costs. Thus, it's a divergence in "costs". The Civil Aviation Department (a government body) considering "regulation" is clearly a "government" solution.

Option C & D: 利益 (benefits) - ❌ Incorrect
題目描述的是負面影響（噪音、安全威脅），這會增加社會成本，而不是影響社會利益。因此，這不是私人與社會利益的分歧。
The question describes negative impacts (noise, safety threats), which increase social costs rather than affecting social benefits. Therefore, this is not a divergence of benefits.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

  • Pivot 1 (關鍵點 1): "noise and safety concerns" (噪音和安全方面的關注) ➔ 這是對旁人造成的滋擾 ➔ 屬於外部成本 (External Costs)。
  • Pivot 2 (關鍵點 2): "Civil Aviation Department... regulating" (民航處...作出監管) ➔ 民航處是政府機構，監管是行政手段 ➔ 屬於政府方法 (Government Solution)。

By identifying these two keywords, you can instantly map "concerns/nuisance" to "costs" and "Department regulation" to "government".

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

學生常混淆「市場方法」與「政府方法」。如果題目提到「政府發放可交易的污染許可證 (tradable pollution permits)」，這其實是結合了政府（設定上限）與市場（自由交易）的方法。但本題純粹是「監管 (regulating)」，屬於傳統的指令性控制 (Command and control)，絕對是政府方法。

Students often confuse market and government solutions. If the question mentioned "tradable pollution permits," it would involve a market mechanism. However, pure "regulation" is a command-and-control approach, strictly a government solution.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 什麼情況下第一個空格會填「利益 (benefits)」？
A: 當活動對第三者帶來好處時（正面外部性 Positive Externality）。例如：某人種植美麗的花園，鄰居也能欣賞（免費獲得利益），這時邊際社會利益 \(MSB\) 大於邊際私人利益 \(MPB\)，這就是私人和社會「利益」的分歧。

Q: Under what circumstances would the first blank be "benefits"?
A: When the activity brings advantages to third parties (Positive Externality). For example: someone plants a beautiful garden and neighbors enjoy the view for free. Here, Marginal Social Benefit \(MSB\) > Marginal Private Benefit \(MPB\), creating a divergence in "benefits".

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧 (Instant Kill Trick):
看到「民航處 (Civil Aviation Department)」這個政府部門的名字，第二個空格必定是「政府 (government)」。直接淘汰 A 和 C。接著，看到「噪音 (noise)」這種負面詞彙，必定是「成本 (costs)」。答案 B 呼之欲出，全程只需 5 秒。

Shortcut: Seeing a government body like the "Civil Aviation Department" immediately means the second blank is "government". Eliminate A and C. Seeing a negative word like "noise" means it's about "costs". The answer must be B. This takes 5 seconds.
