Topic: 總需求與總供應 (Aggregate Demand & Aggregate Supply) Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆
Key Concepts: 總需求 (Aggregate Demand) 短期總供應 (Short-run Aggregate Supply) 消費 (Consumption)

1. 題目 (The Question)

下列哪項引致物價水平和總產出在短期均會上升？
Which of the following will lead to an increase in both price level and aggregate output in the short run?
  • A. 生產技術提升。
    A. There is an improvement in production technology.
  • B. 油價上升。
    B. There is an increase in the oil price.
  • C. 貨幣供應下降。
    C. There is a decrease in money supply.
  • D. 人們儲蓄意慾下降。
    D. There is a decrease in the desire to save.

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

總需求 (Aggregate Demand, \(AD\))： 指在不同物價水平下，經濟體系內各部門計劃購買的本地實質產出總量。
Aggregate Demand (\(AD\)): The total quantity of domestic real output that all sectors in the economy plan to buy at different price levels.
$$ AD = C + I + G + NX $$
當中 \(C\) 是私人消費開支，\(I\) 是投資開支，\(G\) 是政府消費開支，\(NX\) 是淨出口（出口減進口）。
Where \(C\) is private consumption expenditure, \(I\) is investment expenditure, \(G\) is government consumption expenditure, and \(NX\) is net exports (exports minus imports).
短期總供應 (Short-run Aggregate Supply, \(SRAS\))： 受生產成本（如工資、原料價格）及生產力影響。
Short-run Aggregate Supply (\(SRAS\)): Affected by costs of production (e.g., wages, raw material prices) and productivity.

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option)： D

A. 生產技術提升 (Improvement in production technology) - ❌ 錯誤 (Wrong)
生產技術提升會降低生產成本或提高生產力，導致短期總供應 (\(SRAS\)) 增加（曲線向右移）。這會令物價水平 (\(P\)) 下降，而總產出 (\(Y\)) 上升。與題目要求的「物價上升」不符。
An improvement in technology lowers production costs or raises productivity, causing an increase in short-run aggregate supply (\(SRAS\)) (rightward shift). This leads to a decrease in the price level (\(P\)) and an increase in aggregate output (\(Y\)). This contradicts the "price level increase" required by the question.
B. 油價上升 (Increase in the oil price) - ❌ 錯誤 (Wrong)
油價上升會增加企業的生產成本，導致短期總供應 (\(SRAS\)) 減少（曲線向左移）。這會令物價水平 (\(P\)) 上升，但總產出 (\(Y\)) 會下降（出現滯脹）。與題目要求的「產出上升」不符。
An increase in oil prices raises the cost of production for firms, causing a decrease in short-run aggregate supply (\(SRAS\)) (leftward shift). This leads to an increase in the price level (\(P\)) but a decrease in aggregate output (\(Y\)) (stagflation). This contradicts the "output increase" required by the question.
C. 貨幣供應下降 (Decrease in money supply) - ❌ 錯誤 (Wrong)
貨幣供應下降會推高利率，從而減少消費 (\(C\)) 和投資 (\(I\))。總需求 (\(AD\)) 會減少（曲線向左移），導致物價水平 (\(P\)) 和總產出 (\(Y\)) 雙雙下降。
A decrease in money supply pushes up interest rates, which reduces consumption (\(C\)) and investment (\(I\)). Aggregate demand (\(AD\)) decreases (leftward shift), leading to a decrease in both the price level (\(P\)) and aggregate output (\(Y\)).
D. 人們儲蓄意慾下降 (Decrease in the desire to save) - ✅ 正確 (Correct)
當人們減少儲蓄，意味著他們將更大比例的收入用於消費。私人消費開支 (\(C\)) 上升，帶動總需求 (\(AD\)) 增加（曲線向右移）。在總需求增加的情況下，物價水平 (\(P\)) 和總產出 (\(Y\)) 在短期內均會上升。
When people decrease their desire to save, it means they spend a larger proportion of their income on consumption. Private consumption expenditure (\(C\)) increases, driving up aggregate demand (\(AD\)) (rightward shift). An increase in aggregate demand leads to an increase in both the price level (\(P\)) and aggregate output (\(Y\)) in the short run.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

破題關鍵 (The Pivot Point)：
題目要求找出一個令「物價 (\(P\))」和「產出 (\(Y\))」同時上升的因素。在 AS-AD 模型中，只有總需求 (\(AD\)) 增加（向右移）才能同時推高物價和產出。因此，你的目標是從選項中找出哪一個會令 \(AD\) 增加。
The question asks for a factor that causes BOTH "Price (\(P\))" and "Output (\(Y\))" to increase. In the AS-AD model, only an increase in Aggregate Demand (\(AD\)) (rightward shift) can push up both price and output simultaneously. Therefore, your goal is to find the option that causes \(AD\) to increase.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

混淆儲蓄與投資 (Confusing saving with investment)：
學生常誤以為儲蓄減少會導致銀行可用於借貸的資金減少，從而降低投資 (\(I\)) 和 \(AD\)。但在 DSE 宏觀經濟學的短期分析中，我們主要關注消費者的直接行為：「不儲蓄的錢去哪了？去了消費。」因此，儲蓄意慾下降最直接和主要的效果是消費 (\(C\)) 增加。
Students often mistakenly think that a decrease in saving reduces the funds available for bank lending, thereby lowering investment (\(I\)) and \(AD\). However, in the short-run analysis of DSE Macroeconomics, we focus on the direct consumer behavior: "Where does the unsaved money go? It goes to consumption." Thus, the most direct and primary effect of a decreased desire to save is an increase in consumption (\(C\)).

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 如果題目問的是「長期 (Long-run)」，答案會有不同嗎？
Q: If the question asked about the "Long-run", would the answer be different?
A: 是的！在長期，長期總供應曲線 (\(LRAS\)) 是垂直的。如果 \(AD\) 增加（如選項 D），長期只會導致物價水平 (\(P\)) 上升，而總產出 (\(Y\)) 會回復到充分就業水平（不變）。所以如果題目是問長期，選項 D 就不會令產出上升了。
A: Yes! In the long run, the Long-run Aggregate Supply (\(LRAS\)) curve is vertical. If \(AD\) increases (like in Option D), it will only lead to a higher price level (\(P\)) in the long run, while aggregate output (\(Y\)) will return to the full-employment level (remains unchanged). So if the question specified the long run, Option D would not cause an increase in output.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

箭頭速記法 (The Arrow Shortcut)：
在選擇題中，不用每次都畫圖。記住以下這四組黃金規律：
  • \(AD\) 升 (向右) ➔ \(P\) ↑, \(Y\) ↑ (本題目標)
  • \(AD\) 跌 (向左) ➔ \(P\) ↓, \(Y\) ↓
  • \(SRAS\) 升 (向右) ➔ \(P\) ↓, \(Y\) ↑
  • \(SRAS\) 跌 (向左) ➔ \(P\) ↑, \(Y\) ↓
一看題目「\(P\) ↑, \(Y\) ↑」，1秒鎖定尋找「有利 \(AD\)」的選項，直接略過影響生產成本的 A 和 B，再排除不利 \(AD\) 的 C，答案 D 秒殺！
The Arrow Shortcut: You don't need to draw a graph for every MC question. Memorize these four golden rules:
  • \(AD\) increases (Right) ➔ \(P\) ↑, \(Y\) ↑ (Target of this question)
  • \(AD\) decreases (Left) ➔ \(P\) ↓, \(Y\) ↓
  • \(SRAS\) increases (Right) ➔ \(P\) ↓, \(Y\) ↑
  • \(SRAS\) decreases (Left) ➔ \(P\) ↑, \(Y\) ↓
Upon seeing "\(P\) ↑, \(Y\) ↑" in the question, instantly lock your target on "factors that increase \(AD\)". Skip options A and B (which affect production costs), eliminate C (which decreases \(AD\)), and you can instantly select D!
