⭐ 難度 (Difficulty): ★★☆☆☆ (2/5)

🔑 關鍵字 (Key Concepts): 香港金融管理局 (HKMA), 中央銀行功能 (Central Bank Functions), 外匯基金 (Exchange Fund)

題目 (The Question)

下列哪項不是香港金融管理局的功能？

A. 穩定按揭利率
B. 發行鈔票
C. 監管商業銀行
D. 管理外匯基金

Which of the following is NOT a function of the Hong Kong Monetary Authority?

A. to stabilise the mortgage rate
B. to issue currency notes
C. to supervise the commercial banks
D. to manage the Exchange Fund

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

香港金融管理局的功能 (Functions of the HKMA)

香港金融管理局（金管局）是香港的實質中央銀行。其主要職責包括：

The Hong Kong Monetary Authority (HKMA) is the de facto central bank of Hong Kong. Its main responsibilities include:
  1. 維持貨幣穩定 (Maintaining Currency Stability): 透過聯繫匯率制度維持港元匯率穩定。
    Maintaining the stability of the HKD exchange rate through the Linked Exchange Rate System.
  2. 管理外匯基金 (Managing the Exchange Fund): 運用外匯基金來捍衛港元。
    Using the Exchange Fund to defend the Hong Kong dollar.
  3. 促進金融體系穩定 (Promoting Financial System Stability): 監管商業銀行（認可機構）。
    Supervising commercial banks (authorized institutions).
  4. 發行貨幣 (Issuing Currency): 金管局負責發行硬幣及十元紙幣，並授權三間發鈔銀行發行其他面額的紙幣。
    The HKMA issues coins and $10 notes, and authorizes three note-issuing banks to issue notes of other denominations.

注意：在香港，利率（如按揭利率 \(r\)）主要由市場力量決定，金管局不會直接設定或穩定商業銀行的按揭利率。

Note: In Hong Kong, interest rates (such as the mortgage rate \(r\)) are primarily determined by market forces. The HKMA does not directly set or stabilize commercial banks' mortgage rates.

答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): A

正確答案 A. 穩定按揭利率 (to stabilise the mortgage rate)

香港的按揭利率是由商業銀行根據市場資金成本（如銀行同業拆息 HIBOR 或最優惠利率 Prime Rate）自行釐定的。金管局會推出宏觀審慎措施（如調整按揭成數上限），但不會直接干預或穩定按揭利率。因此，這不是金管局的功能。

Mortgage rates in Hong Kong are determined by commercial banks based on market funding costs (e.g., HIBOR or Prime Rate). While the HKMA introduces macroprudential measures (like adjusting loan-to-value ratios), it does NOT directly intervene or stabilize mortgage rates. Therefore, this is not a function of the HKMA.

錯誤選項 B. 發行鈔票 (to issue currency notes)

這是一個常見的陷阱。雖然大部分紙幣由三間發鈔銀行發行，但金管局本身確實負責發行十元紙幣（塑膠及紙製）以及所有硬幣。因此，發行貨幣是其功能之一。

This is a common trap. Although most banknotes are issued by the three note-issuing banks, the HKMA itself is responsible for issuing the ten-dollar note (both polymer and paper) and all coins. Therefore, issuing currency is one of its functions.

錯誤選項 C. 監管商業銀行 (to supervise the commercial banks)

金管局是香港銀行業的監管機構，負責確保存款人的利益和銀行體系的穩定。這是其核心功能。

The HKMA is the regulator of the banking sector in Hong Kong, responsible for ensuring the interests of depositors and the stability of the banking system. This is a core function.

錯誤選項 D. 管理外匯基金 (to manage the Exchange Fund)

金管局負責管理外匯基金，該基金主要用於支持聯繫匯率制度及維持香港金融體系的穩定。這是其核心功能。

The HKMA manages the Exchange Fund, which is primarily used to back the Linked Exchange Rate System and maintain the stability of Hong Kong's financial system. This is a core function.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解答此題的關鍵在於理解「中央銀行功能」在香港的特殊性。香港實行自由市場經濟及聯繫匯率制度：

The key to solving this question lies in understanding the uniqueness of "central bank functions" in Hong Kong. Hong Kong operates a free market economy and a Linked Exchange Rate System:
  • 破題點 (Pivot Point): 尋找金管局「無權」或「不參與」的市場行為。利率（Interest Rate）在香港是由市場決定的，金管局只控制基礎貨幣，不控制零售銀行的具體貸款利率。
  • 干擾項 (Distractor): 選項 B 是最大的干擾。學生容易誤以為「只有滙豐、中銀、渣打可以發鈔」，而忘記了金管局發行的紫色十元鈔票。

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

❌ 誤解： 以為金管局像美國聯儲局 (Federal Reserve) 一樣，會主動透過貨幣政策去控制市場利率。
✅ 真相： 在聯繫匯率制度下，香港放棄了獨立的貨幣政策。香港的利率走勢大致跟隨美國，金管局不會主動去「穩定」或「設定」按揭利率。

❌ Misconception: Thinking the HKMA actively controls market interest rates through monetary policy like the US Federal Reserve.
✅ Truth: Under the Linked Exchange Rate System, Hong Kong has given up independent monetary policy. HK's interest rates generally follow the US, and the HKMA does not actively "stabilize" or "set" mortgage rates.

學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 金管局平時會收緊按揭成數（LTV ratio），這不算是在「穩定按揭市場」嗎？

A: 收緊按揭成數（例如規定買樓最多只能借 60%）是為了管理銀行的信貸風險，維持銀行體系穩定。這屬於「宏觀審慎措施」，但這並不等於「穩定按揭利率」。利率（借錢的利息成本）依然是由銀行根據市場決定的。

Q: The HKMA often tightens the Loan-to-Value (LTV) ratio. Isn't that "stabilizing the mortgage market"?

A: Tightening the LTV ratio (e.g., allowing buyers to borrow only up to 60%) is to manage banks' credit risks and maintain banking stability. These are "macroprudential measures," but they do not equate to "stabilizing the mortgage rate." The interest rate (the cost of borrowing) is still determined by banks based on the market.

考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

💡 絕對字眼排除法 (Elimination Method):

在 DSE 經濟科中，香港政府和金管局極少直接干預「價格」（Price）。按揭利率本質上就是借貸資金的「價格」。只要記住「香港是自由市場，政府不輕易干預價格/利率」，就能迅速鎖定選項 A 為非金管局功能。

In DSE Economics, the HK Government and HKMA rarely intervene directly in "Prices". The mortgage rate is essentially the "price" of borrowing funds. By remembering that "Hong Kong is a free market and the government does not easily intervene in prices/interest rates," you can quickly identify Option A as the non-function.
