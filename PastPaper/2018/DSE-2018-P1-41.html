The Question (題目)

繁體中文:

(2) 本地生產商的收入會上升$110。
(3) 進口數量下降15單位。

B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)

English:
The following diagram shows an imposition of import tariff on a good of a small open economy.
Referring to the above diagram, which of the following statements are correct after the imposition of import tariff (t)?
(1) The total amount of tariff received by the government is $105.
(2) The revenue of domestic producers will increase by $110.
(3) The volume of imports drops by 15 units.

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

Concept Review (概念重溫)

小型開放經濟與關稅 (Small Open Economy & Tariffs)

在小型開放經濟中，世界價格由國際市場決定。當政府徵收關稅（Tariff, $t$）時，本地市場價格會由原來的世界價格上升至新的世界價格加關稅。

In a small open economy, the world price is determined by the global market. When the government imposes a tariff ($t$), the domestic market price rises from the original world price to the new world price plus the tariff.

  • 進口量 (Import Volume): 等于本地需求量減去本地供應量。
    Equals domestic quantity demanded minus domestic quantity supplied.
    $$Imports = Q_d - Q_s$$
  • 關稅收入 (Tariff Revenue): 政府從每單位進口物品中收取稅款，因此總額等於單位關稅乘以新的進口量。
    The government collects tax on each imported unit, so total revenue equals the per-unit tariff multiplied by the new import volume.
    $$Tariff\ Revenue = t \times New\ Imports$$
  • 本地生產商收入 (Domestic Producers' Revenue): 等於市場價格乘以本地供應量。
    Equals the market price multiplied by the domestic quantity supplied.
    $$Revenue = P \times Q_s$$

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): D

陳述 (1): 政府收取的關稅總金額是$105。 ✔ 正確 (Correct)

Statement (1): The total amount of tariff received by the government is $105.

分析: 每單位關稅 $t = 10 - 7 = 3$。徵收關稅後，價格升至 10。此時，本地需求量是 60，本地供應量是 25。新的進口量 $= 60 - 25 = 35$。因此，關稅總金額 $= 3 \times 35 = 105$。

Analysis: Per-unit tariff $t = 10 - 7 = 3$. After the tariff, the price rises to 10. At this price, domestic quantity demanded is 60, and domestic quantity supplied is 25. The new import volume $= 60 - 25 = 35$. Therefore, total tariff revenue $= 3 \times 35 = 105$.

陳述 (2): 本地生產商的收入會上升$110。 ✔ 正確 (Correct)

Statement (2): The revenue of domestic producers will increase by $110.

分析: 原本的收入（價格為 7 時）$= 7 \times 20 = 140$。新的收入（價格為 10 時）$= 10 \times 25 = 250$。收入的增加量 $= 250 - 140 = 110$。

Analysis: Original revenue (at price 7) $= 7 \times 20 = 140$. New revenue (at price 10) $= 10 \times 25 = 250$. The increase in revenue $= 250 - 140 = 110$.

陳述 (3): 進口數量下降15單位。 ✔ 正確 (Correct)

Statement (3): The volume of imports drops by 15 units.

分析: 徵稅前的進口量 $= 70 - 20 = 50$。徵稅後的進口量 $= 60 - 25 = 35$。進口量下降 $= 50 - 35 = 15$ 單位。

Analysis: Pre-tariff imports $= 70 - 20 = 50$. Post-tariff imports $= 60 - 25 = 35$. The drop in import volume $= 50 - 35 = 15$ units.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解決這類題目的「關鍵點」(Pivot Point) 是準確讀取兩條水平線上的四個交點。不要被圖中交叉的X形供需曲線嚇倒，你只需要關注價格為 7 和 10 時的數據：

The "Pivot Point" to solving this type of question is accurately reading the four intersection points on the two horizontal lines. Don't be intimidated by the crossing X-shaped supply and demand curves; you only need to focus on the data at prices 7 and 10:

  • When P = 7 (S0): $Q_s = 20, Q_d = 70$
  • When P = 10 (S1): $Q_s = 25, Q_d = 60$

只要將這四個數字寫在草稿紙上，所有的計算（進口量、收入、關稅）都只是簡單的加減乘除。

As long as you write these four numbers on your scratch paper, all calculations (imports, revenue, tariffs) become simple arithmetic.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱 1: 計算關稅收入時用錯進口量
很多學生會用舊的進口量 (50) 或下降的進口量 (15) 去乘以關稅 (3)。記住，政府只能對實際進入境內的貨品徵稅，所以必須用新的進口量 (35)。

Trap 1: Using the wrong import volume for tariff revenue
Many students multiply the tariff (3) by the old import volume (50) or the drop in imports (15). Remember, the government only taxes goods that actually enter the border, so you must use the new import volume (35).

陷阱 2: 混淆本地收入與總支出
計算本地生產商收入時，有些同學會錯誤地拿 $10 \times 60$ (總需求量)。請記住，本地生產商只負責生產 $Q_s$ (25單位)，其餘的是外國進口的。

Trap 2: Confusing domestic revenue with total expenditure
When calculating domestic producers' revenue, some incorrectly use $10 \times 60$ (total quantity demanded). Remember, domestic producers only supply up to $Q_s$ (25 units); the rest is imported from abroad.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼本地生產商的產量會從 20 增加到 25？(Why does domestic production increase from 20 to 25?)

A: 因為進口關稅提高了市場整體的價格（從 7 升到 10）。根據供應定律 (Law of Supply)，當價格上升，本地生產商會預期有更高利潤，因此願意且有能力提供更多的數量。
Because the import tariff raises the overall market price (from 7 to 10). According to the Law of Supply, when the price increases, domestic producers expect higher profits, so they are willing and able to supply a larger quantity.
