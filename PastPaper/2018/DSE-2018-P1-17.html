HKDSE Economics Question Analysis
經濟科試題分析

Topic: Price Elasticity of Demand (需求價格彈性) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts: Price Elasticity (價格彈性), Total Expenditure (總支出), Change in Quantity Demanded (需求量改變)

題目 / The Question

豬柳蛋漢堡的原價是每個$11。麥當勞推出下列的限時折扣。
大減價：$10兩個！豬柳蛋漢堡
結果，更多人在優惠期間購買豬柳蛋漢堡。下列哪項有關這情況的陳述是正確的？

A. 在優惠期間麥當勞沒有爭取最高利潤。
B. 豬柳蛋漢堡的需求在優惠期間會上升。
C. 若需求屬高彈性，消費者對豬柳蛋漢堡的總支出會上升。
D. 製作豬柳蛋漢堡所需的食材會暫時出現短缺。

The original price of Sausage McMuffin with Egg was $11 each. McDonald’s introduced the following discount in a limited time.
Big Discount: $10 for 2! Sausage McMuffin with Egg
As a result, more people bought Sausage McMuffin with Egg during the promotion period. Which of the following statements about this situation is correct?

A. McDonald’s was not maximising profit during the promotion period.
B. The demand for Sausage McMuffin with Egg would be higher during the promotion period.
C. The total expenditure of consumers on Sausage McMuffin with Egg would increase if the demand was elastic.
D. There would be a temporary shortage of the food ingredients for making Sausage McMuffin with Egg.

📚 概念重溫 / Concept Review

解答此題需要理解以下幾個核心經濟學概念：

To solve this question, you need to understand the following core economic concepts:
  • 需求量改變 (Change in Quantity Demanded) vs 需求改變 (Change in Demand)： 物品自身價格下降只會導致「需求量」上升（沿著同一條需求曲線移動），而不會導致「需求」上升（需求曲線整條移動）。
    A decrease in the good's own price only leads to an increase in "quantity demanded" (movement along the curve), not an increase in "demand" (shift of the entire curve).
  • 總支出與價格彈性的關係 (Total Expenditure and Price Elasticity)： 總支出 $TE$ 的計算公式為：
    $$TE = P \times Q$$
    當需求屬高彈性 ($E_d > 1$) 時，價格 $P$ 下降的百分比會小於需求量 $Q$ 增加的百分比。因此，價格下降會導致總支出上升。
    When demand is elastic ($E_d > 1$), the percentage decrease in price $P$ is smaller than the percentage increase in quantity demanded $Q$. Thus, a price drop leads to an increase in total expenditure.
  • 爭取極大化假設 (Maximization Postulate)： 經濟學基本假設之一，生產者必定在局限條件下爭取最高利潤。
    One of the basic postulates in economics: producers always maximize their profits subject to constraints.

✅ 答案與詳細分析 / Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案是 / The correct option is: Option C

A 在優惠期間麥當勞沒有爭取最高利潤。

錯誤。 根據經濟學的基本假設，每個人（包括廠商）在任何時候都在爭取極大化（利潤或財富）。減價促銷本身就是一種爭取最高利潤的商業策略（例如透過薄利多銷增加總收益）。

Incorrect. According to the basic postulate of economics, individuals (including firms) are always maximizing (profit or wealth) at all times. A price discount is simply a business strategy to maximize profit (e.g., increasing total revenue through higher sales volume).
B 豬柳蛋漢堡的需求在優惠期間會上升。

錯誤。 這是一個極常見的DSE陷阱！題目指出價格由原本的每個$11，下降至$10兩個（每個$5）。物品「自身價格」(Own price)的改變，只會導致需求量 (Quantity Demanded)的改變，並不會改變需求 (Demand)。

Incorrect. This is a very common DSE trap! The question states the price dropped from $11 each to $10 for two ($5 each). A change in the "own price" of a good only leads to a change in Quantity Demanded, not a change in Demand.
C 若需求屬高彈性，消費者對豬柳蛋漢堡的總支出會上升。

正確。 總支出 $TE = P \times Q$。當價格下降（$P \downarrow$），而需求屬高彈性 ($E_d > 1$) 時，需求量增加的百分比會大於價格下降的百分比（$\% \Delta Q > \% \Delta P$）。因此，$P \times Q$ 的總數值會上升，消費者的總支出（即麥當勞的總收益）會增加。

Correct. Total Expenditure $TE = P \times Q$. When price decreases ($P \downarrow$) and demand is elastic ($E_d > 1$), the percentage increase in quantity demanded is greater than the percentage decrease in price ($\% \Delta Q > \% \Delta P$). Therefore, the total value of $P \times Q$ will increase, meaning consumers' total expenditure (which is the firm's total revenue) will rise.
D 製作豬柳蛋漢堡所需的食材會暫時出現短缺。

錯誤。 短缺 (Shortage) 在經濟學上有嚴格定義：在某一價格下，需求量大於供應量 ($Q_d > Q_s$)。麥當勞主動推出減價優惠，必然有相應的食材準備以滿足增加的需求量。銷量增加並不代表市場處於供不應求的「短缺」狀態。

Incorrect. Shortage has a strict definition in economics: at a specific price, quantity demanded exceeds quantity supplied ($Q_d > Q_s$). McDonald's voluntarily launched a discount and would have prepared ingredients to meet the increased quantity demanded. An increase in sales volume does not mean the market is in a state of "shortage".

🧩 題目拆解 / Problem Decomposition

做這類題目時，我們應該如何拆解？

How should we break down this type of question?
  1. 找出關鍵轉變 (Identify the Pivot Point): 題目唯一的轉變是：價錢由 $11 下跌到 $5。這是「物品自身價格下降」。
    The only change is: Price dropped from $11 to $5. This is a "decrease in the good's own price".
  2. 過濾干擾資訊 (Filter Distractors): 「$10兩個」只是用來包裝降價的說法。核心事實依然是 $P \downarrow, Q_d \uparrow$。
    "$10 for 2" is just packaging for a price cut. The core fact remains $P \downarrow, Q_d \uparrow$.
  3. 連接理論 (Connect to Theory): 看到價格改變，立刻聯想起兩個考點：(1) 需求 vs 需求量；(2) 價格彈性與總收益的關係。
    When seeing a price change, immediately recall two exam focuses: (1) Demand vs Quantity Demanded; (2) The relationship between Price Elasticity and Total Revenue.

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 / Common Mistakes & Traps

陷阱一：混淆 Demand 與 Quantity Demanded
每年都有大量考生死於這個陷阱！緊記：只有「非價格因素」(Non-price factors，例如收入增加、替代品價格上升) 才會令「需求」(Demand) 上升。自身價格改變只影響「需求量」(Quantity Demanded)。選項B就是利用這點來淘汰粗心的學生。

Trap 1: Confusing Demand with Quantity Demanded
Many candidates fall for this every year! Remember: Only "non-price factors" (e.g., an increase in income, an increase in the price of substitutes) can shift "Demand". A change in its own price only affects "Quantity Demanded". Option B uses this to filter out careless students.

🙋‍♂️ 學生常見疑問 / Student Q&A

Q: 為什麼麥當勞要減價？減價不是會賺少一點嗎？ (Why did McDonald's cut the price? Wouldn't they earn less?)

A: 不一定！這正是選項C考核的概念。如果大眾對豬柳蛋漢堡的需求是「高彈性」(Elastic) 的，代表消費者對價格非常敏感。麥當勞稍微減價，就能吸引大量顧客購買，增加的銷量足以彌補單個漢堡利潤的下降，最終令「總收益」上升。

A: Not necessarily! This is exactly what Option C tests. If the demand is "elastic", it means consumers are very sensitive to price. A slight price drop by McDonald's can attract a massive number of customers. The increase in sales volume more than compensates for the drop in per-unit profit, ultimately leading to an increase in "Total Revenue".

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 / Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

在DSE MC卷中，時間就是分數。當你看到題目描述「減價」或「加價」時：

  • 秒殺技巧 1： 看到選項寫「需求 (Demand)」上升/下降，立刻打交叉 ❌。
  • 秒殺技巧 2： 看到選項寫「沒有爭取最高利潤 (not maximizing profit)」，立刻打交叉 ❌，因為這違反了經濟學最底層的假設。
  • 剩下的一定是跟彈性 (Elasticity) 或總收益 (Total Revenue) 有關的選項。
In DSE MC papers, time is score. When you see a question describing a "price cut" or "price increase":
- Shortcut 1: Immediately cross out ❌ any option saying "Demand" increases/decreases.
- Shortcut 2: Immediately cross out ❌ any option stating a firm is "not maximizing profit", as this violates the most fundamental postulate of economics.
- The remaining valid options will almost always relate to Elasticity or Total Revenue.
