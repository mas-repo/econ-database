📊 Topic: Theory of the Firm - Cost & Profit Maximization (廠商理論：成本與利潤極大化)

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Key Concepts (核心概念): Marginal Cost (邊際成本), Profit Maximization (利潤極大化), Short Run vs Long Run (短期與長期), Economies of Scale (規模經濟)

📝 The Question (題目)

下表顯示一家利潤極大化廠商其成本與產出的關係。該廠商的固定成本是$5。

The following table shows the cost-output relationship of a profit-maximising firm. The firm has a fixed cost of $5.

產出 Output (units) 1 2 3 4 5 6
平均成本 Average cost ($) 7 6 7 8 9.6 12

下列哪項陳述是正確的？

Which of the following statements is correct?

A. 最優的生產規模是2單位。
B. 在生產第三個單位的產出後，該廠商出現規模負經濟。
C. 若市價是$8，產出是4單位。
D. 若市價是$9，利潤是$6。

A. The optimal scale of production is 2 units.
B. There exist diseconomies of scale after the third unit of output is produced.
C. If the market price is $8, the output is 4 units.
D. If the market price is $9, the profit is $6.

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 總成本與邊際成本 (Total Cost and Marginal Cost): 要找出利潤極大化的產量，我們必須先計算總成本 (TC) 和邊際成本 (MC)。

1. Total Cost and Marginal Cost: To find the profit-maximizing output, we must first calculate Total Cost (TC) and Marginal Cost (MC).

$$TC = AC \times Q$$ $$MC = \frac{\Delta TC}{\Delta Q}$$

2. 利潤極大化原則 (Profit Maximization Rule): 在完全競爭市場中（假設廠商是受價者），廠商會生產直到價格等於邊際成本為止。

2. Profit Maximization Rule: In a perfectly competitive market (assuming the firm is a price taker), a firm produces up to the point where Price equals Marginal Cost.

$$P = MC$$

3. 短期與長期 (Short Run vs Long Run): 題目提到「固定成本」(Fixed Cost)，這代表這是一個短期的情況。「生產規模」(Scale of production) 和「規模經濟」(Economies of scale) 都是長期概念。

3. Short Run vs Long Run: The question mentions "Fixed Cost", which implies this is a short-run scenario. "Scale of production" and "Economies of scale" are strictly long-run concepts.

🛠️ Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的「破局點」在於擴展數據表。不要只看著平均成本 (AC) 猜答案，必須立刻計算出總成本 (TC) 和邊際成本 (MC)。注意：當產出 \(Q = 0\) 時，總成本 \(TC = FC = \$5\)。

The "pivot point" for this type of question is expanding the data table. Do not guess the answer just by looking at Average Cost (AC). You must immediately calculate Total Cost (TC) and Marginal Cost (MC). Note: When output \(Q = 0\), \(TC = FC = \$5\).

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: D

Option A - Incorrect (錯誤)

「最優生產規模」(Optimal scale of production) 是指長期平均成本曲線 (LRAC) 的最低點。由於存在固定成本，這是短期數據。我們只能說 \(Q=2\) 是「最適度產能」(Optimum capacity)，不能說是生產規模。這是考評局常見的文字陷阱。

"Optimal scale of production" refers to the minimum point of the Long-Run Average Cost (LRAC) curve. Since there is a fixed cost, this is short-run data. We can only say \(Q=2\) is the "optimum capacity", not the optimal scale. This is a common terminology trap.

Option B - Incorrect (錯誤)

「規模負經濟」(Diseconomies of scale) 同樣是長期概念。短期內平均成本上升，是因為「邊際回報遞減定律」(Law of Diminishing Marginal Returns)，而不是規模負經濟。

"Diseconomies of scale" is also a long-run concept. The increase in average cost in the short run is due to the "Law of Diminishing Marginal Returns", not diseconomies of scale.

Option C - Incorrect (錯誤)

根據 \(P = MC\) 原則，若市價 \(P = \$8\)，廠商會比較每單位的邊際成本。第2單位的 \(MC = \$5\) (小於$8，生產)，第3單位的 \(MC = \$9\) (大於$8，不生產)。因此，產出應為2單位，而非4單位。

According to the \(P = MC\) rule, if the market price \(P = \$8\), the firm compares it with MC. The 2nd unit's \(MC = \$5\) (< $8, produce), and the 3rd unit's \(MC = \$9\) (> $8, do not produce). Therefore, output should be 2 units, not 4.

Option D - Correct (正確)

若市價 \(P = \$9\)，廠商會生產到 \(P = MC\) 的位置。查看表格，第3單位的 \(MC = \$9\)，所以利潤極大化的產量是 \(Q = 3\)。
總收入 \(TR = P \times Q = \$9 \times 3 = \$27\)。
總成本 \(TC = \$21\) (從表格得知)。
利潤 = \(TR - TC = \$27 - \$21 = \$6\)。計算完全正確！

If market price \(P = \$9\), the firm produces where \(P = MC\). Looking at the table, the 3rd unit's \(MC = \$9\), so the profit-maximizing output is \(Q = 3\).
Total Revenue \(TR = P \times Q = \$9 \times 3 = \$27\).
Total Cost \(TC = \$21\) (from the table).
Profit = \(TR - TC = \$27 - \$21 = \$6\). The calculation is perfectly correct!

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

1. 忘記固定成本： 很多學生在計算第一單位的邊際成本時，直接用 \(TC(1) - 0 = 7\)。這是錯的！因為當 \(Q=0\) 時，\(TC = FC = 5\)。所以第一單位的 \(MC = 7 - 5 = 2\)。

1. Forgetting Fixed Cost: Many students calculate the MC of the first unit as \(TC(1) - 0 = 7\). This is wrong! When \(Q=0\), \(TC = FC = 5\). So the MC of the first unit is \(7 - 5 = 2\).

2. 混淆長短期概念： 看到「固定成本」就要立刻在腦海中亮起紅燈：這是短期！任何選項帶有「規模」(Scale) 字眼的，極大機會是錯誤的干擾項。

2. Confusing Short/Long Run Concepts: Seeing "Fixed Cost" should immediately trigger a red light: this is the Short Run! Any option containing the word "Scale" is highly likely to be an incorrect distractor.

🙋 Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼選項 C 中，當價格是 $8 時，我們不生產第 3 單位？即使 MC 是 $9，平均成本也才 $7 啊，不是還有賺嗎？

Q: For Option C, when the price is $8, why don't we produce the 3rd unit? Even though MC is $9, the Average Cost is only $7, so aren't we still making a profit?

A: 這是一個非常好的問題！雖然生產 3 單位的總利潤仍然是正數（TR=$24, TC=$21, 利潤=$3），但我們的目標是「利潤極大化」。如果你只生產 2 單位（TR=$16, TC=$12, 利潤=$4），你的利潤會更高！因為第 3 單位的生產成本 ($9) 高於它帶來的收入 ($8)，生產它反而會拉低你的總利潤。這就是為什麼我們必須看「邊際成本」而不是「平均成本」來決定產量。

A: This is an excellent question! While total profit is still positive at 3 units (TR=$24, TC=$21, Profit=$3), our goal is "profit maximization". If you only produce 2 units (TR=$16, TC=$12, Profit=$4), your profit is higher! Because the cost of producing that specific 3rd unit ($9) is higher than the revenue it brings in ($8), producing it actually drags down your total profit. This is why we must look at Marginal Cost, not Average Cost, to determine output.
