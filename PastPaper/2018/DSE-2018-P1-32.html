HKDSE Economics / DSE 經濟

Topic: Money & Banking (貨幣與銀行) Difficulty: ★★★★☆ Key Concepts: Money Supply, Reserves, Multiplier
The Question (題目)

參考有關某經濟的以下資料：

貨幣供應 $35 000
實際儲備比率 25%
超額儲備 $1 150

假設公眾總是持有現金$12 000。若所有銀行借出其超額儲備，而所有貸款均再存入銀行系統，則

Refer to the following information about an economy.

Money supply $35 000
Actual reserve ratio 25%
Excess reserves $1 150

Suppose the public always holds $12 000 cash. If all banks loan out their excess reserves and all the loans are re-deposited into the banking system,

  • A. 貨幣供應的最大可能金額會是$28 750。
    the maximum possible amount of money supply will be $28 750.
  • B. 該經濟的貨幣基礎會是$16 600。
    the monetary base of the economy will be $16 600.
  • C. 貸款的最大可能金額會是$23 000。
    the maximum possible amount of loans will be $23 000.
  • D. 最大銀行乘數會是4。
    the maximum banking multiplier will be 4.
Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 貨幣供應定義 (Money Supply Definitions):

$$M_s = C_p + D$$

Where \(M_s\) is Money Supply, \(C_p\) is Cash held by public, and \(D\) is Deposits.

2. 儲備與比率 (Reserves & Ratios):

$$R_a = RR + ER$$
$$r_a = \frac{R_a}{D} \quad , \quad r_r = \frac{RR}{D}$$

\(R_a\): Actual Reserves, \(RR\): Required Reserves, \(ER\): Excess Reserves.
\(r_a\): Actual Reserve Ratio, \(r_r\): Required Reserve Ratio.

3. 最大存款創造 (Maximum Deposit Creation):

當銀行借出所有超額儲備時，實際儲備比率會下降至法定儲備比率 (\(r_a \rightarrow r_r\))。

When banks lend out all excess reserves, the actual reserve ratio drops to the required reserve ratio.

$$D_{max} = \frac{R}{r_r}$$
Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)
Correct Option: C
Step 1: Calculate Initial Situation (計算初始狀態)

首先，我們需要找出初始的存款額 (\(D\)) 和法定儲備比率 (\(r_r\))。

First, we need to find the initial Deposits (\(D\)) and the Required Reserve Ratio (\(r_r\)).

$$M_s = C_p + D \Rightarrow 35,000 = 12,000 + D$$ $$\therefore D = 23,000$$

接著計算實際儲備 (\(R_a\))：

Next, calculate Actual Reserves (\(R_a\)):

$$R_a = D \times r_a = 23,000 \times 25\% = 5,750$$

找出法定儲備 (\(RR\)) 與法定儲備比率 (\(r_r\))：

Find Required Reserves (\(RR\)) and Required Reserve Ratio (\(r_r\)):

$$R_a = RR + ER \Rightarrow 5,750 = RR + 1,150$$ $$\therefore RR = 4,600$$ $$r_r = \frac{RR}{D} = \frac{4,600}{23,000} = 0.2 \text{ (or 20\%)}$$

關鍵發現：銀行系統擴張的極限是由 20% 的法定比率決定的，而不是 25%。

Key Finding: The limit of banking expansion is determined by the 20% required ratio, not 25%.

Step 2: Analyze Each Option (分析各選項)

Option A: WRONG

最大存款 (\(D_{max}\)) = \(\frac{R}{r_r} = \frac{5,750}{0.2} = 28,750\)。
最大貨幣供應 (\(M_{max}\)) = \(C_p + D_{max} = 12,000 + 28,750 = 40,750\)。
選項 A 混淆了存款與貨幣供應。

Max Deposits (\(D_{max}\)) = 28,750. Max Money Supply is \(C_p + D_{max} = 40,750\). Option A confuses Deposits with Money Supply.

Option B: WRONG

貨幣基礎 (\(MB\)) = \(C_p + R\)。在借貸過程中，儲備留在銀行體系內，現金持有量不變，故 \(MB\) 不變。
\(MB = 12,000 + 5,750 = 17,750\)。

Monetary Base (\(MB\)) = \(C_p + R\). During lending, reserves stay in the system and cash holding is constant, so \(MB\) is unchanged. \(MB = 17,750\).

Option C: CORRECT

資產負債表恆等式：\(R + L = D\)。
在最大擴張時：\(R = 5,750\)，\(D_{max} = 28,750\)。
\(L_{max} = D_{max} - R = 28,750 - 5,750 = 23,000\)。

Balance Sheet Identity: \(R + L = D\). At max expansion: \(R = 5,750\), \(D_{max} = 28,750\). So \(L_{max} = 23,000\).

Option D: WRONG

最大銀行乘數 = \(\frac{1}{r_r} = \frac{1}{0.2} = 5\)。
選項 D 使用了實際儲備比率 (25%) 計算，這是錯誤的。

Max Banking Multiplier = \(\frac{1}{r_r} = 5\). Option D incorrectly uses the actual reserve ratio (25%).

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的「破題點」(Pivot Point) 在於找出隱藏的法定儲備比率 (Required Reserve Ratio)。

The Pivot Point of this question is finding the hidden Required Reserve Ratio.

邏輯鏈 (Logic Chain):

  1. 題目給了「實際比率」和「超額儲備金額」。 (Given Actual Ratio & Excess Amount)
  2. 這暗示：實際比率 \(\neq\) 法定比率。 (Implies Actual \(\neq\) Required)
  3. 必須先算出金額，相減得到法定儲備，再算出法定比率。 (Must calculate amounts, subtract to get Required Reserves, then find the Required Ratio.)
  4. 所有「最大值」(Max) 的計算都必須使用法定比率 (0.2)，而非實際比率 (0.25)。 (All "Max" calculations must use the Required Ratio.)
Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)
  • 混淆 \(M_s\) 與 \(D\) (Confusing \(M_s\) and \(D\)):

    學生常算出最大存款 \(28,750\) 後就直接選 A，忘記加上現金 \(12,000\)。

    Students often calculate Max Deposits ($28,750) and pick A, forgetting to add Cash ($12,000).

  • 誤用實際儲備比率 (Using Actual Reserve Ratio):

    計算乘數時直接用 \(1/0.25 = 4\)。記住：乘數是基於法定要求的限制。

    Calculating multiplier as \(1/0.25 = 4\). Remember: The multiplier is based on the legal requirement limit.

  • 誤解貨幣基礎 (Misunderstanding Monetary Base):

    以為借貸創造貨幣會改變貨幣基礎。事實上，沒有央行介入，\(MB\) 保持不變。

    Thinking credit creation changes the Monetary Base. Actually, without Central Bank intervention, \(MB\) remains constant.
