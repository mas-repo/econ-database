📊 HKDSE Economics Challenge

主題 (Topic): 勞力供應與勞動人口 (Labour Supply and Working Population)

難度 (Difficulty): ★★☆☆☆ (2/5)

關鍵概念 (Key Concepts): 勞力供應 (Labour Supply) 工作時數 (Working Hours) 從事經濟活動人口 (Economically Active Population)

1. 題目 (The Question)

若 __________，香港的勞力供應會增加。

The labour supply in Hong Kong would increase if __________.

A. 更多香港人移民外國
more Hong Kong people migrated to foreign countries.
B. 更多香港學生入讀大專院校
more Hong Kong students studied in tertiary institutions.
C. 政府降低「優才入境計劃」的資歷要求
the Government lowered the qualification requirements for the Quality Migrant Admission Scheme.
D. 所有在職父親可多得兩天的侍產假
all working fathers were allowed to take two extra days of paternity leave.

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

💡 什麼是勞力供應？ (What is Labour Supply?)

勞力供應是指在一定時間內，人們願意且能夠提供的總工作時數。這不單單取決於工人的人數，還取決於他們工作的時間長度。

Labour supply refers to the total amount of working hours that people are willing and able to provide in a given period. It depends not only on the number of workers but also on how long they work.

$$\text{Labour Supply} = \text{Number of Workers} \times \text{Average Working Hours per Worker}$$

影響勞力供應的主要因素 (Key Factors Affecting Labour Supply):

  • 勞動人口數量 (Size of Working Population): 受出生率、死亡率、移民出入境 (migration) 等影響。
  • 法定年齡與教育 (Legal Age & Education): 更多人繼續升學會延遲進入勞動市場，短期內減少勞力供應。全職學生屬於非從事經濟活動人口。
  • 工作時數 (Working Hours): 假期增加（如侍產假、法定假日）會減少平均工作時數，從而減少勞力供應總量。

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option)： Option C

A. 更多香港人移民外國 (more Hong Kong people migrated to foreign countries.) ❌

人口外流會直接減少本港的勞動人口（即上述公式中的 Number of Workers 下降），導致勞力供應減少，而非增加。

Emigration directly reduces the working population in Hong Kong (a decrease in the Number of Workers in the formula), which leads to a decrease in labour supply, not an increase.

B. 更多香港學生入讀大專院校 (more Hong Kong students studied in tertiary institutions.) ❌

全職學生屬於「非從事經濟活動人口」。更多人選擇繼續升學，意味著他們會延遲進入勞動市場，這會在短期內減少市場上的勞動力數量。

Full-time students are classified as "economically inactive population". More people choosing to pursue tertiary education means they delay their entry into the labour market, thus reducing the available labour force in the short term.

C. 政府降低「優才入境計劃」的資歷要求 (the Government lowered the qualification requirements for the Quality Migrant Admission Scheme.) ✅

降低要求會吸引更多外地人才透過該計劃移居香港。這會增加香港的整體勞動人口數量（Number of Workers 上升），從而增加勞力供應。

Lowering the requirements would attract more foreign talents to move to Hong Kong through the scheme. This increases the overall size of the working population in Hong Kong (Number of Workers increases), thereby increasing the labour supply.

D. 所有在職父親可多得兩天的侍產假 (all working fathers were allowed to take two extra days of paternity leave.) ❌

假期增加意味著每位在職父親的平均工作時數減少（Average Working Hours 下降）。根據公式，在人數不變的情況下，總工時下降代表勞力供應減少。

More holidays mean a decrease in the average working hours per working father (Average Working Hours decreases). According to the formula, with the number of workers unchanged, a drop in total man-hours means a decrease in labour supply.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

核心考點 (The Pivot Point):
這題的關鍵在於你能否準確分辨什麼因素會影響勞力供應的「質」(Quality) 與「量」(Quantity)。勞力供應是指「量」（總工時）。要增加勞力供應，我們必須尋找能增加「工作人數」或「工作時數」的選項。

The key to solving this is distinguishing factors that affect the "Quality" versus "Quantity" of labour. Labour supply refers to "Quantity" (total man-hours). To increase it, we must look for an option that increases either the "number of workers" or "working hours".

干擾項分析 (Distractor Analysis):
選項 A、B、D 實際上都會減少勞力供應。出題者故意加入了「入讀大專院校」和「侍產假」這類聽起來很「正面」的政策，來測試學生是否會把「社會福利 / 勞工質素」與「勞動力數量」混淆。

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • 陷阱一：混淆「勞工質素」與「勞力供應」。很多學生誤選 B，因為他們覺得讀大學會令工人更聰明、生產力更高。這沒錯，但這屬於人力資本 (Human Capital) 和勞工生產力 (Labour Productivity) 的提升，而不是勞力「供應量」的增加。
  • 陷阱二：忽略「工時」的影響。學生容易以為「工人開心就會做多點事」，誤選 D。但在經濟學定義中，放假等同於不工作，直接扣減了可用的工作時數 (Man-hours)。

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q1: 如果選項 B 的學生畢業了，勞力供應不就增加了嗎？為什麼 B 是錯的？

A: 經濟學題目通常考慮的是「即時/短期影響」 (Immediate/Short-run effect)。當學生「正在」入讀大專院校時，他們退出了勞動市場（不找工作），因此當下的勞力供應是減少的。我們不能用幾年後的未來情況來回答當前的政策影響。

Q1: Wouldn't the labour supply increase once the students in Option B graduate? Why is B wrong?
A: Economics questions typically consider the "immediate/short-run effect". While students are "currently" studying, they withdraw from the labour market (not seeking jobs), so the current labour supply decreases. We shouldn't answer based on hypothetical future outcomes.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

🎯 方向排除法 (Directional Elimination):

在考場上，你可以快速在每個選項旁邊畫上箭頭 (↑ 或 ↓) 來表示對勞動力的影響方向：

  • A: 人走咗 -> 勞動力 ↓
  • B: 讀書唔返工 -> 勞動力 ↓
  • C: 外來人才加入 -> 勞動力 ↑
  • D: 放假唔做嘢 -> 總工時 ↓ -> 勞動力 ↓

選項 C 是唯一一個方向向上的選項，一秒鐘就能鎖定正確答案！

In the exam, quickly draw arrows (↑ or ↓) next to options to indicate their impact direction. A, B, and D all remove people/hours from the workforce (↓). C is the only option bringing resources in (↑). You can spot the odd one out instantly!
