📚 HKDSE Economics Analysis

📌 Topic: Types of Integration / Motives of Expansion (廠商擴張的動機 / 結合種類) ⭐ Difficulty: 2.5 / 5
🔑 Key Concepts: Lateral Integration (側向結合) 🔑 Economies of Scale (規模經濟) 🔑 Factor Inputs (要素投入)

1. The Question (題目)

日本餐飲龍頭企業「東利多控股」收購了本港麵食連鎖店譚仔雲南米線。下列哪項最不可能是該擴張背後的動機？

Toridoll Holdings Corporation, a leading company in the restaurant industry in Japan, acquired a Hong Kong noodle chain Tam Chai Yunnan Noodles (譚仔雲南米線). Which of the following is the LEAST likely motive behind the integration?

  1. 確保要素投入的供應
    to secure the supply of factor inputs
  2. 藉產品多元化分散風險
    to spread risk through product diversification
  3. 享有規模經濟
    to enjoy economies of scale
  4. 將業務擴展至其他市場
    to expand its business to other markets

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

Types of Expansion (擴張的種類)

  • 橫向結合 (Horizontal Integration): 合併生產同類產品的競爭者。目的：增加市場佔有率。
    Merging with competitors producing the same products. Motive: Increase market share.
  • 側向結合 (Lateral Integration): 合併生產相關但非直接競爭產品的企業。目的：產品多元化、分散風險。
    Merging with firms producing related but non-competing products. Motive: Product diversification, spreading risks.
  • 垂直結合 (Vertical Integration):
    • 向後 (Backward): 收購供應商以確保原料（要素投入）供應。
      Acquiring suppliers to secure raw materials (factor inputs).
    • 向前 (Forward): 收購分銷商以確保銷售渠道。
      Acquiring distributors to secure retail outlets.

Economies of Scale (規模經濟)

當企業的生產規模擴大，導致長期平均成本下降的現象。
The phenomenon where a firm's long-run average cost decreases as its scale of production increases.

$$AC = \frac{TC}{Q}$$

Where $AC$ is Average Cost, $TC$ is Total Cost, and $Q$ is Output.
在規模經濟下，當 $Q$ 上升，長期 $AC$ 會下降。

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): Option A (因為題目問「最不可能 LEAST likely」)

A. 確保要素投入的供應 (to secure the supply of factor inputs) - LEAST LIKELY

「確保要素投入的供應」是向後垂直結合 (Backward Vertical Integration) 的動機。譚仔雲南米線是一間直接向消費者提供餐飲服務的餐廳，它並不是生產食材（如麵粉、蔬菜、肉類等）的供應商。因此，東利多收購譚仔並不能為其原本的日本餐廳業務確保原材料供應。這是不合理的動機。

"Securing the supply of factor inputs" is a motive for backward vertical integration. Tam Chai is a restaurant chain serving end-consumers; it is NOT a supplier of raw materials (like flour, vegetables, or meat). Therefore, acquiring Tam Chai does not help Toridoll secure raw materials for its existing Japanese restaurants. This is an invalid motive.

B. 藉產品多元化分散風險 (to spread risk through product diversification) - LIKELY

日本料理與雲南米線屬於不同類型的餐飲產品。這種擴張（側向結合）讓公司不再單一依賴日本食品市場，能夠達到產品多元化並分散投資風險。

Japanese cuisine and Yunnan noodles are different types of F&B products. This expansion (lateral integration) prevents the company from relying solely on the Japanese food market, achieving product diversification and spreading investment risks.

C. 享有規模經濟 (to enjoy economies of scale) - LIKELY

合併後，擴大了的企業實體可以享有管理規模經濟（共用後勤或管理團隊）或財務規模經濟（以更低利率借款）。這會降低整體平均成本 $AC$。

After the merger, the enlarged corporate entity can enjoy managerial economies of scale (sharing back-office/management teams) or financial economies of scale (borrowing at lower interest rates). This reduces the overall average cost $AC$.

D. 將業務擴展至其他市場 (to expand its business to other markets) - LIKELY

東利多原本的基地在日本，而譚仔主要在香港營運。收購譚仔顯然能讓該日本企業迅速打入香港（甚至中國大陸及海外）的餐飲市場。

Toridoll was originally based in Japan, while Tam Chai operates mainly in Hong Kong. Acquiring Tam Chai obviously allows the Japanese firm to swiftly penetrate the F&B market in Hong Kong (and potentially mainland China and overseas).

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

  • Pivot Point (解題樞紐): 辦認兩間公司的業務性質及供應鏈位置。你必須意識到「餐廳」與「餐廳」之間的收購，不是為了獲取原材料（它們都不是農場或食材加工廠）。
    Identify the business nature and supply chain position of both firms. You must realize that an acquisition between two "restaurants" is not for obtaining raw materials (neither is a farm or food processing plant).
  • Distractors (干擾資訊): 題目強調了「日本」與「本港」，這引導你確認選項 D 是正確的動機（地理上的擴張），從而排除 D 成為答案。
    The question emphasizes "Japan" and "Hong Kong". This guides you to confirm Option D as a valid motive (geographical expansion), thus eliminating D as the answer.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 誤解「要素投入」(Misunderstanding "Factor Inputs"): 很多同學以為收購任何公司都會增加資源。但「要素投入的供應」特指生產過程所需的原材料或半製成品。除非被收購的是農田或製麵廠，否則不能選 A。
    Many students think acquiring any company adds resources. However, "supply of factor inputs" specifically refers to raw materials or semi-finished goods needed for production. Unless the acquired firm is a farm or a noodle factory, A is not a valid motive.
  • 忽略「最不可能」(Ignoring the "LEAST likely"): 考試時太緊張，看到 B、C、D 都是合理的商業好處就亂選，忘記了題目要求找出不合理的一項。
    Being too nervous during the exam, seeing B, C, and D as valid business benefits and picking one randomly, forgetting the question asks for the invalid one.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 既然它們賣不同種類的食物，為什麼還會有「規模經濟」(Economies of Scale)？它們又不能共用同一種食材！

Q: Since they sell different types of food, why would there be "economies of scale"? They can't share the same food ingredients!

A: 規模經濟不只限於「採購經濟」(Purchasing economies)。雖然食材不同，但母公司在合併後規模變得極大，它可以享有財務經濟 (Financial economies)（大公司更容易向銀行借取低息貸款）及管理經濟 (Managerial economies)（例如共用同一套人力資源系統或會計軟件）。這些都能有效降低長期平均成本 $AC$。
A: Economies of scale are not limited to "purchasing economies". Although ingredients differ, the combined parent company becomes much larger, allowing it to enjoy financial economies (large firms borrow at lower interest rates) and managerial economies (e.g., sharing the same HR system or accounting software). These effectively reduce the long-run average cost $AC$.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

💡 速解法 (Shortcut):

在經濟學多項選擇題中，一看到「確保要素投入 / secure factor inputs」，大腦應該立刻條件反射連結到「向後垂直結合 / Backward Vertical Integration」。一旦確認題目的兩間公司處於同一生產階段（大家都是直接面對客人的零售餐廳），這句選項必定是錯的（即「最不可能」的答案）。

In Economics MCQs, whenever you see "secure factor inputs", your brain should immediately link it to "Backward Vertical Integration". Once you confirm both companies are at the same stage of production (both are retail restaurants facing end-consumers), this option must be factually wrong in the context (hence, the "LEAST likely" answer).
