HKDSE Economics Study Guide

Topic (課題): Market Equilibrium & Shifts in Curves (市場均衡與供求曲線移動)
Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆
Key Concepts (關鍵概念): Supply (供給) Demand (需求) Technological Advance (技術進步) Equilibrium Point (均衡點)

The Question (題目)

下圖顯示空氣清新機的供需曲線。原初的均衡點是E。

假設空氣清新機的生產技術有所提升。同時，平均空氣污染指數近十年不斷上升。新的均衡點是 ____。

  1. E1
  2. E2
  3. E3
  4. E4

The diagram below shows the supply and demand curves of air-purifiers. The original equilibrium point is E.

Suppose there is a technological advance in producing air-purifiers. At the same time, the average air pollution index has been increasing over the last decade. The new equilibrium point will be at ____________.

  1. E1
  2. E2
  3. E3
  4. E4

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解決這類題目，我們需要分別分析事件對「需求 (Demand)」和「供給 (Supply)」的影響，並找出兩條新曲線的相交點。

To solve this type of question, we need to analyze the impact of the events on "Demand" and "Supply" separately, and find the intersection point of the two new curves.

  • 供給的增加 (Increase in Supply): 當生產成本下降或生產技術進步時，即使價格不變，生產者也願意提供更多產品。供給曲線會向右/下移動 \( S_0 \rightarrow S_1 \)。
    When production costs decrease or technology advances, producers are willing to supply more at the same price. The supply curve shifts to the right/down \( S_0 \rightarrow S_1 \).
  • 需求的增加 (Increase in Demand): 當消費者對產品的偏好增加（例如因為污染嚴重而更需要空氣清新機），即使價格不變，消費者也願意購買更多。需求曲線會向右/上移動 \( D_0 \rightarrow D_1 \)。
    When consumer preference for a product increases (e.g., needing air purifiers due to severe pollution), consumers are willing to buy more at the same price. The demand curve shifts to the right/up \( D_0 \rightarrow D_1 \).
Equilibrium Condition (均衡條件): $$ Q_d = Q_s $$

(Quantity Demanded = Quantity Supplied 需求量 = 供給量)

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): A

Step 1: 分析生產技術提升 (Analyze technological advance)
生產技術提升會降低生產成本，導致供給增加。圖表上的供給曲線會由原來的 \( S_0 \) 向右移至 \( S_1 \)。
Technological advance lowers production costs, leading to an increase in supply. The supply curve shifts to the right from \( S_0 \) to \( S_1 \).

Step 2: 分析空氣污染指數上升 (Analyze rising air pollution index)
空氣污染加劇會令大眾對空氣清新機的需求增加。圖表上的需求曲線會由原來的 \( D_0 \) 向右移至 \( D_1 \)。
Worsening air pollution increases public demand for air-purifiers. The demand curve shifts to the right from \( D_0 \) to \( D_1 \).

Step 3: 尋找新均衡點 (Locate the new equilibrium)
新的市場均衡點會是新的需求曲線 \( D_1 \) 與新的供給曲線 \( S_1 \) 的相交點。觀察圖表，\( D_1 \) 和 \( S_1 \) 相交於點 \( E_1 \)。
The new market equilibrium is the intersection of the new demand curve \( D_1 \) and the new supply curve \( S_1 \). Looking at the graph, \( D_1 \) and \( S_1 \) intersect at point \( E_1 \).

Distractor Analysis (干擾選項分析):

Option B (E2): 這是 \( D_1 \) 與 \( S_2 \) 的相交點。這代表需求增加，但供給減少了。與題目描述不符。
This is the intersection of \( D_1 \) and \( S_2 \). It represents an increase in demand but a decrease in supply. This contradicts the question.
Option C (E3): 這是 \( D_2 \) 與 \( S_2 \) 的相交點。這代表需求與供給雙雙減少了。與題目描述完全相反。
This is the intersection of \( D_2 \) and \( S_2 \). It represents a decrease in both demand and supply. This is exactly opposite to the question.
Option D (E4): 這是 \( D_2 \) 與 \( S_1 \) 的相交點。這代表供給增加，但需求減少了。與污染加劇的條件不符。
This is the intersection of \( D_2 \) and \( S_1 \). It represents an increase in supply but a decrease in demand. This contradicts the condition of worsening pollution.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的核心在於「翻譯」文字為圖像移動 (Translating text into graphical shifts)。

  • Pivot Point 1 (關鍵點 1): 「生產技術提升 (technological advance)」= 生產更容易/便宜 = Supply Shift Right (\( S_0 \rightarrow S_1 \)).
  • Pivot Point 2 (關鍵點 2): 「污染上升 (pollution increasing)」= 產品變得更必需 = Demand Shift Right (\( D_0 \rightarrow D_1 \)).
  • Action (行動): 只需要用手指在圖上跟著新線 \( S_1 \) 和 \( D_1 \)，找出它們相遇的點即可。

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

1. 混淆「上下」與「左右」 (Confusing Up/Down with Left/Right):
很多學生會直覺認為「增加」就是「向上」。對於需求曲線 \( D \) 來說，向右移剛好也是向上移；但對於供給曲線 \( S \) 來說，供給增加是向「右」移，看起來在圖表上是向「下」移的。如果誤以為供給增加是向上移（移向 \( S_2 \)），就會錯誤選擇答案 B 或 C。
Many students intuitively think "increase" means "moving up". For the demand curve \( D \), shifting right also looks like shifting up. However, for the supply curve \( S \), an increase in supply is a shift to the "right", which visually looks like shifting "down" on the graph. If you mistakenly think supply increase shifts the curve up (towards \( S_2 \)), you will incorrectly choose B or C. Always use Left/Right for shifts!

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 在這條題目中，新均衡點 E1 的價格 (Price) 好像跟原來 E 的價格差不多，這是一定的嗎？
(Is the price at the new equilibrium E1 always similar to the original E?)
A: 不一定！當需求和供給同時增加時，交易量 (Quantity) 必定會增加。但是，價格 (Price) 的變動是不確定的 (Ambiguous)。這取決於兩條曲線移動的幅度。如果需求增加的幅度大於供給，價格會上升；如果供給增加的幅度大於需求，價格會下降。題目中的圖剛好畫成兩者移動幅度相若，所以價格看似不變，但這不是必然的規律。
Not necessarily! When both demand and supply increase, the Quantity will definitely increase. However, the change in Price is ambiguous. It depends on the magnitude of the shifts. If demand increases more than supply, price rises. If supply increases more than demand, price falls. The graph just happens to show similar shift magnitudes, but it's not a universal rule.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

畫筆標記法 (The Arrow Method):
在選擇題考試中，不要只靠大腦想像。直接用鉛筆在試卷上畫箭嘴！
1. 看到「技術提升」 \( \rightarrow \) 在 \( S_0 \) 旁邊畫一個指向右邊的箭嘴，直指 \( S_1 \)。
2. 看到「污染上升」 \( \rightarrow \) 在 \( D_0 \) 旁邊畫一個指向右邊的箭嘴，直指 \( D_1 \)。
3. 眼睛直接鎖定兩個箭頭指向的目標線，它們的交點立刻原形畢露，只需 5 秒鐘就能找到答案 \( E_1 \)，絕不粗心！
In multiple-choice exams, don't rely solely on mental visualization. Draw arrows directly on the paper! Draw a right-pointing arrow from \( S_0 \) to \( S_1 \), and another from \( D_0 \) to \( D_1 \). Your eyes will naturally lock onto the intersection of the targeted lines. It takes 5 seconds and prevents careless errors!
