Topic: 國民收入會計與國際收支平衡 (National Income Accounting & BoP) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: C, X, Balance of Payments

1. 題目 (The Question)

香港旅遊發展局在2017年夏天籌辦了電競音樂節。有外國電競選手和韓國流行巨星參與盛會，吸引了本地觀眾及來自世界各地的遊客到場。籌辦該節目會導致

(1) 香港的私人消費支出增加。
(2) 香港的服務出口增加。
(3) 香港的國際收支平衡逆差下降。

A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

The Hong Kong Tourism Board organised the E-sports and Music Festival in summer 2017. Foreign E-sports players and K-pop superstars participated in the festival and they attracted locals and tourists from other parts of the world to attend the event. Organising the festival will lead to

(1) an increase in private consumption expenditure in Hong Kong.
(2) an increase in export of services in Hong Kong.
(3) a decrease in balance of payment deficit in Hong Kong.

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

私人消費支出 (Private Consumption Expenditure, \(C\)): 本地常住居民對最終物品及服務的消費。
Consumption of final goods and services by domestic residents.

服務出口 (Export of Services, \(X\)): 非本地居民在本地境內的消費（例如遊客在香港的消費）。
Consumption by non-residents within the domestic territory (e.g., tourists spending in HK).

國際收支平衡 (Balance of Payments, BoP): 記錄一個經濟體與世界其他地方之間的所有經濟交易。包含經常帳及資本與金融帳。
A record of all economic transactions between an economy and the rest of the world. It includes the current account and the capital and financial account.

$$ GDP = C + I + G + X - M $$

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): A

(1) 香港的私人消費支出增加 / an increase in private consumption expenditure in Hong Kong. (正確 / Correct)

題目提到「吸引了本地觀眾」。本地居民購買門票及在活動中消費，屬於本地常住居民的消費，因此 \(C\) 會增加。

The question mentions it "attracted locals". When local residents buy tickets and spend at the event, it counts as consumption by domestic residents, so \(C\) increases.

(2) 香港的服務出口增加 / an increase in export of services in Hong Kong. (正確 / Correct)

題目提到「來自世界各地的遊客到場」。非本地居民（遊客）在香港境內的消費（如買門票、住宿、餐飲）被計算為香港的服務出口，因此 \(X\) 會增加。

The question mentions "tourists from other parts of the world". Spending by non-residents (tourists) within Hong Kong (e.g., tickets, hotel, food) is counted as Hong Kong's export of services, so \(X\) increases.

(3) 香港的國際收支平衡逆差下降 / a decrease in balance of payment deficit in Hong Kong. (錯誤 / Incorrect)

這是一個經典陷阱。首先，香港需要支付酬勞給「外國電競選手和韓國流行巨星」，這屬於服務進口或要素收益流出，會導致資金流出。由於我們不知道資金流入（遊客消費）和資金流出（支付外國明星）哪一個較大，因此對國際收支的淨影響是未知的。其次，題目沒有給出香港原本是否處於「逆差」狀態，因此不能斷言逆差一定會下降。

This is a classic trap. First, Hong Kong has to pay the "foreign E-sports players and K-pop superstars", which is an import of services or outflow of factor income, leading to capital outflow. Since we don't know whether the inflow (tourist spending) or outflow (paying foreign stars) is larger, the net effect on BoP is uncertain. Second, the question doesn't state if Hong Kong was initially in a "deficit", so we cannot conclude that a deficit will definitely decrease.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

這類題目的關鍵點 (Pivot Point)在於辨識「誰在消費」以及「錢流向哪裡」。

  • 本地人消費 \(\rightarrow\) 影響 \(C\)
  • 遊客消費 \(\rightarrow\) 影響 \(X\) (服務出口)
  • 聘請外國人 \(\rightarrow\) 影響 \(M\) (進口) 或 要素收益流出

The Pivot Point of such questions is identifying "who is spending" and "where the money goes". Local spending affects \(C\); tourist spending affects \(X\); hiring foreigners affects \(M\) or factor income outflow.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

學生常以為只要有外國遊客來港，國際收支平衡就一定會改善。忽略了舉辦國際活動往往需要支付巨額費用給外國表演者或機構。此外，「逆差下降」這個字眼本身就是一個陷阱，因為我們根本不知道原本的基數是盈餘還是逆差。

Students often assume that an influx of foreign tourists automatically improves the BoP. They ignore that hosting international events usually requires paying huge fees to foreign performers. Also, the phrase "decrease in deficit" is a trap itself because we don't know if the initial baseline was a surplus or a deficit.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼支付給韓國明星的錢不是「物品進口 (Import of Goods)」？
Why isn't the money paid to K-pop stars considered an "Import of Goods"?

A: 因為明星提供的是表演，屬於無形服務 (intangible services) 或勞工服務。因此，這筆支出會被記錄在服務進口 (Import of Services) 或初次收入帳 (Primary Income Account) 的流出中，而不是實體物品的進口。
Because stars provide performances, which are intangible services or labor services. Therefore, this expenditure is recorded as an Import of Services or an outflow in the Primary Income Account, not an import of physical goods.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

「未知狀態」秒殺法 (The "Unknown State" Shortcut):
在 DSE 經濟科中，如果選項提到「逆差下降 (deficit decreases)」或「盈餘增加 (surplus increases)」，請立刻檢查題目有沒有告訴你該經濟體目前的狀態。如果題目隻字未提，這個選項有 99% 是錯的，因為你不能減少一個可能根本不存在的逆差！

In DSE Economics, if an option mentions "deficit decreases" or "surplus increases", immediately check if the question states the current status of the economy. If it doesn't mention it at all, this option is 99% wrong because you cannot decrease a deficit that might not even exist!
