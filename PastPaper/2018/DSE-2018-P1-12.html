HKDSE Economics Tutorial

Topic: Economies of Scale & Market Structure (規模經濟與市場結構) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts: Economies of Scale (規模經濟), Long-Run Average Cost (長期平均成本), Natural Monopoly (自然壟斷), Diminishing Marginal Returns (邊際回報遞減)

2. The Question (題目)

下列哪項有關規模經濟的陳述是正確的？
A. 它可以是壟斷能力的來源。
B. 當平均固定成本下降，規模經濟便會出現。
C. 只有大型廠商才享有規模經濟。
D. 它是邊際回報遞減定律的結果。
Which of the following statements about economies of scale is correct?
A. It could be a source of monopoly power.
B. It exists when the average fixed cost is falling.
C. Only large firms enjoy economies of scale.
D. It is the result of the law of diminishing marginal returns.

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

規模經濟 (Economies of Scale): 指在長期（Long Run）中，當所有生產要素的數量增加（即生產規模擴大）時，長期平均成本（LRAC）隨產量增加而下降的現象。
Economies of Scale: A long-run concept where the Long-Run Average Cost (LRAC) falls as the scale of production (output) increases.
$$ \text{LRAC} = \frac{\text{Total Cost (TC)}}{\text{Quantity (Q)}} $$
自然壟斷 (Natural Monopoly): 如果一個行業具有極大的規模經濟，單一廠商生產整個市場的產量會比多間廠商生產的平均成本更低，這會形成進入障礙（Barrier to entry），從而產生壟斷能力。
Natural Monopoly: If an industry has extensive economies of scale, a single firm can supply the entire market at a lower average cost than two or more firms. This creates a barrier to entry, leading to monopoly power.

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option A

Option A (正確): 龐大的規模經濟會導致長期平均成本持續下降。這使得現有的大型廠商能以極低的成本生產，新競爭者難以進入市場（因為他們的初始產量低，平均成本高）。這形成了進入障礙，是自然壟斷（Natural Monopoly）的來源。
Option A (Correct): Extensive economies of scale cause the LRAC to fall continuously. This allows existing large firms to produce at very low costs, making it difficult for new competitors to enter (as their initial output is low and average cost is high). This creates a barrier to entry and is the source of a natural monopoly.
Option B (錯誤): 平均固定成本 \( \text{AFC} \) 下降是短期的必然現象（因為 \( \text{AFC} = \frac{\text{TFC}}{Q} \)，當 \( Q \) 增加，\( \text{AFC} \) 必定下降）。但規模經濟是指「長期平均成本」\( \text{LRAC} \) 的下降，兩者概念不同。
Option B (Incorrect): Falling Average Fixed Cost \( \text{AFC} \) is a mathematical certainty in the short run (since \( \text{AFC} = \frac{\text{TFC}}{Q} \), as \( Q \) increases, \( \text{AFC} \) must fall). However, economies of scale refer to the fall in Long-Run Average Cost \( \text{LRAC} \), which is a different concept.
Option C (錯誤): 規模經濟看的是「成本變化的趨勢」而非「絕對規模」。小型廠商在擴張初期，也可能因為分工或更有效地使用機器而享有規模經濟。
Option C (Incorrect): Economies of scale refer to the "trend of cost changes" rather than "absolute size". Small firms can also enjoy economies of scale during their initial expansion due to division of labor or more efficient use of machinery.
Option D (錯誤): 邊際回報遞減定律（Law of diminishing marginal returns）是一個「短期」概念（假設至少有一種生產要素是固定的）。而規模經濟是一個「長期」概念（所有生產要素皆可變）。兩者互不相干。
Option D (Incorrect): The law of diminishing marginal returns is a "short-run" concept (assuming at least one fixed factor). Economies of scale is a "long-run" concept (all factors are variable). They are fundamentally different.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point): 區分「短期 (Short Run)」與「長期 (Long Run)」概念。
這道題目的核心在於測試學生是否清楚「規模經濟」的本質。只要看到「規模經濟」，腦海中必須立刻浮現：長期、所有要素可變、LRAC下降。
Pivot Point: Distinguishing between "Short Run" and "Long Run" concepts.
The core of this question tests if students understand the nature of "economies of scale". Whenever you see this term, immediately think: Long Run, all factors variable, falling LRAC.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱一：混淆短期與長期成本。 很多學生會選 B，因為他們知道產量增加會攤薄固定成本。但請記住，長期是沒有固定成本的（No fixed costs in the long run），因此 B 選項的 AFC 屬於短期概念。
陷阱二：絕對字眼。 看到 C 選項的「只有 (Only)」通常要提高警覺，在經濟學中過於絕對的陳述往往是錯誤的。
Trap 1: Confusing Short-Run and Long-Run Costs. Many students choose B because they know increasing output spreads out fixed costs. But remember, there are no fixed costs in the long run, so AFC in Option B is a short-run concept.
Trap 2: Absolute Terms. Be highly suspicious of the word "Only" in Option C. In economics, overly absolute statements are often incorrect distractors.

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼規模經濟會產生壟斷？
A: 想像你要開一家自來水公司。鋪設地下水管的初始成本極高（享有極大的規模經濟）。如果市場上已經有一家大公司，它的產量極大，平均成本極低。你作為新加入者，初期客源少，平均成本極高，根本無法在價格上與大公司競爭，最終只能退出。這就形成了「自然壟斷」。
Q: Why do economies of scale create monopolies?
A: Imagine starting a tap water company. The initial cost of laying underground pipes is enormous (massive economies of scale). If a large company already exists, its output is huge and its average cost is very low. As a new entrant with few initial customers, your average cost would be extremely high. You couldn't compete on price and would be forced to exit. This creates a "Natural Monopoly".

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

排除法 (Elimination Method):
1. 看到「規模經濟」，標記為「長期 (LR)」。
2. 掃視選項：B 提到「固定成本」，D 提到「邊際回報遞減」，這兩個都是「短期 (SR)」概念，直接秒殺排除！
3. C 有「只有 (Only)」這種極端字眼，通常錯。
4. 剩下 A，直接選出正確答案。
Elimination Method:
1. See "Economies of scale", tag it as "Long Run (LR)".
2. Scan options: B mentions "fixed cost", D mentions "diminishing marginal returns". Both are "Short Run (SR)" concepts. Eliminate them instantly!
3. C has the extreme word "Only", usually incorrect.
4. Only A remains. Select it confidently.
