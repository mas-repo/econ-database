HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Principles of Taxation (賦稅原則)
Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆
Key Concepts: Equity, Regressive Tax, Adam Smith

題目 (Question)

在二零零九年七月，政府在零售層面開徵每個塑膠購物袋5角的環保費，首階段範圍包括連鎖或大型超市、便利店及個人護理和美容產品店。在首階段小商戶獲得豁免。顧客可使用信用卡、易辦事（EPS）或現金一併支付原初購物金額和環保費。

徵收上述的環保費最不符合亞當史密所提出的賦稅原則。

In July 2009, the government introduced an environmental levy of 50 cents on each plastic shopping bag at the retail level, with the first phase covering chain or large supermarkets, convenience stores and personal health and beauty product stores. Small shops are exempted in the first phase. Customers can pay the levy, with the original amount of purchase of goods, by credit card, Easy Pay System (EPS) or cash.

The collection of the above environmental levy is LEAST in line with the _______ principle of taxation put forward by Adam Smith.

A. 公平 (Equality)

B. 經濟 (Economy)

C. 方便 (Convenience)

D. 明確 (Certainty)

概念重溫：亞當史密的賦稅原則 (Concept Review: Adam Smith's Canons of Taxation)

亞當史密在《國富論》中提出了良好的稅制應具備以下四項原則：

Adam Smith, in "The Wealth of Nations", proposed four maxims (canons) of a good tax system:
  • 1. 公平原則 (Equality / Equity): 納稅人應按其能力納稅。收入越高，納稅越多。
    Subjects should contribute towards the support of the government in proportion to their respective abilities (income).
  • 2. 明確原則 (Certainty): 稅項的繳納時間、方式及金額必須清晰明確，不可由收稅者隨意決定。
    The tax which each individual is bound to pay ought to be certain, and not arbitrary. The time of payment, the manner of payment, the quantity to be paid, ought all to be clear.
  • 3. 方便原則 (Convenience): 徵稅的時間和方式應便於納稅人繳付。
    Every tax ought to be levied at the time, or in the manner, in which it is most likely to be convenient for the contributor to pay it.
  • 4. 經濟原則 (Economy): 稅收的行政費用（如收集成本）應減至最低，確保大部分稅款進入國庫。
    Every tax ought to be so contrived as both to take out and to keep out of the pockets of the people as little as possible over and above what it brings into the public treasury (low collection cost).

答案與詳細分析 (Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): A

A. 公平 (Equality) - Correct

分析： 膠袋稅是按量收費（每個5角），這是一種「定額稅」。對於富人和窮人來說，5角錢佔富人收入的比例極低，但佔窮人收入的比例較高。這種稅制具有累退性質 (Regressive nature)，違反了「能力支付」的公平原則。
此外，題目提到「小商戶獲得豁免」，這導致在不同店舖購買相同物品的消費者受到不同對待，這也違反了橫向公平 (Horizontal Equity)。

Analysis: The plastic bag levy is a specific charge (50 cents per bag). This is a flat tax. 50 cents represents a tiny fraction of a rich person's income but a larger fraction of a poor person's income. This tax is regressive in nature, violating the principle of Equality based on "ability to pay".
Furthermore, the question states "Small shops are exempted". This means consumers buying the same item are treated differently depending on the shop, violating Horizontal Equity.
B. 經濟 (Economy) - Incorrect

分析： 題目提到稅款可與購物金額一併支付，且主要針對已經有完善收款系統（如EPS、信用卡）的大型連鎖店。這意味著政府不需要建立全新的龐大系統來收稅，行政成本相對較低。豁免小商戶正是為了避免高昂的監管成本，這反而是在遵守經濟原則。

Analysis: The question mentions the levy is paid together with the purchase, mainly in large chains with established payment systems (EPS, credit card). This implies low administrative costs for collection. Exempting small shops is actually done to avoid high monitoring costs, which means the policy is following the Economy principle.
C. 方便 (Convenience) - Incorrect

分析： 顧客在付款購物時一併支付膠袋稅，無需另外填表或去特定地點繳費，這非常方便。

Analysis: Customers pay the levy at the checkout along with their goods. No extra forms or trips are needed. This is highly convenient.
D. 明確 (Certainty) - Incorrect

分析： 稅額清楚訂明為「每個5角」，沒有含糊之處，納稅人非常清楚自己要付多少錢。

Analysis: The rate is clearly fixed at "50 cents each". There is no ambiguity. Taxpayers know exactly how much they need to pay.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

這類題目的關鍵在於將題目中的具體描述與四個抽象原則進行配對。

關鍵破題點 (The Pivot Point):
「每個塑膠購物袋5角」(50 cents on each bag)

這是一個定額稅 (Specific Tax)。在經濟學中，凡是與收入無關的定額收費（如銷售稅、煙草稅、膠袋稅），通常都被視為具有累退性 (Regressive)，因為低收入者需付出收入的較大百分比來支付此稅。

一旦確認它是累退稅，它就直接違反了亞當史密的公平原則 (Equality)，因為亞當史密強調稅收應與收入能力成正比。

This is a Specific Tax. In Economics, flat charges unrelated to income (like sales tax, tobacco tax, bag levy) are generally considered regressive, as lower-income groups pay a higher percentage of their income towards the tax. Once identified as regressive, it directly violates Adam Smith's Equality principle, which emphasizes contribution based on ability (income).

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • 誤區：以為「豁免小商戶」違反了「經濟原則」。
    解釋：學生可能認為豁免某些人是不「經濟」的。其實相反，向成千上萬的小商戶逐一收稅的行政成本極高（要派人巡查），豁免他們反而節省了政府開支，是符合經濟原則的做法。
    Misconception: Thinking exemption of small shops violates "Economy". Actually, collecting from tiny shops has high administrative costs. Exempting them saves money, thus adhering to the Economy principle.
  • 誤區：混淆「公平」與「一視同仁」。
    解釋：有些學生認為每個人都付5角就是「公平」。但在稅務原則中，公平通常指「垂直公平」(Vertical Equity)，即有錢人應多付。每人付一樣的錢，對窮人其實是不公平的。
    Misconception: Confusing "Equality" with "paying the same amount". Students might think everyone paying 50 cents is fair. In tax principles, Equality refers to Vertical Equity (rich pay more). A flat fee is unfair to the poor.

學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼這不是違反「中立原則」(Neutrality)？

A: 首先，題目選項中沒有「中立原則」。其次，膠袋稅的目的正是不中立！政府故意透過徵稅來干預市場，增加膠袋成本，從而減少其使用量（糾正界外效應）。如果題目問及現代稅務原則（非亞當史密），這確實違反中立，但這是政府有意為之的。
First, "Neutrality" is not an option. Second, the purpose of the levy is explicitly non-neutral! The government intends to interfere with the market to reduce usage (correcting externalities).

Q: 「豁免小商戶」這一點是否違反了公平原則？

A: 是的，這是一個很好的觀點。這違反了橫向公平 (Horizontal Equity)。兩個消費者購買同樣的膠袋，一個在大超市買要付稅，一個在小士多買不用付稅，這造成了不公平的待遇。這進一步支持了答案 A。
Yes, excellent point. This violates Horizontal Equity. Two consumers buying the same bag are treated differently based on where they shop. This further supports Option A.
