Topic / 課題： Money and Banking: Credit Creation (貨幣與銀行：信用創造)

Difficulty / 難度： ★★★★☆

Key Concepts / 核心概念： Required Reserve Ratio (法定儲備比率), Money Supply (貨幣供應), Excess Reserves (超額儲備)

1. The Question (題目)

某銀行系統原初的超額儲備是 160百萬元，而公眾持有 200百萬元 的現金。該銀行系統的資產負債表如下：

Initially, a banking system has an excess reserve of $160 million and the public holds $200 million cash. The balance sheet of the banking system is as follows:

資產 Assets ($million) 負債 Liabilities ($million)
儲備 Reserves 400 存款 Deposits 1 200
貸款 Loans 800

假設公眾從該銀行系統提取 100百萬元 並以現金方式持有。若銀行借出全部超額儲備，貨幣供應會是多少？

Suppose $100 million is withdrawn from the banking system and held as cash by the public. What will the money supply be if the banks lend out all excess reserves?

A. 1 500百萬元
B. 1 700百萬元
C. 1 800百萬元
D. 2 300百萬元

A. $1 500 million
B. $1 700 million
C. $1 800 million
D. $2 300 million

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解決這道題目，我們需要掌握「法定儲備比率」及「貨幣供應」的定義。

To solve this problem, we need to master the definitions of the "Required Reserve Ratio" and "Money Supply".

  • 實際儲備 (Actual Reserves): 銀行體系中持有的總現金。包含法定儲備和超額儲備。
    Actual Reserves: Total cash held within the banking system. It consists of Required Reserves and Excess Reserves.
  • 法定儲備比率 (Required Reserve Ratio): 銀行被要求保留作儲備的存款比例。
    Required Reserve Ratio (rrr): The fraction of deposits that banks are legally obliged to keep as reserves.
  • 貨幣供應 (Money Supply): 指公眾持有的現金加上銀行的存款。
    Money Supply: The sum of cash held by the non-bank public and deposits in banks.
$$ \text{Actual Reserves} (R) = \text{Required Reserves} (RR) + \text{Excess Reserves} (ER) $$
$$ rrr = \frac{RR}{\text{Deposits} (D)} $$
$$ \text{Maximum Deposits} (D_{max}) = \frac{R}{rrr} $$
$$ \text{Money Supply} (M_s) = \text{Cash held by public} (C_p) + \text{Deposits} (D) $$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Answer: Option C

第一步：找出法定儲備比率 (rrr)
根據原初數據，實際儲備是 400，超額儲備是 160。
因此，法定儲備 \(RR\) = \(400 - 160 = 240\)。
存款是 1 200，所以 \(rrr = 240 / 1200 = 0.2\) (即 20%)。

Step 1: Find the Required Reserve Ratio (rrr)
From initial data, Actual Reserves = 400, Excess Reserves = 160.
Therefore, Required Reserves \(RR\) = \(400 - 160 = 240\).
Deposits = 1 200, so \(rrr = 240 / 1200 = 0.2\) (or 20%).

第二步：處理提款的影響
公眾提取了 100。
新的銀行儲備 = \(400 - 100 = 300\)。
新的公眾持有現金 = \(200 + 100 = 300\)。

Step 2: Process the impact of the withdrawal
The public withdraws 100.
New Bank Reserves = \(400 - 100 = 300\).
New Cash held by public = \(200 + 100 = 300\).

第三步：計算最大存款額及新貨幣供應
題目指「銀行借出全部超額儲備」，意味著信用創造達到極限，銀行沒有多餘的超額儲備。
最高存款額 \(D_{max}\) = 新儲備 / \(rrr\) = \(300 / 0.2 = 1 500\)。
新貨幣供應 \(M_s\) = 新公眾現金 + 最高存款 = \(300 + 1 500 = 1 800\)。

Step 3: Calculate maximum deposits and new Money Supply
The phrase "lend out all excess reserves" means maximum credit creation has occurred (Excess Reserves = 0).
Max Deposits \(D_{max}\) = New Reserves / \(rrr\) = \(300 / 0.2 = 1 500\).
New Money Supply \(M_s\) = New Public Cash + Max Deposits = \(300 + 1 500 = 1 800\).

Distractor Analysis (選項分析)

  • A (1 500): 錯誤。這是最高存款額，考生忘記加上公眾持有的現金。 Incorrect. This is only the Maximum Deposits. The student forgot to add the cash held by the public.
  • B (1 700): 錯誤。考生錯誤使用了提款前的舊現金數值 (200 + 1 500 = 1 700)。 Incorrect. The student used the old cash figure before withdrawal (200 + 1 500 = 1 700).
  • D (2 300): 錯誤。考生可能錯誤地用舊的儲備額(400)去計算存款 (400/0.2 = 2 000)，再加上新現金(300)。 Incorrect. The student likely calculated max deposits using the old reserve figure (400/0.2 = 2 000) and added the new cash (300).

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

突破點 (Pivot Point)：
這類題目的核心通常是隱藏的「法定儲備比率 (rrr)」。你必須利用原初資產負債表找出 \(rrr\)，這是解決後續變化的唯一鑰匙。

Pivot Point:
The core of these questions is usually the hidden "Required Reserve Ratio (rrr)". You must use the initial balance sheet to find the \(rrr\); it is the key to solving all subsequent changes.

干擾資訊 (Distractors)：
資產表中的「貸款 800」是多餘資訊，在此題的計算中完全不需要用到。

Irrelevant Info:
The "Loans 800" in the asset column is a distractor and is not needed for the calculation.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ 陷阱 1：現金的流向 (Cash Flow)
許多學生在計算提款時，只記得扣減銀行的儲備，卻忘記了提取出來的錢並沒有消失，而是變成了「公眾持有的現金」(\(C_p\))！

⚠️ Trap 1: Cash Flow
When calculating a withdrawal, many students remember to deduct from bank reserves, but forget that the withdrawn money doesn't disappear—it becomes "Cash held by public" (\(C_p\))!

⚠️ 陷阱 2：貨幣供應 vs 存款 (Money Supply vs Deposits)
題目問的是「貨幣供應」(Money Supply)，但很多同學計算到「存款」(Deposits = 1 500) 就以為做完了，誤選 A。

⚠️ Trap 2: Money Supply vs Deposits
The question asks for "Money Supply", but students often stop after calculating "Deposits" (1 500) and mistakenly choose Option A.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q1：為什麼提款 100百萬，銀行儲備就剛好減少 100百萬？

Q1: Why does a $100 million withdrawal reduce bank reserves by exactly $100 million?

A1： 銀行的「儲備」本質上就是存放在銀行金庫（或中央銀行）的現金。當市民提款時，銀行必須從金庫拿出現金交給市民。因此，銀行體系內的現金（儲備）減少，而市民手中的現金增加，兩者是一對一的轉移。

A1: Bank "Reserves" are essentially the physical cash sitting in the bank's vault (or at the central bank). When the public withdraws money, the bank hands over cash from its vault. Thus, cash inside the banking system (reserves) decreases, and cash in the public's hands increases in a 1:1 ratio.

Q2：既然銀行會借出所有超額儲備，為何我們不計算新的貸款額？

Q2: Since the bank lends out all excess reserves, why don't we calculate the new loan amounts?

A2： 因為我們不需要。存款乘數公式 (\(D = R/rrr\)) 已經直接告訴我們，經過無數次的「借貸—存款」循環後，最終的存款總額是多少。我們直接利用最終的存款額加上現金即可計算出貨幣供應，省去了計算貸款的麻煩。

A2: Because it's unnecessary. The deposit multiplier formula (\(D = R/rrr\)) directly calculates the ultimate total deposits after the infinite loop of "lending-and-depositing" is completed. By adding this final deposit amount to the public cash, we get the money supply directly, bypassing the need to calculate loans.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

🚀 貨幣基礎不變法則 (The Constant Monetary Base Rule)

🚀 The Constant Monetary Base Rule

在沒有中央銀行干預的情況下，市民純粹提款或存款，是不會改變經濟體系內的「貨幣基礎」(Monetary Base, \(MB\)) 的。
貨幣基礎 = 公眾現金 + 銀行儲備 (\(MB = C_p + R\))。

Without central bank intervention, a simple cash withdrawal or deposit by the public does not change the economy's "Monetary Base" (\(MB\)).
Monetary Base = Cash held by public + Bank Reserves (\(MB = C_p + R\)).

原初 / Initial \(MB\) = \(200 + 400 = 600\)

提款後，既然 \(MB\) 還是 600，而公眾現金變成了 300，那麼新儲備必然是 \(600 - 300 = 300\)。
這能幫你快速檢查你的 \(C_p\) 和 \(R\) 數字是否出錯！

After withdrawal, since \(MB\) remains 600 and public cash became 300, the new reserves MUST be \(600 - 300 = 300\).
This is a great shortcut to quickly verify if your \(C_p\) and \(R\) numbers are correct!
