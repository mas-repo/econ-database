Topic: Macroeconomics - Exchange Rates & Trade Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5) Key Concepts: Currency Appreciation, Import Value, Elasticity

1. The Question (題目)

[中文版] 相對於其貿易伙伴的貨幣，某國的貨幣升值。若該國對進口的需求屬_______，貨幣升值會令進口總值（以本國貨幣計算）___________。

A. 單一彈性 … 上升
B. 低彈性 … 上升
C. 高彈性 … 下跌
D. 低彈性 … 下跌

[English] A country’s currency appreciates against its trading partners' currencies. If the country's demand for imports is _______, the appreciation of its currency will lead to _______ in the total import value (in terms of domestic currency).

A. unitarily elastic … a rise
B. inelastic … a rise
C. elastic … a fall
D. inelastic … a fall

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

Currency Appreciation (貨幣升值)

當本國貨幣升值，意味著用相同數量的本國貨幣可以兌換更多的外國貨幣。因此，以本國貨幣計算的進口價格 \(P\) 會下降。

When the domestic currency appreciates, it means the same amount of domestic currency can exchange for more foreign currency. Therefore, the price of imports in terms of domestic currency \(P\) falls.

Total Value & Elasticity (總值與彈性)

進口總值 (Total Import Value, \(TV\)) 取決於進口物品的價格 \(P\) 和需求量 \(Q\)。

Total Import Value (\(TV\)) depends on the price of imports \(P\) and the quantity demanded \(Q\).

$$TV = P \times Q$$

當價格 \(P\) 下降時，根據需求定律，需求量 \(Q\) 會上升。但 \(TV\) 最終上升還是下跌，取決於需求價格彈性 \(E_d\)：

When price \(P\) falls, according to the law of demand, quantity demanded \(Q\) rises. However, whether \(TV\) ultimately rises or falls depends on the price elasticity of demand \(E_d\):

  • 高彈性 Elastic (\(E_d > 1\)): \(%\Delta Q > %\Delta P\)。\(Q\) 增加的百分比大於 \(P\) 下降的百分比，\(TV\) 上升。
  • 低彈性 Inelastic (\(E_d < 1\)): \(%\Delta Q < %\Delta P\)。\(Q\) 增加的百分比小於 \(P\) 下降的百分比，\(TV\) 下跌。
  • 單一彈性 Unitarily Elastic (\(E_d = 1\)): \(%\Delta Q = %\Delta P\)。\(TV\) 不變。

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option / 正確答案: D

Option A - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

若需求屬單一彈性，價格下跌的百分比等於需求量上升的百分比，進口總值應維持不變，而非上升。

If demand is unitarily elastic, the percentage fall in price equals the percentage rise in quantity demanded. The total import value should remain unchanged, not rise.

Option B - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

若需求屬低彈性，價格下跌（主導因素）的百分比大於需求量上升的百分比，進口總值應下跌，而非上升。

If demand is inelastic, the percentage fall in price (the dominant factor) is greater than the percentage rise in quantity demanded. The total import value should fall, not rise.

Option C - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

若需求屬高彈性，需求量上升（主導因素）的百分比大於價格下跌的百分比，進口總值應上升，而非下跌。

If demand is elastic, the percentage rise in quantity demanded (the dominant factor) is greater than the percentage fall in price. The total import value should rise, not fall.

Option D - 正確 (Correct)

本國貨幣升值導致以本國貨幣計算的進口價格下跌。若需求屬低彈性，由於價格下跌的幅度大於需求量增加的幅度，進口總值必然下跌。完全符合題目描述。

The appreciation of domestic currency leads to a fall in import prices in terms of domestic currency. If demand is inelastic, since the magnitude of the price fall is greater than the magnitude of the quantity increase, total import value must fall. This perfectly matches the statement.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

遇到這類題目，我們需要採取「兩步走」的策略：

When encountering this type of question, we need to adopt a "two-step" strategy:

  1. Pivot Point 1 (關鍵點 1): 找出價格 \(P\) 的變化方向。
    貨幣升值 \(\rightarrow\) 進口貨品相對變平 \(\rightarrow\) \(P\) 下跌。
    Find the direction of change in price \(P\).
    Currency appreciates \(\rightarrow\) Imports become relatively cheaper \(\rightarrow\) \(P\) falls.
  2. Pivot Point 2 (關鍵點 2): 根據總收益/總值與彈性的關係配對。
    既然已知 \(P \downarrow\)，我們只需將不同彈性代入，檢查 \(TV\) 的變化方向是否與選項吻合。
    Match it with the relationship between Total Value and Elasticity.
    Since we know \(P \downarrow\), we just plug in different elasticities to check if the change in \(TV\) matches the options.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

計價貨幣的陷阱 (The Denomination Currency Trap)

很多同學沒有仔細閱讀括號內的「以本國貨幣計算」(in terms of domestic currency)。

Many students fail to carefully read the bracketed text "in terms of domestic currency".

如果題目問的是「以外國貨幣計算」(in terms of foreign currency)：外幣計價的進口價格 \(P_m\) 是不變的。因為本幣升值，進口數量 \(Q_m\) 增加。在 \(P_m\) 不變而 \(Q_m\) 上升的情況下，以外幣計算的進口總值必然上升，這與需求彈性完全無關！

If the question asked "in terms of foreign currency": The import price in foreign currency \(P_m\) remains constant. Because the domestic currency appreciates, the quantity of imports \(Q_m\) increases. With \(P_m\) constant and \(Q_m\) rising, the total import value in foreign currency must rise, regardless of the elasticity of demand!

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼貨幣升值會令進口價格 (以本國貨幣計算) 下跌？

Q: Why does currency appreciation cause the price of imports (in terms of domestic currency) to fall?

A: 想像一下，假設原本 8 港元兌換 1 美元。一件 10 美元的美國進口貨，在香港的售價（本幣計算）是 80 港元。如果港元升值到 7 港元兌換 1 美元，同樣一件 10 美元的貨物，現在只需要 70 港元就能買到。所以，價格 \(P\) 下跌了。

A: Imagine initially 8 HKD exchanges for 1 USD. A $10 USD imported good from the US costs 80 HKD in Hong Kong (domestic currency). If the HKD appreciates to 7 HKD for 1 USD, the exact same $10 USD good now only costs 70 HKD. Therefore, the price \(P\) has fallen.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

口訣記憶法 (Mnemonic Trick): 處理價格下跌的情況時，記住「同向低彈，反向高彈」。

Mnemonic Trick: When dealing with a price decrease, remember "Same direction = Inelastic, Opposite direction = Elastic".

  • 如果價格下跌 \(P \downarrow\)，總值也下跌 \(TV \downarrow\) (兩者同方向) \(\rightarrow\) 必然是低彈性 (Inelastic)。一看選項 D，完美契合！
  • If Price falls \(P \downarrow\), and Total Value also falls \(TV \downarrow\) (both move in the same direction) \(\rightarrow\) It must be Inelastic. Looking at Option D, it's a perfect match!
  • 如果價格下跌 \(P \downarrow\)，總值卻上升 \(TV \uparrow\) (兩者反方向) \(\rightarrow\) 必然是高彈性 (Elastic)。選項 C 寫成了下跌，所以錯。
  • If Price falls \(P \downarrow\), but Total Value rises \(TV \uparrow\) (they move in opposite directions) \(\rightarrow\) It must be Elastic. Option C says "a fall", so it's wrong.
