📊 Economics Concept Guide / 經濟學概念指南

Topic (課題): Law of Demand & Derivation of Demand Curve (需求定律與需求曲線的引伸)
Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐ (2/5)
Key Concepts (關鍵概念): Ceteris Paribus (其他因素不變), Demand vs. Quantity Demanded (需求與需求量的分別), Movement vs. Shift (沿線移動與曲線移動)

1. The Question (題目)

當導出某物品的市場需求曲線時，下列哪項是假設不變的？

Which of the following is assumed to be constant when the market demand curve of a good is derived?

  • (1) 消費者的收入 / the income of the consumers
  • (2) 該物品的價格 / the price of the good
  • (3) 相關物品的價格 / prices of related goods
  • (4) 消費者的偏好 / preference of the consumers

A. 只有(1)、(3)
B. 只有(2)、(3)
C. 只有(1)、(2)、(4)
D. 只有(1)、(3)、(4)

A. (1) and (3) only
B. (2) and (3) only
C. (1), (2) and (4) only
D. (1), (3) and (4) only

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

其他因素不變 (Ceteris Paribus)：在經濟學中，我們經常想分析兩個變量之間的關係。為了解除其他因素的干擾，我們必須假設其他所有可能影響結果的變量保持不變。這就是「其他因素不變」的假設。

Ceteris Paribus (All other things being equal): In economics, when we want to analyze the relationship between two variables, we must assume that all other variables that could affect the outcome remain constant. This isolates the effect of the variable we are studying.

需求曲線的定義 (Definition of Demand Curve)：需求曲線顯示了在不同價格 \((P)\) 之下，消費者願意並有能力購買的需求量 \((Q_d)\)。由於我們只觀察 \((P)\) 和 \((Q_d)\) 之間的關係，除了該物品的價格 \((P)\) 以外，所有其他影響需求的因素都必須假設為不變。

Definition of Demand Curve: The demand curve illustrates the relationship between the price \((P)\) of a good and the quantity demanded \((Q_d)\). Because we are only observing the relationship between \((P)\) and \((Q_d)\), all factors affecting demand other than the good's own price \((P)\) must be assumed constant.

$$ Q_d = f(P) \quad \text{(Ceteris Paribus)} $$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案：Option D (只有 1, 3, 4)

Correct Option: Option D ((1), (3) and (4) only)

(1) 消費者的收入 (Income of the consumers): 必須假設不變。收入改變會導致整條需求曲線移動（左移或右移）。為了畫出一條固定的需求曲線，我們必須把收入固定。

Must be assumed constant. A change in income will cause the entire demand curve to shift. To draw a single, stationary demand curve, income must be held constant.

(2) 該物品的價格 (Price of the good): 不能假設不變。這是題目最關鍵的一點！需求曲線的Y軸就是「價格」，X軸是「數量」。需求曲線正是用來表達當「價格改變」時，「需求量」會如何跟著改變。如果價格不變，圖表上只會有一點，而不會「導出」一條線。

CANNOT be assumed constant. This is the crucial point! The Y-axis of a demand graph is "Price" and the X-axis is "Quantity". The demand curve explicitly shows how quantity demanded responds as price changes. If price were constant, we would only have a single point, not a curve.

(3) 相關物品的價格 (Prices of related goods): 必須假設不變。替代品或補充品的價格改變會導致該物品的需求曲線移動。因此，在導出曲線時必須假設它們不變。

Must be assumed constant. Changes in the prices of substitutes or complements will shift the demand curve. Therefore, they must be held constant when deriving the curve.

(4) 消費者的偏好 (Preference of the consumers): 必須假設不變。偏好也是影響整條需求曲線位置的因素（Shift factor），必須被固定。

Must be assumed constant. Consumer preference is also a shift factor that determines the position of the demand curve and must be fixed.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解答這類題目的「樞紐點」(Pivot Point) 在於理解甚麼是「導出需求曲線」(Deriving a demand curve)。

The "Pivot Point" for solving this type of question is understanding what it means to "derive a demand curve."

  • 尋找變量 (Identify the variables): 任何二維圖表（如需求圖）都有兩個不斷改變的變數。在需求圖中，這兩個變數是價格 \((P)\) 和 需求量 \((Q_d)\)。

    Identify the variables: Any 2D graph has two changing variables. In a demand graph, these are Price \((P)\) and Quantity Demanded \((Q_d)\).

  • 區分沿線移動與曲線移動 (Distinguish Movement vs. Shift): 影響 \((Q_d)\) 的因素有很多。除了本身的價格 \((P)\) 之外，其他所有因素都被稱為「移線因素」(Shift factors)。當我們在畫（導出）曲線時，只允許 \((P)\) 變動，強制鎖死所有「移線因素」。

    Distinguish Movement vs. Shift: Many factors affect \((Q_d)\). All factors other than the good's own price \((P)\) are called "shift factors." When drawing (deriving) the curve, we only allow \((P)\) to change, forcing all shift factors to be locked (constant).

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱一：誤以為「所有」因素都不變。
有些同學死記硬背「Ceteris Paribus」代表「其他所有東西不變」，結果連「該物品的價格」也以為是不變的。請記住，如果連價格都不變，需求定律（價格與需求量呈反向關係）就不成立了！

Trap 1: Assuming "EVERYTHING" is constant.
Some students blindly memorize "Ceteris Paribus" as "everything remains unchanged," mistakenly assuming the good's own price is also constant. Remember, if price doesn't change, the Law of Demand (inverse relationship between price and quantity) cannot be demonstrated!

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

🙋‍♂️ 學生問：「導出『市場』需求曲線和『個人』需求曲線，假設不變的因素有分別嗎？」

Student asks: "Is there a difference in the factors assumed constant when deriving a 'market' demand curve versus an 'individual' demand curve?"

👨‍🏫 老師答：好問題！大部分假設是相同的（例如收入、偏好、相關物品價格）。但是，導出「市場」需求曲線時，我們還必須額外假設「消費者的人數 (Number of consumers)」不變。如果人口突然增加，市場需求曲線就會向右移。

Tutor answers: Great question! Most assumptions are the same (e.g., income, preferences, prices of related goods). However, when deriving a "market" demand curve, we must additionally assume that the "number of consumers" (population) remains constant. If the population suddenly increases, the market demand curve will shift to the right.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法：剔除法 (Elimination Method)

Shortcut: Elimination Method

在選擇題中，一旦你認清了「價格 \((P)\) 必須變動才能畫出曲線」這個核心概念，你就可以馬上判定選項 (2) 必定是錯誤的（不能假設不變）。

In multiple-choice questions, once you recognize the core concept that "Price \((P)\) MUST vary to draw the curve," you can immediately determine that item (2) is FALSE (it cannot be constant).

  1. 因為 (2) 錯誤，立刻刪除包含 (2) 的選項：刪除 B 和 C。
  2. Because (2) is false, immediately eliminate options containing (2): Cross out B and C.
  3. 剩下 A (1,3) 和 D (1,3,4)。現在只需判斷 (4) 偏好是否需要不變。
  4. We are left with A (1,3) and D (1,3,4). Now simply decide if (4) preference needs to be constant.
  5. 偏好明顯會改變整體需求（移線因素），所以必須假設不變。因此 (4) 正確。答案鎖定為 D。
  6. Preference obviously shifts overall demand, so it must be held constant. Therefore (4) is correct. The answer is locked in as D.
