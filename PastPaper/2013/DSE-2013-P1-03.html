📚 Topic (課題): Short-run Costs of Production (短期生產成本)

⭐ Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆

🔑 Key Concepts (核心概念): Total Fixed Cost (總固定成本), Average Variable Cost (平均可變成本), Short-run vs Long-run (短期與長期)

📝 The Question (題目)

下表顯示某廠商的成本與產出的關係。

The following table shows the cost-output relationship of a firm.

產出 Outputs (units) 012345
總成本 Total cost ($) 102842526072

根據上表，我們可得出結論如下：

Based on the above table, we can conclude that

  • A. 該廠商享有規模經濟的好處。
    the firm enjoys the benefits of economies of scale.
  • B. 該廠商的最佳生產規模是5單位的產出。
    the firm’s optimal scale of production is 5 units of outputs.
  • C. 平均可變成本正上升。
    the average variable cost is increasing.
  • D. 總固定成本是$10。*(註：中文原題可能有誤植為$100，此處依據英文版修正為$10)*
    the total fixed cost is $10.

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這道題目，我們必須清楚了解短期成本的結構。在短期內，總成本 \(TC\) 由總固定成本 \(TFC\) 和總可變成本 \(TVC\) 組成。固定成本不會隨產量改變，即使產量為0，固定成本依然存在。

To solve this question, we must understand the structure of short-run costs. In the short run, Total Cost \(TC\) consists of Total Fixed Cost \(TFC\) and Total Variable Cost \(TVC\). Fixed costs do not change with output; even when output is 0, fixed costs still exist.

$$ TC = TFC + TVC $$ $$ \text{When } Q = 0, \quad TVC = 0 \implies TC = TFC $$ $$ AVC = \frac{TVC}{Q} = \frac{TC - TFC}{Q} $$

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 Correct Option: D

Option A Analysis:

錯誤。「規模經濟」是一個長期 (Long-run) 概念。從數據可見，當產出為0時，總成本為$10，這代表存在固定成本。有固定成本即代表這是短期 (Short-run) 情況，因此無法判斷長期規模經濟。

Incorrect. "Economies of scale" is a long-run concept. The data shows that at 0 output, total cost is $10, indicating the existence of fixed costs. The presence of fixed costs means this is a short-run situation, so we cannot determine long-run economies of scale.

Option B Analysis:

錯誤。要找出最佳生產規模（利潤極大化產量），我們需要邊際收益 (MR) 或價格 (Price) 的數據來與邊際成本 (MC) 比較 (\(MR = MC\))。題目沒有提供收益數據，因此無法得知最佳產量。

Incorrect. To find the optimal scale of production (profit-maximizing output), we need data on Marginal Revenue (MR) or Price to compare with Marginal Cost (MC) (\(MR = MC\)). The question provides no revenue data, so we cannot determine the optimal output.

Option C Analysis:

錯誤。我們需要計算平均可變成本 \(AVC\)。已知 \(TFC = 10\)，所以 \(TVC = TC - 10\)。
當 \(Q=1\), \(TVC = 28 - 10 = 18\), \(AVC = 18/1 = 18\)
當 \(Q=2\), \(TVC = 42 - 10 = 32\), \(AVC = 32/2 = 16\)
當 \(Q=3\), \(TVC = 52 - 10 = 42\), \(AVC = 42/3 = 14\)
可見 \(AVC\) 正在下降，而非上升。

Incorrect. We need to calculate Average Variable Cost \(AVC\). Since \(TFC = 10\), \(TVC = TC - 10\).
At \(Q=1\), \(TVC = 28 - 10 = 18\), \(AVC = 18/1 = 18\)
At \(Q=2\), \(TVC = 42 - 10 = 32\), \(AVC = 32/2 = 16\)
At \(Q=3\), \(TVC = 52 - 10 = 42\), \(AVC = 42/3 = 14\)
Clearly, \(AVC\) is decreasing, not increasing.

Option D Analysis:

正確。根據定義，當產出 \(Q = 0\) 時，總可變成本 \(TVC = 0\)。因此，此時的總成本 \(TC\) 就等於總固定成本 \(TFC\)。表格顯示 \(Q=0\) 時 \(TC=10\)，所以 \(TFC = \$10\)。

Correct. By definition, when output \(Q = 0\), Total Variable Cost \(TVC = 0\). Therefore, the Total Cost \(TC\) at this point equals the Total Fixed Cost \(TFC\). The table shows that at \(Q=0\), \(TC=10\), so \(TFC = \$10\).

🔍 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

破題關鍵 (Pivot Point)： 表格的第一列「產出 = 0」。在任何成本計算題中，尋找 \(Q=0\) 的數據是第一步，因為它直接告訴你總固定成本 (TFC) 的數值。

Pivot Point: The first column of the table "Outputs = 0". In any cost calculation question, finding the data for \(Q=0\) is the first step because it directly tells you the value of Total Fixed Cost (TFC).

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺法： 在選擇題中看到成本表格，第一眼先看 \(Q=0\)。如果 \(TC > 0\)，立刻知道這是短期 (Short-run)，並且該數值就是 \(TFC\)。掃視選項，發現選項 D 直接描述了這個事實，即可在 5 秒內選出答案，完全不需要計算選項 C 的 \(AVC\)。

Shortcut: When you see a cost table in an MC question, look at \(Q=0\) first. If \(TC > 0\), you instantly know it's the short run, and that value is the \(TFC\). Scanning the options, you see Option D directly states this fact. You can choose the answer in 5 seconds without ever calculating \(AVC\) for Option C.
