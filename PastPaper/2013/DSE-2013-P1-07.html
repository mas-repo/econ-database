HKDSE Economics MCQ Analysis

Topic 勞工收入與計薪方法 / Wage Payment Methods
Difficulty ★★☆☆☆ (2/5)
Key Concepts 計時工資 (Time Rate), 量度成本 (Measurement Costs), 交易費用 (Transaction Costs)

2. 題目 / The Question

中學教師一般按工時支薪，因為
Teachers in secondary schools are usually paid by time rate because
  • A. 這可改善他們的工作意欲。
    A. this can improve their work incentive.
  • B. 他們偏好穩定收入。
    B. they prefer a stable income.
  • C. 大部分教師都是勤力的。
    C. Most teachers are hardworking.
  • D. 量度他們產出的成本很高。
    D. It is costly to measure their outputs.

3. 概念重溫 / Concept Review

在微觀經濟學中，僱主選擇計薪方法（如計時或計件）的主要考量是交易費用 (Transaction Costs)，特別是量度成本 (Measurement Costs)。
In microeconomics, an employer's choice of payment method (e.g., time rate or piece rate) is primarily driven by Transaction Costs, specifically Measurement Costs.
  • 計時工資 (Time Rate): 按工人的工作時間支付報酬。適用於產出難以量化、需要團隊合作或注重質素的工作。
    Time Rate: Payment based on the time a worker spends on the job. Suitable when output is hard to quantify, requires teamwork, or when quality is paramount.
  • 計件工資 (Piece Rate): 按工人的產出數量支付報酬。適用於產出容易量化、標準化且注重數量的工作。
    Piece Rate: Payment based on the quantity of output produced. Suitable when output is standardized, easily quantifiable, and quantity is the main focus.
計時工資公式：
Time Rate Formula:
$$\text{Total Wage} = \text{Wage Rate per Hour} \times \text{Hours Worked}$$
計件工資公式：
Piece Rate Formula:
$$\text{Total Wage} = \text{Rate per Unit} \times \text{Quantity of Output}$$

4. 答案與詳細分析 / The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案：選項 D
Correct Option: Option D

Option A

錯誤。 計時工資（Time rate）與員工的實際產出沒有直接掛鈎，因此相對於計件工資（Piece rate），它提供的工作意欲（work incentive）通常較低。如果為了提高意欲，僱主理應採用計件或花紅制。
Incorrect. Time rate is not directly linked to actual output, so it generally provides a lower work incentive compared to piece rate. If the goal was strictly to improve incentive, employers would use piece rates or bonus systems.

Option B

錯誤。 雖然員工可能偏好穩定收入，但在經濟學分析中，決定計薪方式的關鍵在於僱主如何將交易費用減至最低，而非單純滿足員工的偏好。
Incorrect. While workers might prefer a stable income, in economic analysis, the determining factor for payment methods is how employers can minimize transaction costs, not merely satisfying worker preferences.

Option C

錯誤。 這是一個主觀的價值判斷（Value judgment），並且與僱主選擇計薪方式的經濟考量（成本與效益）無關。
Incorrect. This is a subjective value judgment and is irrelevant to the economic considerations (cost vs. benefit) of an employer choosing a payment method.

Option D

正確！ 教師的「產出」包括學生的知識增長、品格培養等，這些是多維度且無形的。要準確地分離並量度單一教師的貢獻極為困難且成本高昂（量度成本極高）。因此，按時間支付工資能最有效地減低交易費用。
Correct! A teacher's "output" includes students' knowledge growth, moral development, etc., which are multi-dimensional and intangible. Accurately isolating and measuring a single teacher's contribution is extremely difficult and costly (very high measurement costs). Therefore, paying by time effectively minimizes transaction costs.

5. 題目拆解 / Problem Decomposition

解題支點 (Pivot Point)： 本題的關鍵在於識別「中學教師」這個職業的性質。當你看到職業類型時，必須立刻聯想到該職業的「產出」(Output) 是否容易被量度。
Pivot Point: The key to this question is identifying the nature of the "Secondary school teacher" profession. When you see a job type, immediately think about whether its "Output" is easily measurable.

干擾項分析 (Distractors)： 選項 A 和 B 是常見的常識性陷阱。日常生活中我們可能會覺得「穩定收入好」，但這並非經濟學上解釋市場現象（僱主決策）的核心理論。
Distractors: Options A and B are common "common sense" traps. In daily life, we might think "stable income is good," but this is not the core economic theory used to explain market phenomena (employer decisions).

6. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 / Common Mistakes & Traps

  • 以「常理」代替「理論」： 學生常因為覺得「打工仔都鍾意穩定收入」而誤選 B。記住，DSE 經濟科要求你從「產權」和「交易費用」的角度去解釋制度的形成。
    Substituting "Common Sense" for "Theory": Students often choose B because they feel "employees like stable income." Remember, DSE Economics requires you to explain institutional arrangements from the perspective of "Property Rights" and "Transaction Costs."

7. 學生常見疑問 / Student Q&A

問：點解唔可以用學生的考試成績數量度教師的產出？這樣不就可以用計件工資了嗎？
Q: Why can't we use students' exam scores to measure a teacher's output? Then we could use piece rates, right?
答： 學生的成績受非常多因素影響，例如學生本身的資質、家庭背景、補習老師的幫助，甚至考試當天的運氣。要準確計算出成績中有多少百分比是完全歸功於某一位學校老師，在技術上極度困難，這意味著量度成本 (Measurement Costs) 高得不切實際。
A: Student grades are influenced by many factors, such as innate ability, family background, private tutors, and even luck on exam day. Accurately calculating the exact percentage of the grade solely attributable to one specific school teacher is technically extremely difficult, meaning the Measurement Costs are impractically high.

8. 考試技巧與速解 / Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

秒殺技巧： 在 DSE 選擇題中，當題目問及為何某些專業人士（如醫生、律師、教師、經理）採用計時工資 / 基本薪金時，90% 的答案都離不開「量度產出的成本很高」(Costly to measure outputs) 或者「難以監控質素」。看到這類職業，直接在選項中尋找與「量度成本 / 交易費用」相關的字眼！
Shortcut: In DSE MCQs, when asked why professionals (like doctors, lawyers, teachers, managers) are paid by time rate / basic salary, 90% of the time the answer relates to "Costly to measure outputs" or "difficult to monitor quality". When you see these professions, immediately look for options containing "measurement costs / transaction costs"!
