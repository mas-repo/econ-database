HKDSE Economics Tutor: Microeconomics

主題 (Topic): 政府干預：從量稅 (Government Intervention: Per-unit Tax)

難度 (Difficulty): ★★★☆☆

關鍵概念 (Key Concepts): 從量稅 (Per-unit Tax), 淨銷售收益 (Net Sales Revenue), 供需均衡 (Market Equilibrium)

題目 (The Question)

以下是物品X的供需表。

The following table shows the demand and supply schedule of good X.

價格 / Price ($) 2345678
需求量 / Quantity demanded (units) 90807060504030
供應量 / Quantity supplied (units) 30405060708090

假設政府就每售賣一單位物品X收取$2從量銷量稅。除稅後的總銷售收益是_________。

Suppose the government imposes a $2 per-unit sales tax on each unit of good X sold. The total sales revenue net of tax is ___________.

  • A. $180
  • B. $200
  • C. $280
  • D. $300

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

當政府向生產者徵收從量稅 (Per-unit Tax)時，生產成本會上升，導致供應曲線垂直向上移動（移動幅度相等於稅款）。在新的均衡點下，消費者支付的價格與生產者實際收取的價格會出現一個差額，這個差額正是稅款。

When the government imposes a per-unit tax on producers, production costs rise, shifting the supply curve vertically upwards by the amount of the tax. At the new equilibrium, there is a wedge between the price consumers pay and the price producers actually keep. This difference is exactly the tax amount.

核心公式 (Core Formulas):

$$ P_c - P_s = t $$

這裡 \(P_c\) 是消費者支付的價格 (Price paid by consumers)，\(P_s\) 是賣家除稅後實際收取的價格 (Price received by sellers net of tax)，而 \(t\) 是從量稅金額 (Per-unit tax amount)。

Here, \(P_c\) is the price paid by consumers, \(P_s\) is the price received by sellers net of tax, and \(t\) is the per-unit tax amount.

$$ \text{除稅後的總銷售收益 (Net Sales Revenue)} = P_s \times Q_1 $$

這裡 \(Q_1\) 是徵稅後的新交易量 (New quantity transacted after tax)。

Here, \(Q_1\) is the new quantity transacted after tax.

答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): B

第一步：尋找徵稅後的新均衡 (Step 1: Find the new equilibrium after tax)
根據公式 \(P_c - P_s = 2\)，我們需要在表格中尋找一個交易量 \(Q\)，在該交易量下，需求價格（\(P_c\)）減去供應價格（\(P_s\)）剛好等於$2。
觀察表格，當 \(Q = 50\) 時：
消費者願意支付的價格 (Demand Price) \(P_c = \$6\)
生產者願意供應的價格 (Supply Price) \(P_s = \$4\)
差額剛好是：\$6 - \$4 = \$2（等於稅款）。因此，新的交易量 \(Q_1 = 50\)。

Based on the formula \(P_c - P_s = 2\), we need to look at the table to find a specific quantity \(Q\) where the demand price (\(P_c\)) minus the supply price (\(P_s\)) equals exactly $2.
Looking at the table, when \(Q = 50\):
Price consumers are willing to pay (Demand Price) \(P_c = \$6\)
Price producers require to supply (Supply Price) \(P_s = \$4\)
The difference is exactly: $6 - $4 = $2 (which equals the tax). Thus, the new transacted quantity \(Q_1 = 50\).

第二步：計算除稅後的總銷售收益 (Step 2: Calculate net sales revenue)
除稅後的總收益 = 生產者實際收取的價格 \(\times\) 新交易量。
收益 = \(P_s \times Q_1 = 4 \times 50 = 200\)。

Net sales revenue = Price received by producers \(\times\) New quantity transacted.
Revenue = \(P_s \times Q_1 = 4 \times 50 = 200\).

選項分析 (Option Breakdown):

  • A ($180): 錯誤。 這是隨機數字的組合，例如可能誤用了 \(Q=30\) 及 \(P_c=\$6\)。
    Incorrect. This is likely a random combination of numbers, such as misusing \(Q=30\) and \(P_c=\$6\).
  • B ($200): 正確。 \(P_s \times Q_1 = 4 \times 50 = 200\)。
    Correct. \(P_s \times Q_1 = 4 \times 50 = 200\).
  • C ($280): 錯誤。 可能是錯誤使用了 \(P_s=4\) 但配錯了 \(Q=70\)。
    Incorrect. This might result from using \(P_s=4\) but incorrectly pairing it with \(Q=70\).
  • D ($300): 錯誤。 這是消費者總開支 (Total Consumer Expenditure)，即 \(P_c \times Q_1 = 6 \times 50 = 300\)。這是一個非常常見的陷阱！
    Incorrect. This represents the Total Consumer Expenditure, calculated as \(P_c \times Q_1 = 6 \times 50 = 300\). This is a very common trap!

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解答這類表格題的「解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)」是找出符合稅款的價格差額。不要急著畫圖，也不要盲目將所有價格加$2。
很多同學會先找原來的均衡點（價格=$5，數量=60）。雖然這能讓你了解市場原狀，但對於計算徵稅後的新收益，原來的均衡點其實是干擾資訊 (Distractor)。你真正需要的是：哪一個數量下的 \(P_c\) 和 \(P_s\) 相差$2。

The "Pivot Point" for solving table-based questions like this is to find the price difference that matches the tax amount. Don't rush to draw a graph, and don't blindly add $2 to all prices.
Many students start by finding the initial equilibrium (Price=$5, Quantity=60). While this helps you understand the initial state of the market, for calculating the new post-tax revenue, the original equilibrium is actually a distractor. What you strictly need is: at which quantity do \(P_c\) and \(P_s\) differ by exactly $2.

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

概念混淆：總收益 vs 總開支
題目要求計算「除稅後的總銷售收益 (Net sales revenue)」，這屬於賣家 (Sellers) 實際落袋的錢，所以必須使用 \(P_s\) ($4)。如果題目問的是「總消費者開支 (Total consumer expenditure)」，或是「包含稅款的總銷售收益 (Gross sales revenue)」，那麼就應該用消費者支付的價格 \(P_c\) ($6) 乘以數量 50，得出 $300（即選項 D）。考試時必須看清楚題目問的是誰的收益！

Conceptual Confusion: Total Revenue vs. Total Expenditure
The question asks for the "Net sales revenue", which is the money that sellers actually keep in their pockets, so you must use \(P_s\) ($4). If the question asked for "Total consumer expenditure" or "Gross sales revenue (including tax)", then you would use the price consumers pay \(P_c\) ($6) multiplied by the quantity 50, resulting in $300 (Option D). Always read carefully whose revenue the question is asking for!

學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼我們知道新的交易數量一定是 50，而不是其他數字？
Q: How do we know for sure the new quantity is exactly 50 and not another number?

A: 因為在自由市場中，買賣雙方必須在同一數量下達成交易。徵收$2稅款後，消費者願意付出的錢，減去賣家實際想收到的錢，必須剛好彌補這$2的稅款。在表格中，只有當 \(Q = 50\) 時，消費者願意出$6，賣方願意收$4（差距剛好$2）。在其他數量（例如 \(Q=40\)）時，差價是 $7 - $3 = $4，不符合$2的稅款條件。

A: Because in a free market, buyers and sellers must transact at the same quantity. After a $2 tax is imposed, what consumers are willing to pay minus what sellers require to receive must exactly cover this $2 tax wedge. In the table, only when \(Q = 50\) is the consumer willing to pay $6 and the seller willing to receive $4 (a difference of exactly $2). At other quantities (e.g., \(Q=40\)), the price gap is $7 - $3 = $4, which doesn't match the $2 tax condition.

Q: 如果題目問政府的總稅收 (Total tax revenue)，我該怎麼算？
Q: If the question asked for the government's total tax revenue, how would I calculate it?

A: 政府總稅收 = 單位稅款 \(\times\) 交易量 (Tax per unit \(\times\) Transacted quantity)。在這一題中，就是 $2 \times 50 = \$100。

A: Total tax revenue = Tax per unit \(\times\) Transacted quantity. In this question, it would be $2 \times 50 = $100.

考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

「手指平移法」尋找稅額缺口：
在做這種表格題時，不需要畫圖。你可以將左手食指放在「需求量」那一列，右手食指放在「供應量」那一列。尋找兩邊數量相同的格子（例如兩邊都是50）。找到後，往上看它們對應的「價格」。如果這兩個價格相減剛好等於題目的稅額（這題是$2），你就瞬間找到了新的均衡點！

"Finger Sliding Method" to find the tax wedge:
When dealing with table questions like this, you don't need to draw a graph. Place your left index finger on the "Quantity demanded" row and your right index finger on the "Quantity supplied" row. Look for boxes where the quantities are identical (e.g., both are 50). Once found, look up at their corresponding "Prices". If the difference between these two prices exactly equals the tax amount in the question ($2 in this case), you have instantly found the new equilibrium!
