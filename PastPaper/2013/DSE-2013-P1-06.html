HKDSE Economics: Classification of Goods

Topic: 物品的分類 (Types of Goods) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Concepts: 共用品, 私用品, 免費物品, 生產者物品

The Question (題目)

下列哪項有關歐洲足球賽事的電視廣播的陳述是正確的？
Which of the following statements about the television broadcast of European football matches is correct?
A. 若市民可在公眾場所如餐廳和卡拉OK 欣賞賽事廣播，這些賽事的電視廣播便是共用品。
A. a public good if citizens can enjoy the broadcast of matches in public places like restaurants and Karaoke lounges.
B. 若某本地電視台購買了在本港的獨家播映權，這些賽事的電視廣播便是私用品。
B. a private good if a local television broadcasting company has bought the exclusive broadcast right in Hong Kong.
C. 這些賽事的電視廣播是免費物品，因為服務額外一位觀眾是沒有成本的。
C. a free good because there is no cost in serving an additional viewer.
D. 若足球訓練學校播放這些比賽作教導兒童之用， 這些賽事的電視廣播便是生產者物品。
D. a producer good if the broadcast is used to teach the children in a football training school.

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 共用品 (Public Goods) vs 私用品 (Private Goods)
物品的分類取決於其消費性質：
- 競爭性 (Rivalry): 一個人的消費會否減少其他人可消費的數量。
- 排他性 (Excludability): 能否阻止沒有付費的人消費該物品。
純共用品是非競爭性且非排他性的。電視廣播訊號本身是非競爭性的（多一位觀眾不會影響其他觀眾的享受）。
The classification depends on the nature of consumption:
- Rivalry: Whether one person's consumption reduces the amount available for others.
- Excludability: Whether it is possible to prevent non-payers from consuming it.
A pure public good is non-rivalrous and non-excludable. A TV broadcast signal itself is non-rivalrous (an additional viewer does not affect others' enjoyment).
2. 免費物品 (Free Goods) vs 經濟物品 (Economic Goods)
- 免費物品: 生產該物品的機會成本為零。其數量足以滿足所有人的慾望。
- 經濟物品: 生產該物品涉及機會成本（即 \(Cost > 0\)）。
- Free Goods: The opportunity cost of producing the good is zero. Its quantity is sufficient to satisfy all human wants.
- Economic Goods: Producing the good involves an opportunity cost (i.e., \(Cost > 0\)).
3. 消費者物品 (Consumer Goods) vs 生產者物品 (Producer Goods)
取決於物品的用途：
- 消費者物品: 用作直接滿足人類慾望。
- 生產者物品 (資本物品): 用作輔助生產其他物品或服務。
Depends on the use of the good:
- Consumer Goods: Used for direct satisfaction of human wants.
- Producer Goods (Capital Goods): Used to assist in the production of other goods or services.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案：Option D
Correct Answer: Option D
分析 A (錯誤):
物品是否共用品取決於其本質（非競爭性及非排他性），而非消費的地點。在公眾場所播放並不代表它符合共用品的嚴格經濟學定義。
Analysis A (Incorrect):
Whether a good is a public good depends on its intrinsic nature (non-rivalrous and non-excludable), not the location of consumption. Being played in a public place does not fulfill the strict economic definition of a public good.
分析 B (錯誤):
即使電視台購買了獨家播映權（具備排他性），電視廣播訊號本身仍然是非競爭性的。因為多一位觀眾收看，並不會減少其他觀眾收看的質素或數量。因此它不是純私用品。
Analysis B (Incorrect):
Even if the TV station buys exclusive rights (making it excludable), the broadcast signal itself remains non-rivalrous. An additional viewer does not reduce the quality or quantity available to others. Therefore, it is not a pure private good.
分析 C (錯誤):
「服務額外一位觀眾沒有成本」是指其邊際成本為零（非競爭性的特徵），但這不代表它是免費物品。製作及轉播足球賽事需要龐大資源（如攝影、旁述、購買版權），生產該廣播的機會成本大於零，因此它是經濟物品。
Analysis C (Incorrect):
"No cost in serving an additional viewer" means its marginal cost is zero (a feature of non-rivalry), but this does not make it a free good. Producing and broadcasting the match requires massive resources (e.g., filming, commentary, broadcasting rights). The opportunity cost of producing the broadcast is greater than zero, making it an economic good.
分析 D (正確):
當足球學校利用該廣播來教導兒童，該廣播便成為了提供「足球教學服務」的投入要素（Input）。因為它被用作進一步生產，所以被界定為生產者物品。
Analysis D (Correct):
When the football school uses the broadcast to teach children, the broadcast becomes an input for providing "football teaching services". Because it is used for further production, it is classified as a producer good.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point):
這類題目的核心在於測試學生是否明白「同一件物品，會因為其用途或性質的不同而有不同的經濟學分類」。
解題關鍵是逐一檢驗每個選項的因果關係是否成立。例如，C選項故意混淆了「服務多一人的成本 (Marginal Cost)」與「生產該物品的成本 (Opportunity Cost)」。
Pivot Point:
The core of this type of question is to test if students understand that "the same good can have different economic classifications based on its use or nature."
The key is to verify the causal relationship in each option. For example, Option C deliberately confuses "the cost of serving one more person (Marginal Cost)" with "the cost of producing the good (Opportunity Cost)".

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：混淆「非競爭性」與「免費物品」
很多學生看到「服務額外一位觀眾是沒有成本的 (Cost of serving an additional user is zero)」，就立刻聯想到「免費 (Free)」。
記住：
- 服務額外一人的成本 = 0 \(\rightarrow\) 非競爭性 (Non-rivalrous)
- 生產該物品的總機會成本 = 0 \(\rightarrow\) 免費物品 (Free Good)
Trap: Confusing "Non-rivalrous" with "Free Good"
Many students see "no cost in serving an additional user" and immediately think "Free".
Remember:
- Cost of serving one extra person = 0 \(\rightarrow\) Non-rivalrous
- Total opportunity cost of producing the good = 0 \(\rightarrow\) Free Good

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 電視廣播不是免費給市民看的嗎？為什麼不是免費物品？
Q: Isn't TV broadcast free for citizens to watch? Why isn't it a free good?
A: 經濟學上的「免費物品」是指生產該物品不需要放棄其他選擇（機會成本為零），例如空氣。雖然市民可能不需要直接付費觀看電視，但電視台製作節目需要花費人力物力，因此它是經濟物品。消費者不用付費，不代表生產沒有成本。
A: In economics, a "free good" means producing the good requires giving up no other alternatives (opportunity cost is zero), like air. Although citizens might not pay directly to watch TV, the station spends resources to produce the program, making it an economic good. A zero price for consumers does not mean zero production cost.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

用途判定法 (The "Purpose" Rule)
在DSE考試中，區分消費者物品和生產者物品，只需問一個問題：「這件物品是用來自己享受，還是用來幫忙賺錢/提供服務？」
選項D中，足球學校用廣播來「教導兒童」（提供服務），明顯是為了進一步生產，秒選D！
The "Purpose" Rule
In the DSE exam, to distinguish between consumer and producer goods, just ask one question: "Is this good used for personal enjoyment, or used to help make money/provide a service?"
In Option D, the football school uses the broadcast to "teach children" (providing a service), which is clearly for further production. Choose D instantly!
