HKDSE Economics Masterclass

📚 Topic (課題): 總需求與總供應 (AD-AS Model) & 財政政策 (Fiscal Policy)

⭐ Difficulty (難度): ★★★★☆ (4/5)

🔑 Key Concepts (核心概念): 通縮差距 (Deflationary Gap), 財政赤字 (Budget Deficit), 貨幣供應 (Money Supply)

1. The Question (題目)

原初某經濟在全民充分就業均衡運作，而政府支出相等於其稅項收入。下列哪項會導致通縮（產出）差距和財政赤字同時出現？

  • A. 減稅
  • B. 減少貨幣供應
  • C. 減少政府支出
  • D. 進口意欲下降

Initially an economy operates at the full-employment equilibrium and the government expenditure equals its tax revenue. Which of the following will result in a coexistence of deflationary (output) gap and budget deficit?

  • A. a reduction in taxes
  • B. a reduction in money supply
  • C. a reduction in government expenditure
  • D. a reduction in desire to import

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要拆解這條題目，我們需要掌握兩個核心概念的定義與成因：

To solve this question, we must understand the definitions and causes of two core concepts:

1. 通縮差距 (Deflationary Gap)

當均衡產出 \(Y\) 低於充分就業產出 \(Y_f\) 時出現。通常由總需求 \(AD\) 下降引起。

Occurs when the equilibrium output \(Y\) is lower than the full-employment output \(Y_f\). It is usually caused by a decrease in Aggregate Demand \(AD\).

2. 財政赤字 (Budget Deficit)

當政府支出 \(G\) 大於稅收 \(T\) 時出現。注意：稅收 \(T\) 會隨着國民收入 \(Y\) 的升跌而同向變動（假設存在入息稅）。

Occurs when government expenditure \(G\) is greater than tax revenue \(T\). Note: Tax revenue \(T\) moves in the same direction as national income \(Y\) (assuming an income tax exists).

$$ AD = C + I + G + (X - M) $$ $$ \text{Budget Deficit occurs when: } G > T $$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是：選項 B (Option B)

The correct answer is: Option B

❌ A. 減稅 (a reduction in taxes)

減稅會增加可支配收入，刺激消費 \(C\)，導致 \(AD\) 上升。這會產生通脹差距 (Inflationary gap)，而非通縮差距。雖然稅收減少會導致財政赤字，但產出差距的方向錯誤。

A tax cut increases disposable income, stimulating consumption \(C\), leading to an increase in \(AD\). This creates an inflationary gap, not a deflationary gap. Although lower taxes cause a budget deficit, the output gap direction is wrong.

✅ B. 減少貨幣供應 (a reduction in money supply)

減少貨幣供應會推高利率 \(r\)，導致投資 \(I\) 和消費 \(C\) 下降，\(AD\) 隨之下降，產生通縮差距 (Deflationary gap)。同時，由於國民收入 \(Y\) 下降，政府的入息稅收 \(T\) 會自動減少。在政府支出 \(G\) 不變的情況下，\(G > T\)，導致財政赤字 (Budget deficit)。兩者同時成立！

A decrease in money supply pushes up the interest rate \(r\), causing investment \(I\) and consumption \(C\) to fall. \(AD\) decreases, creating a deflationary gap. Meanwhile, as national income \(Y\) falls, government income tax revenue \(T\) automatically decreases. With government expenditure \(G\) unchanged, \(G > T\), resulting in a budget deficit. Both conditions are met!

❌ C. 減少政府支出 (a reduction in government expenditure)

減少 \(G\) 會令 \(AD\) 下降，產生通縮差距。然而，雖然 \(Y\) 下降會令稅收 \(T\) 減少，但由於乘數效應及邊際稅率小於1，\(G\) 的跌幅通常大於 \(T\) 的跌幅。因此，這項政策會傾向導致財政盈餘 (Budget surplus)，而非赤字。

A decrease in \(G\) lowers \(AD\), creating a deflationary gap. However, while the fall in \(Y\) reduces tax revenue \(T\), the initial drop in \(G\) is larger than the induced drop in \(T\). Therefore, this policy tends to result in a budget surplus, not a deficit.

❌ D. 進口意欲下降 (a reduction in desire to import)

進口 \(M\) 減少會令淨出口 \(NX\) 上升，導致 \(AD\) 增加，產生通脹差距 (Inflationary gap)。國民收入 \(Y\) 上升會增加稅收 \(T\)，導致財政盈餘。兩者皆不符合題目要求。

A decrease in imports \(M\) increases net exports \(NX\), leading to an increase in \(AD\), creating an inflationary gap. The rise in national income \(Y\) increases tax revenue \(T\), leading to a budget surplus. Neither condition matches the question.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)：
這題的關鍵在於明白「稅收 \(T\) 是國民收入 \(Y\) 的函數」。當題目沒有提及政府主動改變稅率時，任何令 \(AD\) 下降（從而令 \(Y\) 下降）的因素，都會被動地令稅收 \(T\) 減少。

Pivot Point:
The key is understanding that "Tax revenue \(T\) is a function of national income \(Y\)". When the government doesn't actively change tax rates, any factor that decreases \(AD\) (and thus decreases \(Y\)) will passively reduce tax revenue \(T\).

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ 陷阱： 很多學生會誤選 C。他們知道減少政府支出會導致通縮差距，並錯誤地以為「經濟差 = 政府一定財赤」。

⚠️ Trap: Many students mistakenly choose C. They know cutting government spending causes a deflationary gap, and falsely assume "bad economy = government must have a deficit".

真相： 減少政府支出 (\(G \downarrow\)) 本身就是一種改善財政狀況（減少赤字/增加盈餘）的手段！主動減少支出所省下的錢，會多於因經濟變差而流失的稅收。

The Truth: Cutting government spending (\(G \downarrow\)) is inherently a measure to improve the fiscal balance! The money saved from actively cutting spending outweighs the tax revenue lost due to a worsening economy.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

🙋‍♂️ 學生問： 為什麼貨幣供應減少會影響政府的財政預算？題目明明沒有提及政府政策！

Student: Why does a decrease in money supply affect the government budget? The question didn't mention fiscal policy!

👨‍🏫 導師答： 這是宏觀經濟學中非常經典的「連鎖反應」。貨幣政策（改變貨幣供應）雖然不是財政政策，但它會影響整體的國民收入 \(Y\)。在現實和考試設定中，政府的稅收（如利得稅、薪俸稅）是依賴於 \(Y\) 的。當經濟轉差（\(Y\) 下降），市民和企業賺少了錢，交給政府的稅款 \(T\) 自然減少。在政府開支 \(G\) 鎖定不變的情況下，收入 \(T\) 減少就會造成赤字！

Tutor: This is a classic "chain reaction" in macroeconomics. Monetary policy (changing money supply) isn't fiscal policy, but it affects overall national income \(Y\). Tax revenues (like income and profit taxes) depend on \(Y\). When the economy worsens (\(Y\) falls), people and firms earn less, so the tax \(T\) paid to the government naturally falls. With government spending \(G\) locked and unchanged, a drop in revenue \(T\) creates a deficit!

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺法 (The 10-Second Shortcut)：

  1. 題目要求「通縮差距」，即 \(AD\) 必須下降。立即淘汰 A (減稅令 \(AD \uparrow\)) 和 D (進口意欲下降令 \(NX \uparrow\)，\(AD \uparrow\))。
  2. 剩下 B 和 C。題目要求「財政赤字」。選項 C 是「減少政府支出」，這明顯是幫政府省錢（走向盈餘）的政策，不可能導致赤字。
  3. 因此，答案只能是 B！完全不需要畫圖或寫複雜算式。

The 10-Second Shortcut:

  1. The question asks for a "deflationary gap", meaning \(AD\) must fall. Immediately eliminate A (tax cuts make \(AD \uparrow\)) and D (lower import desire makes \(NX \uparrow\), \(AD \uparrow\)).
  2. Only B and C remain. The question asks for a "budget deficit". Option C is "reducing government expenditure", which obviously saves the government money (moving towards a surplus), so it can't cause a deficit.
  3. Therefore, the answer MUST be B! No graphs or complex formulas needed.
