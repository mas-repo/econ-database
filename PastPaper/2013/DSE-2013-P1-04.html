2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

📌 短期 (Short Run) vs 長期 (Long Run)

在經濟學中，「短期」是指至少有一種生產要素（如機器）的數量是固定不變的。而「長期」則是所有生產要素的數量都可以改變。

In economics, the "Short Run" is a period in which the quantity of at least one factor of production (e.g., machines) is fixed. The "Long Run" is a period in which the quantities of all factors of production are variable.

📌 邊際回報遞減定律 (Law of Diminishing Marginal Returns)

這定律指出，在短期內（即有固定生產要素的情況下），當我們不斷增加可變生產要素（如勞工），到某一點之後，其邊際產出（Marginal Product）會開始下降。

This law states that in the short run (with fixed factors of production), as we continuously add successive units of a variable factor (like labour), eventually, the Marginal Product of that variable factor will begin to decline.

🧮 核心公式 (Core Formulas)

$$ Total\ Product\ (TP) = Average\ Product\ (AP) \times Labour\ (L) $$
$$ Marginal\ Product\ (MP) = \frac{\Delta TP}{\Delta L} $$

總產出 \(TP\) 等於平均產出 \(AP\) 乘以勞工數量 \(L\)。而邊際產出 \(MP\) 則是總產出的變動除以勞工數量的變動。

Total Product \(TP\) equals Average Product \(AP\) multiplied by the amount of Labour \(L\). Marginal Product \(MP\) is the change in total product divided by the change in labour.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ 陷阱一：被持續上升的 AP 欺騙 (Fooled by rising AP)

很多學生看到 AP 一直在上升（30 ➔ 40 ➔ 50 ➔ 55），就誤以為回報沒有遞減，從而錯誤選擇了 A。記住，即使邊際產出 (MP) 正在下降（如這裡的 90 跌至 80），只要 \(MP > AP\)，平均產出 (AP) 仍然會被拉高！

Many students see AP constantly rising (30 ➔ 40 ➔ 50 ➔ 55) and mistakenly assume returns are not diminishing, wrongly choosing A. Remember, even if Marginal Product (MP) is falling (like from 90 to 80 here), as long as \(MP > AP\), the Average Product (AP) will still be pulled up!

⚠️ 陷阱二：混淆長短期的定義 (Confusing Short/Long Run definitions)

有些學生以為只有 2 部機器代表「規模很小」，就隨便亂選。在經濟學中，長短期的唯一分別在於有沒有固定的生產要素。只要數量不變，就是短期。

Some students think having only 2 machines means "small scale" and guess blindly. In economics, the ONLY difference between the short and long run is whether there are fixed factors of production. If the quantity doesn't change, it's the short run.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

🙋‍♂️ 學生問：為什麼當第 6 個工人的 MP 下降到 80，但 AP 卻還能從 50 升到 55？

Student: Why is it that when the 6th worker's MP drops to 80, the AP can still rise from 50 to 55?

👨‍🏫 導師答：

想像一下，你之前的平均考試分數是 50 分。你最新一次的考試（邊際分數）考了 80 分。雖然你這次考的 80 分比上次（假設上次考了 90 分）退步了，但因為 80 分仍然高於你原本的平均分（50分），所以你的總平均分還是會被拉高到 55 分！只要 \(MP > AP\)，\(AP\) 就會上升。

Tutor: Imagine your past average test score is 50. In your newest test (your marginal score), you get an 80. Even though your 80 is a drop from your previous test (let's say you got 90 before), because 80 is still higher than your old average of 50, your new overall average will still be pulled up to 55! As long as \(MP > AP\), \(AP\) will rise.
