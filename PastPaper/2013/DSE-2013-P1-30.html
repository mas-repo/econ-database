Economics HKDSE Tutor Guide

Topic: Macroeconomics - Aggregate Demand & Money Demand 總體經濟學：總需求與貨幣需求

Difficulty (難度): ★★★★☆

Key Concepts (核心概念): Consumption Function (消費函數), Net Exports (淨出口), Transaction Demand for Money (交易貨幣需求), Aggregate Demand (總需求)

2. The Question (題目)

假設某經濟的消費意欲下降，其淨出口會_______，而持有作交易用途的貨幣金額會_______。
If there is a decrease in the desire to consume, the net exports of an economy will _______ and the amount of money held for transaction purposes will _______.
  • A. 下跌 … 下降 (decrease … decrease)
  • B. 下跌 … 維持不變 (decrease … remain constant)
  • C. 上升 … 上升 (increase … increase)
  • D. 上升 … 下降 (increase … decrease)

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 淨出口與進口函數 (Net Exports and Import Function)
淨出口是指入口與出口的差額。當本地消費意欲下降時，市民整體的消費減少，這包含了對本地產品及外國進口產品的消費。因此，進口值會下降。
1. Net Exports and Import Function
Net exports refer to the difference between exports and imports. When the local desire to consume decreases, citizens spend less overall, which includes spending on both domestic and imported foreign goods. Therefore, the value of imports will decrease.
$$ \text{Net Exports (NX)} = \text{Exports (X)} - \text{Imports (M)} $$
2. 交易貨幣需求 (Transaction Demand for Money)
人們持有貨幣作交易用途是為了應付日常買賣。這個金額與國民收入（或名義 GDP）成正比。當消費下降，總需求下跌，導致國民收入下跌，人們需要的交易貨幣金額也會隨之減少。
2. Transaction Demand for Money
People hold money for transaction purposes to meet daily trading needs. This amount is positively related to national income (or nominal GDP). When consumption drops, aggregate demand falls, leading to a decrease in national income, and subsequently, people need less money for transactions.
$$ M_t = f(Y) \quad \text{where} \quad Y = \text{National Income (國民收入)} $$

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option / 正確答案: D

A. 下跌 … 下降 ❌
分析：後半部正確（交易貨幣下降），但前半部錯誤。消費意欲下降會導致進口 \( M \) 減少。由於出口 \( X \) 不受本地消費直接影響（假設不變），淨出口 \( NX = X - M \) 實際上會上升，而不是下跌。
A. decrease … decrease ❌
Analysis: The second part is correct (transaction money decreases), but the first part is wrong. A decrease in the desire to consume reduces imports \( M \). Since exports \( X \) are not directly affected by local consumption (assumed constant), net exports \( NX = X - M \) will actually increase, not decrease.
B. 下跌 … 維持不變 ❌
分析：前半部錯誤（如上所述淨出口會上升）。後半部也錯誤，國民收入 \( Y \) 減少必定會導致作交易用途的貨幣金額下降，不會維持不變。
B. decrease … remain constant ❌
Analysis: The first part is wrong (net exports increase, as explained above). The second part is also wrong; a decrease in national income \( Y \) will definitely lead to a decrease in transaction money, it will not remain constant.
C. 上升 … 上升 ❌
分析：前半部正確（淨出口上升）。但後半部錯誤，總需求下跌導致名義國民收入 \( Y \) 下降，交易貨幣需求 \( M_t \) 應該跟隨下降，而非上升。
C. increase … increase ❌
Analysis: The first part is correct (net exports increase). However, the second part is wrong; a drop in aggregate demand lowers nominal national income \( Y \), so the transaction demand for money \( M_t \) should decrease, not increase.
D. 上升 … 下降 ✅
分析：完全正確。消費 \( C \) 下降 $\rightarrow$ 購買進口貨減少，即進口 \( M \) 下降 $\rightarrow$ 淨出口 \( NX \) 上升。同時，消費 \( C \) 下降 $\rightarrow$ 總需求 \( AD \) 下降 $\rightarrow$ 國民收入 \( Y \) 下降 $\rightarrow$ 用作日常交易的貨幣需求 \( M_t \) 下降。
D. increase … decrease ✅
Analysis: Perfectly correct. Consumption \( C \) decreases $\rightarrow$ fewer imported goods are bought, meaning imports \( M \) decrease $\rightarrow$ net exports \( NX \) increase. Simultaneously, consumption \( C \) decreases $\rightarrow$ aggregate demand \( AD \) decreases $\rightarrow$ national income \( Y \) decreases $\rightarrow$ money demanded for daily transactions \( M_t \) decreases.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這道題目的「解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)」在於理解一個行為（消費下降）會引發兩個不同市場的連鎖反應：
  1. 商品市場 (Goods Market)： 消費不單指本地商品，還包含外地商品。學生必須把「消費」連結到「進口」。
  2. 貨幣市場 (Money Market)： 學生必須知道「作交易用途的貨幣金額」的唯一決定因素是「收入 (Income)」。找出了收入的變化，就能決定此金額的變化。
The "Pivot Point" of this question lies in understanding that one action (decrease in consumption) triggers chain reactions in two different markets:
  1. Goods Market: Consumption doesn't just mean local goods; it includes foreign goods. Students must link "consumption" to "imports".
  2. Money Market: Students must know that the sole determinant for "amount of money held for transaction purposes" is "Income". Once you determine the change in income, you can determine the change in this amount.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 陷阱一：誤以為消費只影響本地 GDP。 很多學生忘記了香港是一個開放型經濟體，市民的消費包含了大量進口貨。消費意欲下降，必然導致進口 \( M \) 下降。
    Trap 1: Assuming consumption only affects domestic GDP. Many students forget that in an open economy, citizens' consumption includes a lot of imported goods. A drop in the desire to consume inevitably leads to a drop in imports \( M \).
  • 陷阱二：混淆資產貨幣需求與交易貨幣需求。 題目明確指出「交易用途 (transaction purposes)」。如果題目問「資產用途 (asset purposes)」，那就要考慮利率的變化。但針對交易需求，我們只看收入 \( Y \)。
    Trap 2: Confusing asset demand with transaction demand for money. The question specifically states "transaction purposes". If the question asked about "asset purposes", you would need to consider interest rate changes. But for transaction demand, we only look at income \( Y \).

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼消費意欲下降，不會導致出口下跌？
A: 因為一個國家的出口額，主要取決於外國人的收入和外國人的消費意欲，而不是本國國民的消費意欲。因此我們假設出口 \( X \) 維持不變。

Q: Why doesn't a decrease in the desire to consume cause exports to fall?
A: Because a country's export volume mainly depends on foreigners' income and foreigners' desire to consume, not the domestic citizens' desire to consume. Therefore, we assume exports \( X \) remain constant.

Q: 利率會改變嗎？這會影響答案嗎？
A: 貨幣需求下跌可能會導致利率下跌。但是，利率只會影響「作資產用途的貨幣金額 (Asset demand for money)」。題目精準地問「作交易用途的貨幣金額」，這個部分是與利率無關 (Interest-inelastic) 的，只受收入影響。

Q: Will the interest rate change? Does it affect the answer?
A: The drop in money demand might cause the interest rate to fall. However, the interest rate only affects the "asset demand for money". The question accurately asks for the "amount of money held for transaction purposes", which is interest-inelastic and affected solely by income.

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

消去法 (Elimination Method)：
在考場上，你可以先分析比較有把握的一半。
第一步：消費減少 $\rightarrow$ 大家少買了外國貨 $\rightarrow$ 進口減少 $\rightarrow$ 淨出口變好（上升）。
即時刪除選項 A 和 B！
第二步：只剩下 C 和 D。消費減少，經濟變差（收入跌）。大家口袋裡不需要放那麼多現金去逛街購物了 $\rightarrow$ 交易貨幣需求下降。
選 D，完成！
Elimination Method:
In the exam, analyze the half you are more confident about first.
Step 1: Less consumption $\rightarrow$ people buy fewer foreign goods $\rightarrow$ imports drop $\rightarrow$ net exports improve (increase).
Immediately eliminate options A and B!
Step 2: Only C and D remain. Less consumption means the economy slows down (income drops). People don't need to hold as much cash in their pockets for shopping $\rightarrow$ transaction money demand decreases.
Choose D, done!
