HKDSE Economics Tutorial

✏️ 主題 (Topic): 廠商的擴張與合併類型 (Expansion of Firms and Types of Integration)

⭐ 難度 (Difficulty): ★★☆☆☆ (2/5)

🔑 關鍵字 (Key Concepts): 後向合併 (Backward Integration), 橫向合併 (Horizontal Integration), 市場佔有率 (Market Share)

1. 題目 (The Question)

某廠商可透過進行___________確保原料供應穩定，而它可以透過進行___________增加市場佔有率。

  • A. 後向合併 ... 橫向合併
  • B. 後向合併 ... 側向合併
  • C. 前向合併 ... 橫向合併
  • D. 前向合併 ... 側向合併

A firm can ensure a steady supply of raw materials by engaging in ___________ while it can increase its market share by engaging in ___________.

  • A. backward integration ... horizontal integration
  • B. backward integration ... lateral integration
  • C. forward integration ... horizontal integration
  • D. forward integration ... lateral integration

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解答這條問題，我們必須清楚分辨四種主要的合併類型 (Types of Integration)：

To solve this question, we must clearly distinguish between the four main types of integration:

  • 橫向合併 (Horizontal Integration): 處於同一行業、同一生產階段的企業合併（例如：兩家珍珠奶茶店合併）。其主要目的是減少競爭及增加市場佔有率 (Increase Market Share)，長遠可達致規模經濟。 Firms in the same industry and same stage of production merging (e.g., two bubble tea shops). The main motive is to reduce competition and increase market share, leading to economies of scale.
  • 垂直合併 (Vertical Integration): 處於同一行業，但不同生產階段的企業合併。分為兩種：
    Firms in the same industry but different stages of production. Divided into two types:
    • 後向合併 (Backward Integration): 向前一個生產階段擴張（即走向供應商）。主要目的是確保原料供應穩定 (Ensure steady supply of raw materials)。
      Expanding to the previous stage of production (towards suppliers). Motive: Ensure a steady supply of raw materials.
    • 前向合併 (Forward Integration): 向下一個生產階段擴張（即走向零售/消費者）。主要目的是確保產品有穩定的銷售渠道。
      Expanding to the next stage of production (towards retail/consumers). Motive: Ensure steady retail outlets for products.
  • 側向合併 (Lateral Integration): 生產相關但不相同產品的企業合併（例如：報社與雜誌社）。主要目的是發揮協同效應或分散投資風險。 Firms producing related but different products merging (e.g., a newspaper and a magazine). Motive: Synergy and risk diversification.

概念公式補充 (Concept Formula):

Market Share Formula:

$$ \text{Market Share} = \left( \frac{\text{Firm's Output } (Q)}{\text{Total Market Output}} \right) \times 100\% $$

當兩家競爭對手（橫向）合併時，它們的產量 \( Q \) 相加，總分母不變，因此市場佔有率必然上升。

When two competitors (horizontal) merge, their combined output \( Q \) increases while the market total remains the same, definitively increasing market share.

3. 答案與詳細分析 (Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): Option A

選項 A (Option A): 正確 (Correct)。題目的第一條橫線提到「確保原料供應穩定」，這意味著廠商需要控制它的上游供應商，這正是後向合併的定義。第二條橫線提到「增加市場佔有率」，要做到這一點，廠商需要吞併現有的競爭對手，這正是橫向合併的特徵。

The first blank mentions "ensure a steady supply of raw materials", meaning the firm needs to control its upstream suppliers, which defines backward integration. The second blank mentions "increase market share", which requires acquiring current competitors, characteristic of horizontal integration.

選項 B (Option B): 錯誤 (Incorrect)。雖然第一部分「後向合併」正確，但第二部分「側向合併」是錯的。側向合併（如麵包店收購蛋糕店）雖然擴大了公司總規模，但並不會直接增加單一產品（如麵包）的市場佔有率。

Although the first part "backward integration" is correct, "lateral integration" is wrong. Lateral integration (e.g., a bakery buying a cake shop) increases overall company size but does not directly increase market share in a specific product market (e.g., bread).

選項 C (Option C): 錯誤 (Incorrect)。「前向合併」是指收購下游的零售商或分銷商，目的是確保銷售渠道，而不是為了確保「原料供應」。

"Forward integration" refers to acquiring downstream retailers or distributors to secure retail outlets, not to secure "raw materials".

選項 D (Option D): 錯誤 (Incorrect)。這個選項的兩個部分都與題目描述的經濟動機完全不符。

Both parts of this option completely mismatch the economic motives described in the question.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

做這類題目時，我們需要尋找「關鍵字（Pivot Points）」：

When tackling this type of question, we must look for the Pivot Points (Keywords):

  • 關鍵字 1：原料 (Raw Materials) ➔ 原料位於生產鏈的起始端。走向起始端 = 向「後」退 (Backward)。
    Keyword 1: Raw Materials ➔ Raw materials are at the start of the supply chain. Moving towards the start = Moving "Backward".
  • 關鍵字 2：市場佔有率 (Market Share) ➔ 市場佔有率反映你在同業競爭者中的地位。要消滅競爭對手來壯大自己 = 合併同行 = 「橫」向發展 (Horizontal)。
    Keyword 2: Market Share ➔ Market share reflects your status among competitors. Eliminating rivals to grow = merging with peers = Moving "Horizontally".

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

⚠️ 混淆前向與後向合併 (Confusing Forward and Backward Integration):
很多學生會以「產品的流動方向」去記，誤以為獲取原料是為了生產，所以是「向前」。正確的記憶方法是：以廠商現在所處的階段為中心。供應商在你之前（Past/Behind），所以是後向；零售商在你之後（Future/Ahead），所以是前向。

⚠️ Confusing Forward and Backward:
Many students trace the "flow of the product" and mistakenly think getting materials to make a product is moving "forward". The correct way to remember is centering on the firm's current stage. Suppliers are before you (Past/Behind), hence Backward. Retailers are after you (Future/Ahead), hence Forward.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

🙋‍♂️ 學生問： 為什麼側向合併 (Lateral Integration) 不能增加市場佔有率？公司規模不是變大了嗎？

🙋‍♂️ Student: Why doesn't Lateral Integration increase market share? Doesn't the company size get bigger?

👨‍🏫 導師答： 公司整體的資產和利潤確實會增加，但是「市場佔有率」是針對單一特定市場而言的。例如，麥當勞收購一家服裝店（側向合併），麥當勞的母公司變大了，但它在「快餐市場」的佔有率並沒有因為賣衣服而增加。只有收購另一家快餐店（橫向合併），才能直接增加在快餐市場的佔有率。

👨‍🏫 Tutor: The company's overall assets and profits do increase, but "market share" applies to a single specific market. If McDonald's buys a clothing store (Lateral), the parent company grows, but its market share in the "fast-food market" doesn't increase. Only buying another fast-food chain (Horizontal) directly increases market share in that specific market.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

⚡ 消除法速解 (Elimination Method):
對於這種「雙空格」題目，千萬不要兩個空格一起看！
第一步：只看第一句「原料供應穩定」，這絕對是後向合併。這時立刻劃走 C 和 D 選項。
第二步：現在只剩下 A 和 B。比較「橫向」和「側向」，看到「市場佔有率」秒選「橫向」。答案 A 瞬間得出，節省考試時間！

⚡ Elimination Method:
For "double-blank" questions, never look at both blanks simultaneously!
Step 1: Look ONLY at the first phrase "steady supply of raw materials". This is definitely backward integration. Immediately cross out options C and D.
Step 2: You are left with A and B. Compare "Horizontal" vs "Lateral". Seeing "market share", instantly pick "Horizontal". You arrive at Option A in seconds, saving valuable exam time!
