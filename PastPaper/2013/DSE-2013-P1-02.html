HKDSE Economics MCQ Analysis

📊 Topic: Opportunity Cost (機會成本) ⭐ Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ (3/5) 🔑 Concepts: Cost vs Value, Time Cost

1. The Question (題目)

書豪將觀看大滿貫得主李娜參與的一場網球比賽。下列哪項會令書豪觀看該場網球比賽的成本上升？
Jeremy is going to watch a tennis match in which Li Na, a Grand Slam winner, will play. Which of the following will increase the cost to Jeremy of watching the tennis match?
  • A. 由於交通擠塞，他遲到了30分鐘。
    A. He is 30 minutes late for the match due to traffic congestion.
  • B. 他的視線被前面的觀眾阻擋。
    B. His view is blocked by the spectators in front of him.
  • C. 由於暴雨關係，比賽時間延長了一小時。
    C. Due to a rainstorm, the match is extended by an hour.
  • D. 李娜受傷，沒有出場比賽。
    D. Li Na is injured and does not show up for the match.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

機會成本 (Opportunity Cost)
機會成本是指在作出一項選擇時，所放棄的選項中價值最高的一個。簡單來說，成本就是你為了得到某事物而「付出了甚麼代價」（包括金錢和時間）。
Opportunity cost is defined as the highest-valued option forgone. In simple terms, cost is what you have to "give up" (including both money and time) to acquire or do something.
$$\text{Opportunity Cost} = \text{Highest-valued Option Forgone}$$ $$\text{Total Cost} = \text{Explicit Cost (Money)} + \text{Implicit Cost (Time)}$$
核心概念：成本 \( C \) 與價值 \( V \) 的區別
經濟學中，觀看比賽的「價值」(Value/Utility) 是指你從中獲得的滿足感或快樂。而「成本」(Cost) 是指你付出的門票錢和時間。體驗變差（不開心）會降低價值，但絕不會增加成本！
Core Concept: The difference between Cost \( C \) and Value \( V \)
In economics, the "value" or "utility" of watching a match refers to the satisfaction or happiness you derive from it. The "cost" refers to the ticket price and time sacrificed. A worse experience reduces value, but it does NOT increase cost!

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: C (正確答案：C)

選項 A 錯在哪裡？
由於遲到了30分鐘，書豪實際觀看比賽的時間減少了。他犧牲的時間並沒有增加，反而他因為錯過了精彩片段而令觀看比賽的價值 (Value) 下降。成本並沒有上升。
Why is Option A wrong?
Being 30 minutes late means Jeremy spends less time watching the match. The time he sacrificed did not increase. Instead, missing part of the match decreases the value/utility he receives. The cost remains unchanged or even decreases.
選項 B 錯在哪裡？
視線被阻擋是一件不愉快的事，這會大大降低他觀看比賽的樂趣（效用/價值下降）。但他為觀看比賽而放棄的金錢或時間沒有任何改變，因此成本不變。
Why is Option B wrong?
Having a blocked view is an unpleasant experience that greatly reduces his enjoyment (utility/value drops). However, the amount of money and time he gave up to watch the match remains exactly the same, so the cost does not change.
選項 C 為何正確？
比賽時間延長了一小時，這意味著書豪需要額外付出多一個小時來觀看整場比賽。這個額外的小時本來可以帶給他其他收入或休閒活動（即被放棄的最高價值選項增加），因此他的時間成本 (Time Cost) 上升了。
Why is Option C correct?
The match is extended by an hour, meaning Jeremy has to sacrifice an extra hour to finish watching the match. This extra hour could have been used to earn income or enjoy other leisure activities (the forgone option increases). Thus, his time cost increases.
選項 D 錯在哪裡？
李娜缺陣會令這場大滿貫級別的比賽失色不少，導致觀看比賽的得益或價值 (Value) 大幅下降。但他付出的門票費用和時間資源並無增加，故成本不變。
Why is Option D wrong?
Li Na's absence makes the match much less attractive, leading to a massive drop in the benefit or value of watching. But the ticket fee and time resources he committed have not increased, so the cost is unchanged.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)：
做這類題目時，必須在腦海中區分兩條獨立的算式：
1. 成本 (Cost) = 放棄的錢 + 放棄的時間
2. 價值/效用 (Value) = 獲得的快樂 - 經歷的痛苦
Pivot Point:
When tackling this type of question, mentally separate these two formulas:
1. Cost = Money given up + Time given up
2. Value/Utility = Happiness gained - Pain experienced

干擾資訊 (Distractors)：
題目中的「交通擠塞」、「視線被阻」和「球星受傷」全都是干擾項。考評局故意用這些「負面經歷」來誘使同學以為這就是「代價（成本）」。記住，心情變差不是成本上升！
Distractors:
"Traffic congestion", "blocked view", and "injured star" are all distractors. The HKEAA purposely uses these "negative experiences" to trick students into thinking they are "costs/prices paid". Remember, feeling unhappy is not an increase in economic cost!

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ 致命陷阱：將「效用下降」(Decrease in Utility) 當作「成本上升」(Increase in Cost)
很多同學覺得：「我花了幾百塊買門票，結果李娜不來，我這幾百塊『白花了』，所以成本很高！」
糾正： 門票錢已經付了（沉沒成本 Sunk Cost，或早已固定的顯性成本）。你看得不開心，代表這場比賽帶給你的價值低於預期，但你付出的成本由始至終都只是那張門票的價錢和原定的時間。

⚠️ Fatal Trap: Confusing "Decrease in Utility" with "Increase in Cost"
Many students think: "I spent hundreds of dollars on the ticket, but Li Na didn't show up. My money is 'wasted', so the cost is high!"
Correction: The ticket price is already paid (sunk cost, or fixed explicit cost). Being unhappy means the value is lower than expected, but the cost you paid is exactly the same ticket price and the scheduled time.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 在選項 C 中，如果書豪決定不看那延長的一小時，提早離場，他的成本還會上升嗎？
Q: In Option C, if Jeremy decides NOT to watch the extended hour and leaves early, does his cost still increase?
A: 不會。如果他提早離場，他實際犧牲的時間就沒有增加，因此觀看的成本不變。然而，DSE 題目的預設語境（觀看該場網球比賽）通常暗示「觀看整場比賽」。若比賽延長而他選擇看完，他必然犧牲了額外一小時的替代用途，故成本上升。
A: No. If he leaves early, the actual time he sacrifices doesn't increase, so the cost of watching remains unchanged. However, the default context in DSE questions ("watching the match") usually implies "watching the entire match". Since the match is extended and he chooses to finish it, he necessarily sacrifices the alternative use of that extra hour, increasing the cost.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

💡 秒殺技巧：尋找「資源投入」的變化
當被問到「甚麼會令成本上升？」時，立刻掃描四個選項，問自己一個問題：哪一個選項逼使主角投入更多的「時間」或「金錢」？
💡 Speed-solving Trick: Look for changes in "Resource Input"
When asked "What increases the cost?", immediately scan the four options and ask yourself one question: Which option forces the protagonist to input MORE "time" or "money"?

  • A = 時間減少 (Less time) ❌
  • B = 時間/金錢不變 (No change in time/money) ❌
  • C = 時間增加 (More time spent) ✅
  • D = 時間/金錢不變 (No change in time/money) ❌
只需 5 秒鐘，就能鎖定答案 C！
You can lock in answer C in just 5 seconds!
