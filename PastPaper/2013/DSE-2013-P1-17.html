DSE Economics: Market Structure

Topic: Market Structure (市場結構) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Monopoly, Geographical Market
The Question (題目)

香港電力行業的市場結構屬

The market structure of the electric power industry in Hong Kong is

  • A. 寡頭壟斷，因為市場上有兩間具支配力的公司。 an oligopoly because there are two dominating companies in the market.
  • B. 寡頭壟斷，因為各公司的訂價策略互相影響。 an oligopoly because the pricing strategies of the companies are interdependent.
  • C. 壟斷，因為各公司可提高價格而不會流失顧客。 a monopoly because the companies can increase the price without losing customers.
  • D. 壟斷，因為在任何地區只有一間電力公司提供電力。 a monopoly because there is only one electric company providing electricity in any district.
Concept Review (概念重溫)

要判斷市場結構，我們必須先定義「市場」的範圍。 To determine market structure, we must first define the scope of the "market".

1. 壟斷 (Monopoly)

  • 市場上只有一個賣家 \( (N=1) \)。 There is only one seller in the market \( (N=1) \).
  • 產品沒有相近替代品。 No close substitutes for the product.
  • 進入壁壘極高。 Extremely high barriers to entry.

2. 寡頭壟斷 (Oligopoly)

  • 市場上有少數具支配力的賣家 \( (\text{Few dominant sellers}) \)。 There are a few dominant sellers in the market.
  • 互相影響 (Interdependence): 一間公司的決策會直接影響對手。 Interdependence: The decision of one firm directly affects its rivals.
Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)
正確答案： Option D
Option A (錯誤 / Incorrect)

雖然香港整體有兩間電力公司（中電和港燈），但這是一個陷阱。這兩間公司在地理上是分割的，它們並不互相競爭。對於消費者而言，並沒有選擇權。

Although there are two power companies in Hong Kong overall (CLP and HK Electric), this is a trap. These two companies are geographically segmented and do not compete with each other. Consumers have no choice.

Option B (錯誤 / Incorrect)

寡頭壟斷的特徵是「互相影響」。由於兩間公司服務不同地區，它們不需要考慮對手的價格反應。港燈減價，不會搶走中電的九龍區客戶。因此不存在策略性的互相影響。

A key feature of oligopoly is "interdependence". Since the two companies serve different districts, they do not need to consider the rival's pricing reaction. If HK Electric cuts prices, it won't steal CLP's Kowloon customers. Thus, there is no strategic interdependence.

Option C (錯誤 / Incorrect)

這是壟斷者的行為（覓價者），但不是定義市場結構的最佳理由。即使是壟斷者，提高價格也可能會流失部分顧客（根據需求定律，需求量下降）。選項 D 才是導致壟斷結構的根本原因。

This describes the behavior of a monopolist (price searcher), but it is not the best reason for defining the market structure. Even a monopolist may lose some customers if they raise prices (Quantity demanded drops due to Law of Demand). Option D is the fundamental cause of the monopoly structure.

Option D (正確 / Correct)

這是正確的定義。由於聯網沒有重疊，對於住在特定地區（例如九龍）的市民來說，只有一間公司提供服務。這被稱為「地理壟斷」。

This is the correct definition. Since the power grids do not overlap, for a citizen living in a specific district (e.g., Kowloon), there is only one company providing service. This is known as a "Geographical Monopoly".

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (關鍵點): "Relevant Market" (相關市場)

解題的關鍵在於從消費者的角度看市場，而不是從地圖上看。

The key to solving this is to look at the market from the consumer's perspective, not just looking at a map.

The Logic Chain:
1. Can I choose between Company A and Company B? \( \rightarrow \) No.
2. How many sellers are available to me at my home? \( \rightarrow \) Only 1.
3. \( N=1 \) implies Monopoly.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

錯誤觀念： 看到香港有「兩間」公司（中電、港燈），或超級市場有「兩大」龍頭（百佳、惠康），就直接認為全部都是寡頭壟斷。

Misconception: Seeing "two" companies in HK (CLP, HK Electric) or "two" supermarket giants (ParknShop, Wellcome) and immediately assuming they are all oligopolies.

區分方法： 超級市場是寡頭壟斷，因為它們在同一條街競爭（互相影響）。電力公司是壟斷，因為它們在不同地區獨市經營（沒有競爭）。

Differentiation: Supermarkets are oligopolies because they compete on the same street (interdependent). Power companies are monopolies because they operate exclusively in different districts (no competition).
