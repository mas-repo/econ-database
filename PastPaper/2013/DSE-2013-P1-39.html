📝 HKDSE Economics Concept Guide

課題 (Topic): 國際貿易與比較優勢 / International Trade and Comparative Advantage

難度 (Difficulty): ★★★★☆ (4/5 Stars)

核心概念 (Key Concepts): 比較優勢 (Comparative Advantage), 機會成本 (Opportunity Cost), 貿易比率 (Terms of Trade), 運輸成本 (Transportation Cost)

1. 題目 (The Question)

參考以下資料。

貿易前，泰國和日本將他們所有的資源平均分配在生產白米和電腦上，其產量如下：

白米 (單位) 電腦 (單位)
泰國 15 5
日本 24 12

假設貿易比率是 1單位電腦 = 2.4單位白米，而出口電腦的國家每單位出口須支付 0.1單位白米的運輸成本。當該國出口 5單位的電腦，該國的淨得益是______單位白米。

A. 1.5
B. 2
C. 2.5
D. 3

Refer to the following information.

Before trade, the output of Thailand and Japan with all their resources evenly allocated between the production of rice and computers is shown below:

Rice (units) Computers (units)
Thailand 15 5
Japan 24 12

Suppose the terms of trade is 1 unit of computers = 2.4 units of rice and the computer-exporting country has to pay a transportation cost of 0.1 units of rice for each unit of export. To export 5 units of computers, the net gain of the country is _______ units of rice.

A. 1.5
B. 2
C. 2.5
D. 3

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

1. 機會成本 (Opportunity Cost): 放棄了的其他選擇中，價值最高的一個。在生產層面，生產多一單位某物品，必須放棄生產另一物品的數量。

The highest-valued option forgone. In production, it's the amount of another good that must be given up to produce one more unit of a good.

2. 比較優勢 (Comparative Advantage): 如果一個國家生產某物品的機會成本低於另一個國家，該國便擁有生產該物品的比較優勢，並會出口該物品。

If a country can produce a good at a lower opportunity cost than another country, it has a comparative advantage in that good and should export it.

3. 貿易得益 (Gains from Trade): 進行國際貿易後，國家所賺取的淨利潤。計算時需考慮貿易比率、機會成本以及交易/運輸成本。

The net profit earned from international trade. It requires considering the terms of trade, opportunity cost, and transaction/transportation costs.

$$\text{Net Gain (per unit)} = \text{Export Revenue (TOT)} - \text{Opportunity Cost} - \text{Transportation Cost}$$

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是 (Correct Option): Option A

步驟一：計算機會成本並找出出口國
雖然題目指「所有資源平均分配」，但計算機會成本只需看產量比例：
泰國生產 1單位電腦的成本 = 15 / 5 = 3單位白米。
日本生產 1單位電腦的成本 = 24 / 12 = 2單位白米。
由於日本生產電腦的機會成本較低 (2 < 3)，因此日本擁有比較優勢，是電腦的出口國。

Step 1: Calculate Opportunity Cost and Identify the Exporter
Although resources are "evenly allocated," the opportunity cost only requires the output ratio:
Thailand's cost for 1 Computer = 15 / 5 = 3 units of Rice.
Japan's cost for 1 Computer = 24 / 12 = 2 units of Rice.
Since Japan has a lower opportunity cost (2 < 3), Japan has the comparative advantage and is the exporter of computers.

步驟二：計算淨得益
每單位電腦的貿易比率 (收入) = 2.4單位白米。
每單位電腦的生產成本 = 2單位白米。
每單位電腦的運輸成本 = 0.1單位白米。
每單位的淨得益 = 2.4 - 2 - 0.1 = 0.3單位白米。
總淨得益 (出口5單位) = 0.3 x 5 = 1.5單位白米。

Step 2: Calculate the Net Gain
Revenue per unit from Terms of Trade = 2.4 units of Rice.
Production cost per unit = 2 units of Rice.
Transport cost per unit = 0.1 units of Rice.
Net gain per unit = 2.4 - 2 - 0.1 = 0.3 units of Rice.
Total net gain (for 5 units) = 0.3 x 5 = 1.5 units of Rice.

錯誤選項分析 (Analysis of Distractors)

選項 B (2): 考生可能忘記扣除 0.1 的運輸成本。如果計算 (2.4 - 2) x 5，得出的結果就是 2。

Option B (2): Students might forget to deduct the 0.1 transport cost. Calculating (2.4 - 2) x 5 yields 2.

選項 C (2.5) & D (3): 這些通常是因計錯機會成本，或者將出口國與進口國混淆（例如誤以為泰國是出口國）而產生的隨機計算錯誤。

Option C (2.5) & D (3): These typically result from calculation errors regarding opportunity cost or confusing the exporter with the importer (e.g., mistakenly believing Thailand is the exporter).

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point): 第一步必須正確判斷「誰出口什麼」。不要急著用數字計算利潤。釐清日本出口電腦後，這條題目就變成了簡單的數學題：(售價 - 成本 - 運費) x 數量。

Pivot Point: The very first step MUST be determining "who exports what". Don't rush into calculating profits. Once you clarify that Japan exports computers, the problem becomes simple math: (Selling Price - Cost - Freight) x Quantity.

干擾資訊 (Distractors in text): 「資源平均分配」這句話容易令同學以為要把數字乘二或除二。實際上，在計算機會成本的「比例」時，不論使用多少資源，該比例都是不變的，直接運用表內數字即可。

Distractors in text: The phrase "evenly allocated" confuses students into multiplying or dividing the numbers. In reality, when calculating the "ratio" for opportunity cost, the proportion remains constant regardless of resource usage. Just use the numbers provided in the table directly.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • 陷阱一：忘記乘總數量。 算出每單位賺 0.3 後，考生可能會在選項尋找 0.3，找不到時便會慌張，忘記了題目要求的是「5單位」的總得益。
  • Trap 1: Forgetting to multiply by total quantity. After finding the per-unit gain of 0.3, students might panic when 0.3 isn't an option, forgetting the question asks for the total gain of "5 units".
  • 陷阱二：不看清楚誰支付運輸費。 題目明確指出「出口電腦的國家支付」，如果改為「進口國支付」，日本的得益將變成 (2.4 - 2) x 5 = 2單位白米。
  • Trap 2: Not checking who pays the transport cost. The question explicitly states the "computer-exporting country" pays. If it were the importing country, Japan's gain would simply be (2.4 - 2) x 5 = 2 units of rice.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 題目說「資源平均分配」，即各自用了 50% 資源。那麼計算總產量時，不需要將表格數字乘 2 嗎？

Q: The question says "evenly allocated," meaning 50% of resources are used for each. Shouldn't we multiply the table numbers by 2 to find maximum output?

A: 不需要。因為機會成本看的是兩種物品產量的「比例」(Trade-off ratio)。不論你用 10%、50% 還是 100% 的資源，假設成本不變 (constant returns)，日本生產 12 部電腦要放棄 24 單位白米這個 1:2 的比例是絕對不變的。

A: No. Opportunity cost is about the "trade-off ratio" between the two goods. Whether you use 10%, 50%, or 100% of resources, assuming constant returns, Japan's ratio of giving up 24 Rice for 12 Computers (1:2) remains strictly identical.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

💡 秒殺技巧： 在試卷上，直接在表格旁邊寫下兩國生產 1 單位電腦的成本（泰國=3，日本=2）。一眼看出日本是出口國。接著把公式寫在旁邊：Gain = (TOT - Cost - Transport) * Q。代入數字 (2.4 - 2 - 0.1) * 5 = 1.5，30秒內即可選出正確答案，無需寫出冗長的計算步驟。

💡 Speed Trick: Directly on your exam paper, jot down the cost of 1 Computer next to the table (TH=3, JP=2). You instantly see Japan is the exporter. Write out the formula skeleton: Gain = (TOT - Cost - Transport) * Q. Plug in the numbers: (2.4 - 2 - 0.1) * 5 = 1.5. You can secure the correct answer in under 30 seconds without writing long, formal steps.
