HKDSE Economics Concept Analysis

Topic: 效率與市場干預 (Efficiency & Market Intervention) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts: 效率產出 (Efficient Output) 無謂損失 (Deadweight Loss) 邊際得益與成本 (MB & MC)

1. The Question (題目)

假設市場均衡產出水平高於效率產出水平。這意味著

Suppose the market equilibrium output level is greater than the efficient output level. It implies that

A. 若減少產出令其更接近效率產出水平，總社會盈餘會上升。
total social surplus will increase if output is reduced towards the efficient output level.
B. 在均衡產出水平，邊際得益大於邊際成本。
at the equilibrium output level, marginal benefit is greater than marginal cost.
C. 消費者盈餘和生產者盈餘之和達到最大，因為市場產出水平滿足均衡條件。
the sum of consumer surplus and producer surplus is maximized as the market output level satisfies the equilibrium condition.
D. 政府有需要作出干預以令產出下降，因為出現過度生產。
government intervention is necessary to reduce output as there is over-production.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

Efficiency & Overproduction (效率與過度生產)

效率產出水平 (Efficient Output Level): 這是指總社會盈餘（Total Social Surplus）達到最大的產出水平。在此水平下，社會資源得到最佳分配，其條件是邊際得益等於邊際成本。

This refers to the output level where Total Social Surplus is maximized. At this level, social resources are optimally allocated, and the condition is Marginal Benefit equals Marginal Cost.

$$ MB = MC $$

過度生產 (Overproduction): 當市場均衡產出 \(Q_e\) 大於效率產出 \(Q_{eff}\) 時，就出現了過度生產。此時，對於超出 \(Q_{eff}\) 的每一單位產量，生產該單位的成本都高於消費者從中獲得的得益。

When the market equilibrium output \(Q_e\) is greater than the efficient output \(Q_{eff}\), overproduction occurs. At this point, for every unit produced beyond \(Q_{eff}\), the cost of producing that unit is higher than the benefit consumers receive from it.

$$ \text{When } Q_e > Q_{eff}, \text{ it implies } MC > MB $$

無謂損失 (Deadweight Loss, DWL): 由於市場未能以效率產出水平運作（不論是生產過多還是過少），導致總社會盈餘的淨流失。

The net loss of total social surplus due to the market failing to operate at the efficient output level (whether due to overproduction or underproduction).

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: Option A

Option A: 正確 (Correct)

因為目前市場處於過度生產（\(Q_e > Q_{eff}\)），這意味著存在無謂損失（Deadweight Loss）。如果我們減少產出，將數量拉回並「更接近」效率產出水平，我們就能減少這些無謂損失，從而令總社會盈餘（Total Social Surplus）上升。

Because the market is currently experiencing overproduction (\(Q_e > Q_{eff}\)), this implies the existence of a Deadweight Loss. If we reduce output and pull the quantity "towards" the efficient output level, we eliminate some of this deadweight loss, thereby causing the total social surplus to increase.

Option B: 錯誤 (Incorrect)

在過度生產的均衡產出水平（即 \(Q_e\) 高於 \(Q_{eff}\) 時），邊際成本（\(MC\)）實際上是大於邊際得益（\(MB\)）的。社會為了生產這些額外單位所付出的代價大於其獲得的價值。

At the equilibrium output level of overproduction (i.e., when \(Q_e\) is higher than \(Q_{eff}\)), Marginal Cost (\(MC\)) is actually greater than Marginal Benefit (\(MB\)). The cost society pays to produce these extra units is greater than the value gained from them.

Option C: 錯誤 (Incorrect)

消費者盈餘和生產者盈餘之和（即總社會盈餘）只有在效率產出水平（\(Q_{eff}\)）時才會達到最大。只要市場均衡產出偏離了效率產出（無論是過度生產還是生產不足），總盈餘就不會是最大化的。

The sum of consumer surplus and producer surplus (i.e., total social surplus) is ONLY maximized at the efficient output level (\(Q_{eff}\)). As long as the market equilibrium output deviates from the efficient output (whether overproduction or underproduction), the total surplus is not maximized.

Option D: 錯誤 (Incorrect)

雖然市場存在過度生產的無效率情況，但在經濟學中，這並不一定意味著政府「必須」或「有需要」干預。政府干預本身可能帶來成本或導致政府失效（Government Failure）。這是一個規範性陳述（Normative statement），而非必然的客觀結論。

Although there is inefficiency due to overproduction in the market, in economics, this does not necessarily mean government intervention is "necessary" or a "must". Government intervention itself incurs costs and may lead to Government Failure. This is a normative statement, not an inevitable positive conclusion.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解答此題的關鍵點 (Pivot Point) 在於分辨「均衡產出」(Equilibrium Output) 與「效率產出」(Efficient Output) 的分別。

  • 找出題目的核心情境： 題目指出 \(Q_e > Q_{eff}\)。你要立刻在腦海中（或在草稿紙上）畫出供需圖，並標示均衡點在效率點的右方。
  • 推導結果： 既然超出了效率點，代表額外生產的單位是「弊多於利」（即 \(MC > MB\)）。
  • 尋找解決方案： 要修正這個錯誤，理所當然是要減少生產，回到 \(MB = MC\) 的完美狀態。減少錯誤生產，總社會盈餘自然會上升。這直接導向了選項 A。

The Pivot Point of solving this question lies in distinguishing between "Equilibrium Output" and "Efficient Output". Identify the core scenario: the question states \(Q_e > Q_{eff}\). Deduce the outcome: beyond the efficient point, extra units cost more than they benefit (\(MC > MB\)). Finding the solution: to fix this, output must be reduced towards \(MB = MC\), which increases total social surplus. This leads directly to Option A.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 陷阱一：將「均衡」等同於「效率」。 很多學生誤以為只要市場達到均衡（Demand = Supply），就一定是效率最大化（選項 C 的陷阱）。如果市場存在外部性（Externalities）或價格管制，均衡點和效率點就會分開。
  • 陷阱二：忽略干預成本。 看到市場失效（Market Failure）就立刻認為「政府必須干預」（選項 D）。在 DSE 考試中，帶有「必須」(must / necessary) 的政策建議選項通常是錯的，因為干預有交易費用。
  • Trap 1: Equating "Equilibrium" with "Efficiency". Students often assume market equilibrium (Demand = Supply) always means maximized efficiency (Option C trap). If there are externalities or price controls, the equilibrium and efficient points will separate.
  • Trap 2: Ignoring intervention costs. Assuming "government must intervene" immediately upon seeing Market Failure (Option D). In DSE, policy recommendations with absolute words like "must" or "necessary" are usually wrong because intervention involves transaction costs.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼會有「均衡產出」大於「效率產出」的情況發生？正常自由市場不是會自動達到效率嗎？

Why would "equilibrium output" be greater than "efficient output"? Doesn't a normal free market automatically achieve efficiency?

A: 正常在完全競爭且沒有外部性的市場中，兩者的確是相等的（即 \(Q_e = Q_{eff}\)）。但如果存在負外部性 (Negative Externality)，例如工廠生產時造成嚴重污染，社會邊際成本 (MSC) 會高於私人邊際成本 (MPC)。此時自由市場算出的均衡點會生產過多，導致 \(Q_e > Q_{eff}\)。

Normally, in a perfectly competitive market with no externalities, they are indeed equal (\(Q_e = Q_{eff}\)). However, if there is a Negative Externality, such as a factory causing severe pollution, the Marginal Social Cost (MSC) will be higher than the Marginal Private Cost (MPC). The free market equilibrium will result in overproduction, leading to \(Q_e > Q_{eff}\).

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

💡 DSE 速解法 (Shortcut):

在 DSE 經濟科 MC 中處理「效率 (Efficiency)」題目時，記住一個黃金法則：「向效率點靠近 = 盈餘增加；遠離效率點 = 無謂損失增加」。

既然題目說目前「產出高於效率產出」，那麼「減少產出」就是向效率點靠近的行為。根據黃金法則，盈餘必然增加。看穿這點，5秒內就能直接選出 A，連其他選項都不用深入分析！

When dealing with "Efficiency" MCQs in DSE Economics, remember this golden rule: "Moving towards the efficient point = Surplus increases; Moving away from the efficient point = Deadweight loss increases". Since the output is higher than the efficient output, "reducing output" moves it towards the efficient point. Therefore, surplus must increase. You can pick Option A in 5 seconds!
