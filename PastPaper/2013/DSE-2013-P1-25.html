(4) 持牌銀行發出而由非銀行的公眾人士持有的可轉讓存款證

Which of the following is included in the money supply M2?

(1) legal tender held by licensed banks
(2) demand deposits with licensed banks
(3) time deposits with restricted licence banks
(4) negotiable certificates of deposit issued by licensed banks held by the non-bank public

B. (2) and (4) only
C. (1), (2) and (4) only

3. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

香港的貨幣供應分為三個層次：M1、M2 和 M3。要分辨它們，關鍵在於存款所在的銀行機構類別（持牌銀行、有限制牌照銀行、接受存款公司）以及資產的流動性。

$$ M1 = C_p + D_d $$

其中 \(C_p\) 是公眾持有的法定貨幣（紙幣及硬幣），\(D_d\) 是持牌銀行的活期存款。

$$ M2 = M1 + S_d + T_d \text{ (in LBs)} + NCDs \text{ (issued by LBs, held by public)} $$

M2 包括 M1，加上持牌銀行 (LBs) 的儲蓄存款 (\(S_d\)) 及定期存款 (\(T_d\))，以及由持牌銀行發行並由非銀行公眾持有的可轉讓存款證 (NCDs)。

$$ M3 = M2 + T_d \text{ (in RLBs & DTCs)} + NCDs \text{ (issued by RLBs & DTCs, held by public)} $$

Hong Kong's money supply is classified into three tiers: M1, M2, and M3. The key to distinguishing them lies in the type of deposit-taking institution (Licensed Banks, Restricted Licence Banks, Deposit-taking Companies) and the liquidity of the assets.

Where \(C_p\) is legal tender held by the public, and \(D_d\) is demand deposits with licensed banks (LBs).

M2 includes M1, plus savings (\(S_d\)) and time deposits (\(T_d\)) with LBs, and negotiable certificates of deposit (NCDs) issued by LBs and held by the non-bank public.

4. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): B

(1) 持牌銀行持有的法定貨幣：錯誤。
貨幣供應只計算「非銀行公眾」持有的貨幣。銀行金庫內的現金屬於銀行儲備，不屬於貨幣供應的一部份，否則會造成雙重計算。

(1) Legal tender held by licensed banks: Incorrect.
Money supply only counts money held by the "non-bank public". Cash in bank vaults is considered bank reserves and is excluded to avoid double counting.

(2) 存於持牌銀行的活期存款：正確。
這是 M1 的一部份，而 M2 是包含 M1 的，因此它屬於 M2。

(2) Demand deposits with licensed banks: Correct.
This is part of M1. Since M2 includes M1, it is automatically part of M2.

(3) 存於有限制牌照銀行的定期存款：錯誤。
有限制牌照銀行 (RLBs) 和接受存款公司 (DTCs) 的存款只會計算在 M3 之內，不屬於 M2。

(3) Time deposits with restricted licence banks: Incorrect.
Deposits in Restricted Licence Banks (RLBs) and Deposit-taking Companies (DTCs) are only included in M3, not M2.

(4) 持牌銀行發出而由非銀行的公眾人士持有的可轉讓存款證：正確。
只要是由「持牌銀行」發行並由公眾持有的 NCDs，就屬於 M2 的定義範圍。

(4) Negotiable certificates of deposit issued by licensed banks held by the non-bank public: Correct.
NCDs issued by "Licensed Banks" and held by the public are strictly part of the M2 definition.

5. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point):
解答此題需要兩個過濾條件：第一，資金持有者必須是「公眾」而非銀行；第二，必須認清存款機構的級別。M1 和 M2 只涉及「持牌銀行 (Licensed Banks)」。一看到「有限制牌照銀行 (RLBs)」或「接受存款公司 (DTCs)」，就必定是 M3。

Pivot Point:
Solving this requires two filters: First, the holder must be the "public", not banks. Second, identify the tier of the institution. M1 and M2 only involve "Licensed Banks". The moment you see "Restricted Licence Banks" or "Deposit-taking Companies", it belongs strictly to M3.

6. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱： 學生常以為所有「法定貨幣 (Legal Tender)」都計入貨幣供應。
糾正： 只有公眾持有的現金 \(C_p\) 才計算在內。銀行持有的現金是儲備，若將其計入，再加上市民存入的存款，就會出現雙重計算 (Double Counting)。

Trap: Students often assume ALL "legal tender" is part of the money supply.
Correction: Only cash held by the public \(C_p\) is counted. Cash held by banks is reserves. Counting it alongside the deposits created from it would result in double counting.

7. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼 (2) 活期存款明明是 M1，卻可以選為 M2 的答案？
A: 因為貨幣供應的定義是層層遞進的 (Cumulative)。公式是 \(M2 = M1 + ...\)。所以任何屬於 M1 的項目，必定同時屬於 M2 和 M3。

Q: Why is (2) demand deposits chosen for M2 when it's clearly M1?
A: Because money supply definitions are cumulative. The formula is \(M2 = M1 + ...\). Therefore, any item that belongs to M1 automatically belongs to M2 and M3 as well.

8. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

秒殺技巧 (Elimination Method):
在考場上，看到選項 (1) 寫著「銀行持有」，立刻打交叉，排除 C 和 D。接著看選項 (3)，看到「有限制牌照銀行」，立刻聯想到 M3，打交叉，排除 A。答案瞬間鎖定為 B，完全不需要逐一死記硬背。

Shortcut:
In the exam, the moment you see "held by banks" in (1), cross it out. This eliminates C and D. Next, look at (3): "restricted licence banks" immediately means M3. Cross it out, eliminating A. The answer is instantly B, saving you time!
