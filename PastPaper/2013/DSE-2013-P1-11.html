• Topic (課題): 市場均衡與供求變化 (Market Equilibrium and Changes in Demand/Supply)
  • Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆ (Moderate / 中等)
  • Key Concepts (核心概念): 生產成本 (Cost of Production), 正常物品 (Normal Goods), 需求與供應曲線的移動 (Shifts in Demand and Supply Curves)

2. 題目 (The Question)

參閱以下有關本港名貴手錶的供需圖。原初的均衡點是 \(E_0\)。

假設本港商鋪的租金大幅上升，而內地的經濟出現衰退。新的均衡點將會是____________。
A. \(E_1\)
B. \(E_2\)
C. \(E_3\)
D. \(E_4\)
Refer to the following demand-supply diagram showing the market of luxury watches in Hong Kong. The initial equilibrium point is \(E_0\).

Suppose the rental of shopping premises in Hong Kong rises sharply and the economy in the mainland experiences a recession. The new equilibrium point will be ________.
A. \(E_1\)
B. \(E_2\)
C. \(E_3\)
D. \(E_4\)

3. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解決這類題目，我們必須分別分析兩個獨立事件對「需求 (Demand)」和「供應 (Supply)」的影響：
To solve this type of question, we must analyze the impact of the two independent events on "Demand" and "Supply" separately:
  • 影響供應的因素 - 生產成本： 商鋪租金是零售商營運的生產成本之一。當租金上升，利潤空間減少，供應者在同一價格下願意及能夠提供的數量減少，導致供應曲線向左移動。
    Factors affecting Supply - Cost of Production: Shop rental is a cost of production for retailers. When rent increases, profit margins shrink, and suppliers are willing and able to provide less at the same price, shifting the supply curve to the left.
  • 影響需求的因素 - 買家收入與經濟狀況： 名貴手錶通常被視為「正常物品 (Normal Goods)」，甚至「優越物品 (Superior Goods)」。內地經濟衰退意味著內地旅客（香港奢侈品市場的主要買家）的收入下跌。這會導致在同一價格下，買家願意及能夠購買的數量減少，需求曲線向左移動。
    Factors affecting Demand - Buyers' Income and Economy: Luxury watches are generally considered "Normal Goods" or even "Superior Goods". A mainland economic recession implies a drop in income for mainland tourists (major buyers in HK's luxury market). This causes buyers to be willing and able to purchase less at the same price, shifting the demand curve to the left.
$$ \text{New Equilibrium} = \text{New Supply Curve} \cap \text{New Demand Curve} $$

4. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): B
Correct Answer: Option B

逐步分析 (Step-by-Step Analysis):

第一步：分析「租金大幅上升」
這會增加名貴手錶零售商的營運成本。供應會減少，供應曲線 (Supply Curve) 由 \(S_0\) 向左/向上移動至 \(S_1\)。
Step 1: Analyze "rental of shopping premises rises sharply"
This increases the operating cost for luxury watch retailers. Supply decreases, and the supply curve shifts to the left/upwards from \(S_0\) to \(S_1\).
第二步：分析「內地經濟出現衰退」
這會降低消費者的收入。由於名貴手錶是正常物品，需求會減少，需求曲線 (Demand Curve) 由 \(D_0\) 向左/向下移動至 \(D_2\)。
Step 2: Analyze "economy in the mainland experiences a recession"
This lowers consumer income. Since luxury watches are normal goods, demand decreases, and the demand curve shifts to the left/downwards from \(D_0\) to \(D_2\).
第三步：尋找新均衡點
新的市場均衡點是新供應曲線 (\(S_1\)) 與新需求曲線 (\(D_2\)) 的相交點。觀察圖表，兩線相交於 \(E_2\)。
Step 3: Find the new equilibrium
The new market equilibrium is the intersection of the new supply curve (\(S_1\)) and the new demand curve (\(D_2\)). Looking at the graph, these two curves intersect at \(E_2\).

選項拆解 (Options Breakdown):

  • A. \(E_1\)： 錯誤。這是 \(S_1\) (供應減少) 與 \(D_1\) (需求增加) 的相交點。與題目情景不符，因為內地經濟衰退應導致需求減少而非增加。
    A. \(E_1\): Incorrect. This is the intersection of \(S_1\) (decrease in supply) and \(D_1\) (increase in demand). This contradicts the scenario as a recession should decrease demand.
  • B. \(E_2\)： 正確。同時反映了供應減少 (\(S_1\)) 和需求減少 (\(D_2\)) 的均衡狀態。
    B. \(E_2\): Correct. It reflects the equilibrium state where both supply has decreased (\(S_1\)) and demand has decreased (\(D_2\)).
  • C. \(E_3\)： 錯誤。這是 \(S_2\) (供應增加) 與 \(D_1\) (需求增加) 的相交點。這與題目描述的租金上升和經濟衰退情況完全相反。
    C. \(E_3\): Incorrect. This is the intersection of \(S_2\) (increase in supply) and \(D_1\) (increase in demand), which is opposite to the scenario provided.
  • D. \(E_4\)： 錯誤。這是 \(S_2\) (供應增加) 與 \(D_2\) (需求減少) 的相交點。雖然需求正確地減少了，但供應不應增加。
    D. \(E_4\): Incorrect. This is the intersection of \(S_2\) (increase in supply) and \(D_2\) (decrease in demand). While demand correctly shifts, supply should not increase.

5. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)：
這類圖表題的核心在於「將文字轉化為曲線移動方向」。每讀到一個事件，立即在試卷旁邊寫下它是影響 D (需求) 還是 S (供應)，以及移動方向。
1. 租金升 = 成本升 = 供應跌 (\(S \leftarrow\))
2. 經濟衰退 = 收入跌 = 需求跌 (\(D \leftarrow\))
結論：找左邊的S線 (\(S_1\)) 和左邊的D線 (\(D_2\)) 的交點。
Pivot Point:
The core of this type of graph question is "translating text into curve shifts." Every time you read an event, immediately jot down whether it affects D (Demand) or S (Supply), and the direction.
1. Rent rises = Cost rises = Supply drops (\(S \leftarrow\))
2. Recession = Income drops = Demand drops (\(D \leftarrow\))
Conclusion: Look for the intersection of the leftward S-curve (\(S_1\)) and the leftward D-curve (\(D_2\)).

6. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱一：混淆影響需求與供應的因素
部分同學會誤以為「商鋪租金上升」會導致手錶價格上升，從而令「需求量」下跌，然後在圖中亂找一點。記住，租金是**生產成本**，直接影響的是**供應 (Supply)**，導致整條供應曲線移動，而不是需求曲線。
Trap 1: Confusing factors affecting Demand and Supply
Some students mistakenly think "rent increases" directly cause watch prices to rise, which in turn drops the "quantity demanded", leading them to pick a random point. Remember, rent is a **cost of production**; it directly affects **Supply**, causing the entire supply curve to shift, not the demand curve.
陷阱二：錯看圖表標籤
考試緊張時，很容易將 \(D_1\) 和 \(D_2\) 混淆。在經濟學圖表中，習慣上向下/向左代表減少，向上/向右代表增加。一定要仔細看清楚哪條線是 \(D_2\) (需求減少)，哪條是 \(S_1\) (供應減少)。
Trap 2: Misreading graph labels
Under exam pressure, it's easy to mix up \(D_1\) and \(D_2\). In economics graphs, a downward/leftward shift typically represents a decrease, while an upward/rightward shift represents an increase. Make sure to carefully trace which line is \(D_2\) (decrease in demand) and which is \(S_1\) (decrease in supply).

7. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

問：為什麼「內地」經濟衰退會影響「香港」名貴手錶的市場？
答： 這是基於現實世界的經濟常識。香港的奢侈品市場（如名貴手錶、珠寶）高度依賴旅遊業，尤其是來自內地的旅客。內地旅客是這些商品的龐大買家群（龐大需求來源）。因此，即使衰退發生在內地，也會直接牽連香港市場的整體需求。
Q: Why does a recession in the "Mainland" affect the market for luxury watches in "Hong Kong"?
A: This is based on real-world economic common sense. Hong Kong's luxury market (like watches and jewelry) relies heavily on tourism, especially tourists from the mainland. Mainland tourists constitute a massive group of buyers (a huge source of demand) for these goods. Therefore, even though the recession is in the mainland, it directly impacts the overall demand in the Hong Kong market.

8. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

四象限消去法 (Quadrant Elimination Method)：
以原初均衡點 \(E_0\) 為中心畫一個十字。
  • 左上方：供應跌、需求升 (價格一定升)
  • 右上方：供應升、需求升 (數量一定升)
  • 左下方：供應升、需求跌 (價格一定跌)
  • 右下方... 等等，我們重新整理最快的方法：
直接看兩者的變化：
事件1令供應左移 (\(S_0 \rightarrow S_1\))
事件2令需求左移 (\(D_0 \rightarrow D_2\))
直接用手指在圖上跟著 \(S_1\) 和 \(D_2\) 兩條線滑動，它們相交的地方就是答案 (\(E_2\))，無需理會價格和數量的最終升跌，這樣能節省最多時間！
Finger Tracing Shortcut:
Don't overcomplicate by analyzing the net effect on Price and Quantity unless the question asks for it. Directly determine the shifts:
Event 1 shifts Supply left (\(S_0 \rightarrow S_1\))
Event 2 shifts Demand left (\(D_0 \rightarrow D_2\))
Simply use your fingers to trace along the \(S_1\) line and the \(D_2\) line on the diagram. The exact spot where they cross is your answer (\(E_2\)). This saves maximum time!
