📊 HKDSE Economics: Question Analysis

Topic (課題): 需求定律與艾智仁一般性概念 (Law of Demand & Alchian's Generalization)

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)

Key Concepts (關鍵概念): 需求定律 (Law of Demand), 相對價格 (Relative Price), 艾智仁一般性概念 (Alchian's Generalization)

📝 The Question (題目)

下列哪項與需求定律並不相符？

Which of the following is INCONSISTENT with the law of demand?

  • A. 當實施更高的從量進口關稅，進口成衣的質素下降。
    When a higher per-unit import tariff is imposed, the quality of garment imports worsens.
  • B. 當政府削減失業救濟金，失業人數下跌。
    When a government reduces the unemployment benefit, the number of unemployed people drops.
  • C. 當某國政局不穩引致它的主要城市發生暴亂，到該國遊覽的旅客人數下跌。
    When political unrest in a country causes riots in its major cities, the number of tourists visiting the country drops.
  • D. 當人們預期某上市公司股票的價格上升，該股票的價格上升。
    When people expect the price of the shares of a listed company to rise, the price of those shares rises.

💡 Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 需求定律 (Law of Demand): 假設其他因素不變，當物品的價格上升，其需求量會下降。

Assuming other factors remain constant (ceteris paribus), when the price of a good increases, its quantity demanded decreases.

2. 艾智仁一般性概念 (Alchian's Generalization): 這是需求定律的延伸。當優質物品和劣質物品同時加上一個固定的從量費用（如運費、稅項）時，優質物品的相對價格 (Relative Price) 會下降，導致消費者傾向購買更多優質物品（即「好蘋果定律」）。

A corollary of the Law of Demand. When a fixed per-unit charge (e.g., transport cost, tariff) is added to both high-quality and low-quality goods, the relative price of the high-quality good falls. Consumers will therefore buy a higher proportion of high-quality goods ("shipping the good apples out").

假設優質物品價格為 \(P_H\)，劣質物品價格為 \(P_L\) (\(P_H > P_L\))。加上固定費用 \(T\) 後：

$$ \frac{P_H + T}{P_L + T} < \frac{P_H}{P_L} $$

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): A

選項 A 分析 (正確答案，因為它與定律不符): 根據艾智仁一般性概念，增加固定的從量關稅會令高質素成衣的相對價格下降。因此，進口成衣的平均質素應該上升，而非下降。此選項的描述與需求定律矛盾。

Analysis for Option A (Correct answer because it is INCONSISTENT): According to Alchian's Generalization, adding a fixed per-unit tariff lowers the relative price of high-quality garments. Therefore, the average quality of imported garments should improve, not worsen. This statement contradicts the law of demand.

選項 B 分析: 失業救濟金可視為「選擇失業的收入/代價」。削減救濟金代表失業的代價（機會成本）增加。代價上升，需求量（失業人數）下降，這完全符合需求定律。

Analysis for Option B: Unemployment benefits can be seen as the "income/opportunity cost of choosing to be unemployed". Reducing the benefit increases the cost of being unemployed. Higher cost leads to a lower quantity demanded (fewer unemployed people), which is consistent with the law of demand.

選項 C 分析: 政局不穩增加了旅客前往該國的非金錢成本（如安全風險）。旅遊的總成本上升，導致旅遊的需求量下降，符合需求定律。

Analysis for Option C: Political unrest increases the non-pecuniary cost (e.g., safety risks) of visiting the country. As the total cost of traveling rises, the quantity demanded for travel falls, consistent with the law of demand.

選項 D 分析: 預期未來價格上升，會導致現在的需求增加（需求曲線向右移）。需求增加導致現時的均衡價格上升。這是基於供求模型的結果，而該模型正是建立在需求定律之上的，因此兩者相符。

Analysis for Option D: Expecting future prices to rise increases current demand (shifting the demand curve to the right). Higher demand leads to a higher equilibrium price. This is a standard supply and demand outcome, which is built upon the law of demand, making it consistent.

🔍 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point): 看到「從量」(per-unit) 以及「質素」(quality) 兩個關鍵字，必須立刻聯想到「艾智仁一般性概念」。

Pivot Point: Whenever you see the keywords "per-unit" and "quality", you must immediately think of "Alchian's Generalization".

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧: 在 DSE 經濟科中，只要題目提到「從量稅/運費」加上「質素」，結果永遠是「質素上升 / 買更多靚貨」。如果選項說「質素下降」，那它100%是錯的（即不符合需求定律）。

Shortcut: In HKDSE Economics, whenever a question pairs "per-unit tax/transport cost" with "quality", the outcome is ALWAYS "quality improves / buy more good stuff". If an option says "quality worsens", it is 100% wrong (i.e., inconsistent with the law of demand).
