Economics Question Analysis (經濟學題目分析)

Topic (課題): 供需分析與政府干預 (D&S and Market Intervention) Difficulty (難度): ★★★★☆ (4/5) Key Concepts (關鍵字): Subsidy, Price Floor, Total Revenue

2. The Question (題目)

參閱以下某物品的供需表。

Refer to the following demand and supply schedules of a good.

Price / 價格 ($) 1 2 3 4 5
Quantity demanded / 需求量 (unit/單位) 120 100 80 75 60
Quantity supplied / 供應量 (unit/單位) 60 75 80 100 120

根據以上資料，我們可得出結論如下：

  • A. 當價格是$2，總銷售收益會是$200。
  • B. 當政府把價格下限定為$4，市場會出現25單位的短缺。
  • C. 當政府給予賣家每單位$2的補貼，總收益(包括補貼)會增加$160。
  • D. 當政府給予賣家每單位$2的補貼，均衡價格會下降至$1。

According to the above information, we can conclude that

  • A. the total sales revenue would be $200 when the price is $2.
  • B. there would be a shortage of 25 units when the government imposes a price floor at $4.
  • C. when a per unit subsidy of $2 was granted to the seller, the total revenue (including the subsidy) would increase by $160.
  • D. when a per unit subsidy of $2 was granted to the seller, the equilibrium price would drop to $1.

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 實際交易量 (Transacted Quantity):
在任何非均衡價格下，市場的實際交易量由需求量和供應量之中較小的一方決定（因為買賣雙方不能強迫對方交易）。
At any non-equilibrium price, the actual transacted quantity in the market is determined by the smaller of the quantity demanded or supplied (because neither side can be forced to trade).
$$ Q_t = \min(Q_d, Q_s) $$
2. 總收益與補貼 (Total Revenue and Subsidy):
總銷售收益是價格乘以交易量。如果政府提供從量補貼，賣家的總收入應包含市場價格的收入加上政府的補貼。
Total sales revenue is price multiplied by transacted quantity. If the government provides a per-unit subsidy, the sellers' total receipt must include both the market price revenue and the government subsidy.
$$ \text{Total Revenue (Without Subsidy)} = P_{\text{market}} \times Q_t $$ $$ \text{Total Revenue (Including Subsidy)} = (P_{\text{market}} + \text{Subsidy}) \times Q_t $$
3. 從量補貼對供應的影響 (Effect of Per-Unit Subsidy on Supply):
補貼會令供應曲線向下垂直移動（幅度等於補貼金額）。這代表賣家願意以較低的市場價格提供相同的數量。
A subsidy shifts the supply curve vertically downwards by the subsidy amount. This means sellers are willing to supply the same quantity at a correspondingly lower market price.

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option) : C

Option A: 錯誤 (Incorrect)

當價格為 $2 時，需求量 \(Q_d = 100\)，但供應量 \(Q_s = 75\)。實際交易量取決於較小的一方，即 75 單位。因此，總銷售收益 \(= \$2 \times 75 = \$150\)，而不是 $200。

When the price is $2, \(Q_d = 100\) but \(Q_s = 75\). The transacted quantity is determined by the smaller amount, which is 75 units. Therefore, the total sales revenue \(= \$2 \times 75 = \$150\), not $200.

Option B: 錯誤 (Incorrect)

當政府實施 $4 的價格下限（高於原來的均衡價格 $3），市場價格會上升至 $4。此時，供應量 (100) 大於需求量 (75)。這會造成 \(100 - 75 = 25\) 單位的**過剩 (Surplus)**，而不是短缺 (Shortage)。

When the government imposes a price floor at $4 (which is above the original equilibrium price of $3), the market price becomes $4. At this price, the quantity supplied (100) is greater than the quantity demanded (75). This creates a **surplus** of \(100 - 75 = 25\) units, not a shortage.

Option D: 錯誤 (Incorrect)

我們首先找出新的均衡。政府提供 $2 補貼後，賣家在每一個數量下願意接受的市場價格都會減少 $2。觀察原本的供應表：在原本價格 $4 時，供應量是 100 單位；獲得 $2 補貼後，賣家在市場價格為 $2 時就願意供應 100 單位。剛剛好，在價格為 $2 時，需求量也是 100。因此，新的均衡價格下降至 **$2**，而不是 $1。

Let's find the new equilibrium first. With a $2 subsidy, the market price sellers are willing to accept drops by $2 for any given quantity. Looking at the original schedule: at $4, \(Q_s = 100\). With the $2 subsidy, sellers will now supply 100 units at a market price of $2. Coincidentally, at $2, the quantity demanded is exactly 100. Thus, the new equilibrium price drops to **$2**, not $1.

Option C: 正確 (Correct)

在實施補貼前（原均衡）：價格 \(= \$3\)，數量 \(= 80\)。原總收益 \(= \$3 \times 80 = \$240\)。
在實施補貼後（新均衡）：市場價格 \(= \$2\)，數量 \(= 100\)。總收益（包括補貼） \(= (\text{市場價格} + \text{補貼}) \times \text{數量} = (\$2 + \$2) \times 100 = \$400\)。
總收益的增加 \(= \$400 - \$240 = \$160\)。

Before subsidy (original equilibrium): Price \(= \$3\), Quantity \(= 80\). Original Total Revenue \(= \$3 \times 80 = \$240\).
After subsidy (new equilibrium): Market Price \(= \$2\), Quantity \(= 100\). Total Revenue (including subsidy) \(= (\text{Market Price} + \text{Subsidy}) \times \text{Quantity} = (\$2 + \$2) \times 100 = \$400\).
Increase in total revenue \(= \$400 - \$240 = \$160\).

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (解題關鍵): 處理表格型題目的補貼問題時，你不需要畫出圖表。關鍵在於**「平移」供應表**。補貼為 $2，意味著要找到新的供應量，你可以查看原來高於目標價格 $2 的那個供應量。例如，新價格為 $2 時的供應量，等於舊價格為 $4 (\(2+2\)) 時的供應量 (即 100 單位)。

Pivot Point: When dealing with subsidy problems in a tabular format, you don't need to draw a graph. The key is to **"shift" the supply schedule**. A subsidy of $2 means to find the new quantity supplied at any price, look at the old quantity supplied at a price $2 higher. For example, the new \(Q_s\) at $2 equals the old \(Q_s\) at $4 (\(2+2\)), which is 100 units.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 陷阱一：計算交易量時沒有取最小值。 (Trap 1: Not taking the minimum for transacted quantity.) 很多學生在 Option A 直接將 \(P \times Q_d\)，忘記了沒有供應就無法交易。
  • 陷阱二：混淆 Shortage (短缺) 和 Surplus (過剩)。 (Trap 2: Confusing Shortage and Surplus.) 價格下限 (Price floor) 高於均衡，會導致供過於求，這是 Surplus。
  • 陷阱三：計算連補貼的總收益時忘記加上補貼。 (Trap 3: Forgetting to add the subsidy back to total revenue.) 賣家的總收入是從消費者那裡收取的市場價格加上政府發放的補貼。

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 老師，我在考試時如何快速知道選項 D 的新均衡價格在哪裡？

A: 你可以逐個價格測試！因為補貼是 $2，你只要將原本的 $Q_s$ 數值全部向左（價格較低的方向）移動兩格。例如，原本賣 100 個要收 $4；現在有 $2 補貼，賣 100 個只要收 $2。剛好在 $2 時，買家也想要 100 個，需求和新的供應吻合，這就是新均衡了。

Q: Teacher, how can I quickly find the new equilibrium price for Option D during the exam?

A: You can test it price by price! Since the subsidy is $2, simply shift all original \(Q_s\) values two steps to the left (towards lower prices). For instance, sellers originally needed $4 to supply 100 units; with a $2 subsidy, they only need $2 from the market. Exactly at $2, buyers also demand 100 units. Demand matches the new supply, so that's your new equilibrium.

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

淘汰法 (Process of Elimination):

  1. Option A 可以在 5 秒內排除：看到非均衡狀態，立即尋找 \(Q_d\) 和 \(Q_s\) 較小的一個 (\(75\))，心算 \(2 \times 75 = 150 \neq 200\)。
  2. Option B 也可以在 5 秒內排除：Price floor 高於均衡必產生 Surplus，而選項寫的是 Shortage，直接畫交叉。
  3. 剩下 C 和 D，兩者都要求你找出「新均衡」。一旦你運用了供應表平移法找出新均衡價格是 $2，D 選項不攻自破。C 選項連計算都不用做就必為正確答案！

Process of Elimination:

  1. Option A can be eliminated in 5 seconds: In a non-equilibrium state, immediately look for the smaller of \(Q_d\) and \(Q_s\) (\(75\)), mental math \(2 \times 75 = 150 \neq 200\).
  2. Option B can also be eliminated in 5 seconds: A price floor above equilibrium MUST create a Surplus, but the option says Shortage. Cross it out immediately.
  3. This leaves C and D, both requiring you to find the "new equilibrium". Once you shift the supply schedule to find the new equilibrium price is $2, Option D is instantly proven wrong. Option C MUST be the correct answer even without calculating the revenue!
