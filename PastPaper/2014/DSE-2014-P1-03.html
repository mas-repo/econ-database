Topic: Basic Economic Concepts (基本經濟概念) Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆
Key Concepts: Economic Good (經濟物品), Free Good (免費物品), Scarcity (稀少性)

1. The Question (題目)

下列哪項有關經濟物品的陳述是正確的？

An economic good

A. 經濟物品的需求比供應大。
A. is a good with its demand greater than its supply.
B. 與免費物品相比，經濟物品帶給我們較大的滿足感。
B. brings us more satisfaction than a free good.
C. 當價格是零，現有經濟物品的數量未能滿足人類的需要。
C. is a good whose quantity available cannot satisfy human wants at zero price.
D. 在任何經濟中找不到免費的經濟物品。
D. cannot be found free of charge in any economy.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

經濟物品 (Economic Good)：是指數量不足以滿足人類所有慾望的物品。因為它具有稀少性 (Scarcity)，要獲得它就必須放棄其他東西（即機會成本大於零）。

Economic Good: A good whose quantity is insufficient to satisfy all human wants. Because it is scarce, obtaining it requires giving up something else (i.e., Opportunity Cost > 0).

免費物品 (Free Good)：是指數量足以滿足人類所有慾望的物品。要獲得它不需要放棄其他東西（即機會成本等於零）。

Free Good: A good whose quantity is sufficient to satisfy all human wants. Obtaining it does not require giving up anything else (i.e., Opportunity Cost = 0).

Scarcity Condition (稀少性條件): $$ \text{At } P = 0, \quad Q_d > Q_s $$

這意味著當價格 \(P\) 為零時，人們想要的需求量 \(Q_d\) 大於現有的供應量 \(Q_s\)。

This means that when price \(P\) is zero, the quantity demanded \(Q_d\) is greater than the quantity supplied \(Q_s\).

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: C

分析選項 A (錯誤): 需求 (Demand) 和供應 (Supply) 的大小不能直接比較。我們只能比較「需求量」和「供應量」。在市場均衡時，需求量等於供應量，但它仍然是經濟物品。

Analysis of Option A (Incorrect): Demand and supply cannot be directly compared as entire curves. We can only compare "quantity demanded" and "quantity supplied". At market equilibrium, quantity demanded equals quantity supplied, yet it remains an economic good.

分析選項 B (錯誤): 物品帶來的滿足感（效用）是主觀的。例如，空氣是免費物品，但它對我們生存至關重要，帶來的滿足感可能比鑽石（經濟物品）更大。

Analysis of Option B (Incorrect): The satisfaction (utility) brought by a good is subjective. For example, air is a free good, but it is essential for survival and may bring more satisfaction than diamonds (an economic good).

分析選項 C (正確): 這是稀少性 (Scarcity) 的標準定義。當價格為零時 (\(P=0\))，人們想要的數量大於現有數量，這正是經濟物品的特徵。

Analysis of Option C (Correct): This is the standard definition of scarcity. When the price is zero (\(P=0\)), the quantity people want is greater than the available quantity, which is the exact characteristic of an economic good.

分析選項 D (錯誤): 經濟物品可以免費提供（例如政府提供的免費教育，或朋友送的禮物）。「免費提供 (free of charge)」是指市場價格為零，但生產它仍涉及社會的機會成本，因此它仍是經濟物品。

Analysis of Option D (Incorrect): Economic goods can be provided free of charge (e.g., free education provided by the government, or a gift from a friend). "Free of charge" means the market price is zero, but producing it still involves an opportunity cost to society, so it remains an economic good.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題的「樞紐點」(Pivot Point) 在於區分「價格 (Price)」與「成本 (Cost)」。題目測試你是否知道經濟物品的本質是「稀少性」。

The "Pivot Point" of solving this question lies in distinguishing between "Price" and "Cost". The question tests whether you know the essence of an economic good is "scarcity".

  • 看到「經濟物品」，立刻聯想：機會成本 \(> 0\)，以及在 \(P=0\) 時，\(Q_d > Q_s\)。
    When you see "Economic Good", immediately associate it with: Opportunity Cost \(> 0\), and at \(P=0\), \(Q_d > Q_s\).

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：混淆「免費物品 (Free Good)」與「免費提供的物品 (Free of charge good)」

Trap: Confusing "Free Good" with "Good provided free of charge"

很多學生會誤選 D。記住：免費派發的口罩、政府提供的免費醫療，雖然你不需要付錢（價格 \(P=0\)），但社會仍需要消耗資源（如人力、物料）來生產它們，機會成本不為零。因此，它們是免費提供的經濟物品。

Many students mistakenly choose D. Remember: Free masks or free public healthcare might not cost you money (Price \(P=0\)), but society still consumes resources (labor, materials) to produce them, meaning the opportunity cost is not zero. Therefore, they are economic goods provided free of charge.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼選項 A 的說法不準確？如果需求不大於供應，物品怎麼會稀少？

Q: Why is Option A inaccurate? If demand isn't greater than supply, how can the good be scarce?

A: 在經濟學中，「需求 (Demand)」是一條曲線，代表不同價格下的需求量；「供應 (Supply)」也是一條曲線。我們不能說一條曲線大於另一條曲線。我們必須指定一個價格（即價格為零時），比較「需求量 (Quantity Demanded)」和「供應量 (Quantity Supplied)」。

A: In economics, "Demand" is a curve representing quantities demanded at various prices; "Supply" is also a curve. We cannot say one curve is greater than another. We must specify a price (i.e., at zero price) and compare the "Quantity Demanded" and "Quantity Supplied".

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧：尋找「價格為零 (at zero price)」

Shortcut: Look for "at zero price"

在 DSE 選擇題中，當考問「經濟物品」或「稀少性」的定義時，正確答案通常會包含「當價格為零 (at zero price)」這個條件。只要看到這個關鍵字，並確認後半句是描述「數量不足 (quantity insufficient / cannot satisfy wants)」，通常就是正確答案。

In DSE MCQs, when asked about the definition of "economic goods" or "scarcity", the correct answer almost always includes the condition "at zero price". Once you spot this keyword and confirm the latter half describes "quantity insufficient / cannot satisfy wants", it is usually the correct answer.
