📝 The Question (題目)

下表顯示某廠商的生產計劃。該廠商只採用兩種生產要素：勞工和機器。
當第_______個單位的勞工加入工作行列，勞工的邊際產量開始下降。
The following table shows a production plan of a firm which employs two factors only, namely labour and machinery.
The marginal product of labour will start to diminish when the _______ unit of labour is put to work.
Labour / 勞工 (unit) Machinery / 機器 (unit) Total product / 總產量 (unit)
$1$$4$$100$
$2$$4$$120$
$3$$4$$150$
$4$$4$$170$
$5$$4$$165$
A. 二    B. 三    C. 四    D. 五
A. second    B. third    C. fourth    D. fifth

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 短期生產 (Short Run Production)：
A period where at least one factor of production is fixed in quantity. In this question, Machinery is the fixed factor ($4$ units).
2. 邊際產量 (Marginal Product, $MP$)：
The extra output produced by employing one additional unit of a variable factor.
$$MP_n=TP_n-TP_{n-1}$$
3. 邊際回報遞減定律 (Law of Diminishing Marginal Returns)：
In the short run, as successive units of a variable factor are added to a given quantity of fixed factors, the marginal product of the variable factor will eventually and continuously diminish.

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是：C. 四 (fourth)
The correct option is: Option C

為了解決這個問題，我們必須先計算出每一單位的邊際產量 ($MP$)：

To solve this problem, we must first calculate the Marginal Product ($MP$) for each unit:

Labour ($L$) Total Product ($TP$) Marginal Product ($MP$) Calculation
$1$$100$$100-0=100$
$2$$120$$120-100=20$
$3$$150$$150-120=30$
$4$$170$$170-150=20$
$5$$165$$165-170=-5$
❌ Option A (second / 二)
雖然加入第二名勞工時，邊際產量由 $100$ 下降至 $20$，但在第三名勞工加入時，邊際產量回升至 $30$。定律強調的是「最終且持續」的下降，這只是短暫的波動，不是定律的起點。
Although $MP$ drops from $100$ to $20$ at the second unit, it bounces back to $30$ at the third. The law dictates an "eventual and continuous" decline, so this temporary fluctuation does not mark the onset.
❌ Option B (third / 三)
當加入第三名勞工時，邊際產量由 $20$ 增加至 $30$。邊際產量正在上升，這顯然不符合「遞減」的定義。
At the third unit, $MP$ increases from $20$ to $30$. Since $MP$ is rising, this clearly contradicts the definition of diminishing returns.
✅ Option C (fourth / 四)
當加入第四名勞工時，邊際產量由高峰的 $30$ 下降至 $20$，並在其後持續下跌（第五單位時為 $-5$）。這標誌著邊際回報開始出現真正的、持續的遞減，因此是正確答案。
At the fourth unit, $MP$ falls from its peak of $30$ down to $20$, and continues to decline thereafter (to $-5$). This marks the true, continuous onset of diminishing marginal returns.
❌ Option D (fifth / 五)
雖然加入第五名勞工時邊際產量為負數（$-5$），且總產量開始下降，但邊際產量的「持續下降」早於第四名勞工時已經發生。因此，第五單位不是「開始」的轉捩點。
Although $MP$ becomes negative ($-5$) and Total Product starts to fall at the fifth unit, the "continuous decline" of $MP$ had already begun at the fourth unit. Therefore, the fifth is not the starting point.

🔍 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

樞紐點 (Pivot Point)：
Pivot Point:
此題的關鍵在於分辨「短暫下降」與「持續下降」。同學必須完整計算出整個 $MP$ 數列，才能找出真正的轉捩點。
The key is distinguishing between a "temporary drop" and a "continuous decline." You must calculate the entire $MP$ column to find the true turning point.

干擾資訊 (Distractors)：
Distractors:
表中的「機器 ($4$ 單位)」純粹是為了滿足「短期」生產必有固定生產要素這個定義。在實際計算邊際產量時，這個數字是完全不需要用到的。
The "Machinery ($4$ units)" column exists solely to satisfy the definition of a "short-run" period, which requires at least one fixed factor. You do not need to use the number $4$ in any calculations.

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱 1：見跌即選 (Premature Conclusion)
Trap 1: Choosing the very first drop. Students often see $MP$ fall from $100$ to $20$ at unit $2$ and immediately select Option A, failing to notice it rises again at unit $3$.

陷阱 2：混淆總產量與邊際產量 (Confusing $TP$ with $MP$)
Trap 2: Looking at Total Product instead of Marginal Product. Some students select Option D because Total Product drops at unit $5$ ($170 \rightarrow 165$). Remember, the law is about Diminishing Marginal Returns, not Diminishing Total Returns!

🙋‍♂️ Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 邊際產量 ($MP$) 可以是負數嗎？
Q: Can Marginal Product be a negative number?
A: 絕對可以。在第 $5$ 單位勞工加入時，$MP$ 為 $-5$。這代表額外工人的加入反而阻礙了生產（例如廠房過度擁擠，工人互相妨礙），導致總產量不升反跌。
A: Absolutely. At the $5$th unit, $MP$ is $-5$. This means adding another worker actually hinders production (e.g., overcrowding, getting in each other's way), causing the Total Product to fall.

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

「最後高峰」速算法：
考試時，在試卷的空白處快速寫下整個 $MP$ 數列 ($100, 20, 30, 20, -5$)。找出 $MP$ 在持續下跌前的「最後一個高峰」（此題中為 $30$，在第 $3$ 單位）。定律必然在該高峰出現後的下一個單位開始生效！因此，$3+1=4$，答案秒選 C！
The "Final Peak" Shortcut:
During the exam, quickly scribble the entire $MP$ sequence ($100, 20, 30, 20, -5$) next to the table. Locate the "final peak" before the continuous drop begins (which is $30$, at the $3$rd unit). The law always sets in at the unit immediately following this peak! Thus, $3+1=4$. Choose C instantly!
