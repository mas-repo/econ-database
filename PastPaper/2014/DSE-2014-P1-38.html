Economics MC Analysis 經濟學多項選擇題分析

Topic / 課題 總供給與經濟增長 | Aggregate Supply & Economic Growth
Difficulty / 難度 ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts / 關鍵概念 長期總供給 (LRAS), 勞動人口 (Labor Force), 生產要素 (Factors of Production)

The Question (題目)

長期而言，下列哪些政府政策會增加某國的總產出？

Which of the following government policies will lead to an increase in the aggregate output of a country in the long run?

  • (1) 收緊環境保護的法例 (1) to tighten regulations on environmental protection
  • (2) 特赦非法入境者 (2) to grant an amnesty to illegal immigrants
  • (3) 禁止輸入外籍家庭傭工 (3) to ban the importation of foreign domestic helpers
  • (4) 興建更多國際學校以吸引外國人到該國工作 (4) to build more international schools for attracting expatriates to work in the country

A. 只有(1)、(3) (1) and (3) only

B. 只有(1)、(4) (1) and (4) only

C. 只有(2)、(3) (2) and (3) only

D. 只有(2)、(4) (2) and (4) only

Concept Review (概念重溫)

長期總供給 (Long-Run Aggregate Supply, LRAS)

在長期而言，一個經濟體的總產出取決於其長期總供給 (LRAS)。LRAS 代表在充分就業下的產出水平，它不受物價水平影響，而是取決於經濟體系內生產要素的數量與質素，以及技術水平。

In the long run, an economy's aggregate output is determined by its Long-Run Aggregate Supply (LRAS). LRAS represents the full-employment output level. It is independent of the price level and depends entirely on the quantity and quality of factors of production, as well as the level of technology.

$$Y_f = A \cdot F(L, K, N, E)$$

當中 $Y_f$ 代表充分就業產出 (Full-employment output)，$A$ 代表科技水平 (Technology)，而 $L, K, N, E$ 分別代表勞力 (Labor)、資本 (Capital)、土地 (Land) 及 企業家職能 (Entrepreneurship)。要令 $Y_f$ 增加，政府必須增加這些要素的數量或質素。

Where $Y_f$ represents full-employment output, $A$ is technology, and $L, K, N, E$ represent Labor, Capital, Land, and Entrepreneurship respectively. To increase $Y_f$, the government must increase the quantity or quality of these factors.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): D

✘ 項目 (1) 分析 / Statement (1) Analysis

收緊環境保護的法例： 錯誤。更嚴格的環保法例會增加企業的生產成本，並可能限制某些資源的開採或使用。這會降低生產力或減少可用的生產要素，導致長期總供給曲線 ($LRAS$) 向左移，長期總產出下降。

Tighten environmental regulations: Incorrect. Stricter environmental laws increase the cost of production and may restrict the extraction or use of certain resources. This lowers productivity or reduces available factors of production, shifting the $LRAS$ curve to the left, which decreases aggregate output in the long run.

✔ 項目 (2) 分析 / Statement (2) Analysis

特赦非法入境者： 正確。將非法入境者合法化意味著他們可以正式進入合法勞動市場。這增加了官方記錄的勞動人口 ($L$) 數量，提升了國家的生產能力，從而令 $LRAS$ 向右移。

Grant amnesty to illegal immigrants: Correct. Legalizing illegal immigrants allows them to officially enter the formal labor market. This increases the officially recorded quantity of labor ($L$), enhancing the country's productive capacity and shifting $LRAS$ to the right.

✘ 項目 (3) 分析 / Statement (3) Analysis

禁止輸入外籍家庭傭工： 錯誤。這政策不僅直接減少了勞動人口（外籍傭工本身），更會迫使部分本地勞工（如父母）退出勞動市場以照顧家庭。勞動力總量下降，導致 $LRAS$ 向左移。

Ban importation of foreign domestic helpers: Incorrect. This policy not only directly reduces the labor force (the helpers themselves) but also forces some local workers (e.g., parents) to leave the labor market to take care of their families. The total labor force decreases, shifting $LRAS$ to the left.

✔ 項目 (4) 分析 / Statement (4) Analysis

興建更多國際學校以吸引外國人： 正確。吸引外國專業人士來國工作，能同時增加勞動力的數量與質素（因為外籍專業人士通常具備較高技能）。這大大提升了生產力，$LRAS$ 向右移。

Build more international schools to attract expatriates: Correct. Attracting foreign professionals increases both the quantity and quality of labor (since expatriates are often highly skilled). This boosts productivity and shifts $LRAS$ to the right.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (破題關鍵)

題目的關鍵字是「長期」(in the long run)。這意味著你不能考慮總需求 ($AD$) 的短期波動，而必須專注於長期總供給 ($LRAS$)。判斷標準非常簡單：該政策是否增加了生產要素（尤其是勞動力或資本）的數量或質素？

The key phrase in the question is "in the long run". This means you must ignore short-run fluctuations in Aggregate Demand ($AD$) and focus strictly on Long-Run Aggregate Supply ($LRAS$). The evaluation criterion is simple: Does the policy increase the quantity or quality of factors of production (especially labor or capital)?

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱一：混淆短期與長期效應 (Confusing Short-run and Long-run Effects)
很多學生看到「興建學校」(項目4) 就會聯想到政府支出 ($G$) 增加，導致總需求 ($AD$) 上升。雖然這是對的，但這是短期效應。題目問的是「長期」，因此必須看它對生產力及勞動人口的影響（吸引人才）。

Trap 1: Many students see "building schools" (Statement 4) and immediately think of an increase in government expenditure ($G$), leading to higher Aggregate Demand ($AD$). While true, this is a short-run effect. The question asks for the "long run", so you must analyze its impact on productivity and the labor force (attracting talent).

陷阱二：道德判斷干擾經濟邏輯 (Moral Judgments Interfering with Economic Logic)
部分學生認為「特赦非法入境者」(項目2) 是「不良」政策，從而直覺認為它不會增加產出。在經濟學中，我們只看客觀數據：非法勞工合法化能增加官方市場的勞動力供給，從而推高實質 GDP。

Trap 2: Some students feel that "granting amnesty to illegal immigrants" (Statement 2) is a "bad" policy and intuitively assume it can't increase output. In economics, we look strictly at objective data: legalizing illegal workers increases the labor supply in the formal market, thus boosting real GDP.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

🙋‍♂️ 學生問：「收緊環保法例不是會創造綠色產業和相關就業機會嗎？為什麼總產出會下降？」

Student: "Wouldn't tightening environmental regulations create green industries and jobs? Why would aggregate output decrease?"

👨‍🏫 老師答：在宏觀經濟學的標準模型中，法規限制通常被視為對生產的約束（例如工廠需要減產以符合排放標準，或花費更多成本購買過濾器而非新機器）。雖然它可能促進了某一特定行業（環保產業），但對整體經濟 ($Y_f$) 而言，它增加了生產成本並降低了資源分配的整體效率，因此長遠來說會壓抑總產出。

Teacher: In standard macroeconomic models, regulations are generally viewed as constraints on production (e.g., factories must reduce output to meet emission standards, or spend capital on filters instead of productive machinery). While it might boost one specific sector (green tech), for the overall economy ($Y_f$), it increases production costs and reduces allocative efficiency, thus depressing total aggregate output in the long run.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

Elimination Method (消去法)

在多項選擇題中，善用消去法能大大節省時間。
1. 看到項目 (1) 是「收緊法規」，這通常意味著增加成本、降低產能。因此 (1) 必定會減少長期產出。
2. 既然 (1) 是錯的，我們可以立刻刪去選項 A 和 B。
3. 現在只剩下 C 和 D。兩者都包含 (2)，所以我們甚至不需要分析 (2)！
4. 直接對比 (3) 和 (4)。項目 (3) 是「禁止」，會流失勞動力；項目 (4) 是「吸引外國人」，會增加勞動力。顯然 (4) 才是增加產出的方法。答案秒選 D。

Using the elimination method saves a lot of time in MCQs.
1. You see Statement (1) is "tighten regulations", which typically means increased costs and reduced capacity. Thus, (1) definitely decreases long-run output.
2. Since (1) is wrong, we can instantly eliminate options A and B.
3. We are left with C and D. Both contain (2), so we don't even need to analyze (2)!
4. Just compare (3) and (4). Statement (3) is a "ban", which loses labor. Statement (4) "attracts expatriates", which adds labor. Clearly, (4) is the way to increase output. The answer is instantly D.
