📚 Topic (課題): Types of Demand 需求種類 (Microeconomics 微觀經濟學)

⭐ Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆ (Basic 基礎)

🔑 Key Concepts (核心概念): Derived Demand 引申需求 Joint Demand 連帶需求 Competitive Demand 競爭性需求

1. The Question (題目)

下列哪一對物品中，對首項物品的需求會導致對次項物品的引申需求？
In which of the following pairs does the demand for the first good create a derived demand for the second one?
  • A. 航空服務及飛機師 (Aviation service and pilots)
  • B. 西鐵線列車服務及輕鐵列車服務 (West Rail Line train service and Light Rail train service)
  • C. 藍光(Blu-ray)光碟及藍光光碟影碟機 (Blu-ray discs and Blu-ray disc players)
  • D. 香港迪土尼樂園和香港海洋公園 (Hong Kong Disneyland and Ocean Park Hong Kong)

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

引申需求 (Derived Demand): 當對一種物品（通常是生產要素）的需求，是源於對另一種物品（最終產品）的需求時，便稱為引申需求。
When the demand for a good (usually a factor of production) is derived from the demand for another good (the final product), it is called derived demand.
連帶需求 (Joint Demand): 兩種物品必須一起使用才能滿足某種慾望（即輔助品）。
Two goods must be used together to satisfy a want (i.e., complements).
競爭性需求 (Competitive Demand): 兩種物品可以互相替代以滿足同一種慾望（即替代品）。
Two goods can substitute each other to satisfy the same want (i.e., substitutes).
$$ D_{factor} = f(D_{product}) $$
生產要素的需求 \(D_{factor}\) 是最終產品需求 \(D_{product}\) 的函數。
The demand for a factor of production \(D_{factor}\) is a function of the demand for the final product \(D_{product}\).

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): A

Option A (正確): 航空服務是「最終產品」，而飛機師是提供該服務所需的「生產要素」（勞力）。航空公司聘請飛機師，是因為乘客對航空服務有需求。這完全符合引申需求的定義。
Option A (Correct): Aviation service is the "final product", while pilots are the "factor of production" (labor) needed to provide it. Airlines hire pilots because passengers demand aviation services. This perfectly fits the definition of derived demand.
Option B (錯誤): 兩者皆為交通工具，通常被視為替代品（競爭性需求），或在某些轉乘情況下為輔助品（連帶需求），但絕不是產品與生產要素的關係。
Option B (Wrong): Both are modes of transport, usually considered substitutes (competitive demand) or sometimes complements in transit (joint demand), but definitely not a product-factor relationship.
Option C (錯誤): 光碟和影碟機必須一起使用才能播放電影，它們是輔助品，屬於連帶需求 (Joint Demand)。
Option C (Wrong): Discs and players must be used together to watch movies. They are complements, which is an example of Joint Demand.
Option D (錯誤): 迪士尼和海洋公園都是主題樂園，互為競爭對手，屬於替代品，這是競爭性需求 (Competitive Demand)。
Option D (Wrong): Disneyland and Ocean Park are both theme parks competing for visitors. They are substitutes, illustrating Competitive Demand.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (解題關鍵): 尋找「產品 (Product)」與「生產要素 (Factor of Production)」的組合。

當題目問及引申需求時，立即在腦海中尋找：哪一個選項是「為了製造 A，所以我必須購買/聘請 B」？只有選項 A 符合這個邏輯（為了提供航空服務，必須聘請飛機師）。

Pivot Point: Look for a "Product" and "Factor of Production" combination.

When asked about derived demand, immediately ask yourself: Which option fits the logic "To produce A, I must buy/hire B"? Only Option A fits this (to provide aviation services, one must hire pilots).

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ 混淆「引申需求」與「連帶需求」: 很多學生會誤選 C。他們認為「因為我買了光碟，所以我『引申』出對影碟機的需求」。在經濟學中，這叫作連帶需求（輔助品），因為兩者都是最終消費品。引申需求嚴格應用於生產要素（如勞力、土地、資本）。

⚠️ Confusing "Derived Demand" with "Joint Demand": Many students mistakenly choose C. They think "Because I bought a disc, it 'derives' a demand for a player." In economics, this is Joint Demand (complements) because both are final consumer goods. Derived demand strictly applies to factors of production (like labor, land, capital).

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 引申需求只適用於勞力（如飛機師、建築工人）嗎？
A: 不是！它適用於所有生產要素。例如，對「木傢俬」的需求會導致對「木材」（原料/土地要素）的引申需求；對「網約車服務」的需求會導致對「汽車」（資本要素）的引申需求。
Q: Does derived demand only apply to labor (like pilots, construction workers)?
A: No! It applies to all factors of production. For example, the demand for "wooden furniture" creates a derived demand for "wood" (raw material/land factor); the demand for "ride-hailing services" creates a derived demand for "cars" (capital factor).

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

⚡ 秒殺技巧 (Shortcut): 看到「引申需求 (Derived Demand)」，直接掃描選項找「職業 (Occupations)」或「原材料 (Raw Materials)」。選項 A 有「飛機師 (Pilots)」，這是一個職業（勞力），通常就是正確答案！

⚡ Shortcut: When you see "Derived Demand", scan the options immediately for "Occupations" or "Raw Materials". Option A contains "Pilots", which is an occupation (labor), making it the highly likely correct answer!
