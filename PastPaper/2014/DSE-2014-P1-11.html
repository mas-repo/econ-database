📚 課題 (Topic): 勞工流動性 (Labour Mobility)
⭐ 難度 (Difficulty): ★★☆☆☆
🔑 核心概念 (Key Concepts): 職業流動性 (Occupational Mobility), 地域流動性 (Geographical Mobility), 資訊成本 (Information Cost)

1. 題目 (The Question)

下列哪項最有可能增加某經濟中勞工的職業流動性？
Which of the following would most likely increase the occupational mobility of labour in an economy?
A. 工會有更大的討價運價能力為其會員爭取福利。
A. The labour unions have stronger bargaining power for the welfare of their members.
B. 勞工處為再培訓計劃的參加者提供學費回贈。
B. The labour department provides tuition fee rebates for participants of retraining programmes.
C. 高速公路的擴展改善了交通網絡。
C. The transport network is improved by the extension of highways.
D. 政府停止為職位空缺網上資料系統提供資金。
D. The government stops granting funding for an online data system about job vacancies.

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

勞工流動性 (Labour Mobility)

勞工流動性是指工人改變其工作地點或職業的容易程度。它主要分為兩類：

Labour mobility refers to the ease with which workers can change their work location or occupation. It is mainly divided into two types:

  • 職業流動性 (Occupational Mobility): 工人從一種行業/職業轉移到另一種行業/職業的容易程度。受技能要求、培訓成本及時間影響。 The ease with which a worker can move from one trade/profession to another. It is affected by skill requirements, training costs, and time.
  • 地域流動性 (Geographical Mobility): 工人從一個地區轉移到另一個地區工作的容易程度。受交通網絡、搬遷成本及家庭因素影響。 The ease with which a worker can move from one location to another for work. It is affected by transport networks, relocation costs, and family ties.

經濟學決策模型 (Economic Decision Model)

工人決定是否轉換職業，可以理解為一個成本與效益的計算。設 $W_{new}$ 為新職業預期收入，$W_{old}$ 為現時收入，$C_{train}$ 為轉職的培訓及資訊成本。當以下條件成立時，工人會傾向轉職（流動性增加）：

A worker's decision to change occupations can be understood as a cost-benefit analysis. Let $W_{new}$ be the expected income from the new job, $W_{old}$ be the current income, and $C_{train}$ be the training and information cost of changing jobs. A worker is inclined to switch jobs (mobility increases) when:

$$ (W_{new} - W_{old}) > C_{train} $$

因此，任何降低 $C_{train}$ 或增加預期淨收入的政策，都會提升職業流動性。

Therefore, any policy that decreases $C_{train}$ or increases the expected net income will enhance occupational mobility.

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): Option B

Option A 錯誤 Incorrect

工會成功爭取更好的福利，意味著工人保留現有工作的機會成本（Opportunity Cost）增加（即 $W_{old}$ 上升）。這會使工人更不願意離開現有行業，從而降低職業流動性。

If unions successfully negotiate better welfare, the opportunity cost for workers to leave their current jobs increases (i.e., $W_{old}$ rises). This makes workers less willing to leave their current industry, thereby decreasing occupational mobility.

Option B 正確 Correct

再培訓計劃直接幫助工人獲取新技能，而學費回贈降低了獲取這些技能的財務成本（即公式中的 $C_{train}$ 下降）。這使工人更容易轉換到不同職業，直接增加職業流動性。

Retraining programmes directly help workers acquire new skills, and tuition fee rebates lower the financial cost of acquiring these skills (i.e., $C_{train}$ in the formula decreases). This makes it easier for workers to switch to different professions, directly increasing occupational mobility.

Option C 錯誤 Incorrect

改善交通網絡（如高速公路）降低了通勤時間和成本。這使工人更容易到較遠的地方工作，從而增加地域流動性 (Geographical Mobility)，而不是職業流動性。

Improving transport networks (like highways) reduces commuting time and costs. This makes it easier for workers to take jobs in farther locations, thereby increasing geographical mobility, not occupational mobility.

Option D 錯誤 Incorrect

停止為職位空缺系統提供資金會增加工人尋找新工作的資訊成本（Information Cost）。找工作變得更困難，因此會降低勞工流動性。

Stopping funding for a job vacancy system increases the information cost for workers looking for new jobs. Finding a job becomes harder, which would decrease labour mobility.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

  • 尋找「支點」(The Pivot Point): 題目的關鍵字是「職業流動性 (Occupational)」。這意味著我們必須尋找與「技能 (Skills)」、「知識 (Knowledge)」或「跨行業 (Across industries)」相關的選項。 The keyword in the question is "Occupational". This means we must look for an option related to "Skills", "Knowledge", or moving "Across industries".
  • 干擾項 (Distractors): 選項 C 是一個經典的考評局陷阱。它確實增加了流動性，但增加的是「地域 (Geographical)」而非「職業 (Occupational)」流動性。如果同學審題時只看到「流動性」三個字，極易選錯。 Option C is a classic HKEAA trap. It does increase mobility, but it increases "Geographical" rather than "Occupational" mobility. Students who only read the word "mobility" without paying attention to the specific type will easily fall for this.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

🚨 概念混淆： 學生經常將工會的作用（選項 A）理想化，認為工會保障工人，所以一切對工人好的事情都會增加流動性。事實上，經濟學中，現有工作的福利越好，轉換工作的「放棄代價」（機會成本）就越大，流動性反而越低！

🚨 Concept Confusion: Students often idealize the role of trade unions (Option A), thinking that since unions protect workers, anything good for workers increases mobility. In economics, the better the welfare of the current job, the higher the "cost of giving it up" (opportunity cost) to change jobs, which actually lowers mobility!

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼改善交通網絡 (選項 C) 不能幫助職業流動性？如果我能去更遠的地方，不是有更多不同種類的工作可以選擇嗎？

Q: Why doesn't improving transport (Option C) help occupational mobility? If I can travel further, don't I have more types of jobs to choose from?

A: 雖然交通改善擴大了你的求職範圍，但它並沒有賦予你轉換職業所需的技能。例如，一個收銀員即使能搭高鐵去另一個城市，如果他沒有編寫程式的技能（缺乏再培訓），他依然無法轉職成為軟件工程師。因此，交通只解決「地點」問題，不解決「技能」問題。

A: While improved transport expands your radius for job hunting, it does not equip you with the skills needed to change professions. For example, even if a cashier can take a high-speed train to another city, without programming skills (lack of retraining), they still cannot switch to being a software engineer. Therefore, transport only solves the "location" issue, not the "skills" issue.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

💡 關鍵字對應法 (Keyword Matching): 在 DSE 選擇題中處理流動性問題，可以直接使用這套對應法則秒殺題目：

💡 Keyword Matching: When handling mobility questions in DSE MCQs, use this matching rule to quickly eliminate wrong answers:

  • 看到 「職業 (Occupational)」 $\rightarrow$ 馬上尋找：培訓 (Training)、技能 (Skills)、教育 (Education)、發牌制度 (Licensing)。
  • 看到 「地域 (Geographical)」 $\rightarrow$ 馬上尋找：交通 (Transport)、房屋/租金 (Housing/Rent)、搬遷津貼 (Relocation allowance)、簽證 (Visas)。

由於題目問「職業」，掃視四個選項，只有 B 出現了「再培訓 (Retraining)」，可直接鎖定答案。

Since the question asks for "Occupational", scanning the options reveals that only Option B contains "Retraining". You can lock in the answer immediately.
