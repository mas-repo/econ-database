經濟學導師解析

Topic (課題): Market Intervention & Interrelated Markets / 市場干預與關連市場

Difficulty (難度): ★★★★☆

Key Concepts (關鍵概念): Quota (配額), Complements (補充品), Total Social Surplus (總社會盈餘)

1. The Question (題目)

假設汽車和汽油的供應曲線是向右上斜的。若政府對汽車的生產實施一有效的配額，

Suppose the supply curves of cars and petrol are upward-sloping. If the government imposes an effective quota on the production of cars,

A. 汽車市場的總社會盈餘會上升。

A. the total social surplus in the car market will increase.

B. 汽車市場的生產者盈餘會下降。

B. the producer surplus in the car market will decrease.

C. 汽油市場的總社會盈餘會下降。

C. the total social surplus in the petrol market will decrease.

D. 汽油市場的消費者盈餘會上升。

D. the consumer surplus in the petrol market will increase.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

有效配額 (Effective Quota): 政府設定的最高生產或進口數量限制。有效配額必須低於均衡數量 \(Q_e\)。這會導致交易量下降，價格上升，並產生無謂損失 (Deadweight Loss, DWL)。

Effective Quota: A maximum limit set by the government on the quantity produced or imported. An effective quota must be set below the equilibrium quantity \(Q_e\). This leads to a drop in transacted quantity, a rise in price, and creates a Deadweight Loss (DWL).

補充品 (Complements): 必須一起使用的物品（如汽車與汽油）。當汽車的交易量下降時，對汽油的需求也會隨之下降（需求曲線向左移）。

Complements: Goods that are consumed together (e.g., cars and petrol). When the transacted quantity of cars decreases, the demand for petrol will also decrease (demand curve shifts to the left).

總社會盈餘 (Total Social Surplus, TSS): 消費者盈餘與生產者盈餘的總和。在沒有界外效應的情況下，它代表市場的總利益。

Total Social Surplus (TSS): The sum of consumer surplus and producer surplus. In the absence of externalities, it represents the total benefit of the market.

$$ TSS = CS + PS $$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option C

Wrong Option A

實施有效配額會限制汽車的交易量，導致資源錯配並產生無謂損失 (DWL)。因此，汽車市場的總社會盈餘 (TSS) 必然會下降，而不是上升。

Imposing an effective quota restricts the transacted quantity of cars, causing resource misallocation and creating a deadweight loss (DWL). Therefore, the total social surplus (TSS) in the car market will definitively decrease, not increase.

Wrong Option B

雖然汽車的交易量下降，但汽車的價格會上升。生產者盈餘 (PS) 的變化取決於需求彈性。如果需求屬低彈性，價格上升的得益可能大於數量下降的損失，PS 可能會上升。因此，PS 不一定會下降。

Although the transacted quantity of cars decreases, the price of cars will increase. The change in producer surplus (PS) depends on the price elasticity of demand. If demand is inelastic, the gain from the higher price might outweigh the loss from the lower quantity, and PS could increase. Thus, PS does not necessarily decrease.

Correct Option C

汽車和汽油是補充品。配額導致汽車交易量減少，人們對汽油的需求隨之下降（需求曲線左移）。在汽油市場中，需求減少會導致均衡價格和數量同時下降。由於交易量減少及最高願付價格下降，汽油市場的總社會盈餘 (TSS) 必然會下降。

Cars and petrol are complements. The quota reduces the transacted quantity of cars, which in turn decreases the demand for petrol (demand curve shifts left). In the petrol market, a decrease in demand leads to a lower equilibrium price and quantity. Due to the lower transacted quantity and lower maximum willingness to pay, the total social surplus (TSS) in the petrol market definitively decreases.

Wrong Option D

汽油需求下降（向左移）通常會導致消費者盈餘 (CS) 下降（或至少變化不確定，取決於曲線形狀），但絕對不會明確地「上升」。

A decrease in petrol demand (leftward shift) generally leads to a decrease in consumer surplus (CS) (or at least the change is uncertain depending on the curve shapes), but it definitively will not "increase".

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解答此題的「關鍵點 (Pivot Point)」在於理解關連市場 (Interrelated Markets) 的傳導機制：

  1. 市場一 (汽車): 配額 \(\rightarrow\) 交易量 \(Q\) 下降。
  2. 跨市場連結: 汽車與汽油是補充品 \(\rightarrow\) 汽車 \(Q\) 下降導致汽油需求 \(D\) 下降。
  3. 市場二 (汽油): 需求 \(D\) 下降 \(\rightarrow\) 價格 \(P\) 和數量 \(Q\) 下降 \(\rightarrow\) 總社會盈餘 (TSS) 縮少。

The "Pivot Point" to solve this question is understanding the transmission mechanism in Interrelated Markets:

  1. Market 1 (Cars): Quota \(\rightarrow\) Transacted quantity \(Q\) drops.
  2. Cross-market link: Cars and petrol are complements \(\rightarrow\) Drop in car \(Q\) leads to a drop in petrol demand \(D\).
  3. Market 2 (Petrol): Demand \(D\) drops \(\rightarrow\) Price \(P\) and Quantity \(Q\) drop \(\rightarrow\) Total Social Surplus (TSS) shrinks.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ 陷阱一：混淆生產者盈餘 (PS) 的變化
很多學生以為數量減少，生產者盈餘就一定減少。記住：配額會推高價格！如果需求缺乏彈性，生產者實際上可能從配額中獲益（PS 上升）。

⚠️ Trap 1: Confusing the change in Producer Surplus (PS)
Many students assume that if quantity falls, PS must fall. Remember: a quota pushes the price up! If demand is inelastic, producers might actually gain from the quota (PS increases).

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼汽油市場的需求會下降，而不是供應下降？

A: 因為汽車和汽油是「聯合需求 (Joint Demand)」。消費者需要同時擁有汽車才能消耗汽油。當市場上流通的新車減少（配額影響），買車的人少了，自然去油站入油的人（需求）就減少了。這不影響汽油供應商的生產成本（供應不變）。

Q: Why does the demand for petrol decrease, rather than the supply?

A: Because cars and petrol have "Joint Demand". Consumers need a car to consume petrol. When there are fewer cars transacted in the market (due to the quota), fewer people buy cars, so naturally fewer people go to gas stations to buy petrol (demand decreases). This does not affect the production costs of petrol suppliers (supply remains unchanged).

7. Exam Tricks (考試技巧)

💡 速解法 (Shortcut): 看到「有效配額 / 價格下限 / 價格上限 / 稅」，第一時間反應：一定有 DWL，TSS 一定跌！ 這樣可以直接秒殺選項 A。接著看到「補充品」，得知另一市場需求跌，規模縮小，TSS 也一定跌，直接選 C。

💡 Shortcut: When you see "effective quota / price floor / price ceiling / tax", your first reaction should be: There must be DWL, TSS must fall! This instantly eliminates Option A. Then, seeing "complements", you know the demand in the other market falls, market size shrinks, so its TSS must also fall. Choose C directly.
