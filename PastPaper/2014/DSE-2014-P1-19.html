1. 題目 (The Question)

在香港，下列哪些是減輕私人與社會成本分歧這個問題的方法？

(1) 收取排污費
(2) 收取大學學費
(3) 收取電費
(4) 對使用膠袋徵費

A. 只有(1)、(3)
B. 只有(1)、(4)
C. 只有(2)、(3)
D. 只有(2)、(4)
Which of the following are ways to lessen the problem of divergence between private and social costs in Hong Kong?

(1) to collect a sewage charge
(2) to collect a university tuition fee
(3) to collect a charge on electricity
(4) to collect a levy on plastic bags

A. (1) and (3) only
B. (1) and (4) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (2) and (4) only

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

社會成本與私人成本的分歧 (Divergence between Social and Private Costs)：
在生產或消費過程中，若產生了第三方需要承擔的額外成本（即邊際外部成本 Marginal External Cost, MEC），就會出現「負界外效應」(Negative Externality)。此時，社會承擔的總成本大於私人承擔的成本，導致市場產量過多（出現無效率）。政府透過徵收等同於邊際外部成本的稅項（庇古稅 Pigouvian Tax），可以將界外效應「內部化」(Internalize)，從而減輕分歧。
When third parties bear extra costs (i.e., Marginal External Cost, MEC) during production or consumption, a "Negative Externality" occurs. In this case, the total cost borne by society is greater than the private cost, leading to overproduction (inefficiency). By imposing a tax equal to the marginal external cost (Pigouvian Tax), the government can "internalize" the externality, thereby lessening the divergence.
$$ \text{Marginal Social Cost (MSC)} = \text{Marginal Private Cost (MPC)} + \text{Marginal External Cost (MEC)} $$
當 \( \text{MEC} > 0 \) 時，\( \text{MSC} > \text{MPC} \)，這就是「私人與社會成本的分歧」。政府徵稅能使 \( \text{MPC} \) 上升，從而拉近與 \( \text{MSC} \) 的距離。
When \( \text{MEC} > 0 \), then \( \text{MSC} > \text{MPC} \). This is the "divergence between private and social costs". Government taxation increases \( \text{MPC} \), closing the gap with \( \text{MSC} \).

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option)： Option B

  • (1) 收取排污費 (Sewage charge) - 正確 (Correct)
    排放污水會破壞環境，對第三方造成損害，這屬於負界外效應（邊際外部成本大於零）。政府收取排污費，迫使排污者承擔污染的社會成本，成功將外部成本內部化，減輕了私人與社會成本的分歧。
    Discharging sewage damages the environment and harms third parties, which is a negative externality (marginal external cost > 0). By collecting a sewage charge, the government forces polluters to bear the social cost of pollution, successfully internalizing the external cost and lessening the divergence.
  • (2) 收取大學學費 (University tuition fee) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
    大學學費是接受高等教育的私人成本，用以支付教育服務。教育通常帶來正界外效應（社會利益大於私人利益），因此重點在於「利益分歧」而非「成本分歧」。收取學費並不能減輕「成本」上的分歧。
    University tuition is the private cost paid for receiving higher education services. Education generally brings positive externalities (social benefits > private benefits), so the focus is on the divergence of "benefits," not "costs." Collecting tuition does not lessen the divergence in costs.
  • (3) 收取電費 (Charge on electricity) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
    電費是消費者購買電力時向電力公司支付的市場價格，這純粹是反映生產電力的私人成本（如燃料、人工）。它並不是政府為了糾正污染而徵收的稅款或費用，因此沒有將發電造成的外部成本內部化。
    The electricity charge is the market price consumers pay to the power company, strictly reflecting the private cost of producing electricity (e.g., fuel, labor). It is not a tax or levy imposed by the government to correct pollution, thus it does not internalize the external cost of power generation.
  • (4) 對使用膠袋徵費 (Levy on plastic bags) - 正確 (Correct)
    使用塑膠袋會造成堆填區壓力及環境污染（負界外效應）。政府實施膠袋徵費，增加了市民使用膠袋的私人成本 \( \text{MPC} \)，使其更接近社會成本 \( \text{MSC} \)，從而減輕分歧。
    Using plastic bags creates pressure on landfills and causes environmental pollution (a negative externality). The government's levy on plastic bags increases the private cost \( \text{MPC} \) of using them, moving it closer to the social cost \( \text{MSC} \), thereby lessening the divergence.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題關鍵 (Pivot Point)：
看到「私人與社會成本分歧」這幾個字，你的大腦應該立即聯想到「負界外效應」及「政府徵稅/收費以將外部成本內部化」。你需要找出哪一個選項是針對污染/損害第三方的行為所作出的懲罰性收費。
Pivot Point:
When you see the phrase "divergence between private and social costs," your brain should immediately link it to "Negative Externalities" and "Government taxation/levies to internalize external costs." You need to identify which option represents a punitive charge aimed at penalizing behavior that pollutes or harms third parties.
干擾資訊 (Distractors)：
選項 (3) 的「電費」是最大的陷阱。許多同學認為發電會產生污染，所以交電費等於補償社會。但事實上，「電費」只是電力這項商品本身的市場價格（私人成本），並非針對污染的額外懲罰稅。
Distractors:
Option (3) "electricity charge" is the biggest trap. Many students think power generation causes pollution, so paying the electricity bill compensates society. In reality, the "electricity bill" is merely the market price of the commodity itself (private cost), not an additional punitive tax for pollution.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • 混淆市場價格與界外效應費用： 將一般的商業交易費用（如交學費、交水電費）誤以為是政府糾正市場失效的手段。記住，只有針對 \( \text{MEC} \) 額外徵收的費用（如環保稅）才能消除分歧。
  • 未分清「成本分歧」與「利益分歧」： 題目明確指出是「成本分歧」（即負界外效應）。教育和疫苗等屬於正界外效應，牽涉的是「私人利益與社會利益的分歧」。
  • Confusing market prices with externality charges: Mistaking ordinary commercial transaction fees (like paying tuition or utility bills) for government tools to correct market failure. Remember, only additional charges targeted at \( \text{MEC} \) (like environmental taxes) can eliminate the divergence.
  • Failing to distinguish between "cost divergence" and "benefit divergence": The question specifically mentions "cost divergence" (i.e., negative externalities). Goods like education and vaccines create positive externalities, which involve the "divergence between private and social benefits."

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 發電廠明明會噴廢氣造成污染，為什麼交電費不能減輕成本分歧？
A: 好問題！發電廠確實會造成污染。但是，你平時交的「電費」是交給中電或港燈，用來支付他們買煤炭、發工資的「私人成本 \( \text{MPC} \)」。這筆錢並沒有反映噴出廢氣造成的「外部成本 \( \text{MEC} \)」。如果政府在電費之外，額外向發電廠徵收「碳排放稅」，那這項稅才是減輕成本分歧的方法，而不是電費本身。
Q: Power plants emit exhaust and cause pollution. Why doesn't paying the electricity bill lessen the cost divergence?
A: Great question! Power plants do cause pollution. However, the "electricity bill" you pay goes to the power companies to cover their "private costs \( \text{MPC} \)" like buying coal and paying wages. This money does not reflect the "external cost \( \text{MEC} \)" of the exhaust fumes. If the government imposed an additional "carbon emission tax" on top of the regular costs, that tax would be the method to lessen the cost divergence, not the electricity bill itself.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

關鍵字秒殺法 (Keyword Elimination Method)：
在 DSE 經濟科中，若要解決「私人與社會成本的分歧」，你只需在選項中尋找「針對負面行為的罰款 / 徵費 / 稅」（Levy, Charge on pollution, Pigouvian tax）。
- 看到「大學學費」(Tuition) -> 正常消費，即刪。
- 看到「電費」(Electricity bill) -> 正常消費，即刪。
剩下的 (1) 污水和 (4) 膠袋都明顯是政府為了環保而設立的懲罰性費用，答案秒選 B。
Keyword Elimination Method:
In DSE Economics, to solve "divergence between private and social costs," simply look for "fines / levies / taxes targeting negative behaviors" (Levy, Charge on pollution, Pigouvian tax) among the options.
- See "Tuition" -> Normal consumption, eliminate immediately.
- See "Electricity bill" -> Normal consumption, eliminate immediately.
The remaining (1) sewage and (4) plastic bags are clearly punitive charges implemented by the government for environmental protection. The answer is instantly B.
