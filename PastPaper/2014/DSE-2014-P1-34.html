HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Aggregate Demand & Supply (總需求與總供應) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Deflationary Gap (通縮差距), Self-correction Mechanism (自動調節機制), SRAS (短期總供應)
The Question (題目)

下列哪項有關通縮(產出)差距的陳述是正確的？

Which of the following statements about the deflationary (output) gap is correct?

A. 當本國的貨幣貶值，通縮(產出)差距會被擴闊。
A. will be widened when there is a depreciation of the domestic currency.
B. 當市場由短期均衡調整至長期均衡，總需求曲線的向右移動會消除通縮(產出)差距。
B. will be eliminated by a rightward shift of the aggregate demand curve when the market adjusts from short run equilibrium to long run equilibrium.
C. 通縮(產出)差距會引致投入的價格有下調壓力。
C. will result in a downward pressure on the prices of inputs.
D. 若總需求小於短期總供應，通縮(產出)差距便會存在。
D. will exist if the aggregate demand falls short of the short run aggregate supply.
Concept Review (概念重溫)

通縮差距 (Deflationary Gap) 的定義：

Definition of Deflationary Gap:

$$ Y < Y_f $$

當短期均衡產出 (\(Y\)) 小於 潛在產出 / 充分就業產出 (\(Y_f\)) 時，便會出現通縮差距。

A deflationary gap occurs when the short-run equilibrium output (\(Y\)) is less than the potential output / full-employment output (\(Y_f\)).

自動調節機制 (Self-correction Mechanism)：

Self-correction Mechanism:

  • 在通縮差距下，失業率高於自然失業率 (資源過剩)。
    Under a deflationary gap, the unemployment rate is higher than the natural rate (excess supply of resources).
  • 要素價格（如工資）會下降，導致短期總供應 (SRAS) 增加（曲線向右移）。
    Factor prices (e.g., wages) will fall, leading to an increase in Short Run Aggregate Supply (SRAS shifts right).
The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)
Correct Option: C
A. Incorrect (錯誤)

分析：本國貨幣貶值會令出口價格下降，進口價格上升，從而增加淨出口 (\(X-M\))。這會導致總需求 (\(AD\)) 增加並向右移動。當 \(AD\) 增加，均衡產出 (\(Y\)) 會上升，這會收窄甚至消除通縮差距，而不是擴闊。

Analysis: Depreciation of domestic currency makes exports cheaper and imports more expensive, increasing Net Exports (\(X-M\)). This causes Aggregate Demand (\(AD\)) to increase and shift right. When \(AD\) increases, equilibrium output (\(Y\)) rises, which would narrow or eliminate the deflationary gap, not widen it.

B. Incorrect (錯誤)

分析：這是學生常犯的錯誤。市場的「自動調節機制」是透過短期總供應 (SRAS) 的移動來達成的，而非總需求 (AD)。在通縮差距下，工資下調會令 SRAS 向右移，直至回到長期均衡。AD 曲線本身不會在自動調節過程中移動（除非有政府政策介入，但題目指明是「市場調整」）。

Analysis: This is a common student mistake. The market's "self-correction mechanism" works through the shift of the Short Run Aggregate Supply (SRAS) curve, not Aggregate Demand (AD). Under a deflationary gap, falling wages cause SRAS to shift right until long-run equilibrium is reached. The AD curve does not shift autonomously during self-correction (unless there is government policy intervention, but the question specifies "market adjustment").

C. Correct (正確)

分析：通縮差距意味著經濟體系中有閒置資源（例如高失業率）。勞動力市場出現供過於求 (Excess Supply of Labor)。為了爭奪工作，工人願意接受較低的工資，這對投入要素的價格（如工資、租金）構成下調壓力。

Analysis: A deflationary gap implies there are idle resources in the economy (e.g., high unemployment). There is an excess supply of labor in the factor market. To compete for jobs, workers are willing to accept lower wages, creating a downward pressure on the prices of inputs (like wages and rent).

D. Incorrect (錯誤)

分析：這是一個概念混淆。均衡永遠發生在 \(AD = SRAS\) 的點上。通縮差距的定義並非 \(AD < SRAS\)，而是均衡產出小於潛在產出 (\(Y < Y_f\))。如果 \(AD < SRAS\)（在特定價格水平下），那是「超額供應」(Excess Supply) 的失衡狀態，價格水平會調整，而不是定義差距。

Analysis: This is a conceptual confusion. Equilibrium always occurs where \(AD = SRAS\). A deflationary gap is not defined by \(AD < SRAS\), but by equilibrium output being less than potential output (\(Y < Y_f\)). If \(AD < SRAS\) (at a specific price level), that represents a disequilibrium state of "Excess Supply," causing the price level to adjust, but it is not the definition of the gap itself.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的關鍵點 (Pivot Point) 在於分辨「成因」、「特徵」與「結果」。

The Pivot Point of this type of question lies in distinguishing between "Causes," "Characteristics," and "Consequences."

  • 選項 A (成因分析)：測試你是否知道 AD 的變動方向。貶值 \(\rightarrow\) AD \(\uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) 差距收窄。
    Option A (Cause Analysis): Tests if you know the direction of AD shift. Depreciation \(\rightarrow\) AD \(\uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) Gap narrows.
  • 選項 B (調節過程)：測試你是否知道是哪條曲線移動。記住：市場自動調節 = 供應方 (SRAS) 反應。
    Option B (Adjustment Process): Tests if you know which curve shifts. Remember: Market self-correction = Supply side (SRAS) response.
  • 選項 C (結果/特徵)：測試你對差距背後資源市場狀況的理解（失業 \(\rightarrow\) 工資跌）。
    Option C (Consequence/Characteristic): Tests your understanding of the resource market condition behind the gap (Unemployment \(\rightarrow\) Wages fall).
⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

1. 混淆「政府政策」與「市場調節」：
如果是政府使用財政政策，AD 確實會向右移。但題目說是「市場調整」(Market adjusts)，這必然是指要素價格改變引致 SRAS 移動。

1. Confusing "Government Policy" with "Market Adjustment":
If the government uses fiscal policy, AD does shift right. But the question specifies "market adjusts," which inevitably refers to SRAS shifting due to changes in factor prices.

2. 誤解差距的定義：
很多學生看到「通縮」就以為是 AD 少於 AS。請緊記：差距是比較 \(Y\) 和 \(Y_f\) 的位置。

2. Misunderstanding the definition of Gap:
Many students see "deflationary" and assume it means AD is less than AS. Please remember: The gap is a comparison between the positions of \(Y\) and \(Y_f\).
