📊 HKDSE Economics Topic Analysis

Topic (課題): Money and Banking - Three-tier Banking System (貨幣與銀行：香港三級銀行體系)

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐½ (2.5 / 5)

Key Concepts (關鍵概念): Licensed Banks, Restricted Licence Banks, Deposit-taking Companies

1. The Question (題目)

瑪姬計劃在香港的金融機構存入$500 000，為期兩個月。下列哪些機構可接受這筆存款？

Maggie plans to deposit $500 000 in a financial institution in Hong Kong for two months. Which of the following institutions can accept this deposit?

(1) 持牌銀行
(2) 有限制牌照銀行
(3) 接受存款公司

(1) licensed banks
(2) restricted licence banks
(3) deposit-taking companies

  • A. 只有(1)、(2)
  • B. 只有(1)、(3)
  • C. 只有(2)、(3)
  • D. (1)、(2)及(3)
  • A. (1) and (2) only
  • B. (1) and (3) only
  • C. (2) and (3) only
  • D. (1), (2) and (3)

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這題，我們必須清楚了解香港「三級銀行體系」對接受存款的法定限制。這是考試常客！

To answer this question, we must clearly understand the statutory restrictions on accepting deposits under Hong Kong's "Three-tier Banking System". This is a frequent exam topic!

Institution (機構) Minimum Deposit Amount
(最低存款額)		 Minimum Maturity
(最短存款期)
持牌銀行 (Licensed Banks, LB) 無限制 (No restriction) 無限制 (No restriction)
有限制牌照銀行 (Restricted Licence Banks, RLB) $500,000 無限制 (No restriction)
接受存款公司 (Deposit-taking Companies, DTC) $100,000 3 個月 (3 months)

在數學邏輯上，機構能否接受存款的條件可寫成：

In mathematical logic, the conditions for an institution to accept a deposit can be written as:

$$ \text{RLB Condition: } \text{Amount} \ge \$500,000 $$ $$ \text{DTC Condition: } \text{Amount} \ge \$100,000 \quad \text{AND} \quad \text{Time} \ge 3 \text{ months} $$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): A

讓我們根據題目的兩個關鍵數據逐一分析：存款額 = $500,000 及 存款期 = 2個月。

Let's analyze step-by-step based on the two key figures in the question: Deposit Amount = $500,000 and Maturity = 2 months.

(1) 持牌銀行 (Licensed Banks):
持牌銀行對存款金額和期限沒有任何限制。因此，它們絕對可以接受這筆存款。 (1) 正確。

(1) Licensed Banks:
Licensed banks have no restrictions on deposit amount and maturity. Therefore, they can absolutely accept this deposit. (1) is correct.

(2) 有限制牌照銀行 (Restricted Licence Banks):
法定要求是最低存款額為 $500,000。瑪姬剛好存入 $500,000，符合金額要求。另外，這類銀行對存款期限沒有限制，因此2個月的期限也是合法的。 (2) 正確。

(2) Restricted Licence Banks:
The statutory requirement is a minimum deposit amount of $500,000. Maggie deposits exactly $500,000, which satisfies the amount requirement. Additionally, there are no maturity restrictions for RLBs, so a 2-month period is fully legal. (2) is correct.

(3) 接受存款公司 (Deposit-taking Companies):
法定要求是最低存款額 $100,000 及 最短存款期為 3 個月。雖然瑪姬的存款額 ($500,000) 滿足了條件，但她的存款期只有 2 個月，未能達到最少 3 個月的門檻。因此，接受存款公司不能接受這筆存款。 (3) 錯誤。

(3) Deposit-taking Companies:
The statutory requirement is a minimum deposit amount of $100,000 AND a minimum maturity of 3 months. Although Maggie's deposit amount ($500,000) satisfies the condition, her deposit period is only 2 months, which fails to meet the minimum 3-month threshold. Therefore, DTCs cannot accept this deposit. (3) is incorrect.

綜合以上分析，只有 (1) 和 (2) 正確，故答案選 A。

Based on the above analysis, only (1) and (2) are correct, so the answer is A.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

🎯 拆題樞紐 (Pivot Point):
這類題目的核心在於「雙重測試」(Double Test)：必須同時測試金額 (Amount) 與 期限 (Time)。很多同學只看金額就匆忙作答，這正是考評局設下的陷阱。

🎯 Pivot Point:
The core of this type of question lies in the "Double Test": you must test both the Amount and the Time simultaneously. Many students rush to answer by only looking at the amount, which is exactly the trap set by the HKEAA.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ 陷阱一：剛好踩線的金額
題目設定金額為剛好 $500,000。有些同學誤以為 RLB 要求「大於」( \(>\) ) $500,000，其實法例是「大於或等於」( \(\ge\) ) $500,000。瑪姬的錢剛好足夠！

⚠️ Trap 1: The exact borderline amount
The amount is set exactly at $500,000. Some students mistakenly think RLBs require "greater than" ( \(>\) ) $500,000, but the law states "greater than or equal to" ( \(\ge\) ) $500,000. Maggie's money is just enough!

⚠️ 陷阱二：忽略「及」(AND) 的邏輯
對於接受存款公司 (DTC)，必須同時滿足「≥ $100,000」和「≥ 3個月」兩個條件。金額達標但時間不達標，依然是拒絕接受的。

⚠️ Trap 2: Ignoring the "AND" logic
For Deposit-taking Companies (DTCs), both conditions ("≥ $100,000" and "≥ 3 months") must be met simultaneously. Meeting the amount but failing the time requirement results in a rejection.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

👦🏻 學生問： 如果瑪姬把存款期改為「3個月」，答案會有改變嗎？

👦🏻 Student: If Maggie changed her deposit period to "3 months", would the answer change?

👨🏻‍🏫 導師答： 會的！如果存款期是3個月，那麼接受存款公司 (DTC) 也能接受這筆錢（因為 $500,000 ≥ $100,000 且 3個月 ≥ 3個月）。屆時 (1)、(2) 及 (3) 皆可，答案就會變成 D。

👨🏻‍🏫 Tutor: Yes! If the deposit period were 3 months, Deposit-taking Companies (DTCs) could also accept the money (since $500,000 ≥ $100,000 and 3 months ≥ 3 months). In that case, (1), (2), and (3) would all be able to accept it, and the answer would become D.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

⚡ 5秒速解法 (5-Second Shortcut):
做多項選擇題時，養成「先看時間，後看金額」的習慣！
一看題目「為期兩個月」(< 3個月)，你就知道 DTC 絕對不行！所以 (3) 必定錯誤。
立刻劃掉選項 B、C、D。答案 A 秒殺得出！根本不用檢查 (1) 和 (2) 的金額。

⚡ 5-Second Shortcut:
When doing MC questions, develop the habit of "Checking TIME first, AMOUNT second"!
The moment you see "for two months" (< 3 months) in the question, you know DTC is absolutely out! So (3) must be wrong.
Immediately eliminate options B, C, and D. You instantly arrive at answer A! You don't even need to verify the amounts for (1) and (2).
