📝 HKDSE Economics MCQ Analysis

Topic (課題): 勞工市場 - 勞力供應 | Labour Market - Labour Supply

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)

Key Concepts (核心概念):

勞力供應 Labour Supply 勞動人口 Working Population 工作時數 Working Hours

1. The Question (題目)

下列哪項會增加某經濟的勞力供應？

A. 更多廠商允許男性僱員請侍產假。
B. 更多公司為加班的僱員提供免費交通。
C. 大學為中學畢業生開辦更多全日制副學士學位課程。
D. 政府對公務員實施最高工時。

Which of the following will increase the labour supply of an economy?

A. More firms allow male employees to take paternity leave.
B. More companies offer free transport to employees working overtime.
C. Universities provide more full-time associate degree programmes for secondary school graduates.
D. The government adopts maximum working hours for the civil servants.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

勞力供應 (Labour Supply) 是指在特定時間和工資率下，勞動人口願意且能夠提供的總工作時數。它不僅僅計算「人數」，更重要的是計算「總工時」。

Labour Supply refers to the total number of working hours that the working population is willing and able to provide at a given wage rate in a specific period. It is not just about the "number of people", but more importantly, the "total working hours".

$$ \text{Labour Supply} = \text{Working Population} \times \text{Average Working Hours} $$ $$ \text{勞力供應} = \text{勞動人口} \times \text{平均工作時數} $$
  • 勞動人口 (Working Population): 願意並有能力工作的人數（包括就業及失業人士）。
    The number of people who are willing and able to work (including employed and unemployed).
  • 平均工作時數 (Average Working Hours): 每個工人平均願意工作的時間。改善非金錢回報（附帶福利）會增加工人延長工時的意欲。
    The average time each worker is willing to work. Improving non-monetary rewards (fringe benefits) increases workers' willingness to work longer hours.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 Correct Option: Option B

Option A: Incorrect (錯誤)

侍產假意味著男性僱員會休假，這會減少他們的平均工作時數 \( \text{Average Working Hours} \)。因此，整體的勞力供應會下降。

Paternity leave means male employees will take time off work, which decreases their average working hours \( \text{Average Working Hours} \). Therefore, the overall labour supply will decrease.

Option B: Correct (正確)

為加班僱員提供免費交通是一種附帶福利（非金錢回報）。這降低了加班的成本，增加了僱員加班的誘因，從而增加了平均工作時數。根據公式，這會導致勞力供應增加。

Providing free transport for overtime workers is a fringe benefit (non-monetary reward). This lowers the cost of working overtime and provides an incentive for employees to work longer hours, increasing average working hours. According to the formula, this increases labour supply.

Option C: Incorrect (錯誤)

更多中學畢業生選擇全日制進修，意味著他們會延遲投入勞工市場。這會減少勞動人口 \( \text{Working Population} \)，從而導致勞力供應下降。

More secondary school graduates choosing full-time study means they will delay entering the labour market. This reduces the working population \( \text{Working Population} \), thereby decreasing the labour supply.

Option D: Incorrect (錯誤)

實施最高工時法例會限制公務員的工作時間，強制降低了他們的平均工作時數 \( \text{Average Working Hours} \)。這無疑會減少勞力供應。

Adopting maximum working hours limits the working time of civil servants, forcibly reducing their average working hours \( \text{Average Working Hours} \). This will undoubtedly decrease the labour supply.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (解題關鍵): 題目問的是「勞力供應」(Labour Supply) 的「增加」(Increase)。你必須在腦海中立刻浮現公式：\( \text{Labour Supply} = \text{Working Population} \times \text{Average Working Hours} \)。然後逐一檢視每個選項是影響「人數」還是「時數」，以及是「增加」還是「減少」。

Pivot Point: The question asks for an "increase" in "labour supply". You must immediately recall the formula: \( \text{Labour Supply} = \text{Working Population} \times \text{Average Working Hours} \). Then, examine each option to see if it affects "number of people" or "hours", and whether it is an "increase" or "decrease".

Distractors (干擾資訊): 選項中包含了不同的人群（男性僱員、中學畢業生、公務員）。這些具體群體只是包裝，核心依然是評估他們的「工作意願」或「可工作時間」發生了什麼變化。

Distractors: The options include different groups of people (male employees, secondary graduates, civil servants). These specific groups are just packaging; the core is still to evaluate what happens to their "willingness to work" or "available working time".

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ 混淆「勞力供應」與「勞工生產力」 (Confusing Labour Supply with Labour Productivity)

很多學生誤以為勞力供應是指「生產力」或「工作效率」。例如，有人可能會想：「放侍產假 (Option A) 可以讓員工休息好，回來工作更有效率，所以勞力供應增加。」這是錯誤的！效率提升屬於「勞工生產力」(Labour Productivity)，而「勞力供應」純粹計算「投入的總工時」。放假 = 零工時 = 勞力供應減少。

Many students mistake labour supply for "productivity" or "working efficiency". For example, some might think: "Taking paternity leave (Option A) lets employees rest, so they are more efficient when they return, increasing labour supply." This is WRONG! An increase in efficiency is "Labour Productivity", whereas "Labour Supply" purely calculates the "total man-hours inputted". Taking leave = zero working hours = decreased labour supply.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼選項C提供學位課程不是增加勞力供應？他們畢業後不是會提供更高質素的勞力嗎？
Q: Why doesn't Option C (providing degree programmes) increase labour supply? Won't they provide higher quality labour after graduation?

A: 經濟學分析通常著眼於即時/短期的影響（除非題目特別標明長期）。當學生選擇「全日制」(full-time) 讀書時，他們此刻退出了勞工市場，不屬於就業或失業人口。因此，目前的勞動人口減少了。你提到畢業後的「高質素」，那是指未來的「勞工生產力」提升，而不是當前的「勞力供應總工時」。

A: Economic analysis usually focuses on immediate/short-term impacts (unless specified as long-term). When students choose "full-time" study, they exit the labour market at this moment and are neither employed nor unemployed. Thus, the current working population decreases. Your point about "higher quality" after graduation refers to an increase in future "Labour Productivity", not the current "total man-hours of Labour Supply".

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法：邏輯排除 (The Logic Elimination Method)

尋找「增加工作時間」的唯一選項。在考場上，你可以快速掃描關鍵詞：

  • A: 「假」(Leave) ➡️ 減少工時 ➡️ 減少。
  • C: 「全日制讀書」(Full-time study) ➡️ 不工作 ➡️ 減少。
  • D: 「最高工時」(Maximum hours) ➡️ 限制工作 ➡️ 減少。
  • B: 「加班」(Overtime) 的誘因 ➡️ 增加工時 ➡️ 唯一增加的選項！秒殺！

Shortcut: Look for the ONLY option that "increases working time". In the exam, you can quickly scan for keywords:

  • A: "Leave" ➡️ reduces hours ➡️ Decrease.
  • C: "Full-time study" ➡️ not working ➡️ Decrease.
  • D: "Maximum hours" ➡️ limits work ➡️ Decrease.
  • B: Incentive for "Overtime" ➡️ increases hours ➡️ The ONLY option that increases! Instant solve!
