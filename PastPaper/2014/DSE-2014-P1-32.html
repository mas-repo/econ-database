HKDSE Economics Tutorial

Topic: Money and Inflation (貨幣與通脹) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Fisher Equation, Real Interest Rate, Return on Cash

1. The Question (題目)

一年期債券的名義利率和預期通脹率分別是 8%和 3%。若實際通脹率是 6%，則實際實質利率是 __________ 而持有現金的實際回報率是 __________。

  • A. 2% …… -6%
  • B. 2% …… -8%
  • C. 5% …… -6%
  • D. 5% …… -8%

The nominal interest rate of a one-year bond and the expected inflation rate are 8% and 3% respectively. If the actual inflation rate is 6%, then the actual real interest rate is _______ and the actual rate of return of holding cash is _______.

  • A. 2% …… -6%
  • B. 2% …… -8%
  • C. 5% …… -6%
  • D. 5% …… -8%

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

費雪方程式 (Fisher Equation)

在經濟學中，我們必須區分「名義 (Nominal)」與「實質 (Real)」數值，以及「預期 (Expected)」與「實際 (Actual)」數值。實質利率反映了借貸的真實購買力變化。
In economics, we must distinguish between "Nominal" and "Real" values, as well as "Expected" and "Actual" values. The real interest rate reflects the true change in purchasing power of borrowing and lending.

$$ r = R - \pi $$ $$ \text{Real Interest Rate} = \text{Nominal Interest Rate} - \text{Inflation Rate} $$
  • 若計算實際實質利率 (Actual Real Interest Rate)：必須使用實際通脹率 (Actual Inflation Rate)。
    If calculating the Actual Real Interest Rate: You must use the Actual Inflation Rate.
  • 若計算預期實質利率 (Expected Real Interest Rate)：必須使用預期通脹率 (Expected Inflation Rate)。
    If calculating the Expected Real Interest Rate: You must use the Expected Inflation Rate.

持有現金的回報 (Rate of Return of Holding Cash)

現金的「名義利率」永遠是 \(0\%\)（把錢放在錢包裡不會產生利息）。因此，持有現金的實際回報純粹取決於通脹導致的購買力流失。
The "nominal interest rate" of cash is always \(0\%\) (keeping money in your wallet generates no interest). Therefore, the actual rate of return of holding cash depends purely on the loss of purchasing power due to inflation.

$$ \text{Return on Cash} = 0\% - \text{Actual Inflation Rate} = -\pi $$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 Correct Option: A

分析 Option A (正確 Correct)

第一部分 (實際實質利率)： 根據公式，實際實質利率 \(= \text{名義利率} (8\%) - \text{實際通脹率} (6\%) = 2\%\)。
Part 1 (Actual Real Interest Rate): According to the formula, Actual Real Interest Rate \(= \text{Nominal Interest Rate} (8\%) - \text{Actual Inflation Rate} (6\%) = 2\%\).

第二部分 (持有現金的實際回報率)： 持有現金的名義回報是 \(0\%\)。實際回報率 \(= 0\% - \text{實際通脹率} (6\%) = -6\%\)。
Part 2 (Actual rate of return of holding cash): The nominal return of holding cash is \(0\%\). Actual rate of return \(= 0\% - \text{Actual Inflation Rate} (6\%) = -6\%\).

分析 Option B (錯誤 Incorrect)

錯在第二部分。持有現金的回報率不是負的名義利率 (\(-8\%\))。\(8\%\) 是持有現金的「機會成本 (Opportunity Cost)」，而非「回報率 (Rate of Return)」。
Incorrect in the second part. The rate of return of holding cash is not the negative nominal interest rate (\(-8\%\)). \(8\%\) is the "Opportunity Cost" of holding cash, not its "Rate of Return".

分析 Option C (錯誤 Incorrect)

錯在第一部分。\(5\%\) 是預期實質利率（\(8\% - 3\% = 5\%\)），但題目要求的是實際實質利率。這是一個引誘你使用錯誤通脹率的陷阱。
Incorrect in the first part. \(5\%\) is the expected real interest rate (\(8\% - 3\% = 5\%\)), but the question asks for the actual real interest rate. This is a trap enticing you to use the wrong inflation rate.

分析 Option D (錯誤 Incorrect)

兩個部分皆錯誤。既錯誤地計算了預期實質利率 (\(5\%\))，又把名義利率當作了現金的購買力跌幅 (\(-8\%\))。
Both parts are incorrect. It wrongly calculates the expected real interest rate (\(5\%\)) and incorrectly uses the nominal interest rate as the drop in cash purchasing power (\(-8\%\)).

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (破題關鍵):
認清題目要求的字眼：「實際 (Actual)」。這意味著所有與「預期 (Expected)」相關的數據都是無用的干擾訊息 (Distractors)。
Identify the key word in the question: "Actual". This means any data related to "Expected" is useless distracting information.

Distractor (干擾數據):
預期通脹率 \(3\%\) 完全不需要用到。出題者故意提供此數據，就是為了測試考生能否分辨「預期」與「實際」的概念。
The Expected Inflation Rate of \(3\%\) is completely unneeded. The examiner intentionally provides this data to test if students can differentiate between "expected" and "actual" concepts.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱 1：混淆「機會成本」與「回報率」
Trap 1: Confusing "Opportunity Cost" with "Rate of Return"
很多同學會選錯現金回報率為 \(-8\%\)。請記住：
- 持有現金的機會成本 (Opportunity Cost) = 放棄的最高名義利率 (\(8\%\))。
- 持有現金的實際回報率 (Actual Rate of Return) = 購買力的改變 (\(-\text{實際通脹率} = -6\%\))。

陷阱 2：見數字就減
Trap 2: Subtracting numbers blindly
有些同學看到 \(8\%\) 和 \(3\%\) 就立刻相減得出 \(5\%\)。在答題前，務必圈出題目中的時間性字眼（實際 vs 預期）。

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q1: 為什麼現金的名義利率是 0%？如果是存在銀行的活期存款呢？
Q1: Why is the nominal interest rate of cash 0%? What if it's demand deposits in a bank?
A: 經濟學中定義的「持有現金 (Holding cash)」是指實體貨幣（紙幣和硬幣），它本身不會生息，所以名義利率永遠是 \(0\%\)。如果題目說「持有活期存款 (Demand deposits)」，則要看銀行提供的活期名義利率是多少（通常極低，但未必是 \(0\%\)）。本題明確說明是「現金」。
A: In economics, "holding cash" refers to physical currency (banknotes and coins), which does not bear interest, so its nominal interest rate is always \(0\%\). If the question specified "demand deposits", you would need to use the nominal interest rate provided by the bank. This question explicitly says "cash".

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

「反向解題法」秒殺 MC (The "Reverse Elimination" Method)

在應付這類雙重填空的 MC 題時，通常後半部分會比較容易計算，或者是多數考生的盲點。我們可以直接先看後半部：
When tackling double-blank MC questions, the second half is often easier to calculate or acts as a blind spot. We can look at the second half first:

  1. 題目問「持有現金的實際回報率」。
    Question asks for "actual rate of return of holding cash".
  2. 腦海中立刻浮現公式：\(0\% - \text{實際通脹率} (6\%) = -6\%\)。
    Formula instantly pops into mind: \(0\% - \text{Actual Inflation Rate} (6\%) = -6\%\).
  3. 看看選項，B 和 D 的後半部是 \(-8\%\)，立刻刪去 (Eliminate B and D)。
    Look at options, the second half for B and D is \(-8\%\), eliminate them immediately.
  4. 現在只剩 A 和 C。題目問「實際 (Actual)」，所以我們用實際通脹率 (\(6\%\))。名義 (\(8\%\)) - 實際通脹 (\(6\%\)) = \(2\%\)。選 A！
    Now only A and C remain. The question asks for "Actual", so we use actual inflation (\(6\%\)). Nominal (\(8\%\)) - Actual inflation (\(6\%\)) = \(2\%\). Choose A!
