1. 基本資料 (Header)

Topic: 政府干預與市場效率 (Government Intervention & Market Efficiency) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: 從量稅 (Per-unit Tax), 總支出 (Total Expenditure), 絕對損失 (Deadweight Loss)

2. 題目 (The Question)

政府對化妝品開徵從量銷售稅會：
The imposition of a per-unit sales tax on cosmetics will:
  • A. 引致對化妝品的總支出下降。
    A. reduce the total expenditure on cosmetics.
  • B. 引致除稅後總銷售收益下降。
    B. reduce the total sales revenue (net-of-the-tax).
  • C. 增加化妝品市場的總社會盈餘。
    C. increase the total social surplus in the cosmetics market.
  • D. 增加化妝品市場的生產者盈餘。
    D. increase in producer surplus in the cosmetics market.

3. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

當政府徵收從量稅 (Per-unit tax) 時，供應曲線會向上/向左平移，平移的垂直距離相等於稅額 ($t$)。這會導致消費者支付的價格 ($P_c$) 上升，而生產者實際收取的價格 ($P_p$) 下降，同時市場交易量 ($Q$) 減少。

When the government imposes a per-unit tax, the supply curve shifts upward/leftward by a vertical distance equal to the tax per unit ($t$). This causes the price paid by consumers ($P_c$) to increase, the net price received by producers ($P_p$) to decrease, and the market equilibrium quantity ($Q$) to fall.

生產者實收價格 (Net Price Received by Producers): $$P_p = P_c - t$$
總支出 (Total Expenditure) / 總銷售收益 (Gross Total Revenue): $$TE = P_c \times Q$$
除稅後總銷售收益 (Total Sales Revenue net-of-tax): $$TR_{net} = P_p \times Q$$

4. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案：Option B

Correct Option: Option B

❌ 選項 A (Option A)

分析：總支出 ($TE$) 等於消費者支付的價格 ($P_c$) 乘以交易量 ($Q$)。徵稅後，$P_c$ 上升而 $Q$ 下降。總支出是上升還是下降，取決於化妝品的需求價格彈性。如果需求屬低彈性 (Inelastic)，價格上升的幅度大於需求量下降的幅度，總支出反而會增加。因此，我們無法確定總支出必然下降。

Analysis: Total expenditure ($TE$) equals the price consumers pay ($P_c$) multiplied by the quantity ($Q$). After the tax, $P_c$ increases and $Q$ decreases. Whether $TE$ rises or falls depends on the price elasticity of demand. If demand is inelastic, the percentage increase in price will outweigh the percentage decrease in quantity, causing $TE$ to increase. Thus, we cannot definitively say it will reduce.

✅ 選項 B (Option B)

分析：除稅後總銷售收益等於生產者實際收取的價格 ($P_p$) 乘以交易量 ($Q$)。由於徵稅導致 $P_p$ 必然下降，同時 $Q$ 也必然下降。當兩個相乘的變量都同時下降時，它們的乘積 (除稅後收益) 必然下降。這是100%確定的結果。

Analysis: Total sales revenue net-of-the-tax equals the net price producers receive ($P_p$) multiplied by the quantity ($Q$). Because the tax guarantees that both $P_p$ falls and $Q$ falls, their product (net revenue) must definitively fall. This is a 100% certain outcome.

❌ 選項 C (Option C)

分析：任何扭曲市場均衡價格的干預（如徵稅、補貼、價格管制）都會導致無效率的資源分配，從而產生絕對損失 (Deadweight Loss)。因此，總社會盈餘 (Total Social Surplus) 必然會減少，而不是增加。

Analysis: Any intervention that distorts the market equilibrium price (like taxes, subsidies, or price controls) leads to an inefficient allocation of resources, creating a Deadweight Loss. Therefore, total social surplus must decrease, not increase.

❌ 選項 D (Option D)

分析：生產者盈餘 (Producer Surplus) 幾何上是供給曲線以上、生產者實收價格 ($P_p$) 以下的面積。由於 $P_p$ 下降且交易量 $Q$ 減少，生產者盈餘必然減少。

Analysis: Producer Surplus is geometrically the area above the supply curve and below the net price received by producers ($P_p$). Since both $P_p$ falls and quantity $Q$ decreases, the producer surplus must decrease.

5. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

這道題目的「破局點 (Pivot Point)」在於你能否清晰區分「總支出 (Total Expenditure)」與「除稅後總銷售收益 (Net-of-tax Revenue)」的分別。

The "Pivot Point" of this question is whether you can clearly distinguish between "Total Expenditure" and "Net-of-tax Revenue".

  • 干擾資訊 (Distractor): 選項A利用了「徵稅會打擊市場」的直覺，誘使考生以為所有與錢有關的指標都會下跌。但如果忘記了考慮彈性 (Elasticity)，就會跌入陷阱。
    Distractor: Option A preys on the intuition that "taxes hurt the market," tempting students to think all money-related metrics drop. But if you forget to consider elasticity, you fall into the trap.
  • 關鍵資訊 (Crucial Info): 選項B的兩個組成部分（$P_p$ 和 $Q$）在徵稅後的方向是完全一致的（雙雙下跌），這使得我們無需知道彈性也能得出確定結論。
    Crucial Info: Both components of Option B ($P_p$ and $Q$) move in the exact same direction (both fall) after a tax, allowing us to reach a definitive conclusion without needing to know elasticity.

6. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

⚠️ 混淆兩種 Revenue / Expenditure 的價格變數

⚠️ Confusing the price variables for the two types of Revenue / Expenditure

很多同學會以為 $TE$ (總支出) 和 除稅後總收益是同一個概念。記住：
消費者付的錢 = $P_c \times Q$
生產者真正落袋的錢 = $P_p \times Q$
中間的差額 $(P_c - P_p) \times Q$ 就是政府收走的總稅收 (Total Tax Revenue)。

Many students think $TE$ (Total Expenditure) and net revenue are the same concept. Remember:
Money consumers pay = $P_c \times Q$
Money producers actually keep = $P_p \times Q$
The difference in between, $(P_c - P_p) \times Q$, is the Total Tax Revenue taken by the government.

7. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

問 (Q): 如果題目說「化妝品的需求屬低彈性 (Inelastic demand)」，答案會變嗎？

Q: If the question stated "the demand for cosmetics is inelastic", would the answer change?

答 (A): 如果加上這個條件，選項 B 依然是正確的（因為 $P_p$ 和 $Q$ 依然會跌）。但此時選項 A 就會變成絕對錯誤的陳述，因為在低彈性下，價格上升的百分比大於需求量下跌的百分比，總支出 ($P_c \times Q$) 反而會上升！

A: If this condition were added, Option B would still be correct (since $P_p$ and $Q$ still fall). However, Option A would then become an absolutely false statement. With inelastic demand, the percentage increase in price is greater than the percentage decrease in quantity, so total expenditure ($P_c \times Q$) would actually increase!

8. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

💡 方向判定法 (The Direction Method)

在處理「總額 (Total XXX)」是升是跌的 MC 題時，寫出它的公式並畫箭頭：

When dealing with MC questions asking if a "Total XXX" increases or decreases, write down its formula and draw arrows:

  • 如果一個箭頭向上 (↑)，一個箭頭向下 (↓) ➔ 答案必定是「不確定 / 取決於彈性 (Uncertain / Depends on elasticity)」。 (例如本題的 Option A: $P_c$↑, $Q$↓)
  • If one arrow points up (↑) and one points down (↓) ➔ The answer is definitely "Uncertain / Depends on elasticity". (e.g., Option A here: $P_c$↑, $Q$↓)
  • 如果兩個箭頭方向一致 (↓↓ 或 ↑↑) ➔ 答案就是確定的。 (例如本題的 Option B: $P_p$↓, $Q$↓ ➔ 乘積必定↓)
  • If both arrows point the same way (↓↓ or ↑↑) ➔ The answer is definitive. (e.g., Option B here: $P_p$↓, $Q$↓ ➔ Product must ↓)

用這個方法，你可以在 10 秒內秒殺這類題目，連圖都不用畫！

Using this method, you can kill this type of question in 10 seconds without even drawing a graph!
