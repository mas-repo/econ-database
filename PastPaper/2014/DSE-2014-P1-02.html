Topic: Money & Barter Economy (貨幣與以物易物經濟) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Barter Economy, Inflation, Interest

📝 The Question (題目)

在以物易物的經濟，下列哪(幾)項並不存在？

(1) 通脹
(2) 利息
(3) 比較優勢

A. 只有(1)
B. 只有(1)、(2)
C. 只有(2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2)及(3)

Which of the following do(es) NOT exist in a barter economy?

(1) inflation
(2) interest
(3) comparative advantage

A. (1) only
B. (1) and (2) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. Barter Economy (以物易物經濟)

一個沒有貨幣作為交易媒介的經濟體系。物品與物品直接交換。

An economic system with no money acting as a medium of exchange. Goods are traded directly for other goods.

2. Inflation (通脹)

一般物價水平持續上升。物價水平必須以「貨幣」來量度。沒有貨幣，就沒有一般物價水平。

A persistent increase in the general price level. The price level must be measured in terms of "money". Without money, there is no general price level.

$$ \text{Inflation Rate } (\pi) = \frac{P_t - P_{t-1}}{P_{t-1}} \times 100\% $$

Where \(P\) is the general price level (measured in fiat/money).

3. Interest (利息)

提早獲取物品的代價（或延遲消費的補償）。利息是基於「時間偏好」(Time Preference)，可以用實物計算（實質利率），不一定需要貨幣。

The premium paid for earlier availability of goods (or compensation for deferring consumption). Interest is based on "Time Preference" and can be calculated in real goods (real interest rate), not necessarily requiring money.

4. Comparative Advantage (比較優勢)

以較低的機會成本生產某物品。只要存在稀少性(Scarcity)和不同的生產力，比較優勢就會存在。

Producing a good at a lower opportunity cost. As long as scarcity and different productivities exist, comparative advantage exists.

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A

(1) 通脹 (Inflation) - 不存在 (Does NOT exist)

通脹是指「一般物價水平」的上升。在以物易物的經濟中，沒有貨幣，因此無法計算一般物價水平，只能計算物品之間的相對價格（例如1個蘋果換2個橙）。因此通脹不存在。

Inflation refers to the rise in the "general price level". In a barter economy, there is no money, so a general price level cannot be calculated; only relative prices between goods exist (e.g., 1 apple for 2 oranges). Therefore, inflation does not exist.

(2) 利息 (Interest) - 存在 (Exists)

利息是提早消費的代價。在以物易物經濟中，你可以今天借入10個蘋果，承諾明年歸還11個蘋果。多出的1個蘋果就是實質利息。因此利息存在。

Interest is the cost of earlier consumption. In a barter economy, you can borrow 10 apples today and promise to return 11 apples next year. The 1 extra apple is the real interest. Therefore, interest exists.

(3) 比較優勢 (Comparative Advantage) - 存在 (Exists)

比較優勢建基於機會成本的差異。只要不同人/國家在生產不同物品時的機會成本不同，比較優勢就存在，這與有沒有貨幣完全無關。

Comparative advantage is based on differences in opportunity cost. As long as different people/countries have different opportunity costs in producing goods, comparative advantage exists, which is completely unrelated to the presence of money.

結論 (Conclusion): 只有 (1) 不存在。答案是 A。 / Only (1) does not exist. The answer is A.

🔍 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的核心考點 (Pivot Point) 在於區分「名義概念」(Nominal concepts) 與「實質概念」(Real concepts)。

The Pivot Point of this type of question lies in distinguishing between "Nominal concepts" and "Real concepts".

  • 通脹 (Inflation): 是一個名義概念，必須依賴貨幣來定義。 / A nominal concept that must rely on money to be defined.
  • 利息與比較優勢 (Interest & Comparative Advantage): 是實質概念，源於人類的時間偏好(Time preference)和稀少性(Scarcity)，即使沒有貨幣也依然存在。 / Real concepts stemming from human time preference and scarcity, which exist even without money.

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：認為利息只與銀行和金錢有關。

很多學生誤以為利息（Interest）必須是「借錢」時支付的貨幣。在經濟學中，利息的本質是「時間的價值」。只要你提早獲得資源（無論是錢還是蘋果），並為此付出額外代價，這就是利息。

Trap: Thinking interest is only related to banks and money.

Many students mistakenly believe that interest must be money paid when "borrowing money". In economics, the essence of interest is the "value of time". As long as you obtain resources earlier (whether money or apples) and pay an extra cost for it, that is interest.

🙋 Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 在以物易物經濟中，如果蘋果換橙的比例改變了（例如原本 1個蘋果換 2個橙，變成換 3個橙），這不算通脹嗎？

Q: In a barter economy, if the exchange ratio between apples and oranges changes (e.g., from 1 apple for 2 oranges to 3 oranges), isn't this inflation?

A: 不算。這只是「相對價格」(Relative Price) 的改變。通脹是指「一般物價水平」(General Price Level) 的上升，這需要一個共同的計價單位（即貨幣）來衡量所有物品的平均價格。沒有貨幣，就無法計算一般物價水平。

A: No. This is only a change in "Relative Price". Inflation refers to a rise in the "General Price Level", which requires a common unit of account (i.e., money) to measure the average price of all goods. Without money, a general price level cannot be calculated.

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

消除法 (Elimination Method):

在微觀經濟學中，我們學過「比較優勢」(3) 是因為資源稀少和機會成本不同而產生的。這是一個最基本的經濟定律，無論有沒有貨幣都必然存在。因此，(3) 必定存在。

既然問「哪項不存在」，答案必定不包含 (3)。

立刻刪去選項 C 和 D。剩下 A 和 B，你只需要判斷 (2) 利息是否存在即可，大大提高答對機率！

In microeconomics, we learned that "comparative advantage" (3) arises from scarcity and different opportunity costs. This is a fundamental economic law that exists with or without money. Therefore, (3) MUST exist.

Since the question asks what does NOT exist, the answer must NOT contain (3).

Immediately eliminate options C and D. You are left with A and B, so you only need to determine if (2) interest exists, greatly increasing your chances of getting it right!
