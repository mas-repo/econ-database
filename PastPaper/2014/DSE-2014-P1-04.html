基本經濟問題 (Basic Economic Problems)

HKDSE Economics / Microeconomics

Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
資源分配 Resource Allocation 生產什麼 What to produce 怎樣生產 How to produce

題目 (The Question)

美荷樓是一幢在五十年代興建的公共樓宇。近日該樓宇被評為香港歷史建築後，它被改建為旅舍，提供雙人房、家庭房及傷健人士房間。上述個案和經濟學中的_______問題有關。

Mei Ho House, a public housing block built in the 1950s, was recently graded as a historic building in Hong Kong. The building was then turned into a hostel which provides double rooms, family rooms and disabled rooms. The above case is related to the _______ question(s) in Economics.

A. 「怎樣生產」 ("how to produce")
B. 「為誰生產」 ("for whom to produce")
C. 「生產什麼」及「怎樣生產」 ("what to produce" and "how to produce")
D. 「生產什麼」及「為誰生產」 ("what to produce" and "for whom to produce")

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

每個社會都面對稀少性問題，因此必須作出以下三個基本的資源分配決定：
Every society faces the problem of scarcity and must make three basic resource allocation decisions:

1. 生產什麼 (What to produce)

涉及決定生產哪些種類的物品與服務，以及生產的數量。

Involves deciding the types and quantities of goods and services to be produced.

例子 Example: 興建公屋還是興建酒店？生產 iPhone 還是 iPad？

2. 怎樣生產 (How to produce)

涉及決定生產的方法、技術，以及如何組合生產要素（勞力密集 vs 資本密集）。

Involves deciding the method of production, technology, and the combination of production factors.

例子 Example: 用機器耕種還是人手耕種？拆卸重建還是活化舊建築？

3. 為誰生產 (For whom to produce)

涉及決定誰可以獲得該物品，即產品的分配原則（價格競爭、排隊、抽籤）。

Involves deciding who gets the goods, i.e., the principle of distribution (price competition, queuing, drawing lots).

答案與詳細分析 (Answer & Detailed Analysis)

理論正確答案: C
Theoretically Correct Option: C

分析步驟 1：識別「生產什麼」(Analyzing "What to produce")

題目提到美荷樓被改建為「旅舍」，並提供「雙人房、家庭房及傷健人士房間」。這說明了資源被用來生產哪種類型的服務（住宿服務）以及具體的產品組合。這屬於選擇生產物品的種類。

The question states that Mei Ho House was turned into a "hostel" providing "double rooms, family rooms, and disabled rooms". This indicates what type of service the resources are used for (accommodation) and the specific product mix. This belongs to the choice of the type of goods.

分析步驟 2：識別「怎樣生產」(Analyzing "How to produce")

題目指出美荷樓是「改建」(converted) 而成的，而不是拆卸後重新興建。這涉及生產方法的選擇：利用現有的歷史建築進行活化，而非使用全新的建築材料從零開始。這是關於如何運用資源進行生產的技術性決定。

The question points out that Mei Ho House was "converted" (or revitalized), rather than demolished and rebuilt. This involves the choice of production method: utilizing an existing historic building versus building from scratch with new materials. This is a technical decision on how to use resources for production.

為什麼不是「為誰生產」？(Why not "For whom to produce"?)

雖然題目提到「家庭房」和「傷健人士房間」，這看起來像是在說對象，但在經濟學上，「為誰生產」主要關注產品如何分配（例如：價高者得、政府配給、抽籤）。題目並沒有提及這些房間是如何分配給使用者的（例如是否免費給傷健人士，還是需要付費預訂）。因此，這裡的房間類型更多是指產品的規格 (Specification)，屬於「生產什麼」。

Although "family rooms" and "disabled rooms" are mentioned, which sounds like target groups, in Economics, "for whom to produce" focuses on the distribution mechanism (e.g., highest bidder, rationing, lucky draw). The question does not mention how these rooms are allocated. Therefore, the room types here refer more to the product specifications, falling under "what to produce".

官方答案備註 (Remark on Official Answer)

此題的官方答案為 D (生產什麼及為誰生產)。

雖然根據嚴謹的經濟學定義，本題理應選 C，但考評局 (HKEAA) 在此題的邏輯傾向於認為：列出「傷健人士房間」即暗示了特定的服務對象，因此歸類為「為誰生產」。

The official answer is D. While C is theoretically more accurate, the HKEAA likely considers the mention of "disabled rooms" as implying a specific target audience, thus categorizing it under "for whom to produce". Students should be aware of this discrepancy.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

關鍵字 (Pivot Points)

  • "改建為旅舍" (Turned into a hostel):
    決定了產品種類 \(\rightarrow\) 生產什麼 (What)
  • "改建" (Converted/Revitalized):
    決定了使用舊樓翻新的方法 \(\rightarrow\) 怎樣生產 (How)

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes)

  • 誤解「為誰生產」 (Misinterpreting "For whom"):
    見到「某類人」（如家庭、傷健人士）不一定等於「為誰生產」。除非題目涉及分配機制（如誰付錢、誰有權獲得），否則這通常只是產品設計的一部分。
