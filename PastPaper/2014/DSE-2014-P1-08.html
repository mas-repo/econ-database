HKDSE Economics Concept Guide

課題 (Topic): 商業擁有權形式 (Forms of Business Ownership)

Topic: Forms of Business Ownership

難度 (Difficulty): ★★☆☆☆

獨資經營 Sole Proprietorship 合夥經營 Partnership 決策靈活性 Flexibility 延續性 Continuity

2. 題目 (The Question)

下列哪項有關合夥經營與獨資經營的陳述是正確的？

Which of the following statements about partnership and sole proprietorship is correct?

A. 與獨資經營相比，合夥經營的持續性較佳。

A. Partnership has better continuity than sole proprietorship.

B. 與獨資經營相比，合夥經營在決策上靈活性較低。

B. Partnership has less flexibility in decision making than sole proprietorship.

C. 與獨資經營相比，合夥經營有更多資本。

C. Partnership has more capital than sole proprietorship.

D. 與獨資經營相比，合夥經營享有更多規模經濟。

D. Partnership enjoys more economies of scale than sole proprietorship.

3. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解答這類題目，我們必須清楚比較獨資經營（由一人擁有及控制）與合夥經營（由兩名或以上合夥人共同擁有）的特徵。

To solve this type of question, we must clearly compare the characteristics of a sole proprietorship (owned and controlled by one person) and a partnership (jointly owned by two or more partners).

  • 延續性 (Continuity): 兩者均沒有獨立的法定地位。如果擁有者破產、死亡或退出，企業便會解散（缺乏延續性）。

    Continuity: Neither has a separate legal entity. If the owner(s) go bankrupt, die, or withdraw, the business is dissolved (lacks continuity).

  • 決策靈活性 (Flexibility in Decision Making): 獨資經營者可以自行作出所有決定，速度極快；合夥經營則需要合夥人之間互相協商，靈活性較低。

    Flexibility in Decision Making: A sole proprietor can make all decisions alone quickly; partners need to consult each other, leading to lower flexibility.

在經濟學中，企業營運的基本目標是利潤極大化。無論是獨資還是合夥，其利潤的計算概念如下：

In economics, the fundamental goal of a firm's operation is profit maximization. Whether it is a sole proprietorship or a partnership, the conceptual calculation of profit is as follows:

$$ \pi = TR - TC $$

其中 \( \pi \) 代表利潤 (Profit)，\( TR \) 代表總收入 (Total Revenue)，\( TC \) 代表總成本 (Total Cost)。在獨資經營中，擁有者獨佔所有 \( \pi \)；而在合夥經營中，\( \pi \) 需按合夥協議在各合夥人之間分配。

Where \( \pi \) represents Profit, \( TR \) represents Total Revenue, and \( TC \) represents Total Cost. In a sole proprietorship, the owner claims all \( \pi \); in a partnership, \( \pi \) is distributed among partners according to the partnership agreement.

4. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案：選項 B (Option B)

Correct Answer: Option B

選項 A 分析：錯誤。
合夥經營與獨資經營同樣缺乏延續性。在一般合夥企業中，任何一位合夥人離世或破產，該合夥經營在法律上便須解散。

Analysis of Option A: Incorrect.
Both partnership and sole proprietorship lack continuity. In a general partnership, if any partner dies or goes bankrupt, the partnership must be legally dissolved.

選項 B 分析：正確。
獨資經營的東主可以一人獨斷獨行，對市場變化作出迅速反應。相反，合夥經營在作出重大商業決定前，合夥人之間需要開會討論及尋求共識，這無可避免地降低了決策的靈活性和效率。

Analysis of Option B: Correct.
A sole proprietor can make decisions independently and react quickly to market changes. Conversely, in a partnership, partners must discuss and reach a consensus before making major business decisions, which inevitably reduces the flexibility and efficiency of decision-making.

選項 C 分析：錯誤。
這是一個常見的陷阱！雖然合夥經營集資的能力 (capacity to raise capital) 通常比獨資經營強（因為有更多人可以注資或向銀行借貸），但我們不能絕對地說一間合夥企業的資本一定多於一間獨資企業。例如，李嘉誠個人的獨資企業，其資本絕對遠多於兩名普通大學生合資開設的網店。

Analysis of Option C: Incorrect.
This is a common trap! Although a partnership's capacity to raise capital is generally stronger than a sole proprietorship (because more people can inject funds or borrow from banks), we cannot state absolutely that a partnership always has more capital. For example, a sole proprietorship owned by a billionaire will have far more capital than a partnership formed by two university students.

選項 D 分析：錯誤。
規模經濟 (Economies of scale) 取決於企業的生產規模和產量，而不是其擁有權形式。一間龐大的獨資企業可以享有極大的規模經濟，而一間小型的合夥企業則未必能夠享有。

Analysis of Option D: Incorrect.
Economies of scale depend on the firm's scale of production and output, not its form of ownership. A massive sole proprietorship can enjoy immense economies of scale, while a small partnership might not.

5. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

拆解步驟：

Steps to approach:

  1. 辨清考點： 題目要求比較兩種最基礎的企業擁有權形式。
  2. Identify the core topic: The question asks to compare the two most basic forms of business ownership.
  3. 尋找絕對字眼 (Pivot Point)： 在經濟學多項選擇題中，像「有更多 (has more)」這類絕對的陳述往往是陷阱。選項 C 和 D 都犯了這個毛病。它們混淆了「可能性/能力」與「絕對事實」。
  4. Spot the absolute terms (Pivot Point): In Econ MCQs, absolute statements like "has more" are often traps. Options C and D fall into this category. They confuse "potential/capacity" with "absolute fact".
  5. 邏輯驗證： 測試剩下的選項。A 選項關於延續性，事實上兩者皆無。B 選項關於決策，人多口雜自然決策慢，這是一個必然的邏輯結果。因此 B 必定正確。
  6. Logical verification: Test the remaining options. Option A is about continuity, which neither has. Option B is about decision-making; more people logically means slower decisions. Hence, B must be correct.

6. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

⚠️ 陷阱 1：混淆「集資能力較高」與「資本較多」

⚠️ Trap 1: Confusing "higher capacity to raise capital" with "has more capital"

很多同學會秒選 C。記住，合夥經營的集資渠道的確較廣（上限20人），但實際的資本金額取決於擁有者的財力，而不是企業形式。這是一個經典的 DSE 陷阱！

Many students immediately choose C. Remember, a partnership's channels for raising capital are indeed broader (up to 20 people), but the actual amount of capital depends on the owners' wealth, not the form of ownership. This is a classic DSE trap!

⚠️ 陷阱 2：誤以為合夥經營有獨立法定地位

⚠️ Trap 2: Mistakenly believing partnerships have a separate legal entity

只有有限公司 (Limited Companies)（私人及公眾）才擁有獨立的法定地位及良好的延續性。獨資和一般合夥企業的東主與企業在法律上被視為一體。

Only Limited Companies (private and public) possess a separate legal entity and good continuity. In sole proprietorships and general partnerships, the owners and the business are legally considered the same entity.

7. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q1: 老師，如果選項 C 改為「與獨資經營相比，合夥經營通常有較廣的集資渠道」，這樣會是對的嗎？

Q1: Teacher, if Option C was changed to "Partnership generally has wider channels to raise capital than sole proprietorship", would it be correct?

A1: 是的！如果字眼改為「集資渠道較廣 (wider sources of capital)」或「集資能力較強 (higher ability to raise capital)」，這句陳述就是正確的，因為合夥經營可以從多個合夥人中籌集資金，而獨資只有一個人。

A1: Yes! If the wording was changed to "wider sources of capital" or "higher ability to raise capital", the statement would be correct because a partnership can raise funds from multiple partners, whereas a sole proprietorship relies on just one person.

Q2: 甚麼情況下企業才會享有規模經濟 (Economies of Scale)?

Q2: Under what circumstances does a firm enjoy economies of scale?

A2: 當一間企業的產量增加，導致其平均成本 (Average Cost) 下降時，它就享有規模經濟。這取決於企業營運的規模大小（例如能否大量採購獲得折扣、能否實行分工），而與它是獨資、合夥還是有限公司無直接關係。

A2: A firm enjoys economies of scale when an increase in its output leads to a decrease in its Average Cost. This depends on the size of the firm's operations (e.g., ability to get bulk purchase discounts, division of labor), and has no direct relationship with whether it is a sole proprietorship, partnership, or limited company.

8. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

💡 速解技巧：消去法 (Elimination Method) 配合「找碴法」

💡 Shortcut: Elimination Method combined with "Fault-finding"

在處理比較兩事物的題目時，特別留意句子中的「有更多 (has more)」、「享有較高 (enjoys higher)」等字眼。如果該特徵不是該制度必然導致的結果，請毫不猶豫地刪除該選項（如本題的 C 和 D）。然後在剩下的選項中找一個符合常理的邏輯（人多 = 決策慢），就能在 15 秒內鎖定答案 B。

When dealing with questions comparing two things, pay special attention to words like "has more" or "enjoys higher". If that characteristic is not an inevitable result of the system, eliminate the option without hesitation (like C and D here). Then find the logical statement among the remaining ones (more people = slower decisions), and you can lock in Answer B in under 15 seconds.
