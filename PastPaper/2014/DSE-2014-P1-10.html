HKDSE Economics Multiple Choice Analysis

Topic: Stages of Production (生產階段) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts: Primary Production, Secondary Production, Tertiary Production, Producer Goods, Raw Materials

1. The Question (題目)

下圖顯示三個生產級別的關係。E 和 F分別代表什麼？
The following graph shows the relationship of the three stages of production. What do E and F stand for respectively?
[Graph Description: A diagram with three boxes: Primary Production (初級生產) at the top, Secondary Production (次級生產) at the bottom left, and Tertiary Production (三級生產) at the bottom right. There are pairs of arrows flowing between each box. Arrow E points from Secondary Production UP to Primary Production. Arrow F points from Primary Production DOWN to Tertiary Production.]
  • A. 消費者物品；原料
    A. consumer goods; raw materials
  • B. 生產者物品；服務
    B. producer goods; services
  • C. 原料；服務
    C. raw materials; services
  • D. 生產者物品；原料
    D. producer goods; raw materials

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

生產階段的互賴關係 (Interdependence of Stages of Production):
這三個生產階段並不是獨立運作的，而是互相依賴的。
These three stages of production do not operate independently; they rely on each other to function.
  • 初級生產 (Primary Production): 開採天然資源（如農業、漁業、採礦業）。它為次級和三級生產提供原料 (Raw materials)。
    Extracting natural resources (e.g., agriculture, fishing, mining). It provides raw materials to secondary and tertiary sectors.
  • 次級生產 (Secondary Production): 將原料加工為半製成品或製成品。它為初級和三級生產提供生產者物品 (Producer goods)（如機器、工具）或為消費者提供消費者物品。
    Processing raw materials into semi-finished or finished products. It provides producer goods (e.g., machinery, tools) to the primary and tertiary sectors, or consumer goods to end-users.
  • 三級生產 (Tertiary Production): 提供服務（如零售、銀行、物流）。它為初級和次級生產提供服務 (Services) 支援。
    Providing services (e.g., retail, banking, logistics). It provides service support to the primary and secondary sectors.
附加價值 (Value Added): 在每個生產階段中，生產者都會為物品增加價值。這在計算本地生產總值 (GDP) 時尤為重要。
Value Added: In each stage of production, producers add value to the goods. This is particularly important when calculating Gross Domestic Product (GDP).
$$\text{Value Added} = \text{Value of Output} - \text{Value of Intermediate Consumption}$$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是：Option D
The correct answer is: Option D
分析 E (次級生產 $\rightarrow$ 初級生產):
箭頭 E 由「次級生產」指向「初級生產」。次級生產者（如工廠）會製造工具、機器、肥料或漁船，賣給初級生產者（如農夫、漁民）以協助他們生產。這些用作輔助生產的製成品，在經濟學上稱為生產者物品 (Producer goods) 或資本物品。
Analyzing E (Secondary $\rightarrow$ Primary):
Arrow E points from "Secondary Production" to "Primary Production". Secondary producers (e.g., factories) manufacture tools, machinery, fertilizers, or fishing boats and sell them to primary producers (e.g., farmers, fishermen) to assist in their production. These manufactured goods used to aid further production are economically classified as producer goods or capital goods.
分析 F (初級生產 $\rightarrow$ 三級生產):
箭頭 F 由「初級生產」指向「三級生產」。初級生產者（如農場、漁場）會將開採出來的未加工資源，直接提供給三級生產者（如超級市場、餐廳）。對於餐廳或超市而言，這些新鮮食材或貨品是他們提供服務和產品的原料 (Raw materials)。
Analyzing F (Primary $\rightarrow$ Tertiary):
Arrow F points from "Primary Production" to "Tertiary Production". Primary producers (e.g., farms, fisheries) provide extracted, unprocessed resources directly to tertiary producers (e.g., supermarkets, restaurants). For a restaurant or supermarket, these fresh ingredients or goods act as raw materials for the services and products they provide.
為何其他選項錯誤？ (Why are other options wrong?)
  • A is wrong: E is not a consumer good. Primary producers use these tools for business production, not personal consumption.
  • B is wrong: F is not a service. The primary sector deals with tangible natural resources, not intangible services. It is the tertiary sector that provides services to the primary sector.
  • C is wrong: E is not raw materials. Raw materials flow from Primary to Secondary, not the other way around.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解答這類題目的「關鍵點 (Pivot Point)」在於準確判斷箭頭的方向。
The "Pivot Point" to solving this type of question is accurately identifying the direction of the arrows.
  1. 定位起點與終點：看清楚箭頭的頭和尾。E的箭頭由次級指向初級；F的箭頭由初級指向三級。
    Locate Origin and Destination: Look closely at the arrowhead and tail. Arrow E goes from Secondary to Primary; Arrow F goes from Primary to Tertiary.
  2. 代入實質例子：不要死記硬背，嘗試代入真實世界的例子。次級（機器製造廠）提供給初級（農夫）的東西是什麼？是拖拉機（生產者物品）。
    Substitute Real Examples: Don't just memorize; try to plug in real-world examples. What does the secondary sector (machinery factory) provide to the primary sector (farmer)? A tractor (Producer goods).

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱 1：忽略箭頭方向
很多同學一看到「初級」和「次級」，就立刻聯想到「原料 (Raw materials)」，因為初級生產最常提供的就是原料。但他們忽略了箭頭 E 是反方向的（次級 $\rightarrow$ 初級），因此選錯了C。
Trap 1: Ignoring Arrow Direction
Many students immediately associate "Primary" and "Secondary" with "Raw materials" because that is the most common output of the primary sector. However, they ignore that Arrow E is in the reverse direction (Secondary $\rightarrow$ Primary), leading them to incorrectly choose option C.
陷阱 2：混淆消費者物品與生產者物品
有些同學以為製成品必定是消費者物品。緊記：物品的分類取決於其用途。農夫購買鋤頭是用來賺取利潤和協助生產的，因此對農夫而言，鋤頭是生產者物品，而非消費者物品。
Trap 2: Confusing Consumer Goods and Producer Goods
Some students assume all finished manufactured goods are consumer goods. Remember: The classification of a good depends on its usage. A farmer buys a hoe to make a profit and assist in production, so to the farmer, it is a producer good, not a consumer good.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 初級生產直接提供貨品給三級生產，難道不需要經過次級生產（加工）嗎？
Q: Can the primary sector directly provide goods to the tertiary sector without going through the secondary (manufacturing) sector?
A: 可以的！ 雖然很多物品需要加工（例如：小麥 $\rightarrow$ 麵粉廠 $\rightarrow$ 麵包店），但在現實中，很多初級生產的物品是不需深度加工就能直接送到三級生產者的。例如：漁民捕獲鮮魚後，直接賣給海鮮酒家（三級生產者）烹調給客人吃；或者農夫把新鮮蔬菜直接批發給超級市場作零售。
A: Yes, absolutely! While many goods require processing (e.g., Wheat $\rightarrow$ Flour Mill $\rightarrow$ Bakery), in reality, many primary products are sent directly to tertiary producers without deep processing. For example, a fisherman catching fresh fish and selling it directly to a seafood restaurant (tertiary) to be cooked for customers; or farmers wholesaling fresh vegetables directly to supermarkets for retail.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法：邏輯排除法 (Process of Elimination)
Shortcut: Process of Elimination
  • 看到 F 是由「初級生產」出發。初級生產只能產出從大自然開採的資源（原料）。它絕不可能產出「服務 (Services)」。因此，選項 B 和 C 的 F 是「服務」，可以立即被剔除。
    Look at F, which originates from "Primary Production". The primary sector can only output resources extracted from nature (raw materials). It is impossible for it to output "Services". Therefore, options B and C, which list F as "services", can be eliminated immediately.
  • 剩下 A 和 D。再看 E，由「次級生產」流向「初級生產」。初級生產者（如農場）購買這些物品是為了做生意和生產，絕不會是為了個人享受（消費者物品）。因此剔除 A。答案秒殺為 D。
    You are left with A and D. Look at E, which flows from "Secondary" to "Primary". Primary producers (like farms) buy these goods for business and production purposes, never for personal enjoyment (consumer goods). Thus, eliminate A. The answer is instantly D.
