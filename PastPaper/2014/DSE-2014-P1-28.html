Topic / 課題: Macroeconomics: National Income & Aggregate Demand (總體經濟學：國民收入與總需求)

Difficulty / 難度: ★★★☆☆ (Moderate / 中等)

Key Concepts / 核心概念: Transfer Payments (轉移支付), Private Consumption Expenditure (私人消費支出), Aggregate Demand (總需求)

1. The Question (題目)

香港政府增設長者生活津貼令本地生產總值內的 __________ 增加，並引致總需求曲線向 __________ 移。

The Old Age Living Allowance (長者生活津貼) introduced by the Hong Kong government increases the _______ in GDP and results in a _______ shift of the aggregate demand curve.

  • A. 政府消費支出 …… 右
    A. government consumption expenditure …… rightward
  • B. 政府消費支出 …… 左
    B. government consumption expenditure …… leftward
  • C. 私人消費支出 …… 右
    C. private consumption expenditure …… rightward
  • D. 私人消費支出 …… 左
    D. private consumption expenditure …… leftward

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

轉移支付 (Transfer Payments): 這是指政府單方面將財富轉移給市民，過程中政府並沒有獲得任何實質的物品或服務作為回報（例如：長者津貼、失業救濟金）。因此，轉移支付不會直接計入政府消費支出 \(G\) 中。

Transfer Payments: These are unilateral transfers of wealth from the government to citizens, where the government receives no goods or services in return (e.g., elderly allowances, unemployment benefits). Therefore, transfer payments are not directly counted in Government Consumption Expenditure \(G\).

本地生產總值 (GDP) 與 總需求 (Aggregate Demand, AD): 根據支出計算法，GDP 及 AD 的組成部分相同：

GDP and Aggregate Demand (AD): According to the expenditure approach, the components of GDP and AD are the same:

$$ AD = C + I + G + NX $$

其中 \(C\) 是私人消費支出，\(I\) 是投資開支，\(G\) 是政府消費支出，\(NX\) 是淨出口。當長者收到津貼後，他們的可支配收入增加，從而增加日常消費。這些消費會被計入 私人消費支出 \(C\)。

Where \(C\) is Private Consumption Expenditure, \(I\) is Investment Expenditure, \(G\) is Government Consumption Expenditure, and \(NX\) is Net Exports. When the elderly receive the allowance, their disposable income increases, leading to more daily consumption. This spending is counted as Private Consumption Expenditure \(C\).

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是 / Correct Option: C

分析選項 A & B (Analysis of Options A & B): 錯誤 (Incorrect)
長者生活津貼是一種「轉移支付」。由於政府發放這筆津貼時並沒有換取任何當期的物品或服務，這筆錢本身不屬於政府消費支出 \(G\)。因此，GDP 內的政府消費支出並不會因為增設津貼而直接增加。

The Old Age Living Allowance is a "transfer payment". Since the government does not receive any current goods or services in exchange for giving out this allowance, the money itself is not a government consumption expenditure \(G\). Therefore, the government consumption expenditure in GDP does not directly increase because of the allowance.

分析選項 C (Analysis of Option C): 正確 (Correct)
當長者獲得津貼，他們的購買力（可支配收入）上升，他們會將這筆錢用於購買食物、日用品等。這會導致「私人消費支出 \(C\)」增加。根據公式 \(AD = C + I + G + NX\)，當 \(C\) 增加時，整體總需求 \(AD\) 會上升，在圖表上顯示為總需求曲線向右移動。

When the elderly receive the allowance, their purchasing power (disposable income) rises, and they will spend this money on food, daily necessities, etc. This leads to an increase in "Private Consumption Expenditure \(C\)". According to the formula \(AD = C + I + G + NX\), when \(C\) increases, the overall Aggregate Demand \(AD\) rises, which is represented by a rightward shift of the AD curve on a graph.

分析選項 D (Analysis of Option D): 錯誤 (Incorrect)
雖然前半部分「私人消費支出」正確，但消費增加代表總需求上升，總需求曲線應該向右移，而不是向左移（向左移代表需求減少）。

Although the first half "private consumption expenditure" is correct, an increase in consumption means an increase in aggregate demand. The aggregate demand curve should shift rightward, not leftward (a leftward shift represents a decrease in demand).

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目考驗同學是否能看穿政策背後的實際經濟活動。拆解步驟如下：

This type of question tests whether students can see through the actual economic activity behind a policy. The decomposition steps are as follows:

  1. 尋找關鍵字 (Find the Pivot Point): 題目中的「長者生活津貼」。你要立刻聯想到這是 轉移支付 (Transfer Payment)。
    "Old Age Living Allowance" in the question. You should immediately associate this with a Transfer Payment.
  2. 判定支出歸屬 (Determine the Expenditure Category): 轉移支付 \(\neq\) 政府消費。這筆錢只有在市民「花出去」買東西時，才會作為 私人消費 \(C\) 計入 GDP。
    Transfer payment \(\neq\) Government consumption. This money is only counted in GDP as Private Consumption \(C\) when citizens "spend it" to buy things.
  3. 判斷曲線移動方向 (Determine Curve Shift Direction): 支出增加代表需求擴張，任何組成的擴張 (\(C\uparrow, I\uparrow, G\uparrow, NX\uparrow\)) 都會令 AD 曲線向右移。
    An increase in expenditure represents an expansion in demand. Any expansion in the components (\(C\uparrow, I\uparrow, G\uparrow, NX\uparrow\)) will shift the AD curve to the right.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ 陷阱：見到「政府」就選「政府消費」
很多同學一看到題目有「香港政府增設...」，就直覺認為這是政府支出 \(G\)。考試局非常喜歡利用這一點來設計陷阱！記住，政府派錢（津貼、消費券、綜援）本身不創造產出，必須等市民拿去消費，才會變成私人消費支出 \(C\)。

⚠️ Trap: Seeing "Government" and choosing "Government Consumption"
Many students see "Hong Kong government introduced..." and intuitively think it's government expenditure \(G\). The examination authority loves to use this to set traps! Remember, the government giving out money (allowances, consumption vouchers, CSSA) does not create output itself. It only becomes Private Consumption Expenditure \(C\) when citizens spend it.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼政府發放津貼不算是政府消費支出 \(G\)？錢明明是從政府口袋出去的啊？

Q: Why isn't the government allowance considered government consumption expenditure \(G\)? The money clearly comes from the government's pocket!

A: 經濟學上的「政府消費支出」要求政府必須在市場上「買」到當期的物品或服務（例如：買警車、支付公務員薪水）。派發津貼只是一種財富轉移（從納稅人轉移到長者），過程中沒有新的物品被生產出來，所以不能計入 \(G\)。

A: In economics, "government consumption expenditure" requires the government to "buy" current goods or services in the market (e.g., buying police cars, paying civil servants' salaries). Distributing allowances is merely a wealth transfer (from taxpayers to the elderly). No new goods are produced in this process, so it cannot be counted in \(G\).

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

💡 兩秒刪除法 (The 2-Second Elimination Method):

1. 看到「津貼 (Allowance)」、「援助 (Assistance)」或「派錢 (Handout)」，立刻刪去所有包含「政府消費支出」的選項（秒殺 A 和 B）。

2. 「增設 (Increases/Introduces)」意味著整體經濟注入了更多資金，帶來正面刺激，所以 AD 一定是向「右」移。直接選 C！

1. When you see "Allowance", "Assistance", or "Handouts", immediately eliminate all options containing "Government consumption expenditure" (Instantly kills A and B).

2. "Introduces/Increases" means more money is injected into the economy, bringing a positive stimulus, so AD must shift to the "right". Choose C directly!
