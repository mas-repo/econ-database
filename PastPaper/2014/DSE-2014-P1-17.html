2. 概念重溫 Concept Review

寡頭壟斷 (Oligopoly) 的核心特徵：
寡頭壟斷是指市場由少數幾個大賣家主導的市場結構。雖然市場上可能還有其他小賣家，但主要銷售量集中在幾個巨頭手中。經濟學上，我們通常會用「市場集中度」(Market Concentration) 來量度。
Core Features of Oligopoly:
An oligopoly is a market structure dominated by a few large sellers. Although there may be other small sellers in the market, the majority of the total sales are concentrated in the hands of a few giants. In economics, we often measure this using "Market Concentration".
為了量度市場的集中程度，我們會計算前 \(n\) 大企業的市場份額總和，稱為 集中比率 (Concentration Ratio, \(CR_n\))：
To measure the degree of market concentration, we calculate the sum of the market shares of the top \(n\) largest firms, known as the Concentration Ratio (\(CR_n\)):
$$ CR_n = S_1 + S_2 + \dots + S_n = \sum_{i=1}^{n} S_i $$
這裡的 \(S_i\) 代表第 \(i\) 大企業的市場份額 (Market Share)。如果最大的兩至三間公司已經佔據了市場的絕大部份份額（例如 \(CR_3\) 超過 70% 或 80%），這就是一個典型的寡頭壟斷市場。
Here, \(S_i\) represents the market share of the \(i\)-th largest firm. If the top two or three companies already occupy the vast majority of the market share (e.g., \(CR_3\) exceeds 70% or 80%), this is a typical oligopolistic market.

3. 答案與詳細分析 The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Option: Option B
選項 A 錯誤 (Incorrect):
雖然「賣家的營銷策略互相影響」(Interdependence) 的確是寡頭壟斷的重要特徵，但這個結論無法從題目提供的表格中得出。表格只顯示了數字（市場份額），並沒有顯示它們之間如何進行價格戰或廣告戰。
While "sellers are interdependent in their market strategies" is indeed a key feature of an oligopoly, this conclusion cannot be drawn from the table provided. The table only shows numbers (market shares) and does not display how they engage in price wars or advertising wars.
選項 B 正確 (Correct):
從表格數據可見，首兩大賣家（惠康和百佳）的市場份額合共為 \(39.8\% + 33.1\% = 72.9\%\)。若加上第三大賣家（華潤萬家），則高達 \(80.7\%\)。這完美地展示了「幾位主要賣家操控了市場的總銷售量」，符合表格所能證明的數據現象。
Looking at the data in the table, the combined market share of the top two sellers (Wellcome and Parknshop) is \(39.8\% + 33.1\% = 72.9\%\). If we add the third largest seller (Vanguard), it reaches an overwhelming \(80.7\%\). This perfectly demonstrates that "a few major sellers are in control of the total sales", which matches the data phenomenon proven by the table.
選項 C & D 錯誤 (Incorrect):
壟斷性競爭 (Monopolistic Competition) 的首要條件是市場上有許多賣家 (many sellers)，且沒有單一或少數賣家能夠主導市場。表格中少數幾間公司佔據超過 80% 的市場，直接推翻了壟斷性競爭的可能性。
The primary condition for Monopolistic Competition is having many sellers in the market, with no single or few sellers dominating. The fact that a few companies in the table occupy over 80% of the market directly refutes the possibility of monopolistic competition.

4. 題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

解答這題的 關鍵支點 (Pivot Point) 在於題目的這句話：「我們可以由上表得出結論如下」。

這是一條典型的「資料回應題」(Data-based Question)。你需要區分兩件事：
  1. 一個市場結構的客觀定義與特徵（例如：寡頭壟斷有互相牽制）。
  2. 這份數據能夠證明的特徵（這份數據只有百分比，只能證明「集中度高」）。
很多學生看到 A 選項的描述完全正確就立刻選擇，忘記了 A 選項的描述並沒有出現在表格之中。
The Pivot Point to solving this question lies in this phrase from the stem: "From the above table, we can conclude that...".

This is a classic "Data-based Question". You must distinguish between two things:
  1. The objective definition and features of a market structure (e.g., interdependence in oligopoly).
  2. The features that this specific data can prove (this data only has percentages, so it can only prove "high concentration").
Many students immediately choose Option A because the description itself is factually correct, forgetting that the behavior described in Option A is not shown in the table.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

陷阱：張冠李戴 (Mismatching Evidence)
在DSE經濟科中，考評局非常喜歡設定這種陷阱。選項前半部分的市場結構分類是正確的（寡頭壟斷），後半部分的解釋也是該市場的真實特徵（營銷互相影響），但後半部分無法由題目的資料推導出來。學生必須確保「原因 (Because...)」與「提供的資料 (Provided Data)」有直接的邏輯關聯。
Trap: Mismatching Evidence
In HKDSE Economics, the HKEAA loves to set this type of trap. The first half of the option correctly identifies the market structure (Oligopoly), and the second half states a true feature of that market (interdependent strategies), but the second half cannot be deduced from the provided material. Students must ensure the "Because..." part has a direct logical connection to the "Provided Data".

6. 學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

Q: 表格中明明有「其他 19.3%」，代表市場上有很多小賣家。有「許多賣家」不就是壟斷性競爭 (Monopolistic Competition) 嗎？為什麼不能選 D？
Q: The table clearly shows "Others 19.3%", meaning there are many small sellers. Doesn't having "many sellers" mean it's Monopolistic Competition? Why can't we choose D?
A: 雖然市場上可能同時存在很多小賣家（「其他」），但判斷市場結構的關鍵在於市場主導權 (Market Dominance)。在壟斷性競爭中，每一間公司的市場份額都很小，沒有人能單獨操控大局。但這題中，單是惠康和百佳兩家公司就拿走了超過七成的銷售額。當少數幾家公司佔據了絕對多數的市場份額時，這個市場就是寡頭壟斷。
A: While many small sellers might exist simultaneously (the "Others"), the key to determining market structure is Market Dominance. In monopolistic competition, every firm has a very small market share, and no one can dominate the market. But in this question, Wellcome and Parknshop alone take up over 70% of the sales. When a few firms hold an absolute majority of the market share, the market is an oligopoly.

7. 考試技巧與速解 Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

速解法 (Elimination Shortcut)：
1. 看到表格全是「市場份額 (%)」數字 \(\rightarrow\) 立即聯想到「集中度 / 銷售控制權」。
2. 掃視選項後半部分：
- A 講策略影響 (文字上無證據，刪去)
- B 講銷售量控制 (與 % 吻合，保留)
- C 講異質物品 (文字上無證據，刪去)
- D 講非價格競爭 (文字上無證據，刪去)
3. 5秒內即可鎖定答案為 B！
Elimination Shortcut:
1. See a table full of "Market share (%)" numbers \(\rightarrow\) Immediately associate it with "Concentration / Control of sales".
2. Scan the second half of the options:
- A talks about strategic interdependence (no textual evidence, eliminate).
- B talks about control of sales (matches the %, keep).
- C talks about heterogeneous products (no textual evidence, eliminate).
- D talks about non-price competition (no textual evidence, eliminate).
3. You can lock in answer B within 5 seconds!
