HKDSE Economics Masterclass

Topic: 貨幣數量論 (Quantity Theory of Money) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts: Equation of Exchange (交易方程式), Nominal Output (名義產出), Real Output (實質產出)

題目 / The Question

假設貨幣流通速率維持不變。若中央銀行減少貨幣供應10%，

Suppose the velocity of circulation of money remains constant. If a central bank reduces money supply by 10%,

  1. 物價水平會下跌10%。
    the price level will decrease by 10%.
  2. 實質產出會下跌 10%。
    real output will decrease by 10%.
  3. 名義產出可能下跌少於 10%。
    nominal output may decrease by less than 10%.
  4. 實質產出可能下跌少於 10%。
    real output may decrease by less than 10%.

📚 概念重溫 / Concept Review

解答此題的核心是交易方程式 (Equation of Exchange)。它展示了貨幣供應、流通速率、物價水平和實質產出之間的關係。

The core to solving this question is the Equation of Exchange. It shows the relationship between money supply, velocity of circulation, price level, and real output.

$$ M \times V = P \times Y $$
  • \(M\) = 貨幣供應 (Money Supply)
  • \(V\) = 貨幣流通速率 (Velocity of Circulation)
  • \(P\) = 物價水平 (Price Level)
  • \(Y\) = 實質產出 / 實質 GDP (Real Output / Real GDP)
  • \(P \times Y\) = 名義產出 / 名義 GDP (Nominal Output)

當我們將其轉換為百分比變動 (Percentage Change) 形式時，公式如下：

When converted into percentage change form, the formula is:

$$ \% \Delta M + \% \Delta V = \% \Delta P + \% \Delta Y $$

✅ 答案與詳細分析 / Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 / Correct Option: D

根據題目：\(\% \Delta M = -10\%\)，且 \(V\) 不變（即 \(\% \Delta V = 0\%\)）。代入公式：

According to the question: \(\% \Delta M = -10\%\), and \(V\) is constant (\(\% \Delta V = 0\%\)). Substituting into the formula:

$$ -10\% + 0\% = \% \Delta P + \% \Delta Y $$ $$ \% \Delta (P \times Y) = -10\% $$
A. 錯誤 (Incorrect)

物價水平 (\(P\))「會」(will) 下跌 10% 只有在實質產出 (\(Y\)) 完全不變 (\(\% \Delta Y = 0\)) 的情況下才成立。題目沒有說明經濟處於充分就業狀態，因此 \(Y\) 可能改變。

The price level (\(P\)) "will" decrease by 10% ONLY IF real output (\(Y\)) is constant (\(\% \Delta Y = 0\)). The question does not state the economy is at full employment, so \(Y\) might change.

B. 錯誤 (Incorrect)

同樣道理，實質產出 (\(Y\))「會」(will) 下跌 10% 只有在物價水平 (\(P\)) 完全不變 (\(\% \Delta P = 0\)) 的情況下才成立。這並非必然發生。

Similarly, real output (\(Y\)) "will" decrease by 10% ONLY IF the price level (\(P\)) is constant (\(\% \Delta P = 0\)). This is not guaranteed to happen.

C. 錯誤 (Incorrect)

名義產出是 \(P \times Y\)。由於 \(\% \Delta M + \% \Delta V = -10\%\)，名義產出必定準確地下跌 10%，而不是「可能少於 10%」。

Nominal output is \(P \times Y\). Since \(\% \Delta M + \% \Delta V = -10\%\), nominal output MUST decrease by exactly 10%, not "may decrease by less than 10%".

D. 正確 (Correct)

由於 \(\% \Delta P + \% \Delta Y = -10\%\)，這 10% 的跌幅會由 \(P\) 和 \(Y\) 共同分擔。例如，如果 \(P\) 下跌 4%，那麼 \(Y\) 就會下跌 6%。因此，實質產出 (\(Y\)) 可能下跌少於 10%。

Since \(\% \Delta P + \% \Delta Y = -10\%\), this 10% decrease is shared between \(P\) and \(Y\). For example, if \(P\) drops by 4%, \(Y\) will drop by 6%. Therefore, real output (\(Y\)) may decrease by less than 10%.

🔍 題目拆解 / Problem Decomposition

拆題關鍵 (Pivot Point): 留意題目中的「會」(will) 和「可能」(may)。

Pivot Point: Pay attention to the absolute word "will" versus the conditional word "may".

  • 已知 \(\% \Delta (PY) = -10\%\)。這是一個絕對事實。
  • 因為總跌幅是 10%，除非其中一個變量不變，否則 \(P\) 和 \(Y\) 各自的跌幅一定小於 10%。

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 / Common Mistakes & Traps

陷阱：混淆名義 (Nominal) 與實質 (Real) 變量

很多學生忘記了 \(P \times Y\) 等於「名義產出」。當 \(M\) 下跌 10% 且 \(V\) 不變時，必然是「名義產出」下跌 10%，而不是單一的物價或實質產出。

Trap: Confusing Nominal and Real variables. Many students forget that \(P \times Y\) equals "Nominal Output". When \(M\) drops by 10% and \(V\) is constant, it is the "Nominal Output" that strictly drops by 10%, not just the price level or real output alone.

🙋 學生常見疑問 / Student Q&A

Q: 甚麼情況下選項 A 才會是正確答案？

Q: Under what condition would Option A be the correct answer?

A: 如果題目加上一句「假設經濟處於充分就業水平 (Assuming the economy is at full employment level)」，根據古典學派理論，實質產出 \(Y\) 將維持不變 (\(\% \Delta Y = 0\))。那樣的話，\(\% \Delta P\) 就會完全吸收那 -10% 的變動，選項 A 就會正確。

A: If the question added "Assuming the economy is at full employment level", according to classical theory, real output \(Y\) remains constant (\(\% \Delta Y = 0\)). In that case, \(\% \Delta P\) would fully absorb the -10% change, making Option A correct.

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 / Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

字眼排除法 (Word Elimination Method):

在 DSE 經濟科中，當一個總量（如 \(PY\)）改變時，除非有額外假設，否則我們無法確定單一變量（\(P\) 或 \(Y\)）的確切改變幅度。因此，帶有「會 / 必定」(will / must) 字眼的選項 (A 和 B) 通常是錯誤的。直接將注意力集中在帶有「可能」(may) 的選項 (C 和 D) 上，然後利用公式 \(PY = \text{Nominal Output}\) 快速秒殺 C，選出 D。

In DSE Economics, when a total value (like \(PY\)) changes, unless there are extra assumptions, we cannot determine the exact change of a single variable (\(P\) or \(Y\)). Therefore, options with absolute words like "will/must" (A and B) are usually wrong. Focus on options with "may" (C and D), use the fact that \(PY = \text{Nominal Output}\) to eliminate C, and choose D instantly.
