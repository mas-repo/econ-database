DSE Economics Guide: AD-AS Model

Topic: 總需求與總供應 (Aggregate Demand & Supply) Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆

關鍵概念 (Key Concepts): 總需求 (Aggregate Demand), 出口 (Exports), 短期宏觀經濟均衡 (Short-run Macroeconomic Equilibrium), 就業 (Employment)

1. 題目 (The Question)

若某經濟一個主要貿易夥伴的國民收入下跌，在短期該經濟的就業會 __________ 而物價水平會 __________ 。

If there is a decrease in national income of a major trading partner of an economy, the employment of the economy will _______ and the price level will _______ in the short run.

  • A. 增加 …… 上升
    A. increase …… increase
  • B. 增加 …… 下跌
    B. increase …… decrease
  • C. 減少 …… 上升
    C. decrease …… increase
  • D. 減少 …… 下跌
    D. decrease …… decrease

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

1. 總需求 (Aggregate Demand, AD): 總需求是經濟體系內對本地生產物品和服務的需求總和。其公式為：

1. Aggregate Demand (AD): The total demand for domestically produced goods and services in an economy. The formula is:

$$AD = C + I + G + X - M$$

當中 \(X\) 代表出口 (Exports)。當我們貿易夥伴的國民收入下跌時，他們的購買力下降，因此會減少購買我們的產品，導致我們的出口 \(X\) 減少。這會使總需求曲線 (AD curve) 向左移。

Where \(X\) represents Exports. When the national income of our trading partner decreases, their purchasing power drops, leading them to buy fewer of our goods. This causes our exports \(X\) to fall, shifting the Aggregate Demand (AD) curve to the left.

2. 就業與實質產出的關係 (Link between Employment and Real Output): 短期內，當總需求 (AD) 減少，均衡實質產出 (Real GDP, \(Y\)) 會下降。為了生產較少的物品，廠商對勞工的需求會減少，導致就業 (Employment) 下跌。

2. Link between Employment and Real Output: In the short run, when AD decreases, the equilibrium real output (Real GDP, \(Y\)) falls. To produce fewer goods, firms demand less labour, causing employment to drop.

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案：Option D

選項 D 解析 (正確): 因為貿易夥伴收入減少，本國出口 (\(X\)) 減少，導致總需求 (AD) 下降（AD 曲線向左移）。在短期宏觀經濟模型中，AD 左移會導致物價水平 (\(P\)) 下跌，以及實質產出 (\(Y\)) 下降。實質產出下降意味著廠商減少生產，自然會解僱工人或減少聘請，令就業人數減少。因此，就業會「減少」，物價水平會「下跌」。

Option D Analysis (Correct): Because the trading partner's income falls, our exports (\(X\)) decrease, leading to a drop in Aggregate Demand (AD shifts left). In the short-run macroeconomic model, a leftward shift of AD leads to a lower general price level (\(P\)) and a lower real output (\(Y\)). A fall in real output means firms produce less and thus require fewer workers, leading to a decrease in employment. Therefore, employment will "decrease" and the price level will "decrease".

選項 A 解析 (錯誤): 只有在總需求 (AD) 增加時，物價水平和就業才會雙雙上升。此題中 AD 是減少的。

Option A Analysis (Incorrect): Both price level and employment will increase only if Aggregate Demand (AD) increases. In this question, AD decreases.

選項 B 解析 (錯誤): 如果物價下跌但就業增加，這通常代表短期總供應 (SRAS) 增加（向右移）。但本題的衝擊來自外部需求（出口），影響的是 AD 而非 SRAS。

Option B Analysis (Incorrect): If the price level falls but employment increases, this typically indicates an increase in Short-Run Aggregate Supply (SRAS shifts right). However, the shock in this question comes from external demand (exports), affecting AD, not SRAS.

選項 C 解析 (錯誤): 如果就業減少（產出下降）但物價上升，這代表發生了「滯脹」(Stagflation)，是由短期總供應 (SRAS) 減少引起的。這與本題的需求衝擊不符。

Option C Analysis (Incorrect): If employment decreases (output falls) but the price level rises, this indicates "stagflation", which is caused by a decrease in Short-Run Aggregate Supply (SRAS). This contradicts the demand-side shock in this question.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

拆題樞紐 (The Pivot Point): 看到「主要貿易夥伴的國民收入」，你必須立刻聯想到本國的「出口」(Exports)。貿易夥伴就是我們的顧客，顧客沒錢，自然買少一點我們的貨品。

The Pivot Point: When you see "national income of a major trading partner", you must immediately link it to our domestic "Exports". Trading partners are our customers; when customers have less money, they naturally buy fewer of our goods.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

⚠️ 陷阱一：混淆「貿易夥伴的收入」與「本國的收入」
很多同學會誤以為是本國國民收入下跌，然後開始推論消費 \(C\) 和進口 \(M\) 的改變。請仔細看題目，是「貿易夥伴」的收入跌，所以直接受打擊的是我們的出口 \(X\)。

⚠️ Trap 1: Confusing "Trading Partner's Income" with "Domestic Income"
Many students mistakenly think domestic national income falls and start deducing changes in Consumption \(C\) and Imports \(M\). Read carefully: it's the "trading partner's" income that falls, so the direct hit is on our exports \(X\).

⚠️ 陷阱二：忘記「就業」與「實質產出」的同向關係
題目問「就業」(Employment) 而不是直接問產出 \(Y\)。同學必須記得：\(Y\) 升 = 廠商多請人 = 就業升；\(Y\) 跌 = 廠商裁員 = 就業跌。

⚠️ Trap 2: Forgetting the positive relationship between "Employment" and "Real Output"
The question asks about "Employment" rather than directly about output \(Y\). You must remember: \(Y\) rises = firms hire more = employment rises; \(Y\) falls = firms lay off workers = employment falls.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼這件事不會影響短期總供應 (SRAS)？ / Why doesn't this event affect Short-Run Aggregate Supply (SRAS)?

A: 影響 SRAS 的主要因素是生產成本（例如工資、油價）或生產力。外國人收入減少只是他們「不買」我們的東西（需求改變），並沒有令我們本地廠商的「生產成本」改變。因此，SRAS 曲線在短期內不會移動。

A: The main factors affecting SRAS are production costs (e.g., wages, oil prices) or productivity. A drop in foreigners' income simply means they "don't buy" our goods (a demand change); it does not change the "production costs" for our domestic firms. Therefore, the SRAS curve does not shift in the short run.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

💡 同向移動法則 (Demand Shock Rule)：
在正常的 AD-AS 模型中，如果只有總需求 (AD) 發生移動（不管是向左還是向右），物價水平 (\(P\)) 和實質產出 (\(Y\)) 一定是同方向移動的！
- AD 升 ➔ \(P\) 升，\(Y\) 升 (就業升)
- AD 跌 ➔ \(P\) 跌，\(Y\) 跌 (就業跌)
一看到題目是 AD 減少的衝擊，你可以直接找「雙跌」或「雙升」的選項。本題是負面衝擊，必定是「減少...下跌」，10秒內即可鎖定答案 D！

💡 Demand Shock Rule:
In a standard AD-AS model, if ONLY Aggregate Demand (AD) shifts (left or right), the general price level (\(P\)) and real output (\(Y\)) will ALWAYS move in the same direction!
- AD increases ➔ \(P\) rises, \(Y\) rises (Employment rises)
- AD decreases ➔ \(P\) falls, \(Y\) falls (Employment falls)
Once you identify it's a negative AD shock, you can immediately look for the "double down" option. It must be "decrease...decrease". You can lock in Option D within 10 seconds!
