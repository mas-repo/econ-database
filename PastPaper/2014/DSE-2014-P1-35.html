DSE 經濟學題目分析指南

主題 (Topic): 總需求與總供應模型、宏觀經濟政策 (AD-AS Model & Macroeconomic Policies)

難度 (Difficulty): ★★★☆☆ (3/5)

關鍵字 (Key Concepts): 總供應 (Aggregate Supply), 生產成本 (Cost of Production), 物價水平 (Price Level), 失業率 (Unemployment Rate)

1. 題目 (The Question)

政府可以採用下列哪項政策同時調低物價和失業水平？

  1. 提高貼現率
  2. 增加對中小型企業的補貼
  3. 提高薪俸稅率
  4. 增加社會福利支出

Which of the following policies can the government introduce to reduce the price and unemployment levels at the same time?

  1. to raise the discount rates
  2. to raise the subsidies to small and medium-sized enterprises
  3. to raise the salaries tax rate
  4. to raise the expenditure on social welfare

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

總需求與總供應模型 (The AD-AS Model)

要解答這類題目，我們必須運用「總需求與總供應」(AD-AS) 模型。宏觀經濟的兩個重要指標是物價水平 \(P\) (Price Level) 和實質產出 \(Y\) (Real GDP)。

To answer this type of question, we must use the Aggregate Demand and Aggregate Supply (AD-AS) model. Two important macroeconomic indicators are the Price Level \(P\) and Real GDP \(Y\).

根據奧肯法則 (Okun's Law) 以及 DSE 的基本假設，實質產出 \(Y\) 與失業率是成反比的。當產出 \(Y\) 增加，企業需要聘請更多工人，失業率便會下降。

According to basic DSE assumptions (related to Okun's Law), Real Output \(Y\) is inversely related to the unemployment rate. When output \(Y\) increases, firms hire more workers, and the unemployment rate decreases.

$$ Y \uparrow \implies \text{Unemployment (失業率)} \downarrow $$

因此，題目要求「同時調低物價 (\(P \downarrow\)) 和失業水平 (\(Y \uparrow\))」。在 AD-AS 圖中，唯一能夠同時導致物價下降和產出增加的情況，是短期總供應 (SRAS) 向右移。

Therefore, the question requires "reducing price (\(P \downarrow\)) and unemployment levels (\(Y \uparrow\)) simultaneously". In the AD-AS graph, the ONLY shift that can simultaneously lower the price level and increase output is a rightward shift of the Short-Run Aggregate Supply (SRAS) curve.

  • SRAS 增加的因素： 生產成本下降 (例如：工資下降、原材料價格下降、政府提供補貼)。
  • Factors increasing SRAS: A decrease in the cost of production (e.g., lower wages, lower raw material prices, government subsidies).

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是 (The correct option is): Option B

A (錯誤 Incorrect) 提高貼現率 / to raise the discount rates

這是緊縮性貨幣政策。提高貼現率會導致市場利率上升，從而減少投資 (\(I\)) 和消費 (\(C\))。這會使總需求 (AD) 向左移。結果是物價水平下降，但實質產出減少，導致失業率上升。未能達到同時降低失業率的目標。

This is a contractionary monetary policy. Raising the discount rate increases market interest rates, reducing investment (\(I\)) and consumption (\(C\)). This shifts Aggregate Demand (AD) to the left. As a result, the price level falls, but real output decreases, causing the unemployment rate to rise. It fails the goal of reducing unemployment.

B (正確 Correct) 增加對中小型企業的補貼 / to raise the subsidies to SMEs

政府對企業提供補貼，會直接降低企業的生產成本 (Cost of Production)。這會導致短期總供應 (SRAS) 增加（向右移）。在新的均衡點上，物價水平 (\(P\)) 會下降，而實質產出 (\(Y\)) 會增加，導致失業率下降。完美符合題目要求。

Providing government subsidies directly lowers the firms' cost of production. This leads to an increase (rightward shift) in Short-Run Aggregate Supply (SRAS). At the new equilibrium, the price level (\(P\)) falls, and real output (\(Y\)) increases, leading to a fall in the unemployment rate. This perfectly matches the question's requirement.

C (錯誤 Incorrect) 提高薪俸稅率 / to raise the salaries tax rate

這是緊縮性財政政策。提高薪俸稅會減少市民的可支配收入，從而減少私人消費開支 (\(C\))。這會使總需求 (AD) 向左移。如同選項 A，物價會下降，但失業率會上升。

This is a contractionary fiscal policy. Raising salaries tax reduces citizens' disposable income, thereby reducing private consumption expenditure (\(C\)). This shifts AD to the left. Similar to Option A, the price level falls, but unemployment will rise.

D (錯誤 Incorrect) 增加社會福利支出 / to raise the expenditure on social welfare

這是擴張性財政政策。增加社會福利（轉移支付）會增加低收入人士的可支配收入，刺激消費 (\(C\))。這會使總需求 (AD) 向右移。雖然產出增加會令失業率下降，但物價水平會上升。未能達到降低物價的目標。

This is an expansionary fiscal policy. Increasing social welfare (transfer payments) increases the disposable income of low-income groups, stimulating consumption (\(C\)). This shifts AD to the right. Although increased output lowers unemployment, the price level will rise. It fails the goal of reducing prices.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point):

這道題目的核心在於明白「總需求 (AD)」政策總是帶來取捨 (Trade-off)：

  • 推動 AD \(\implies\) 失業跌，但物價升（通脹）。
  • 收縮 AD \(\implies\) 物價跌，但失業升（經濟衰退）。

因此，當題目要求兩個宏觀經濟問題同時改善（物價跌 + 失業跌），這是一個強烈信號：答案必定是關於「總供應 (AS)」的增加。 只有 SRAS 向右移，才能創造這種「雙贏」局面。

The Pivot Point: The core of this question is realizing that Aggregate Demand (AD) policies always involve a trade-off: increasing AD lowers unemployment but raises prices; decreasing AD lowers prices but raises unemployment. Therefore, when a question asks for both problems to be solved simultaneously (lower prices + lower unemployment), it's a strong signal: The answer MUST involve an increase in Aggregate Supply (AS). Only a rightward shift in SRAS creates this "win-win" scenario.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

❌ 陷阱一：忽略生產成本 (Ignoring Cost of Production)

很多同學看到「補貼 (Subsidies)」或政府開支，就自動聯想到政府開支 (\(G\)) 增加，以為只會影響總需求 (AD)。要小心分辨：直接給予企業的補貼，主要效果是降低生產成本，這屬於供應面政策 (Supply-side factor)。

Many students see "subsidies" or government spending and automatically link it to an increase in Government Expenditure (\(G\)), thinking it only affects AD. Be careful: subsidies given directly to producers mainly lower the cost of production, making it a supply-side factor.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

問 (Q)：選項 B 的「補貼」難道不會增加政府支出，從而令總需求 (AD) 也向右移嗎？如果 AD 也向右移，物價還會下降嗎？

Question: Doesn't the "subsidy" in Option B increase government spending, thereby shifting AD to the right as well? If AD also shifts right, will the price level still fall?

答 (A)： 這是個非常好的進階問題！在現實世界中，補貼確實可能透過乘數效應輕微影響 AD。但在 DSE 考試的框架下，我們通常會找出該政策最直接和最主要的影響。給予企業的補貼，其最直接的作用機制是改變微觀的生產誘因（降低邊際成本），因此考評局預期考生將其視為 SRAS 的移動。即使我們假設 AD 真的有輕微增加，只要 SRAS 的增幅遠大於 AD 的增幅，整體物價水平依然會下降。因此，B 依然是「最可能」達到雙重目標的唯一選項。

Answer: This is a great advanced question! In the real world, subsidies might slightly affect AD. However, in the DSE framework, we look for the most direct and primary effect. Subsidies to firms directly change production incentives (lowering marginal costs), so the HKEAA expects candidates to treat it as an SRAS shift. Even if AD shifted right slightly, as long as the SRAS increase dominates, the net effect is still a lower price level. Thus, B remains the ONLY valid option to achieve the dual goal.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

🔥 10秒速解法 (10-Second Shortcut):

在 MC 卷中，遇到「同時降物價和降失業」這句咒語，大腦直接彈出 "Cost of Production \(\downarrow\)" 或 "SRAS \(\rightarrow\)"。然後掃視 4 個選項：

  • A 是貨幣政策 (影響 AD)
  • B 是降低成本 (影響 SRAS) \(\implies\) 直接選 B！
  • C 是稅收政策 (影響 AD)
  • D 是財政政策/轉移支付 (影響 AD)

In MC papers, when you see the magic phrase "reduce price and unemployment simultaneously", your brain should immediately flash "Cost of Production \(\downarrow\)" or "SRAS \(\rightarrow\)". Scan the options: A, C, and D are all demand-side (AD) policies. Only B lowers production costs (AS). Pick B instantly!
