📝 HKDSE Economics Question Analysis

Topic: 聯繫匯率制度 (Linked Exchange Rate System) Difficulty: ★★★★☆
Key Concepts: Note-Issuing Banks Market vs Linked Rate Currency Peg

題目 The Question

下列哪項有關香港聯繫匯率制度的陳述是正確的？

Which of the following statements about the Linked Exchange Rate System of Hong Kong is correct?

A. 公眾能以市場匯率向香港金融管理局賣出港元以兌換美元。

A. The public can sell their Hong Kong dollars to the Hong Kong Monetary Authority in exchange for US dollars at the market exchange rate.

B. 公眾能以聯繫匯率向三家發鈔銀行賣出港元以兌換美元。

B. The public can sell their Hong Kong dollars to the three note-issuing banks in exchange for US dollars at the linked exchange rate.

C. 港元兌日圓升值會令美元兌日圓升值。

C. An appreciation of Hong Kong dollar against Japanese yen would lead to an appreciation of US dollar against Japanese yen.

D. 美元兌日圓升值會令港元兌日圓升值。

D. An appreciation of US dollar against Japanese yen would lead to an appreciation of Hong Kong dollar against Japanese yen.

概念重溫 Concept Review

1. 雙層兌換機制 (Two-Tier Exchange Mechanism): 香港的聯繫匯率制度分為兩層。金管局 (HKMA) 只與發鈔銀行進行交易。一般公眾只能與商業銀行（包括發鈔銀行）進行交易，而不能直接與金管局交易。

1. Two-Tier Exchange Mechanism: The LERS in Hong Kong operates on two tiers. The HKMA only deals with note-issuing banks. The general public can only deal with commercial banks (including note-issuing banks) and cannot trade directly with the HKMA.

2. 匯率的差異 (Linked Rate vs. Market Rate): 金管局與發鈔銀行之間的交易使用法定的聯繫匯率；而公眾與銀行之間的交易則使用受市場供求影響的市場匯率。

2. Linked Rate vs. Market Rate: Transactions between the HKMA and note-issuing banks use the official Linked Exchange Rate. However, transactions between the public and banks use the Market Exchange Rate, which fluctuates based on supply and demand.

$$ \text{Linked Exchange Rate: } 1 \text{ USD} = 7.80 \text{ HKD} $$

3. 貨幣掛鉤的因果關係 (Causality of Currency Peg): 在聯繫匯率下，港元 (HKD) 掛鉤美元 (USD)。美元是主導貨幣，港元是跟隨者。因此，美元兌第三貨幣的匯率變動，會導致港元跟隨變動，而不是反過來。

3. Causality of Currency Peg: Under the LERS, the HKD is pegged to the USD. The USD is the leading currency, and the HKD is the follower. Therefore, changes in the USD against third currencies will cause the HKD to follow suit, not the other way around.

答案與詳細分析 The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Option: D

❌ 選項 A 分析 (Option A Analysis): 錯誤。一般公眾不能直接與香港金融管理局 (HKMA) 進行外匯交易，公眾只能透過商業銀行兌換貨幣。

Incorrect. The general public cannot trade foreign exchange directly with the HKMA. The public can only exchange currencies through commercial banks.

❌ 選項 B 分析 (Option B Analysis): 錯誤。雖然公眾可以向發鈔銀行賣出港元，但交易是以市場匯率 (Market Rate) 進行，而非固定的聯繫匯率 (7.80)。聯繫匯率只適用於發鈔銀行與金管局之間關於負債證明書 (CIs) 的交易。

Incorrect. While the public can sell HKD to note-issuing banks, the transaction is conducted at the market exchange rate, not the fixed linked exchange rate (7.80). The linked rate strictly applies to transactions for Certificates of Indebtedness (CIs) between note-issuing banks and the HKMA.

❌ 選項 C 分析 (Option C Analysis): 錯誤（因果倒置）。因為港元與美元掛鉤，港元的價值是被動地由美元決定的。港元兌日圓升值只是一個結果，它不能「導致」美元升值。主導權在美元手上。

Incorrect (reversed causality). Because the HKD is pegged to the USD, the value of the HKD is passively determined by the USD. An appreciation of the HKD against JPY is merely a result; it cannot "lead to" an appreciation of the USD. The USD is the driver.

✅ 選項 D 分析 (Option D Analysis): 正確。由於港元與美元掛鉤（匯率固定在大約 7.80），當美元兌第三貨幣（如日圓）升值時，1美元能換到更多日圓；這意味著與1美元等值的7.8港元也能換到更多日圓，因此港元必然跟隨美元兌日圓升值。

Correct. Since the HKD is pegged to the USD (fixed around 7.80), when the USD appreciates against a third currency (like the JPY), 1 USD can buy more JPY. This implies that 7.8 HKD (the equivalent of 1 USD) can also buy more JPY. Therefore, the HKD must follow the USD and appreciate against the JPY.

題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

面對這類聯繫匯率的題目，我們需要將複雜的機制拆解為兩個「過濾器」 (Filters)：

When facing questions about the LERS, we need to break down the complex mechanism into two "Filters":

  • 過濾器一：參與者與適用匯率 (Participants & Applicable Rates)
    看到「公眾 (Public)」，立刻聯想「不能找金管局 (No HKMA)」及「只用市場匯率 (Market Rate only)」。這樣可以一秒刪除選項 A 和 B。

    Filter 1: Participants & Applicable Rates
    When you see "Public", immediately link it to "No HKMA" and "Market Rate only". This allows you to eliminate options A and B in one second.

  • 過濾器二：誰是老大？ (Who is the Boss?)
    美元是掛鉤制度中的「老大」，港元是「跟班」。只有老大的變動會帶動跟班（美元影響港元），跟班的變動不能帶動老大（選項 C 錯，選項 D 對）。

    Filter 2: Who is the Boss?
    The USD is the "boss" in the peg system, and the HKD is the "follower". Only the boss's movement drives the follower (USD affects HKD), not the other way around (Option C is wrong, Option D is right).

常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

⚠️ 陷阱：混淆「發鈔銀行」的角色
很多學生以為「發鈔銀行」代表官方，所以公眾向發鈔銀行兌換貨幣就能享有7.80的「官方保證價」。這是大錯特錯！對公眾而言，發鈔銀行（如匯豐、渣打、中銀）只是一間普通的商業銀行，必須按市場浮動匯率進行零售交易。

⚠️ Trap: Confusing the role of "Note-issuing banks"
Many students mistakenly believe that because note-issuing banks represent official authority, the public can exchange currencies with them at the "official guaranteed rate" of 7.80. This is completely wrong! To the public, note-issuing banks (like HSBC, Standard Chartered, BOCHK) act merely as ordinary commercial banks and conduct retail transactions at floating market exchange rates.

學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

🙋‍♂️ 學生問 (Student): 如果公眾只能用「市場匯率」，那為什麼我們去銀行換美金時，匯率總是停留在 7.8 附近，不會變成 8.0 或 9.0？

Q: If the public can only use the "market rate", why is it that when we exchange USD at a bank, the rate always stays around 7.8 and never goes to 8.0 or 9.0?

👨‍🏫 導師答 (Tutor): 因為銀行之間存在套息/套戥活動 (Arbitrage)。如果市場匯率偏離 7.80 太多，銀行就會利用金管局提供的 7.80 官方匯率進行低買高賣賺取無風險利潤。這種市場力量（以及金管局的強方/弱方兌換保證）會將市場匯率自動拉回 7.75 至 7.85 的區間內。因此，公眾享受到的市場匯率，實際上是被銀行套戥行為「穩定下來」的結果。

A: Because of arbitrage activities among banks. If the market rate deviates too much from 7.80, banks will exploit the HKMA's official 7.80 rate to buy low and sell high for risk-free profit. These market forces (along with the HKMA's strong/weak-side convertibility undertakings) automatically pull the market rate back within the 7.75 - 7.85 band. Thus, the market rate the public experiences is actually "stabilized" by the banks' arbitrage behaviors.

考試技巧與速解 Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

🚀 「聯匯三不原則」速殺法 (The "3 NOs" of LERS):

🚀 The "3 NOs" Shortcut for LERS:

  1. 公眾 不 對金管局。

    Public does NOT deal with HKMA.

  2. 公眾 不 享聯繫匯率 (7.80)。

    Public does NOT enjoy the linked rate (7.80).

  3. 港元 不 影響美元。

    HKD does NOT affect USD.

在考場上背熟這三點，這題 15 秒內就能直接選出 D！

Memorize these three points in the exam, and you can directly pick D in under 15 seconds!
