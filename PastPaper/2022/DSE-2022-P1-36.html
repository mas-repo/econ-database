HKDSE Economics MC Analysis

📚 Topic: 總體經濟學 (Macroeconomics) - AD-AS Model
⭐ Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ (3/5)
🔑 Concepts: Deflationary Gap, Aggregate Demand, Transfer Payments

1. 題目 (The Question)

下列哪項會擴大某國的通縮（產出）差距？

A. 它的貿易夥伴經歷經濟復甦。
B. 人們更廣泛地使用信用卡。
C. 轉移支付下降。
D. 中央銀行印發更多貨幣。

Which of the following will widen the deflationary (output) gap of a country?

A. Its trading partners experience an economic recovery.
B. Credit cards are more widely used.
C. There is a decrease in transfer payment.
D. The central bank prints more money.

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

通縮差距 (Deflationary / Output Gap)

當一個經濟體的均衡實質產出 \(Y\) 低於全民就業水平的實質產出 \(Y_f\) 時，就會出現通縮差距。要擴大這個差距，意味著實際產出需要進一步下降，這通常是由於總需求 (Aggregate Demand, AD) 減少或短期總供應 (Short-Run Aggregate Supply, SRAS) 減少所引起。

A deflationary gap occurs when an economy's equilibrium real output \(Y\) is lower than its full-employment real output \(Y_f\). To widen this gap, the actual output must decrease further, which is typically caused by a decrease in Aggregate Demand (AD) or a decrease in Short-Run Aggregate Supply (SRAS).

$$AD = C + I + G + (X - M)$$

其中 \(C\) = 私人消費 (Consumption), \(I\) = 投資 (Investment), \(G\) = 政府開支 (Government Spending), \(X\) = 出口 (Exports), \(M\) = 進口 (Imports)。

Where \(C\) = Consumption, \(I\) = Investment, \(G\) = Government Spending, \(X\) = Exports, and \(M\) = Imports.

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是 (Correct Option)：C

A. 它的貿易夥伴經歷經濟復甦 (不正確)

當貿易夥伴經濟復甦，他們的國民收入增加，對本國出口商品的需求也會增加 (\(X\) 上升)。這會導致總需求 (AD) 增加，實際產出上升，從而收窄通縮差距。

A. Its trading partners experience an economic recovery (Incorrect): When trading partners recover, their national income rises, leading to a higher demand for the country's exports (\(X\) increases). This causes Aggregate Demand (AD) to increase and actual output to rise, thereby narrowing the deflationary gap.

B. 人們更廣泛地使用信用卡 (不正確)

廣泛使用信用卡會減少人們對持有現金的交易需求（貨幣需求下降）。在貨幣供應不變的情況下，利率會下降。較低的利率會刺激私人消費 (\(C\)) 和投資 (\(I\))，令總需求 (AD) 增加，收窄通縮差距。

B. Credit cards are more widely used (Incorrect): Wider use of credit cards reduces the transaction demand for holding cash (money demand decreases). Assuming money supply is constant, interest rates will fall. Lower interest rates stimulate private consumption (\(C\)) and investment (\(I\)), increasing AD and narrowing the deflationary gap.

C. 轉移支付下降 (正確)

轉移支付（如失業救濟金、養老金）減少，會導致市民的可支配收入 (Disposable Income) 下降。因此，私人消費開支 (\(C\)) 會減少，導致總需求 (AD) 下降。總需求曲線向左移會令實質產出進一步下降，從而擴大通縮差距。

C. There is a decrease in transfer payment (Correct): A decrease in transfer payments (e.g., unemployment benefits, pensions) lowers citizens' disposable income. Consequently, private consumption expenditure (\(C\)) decreases, causing Aggregate Demand (AD) to fall. A leftward shift in the AD curve leads to a further decrease in real output, thereby widening the deflationary gap.

D. 中央銀行印發更多貨幣 (不正確)

增加貨幣供應會導致市場利率下降。這同樣會刺激消費 (\(C\)) 和投資 (\(I\))，增加總需求 (AD)，從而收窄通縮差距。

D. The central bank prints more money (Incorrect): An increase in the money supply leads to a drop in market interest rates. This stimulates consumption (\(C\)) and investment (\(I\)), increasing AD and thus narrowing the deflationary gap.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point): 題目的核心字眼是「擴大」和「通縮差距」。通縮差距代表經濟處於低迷狀態。要令經濟「更差」（擴大差距），我們必須尋找一個緊縮性 (Contractionary) 的宏觀經濟因素，即會導致 AD 或 SRAS 下降的選項。

The core keywords are "widen" and "deflationary gap." A deflationary gap indicates an underperforming economy. To make it "worse" (widen the gap), we must look for a contractionary macroeconomic factor—an option that causes AD or SRAS to decrease.

  • 選項 A、B、D 均屬於擴張性 (Expansionary) 因素，會刺激經濟。
  • 只有選項 C 會壓抑總需求，因此直接鎖定 C 為答案。

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

⚠️ 陷阱一：誤解信用卡對貨幣市場的影響

很多同學誤以為「使用信用卡 = 借多了錢 = 貨幣供應增加」。這是錯的！信用卡不是貨幣。廣泛使用信用卡只是改變了人們付款的習慣，令人們減少持有現金，這是貨幣需求 (Money Demand) 的下降，而非貨幣供應的改變。

Trap 1: Misunderstanding the effect of credit cards. Many students mistakenly think "using credit cards = borrowing more = increase in money supply." This is false! Credit cards are not money. Using them simply reduces the need to hold cash, which is a decrease in Money Demand, not a change in money supply.

⚠️ 陷阱二：將轉移支付混淆為政府開支 (G)

雖然轉移支付是政府發放的資金，但它並不直接計算入 GDP 公式中的政府開支 \(G\) 內（因為沒有生產活動的交換）。它只會透過改變市民的可支配收入，間接影響私人消費 \(C\)。

Trap 2: Confusing Transfer Payments with Government Spending (G). Although transfer payments are funds distributed by the government, they are NOT directly counted as \(G\) in the GDP formula (because no productive activity is exchanged). They only indirectly affect Consumption \(C\) by changing disposable income.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 如果題目問的是「通脹差距」(Inflationary Gap)，答案會是甚麼？

A: 如果經濟處於通脹差距（過熱），我們需要「擴張性」政策來「擴大」它（令經濟更過熱），或者需要「緊縮性」政策來「收窄」它。如果題目問「哪項會收窄通脹差距」，答案同樣會是 C，因為 C 是一個降溫（緊縮）因素。

Q: What if the question asked about an "Inflationary Gap"?
A: If the economy is in an inflationary gap (overheating), we would need "expansionary" factors to "widen" it, or "contractionary" factors to "narrow" it. If the question asked "Which of the following will narrow the inflationary gap", the answer would also be C, because C is a cooling (contractionary) factor.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

💡 剔除法 / 找不同法 (The "Odd One Out" Method)

在 DSE 宏觀經濟學 MC 題目中，經常可以使用這種秒殺技巧。試觀察選項的性質：

  • A = 經濟好轉 (利好)
  • B = 利率下降，刺激消費 (利好)
  • D = 貨幣增加，利率下降 (利好)
  • C = 收入減少，消費減少 (利淡)

A、B 和 D 都是刺激總需求 (AD) 的方向。既然這是一條單選題，唯一方向不同的選項 C，必然是正確答案，甚至不需要詳細畫圖分析也能在 10 秒內選出！

In DSE Macroeconomics MC questions, you can often use this shortcut. Observe the nature of the options: A, B, and D are all expansionary factors that stimulate the economy (Good news). Option C is the ONLY contractionary factor (Bad news). Since it's a single-choice question, the "odd one out" (Option C) must be the answer. You can solve this in 10 seconds without drawing graphs!
