主題： 政府干預市場 (取消補貼)
Topic: Market Intervention (Cancellation of Subsidy)

難度： ⭐⭐⭐½ (3.5/5)
Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐½ (3.5/5)

從量補貼 Per-unit Subsidy 消費者盈餘 Consumer Surplus 效率損失 Deadweight Loss

📝 題目 The Question

參考以下有關物品X的供需圖。若政府取消對物品X的從量補貼，這會引致 __________ 下降。

Refer to the following supply-demand diagram of Good X. If the government cancels the per-unit subsidy on Good X, it will lead to a decrease in __________.

(1) 交易量。
(2) 消費者盈餘。
(3) 效率損失。

(1) quantity transacted.
(2) consumer surplus.
(3) deadweight loss.

A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

💡 概念重溫 Concept Review

1. 從量補貼的影響： 補貼會降低生產成本，令供應曲線向右（向下）移動。相反，取消補貼意味著市場回覆到沒有干預的狀態，供應曲線會向左（向上）移動，導致均衡價格上升，均衡交易量下降。

1. Impact of Per-unit Subsidy: A subsidy lowers production costs, shifting the supply curve to the right (downwards). Conversely, cancelling a subsidy means the market returns to its free state; the supply curve shifts to the left (upwards), leading to a higher equilibrium price and a lower equilibrium quantity transacted.

2. 消費者盈餘 (Consumer Surplus, CS)： 這是消費者願意支付的最高價格與實際支付價格之間的差額。在圖表中，它是需求曲線以下、市場價格以上的面積。

2. Consumer Surplus (CS): This is the difference between the maximum price consumers are willing to pay and the actual price they pay. On a graph, it is the area below the demand curve and above the market price.

$$ \text{CS} = \text{Total Benefit} - \text{Total Expenditure} $$

3. 效率損失 / 絕對損失 (Deadweight Loss, DWL)： 當市場未能在邊際利益等於邊際成本 \( (\text{MB} = \text{MC}) \) 的效率產量上運作時，所引致的總社會盈餘減少。補貼會引致過度生產 (Overproduction)，產生效率損失。

3. Deadweight Loss (DWL): The reduction in total social surplus when the market fails to operate at the efficient output level where marginal benefit equals marginal cost \( (\text{MB} = \text{MC}) \). A subsidy causes overproduction, generating a deadweight loss.

✅ 答案與詳細分析 The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案：D

Correct Option: D

分析 (1) 交易量：
取消補貼後，供應曲線由「S 有補貼」向左移回「S 沒有補貼」。新的市場均衡點會沿著需求曲線向左上方移動。因此，交易量必定下降。第 (1) 項正確。

Analysis (1) Quantity transacted:
After cancelling the subsidy, the supply curve shifts leftwards from "S with subsidy" back to "S without subsidy". The new market equilibrium moves up and to the left along the demand curve. Therefore, the quantity transacted must decrease. Statement (1) is correct.

分析 (2) 消費者盈餘：
由於供應減少（向左移），市場價格 \(P\) 會上升，而交易量 \(Q\) 會下跌。消費者現在需要支付更高的價格來購買更少的物品，因此需求曲線下方、價格線以上的三角形面積會縮小。消費者盈餘下降。第 (2) 項正確。

Analysis (2) Consumer surplus:
Due to the decrease in supply (leftward shift), the market price \(P\) rises and quantity \(Q\) falls. Consumers now pay a higher price for fewer goods, so the triangular area below the demand curve and above the price line shrinks. Consumer surplus decreases. Statement (2) is correct.

分析 (3) 效率損失：
在沒有政府干預的自由市場中（即「S 沒有補貼」和 D 的相交點），社會資源分配是最有效率的，效率損失為零。政府實施補貼反而會導致產量過多（即大於效率產量），造成效率損失。因此，當政府取消補貼，市場回復效率水平，這筆效率損失便會消失（減少至零）。效率損失下降。第 (3) 項正確。

Analysis (3) Deadweight loss:
In a free market without intervention (the intersection of "S without subsidy" and D), resource allocation is efficient, and deadweight loss is zero. Implementing a subsidy actually causes overproduction (quantity greater than the efficient level), creating a deadweight loss. Therefore, when the government cancels the subsidy, the market returns to the efficient level, and the deadweight loss is eliminated (reduced to zero). Deadweight loss decreases. Statement (3) is correct.

🔍 題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)： 本題的關鍵在於分辨「實施干預」和「取消干預」的分別。很多同學一看到政府政策變動就直覺認為會「產生效率損失」，但本題是取消補貼，這是一個「消除市場扭曲 (Removing market distortion)」的過程，因此效率必定會改善（損失減少）。

Pivot Point: The key is distinguishing between "implementing" and "cancelling" an intervention. Many students intuitively think any government policy change "creates deadweight loss", but this question is about cancelling a subsidy. This is a process of "removing market distortion", meaning efficiency must improve (loss decreases).

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

誤區 1：認為只有徵稅才有絕對損失 (DWL)。
很多學生忘記了補貼也會引致絕對損失！補貼會誘使生產者生產一些邊際成本高於邊際利益 \( (\text{MC} > \text{MB}) \) 的單位，造成社會資源浪費。

Trap 1: Thinking only taxes cause Deadweight Loss (DWL).
Many students forget that subsidies also create DWL! Subsidies induce producers to produce units where marginal cost is greater than marginal benefit \( (\text{MC} > \text{MB}) \), resulting in a waste of social resources.

誤區 2：審題不清，沒有看見「取消」二字。
如果題目是「實施從量補貼」，那麼 (1) 交易量會上升，(2) 消費者盈餘會上升，(3) 效率損失會上升。同學必須看清楚題目問的是回復原狀 (Cancellation)。

Trap 2: Misreading the question and missing the word "cancels".
If the question were "implementing a per-unit subsidy", then (1) quantity would increase, (2) CS would increase, and (3) DWL would increase. Students must read carefully that it asks about returning to the original state (Cancellation).

🙋‍♂️ 學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

問：既然取消補貼對社會效率有幫助（減少效率損失），為什麼消費者盈餘還會下跌呢？這不是矛盾嗎？

Q: If cancelling the subsidy helps social efficiency (reduces DWL), why does consumer surplus still fall? Isn't that contradictory?

答：不矛盾。 效率損失看的是「整個社會的總盈餘」(Total Social Surplus = Consumer Surplus + Producer Surplus - Government Expenditure)。雖然取消補貼令消費者買貴了東西（CS 下降），生產者也賺少了（PS 下降），但政府卻省下了一大筆龐大的補貼開支！政府節省的開支幅度，遠大於消費者和生產者盈餘的跌幅，因此整體社會是獲益的（效率改善）。

A: Not contradictory. Deadweight loss looks at the "Total Social Surplus" (TSS = CS + PS - Government Expenditure). Although cancelling the subsidy makes consumers pay more (CS falls) and producers earn less (PS falls), the government saves a massive amount of subsidy expenditure! The amount saved by the government is larger than the combined drop in CS and PS, so society as a whole benefits (efficiency improves).

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

邏輯推論法：
在 DSE 選擇題中，自由市場 (沒有外部性時) 永遠是「最有效率」的。所以任何將市場推回自由市場均衡點的動作（例如取消價格上限、取消配額、取消補貼、取消稅收），必定會令效率損失下降 (DWL ↓)。知道了這點，你馬上就能鎖定答案必須包含 (3)，配合價格上升令消費者受損 (CS ↓)，答案直接鎖定 D。

Logical Deduction Method:
In DSE MCQs, a free market (without externalities) is always the "most efficient". Therefore, any action that pushes the market back to the free market equilibrium (e.g., cancelling price ceilings, quotas, subsidies, or taxes) will guarantee a decrease in deadweight loss (DWL ↓). Knowing this, you immediately know the answer must include (3). Combined with the fact that a price rise hurts consumers (CS ↓), you can directly lock in Option D.
