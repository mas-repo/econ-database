📊 HKDSE Economics Topic Analysis

Topic (課題): 國際貿易與市場干預 - 進口配額 (International Trade & Market Intervention - Import Quota)

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)

Key Concepts (關鍵概念): 進口配額 (Import Quota), 世界價格 (World Price), 總供應曲線 (Total Supply Curve)

📝 The Question (題目)

下圖是關於某個對物品X實施進口配額的小型開放經濟體。

The diagram below is about a small open economy imposing an import quota on Good X.

若物品X的世界價格下跌，

If the world price of Good X drops,

  • A. 該配額會變得無效。
    the quota will become ineffective.
  • B. 物品X的本地生產數量會下降。
    the quantity of Good X produced domestically will decrease.
  • C. 物品X的本地消費數量會上升。
    the quantity of Good X consumed domestically will increase.
  • D. 物品X的本地價格會維持不變。
    the domestic price of Good X will remain unchanged.

💡 Concept Review (概念重溫)

進口配額下的總供應曲線 (\(S_1\)): 在實施配額後，總供應曲線由兩部分組成。在世界價格 (\(P_w\)) 之下，只有本地供應。在世界價格之上，總供應等於本地供應加上配額數量。因此，\(S_1\) 是將 \(S_0\) 向右平移一個等於配額數量的水平距離。

Total Supply Curve (\(S_1\)) under Import Quota: After imposing a quota, the total supply curve consists of two parts. Below the world price (\(P_w\)), there is only domestic supply. Above the world price, total supply equals domestic supply plus the quota amount. Therefore, \(S_1\) is formed by shifting \(S_0\) to the right by a horizontal distance equal to the quota.

$$ S_1 = S_0 + \text{Quota (for } P \ge P_w \text{)} $$

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option D

分析 D (正確): 當世界價格下跌時，水平的虛線會向下移。配額的水平線段也會跟隨向下移。然而，由於配額數量（水平寬度）不變，本地供應曲線 \(S_0\) 亦不變，\(S_1\) 傾斜向上的上半部分（即 \(S_0\) 向右平移的部分）的位置是完全不會改變的。因為新的 \(S_1\) 與需求曲線 \(D\) 的相交點仍然位於這段不變的曲線上，所以本地價格維持不變。

Analysis D (Correct): When the world price drops, the horizontal dashed line shifts downwards. The horizontal segment representing the quota also shifts down. However, since the quota amount (horizontal width) and the domestic supply curve \(S_0\) remain unchanged, the position of the upward-sloping upper part of \(S_1\) (which is \(S_0\) shifted right) remains exactly the same. Because the new intersection of \(S_1\) and the demand curve \(D\) still lies on this unchanged part of the curve, the domestic price remains unchanged.

圖解：世界價格下跌的影響
Visual Proof: Impact of a Drop in World Price

分析 A (錯誤): 世界價格下跌會令沒有配額時的進口量增加，這使得原有的配額變得更有效（更具約束力），而不是無效。

Analysis A (Incorrect): A drop in world price would increase the quantity of imports if there were no quota, making the existing quota more effective (more binding), not ineffective.

分析 B & C (錯誤): 由於本地價格維持不變（見選項D的分析），根據需求定律和供應定律，本地的消費數量（由 \(D\) 決定）和本地的生產數量（由 \(S_0\) 決定）都會維持不變。

Analysis B & C (Incorrect): Since the domestic price remains unchanged (see analysis for D), according to the laws of demand and supply, both the domestic quantity consumed (determined by \(D\)) and the domestic quantity produced (determined by \(S_0\)) will remain unchanged.

🙋 Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼整條 \(S_1\) 曲線不會跟著世界價格一起向下移？
Why doesn't the entire \(S_1\) curve shift down with the world price?

A: 因為配額限制的是「數量」（橫軸），而不是「價格」。\(S_1\) 的上半部是 \(S_0\) 加上一個固定的數量（配額）。只要 \(S_0\) 沒變，配額數量沒變，這兩者加起來的結果（\(S_1\) 的上半部）在圖表上的絕對位置就不會變。世界價格下跌只會拉長 \(S_1\) 的斜線部分。
Because a quota restricts "quantity" (horizontal axis), not "price". The upper part of \(S_1\) is \(S_0\) plus a fixed quantity (the quota). As long as \(S_0\) and the quota amount are unchanged, their sum (the upper part of \(S_1\)) remains in the exact same absolute position on the graph. A drop in world price only extends the upward-sloping part of \(S_1\) further down.

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

畫圖法 (Drawing Trick): 在試卷上，直接用鉛筆把 \(P_{world price}\) 的虛線畫低一點。然後，畫一條與原來「配額」括號一樣長度的水平線。接著，從這條線的右端畫一條平行於 \(S_0\) 的斜線。你會發現，這條新畫的斜線會完美重疊在原來的 \(S_1\) 上半部！既然曲線重疊，與 \(D\) 的交點自然不變，價格和數量都不變，秒殺此題！

Drawing Trick: On your exam paper, use a pencil to draw a lower dashed line for \(P_{world price}\). Then, draw a horizontal line with the exact same length as the original "quota" bracket. From the right end of this line, draw an upward-sloping line parallel to \(S_0\). You will see that this new line perfectly overlaps the upper part of the original \(S_1\)! Since the curves overlap, the intersection with \(D\) remains unchanged, meaning price and quantities are unchanged. Instant solve!
