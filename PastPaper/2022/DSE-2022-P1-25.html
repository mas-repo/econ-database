HKDSE Economics Tutorial
經濟學名師解析

Topic: Macroeconomic Indicators (宏觀經濟指標) Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)
Key Concepts: Real GDP, Per Capita GDP, Inflation vs. Deflation

1. 題目 (The Question)

下表顯示某國的一些數據。
增長率
年份 2019 2020
名義本地生產總值 0.3% -5.5%
物價水平 2.0% 0.6%
人口 0.5% -1.2%
下列哪項陳述是正確的？
A. 2020年實質本地生產總值上升。
B. 2020年人均名義本地生產總值上升。
C. 2020年出現通縮。
D. 2020年實質本地生產總值的增長率較2019年的低。
The following table shows some data of a country.
Growth rate
Year 2019 2020
Nominal GDP 0.3% -5.5%
Price level 2.0% 0.6%
Population 0.5% -1.2%
Which of the following statements is correct?
A. Real GDP in 2020 increased.
B. Per capita nominal GDP in 2020 increased.
C. There was deflation in 2020.
D. The growth rate of real GDP in 2020 was smaller than that in 2019.

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

在處理涉及「名義」(Nominal)、「實質」(Real)、「人均」(Per Capita) 及「物價水平」(Price Level) 的增長率題目時，我們通常會使用以下估算公式：
When dealing with growth rate questions involving Nominal, Real, Per Capita, and Price Level data, we generally use the following approximation formulas:
$$ g_{\text{Real}} \approx g_{\text{Nominal}} - g_{\text{Price Level}} $$
公式 1： 實質變量的增長率 $\approx$ 名義變量的增長率 $-$ 物價水平的增長率（即通脹率）。
Formula 1: Growth rate of a real variable $\approx$ Growth rate of the nominal variable $-$ Growth rate of the price level (Inflation rate).
$$ g_{\text{Per Capita}} \approx g_{\text{Total}} - g_{\text{Population}} $$
公式 2： 人均變量的增長率 $\approx$ 總量變量的增長率 $-$ 人口增長率。
Formula 2: Growth rate of a per capita variable $\approx$ Growth rate of the total variable $-$ Population growth rate.
通縮 (Deflation) 的定義： 一般物價水平持續下降。這意味著物價水平的增長率必須為負數（小於 0）。
Definition of Deflation: A persistent decrease in the general price level. This means the growth rate of the price level must be negative (less than 0).

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是 / The correct answer is: Option D

Option A

錯誤。 根據公式，2020年實質 GDP 增長率 $\approx -5.5\% - 0.6\% = -6.1\%$。因為增長率是負數，所以2020年的實質本地生產總值是下降的，而非上升。
Incorrect. According to the formula, the Real GDP growth rate in 2020 $\approx -5.5\% - 0.6\% = -6.1\%$. Since the growth rate is negative, the Real GDP in 2020 decreased, not increased.

Option B

錯誤。 2020年人均名義 GDP 增長率 $\approx -5.5\% - (-1.2\%) = -5.5\% + 1.2\% = -4.3\%$。增長率為負數，表示人均名義 GDP 下降。
Incorrect. The Per capita nominal GDP growth rate in 2020 $\approx -5.5\% - (-1.2\%) = -5.5\% + 1.2\% = -4.3\%$. The negative growth rate indicates that the per capita nominal GDP decreased.

Option C

錯誤。 2020年的物價水平增長率為 0.6%。只要這個數值是大於 0 的正數，就代表物價仍在上升（這稱為通脹，即便升幅收窄也只是「通脹放緩」）。通縮要求物價水平增長率為負數。
Incorrect. The growth rate of the price level in 2020 was 0.6%. As long as this figure is positive (> 0), it means prices are still rising (this is inflation, specifically "disinflation" since the rate dropped from 2.0%). Deflation requires a negative growth rate in the price level.

Option D

正確。
2019年實質 GDP 增長率 $\approx 0.3\% - 2.0\% = -1.7\%$
2020年實質 GDP 增長率 $\approx -5.5\% - 0.6\% = -6.1\%$
因為 -6.1% 小於 -1.7%，所以2020年的實質本地生產總值增長率確實較2019年低。
Correct.
2019 Real GDP growth rate $\approx 0.3\% - 2.0\% = -1.7\%$
2020 Real GDP growth rate $\approx -5.5\% - 0.6\% = -6.1\%$
Since -6.1% is mathematically smaller (more negative) than -1.7%, the growth rate of real GDP in 2020 was indeed smaller than that in 2019.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)： 本題的核心在於測試你是否懂得將三個不同的變數（名義 GDP、物價水平、人口）透過兩條加減公式連結起來。表格提供了所有的原始素材，你需要自己組合出「實質 GDP」和「人均 GDP」的數據。

干擾資訊 (Distractors)： 如果你只看選項 A 和 C，你甚至不需要看「人口」那一行的數據。人口數據專門用來測試選項 B。
Pivot Point: The core of this question tests your ability to link three different variables (Nominal GDP, Price Level, Population) using the two basic addition/subtraction approximation formulas. The table provides the raw ingredients, and you must "cook" the Real GDP and Per Capita GDP data yourself.

Distractors: If you were only evaluating options A and C, you wouldn't even need to look at the "Population" row. The population data is explicitly there to test your evaluation of Option B.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • 陷阱一：混淆「通脹放緩」(Disinflation) 與「通縮」(Deflation)。 很多同學看到物價水平增長率由 2019年的 2.0% 跌至 2020年的 0.6%，就誤以為這是通縮。記住：只要增長率是正數 ($>0$)，物價就是在上升！
  • 陷阱二：正負號運算粗心大意。 在計算選項 B 時，公式是名義 GDP 減去人口增長率。即 $-5.5\% - (-1.2\%)$。如果忘記了負負得正，就會計錯數。
  • 陷阱三：混淆「增長率下降」與「數值下降」。 實質 GDP 增長率為負數，代表實質 GDP 這個「數值」正在下降。
  • Trap 1: Confusing Disinflation with Deflation. Many students see the price level growth drop from 2.0% to 0.6% and mistakenly assume this is deflation. Remember: As long as the growth rate is positive ($>0$), prices are still rising!
  • Trap 2: Careless algebraic errors with negative signs. When calculating Option B, the formula requires subtracting the population growth rate: $-5.5\% - (-1.2\%)$. If you forget that subtracting a negative equals adding a positive, you will get the wrong value.
  • Trap 3: Confusing "a decrease in growth rate" with "a decrease in value". A negative Real GDP growth rate means the actual "value" of Real GDP is decreasing.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 老師，用相減的方法 ($A \approx B - C$) 只是「估算」(approximation)，這樣在 DSE 考試中準確嗎？為什麼不用精確公式計算？
A: 好問題！精確公式是 $ (1 + g_{\text{Real}}) = \frac{1 + g_{\text{Nominal}}}{1 + g_{\text{Price}}} $。但在 DSE 經濟科中，當百分比變化很小（通常在 10% 以內）時，考評局是完全接受並期望學生使用相減估算公式的。這不僅計算更快，而且在判斷方向（升或跌、大或小）時結論是完全一致的。

Q: Teacher, using the subtraction method ($A \approx B - C$) is just an approximation. Is this accurate enough for the DSE exam? Why don't we use the exact formula?
A: Great question! The exact formula is $ (1 + g_{\text{Real}}) = \frac{1 + g_{\text{Nominal}}}{1 + g_{\text{Price}}} $. However, in DSE Economics, when the percentage changes are small (usually under 10%), the HKEAA fully accepts and expects students to use the approximation formula. It is much faster, and the conclusion regarding the direction (increase/decrease, larger/smaller) will be exactly the same.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

速解法 (Elimination Method)： 做這類題目時，先從最容易判斷的選項入手，不需要一開始就狂計數！
1. 秒殺 C： 看一眼 2020 年物價水平，是正數 (0.6%)，沒有通縮，C 錯。瞬間排除！
2. 直覺判斷 A： 2020 年名義 GDP 大跌 (-5.5%)，而物價又在升 (0.6%)，實質 GDP 必定是跌得更慘，不可能是上升。A 錯。
3. 然後你只需要集中精神計算 B 和 D 來分勝負，大大節省時間。
Shortcut (Elimination Method): When tackling these questions, start with the easiest options to judge. Don't just blindly calculate everything right away!
1. Insta-kill C: Glance at the 2020 price level. It's positive (0.6%). No deflation. C is wrong. Eliminated instantly!
2. Intuition for A: In 2020, Nominal GDP dropped heavily (-5.5%) while prices still went up slightly (0.6%). Real GDP must have fallen even harder; it couldn't possibly increase. A is wrong.
3. Now you only need to focus your mental energy on calculating B and D to find the winner, saving you precious exam time.
