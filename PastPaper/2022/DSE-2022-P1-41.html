HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Measurement of Economic Aggregates Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆ Key Concepts: Money Supply, GDP, Balance of Payments
📝 The Question (題目)

下列哪項的答案不可能是負數？

Which of the following results CANNOT be a negative number?

  • A. 總出口減總進口
    total exports minus total imports
  • B. 貨幣供應定義二減貨幣供應定義一
    money supply (M2) minus money supply (M1)
  • C. 從外地賺取的要素收益減支付予外地的要素收益
    factor income received from abroad minus factor income paid abroad
  • D. 以市價計算的本地生產總值減以要素成本計算的本地生產總值
    GDP at market price minus GDP at factor cost
📚 Concept Review (概念重溫)
1. 貨幣供應定義 (Money Supply Definitions)

香港的貨幣供應分為三個層次（M1, M2, M3）。它們是包含關係 (Subset relationship)。

Hong Kong's money supply is classified into three tiers (M1, M2, M3). They have a subset relationship.

  • \( M1 = C_p + D_d \)
    (公眾持有的法定貨幣 + 活期存款 / Legal tender held by public + Demand deposits)
  • \( M2 = M1 + S_d + T_d + NCDs \)
    (M1 + 儲蓄存款 + 定期存款 + 可轉讓存款證 / M1 + Savings deposits + Time deposits + NCDs)
2. 本地生產總值 (GDP Formulas)

市價 (Market Price) 與 要素成本 (Factor Cost) 的關係：

Relationship between Market Price and Factor Cost:

$$ \text{GDP}_{mp} = \text{GDP}_{fc} + \text{Indirect Taxes} - \text{Subsidies} $$
✅ Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)
Correct Option: B
選項 A：總出口減總進口 (Option A: Exports - Imports)

這是「貿易差額」(Trade Balance)。如果進口大於出口，結果便是負數（貿易逆差）。

This is the "Trade Balance". If imports exceed exports, the result is negative (Trade Deficit). This is very common.

結論：可以是負數 (Can be negative)。

選項 B：M2 - M1 (Option B: M2 - M1)

根據定義：\( M2 = M1 + \text{儲蓄存款} + \text{定期存款} + \text{可轉讓存款證} \)。
因此，\( M2 - M1 = \text{儲蓄存款} + \text{定期存款} + \text{可轉讓存款證} \)。
由於存款金額必定是正數（或零），不可能出現「負存款」，所以這個數值必定是非負數。

By definition: \( M2 = M1 + \text{Savings Deposits} + \text{Time Deposits} + \text{NCDs} \).
Therefore, \( M2 - M1 = \text{Savings Deposits} + \text{Time Deposits} + \text{NCDs} \).
Since deposit amounts must be positive (or zero), you cannot have "negative deposits" in the aggregate. Thus, this value must be non-negative.

結論：不可能是負數 (Cannot be negative)。

選項 C：要素收益淨值 (Option C: Net Factor Income from Abroad)

這等於「從外地賺取的要素收益」減「支付予外地的要素收益」。如果外資在本地賺取的收益（如利息、股息）多於本地人在外地賺取的，結果便是負數。

This equals "Factor income from abroad" minus "Factor income paid abroad". If foreign investments in the local economy earn more (e.g., interest, dividends) than what locals earn abroad, the result is negative.

結論：可以是負數 (Can be negative)。

選項 D：GDP(mp) - GDP(fc) (Option D: GDP at market price - GDP at factor cost)

根據公式，兩者之差等於 \( \text{間接稅} - \text{津貼} \) (Indirect Taxes - Subsidies)。
雖然現實中政府通常徵稅多於津貼，但在理論上或某些極端情況下，津貼總額是有可能大於間接稅總額的，導致結果為負。

According to the formula, the difference equals \( \text{Indirect Taxes} - \text{Subsidies} \).
Although in reality, governments usually collect more taxes than they give out in subsidies, theoretically, total subsidies could exceed total indirect taxes, resulting in a negative number.

結論：理論上可以是負數 (Theoretically can be negative)。

🔍 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的關鍵在於區分「會計恆等式」(Accounting Identities) 與 「實質經濟變量」(Real Economic Variables)。

The key to this type of question is distinguishing between Accounting Identities and Real Economic Variables.

  • Pivot Point (關鍵點): 貨幣供應的定義是層遞式的 (Hierarchical)。M2 包含 M1，M3 包含 M2。這是一種「全集與子集」(Set and Subset) 的關係。
  • Logic (邏輯): 全集 (M2) 的數量必然大於或等於子集 (M1) 的數量，除非子集以外的元素（如儲蓄存款）是負數，但在銀行體系中，存款總額不可能是負的。
💡 Student Q&A & Exam Tricks
Q: 為什麼選項 D 的「津貼大於間接稅」是有可能的？

Why is it possible for "Subsidies to be greater than Indirect Taxes" in Option D?

A: 雖然罕見，但如果政府推行大規模的補貼政策（例如能源補貼、交通補貼），同時實行低稅率或免稅政策，淨間接稅 (Net Indirect Taxes) 就會變成負數。題目問的是「不可能」(Impossible)，只要有 0.01% 的理論可能性，該選項就不是答案。

A: While rare, if a government implements massive subsidy policies (e.g., energy or transport subsidies) while maintaining low or zero tax rates, Net Indirect Taxes can become negative. The question asks for "Impossible"; if there is even a 0.01% theoretical possibility, that option is not the answer.

⚡ Exam Trick (考試速解):

看到「貨幣供應」(Money Supply) 的比較，立即聯想圖表：大圓圈包著小圓圈。

大圓圈 (M2) 減去 小圓圈 (M1) = 剩下的圓環部分 (Savings/Time Deposits)。實物資產或存款數量不可能是負數。直接選 B，無需猶豫。

When you see a comparison of "Money Supply", visualize the diagram: A big circle containing a small circle.
Big Circle (M2) minus Small Circle (M1) = The remaining ring (Savings/Time Deposits). Physical assets or deposit quantities cannot be negative. Pick B immediately.
