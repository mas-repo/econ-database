HKDSE Economics Exam Analysis

Topic: Balance of Payments (國際收支平衡表) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ (3/5) Key Concepts: Current Account, Capital Account, Exports of Services

📝 The Question (題目)

一位國際知名的電影導演來港三星期拍攝一齣荷里活電影。下列哪些會包括在香港經常帳差額的計算之內？

An international movie director came to Hong Kong to shoot a Hollywood movie for three weeks. Which of the following would be included in the calculation of Hong Kong’s current account balance?

(1) 她入住了尖沙嘴的一家酒店。
(2) 她在中環某商場購買了紀念品。
(3) 她將該電影的版權售予本港的一家電影發行商。

(1) She stayed in a hotel in Tsim Sha Tsui.
(2) She bought souvenirs in a shopping mall in Central.
(3) She sold the copyright of the movie to a Hong Kong movie distributor.

A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 國際收支平衡表 (Balance of Payments, BOP)
國際收支平衡表記錄了一個經濟體在特定時期內與世界其他地區的所有經濟交易。它主要分為三個帳戶：

1. Balance of Payments (BOP)
The BOP records all economic transactions between an economy and the rest of the world over a specific period. It is mainly divided into three accounts:

$$ BOP = CA + KKA + FA + EO $$

Where \(CA\) = Current Account, \(KKA\) = Capital Account, \(FA\) = Financial Account, \(EO\) = Errors and Omissions.

2. 經常帳 (Current Account)
包括貨物 (Goods)、服務 (Services)（例如：旅遊、運輸）、初級收入 (Primary Income)（如薪金、投資回報）及次級收入 (Secondary Income)（如單向轉移）。非居民在香港的消費（如酒店、購物）會被計入「服務輸出（旅遊）」。

2. Current Account
Includes trade in goods, trade in services (e.g., travel, transportation), primary income (e.g., compensation of employees, investment income), and secondary income (e.g., unilateral transfers). Consumption by non-residents in HK (like hotels and shopping) is recorded as "Export of Services (Travel)".

3. 資本帳 (Capital Account)
包括資本轉移 (Capital transfers) 以及非生產及非金融資產 (non-produced, non-financial assets) 的收購和處置。例如：土地、專利、版權 (Copyrights) 和商標的買賣。

3. Capital Account
Includes capital transfers and the acquisition and disposal of non-produced, non-financial assets. Examples include the sale and purchase of land, patents, copyrights, and trademarks.

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是：A. 只有 (1)、(2)

The correct answer is: A. (1) and (2) only

分析 (1) - 入住尖沙嘴酒店：
這名外國導演屬於香港的「非居民」。非居民在香港購買的酒店住宿服務，屬於香港的「服務輸出 (Export of Services)」。因為這是旅遊相關的服務交易，所以會記錄在經常帳內。

Analysis of (1) - Hotel stay in TST:
The foreign director is a "non-resident" of Hong Kong. Her consumption of hotel accommodation services in HK is classified as an "Export of Services". Since this is a travel-related service transaction, it will be included in the Current Account.

分析 (2) - 購買紀念品：
同理，非居民在香港境內購買紀念品（旅客消費），在國際收支平衡的計算中，通常被歸類為「旅遊」項下的服務輸出（或貨物輸出）。無論細分為貨物還是服務，它都會被包含在經常帳內。

Analysis of (2) - Buying souvenirs:
Similarly, a non-resident purchasing souvenirs within HK (tourist expenditure) is typically categorized under "Travel" as an export of services (or goods) in BOP accounting. Whether sub-categorized as goods or services, it will be included in the Current Account.

分析 (3) - 售賣電影版權：
這是一個常見的陷阱。買賣「版權 (Copyrights)」或「專利 (Patents)」屬於「非生產及非金融資產」的處置及收購。根據國際貨幣基金組織 (IMF) 的準則，這類無形資產的買賣必須記錄在資本帳 (Capital Account) 中，而不是經常帳。

Analysis of (3) - Selling movie copyright:
This is a common trap. The buying and selling of "Copyrights" or "Patents" falls under the disposal and acquisition of "non-produced, non-financial assets". According to IMF guidelines, the outright sale/purchase of such intangible assets must be recorded in the Capital Account, NOT the Current Account.

🧩 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)：
解決這類題目的關鍵在於辨識「交易的性質」。你要問自己兩個問題：
1. 交易雙方是誰？（居民 vs 非居民）—— 題目指明是「國際知名」的導演來港「三星期」，確認了她非居民的身份。
2. 交易涉及什麼？（一般消費品/服務 vs 資本資產）

Pivot Point:
The key to solving this type of question is identifying the "nature of the transaction". You must ask two questions:
1. Who are the transacting parties? (Resident vs Non-resident) — The question specifies an "international" director visiting for "three weeks", confirming her status as a non-resident.
2. What does the transaction involve? (General consumption goods/services vs. Capital assets)

干擾資訊 (Distractors)：
「荷里活電影」這個字眼只是為了進一步確立其外國（非居民）背景，對具體計算公式沒有影響。

Distractors:
The phrase "Hollywood movie" is merely there to further establish her foreign (non-resident) background and does not affect the actual calculation formulas.

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

混淆「版權買賣」與「版權使用費」：
許多學生會錯誤地將 (3) 計入經常帳，因為他們將其與「特許權使用費 (Royalties)」混淆。記住：
- 買賣 / 轉讓版權擁有權 ➔ 資本帳 (Capital Account)
- 支付使用費 / 租用版權 ➔ 經常帳內的初級收入或服務 (Current Account)

Confusing "Sale of Copyright" with "Royalty Fees":
Many students mistakenly include (3) in the Current Account because they confuse it with "Royalties". Remember:
- Outright sale / transfer of copyright ownership ➔ Capital Account
- Paying for the use / renting of a copyright ➔ Current Account (under Primary Income or Services)

🙋‍♂️ Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

問：為什麼買紀念品 (實體物品) 會當作「服務輸出」的一部分？

Q: Why is buying souvenirs (physical goods) often considered part of "Exports of Services"?

答：在 BOP 的編制準則中，非居民在當地旅遊期間購買的所有供自己使用的物品和服務，為求簡化，通常會被統稱為「旅遊 (Travel)」，歸類於服務貿易之下。但無論被界定為貨物還是服務，它都毫無疑問地屬於經常帳 (Current Account)。

A: In BOP compilation guidelines, all goods and services acquired by non-residents for their own use during their visit are often collectively lumped under "Travel" within trade in services for simplicity. However, whether strictly defined as a good or a service, it undeniably falls under the Current Account.

問：如果這位導演在香港買了一層樓呢？

Q: What if the director bought a flat/apartment in Hong Kong?

答：房地產屬於資產投資。非居民在香港購買房地產，會被記錄在金融帳 (Financial Account) 中的直接投資 (Direct Investment) 項目下，因為這涉及金融及實質資產擁有權的跨國轉移，不屬於經常帳。

A: Real estate is an asset investment. A non-resident purchasing property in HK would be recorded in the Financial Account under Direct Investment, as it involves a cross-border transfer of ownership of financial/real assets, not the Current Account.

💡 Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

「無形資產」速記法：
在 DSE 選擇題中，一旦看到「專利 (Patents)」、「版權 (Copyrights)」、「商標 (Trademarks)」的直接買賣，大腦要立刻反射：資本帳 (Capital Account)。用刪除法，只要選項中包含這類資產買賣且題目問經常帳，直接刪掉該選項。這題看到 (3) 即可剔除 B、C、D，三秒鐘選出 A！

"Intangible Assets" Quick Memorization:
In DSE MC questions, the moment you see the outright sale/purchase of "Patents", "Copyrights", or "Trademarks", your brain should instantly trigger: Capital Account. Using the elimination method, if the question asks for the Current Account, cross out any options containing these asset sales. For this question, seeing (3) allows you to instantly eliminate B, C, and D, landing on A in three seconds!
