1. The Question (題目)

下表顯示某國的貨幣基礎和貨幣供應。

日期 貨幣基礎（十億元） 貨幣供應（十億元）
2020年1月1日 150 500
2021年1月1日 150 800

下列哪項／些可以解釋以上數據？
(1) 中央銀行在2020年向公眾購買債券。
(2) 中央銀行在2020年調高貼現率。
(3) 中央銀行在2020年調低法定儲備比率。

A. 只有 (3)
B. 只有 (1)、(2)
C. 只有 (1)、(3)
D. 只有 (2)、(3)

The following table shows the monetary base and money supply of a country.

Date Monetary base ($ billion) Money supply ($ billion)
1 January 2020 150 500
1 January 2021 150 800

Which of the following could explain the above data?
(1) The central bank bought bonds from the public in 2020.
(2) The central bank increased the discount rate in 2020.
(3) The central bank lowered the required reserve ratio in 2020.

A. (3) only
B. (1) and (2) only
C. (1) and (3) only
D. (2) and (3) only

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這道題目，我們必須清楚理解貨幣基礎 (Monetary Base, MB)、貨幣供應 (Money Supply, MS) 以及貨幣乘數 (Money Multiplier, \(m\)) 之間的關係。

To solve this question, we must clearly understand the relationship between the Monetary Base (MB), Money Supply (MS), and the Money Multiplier (\(m\)).
  • 貨幣基礎 (MB): 由公眾持有的法定貨幣 (\(C_p\)) 和銀行的儲備 (\(R\)) 組成。只有中央銀行可以透過公開市場操作 (Open Market Operations) 直接改變貨幣基礎。
    • Monetary Base (MB): Composed of legal tender held by the public (\(C_p\)) and bank reserves (\(R\)). Only the central bank can directly change the monetary base through Open Market Operations.
  • 貨幣供應 (MS): 由公眾持有的法定貨幣 (\(C_p\)) 和銀行存款 (\(D\)) 組成。
    • Money Supply (MS): Composed of legal tender held by the public (\(C_p\)) and bank deposits (\(D\)).
$$ m = \frac{MS}{MB} $$ $$ MS = MB \times m $$

貨幣政策工具 (Monetary Policy Tools):

  • 公開市場操作 (Open Market Operations): 買賣債券會直接改變貨幣基礎 \(MB\)。
    • Open Market Operations: Buying or selling bonds directly changes the Monetary Base \(MB\).
  • 法定儲備比率 (Required Reserve Ratio, RRR) & 貼現率 (Discount Rate): 這些工具不會改變貨幣基礎，但會影響銀行的借貸能力，從而改變貨幣乘數 \(m\)，最終影響貨幣供應 \(MS\)。
    • Required Reserve Ratio (RRR) & Discount Rate: These tools do not change the monetary base, but they affect banks' lending capacity, thereby changing the money multiplier \(m\) and ultimately the money supply \(MS\).

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A. 只有 (3) / (3) only

數據分析 (Data Analysis):
從表格可見，貨幣基礎維持在 150 不變，但貨幣供應由 500 增加至 800。這意味著貨幣乘數 \(m\) 上升了（由 \(500/150 \approx 3.33\) 升至 \(800/150 \approx 5.33\)）。因此，正確的選項必須符合兩個條件：(i) 不改變貨幣基礎；(ii) 增加貨幣乘數。

From the table, the monetary base remains unchanged at 150, but the money supply increases from 500 to 800. This implies that the money multiplier \(m\) has increased (from \(500/150 \approx 3.33\) to \(800/150 \approx 5.33\)). Therefore, the correct option must satisfy two conditions: (i) it does not change the monetary base; (ii) it increases the money multiplier.

(1) 中央銀行向公眾購買債券 (The central bank bought bonds from the public):
❌ 錯誤 (False): 央行向公眾購買債券，會向市場注入資金，直接導致貨幣基礎 \(MB\) 增加。但題目數據顯示 \(MB\) 維持不變。因此，這不能解釋以上數據。

❌ False: When the central bank buys bonds from the public, it injects money into the market, directly increasing the monetary base \(MB\). However, the data shows \(MB\) remains unchanged. Thus, this cannot explain the data.

(2) 中央銀行調高貼現率 (The central bank increased the discount rate):
❌ 錯誤 (False): 調高貼現率會增加商業銀行向央行借款的成本。銀行為了防範流動性風險，會持有更多超額儲備 (Excess Reserves)。這會降低貨幣乘數 \(m\)，導致貨幣供應 \(MS\) 下降。這與題目中 \(MS\) 上升的數據矛盾。

❌ False: Increasing the discount rate raises the cost for commercial banks to borrow from the central bank. To prevent liquidity risks, banks will hold more excess reserves. This decreases the money multiplier \(m\), leading to a decrease in the money supply \(MS\). This contradicts the data where \(MS\) increases.

(3) 中央銀行調低法定儲備比率 (The central bank lowered the required reserve ratio):
✅ 正確 (True): 調低法定儲備比率不會改變貨幣基礎 \(MB\)。但這會釋放更多可用儲備，讓銀行可以創造更多貸款。貨幣乘數 \(m\) 上升，導致貨幣供應 \(MS\) 增加。這完美契合表格中的數據（\(MB\) 不變，\(MS\) 上升）。

✅ True: Lowering the required reserve ratio does not change the monetary base \(MB\). However, it frees up more reserves, allowing banks to create more loans. The money multiplier \(m\) increases, leading to an increase in the money supply \(MS\). This perfectly matches the data in the table (\(MB\) unchanged, \(MS\) increases).

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point):
這題的「破局點」在於觀察到 貨幣基礎 (Monetary Base) 是常數 (150)。一旦你發現 \(MB\) 沒有改變，你就可以立刻排除任何會改變 \(MB\) 的政策。

The "pivot point" of this question is observing that the Monetary Base is constant (150). Once you notice that \(MB\) hasn't changed, you can immediately eliminate any policy that changes \(MB\).

干擾資訊 (Distractors):
具體的數字（500 變 800）其實不需用來進行複雜的計算。你只需要知道趨勢是「上升」即可。不需要精確計算出貨幣乘數由 3.33 變成 5.33。

The specific numbers (500 to 800) don't actually require complex calculations. You only need to know the trend is an "increase". There is no need to precisely calculate that the money multiplier changed from 3.33 to 5.33.

5. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧 (The 10-Second Rule):

  1. 看圖表第一列：貨幣基礎 (MB) 沒變。
  2. 回想知識點：公開市場操作 (買/賣債券) 必然會改變貨幣基礎。
  3. 結論：選項 (1) 必定錯誤。
  4. 看答案選項：包含 (1) 的 B 和 C 直接被淘汰。剩下 A 和 D。
  5. 再看圖表第二列：貨幣供應 (MS) 上升。
  6. 判斷：調高貼現率會令 MS 下降 (緊縮政策)，調低法定儲備會令 MS 上升 (擴張政策)。所以 (2) 錯，(3) 對。答案選 A。
  1. Look at the first column: Monetary Base (MB) is unchanged.
  2. Recall knowledge: Open Market Operations (buying/selling bonds) always change the monetary base.
  3. Conclusion: Statement (1) must be wrong.
  4. Look at the options: Cross out B and C because they contain (1). You are left with A and D.
  5. Look at the second column: Money Supply (MS) increased.
  6. Evaluate: Increasing the discount rate decreases MS (contractionary), while lowering the required reserve ratio increases MS (expansionary). So (2) is wrong, (3) is right. The answer is A.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼中央銀行購買債券不會改變貨幣乘數？
Why doesn't the central bank buying bonds change the money multiplier?

A: 嚴格來說，央行買債券主要且直接的影響是增加貨幣基礎 (MB)。雖然在現實中，這可能間接影響銀行的儲備比例，但在 DSE 經濟學的標準模型中，我們假設公開市場操作改變的是 \(MB\)，而法定儲備比率 (RRR) 才是決定最大貨幣乘數 \(m\) 的核心因素。因為題目中 \(MB\) 根本沒變，所以我們連考慮乘數變化的需要都沒有，直接判定 (1) 錯誤。

A: Strictly speaking, the primary and direct effect of the central bank buying bonds is an increase in the Monetary Base (MB). While in reality it might indirectly affect banks' reserve ratios, in the standard DSE Economics model, we assume open market operations change \(MB\), whereas the Required Reserve Ratio (RRR) is the core factor determining the maximum money multiplier \(m\). Because \(MB\) didn't change at all in the question, we don't even need to consider multiplier effects to immediately rule out statement (1).
