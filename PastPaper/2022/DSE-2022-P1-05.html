HKDSE Economics Topic Analysis

Topic: Types of Business Ownership (企業擁有權) Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆ Key Concepts: Sole Proprietorship, Partnership, Specialisation, Liability

2. The Question (題目)

下列哪項是將一家廠商由獨資經營轉成合夥的可能原因？

Which of the following is a possible reason for turning a firm from a sole proprietorship to a partnership?

  1. 該廠商能賺取更多利潤。
    The firm can get more profit.
  2. 該廠商能享有範疇更廣的專門化。
    The firm can enjoy a wider scope of specialisation.
  3. 該廠商會成為獨立法人。
    The firm will become an independent legal entity.
  4. 由於所有合夥人均承擔無限債務責任，業務風險會減低。
    Business risk will be reduced as all partners have unlimited liability.

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這道題目，我們必須了解獨資經營 (Sole Proprietorship)與合夥 (Partnership)的特徵差異。兩者均屬於非有限公司，因此都不是獨立法人 (Not independent legal entities)，且東主通常需要承擔無限債務責任 (Unlimited liability)。

To answer this question, we must understand the characteristic differences between a Sole Proprietorship and a Partnership. Both are unincorporated businesses, meaning they are not independent legal entities, and the owners generally bear unlimited liability.

轉為合夥的主要經濟誘因之一是勞力分工 (Division of labour)。當有多於一名東主時，他們可以根據各自的專長（如一人負責財務，另一人負責營銷）來分工，這就是專門化。

One of the main economic incentives to form a partnership is the division of labour. When there is more than one owner, they can divide tasks based on their expertise (e.g., one handles finance, another handles marketing), which is known as specialisation.

在經濟學中，我們分析廠商擴張時，經常會考慮利潤函數：

In economics, when analyzing firm expansion, we often consider the profit function:

$$ \pi = TR - TC = (P \times Q) - (TFC + TVC) $$

雖然合夥可能會透過提高產量 \( Q \) 來增加總收益 \( TR \)，但同時總成本 \( TC \) 也可能上升，因此利潤 \( \pi \) 是否必然增加屬未知之數。

Although a partnership might increase total revenue \( TR \) by increasing output \( Q \), total costs \( TC \) might also rise simultaneously. Therefore, whether profit \( \pi \) will definitely increase remains uncertain.

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option B

A (錯誤): 轉為合夥可以籌集更多資金或擴大規模，但並不保證一定能賺取更多利潤。若經營不善，虧損也可能增加。

A (Incorrect): Turning into a partnership allows raising more capital or expanding the scale, but it does not guarantee more profit. If poorly managed, losses could also increase.

B (正確): 獨資經營只有一名東主，難以在管理上實行分工。合夥則擁有多名合夥人，他們可以按各自的才能與專業知識分工（例如甲負責採購，乙負責會計），從而享有範疇更廣的專門化。

B (Correct): A sole proprietorship has only one owner, making management division of labor difficult. A partnership has multiple partners who can divide tasks based on their talents and expertise (e.g., Partner A does procurement, Partner B does accounting), thus enjoying a wider scope of specialisation.

C (錯誤): 獨資經營和合夥均屬於「非法人團體」，在法律上東主與企業被視為同一體。只有「有限公司 (Limited Companies)」才是獨立法人。

C (Incorrect): Both sole proprietorships and partnerships are "unincorporated businesses", meaning the owner and the business are treated as the same entity legally. Only "Limited Companies" are independent legal entities.

D (錯誤): 無限債務責任 (Unlimited liability) 是一個缺點。雖然風險可以由多名合夥人共同分擔，但整體的「業務風險」並不會因為無限債務責任而「減低」。如果公司破產，合夥人的私人財產依然會被用來償還債務。

D (Incorrect): Unlimited liability is a disadvantage. Although the risk can be shared among multiple partners, the overall "business risk" is not "reduced" *because* of unlimited liability. If the company goes bankrupt, partners' personal assets will still be seized to repay debts.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point): 找出「獨資」與「合夥」在經濟特徵上的絕對差異。獨資是一個人，合夥是多個人。多人合作的直接經濟優勢就是可以「分工 / 專門化」。

Pivot Point: Identify the absolute difference in economic characteristics between a "sole proprietorship" and a "partnership". A sole proprietorship is one person; a partnership is multiple people. The direct economic advantage of multiple people working together is "division of labour / specialisation".

干擾項分析 (Distractors): 選項D最具欺騙性。題目故意將「無限債務責任」和「風險減低」放在一起。學生容易誤以為「多人分擔 = 風險減少」。請記住，分擔負債並不等於降低了商業經營的整體風險。

Distractors: Option D is the most deceptive. The question deliberately pairs "unlimited liability" with "reduced risk". Students easily mistake "shared by many = reduced risk". Remember, sharing a burden does not equal lowering the overall risk of business operations.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 概念混淆：法人地位 (Legal Entity Status)
    很多學生以為只要公司有幾個老闆，或者有商業登記，就是獨立法人。記住：在香港DSE課程中，只有私人有限公司及公眾有限公司擁有獨立法人地位。

    Concept Confusion: Legal Entity Status
    Many students think that if a company has multiple owners or business registration, it is an independent legal entity. Remember: In the HKDSE syllabus, ONLY private limited companies and public limited companies have independent legal entity status.

  • 過度推論 (Over-inference)
    學生常以為「擴張 = 賺錢」。經濟學中，我們只能確定擴張會帶來「可能增加的收益」或「規模經濟」，但絕不能保證「必定賺取更多利潤」。

    Over-inference
    Students often assume "expansion = making money". In economics, we can only be certain that expansion brings "potential increased revenue" or "economies of scale", but it NEVER guarantees "getting more profit".

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q1: 老師，選項D說「所有合夥人均承擔無限債務責任」，難道合夥企業不能有有限責任合夥人嗎？ (Can't a partnership have limited liability partners?)

A: 在普通合夥 (General Partnership) 中，所有合夥人都承擔無限債務責任。雖然存在有限合夥 (Limited Partnership)，但DSE課程通常預設討論的是普通合夥。更重要的是，即使是有限合夥，選項D的邏輯依然錯誤：「無限債務責任」本身絕不會導致「業務風險減低」。

A: In a General Partnership, all partners bear unlimited liability. While Limited Partnerships exist, the DSE syllabus generally assumes we are discussing general partnerships by default. More importantly, even if it were a limited partnership, Option D's logic remains flawed: "unlimited liability" itself will never lead to "reduced business risk".

Q2: 為什麼A選項是錯的？轉為合夥不就是為了賺錢嗎？ (Why is A wrong? Isn't turning into a partnership for the sake of making money?)

A: 動機（想賺錢）不等於結果（能賺錢）。選項A的寫法是「該廠商『能』賺取更多利潤」(The firm 'can get' more profit)，這是一個肯定的結果陳述。在經濟學MC題中，這類過於絕對的陳述通常是錯誤的，因為市場環境千變萬化，轉為合夥也可能因為意見不合導致虧損。

A: Motive (wanting to make money) does not equal outcome (actually making money). Option A states "The firm 'can get' more profit", which is a definitive outcome statement. In Economics MCQs, such overly absolute statements are usually incorrect because market conditions change, and a partnership could also lead to losses due to disagreements.

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺法 (Elimination Method):

  • 看到「獨立法人 (Independent legal entity)」，立刻聯想「有限公司」。合夥不是有限公司，秒刪 C。
  • 看到「必定賺取利潤」或類似的絕對詞語，這在商業世界是不現實的，秒刪 A。
  • 看到「無限債務責任」被描述為能「減低風險」這種荒謬邏輯，秒刪 D。
  • 剩下的 B 就是唯一合理的經濟學優勢（資源與技能互補）。

Shortcut Method:

  • See "Independent legal entity", immediately think "Limited Company". Partnership is not, eliminate C.
  • See "can get more profit" or absolute terms, which is unrealistic in business, eliminate A.
  • See "unlimited liability" described as "reducing risk" (absurd logic), eliminate D.
  • The remaining B is the only logical economic advantage (complementary resources and skills).
