HKDSE Economics Question Analysis

Topic / 課題: Basic Economic Problems (Resource Allocation) / 基本經濟問題（資源分配）
Difficulty / 難度: ★★☆☆☆
Keywords / 關鍵字: What to produce, How to produce, For whom to produce

The Question / 題目

中文 參考以下有關新冠肺炎疫苗接種計劃的資料。

  • 所有居民可以免費參加該計劃
  • 政府在一些政府大樓設立了疫苗接種中心，並調派公務員往中心工作。

以上資料和經濟學中的 ____________________ 問題有關。

(1) 「生產什麼」
(2) 「如何生產」
(3) 「為誰生產」

ENG Refer to the following information of the COVID-19 vaccination programme.

  • All residents are eligible for joining the programme free-of-charge.
  • The government has set up vaccination centres in some government buildings and deployed civil servants to work in these centres.

The above information is related to the ______________ question(s) in Economics.

(1) ‘what to produce’
(2) ‘how to produce’
(3) ‘for whom to produce’

Options / 選項:
A. (3) only / 只有 (3)
B. (1) and (2) only / 只有 (1)、(2)
C. (1) and (3) only / 只有 (1)、(3)
D. (2) and (3) only / 只有 (2)、(3)

Concept Review / 概念重溫

In Economics, every society faces scarcity and must answer three basic questions regarding resource allocation:
在經濟學中，每個社會都面對稀少性，因此必須回答關於資源分配的三個基本問題：

  1. What to produce (生產什麼):
    Deciding the types and quantities of goods/services to produce.
    決定生產甚麼種類的物品或服務，以及生產多少（數量）。
  2. How to produce (如何生產):
    Deciding the method of production or the combination of inputs (e.g., Labor \(L\) vs. Capital \(K\)).
    決定生產方法或投入要素的組合（例如：勞力 \(L\) 對 資本 \(K\)）。
  3. For whom to produce (為誰生產):
    Deciding the distribution of goods. Who gets to consume them? (e.g., Price mechanism vs. Queuing/First-come-first-served).
    決定物品的分配。誰可以享用這些物品？（例如：價格機制 對 排隊/先到先得）。

Answer & Detailed Analysis / 答案與詳細分析

Correct Option: D (2) & (3) only

Step 1: Analyze Statement 1 (分析陳述一)

"All residents are eligible for joining the programme free-of-charge." / 「所有居民可以免費參加該計劃」

中文 這句話說明了誰有資格獲得疫苗（所有居民）以及分配的準則（免費）。這涉及物品的分配 (Distribution)。在市場經濟中，通常由「價格」決定誰能獲得物品（付得起錢的人）；但在這裡，政府決定「免費」並讓「所有居民」獲得。這解決了「為誰生產」 (For whom to produce) 的問題。

ENG This statement specifies who is eligible to receive the vaccine (all residents) and the criterion for allocation (free of charge). This concerns the distribution of the good. In a market economy, "price" usually determines who gets the good (those who can pay); here, the government decides it is "free" and for "all residents". This addresses the problem of 'for whom to produce'.

Conclusion: (3) is correct.

Step 2: Analyze Statement 2 (分析陳述二)

"The government has set up vaccination centres in some government buildings and deployed civil servants to work in these centres." / 「政府在一些政府大樓設立了疫苗接種中心，並調派公務員往中心工作。」

中文 這句話描述了疫苗接種服務是如何被提供出來的。它涉及了生產要素的組合：使用了「政府大樓」（資本/土地）和「公務員」（勞力）。這是在選擇生產方法 (Method of production)。這解決了「如何生產」 (How to produce) 的問題。

ENG This statement describes how the vaccination service is provided. It involves the combination of inputs: using "government buildings" (capital/land) and "civil servants" (labor). This is a choice regarding the method of production. This addresses the problem of 'how to produce'.

Conclusion: (2) is correct.

Step 3: Why not ‘what to produce’? (為什麼不是 「生產什麼」？)

中文 題目雖然提到了「疫苗接種計劃」，這暗示了社會決定生產疫苗。但是，題目提供的具體資料 (Specific Information) 並沒有討論「我們應該生產疫苗還是口罩？」或者「我們應該生產 100 萬劑還是 200 萬劑？」。資料的重點在於運作模式（公務員、政府大樓）和分配方式（免費、全民）。因此，資料主要與 (2) 和 (3) 相關。

ENG Although the question mentions a "vaccination programme," which implies a decision to produce vaccines was made, the specific information provided does not discuss "Should we produce vaccines or masks?" or "Should we produce 1 million or 2 million doses?". The focus of the data is on the operational mode (civil servants, buildings) and the allocation method (free, universal). Therefore, the data specifically relates to (2) and (3).

Problem Decomposition / 題目拆解

To solve this type of question, look for the "Pivot Words" (關鍵字眼):

Concept / 概念 Pivot Words (Look for these) / 關鍵字眼（尋找這些）
What to produce
(生產什麼)		 Quantity, Numbers (e.g., 1000 units), Choice between Product A vs Product B.
數量、數字（如 1000 單位）、產品 A 與產品 B 之間的選擇。
How to produce
(如何生產)		 Method, Technology, Labor vs Capital, Machine vs Handmade, Government vs Private resources.
方法、技術、勞力對資本、機器對手工、政府對私人資源。
For whom to produce
(為誰生產)		 Price, Free, Rich vs Poor, Age groups, Residents, Queuing, Lottery.
價格、免費、富人對窮人、年齡組別、居民、排隊、抽籤。

Common Mistakes & Traps / 常犯錯誤與陷阱

⚠️ The "Existence" Trap (「存在」的陷阱)

中文 很多學生認為只要題目提到某種產品（如疫苗），就一定涉及「生產什麼」。這是錯的。必須看題目提供的描述重點。如果描述的是「用什麼去造」或「誰能拿到」，那就是「如何」或「為誰」。

ENG Many students think that as long as a product (like vaccines) is mentioned, it must involve "what to produce". This is wrong. You must look at the focus of the description. If the description is about "what is used to make it" or "who gets it", it is "how" or "for whom".

Exam Trick / 考試技巧:
Identify the specific bullet points provided in the question. Match each bullet point to one concept.
將題目中的每一點資料逐一配對。
  • Bullet 1: "Free" \(\rightarrow\) Allocation \(\rightarrow\) For Whom
  • Bullet 2: "Civil Servants" \(\rightarrow\) Input \(\rightarrow\) How
