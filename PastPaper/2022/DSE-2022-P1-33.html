The diagram below shows the money demand and money supply curves of an economy. The original equilibrium point is at E.

Suppose most of the employers in the economy change the payment of salaries from monthly to bi-weekly. At the same time, the commercial banks tend to hold more excess reserves. The new equilibrium point will be at _____________.

3. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

I. 貨幣需求 (Money Demand, $M_d$)

市民持有貨幣主要是為了應付日常交易。當發薪頻率（Frequency of income receipt）增加（例如由每月一次改為每兩星期一次），市民不需為了一整個月的開支而長期持有大量現金。這會導致平均現金持有量下降，從而使交易性貨幣需求減少。圖表上，$M_d$ 曲線會向左/下方移動。

People hold money primarily for daily transactions. When the frequency of income receipt increases (e.g., from monthly to bi-weekly), individuals do not need to hold a large cash balance to cover a whole month's expenses. This lowers the average cash holding, leading to a decrease in the transaction demand for money. Graphically, the $M_d$ curve shifts to the left/downward.

II. 貨幣供應 (Money Supply, $M_s$)

貨幣供應受銀行的借貸活動影響。當商業銀行傾向持有更多超額儲備 (Excess Reserves) 時，它們會減少借出貸款。這會削弱貨幣創造的過程，導致貨幣供應減少。圖表上，$M_s$ 曲線會向左移動。

Money supply is affected by banks' lending activities. When commercial banks tend to hold more excess reserves, they lend out less money. This weakens the money creation process, resulting in a decrease in the money supply. Graphically, the $M_s$ curve shifts to the left.

$$ \text{Money Multiplier (貨幣乘數)} = \frac{1}{\text{Required Reserve Ratio} + \text{Excess Reserve Ratio}} $$

4. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): B

第一步：分析貨幣需求的改變 (Analyzing $M_d$ Shift)

「由每月支付改為每兩星期支付」代表發薪頻率增加。因此，貨幣需求 $M_d$ 下降。在圖中，原來的需求曲線是中間的 $M_{d1}$。需求下降代表曲線向下平移至 $M_{d2}$（最底部的曲線）。

"Change from monthly to bi-weekly" implies an increase in payment frequency. Therefore, money demand $M_d$ decreases. The original demand curve is the middle one, $M_{d1}$. A decrease means the curve shifts downward to $M_{d2}$ (the bottom curve).

第二步：分析貨幣供應的改變 (Analyzing $M_s$ Shift)

「銀行傾向持有更多超額儲備」代表貸款減少，貨幣供應 $M_s$ 下降。在圖中，原來的供應曲線是中間的 $M_{S1}$。供應下降代表曲線向左平移至 $M_{S2}$（最左邊的垂直線）。

"Banks tend to hold more excess reserves" implies fewer loans are made, so money supply $M_s$ decreases. The original supply curve is the middle one, $M_{S1}$. A decrease means the curve shifts to the left to $M_{S2}$ (the leftmost vertical line).

第三步：尋找新均衡點 (Finding the New Equilibrium)

新的均衡點必須是新的貨幣需求曲線（$M_{d2}$）與新的貨幣供應曲線（$M_{S2}$）的相交點。觀察圖表，最底部的曲線 $M_{d2}$ 與最左邊的垂直線 $M_{S2}$ 相交於 點 B。因此，B 是正確答案。

The new equilibrium must be the intersection of the new money demand curve ($M_{d2}$) and the new money supply curve ($M_{S2}$). Looking at the graph, the bottom curve $M_{d2}$ intersects with the leftmost vertical line $M_{S2}$ exactly at Point B. Hence, B is the correct answer.

錯誤選項分析 (Analysis of Incorrect Options)

  • A: 位於 $M_{d3}$ 及 $M_{S2}$。這代表貨幣供應減少，但貨幣需求增加（錯誤）。
  • C: 位於 $M_{d3}$ 及 $M_{S3}$。這代表貨幣供應增加，且貨幣需求增加（雙重錯誤）。
  • D: 位於 $M_{d2}$ 及 $M_{S3}$。這代表貨幣需求減少，但貨幣供應增加（錯誤）。

5. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

要破解這類雙重移動（Double shifts）的題目，你需要找出兩個「支點（Pivot Points）」：

  • 支點一：「每月」$\rightarrow$「每兩星期」。這是一個典型的陷阱，很多同學以為出糧多了一次等於有錢了，其實這只影響「頻率」。頻率上升必然導致持現需求下降。
  • 支點二：「超額儲備」。你要立刻聯想到貨幣乘數（Money multiplier）。銀行鎖住資金不借出，市面流通的貨幣自然減少。
  • 圖表判讀：不要被密集的線條混淆。先確認原點 E 位於 $M_{S1}$ 及 $M_{d1}$ 的交界。然後獨立找出新曲線 $M_{S2}$（左移）和 $M_{d2}$（下移），它們的唯一交點就是答案。

To crack double-shift questions, identify the two "Pivot Points": First, "monthly to bi-weekly" is a classic trap. It doesn't mean more wealth; it means higher frequency, which strictly lowers cash holding demand. Second, "excess reserves" relates directly to the money multiplier. If banks hoard cash, money supply shrinks. When reading the graph, isolate the new curves $M_{S2}$ and $M_{d2}$—their intersection gives the answer.

6. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

🚨 陷阱一：誤解「每兩星期發薪」的影響
最常見的錯誤是學生認為「出糧次數增加 ＝ 貨幣需求增加」，從而選擇點 A。請記住，貨幣需求是指你口袋裡或活期戶口裡閒置的現金。如果你每兩週就能補充一次資金，你就不需要一次過把一整個月的錢都提取出來帶在身上，因此平均貨幣需求量是下跌的。

🚨 Trap 1: Misinterpreting "Bi-weekly Payment"
The most common error is thinking "paid more often = demand more money," leading to Option A. Remember, money demand is about holding idle cash. If you replenish your wallet every two weeks, you don't need to hoard a whole month's worth of cash at once. Thus, average money demand drops.

7. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q1: 如果題目改為「普遍使用信用卡購物」，這對圖表會有什麼影響？

A1: 結果會是一樣的！廣泛使用信用卡會減少市民對現金的依賴，導致交易性貨幣需求下降。所以 $M_d$ 曲線同樣會由 $M_{d1}$ 向下移至 $M_{d2}$。

A1: The result would be the same! The widespread use of credit cards reduces people's reliance on cash, leading to a decrease in transaction demand for money. The $M_d$ curve would still shift downward from $M_{d1}$ to $M_{d2}$.

Q2: 為什麼點 B 的名義利率（Nominal interest rate）比起點 E 下降了？不是說貨幣供應減少會令利率上升嗎？

A2: 這是個非常好的觀察！單獨來看，貨幣供應減少（左移至 $M_{S2}$）的確會推高利率；但同時，貨幣需求也在減少（下移至 $M_{d2}$），這會拉低利率。因為在圖中點 B 低於點 E 的水平，這暗示了貨幣需求的跌幅大於貨幣供應的跌幅，所以最終淨效應是利率下降。

A2: Excellent observation! Viewed in isolation, a decrease in money supply pushes interest rates up. However, the simultaneous decrease in money demand pulls interest rates down. Since Point B is lower than Point E vertically, it implies the magnitude of the decrease in $M_d$ is greater than the decrease in $M_s$, resulting in a net decrease in the nominal interest rate.

8. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

10秒排除法 (10-Second Elimination Method):

  1. 讀完「改為每兩星期發薪」$\rightarrow$ 知道 $M_d$ 減少（向下移至 $M_{d2}$）。
  2. 新均衡點必定落在最底部的線 $M_{d2}$ 上。看圖可見，只有點 B 和點 D 在這條線上。直接淘汰 A 和 C。
  3. 讀完「銀行增加超額儲備」$\rightarrow$ 知道 $M_s$ 減少（向左移至 $M_{S2}$）。
  4. 在新剩下的 B 和 D 之中，只有 B 位於左邊的 $M_{S2}$ 上。答案必定是 B！

Read the first event -> $M_d$ drops ($M_{d2}$). The answer must be on the bottom curve, leaving only points B and D. Read the second event -> $M_s$ drops ($M_{S2}$). Between B and D, only point B is on the leftmost vertical line. Done!
