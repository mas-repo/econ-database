HKDSE Economics Study Guide

Topic / 課題: 宏觀經濟學：總需求與總供應模型 (Macroeconomics: AD-AS Model)

Difficulty / 難度: ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts / 關鍵概念: 緊縮性裂口 (Deflationary Gap), 長期均衡 (Long-run Equilibrium), 技術進步 (Technological Progress), 資源自理機制 (Self-correction Mechanism)

1. The Question (題目)

細閱下圖。

該經濟原初的產出水平是 \(Y_0\)。假設科學家發明了新的煉油技術。長期而言，

  1. 物價水平會低於 \(P_0\)。
  2. 產出水平會高於 \(Y_1\)。
  3. 總需求曲線會向右移以達至均衡。

A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

Study the following diagram.

The initial output level of the economy is \(Y_0\). Suppose scientists have invented a new oil-refining technology. In the long run,

  1. the price level will be lower than \(P_0\).
  2. the output level will be higher than \(Y_1\).
  3. the aggregate demand curve will shift to the right to attain equilibrium.

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這道題目，我們需要結合兩個核心宏觀經濟概念：緊縮性裂口 (Deflationary Gap) 的自我修正 以及 技術進步對長期總供應 (LRAS) 的影響。

  • 緊縮性裂口： 當目前的均衡產出 \(Y_0\) 低於充分就業產出 \(Y_1\) 時，經濟存在緊縮性裂口。由於資源未被充分利用（例如失業率上升），要素價格（如工資）在長期會下降，導致短期總供應曲線 (SRAS) 向右移。
  • 技術進步： 新技術的發明提高了生產力，這意味著經濟體的潛在產出增加了。因此，長期總供應曲線 (LRAS) 會向右移至一個新的水平（大於 \(Y_1\)）。

To solve this problem, we need to combine two core macroeconomic concepts: the self-correction of a Deflationary Gap and the impact of technological progress on Long-Run Aggregate Supply (LRAS).

  • Deflationary Gap: When the current equilibrium output \(Y_0\) is below the full-employment output \(Y_1\), a deflationary gap exists. Because resources are underutilized (e.g., rising unemployment), factor prices (like wages) will fall in the long run, causing the Short-Run Aggregate Supply (SRAS) curve to shift to the right.
  • Technological Progress: The invention of new technology increases productivity, meaning the economy's potential output has increased. Therefore, the Long-Run Aggregate Supply (LRAS) curve will shift to the right to a new level (greater than \(Y_1\)).

Condition for Deflationary Gap:

$$ Y_e < Y_f \implies \text{Deflationary Gap (緊縮性裂口)} $$

Long-Run Self Correction (Without Tech Progress):

$$ \text{Input Prices} \downarrow \implies SRAS \rightarrow \text{Right} \implies P \downarrow , Y \uparrow \text{to } Y_f $$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是：選項 A
The correct option is: Option A
分析 (1)：正確。 原本經濟處於 \(Y_0 < Y_1\) 的狀態。即使沒有技術進步，單純透過長期自我修正機制（要素價格下降，SRAS 右移），新的長期均衡點會落在 AD 與原有 LRAS (\(Y_1\)) 的交點，此時的物價已經低於 \(P_0\)。再加上技術進步令 LRAS 進一步向右移，SRAS 也會隨之右移以達到新的長期均衡，沿著向下傾斜的 AD 曲線，最終的物價水平必定會比 \(P_0\) 更低。
Analysis of (1): Correct. The economy initially operates at \(Y_0 < Y_1\). Even without technological progress, through the long-run self-correction mechanism (factor prices fall, SRAS shifts right), the new long-run equilibrium would be at the intersection of AD and the original LRAS (\(Y_1\)), where the price level is already lower than \(P_0\). Coupled with technological progress shifting LRAS further to the right, SRAS shifts right to reach the new long-run equilibrium. Moving along the downward-sloping AD curve, the final price level must definitely be lower than \(P_0\).
分析 (2)：正確。 「新的煉油技術」代表技術進步，這會提升國家的整體生產力。因此，代表潛在產出的長期總供應曲線 (LRAS) 會向右平移。新的充分就業產出水平必定大於原來的 \(Y_1\)。
Analysis of (2): Correct. "A new oil-refining technology" represents technological progress, which enhances the overall productivity of the country. Therefore, the Long-Run Aggregate Supply (LRAS) curve, which represents potential output, will shift to the right. The new full-employment output level must be strictly greater than the original \(Y_1\).
分析 (3)：錯誤。 達至長期均衡的過程是由於總供應 (Aggregate Supply) 發生變化（包括要素價格下跌令 SRAS 右移，以及技術進步令 LRAS 右移）。在此過程中，總需求曲線 (AD) 本身並沒有移動，經濟只是沿著 (move along) 原有的 AD 曲線移動至新的均衡點。
Analysis of (3): Incorrect. The process of attaining long-run equilibrium is driven by changes in Aggregate Supply (including falling factor prices shifting SRAS rightward, and technological progress shifting LRAS rightward). In this process, the Aggregate Demand (AD) curve itself does not shift; the economy merely moves along the existing AD curve to the new equilibrium point.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

尋找「破局點」(Pivot Point)：

這題的關鍵在於看懂圖表隱藏的兩個資訊：

  1. 圖中目前的均衡點在 \(Y_0\)，而 LRAS 在 \(Y_1\)，這暗示了經濟處於衰退狀態 (緊縮性裂口)。即使沒有新技術，物價在長期也注定要下跌（因為 SRAS 會向右移）。
  2. 題目文字提到「科學家發明了新技術」，這是外在衝擊 (Shock)。這告訴我們 LRAS 本身也不能停留在 \(Y_1\)，必須向右移。

Identifying the "Pivot Point":

The key to this question lies in decoding two hidden pieces of information in the diagram:

  1. The current equilibrium is at \(Y_0\), while LRAS is at \(Y_1\), implying the economy is in a recessionary state (deflationary gap). Even without new technology, the price level is destined to fall in the long run (because SRAS will shift right).
  2. The text states "scientists have invented a new technology," which is an external shock. This tells us that LRAS itself cannot stay at \(Y_1\) and must shift to the right.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 混淆「總需求量的改變」與「總需求曲線的移動」： 很多同學看到「物價下跌，產出增加」，就誤以為是總需求 (AD) 增加（向右移）。實際上，這是總供應增加導致物價下跌，消費者因此購買更多（沿著 AD 曲線向下移動），AD 曲線的位置並沒有改變。
  • 忽略了長期的雙重效應： 部分同學只記得技術進步令 LRAS 右移，卻忘記了原本的經濟存在緊縮性裂口，SRAS 自身也會因為要素價格下跌而右移。這兩個因素共同推低了物價水平。
  • Confusing "change in quantity of AD" with "shift in AD curve": Many students see "price falls, output increases" and mistakenly think Aggregate Demand (AD) has increased (shifted right). In reality, an increase in Aggregate Supply lowers prices, prompting consumers to buy more (moving downwards along the AD curve). The position of the AD curve hasn't changed.
  • Ignoring the dual effect in the long run: Some students only remember that technological progress shifts LRAS right, forgetting that the economy initially had a deflationary gap, meaning SRAS will also shift right due to falling factor prices. Both factors work together to push the price level down.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 點解總需求 (AD) 曲線唔會向右移？新技術唔係會吸引投資咩？

A: 這是個很好的問題！在現實中，新技術確實可能刺激投資 (Investment, I)，從而令 AD 短期內右移。但在標準的 DSE 宏觀經濟學長期模型中，如果題目沒有特別指明投資需求的增加，我們通常將「技術進步」純粹視作供應側 (Supply-side) 的衝擊，主要影響 LRAS 及 SRAS。即使 AD 真的有輕微右移，只要右移幅度不及 AS 右移的幅度，物價最終依然會跌穿 \(P_0\)。DSE 考試中，除非題目明確表示投資意欲上升，否則不要自行假設 AD 移動。

Q: Why doesn't the Aggregate Demand (AD) curve shift right? Doesn't new technology attract investment?

A: That's a great question! In reality, new technology might stimulate Investment (I), causing AD to shift right in the short run. However, in the standard DSE macroeconomic long-run model, unless the question specifically mentions an increase in investment demand, we strictly treat "technological progress" as a supply-side shock, affecting mainly LRAS and SRAS. Even if AD did shift slightly right, as long as the shift is smaller than the AS shift, the price would still fall below \(P_0\). For DSE purposes, do not assume AD shifts unless the prompt explicitly implies rising investment desires.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

「消去法」秒殺技巧 (Elimination Method)：

在考場上，你可以不用完全分析所有選項就能找到答案。當你讀到句子 (3) 時，你應該立即察覺到問題：這題探討的是「技術進步（供應面）」以及「緊縮性裂口的自我修正」，這兩者都只會引起總供應 (AS) 的移動。既然沒有需求面的衝擊（如政府增加開支、減稅等），AD 曲線根本不會為了「達至均衡」而移動。

既然 (3) 必定是錯的，你可以立即劃去選項 B、C 和 D。剩下的唯一可能答案就只有 A！你甚至不需要去深入思考物價水平的最終落點，就能拿到這題的分數。

The Elimination Shortcut:

In the exam, you don't always need to fully analyze every statement to find the answer. When you read statement (3), alarm bells should ring: this question is about "technological progress (supply-side)" and "self-correction of a deflationary gap," both of which only cause Aggregate Supply (AS) to shift. Since there are no demand-side shocks (like increased government spending or tax cuts), the AD curve will NOT shift just to "attain equilibrium."

Since (3) is definitively false, you can immediately cross out options B, C, and D. The only remaining possible answer is A! You can secure the mark without even deeply contemplating the final price level.
