[繁體中文]
在粵港澳大灣區發展規劃綱要中，香港是國際金融中心和運輸中心，澳門是旅遊休閒中心，廣州是國際商貿中心。

A. 簡單 …… 每個城市各有自己的比較優勢
B. 簡單 …… 每個城市都涉及不同生產階段
C. 區域 …… 每個城市攜手合作共建大灣區

[English]
In the Outline Development Plan for the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area, Hong Kong is an international financial and transportation centre; Macao is a leisure and tourism centre; Guangzhou is an international commercial centre.
This is an example of __________ division of labour because _______________.

A. simple …… each city has its own comparative advantage
B. simple …… each city engages in different production stages
C. regional …… multiple cities cooperate to build the Greater Bay Area
D. regional …… each city has its own specialty of production

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

Types of Division of Labour (分工的種類)

在經濟學中，分工主要分為三大類：

In economics, division of labour is mainly classified into three types:

  • 簡單分工 (Simple Division of Labour): 不同工人專門生產不同的完整物品。例如：甲造鞋，乙造衣服。
    Different workers specialize in producing different complete goods. E.g., Worker A makes shoes, Worker B makes clothes.
  • 複雜分工 (Complex Division of Labour): 不同工人專門負責同一物品的不同生產階段。例如：工廠流水線。
    Different workers specialize in different production stages of the same good. E.g., Assembly line.
  • 區域/地域分工 (Regional/Territorial Division of Labour): 不同地區或國家根據其比較優勢，專門生產不同的物品或服務。
    Different regions or countries specialize in producing different goods or services based on their comparative advantages.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: D

Option A: Incorrect (錯誤)

雖然各城市確實有其比較優勢，但題目描述的是「城市（地區）」之間的分工，而不是個人之間的「簡單分工」。

Although each city indeed has its comparative advantage, the question describes division of labour among "cities (regions)", not "simple division of labour" among individuals.

Option B: Incorrect (錯誤)

「不同生產階段」描述的是複雜分工（Complex division of labour），而且主體同樣不應該是「簡單分工」。

"Different production stages" describes complex division of labour. Also, the subject should not be "simple division of labour".

Option C: Incorrect (錯誤)

前半部分「區域」正確。但後半部的「攜手合作共建大灣區」只是一個政治/社會口號，並非經濟學上解釋分工的定義。分工的核心在於「專門化 (Specialization)」。

The first part "regional" is correct. However, the second part "cooperate to build the Greater Bay Area" is merely a political/social slogan, not an economic definition of division of labour. The core of division of labour is "Specialization".

Option D: Correct (正確)

前半部「區域」正確，因為涉及不同城市（香港、澳門、廣州）。後半部精準點出了經濟學定義：每個城市在生產不同的服務上進行「專門化」（香港專注金融，澳門專注旅遊等）。

The first part "regional" is correct because it involves different cities (HK, Macao, Guangzhou). The second part accurately points out the economic definition: each city "specializes" in producing different services (HK in finance, Macao in tourism, etc.).

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解答這類題目的關鍵在於尋找「樞紐點 (Pivot Point)」：

The key to solving this type of question is finding the "Pivot Point":

  • Step 1: Identify the Subject (辨認主體)
    題目中的主體是「香港、澳門、廣州」，這些是地區 (Regions)。因此，這必然是區域分工 (Regional Division of Labour)。我們可立即剔除選項 A 和 B。
    The subjects in the question are "HK, Macao, Guangzhou", which are Regions. Therefore, it must be Regional Division of Labour. We can immediately eliminate Options A and B.
  • Step 2: Identify the Economic Rationale (辨認經濟學理據)
    比較 C 和 D 的後半部。經濟學中，分工的本質是生產上的專門化 (Specialization in production)，而不是單純的「合作 (Cooperation)」。因此 D 是唯一的學術性答案。
    Compare the second halves of C and D. In economics, the essence of division of labour is specialization in production, not merely "cooperation". Thus, D is the only academic answer.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：被「比較優勢」一詞誤導 (Trap: Misled by the term "Comparative Advantage")

很多學生看到選項 A 中的「比較優勢 (Comparative Advantage)」就覺得很符合經濟學原理而選擇它。的確，區域分工的成因是比較優勢，但選項 A 的前半部標示為「簡單分工 (Simple)」，這使得整個選項變成錯誤。考試時必須完整閱讀選項的每一部分！

Many students see "comparative advantage" in Option A and choose it because it sounds economically sound. Indeed, comparative advantage is the cause of regional division of labour, but the first half of Option A labels it as "Simple", making the entire option incorrect. You must read every part of the option carefully during exams!

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼不能選 C？「合作」不就是分工的目的嗎？
Q: Why can't I choose C? Isn't "cooperation" the purpose of division of labour?

A: 在經濟學嚴格定義中，分工 (Division of Labour) 指的是將生產過程或任務分配給不同的人或地區，即專門化 (Specialization)。雖然分工最終會帶來合作與交換，但「攜手合作共建」這句話缺乏經濟學的具體描述，它沒有指出各城市「做不同的事」這個核心特徵。選項 D 的「各有自己專長的生產」完美對應了 Specialization 的概念。
A: In strict economic terms, Division of Labour refers to assigning production processes or tasks to different people or regions, i.e., Specialization. While division of labour ultimately leads to cooperation and exchange, the phrase "cooperate to build" lacks specific economic description; it fails to point out the core feature that each city "does different things". Option D's "own specialty of production" perfectly matches the concept of Specialization.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

「主體排除法」 (Subject Elimination Method):

在處理分工題目時，第一時間看「誰在分工」：

When dealing with division of labour questions, immediately look at "who is dividing the labour":

  • 如果是 個人/工人 (Individuals/Workers) \(\rightarrow\) 考慮簡單分工或複雜分工。
    If it's Individuals/Workers \(\rightarrow\) Consider simple or complex division of labour.
  • 如果是 國家/城市/地區 (Countries/Cities/Regions) \(\rightarrow\) 必定是區域分工 (Regional)。
    If it's Countries/Cities/Regions \(\rightarrow\) It MUST be regional division of labour.

只要看到「香港、澳門、廣州」，1秒鐘內就可以劃掉 A 和 B，將命中率提升至 50%！

As soon as you see "HK, Macao, Guangzhou", you can cross out A and B in 1 second, increasing your hit rate to 50%!
