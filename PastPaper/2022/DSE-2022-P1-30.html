Topic: Money and Banking 貨幣與銀行 Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5) Keywords: Money Supply, Reserve Ratio, Credit Contraction

The Question 題目

某銀行系列的資產負債表如下：

The balance sheet of a banking system is as follows:

資產 Assets ($ million / 百萬元) 負債 Liabilities ($ million / 百萬元)
儲備 Reserves 500 存款 Deposits 2 000
貸款 Loans 1 500

法定儲備比率為20%，公眾原初並不持有現金。

The required reserve ratio is 20% and the public does not hold cash initially.

假設某顧客在其銀行戶口提款200百萬元，並以現金持有該筆款項。若所有銀行均沒持有超額儲備，在信貸創造／收縮的過程完結後，貨幣供應的金額是 __________。

Suppose a customer withdraws $200 million from his bank account and holds it as cash. If all banks do not hold any excess reserves, the amount of money supply is _____________ after the process of credit creation/contraction has been completed.

A. 1 200百萬元
B. 1 400百萬元
C. 1 500百萬元
D. 1 700百萬元

A. $1 200 million
B. $1 400 million
C. $1 500 million
D. $1 700 million

Concept Review 概念重溫

要解答這類題目，我們必須清楚理解貨幣供應的定義以及銀行體系的乘數效應：

To solve this type of question, we must clearly understand the definition of money supply and the multiplier effect within the banking system:

  • 貨幣供應 (Money Supply, \(M_s\))： 在香港的課程中，狹義的貨幣供應（如 M1）等於公眾持有的現金加上銀行存款。

    Money Supply (\(M_s\)): In the HKDSE syllabus, money supply (like M1) equals cash held by the non-bank public plus bank deposits.

    $$ M_s = C_p + D $$

  • 最高存款額 (Maximum Deposits, \(D_{max}\))： 當銀行體系沒有超額儲備（Excess Reserves = 0）時，存款會達到最高水平。這取決於實際儲備及法定儲備比率。

    Maximum Deposits (\(D_{max}\)): When the banking system holds zero excess reserves, deposits reach their maximum level. This depends on actual reserves and the required reserve ratio (rrr).

    $$ D_{max} = \frac{\text{Reserves } (R)}{\text{Required Reserve Ratio } (rrr)} $$

The Answer & Detailed Analysis 答案與詳細分析

Correct Answer / 正確答案: D

選項 D (1 700百萬元) - 正確：

當顧客提款 200 百萬元時，銀行體系的實際儲備會減少 200 百萬元。新的實際儲備 \(R\) = \(500 - 200 = 300\) 百萬元。
因為題目指明「所有銀行均沒持有超額儲備」，銀行會透過收縮信貸（追討貸款）來維持最低要求。最終的存款額 \(D\) = \(\frac{300}{20%}\) = 1 500 百萬元。
最後，計算新的貨幣供應。顧客提取的 200 百萬元現金現由公眾持有（\(C_p = 200\)），加上新的存款額（\(D = 1 500\)）。
\(M_s = C_p + D = 200 + 1 500 = 1 700\) 百萬元。

Option D ($1 700 million) - Correct:

When the customer withdraws $200 million, the actual reserves of the banking system decrease by $200 million. The new actual reserves \(R\) = \(500 - 200 = 300\) million.
Since the question states "all banks do not hold any excess reserves", banks will contract credit (call in loans) to maintain the minimum requirement. The final deposit amount \(D\) = \(\frac{300}{20%}\) = $1 500 million.
Finally, calculate the new money supply. The $200 million cash withdrawn is now held by the public (\(C_p = 200\)), plus the new deposit amount (\(D = 1 500\)).
\(M_s = C_p + D = 200 + 1 500 = 1 700\) million.

選項 A & B - 錯誤：

這些是隨機的錯誤計算，通常是因為混淆了儲備和貸款的變動，或是錯誤地將新的儲備總額直接減去某些數值。

Options A & B - Incorrect:

These are random calculation errors, usually caused by confusing changes in reserves and loans, or mistakenly subtracting random values from the new reserve total.

選項 C (1 500百萬元) - 錯誤 (最強烈的干擾項)：

這個選項是學生最容易中計的地方！1 500 百萬元是最終存款總額（\(D\)），而非貨幣供應（\(M_s\)）。考生忘記了把公眾手上的現金（200百萬元）加回總數之中。

Option C ($1 500 million) - Incorrect (The strongest distractor):

This is the most common trap! $1 500 million is the final deposit amount (\(D\)), not the money supply (\(M_s\)). Candidates forgot to add the cash held by the public ($200 million) back into the total.

Problem Decomposition 題目拆解

解答這類題目的「黃金三步曲」：

The "Golden 3-Step Rule" for solving these problems:

  1. 找出「現金流向」： 現金是存入銀行還是被提取？這會直接改變銀行的「實際儲備 (Actual Reserves)」。

    Identify the "Cash Flow": Is cash deposited or withdrawn? This directly changes the banks' "Actual Reserves".

  2. 尋找「關鍵字 (Pivot Point)」計算存款： 題目說「沒有超額儲備 (do not hold any excess reserves)」，這是一把鑰匙，代表你可以直接使用公式 \(D = \frac{R}{rrr}\) 來找出最終存款。

    Find the "Pivot Point" to calculate deposits: The phrase "do not hold any excess reserves" is the key. It means you can directly use the formula \(D = \frac{R}{rrr}\) to find the final deposits.

  3. 組合最終答案： 永遠記得檢查題目問的是「存款 (Deposits)」還是「貨幣供應 (Money Supply)」。如果是後者，必須加上公眾持有的現金！

    Assemble the final answer: Always check if the question asks for "Deposits" or "Money Supply". If it's the latter, you MUST add the cash in public hands!

Common Mistakes & Traps 常犯錯誤與陷阱

陷阱 1：被原來的「實際儲備比率」誤導。
起初，儲備是 500，存款是 2 000，實際儲備比率是 25% (\(\frac{500}{2000}\))。很多學生會錯誤地用 25% 來計算乘數。但記住，一旦信貸收縮/創造過程完成，且「沒有超額儲備」，銀行會貼著 20% 的法定要求來運作。

Trap 1: Misled by the initial "Actual Reserve Ratio".
Initially, reserves are 500 and deposits are 2 000, meaning the actual reserve ratio is 25% (\(\frac{500}{2000}\)). Many students mistakenly use 25% for the multiplier. Remember, once the process completes and there are "no excess reserves", banks operate exactly at the 20% legal requirement.

Student Q&A 學生常見疑問

Q: 題目給出的「貸款 1 500」數字有用嗎？

Q: Is the "Loans 1 500" figure provided in the question useful?

A: 在這道題目中是多餘的資訊（Distractor）！ 計算最高存款或貨幣供應時，我們只需要知道「儲備」和「法定儲備比率」。貸款的改變是信貸收縮的結果，我們不需要計算它也能得出答案。

A: It is redundant information (a distractor) for this specific question! To calculate maximum deposits or money supply, we only need "Reserves" and the "Required Reserve Ratio". The change in loans is the *result* of credit contraction, we don't need to calculate it to find the answer.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts 考試技巧與速解

貨幣基礎 (Monetary Base, MB) 恆定法：

The Monetary Base (MB) Constant Method:

貨幣基礎 (MB) = 銀行儲備 (R) + 公眾持有現金 (\(C_p\))。
只要中央銀行沒有干預，市民單純的提款或存款，不會改變貨幣基礎的總量！

Monetary Base (MB) = Bank Reserves (R) + Cash in Public Hands (\(C_p\)).
As long as the central bank doesn't intervene, simple public deposits or withdrawals DO NOT change the total Monetary Base!

1. 初始 MB = 500 (R) + 0 (\(C_p\)) = 500。
2. 提款後 MB 依然是 500。由於 \(C_p\) 變成了 200，所以新的 R 必然是 300。
3. 新存款 \(D = \frac{300}{0.2} = 1 500\)。
4. \(M_s = C_p + D = 200 + 1 500 = 1 700\)。

1. Initial MB = 500 (R) + 0 (\(C_p\)) = 500.
2. After withdrawal, MB is still 500. Since \(C_p\) is now 200, the new R must be 300.
3. New Deposits \(D = \frac{300}{0.2} = 1 500\).
4. \(M_s = C_p + D = 200 + 1 500 = 1 700\).
