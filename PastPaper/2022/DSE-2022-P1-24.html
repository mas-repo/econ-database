HKDSE Economics Guide

Topic (課題): National Income Accounting (國民收入會計) Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: GDP at factor cost, Expenditure Approach, Inventory Changes

The Question (題目)

細閱以下某經濟的數據。 Study the following data of an economy.

Components (組成部分) $ million (百萬元)
Private consumption expenditure (私人消費支出)500
Gross domestic fixed capital formation (本地固定資本形成總值)260
Decrease in stock (存貨減少)120
Government consumption expenditure (政府消費支出)470
Total exports of goods (物品出口總值)380
Re-exports of goods (物品轉口)140
Total imports of goods (物品進口總值)220
Net exports of services (服務淨出口)730
Depreciation (折舊)90
Indirect taxes (間接稅)75

以要素成本計算的本地生產總值是 __________ 百萬元。 The gross domestic product (GDP) at factor cost is $ __________ million.

A. 1925
B. 2065
C. 2075
D. 2165
Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 支出面計算法 (Expenditure Approach):
基本公式為： 1. Expenditure Approach:
The basic formula is:

$$ \text{GDP}_\text{MP} = C + I + G + (X - M) $$

其中 \(I\) (投資總額) 包含兩個部分： Where \(I\) (Gross Investment) consists of two parts:

$$ I = \text{Gross Fixed Capital Formation} + \text{Change in Stock} $$ $$ I = \text{本地固定資本形成總值} + \text{存貨變動} $$

2. 市價 vs 要素成本 (Market Price vs Factor Cost):
要從市價 (MP) 轉換為要素成本 (FC)，我們需要扣除間接稅並加上補貼。 2. Market Price vs Factor Cost:
To convert from Market Price (MP) to Factor Cost (FC), we deduct indirect taxes and add subsidies.

$$ \text{GDP}_\text{FC} = \text{GDP}_\text{MP} - \text{Indirect Taxes} + \text{Subsidies} $$

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A (1925)

Step-by-Step Calculation (逐步計算):

Step 1: Calculate Gross Investment (計算投資總額 \(I\))

題目給出「本地固定資本形成總值」為 260，但「存貨減少」為 120。注意「減少」代表負數。 The question gives "Gross domestic fixed capital formation" as 260, but "Decrease in stock" is 120. Note that "Decrease" implies a negative value.

\( I = 260 + (-120) = 140 \)

Step 2: Calculate Net Exports (計算淨出口 \(X-M\))

淨出口 = (物品出口 - 物品進口) + 服務淨出口。
注意：「物品出口總值」通常已包含「物品轉口」，所以不需要額外處理轉口數據。 Net Exports = (Total exports of goods - Total imports of goods) + Net exports of services.
Note: "Total exports of goods" usually includes "Re-exports", so no extra calculation is needed for re-exports.

\( (X - M) = (380 - 220) + 730 = 160 + 730 = 890 \)

Step 3: Calculate GDP at Market Price (計算以市價計算的 GDP)

\( \text{GDP}_\text{MP} = C + I + G + (X - M) \)
\( \text{GDP}_\text{MP} = 500 + 140 + 470 + 890 = 2000 \)

Step 4: Convert to Factor Cost (轉換為要素成本)

最後，扣除間接稅。 Finally, deduct indirect taxes.

\( \text{GDP}_\text{FC} = 2000 - 75 = 1925 \)

Why other options are wrong (為何其他選項是錯誤的):

B. 2065:
這可能是錯誤地加上了「物品轉口」(140)。
\( 1925 + 140 = 2065 \)。這是重複計算 (Double Counting)。

C. 2075:
這可能是錯誤地加上了間接稅，而不是減去。
\( 2000 + 75 = 2075 \)。

D. 2165:
這可能是錯誤地處理了存貨變動，將「存貨減少 120」當作「增加」來計算。
如果 \( I = 260 + 120 = 380 \)，則 \( \text{GDP}_\text{MP} = 2240 \)。
\( 2240 - 75 = 2165 \)。這是學生最常犯的錯誤。

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的關鍵在於分辨哪些數據是「陷阱 (Distractors)」，哪些是「核心數據 (Core Data)」。 The key to this type of question is distinguishing between "Distractors" and "Core Data".

  • 陷阱 1 (Trap 1) - Depreciation (折舊): 題目問的是 GDP (Gross/總值)，不是 NDP (Net/淨值)。所以折舊是多餘資訊。
    The question asks for GDP (Gross), not NDP (Net). So depreciation is irrelevant info.
  • 陷阱 2 (Trap 2) - Re-exports (轉口): 「物品出口總值 (Total exports)」已經包含了轉口。單獨列出轉口是為了引誘你重複計算。
    "Total exports" already includes re-exports. Listing it separately is to tempt you into double counting.
  • 關鍵點 (Pivot Point) - Decrease in Stock (存貨減少): 必須記住公式是 \( + \text{Change in Stock} \)。如果是減少，就要加上一個負數 (即減去)。
    Remember the formula is \( + \text{Change in Stock} \). If it's a decrease, you add a negative number (i.e., subtract).

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼不用減去折舊 (Depreciation)?

A: 因為題目要求計算的是「本地生產總值 (Gross Domestic Product)」。"Gross" (總) 的意思就是尚未扣除折舊。如果題目問的是「本地生產淨值 (Net Domestic Product)」，我們才需要減去折舊。

Why didn't we subtract depreciation?
Because the question asks for "Gross Domestic Product". "Gross" means depreciation has not been deducted yet. If the question asked for "Net Domestic Product", then we would subtract depreciation.

Q: 為什麼「物品轉口」不需要加進去？

A: 在香港的統計定義中，「物品出口總值 (Total Exports)」=「本地產品出口 (Domestic Exports)」+「轉口 (Re-exports)」。既然題目已經給了「總值 (Total)」，轉口就已經包含在內了。

Why isn't "Re-exports" added?
In HK statistics, "Total Exports" = "Domestic Exports" + "Re-exports". Since the question gave the "Total", re-exports are already included.

Exam Tricks (考試技巧)

快速檢查正負號 (Sign Check):
做這類題目時，先在試卷上把所有需要「減」的項目圈出來或標上負號。 Sign Check:
When doing this type of question, first circle or mark with a minus sign all items that need to be "subtracted" on the question paper.

  • Imports (進口) \(\rightarrow\) \(-\)
  • Decrease in stock (存貨減少) \(\rightarrow\) \(-\)
  • Indirect Taxes (間接稅) \(\rightarrow\) \(-\) (若求 FC)

這樣計算機輸入時就不容易出錯！
