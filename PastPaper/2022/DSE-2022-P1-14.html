HKDSE 經濟學題解分析

HKDSE Economics Question Analysis Guide
📚 主題 Topic: 需求與供應 (Demand and Supply) ⭐ 難度 Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ 🔑 關鍵字 Key Concepts: 需求量改變 (Change in Quantity Demanded), 供應轉變 (Change in Supply), 生產成本 (Cost of Production)

1. 題目 (The Question)

下圖顯示私人房屋的需求曲線。下列哪項最能解釋均衡點由X移至Y？

A. 住戶收入下降。
B. 人們預期私人房屋價格下降。
C. 建築工人的工資率上升。
D. 商業樓宇的需求下降。

The following diagram shows the demand curve of private housing. Which of the following can best explain the change in equilibrium position from X to Y?

A. The household income decreases.
B. People expect a price drop in private housing.
C. The wage rates of construction workers increase.
D. The demand for commercial buildings decreases.

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

需求量改變 vs 需求改變

這題考核的核心概念是區分「沿著曲線的移動」與「整條曲線的移動」。

  • 需求量改變 (Change in Quantity Demanded)： 只有當物品的自身價格 \( P \) 改變時，才會導致需求量 \( Q_d \) 改變。這在圖表上顯示為沿著同一條需求曲線（Demand Curve）的移動（Movement along the curve）。
  • 需求改變 (Change in Demand)： 當價格以外的因素（如收入、預期、代替品價格等）改變時，整條需求曲線會向左或向右移動（Shift of the curve）。
$$ Q_d = f(P) $$

根據需求法則（Law of Demand），在其他因素不變的情況下（ceteris paribus），價格與需求量呈反向關係：

$$ P \uparrow \implies Q_d \downarrow $$

Change in Quantity Demanded vs. Change in Demand

The core concept tested here is distinguishing between a "movement along the curve" and a "shift of the curve".

  • Change in Quantity Demanded: This occurs ONLY when the own price \( P \) of the good changes, leading to a change in quantity demanded \( Q_d \). This is shown graphically as a movement along the same demand curve.
  • Change in Demand: When non-price determinants (e.g., income, expectations, price of substitutes) change, the entire demand curve shifts to the left or right.

According to the Law of Demand, ceteris paribus, there is an inverse relationship between price and quantity demanded.

3. 答案與詳細分析 (Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是：選項 C (Option C)

A. 住戶收入下降 (The household income decreases) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

住戶收入下降（假設私人房屋是正常物品 Normal Good），會導致人們對房屋的整體需求減少。這在圖表上會呈現為整條需求曲線向左移動（Shift to the left），而不是沿著同一條曲線移動。圖中只有一條固定不變的需求曲線 D。

A decrease in household income (assuming private housing is a normal good) reduces the overall demand. Graphically, this is represented by a leftward shift of the entire demand curve, not a movement along it. The diagram shows a single, unchanged demand curve D.
B. 人們預期私人房屋價格下降 (People expect a price drop...) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

如果買家預期未來價格會下跌，他們會延遲現在的購買決定。這同樣會導致現時的房屋需求減少，即整條需求曲線向左移動。這不符合圖中顯示的狀況。

If buyers expect prices to fall in the future, they will delay their current purchases. This also leads to a decrease in current demand, meaning a leftward shift of the demand curve. This does not match the diagram.
C. 建築工人的工資率上升 (Wage rates of construction workers increase) - 正確 (Correct)

建築工人的工資是興建房屋的「生產成本」（Cost of Production）。工資上升會導致生產成本增加，供應因此減少，供應曲線向左移動。當供應減少，市場的均衡價格 \( P \) 會上升，而均衡交易量 \( Q \) 會下降。這正正反映了買家面對更高的價格，因而減少購買量，即在需求曲線 D 上由點 X（較低價、較多量）移動至點 Y（較高價、較少量）。

Construction workers' wages are a "cost of production" for building houses. An increase in wages raises production costs, leading to a decrease in supply. The supply curve shifts to the left. When supply decreases, the equilibrium price \( P \) increases and the equilibrium quantity \( Q \) decreases. This exactly reflects buyers facing a higher price and reducing their quantity demanded, moving from point X (lower price, higher quantity) to point Y (higher price, lower quantity) along the demand curve D.
D. 商業樓宇的需求下降 (Demand for commercial buildings decreases) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

如果商業樓宇的需求下降，發展商可能會將資源（如土地、工人）轉移去興建私人房屋（這是競爭性供應 Competitive Supply 的概念）。這會導致私人房屋的供應增加（供應曲線向右移動）。這會令均衡價格下降、交易量上升，即在圖表上應該是由 Y 點移向 X 點，與題目方向相反。

If the demand for commercial buildings decreases, developers might reallocate resources (like land and labor) to build private housing (concept of competitive supply). This would cause an increase in the supply of private housing (rightward shift of the supply curve). This would lead to a lower equilibrium price and higher quantity, meaning a movement from point Y to point X, which is the opposite of the question.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

做這類圖表題時，我們必須先找出「支點」(Pivot Point)：

  1. 觀察圖表特徵： 圖中只有一條需求曲線 D，並沒有 \( D_1 \)、\( D_2 \) 的出現。均衡點的改變（X 到 Y）是沿著曲線的移動。
  2. 解讀移動方向： 比較 X 和 Y。Y 點的價格比 X 點高，但數量比 X 點少。甚麼會導致價格上升而交易量下跌？唯一答案是：供應減少 (Decrease in Supply)。
  3. 尋找對應選項： 我們要找的選項，必須是一個能導致「私人房屋供應減少」的因素。選項 C 的生產成本上升完美符合這個條件。

When tackling chart-based MCQs, we must first find the Pivot Point:

  1. Observe the graph: There is only one demand curve D, no \( D_1 \) or \( D_2 \). The change in equilibrium (X to Y) is a movement along the curve.
  2. Decode the movement: Compare X and Y. Point Y has a higher price but a lower quantity than point X. What causes price to rise and quantity to fall? The only answer is: Decrease in Supply.
  3. Find the matching option: We need to look for an option that causes a "decrease in the supply of private housing". Option C's increase in production cost perfectly fits.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

🚨 死穴：混淆「需求」與「需求量」

很多同學一看到點 X 移向點 Y，發現「數量減少了」，就直覺以為是「需求下降」，於是急急忙忙選了 A 或 B。但請緊記：只有價格改變才會導致點在同一條線上移動！如果真的是 A 或 B 發生，考評局的圖表必須畫出另一條新的需求曲線在原有的左邊。

🚨 Fatal Flaw: Confusing "Demand" with "Quantity Demanded"

Many students see the movement from X to Y, notice the "quantity decreased," and intuitively think "Demand decreased," hastily choosing A or B. Remember: ONLY a change in price causes a point to move along the SAME line! If A or B actually happened, the HKEAA diagram would have to show a brand new demand curve drawn to the left of the original one.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

🙋‍♂️ 學生問：這題完全沒有畫出供應曲線 (Supply Curve)，我怎麼知道跟供應有關？
👨‍🏫 導師答： 這是考評局常用的「隱藏曲線」技巧！雖然圖中沒有畫出供應曲線 \( S \)，但市場均衡點必定是需求曲線和供應曲線的相交點。既然 X 和 Y 都在同一條需求曲線上，代表需求曲線沒動。要令交叉點由 X 跑到 Y，必定是隱藏的那條供應曲線往左上方移動（減少）了。這就是經濟學上的逆向推理！
🙋‍♂️ Student: The supply curve isn't even drawn in this diagram! How am I supposed to know it's about supply?
👨‍🏫 Tutor: This is a classic "hidden curve" trick used by examiners! Although the supply curve \( S \) isn't drawn, a market equilibrium point is ALWAYS the intersection of supply and demand. Since both X and Y are on the same demand curve, the demand curve hasn't moved. For the intersection to travel from X up to Y, the hidden supply curve MUST have shifted to the upper-left (decreased). This is economic reverse-engineering!

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

⚡ 三秒必殺技 (The 3-Second Rule)：

在考場上看到這種圖，立刻在腦海中進行「兩步篩選法」：

  1. 圖中只有一條 D 線 ➡️ 尋找改變供應 (Supply) 的選項，直接劃掉所有改變需求 (Demand) 的選項 (A, B)。
  2. 價格上升，數量下降 ➡️ 尋找令供應減少 (Supply ↓) 的因素。選項 C 是成本上升 = 供應減少。秒殺！

⚡ The 3-Second Rule:

When you see this diagram in the exam hall, run the "2-Step Filter" in your head immediately:

  1. Only one D-curve shown ➡️ Look for options that change Supply. Cross out all options that change Demand (A, B).
  2. Price goes UP, Quantity goes DOWN ➡️ Look for factors that decrease Supply (Supply ↓). Option C is an increase in cost = decrease in supply. Solved!
