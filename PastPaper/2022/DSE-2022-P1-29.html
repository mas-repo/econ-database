HKDSE Economics: Question Analysis

Topic (課題): 總需求與總供應 (Aggregate Demand & Aggregate Supply) / 勞動人口 (Labour Force)

Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆ (3/5)

Key Concepts (核心概念): Aggregate Demand (AD) Short-Run Aggregate Supply (SRAS) Labour Productivity

2. The Question (題目)

某國放寬移民的要求以吸引外國人到該國工作、生活和升學。據估計本年內會有200 000外國人在該國定居。下列哪項有關放寬移民要求的陳述是正確的？

(1) 該國的總需求會上升。
(2) 該國的勞工生產力會上升。
(3) 該國的短期總供應會上升。

A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)
A country relaxed its immigration requirements to attract foreigners to work, live and study there. It is estimated that 200 000 foreigners will be settling there this year. Which of the following statements about the relaxation of immigration requirements is correct?

(1) The aggregate demand of the country will rise.
(2) The labour productivity of the country will rise.
(3) The short run aggregate supply of the country will rise.

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

總需求 (Aggregate Demand, AD): 是指在不同物價水平下，經濟體系內對本地生產物品及服務的總需求量。當人口增加（如移民湧入），私人消費開支 \(C\) 會隨之增加，導致總需求上升。

Aggregate Demand (AD): The total quantity of domestically produced goods and services demanded at various price levels. When population increases (e.g., influx of immigrants), private consumption expenditure \(C\) increases, leading to a rise in AD.

$$AD = C + I + G + NX$$

勞工生產力 (Labour Productivity): 是指每名工人或每工時的平均產量。它的改變取決於教育、科技、資本數量等因素。勞動人口數量的單純增加，並不能保證每個人的平均產出會提高。

Labour Productivity: The average output per worker or per man-hour. Changes in productivity depend on factors like education, technology, and capital. A simple increase in the number of workers does not guarantee an increase in average output per worker.

$$\text{Labour Productivity} = \frac{\text{Total Output (Y)}}{\text{Total Labour Hours or Number of Workers}}$$

短期總供應 (Short-Run Aggregate Supply, SRAS): 經濟體系在短期內於不同物價水平下所提供的總產出。當勞動人口（生產要素）增加，經濟整體的生產能力會擴大，導致短期及長期總供應均會增加。

Short-Run Aggregate Supply (SRAS): The total output supplied by the economy at various price levels in the short run. When the labour force (a factor of production) increases, the overall productive capacity of the economy expands, leading to an increase in both SRAS and LRAS.

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): Option B

Statement Analysis (陳述分析)

陳述 (1)：該國的總需求會上升。(正確)
由於有200,000名外國人到該國定居、生活和升學，他們需要在當地消費（例如購買食物、租屋、支付學費等）。這會直接增加經濟體系內的私人消費開支 \(C\)，從而令總需求 \(AD\) 向右移（上升）。

Statement (1): The aggregate demand of the country will rise. (Correct)
Since 200,000 foreigners will settle, live, and study in the country, they will need to consume locally (e.g., buying food, renting apartments, paying tuition). This directly increases private consumption expenditure \(C\) in the economy, causing aggregate demand \(AD\) to shift to the right (rise).

陳述 (2)：該國的勞工生產力會上升。(錯誤)
勞工生產力是指「每名工人的平均產量」。雖然新移民增加了勞動力的總量，這只代表「總產量」潛力增加，但並不代表「平均每人的生產力」會提高。除非題目說明這些移民全都是高技術專才，否則我們不能斷定整體勞工生產力會上升。

Statement (2): The labour productivity of the country will rise. (Incorrect)
Labour productivity refers to "average output per worker". While new immigrants increase the total size of the labour force, which implies an increase in potential "total output", it does not mean the "average output per person" will improve. Unless the question specifies that these immigrants are highly skilled professionals, we cannot conclude that overall labour productivity will rise.

陳述 (3)：該國的短期總供應會上升。(正確)
移民到該國工作增加了該國的勞動力資源。生產要素數量的增加會擴大該國的生產潛力，降低整體的生產成本壓力，從而導致短期總供應 \(SRAS\)（以及長期總供應 \(LRAS\)）增加。

Statement (3): The short run aggregate supply of the country will rise. (Correct)
Immigrants coming to work increase the country's labour resources. An increase in the quantity of factors of production expands the country's productive capacity and eases overall production cost pressures, leading to an increase in Short-Run Aggregate Supply \(SRAS\) (as well as LRAS).

Option Breakdown (選項拆解)

  • A. 只有 (1)、(2) / (1) and (2) only: 錯誤。因為陳述(2)是不正確的。
    Incorrect because Statement (2) is wrong.
  • B. 只有 (1)、(3) / (1) and (3) only: 正確。陳述(1)和(3)皆符合經濟學理。
    Correct. Both Statements (1) and (3) are economically sound based on the context.
  • C. 只有 (2)、(3) / (2) and (3) only: 錯誤。因為陳述(2)是不正確的，且遺漏了正確的(1)。
    Incorrect because Statement (2) is wrong, and it misses the correct Statement (1).
  • D. (1)、(2) 及 (3) / (1), (2) and (3): 錯誤。因為陳述(2)是不正確的。
    Incorrect because Statement (2) is wrong.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point): 區分「數量」與「效率」。這題的關鍵在於分辨「勞工總人數」與「勞工生產力」。學生必須清楚知道，投入更多資源（人口）能增加總量（\(AD\) 和 \(AS\)），但並不等於資源的品質或平均效率（生產力）有所提升。

Pivot Point: Distinguishing between "Quantity" and "Efficiency". The key to solving this question is differentiating between the "total number of workers" and "labour productivity". Students must know that putting in more resources (population) increases the totals (\(AD\) and \(AS\)), but it doesn't equate to an improvement in the quality or average efficiency (productivity) of the resources.

干擾資訊 (Distractors): 「200 000外國人」。這個具體數字並不重要，它只是為了建立一個「人口顯著增加」的情境。不要因為有具體數字就去猜想會對生產力產生特定的數學影響。

Distractors: "200 000 foreigners". This specific number is irrelevant; it simply establishes a scenario of a "significant increase in population". Don't assume this specific number implies a mathematical impact on productivity.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

概念混淆 (Conceptual Confusion): 很多考生會將「生產量 (Production)」與「生產力 (Productivity)」混為一談。移民湧入會增加整體的潛在生產量（Total Production / Output），但生產力是計算平均值（Output per worker）。如果新增的勞工缺乏技術，整體的平均生產力甚至有可能下降！

Conceptual Confusion: Many candidates confuse "Production" with "Productivity". An influx of immigrants will increase the overall potential Total Production / Output, but productivity is an average (Output per worker). If the new workers lack skills, the overall average productivity might even decrease!

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q1: 為什麼移民來工作，短期總供應 (SRAS) 和長期總供應 (LRAS) 都會增加？不是只有長期才會受資源影響嗎？

Q1: Why do both SRAS and LRAS increase when immigrants come to work? Isn't only LRAS affected by resources?

A1: 資源數量的增加（例如勞動力增加）會擴大經濟的生產能力，這使得垂直的長期總供應曲線 (LRAS) 向右移。同時，更多勞工在市場上尋找工作，會對工資產生下調壓力或減輕生產成本的上升壓力，這亦會使向上傾斜的短期總供應曲線 (SRAS) 向右移。因此兩者都會上升。

A1: An increase in the quantity of resources (like labour) expands the economy's productive capacity, which shifts the vertical LRAS curve to the right. At the same time, more workers looking for jobs puts downward pressure on wages or eases upward pressure on production costs, which also shifts the upward-sloping SRAS curve to the right. Thus, both rise.

Q2: 總需求 (AD) 上升是因為他們工作賺錢嗎？

Q2: Does AD rise because they are working and earning money?

A2: 更直接的原因是他們「在該國生活和升學」。只要人在該國，就需要食宿、交通等基本消費。這種人口的淨流入會直接推高總體經濟的消費開支 \(C\)，導致 \(AD\) 增加。

A2: The more direct reason is that they are "living and studying" in the country. As long as people are physically present, they require basic consumption like food, housing, and transport. This net inflow of population directly boosts total consumption expenditure \(C\) in the economy, causing \(AD\) to increase.

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

「一擊必殺」排除法 (The "One-Shot" Elimination Method):
這類包含 (1) (2) (3) 的多項選擇題最適合使用排除法。當你讀到陳述 (2) 提到「生產力 (Productivity)」，你應該立刻警覺：題目沒有提供任何關於移民教育程度或技術水平的資訊，所以「生產力必然上升」這個結論是無根據的。一旦你確定 (2) 是錯誤的，你可以立刻刪去包含 (2) 的選項：A、C 和 D。唯一剩下的答案就是 B。你甚至不需要花太多時間去深究 (1) 和 (3)！

The "One-Shot" Elimination Method:
Multiple-choice questions with (1) (2) (3) statements are perfect for the elimination method. When you read statement (2) about "Productivity", red flags should go up: the question provides zero information about the education or skill level of the immigrants, so concluding that productivity will definitively rise is baseless. Once you determine (2) is false, you can immediately eliminate any option containing (2): Options A, C, and D. The only remaining answer is B. You don't even need to overthink statements (1) and (3)!
