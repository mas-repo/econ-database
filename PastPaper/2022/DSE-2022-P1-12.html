HKDSE Economics: Multiple Choice Analysis

Topic (課題): Price Elasticity of Demand & Total Expenditure (需求價格彈性與總開支)

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 總開支 (Total Expenditure, TE) 與 需求量 (Quantity Demanded, Q) 的關係：
總開支等於價格乘以需求量。如果我們知道總開支和價格，我們可以找出需求量。

1. Relationship between Total Expenditure (TE) and Quantity Demanded (Q):
Total Expenditure equals Price multiplied by Quantity Demanded. If we know TE and Price, we can find Q.

$$ TE = P \times Q \implies Q = \frac{TE}{P} $$

2. 需求價格彈性 (Price Elasticity of Demand, \(E_d\)) 與 總開支的關係：
- 完全缺乏彈性 (\(E_d = 0\))： 需求量 \(Q\) 保持不變。價格 \(P\) 改變時，總開支 \(TE\) 會按相同比例改變。
- 單一彈性 (\(E_d = 1\))： 無論價格 \(P\) 如何改變，總開支 \(TE\) 都保持不變。

2. Relationship between Price Elasticity of Demand (\(E_d\)) and Total Expenditure:
- Perfectly Inelastic (\(E_d = 0\)): Quantity demanded \(Q\) remains constant. When price \(P\) changes, TE changes in the exact same proportion.
- Unitary Elastic (\(E_d = 1\)): Total Expenditure \(TE\) remains constant regardless of changes in price \(P\).

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是 D (Correct Option: D)

分析瑪莉 (Mary's Analysis):
我們利用公式 \(Q = \frac{TE}{P}\) 計算瑪莉的需求量：
當 \(P = 1\)，\(Q = 20 / 1 = 20\)
當 \(P = 2\)，\(Q = 40 / 2 = 20\)
當 \(P = 3\)，\(Q = 60 / 3 = 20\)
當 \(P = 4\)，\(Q = 80 / 4 = 20\)
由於價格改變時，瑪莉的需求量始終保持在 20 不變，這代表她的需求是完全缺乏彈性 (Perfectly Inelastic)，即 \(E_d = 0\)。

Mary's Analysis:
We calculate Mary's quantity demanded using \(Q = \frac{TE}{P}\):
When \(P = 1\), \(Q = 20 / 1 = 20\)
When \(P = 2\), \(Q = 40 / 2 = 20\)
When \(P = 3\), \(Q = 60 / 3 = 20\)
When \(P = 4\), \(Q = 80 / 4 = 20\)
Since Mary's quantity demanded remains constant at 20 regardless of the price change, her demand is perfectly inelastic, meaning \(E_d = 0\).

分析彼德 (Peter's Analysis):
觀察表格，無論價格是 $1、$2、$3 還是 $4，彼德的總支出始終保持在 $60 不變。根據經濟學定律，當總支出不隨價格改變時，需求彈性為單一彈性 (Unitary Elastic)，即 \(E_d = 1\)。

Peter's Analysis:
Observing the table, whether the price is $1, $2, $3, or $4, Peter's total expenditure remains constant at $60. According to economic principles, when total expenditure does not change with price, the demand is unitary elastic, meaning \(E_d = 1\).

為什麼 A, B, C 是錯的？
選項 A 和 B 認為瑪莉的彈性在 0 和 1 之間（缺乏彈性），這是不對的，因為她的需求量是完全沒有改變的（\(E_d = 0\)）。
選項 C 認為彼德的彈性是 0，這是混淆了「總開支不變」和「需求量不變」。彼德是總開支不變（\(E_d = 1\)），而不是需求量不變。

Why are A, B, C wrong?
Options A and B suggest Mary's elasticity is between 0 and 1 (inelastic), which is incorrect because her quantity demanded does not change at all (\(E_d = 0\)).
Option C suggests Peter's elasticity is 0, which confuses "constant total expenditure" with "constant quantity demanded". Peter has constant total expenditure (\(E_d = 1\)), not constant quantity.

🔍 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題關鍵 (Pivot Point): 題目給出的數據是總支出 (Total Expenditure)，而不是需求量 (Quantity Demanded)。很多學生會直接把表格裡的數字當成需求量來計算，這是致命的錯誤。

Pivot Point: The data provided in the question is Total Expenditure, NOT Quantity Demanded. A fatal error many students make is treating the numbers in the table directly as quantity demanded.

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：混淆「總開支不變」與「需求量不變」
看到彼德的數字全是 60，很多同學會直覺認為「沒有改變 = 彈性為零」，從而錯選 C。記住：
- 數量 (Q) 不變 \(\implies\) 彈性 = 0
- 總開支 (TE) 不變 \(\implies\) 彈性 = 1

Trap: Confusing "Constant TE" with "Constant Q"
Seeing Peter's numbers are all 60, many students intuitively think "no change = zero elasticity", leading them to wrongly choose C. Remember:
- Constant Quantity (Q) \(\implies\) Elasticity = 0
- Constant Total Expenditure (TE) \(\implies\) Elasticity = 1

🙋 Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

問：如果我不用「總開支法則」，可以硬算彼德的彈性嗎？

Q: Can I brute-force calculate Peter's elasticity if I don't use the "Total Expenditure Rule"?

答：可以的！我們先算出彼德的需求量 \(Q\)。當 \(P=2\)，\(Q = 60/2 = 30\)。當 \(P=3\)，\(Q = 60/3 = 20\)。
使用弧彈性公式 (Arc Elasticity Formula):
\(E_d = -\frac{\Delta Q}{\Delta P} \times \frac{P_1 + P_2}{Q_1 + Q_2} = -\frac{20 - 30}{3 - 2} \times \frac{2 + 3}{30 + 20} = -\frac{-10}{1} \times \frac{5}{50} = 1\)。
結果確實是 1！但考試時直接背熟法則會快很多。

A: Yes, you can! First, find Peter's Q. When \(P=2\), \(Q = 60/2 = 30\). When \(P=3\), \(Q = 60/3 = 20\).
Using the Arc Elasticity Formula:
\(E_d = -\frac{\Delta Q}{\Delta P} \times \frac{P_1 + P_2}{Q_1 + Q_2} = -\frac{20 - 30}{3 - 2} \times \frac{2 + 3}{30 + 20} = -\frac{-10}{1} \times \frac{5}{50} = 1\).
The result is indeed 1! But memorizing the rule is much faster for exams.

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧 (One-Second Kill Trick):
在 DSE 選擇題中，遇到「價格」與「總開支」的表格題，請直接背誦以下口訣：
1. TE 數字全一樣 \(\implies\) 彈性必定是 1 (單一彈性)。立刻知道彼德是 1。
2. TE 隨 P 按正比例上升 (例如 P 變 2 倍，TE 也變 2 倍) \(\implies\) 彈性必定是 0 (完全缺乏彈性)。立刻知道瑪莉是 0。
無需動筆計算，3 秒內就能選出答案 D！

Shortcut:
For DSE MCQs involving "Price" and "Total Expenditure" tables, memorize this rule of thumb:
1. TE numbers are all identical \(\implies\) Elasticity is exactly 1 (Unitary). You immediately know Peter is 1.
2. TE increases strictly proportionally with P (e.g., P doubles, TE doubles) \(\implies\) Elasticity is exactly 0 (Perfectly Inelastic). You immediately know Mary is 0.
No calculation needed, you can pick D in 3 seconds!
