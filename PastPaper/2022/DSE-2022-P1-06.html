HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: 企業擴張與整合 (Business Expansion & Integration) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts: Diversification (多元化) Economies of Scale (規模經濟) Brand Extension (品牌延伸)

📝 題目 (The Question)

華特迪士尼公司擴張其業務，以「迪士尼+」(Disney+)提供視訊串流服務。下列哪些最可能是該擴張的動機？

(1) 透過服務多元化分散風險。
(2) 將其品牌推廣至其他產品。
(3) 享有規模經濟。

A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

The Walt Disney Company expanded its business to provide video streaming service, Disney+. Which of the following are most likely the motives behind the expansion?

(1) to spread risk by service diversification
(2) to extend its brand name to other products
(3) to enjoy economies of scale

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

🧠 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

當企業進行擴張（特別是側向擴張或垂直擴張）時，通常有幾個主要動機：

  • 分散風險 (Spreading Risks): 透過提供不同的產品或服務，當某一市場需求下降時，其他市場的收入可以彌補損失。
  • 品牌延伸 (Brand Extension): 利用現有知名的品牌名稱來推廣新產品，從而節省宣傳成本並增加顧客信心。
  • 規模經濟 (Economies of Scale): 當產量或業務規模增加時，長期平均成本 \(AC\) 下降。這包括市場營銷規模經濟（共用廣告）或財務規模經濟。

When a firm expands (especially lateral or vertical expansion), there are several main motives:

  • Spreading Risks: By offering different products/services, if demand in one market falls, revenue from other markets can offset the loss.
  • Brand Extension: Using an existing well-known brand name to promote new products, thereby saving promotional costs and increasing customer confidence.
  • Economies of Scale: When output or business scale increases, the long-run average cost \(AC\) decreases. This includes marketing economies (sharing ads) or financial economies.
$$ AC = \frac{TC}{Q} $$

Average Cost = Total Cost / Quantity

✅ 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): Option D

分析 (1) - 正確: 迪士尼原本依賴主題樂園、電影票房和周邊商品。推出串流平台是進入一個新的服務領域（訂閱制線上影片），這使得其收入來源多元化。如果電影院關閉（例如疫情期間），串流平台的收入可以幫助分散風險。
Analysis (1) - Correct: Disney originally relied on theme parks, box office, and merchandise. Launching a streaming platform enters a new service area (subscription-based online video), diversifying its revenue streams. If cinemas close (e.g., during a pandemic), streaming revenue helps spread the risk.
分析 (2) - 正確: 「迪士尼」是一個極具價值的全球品牌。將這個品牌名稱應用於新的串流服務（命名為 Disney+），正是品牌延伸的經典例子，能迅速吸引大量現有粉絲訂閱。
Analysis (2) - Correct: "Disney" is a highly valuable global brand. Applying this brand name to a new streaming service (naming it Disney+) is a classic example of brand extension, quickly attracting a massive existing fanbase to subscribe.
分析 (3) - 正確: 迪士尼可以利用其現有的龐大內容庫（電影、動畫），在不大幅增加製作成本的情況下，透過自家平台分發給全球數千萬訂閱者。這降低了每位用戶的平均分發和營銷成本 \(AC\)，從而享有規模經濟。
Analysis (3) - Correct: Disney can utilize its massive existing content library (movies, animations) and distribute it to tens of millions of subscribers globally without significantly increasing production costs. This lowers the average distribution and marketing cost \(AC\) per user, thus enjoying economies of scale.

🔍 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題關鍵 (Pivot Point): 辨識這項擴張的性質。這屬於側向擴張 (Lateral Expansion) 或 前向垂直擴張 (Forward Vertical Integration) 的混合體。在DSE經濟學中，這類擴張的標準好處就是 (1)、(2) 和 (3)。

不要被「串流服務」這個科技詞彙嚇倒，只需將其視為「企業推出一項與原有業務相關但不同的新產品」。

Pivot Point: Identify the nature of this expansion. It is a mix of Lateral Expansion and Forward Vertical Integration. In DSE Economics, the standard benefits of such expansions are exactly (1), (2), and (3).

Don't be intimidated by the tech term "streaming service"; just treat it as "a firm launching a new product related to, but different from, its original business."

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

誤區：認為只有「橫向擴張 (Horizontal Expansion)」才有規模經濟。

很多學生以為只有生產完全相同的產品才能降低平均成本。事實上，側向擴張也能帶來市場營銷規模經濟 (Marketing economies)（例如：同一個廣告可以同時宣傳迪士尼電影和 Disney+）以及管理規模經濟 (Managerial economies)。

Misconception: Thinking economies of scale only apply to Horizontal Expansion.

Many students think average costs only fall when producing the exact same product. In fact, lateral expansion can also bring marketing economies (e.g., the same ad promoting both a Disney movie and Disney+) and managerial economies.

🙋‍♂️ 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 迪士尼本來就是做娛樂的，推出 Disney+ 真的算「多元化」嗎？

A: 算的。雖然同屬娛樂產業，但「電影院票房/實體光碟」與「月費訂閱制串流平台」是完全不同的商業模式和分發渠道。這足以構成服務多元化，有效分散單一渠道（如戲院倒閉）的風險。

Q: Disney is already in entertainment. Does launching Disney+ really count as "diversification"?

A: Yes. Although it's the same industry, "cinema box office/physical discs" and "monthly subscription streaming" are entirely different business models and distribution channels. This constitutes service diversification and effectively spreads the risk of a single channel failing (like cinemas closing).

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

常識判斷法 (Common Sense Check): 在DSE中，大型跨國企業（如迪士尼、蘋果）推出新服務時，選項中提及的「分散風險」、「品牌延伸」和「規模經濟」幾乎都是其必然的商業動機。除非題目特別強調某個極端限制，否則這三個優點通常都是正確的。

Common Sense Check: In the DSE, when large multinational corporations (like Disney, Apple) launch new services, motives like "spreading risks", "brand extension", and "economies of scale" are almost always correct. Unless the question highlights a specific extreme limitation, all three benefits generally apply.
