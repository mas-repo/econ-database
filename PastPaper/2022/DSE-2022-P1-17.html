Topic: Factors Affecting Supply (供給的因素)

Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Competitive Supply, Joint Supply, Input Prices

The Question (題目)

若 __________ 的價格上升， __________ 的供應會下降。

If the price of ____________ increases, the supply of ____________ will decrease.
  • (1) 麵粉 …… 麵包
    flour …… bread
  • (2) 牛肉 …… 皮革
    beef …… leather
  • (3) 汽車 …… 汽油
    cars …… gasoline
  • (4) 商業樓宇 …… 工業樓宇
    commercial buildings …… industrial buildings

Options:

  • A. 只有 (1)、(3) (1) and (3) only
  • B. 只有 (1)、(4) (1) and (4) only
  • C. 只有 (2)、(3) (2) and (3) only
  • D. 只有 (2)、(4) (2) and (4) only

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 生產成本 (Cost of Production)

當投入要素（如原材料）的價格上升，生產成本增加，利潤減少，導致產品供應下降。

When the price of inputs (e.g., raw materials) rises, production costs increase, profits decrease, leading to a decrease in the supply of the product.
\( P_{input} \uparrow \rightarrow Cost \uparrow \rightarrow Supply_{output} \downarrow \)
2. 競逐供應 (Competitive Supply)

兩種物品需要使用相同的資源來生產。若物品 A 的價格上升，生產者會將資源轉移去生產 A，導致物品 B 的供應下降。

Two goods use the same resources for production. If the price of Good A rises, producers shift resources to produce A, causing the supply of Good B to decrease.
\( P_A \uparrow \rightarrow QS_A \uparrow \rightarrow Supply_B \downarrow \)
3. 聯合供應 (Joint Supply)

兩種物品是源自同一來源的副產品。若物品 A 的價格上升，其供應量增加，自然會產生更多的物品 B，導致物品 B 的供應上升。

Two goods are by-products from the same source. If the price of Good A rises, its quantity supplied increases, naturally producing more of Good B, leading to an increase in the supply of Good B.
\( P_A \uparrow \rightarrow QS_A \uparrow \rightarrow Supply_B \uparrow \)

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Answer: Option B (1) & (4)

Correct (1) 麵粉 (Flour) ... 麵包 (Bread)

麵粉是生產麵包的原材料（投入要素）。當麵粉價格上升，生產麵包的邊際成本上升，供應曲線向左移動（供應下降）。

Flour is a raw material (input) for producing bread. When the price of flour rises, the marginal cost of producing bread increases, shifting the supply curve to the left (supply decreases).
Incorrect (2) 牛肉 (Beef) ... 皮革 (Leather)

這是聯合供應 (Joint Supply)。牛肉和皮革都來自牛。當牛肉價格上升，農夫會飼養更多牛來出售牛肉（牛肉供應量上升），這會順帶產生更多的牛皮，因此皮革的供應會上升，而不是下降。

This is Joint Supply. Beef and leather both come from cattle. When the price of beef rises, farmers raise more cattle to sell beef (quantity supplied of beef rises), which incidentally produces more cowhides, so the supply of leather increases, not decreases.
Incorrect (3) 汽車 (Cars) ... 汽油 (Gasoline)

這是互補品 (Complements)，但這是需求面 (Demand side) 的關係。汽車價格上升，汽車的需求量下降，導致對汽油的需求 (Demand) 下降。題目問的是「供應 (Supply)」，汽油的供應曲線本身不會因為汽車價格變動而直接移動。

These are Complements, but this is a Demand side relationship. When the price of cars rises, the quantity demanded for cars falls, leading to a decrease in the Demand for gasoline. The question asks about "Supply"; the supply curve of gasoline does not shift directly just because car prices change.
Correct (4) 商業樓宇 (Commercial Bldgs) ... 工業樓宇 (Industrial Bldgs)

這是競逐供應 (Competitive Supply)。兩者都需要土地和建築資源。當商業樓宇價格上升，發展商會將資源轉去興建商業樓宇，導致工業樓宇的供應下降。

This is Competitive Supply. Both require land and construction resources. When the price of commercial buildings rises, developers shift resources to build commercial buildings, causing the supply of industrial buildings to decrease.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的關鍵在於分辨兩個物品之間的關係 (Identify the relationship between the two goods)：

  1. 投入與產出 (Input-Output): 原料貴 \(\rightarrow\) 產品供應少。
    Input expensive \(\rightarrow\) Output supply drops. (Matches Option 1)
  2. 生產上的關係 (Production Relationship):
    • 搶奪資源 (Competitive Supply) \(\rightarrow\) 此消彼長 (Inverse relationship). (Matches Option 4)
    • 共同生產 (Joint Supply) \(\rightarrow\) 同生共死 (Direct relationship). (Matches Option 2)
  3. 消費上的關係 (Consumption Relationship): 這是陷阱！
    This is a trap! Complements/Substitutes in demand affect the Demand curve, not the Supply curve. (Matches Option 3)

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼 (3) 汽車和汽油不對？如果沒人買車，汽油供應商不是會減少供應嗎？
Why is (3) wrong? If no one buys cars, won't gas stations reduce supply?

A: 這是經濟學定義的嚴謹性問題。
汽車價格上升 \(\rightarrow\) 汽油需求減少 \(\rightarrow\) 汽油價格下降 \(\rightarrow\) 汽油供應量 (Quantity Supplied) 沿著線減少。
這只是沿著供應線移動 (Movement along the curve)，而不是供應線本身的移動 (Shift in Supply)。題目問的是「供應下降 (Supply decreases)」，指的是整條線向左移。

A: This is about strict economic definitions.
Price of cars \(\uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) Demand for gas \(\downarrow\) \(\rightarrow\) Price of gas \(\downarrow\) \(\rightarrow\) Quantity Supplied of gas decreases along the curve.
This is a movement along the supply curve, not a shift of the supply curve. The question asks for "Supply decreases", which implies a leftward shift of the entire curve.

Exam Tricks (考試技巧)

  • 分辨「需求」與「供應」： 看到題目問「供應」，立刻刪除所有屬於「消費者行為」或「互補品/替代品 (需求面)」的選項。選項 (3) 是典型的需求面互補品，可以直接排除。
    Distinguish Demand vs. Supply: When asked about "Supply", immediately eliminate options related to "consumer behavior" or "complements/substitutes in demand". Option (3) is a classic demand-side complement and can be eliminated instantly.
  • 分辨「競逐」與「聯合」：
    競逐 (Competitive) = 爭奪資源 = 方向相反 (Inverse)。
    聯合 (Joint) = 同一來源 = 方向相同 (Direct)。
