HKDSE Economics MCQ Analysis

Topic / 課題: 廠商的生產與利潤極大化 (Theory of Firm and Profit Maximization) / 成本分類 (Cost Classification)

Difficulty / 難度: ★★★☆☆ (3/5)

Key Concepts / 核心概念: Marginal Cost (邊際成本), Fixed vs Variable Cost (固定與可變成本), Profit Maximization Rule (利潤極大化法則)

1. The Question (題目)

雲妮在深水埗經營一家咖啡店。若 _______________ ，雲妮會增加生產使其利潤最大化。

A. 商業用戶水費下降
B. 商業登記費下降
C. 該咖啡店的按月租金上升
D. 兼職侍應的工資率上升
Winnie operates a café in Sham Shui Po. To maximise her profit, Winnie will produce more output if

A. water charges for commercial use fall.
B. business registration fee falls.
C. the monthly rental payment of the café rises.
D. the wage rate of part-time waiters increases.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

利潤極大化法則 (Profit Maximization Rule)

為了實現利潤最大化，廠商會生產直到邊際收益 (Marginal Revenue, MR) 等於邊際成本 (Marginal Cost, MC)。在完全競爭市場中，價格 \( P = MR \)。

To maximize profit, a firm will produce up to the point where Marginal Revenue (MR) equals Marginal Cost (MC). In a perfectly competitive market, Price \( P = MR \).

$$ MR = MC $$

成本分類與邊際成本 (Cost Classification and Marginal Cost)

  • 固定成本 (Fixed Cost, FC): 不隨產量變動的成本（例如：租金、商業登記費）。固定成本的改變不會影響邊際成本。
    Fixed Cost (FC): Costs that do not vary with output level (e.g., rent, business registration fee). Changes in FC DO NOT affect Marginal Cost.
  • 可變成本 (Variable Cost, VC): 隨產量變動的成本（例如：水費、兼職工資、原材料）。可變成本的改變會影響邊際成本。
    Variable Cost (VC): Costs that vary with output level (e.g., water charges, part-time wages, raw materials). Changes in VC WILL affect Marginal Cost.
  • 邊際成本 (Marginal Cost, MC): 每增加一單位產量所帶來的總成本增加額。
    Marginal Cost (MC): The additional total cost incurred from producing one extra unit of output.
$$ MC = \frac{\Delta TC}{\Delta Q} = \frac{\Delta VC}{\Delta Q} $$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option A

選項 A (正確): 商業用戶水費下降
水費是可變成本 (Variable Cost)，因為沖調更多咖啡需要更多水。水費下降會導致可變成本減少，從而使邊際成本 (MC) 下降。當 MC 下降時，原本的 \( MR = MC \) 均衡被打破，變成 \( MR > MC \)。為了重新達到利潤極大化，雲妮必須增加產量，直到 MC 重新上升並等於 MR。
Option A (Correct): Water charges for commercial use fall
Water charges are a Variable Cost, as brewing more coffee requires more water. A fall in water charges reduces variable costs, which in turn decreases Marginal Cost (MC). When MC falls, the firm faces \( MR > MC \). To maximize profit again, Winnie must produce more output until MC rises to equal MR.
選項 B (錯誤): 商業登記費下降
商業登記費是固定成本 (Fixed Cost)，無論賣出多少杯咖啡，這筆費用都不變。固定成本的下降不會改變邊際成本 (MC 保持不變)。因此，利潤極大化的產量（\( MR = MC \) 的位置）不會改變。
Option B (Incorrect): Business registration fee falls
The business registration fee is a Fixed Cost; it remains the same regardless of how many cups of coffee are sold. A fall in fixed costs does not change the Marginal Cost (MC remains unchanged). Therefore, the profit-maximizing output level (where \( MR = MC \)) remains unchanged.
選項 C (錯誤): 該咖啡店的按月租金上升
按月租金是固定成本 (Fixed Cost)。與選項 B 的原理相同，固定成本的上升不會影響邊際成本。只要廠商繼續營業，產出量將保持不變。（如果租金過高導致廠商停業，產量會降至零，而不是增加。）
Option C (Incorrect): The monthly rental payment of the café rises
Monthly rent is a Fixed Cost. Similar to Option B, a rise in fixed cost has no effect on Marginal Cost. As long as the firm continues to operate, the output level remains unchanged. (If the rent is too high and causes a shutdown, output would drop to zero, not increase.)
選項 D (錯誤): 兼職侍應的工資率上升
兼職侍應的工資屬於可變成本 (Variable Cost)。工資率上升會導致可變成本增加，從而使邊際成本 (MC) 上升。當 MC 上升時，\( MC > MR \)，為減少損失並實現利潤最大化，雲妮應該減少產量，而不是增加產量。
Option D (Incorrect): The wage rate of part-time waiters increases
Part-time wages are a Variable Cost. An increase in the wage rate raises variable costs, which increases Marginal Cost (MC). When MC rises, \( MC > MR \). To minimize loss and maximize profit, Winnie should produce less output, not more.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point):
這道題目的核心在於連結「產量變化」與「成本變化」。你必須知道：
1. 廠商只有在邊際成本 (MC) 下降時，才會增加產量 (假設 MR 不變)。
2. 只有可變成本 (VC) 的變化會影響邊際成本 (MC)。固定成本 (FC) 的變化與產量決策無關。
因此，解題步驟就是尋找哪一個選項描述了「可變成本下降」。
Pivot Point:
The core of this question lies in connecting "change in output" with "change in costs". You must know:
1. A firm will only increase output if its Marginal Cost (MC) falls (assuming MR is constant).
2. Only changes in Variable Cost (VC) affect Marginal Cost (MC). Changes in Fixed Cost (FC) are irrelevant to output decisions.
Therefore, the logical step is to find the option that describes a "decrease in variable cost".

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：混淆固定成本與可變成本對邊際決策的影響
許多學生看到「租金上升」(選項C) 或「登記費下降」(選項B)，會直覺認為「成本變便宜了，所以可以多生產」或「成本太貴了，要多賣一點來彌補」。這是非常嚴重的經濟學謬誤。
在經濟學中，產出決策是在「邊際」(Margin) 上做出的。多生產一杯咖啡並不需要多交一次租金或商業登記費，所以這些固定成本不影響多生產一杯咖啡的成本 (MC)。
Trap: Confusing the impact of Fixed vs Variable costs on marginal decisions
Many students see "rent rises" (Option C) or "fee falls" (Option B) and intuitively think "Costs are lower, so they can produce more" or "Costs are too high, they must sell more to cover it." This is a major economic fallacy.
In economics, output decisions are made at the "margin". Producing one more cup of coffee does not require paying rent or registration fees again. Therefore, these fixed costs do not affect the cost of producing one extra cup (MC).

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 兼職工資 (Part-time wage) 和全職工資 (Full-time wage) 在成本分類上有分別嗎？
A: 有的！在DSE經濟學中，全職員工的底薪通常被視為固定成本 (FC)，因為無論店舖今天賣出1杯還是100杯咖啡，全職薪水照樣要付。但「兼職侍應」通常按小時或按需求排班，產量增加時需要更多兼職人手，因此兼職工資被視為可變成本 (VC)。
Q: Is there a difference in cost classification between part-time wages and full-time wages?
A: Yes! In DSE Economics, the basic salary of full-time staff is usually considered a Fixed Cost (FC) because they are paid the same whether the shop sells 1 cup or 100 cups today. However, "part-time waiters" are usually scheduled by the hour or by demand; more output requires more part-time shifts. Thus, part-time wages are considered a Variable Cost (VC).

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

三秒速殺法 (3-Second Kill Method):
題目關鍵字：「增加生產」(Produce more)。
推論：增加生產 $\rightarrow$ 需要 MC 下降 $\rightarrow$ 尋找「可變成本 (VC) 下降」的選項。
掃視選項：A 是 VC 跌，B 是 FC 跌，C 是 FC 升，D 是 VC 升。
秒選 A！無需計算！
3-Second Kill Method:
Keyword in question: "Produce more".
Logic chain: Produce more $\rightarrow$ Requires MC to fall $\rightarrow$ Look for "Variable Cost (VC) falls".
Scan options: A is VC fall, B is FC fall, C is FC rise, D is VC rise.
Instantly pick A! No calculations needed!
