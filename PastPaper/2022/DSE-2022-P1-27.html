📝 Topic: National Income Accounting (國民收入會計)

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4/5)

Key Concepts (核心概念): GDP GNI Resident Status (居民身分) Net Factor Income from Abroad (對外要素收益流動淨值)

1. The Question (題目)

在計算香港的本地居民總收入(GNI)時，下列哪項會從香港的本地生產總值(GDP)中扣除？

Which of the following would be deducted from the GDP of Hong Kong to calculate the gross national income (GNI) of Hong Kong?

  1. 一位香港教授在日本某家大學以長期合約工作所收取的薪金
    the salary received by a Hong Kong professor who worked in a university in Japan on a permanent contract
  2. 一位菲律賓億萬富翁從持有某香港公司的股票賺獲的股息
    the dividend earned by a Filipino billionaire from his holding of shares of a Hong Kong company
  3. 一位印尼家庭傭工為本港某家庭工作兩年所收取的薪金
    the salary received by an Indonesian domestic worker who worked for a Hong Kong family for two years
  4. 一位香港居民從自置的英國住宅物業賺獲的租金收入
    the rental income earned from a residential property in the United Kingdom owned by a Hong Kong resident

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這條問題，我們必須清楚區分 GDP（本地生產總值） 和 GNI（本地居民總收入） 的定義，以及「居民」在經濟學上的定義。

To answer this question, we must clearly distinguish between the definitions of GDP (Gross Domestic Product) and GNI (Gross National Income), as well as the economic definition of a "resident".

  • GDP: 著重於「地域」(Territory)。只要在香港境內生產，無論生產者是否香港居民，都計算在內。
    GDP: Focuses on "Territory". As long as it is produced within the economic territory of Hong Kong, regardless of whether the producer is a resident, it is included.
  • GNI: 著重於「居民身分」(Residency)。只要是香港居民賺取的收入，無論在世界哪個角落賺取，都計算在內。
    GNI: Focuses on "Residency". As long as the income is earned by a Hong Kong resident, regardless of where in the world it is earned, it is included.
$$ GNI = GDP + \text{Net Factor Income from Abroad (NIA)} $$ $$ \text{NIA} = \text{Factor income from abroad} - \text{Factor income paid abroad} $$

因此，要從 GDP 轉換為 GNI，我們需要：
加上 (+): 本地居民從外地賺取的要素收益 (Factor income earned by residents from abroad)
扣除 (-): 非本地居民從本地賺取的要素收益 (Factor income earned by non-residents from domestic territory)

Therefore, to convert GDP to GNI, we need to:
Add (+): Factor income earned by residents from abroad.
Deduct (-): Factor income earned by non-residents from domestic territory.

💡 關鍵定義：什麼是「居民」(Resident)？
在國民收入會計中，一個人只要在一個經濟體內居住或打算居住一年或以上，即被視為該經濟體的居民。國籍 (Nationality) 完全不重要！

💡 Key Definition: What is a "Resident"?
In national income accounting, an individual is considered a resident of an economy if they stay or intend to stay in that economy for one year or more. Nationality is completely irrelevant!

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): B

A. 一位香港教授在日本某家大學以長期合約工作所收取的薪金

the salary received by a Hong Kong professor who worked in a university in Japan on a permanent contract

❌ 分析： 由於合約是「長期」的（超過一年），這位教授在經濟學上已成為日本居民，不再是香港居民。他的收入既不在香港產生（不計入香港 GDP），也不是香港居民賺取（不計入香港 GNI）。因此不需要進行任何加減調整。

❌ Analysis: Because the contract is "permanent" (more than one year), this professor is economically considered a resident of Japan, not a Hong Kong resident. His income is neither produced in HK (not in HK GDP) nor earned by a HK resident (not in HK GNI). Thus, no adjustment is needed.

B. 一位菲律賓億萬富翁從持有某香港公司的股票賺獲的股息

the dividend earned by a Filipino billionaire from his holding of shares of a Hong Kong company

✅ 分析： 這筆股息由香港公司產生，因此已包含在香港的 GDP 內。然而，這位富翁是非本地居民。根據公式，非本地居民從本地賺取的收入必須從 GDP 中扣除，才能得出 GNI。這完全符合題目的要求。

✅ Analysis: The dividend is generated by a Hong Kong company, so it is included in HK's GDP. However, the billionaire is a non-resident. According to the formula, factor income earned by non-residents from the domestic territory must be deducted from GDP to calculate GNI. This perfectly matches the question's requirement.

C. 一位印尼家庭傭工為本港某家庭工作兩年所收取的薪金

the salary received by an Indonesian domestic worker who worked for a Hong Kong family for two years

❌ 分析： 這位傭工在香港工作了「兩年」（超過一年），因此她被視為香港居民。她的薪金在香港產生（計入 GDP），同時也是香港居民的收入（計入 GNI）。因為它同時存在於 GDP 和 GNI 中，所以不需要扣除。

❌ Analysis: The domestic worker has worked in HK for "two years" (more than one year), so she is considered a Hong Kong resident. Her salary is generated in HK (included in GDP) and is earned by a HK resident (included in GNI). Since it belongs to both GDP and GNI, no deduction is needed.

D. 一位香港居民從自置的英國住宅物業賺獲的租金收入

the rental income earned from a residential property in the United Kingdom owned by a Hong Kong resident

❌ 分析： 這筆租金在英國產生，不包括在香港的 GDP 中。但因為是由香港居民賺取，所以屬於香港的 GNI。在計算時，這項收入應該被加上到 GDP 中，而不是扣除。

❌ Analysis: This rental income is generated in the UK, so it is not in HK's GDP. But since it is earned by a HK resident, it belongs to HK's GNI. In the calculation, this income should be added to GDP, not deducted.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的核心在於找出「樞紐點」(Pivot Point)。當題目問「什麼會從 GDP 中扣除以計算 GNI」時，你腦海中應該立刻浮現一個條件：

The core of this type of question is finding the "Pivot Point". When the question asks "what is deducted from GDP to calculate GNI", one condition should immediately pop into your mind:

目標 = 尋找「非本地居民」在「本地」賺取的收入
Target = Find income earned by "Non-residents" in the "Domestic Territory"

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ 陷阱：混淆「國籍」(Nationality) 與「居民身分」(Residency)

很多學生看到選項 C 的「印尼家庭傭工」，就直覺認為她是外國人，所以她的收入應該被扣除。這是 DSE 經濟科最常見的陷阱！記住，經濟學只看逗留時間（通常為一年）。只要在香港工作超過一年，無論持有什麼護照，都是香港的經濟居民。

⚠️ Trap: Confusing "Nationality" with "Residency"
Many students see "Indonesian domestic worker" in Option C and intuitively think she is a foreigner, so her income should be deducted. This is the most common trap in DSE Economics! Remember, economics only looks at the duration of stay (usually one year). As long as they work in HK for over a year, regardless of their passport, they are economic residents of HK.

6. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法 (Shortcut):

  1. 題目要求「扣除」(Deduct) \(\rightarrow\) 必須是非居民賺的錢。
  2. 掃描選項尋找「非居民」：
    • A: 教授在日本長期工作 \(\rightarrow\) 日本居民。
    • B: 菲律賓富翁在香港買股票 \(\rightarrow\) 沒說住在香港，通常視為非居民。
    • C: 傭工工作兩年 \(\rightarrow\) 香港居民。
    • D: 香港居民 \(\rightarrow\) 已經寫明是香港居民。
  3. 只有 B 涉及非居民在香港賺錢，秒殺！

Shortcut:
1. The question asks to "Deduct" \(\rightarrow\) It must be money earned by a non-resident.
2. Scan options for "non-residents": A is a Japan resident, C is a HK resident, D is a HK resident. B is the only clear non-resident earning money in HK. Instant kill!
