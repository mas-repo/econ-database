2. The Question (題目)

一年期存款的名義利率是 1%，而通脹率是 3%。持有現金的成本是 __________ 而持有現金的實質回報率是 __________。

  • A. 1% …… -3%
  • B. 1% …… -2%
  • C. 3% …… -3%
  • D. 3% …… -2%

The nominal interest rate on a one-year deposit is 1% and the inflation rate is 3%. The cost of holding cash is ____________ and the real rate of return of holding cash is ___________.

  • A. 1% …… -3%
  • B. 1% …… -2%
  • C. 3% …… -3%
  • D. 3% …… -2%

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

A. Cost of Holding Cash (持有現金的成本)

持有現金意味著你放棄了將這筆錢存入銀行所能賺取的利息。因此，持有現金的機會成本（Opportunity Cost）就是名義利率 (Nominal Interest Rate)。

Holding cash means you are giving up the interest you could have earned if you deposited the money in a bank. Therefore, the opportunity cost of holding cash is the Nominal Interest Rate.

B. Fisher Equation (費雪方程式)

實質回報率是扣除通脹影響後的購買力增長。公式如下：

The real rate of return is the growth in purchasing power after adjusting for inflation. The formula is:

$$r = R - \pi$$

$r$ = 實質利率 (Real Interest Rate)
$R$ = 名義利率 (Nominal Interest Rate)
$\pi$ = 通脹率 (Inflation Rate)

C. Return on Cash (現金的回報率)

這是一個非常重要的隱藏設定：現金的名義回報率永遠是 0%（放在錢包裡的 $100 紙幣，一年後依然是 $100）。

This is a crucial hidden assumption: The nominal rate of return on cash is always 0% (A $100 banknote in your wallet will still be $100 a year later nominally).

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option 正確答案: A

Option A (1% …… -3%) - CORRECT (正確)

第一格（持有現金的成本）：等於存款的名義利率，即 1%。
第二格（現金的實質回報率）：現金的名義利率是 0%。利用公式：現金實質回報 = 現金名義回報 (0%) - 通脹率 (3%) = -3%。

First blank (Cost of holding cash): Equals the nominal interest rate on deposits, which is 1%.
Second blank (Real rate of return of cash): The nominal return on cash is 0%. Using the formula: Real return on cash = Nominal return on cash (0%) - Inflation rate (3%) = -3%.

Option B (1% …… -2%) - WRONG (錯誤)

第一格正確。但第二格的 -2% 是存款的實質回報率（1% - 3% = -2%），而不是現金的實質回報率。題目問的是持有現金的回報，而非存款。

The first blank is correct. However, -2% in the second blank is the real rate of return of the deposit (1% - 3% = -2%), not of cash. The question specifically asks for the return on holding cash.

Option C (3% …… -3%) - WRONG (錯誤)

第一格錯誤。3% 是通脹率，代表購買力下降的速度，但持有現金的「機會成本」是你放棄的銀行利息（1%）。第二格計算正確。

The first blank is wrong. 3% is the inflation rate, representing the loss of purchasing power, but the "opportunity cost" of holding cash is the bank interest you gave up (1%). The second blank is calculated correctly.

Option D (3% …… -2%) - WRONG (錯誤)

第一格與第二格的觀念皆錯誤。混合了上述 C 選項與 B 選項的常犯錯誤。

Both concepts in the first and second blanks are wrong. This option combines the common mistakes found in options C and B.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

  • Pivot Point (破題關鍵): 必須意識到「現金 (Cash)」與「存款 (Deposit)」在回報率上的分別。題目給予的 1% 是存款利率；而現金的名義利率永遠隱含為 0%。
  • Pivot Point: You must recognize the difference in returns between "Cash" and "Deposits". The 1% given is the deposit rate; the nominal interest rate of cash is always implicitly 0%.
  • Distractor (陷阱資訊): 存款的實質回報率 (-2%)。這是一個誘餌，引誘那些死背公式但忽略了主語（現金 vs 存款）的學生。
  • Distractor: The real rate of return on deposits (-2%). This is bait designed to catch students who blindly apply the formula without paying attention to the subject (cash vs deposit).

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

❌ Careless Error: 很多同學一看到公式 $r = R - \pi$，就直接將題目提供的數字代入（$1\% - 3\% = -2\%$），然後選擇了包含 -2% 的選項 (B 或 D)。

💡 Correction: 公式沒錯，但代入的數字錯了！計算「現金」的實質回報，名義利率 $R$ 必須代入 0%，而不是存款的 1%。

❌ Careless Error: Many students see the formula and immediately plug in the provided numbers ($1\% - 3\% = -2\%$), choosing options with -2% (B or D).
💡 Correction: The formula is correct, but the inputs are wrong! To calculate the real return of "cash", the nominal interest rate $R$ must be 0%, not the deposit's 1%.

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼持有現金的成本不是通脹率 (3%)，而是名義利率 (1%)？
Why isn't the cost of holding cash the inflation rate (3%), but rather the nominal interest rate (1%)?

A: 在經濟學中，成本指的是「機會成本 (Opportunity Cost)」。如果你不持有現金，你會把錢放在哪裡？你會存入銀行賺取 1% 的利息。因此，你因為選擇持有現金而「放棄」的最高價值選擇，就是那 1% 的名義利息。通脹的影響已經被包含在銀行設定的利率考量中了。

A: In Economics, cost refers to "Opportunity Cost". If you didn't hold cash, where would you put the money? You would deposit it in a bank to earn 1% interest. Therefore, the highest-valued option you "give up" by choosing to hold cash is that 1% nominal interest. The impact of inflation is already factored into the interest rate decisions of banks.

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

做這類 MC 題目時，可以使用「消去法 (Elimination Method)」：

When solving this type of MC question, use the Elimination Method:

  1. 讀到「持有現金的成本 (Cost of holding cash)」，腦海應立即浮現法則：成本 = 名義利率。
  2. Upon reading "Cost of holding cash", the rule should instantly pop into your mind: Cost = Nominal Interest Rate.
  3. 立刻將第一格不是 1% 的選項刪除，即瞬間排除 C 和 D。
  4. Immediately eliminate options where the first blank is not 1%. You instantly cross out C and D.
  5. 剩下 A 和 B。再看第二格「持有現金的實質回報」，記得現金不會生息，所以是 $0\% - 3\% = -3\%$。秒選 A！
  6. You are left with A and B. Look at the second blank "real rate of return of holding cash". Remember cash bears no interest, so it is $0\% - 3\% = -3\%$. Select A instantly!
