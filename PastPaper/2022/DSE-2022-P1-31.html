Economics MCQ Analysis 經濟學選擇題分析

Topic / 課題: Hong Kong's Three-Tier Banking System (香港三級銀行體制)

Difficulty / 難度: ★★☆☆☆ (2/5 Stars)

Key Concepts / 核心概念: Three-Tier Banking System (三級銀行體制), Savings Deposit (儲蓄存款), Licensed Banks (持牌銀行)

1. The Question (題目)

大衛計劃將1百萬元存入香港一家金融機構作儲蓄存款。下列哪類／些機構能接納該筆儲蓄存款？

(1) 持牌銀行
(2) 有限制牌照銀行
(3) 接受存款公司

A. 只有 (1)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

David plans to deposit $1 million to a financial institution in Hong Kong as savings deposit. Which of the following types of institution can receive this amount of savings deposits?

(1) a licensed bank
(2) a restricted licence bank
(3) a deposit-taking company

A. (1) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這道題目，我們必須清楚了解香港「三級銀行體制」中不同機構接受存款的限制。這與香港貨幣供應量的定義息息相關。

To solve this question, we must clearly understand the deposit-taking restrictions of different institutions in Hong Kong's "Three-Tier Banking System". This is also closely related to the definitions of Hong Kong's money supply.

  • 持牌銀行 (Licensed Banks, LBs): 唯一可以接受往來存款 (Demand/Current Deposits) 及 儲蓄存款 (Savings Deposits) 的機構。接受存款沒有金額和期限的限制。

    Licensed Banks (LBs): The ONLY institutions that can accept demand/current deposits and savings deposits. There are no restrictions on the amount or maturity of deposits.

  • 有限制牌照銀行 (Restricted Licence Banks, RLBs): 只能接受定期存款 (Time Deposits)，且最低存款額為 \( \$500,000 \)。

    Restricted Licence Banks (RLBs): Can ONLY accept time deposits, with a minimum deposit amount of \( \$500,000 \).

  • 接受存款公司 (Deposit-taking Companies, DTCs): 只能接受定期存款 (Time Deposits)，最低存款額為 \( \$100,000 \)，且最初存款期最少為 3 個月。

    Deposit-taking Companies (DTCs): Can ONLY accept time deposits, with a minimum deposit amount of \( \$100,000 \), and an original maturity of at least 3 months.

相關公式 (貨幣供應量 Money Supply):

Relevant Formulas (Money Supply):

$$ M1 = C + D $$ $$ M2 = M1 + S + T_{LB} + NCD_{LB} $$ $$ M3 = M2 + T_{RLB, DTC} + NCD_{RLB, DTC} $$

在此，\( S \) 代表儲蓄存款。由於只有持牌銀行能接受儲蓄存款，所以 \( S \) 只會出現在 \( M2 \) 及 \( M3 \) 的計算中。

Here, \( S \) represents savings deposits. Since only LBs can accept savings deposits, \( S \) only appears in the calculation of \( M2 \) and \( M3 \).

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 Correct Option: A

選項 A 分析： 正確。由於大衛希望存入的是「儲蓄存款」(Savings Deposit)，在香港的三級銀行體制下，只有持牌銀行 (Licensed Banks) 獲授權經營儲蓄存款及往來存款業務。

Analysis for Option A: Correct. Since David wants to make a "savings deposit", under Hong Kong's three-tier banking system, only licensed banks are authorized to operate savings and demand/current deposit accounts.

選項 B, C, D 分析： 錯誤。雖然大衛的存款金額為 1百萬元，這筆金額在數學上超過了有限制牌照銀行的門檻 (\( \$1,000,000 > \$500,000 \)) 以及接受存款公司的門檻 (\( \$1,000,000 > \$100,000 \))，但這兩類機構依法不能接受任何儲蓄存款。因此，(2) 和 (3) 均不能接納這筆款項。

Analysis for Options B, C, D: Incorrect. Although David's deposit amount is $1 million, which mathematically exceeds the threshold for RLBs (\( \$1,000,000 > \$500,000 \)) and DTCs (\( \$1,000,000 > \$100,000 \)), these two types of institutions are legally prohibited from accepting savings deposits. Therefore, neither (2) nor (3) can accept this sum.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解決這類題目的關鍵在於辨識「關鍵字」與「干擾資訊」。

The key to solving this type of question is distinguishing between the "pivot point" and the "distracting information".

  • 拆題樞紐 (Pivot Point): 題目中的 「儲蓄存款」(Savings Deposit) 是解題的最核心關鍵。一看到這四個字，就可以立即將範圍縮小至持牌銀行。

    Pivot Point: The phrase "savings deposit" in the question is the absolute core to solving it. The moment you see these words, you can immediately narrow the scope down to Licensed Banks only.

  • 干擾資訊 (Distractors): 題目刻意給出 「1百萬元」(\$1 million) 這個數字。這是為了引誘那些背誦了最低存款額 (\( \$500,000 \) 及 \( \$100,000 \)) 但忘記了「存款種類」限制的學生。學生可能會誤以為只要金額達標，任何機構都可以接受存款。

    Distractors: The question deliberately provides the number "$1 million". This is to lure students who have memorized the minimum deposit thresholds (\( \$500,000 \) and \( \$100,000 \)) but forgot the restrictions on the "type of deposit". Students might mistakenly think that as long as the amount meets the requirement, any institution can accept the deposit.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱一：死背數字，忽略性質
很多考生在溫習時只專注背誦 \( \$500,000 \) (RLB) 和 \( \$100,000 \) (DTC)，導致在考試時一看到「1百萬」，就直覺認為「大於50萬，所以三間都可以」，從而錯選 D。

Trap 1: Rote memorizing numbers while ignoring the nature of deposits
Many candidates solely focus on memorizing \( \$500,000 \) (RLB) and \( \$100,000 \) (DTC) during revision. As a result, when they see "$1 million" in the exam, their intuition tells them "it's greater than 500k, so all three can accept it," leading them to wrongly choose D.

防錯口訣：「先看戶口種類，再看金額大小。」

Error Prevention Rule: "Check the account TYPE first, then check the deposit AMOUNT."

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

問：如果題目把「儲蓄存款」改成「定期存款」(Time Deposit)，答案會不同嗎？

Q: If the question changed "savings deposit" to "time deposit", would the answer be different?

答：會！如果是定期存款，那我們就需要考慮金額了。因為 1百萬元大於 RLB 的下限 (\( \$500,000 \))，也大於 DTC 的下限 (\( \$100,000 \))，而持牌銀行則沒有限制。所以在這情況下，(1)、(2) 及 (3) 都能接受該筆存款，答案將會變成 D。

A: Yes! If it were a time deposit, then we WOULD need to consider the amount. Because $1 million is greater than the RLB threshold (\( \$500,000 \)) and greater than the DTC threshold (\( \$100,000 \)), and LBs have no limits. In that scenario, all (1), (2), and (3) could accept the deposit, and the answer would become D.

問：為什麼政府要限制 RLB 和 DTC 不能接受儲蓄存款？

Q: Why does the government restrict RLBs and DTCs from accepting savings deposits?

答：這是為了保障小存戶和維持金融體系穩定。儲蓄及往來存款通常屬於小市民的日常資金，流動性極高。限制只有資本要求最雄厚、監管最嚴格的「持牌銀行」才能處理這類存款，可以減低銀行擠提（Bank Run）的風險。

A: This is to protect small depositors and maintain financial system stability. Savings and demand deposits usually consist of the daily funds of the general public and are highly liquid. Restricting these deposits to only "Licensed Banks"—which have the strongest capital requirements and strictest regulations—reduces the risk of bank runs.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

三秒速解法：
做有關三級銀行的題目時，執行以下條件反射：
看到 「往來存款 (Demand)」 或 「儲蓄存款 (Savings)」 ➔ 立即劃掉 (2) RLB 和 (3) DTC。連數字都不用看，直接選只有 (1) 持牌銀行的選項。

3-Second Shortcut:
When doing questions about the three-tier banking system, execute this conditioned reflex:
See "Demand deposit" or "Savings deposit" ➔ Immediately cross out (2) RLBs and (3) DTCs. You don't even need to look at the numbers. Directly choose the option with (1) Licensed Banks only.
