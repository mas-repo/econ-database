HKDSE Economics Tutorial

課題 Topic 物品的種類 Types of Goods
難度 Difficulty ★★★☆☆
關鍵詞 Keywords 消費者物品, 共用品, 免費物品

1. 題目 The Question

政府購入了2020東京奧運的轉播權，令全港市民能免費觀賞各項賽事。該奧運廣播屬 ____________，因為 ____________。

The Government purchased the broadcasting rights of the Tokyo 2020 Olympic Games. This enabled all Hong Kong people to watch the events for free. The Olympic Games broadcast is a _____________ because ____________.

  • A. 共用品 …… 政府支付了轉播權費用
    A. public good …… the Government paid for the broadcasting rights
  • B. 私用品 …… 某一個人的消費會令其他人可享用的數量減少
    B. private good …… one individual’s consumption reduces its amount available to others
  • C. 免費物品 …… 人們並不偏好更多該物品
    C. free good …… more is not preferred
  • D. 消費者物品 …… 它能直接滿足人們的欲望
    D. consumer good …… it can directly satisfy human wants

2. 概念重溫 Concept Review

要破解這道題目，我們需要釐清三組核心的經濟學概念：

To crack this question, we need to clarify three core pairs of economic concepts:

  1. 消費者物品 vs. 生產者物品 (Consumer Good vs. Capital Good)

    分類準則在於物品的用途。如果物品被用作直接滿足人類欲望，它就是消費者物品。如果物品被用作輔助生產其他物品，它就是資本/生產者物品。

    The classification depends on the use of the good. If it is used to directly satisfy human wants, it is a consumer good. If it is used to assist in producing other goods, it is a capital good.

  2. 共用品 vs. 私用品 (Public Good vs. Private Good)

    分類準則在於消費時是否具有競爭性 (Rivalrous) 及 排他性 (Excludable)。共用品是指一個人的消費不會減少其他人可享用的數量（非競爭性）。這與「由誰支付或提供」（例如政府）完全無關！

    The classification depends on whether consumption is rivalrous and excludable. A public good means one person's consumption does not reduce the amount available to others (non-rivalrous). This has absolutely NOTHING to do with "who pays or provides it" (e.g., the government)!

  3. 免費物品 vs. 經濟物品 (Free Good vs. Economic Good)

    經濟物品是指其數量不足以滿足所有人的欲望（即存在稀少性），因此生產或獲取它涉及機會成本 (Opportunity Cost)。相反，免費物品的機會成本為零。

    An economic good is one whose quantity is insufficient to satisfy all human wants (i.e., scarcity exists), so producing or obtaining it involves an opportunity cost. Conversely, the opportunity cost of a free good is zero.

$$ \text{Economic Good} \iff \text{Opportunity Cost} > 0 $$ $$ \text{Free Good} \iff \text{Opportunity Cost} = 0 $$

3. 答案與詳細分析 Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Answer: Option D
A 錯誤 Wrong: 雖然電視廣播一般具有非競爭性，因此帶有共用品 (Public Good) 的特質，但選項後半部的原因錯誤。一件物品是否共用品，取決於其消費性質（非競爭性），而不是因為「政府支付了費用」。政府也會購買私用品（例如辦公室文具）。
While a TV broadcast is generally non-rivalrous and thus has public good characteristics, the reasoning in the second half is wrong. Whether a good is a public good depends on the nature of its consumption (non-rivalrous), not because "the Government paid for it." The government also buys private goods (e.g., office stationery).
B 錯誤 Wrong: 電視廣播的性質是：多一個人收看，並不會減弱或減少其他觀眾欣賞該節目的質量或數量。因此，廣播是非競爭性 (Non-rivalrous) 的，不符合私用品的定義。
The nature of a TV broadcast is that one additional viewer does not diminish or reduce the quality or quantity available to other viewers. Therefore, broadcasting is non-rivalrous and does not fit the definition of a private good.
C 錯誤 Wrong: 奧運廣播絕對是經濟物品 (Economic Good)，因為政府動用了公帑（涉及機會成本 \( \text{Cost} > 0 \)）去購買轉播權。此外，人們普遍「偏好更多」精彩的奧運賽事。
The Olympic broadcast is definitely an economic good because the government used public funds (involving an opportunity cost \( \text{Cost} > 0 \)) to purchase the rights. Additionally, people generally "prefer more" exciting Olympic events.
D 正確 Correct: 市民觀賞奧運廣播是為了娛樂和支持運動員，這項行為直接滿足了市民的欲望。因此，按照物品的用途分類，它正確地被歸類為消費者物品 (Consumer Good)。
Citizens watch the Olympic broadcast for entertainment and to support athletes; this action directly satisfies their wants. Therefore, according to the classification by use, it is correctly categorized as a consumer good.

4. 題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

這類題目的「樞紐點 (Pivot Point)」在於連接詞「因為 (because)」。在 DSE 經濟科中，很多選項的「結論」（前半截）可能是對的，但「解釋」（後半截）卻錯得離譜。

  • 干擾資訊 (Distractors): 題目特意寫出「政府購入」和「免費觀賞」，這兩個詞語是為了誘導你選擇 (A) 共用品 或 (C) 免費物品。
  • 解題關鍵: 檢驗每一個選項時，必須同時問自己兩個問題：(1) 這個分類正確嗎？ (2) 這個定義/原因正確嗎？ 只有兩者皆對，才是最終答案。

The "Pivot Point" of this type of question lies in the conjunction "because". In DSE Economics, the "conclusion" (first half) of an option might be correct, but the "explanation" (second half) is completely wrong.

  • Distractors: The question deliberately includes "Government purchased" and "watch for free" to trick you into choosing (A) public good or (C) free good.
  • Key to Solving: When checking each option, you must ask yourself two questions simultaneously: (1) Is the classification correct? (2) Is the definition/reason correct? Only when BOTH are true is it the final answer.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Traps

陷阱 1：混淆「免費提供 (Free of charge)」與「免費物品 (Free good)」
政府讓你「免費」看奧運，只是免去了你的金錢代價 (Zero money price)。但生產/獲取這廣播依然動用了社會資源（政府付了版權費），有機會成本，所以它是經濟物品 (Economic good)。

Trap 1: Confusing "Free of charge" with "Free good"
The government letting you watch the Olympics for "free" simply means a zero money price for you. However, producing/obtaining this broadcast still used society's resources (the government paid royalties), so it has an opportunity cost, making it an economic good.

陷阱 2：以為「政府提供 = 共用品 (Public Good)」
DSE 考評局最喜歡考這個謬誤！共用品的唯一定義是「非競爭性」，與提供者是誰無關。政府提供的醫療、教育，很多都具有競爭性（床位有限），所以是私用品！

Trap 2: Assuming "Government provision = Public Good"
The HKEAA loves testing this fallacy! The ONLY definition of a public good is "non-rivalrous consumption," regardless of who provides it. Government-provided healthcare and education are often rivalrous (limited beds/seats), making them private goods!

6. 學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

Q: 如果選項 A 改成「共用品 …… 某一個人的消費不會減少其他人可享用的數量」，那 A 會是正確答案嗎？
Q: If Option A was changed to "public good …… one individual’s consumption does not reduce the amount available to others", would A be correct?
A: 是的！如果選項 A 這樣寫，A 就會變成完全正確的描述。電視廣播的確具有非競爭性，屬於共用品的範疇。這更證明了原題選項 A 的錯處在於它的「原因 (because)」部分。
A: Yes! If Option A were written this way, it would be a completely correct description. TV broadcasting is indeed non-rivalrous and falls under the category of public goods. This further proves that the original Option A was wrong solely because of its "because" section.
Q: 奧運廣播有沒有可能變成「生產者物品 (Capital good)」？
Q: Is it possible for the Olympic broadcast to become a "capital good"?
A: 有可能！分類取決於用途。如果一間酒吧播放奧運是為了吸引顧客來買啤酒（輔助生產/提供服務），那麼對酒吧老闆而言，這廣播就是生產者物品。但在本題中，題目強調「市民觀賞」，所以是直接滿足欲望的消費者物品。
A: It is possible! Classification depends on use. If a bar broadcasts the Olympics to attract customers to buy beer (assisting in production/providing service), then to the bar owner, the broadcast is a capital good. But in this question, it emphasizes "Hong Kong people watch," so it directly satisfies human wants, making it a consumer good.

7. 考試技巧與速解 Exam Tricks

速解法：剔除法 (Elimination Method) 專攻「定義」
做這類 [物品] ... 因為 ... [原因] 的題目時，先不要管前半截的物品名稱！先掃視後半截的定義是否符合經濟學常理。
- 掃視 A：政府付款 = 共用品？ (錯，秒殺 A)
- 掃視 C：人們不偏好更多 = 免費物品？ (錯，免費物品是指機會成本為零，人們仍然偏好更多，秒殺 C)
只剩下 B 和 D 要認真看前半截，大大節省時間！

Shortcut: Elimination Method targeting "Definitions"
When doing [Good] ... because ... [Reason] questions, ignore the first half (the good's name) initially! First, scan the second half to see if the definition makes economic sense.
- Scan A: Gov pays = Public good? (False, eliminate A instantly).
- Scan C: More is not preferred = Free good? (False, a free good means zero opportunity cost, people still prefer more, eliminate C instantly).
You are only left with B and D to read carefully, saving a lot of time!
