📚 課題 (Topic): 貨幣與銀行 - 貨幣創造 (Money and Banking - Money Creation)

⭐ 難度 (Difficulty): ★★★☆☆ (3/5 Stars)

🔑 關鍵字 (Key Concepts): 百分百儲備 (100% Reserve), 銀行乘數 (Banking Multiplier), 貨幣基礎 (Monetary Base), 貨幣供應 (Money Supply)

📝 題目 (The Question)

假設中央銀行要求商業銀行保留全部存款作為儲備。下列哪項陳述是不正確的？

A. 貨幣基礎會等於貨幣供應。
B. 法定儲備比率會等於一。
C. 最大銀行乘數會等於零。
D. 相對於一個採用部分儲備制度的銀行系統，該銀行系統的貨幣創造能力會較低。

Suppose the central bank requires the commercial banks to keep all deposits as reserves. Which of the following statements is INCORRECT?

A. The monetary base will be equal to the money supply.
B. The required reserve ratio will be equal to one.
C. The maximum banking multiplier will be equal to zero.
D. The money creation ability of the banking system will be smaller than that of a fractional reserve banking system.

🧠 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

百分百儲備制度 (100% Reserve System): 當銀行必須將所有存款作為儲備保留時，儲備 \( R \) 等於存款 \( D \)。
When banks must keep all deposits as reserves, Reserves \( R \) equal Deposits \( D \).

法定儲備比率 (Required Reserve Ratio, RRR): 銀行必須保留作儲備的存款比例。
The fraction of deposits that banks are required to keep as reserves.

$$ RRR = \frac{\text{Reserves } (R)}{\text{Deposits } (D)} $$ $$ \text{Maximum Banking Multiplier (最大銀行乘數)} = \frac{1}{RRR} $$ $$ \text{Monetary Base (貨幣基礎, MB)} = C_p + R $$ $$ \text{Money Supply (貨幣供應, Ms)} = C_p + D $$

註：\( C_p \) 為公眾持有的法定貨幣 (Cash in public hands)。

✅ 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): C (因為題目要求選出「不正確」的陳述)

A. 貨幣基礎會等於貨幣供應 - 正確陳述 (True Statement)
在百分百儲備下，儲備等於存款 (\( R = D \))。因此，貨幣基礎 (\( MB = C_p + R \)) 會完全等於貨幣供應 (\( M_s = C_p + D \))。這陳述是正確的，所以不選。
Under 100% reserves, \( R = D \). Therefore, Monetary Base (\( MB = C_p + R \)) equals Money Supply (\( M_s = C_p + D \)). This statement is true, so it is not the answer.

B. 法定儲備比率會等於一 - 正確陳述 (True Statement)
保留「全部」存款作為儲備，意味著 \( RRR = 100\% = 1 \)。這陳述是正確的，所以不選。
Keeping "all" deposits as reserves means \( RRR = 100\% = 1 \). This statement is true, so it is not the answer.

C. 最大銀行乘數會等於零 - 不正確陳述 (INCORRECT Statement) -> 答案 (Answer)
最大銀行乘數公式為 \( \frac{1}{RRR} \)。既然 \( RRR = 1 \)，最大銀行乘數 = \( \frac{1}{1} = 1 \)，而不是零。因為這陳述是錯的，所以這是本題的答案！
The maximum banking multiplier formula is \( \frac{1}{RRR} \). Since \( RRR = 1 \), the multiplier is \( \frac{1}{1} = 1 \), NOT zero. Because this statement is false, it is the correct answer!

D. 相對於部分儲備制度，貨幣創造能力會較低 - 正確陳述 (True Statement)
部分儲備制度 (Fractional reserve) 的乘數大於 1，能創造新存款；而百分百儲備的乘數為 1，無法創造新存款（創造能力為 0）。因此其創造能力確實較低。這陳述是正確的，所以不選。
A fractional reserve system has a multiplier > 1, creating new deposits. A 100% reserve system has a multiplier of 1, creating no new deposits. Thus, its creation ability is indeed smaller. This statement is true, so it is not the answer.

🔍 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point):
看到「保留全部存款作為儲備」(keep all deposits as reserves)，必須立刻聯想到：
1. \( RRR = 100\% = 1 \)
2. 乘數 (Multiplier) = \( \frac{1}{1} = 1 \)
3. 留意題目是問「不正確 (INCORRECT)」的選項！

When you see "keep all deposits as reserves", immediately deduce: 1. \( RRR = 1 \). 2. Multiplier = 1. 3. Watch out for the word "INCORRECT" in the question!

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

混淆「銀行乘數」與「貨幣創造量」 (Confusing Multiplier with Money Creation)
很多同學誤以為「不能創造貨幣，所以乘數是 0」。這是錯的！
總存款 = 初始存款 \( \times \) 銀行乘數。
如果初始存款是 $100，總存款也是 $100（沒有新增），那麼乘數是 \( 100 / 100 = 1 \)。乘數為 1 代表「維持原狀」，乘數為 0 在數學上是不可能的（因為 \( RRR \) 最大只有 100%）。
Many students think "no money is created, so the multiplier is 0". This is wrong! Total Deposits = Initial Deposit \( \times \) Multiplier. If Initial Deposit is $100 and Total Deposits remain $100, the multiplier is 1. A multiplier of 1 means "no change". A multiplier of 0 is mathematically impossible here.

🙋 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼 \( MB = M_s \) (選項A) 是對的？公式不是不一樣嗎？
Why is Option A (\( MB = M_s \)) correct? Aren't their formulas different?

A: 公式確實不同：\( MB = C_p + R \)，而 \( M_s = C_p + D \)。但在「百分百儲備」的特殊情況下，銀行把所有存款 \( D \) 都變成了儲備 \( R \)，所以 \( R = D \)。將 \( D \) 代入 \( MB \) 的公式中，就會得出 \( MB = C_p + D = M_s \)。
The formulas are different: \( MB = C_p + R \) and \( M_s = C_p + D \). However, under 100% reserves, banks keep all deposits \( D \) as reserves \( R \), so \( R = D \). Substituting \( D \) for \( R \), we get \( MB = C_p + D = M_s \).

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

「百分百儲備」秒殺四部曲 (The 100% Reserve 4-Step Rule):
在 DSE 考試中，只要見到「100%儲備 / 全部存款作儲備」，請立刻在題目旁邊寫下這四個等式：
1. \( RRR = 1 \)
2. \( \text{Multiplier} = 1 \)
3. \( R = D \)
4. \( MB = M_s \)
寫完後再看選項，答案 (C 說乘數是 0) 瞬間無所遁形！
Whenever you see "100% reserve", immediately write down these four equations: 1. \( RRR = 1 \), 2. Multiplier = 1, 3. \( R = D \), 4. \( MB = M_s \). The wrong statement (C) will be instantly obvious!
