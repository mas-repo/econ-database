📝 題目 / The Question

假設中央銀行要求商業銀行保留全部存款作為儲備。下列哪項陳述是不正確的？
Suppose the central bank requires the commercial banks to keep all deposits as reserves. Which of the following statements is INCORRECT?
  • A. 貨幣基礎會等於貨幣供應。
    The monetary base will be equal to the money supply.
  • B. 法定儲備比率會等於一。
    The required reserve ratio will be equal to one.
  • C. 最大銀行乘數會等於零。
    The maximum banking multiplier will be equal to zero.
  • D. 相對於一個採用部分儲備制度的銀行系統，該銀行系統的貨幣創造能力會較低。
    The money creation ability of the banking system will be smaller than that of a fractional reserve banking system.

🧠 概念重溫 / Concept Review

要解答這條題目，我們需要理解「百分百儲備制度」（100% Reserve System）下的貨幣創造機制。當銀行必須把所有存款作為儲備時，它們無法借出任何款項。
To answer this question, we must understand the money creation mechanism under a "100% Reserve System". When banks must keep all deposits as reserves, they cannot lend out any money.
法定儲備比率 (Required Reserve Ratio, \(rrr\)): $$ rrr = \frac{\text{儲備 Reserves } (R)}{\text{存款 Deposits } (D)} $$ 最大銀行乘數 (Maximum Banking Multiplier, \(m\)): $$ m = \frac{1}{rrr} $$ 貨幣基礎 (Monetary Base, \(MB\)) 與 貨幣供應 (Money Supply, \(M_s\)): $$ MB = C_p + R $$ $$ M_s = C_p + D $$ (\(C_p\) = 公眾持有現金 Cash in public hands)

✅ 答案與詳細分析 / Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 / Correct Option: C (因為題目要求選出不正確的陳述 / Because the question asks for the INCORRECT statement)

Option A (正確陳述 True Statement)
因為銀行保留全部存款作為儲備，所以儲備等於存款 \(R = D\)。代入公式：\(MB = C_p + R\) 及 \(M_s = C_p + D\)，得出 \(MB = M_s\)。
Since banks keep all deposits as reserves, Reserves equal Deposits \(R = D\). Substituting into the formulas: \(MB = C_p + R\) and \(M_s = C_p + D\), we get \(MB = M_s\).
Option B (正確陳述 True Statement)
法定儲備比率 \(rrr = R / D\)。既然 \(R = D\)，那麼 \(rrr = 1\)（即 100%）。
Required reserve ratio \(rrr = R / D\). Since \(R = D\), then \(rrr = 1\) (or 100%).
Option C (不正確陳述 INCORRECT Statement - 此為答案 This is the answer)
最大銀行乘數公式為 \(1 / rrr\)。既然 \(rrr = 1\)，最大銀行乘數應為 \(1 / 1 = 1\)，而不是零。
The maximum banking multiplier formula is \(1 / rrr\). Since \(rrr = 1\), the maximum multiplier should be \(1 / 1 = 1\), NOT zero.
Option D (正確陳述 True Statement)
部分儲備制度下，銀行乘數大於 1，能創造存款。但在百分百儲備制下，乘數為 1，銀行無法創造額外貨幣，因此能力較低。
Under a fractional reserve system, the multiplier is greater than 1, allowing deposit creation. Under a 100% reserve system, the multiplier is 1, so banks cannot create extra money, meaning their ability is smaller.

🔍 題目拆解 / Problem Decomposition

  • 關鍵字 (Pivot Point): 「保留全部存款作為儲備」 (keep all deposits as reserves)。這句話直接告訴你 \(rrr = 100\% = 1\)。
  • 解題目標: 找出「不正確」的選項。這類題目容易因為粗心而選錯，建議在試卷上將「不正確」三個字圈起來。
  • Pivot Point: "Keep all deposits as reserves". This phrase directly translates to \(rrr = 100\% = 1\).
  • Goal: Find the "INCORRECT" option. Students often lose marks here due to carelessness. Always circle the word "INCORRECT" on your exam paper.

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 / Common Mistakes & Traps

很多學生會誤以為「不能創造貨幣」就等於「乘數為零」。實際上，乘數為 1 代表初始存款沒有被放大（100元存進去，總存款還是100元）。如果乘數為 0，代表存進去的錢消失了，這在邏輯上是不可能的！
Many students mistakenly believe that "cannot create money" means "the multiplier is zero". In reality, a multiplier of 1 means the initial deposit is not magnified (deposit $100, total deposits remain $100). If the multiplier were 0, it would mean the deposited money vanished, which is logically impossible!

🙋 學生常見疑問 / Student Q&A

問：為什麼數學上銀行乘數不可能是 0？
Q: Why is it mathematically impossible for the banking multiplier to be 0?
答： 因為乘數的公式是 \(1 / rrr\)。法定儲備比率 (\(rrr\)) 的最大值是 100% (即 1)。\(1 \div 1 = 1\)。在數學上，除非分母無限大，否則分數不可能等於 0。
A: Because the multiplier formula is \(1 / rrr\). The maximum value of the required reserve ratio (\(rrr\)) is 100% (or 1). \(1 \div 1 = 1\). Mathematically, a fraction cannot equal 0 unless the denominator is infinitely large.

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 / Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

極限排除法： 當你看到「乘數等於零」這種極端的數字時，要特別警惕。在經濟學中，乘數（Multiplier）通常代表一個變數對另一個變數的倍數效應。即使沒有放大效應，原本的數值（1倍）依然存在，因此乘數極少會是 0。看到 Option C 就可以高度懷疑它是錯的。
Extreme Value Elimination: Be highly suspicious when you see extreme numbers like "multiplier equals zero". In economics, a multiplier represents a multiple effect. Even with no magnification, the original value (1x) remains, so multipliers are rarely 0. Seeing Option C should immediately make it your primary suspect for the "incorrect" statement.
