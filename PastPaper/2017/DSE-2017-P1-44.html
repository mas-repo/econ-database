📚 Topic (主題): Macroeconomics - Exchange Rates (總體經濟學 - 匯率)
⭐ Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆ (3/5)
🔑 Key Concepts (核心概念): Exchange Rate (匯率), Currency Appreciation (貨幣升值), Export Competitiveness (出口競爭力), Total Value (總值)

2. The Question (題目)

日期與匯率：
2015年11月22日 1美元 = 6.39元人民幣
2016年11月22日 1美元 = 6.89元人民幣
Date & Exchange rates:
22 November 2015 US$1 = RMB￥6.39
22 November 2016 US$1 = RMB￥6.89
以上美元兌人民幣匯率的改變會令：
The above change in the exchange rate between the US dollar and the Renminbi would lead to:
  • (1) 到訪美國的中國旅客人數減少。
    (1) a decrease in the number of Chinese tourists to the US.
  • (2) 美國出口往中國的物品競爭力下降。
    (2) a decrease in the competitiveness of US exports to China.
  • (3) 美國出口往中國的物品總值上升(以美元計算)。
    (3) an increase in the total value of US exports to China (in terms of the US Dollar).
A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)
A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 升值與貶值 (Appreciation and Depreciation):
當 1 單位外幣能兌換更多本國貨幣時，代表外幣升值 (Appreciated)，本幣貶值 (Depreciated)。在這個題目中，1美元能換到的由 6.39人民幣 增加到 6.89人民幣。因此，美元升值，人民幣貶值。
1. Appreciation and Depreciation:
When 1 unit of foreign currency can exchange for more domestic currency, the foreign currency has appreciated, and the domestic currency has depreciated. In this question, 1 USD can buy more RMB (from 6.39 to 6.89). Thus, the USD has appreciated, and the RMB has depreciated.
2. 匯率與貿易 (Exchange Rates and Trade):
當本國貨幣升值，本國出口的貨物在外國人眼中會變得更昂貴，導致出口競爭力下降，出口量減少。相反，外國進口貨物會變得更便宜。
2. Exchange Rates and Trade:
When the domestic currency appreciates, domestic exports become more expensive for foreigners, leading to a decrease in export competitiveness and a drop in the quantity exported. Conversely, imported goods become cheaper.
總收入/總值公式 (Total Revenue / Total Value Formula):
$$ TR = P \times Q $$
Where \(P\) is Price and \(Q\) is Quantity demanded.

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): A. 只有 (1)、(2) / (1) and (2) only

分析 句子 (1)：正確 ✅
由於人民幣貶值，中國旅客需要用更多人民幣才能換取相同的美元。這意味著去美國旅遊的成本增加了。根據需求定律，價格上升，需求量下降，因此到訪美國的中國旅客人數會減少。
Analysis of Statement (1): Correct ✅
Due to the depreciation of the RMB, Chinese tourists need more RMB to exchange for the same amount of USD. This means the cost of traveling to the US increases. According to the law of demand, as price rises, quantity demanded falls. Thus, the number of Chinese tourists to the US will decrease.
分析 句子 (2)：正確 ✅
美元升值意味著美國出口到中國的商品，如果以美元定價，轉換成人民幣後會變得更貴。對於中國買家來說，美國商品變得昂貴了，這自然導致美國物品在中國市場的競爭力下降。
Analysis of Statement (2): Correct ✅
The appreciation of the USD means that US goods exported to China, when priced in USD, become more expensive after converting to RMB. For Chinese buyers, US goods are now costlier, which naturally leads to a decrease in the competitiveness of US exports in the Chinese market.
分析 句子 (3)：錯誤 ❌
這是一個常見的陷阱！題目強調是「以美元計算」的總值。假設美國物品的美元價格 \(P_{\text{US}}\) 保持不變。由於匯率改變導致人民幣價格上升，中國人會買少一些美國貨，即出口數量 \(Q\) 下降。
根據公式 \(TR_{\text{US}} = P_{\text{US}} \times Q\)，當 \(P_{\text{US}}\) 不變而 \(Q\) 減少時，以美元計算的總出口值 \(TR_{\text{US}}\) 必定會下降，而不會上升。
Analysis of Statement (3): Incorrect ❌
This is a classic trap! The question specifies the total value "in terms of the US Dollar". Let's assume the USD price of US goods, \(P_{\text{US}}\), remains constant. Because the exchange rate makes the RMB price higher, Chinese consumers will buy less, meaning the quantity exported \(Q\) decreases.
Using the formula \(TR_{\text{US}} = P_{\text{US}} \times Q\), if \(P_{\text{US}}\) is constant and \(Q\) decreases, the total export value in USD terms \(TR_{\text{US}}\) must decrease, not increase.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point):
解答這題的第一步，也是最關鍵的一步，是釐清哪種貨幣升值、哪種貨幣貶值。只要看出「1美元換取更多人民幣 = 美元強 = 美國貨貴」，選項 (1) 和 (2) 就能瞬間迎刃而解。
Pivot Point:
The first and most critical step is to clarify which currency appreciated and which depreciated. Once you identify that "1 USD buys more RMB = strong USD = expensive US goods", options (1) and (2) become straightforward.
魔鬼細節 (Crucial Info):
句子 (3) 中的括號 (以美元計算) 是整題的生殺大權。忽略了這個括號，你就會跌入考慮需求價格彈性 (Price Elasticity of Demand) 的陷阱。
Crucial Info:
The bracketed text (in terms of the US Dollar) in statement (3) determines the fate of the question. If you ignore this bracket, you will fall into the trap of considering the Price Elasticity of Demand.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ 陷阱：混淆「以本幣計算」與「以外幣計算」的出口總值
很多學生一看到「價格改變對總值的影響」，就立刻聯想到「需求彈性 (Elasticity)」。
注意：如果題目問的是「以人民幣計算的總值」，因為人民幣價格上升，總值是升是跌就取決於中國對美國貨物的需求彈性（彈性高則跌，彈性低則升）。但由於題目問的是「以美元計算」，美元定價不變，只有數量下跌，因此總值必定下跌。完全不需要考慮彈性！
⚠️ Trap: Confusing Export Value in Domestic vs. Foreign Currency
Many students immediately think of "Elasticity" when they see a question about "the effect of price changes on total value."
Note: If the question asked for the value "in terms of RMB", since the RMB price increased, the total value would depend on the price elasticity of China's demand for US goods (elastic -> drops, inelastic -> rises). But because the question asks for the value "in terms of USD", the USD price is unchanged and only the quantity drops, so the total value must decrease. Elasticity is irrelevant here!

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果題目把 (3) 改成「美國進口中國物品的總值 (以美元計算)」，結果會怎樣？
Q: What if statement (3) was changed to "the total value of US imports from China (in terms of USD)"?
A: 這是個好問題！如果人民幣貶值，中國貨品對美國人來說變得更便宜（美元價格 \(P\) 下降）。這時美國人會買更多中國貨（數量 \(Q\) 上升）。根據 \(TR = P \times Q\)，總值會上升還是下降？這就必須取決於美國對中國貨物的需求價格彈性了！
A: Excellent question! If the RMB depreciates, Chinese goods become cheaper for Americans (USD price \(P\) decreases). Americans will buy more Chinese goods (Quantity \(Q\) increases). According to \(TR = P \times Q\), will the total value rise or fall? This would depend on the price elasticity of US demand for Chinese goods!

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

💡 速解法 (Elimination Method):
在DSE考試中，時間就是分數。當你快速分析出 (3) 必定錯誤（因為價格不變、數量跌，總值必跌），你就可以直接劃掉選項 B, C, 和 D。即使你對 (1) 和 (2) 有一絲猶豫，你也能在一秒內選出正確答案 A！
💡 Shortcut (Elimination Method):
In the DSE exam, time is marks. Once you quickly deduce that statement (3) is definitely false (because price is constant, quantity drops, so total value must drop), you can immediately eliminate options B, C, and D. Even if you were slightly unsure about (1) and (2), you can lock in the correct answer A in one second!
