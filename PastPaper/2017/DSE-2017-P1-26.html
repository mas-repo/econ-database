HKDSE Economics Tutorial

Topic (課題): 宏觀經濟學：國民收入與物價水平 (Macroeconomics: National Income and Price Level)

Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts (核心概念): 名義/實質本地生產總值 (Nominal/Real GDP), 增長率 (Growth Rate), 人均本地生產總值 (Per Capita GDP)

1. 題目 (The Question)

下表顯示某經濟的一些數據。

增長率 2015 2016
本地生產總值 -3% -2%
價格水平 1% 0%
人口 4% 2%

下列哪項有關該經濟的陳述是正確的？

A. 2016年的實質本地生產總值下降。
B. 2016年的人均本地生產總值上升。
C. 2016年的價格水平下降。
D. 2016年的實質本地生產總值增長率較2015年的為低。

The following table shows some data of an economy.

Growth rate 2015 2016
GDP -3% -2%
Price level 1% 0%
Population 4% 2%

Which of the following statements about the economy is correct?

A. Real GDP in 2016 decreases.
B. Per capita GDP in 2016 increases.
C. The price level in 2016 decreases.
D. The growth rate of real GDP in 2016 is lower than that in 2015.

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

在經濟學中，若題目僅寫上「本地生產總值 (GDP)」而沒有標明「實質 (Real)」，我們一律將其視為名義本地生產總值 (Nominal GDP)。要解答此題，我們需要運用增長率的近似值公式 (Approximation formulas for growth rates)。

In Economics, if a question simply states "GDP" without specifying "Real", we treat it as Nominal GDP. To solve this question, we need to use the approximation formulas for growth rates.

1. 實質本地生產總值增長率 (Real GDP Growth Rate):
實質數值的增長率大約等於名義數值的增長率減去通脹率（價格水平增長率）。

The growth rate of a real variable is approximately equal to the growth rate of the nominal variable minus the inflation rate (growth rate of the price level).

$$ g_{Real} \approx g_{Nominal} - \pi $$

($g$ = 增長率 Growth rate, $\pi$ = 價格水平增長率/通脹率 Inflation rate)

2. 人均本地生產總值增長率 (Per Capita GDP Growth Rate):
人均數值的增長率大約等於總體數值的增長率減去人口增長率。

The growth rate of a per capita variable is approximately equal to the growth rate of the total variable minus the population growth rate.

$$ g_{Per\ Capita} \approx g_{Total} - g_{Population} $$

定義釐清 (Definitions):
如果一個變量的增長率是負數 ($g < 0$)，代表該變量的絕對數值正在下降 (Decreasing)。
如果增長率是零 ($g = 0$)，代表數值維持不變 (Unchanged)。

If the growth rate of a variable is negative ($g < 0$), it means the absolute value of the variable is decreasing.
If the growth rate is zero ($g = 0$), it means the value remains unchanged.

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是 (Correct Option) : Option A

分析選項 A (Analysis of Option A) - 正確 (Correct)
根據公式，2016年實質本地生產總值增長率 = 名義GDP增長率 - 價格水平增長率 = $-2\% - 0\% = -2\%$。
因為增長率是負數 ($-2\% < 0$)，所以2016年的實質本地生產總值下跌。A屬正確。

According to the formula, 2016 Real GDP growth rate = Nominal GDP growth rate - Price level growth rate = $-2\% - 0\% = -2\%$.
Since the growth rate is negative ($-2\% < 0$), the Real GDP in 2016 decreases. Option A is correct.

分析選項 B (Analysis of Option B) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
2016年人均GDP增長率 = 總GDP增長率 - 人口增長率 = $-2\% - 2\% = -4\%$。
因為增長率為負數，人均本地生產總值應該是下降，而不是上升。

2016 Per capita GDP growth rate = Total GDP growth rate - Population growth rate = $-2\% - 2\% = -4\%$.
Since the growth rate is negative, the per capita GDP should be decreasing, not increasing.

分析選項 C (Analysis of Option C) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
2016年的價格水平增長率是 $0\%$。增長率為零代表物價沒有變動（維持不變），而不是下降。若要價格水平下降，其增長率必須為負數（通縮）。

The growth rate of the price level in 2016 is $0\%$. A zero growth rate means the price level is unchanged, not decreasing. For the price level to decrease, its growth rate must be negative (deflation).

分析選項 D (Analysis of Option D) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
我們需要比較兩年的實質GDP增長率：
2015年實質GDP增長率 = $-3\% - 1\% = -4\%$
2016年實質GDP增長率 = $-2\% - 0\% = -2\%$
數學上，$-2\%$ 大於 $-4\%$。因此，2016年的增長率較2015年的為高，而非較低。

We need to compare the real GDP growth rates of the two years:
2015 Real GDP growth rate = $-3\% - 1\% = -4\%$
2016 Real GDP growth rate = $-2\% - 0\% = -2\%$
Mathematically, $-2\%$ is greater than $-4\%$. Therefore, the growth rate in 2016 is higher, not lower.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point):
這條題目的核心在於明白「增長率 (Growth Rate)」與「實際數值 (Absolute Value)」的分別。增長率反映的是轉變的方向與幅度。看到「GDP」，你必須立即聯想到這是「名義 (Nominal)」，並自覺地運用 $g_R = g_N - \pi$ 公式找出實質數據。

The core of this question lies in understanding the difference between a "Growth Rate" and an "Absolute Value". The growth rate reflects the direction and magnitude of change. When you see "GDP", you must instantly recognize it as "Nominal" and consciously apply the formula $g_R = g_N - \pi$ to find the real data.

干擾資訊 (Distractors):
表中的所有數據都有其用途，用來驗證不同的選項。然而，最大的干擾在於文字描述。「下降」對應的是「增長率 < 0」，而「較低」對應的是「對比兩年的增長率數值」。

All data in the table serves a purpose to verify different options. However, the biggest distractor is the text description. "Decreases" corresponds to "Growth rate < 0", while "Lower" corresponds to "Comparing the growth rate values of two years".

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • 陷阱一：負數的大小比較 (Comparing negative numbers)
    很多同學在看選項 D 時，直覺認為 4 大於 2，所以 $-4\%$ 應該大於 $-2\%$，因而錯誤選擇 D。緊記在數線上，$-2$ 是在 $-4$ 的右邊，所以 $-2 > -4$。

    Many students intuitively think 4 is greater than 2, so $-4\%$ must be greater than $-2\%$, leading to wrongly choosing D. Remember that on a number line, $-2$ is to the right of $-4$, so $-2 > -4$.

  • 陷阱二：混淆增長率下降與數值下降 (Confusing decreasing growth rate with decreasing value)
    雖然本題的價格增長率從 2015 年的 $1\%$ 跌至 2016 年的 $0\%$ (增長率下降了)，但這不代表價格水平下降！這只代表物價停止上升。

    Although the price level growth rate drops from $1\%$ in 2015 to $0\%$ in 2016 (the growth rate decreases), this does NOT mean the price level decreases! It only means prices have stopped rising.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼我們計算實質數據的增長率時，是直接用減法 (Subtraction)，而不是除法 (Division)？
Why do we use subtraction instead of division when calculating the growth rate of real data?

A: 嚴格來說，實質 GDP = 名義 GDP / 價格指數。但在中學經濟學的範疇內，當增長率的數值較小（通常小於 10%）時，數學上的近似值公式（即直接相減）是非常接近真實結果的，且能大幅節省計算時間。因此在 DSE 考試中，我們一律接受並使用減法公式。
Strictly speaking, Real GDP = Nominal GDP / Price Index. However, in high school Economics, when the growth rates are relatively small (usually < 10%), the mathematical approximation formula (direct subtraction) is very close to the true result and saves calculation time significantly. Therefore, in the DSE exam, we uniformly accept and use the subtraction formula.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

極速秒殺法 (The Elimination Shortcut):

在多項選擇題中，先從最容易判斷的選項入手：
1. 一眼看選項 C：2016年價格增長率是 $0\%$。$0\%$ = 沒變動，絕對不是「下降」。立即淘汰 C。
2. 看選項 A：一眼心算 $g_R = -2\% - 0\% = -2\%$。負數代表下降。A 是完美正確的描述。
找到絕對正確的答案後，如果時間緊迫，你甚至不需要去計算 B 和 D 的複雜比較，直接選 A 並跳到下一題！

In MCQs, start with the easiest options to judge:
1. Glance at Option C: 2016 price growth rate is $0\%$. $0\%$ = Unchanged, definitely not "decreases". Eliminate C immediately.
2. Look at Option A: Quick mental math $g_R = -2\% - 0\% = -2\%$. Negative means decreasing. A is a perfectly correct description.
Once you find the absolutely correct answer, if you are pressed for time, you don't even need to calculate the complex comparisons for B and D. Just pick A and move on!
