1. The Question (題目)

下圖顯示香港的堅尼系數(按原初住戶收入計算)。根據上圖，我們可以得出的結論是：
The following diagram shows the Gini coefficient of Hong Kong based on original household income. Based on the above diagram, we can conclude that:
  • A. 香港人的收入水平上升。
    A. the income level of Hong Kong people increased.
  • B. 高收入住戶的收入增加而低收入住戶的收入減少。
    B. income for high income household increased while income for low income household decreased.
  • C. 福利政策未能減低收入不均。
    C. the welfare policy failed to reduce the income inequality.
  • D. 收入差距日漸擴大。
    D. income gap had been widening.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

堅尼系數 (Gini Coefficient)：
堅尼系數是一個用來量度一個國家或地區收入不均（Income Inequality）程度的指標。數值介乎 0 到 1 之間。
Gini Coefficient:
The Gini coefficient is an indicator used to measure the degree of income inequality within a country or region. Its value ranges between 0 and 1.
$$ 0 \le G \le 1 $$
  • 當 \( G = 0 \)：絕對平均 (Perfect Equality) / Perfect Equality
  • 當 \( G = 1 \)：絕對不均 (Perfect Inequality) / Perfect Inequality
  • 數值越大：代表收入差距越大 (收入越不平均)。 / The larger the value: the wider the income gap (more unequal distribution).
原初住戶收入 (Original Household Income)：
指政府介入之前的收入。即未扣除直接稅（如薪俸稅）及未計算任何社會福利轉移（如綜援、生果金）的收入。
Original Household Income:
This refers to income before government intervention. It is the income before the deduction of direct taxes (e.g., salaries tax) and before adding any social welfare transfers (e.g., CSSA, old age allowance).

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: D (正確答案：D)

Option A
錯誤。堅尼系數反映的是收入的「分佈」(Distribution)，而不是整體的「絕對收入水平」(Absolute income level)。即使全社會所有人的收入都同時大幅下降，只要高收入人士的跌幅小於低收入人士，堅尼系數依然會上升。
Incorrect. The Gini coefficient reflects the "distribution" of income, not the overall "absolute income level". Even if everyone's income in society drops significantly, as long as the high-income group's income drops less than the low-income group's, the Gini coefficient will still rise.
Option B
錯誤。雖然這個情況的確會導致堅尼系數上升，但我們無法單憑堅尼系數的上升就「斷定」必定是這個情況。另一種可能性是：高低收入住戶的收入「同時增加」，但高收入住戶增加的速度遠快於低收入住戶（Rich getting richer faster）。
Incorrect. While this scenario would indeed cause the Gini coefficient to rise, we cannot "conclude" that this is definitively what happened just from the index. Another possibility is that both groups' incomes increased, but the high-income households' income increased much faster than that of low-income households.
Option C
錯誤 (這是一個常見陷阱！)。題目圖表的Y軸清楚註明是「按原初住戶收入計算」。這意味著這些數據是政府進行稅收及福利轉移之前的情況，因此我們無法從此圖得知福利政策(政府介入)的成效。
Incorrect (This is a common trap!). The Y-axis of the diagram clearly states it is based on "original household income". This means the data reflects the situation before government taxation and welfare transfers. Therefore, we cannot deduce the effectiveness of welfare policies from this graph.
Option D
正確。圖表顯示堅尼系數從 1971 年的 0.430 持續上升至 2011 年的 0.537。根據定義，堅尼系數上升直接代表「收入差距擴大」(Income gap widening) 或「收入不均惡化」。
Correct. The diagram shows the Gini coefficient rising steadily from 0.430 in 1971 to 0.537 in 2011. By definition, an increase in the Gini coefficient directly means that the "income gap is widening" or income inequality is worsening.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解答這類題目的「關鍵點」(Pivot Point) 有兩個：
There are two "Pivot Points" to solving this type of question:
  • 看清變數性質：堅尼系數是「相對」(Relative) 概念，量度的是差距。所有關於「絕對」(Absolute) 數值上升或下降的選項 (如選項A和B的特定情況) 通常都是干擾項。
    Identify the nature of the variable: The Gini coefficient is a "relative" concept measuring a gap. Any options making claims about "absolute" increases or decreases (like Option A, and the specific scenario in B) are usually distractors.
  • 留意括號內的定義：題目中的「原初」(Original) 二字是秒殺選項C的關鍵。考評局非常喜歡用「除稅及福利轉移後」(Post-tax and post-social transfer) 與「原初」(Original) 的對比來設下陷阱。
    Pay attention to definitions in brackets: The word "original" in the question is the key to immediately eliminating Option C. The HKEAA loves setting traps by contrasting "Original" with "Post-tax and post-social transfer" income.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：將「差距變大」等同於「窮人變窮」
Trap: Equating a "widening gap" with "the poor getting poorer"
很多同學誤以為堅尼系數上升，就代表低收入階層的實際生活水平下降或收入減少（即誤選B）。記住：如果一個社會的經濟起飛，基層市民收入增加了 \(10\%\)，但富人收入增加了 \(50\%\)，整體社會都富裕了，但堅尼系數依然會大幅上升。
Many students mistakenly believe that a rising Gini coefficient means the actual living standards or incomes of the lower-income class are falling (thus incorrectly choosing B). Remember: If an economy takes off and grassroots income increases by \(10\%\), but the rich's income increases by \(50\%\), the whole society has become wealthier, yet the Gini coefficient will still rise significantly.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 怎樣才能知道政府的「福利政策」是否有效改善收入不均？
A (Zh): 我們需要比較「原初住戶收入的堅尼系數」與「除稅及福利轉移後的堅尼系數」。如果後者的數值低於前者（例如原初是 0.537，除稅及福利後變成 0.475），就證明政府的政策成功拉近了貧富懸殊。單看一條線是看不出來的！
A (En): We need to compare the "Gini coefficient based on original income" with the "Gini coefficient based on post-tax and post-social transfer income". If the latter is lower than the former (e.g., original is 0.537, post-intervention is 0.475), it proves that government policies successfully narrowed the wealth gap. You cannot tell from just one single line!

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法 (Elimination Method)：
Shortcut (Elimination Method):
  1. 看到圖表向上升 ➔ 代表「不平均惡化/差距拉大」。直接尋找意思相同的選項，D 完美吻合。
    See the graph trending upwards ➔ Means "inequality worsening / gap widening". Look directly for the option with this meaning, D is a perfect match.
  2. 看到「原初」(Original) ➔ 立即劃掉任何關於「稅收」(Taxes) 或「福利」(Welfare) 的選項，秒殺 C。
    See the word "Original" ➔ Immediately cross out any option mentioning "Taxes" or "Welfare", instantly killing C.
