HKDSE Economics Tutor Guide

Topic 國際收支平衡 (Balance of Payments)
Difficulty ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts 常住居民 (Residents), 非常住居民 (Non-residents), 經常賬 (Current Account)

1. 題目 (The Question)

下列哪些交易會包括在香港國際收支平衡的計算內？

Which of the following transactions will be included in the calculation of the balance of payments of Hong Kong?

(1) 一位擁有香港住宅的內地居民向租客收取租金。
(1) A Mainland resident who owns an apartment in Hong Kong receives rental payments from his tenant.

(2) 一位日本居民出售香港公司的股票予韓國居民。
(2) A Japanese resident sells the shares of a Hong Kong firm to a Korean resident.

(3) 一位香港居民向一家以香港為基地的航空公司購買機票前往倫敦。
(3) A Hong Kong resident buys an air ticket to London from a Hong Kong based airline.

(4) 一位在本港當家傭的印尼人匯款給住在印尼的丈夫。
(4) An Indonesian who works in Hong Kong as a domestic helper remits money to her husband who lives in Indonesia.

A. 只有 (1)、(3) / (1) and (3) only
B. 只有 (1)、(4) / (1) and (4) only
C. 只有 (2)、(3) / (2) and (3) only
D. 只有 (2)、(4) / (2) and (4) only

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

國際收支平衡 (Balance of Payments, BOP) 的核心原則是：它只記錄一個經濟體的常住居民 (Residents) 與非常住居民 (Non-residents) 之間的經濟交易。

The core principle of the Balance of Payments (BOP) is that it only records economic transactions between Residents of an economy and Non-residents.

  • 常住居民 (Resident)： 在該經濟體系內居住或打算居住最少 12 個月的個人或機構（例如：在港工作超過一年的外籍家庭傭工屬於香港常住居民）。
    Individuals or institutions staying or intending to stay in the economic territory for at least 12 months (e.g., foreign domestic helpers working in HK for >1 year are HK residents).
  • 非常住居民 (Non-resident)： 不符合上述條件的個人或機構（例如：來港數天的遊客、長居內地的人士）。
    Those who do not meet the above criteria (e.g., tourists, people living permanently in the Mainland).
$$ BOP = CA + KA + FA + NEO = 0 $$

\(CA\) = Current Account (經常賬), \(KA\) = Capital Account (資本賬),
\(FA\) = Financial Account (金融賬), \(NEO\) = Net Errors and Omissions (淨誤差及遺漏)

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是 (Correct Option): Option B

✓ 交易 (1) 包括在內 (Included)

內地居民（非常住居民）向香港租客（常住居民）收取租金。這是常住居民與非常住居民之間的交易，記錄在經常賬的「初級收入」(Primary Income) 中。

A Mainland resident (Non-resident) receives rent from a HK tenant (Resident). This is a transaction between a resident and a non-resident, recorded under "Primary Income" in the Current Account.

✗ 交易 (2) 不包括在內 (Excluded)

日本居民（非常住居民）與韓國居民（非常住居民）之間的交易。由於雙方均不是香港的常住居民，因此不計入香港的國際收支平衡。

A transaction between a Japanese resident (Non-resident) and a Korean resident (Non-resident). Since neither party is a HK resident, it is NOT included in HK's BOP.

✗ 交易 (3) 不包括在內 (Excluded)

香港居民（常住居民）向以香港為基地的航空公司（常住居民）購買機票。這是兩名常住居民之間的交易，不計入 BOP。

A HK resident (Resident) buys a ticket from a HK-based airline (Resident). This is a transaction between two residents, so it is NOT included in the BOP.

✓ 交易 (4) 包括在內 (Included)

在港印尼家傭（常住居民）匯款給在印尼的丈夫（非常住居民）。這是常住居民與非常住居民之間的交易，記錄在經常賬的「次級收入 / 經常轉移」(Secondary Income / Current Transfers) 中。

An Indonesian domestic helper in HK (Resident) remits money to her husband in Indonesia (Non-resident). This is a transaction between a resident and a non-resident, recorded under "Secondary Income / Current Transfers" in the Current Account.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

這類題目的「破題關鍵」(Pivot Point) 在於辨識交易雙方的「居民身分」(Residency status)。

The Pivot Point for this type of question is identifying the residency status of both parties involved in the transaction.

  • 常住居民 (Resident) + 非常住居民 (Non-resident) = 計入 BOP (Included)
  • 常住居民 (Resident) + 常住居民 (Resident) = 不計入 BOP (Excluded)
  • 非常住居民 (Non-resident) + 非常住居民 (Non-resident) = 不計入 BOP (Excluded)

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱 1：誤以為外籍家庭傭工是非常住居民
很多學生以為「外籍」就等於「非常住居民」。實際上，只要在香港居住或工作滿 12 個月，就會被視為香港常住居民。

Trap 1: Mistaking foreign domestic helpers for non-residents. Many students think "foreign" means "non-resident". In fact, anyone living or working in HK for 12 months or more is considered a HK resident.

陷阱 2：被資產的所在地誤導
在交易 (2) 中，雖然買賣的是「香港公司的股票」，但交易雙方都是非常住居民，因此與香港的 BOP 無關。

Trap 2: Misled by the location of the asset. In transaction (2), although the asset is "shares of a HK firm", both trading parties are non-residents, so it does not affect HK's BOP.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼交易 (2) 中，外國人買賣香港股票不計入香港的 BOP？香港公司的外債不是改變了嗎？

Q: Why isn't transaction (2) included? Doesn't the foreign liability of the HK company change?

A: 雖然股票的擁有權改變了，但對於香港整體而言，對外的負債總額並沒有改變（只是債權人由日本人變成了韓國人）。因為沒有資金在香港常住居民與非常住居民之間流動，所以不計入 BOP。

A: Although ownership changes, the total foreign liability of HK remains unchanged (the creditor simply changes from a Japanese to a Korean). Since no funds flow between a HK resident and a non-resident, it is not recorded in the BOP.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

標記法 (The R/NR Labeling Method):

在考試時，直接在題目上為每一方寫上 R (Resident) 或 NR (Non-resident)。只有出現 (R + NR) 組合的選項才是正確答案！

During the exam, quickly label each party as R (Resident) or NR (Non-resident). Only transactions with an (R + NR) combination are correct!

  • (1) 內地居民 (NR) + 租客 (R) ➔ R + NR (✓)
  • (2) 日本居民 (NR) + 韓國居民 (NR) ➔ NR + NR (✗)
  • (3) 香港居民 (R) + 香港航空公司 (R) ➔ R + R (✗)
  • (4) 印尼家傭 (R) + 印尼丈夫 (NR) ➔ R + NR (✓)
