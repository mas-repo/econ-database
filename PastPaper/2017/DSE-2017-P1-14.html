Topic (課題): Demand and Supply - Changes in Demand vs. Quantity Demanded (需求與供應 - 需求與需求量的變動)

Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts (核心概念): Substitutes (替代品), Change in Demand (需求變動), Change in Quantity Demanded (需求量變動)

📝 The Question (題目)

下列哪種情況最有可能導致蘋果公司生產的iPhone的需求上升？

Which of the following situations would most likely lead to an increase in the demand for iPhone produced by Apple Inc.?

  • A. 電池業技術突破令其生產成本下降。
    A technological breakthrough in the battery industry reduces its production cost.
  • B. 人們預期下一款iPhone會有更多創新的特點。
    People expect the next model of iPhone will have more innovative features.
  • C. 三星公司的下一款智能手機被很多航空公司禁止攜帶登機。
    A model of Samsung smartphone is banned by many airlines.
  • D. 蘋果公司下調iPhone的售價。
    Apple Inc. reduces the selling price of iPhone.

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 需求 (Demand) vs. 需求量 (Quantity Demanded)

「需求量」的改變是由物品自身價格 (Own Price) 改變引起的，在圖表上顯示為沿著同一條需求曲線移動 (Movement along the curve)。「需求」的改變則是由非價格因素（如收入、替代品價格、預期等）引起的，顯示為整條需求曲線的移動 (Shift of the curve)。

A change in "Quantity Demanded" is caused by a change in the good's own price, represented by a movement along the demand curve. A change in "Demand" is caused by non-price factors (e.g., income, price of substitutes, expectations), represented by a shift of the entire demand curve.

2. 替代品 (Substitutes)

當兩種物品滿足相同的慾望時，它們就是替代品（例如 iPhone 和 Samsung）。如果物品 Y 的吸引力下降或價格上升，消費者會轉向購買物品 X，導致物品 X 的需求 \(D_x\) 上升。

When two goods satisfy the same want, they are substitutes (e.g., iPhone and Samsung). If good Y becomes less attractive or more expensive, consumers will switch to good X, increasing the demand \(D_x\) for good X.

需求函數 (Demand Function):

$$ Q_d = f(P, P_s, P_c, Y, E) $$

Where \(P\) = Own Price, \(P_s\) = Price of Substitutes, \(P_c\) = Price of Complements, \(Y\) = Income, \(E\) = Expectations

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): C

Option A: 錯誤 (Incorrect)

生產成本下降會導致供應 (Supply) 增加，供應曲線向右移。這並不影響需求曲線。

A decrease in production costs leads to an increase in Supply (supply curve shifts to the right). It does not affect the demand curve.

Option B: 錯誤 (Incorrect)

如果消費者預期未來的型號更好，他們會延遲購買計劃。這會導致現在 (Current) 的 iPhone 需求下降，而不是上升。

If consumers expect a better future model, they will delay their purchases. This decreases the current demand for iPhones, rather than increasing it.

Option C: 正確 (Correct)

Samsung 手機是 iPhone 的替代品 (Substitute)。當 Samsung 手機被禁上機，其效用/吸引力大幅下降，消費者會轉而購買 iPhone，導致 iPhone 的需求增加（需求曲線向右移）。

Samsung smartphones are substitutes for iPhones. When Samsung phones are banned, their utility/attractiveness drops significantly. Consumers will switch to iPhones, increasing the demand for iPhones (demand curve shifts to the right).

Option D: 錯誤 (Incorrect)

下調售價只會導致需求量 (Quantity Demanded) 上升，而不是需求 (Demand) 上升。這是沿著需求曲線的移動。

Reducing the selling price only leads to an increase in Quantity Demanded, not Demand. This is a movement along the demand curve.

🧩 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的核心在於區分「需求 (Demand)」和「需求量 (Quantity Demanded)」，以及分辨影響需求和供應的因素。

The core of this question type is to distinguish between "Demand" and "Quantity Demanded", and to separate factors affecting demand from those affecting supply.

  • Pivot Point (關鍵點): 題目問的是「需求上升 (Increase in demand)」，意味著整個需求曲線向右移。任何涉及物品自身價格改變的選項都必定錯誤。
  • Distractor (干擾項): 選項 D 是最常見的陷阱。看到「減價」，很多學生會直覺認為「需求」上升，但經濟學上這叫「需求量」上升。

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：混淆「預期」的時間線 (Confusing the timeline of expectations)

在選項 B 中，學生常以為「有創新特點 = 好消息 = 需求上升」。但請注意，創新特點屬於下一款 (Next model)。對未來產品的良好預期，會扼殺對現有產品 (Current model) 的需求。

In Option B, students often think "innovative features = good news = higher demand". However, the innovation belongs to the next model. Positive expectations for a future product will kill the demand for the current product.

🙋 Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 點解減價 (Option D) 唔會令需求上升？
Why doesn't a price reduction (Option D) increase demand?

A: 在經濟學中，我們假設其他因素不變 (Ceteris Paribus)。當一件物品減價，我們買多了，這純粹是因為價格平了，這反映在圖表上是「沿著同一條線向下滑動」，我們稱之為「需求量 (Quantity Demanded) 上升」。只有當價格以外的因素（如人工加了、對手出事了）發生改變，令你在同一個價格下願意買更多，這才是「需求 (Demand) 上升」。

In Economics, we assume Ceteris Paribus. When a good gets cheaper and we buy more, it's purely due to the price drop, shown as a movement down along the same curve, called an increase in "Quantity Demanded". Only when non-price factors change (e.g., higher income, rival's failure) causing you to buy more at the same price, is it an increase in "Demand".

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

「秒殺」技巧 (Instant Kill Technique):

當題目問「需求 (Demand)」或「供應 (Supply)」的變動時：

  1. 第一步：掃描選項，立刻剔除所有改變該物品自身價格的選項（即秒殺 Option D）。
  2. 第二步：確認題目問的是需求還是供應。題目問需求，立刻剔除所有關於生產成本 / 技術的選項（即秒殺 Option A）。
  3. 剩下 B 和 C，只需判斷哪個會令消費者「現在立刻想買」，答案呼之欲出。

When the question asks for a change in "Demand" or "Supply":
1. Scan options and immediately eliminate any option changing the own price of the good (Eliminate D).
2. Check if it asks for Demand or Supply. It asks for Demand, so eliminate production cost/technology options (Eliminate A).
3. Between B and C, just judge which makes consumers want to buy "right now".
