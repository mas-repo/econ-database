3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

在處理這類題目時，我們需要重溫完全競爭市場（受價者）的利潤極大化原則。受價者無法改變市場價格，因此其邊際收益 (MR) 等於價格 (P)。

When handling this type of question, we need to review the profit maximization principle for a perfectly competitive market (price-taker). A price-taker cannot change the market price, so its Marginal Revenue (MR) equals the Price (P).

核心公式 (Core Formulas):

$$ Total\ Cost\ (TC) = Average\ Cost\ (AC) \times Quantity\ (Q) $$
$$ Marginal\ Cost\ (MC) = \frac{\Delta TC}{\Delta Q} $$
$$ Profit\ (\pi) = Total\ Revenue\ (TR) - Total\ Cost\ (TC) = (P \times Q) - TC $$

利潤極大化條件 (Profit Maximization Condition):

廠商會生產直到邊際成本等於價格（或邊際收益），即 \( P = MC \)。只要 \( P > MC \)，多生產一件就能帶來額外利潤；當 \( MC > P \)，多生產就會虧損。

The firm will produce up to the point where Marginal Cost equals Price (or Marginal Revenue), i.e., \( P = MC \). As long as \( P > MC \), producing one more unit brings extra profit; when \( MC > P \), producing more incurs a loss.

錯誤選項分析 (Analysis of Distractors):

  • A & B: 成本上升必然導致利潤下降，不可能增加。(Costs increased, so profit must decrease, not increase.)
  • C (減少$16): 這是最大的陷阱！如果學生忘記將產量從 \( Q=8 \) 調整到 \( Q=7 \)，他們會計算出錯誤的新利潤：\( (20 \times 8) - 120 = 40 \)。然後 \( 40 - 56 = -16 \)。這表示學生沒有應用利潤極大化原則重新尋找最優產量。
    This is the biggest trap! If a student forgets to adjust the output from \( Q=8 \) to \( Q=7 \), they would calculate an incorrect new profit: \( (20 \times 8) - 120 = 40 \). Then \( 40 - 56 = -16 \). This shows the student failed to re-apply the profit-maximization rule to find the new optimal output.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point): 知道必須計算兩次利潤極大化產量。許多學生只計算一次，並假設產量維持不變。這是錯誤的，因為成本結構的改變會改變 \( MC \)，從而改變 \( P=MC \) 的交點。

Pivot Point: Knowing that you must calculate the profit-maximizing output twice. Many students only calculate it once and assume the output level remains unchanged. This is incorrect because a change in the cost structure shifts the \( MC \) curve, thus changing the \( P=MC \) intersection point.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

❌ 陷阱一：直接用 AC 找產量 (Using AC to find output directly)
有些學生會誤以為 \( P = AC \) 是決定產量的地方。緊記，決定產量的是邊際成本 (MC)，不是平均成本 (AC)。

❌ Trap 1: Using AC to find output directly. Some students mistakenly think \( P = AC \) determines output. Remember, it is Marginal Cost (MC) that determines the profit-maximizing output, not Average Cost (AC).

❌ 陷阱二：忽略產量調整 (Ignoring Output Adjustment)
如選項 C 的分析所述，忽略了成本上升後，生產第 8 件產品的 \( MC = \$22 \)，高於售價 \( \$20 \)，生產這件產品會虧損 \( \$2 \)，所以理性的廠商會減產至 7 件。

❌ Trap 2: Ignoring Output Adjustment. As explained in Option C, ignoring the fact that after the cost increase, the \( MC \) of the 8th unit is $22, which is higher than the price of $20. Producing this unit incurs a $2 loss, so a rational firm will reduce output to 7 units.

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼利潤極大化的產量會從 8 跌到 7？

Q: Why did the profit-maximizing output drop from 8 to 7?

A: 因為成本增加了！在成本增加前，生產第 8 件產品的邊際成本是 $20，剛好等於市場價格 $20，廠商不會虧本。但成本增加後，生產第 8 件的邊際成本變成了 $22。如果你賣出去只收回 $20，但製造它需要 $22，你每造一件就虧損 $2。所以理性的做法是停止生產第 8 件，停留在第 7 件（其新 MC = $20）。

A: Because costs increased! Before the increase, the MC of the 8th unit was $20, exactly equal to the market price, so the firm broke even on that margin. But after the increase, the MC of the 8th unit became $22. If you sell it for $20 but it costs $22 to make, you lose $2 on that specific unit. A rational firm will stop producing the 8th unit and stay at the 7th unit (where new MC = $20).

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法 (Shortcut): MC 平移法則 (MC Shift Rule)

Shortcut: MC Shift Rule

當每一件產出的平均成本 (AC) 都上升固定的 $2，這意味著總成本 (TC) 增加了 \( 2 \times Q \)。由於邊際成本 \( MC = \Delta TC / \Delta Q \)，這代表每一件的邊際成本 (MC) 剛好也上升了 $2！

When the Average Cost (AC) for every unit increases by a constant $2, it means Total Cost (TC) increases by \( 2 \times Q \). Since Marginal Cost \( MC = \Delta TC / \Delta Q \), this mathematical relationship means the Marginal Cost (MC) for every unit also increases exactly by $2!

秒殺步驟 (Speed-run steps):
1. 快速算出舊的 MC: 16, 18, 20, 22. (發現在 Q=8 時 P=MC=20)。舊利潤 = \( 20 \times 8 - 13 \times 8 = 160 - 104 = 56 \)。
2. 新的 MC 直接把舊 MC 加 2: 18, 20, 22, 24。 (發現在 Q=7 時 P=MC=20)。新利潤 = \( 20 \times 7 - (12+2) \times 7 = 140 - 98 = 42 \)。
3. \( 42 - 56 = -14 \)。

Speed-run steps:
1. Quickly calculate old MC: 16, 18, 20, 22. (At Q=8, P=MC=20). Old profit = \( 20 \times 8 - 13 \times 8 = 160 - 104 = 56 \).
2. New MC is simply old MC + 2: 18, 20, 22, 24. (At Q=7, P=MC=20). New profit = \( 20 \times 7 - 14 \times 7 = 140 - 98 = 42 \).
3. \( 42 - 56 = -14 \).
