HKDSE Economics: Question Analysis
經濟學試題分析

📚 Topic: Basic Economic Problems (基本經濟問題) ⭐ Difficulty: 2/5 🔑 Keywords: Market Mechanism, What to Produce

1. The Question (題目)

政府總部某間食堂的營運商決定售賣壽司而不再售賣漢堡包，因為前者能賺獲更高利潤。這是以___________ 去解決 ____________的問題的一個例子。

The operator of a canteen in the government headquarters decides to sell sushi and not burgers anymore because the former can earn a higher profit. This is an example of using ___________ to solve the problem of ____________.

  • A. 政府指令 …… 「生產什麼」
    A. government command …… ‘what to produce’
  • B. 政府指令 …… 「為誰生產」
    B. government command …… ‘for whom to produce’
  • C. 市場機制 …… 「生產什麼」
    C. market mechanism …… ‘what to produce’
  • D. 市場機制 …… 「為誰生產」
    D. market mechanism …… ‘for whom to produce’

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這條題目，我們需要釐清兩大核心概念：資源分配機制 和 基本經濟問題。

To solve this question, we need to clarify two core concepts: Resource Allocation Mechanisms and Basic Economic Problems.

I. Resource Allocation Mechanisms (資源分配機制)

  • 市場機制 / 價格機制 (Market Mechanism): 決策由私人個體（消費者和生產者）基於價格信號和自身利益作出。生產者的目標是利潤極大化。

    Decisions are made by private individuals (consumers and producers) based on price signals and self-interest. Producers aim for profit maximization.

  • 政府指令 (Government Command): 資源分配的決策由政府當局透過命令或法規直接作出，而非基於利潤。

    Decisions on resource allocation are made directly by a government authority through orders or regulations, not based on profit.

Profit Maximization Formula (利潤極大化公式): $$ \pi = TR - TC $$ (Profit \(\pi\) = Total Revenue \(TR\) - Total Cost \(TC\))

II. Basic Economic Problems (基本經濟問題)

  • 生產什麼 (What to produce): 決定生產哪種物品及多少數量（例如：壽司還是漢堡包？）。

    Deciding the types and quantities of goods to produce (e.g., Sushi or Burgers?).

  • 如何生產 (How to produce): 決定生產的方法，例如勞力密集還是資本密集。

    Deciding the methods of production, such as labor-intensive vs. capital-intensive.

  • 為誰生產 (For whom to produce): 決定產品如何分配給社會上的人（取決於購買力）。

    Deciding how products are distributed among members of society (depends on purchasing power).

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案：選項 C (Option C)

Correct Answer: Option C

  • Option AIncorrect

    分析： 雖然地點是「政府總部」，但作決定的是「營運商」（私人承包商）。營運商改變餐單的動機是「賺獲更高利潤」，這明顯是市場機制的特徵，而非政府強制的指令。後半部的「生產什麼」是正確的。

    Analysis: Although the location is "government headquarters", the decision-maker is the "operator" (a private contractor). The motive for changing the menu is to "earn a higher profit", which is a clear characteristic of the market mechanism, not a mandatory government order. The second part, "what to produce", is correct.

  • Option BIncorrect

    分析： 錯誤原因同上，這並非政府指令。此外，「為誰生產」是指產品分配給誰（例如：只賣給高官或有能力負擔的員工），而非決定賣什麼食物。

    Analysis: Incorrect for the same reason above; this is not a government command. Additionally, "for whom to produce" refers to how products are distributed (e.g., selling only to high officials or staff who can afford it), not deciding what food to sell.

  • Option CCorrect

    分析： 完全正確。營運商追求「更高利潤」\(\pi\) 體現了市場機制；而在壽司與漢堡包之間作選擇，決定了物品的種類，正是解決了「生產什麼」的問題。

    Analysis: Completely correct. The operator's pursuit of a "higher profit" \(\pi\) embodies the market mechanism. Choosing between sushi and burgers determines the type of goods, which precisely solves the "what to produce" problem.

  • Option DIncorrect

    分析： 前半部的「市場機制」正確，但決定食物款式（壽司代替漢堡包）是「生產什麼」的問題，與分配產品的「為誰生產」無關。

    Analysis: The first part, "market mechanism", is correct. However, deciding on the food type (sushi instead of burgers) is a "what to produce" problem, totally unrelated to "for whom to produce" which focuses on product distribution.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

在經濟學多項選擇題中，找出「關鍵字」和「干擾項」是得分的秘訣：

In Economics MCQs, identifying the "Pivot Points" (keywords) and "Distractors" is the secret to scoring:

  • 🎯 關鍵字 1 (Pivot Point 1)：「賺獲更高利潤」 (earn a higher profit)。這明確指出了資源分配的誘因。只要看到「利潤」或「價格信號」，就可以鎖定 市場機制。

    🎯 Pivot Point 1: "Earn a higher profit". This explicitly points out the incentive for resource allocation. Whenever you see "profit" or "price signals", you can lock in Market Mechanism.

  • 🎯 關鍵字 2 (Pivot Point 2)：「售賣壽司而不再售賣漢堡包」 (sell sushi and not burgers)。這涉及產品種類的選擇，鎖定 生產什麼。

    🎯 Pivot Point 2: "Sell sushi and not burgers". This involves the choice of product types, locking in What to produce.

  • ⚠️ 干擾項 (Distractor)：「政府總部」 (government headquarters)。考評局故意加入這個詞，試圖引導不小心的考生選擇「政府指令」。

    ⚠️ Distractor: "Government headquarters". The HKEAA intentionally included this phrase to trick careless students into choosing "government command".

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

❌ 見「政府」選「指令」： 很多同學閱讀題目時太快，看到「政府」二字就反射性地選擇「政府指令」。必須看清楚誰是決策者（營運商）以及決策基準（利潤）。

❌ Choosing "Command" upon seeing "Government": Many students read too quickly and reflexively choose "government command" upon seeing the word "government". You must clearly identify who the decision-maker is (the operator) and the basis of the decision (profit).

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果要符合「政府指令」和「為誰生產」，題目會怎樣問？

Q: What would the question look like if the answer was "government command" and "for whom to produce"?

A: 題目可能會改成：「政府強制規定，政府總部的食堂只能將膳食供應給首長級官員，以確保高級人員的安全。」（政府直接下令 = 政府指令；決定誰有資格享用膳食 = 為誰生產）。

A: The question might be altered to: "The government mandates that the canteen in the headquarters can only serve meals to directorate-grade officials to ensure the safety of senior staff." (Direct government order = government command; deciding who gets to consume the meals = for whom to produce).

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

💡 淘汰法 (Elimination Method)：
1. 看到「利潤 (profit)」 → 立即刪去包含「政府指令 (government command)」的 A 和 B。
2. 看到食物種類的改變 (sushi vs burgers) → 知道不是分配問題，刪去包含「為誰生產 (for whom to produce)」的 B 和 D。
3. 唯一剩下的就是正確答案 C。

💡 Elimination Method:
1. See "profit" → immediately eliminate A and B which contain "government command".
2. See a change in food types (sushi vs burgers) → know it's not a distribution issue, eliminate B and D which contain "for whom to produce".
3. The only remaining option is C.
