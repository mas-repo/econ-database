2. 概念重溫 Concept Review

A. 貨幣的交易需求 (Transactions Demand for Money)
指市民為了應付日常交易（如買餸、食飯、搭車）而持有貨幣（現金及活期存款）的需求。當支付科技進步（如信用卡、電子支付普及），市民不需要在身邊保留大量現金，這會降低交易貨幣需求。
Transactions Demand for Money:
The demand for holding money (cash and demand deposits) to meet daily transaction needs (e.g., groceries, dining, transport). When payment technology advances (like credit cards or e-payments), people do not need to keep large amounts of cash on hand, which decreases the transactions demand for money.
B. 實際銀行乘數 (Actual Banking Multiplier)
銀行體系創造存款的實際倍數。它通常小於最大銀行乘數，因為存在「現金流失」（市民提取現金）和「超額儲備」（銀行保留額外儲備）。
Actual Banking Multiplier:
The actual multiple by which the banking system creates deposits. It is usually smaller than the maximum banking multiplier due to "cash leakages" (public holding cash) and "excess reserves" (banks holding extra reserves).
$$ \text{Actual Banking Multiplier} \le \frac{1}{\text{Required Reserve Ratio } (r)} $$
當公眾減少使用現金（現金流失減少），更多的錢會留在銀行體系內成為存款。這增加了銀行的實際儲備，使銀行能借出更多貸款，從而令實際銀行乘數上升。
When the public reduces the use of cash (cash leakage decreases), more money stays within the banking system as deposits. This increases the banks' actual reserves, allowing them to lend out more, thereby increasing the actual banking multiplier.

3. 答案與詳細分析 The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案是： The correct option is: Option A
Correct / 正確
(1) 它們處理輔幣和鈔票的成本會下降。
正確。當顧客多使用 ApplePay 或信用卡，店舖收到的實體現金減少。這直接減少了店舖點算現金、保管現金以及聘請解款車押運現金的時間和行政成本。
(1) their cost of handling coins and notes will fall.
Correct. As customers use ApplePay or credit cards more, shops receive less physical cash. This directly reduces the administrative and time costs associated with counting, securing, and transporting physical cash (e.g., hiring armored vehicles).
Correct / 正確
(2) 實際銀行乘數可能上升。
正確。電子支付普及意味著市民對持有實體現金的需求下降（現金流失率下降）。市民將更多資金存放在銀行戶口中，銀行的儲備因此增加，能夠創造更多的貸款和存款。在乘數效應下，存款創造的過程阻力減少，實際銀行乘數 \(m\) 會上升。
(2) the actual banking multiplier may increase.
Correct. The popularity of e-payments implies a lower demand for holding physical cash by the public (a drop in the cash leakage ratio). People keep more funds in bank accounts, which increases bank reserves and allows them to create more loans and deposits. With less resistance in the deposit creation process, the actual banking multiplier \(m\) increases.
Incorrect / 錯誤
(3) 貨幣的交易需求會上升。
錯誤。這是一個經典的陷阱。電子支付（特別是連結信用卡的支付方式）容許市民「先買後付」，減少了為了日常交易而必須預留在錢包或活期戶口中的閒置資金。因此，貨幣的交易需求實際上是下降了。
(3) transactions demand for money will increase.
Incorrect. This is a classic trap. Electronic payments (especially those linked to credit cards) allow people to "buy now, pay later," reducing the need to keep idle funds in wallets or checking accounts specifically for daily transactions. Therefore, the transactions demand for money actually decreases.

4. 題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)：
本題的核心在於區分「交易次數」與「交易貨幣需求」。很多學生誤以為買東西方便了，交易多了，貨幣需求就會上升。事實上，經濟學上的「貨幣需求」是指持有流動資金（Holding liquidity）的意欲。有更好的支付替代品（Substitute for money balances），自然不需要持有那麼多貨幣！
Pivot Point:
The core of this question lies in distinguishing between the "number of transactions" and the "transactions demand for money." Many students mistakenly believe that easier purchases mean more transactions, hence higher money demand. In economics, "money demand" refers to the desire of holding liquidity. With better payment substitutes, you naturally don't need to hold as much money!

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

⚠️ 陷阱： 混淆了「支付媒介」與「貨幣供應」。
學生常以為 ApplePay 本身是貨幣。注意，ApplePay 只是轉移資金的工具（Tool to transfer funds）。它改變的是公眾持有現金的習慣，從而透過減少現金流失（Cash drain）來間接影響銀行的貨幣創造能力。
⚠️ Trap: Confusing "Medium of Exchange" with "Money Supply".
Students often think ApplePay itself is money. Note that ApplePay is merely a tool to transfer funds. It changes the public's cash-holding habits, which indirectly affects banks' ability to create money by reducing the cash drain.

6. 學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

問：既然電子支付是用銀行戶口的錢，為什麼不會增加對活期存款的需求，從而令整體貨幣交易需求上升？
Q: Since e-payments use money from bank accounts, why doesn't it increase the demand for demand deposits, thereby increasing the overall transactions demand for money?
答：好問題！雖然交易透過存款進行，但電子支付大大提升了資金流轉的效率（Velocity of money）。以前你可能要提款 \( \$1000 \) 放銀包備用；現在你只需在戶口放少量錢，甚至用信用卡透支，月尾才找數。因此，你平均每天需要「閒置」準備交易的資金總額（平均貨幣餘額）其實是減少了的。
A: Great question! Although transactions are settled via deposits, e-payments vastly improve the velocity of money. In the past, you might withdraw \( \$1000 \) to keep in your wallet just in case; now, you only need minimal funds in your account, or you can use credit and pay at the end of the month. Thus, the average total funds you need to keep "idle" for transactions (average money balances) actually drops.
