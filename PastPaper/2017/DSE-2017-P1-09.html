Topic: Business Expansion (企業擴張) Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆
Key Concepts: Horizontal, Vertical, Lateral, Conglomerate Expansion

1. 題目 (The Question)

佐丹奴國際有限公司是一間便服零售商。它開設了佐丹奴女裝Giordano Ladies，提供高級女士服裝和套裝。這是 ____________ 擴張的一個例子。

A. 橫向
B. 縱向
C. 側向
D. 集團

Giordano International Limited is a retailer of casual wear. It has launched the Giordano Ladies, which provides luxury clothes and suits for ladies. It is an example of ____________ expansion.

A. horizontal
B. vertical
C. lateral
D. conglomerate

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

在香港中學文憑試（HKDSE）經濟科中，企業擴張（或整合）主要分為四類：

  • 橫向擴張 (Horizontal Expansion)： 企業擴張至生產相同產品，並處於相同生產階段。（例如：兩間超級市場合併）
  • 縱向擴張 (Vertical Expansion)： 企業擴張至生產同一行業中不同生產階段的產品。分為後向（向原材料供應商擴張）及前向（向零售或分銷商擴張）。
  • 側向擴張 (Lateral Expansion)： 企業擴張至生產相關但不同的產品。（例如：報紙出版商收購雜誌社）
  • 集團擴張 (Conglomerate Expansion)： 企業擴張至生產完全不相關的產品。（例如：服裝店開設地產代理公司）

In HKDSE Economics, business expansion (or integration) is mainly classified into four types:

  • Horizontal Expansion: Expanding into the same product at the same stage of production. (e.g., Two supermarkets merging)
  • Vertical Expansion: Expanding into a different stage of production within the same industry. Can be backward (towards suppliers) or forward (towards retailers).
  • Lateral Expansion: Expanding into related but different products. (e.g., A newspaper publisher acquiring a magazine company)
  • Conglomerate Expansion: Expanding into completely unrelated products. (e.g., A clothing retailer opening a real estate agency)

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): C

A. 橫向 (Horizontal) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
雖然兩者都是服裝零售，但「便服」與「高級女士服裝和套裝」屬於不同的產品市場，針對不同的顧客群。因此，這不是生產完全相同的產品。
Although both are clothing retail, "casual wear" and "luxury clothes and suits" belong to different product markets targeting different customer segments. Therefore, they are not exactly the same product.
B. 縱向 (Vertical) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
兩者都處於「零售」階段（即生產的最後階段），並沒有涉及向供應商（後向）或分銷商（前向）的擴張。
Both are at the "retail" stage (the final stage of production). It does not involve expanding towards suppliers (backward) or distributors (forward).
C. 側向 (Lateral) - 正確 (Correct)
「便服」和「高級女士服裝」是相關的產品（同屬服裝業，可共享設計或布料採購的經驗），但它們是不同的產品，針對不同的市場區隔。這完全符合側向擴張的定義。
"Casual wear" and "luxury clothes" are related products (both in the clothing industry, potentially sharing design or fabric sourcing expertise), but they are different products targeting different market segments. This perfectly fits the definition of lateral expansion.
D. 集團 (Conglomerate) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
這兩類產品明顯相關（都是衣服），並非完全無關的業務（如服裝與餐飲）。
These two products are clearly related (both are clothes), not completely unrelated businesses (like clothing and catering).

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)： 比較原有業務與新業務的關係。

  1. 原有業務： 便服零售 (Retail of casual wear)
  2. 新業務： 高級女士服裝零售 (Retail of luxury clothes)
  3. 分析： 兩者是否相同？不是（便服 vs 高級服裝）。兩者是否相關？是（都是衣服）。生產階段是否相同？是（都是零售）。
  4. 結論： 相關但不同的產品 = 側向擴張。

Pivot Point: Compare the relationship between the original business and the new business.

  1. Original: Retail of casual wear
  2. New: Retail of luxury clothes
  3. Analysis: Are they the same? No (casual vs luxury). Are they related? Yes (both are clothes). Are they at the same production stage? Yes (both retail).
  4. Conclusion: Related but different products = Lateral expansion.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

混淆「橫向」與「側向」： 很多學生一看到「都是賣衣服」，就立刻選擇「橫向擴張」。記住，在DSE經濟學中，橫向擴張要求產品是直接競爭替代品（例如：百佳收購惠康）。便服和高級套裝的目標客群不同，不能互相替代，因此屬於側向。

Confusing "Horizontal" and "Lateral": Many students see "both sell clothes" and immediately choose "Horizontal". Remember, in DSE Economics, horizontal expansion requires the products to be direct competitive substitutes (e.g., ParknShop acquiring Wellcome). Casual wear and luxury suits target different customers and are not perfect substitutes, hence it is lateral.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

問 (Q)： 如果佐丹奴開設的是另一間賣「平價便服」的店舖，那會是什麼擴張？

答 (A)： 那就會是橫向擴張 (Horizontal Expansion)，因為新舊業務銷售的是相同類型、針對相同市場的產品。

Q: What if Giordano opened another shop selling "cheap casual wear"? What type of expansion would that be?

A: That would be Horizontal Expansion, because the old and new businesses would be selling the same type of product targeting the same market.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

三秒判定法： 在考場上，問自己三個問題：

  1. 產品完全一樣嗎？(是 = 橫向)
  2. 是買賣雙方關係嗎？(是 = 縱向)
  3. 產品完全九唔搭八嗎？(是 = 集團)

如果以上皆非，但產品之間有某種關聯（例如共用技術、品牌效應），那就是側向 (Lateral)！

The 3-Second Rule: In the exam, ask yourself three questions:

  1. Are the products exactly the same? (Yes = Horizontal)
  2. Is it a buyer-seller relationship? (Yes = Vertical)
  3. Are the products completely unrelated? (Yes = Conglomerate)

If all answers are NO, but there is some connection (e.g., shared technology, brand effect), it is Lateral!
