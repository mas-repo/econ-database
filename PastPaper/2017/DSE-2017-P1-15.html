HKDSE Economics Topic Analysis
經濟科試題分析

Topic (課題): Price Elasticity of Demand (價格需求彈性)

Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆ (2/5 - Fundamental 基礎)

Key Concepts (核心概念): Price Elasticity (價格彈性) Total Expenditure (總開支) Perfectly Inelastic (完全沒有彈性) Unitary Elastic (單一彈性)

2. The Question (題目)

參看文迪和莎莉的對話。

文迪： 我酷愛咖啡！不論其價格如何轉變，每週我總是購買相同數量的咖啡。
莎莉： 我也酷愛咖啡！不論其價格如何轉變，每週我總是花費相同金額購買咖啡。

根據他們的對話，文迪對咖啡的需求屬__________ 而莎莉對咖啡的需求屬 ____________。
  • A. 完全沒有彈性 …… 完全沒有彈性
  • B. 完全沒有彈性 …… 單一彈性
  • C. 單一彈性 …… 完全沒有彈性
  • D. 單一彈性 …… 單一彈性
Refer to the following conversation between Mandy and Shirley.

Mandy: I like coffee very much! No matter how its price changes, I always buy the same amount of coffee every week!
Shirley: I like coffee very much too! No matter how its price changes, I always spend the same amount of money on coffee every week!

Based on their conversation, Mandy’s demand for coffee is __________ and Shirley’s demand for coffee is ____________.
  • A. perfectly inelastic …… perfectly inelastic
  • B. perfectly inelastic …… unitary elastic
  • C. unitary elastic …… perfectly inelastic
  • D. unitary elastic …… unitary elastic

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這條問題，我們必須清楚理解「需求價格彈性」與「總開支」之間的數學關係。
To solve this problem, we must clearly understand the mathematical relationship between "Price Elasticity of Demand" and "Total Expenditure".
Price Elasticity of Demand Formula (需求價格彈性公式): $$ E_d = -\frac{\% \Delta Q_d}{\% \Delta P} $$
Total Expenditure / Total Revenue Formula (總開支/總收入公式): $$ TE = P \times Q $$
  • 完全沒有彈性 (Perfectly Inelastic, \(E_d = 0\)):
    無論價格 \(P\) 如何改變，需求量 \(Q\) 保持不變 (\(\Delta Q = 0\))。
    No matter how price \(P\) changes, quantity demanded \(Q\) remains constant (\(\Delta Q = 0\)).
  • 單一彈性 (Unitary Elastic, \(E_d = 1\)):
    價格的百分比變動剛好抵銷需求量的百分比變動，導致總開支 (\(P \times Q\)) 保持不變。
    The percentage change in price perfectly offsets the percentage change in quantity demanded, leading to a constant Total Expenditure (\(P \times Q\)).

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option B

Step-by-Step Breakdown (逐項分析):

文迪的情況 (Mandy's Situation):
文迪指出「總是購買相同數量」。這意味著無論價格 \(P\) 是上升還是下降，需求量 \(Q\) 的變動均為零（即 \(\%\Delta Q = 0\)）。代入公式，彈性 \(E_d = 0\)。因此，她的需求是「完全沒有彈性」。
Mandy's Situation:
Mandy states she "always buys the same amount". This means whether price \(P\) goes up or down, the change in quantity demanded \(Q\) is zero (i.e., \(\%\Delta Q = 0\)). Plugging this into the formula, elasticity \(E_d = 0\). Therefore, her demand is "perfectly inelastic".
莎莉的情況 (Shirley's Situation):
莎莉指出「總是花費相同金額」。總開支的公式為 \(TE = P \times Q\)。如果總開支保持不變，代表價格 \(P\) 的變動百分比完全被數量 \(Q\) 的反方向變動百分比所抵銷。這正正是「單一彈性」(\(E_d = 1\)) 的定義。
Shirley's Situation:
Shirley states she "always spends the same amount of money". The formula for Total Expenditure is \(TE = P \times Q\). If total expenditure remains constant, it means the percentage change in price \(P\) is perfectly offset by the opposite percentage change in quantity \(Q\). This is the exact definition of "unitary elastic" (\(E_d = 1\)).
為何 A, C, D 選項錯誤：
選項 A 錯在誤判了莎莉的彈性；選項 C 錯在將兩人的情況完全對調；選項 D 錯在誤判了文迪的彈性。只有 B 準確對應了「數量不變 = 無彈性」和「開支不變 = 單一彈性」的法則。
Why Options A, C, D are wrong:
Option A incorrectly judges Shirley's elasticity; Option C reverses the situations of the two individuals; Option D incorrectly judges Mandy's elasticity. Only B accurately maps the rules "constant quantity = perfectly inelastic" and "constant expenditure = unitary elastic".

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (破題關鍵):
這條題目的核心在於分辨兩個詞語：「數量 (amount / quantity)」與「金額 (amount of money / expenditure)」。
Pivot Point:
The core of this question lies in distinguishing between two terms: "amount (quantity)" and "amount of money (expenditure)".
很多題目會利用文字遊戲來混淆考生。當你看到「相同數量」，你的大腦應該立即聯想到 \(Q\) 是常數；當你看到「相同金額」，你應該聯想到 \(P \times Q\) 是常數。
Many questions use wordplay to confuse candidates. When you see "same amount/quantity", your brain should immediately associate it with \(Q\) being constant; when you see "same amount of money", you should associate it with \(P \times Q\) being constant.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

致命陷阱：將「買得一樣多」和「用得一樣多錢」混為一談。
有些學生憑直覺認為，既然需求是「完全沒有彈性」（十分死板），那麼消費者花費的「金錢」也必定是死板不變的。這是**大錯特錯**！
如果你的需求完全無彈性（例如救命藥物），當價格上升一倍，你要買的數量一樣，你花費的金額其實會**增加一倍**，而不是保持不變！
Deadly Trap: Confusing "buying the same amount" with "spending the same amount of money".
Some students intuitively think that if demand is "perfectly inelastic" (very rigid), the "money" spent by the consumer must also be rigidly unchanged. This is **completely wrong**!
If your demand is perfectly inelastic (e.g., life-saving medicine), when the price doubles and you buy the same quantity, the amount of money you spend will actually **double**, not remain the same!

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 老師，如果莎莉總是花費相同金額，當咖啡價格上升時，她買的數量會發生甚麼變化？
Q: Teacher, if Shirley always spends the same amount of money, what happens to the quantity she buys when the price of coffee increases?
A: 由於 \(TE = P \times Q\)，而且 \(TE\) 固定不變。當價格 \(P\) 上升，數量 \(Q\) 必須以相同比例下降。例如，如果她每週花 $100 買咖啡，當咖啡從 $10 一杯升到 $20 一杯（價格升一倍），她買的數量就會從 10 杯跌到 5 杯（數量跌一半）。這完美展示了單一彈性 (\(E_d = 1\)) 的特徵。
A: Since \(TE = P \times Q\), and \(TE\) is strictly fixed. When price \(P\) increases, quantity \(Q\) must decrease by the same proportion. For example, if she spends $100 a week, when coffee goes from $10/cup to $20/cup (price doubles), her quantity bought drops from 10 cups to 5 cups (quantity halves). This perfectly demonstrates the characteristic of unitary elasticity (\(E_d = 1\)).

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

一秒解題法 (The 1-Second Rule):
在DSE多項選擇題中，遇到這類題目不需要做任何計算。請死記以下配對：
  • 見到 「相同數量 (Same quantity/amount)」 ➜ 立即選 完全沒有彈性 (\(E_d = 0\))
  • 見到 「相同開支/收入 (Same expenditure/revenue)」 ➜ 立即選 單一彈性 (\(E_d = 1\))
看著文迪的「相同數量」鎖定 \(E_d = 0\)，看著莎莉的「相同金額」鎖定 \(E_d = 1\)。直接圈出 B，節省時間做下一題！
The 1-Second Rule:
In DSE MCQs, you don't need any calculations for this type of question. Memorize these pairs:
  • See "Same quantity/amount" ➜ Instantly pick Perfectly Inelastic (\(E_d = 0\))
  • See "Same expenditure/revenue" ➜ Instantly pick Unitary Elastic (\(E_d = 1\))
Seeing Mandy's "same amount" locks in \(E_d = 0\); seeing Shirley's "same amount of money" locks in \(E_d = 1\). Circle B immediately and save time for the next question!
