HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Money & Banking (貨幣與銀行)
Difficulty: ★★★★☆
Key Concepts: Balance Sheet, Money Multiplier, Money Supply

題目 (The Question)

下表顯示某銀行系統的資產負債表。

The following table shows the balance sheet of a banking system.
資產 Assets ($) 負債 Liabilities ($)
儲備 Reserves 300 存款 Deposits 900
貸款 Loans 600

假設公眾並沒有持有現金，而法定儲備比率調低至25%。在存款創造後，銀行系統的貸款額是$750。下列哪項有關該銀行系統的陳述是正確的？

Suppose the public does not hold cash and the required reserve ratio is reduced to 25%. After deposit creation, the amount of loans in the banking system is $750. Which of the following statements about the banking system is correct?
  • A. 貨幣供應增加$300。
    The money supply increases by $300.
  • B. 實際銀行乘數為3.5。
    The actual banking multiplier is 3.5.
  • C. 存款增加$600。
    The deposits increase by $600.
  • D. 銀行儲備是$450。
    Bank reserves are $450.

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

1. 資產負債表恆等式 (Balance Sheet Identity):
銀行的資產必須等於負債。 Assets must equal Liabilities. $$ \text{Reserves (儲備)} + \text{Loans (貸款)} = \text{Deposits (存款)} $$
2. 貨幣供應 (Money Supply) \(M_s\):
定義為公眾持有的現金加上存款。 Defined as cash held by the public plus deposits. $$ M_s = C_p + D $$ 由於題目假設公眾不持有現金 ($C_p=0$)，所以 \(M_s = D\)。
3. 銀行乘數 (Banking Multiplier):
這是存款總額與儲備總額的比率。 This is the ratio of total deposits to total reserves. $$ \text{Actual Banking Multiplier} = \frac{\text{Total Deposits}}{\text{Total Reserves}} = \frac{1}{\text{Actual Reserve Ratio}} $$

答案與詳細分析 (Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確選項 Correct Option: B

關鍵計算 (Critical Calculation):
在這個封閉的銀行系統中（公眾不持有現金），除非中央銀行注入資金，否則系統的總儲備 (Total Reserves) 保持不變，仍然是 $300。 In this closed banking system (no cash leakage), unless the Central Bank injects funds, the Total Reserves remain constant at $300.

新狀態 (New State):
  • 儲備 Reserves ($R$) = $300 (不變 constant)
  • 貸款 Loans ($L$) = $750 (題目給定 given)
  • 存款 Deposits ($D$) = $R + L = 300 + 750 = 1050$
錯誤 Incorrect 選項 A (Option A)

分析：
原來的存款是 $900，新的存款是 $1050。
由於 \(M_s = C_p + D\) 且 \(C_p=0\)，貨幣供應等於存款額。
貨幣供應增加 = $1050 - 900 = $150，而不是 $300。

Analysis:
Original deposits were $900, new deposits are $1050.
Since \(M_s = C_p + D\) and \(C_p=0\), Money Supply equals Deposits.
Increase in Money Supply = $1050 - 900 = $150, not $300.
正確 Correct 選項 B (Option B)

分析：
實際銀行乘數定義為 \(\frac{\text{存款總額}}{\text{儲備總額}}\)。
$$ \text{Multiplier} = \frac{1050}{300} = 3.5 $$ 注意：雖然法定儲備比率是 25% (最大乘數為 4)，但銀行並未借盡所有超額儲備（貸款僅為 750），所以實際乘數較小。

Analysis:
The actual banking multiplier is defined as \(\frac{\text{Total Deposits}}{\text{Total Reserves}}\).
$$ \text{Multiplier} = \frac{1050}{300} = 3.5 $$ Note: Although the Required Reserve Ratio is 25% (Max Multiplier is 4), the bank has not fully lent out all excess reserves (Loans are only 750), so the actual multiplier is smaller.
錯誤 Incorrect 選項 C (Option C)

分析：
存款由 $900 增加至 $1050。
存款增加額 = $1050 - 900 = $150。

Analysis:
Deposits increased from $900 to $1050.
Increase in deposits = $1050 - 900 = $150.
錯誤 Incorrect 選項 D (Option D)

分析：
如前所述，公眾不持有現金，資金只是在銀行系統內的「儲備」與「貸款」之間轉換，或是在不同銀行間轉移。總現金（儲備）沒有離開過銀行系統。
因此，銀行儲備維持 $300 不變。

Analysis:
As mentioned, the public holds no cash. Money only shifts between "Reserves" and "Loans" or between banks. The physical cash (Reserves) never left the banking system.
Therefore, bank reserves remain constant at $300.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

破題關鍵 (Pivot Point):

這題最容易讓人困惑的地方是「存款創造後」(After deposit creation) 這句話。學生通常會以為這代表「創造至最大值」(Maximized creation)。

然而，題目給了一個具體約束條件 (Constraint)：貸款額是 $750。這意味著我們不能直接用 \( \text{Reserves} \times \frac{1}{RRR} \) 來計算存款，而必須用資產負債表的平衡原則來推算。

Pivot Point: The most confusing part is the phrase "After deposit creation". Students often assume this means "Maximized creation".
However, the question gives a specific constraint: Loans are $750. This means we cannot simply use \( \text{Reserves} \times \frac{1}{RRR} \) to find deposits. We must use the balance sheet identity.

解題邏輯 (Logic Flow):

  1. 確認系統總儲備不變 ($R=300$)。 (Confirm Total Reserves are constant.)
  2. 利用新貸款額 ($L=750$) 計算新存款 ($D = 300 + 750 = 1050$)。 (Calculate New Deposits using New Loans.)
  3. 檢查各選項數據。 (Verify data in options.)

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • 誤以為儲備會改變 (Mistaking Reserves change):
    學生常誤以為貸款增加，儲備就會減少。但在整個銀行系統中，貸款借出去的錢最終會存回銀行，變回儲備。只要沒有現金流失 (Cash Leakage)，系統總儲備不變。 Students often think if loans increase, reserves decrease. But in the whole banking system, money lent out is deposited back, becoming reserves again. Total reserves stay constant if there's no cash leakage.
  • 混淆「最大乘數」與「實際乘數」 (Confusing Max vs. Actual Multiplier):
    最大乘數 = \(1/0.25 = 4\)。但這題銀行還持有超額儲備 (Excess Reserves)，所以實際乘數只有 3.5。 Max multiplier = \(1/0.25 = 4\). But here, banks still hold excess reserves, so the actual multiplier is only 3.5.
