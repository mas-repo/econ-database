HKDSE Economics Analysis

課題 Topic: 需求定律與相對價格 (Law of Demand & Relative Price)
難度 Difficulty: ★★★★☆
關鍵概念 Key Concepts: Alchian-Allen Effect, Relative Price, Per-unit Tax/Tariff

題目 The Question

下列哪項與需求定律並不相符？
Which of the following is INCONSISTENT with the law of demand?

A. 取消對成衣的從量進口關稅令進口成衣的質素上升。
The abolishment of per unit import tariff on garment results in a rise in the quality of imported garments.
B. 與距離養魚場較遠的市場售出的冰鮮魚比較，在養魚場附近市場售出的活魚價格較高。
Live fishes sold in a market near the fish farm have a higher price than frozen fishes sold in a faraway market.
C. 當政府對白酒開徵從價稅，高檔白酒售出量相對於白酒總售出量的比率維持不變。
The ratio of high-quality white wine sold to total white wine sold remains unchanged when the government imposes an ad valorem tax on white wine.
D. 發展商傾向在一幅擁有良好景觀的地皮上興建較優質的住宅。
Developers tend to build higher-quality housing units on a piece of land with a good view.

概念重溫 Concept Review

此題的核心概念是相對價格 (Relative Price) 對需求量的影響，這通常被稱為「艾智仁－艾倫效應」(Alchian-Allen Effect)。
The core concept here is the effect of Relative Price on quantity demanded, often referred to as the "Alchian-Allen Effect".

1. 相對價格公式 (Formula of Relative Price)

$$ \text{相對價格 (Relative Price)} = \frac{P_{\text{高質素 (High Quality)}}}{P_{\text{低質素 (Low Quality)}}} $$

2. 固定收費/從量稅的影響 (Effect of Fixed Charge / Per-unit Tax)

當高質素和低質素的產品同時加上一個相同的固定金額（如運費、從量稅）時，高質素產品的相對價格會下降。
When a fixed amount (like transportation cost or per-unit tax) is added to both high-quality and low-quality products, the relative price of the high-quality product decreases.
$$ \frac{P_H + T}{P_L + T} < \frac{P_H}{P_L} \quad (\text{其中 } P_H > P_L) $$
結果：人們會傾向購買更多高質素產品（「買貴價貨」現象）。
Result: People tend to buy more high-quality products.

3. 從價稅的影響 (Effect of Ad Valorem Tax)

如果徵收的是從價稅（例如收取價格的 10%），兩者的價格會按相同比例上升，相對價格維持不變。
If an ad valorem tax is imposed (e.g., 10% of the price), both prices rise by the same proportion, so the relative price remains unchanged.
$$ \frac{P_H (1+t)}{P_L (1+t)} = \frac{P_H}{P_L} $$

答案與詳細分析 The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Option: A
題目要求：找出與需求定律（具體來說是相對價格理論）不相符的選項。
Requirement: Find the option that is INCONSISTENT with the law of demand (specifically relative price theory).

選項 A 分析 (Option A Analysis) - 不相符 Inconsistent

情境：取消從量關稅 (Abolishment of per unit tariff)。
理論推導：加上從量稅會令高質素產品相對便宜（相對價格下降），導致質素上升。相反，取消從量稅即是減去一個固定金額 \(T\)。
$$ \frac{P_H - T}{P_L - T} > \frac{P_H}{P_L} $$ 取消關稅後，高質素成衣的相對價格會上升。根據需求定律，人們應減少購買高質素成衣，導致進口成衣的平均質素下降。
結論：選項 A 聲稱質素上升，這與理論預測相反，因此是不相符的。
Scenario: Abolishment of per unit tariff.
Theoretical Deduction: Adding a per unit tax makes high-quality goods relatively cheaper, increasing average quality. Conversely, removing the tax means subtracting a fixed amount \(T\).
After removing the tariff, the relative price of high-quality garments increases. According to the law of demand, people should buy fewer high-quality garments, leading to a drop in average quality.
Conclusion: Option A claims quality rises, which contradicts the theory. Thus, it is INCONSISTENT.

選項 B 分析 (Option B Analysis) - 無關 Irrelevant / 混淆視聽 Distractor

這選項比較的是「養魚場附近的活魚」與「遠處的冰鮮魚」。這兩者並非同一種商品在不同市場的比較，而且涉及運輸導致的產品性質改變（活魚變冰鮮）。這更像是供應成本或產品質素的差異，而非單純的相對價格需求分析。即使它描述了一個現象，它並沒有直接違反需求定律，但也不是本題考核相對價格效應的最佳例子。不過，由於我們要找「明顯違反」的，A 選項在邏輯上是完全錯誤的，而 B 只是描述事實。
This compares "live fish near farm" vs "frozen fish far away". These are different products. While it describes a market phenomenon, it doesn't directly test the Alchian-Allen effect regarding relative prices of the same good categories. However, since we are looking for a direct violation, A is logically the opposite of the law, making B a distractor.

選項 C 分析 (Option C Analysis) - 相符 Consistent

情境：開徵從價稅 (Imposing ad valorem tax)。
理論推導：如概念重溫所述，從價稅不會改變相對價格。因此，高檔白酒與普通白酒的銷售比例應該維持不變。
結論：這與需求定律相符。
Scenario: Imposing ad valorem tax.
Theoretical Deduction: As mentioned in the concept review, ad valorem tax does not change relative price. Thus, the ratio of sales should remain unchanged.
Conclusion: This is consistent with the law of demand.

選項 D 分析 (Option D Analysis) - 相符 Consistent

情境：地價是固定成本 (Land cost is a fixed cost)。
理論推導：地價（尤其是景觀好的地皮）對於建築成本來說是一個巨大的固定成本 (Fixed Cost)。假設建築成本（優質 vs 普通）差異不大，加上昂貴的地價後：
$$ \frac{\text{優質建築費} + \text{高地價}}{\text{普通建築費} + \text{高地價}} < \frac{\text{優質建築費}}{\text{普通建築費}} $$ 高地價降低了優質住宅的相對價格。因此，發展商在貴地皮上建豪宅更划算。
結論：這與需求定律（艾智仁－艾倫效應）相符。
Scenario: Land cost is a fixed cost.
Theoretical Deduction: High land cost acts as a large fixed cost. Adding this fixed cost to the construction cost reduces the relative price of building high-quality units compared to low-quality ones.
Conclusion: This is consistent with the law of demand (Alchian-Allen effect).

題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

這類題目的關鍵點 (Pivot Point) 在於分辨「固定金額」(Fixed Amount) 還是「比例金額」(Percentage)。
The Pivot Point of this question is distinguishing between a "Fixed Amount" and a "Percentage".
  • 加上固定金額 (Adding Fixed Cost): 相對價格下降 \(\rightarrow\) 質素上升。
    Relative price drops \(\rightarrow\) Quality rises. (e.g., Option D, Export goods)
  • 減去固定金額 (Removing Fixed Cost): 相對價格上升 \(\rightarrow\) 質素下降。
    Relative price rises \(\rightarrow\) Quality drops. (e.g., Option A)
  • 從價稅 (Ad Valorem Tax): 相對價格不變 \(\rightarrow\) 質素組合不變。
    Relative price unchanged \(\rightarrow\) Quality mix unchanged. (e.g., Option C)

常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

1. 忽略「取消」二字 (Ignoring the word "Abolishment")
學生常背誦「有運費/關稅 \(\rightarrow\) 買貴價貨」。看到選項 A 提及關稅，就直覺認為質素會上升，卻忽略了題目說是取消 (Abolishment) 關稅。取消關稅的效果與徵收關稅剛好相反。
Students often memorize "Transport cost/Tariff \(\rightarrow\) Buy expensive goods". Seeing Option A mentions tariff, they assume quality rises, ignoring the word "Abolishment". Removing a tariff has the opposite effect of imposing one.
2. 混淆從量稅與從價稅 (Confusing Per-unit vs. Ad Valorem Tax)
必須清楚區分：從量稅 (Per unit) 改變相對價格；從價稅 (Ad Valorem / Percentage) 不改變相對價格。
Must distinguish: Per unit tax changes relative price; Ad Valorem tax does not.
