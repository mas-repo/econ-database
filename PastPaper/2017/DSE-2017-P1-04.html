HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Efficiency, Social Cost & Benefit Analysis (效率、社會成本與效益分析)

Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts: Social Cost, Private Cost, Externalities

Q 題目 The Question

對某經濟而言，下列哪(幾)項屬於延遲建成一條新鐵路的損失？
Which of the following would be the loss(es) of delaying the completion of a new railway to an economy?

  • 該新鐵路原本在延遲期間能為乘客節省的交通時間
    the travel time of passengers that would have been saved by the new railway during the period of delay
  • 因新鐵路工程延遲對承建商公司形象所造成的損害
    the damage to the corporate image of the railway constructor for the delay
  • 對新鐵路附近的商店及居民在延遲期間所造成的滋擾
    the nuisance to the shops and residents nearby during the period of delay
A. 只有 (1) (1) only
B. 只有 (2) (2) only
C. 只有 (3) (3) only
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3) (1), (2) and (3)

概念重溫 Concept Review

1. 社會成本 (Social Cost)

社會成本是社會為進行某項生產或活動所付出的總代價。它包括了私人成本和界外成本（外部成本）。
Social cost is the total cost to society for a production or activity. It includes private cost and external cost.

$$ \text{Social Cost} = \text{Private Cost} + \text{External Cost} $$ $$ \text{社會成本} = \text{私人成本} + \text{界外成本} $$

2. 對「經濟」的損失 (Loss to an Economy)

當題目問及「對經濟的損失」時，我們必須考慮所有持份者（Stakeholders）的損失總和，包括：
When asked about the "loss to an economy", we must consider the sum of losses to all stakeholders, including:

  • 生產者 (Producers): 如承建商 (Contractors)
  • 消費者 (Consumers): 如乘客 (Passengers)
  • 第三方 (Third Parties): 如附近居民 (Residents nearby)

答案與詳細分析 Answer & Analysis

正確答案 Correct Option: D

(1) 節省的交通時間 (Saved travel time)

✅ 這是損失 (This is a loss)

分析：時間是一種稀缺資源。鐵路的目的是為了節省時間（效益）。延遲建成意味著原本可以獲得的「時間節省」無法實現。這種「放棄了的潛在得益」是機會成本，也是社會福利的淨損失。

Analysis: Time is a scarce resource. The purpose of the railway is to save time (benefit). Delay means the "time savings" that would have been gained are not realized. This "foregone potential benefit" is an opportunity cost and a net welfare loss to society.

(2) 承建商公司形象的損害 (Damage to corporate image)

✅ 這是損失 (This is a loss)

分析：承建商是經濟體系的一部分（生產者）。公司形象（商譽）是公司的無形資產。形象受損代表資產價值的下降，這對承建商來說是私人成本 (Private Cost)。既然承建商是經濟的一份子，其私人損失亦計算在「對經濟的總損失」之內。

Analysis: The constructor is part of the economy (producer). Corporate image (goodwill) is an intangible asset. Damage to image represents a drop in asset value, which is a Private Cost to the constructor. Since the constructor is part of the economy, their private loss counts towards the "total loss to the economy".

(3) 對附近居民的滋擾 (Nuisance to residents)

✅ 這是損失 (This is a loss)

分析：工程延誤意味著建築工地存在的時間變長，噪音和污染（滋擾）持續的時間也變長。這對第三方（居民和商店）造成了負面影響，屬於界外成本 (External Cost)。這顯然是社會福利的損失。

Analysis: Project delay means the construction site exists for longer, and the noise/pollution (nuisance) persists for a longer duration. This negatively affects third parties (residents and shops), constituting an External Cost. This is clearly a loss of social welfare.

題目拆解與技巧 Problem Decomposition

關鍵字 Pivot Point

題目問的是「對經濟的損失」(Loss to an economy)，而不是單問「界外成本」(External cost) 或「無謂損失」(Deadweight loss)。

The question asks for "Loss to an economy", not just "External cost" or "Deadweight loss". This implies an aggregate of ALL losses.

邏輯推演 Logic Check

1. 選項 (1) 和 選項 (3) 是最明顯的損失（分別為消費者和第三方的損失）。
2. 觀察選項組合：A(1), B(2), C(3), D(1,2,3)。
3. 由於 (1) 和 (3) 必定正確，而沒有「只有 (1) 和 (3)」這個選項，根據邏輯排除法，答案必須是 D。

Since (1) and (3) are definitely correct, and there is no option for "(1) and (3) only", by elimination, the answer must be D.

常犯錯誤 Common Mistakes

  • 誤以為「形象受損」不計算在內

    學生常誤以為只有實質資源（如時間、材料）才算經濟損失。其實，企業資產（包括商譽）的減值也是該企業（經濟成員）的損失。

    Students often mistake that only tangible resources count. In fact, the devaluation of corporate assets (including goodwill) is also a loss to that firm (a member of the economy).

  • 混淆「轉移」與「損失」

    如果是「罰款」(Fine)，這通常是財富轉移（從承建商轉到政府），對整體經濟未必是損失。但「形象受損」或「時間浪費」是價值的蒸發，不是轉移。

    Confusing "Transfer" with "Loss". A fine is a transfer (Contractor to Govt). But "Damage to image" or "Wasted time" is a destruction of value, not a transfer.
