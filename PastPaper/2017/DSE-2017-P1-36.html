HKDSE Economics: MCQ Analysis

Topic (課題): 貨幣量理論 / Quantity Theory of Money

Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts (核心概念): 交易方程式 (Equation of Exchange), 貨幣流通速度 (Velocity of Money), 名義產出 (Nominal Output)

2. 題目 / The Question

根據貨幣量理論，若中央銀行增加貨幣供應，

A. 價格水平會上升。
B. 實質產出會維持不變。
C. 名義產出會上升。
D. 失業率會下降。

According to the quantity theory of money, if a central bank increases money supply,

A. the price level will increase.
B. real output will remain constant.
C. nominal output will increase.
D. the unemployment rate will decrease.

3. 概念重溫 / Concept Review

這道題目考驗你對貨幣量理論 (Quantity Theory of Money) 及其背後的交易方程式 (Equation of Exchange) 的理解。

This question tests your understanding of the Quantity Theory of Money and its underlying Equation of Exchange.

$$ MV = PY $$
  • \(M\) = 貨幣供應 (Money Supply)
  • \(V\) = 貨幣流通速度 (Velocity of Money): 一個單位貨幣在特定時間內用於購買最終物品及服務的平均次數。 / The average number of times a unit of money is used to purchase final goods and services during a specified period.
  • \(P\) = 價格水平 (Price Level)
  • \(Y\) = 實質產出 (Real Output)

關鍵定義 (Key Definition):
方程式右邊的 \(P \times Y\) 就是名義產出 (Nominal Output)，或稱為名義本地生產總值 (Nominal GDP)。
The right side of the equation, \(P \times Y\), represents Nominal Output, or Nominal GDP.

理論核心假設 (Core Assumption of the Theory):
貨幣量理論假設貨幣流通速度 (\(V\)) 是維持不變的 (constant)，因為它取決於公眾的消費習慣和支付制度等長期的制度性因素。
The Quantity Theory of Money assumes that the velocity of money (\(V\)) is constant, because it is determined by long-term institutional factors like spending habits and payment systems.

4. 答案與詳細分析 / The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案是 (The correct option is): Option C

選項 A: 價格水平會上升 (the price level will increase) - ❌ 錯誤 (Wrong)

根據 \(MV = PY\)，若 \(M\) 上升，\(P\) 不一定會上升。如果在短期內，經濟中有閒置資源，實質產出 (\(Y\)) 的升幅可以大於或等於貨幣供應 (\(M\)) 的升幅，此時價格水平 (\(P\)) 可以維持不變甚至下降。

According to \(MV = PY\), if \(M\) increases, \(P\) does not necessarily increase. In the short run, if there are idle resources in the economy, the percentage increase in real output (\(Y\)) could be equal to or greater than the percentage increase in money supply (\(M\)). In this case, the price level (\(P\)) could remain constant or even fall.

選項 B: 實質產出會維持不變 (real output will remain constant) - ❌ 錯誤 (Wrong)

一般版本的貨幣量理論並沒有假設實質產出 (\(Y\)) 必然不變（只有「古典」理論或在長期處於充分就業時才會如此假設）。在短期內，擴張性貨幣政策可以刺激總需求，導致實質產出 (\(Y\)) 上升。

The general version of the Quantity Theory of Money does not assume that real output (\(Y\)) is absolutely constant (only the "Classical" theory or the long-run full-employment model assumes this). In the short run, expansionary monetary policy can stimulate aggregate demand, leading to an increase in real output (\(Y\)).

選項 C: 名義產出會上升 (nominal output will increase) - ✅ 正確 (Correct)

由於理論假設貨幣流通速度 (\(V\)) 固定不變。當貨幣供應 (\(M\)) 增加，方程式左邊的總值 (\(M \times V\)) 必定上升。這意味着方程式右邊的總值 (\(P \times Y\)) 也必然同等地上升。因為 \(P \times Y\) 就是名義產出，所以名義產出必定上升。

Since the theory assumes the velocity of money (\(V\)) is constant, when money supply (\(M\)) increases, the total value on the left side (\(M \times V\)) must increase. Mathematically, this means the total value on the right side (\(P \times Y\)) must also increase. Because \(P \times Y\) is exactly the nominal output, nominal output must increase.

選項 D: 失業率會下降 (the unemployment rate will decrease) - ❌ 錯誤 (Wrong)

雖然貨幣供應增加在短期內可能刺激生產，從而降低失業率，但這不是貨幣量理論中的「必然」結果。如果經濟已經處於充分就業，實質產出 (\(Y\)) 無法再增加，失業率也無法進一步下降（只會引發純粹的通貨膨脹）。

While an increase in money supply might stimulate production in the short run and lower unemployment, this is not a "guaranteed" outcome under the theory. If the economy is already at full employment, real output (\(Y\)) cannot increase further, and the unemployment rate cannot decrease further (it will only cause pure inflation).

5. 題目拆解 / Problem Decomposition

解答這類題目的樞紐點 (Pivot Point) 在於清楚區分「恆等式」和「理論假設」。

The Pivot Point to solving this type of question is to clearly distinguish between an "identity" and a "theoretical assumption".

  • 恆等關係： 名義產出恆等於價格水平乘以實質產出，即 \(\text{Nominal Output} = P \times Y\)。
  • 唯一硬性假設： DSE 考試中，一般的「貨幣量理論」只硬性規定貨幣流通速度 (\(V\)) 是常數。
  • 解題邏輯： \(M \uparrow\) 且 \(V\) 不變 \(\rightarrow\) \(MV \uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) \(PY \uparrow\)。你無法確切知道是 \(P\) 升還是 \(Y\) 升（或是兩者皆升），但兩者的乘積 (\(PY\)) 必定上升。
  • Identity: Nominal output is always equal to price level times real output (\(\text{Nominal Output} = P \times Y\)).
  • Strict Assumption: In DSE exams, the general "Quantity Theory of Money" strictly requires only that velocity (\(V\)) is constant.
  • Logic: \(M \uparrow\) and \(V\) is constant \(\rightarrow\) \(MV \uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) \(PY \uparrow\). You cannot be certain whether \(P\) increases or \(Y\) increases (or both), but their product (\(PY\)) must increase.

6. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 / Common Mistakes & Traps

🚨 陷阱一：誤把「一般貨幣量理論」當作「古典貨幣量理論」
很多同學會秒選 A（物價上升）。他們誤以為 \(Y\)（實質產出）也是固定不變的。記住，除非題目特別說明「在長期 (in the long run)」或「根據古典學派 (Classical theory)」，否則 \(Y\) 是可以變動的，因此 \(M\) 的增加不一定全部反映在 \(P\) 上。

🚨 Trap 1: Confusing general QTM with Classical QTM
Many students quickly choose A (price increases). They mistakenly assume \(Y\) (real output) is also fixed. Remember, unless the question specifically states "in the long run" or "according to the Classical school", \(Y\) can change, so an increase in \(M\) doesn't necessarily translate entirely into an increase in \(P\).

🚨 陷阱二：混淆名義與實質 (Confusing Nominal and Real)
考生經常將「名義產出」和「實質產出」混為一談。見到產出 (output) 就以為是 \(Y\)。必須牢記：名義產出 = \(PY\)，實質產出 = \(Y\)。

🚨 Trap 2: Confusing Nominal and Real
Candidates often mix up "nominal output" and "real output". When they see "output", they assume it's just \(Y\). You must memorize: Nominal Output = \(PY\), Real Output = \(Y\).

7. 學生常見疑問 / Student Q&A

🙋‍♂️ 學生問 (Student): 「平時新聞常說『印銀紙（增加貨幣供應）會導致通貨膨脹（價格上升）』，為甚麼 A 選項不對？」

Question: "News always says 'printing money leads to inflation (price level increases)'. Why is Option A wrong?"

👨‍🏫 老師答 (Tutor): 「現實中印鈔票確實『通常』會引發通脹，但經濟學MC題講求的是『邏輯必然性』。如果經濟正處於嚴重衰退（有很多空置廠房和失業工人），央行增加貨幣供應，企業會先增加生產 (\(Y\)) 而不一定會加價 (\(P\))。因此，\(P\) 上升只是一個『可能性』，而名義產出 \(PY\) 上升才是理論推導出的『必然性』。」

Answer: "In reality, printing money does 'usually' cause inflation. However, economics MCQs test 'logical certainty'. If the economy is in a severe recession (with idle factories and unemployed workers), an increase in money supply will prompt firms to increase production (\(Y\)) without necessarily raising prices (\(P\)). Therefore, an increase in \(P\) is only a 'possibility', whereas an increase in nominal output \(PY\) is a 'mathematical certainty' derived from the theory."

8. 考試技巧與速解 / Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

💡 變化率公式秒殺法 (Percentage Change Formula Trick):

將交易方程式轉換為百分比變動 (percentage change) 格式，在腦海中進行運算：

\( \%\Delta M + \%\Delta V = \%\Delta P + \%\Delta Y \)
  1. 題目說貨幣量理論，立刻代入：\(\%\Delta V = 0\)
  2. 題目說增加貨幣供應：\(\%\Delta M > 0\) (正數)
  3. 得出結果：\(\%\Delta P + \%\Delta Y > 0\)
  4. 要知道 \(\%\Delta P + \%\Delta Y\) 正好等於名義產出 (Nominal Output) 的百分比變動。
  5. 結論：名義產出必定大於 0（即必然上升）。秒選 C！

💡 Percentage Change Formula Trick:

Convert the equation of exchange into its percentage change format and calculate mentally:

\( \%\Delta M + \%\Delta V = \%\Delta P + \%\Delta Y \)
  1. QTM implies: \(\%\Delta V = 0\)
  2. Increase in money supply implies: \(\%\Delta M > 0\) (Positive)
  3. Result: \(\%\Delta P + \%\Delta Y > 0\)
  4. Recognize that \(\%\Delta P + \%\Delta Y\) is exactly the percentage change in Nominal Output.
  5. Conclusion: Nominal output must be greater than 0 (i.e., it must increase). Instantly pick C!
