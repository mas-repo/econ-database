Topic (課題): 企業所有權的形式 / Forms of Business Ownership

Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆ (2/5)

Key Concepts (核心概念): Partnership 合夥 Private Limited Company 私人有限公司 Separate Legal Entity 獨立法人地位

題目 / The Question

亞倫和他的合夥人將他們的合夥轉成私人有限公司。在轉變後，

Alan and his partners turned their partnership into a private limited company. After the change, the firm will

A. 該廠商會有更多資本作發展之用。
A. have more capital for development.
B. 該廠商的平均生產成本會較低。
B. have a lower average production cost.
C. 該廠商會享有較低的利得稅率。
C. enjoy a lower profits tax rate.
D. 該廠商會擁有獨立法人地位。
D. have a separate legal entity.

概念重溫 / Concept Review

要解答這道題目，我們必須了解**合夥 (Partnership)** 與 **私人有限公司 (Private Limited Company)** 的根本法律與經濟特徵差異。

To solve this question, we must understand the fundamental legal and economic differences between a Partnership and a Private Limited Company.

  • 獨立法人地位 (Separate Legal Entity): 有限公司在法律上被視為一個獨立的個體（法人）。它可以以自己的名義擁有資產、簽訂合約以及起訴或被起訴。合夥（除有限責任合夥外）通常不具備此地位。
    A limited company is treated as an independent person in law. It can own assets, enter into contracts, and sue or be sued in its own name. A general partnership lacks this status.
  • 平均生產成本 (Average Production Cost): 這是一個微觀經濟學概念，與企業的生產規模和效率有關。其公式為：
    This is a microeconomic concept related to the scale of production and efficiency. The formula is:
    $$AC = \frac{TC}{Q}$$
    其中 \( TC \) 為總成本 (Total Cost)，\( Q \) 為產出數量 (Quantity of Output)。改變所有權結構並不會自動改變 \( TC \) 或 \( Q \)。
    Where \( TC \) is Total Cost and \( Q \) is Quantity of Output. Changing the ownership structure does not automatically change \( TC \) or \( Q \).
  • 利得稅 (Profits Tax): 在香港，法團（有限公司）的利得稅率通常高於非法團業務（如獨資或合夥）。
    In Hong Kong, the profits tax rate for corporations (limited companies) is generally higher than that for unincorporated businesses (sole proprietorships or partnerships).

答案與詳細分析 / The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案是 / The correct option is: Option D

選項 A (Option A) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

雖然私人有限公司的最高擁有人數限制（上限為 \( 50 \) 人）通常高於合夥（通常上限為 \( 20 \) 人），這意味著它有潛力籌集更多資本，但這並非必然發生。投資者可能不願意投資，因此廠商不一定「會有」更多資本。

Although the maximum number of owners for a private limited company (max \( 50 \) people) is generally higher than that of a partnership (usually max \( 20 \) people), which means it has the potential to raise more capital, this is not guaranteed. Investors might not be willing to invest, so the firm will not necessarily "have" more capital.

選項 B (Option B) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

公司的法律形式改變，與生產效率或規模經濟沒有直接因果關係。平均成本 \( AC \) 不會僅僅因為公司註冊成為有限公司而自動降低。

A change in the legal form of a business has no direct causal relationship with production efficiency or economies of scale. The average cost \( AC \) will not automatically decrease just because the firm incorporates as a limited company.

選項 C (Option C) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

在香港的稅制下，有限公司（法團）的標準利得稅率實際上高於合夥企業（非法團業務）。因此，轉變後廠商會面臨較高（而非較低）的利得稅率。

Under the Hong Kong tax system, the standard profits tax rate for limited companies (corporations) is actually higher than that for partnerships (unincorporated businesses). Therefore, the firm would face a higher, not lower, profits tax rate after the change.

選項 D (Option D) - 正確 (Correct)

這是私人有限公司的決定性特徵。當企業註冊成為有限公司（Incorporation）後，它便依法獲得了「獨立法人地位」，成為一個在法律上與擁有人分開的獨立實體。

This is a defining characteristic of a private limited company. Once a business is incorporated, it legally acquires a "separate legal entity," becoming an independent entity legally separated from its owners.

題目拆解 / Problem Decomposition

解題關鍵 (Pivot Point): 題目詢問的是「必然會發生的結果」(will / 會)。在經濟學多項選擇題中，這要求我們尋找基於法律定義或經濟定律的絕對事實，而非「可能發生的趨勢」。

Pivot Point: The question asks for a guaranteed outcome (indicated by the word "will"). In Economics MCQs, this requires us to look for an absolute fact based on legal definitions or economic laws, rather than a "possible trend."

  • 干擾資訊 (Distractors): 選項A和B都是企業擴張時「可能」帶來的優勢，但轉換所有權形式並不等於業務實質擴張。
  • Options A and B are advantages that "might" occur when a business expands, but changing the ownership structure does not equate to actual business expansion.

常犯錯誤與陷阱 / Common Mistakes & Traps

陷阱：混淆「融資能力 (Ability to raise capital)」與「實際資本 (Actual capital)」
很多學生會秒選 A，因為課本上寫「有限公司能容納更多股東，較易籌集資金」。請注意，「較易籌資」 \( \neq \) 「必然有更多資本」。這是一個非常經典的 DSE 邏輯陷阱（Careless Error）。

Trap: Confusing "Ability to raise capital" with "Actual capital"
Many students quickly choose A because textbooks state "limited companies can accommodate more shareholders and raise capital more easily." Please note that "easier to raise capital" \( \neq \) "will definitively have more capital." This is a very classic DSE logic trap.

學生常見疑問 / Student Q&A

Q1: 獨立法人地位和「有限責任 (Limited Liability)」是一樣的嗎？

A1: 兩者有密切關聯，但概念不同。「獨立法人地位」指公司是一個法律實體（能以自己名義簽約、告人）；正因為公司自己承擔法律和債務責任，股東才享有「有限責任」（即虧損最多為其投資額，個人資產受保護）。

Q1: Is "Separate Legal Entity" the same as "Limited Liability"?

A1: They are closely related but distinct concepts. "Separate Legal Entity" means the company is a legal person (can sign contracts and sue in its own name). Because the company bears its own legal and debt responsibilities, the shareholders enjoy "Limited Liability" (i.e., maximum loss is their investment amount; personal assets are protected).

考試技巧與速解 / Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

關鍵字篩選法 (Keyword Filtering): 當題目出現「會 (will)」這種絕對字眼時，立刻排除所有依賴外部條件（如投資者意願、市場環境）的選項（如選項 A 和 B）。接著，用本地常識（香港稅制）排除 C。D 則是純粹的法律定義，必然正確。

Keyword Filtering: When the question uses absolute words like "will," immediately eliminate all options that depend on external conditions (like investor willingness or market environment), such as Options A and B. Next, use local knowledge (HK tax system) to eliminate C. D is a pure legal definition and is therefore absolutely correct.
