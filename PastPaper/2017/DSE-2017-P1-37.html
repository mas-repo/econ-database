Topic: Macroeconomic Policies (宏觀經濟政策) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts: Fiscal Policy (財政政策), Monetary Policy (貨幣政策), Aggregate Demand (總需求)

1. The Question (題目)

下列哪些政策最有可能會令總產出下降？
A. 政府向公眾購入債券而同時提高免稅額。
B. 政府向公眾出售債券而同時減少給予窮人的轉移支付。
C. 政府調低法定儲備比率而同時調高入息稅的累進程度。
D. 政府調高貼現率而同時調低調低利得稅率。
Which of the following policies would most likely lead to a drop in aggregate output?
A. The government bought bonds from the public and raised the tax allowance at the same time.
B. The government sold bonds to the public and reduced transfer payment to the poor at the same time.
C. The government reduced required reserve ratio and raised the progressivity of income tax at the same time.
D. The government increased the discount rate and lowered profits tax rate at the same time.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

總需求 (Aggregate Demand, AD) 是指在不同物價水平下，經濟體系內各部門對本地生產的最終物品及服務的計劃總開支。其公式為：
Aggregate Demand (AD) is the total planned expenditure on domestically produced final goods and services at different price levels. The formula is:
$$ AD = C + I + G + NX $$
要令總產出 \(Y\) 必定下降，我們需要尋找緊縮性貨幣政策 (Contractionary Monetary Policy) 和 緊縮性財政政策 (Contractionary Fiscal Policy) 的組合。這兩者都會令總需求 \(AD\) 減少（向左移）。
To ensure a definite drop in aggregate output \(Y\), we need to look for a combination of Contractionary Monetary Policy and Contractionary Fiscal Policy. Both will decrease Aggregate Demand \(AD\) (shift it to the left).

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: B

Option A: 購入債券會增加貨幣供應，降低利率，刺激投資 \(I\)（擴張性貨幣政策）。提高免稅額會增加可支配收入，刺激消費 \(C\)（擴張性財政政策）。兩者皆令 \(AD\) 增加，總產出上升。
Option A: Buying bonds increases money supply, lowers interest rates, and stimulates investment \(I\) (Expansionary Monetary). Raising tax allowance increases disposable income and stimulates consumption \(C\) (Expansionary Fiscal). Both increase \(AD\), leading to a rise in output.
Option B: 出售債券會減少貨幣供應，推高利率，減少投資 \(I\)（緊縮性貨幣政策）。減少轉移支付會減少窮人的可支配收入，減少消費 \(C\)（緊縮性財政政策）。兩者皆令 \(AD\) 減少，總產出必定下降。
Option B: Selling bonds decreases money supply, raises interest rates, and reduces investment \(I\) (Contractionary Monetary). Reducing transfer payments lowers disposable income and reduces consumption \(C\) (Contractionary Fiscal). Both decrease \(AD\), leading to a definite drop in output.
Option C: 調低法定儲備比率是擴張性貨幣政策（\(AD\) 升），而調高入息稅累進程度是緊縮性財政政策（\(AD\) 跌）。一增一減，總產出變化不確定。
Option C: Reducing the required reserve ratio is expansionary monetary policy (\(AD\) rises), while raising income tax progressivity is contractionary fiscal policy (\(AD\) falls). The net effect on output is uncertain.
Option D: 調高貼現率是緊縮性貨幣政策（\(AD\) 跌），而調低利得稅率是擴張性財政政策（\(AD\) 升）。一減一增，總產出變化不確定。
Option D: Increasing the discount rate is contractionary monetary policy (\(AD\) falls), while lowering the profits tax rate is expansionary fiscal policy (\(AD\) rises). The net effect on output is uncertain.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題關鍵 (Pivot Point): 題目要求找出「最有可能令總產出下降」的選項。這意味著選項中的兩項政策方向必須一致，且均為「緊縮性 (Contractionary)」。如果一項是擴張性，另一項是緊縮性，最終對總產出 \(Y\) 的影響將會是「不確定 (Uncertain)」。
Pivot Point: The question asks for the combination that "most likely leads to a drop in aggregate output". This means both policies in the option must be "Contractionary". If one is expansionary and the other is contractionary, the final effect on aggregate output \(Y\) will be "Uncertain".

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：混淆公開市場操作的方向
學生常忘記「出售債券 (Selling bonds)」是收回市場資金，導致貨幣供應下跌；而「購入債券 (Buying bonds)」是向市場注入資金，導致貨幣供應上升。
Trap: Confusing the direction of Open Market Operations
Students often forget that "Selling bonds" withdraws money from the market, decreasing money supply; whereas "Buying bonds" injects money into the market, increasing money supply.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼減少轉移支付會影響總需求？
A: 轉移支付（如綜援、失業救濟金）直接影響市民的可支配收入 (Disposable Income)。減少轉移支付會令可支配收入下降，從而減低私人消費開支 \(C\)，導致總需求 \(AD\) 下降。
Q: Why does reducing transfer payments affect aggregate demand?
A: Transfer payments (like social security or unemployment benefits) directly affect citizens' disposable income. Reducing them lowers disposable income, which in turn reduces private consumption expenditure \(C\), leading to a drop in aggregate demand \(AD\).

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

符號速算法 (+ / - 法):
在試卷上，為每個政策標上 "+" (代表擴張性/升) 或 "-" (代表緊縮性/跌)。
A: (+) 及 (+)
B: (-) 及 (-)
C: (+) 及 (-)
D: (-) 及 (+)
題目要求總產出下降，直接尋找兩個都是 "-" 的選項，即選項 B，只需 10 秒即可解題！
The "+ / -" Sign Method:
On your exam paper, mark each policy with a "+" (Expansionary/Increase) or "-" (Contractionary/Decrease).
A: (+) and (+)
B: (-) and (-)
C: (+) and (-)
D: (-) and (+)
Since the question asks for a drop in output, look for the option with two "-" signs. That's Option B. Solved in 10 seconds!
