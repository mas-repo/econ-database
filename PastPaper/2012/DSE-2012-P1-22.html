💡 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解答這條題目，我們需要掌握支出計算法（Expenditure Approach）的兩大核心公式轉換：

To solve this question, we must master two core formula conversions in the Expenditure Approach:

1. 本地總投資 (Gross Investment) 的計算：
題目提供了「本地固定資本形成淨額」(Net domestic fixed capital formation)，但計算本地生產總值需要用「總投資」(Gross Investment)。

1. Calculating Gross Investment:
The question provides "Net domestic fixed capital formation", but we need "Gross Investment" to calculate GDP.

$$I=\text{Net domestic fixed capital formation}+\text{Depreciation}+\text{Increase in inventory}$$

2. 市價 (Market Price) 與 要素成本 (Factor Cost) 的轉換：
用 $C+I+G+NX$ 算出來的是按市價計算的 GDP ($GDP_{mp}$)。要轉換成按要素成本計算的 GDP ($GDP_{fc}$)，需要扣除間接稅並加上補貼。

2. Conversion between Market Price and Factor Cost:
The sum of $C+I+G+NX$ gives GDP at market price ($GDP_{mp}$). To convert it to GDP at factor cost ($GDP_{fc}$), we must subtract indirect taxes and add subsidies.

$$GDP_{mp}=C+I+G+NX$$ $$GDP_{fc}=GDP_{mp}-\text{Indirect Taxes}+\text{Subsidies}$$

📝 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): B

第一步：設立方程式 (Step 1: Set up the equation)
根據公式，我們先表達出總投資 $I$：
$I = 40 + 40 + X = 80 + X$

Based on the formula, we first express Gross Investment $I$:
$I = 40 + 40 + X = 80 + X$

第二步：計算按市價計算的本地生產總值 (Step 2: Calculate GDP at market price)
$GDP_{mp} = C + I + G + NX$
$GDP_{mp} = 200 + (80 + X) + 24 + 10$
$GDP_{mp} = 314 + X$

Substitute the components into the expenditure approach formula to find $GDP_{mp}$.

第三步：轉換為要素成本並解開 X (Step 3: Convert to factor cost and solve for X)
$GDP_{fc} = GDP_{mp} - \text{Indirect Taxes} + \text{Subsidies}$
$264 = (314 + X) - 60 + 30$
$264 = 314 + X - 30$
$264 = 284 + X$
$X = -20$

Apply the factor cost adjustment, substitute $GDP_{fc} = 264$, and solve for $X$.

選項分析 (Option Breakdown)

  • A (-50): 錯誤。這是考生忘記加上「補貼」(30) 所得出的錯誤結果 ($264 = 314 + X - 60 \Rightarrow X = 10$，或者混淆了正負號)。
    Incorrect. This is likely due to forgetting to add "Subsidies" (30) or messing up the mathematical signs.
  • B (-20): 正確。
    Correct.
  • C (20): 錯誤。這是考生錯誤地加上間接稅和扣除補貼 ($264 = 314 + X + 60 - 30 \Rightarrow 264 = 344 + X \Rightarrow X = -80$)，或者是忘記了折舊 (40)。
    Incorrect. The student likely added indirect taxes and subtracted subsidies by mistake, or completely forgot to include Depreciation (40).
  • D (50): 錯誤。這是因為考生直接漏掉了「折舊」($I$ 只用了 $40+X$) 並且錯誤處理了市價與要素成本的關係。
    Incorrect. This happens if the student skips "Depreciation" (using only $40+X$ for $I$) and handles the market price adjustments poorly.

🔍 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

關鍵支點 (The Pivot Point):
題目的破局點在於「淨額」轉「總額」。很多學生看到「本地固定資本形成淨額」就直接當作投資 $I$ 使用。你必須敏銳地找出表中的「折舊」(40)，把它加上去才能得到真正的 Gross Investment。

The Pivot Point:
The breakthrough point of this question is converting "Net" to "Gross". Many students directly use "Net domestic fixed capital formation" as Investment $I$. You must be sharp enough to find "Depreciation" (40) in the table and add it to get the true Gross Investment.

干擾資訊 (The Distractor):
國外要素收益淨值 (Net factor income from abroad) = 16。這是一個徹頭徹尾的陷阱！這項數據是用來將本地生產總值 (GDP) 轉換為本地居民生產總值 (GNP/GNI) 的。題目由始至終只問 GDP，所以這個數字完全不需要使用。

The Distractor:
Net factor income from abroad = 16. This is an absolute trap! This figure is used to convert Gross Domestic Product (GDP) to Gross National Product/Income (GNP/GNI). Since the question only asks for GDP, this number is completely irrelevant.

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  1. 忘記「折舊」(Depreciation)： DSE 考評局最喜歡給出「淨固定資本形成」，測試你是否記得 $Gross = Net + Depreciation$。
  2. 市價與要素成本符號調亂： 記住口訣：要素成本 (Factor Cost) 是生產者真正收到的錢。所以要減去交給政府的間接稅 (- Indirect Taxes)，加上政府給予的補貼 (+ Subsidies)。
  1. Forgetting "Depreciation": The HKEAA loves giving "Net fixed capital formation" to test if you remember that $Gross = Net + Depreciation$.
  2. Mixing up signs for Market Price and Factor Cost: Remember the logic: Factor Cost is the money producers actually receive. Therefore, we subtract the indirect taxes paid to the government (- Indirect Taxes) and add the government subsidies received (+ Subsidies).

🙋‍♂️ 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 存貨增加 (Increase in inventory) 算出來是負數 (-20)，這在經濟學上合理嗎？
A: 完全合理！存貨增加為負數，代表「存貨減少」(Inventory depletion)。這意味著企業在該年度賣出的商品數量，超過了他們當年實際生產的數量（他們動用了過去的庫存來應付今年的需求）。

Q: The calculated "Increase in inventory" is a negative number (-20). Does this make sense in economics?
A: Perfectly sensible! A negative increase in inventory means an "Inventory depletion". This implies that firms sold more goods during the year than they actually produced (they used past inventory stocks to meet current year demand).

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

逆向推算法 (Working Backward Method):
與其帶著未知數 $X$ 做一大串代數運算，不如從最終答案「倒推」回去，這樣能大幅減少算錯正負號的機會！

Working Backward Method:
Instead of carrying the unknown variable $X$ through a long algebraic equation, work "backward" from the final answer. This drastically reduces careless sign errors!

1. 已知 $GDP_{fc} = 264$。先把它推回 $GDP_{mp}$：
$GDP_{mp} = 264 + \text{Indirect Taxes} - \text{Subsidies}$
$GDP_{mp} = 264 + 60 - 30 = 294$

2. 現在我們知道總支出 ($C+I+G+NX$) 必須等於 294：
$200 + I + 24 + 10 = 294$
$234 + I = 294$
$I = 60$

3. 最後拆解總投資 $I$：
$I = \text{Net Capital Formation} + \text{Depreciation} + X$
$60 = 40 + 40 + X$
$60 = 80 + X$
$X = -20$
