HKMA (金管局) Central Bank Functions (央行功能) HIBOR (銀行同業拆息)

1. 題目 The Question

下列哪項不是香港金融管理局的功能？

Which of the following is NOT a function of the Hong Kong Monetary Authority?

  1. 決定香港的銀行同業拆息
    determining the inter-bank offered rate in Hong Kong
  2. 監督私人銀行機構的流動性狀況
    supervising the liquidity position of the private banking institutions
  3. 管理及投資外匯基金
    managing and investing the Exchange Fund
  4. 維持聯繫匯率的穩定
    maintaining the stability of the linked exchange rate

2. 概念重溫 Concept Review

香港金融管理局 (HKMA) 的主要功能

香港金融管理局（金管局）是香港的實質中央銀行。它的四大主要職能包括：

  • 維持貨幣穩定： 透過聯繫匯率制度（Linked Exchange Rate System）維持港元匯價穩定（約 \( USD 1 = HKD 7.8 \)）。
  • 促進金融體系的穩定與健全： 監管銀行業務，確保銀行有足夠的流動性。
  • 管理外匯基金： 投資及管理外匯基金（Exchange Fund）以支持港元。
  • 維持金融基建： 維持支付及結算系統的順利運作。

The Hong Kong Monetary Authority (HKMA) is the de facto central bank of Hong Kong. Its four main functions are:

  • Maintaining monetary stability: Maintaining the stability of the HKD exchange rate (around \( USD 1 = HKD 7.8 \)) through the Linked Exchange Rate System.
  • Promoting the stability and integrity of the financial system: Supervising banking operations and ensuring banks have sufficient liquidity.
  • Managing the Exchange Fund: Investing and managing the Exchange Fund to back the HKD.
  • Maintaining financial infrastructure: Ensuring the smooth operation of payment and settlement systems.

3. 答案與詳細分析 The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Answer: A

選項 A (正確答案 Correct Option) This is NOT a function

決定香港的銀行同業拆息 (HIBOR) 不是金管局的功能。銀行同業拆息是由市場上銀行之間對資金的供求關係決定的。金管局雖然會設定「貼現窗基本利率」(Base Rate)，但不會直接決定 HIBOR。

Determining the Hong Kong Inter-bank Offered Rate (HIBOR) is NOT a function of the HKMA. HIBOR is determined by the market forces of supply and demand for funds among banks. Although the HKMA sets the "Base Rate" for the Discount Window, it does not directly determine HIBOR.

選項 B (錯誤選項 Incorrect Option)

監督私人銀行機構的流動性狀況是金管局的功能。作為銀行業的監管機構，金管局必須確保銀行體系穩健。

Supervising the liquidity position of private banking institutions IS a function of the HKMA. As the banking regulator, the HKMA must ensure the banking system is sound.

選項 C (錯誤選項 Incorrect Option)

管理及投資外匯基金是金管局的核心功能之一，這筆基金用於捍衛港元匯率。

Managing and investing the Exchange Fund IS one of the core functions of the HKMA. This fund is used to defend the HKD exchange rate.

選項 D (錯誤選項 Incorrect Option)

維持聯繫匯率的穩定是金管局最重要的貨幣政策目標。

Maintaining the stability of the linked exchange rate IS the most important monetary policy objective of the HKMA.

4. 題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

這是一條典型的「否定型」(NOT) 題目。解題的關鍵在於：

  • 找出破綻 (Pivot Point)： 香港是一個高度自由的市場經濟體。雖然金管局履行央行職責，但它不干預商業利率的定價。同業拆息 (HIBOR) 和最優惠利率 (Prime Rate) 都是由市場或商業銀行自行決定的。

This is a typical "NOT" question. The key to solving it is:

  • Pivot Point: Hong Kong is a highly free market economy. Although the HKMA performs central bank duties, it does not intervene in the pricing of commercial interest rates. Both HIBOR and the Prime Rate are determined by the market or commercial banks themselves.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

陷阱 Trap：混淆「基本利率」與「同業拆息」

學生常誤以為金管局控制所有利率。事實上，金管局只決定貼現窗基本利率 (Base Rate)（銀行向金管局借錢的利率），而銀行同業拆息 (HIBOR) 是銀行之間互相借貸的利率，由市場供求決定。

Students often mistakenly believe the HKMA controls all interest rates. In fact, the HKMA only determines the Base Rate (the rate at which banks borrow from the HKMA via the Discount Window), while HIBOR is the rate at which banks lend to each other, determined by market supply and demand.

6. 學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

Q: 如果金管局不決定 HIBOR，那麼誰決定？

A: HIBOR 是由香港銀行公會（HKAB）根據每天指定參考銀行提供的報價，計算出來的平均數。它反映了當天市場上資金的充裕程度（供求關係）。

Q: If the HKMA doesn't determine HIBOR, who does?

A: HIBOR is calculated by the Hong Kong Association of Banks (HKAB) as an average based on quotes provided by designated reference banks every day. It reflects the abundance of funds (supply and demand) in the market on that day.

7. 考試技巧與速解 Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

排除法 (Elimination Method)

在 DSE 經濟科中，金管局的三大「招牌」功能就是：聯繫匯率 (D)、外匯基金 (C) 和 銀行監管 (B)。只要你背熟了這三大功能，看到這三個選項就可以立刻排除，剩下的 A 就是答案！

In DSE Economics, the three "signature" functions of the HKMA are: Linked Exchange Rate (D), Exchange Fund (C), and Banking Supervision (B). If you memorize these three, you can immediately eliminate them. The remaining option, A, must be the answer!
