HKDSE Economics 經濟學解題指南

Topic: Interest & Time Preference (利息與時間偏好) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Time Preference, Interest Rate, Real vs Nominal

1. 題目 (The Question)

下列哪項關於利息的陳述是正確的？

A. 當利率下跌時，人們便會減少現時的消費。
B. 若沒有通脹，利率便會是零。
C. 在計劃經濟中，利息不存在。
D. 沒有貨幣，利息仍然存在。

Which of the following statements about interest is correct?

A. People will reduce current consumption when the interest rate drops.
B. The interest rate will be zero if there is no inflation.
C. Interest does not exist in a planned economy.
D. Interest still exists without money.

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

正時間偏好 (Positive Time Preference)

根據經濟學家費沙 (Irving Fisher) 的理論，人類天生具有正時間偏好。這意味著，在其他條件相同的情況下，人們總是偏好「現在」消費多於「將來」消費。為甚麼？因為未來充滿不確定性，而且現在享受帶來的滿足感通常更強烈。

According to economist Irving Fisher, humans naturally possess a positive time preference. This means, ceteris paribus, people always prefer "current" consumption over "future" consumption. Why? Because the future is uncertain, and the satisfaction derived from present enjoyment is usually stronger.

利息的定義 (Definition of Interest)

因此，利息是提早獲得物品或資源而支付的代價（溢價），或者是延遲消費所獲得的補償。它反映了時間的價值，與貨幣或經濟體系無絕對關係。

Therefore, interest is the premium paid for the earlier availability of goods or resources, or the compensation received for deferring consumption. It reflects the value of time and is not strictly tied to money or specific economic systems.

名義利率與實質利率 (Nominal vs. Real Interest Rate)

在宏觀經濟中，我們通常用費沙方程式 (Fisher Equation) 來表達名義利率 \(R\)、實質利率 \(r\) 及預期通脹率 \(\pi^e\) 之間的關係：

In macroeconomics, we usually use the Fisher Equation to express the relationship between the nominal interest rate \(R\), the real interest rate \(r\), and the expected inflation rate \(\pi^e\):

$$ 1 + R = (1 + r)(1 + \pi^e) $$ $$ \text{Approximation: } r \approx R - \pi^e $$

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是：選項 D (Option D)

分析選項 A (錯誤) / Analysis of Option A (Incorrect)

利率是現時消費的機會成本。當利率下跌時，提早消費（或借錢消費）的成本會降低，儲蓄的吸引力也會下降。因此，人們應該會增加現時的消費，而不是減少。

The interest rate is the opportunity cost of current consumption. When the interest rate drops, the cost of early consumption (or borrowing to consume) decreases, and the attractiveness of saving drops. Therefore, people will increase current consumption, not reduce it.

分析選項 B (錯誤) / Analysis of Option B (Incorrect)

即使通脹率為零 (\(\pi^e = 0\))，實質利率 \(r\) 仍然是大於零的，因為人類具有正時間偏好（人們仍需要誘因才肯延遲消費）。根據 \(R \approx r + \pi^e\)，即使 \(\pi^e = 0\)，名義利率 \(R\) 仍然會是正數。

Even if the inflation rate is zero (\(\pi^e = 0\)), the real interest rate \(r\) is still greater than zero due to positive time preference (people still need an incentive to defer consumption). According to \(R \approx r + \pi^e\), even if \(\pi^e = 0\), the nominal interest rate \(R\) will still be positive.

分析選項 C (錯誤) / Analysis of Option C (Incorrect)

利息的存在源於人類的「時間偏好」和資源的「稀少性」(Scarcity)。無論是在自由市場經濟還是計劃經濟，人類希望盡早獲得資源的本質並不會改變。只要時間偏好存在，利息在概念上就必定存在（即使它可能不以市場利率的形式表現出來）。

The existence of interest stems from human "time preference" and the "scarcity" of resources. Whether in a free market or a planned economy, the human nature of wanting resources earlier does not change. As long as time preference exists, interest conceptually must exist (even if not explicitly shown as a market interest rate).

分析選項 D (正確) / Analysis of Option D (Correct)

這是經濟學中極為重要的一個概念：利息不等於金錢。在一個沒有貨幣的以物易物 (Barter) 社會中，如果你今天向我借一個蘋果，因為我延遲了消費，我會要求你明年還我 1.1 個蘋果。這多出來的 0.1 個蘋果就是利息。因此，沒有貨幣，利息仍然存在。

This is a crucial concept in economics: Interest is not equal to money. In a barter economy without money, if you borrow one apple from me today, because I am deferring my consumption, I will demand you return 1.1 apples next year. That extra 0.1 apple is the interest. Thus, interest still exists without money.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point): 處理這類題目的關鍵在於將「利息」(Interest) 從日常生活的「銀行存款 / 鈔票」概念中抽離。你必須使用 Fisher 的經濟學嚴格定義：利息 = 提早獲得物品的代價 (Price of earlier availability of goods)。

只要你緊記這一定義，你就能一眼看出利息與「貨幣」(Money)、「通脹」(Inflation) 或「經濟體制」(Economic System) 沒有必然的因果關係，它純粹建基於人性和稀少性。

The Pivot Point: The key to tackling this type of question is detaching "Interest" from the daily life concept of "bank deposits / fiat money". You must use Fisher's strict economic definition: Interest = Price of earlier availability of goods.

Once you keep this definition in mind, you can immediately see that interest has no absolute causal relationship with "Money", "Inflation", or "Economic System". It is purely based on human nature and scarcity.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

❌ 觀念陷阱： 許多同學會誤選 B 或 C，因為他們將「利息」與「金融體系」掛鈎。他們認為在計劃經濟中，政府分配一切，沒有商業銀行，就沒有利息。這是混淆了「利息的概念」與「市場利率的表達形式」。

❌ 粗心大意： 忽略選項 A 中的「減少」二字。利率下跌，借錢平了，當然是「增加」消費（買車、買樓）。

❌ Conceptual Trap: Many students mistakenly choose B or C because they link "Interest" strictly to the "Financial System". They think in a planned economy where the government allocates everything and there are no commercial banks, there is no interest. This confuses the "concept of interest" with the "expression of market interest rates".

❌ Careless Error: Overlooking the word "reduce" in Option A. When interest rates drop, borrowing is cheaper, so people naturally "increase" current consumption (buying cars, houses).

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

🙋 學生問 (Student): 如果在一個物資無限極度豐富（沒有稀少性）的烏托邦世界，利息還存在嗎？

👨‍🏫 導師答 (Tutor): 好問題！如果資源是無限的（沒有 Scarcity），你可以隨時隨地得到你想要的東西，那麼「提早獲得」就變得毫無意義，因為你根本不需要等待。在這種情況下，正時間偏好失去作用，利息就不存在了。所以，利息存在的前提是稀少性 (Scarcity) 和正時間偏好 (Positive Time Preference)。

🙋 Student: Would interest still exist in a utopian world with infinitely abundant resources (no scarcity)?

👨‍🏫 Tutor: Great question! If resources are infinite (no scarcity) and you can get whatever you want instantly, then the concept of "earlier availability" becomes meaningless because you never have to wait. In this scenario, positive time preference loses its effect, and interest would not exist. Therefore, the prerequisites for interest are Scarcity and Positive Time Preference.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

🚀 秒殺技巧 (Speed Kill Technique):

在 DSE 經濟科中，只要題目問到「利息的本質」，請立刻在腦海中閃過這句咒語：「利息是提早享受的代價，不需要錢也可以發生」。用這句咒語直接掃描選項，你會發現只有 D 選項完美契合這個核心概念，3秒內即可鎖定答案。

🚀 Speed Kill Technique:

In the DSE Economics exam, whenever a question asks about the "nature of interest", immediately flash this mantra in your mind: "Interest is the price of early enjoyment, it can happen without money." Scan the options using this mantra, and you will find that only Option D perfectly aligns with this core concept, allowing you to lock in the answer within 3 seconds.
