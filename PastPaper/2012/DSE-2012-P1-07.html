HKDSE Economics Exam Guide
香港中學文憑試經濟科解題指南

Topic: 支付工資的方法 (Methods of Payment) Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆
Key Concepts: 基本薪金 (Basic Salary), 佣金 (Commission), 經營風險 (Business Risk), 量度成本 (Measurement Costs)

The Question (題目)

一些物業經紀行以基本薪金加佣金支付員工的薪金，因為
(1) 這可提高員工的工作意欲。
(2) 這可將部分經營風險轉嫁給員工。
(3) 計算工資的成本較低。

A. 只有(1)、(2)
B. 只有(1)、(3)
C. 只有(2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2)及(3)
Some property agencies pay their employees a basic salary plus commission because
(1) It can increase the working incentive of the employees.
(2) It can transfer part of the business risk to the employees.
(3) The cost of calculating wages is lower.

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

Concept Review (概念重溫)

基本薪金 (Basic Salary) vs. 佣金 (Commission)
Basic Salary vs. Commission
在勞工市場中，「基本薪金」屬於計時制（Time Rate），僱員的收入取決於工作時間，與產出數量無直接關係。而「佣金」屬於計件制（Piece Rate），僱員的收入直接與其促成的銷售額或利潤掛鈎。物業經紀通常採用兩者混合的薪酬結構。
In the labor market, a "basic salary" is a Time Rate system, where employee income depends on hours worked, regardless of output. "Commission" is a Piece Rate system, where income is directly tied to sales or profits generated. Property agents usually use a mixed remuneration structure.
Formula (公式):
$$ \text{Total Income (總收入)} = W_{base} + (\alpha \times Q_{sales}) $$
Where (當中):
\( W_{base} \) = Fixed Basic Salary (固定基本薪金)
\( \alpha \) = Commission Rate (佣金比率)
\( Q_{sales} \) = Sales Volume / Value (銷售量或銷售額)
交易費用與量度成本 (Transaction Costs & Measurement Costs)
Transaction Costs & Measurement Costs
計算佣金時，僱主必須量度員工的實際產出（如核實交易金額、計算佣金百分比）。這種量度過程會產生較高的行政及計算成本。
To calculate commissions, employers must measure the employee's actual output (e.g., verifying transaction amounts, calculating percentages). This measurement process incurs higher administrative and calculation costs.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Answer (正確答案): Option A
分析選項 (1)：這可提高員工的工作意欲。（正確）
Analysis of (1): It can increase the working incentive of the employees. (CORRECT)
因為加入了佣金（計件制元素），員工只要促成更多交易，其總收入 \( \text{Total Income} \) 就會上升。這直接將多勞多得的概念體現出來，為員工提供了極大的金錢誘因去努力工作。
Because commission (a piece-rate element) is included, the employee's \( \text{Total Income} \) will increase as long as they close more deals. This directly embodies the concept of "more pay for more work," providing a strong financial incentive for employees to work harder.
分析選項 (2)：這可將部分經營風險轉嫁給員工。（正確）
Analysis of (2): It can transfer part of the business risk to the employees. (CORRECT)
經營風險（Business Risk）指生意好壞帶來的不確定性。如果只支付高額固定薪金，無論市況好壞，僱主都要承擔全部工資成本。但改用「底薪加佣金」，當市況差、生意額 \( Q_{sales} \) 減少時，僱主需要支付的佣金總額也會相應減少，從而減輕了僱主的財務壓力。員工的收入會隨市況波動，意味著他們分擔了部分風險。
Business risk refers to the uncertainty of business performance. If only a high fixed salary is paid, the employer bears all wage costs regardless of market conditions. By switching to "basic salary plus commission", when the market is bad and \( Q_{sales} \) drops, the total commission paid by the employer also decreases, relieving financial pressure. The employees' income fluctuates with the market, meaning they share part of the risk.
分析選項 (3)：計算工資的成本較低。（錯誤）
Analysis of (3): The cost of calculating wages is lower. (INCORRECT)
相比起純計時制（每個月只需支付固定金額），計算佣金需要額外的行政工作：例如查核員工的銷售紀錄、防範虛假交易、計算不同階梯的佣金率等。因此，量度員工產出和計算工資的成本實際上是較高的，而非較低。
Compared to a pure time-rate system (paying a fixed amount each month), calculating commissions requires extra administrative work: such as verifying sales records, preventing fraudulent deals, and calculating tiered commission rates. Therefore, the cost of measuring employee output and calculating wages is actually higher, not lower.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

拆題關鍵 (Pivot Point)：這條題目的核心在於明白「佣金」在經濟學上的本質——它是一種需要量度「產出 (Output)」的支付方式。一看到「量度產出」，你就必須聯想到「量度成本上升 (Higher Measurement Costs)」。
Pivot Point: The core of this question lies in understanding the economic nature of "commission"—it is a payment method that requires measuring "output". Whenever you see "measuring output", you must immediately link it to "Higher Measurement Costs".
干擾資訊 (Distractors)：有些同學會根據日常直覺，認為「算個百分比很簡單」，從而誤判選項(3)為正確。在經濟學中，我們考慮的「計算成本」不僅僅是按計算機的時間，還包括監察、核實和記錄交易的整體交易費用 (Transaction costs)。
Distractors: Some students might rely on daily intuition and think "calculating a percentage is simple," thus mistakenly judging option (3) as correct. In economics, the "cost of calculation" we consider isn't just the time spent on a calculator, but the overall transaction costs of monitoring, verifying, and recording transactions.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱一：混淆「勞工成本」與「計算工資成本」
同學常犯的錯誤是認為「生意差時僱主付少點錢，所以成本較低」。請注意！「支付給員工的薪金總額」(Wage bill) 與「計算工資所產生的行政成本」(Cost of calculating wages) 是兩個完全獨立的概念。佣金制可能降低生意差時的薪金支出，但一定會增加計算薪金的行政成本。
Trap 1: Confusing "Labour Cost" with "Cost of Calculating Wages"
A common mistake is thinking "the employer pays less when business is bad, so the cost is lower." Attention! The "Wage bill" (total money paid to employees) and the "Cost of calculating wages" (administrative cost) are entirely separate concepts. A commission system might lower the wage bill during bad times, but it will definitely increase the administrative cost of calculating those wages.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 既然全佣金制最能提高意欲，又最能轉嫁風險，為何經紀行還要支付「基本薪金」呢？
Q: Since a 100% commission system maximizes incentive and risk transfer, why do agencies still pay a "basic salary"?
A: 地產市場波動很大。如果採用100%純佣金制（即基本薪金 \( W_{base} = 0 \)），員工在淡季可能會出現零收入，導致他們無法維持基本生活開支而辭職。提供基本薪金是為了保障員工的最低收入，減少員工流失率（降低招聘和培訓新人的成本）。
A: The property market is highly volatile. If a 100% pure commission system is used (i.e., \( W_{base} = 0 \)), employees might have zero income during off-seasons, causing them to quit because they cannot cover basic living expenses. Providing a basic salary ensures a minimum income, reducing staff turnover rate (lowering the costs of hiring and training new staff).

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

消去法 (Process of Elimination)：
在DSE經濟科中，只要出現「計件制 (Piece rate)」、「佣金 (Commission)」或「花紅 (Bonus)」，它們的計算/量度成本永遠大於「計時制 (Time rate)」。
只要你牢記這個黃金法則，一看到選項(3)說「計算工資的成本較低」，你可以立刻判斷它是錯的。
然後看選項：
B. 只有(1)、(3)
C. 只有(2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2)及(3)
直接秒殺，答案必定是 A。
Process of Elimination:
In HKDSE Economics, whenever you see "Piece rate", "Commission", or "Bonus", their calculation/measurement costs are ALWAYS greater than "Time rate".
Once you memorize this golden rule, the moment you see Statement (3) claiming "cost of calculating is lower", you know it's instantly false.
Look at the options:
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)
You can eliminate B, C, and D immediately. The answer must be A.
