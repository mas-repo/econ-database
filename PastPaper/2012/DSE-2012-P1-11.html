Topic: Price Elasticity of Demand (需求價格彈性) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Total Expenditure (總開支), Unitary Elasticity (單一彈性)

1. The Question (題目)

米雪是珠寶愛好者。她說：「我會將我全部的月薪花在珠寶上，不論它的價格如何。」由此我們可得出結論：她對珠寶的需求屬

A. 高彈性。
B. 低彈性。
C. 單一彈性。
D. 完全沒有彈性。

Michelle is a jewelry lover and she says, “I will spend all my monthly salary on jewelry no matter what the price is.” From this we can conclude that her demand for jewelry is

A. elastic.
B. inelastic.
C. unitarily elastic.
D. perfectly inelastic.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這道題目，我們需要理解總開支 (Total Expenditure, TE)與需求價格彈性 (Price Elasticity of Demand, \(E_d\))之間的關係。

To solve this question, we need to understand the relationship between Total Expenditure (TE) and Price Elasticity of Demand (\(E_d\)).

The formula for Total Expenditure is:

$$ TE = P \times Q $$

當價格 \(P\) 改變時，總開支 \(TE\) 的變化取決於需求彈性：

  • 高彈性 (\(E_d > 1\))： 價格與總開支呈反方向變動。
  • 低彈性 (\(E_d < 1\))： 價格與總開支呈同方向變動。
  • 單一彈性 (\(E_d = 1\))： 不論價格如何變動，總開支保持不變。

When price \(P\) changes, the change in Total Expenditure \(TE\) depends on the elasticity of demand:

  • Elastic (\(E_d > 1\)): Price and TE move in opposite directions.
  • Inelastic (\(E_d < 1\)): Price and TE move in the same direction.
  • Unitarily Elastic (\(E_d = 1\)): TE remains constant regardless of price changes.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: C

A. 高彈性 (Elastic) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
如果需求屬高彈性，當珠寶價格上升時，米雪的總開支應該會減少。但題目指出她會花光所有月薪（固定金額），因此總開支不變。
If demand were elastic, Michelle's total expenditure would decrease when the price of jewelry increases. However, the question states she spends all her monthly salary (a fixed amount), meaning her expenditure is constant.
B. 低彈性 (Inelastic) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
如果需求屬低彈性，當珠寶價格上升時，總開支應該會增加。這與她只花費固定月薪的設定矛盾。
If demand were inelastic, total expenditure would increase when the price increases. This contradicts the premise that she only spends a fixed monthly salary.
C. 單一彈性 (Unitarily elastic) - 正確 (Correct)
米雪將「全部的月薪」花在珠寶上，這代表她的總開支 \(TE\) 是一個固定常數（即她的月薪）。當總開支在任何價格水平下都保持不變時，這正是單一彈性 (\(E_d = 1\)) 的定義。
Michelle spends "all her monthly salary" on jewelry, which means her total expenditure \(TE\) is a fixed constant (her salary). When total expenditure remains unchanged at any price level, this perfectly fits the definition of unitarily elastic demand (\(E_d = 1\)).
D. 完全沒有彈性 (Perfectly inelastic) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
完全沒有彈性是指無論價格如何，購買的「數量」都不變。如果數量不變，當價格改變時，她的總開支也會隨之改變（例如價格下跌時開支會減少），這與她「將全部月薪（固定金額）花在珠寶上」的設定矛盾。
Perfectly inelastic means the "quantity" demanded remains constant regardless of price. If quantity is constant, her total expenditure would change whenever the price changes (e.g., expenditure would fall if the price falls). This contradicts the premise that she always spends exactly her entire fixed monthly salary on jewelry.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題關鍵 (Pivot Point)：「將我全部的月薪花在珠寶上」 (spend all my monthly salary)。

這句話是將現實情境轉化為經濟學變量的關鍵。月薪是一個固定的數值。當消費者決定將一筆固定預算全部用於單一物品時，這意味著：
總開支 (TE) = 常數 (Constant)。

Pivot Point: "spend all my monthly salary".

This phrase translates a real-world scenario into an economic variable. A monthly salary is a fixed amount. When a consumer allocates a fixed budget entirely to a single good, it means:
Total Expenditure (TE) = Constant.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱： 很多學生看到「不論它的價格如何」(no matter what the price is) 就會立刻選擇 D. 完全沒有彈性。

真相： 必須分清楚「不論價格如何，我都買這麼多數量」與「不論價格如何，我都花這麼多錢」。前者是完全沒有彈性 (\(E_d = 0\))，後者是單一彈性 (\(E_d = 1\))。

Trap: Many students see the phrase "no matter what the price is" and immediately jump to D. perfectly inelastic.

The Truth: You must distinguish between "I will buy the same quantity no matter the price" and "I will spend the same amount of money no matter the price". The former is perfectly inelastic (\(E_d = 0\)), while the latter is unitarily elastic (\(E_d = 1\)).

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果價格上升了一倍，米雪的購買量會怎樣改變？

A: 因為她的總開支 \(TE = P \times Q\) 保持不變，如果價格 \(P\) 上升一倍（即變成 \(2P\)），她的購買數量 \(Q\) 就必須減半（即變成 \(0.5Q\)）。這證明了價格變動的百分比與需求量變動的百分比完全抵銷，即單一彈性。

Q: If the price doubles, how will Michelle's quantity demanded change?

A: Since her total expenditure \(TE = P \times Q\) is constant, if the price \(P\) doubles (becomes \(2P\)), her quantity demanded \(Q\) must be halved (becomes \(0.5Q\)). This proves that the percentage change in price is exactly offset by the percentage change in quantity demanded, which is the definition of unitary elasticity.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧： 在 HKDSE 經濟科中，只要題目出現「固定預算」(fixed budget)、「花光所有收入」(spend all income) 等字眼來形容對某物品的消費，答案必定是「單一彈性」(Unitarily elastic)。

Shortcut: In HKDSE Economics, whenever a question uses phrases like "fixed budget" or "spend all income" to describe the consumption of a specific good, the answer is ALWAYS "Unitarily elastic".
