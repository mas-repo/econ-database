主題 (Topic): 國際收支平衡：經常帳 (Balance of Payments: Current Account)

難度 (Difficulty): ★★☆☆☆ (2/5)

核心概念 (Key Concepts): 無形進口 Invisible Import 經常帳 Current Account 服務貿易 Trade in Services

題目 (The Question)

下列哪項是香港經常帳中的無形進口？

Which of the following is an invisible import in the current account of Hong Kong?

A. 一名香港旅客在北京購買門票遊覽當地的博物館。

A. In Beijing, a Hong Kong tourist purchases tickets to visit a local museum.

B. 一名內地居民在香港向醫院求診。

B. A Mainland resident consults a doctor in a hospital in Hong Kong.

C. 一名內地億萬富翁捐款予香港一間大學。

C. A Mainland billionaire donates money to a university in Hong Kong.

D. 一家香港製造商向一家內地廠商購買原料。

D. A Hong Kong manufacturer purchases raw materials from a Mainland firm.

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

國際收支平衡表 (Balance of Payments) 記錄了一個經濟體與世界各地之間的所有交易。當中的經常帳 (Current Account) 主要包含四個部分：

The Balance of Payments (BOP) records all economic transactions between an economy and the rest of the world. The Current Account mainly consists of four components:

$$ \text{Current Account} = \text{Net Goods} + \text{Net Services} + \text{Net Primary Income} + \text{Net Secondary Income} $$

  • 有形貿易 (Trade in Goods / Visible Trade): 涉及實體商品的進出口（例如：電子產品、原料）。

    Trade in Goods (Visible Trade): Involves the import/export of physical products (e.g., electronics, raw materials).

  • 無形貿易 (Trade in Services / Invisible Trade): 涉及非實體的服務交易（例如：旅遊、金融、醫療、運輸）。無形進口 (Invisible Import) 是指本地居民消費外國提供的服務，導致資金流出本地。

    Trade in Services (Invisible Trade): Involves non-physical service transactions (e.g., tourism, finance, medical, transportation). An Invisible Import occurs when local residents consume services provided by foreigners, causing money to flow out of the local economy.

  • 次要收入 / 經常轉移 (Secondary Income / Current Transfers): 單向的資金轉移，不涉及任何商品或服務的交換（例如：捐款、外傭匯款）。

    Secondary Income (Current Transfers): One-way transfers of money without the exchange of goods or services (e.g., donations, remittances by foreign domestic helpers).

答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是：選項 A

The correct answer is: Option A

選項 A (Option A) - 正確 (Correct)

分析： 香港旅客在外地（北京）消費，購買的是「旅遊及娛樂服務」。因為這是一項服務而非實體商品過境，所以屬於「無形」；同時，香港居民付款給外地機構，資金從香港流出，因此屬於「進口」。這完全符合「無形進口」的定義。

Analysis: A Hong Kong tourist spending money abroad (in Beijing) is purchasing "tourism and entertainment services". Since this is a service and not physical goods crossing borders, it is "invisible". Since a Hong Kong resident is paying a foreign entity, money flows out of Hong Kong, making it an "import". This perfectly fits the definition of an invisible import.

選項 B (Option B) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

分析： 醫療屬於「服務」（無形）。但是，這是一非本地居民（內地居民）向香港醫院付款，資金流入香港。因此，這是香港的無形出口 (Invisible Export)。

Analysis: Medical care is a "service" (invisible). However, a non-resident (Mainland resident) is paying a Hong Kong hospital, meaning money flows INTO Hong Kong. Therefore, this is an Invisible Export for Hong Kong.

選項 C (Option C) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

分析： 捐款是一種單向的金錢轉移，大學並沒有提供相應的商品或服務作為交換。因此，這屬於經常帳中的次要收入 / 經常轉移收入 (Secondary Income / Current Transfer Receipt)，而不是貿易。

Analysis: A donation is a one-way transfer of money; the university does not provide goods or services in exchange. Therefore, this falls under Secondary Income / Current Transfer Receipts in the current account, not trade.

選項 D (Option D) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

分析： 「原料」是看得見、摸得著的實體商品。資金雖然流出香港（進口），但因為是實體物品，所以這是有形進口 (Visible Import)。

Analysis: "Raw materials" are physical, tangible goods. While money is flowing out of Hong Kong (making it an import), the physical nature makes this a Visible Import.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

處理這類國際貿易分類題目，你需要尋找兩個「關鍵點 (Pivot Points)」：

To tackle this type of international trade classification question, you need to identify two "Pivot Points":

  1. 關鍵一：是「有形」還是「無形」？(Visible vs. Invisible?)
    問自己：交易的是實體物品（原料、成衣）還是服務（旅遊、教育、醫療）？這能讓你立刻排除選項 D。

    Pivot 1: Is it Visible or Invisible?
    Ask yourself: Is the transaction for physical goods (raw materials, clothes) or services (tourism, education, medical)? This immediately eliminates Option D.

  2. 關鍵二：是「進口」還是「出口」？(Import vs. Export?)
    問自己：資金是流出還是流入？香港人付錢給外地 = 資金流出 = 進口。外地人付錢給香港 = 資金流入 = 出口。這能讓你區分選項 A（進口）和選項 B（出口）。

    Pivot 2: Is it an Import or Export?
    Ask yourself: Is money flowing out or in? HK residents paying foreigners = money flows out = Import. Foreigners paying HK = money flows in = Export. This helps you distinguish between Option A (Import) and Option B (Export).

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

❌ 陷阱一：混淆交易發生的地點與進出口的關係。
很多學生誤以為「在香港發生」的交易就是本地消費。記住，旅遊業是一個特例：當外地遊客在香港消費（如選項 B 的醫院求診），對香港來說是服務的出口，因為賺取了外匯！

❌ Trap 1: Confusing the location of the transaction with import/export status.
Many students mistakenly believe that any transaction "happening in HK" is local consumption. Remember, tourism is a special case: when foreign tourists spend money in HK (like consulting a doctor in Option B), it is an export of services for HK because it earns foreign exchange!

❌ 陷阱二：忽略了「無償轉移」。
看到錢的流動就以為是貿易（進出口）。選項 C 的捐款、或者外傭寄錢回鄉，都沒有涉及實質生產的服務或貨品交換，必須歸類為「次要收入/經常轉移」。

❌ Trap 2: Ignoring "Unrequited Transfers".
Assuming any flow of money is trade (import/export). Donations in Option C, or domestic helpers sending money home, do not involve an exchange of productive services or goods, and MUST be classified as "Secondary Income / Current Transfers".

學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

問 (Q)：選項 A 中，香港旅客明明是在北京買門票，為甚麼算是香港的進口？

Q: In Option A, the HK tourist buys the ticket in Beijing. Why is it counted as an import for Hong Kong?

答 (A)： 因為在國際收支的概念中，「進口」的本質是本地居民消耗了外地的資源/服務，導致本地資金流失。無論這位香港居民是在網上預訂北京博物館的門票，還是親身飛到北京購買，只要付款方是香港居民，收款方是外地機構，且交易涉及服務，這就是香港的服務進口。

A: Because in the context of Balance of Payments, the essence of an "import" is that a local resident consumes foreign resources/services, causing local funds to flow out. Whether the HK resident books the Beijing museum ticket online or flies there to buy it in person, as long as the payer is a HK resident, the payee is a foreign entity, and it involves a service, it is an import of services for Hong Kong.

考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

當你在考場上看到「無形進口 (Invisible Import)」這個詞時，你的大腦應該立即啟動這個過濾機制：

When you see the term "Invisible Import" in the exam, your brain should immediately activate this filtering mechanism:

  • 找「服務」 $\rightarrow$ 劃掉所有提到實體貨品（原料、汽車、成衣）的選項。

    Look for "Services" $\rightarrow$ Cross out any option mentioning physical goods (raw materials, cars, clothing).

  • 找「香港人付錢給外國」 $\rightarrow$ 劃掉所有外國人來香港花錢的選項（因為那是出口）。

    Look for "HK people paying foreigners" $\rightarrow$ Cross out any option where foreigners spend money in HK (because that is an export).

  • 排除「免費/單向」 $\rightarrow$ 劃掉捐款、匯款、禮物等字眼。

    Eliminate "Free/One-way" transfers $\rightarrow$ Cross out words like donations, remittances, or gifts.
