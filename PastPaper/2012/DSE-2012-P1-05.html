HKDSE Economics: Question Analysis

主題 (Topic): 勞工供應 - 勞工流動性 (Labour Supply - Labour Mobility)

難度 (Difficulty): ★★☆☆☆

關鍵字 (Key Concepts): 職業流動性 (Occupational Mobility), 地區流動性 (Geographical Mobility), 專門化 (Specialization)

1. 題目 (The Question)

當__________，該經濟中勞工的職業流動性會上升。
  1. 跨境鐵路網有所改善
  2. 勞力市場中有更多類型的工作需要牌照
  3. 持續進修能獲免稅額
  4. 生產更高度專門化
The occupational mobility of labour in an economy will increase when
  1. the cross-border railway network is improved.
  2. licences are required for more types of job in the labour market.
  3. tax allowances are provided for continuing education.
  4. there is a greater degree of specialization in production.

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

勞工流動性 (Labour Mobility) 是指工人改變工作地點或工作種類的能力和意願。它主要分為兩大類：

Labour Mobility refers to the ease and willingness with which workers can change their work location or job type. It is mainly divided into two categories:

  • 職業流動性 (Occupational Mobility): 勞工由一個行業或職位轉到另一個行業或職位的容易程度。影響因素包括：培訓成本、牌照要求、技能專門化程度等。
    Occupational Mobility: The ease with which a worker can move from one job or industry to another. Influencing factors include: training costs, licensing requirements, degree of skill specialization, etc.
  • 地區流動性 (Geographical Mobility): 勞工由一個地區轉到另一個地區工作的容易程度。影響因素包括：交通網絡、住屋成本、文化/語言差異等。
    Geographical Mobility: The ease with which a worker can move from one geographical location to another for work. Influencing factors include: transport networks, housing costs, cultural/language differences, etc.

在經濟學模型中，職業流動性 \( M_o \) 可以被看作是轉行成本（如金錢、時間）的函數。當獲取新技能的成本 \( C_s \) 下降，或入行門檻 \( B \) 降低時，流動性會增加。

In economic models, occupational mobility \( M_o \) can be viewed as a function of the cost of changing professions (e.g., money, time). When the cost of acquiring new skills \( C_s \) falls, or barriers to entry \( B \) decrease, mobility increases.

$$ M_o = f(C_s, B) $$ $$ \text{where } \frac{\partial M_o}{\partial C_s} < 0, \quad \frac{\partial M_o}{\partial B} < 0 $$

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): C

A. 跨境鐵路網有所改善 錯誤 (Incorrect)
A. the cross-border railway network is improved.

鐵路網改善減少了交通時間和成本，這使工人更容易到不同地區工作。因此，這會增加地區流動性 (Geographical mobility)，而不是職業流動性。

Improving the railway network reduces commuting time and costs, making it easier for workers to work in different regions. Therefore, this increases geographical mobility, not occupational mobility.

B. 勞力市場中有更多類型的工作需要牌照 錯誤 (Incorrect)
B. licences are required for more types of job in the labour market.

牌照要求（Licensing）是一種入行門檻 (Barriers to entry)。如果更多工作需要牌照，工人轉行就需要花費更多時間和金錢去考取資格，這會降低職業流動性。

Licensing is a barrier to entry. If more jobs require licences, workers must spend more time and money to get qualified when changing fields, which decreases occupational mobility.

C. 持續進修能獲免稅額 正確 (Correct)
C. tax allowances are provided for continuing education.

免稅額 (Tax allowances) 實質上降低了工人學習新技能或進修的財務負擔。當獲取新技能的成本降低時，工人更容易學習其他行業所需的技能並成功轉行，從而提高了職業流動性。

Tax allowances effectively reduce the financial burden on workers learning new skills. When the cost of acquiring new skills is lowered, it becomes easier for workers to learn skills required for other industries and successfully switch jobs, thereby increasing occupational mobility.

D. 生產更高度專門化 錯誤 (Incorrect)
D. there is a greater degree of specialization in production.

高度專門化 (Specialization) 意味著工人只專注於生產過程中極微小且特定的工序。他們掌握的技能變得非常狹窄，難以應用到其他行業中，這反而會降低職業流動性。

High specialization means workers focus on very narrow and specific tasks in the production process. Their skills become highly specific and difficult to apply to other industries, which actually decreases occupational mobility.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

拆題樞紐 (The Pivot Point):
解答此題的核心在於區分「轉地區」與「轉行業」，以及理解什麼因素會「降低轉行成本」。題目問的是職業流動性 (Occupational) 的上升 (increase)。你需要尋找一個能讓工人更容易轉換工作種類的選項。

The Pivot Point:
The core of solving this question is distinguishing between "changing locations" and "changing industries", and understanding what "lowers the cost of changing jobs". The question asks for an increase in occupational mobility. You need to look for an option that makes it easier for a worker to change job types.

干擾項分析 (Distractor Analysis):
選項 A 是最常見的干擾項，它確實增加了某種流動性，但考評局故意用「交通改善」來混淆你對「地區」與「職業」的分類概念。

Distractor Analysis:
Option A is the most common distractor. It does increase a type of mobility, but the examination board deliberately uses "transport improvement" to confuse your categorization of "geographical" vs "occupational" concepts.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • 陷阱一：誤解「專門化 (Specialization)」的好處。 學生常以為「專門化」代表工人技術很高超，所以到哪裡都受歡迎。但在經濟學中，專門化代表技能單一性。一個非常專業的鐘錶匠轉行去寫電腦程式是非常困難的，因此專門化反而降低職業流動性。
    Trap 1: Misunderstanding the benefits of "Specialization". Students often think "specialization" means a worker is highly skilled and therefore popular everywhere. But in economics, specialization means narrowness of skills. A highly specialized watchmaker would find it very difficult to switch to computer programming. Hence, specialization decreases occupational mobility.
  • 陷阱二：將所有「流動性」混為一談。 看到「鐵路」就覺得「方便了」，便直覺認為所有流動性都上升。必須嚴格區分 Geographical（地點）和 Occupational（職業）。
    Trap 2: Confusing all types of "mobility". Seeing "railway" makes students feel things are "more convenient", leading to an intuitive assumption that all mobility increases. You must strictly distinguish between Geographical (location) and Occupational (job type).

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

問 (Q): 如果鐵路網改善，工人不就可以去更遠的地方從事不同的行業嗎？這不算增加職業流動性嗎？

Q: If the railway network improves, can't workers go to farther places to work in different industries? Doesn't that count as increasing occupational mobility?

答 (A): 這是個很好的思考！但我們必須抓緊定義。交通改善直接且主要改變的是克服地理距離的成本（增加地區流動性）。雖然他去遠方可能是做另一份工，但他能否「成功轉行」，取決於他有沒有相關技能和資格，這並不是鐵路能解決的。所以針對「職業流動性」，我們必須選擇直接影響技能獲取或入行門檻的因素（即選項C）。

A: This is good critical thinking! However, we must stick to the definitions. Transport improvements directly and primarily reduce the cost of overcoming geographical distance (increasing geographical mobility). While they might travel far to do a different job, their ability to "successfully switch industries" depends on whether they have the relevant skills and qualifications, which a railway cannot provide. Therefore, for "occupational mobility", we must choose the factor that directly affects skill acquisition or barriers to entry (which is Option C).

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

關鍵字秒殺法 (The Keyword Scan Method):
在選擇題中，你可以利用關鍵字快速配對或排除選項：

The Keyword Scan Method:
In MCQs, you can use keywords to quickly match or eliminate options:

  • 看到「交通 / 鐵路 / 網絡 / 簽證 (Transport/Railway/Network/Visa)」 ➔ 地區流動性 (Geographical)
  • 看到「牌照要求 / 法定資格 (Licences/Legal limits)」 ➔ 阻礙入行 ➔ 職業流動性 ↓ (Occupational ↓)
  • 看到「專門化 / 分工 (Specialization/Division of labour)」 ➔ 技能變窄 ➔ 職業流動性 ↓ (Occupational ↓)
  • 看到「教育 / 培訓資助 (Education/Training subsidy)」 ➔ 容易學新技能 ➔ 職業流動性 ↑ (Occupational ↑)
