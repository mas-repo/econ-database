HKDSE Economics Analysis

主題 (Topic): 宏觀經濟學 - 總需求與總供應、財政政策 (Macroeconomics - AD-AS Model, Fiscal Policy)

難度 (Difficulty): ★★★☆☆

核心概念 (Key Concepts): 總需求 (Aggregate Demand) 長期總供應 (LRAS) 財政赤字 (Budget Deficit)

題目 (The Question)

假設政府耗資30億元興建一郵輪碼頭，該筆開支由政府的儲備支付。這會導致下列哪些結果？

(1) 短期而言，總產出上升。
(2) 長期而言，總產出上升。
(3) 短期而言，就業增加。
(4) 在建築年期內出現財政赤字。

A. 只有(1)、(3)
B. 只有(2)、(4)
C. 只有(1)、(2)、(3)
D. 只有(1)、(3)、(4)

Suppose the government spends $3 billion on the construction of a cruise terminal which is financed from its reserves. This will result in

(1) an increase in aggregate output in the short run.
(2) an increase in aggregate output in the long run.
(3) an increase in employment in the short run.
(4) a budget deficit in the years of construction.

A. (1) and (3) only
B. (2) and (4) only
C. (1), (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (3) and (4) only

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解答這道題目，我們需要理解政府開支對宏觀經濟的短期與長期影響，以及政府預算的定義。

To answer this question, we need to understand the short-run and long-run macroeconomic impacts of government spending, as well as the definition of a government budget.

1. 總需求 (Aggregate Demand, AD)

政府興建基建屬於政府開支 \(G\)。當 \(G\) 增加時，總需求 \(AD\) 會上升。

Government spending on infrastructure is part of government expenditure \(G\). When \(G\) increases, aggregate demand \(AD\) increases.
$$AD = C + I + G + NX$$

2. 長期總供應 (Long Run Aggregate Supply, LRAS)

興建碼頭等基礎建設會增加經濟體系的資本存量 (Capital stock) 及生產力，從而令長期總供應 \(LRAS\) 向右移。

Building infrastructure like a cruise terminal increases the economy's capital stock and productivity, which shifts the Long Run Aggregate Supply \(LRAS\) to the right.

3. 財政預算 (Government Budget)

財政赤字發生在政府總開支大於總收入時。單一項目的支出增加，並不代表整體預算必定出現赤字。

A budget deficit occurs when total government expenditure exceeds total revenue. An increase in spending for a single project does not necessarily mean the overall budget will be in deficit.
$$\text{Budget Balance} = \text{Total Revenue } (T) - \text{Total Expenditure } (G)$$

答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是 (Correct Option): C. 只有(1)、(2)、(3) / (1), (2) and (3) only

選項 (1) 分析

正確。 政府耗資30億元興建碼頭，直接增加了政府開支 \(G\)。這會導致總需求 \(AD\) 增加，在短期內，總產出 (Short-run aggregate output) 會隨之上升。

Correct. The $3 billion spent on the terminal directly increases government expenditure \(G\). This increases aggregate demand \(AD\), leading to an increase in short-run aggregate output.

選項 (2) 分析

正確。 郵輪碼頭屬於實質的基礎建設投資。當碼頭落成或建設過程中，它提升了社會的生產能力和資本存量，令長期總供應 \(LRAS\) 增加，因此長期總產出 (Long-run aggregate output) 也會上升。

Correct. A cruise terminal is a real infrastructure investment. It enhances the productive capacity and capital stock of the economy, increasing Long Run Aggregate Supply \(LRAS\). Therefore, long-run aggregate output increases.

選項 (3) 分析

正確。 由於短期總產出上升，廠商（如建築公司）需要聘請更多工人來應付增加的生產需求（引申需求），因此短期就業人數會增加。

Correct. Because short-run output increases, firms (like construction companies) need to hire more workers to meet the increased production demand (derived demand), so short-run employment increases.

選項 (4) 分析

錯誤。 題目只提及這30億元由「儲備」支付，但沒有提供政府整體的總收入 \(T\) 和總開支 \(G\) 的數據。即使政府動用儲備，如果當年的總稅收仍然大於總開支，政府依然可以有財政盈餘。因此，我們無法確定必定會出現財政赤字。

Incorrect. The question only states the $3 billion is financed from reserves, but provides no data on the government's overall total revenue \(T\) and total expenditure \(G\). Even if reserves are used, if total tax revenue still exceeds total expenditure that year, the government can still have a budget surplus. Thus, we cannot conclude a budget deficit will definitely occur.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

破題關鍵 (Pivot Point)： 區分「政府消費」與「政府投資」，以及理解「財政赤字」的嚴格定義。

Pivot Point: Distinguishing between "government consumption" and "government investment", and understanding the strict definition of a "budget deficit".
  • 干擾資訊 (Distractor): 「由政府的儲備支付 (financed from its reserves)」是一個陷阱。很多學生誤以為動用儲備就等於「入不敷支」即赤字。實際上，赤字只看當年的 \(T - G\) 是否為負數。
  • "Financed from its reserves" is a trap. Many students mistakenly think using reserves equates to spending more than earning (a deficit). In reality, a deficit only depends on whether \(T - G\) is negative for that specific year.

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

忽略基建的長期影響： 學生常以為政府開支 \(G\) 增加只會影響短期的 \(AD\)。必須緊記，如果是基礎建設 (Infrastructure) 或教育 (Education) 的開支，它們同時會提升生產力，導致 \(LRAS\) 右移，從而增加長期產出。

Ignoring the long-run effects of infrastructure: Students often think an increase in government spending \(G\) only affects short-run \(AD\). Remember that spending on infrastructure or education also improves productivity, shifting \(LRAS\) to the right and increasing long-run output.

學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 既然動用了儲備，不就代表政府當年的錢不夠花，所以才會有赤字嗎？

A: 不一定。政府可能為了專項基金（例如基建基金）而提早撥款並儲存起來。動用這筆特定儲備去建碼頭，只是資金來源的轉換。只要當年整體的經常性收入加上其他收入大於總支出，預算依然可以是盈餘的。在經濟學選擇題中，除非題目明確指出 \(G > T\)，否則不能武斷推論出赤字。

Q: If reserves are used, doesn't that mean the government didn't have enough money that year, implying a deficit?
A: Not necessarily. The government might have set aside funds in a specific reserve (like a capital works fund) previously. Using this specific reserve is just a transfer of funds. As long as the overall general revenue plus other revenues exceed total expenditure that year, the budget can still be in surplus. In Economics MCQs, unless stated that \(G > T\), you cannot definitively conclude there is a deficit.

考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

消去法 (Elimination Method)： 當你看到選項 (4) 提到「出現財政赤字」，你應該立刻警覺題目資訊不足以判斷整體預算狀況，因此 (4) 必定錯誤。排除了包含 (4) 的選項 B 和 D 後，只剩下 A 和 C。接著只需判斷 (2) 是否正確（基建會增加長期產出，正確），就能快速鎖定答案 C。

Elimination Method: When you see statement (4) mentioning "a budget deficit", you should immediately realize there is insufficient info to determine the overall budget balance, making (4) incorrect. Eliminating options B and D (which contain 4) leaves A and C. Then, you only need to evaluate statement (2) (infrastructure increases long-run output, which is true) to quickly lock in option C.
