3. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解答這道題目，我們必須根據特徵來辨認這兩間企業的類型。在經濟學中，我們經常比較**合夥 (Partnership)** 與 **私人有限公司 (Private Limited Company)**：

To solve this question, we must identify the types of these two firms based on their features. In Economics, we frequently compare Partnerships and Private Limited Companies:

  • 獨立法人 (Separate Legal Entity): 企業是否在法律上被視為一個獨立於其東主的個體。有限公司擁有此地位，因此享有有限債務責任 (Limited Liability)。
    Separate Legal Entity: Whether a business is legally recognized as a separate entity from its owners. Limited companies have this status, thus enjoying limited liability.
  • 延續性 (Continuity): 企業的壽命是否會因東主破產或離世而終止。有限公司擁有延續性，而獨資及合夥通常沒有。
    Continuity: Whether the lifespan of a business terminates upon the bankruptcy or death of an owner. Companies have perpetual continuity, whereas sole proprietorships and partnerships typically do not.

對於香港的利得稅 (Profits Tax) 計算，我們可以參考以下概念：法團 (Corporations) 和非法團業務 (Unincorporated Businesses) 承受不同的法定稅率：

Regarding Profits Tax in Hong Kong, we can refer to this concept: Corporations and Unincorporated Businesses are subject to different statutory tax rates:

$$\text{Corporate Tax Rate} (16.5\%) > \text{Unincorporated Tax Rate} (15\%)$$

4. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案：選項 B (Option B)

Analysis of Option B (Correct): Based on the data, Firm A possesses "continuity" and restricted transfer of ownership, indicating it is a Private Limited Company. Firm B lacks "continuity" (dissolves upon owner's bankruptcy), indicating it is a Partnership. A private limited company (Firm A) is a separate legal entity in law, whereas a partnership (Firm B) is not. Therefore, Option B is correct.

Analysis of Option A (Incorrect): The risk of takeover (especially hostile takeover) typically applies to Public Limited Companies whose shares are freely traded. Since both firms require "consent of other owners" to transfer ownership, neither is a public company. Their takeover risks are both very low, making a comparison invalid.
分析選項 C (錯誤)：甲廠商（私人有限公司）屬於法團，而乙廠商（合夥）屬於非法團業務。在香港稅制下，法團的利得稅稅率（通常為 $16.5\%$）與非法團業務的稅率（通常為 $15\%$）是**不同**的。
Analysis of Option C (Incorrect): Firm A (Company) is a corporation, while Firm B (Partnership) is an unincorporated business. Under the HK tax system, the profits tax rate for corporations (usually $16.5\%$) and unincorporated businesses (usually $15\%$) are different.
分析選項 D (錯誤)：只有**公眾有限公司**才需要向公眾披露（公開）其財務狀況。甲廠商（私人有限公司）和乙廠商（合夥）均**不須要**向公眾披露其財務報表。
Analysis of Option D (Incorrect): Only Public Limited Companies are required to disclose their financial status to the public. Both Firm A (Private Limited) and Firm B (Partnership) are not required to publish their financial statements to the general public.

5. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解答這類題目的關鍵在於**「特徵辨識」 (Feature Identification)**。你需要像偵探一樣，找出決定性的線索（Pivot Point）。

The key to solving this type of question is Feature Identification. You need to act like a detective to find the decisive clue (Pivot Point).

  • 關鍵線索 (Pivot Point)：延續性。這是區分「有限公司」與「合夥/獨資」的最強指標。題目明確指出甲有延續性，乙沒有。這瞬間將甲定位為「公司 (Company)」，乙定位為「合夥 (Partnership)」。
    Pivot Point: Continuity. This is the strongest indicator to distinguish a "Company" from a "Partnership/Sole Proprietorship". The question clearly states Firm A has continuity and Firm B does not. This instantly classifies A as a Company and B as a Partnership.
  • 干擾資訊 (Distractors)：東主數目。雖然私人有限公司最多允許 $N \le 50$ 名股東，合夥通常最多 20 人，但題目提供的「2人」和「10人」同時符合這兩種企業架構的合法範圍，因此這個數字對判斷企業類型**沒有幫助**，純粹是干擾。
    Distractors: Number of owners. Although private limited companies allow up to $N \le 50$ shareholders and partnerships usually up to 20, the numbers "2" and "10" provided in the question fit within the legal limits of both business structures. Thus, these numbers are unhelpful in determining the firm type and serve purely as distractors.

6. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • 混淆「私人」與「公眾」有限公司：學生常誤以為所有「有限公司」都要公開帳目或面臨被收購風險。記住，**私人有限公司**的股份不能在股票市場自由買賣，且享有高度隱私（無需公開帳目）。
    Confusing "Private" and "Public" Limited Companies: Students often mistakenly believe all "limited companies" must publish accounts or face takeover risks. Remember, Private Limited Companies cannot trade shares freely on the stock market and enjoy high privacy (no need to publish accounts).
  • 誤解「法人」定義：很多學生以為只要有多於一個老闆（如合夥），就是獨立法人。只有完成公司註冊程序的「有限公司」，在法律上才是一個獨立於擁有人的個體。
    Misunderstanding the definition of "Legal Entity": Many students think having more than one owner (like a partnership) automatically makes it a legal entity. Only a "Limited Company" that has completed the incorporation process is legally a separate entity from its owners.

7. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 如果乙廠商的東主數目變成 30 人，乙廠商還會是合夥嗎？
Q: If the number of owners for Firm B became 30, could Firm B still be a partnership?
A: 根據香港一般法例，普通合夥企業的合夥人數目上限通常為 20 人。如果達到 30 人，除非它是某些特定專業（如律師、會計師）的合夥，否則它必須註冊為有限公司。
A: Under general Hong Kong law, an ordinary partnership is usually limited to a maximum of 20 partners. If it reaches 30, it must register as a limited company unless it is a partnership of certain specified professions (e.g., lawyers, accountants).

8. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

「跳行閱讀法」：在處理這類表格題時，**不要從上往下讀**。第一行通常是「東主數目」，這幾乎總是干擾項。直接看表格最底部的**「延續性」**或**「債務責任 (有限/無限)」**。這兩個特徵是「一擊必殺」的條件，能讓你在 5 秒內立刻分辨出是法團還是非法團業務！
"Skip-Row Reading Method": When dealing with these table questions, do not read from top to bottom. The first row is usually "Number of owners", which is almost always a distractor. Look straight at the bottom rows for "Continuity" or "Liability (Limited/Unlimited)". These two features are "one-hit kills" that allow you to instantly distinguish between corporations and unincorporated businesses in 5 seconds!
