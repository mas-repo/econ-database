Economics Tutorial: HKDSE MC Analysis

Topic: 國際貿易與比較優勢 (Int. Trade & Comparative Advantage) Difficulty: ★★★★☆ Key Concepts: 機會成本, 貿易比率, 貿易獲利

2. The Question (題目)

下表顯示甲、乙兩國一單位的資源可生產玩具或成衣的數量。
若貿易比率是 2單位成衣 = 1單位玩具：
A. 甲國每一單位進口可獲利 5 單位玩具。
B. 甲國每一單位出口可獲利 0.6 單位成衣。
C. 乙國每一單位進口可獲利 0.5 單位成衣。
D. 乙國會出口玩具，每一單位出口可獲利 0.6 單位成衣。
The following table shows the number of toys or clothes that Country A and Country B can produce with one unit of resources.
If the terms of trade is 2 units of clothes = 1 unit of toys:
A. Country A will gain 5 units of toys per unit of imports.
B. Country A will gain 0.6 units of clothes per unit of exports.
C. Country B will gain 0.5 units of clothes per unit of imports.
D. Country B will export toys and gain 0.6 units of clothes per unit of exports.
玩具 Toys (units) 成衣 Clothes (units)
甲國 Country A 10 10
乙國 Country B 4 10

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 產量問題的機會成本 (Opportunity Cost for Output Problems)
當表格顯示「相同資源下的產出量」時，計算生產物品 X 的機會成本公式是「放棄的 Y / 獲得的 X」。
When the table shows output quantities for a given amount of resources, the opportunity cost of producing Good X is calculated as "Quantity of Y foregone / Quantity of X gained".
$$ \text{Opportunity Cost of X} = \frac{\text{Max Output of Y}}{\text{Max Output of X}} $$
2. 比較優勢 (Comparative Advantage)
一個國家在生產某物品上的機會成本較低，該國就擁有比較優勢，並會出口該物品，進口另一物品。
A country has a comparative advantage if it can produce a good at a lower opportunity cost. It will export this good and import the other.
3. 貿易獲利 (Gains from Trade)
- 出口獲利 (Gains from Exporting): 出口價格 (貿易比率) 減去 國內生產成本。
- 進口獲利 (Gains from Importing): 國內生產成本 減去 進口價格 (貿易比率)。
- Gains per unit of export: Terms of Trade (Export Price) - Domestic Opportunity Cost.
- Gains per unit of import: Domestic Opportunity Cost - Terms of Trade (Import Price).

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是 (Correct Option): C
The correct answer is Option C. Let's break it down step-by-step.
步驟 1：計算兩國的機會成本 (Step 1: Calculate Opportunity Costs)
Country Cost of 1 Toy (1T) Cost of 1 Cloth (1C)
甲國 (A) \( \frac{10}{10} = 1C \) \( \frac{10}{10} = 1T \)
乙國 (B) \( \frac{10}{4} = 2.5C \) \( \frac{4}{10} = 0.4T \)
步驟 2：決定進出口方向 (Step 2: Determine Specialization)
甲國生產玩具的成本較低 (\( 1C < 2.5C \))，所以甲國出口玩具，進口成衣。
乙國生產成衣的成本較低 (\( 0.4T < 1T \))，所以乙國出口成衣，進口玩具。
Country A has a lower cost for Toys (\( 1C < 2.5C \)), so A exports Toys, imports Clothes.
Country B has a lower cost for Clothes (\( 0.4T < 1T \)), so B exports Clothes, imports Toys.

選項分析 (Option Analysis):

貿易比率 (Terms of Trade, TOT): \( 1T = 2C \) （或 \( 1C = 0.5T \)）
A. 錯誤 (Incorrect)
甲國進口成衣。甲國自己生產1單位成衣的成本是 \( 1T \)。以貿易比率進口1單位成衣的代價是 \( 0.5T \)。
每單位進口獲利 = 國內成本 - 進口價格 = \( 1T - 0.5T = 0.5T \)。(非 5T)
Country A imports clothes. Domestic cost of 1C = \( 1T \). Import price = \( 0.5T \). Gain per import = \( 1T - 0.5T = 0.5T \). (Not 5T).
B. 錯誤 (Incorrect)
甲國出口玩具。甲國生產1單位玩具的國內成本是 \( 1C \)。出口1單位玩具可換取 \( 2C \)。
每單位出口獲利 = 出口價格 - 國內成本 = \( 2C - 1C = 1C \)。(非 0.6C)
Country A exports toys. Domestic cost of 1T = \( 1C \). Export price = \( 2C \). Gain per export = \( 2C - 1C = 1C \). (Not 0.6C).
C. 正確 (Correct)
乙國進口玩具。乙國自己生產1單位玩具的成本是 \( 2.5C \)。以貿易比率進口1單位玩具的代價是 \( 2C \)。
每單位進口獲利 = 國內成本 - 進口價格 = \( 2.5C - 2C = 0.5C \)。
Country B imports toys. Domestic cost of 1T = \( 2.5C \). Import price = \( 2C \). Gain per import = \( 2.5C - 2C = 0.5C \). This matches the option.
D. 錯誤 (Incorrect)
乙國沒有生產玩具的比較優勢，所以乙國會進口玩具，而非出口。單憑這點已可剔除此選項。
Country B does not have a comparative advantage in toys, so B will import toys, not export them. This option is fundamentally flawed.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point):
這類題目的核心在於準確分辨產量 (Output) 還是 投入 (Input) 數據。表格頂部寫著「一單位的資源可生產...數量」，這明確指出了這是「產量」問題，因此計算機會成本的公式必須是「放棄量 / 獲得量」。
The Pivot Point:
The core of this question type is accurately distinguishing between Output and Input data. The table states "number... produced with one unit of resources", clearly indicating an Output problem. Hence, the formula must be "Foregone / Gained".
干擾資訊 (Distractors):
選項中特意混合了「進口」與「出口」字眼。同學必須先非常確定兩國的貿易方向，否則會將進口獲利與出口獲利公式調轉。
Distractors:
The options deliberately mix "imports" and "exports". Students must be 100% certain of the trade direction first, otherwise they will swap the formulas for import gains and export gains.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 陷阱一：誤當「投入」計算 (Confusing Output with Input)
    若學生誤以為數字是「生產1單位所需的時間」(投入)，計算出來的機會成本會完全顛倒，導致選錯貿易方向。
  • 陷阱二：搞錯獲利單位 (Confusing Gain Units)
    計算進口/出口獲利時，單位必須是另一種物品。例如，出口玩具的獲利單位必然是「成衣」。若選項寫「出口玩具獲利X單位玩具」，則必定是錯的。
  • Trap 1: Treating it as an Input problem. If students assume the numbers are "hours required to make 1 unit", the opportunity costs will flip, leading to the wrong trade directions.
  • Trap 2: Confusing the units of gain. Gains from exporting/importing a good are always measured in terms of the other good. E.g., the gain from exporting Toys must be measured in Clothes.

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼甲國選擇出口玩具而不是成衣？甲國生產兩者的數量都是10，不是一樣嗎？
Q: Why does Country A choose to export Toys instead of Clothes? Country A can produce 10 of both, aren't they the same?
A: 雖然甲國生產兩種物品的絕對能力一樣，但在國際貿易中，我們看的是比較優勢（機會成本）。甲國生產1玩具的成本是1成衣，而乙國是2.5成衣。因為 \( 1 < 2.5 \)，甲國的成本較低，因此甲國應該專注生產玩具。
A: While Country A has the same absolute capability for both, international trade relies on Comparative Advantage (Opportunity Cost). A's cost for 1 Toy is 1 Cloth, while B's is 2.5 Clothes. Since \( 1 < 2.5 \), A has a lower cost and should specialize in Toys.
Q: 考試時有什麼方法確保不會把「國內成本」和「貿易比率」減反？
Q: Is there a trick during exams to ensure I don't subtract "Domestic Cost" and "Terms of Trade" in the wrong order?
A: 記住一個原則：「貿易必定是有利的（正數）」。
你買東西（進口）當然想便宜，所以 進口獲利 = 本來貴的國內成本 - 便宜的進口價 (TOT)。
你賣東西（出口）當然想高價，所以 出口獲利 = 賣得高的出口價 (TOT) - 本來便宜的國內成本。
A: Remember this rule: "Trade must be beneficial (positive number)".
When buying (importing), you want it cheaper: Gain = Expensive Domestic Cost - Cheaper Import Price (TOT).
When selling (exporting), you want a higher price: Gain = Higher Export Price (TOT) - Cheaper Domestic Cost.

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧 (Elimination Method):
在計算出雙方的機會成本後，馬上寫下：「甲出口 T，乙出口 C」。
掃視選項，選項 D 說「乙國會出口玩具 (T)」。這與我們得出的結論直接矛盾。因此，在不進行任何數字運算的情況下，你可以立刻將 D 畫交叉淘汰，節省寶貴時間！
Shortcut:
Once you calculate the opportunity costs, immediately jot down: "A exports T, B exports C".
Scan the options. Option D says "Country B will export toys (T)". This directly contradicts our finding. Therefore, without even doing the math for D, you can cross it out immediately, saving valuable time!
