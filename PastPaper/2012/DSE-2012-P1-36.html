Topic: Macroeconomics - Aggregate Demand & Policies Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Aggregate Demand, Fiscal Policy, Monetary Policy

1. 題目 / The Question

下列哪項能抵銷入息稅稅率下降對總需求的影響？

Which of the following can offset the effect of a decrease in income tax rate on aggregate demand?

  • A. 資產貨幣需求下降
    A. a decrease in the asset demand for money
  • B. 進口意欲下降
    B. a decrease in the desire to import
  • C. 投資意欲上升
    C. an increase in the desire to invest
  • D. 法定儲備比率上升
    D. an increase in the required reserve ratio

2. 概念重溫 / Concept Review

要解答這道題目，我們需要理解總需求 (Aggregate Demand, AD) 的組成部分以及貨幣政策與財政政策的傳遞機制。

To solve this question, we need to understand the components of Aggregate Demand (AD) and the transmission mechanisms of monetary and fiscal policies.

$$ AD = C + I + G + (X - M) $$

重要定義：

  • \( C \) (Consumption / 私人消費開支): 受到可支配入息 (Disposable Income) 及利率的影響。
  • \( I \) (Investment / 投資開支): 受到利率及投資預期的影響。
  • \( (X - M) \) (Net Exports / 淨出口): 出口減去進口。

Key Definitions:

  • \( C \) (Consumption): Affected by disposable income and interest rates.
  • \( I \) (Investment): Affected by interest rates and business expectations.
  • \( (X - M) \) (Net Exports): Exports minus imports.

貨幣傳遞機制 (Monetary Transmission Mechanism):

$$ M_s \downarrow \text{ (or } M_d \uparrow \text{)} \rightarrow r \uparrow \rightarrow C \downarrow, I \downarrow \rightarrow AD \downarrow $$

Monetary Transmission Mechanism: An decrease in money supply \( M_s \) (or increase in money demand \( M_d \)) leads to an increase in interest rate \( r \), which causes consumption \( C \) and investment \( I \) to decrease, ultimately decreasing \( AD \).

3. 答案與詳細分析 / The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案是：選項 D

The correct option is: Option D

選項 A / Option A (Distractor)

資產貨幣需求下降： 貨幣需求 \( M_d \) 下降會導致利率 \( r \) 下降。較低的利率會刺激私人消費 \( C \) 和投資 \( I \) 增加，從而導致總需求 \( AD \) 上升。這會加劇（而非抵銷）入息稅下降帶來的影響。

A decrease in the asset demand for money: A decrease in money demand \( M_d \) leads to a lower interest rate \( r \). A lower interest rate stimulates consumption \( C \) and investment \( I \), leading to an increase in \( AD \). This reinforces (rather than offsets) the effect of the tax cut.

選項 B / Option B (Distractor)

進口意欲下降： 進口 \( M \) 減少會導致淨出口 \( (X - M) \) 增加，從而令總需求 \( AD \) 上升。這同樣會加劇原本總需求上升的影響。

A decrease in the desire to import: A decrease in imports \( M \) leads to an increase in net exports \( (X - M) \), which increases \( AD \). This also reinforces the initial increase in AD.

選項 C / Option C (Distractor)

投資意欲上升： 投資 \( I \) 是總需求的組成部分。投資上升會直接導致總需求 \( AD \) 上升。這會加劇入息稅下降帶來的影響。

An increase in the desire to invest: Investment \( I \) is a component of AD. An increase in investment directly increases \( AD \). This reinforces the effect of the tax cut.

選項 D / Option D (Correct Answer)

法定儲備比率上升： 這是緊縮性貨幣政策。法定儲備比率上升會降低銀行的貨幣創造能力，導致貨幣供應 \( M_s \) 下降。貨幣供應下降會推高利率 \( r \)。較高的利率會壓抑私人消費 \( C \) 和投資 \( I \)，最終導致總需求 \( AD \) 下降。這正好能抵銷入息稅下降導致總需求上升的影響。

An increase in the required reserve ratio: This is a contractionary monetary policy. An increase in the required reserve ratio reduces banks' ability to create credit, decreasing the money supply \( M_s \). A lower money supply pushes up the interest rate \( r \). Higher interest rates depress consumption \( C \) and investment \( I \), ultimately leading to a decrease in \( AD \). This directly offsets the increase in AD caused by the income tax cut.

4. 題目拆解 / Problem Decomposition

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point): 題目的核心詞是「抵銷 (offset)」。

  1. 第一步：確定初始事件的影響。 入息稅稅率下降 \(\rightarrow\) 市民可支配入息增加 \(\rightarrow\) 消費 \( C \) 上升 \(\rightarrow\) 總需求 \( AD \) 上升。
  2. 第二步：理解「抵銷」的含義。 要抵銷「\( AD \) 上升」，我們需要找到一個會令 \( AD \) 下降 的選項。
  3. 第三步：篩選選項。 選項 A、B 和 C 都會導致 \( AD \) 進一步上升（屬於擴張性因素）。只有選項 D（緊縮性貨幣政策）會導致 \( AD \) 下降。

The Pivot Point: The core word in the question is "offset".

  1. Step 1: Determine the effect of the initial event. Decrease in income tax rate \(\rightarrow\) Higher disposable income \(\rightarrow\) Higher consumption \( C \) \(\rightarrow\) \( AD \) increases.
  2. Step 2: Understand "offset". To offset an "increase in AD", we must find an option that causes \( AD \) to decrease.
  3. Step 3: Filter the options. Options A, B, and C all lead to a further increase in \( AD \) (expansionary factors). Only Option D (a contractionary monetary policy) leads to a decrease in \( AD \).

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 / Common Mistakes & Traps

⚠️ 混淆貨幣需求與貨幣供應 (Confusing Money Demand and Money Supply):
很多學生在看到「選項A: 資產貨幣需求下降」時，會誤以為這等同於市面上的「錢少了」，從而推論利率會上升。事實上，在貨幣供應不變的情況下，貨幣需求下降代表人們想持有較少現金（轉而購買債券），這會推高債券價格，導致利率下跌，從而刺激經濟。

⚠️ Confusing Money Demand and Money Supply:
When seeing "Option A: decrease in money demand", many students mistakenly think this means "less money in the market" and deduce that interest rates will rise. In reality, assuming money supply is constant, a decrease in money demand means people want to hold less cash (they buy bonds instead). This pushes up bond prices, leading to a fall in interest rates, which stimulates the economy.

6. 學生常見疑問 / Student Q&A

Q: 為什麼入息稅下降只影響總需求 (AD)，而不影響總供應 (AS)？

A: 在 DSE 課程中，入息稅 (Income tax) 主要影響消費者的可支配入息，因此直接影響消費 \( C \) 和 \( AD \)。雖然長遠來說可能會增加工作意欲（影響 LRAS），但在標準的宏觀經濟分析中，其對 \( AD \) 的即時影響最為顯著。利得稅 (Profits tax) 則較常被視為會同時影響 \( AD \) (透過投資) 和 \( SRAS \) (透過生產成本)。

Q: Why does a decrease in income tax primarily affect Aggregate Demand (AD) and not Aggregate Supply (AS)?

A: In the DSE syllabus, income tax primarily affects consumers' disposable income, thus directly impacting consumption \( C \) and \( AD \). While in the long run it might increase the incentive to work (affecting LRAS), its immediate and most prominent effect in standard macroeconomic analysis is on \( AD \). Profits tax, on the other hand, is more often seen as affecting both \( AD \) (via investment) and \( SRAS \) (via production costs).

7. 考試技巧與速解 / Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

⚡ 方向性秒殺法 (Directional Elimination):

在處理這類「政策抵銷」題目時，你不需要逐步畫圖。只需判斷原政策的「方向」是擴張性 (Expansionary) 還是緊縮性 (Contractionary)。
入息稅下降 = 擴張性（刺激經濟）。
要抵銷它，你只需要在選項中找出一個 緊縮性（冷卻經濟）的因素。瀏覽四個選項，A、B、C 都在刺激經濟，唯獨 D (提高法定儲備) 是標準的緊縮性貨幣政策。秒殺！

⚡ Directional Elimination:

When dealing with "policy offset" questions, you don't need to draw graphs step-by-step. Just determine if the original policy's "direction" is Expansionary or Contractionary.
Income tax cut = Expansionary (stimulates economy).
To offset it, you simply need to find a Contractionary (cooling economy) factor among the options. Scanning the options, A, B, and C all stimulate the economy. Only D (raising required reserves) is a standard contractionary monetary policy. Instant solve!
