2. The Question (題目)

網上購物日漸普及，人們因而持有較少現金作交易用途。中央銀行可採用下列哪些措施以減低上述情況對利率的影響？

(1) 提高貼現率
(2) 提高法定儲備比率
(3) 在公開市場購入政府債券

A. 只有(1)、(2)
B. 只有(1)、(3)
C. 只有(2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2)及(3)
On-line shopping has become more popular, and hence people hold less cash for transaction purposes. Which of the following measures can be adopted by a central bank to reduce the effect of the above situation on the interest rate?

(1) an increase in discount rate
(2) an increase in required reserve ratio
(3) an open market purchase of government bonds

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

貨幣需求 (Money Demand)
總貨幣需求 \(M_d\) 由交易性貨幣需求 (\(M_t\)) 和資產性貨幣需求 (\(M_a\)) 組成。當人們減少持有現金作交易用途，這代表交易性貨幣需求下降，導致總貨幣需求曲線向左移。
Total money demand \(M_d\) consists of transaction demand (\(M_t\)) and asset demand (\(M_a\)). When people hold less cash for transactions, transaction demand decreases, shifting the total money demand curve to the left.
$$ M_d = M_t + M_a $$
貨幣市場均衡與利率 (Money Market Equilibrium and Interest Rate)
在貨幣供應 \(M_s\) 不變的情況下，貨幣需求 \(M_d\) 下降會導致名義利率 \(r\) 下跌。為了「減低對利率的影響」（即阻止利率下跌或將其拉回原位），中央銀行必須實施緊縮性貨幣政策 (Contractionary Monetary Policy) 來減少貨幣供應 \(M_s\)。
Assuming money supply \(M_s\) is constant, a decrease in money demand \(M_d\) leads to a fall in the nominal interest rate \(r\). To "reduce the effect on the interest rate" (i.e., to prevent it from falling or push it back up), the central bank must implement Contractionary Monetary Policy to decrease the money supply \(M_s\).

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A

初始情況分析 (Initial Situation Analysis):
網購普及導致貨幣需求 \(M_d\) 下降，利率 \(r\) 隨之下跌。央行的目標是提高利率 \(r\)，因此需要減少貨幣供應 \(M_s\)。
The popularity of online shopping decreases money demand \(M_d\), causing the interest rate \(r\) to fall. The central bank's goal is to raise the interest rate \(r\), so it needs to decrease the money supply \(M_s\).
(1) 提高貼現率 (Increase in discount rate) - 正確 (Correct)
提高貼現率會增加商業銀行向央行借款的成本。銀行會減少借款，導致銀行體系儲備減少，貨幣供應 \(M_s\) 下降，從而推高利率。這能抵銷初始的利率下跌。
Increasing the discount rate raises the cost for commercial banks to borrow from the central bank. Banks will borrow less, reducing reserves in the banking system. This decreases the money supply \(M_s\) and pushes the interest rate up, offsetting the initial fall.
(2) 提高法定儲備比率 (Increase in required reserve ratio) - 正確 (Correct)
提高法定儲備比率意味著銀行必須保留更大比例的存款作為儲備，這會降低貨幣乘數 (Money Multiplier)。貨幣供應 \(M_s\) 因而收縮，導致利率上升。
An increase in the required reserve ratio means banks must hold a larger fraction of deposits as reserves, which lowers the money multiplier. The money supply \(M_s\) contracts, leading to an increase in the interest rate.
(3) 在公開市場購入政府債券 (Open market purchase of government bonds) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
當央行購入債券時，會將資金注入銀行體系，增加貨幣供應 \(M_s\)。這是一項擴張性貨幣政策，會導致利率進一步下跌，加劇了原有的問題，而不是減低影響。
When the central bank purchases bonds, it injects money into the banking system, increasing the money supply \(M_s\). This is an expansionary monetary policy that would cause the interest rate to fall further, exacerbating the original problem instead of reducing its effect.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point):
這題的關鍵在於理解「減低上述情況對利率的影響」這句話。很多學生會直接看政策，卻忘記先判斷「上述情況」到底造成了什麼影響。
The key to this question lies in understanding the phrase "reduce the effect of the above situation on the interest rate". Many students jump straight to the policies without first determining what effect the "above situation" actually caused.
  • Step 1: 找出初始影響 (Find initial effect) -> \(M_d\) 下降 -> 利率 \(r\) 下跌。
  • Step 2: 確立目標 (Establish goal) -> 央行要「減低影響」，即是要把跌下去的利率拉高 (Raise \(r\))。
  • Step 3: 選擇政策 (Select policy) -> 甚麼政策能提高利率？答案是減少貨幣供應 (Decrease \(M_s\)) 的緊縮性政策。

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱一：誤解「減低影響」的意思
學生常以為貨幣需求減少了，央行就應該減少貨幣供應來「配合」需求。雖然結論剛好也是減少貨幣供應，但邏輯是錯的。央行的目標是穩定利率，而不是讓供應等於需求。
Trap 1: Misinterpreting "reduce the effect"
Students often think that if money demand decreases, the central bank should decrease money supply to "match" the demand. While the conclusion (decreasing \(M_s\)) happens to be the same, the logic is flawed. The central bank's goal here is to stabilize the interest rate.
陷阱二：混淆公開市場操作的方向
記錯「購入 (Purchase)」和「出售 (Sale)」債券的效果。購入債券 = 央行給錢市場 = 增加貨幣供應。
Trap 2: Confusing the direction of Open Market Operations
Forgetting the difference between "purchase" and "sale" of bonds. Purchase of bonds = Central bank gives money to the market = Increase in money supply.

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼網上購物普及會令交易性貨幣需求下降？
A: 網上購物通常使用信用卡、電子錢包或銀行轉賬，這些屬於廣義貨幣 (如 M2/M3) 或信用額度，減少了人們口袋裡需要持有的實體「現金」(M1 中的公眾持有的法定貨幣) 來進行日常交易。
Q: Why does the popularity of online shopping decrease transaction demand for money?
A: Online shopping typically uses credit cards, e-wallets, or bank transfers. This reduces the need for people to hold physical "cash" in their pockets for daily transactions.

箭頭推導法 (Arrow Method):

事件：\(M_d \downarrow \implies r \downarrow\)
目標：抵銷影響，所以要 \(r \uparrow\)
方法：\(M_s \downarrow\) (尋找緊縮性政策)

(2) 緊縮性 (\(\checkmark\))
(3) 擴張性 (\(\times\))
