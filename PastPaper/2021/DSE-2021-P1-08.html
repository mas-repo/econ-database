🧠 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解答這道題目，我們需要了解廠商擴張的不同類型及其動機。這次收購屬於橫向擴張 (Horizontal Expansion / Integration)。

To solve this question, we need to understand the different types of firm expansion and their motives. This acquisition is an example of Horizontal Expansion / Integration.

1. 橫向擴張 (Horizontal Integration)

定義：兩家或以上生產同行業、同生產階段產品的廠商合併。例如：兩家外賣平台合併。

Definition: The combination of two or more firms engaged in the same industry and at the same stage of production. Example: Two food delivery platforms merging.

主要動機 (Main Motives):

  • 享有規模經濟 (Enjoy economies of scale): 擴大生產規模後，平均成本 \(AC\) 下降。
  • 增加市場佔有率 (Increase market share): 減少競爭對手，從而增加市場力量 (Market power) 和對價格的影響力。
  • 更有效利用資源 (Better utilization of resources): 例如共享外賣車隊或應用程式技術。

2. 跨業擴張 (Conglomerate Integration)

定義：兩家或以上生產完全無關產品的廠商合併。例如：外賣平台收購一家服裝製造廠。

Definition: The combination of two or more firms engaged in producing unrelated products. Example: A food delivery platform acquiring a clothing manufacturer.

主要動機：使業務多元化以分散風險 (Spread risk by business diversification)。

✅ 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是：Option C

分析 (1)：可享有規模經濟 (to enjoy economies of scale) —— 正確 (Correct)

「餓了麼」和「百度外賣」都是網上食品外送平台。合併後，它們的整體營運規模擴大，可以更有效地分攤固定成本（如應用程式開發、伺服器維護等），從而降低平均成本。

"Ele.me" and "Baidu Waimai" are both online food delivery platforms. After the merger, their overall scale of operation expands, allowing them to spread fixed costs (like app development and server maintenance) more effectively, thereby lowering average costs.

分析 (2)：使業務多元化以分散風險 (to spread risk by business diversification) —— 錯誤 (Incorrect)

兩家公司都在同一個行業（網上食品外送）。如果該行業受到打擊（例如政府加強對外賣行業的監管），合併後的公司依然會承受巨大的風險。分散風險是跨業擴張 (Conglomerate integration) 的動機，而不是橫向擴張的動機。

Both companies are in the same industry (online food delivery). If this industry takes a hit (e.g., stricter government regulations on food delivery), the merged company will still bear significant risk. Spreading risk is a motive for conglomerate integration, not horizontal integration.

分析 (3)：可對市場價格有更大影響力 (to have a greater influence on the market price) —— 正確 (Correct)

根據圖表，「餓了麼」原本的市場佔有率是 36.5%，「百度外賣」是 17.3%。收購後，新公司的市場佔有率將超過 50% (\(36.5\% + 17.3\% = 53.8\%\))。市場競爭減少，廠商的壟斷力量 (Monopoly power) 增加，因此對市場價格（如外賣運費、餐廳抽成）有更大的控制權。

According to the chart, Ele.me's original market share was 36.5% and Baidu Waimai's was 17.3%. After the acquisition, the new firm's market share will exceed 50% (\(36.5\% + 17.3\% = 53.8\%\)). With reduced market competition, the firm's monopoly power increases, giving it greater control over market prices (such as delivery fees or commission rates from restaurants).

🛠️ 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解答這類題目的關鍵步驟：

  1. 辨識擴張類型 (Identify the type of integration): 看到兩家公司名稱，第一時間問自己：「它們是做什麼的？」兩者都是「外賣」，所以是同行業同階段 ＝ 橫向擴張。
  2. 配對動機 (Match the motives): 腦海中立刻調出橫向擴張的特點（規模經濟、減少競爭、增加市佔率）。
  3. 排除錯誤概念 (Eliminate wrong concepts): 看到「分散風險 (spread risk)」，立刻聯想到這必須是不同行業（跨業擴張）才能做到，因此果斷排除包含此項的選項。
Key steps to solve this type of question:
  1. Identify the type of integration: When seeing the two company names, ask yourself: "What do they do?" Both are in "food delivery", so they are in the same industry and stage = Horizontal Integration.
  2. Match the motives: Recall the characteristics of horizontal integration (economies of scale, reducing competition, increasing market share).
  3. Eliminate wrong concepts: When you see "spread risk", immediately associate it with different industries (conglomerate integration). Therefore, decisively eliminate any options containing this statement.

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

消去法 (Elimination Method):
在 DSE 經濟科中，「分散風險 (Spread risk)」幾乎總是與「跨業擴張 (Conglomerate integration)」綁定。只要你判斷出這兩家公司是同行業（橫向擴張），就可以立刻判定 (2) 是錯的。
排除了 (2) 之後，選項 B 和 D 就被淘汰了。剩下 A 和 C。由於橫向合併明顯會減少競爭對手，增加定價能力，所以 (3) 必然正確。答案秒選 C！

Elimination Method:
In DSE Economics, "Spread risk" is almost always tied to "Conglomerate integration". As soon as you determine that these two companies are in the same industry (horizontal integration), you can immediately conclude that (2) is wrong.
After eliminating (2), Options B and D are out. You are left with A and C. Since a horizontal merger obviously reduces competitors and increases pricing power, (3) must be correct. You can instantly choose C!
