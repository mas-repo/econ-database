2. The Question (題目)

利豐（股票編號：00494）公布將公司私有化，取消在本港股票交易所的上市地位。在該轉變後，下列哪項有關利豐的陳述是正確的？

Li & Fung (Stock Code: 00494) announced a privatisation of its company, cancelling its listing status at the stock exchange in Hong Kong. Which of the following statements about Li & Fung is correct after the change?

A. 該公司能享有較低利得稅稅率。

A. The firm can enjoy a lower profits tax rate.

B. 該公司仍然能發行股票籌集資金。

B. The firm can still issue shares to raise capital.

C. 東主須在該公司面臨法律訴訟時負上個人責任。

C. The owners will become personally liable for legal charges against the firm.

D. 當其中一名東主破產，該公司不會繼續存在。

D. The firm will no longer exist when one of the owners goes bankrupt.

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

公眾有限公司、私人有限公司與上市地位 (Public vs Private Limited Company & Listing Status)

這是一個非常容易混淆的進階概念。當一間上市公司「私有化」(Privatisation) 時，最直接的改變是取消上市地位（除牌），成為一間非上市公司 (Unlisted Company)。

This is a commonly confused advanced concept. When a listed company undergoes "privatisation," the most direct change is the cancellation of its listing status (delisting), making it an Unlisted Company.

除牌後，它不一定會變成私人有限公司。它可以維持為「非上市的公眾有限公司」(Unlisted Public Limited Company)，或者重組轉變為「私人有限公司」(Private Limited Company)。無論是哪種情況，它都不能再透過股票交易所向普羅大眾公開買賣股票，也絕對不會變成獨資經營或合夥。

After delisting, it does not necessarily become a private limited company. It can remain an Unlisted Public Limited Company, or restructure into a Private Limited Company. In either case, it can no longer publicly trade shares through the stock exchange, and it absolutely will not become a sole proprietorship or a partnership.

關鍵定義 (Key Definitions):

  • 公眾有限公司 (Public Limited Company): 股東人數沒有上限。可以（但不一定）在交易所上市。
    No maximum limit on the number of shareholders. It can (but does not have to) be listed on a stock exchange.
  • 私人有限公司 (Private Limited Company): 不能向公眾發行股票，股份轉讓受限制。股東人數限制在 $1 \le n \le 50$ 之間（$n$ 為股東人數）。
    Cannot issue shares to the general public; transfer of shares is restricted. The number of shareholders $n$ is restricted to $1 \le n \le 50$.
  • 獨立法定地位 (Separate Legal Entity): 公司與東主在法律上是獨立的個體。公司可以擁有資產和被告。這帶來了「延續性」(Continuity)。
    The company is a legal entity separate from its owners. It can own assets and be sued. This grants the firm continuity of existence.

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Answer: Option B (正確答案：B)

選項 A：錯誤 (False)

香港的利得稅稅率並不取決於公司是否上市，也不取決於是私人還是公眾有限公司。皆適用法團利得稅率。

Profits tax rates in Hong Kong do not depend on whether a company is listed, nor whether it is public or private. All apply the corporate profits tax rate.

選項 B：正確 (True)

私有化除牌後，無論公司是維持「非上市公眾有限公司」還是轉為「私人有限公司」，它仍然可以透過發行股票（如私人配售、發行給特定機構投資者或現有股東）來籌集資金，只是不能再透過交易所向「公眾」公開招股。

After privatisation and delisting, regardless of whether the firm remains an "unlisted public limited company" or converts to a "private limited company," it can still issue shares (e.g., through private placements, to specific institutional investors or existing shareholders) to raise capital. It simply cannot offer shares to the "general public" via the stock exchange anymore.

選項 C：錯誤 (False)

所有有限公司（無論上市、非上市、公眾或私人）的股東皆享有「有限債務責任」。如公司面臨訴訟，東主不需要負上個人責任。

Shareholders of all limited companies (listed, unlisted, public, or private) enjoy "limited liability." If the firm faces legal charges, owners are not personally liable.

選項 D：錯誤 (False)

有限公司擁有「獨立法定地位」。即使其中一名東主破產或死亡，公司的存在不會受到影響，享有延續性。

Limited companies possess a "separate legal entity." Even if one owner goes bankrupt or dies, the firm's existence is unaffected, granting it continuity.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

  • Pivot Point (解題關鍵): 釐清「私有化」(Privatisation) 的核心是「取消上市地位」。學生必須知道，取消上市地位並不等於喪失發行股票的能力。只要是一間「有限公司」，就有股本和股票，只是集資的對象和方式受到限制。
    Clarify that the core of "privatisation" is "cancelling the listing status." Students must know that delisting does not mean losing the ability to issue shares. As long as it is a "limited company," it has share capital and shares; only the target audience and methods of raising capital are restricted.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

🚫 陷阱一：以為「公眾有限公司」等同於「上市公司」。
上市公司必定是公眾有限公司，但公眾有限公司不一定有上市！私有化除牌後，公司可能轉為私人有限公司，但也可能維持為「非上市的公眾有限公司」。
Trap 1: Equating "Public Limited Company" with "Listed Company". A listed company must be a public limited company, but a public limited company is not necessarily listed! After delisting, the firm might convert to a private limited company, but it could also remain an "unlisted public limited company."

🚫 陷阱二：以為非上市公司不能發行股票。
學生常把「不能在交易所公開集資」等同於「完全不能發行股票」。無論是非上市公眾公司還是私人有限公司，都能以非公開的方式發行新股籌資。
Trap 2: Thinking unlisted companies cannot issue shares. Students often equate "cannot raise funds publicly on the exchange" with "cannot issue shares at all." Both unlisted public and private limited companies can issue new shares to raise capital privately.

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果不能在股票市場賣股票，除牌後的公司怎麼發行股票籌錢？
Q: If they can't sell shares on the stock market, how does a delisted company issue shares to raise money?

A: 他們可以透過私人配售 (Private Placement)。管理層可以直接尋找機構投資者、私募股權基金或現有的大股東，與他們簽訂合約並發行新股給他們。這個過程不需要透過公眾交易所。
A: They can use Private Placement. Management can approach institutional investors, private equity funds, or existing major shareholders directly, sign contracts, and issue new shares to them. This process does not require a public exchange.

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

The Elimination Method (消去法):

當你看到選項 C（無限債務責任）和選項 D（缺乏延續性）時，你應該立即察覺這是「獨資經營 / 合夥」的特徵。由於利豐是一間具規模的有限公司，它退市後依然是有限公司（享有獨立法定地位與有限債務責任）。因此，C 和 D 可以瞬間被刪除。接著比較 A 和 B，稅率不會因為上市與否而改變，故得出答案 B。

When you see options C (unlimited liability) and D (lack of continuity), you should immediately recognize these as features of a "Sole Proprietorship / Partnership." Since Li & Fung is a large limited company, it remains a limited company after delisting (enjoying separate legal entity and limited liability). Thus, C and D can be instantly eliminated. Comparing A and B, tax rates do not change based on listing status, leading you directly to B.
