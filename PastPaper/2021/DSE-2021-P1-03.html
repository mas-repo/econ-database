Topic: Opportunity Cost (機會成本) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Opportunity Cost, Explicit Cost, Implicit Cost

2. The Question (題目)

中學畢業後，保羅考慮成為全職的社交媒體頻道關鍵意見領袖KOL（俗稱網紅）或成為電競選手。下列哪項會減低保羅畢業後成為全職KOL的成本？

(1) 保羅獲得錄製KOL影片器材的贊助。
(2) 電競隊給予保羅較低的基本薪金。
(3) 社交媒體頻道被駭客攻擊的風險增加。

A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

After graduating from secondary school, Paul considers whether to become a full-time Key Opinion Leader (KOL) in social media channels or to become an e-sports player. Which of the following will lower Paul’s cost of being a full-time KOL after his graduation?

(1) Paul receives sponsored video equipment for KOL.
(2) The e-sports team offers a lower basic salary to Paul.
(3) The risk of social media channels being attacked by hackers increases.

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

機會成本 (Opportunity Cost)：是指在作出選擇時，所放棄的選項中價值最高的那一個。選擇的總成本包括顯性成本（直接金錢支出）和隱性成本（放棄的最高價值選項的收入）。

Opportunity Cost: The highest-valued option forgone when making a choice. The total cost of a choice includes explicit costs (direct monetary expenses) and implicit costs (the income from the highest-valued forgone option).

$$ \text{Cost of Option A} = \text{Explicit Cost of A} + \text{Value of Option B (Implicit Cost)} $$

因此，要降低選擇 A 的成本，必須是 A 的直接支出減少，或者是放棄的選項 B 的價值下降。

Therefore, to lower the cost of choosing Option A, either the direct expenses of A must decrease, or the value of the forgone Option B must decrease.

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A

分析 (1)：正確。獲得器材贊助減少了保羅成為 KOL 的直接金錢支出（顯性成本）。因此，成為 KOL 的總機會成本下降。

Analysis (1): Correct. Receiving sponsored equipment reduces Paul's direct monetary expenses (explicit cost) of becoming a KOL. Therefore, the total opportunity cost of being a KOL decreases.

分析 (2)：正確。電競隊給予較低的薪金，意味著保羅放棄的選項（成為電競選手）的價值下降了。由於放棄的代價減少（隱性成本下降），成為 KOL 的機會成本也會隨之降低。

Analysis (2): Correct. A lower salary from the e-sports team means the value of the forgone option (being an e-sports player) has decreased. Since the sacrifice is smaller (implicit cost decreases), the opportunity cost of being a KOL decreases.

分析 (3)：錯誤。駭客攻擊風險增加會降低成為 KOL 的「預期回報/利益 (Expected Benefit)」，但這並不會改變他成為 KOL 所需要付出的「成本 (Cost)」。利益下降不等於成本下降。

Analysis (3): Incorrect. An increased risk of hacker attacks lowers the "expected benefit" of being a KOL, but it does not change the "cost" he has to pay to become one. A decrease in benefit is not a decrease in cost.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的關鍵樞紐 (Pivot Point) 在於區分「成本 (Cost)」與「利益 (Benefit)」。

  • 影響成本的因素：直接支出（買器材的時間/金錢）、放棄選項的回報（電競選手的薪水）。
  • 影響利益的因素：該選項本身的收入、風險、滿足感（KOL的廣告收入、被駭客攻擊的風險）。

The Pivot Point of this type of question is distinguishing between "Cost" and "Benefit". Factors affecting cost include direct expenses and the return of the forgone option. Factors affecting benefit include the income, risks, and satisfaction of the chosen option itself.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼選項 (3) 風險增加不會增加成本？

A: 在經濟學中，我們必須嚴格區分「成本」和「價值/利益」。風險增加會讓 KOL 這個職業變得不那麼吸引人，這意味著它的「價值 \(V\)」下降了。但是，你為了成為 KOL 所需要放棄的東西（買器材的錢、電競選手的薪水）並沒有因為駭客變多而改變。所以成本 \(C\) 不變，只是淨利益 \(V - C\) 減少了。

Q: Why doesn't the increased risk in option (3) increase the cost?

A: In economics, we strictly separate "cost" from "value/benefit". Increased risk makes the KOL profession less attractive, meaning its "value \(V\)" drops. However, what you give up to become a KOL (money for equipment, e-sports salary) doesn't change just because there are more hackers. So the cost \(C\) remains unchanged; only the net benefit \(V - C\) decreases.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法：一看到問「選擇 X 的成本」，立刻在草稿紙寫下公式：
Cost(X) = Direct Cost of X + Income of Y
然後逐一檢查選項是改變了等式右邊的哪一項。如果選項改變的是 X 本身的收入或風險，直接剔除（那是 Benefit，不是 Cost）。

Shortcut: Whenever asked about the "Cost of choosing X", immediately write down the formula on your scratch paper: Cost(X) = Direct Cost of X + Income of Y. Then check which part of the right side is affected by the statements. If a statement changes the income or risk of X itself, eliminate it immediately (that's Benefit, not Cost).
