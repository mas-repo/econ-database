Topic (課題): Stabilization Policy (穩定政策)
Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)
Key Concepts (核心概念): Deflationary Gap (通縮差距) Budget Deficit (預算赤字) Automatic Stabilizers (自動穩定機制)

The Question (題目)

下列哪項政策可以收窄通縮（產出）差距，並同時減少預算赤字？

A. 在公開市場購入政府債券
B. 提高入息稅的免稅額
C. 減少轉移支付
D. 提高法定儲備比率

Which of the following policies may narrow a deflationary (output) gap and reduce the budget deficit at the same time?

A. an open market purchase of government bonds
B. an increase in income tax allowance
C. a reduction in transfer payment
D. an increase in required reserve ratio

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 通縮差距 (Deflationary Gap):
當實際產出低於潛在產出 \( (Y < Y_f) \) 時出現。要消除此差距，我們需要擴張性政策 (Expansionary Policy) 來增加總需求 (AD)。 Occurs when real output is less than potential output \( (Y < Y_f) \). To eliminate this, we need Expansionary Policy to increase Aggregate Demand (AD).
2. 預算赤字 (Budget Deficit):
當政府支出大於政府收入 \( (G > T) \)。要減少赤字，政府需要增加稅收 \( (T) \) 或減少支出 \( (G) \)。 Occurs when Government Expenditure exceeds Government Revenue \( (G > T) \). To reduce the deficit, the government needs to increase Tax Revenue \( (T) \) or decrease Expenditure \( (G) \).
3. 關鍵聯繫：稅收與收入 (The Key Link: Tax and Income):
稅收 \( T \) 通常取決於國民收入 \( Y \)。這是一個內生變數。 $$ T = t \cdot Y $$ 如果收入 \( Y \) 上升，即使稅率 \( t \) 不變，稅收收入 \( T \) 也會上升。 Tax revenue \( T \) usually depends on National Income \( Y \). It is an endogenous variable. If Income \( Y \) rises, Tax Revenue \( T \) rises, even if the tax rate \( t \) remains constant.

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確選項): A

A. 在公開市場購入政府債券 (An open market purchase of government bonds)

這是擴張性貨幣政策。
  • 對通縮差距： 中央銀行買債 \(\rightarrow\) 貨幣供應 \( (M_s) \uparrow \) \(\rightarrow\) 利率 \( (r) \downarrow \) \(\rightarrow\) 投資/消費 \( (I/C) \uparrow \) \(\rightarrow\) 總需求 \( (AD) \uparrow \) \(\rightarrow\) 產出 \( (Y) \uparrow \)。通縮差距收窄（達成目標 1）。
  • 對預算赤字： 雖然這不是財政政策，但由於產出 \( (Y) \) 上升，且稅收與收入掛鉤，政府的稅收收入 \( (T) \) 會自動上升。在 \( G \) 不變的情況下，\( T \uparrow \) 會令赤字減少（達成目標 2）。
This is Expansionary Monetary Policy.
  • For Gap: Central bank buys bonds \(\rightarrow\) Money Supply \( (M_s) \uparrow \) \(\rightarrow\) Interest Rate \( (r) \downarrow \) \(\rightarrow\) Investment/Consumption \( (I/C) \uparrow \) \(\rightarrow\) AD \(\uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) Output \( (Y) \uparrow \). Gap narrows (Goal 1 met).
  • For Deficit: While not a fiscal policy, as Output \( (Y) \) rises, Tax Revenue \( (T) \) automatically rises (induced tax). With \( G \) constant, \( T \uparrow \) reduces the deficit (Goal 2 met).
B. 提高入息稅的免稅額 (An increase in income tax allowance)

這是擴張性財政政策（減稅）。
  • 對通縮差距： 減稅 \(\rightarrow\) 可支配收入 \(\uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) \( C \uparrow \) \(\rightarrow\) \( AD \uparrow \)。差距收窄（達成目標 1）。
  • 對預算赤字： 提高免稅額直接導致政府稅收 \( (T) \) 減少。雖然 \( Y \) 上升會帶回一點稅收，但通常直接的減稅效應大於誘導的稅收增加，導致赤字增加（目標 2 失敗）。
This is Expansionary Fiscal Policy (Tax Cut).
  • For Gap: Tax cut \(\rightarrow\) Disposable Income \(\uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) \( C \uparrow \) \(\rightarrow\) \( AD \uparrow \). Gap narrows (Goal 1 met).
  • For Deficit: Increasing allowance directly reduces Tax Revenue \( (T) \). The deficit increases (Goal 2 failed).
C. 減少轉移支付 (A reduction in transfer payment)

這是緊縮性財政政策。
  • 對通縮差距： 轉移支付 \(\downarrow\) \(\rightarrow\) 可支配收入 \(\downarrow\) \(\rightarrow\) \( C \downarrow \) \(\rightarrow\) \( AD \downarrow \)。通縮差距擴大（目標 1 失敗）。
  • 對預算赤字： 政府支出 \( (G) \) 減少，赤字改善（達成目標 2）。
This is Contractionary Fiscal Policy.
  • For Gap: Transfer payments \(\downarrow\) \(\rightarrow\) Disposable Income \(\downarrow\) \(\rightarrow\) \( C \downarrow \) \(\rightarrow\) \( AD \downarrow \). Deflationary gap widens (Goal 1 failed).
  • For Deficit: Government Expenditure \( (G) \) decreases, so deficit reduces (Goal 2 met).
D. 提高法定儲備比率 (An increase in required reserve ratio)

這是緊縮性貨幣政策。
  • 對通縮差距 (目標 1)： \( M_s \downarrow \) \(\rightarrow\) \( r \uparrow \) \(\rightarrow\) \( AD \downarrow \) \(\rightarrow\) 產出 \( (Y) \downarrow \)。通縮差距擴大（失敗）。
  • 對預算赤字 (目標 2)： 由於產出 \( (Y) \) 下降，政府的稅收收入 \( (T) \) 會隨之減少（因為 \( T \) 取決於 \( Y \)）。在政府支出 \( (G) \) 不變的情況下，稅收減少會導致赤字增加（失敗）。
This is Contractionary Monetary Policy.
  • For Gap (Goal 1): \( M_s \downarrow \) \(\rightarrow\) \( r \uparrow \) \(\rightarrow\) \( AD \downarrow \) \(\rightarrow\) Output \( (Y) \downarrow \). Deflationary gap widens (Failed).
  • For Deficit (Goal 2): As Output \( (Y) \) falls, Tax Revenue \( (T) \) automatically falls (since \( T \) depends on \( Y \)). With \( G \) constant, lower tax revenue leads to an increase in the deficit (Failed).

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

The Pivot Point (解題關鍵)

這道題目的難點在於「衝突」(Conflict)。

  • 一般來說，要解決通縮（需要擴張），通常會惡化赤字（因為要花錢或減稅）。
  • 要解決赤字（需要緊縮），通常會惡化通縮（因為要省錢或加稅）。

唯一的出路： 尋找一種政策，它能刺激經濟（增加 Y），但不涉及政府直接增加支出或減少稅率。這通常指向貨幣政策 (Monetary Policy)。

因為貨幣政策刺激了 \( Y \)，而 \( Y \) 的增加會通過自動穩定機制 (Automatic Stabilizer) 自動帶來更多的稅收收入。

The difficulty lies in the conflict. Usually, fixing deflation (expansion) worsens the deficit, and fixing the deficit (contraction) worsens deflation. The only solution is a policy that stimulates the economy (increases Y) without directly changing G or tax rates. This points to Monetary Policy. Higher Y leads to higher Tax Revenue via Automatic Stabilizers.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼選項 A 會減少赤字？政府並沒有加稅啊？
(Why does Option A reduce the deficit? The government didn't raise taxes?)

A: 這是因為稅收收入 \( (T) \) 取決於兩個因素：稅率 \( (t) \) 和 收入 \( (Y) \)。
公式：\( T = t \times Y \)。
雖然政府沒有改變稅率 \( (t) \)，但因為擴張性貨幣政策令經濟變好，市民收入 \( (Y) \) 增加了，所以他們繳納的總稅款 \( (T) \) 自然增加了。這是一個間接效應。

A: Tax revenue \( (T) \) depends on tax rate \( (t) \) and Income \( (Y) \). Formula: \( T = t \times Y \). Even though the tax rate didn't change, the expansionary monetary policy increased Income \( (Y) \), so the total tax collected \( (T) \) naturally increased. It's an indirect effect.

Q: 為什麼選項 B (減稅) 不能減少赤字？收入上升不是也會增加稅收嗎？
(Why doesn't Option B reduce the deficit? Doesn't higher income also increase tax revenue?)

A: 這是一個程度問題。減稅（降低 \( t \)）雖然會刺激經濟令 \( Y \) 上升，但 \( Y \) 上升帶來的額外稅收，通常不足以彌補減稅造成的直接損失。因此，淨結果通常是赤字增加。

A: While the tax cut stimulates \( Y \), the extra tax from higher \( Y \) is rarely enough to fully offset the direct loss of revenue from the tax cut itself. The net result is usually a larger deficit.

Exam Tricks (考試技巧)

看到題目要求「同時改善經濟（增加產出）和改善財政（減少赤字）」時，秒選擴張性貨幣政策 (Expansionary Monetary Policy)。

  • 財政政策通常無法兩全其美（Trade-off）。
  • 貨幣政策可以通過推高 GDP 來間接幫助財政。

When you see a question asking to "Boost Output AND Reduce Deficit" simultaneously, immediately look for Expansionary Monetary Policy. Fiscal policy usually involves a trade-off, whereas Monetary policy can help the budget indirectly by boosting GDP.
