Topic: National Income Accounting 國民收入會計 Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)
Key Concepts / 核心概念: GDP / 本地生產總值 Factor Cost / 要素成本 Expenditure Approach / 開支計算法 Value-added / 增值

1. The Question (題目)

某經濟的物品X生產鏈顯示如下。
以上生產鏈對該經濟的本地生產總值（以要素成本計算）的貢獻是 _____________。

A production chain of Good X in an economy is shown below.
The contribution of the above production chain to the economy’s gross domestic product at factor cost is _____________.

  • A. $1 400
  • B. $1 450
  • C. $1 500
  • D. $1 550

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

計算本地生產總值 (Calculating GDP)

要計算本地生產總值，我們可以使用開支計算法 (Expenditure Approach) 或 增值計算法 (Value-added Approach)。在此題中，使用開支計算法會更快捷、更不易出錯。

To calculate GDP, we can use the Expenditure Approach or the Value-added Approach. In this specific question, using the Expenditure Approach is faster and less prone to errors.

$$GDP_{mp} = C + I + G + X - M$$
  • C (Private Consumption / 私人消費開支): 售予本地消費者的價值 (Sales to local consumers).
  • I (Gross Investment / 總投資): 存貨的增加 (Increase in inventory).
  • X (Exports / 出口): 售予外國旅客的價值 (Sales to foreign tourists).
  • M (Imports / 進口): 購買進口原材料的價值 (Value of imported raw materials).

市價 vs 要素成本 (Market Price vs Factor Cost)

題目要求計算要素成本 (Factor Cost)，但我們初步計算得出的通常是市價 (Market Price)。由於市價包含了政府徵收的間接稅 (Indirect Taxes)，我們必須將其扣除。

The question asks for GDP at Factor Cost, but our initial calculation usually yields GDP at Market Price. Since market prices include indirect taxes levied by the government, we must subtract them.

$$GDP_{fc} = GDP_{mp} - \text{Indirect Taxes (間接稅)} + \text{Subsidies (津貼)}$$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Answer: Option B

✓ Option B ($1 450) is CORRECT.

第一步：計算市價計算的 GDP ($GDP_{mp}$)
利用開支計算法：私人消費 $C = 1200$；存貨增加（投資）$I = 50$；出口（售予外國旅客）$X = 500$；進口原材料 $M = 200$。
$GDP_{mp} = 1200 + 50 + 500 - 200 = 1550$

第二步：轉換為要素成本 ($GDP_{fc}$)
$GDP_{fc} = GDP_{mp} - \text{Sales Tax (銷售稅)}$
$GDP_{fc} = 1550 - 100 = 1450$

Step 1: Calculate GDP at Market Price ($GDP_{mp}$)
Using the expenditure approach: Private consumption $C = 1200$; Increase in inventory (Investment) $I = 50$; Exports (Sales to foreign tourists) $X = 500$; Imported raw materials $M = 200$.
$GDP_{mp} = 1200 + 50 + 500 - 200 = 1550$
Step 2: Convert to Factor Cost ($GDP_{fc}$)
$GDP_{fc} = GDP_{mp} - \text{Sales Tax}$
$GDP_{fc} = 1550 - 100 = 1450$

✗ Option A ($1 400)

這是一個陷阱。如果學生忘記將農民的「存貨增加 $50」計算在內（即遺漏了投資 $I$），他們會得出 $1200 + 500 - 200 - 100 = 1400$。記住，未售出的存貨也是當年新生產的價值，必須計入 GDP！

This is a trap. If a student forgets to include the farmers' "increase in inventory $50" (i.e., omitting Investment $I$), they would get $1200 + 500 - 200 - 100 = 1400$. Remember, unsold inventory represents value newly produced in that year and MUST be included in GDP!

✗ Option C ($1 500)

如果學生使用了增值計算法，但錯誤地沒有減去 $200 的進口原材料，然後又減去了 $100 稅款，可能會得出這個錯誤數字。另一種可能是胡亂將數據加減。

If a student used the value-added approach but mistakenly failed to subtract the $200 imported raw materials, and then subtracted the $100 tax, they might arrive at this incorrect figure. Another possibility is just randomly adding/subtracting figures.

✗ Option D ($1 550)

這是市價計算的本地生產總值 ($GDP_{mp}$)。學生成功計算出總開支 ($1200 + 50 + 500 - 200 = 1550$)，但粗心大意忘記了題目要求的是「要素成本」，沒有扣除 $100 的銷售稅。

This is the GDP at Market Price ($GDP_{mp}$). The student successfully calculated total expenditure ($1200 + 50 + 500 - 200 = 1550$), but carelessly forgot that the question asked for "Factor Cost" and failed to deduct the $100 sales tax.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (解題關鍵):

  1. 進口 (Imports): 「原材料 $200」是從外地輸入的，並非本地生產，必須從最終價值中扣除。
  2. 銷售稅 (Sales Tax): 括號內的「包含 $100 銷售稅」是將市價轉換為要素成本的關鍵。
  3. 存貨 (Inventory): 存貨增加屬於廠商的「投資開支 (Investment)」，是當年GDP的一部分。

Pivot Point:
1. Imports: The "raw materials $200" are imported, not produced locally, and must be deducted from the final value.
2. Sales Tax: The bracketed "($100 sales tax inclusive)" is the key to converting market price to factor cost.
3. Inventory: The increase in inventory is treated as "Investment expenditure" by the firm and is part of the current year's GDP.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 混淆增值法與開支法 (Confusing Value-added and Expenditure approaches): 很多學生嘗試用增值法逐個計算（進口商增值 + 農民增值 + 超市增值）。雖然可行，但容易算錯中間物品的價值。直接看圖表最右端（最終產品去向）及最左端（進口），使用開支法會簡單得多。
  • 忘記扣除進口 (Forgetting to deduct imports): 看到超市賣了 $1200 + 500$，就直接當成 GDP。必須減去非本地生產的 $200。
  • 粗心忽略括號 (Ignoring the brackets): 考評局經常將關鍵資訊（如稅款、折舊）放在括號內，不小心看漏就會選錯 D。

* Confusing methods: Many try to calculate value-added step-by-step (Importer VA + Farmer VA + Supermarket VA). While possible, it's easy to miscalculate intermediate goods. Looking at the far right (final destinations) and far left (imports) using the expenditure approach is much simpler.
* Forgetting to deduct imports: Seeing the supermarket sold $1200 + 500, students might just take that as GDP. You MUST subtract the non-locally produced $200.
* Ignoring brackets: The HKEAA often puts crucial info (like taxes or depreciation) in brackets. Missing it leads straight to Option D.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼售予「外國旅客」算作出口 (Exports)？

A: 在經濟學中，「出口」不一定是指將貨物用船運出國外。只要是非本地居民（如外國旅客）在本地消費，為本地帶來外匯收入，這在國民收入會計中就會被記錄為服務或物品的出口 ($X$)。

Q: Why are sales to "foreign tourists" considered Exports?
A: In economics, "exports" don't necessarily mean shipping goods overseas. Whenever non-residents (like foreign tourists) consume locally and bring foreign exchange into the economy, it is recorded as an export of goods or services ($X$) in national income accounting.


Q: 什麼情況下我應該使用「增值計算法 (Value-added Approach)」？

A: 讓我們驗證一下！如果用增值法：
進口商增值 = $400 - 200 = 200$
農民增值 = $(600 + 50) - 0 = 650$
超市增值 = $(1200 + 500) - 400 - 600 = 700$
總增值 ($GDP_{mp}$) = $200 + 650 + 700 = 1550$。
再減去間接稅 $100，得出要素成本 GDP = 1450。
兩種方法答案相同！但在考試時，當你看到圖表給出了清晰的最終買家（消費者、旅客、存貨），直接用開支法通常更快。

Q: Under what circumstances should I use the Value-added Approach?
A: Let's verify it! Using Value-added:
Importer VA = $400 - 200 = 200$
Farmer VA = $(600 + 50) - 0 = 650$
Supermarket VA = $(1200 + 500) - 400 - 600 = 700$
Total VA ($GDP_{mp}$) = $200 + 650 + 700 = 1550$.
Subtract the $100 indirect tax, and you get GDP at factor cost = 1450.
Both methods yield the same answer! However, in an exam, when a flowchart clearly shows final buyers (consumers, tourists, inventory), the expenditure approach is usually much faster.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

「首尾呼應法」秒殺流程圖題目！

當你在試卷上看到這種複雜的生產鏈流程圖時，不要逐個箭頭計算！

直接看最右邊（所有離開生產鏈的箭頭，代表最終用途）和最左邊（所有進入生產鏈的外來箭頭，代表進口）。

  1. 找出右邊的總和：$1200 (\text{Local}) + 500 (\text{Tourists}) + 50 (\text{Inventory}) = 1750$
  2. 減去左邊的外來進口：$1750 - 200 (\text{Raw materials}) = 1550$ (這是 $GDP_{mp}$)
  3. 檢查題目要求（要素成本），減稅：$1550 - 100 = 1450$。完成！

The "Ends-Only" Shortcut for Flowchart Questions!
When you see a complex production chain flowchart like this in an exam, DO NOT calculate arrow by arrow!
Look directly at the far right (all arrows leaving the chain, representing final uses) and the far left (all external arrows entering, representing imports).
1. Sum the right side: $1200 (\text{Local}) + 500 (\text{Tourists}) + 50 (\text{Inventory}) = 1750$
2. Subtract external imports from the left: $1750 - 200 (\text{Raw materials}) = 1550$ (This is $GDP_{mp}$)
3. Check the question requirement (Factor Cost), subtract tax: $1550 - 100 = 1450$. Done!
