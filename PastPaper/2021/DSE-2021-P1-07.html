📊 HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Wage Payment Methods (薪金支付方法) Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5) Key Concepts: Time Rate, Performance Bonus, Perspective Analysis

📝 題目 The Question

某名牌產品店的售貨員是按下列安排計薪：

每月薪金： HK$12 000
達標花紅： 當達成銷售目標，會發放花紅。

從該店的角度看，下列哪些是採用上述安排而非只以時計薪的可能原因？

(1) 售貨員的生產力會較高。
(2) 監察服務質素的成本會較低。
(3) 每月賺取的薪金有可能較高。
The salespersons in a luxury brand product shop are paid with the following arrangement.

Monthly salary: HK$12 000
Target bonus: Bonus will be given when sales target is attained.

From the viewpoint of the shop, which of the following are possible reasons of the above arrangement instead of time rate only?

(1) The salespersons’ productivity will be higher.
(2) The cost of monitoring service quality will be lower.
(3) There is a possibility of earning a higher monthly salary.
A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)
A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

🧠 概念重溫 Concept Review

要解答這條問題，我們必須理解兩種不同薪金支付方法的特徵，以及分辨「僱主」與「僱員」的不同觀點：
To solve this question, we must understand the characteristics of different wage payment methods and distinguish between the perspectives of the "employer" and the "employee":
  • 1. 只以時計薪 (Time Rate Only): 僱員按工作時間獲取報酬。
    1. Time Rate Only: Employees are paid based on working hours.
    $$ Wage = w \times t $$
    由於收入固定，員工缺乏主動推銷或提供優質服務的誘因，僱主需要花費大量成本去監察員工是否偷懶。
    Because income is fixed, employees lack the incentive to proactively sell or provide quality service. The employer must spend high costs to monitor whether employees are slacking off.

  • 2. 基本薪金 + 達標花紅 (Basic Salary + Target Bonus): 結合了計時與按表現計薪的元素。
    2. Basic Salary + Target Bonus: Combines elements of time rate and performance-based pay.
    員工為了獲得額外花紅，會自動自覺變得主動，這大大改變了僱主在監察上的負擔。
    To earn the extra bonus, employees naturally become proactive, which significantly changes the employer's monitoring burden.

✅ 答案與詳細分析 The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Option: A

分析 (1)：售貨員的生產力會較高。(正確)

Analysis of (1): The salespersons’ productivity will be higher. (Correct)

由於設有達標花紅，售貨員的收入直接與其銷售表現掛鉤。這種金錢誘因 (financial incentive) 會鼓勵他們更積極地推銷產品，從而提高整體生產力。這是僱主採用此制度的核心原因。
Because of the target bonus, salespersons' income is directly linked to their sales performance. This financial incentive encourages them to actively promote products, thereby increasing overall productivity. This is a core reason why employers adopt this system.

分析 (2)：監察服務質素的成本會較低。(正確)

Analysis of (2): The cost of monitoring service quality will be lower. (Correct)

如果「只以時計薪」，售貨員即使對客人態度冷淡或不主動招待，每個月依然能穩賺 HK$12,000。在這種情況下，僱主需要長期派經理在場監察，確保員工有提供基本服務。引入「達標花紅」後，售貨員為了開單達標，會自動自覺主動迎賓、熱情講解及跟進客人需求。因此，僱主不再需要花費大量時間和人力去監察員工「有沒有主動服務客人」，降低了相關的監察成本。
If it were "time rate only", salespersons would still earn a steady HK$12,000 even if they were cold to customers or unproactive. Under that system, the employer must constantly have managers on-site to monitor and ensure basic service is provided. With a "target bonus" introduced, salespersons will self-motivate to greet customers proactively, explain products enthusiastically, and follow up on customer needs in order to close deals. Therefore, the employer no longer needs to spend vast time and manpower monitoring "whether employees are actively serving customers", lowering the associated monitoring cost.

分析 (3)：每月賺取的薪金有可能較高。(錯誤)

Analysis of (3): There is a possibility of earning a higher monthly salary. (Incorrect)

這是一個視角陷阱。賺取更高薪金是「僱員 (售貨員)」想要採用這個制度的原因。然而，題目明確問的是「從該店 (僱主) 的角度看」。對僱主而言，支付更高的薪金代表成本增加，這本身絕對不是僱主主動採用該制度的「原因」。僱主關心的是生產力上升帶來的利潤，而非幫助員工賺更多錢。
This is a perspective trap. Earning a higher salary is the reason the "employee (salesperson)" would want this system. However, the question explicitly asks "From the viewpoint of the shop (employer)". To the employer, paying higher salaries represents an increase in costs, which in itself is definitely not a "reason" for the employer to proactively adopt the system. The employer cares about the profit brought by higher productivity, not about helping employees earn more money.

🔍 題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)：
這條題目的絕對關鍵在於「觀點與角度 (Perspective)」。經濟學選擇題經常利用視角切換來設下陷阱。當題目寫明「從該店（僱主）的角度看」，你必須將所有選項代入僱主的成本與效益 (Cost-Benefit Analysis) 中。
The Pivot Point:
The absolute key to this question is "Perspective". Economics MC questions often use perspective shifts to set traps. When the question states "From the viewpoint of the shop (employer)", you must apply all options into the employer's Cost-Benefit Analysis.

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

粗心大意：忽略題目的主語
同學看到 (3)「每月賺取的薪金有可能較高」，會直覺認為這是一件「好事」，因為它能吸引人才。但同學忘記了分辨「對誰而言是好事」。在考試中，必須圈出題目中的持份者（例如：僱主、僱員、政府、消費者），然後嚴格根據該持份者的利益去判斷選項。
Careless Error: Ignoring the subject of the question
When students see (3) "There is a possibility of earning a higher monthly salary", they intuitively think it's a "good thing" because it attracts talent. But students forget to distinguish "who is it a good thing for". In exams, you must circle the stakeholder in the question (e.g., employer, employee, government, consumer) and strictly judge the options based on that stakeholder's interests.

🙋‍♂️ 學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

問：一般按件計酬 (Piece Rate) 不是會令員工只顧數量、忽略質量，導致監察質素的成本上升嗎？為什麼選項 (2) 說監察服務質素的成本會較低？
Q: Doesn't piece rate usually make employees focus only on quantity and ignore quality, leading to an increase in the cost of monitoring quality? Why does option (2) say the cost of monitoring service quality will be lower?
答：這是一個非常好的問題！純粹的「按件計酬」（例如工廠車衣）的確會引發質量下降的問題。但請注意，題目的對比基礎是「而非只以時計薪 (instead of time rate only)」。在零售業中，「服務質素」很大程度包含了員工的「主動性」和「服務態度」。在純計時工資下，員工毫無誘因去主動招待客人，這是一種極差的服務質素，僱主需要長期緊迫盯人。加入了花紅後，員工有了主動提供服務以促成交易的誘因，僱主便能省下「強迫員工去服務客人」的監察成本。
A: This is a great question! Pure "piece rate" (like garment factory workers) indeed triggers quality drop issues. But note that the basis of comparison in the question is "instead of time rate only". In the retail industry, "service quality" largely includes the employee's "proactiveness" and "service attitude". Under pure time rate, employees have zero incentive to proactively serve customers, which is extremely poor service quality, requiring the employer to micro-manage them. Once a bonus is added, employees have the incentive to proactively provide service to close deals, allowing the employer to save on the monitoring costs of "forcing employees to serve customers".

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

邏輯秒殺法 (Logic Check & Elimination)：
這題可以用常理極速破解。
第一步：留意題目視角是「商店 (The Shop)」。
第二步：看選項 (3)「每月賺取的薪金有可能較高」。問自己：老闆會不會因為「我想付更多薪水」而改變制度？當然不會！老闆改變制度是為了更高的利潤或更易管理。所以 (3) 絕對錯。
第三步：利用消除法！
B. 只有 (1)、(3) ❌
C. 只有 (2)、(3) ❌
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3) ❌

答案立刻浮現，必然是 A！就算你在考場上一時想不通 (2) 背後的複雜理論，只要你看穿了 (3) 的視角錯誤，就能在 10 秒內拿下這兩分！
Logic Check & Elimination Method:
This question can be cracked rapidly using common sense.
Step 1: Note the perspective is "The Shop".
Step 2: Look at option (3) "possibility of earning a higher monthly salary". Ask yourself: Would a boss change a system because "I want to pay more salary"? Of course not! The boss changes it for higher profits or easier management. So (3) is definitely wrong.
Step 3: Use elimination!
B. (1) and (3) only ❌
C. (2) and (3) only ❌
D. (1), (2) and (3) ❌

The answer emerges instantly—it must be A! Even if you can't wrap your head around the complex theory behind (2) during the exam, as long as you spot the perspective error in (3), you can secure these 2 marks in 10 seconds!
