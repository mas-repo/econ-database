Topic: 宏觀經濟學 - 國民收入會計 (Macroeconomics - National Income Accounting) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: GDP Deflator, Real GDP, Per-capita GDP

1. 題目 (The Question)

參閱某經濟的本地生產總值(GDP)數據。
Refer to the gross domestic product (GDP) data of an economy.
增長率（相對於去年）
Growth rate (relative to previous year)
名義GDP (Nominal GDP) 0
實質GDP (Real GDP) -1.2%
人均名義GDP (Per-capita nominal GDP) -3.5%
與去年相比，本年度該經濟的一般物價水平 _________ ，人口 ___________。
Compared to the previous year, the general price level has _________ and population has ___________ in the economy in the current year.
  • A. 上升 … 上升 (increased … increased)
  • B. 上升 … 下降 (increased … decreased)
  • C. 下降 … 上升 (decreased … increased)
  • D. 下降 … 下降 (decreased … decreased)

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解決這條題目，我們需要掌握兩個核心關係。在宏觀經濟學中，變量的增長率 (Growth Rate) 可以用簡單的加減法來近似計算。
To solve this problem, we need to master two core relationships. In macroeconomics, the growth rates of variables can be approximated using simple addition and subtraction.

核心公式 1 (Core Formula 1): 物價水平與GDP (Price Level and GDP)

$$ \text{Nominal GDP} = \text{Real GDP} \times \text{Price Level} $$ $$ g_{\text{Nominal}} \approx g_{\text{Real}} + g_{\text{Price}} $$
名義GDP增長率 $\approx$ 實質GDP增長率 $+$ 一般物價水平增長率（通脹率）。
Nominal GDP growth rate $\approx$ Real GDP growth rate $+$ General price level growth rate (Inflation rate).

核心公式 2 (Core Formula 2): 人口與人均GDP (Population and Per-capita GDP)

$$ \text{Per-capita Nominal GDP} = \frac{\text{Nominal GDP}}{\text{Population}} $$ $$ g_{\text{Per-capita Nominal}} \approx g_{\text{Nominal}} - g_{\text{Population}} $$
人均名義GDP增長率 $\approx$ 名義GDP增長率 $-$ 人口增長率。
Per-capita nominal GDP growth rate $\approx$ Nominal GDP growth rate $-$ Population growth rate.

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

Correct Option: A
第一部分：一般物價水平 (Part 1: General Price Level)
根據公式：$g_{\text{Price}} \approx g_{\text{Nominal}} - g_{\text{Real}}$。題目給出名義GDP增長為 $0\%$，實質GDP增長為 $-1.2\%$。
According to the formula: $g_{\text{Price}} \approx g_{\text{Nominal}} - g_{\text{Real}}$. The data shows Nominal GDP growth is $0\%$, and Real GDP growth is $-1.2\%$.
$$ g_{\text{Price}} \approx 0\% - (-1.2\%) = +1.2\% $$
物價增長率為正數 ($+1.2\%$ $\gt$ $0$)，代表一般物價水平上升。因此，選項 C 和 D 是錯誤的。
The price growth rate is positive ($+1.2\%$ $\gt$ $0$), which means the general price level has increased. Therefore, Options C and D are incorrect.
第二部分：人口 (Part 2: Population)
根據公式：$g_{\text{Population}} \approx g_{\text{Nominal}} - g_{\text{Per-capita Nominal}}$。題目給出名義GDP增長為 $0\%$，人均名義GDP增長為 $-3.5\%$。
According to the formula: $g_{\text{Population}} \approx g_{\text{Nominal}} - g_{\text{Per-capita Nominal}}$. The data shows Nominal GDP growth is $0\%$, and Per-capita nominal GDP growth is $-3.5\%$.
$$ g_{\text{Population}} \approx 0\% - (-3.5\%) = +3.5\% $$
人口增長率為正數 ($+3.5\%$ $\gt$ $0$)，代表人口上升。結合第一部分，正確答案是 A。
The population growth rate is positive ($+3.5\%$ $\gt$ $0$), which means the population has increased. Combined with Part 1, the correct answer is A.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point): 這條題目的關鍵在於將三個已知的數據點 (名義GDP、實質GDP、人均名義GDP) 拆解成兩組獨立的計算。名義GDP $0\%$ 是連接兩組計算的橋樑。
The Pivot Point: The key to this question is breaking down the three given data points (Nominal GDP, Real GDP, Per-capita nominal GDP) into two independent calculations. The $0\%$ Nominal GDP acts as the bridge connecting both parts.
  • 任務 1: 利用「名義」與「實質」的差異找出「物價」。 Task 1: Use the difference between "Nominal" and "Real" to find "Price".
  • 任務 2: 利用「總量」與「人均」的差異找出「人口」。 Task 2: Use the difference between "Total" and "Per-capita" to find "Population".

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

直覺陷阱 (The Intuition Trap)： 很多學生看到「人均名義GDP下降了 $-3.5\%$」，會直覺以為這代表「人少了」。
The Intuition Trap: Many students see "Per-capita nominal GDP dropped by $-3.5\%$" and intuitively think this means "there are fewer people".
真相 (The Truth)： 剛好相反！想像名義GDP是一個大披薩。披薩的總大小沒有變 ($0\%$ 增長)，但每個人分到的披薩變小了 ($-3.5\%$ 增長)。這只可能有一個原因：吃披薩的人變多了！所以人口必定是上升的。
The Truth: It's the exact opposite! Imagine Nominal GDP as a big pizza. The total size of the pizza didn't change ($0\%$ growth), but the slice each person gets is smaller ($-3.5\%$ growth). There is only one possible reason: there are more people eating the pizza! Thus, the population must have increased.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 增長率的近似值公式 ($g_A \approx g_B + g_C$) 在考試中總是準確嗎？如果不用公式可以計嗎？
Q: Is the growth rate approximation formula ($g_A \approx g_B + g_C$) always accurate in exams? Can I solve it without the formula?
A: 在 DSE 選擇題中，這個近似值公式絕對足夠判斷「方向」(上升/下降)。如果你不想背公式，可以用指數法 (Index Method)。假設去年的所有數值都是 $100$：
今年名義GDP = $100$
今年實質GDP = $100 \times (1 - 0.012) = 98.8$
今年物價指數 = $(100 / 98.8) \times 100 = 101.2$ (大於 $100$，即上升)。
A: In DSE MC questions, this approximation formula is definitely sufficient to determine the "direction" (increase/decrease). If you don't want to memorize formulas, you can use the Index Method. Assume all values last year were $100$:
This year's Nominal GDP = $100$
This year's Real GDP = $100 \times (1 - 0.012) = 98.8$
This year's Price Index = $(100 / 98.8) \times 100 = 101.2$ (Greater than $100$, so it increased).

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

不需動筆的邏輯速解 (No-pen Logic Shortcut):
No-pen Logic Shortcut:
1. 名義不變 ($0$)，但實質數量跌了 ($-1.2\%$，代表買到的東西少了)。總花費一樣但買到的東西變少，代表東西變貴了 (物價上升)。
1. Nominal is unchanged ($0$), but real quantity dropped ($-1.2\%$, meaning fewer goods are produced/bought). Spending the same total amount but getting fewer goods means things got more expensive (Price increased).
2. 總收入不變 ($0$)，但每個人平均分到的錢少了 ($-3.5\%$，代表分薄了)。總金額不變但平均分薄，代表分錢的人變多了 (人口上升)。
2. Total income is unchanged ($0$), but the average money per person dropped ($-3.5\%$, meaning it was diluted). Same total amount but diluted average means there are more people sharing the money (Population increased).
秒殺得出答案：上升 ... 上升 (A)！
Instantly yielding the answer: increased ... increased (A)!
