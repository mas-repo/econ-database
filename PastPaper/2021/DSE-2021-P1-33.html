Topic (課題): 貨幣市場與公開市場操作 (Money Market & Open Market Operations)

Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts (核心概念): 貨幣需求 (Money Demand), 貨幣供應 (Money Supply), 資產需求 (Asset Demand), 贖回債券 (Redeeming Bonds)

1. 題目 | The Question

下圖顯示某經濟的貨幣需求和貨幣供應曲線。原初均衡點是E。

The diagram below shows the money demand and money supply curves of an economy. The original equilibrium point is at E.

假設股市日趨波動。同時，中央銀行決定向公眾贖回政府債券。新的均衡點會是 ____________。

Suppose the stock market becomes more volatile. At the same time, the central bank decides to redeem government bonds from the public. The new equilibrium point will be at _________.

  • A. A
  • B. B
  • C. C
  • D. D

2. 概念重溫 | Concept Review

貨幣需求 \( (M_d) \): 人們持有貨幣的意欲。當股市變得波動（風險增加），人們會減少持有股票，轉而持有風險較低的貨幣作為資產。這會增加貨幣的資產需求，導致貨幣需求曲線向右移。

Money Demand \( (M_d) \): The desire to hold money. When the stock market becomes volatile (risk increases), people reduce stock holdings and prefer to hold low-risk money as an asset. This increases the asset demand for money, shifting the money demand curve to the right.

貨幣供應 \( (M_s) \): 由中央銀行控制。當央行「贖回」（買入）政府債券時，央行會將金錢支付給公眾，這將資金注入銀行體系，從而增加貨幣基礎和貨幣供應。貨幣供應曲線向右移。

Money Supply \( (M_s) \): Controlled by the central bank. When the central bank "redeems" (buys) government bonds, it pays money to the public, injecting liquidity into the banking system. This increases the monetary base and money supply. The money supply curve shifts to the right.

$$ \Delta M_s = \text{Bond Purchases} \times \text{Money Multiplier} > 0 $$

3. 答案與詳細分析 | Answer & Analysis

正確答案是 Option B。

The correct answer is Option B.

  • 選項 A (錯): 點 A 代表貨幣供應減少 \( (M_{s0} \rightarrow M_{s1}) \) 和貨幣需求增加 \( (M_{d0} \rightarrow M_{d2}) \)。這與央行贖回債券（會增加貨幣供應）的設定矛盾。

    Option A (Incorrect): Point A represents a decrease in money supply \( (M_{s0} \rightarrow M_{s1}) \) and an increase in money demand \( (M_{d0} \rightarrow M_{d2}) \). This contradicts the central bank redeeming bonds, which increases money supply.

  • 選項 B (正確): 點 B 代表貨幣供應增加 \( (M_{s0} \rightarrow M_{s2}) \) 以及貨幣需求增加 \( (M_{d0} \rightarrow M_{d2}) \)。這完全符合題目中「央行贖回債券」及「股市波動」所帶來的雙重效果。

    Option B (Correct): Point B represents an increase in money supply \( (M_{s0} \rightarrow M_{s2}) \) and an increase in money demand \( (M_{d0} \rightarrow M_{d2}) \). This perfectly matches the dual effects of the central bank redeeming bonds and stock market volatility.

  • 選項 C (錯): 點 C 代表貨幣供應減少 \( (M_{s0} \rightarrow M_{s1}) \) 和貨幣需求減少 \( (M_{d0} \rightarrow M_{d1}) \)。這與題目的兩個事件均背道而馳。

    Option C (Incorrect): Point C represents a decrease in money supply \( (M_{s0} \rightarrow M_{s1}) \) and a decrease in money demand \( (M_{d0} \rightarrow M_{d1}) \). This contradicts both events in the question.

  • 選項 D (錯): 點 D 代表貨幣供應增加 \( (M_{s0} \rightarrow M_{s2}) \)，但貨幣需求減少 \( (M_{d0} \rightarrow M_{d1}) \)。雖然貨幣供應的方向正確，但股市波動應增加（而非減少）貨幣需求。

    Option D (Incorrect): Point D represents an increase in money supply \( (M_{s0} \rightarrow M_{s2}) \) but a decrease in money demand \( (M_{d0} \rightarrow M_{d1}) \). While the money supply direction is correct, stock market volatility should increase (not decrease) money demand.

4. 題目拆解 | Problem Decomposition

這類題目的核心在於將複雜情境拆解為兩個獨立的「位移」(Shifts)。我們只需要找出兩條新曲線，它們的交點就是答案。

The core of this type of question is to break down the complex scenario into two independent shifts. We just need to find the two new curves, and their intersection is the answer.

  1. 事件一 (影響 \(M_d\))： 股市波動 \( \rightarrow \) 人們需要避險 \( \rightarrow \) 持有更多現金 \( \rightarrow \) \( M_d \) 增加（向右移至 \(M_{d2}\)）。

    Event 1 (Affects \(M_d\)): Stock market volatile \( \rightarrow \) People seek safe havens \( \rightarrow \) Hold more cash \( \rightarrow \) \( M_d \) increases (shifts right to \(M_{d2}\)).

  2. 事件二 (影響 \(M_s\))： 央行贖回（買入）債券 \( \rightarrow \) 央行向市場派錢 \( \rightarrow \) \( M_s \) 增加（向右移至 \(M_{s2}\)）。

    Event 2 (Affects \(M_s\)): Central bank redeems (buys) bonds \( \rightarrow \) Central bank injects money into the market \( \rightarrow \) \( M_s \) increases (shifts right to \(M_{s2}\)).

  3. 結論： 尋找 \( M_{d2} \) 與 \( M_{s2} \) 的交點，即是點 B。

    Conclusion: Find the intersection of \( M_{d2} \) and \( M_{s2} \), which is point B.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 | Common Mistakes & Traps

⚠️ 陷阱 1：混淆「贖回/買入」與「發行/賣出」債券
很多學生會錯誤以為「央行進行債券操作 = 減少貨幣供應」。記住：央行贖回 (Redeem) / 買入 (Buy) 債券是將現金交給市民，因此貨幣供應會增加。相反，央行發行 (Issue) / 賣出 (Sell) 債券則是向市民收回現金，貨幣供應才會減少。

⚠️ Trap 1: Confusing "Redeeming/Buying" with "Issuing/Selling" bonds
Many students mistakenly think "Central bank bond operations = reducing money supply". Remember: When the central bank redeems / buys bonds, it gives cash to the public, so money supply increases. Conversely, when it issues / sells bonds, it takes cash from the public, decreasing the money supply.

⚠️ 陷阱 2：誤解股市波動的影響
有些學生以為股市波動代表「需要更多錢去炒股」。在經濟學模型中，股市波動代表風險增加。投資者為了規避風險，會將資產從股票轉移到最安全的資產——貨幣（現金/存款）。這屬於資產需求的增加。

⚠️ Trap 2: Misunderstanding the impact of stock volatility
Some students think stock volatility means "needing more money to speculate in stocks". In economic models, stock volatility means increased risk. To avoid risk, investors shift assets from stocks to the safest asset—money (cash/deposits). This is an increase in asset demand.

6. 學生常見疑問 | Student Q&A

問 (Q)：為什麼名義利率 (Nominal Interest Rate) 在新的均衡點 B 似乎沒有改變？

Q: Why does the nominal interest rate seem unchanged at the new equilibrium point B?

答 (A)： 在這張特定的圖表中，\(M_s\) 向右移的幅度剛好抵銷了 \(M_d\) 向右移對利率造成的上升壓力。但在現實或不同的題目設定中，如果 \(M_d\) 增加的幅度大於 \(M_s\) 的增加，利率就會上升；反之則下降。做題時只需專注於尋找正確的曲線交點即可。

A: In this specific diagram, the magnitude of the rightward shift in \(M_s\) happens to exactly offset the upward pressure on the interest rate caused by the rightward shift in \(M_d\). In reality or different questions, if the increase in \(M_d\) is greater than the increase in \(M_s\), the interest rate would rise, and vice versa. When solving, just focus on finding the correct intersection of the curves.

7. 考試技巧與速解 | Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

💡 十秒速解法 (10-Second Shortcut)：
在多項選擇題中，不要一開始就看四個選項 A/B/C/D，這會擾亂你的思緒。
1. 讀到「央行贖回債券」\( \rightarrow \) 立即用筆在圖上的 \(M_{s2}\) 畫一個圈。
2. 讀到「股市波動」\( \rightarrow \) 立即在圖上的 \(M_{d2}\) 畫一個圈。
3. 找出兩個圈相交的那一點，就是答案。完全不需要死記硬背！

💡 10-Second Shortcut:
In MCQs, don't look at the options A/B/C/D first, as it will confuse you.
1. Read "central bank redeems bonds" \( \rightarrow \) Immediately circle \(M_{s2}\) on the graph with your pen.
2. Read "stock market volatile" \( \rightarrow \) Immediately circle \(M_{d2}\) on the graph.
3. Find the exact point where your two circled lines intersect. That's your answer. No rote memorization needed!
