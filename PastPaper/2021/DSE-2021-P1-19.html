難度 Difficulty：⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)

關鍵概念 Key Concepts：

Demand Shift 需求改變 Marginal Cost 邊際成本 Producer Surplus 生產者盈餘

📝 題目 The Question

由於大部分學校進行網上授課，更多學生需要購買平板電腦。最後一單位的平板電腦的邊際成本會 ___________ ，而生產者盈餘會 ____________。

Since most schools deliver their lessons online, more students need to buy tablet computers. The marginal cost of the last unit of tablet computers will ________ and the producer surplus will _________.

A. 下降 … 上升
A. decrease … increase
B. 下降 … 下降
B. decrease … decrease
C. 上升 … 上升
C. increase … increase
D. 上升 … 下降
D. increase … decrease

💡 概念重溫 Concept Review

1. 邊際成本 (Marginal Cost, $MC$) 與 供應曲線 (Supply Curve)：
在完全競爭市場中，供應曲線就是邊際成本曲線（高於平均可變成本最低點的部分）。由於邊際回報遞減定律 (Law of Diminishing Marginal Returns)，短期供應曲線是向上傾斜的。因此，當生產數量 ($Q$) 增加時，最後一單位的邊際成本 ($MC$) 必然上升。

1. Marginal Cost ($MC$) and Supply Curve:
In a perfectly competitive market, the supply curve is the marginal cost curve (above the minimum point of average variable cost). Due to the Law of Diminishing Marginal Returns, the short-run supply curve is upward sloping. Therefore, when quantity produced ($Q$) increases, the marginal cost ($MC$) of the last unit must increase.

2. 生產者盈餘 (Producer Surplus, $PS$)：
生產者盈餘是指生產者實際收取的價格與他們願意接受的最低價格（即邊際成本）之間的差額。在圖表上，它代表市場價格以下、供應曲線以上的面積。

2. Producer Surplus ($PS$):
Producer surplus is the difference between the actual price producers receive and the minimum price they are willing to accept (which is the marginal cost). Graphically, it is the area below the market price and above the supply curve.

生產者盈餘公式：

Producer Surplus Formula:

$$PS = Total\ Revenue\ (TR) - Total\ Variable\ Cost\ (TVC)$$

✅ 答案與詳細分析 Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Option： C

為什麼選 C？
「更多學生需要購買」代表對平板電腦的需求增加 (Demand increases)。在供應不變的情況下，需求曲線向右移會導致市場均衡價格 ($P$) 和均衡交易量 ($Q$) 同時上升。
1. 因為交易量 ($Q$) 增加，沿著向上傾斜的供應曲線向右上方移動，最後一單位的邊際成本 ($MC$) 會上升。
2. 因為價格 ($P$) 和交易量 ($Q$) 都上升了，價格水平線和供應曲線之間的三角形面積擴大了，所以生產者盈餘 ($PS$) 會上升。

Why Option C?
"More students need to buy" signifies an increase in demand for tablet computers. Assuming supply remains unchanged, a rightward shift of the demand curve leads to an increase in both the equilibrium price ($P$) and quantity ($Q$).
1. As quantity ($Q$) increases, we move up along the upward-sloping supply curve, meaning the marginal cost ($MC$) of the last unit increases.
2. Because both price ($P$) and quantity ($Q$) have increased, the triangular area between the price level and the supply curve expands, so the producer surplus ($PS$) increases.

為什麼 A, B, D 是錯誤的？
選項 A 及 B 錯誤，因為它們假設邊際成本會下降，這違背了供應曲線向上傾斜的原則。選項 D 錯誤，因為當價格上升且交易量增加時，生產者盈餘的面積絕對不可能減少。

Why are A, B, and D wrong?
Options A and B are incorrect because they assume marginal cost decreases, which contradicts the upward-sloping nature of the supply curve. Option D is incorrect because when both price and quantity increase, the area of producer surplus cannot possibly decrease.

🔍 題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)：
這道題目的關鍵在於分清「需求改變」還是「供應改變」。學校改為網上授課是影響消費者（學生）的因素，因此只會移動需求曲線 ($D$)。供應曲線 ($S$) 保持不變，這意味著我們只能沿著原有的供應曲線移動 (Movement along the supply curve)。

Pivot Point:
The key to solving this question is distinguishing between a "change in demand" and a "change in supply". Schools switching to online lessons is a factor affecting consumers (students), therefore it only shifts the demand curve ($D$). The supply curve ($S$) remains unchanged, meaning we only experience a movement along the original supply curve.

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

陷阱 1：誤把「規模經濟」套用到短期分析
有些同學會覺得「大量生產，成本不是會降低嗎？」，從而誤選邊際成本下降。記住，HKDSE 的基本供求模型預設是短期分析，受「邊際回報遞減定律」限制，邊際成本必定是上升的，除非題目明確提到「規模經濟 (Economies of scale)」或技術進步。

Trap 1: Misapplying "Economies of Scale" to short-run analysis
Some students might think, "Doesn't mass production lower costs?", leading them to incorrectly choose a decrease in marginal cost. Remember, the basic supply-demand model in HKDSE assumes short-run analysis. Bound by the "Law of Diminishing Marginal Returns," marginal cost must be strictly increasing unless the question explicitly mentions "economies of scale" or technological advancement.

🙋 學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

問：為什麼我們不移動供應曲線 ($S$)？電腦製造商不能生產更多嗎？

Q: Why don't we shift the supply curve ($S$)? Can't computer manufacturers just produce more?

答：製造商確實生產了更多（交易量 $Q$ 增加了），但這是在原有的供應曲線上滑動（因為市價 $P$ 升了，吸引他們生產更多），而不是整個供應曲線向右平移。只有當生產成本（如零件變平）或科技進步時，供應曲線才會向右移。

A: Manufacturers *do* produce more (quantity $Q$ increases), but this is a movement along the existing supply curve (the higher market price $P$ incentivizes them to produce more), not a rightward shift of the entire supply curve. The supply curve itself only shifts right if production costs fall (e.g., cheaper components) or if there is technological progress.

🚀 考試技巧與速解 Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

「五秒畫圖法」
遇到這類文字題，千萬不要只在腦海中空想！立即在試卷空白處畫一個標準的交叉供求圖（向下的 $D$ 加上向上的 $S$）。
1. 畫一條新的 $D$ 線在右邊 ($D_2$)。
2. 點出新的均衡點。
3. 眼睛一看：新點比舊點更高（$MC$ 上升），左邊的三角形面積也明顯變大了（$PS$ 上升）。答案秒出！

"The 5-Second Graphing Trick"
Never rely solely on mental imagination for text-based questions! Immediately sketch a standard supply-demand cross (downward $D$ and upward $S$) on the margin of your paper.
1. Draw a new $D$ curve to the right ($D_2$).
2. Plot the new equilibrium point.
3. Visually confirm: The new point is higher on the $S$ curve (higher $MC$), and the triangle area on the left is visibly larger (higher $PS$). Answer solved in seconds!
