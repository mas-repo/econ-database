Key Concepts: Exchange Rate Calculation, Interest Rate Return, Appreciation/Depreciation

The Question (題目)

細閱下表。 Study the following table.
Date (日期) Exchange rate (匯率)
2019-12-03 1 HKD = 0.902 RMB
2020-12-03 1 HKD = 0.923 RMB
在2019年12月3日，陳先生用港幣兌換了300 000人民幣，並將該筆款項存入銀行作定期存款，年利率0.8%。假設他在2020年12月3日提取該筆款項，並把它兌回港幣。在上述的交易後，陳先生 ______________。 On 3 December 2019, Mr Chan exchanged a total of RMB 300 000 from Hong Kong dollar and deposited the sum to a bank as time deposits at an annual interest rate of 0.8%. Suppose he withdrew the sum of 3 December 2020 and exchanged it back to Hong Kong dollar. From the above transactions, Mr Chan ______________.
  • A. 損失了約港幣$4 967 / lost about HK$4 967
  • B. 損失了約港幣$7 570 / lost about HK$7 570
  • C. 賺取了港幣$6 300 / gained about HK$6 300
  • D. 賺取了港幣$8 515 / gained about HK$8 515

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 外幣兌換本幣 (Converting Foreign Currency to Local Currency)

當匯率表示為 \( 1 \text{ HKD} = X \text{ RMB} \) 時：

When the exchange rate is expressed as \( 1 \text{ HKD} = X \text{ RMB} \):

$$ \text{HKD Amount} = \frac{\text{RMB Amount}}{X} $$

注意：如果匯率是 \( 1 \text{ RMB} = Y \text{ HKD} \)，則是用乘法。

Note: If the rate is \( 1 \text{ RMB} = Y \text{ HKD} \), you would multiply.

2. 投資回報計算 (Investment Return Calculation)

利息是基於外幣本金計算的，而不是港幣本金。

Interest is calculated based on the foreign currency principal, not the HKD principal.

$$ \text{Total Amount} = \text{Principal} \times (1 + \text{Interest Rate}) $$
3. 匯率風險 (Exchange Rate Risk)

如果在持有外幣期間，港元升值（即兌換率數字變大，例如 0.902 -> 0.923），換回港元時會變得「不划算」。

If the HKD appreciates (the exchange rate figure increases, e.g., 0.902 -> 0.923) while holding foreign currency, converting back to HKD becomes less favorable.

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A
1
計算2019年的港幣成本 (Initial Cost in HKD) Calculate how much HKD was spent to buy RMB 300,000 in 2019.

匯率：\( 1 \text{ HKD} = 0.902 \text{ RMB} \)

$$ \text{Cost} = \frac{300,000}{0.902} \approx 332,594.24 \text{ HKD} $$
2
計算連本帶利的人民幣總額 (Total RMB after Interest) Calculate the total RMB accumulated after 1 year at 0.8% interest.

本金：300,000 RMB，年利率：0.8%

$$ 300,000 \times (1 + 0.8\%) = 300,000 \times 1.008 = 302,400 \text{ RMB} $$
3
計算2020年換回的港幣價值 (Final Value in HKD) Convert the total RMB (principal + interest) back to HKD using the 2020 rate.

匯率：\( 1 \text{ HKD} = 0.923 \text{ RMB} \)

$$ \text{Value} = \frac{302,400}{0.923} \approx 327,627.30 \text{ HKD} $$
4
計算損益 (Calculate Gain/Loss) Compare the final value with the initial cost.
$$ \text{Profit/Loss} = 327,627.30 - 332,594.24 = -4,966.94 $$

結果為負數，代表損失約 $4,967。

The result is negative, indicating a loss of approximately $4,967.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目通常由三個變數組成：買入匯率、利息回報、賣出匯率。

This type of question usually involves three variables: Entry Exchange Rate, Interest Return, Exit Exchange Rate.

關鍵轉折點 (The Pivot Point):

注意匯率的變化方向。1港元兌換的人民幣由 0.902 變為 0.923。這代表港元升值 (HKD appreciated)，相對地人民幣貶值 (RMB depreciated)。當你持有貶值的貨幣（人民幣）時，即使有利息收入，如果貶值幅度大於利息，你最終仍會虧損。

Notice the direction of the exchange rate. 1 HKD went from 0.902 to 0.923 RMB. This means HKD appreciated, and consequently RMB depreciated. When you hold a depreciating currency (RMB), even with interest income, if the depreciation is larger than the interest rate, you will suffer a loss.

Tips & Traps (技巧與陷阱)

Common Mistakes (常犯錯誤)
  • 乘除混淆： 看到 0.902 就直接用乘法。記住：如果單位是 \(1 \text{ HKD} = \dots\)，計算 HKD 時要用除法。 Confusing Mult/Div: Multiplying by 0.902 directly. Remember: if the unit is \(1 \text{ HKD} = \dots\), use division to find HKD.
  • 忽略利息： 忘記把 0.8% 的利息加到人民幣本金上。 Ignoring Interest: Forgetting to add the 0.8% interest to the RMB principal.
Exam Shortcuts (考試速解)

估算法 (Estimation):

Estimation Method:

1. 利息賺了 \(0.8\%\)。
2. 匯率虧損了多少？ \(\frac{0.923 - 0.902}{0.902} \approx 2.3\%\)。
3. 由於匯率虧損 (\(2.3\%\)) 遠大於利息收入 (\(0.8\%\))，結果必然是虧損 (Loss)。
4. 直接刪除選項 C 和 D。

1. Interest gain is \(0.8\%\).
2. Exchange rate loss? \(\approx 2.3\%\).
3. Since currency loss (\(2.3\%\)) > interest gain (\(0.8\%\)), it must be a Loss.
4. Eliminate options C and D immediately.
