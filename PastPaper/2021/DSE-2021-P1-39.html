📝 HKDSE Economics Question Analysis

Topic (課題): Business Cycle & Economic Growth / 經濟週期與經濟增長

Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts (核心概念): Real GDP Growth Rate (實質 GDP 增長率), Inflation (通貨膨脹), Business Confidence (商業信心)

1. The Question (題目)

細閱下圖。當某經濟在 A 點運作，下列哪些是該經濟的特徵？

Study the following diagram. Which of the following are the features of an economy when it is operating at point A?
  • (1) 勞力供應正在增加。
    Labour supply is increasing.
  • (2) 貨幣的購買力正在減少。
    Purchasing power of money is decreasing.
  • (3) 政府支出正在增加。
    Government expenditure is increasing.
  • (4) 公司的商業信心正在增加。
    Business confidence of companies is increasing.

A. 只有 (1)、(3) / (1) and (3) only
B. 只有 (1)、(4) / (1) and (4) only
C. 只有 (2)、(3) / (2) and (3) only
D. 只有 (2)、(4) / (2) and (4) only

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這條問題，我們必須清楚理解圖表中 Y 軸的含義。Y 軸是「實質 GDP 變動百分率」(Percentage change in real GDP)，而不是實質 GDP 的絕對值。

To solve this question, we must clearly understand the meaning of the Y-axis. The Y-axis represents the "Percentage change in real GDP", not the absolute level of Real GDP.

當增長率為正數且正在上升（如 A 點所示，高於平均增長率並朝向頂峰），這代表經濟處於擴張期 (Expansion) 或繁榮期 (Boom)。在這個階段：

When the growth rate is positive and rising (as shown at point A, above the average growth rate and heading towards a peak), it signifies that the economy is in an Expansion or Boom phase. During this phase:
  • 總需求 \(AD\) 急速增加。 / Aggregate demand \(AD\) increases rapidly.
  • 失業率下降，就業人數上升。 / Unemployment rate falls, employment level rises.
  • 一般物價水平上升，引發通貨膨脹。 / General price level rises, leading to inflation.
$$ \text{Growth Rate (增長率)} = \frac{\text{Real GDP}_t - \text{Real GDP}_{t-1}}{\text{Real GDP}_{t-1}} \times 100\% $$

貨幣購買力 (Purchasing Power of Money): 指一單位貨幣能買到的物品數量。當物價上升（通脹），貨幣的購買力就會下降，公式關係為 \( \frac{1}{P} \)。

Purchasing Power of Money: The amount of goods a unit of money can buy. When the price level rises (inflation), the purchasing power of money falls. The relationship is \( \frac{1}{P} \).

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是 (Correct Option): D

錯誤 / Incorrect 選項 (1) 勞力供應正在增加 / Labour supply is increasing

在經濟擴張期，廠商會增加生產，導致對勞工的需求 (Labour Demand) 上升，就業人數會增加。然而，「勞力供應」(Labour Supply) 取決於人口結構、出生率及勞動人口參與率等長期因素，不會單純因為經濟處於 A 點而自動增加。學生常混淆「勞力供應」與「就業水平」。

During an economic expansion, firms increase production, leading to a rise in Labour Demand, and employment increases. However, "Labour Supply" depends on long-term factors like demographics, birth rates, and labor force participation rates. It does not automatically increase simply because the economy is at point A. Students often confuse "Labour Supply" with "Employment Level".

正確 / Correct 選項 (2) 貨幣的購買力正在減少 / Purchasing power of money is decreasing

A 點顯示經濟增長強勁（高於平均）。強勁的經濟增長通常伴隨總需求的急劇上升，從而推高一般物價水平（通貨膨脹）。當物價上升，每一元能買到的東西減少，代表貨幣購買力下降。

Point A shows strong economic growth (above average). Strong economic growth is typically accompanied by a rapid increase in aggregate demand, which pushes up the general price level (inflation). When prices rise, the amount of goods that one dollar can buy decreases, meaning the purchasing power of money is decreasing.

錯誤 / Incorrect 選項 (3) 政府支出正在增加 / Government expenditure is increasing

政府支出是一項財政政策 (Fiscal Policy)。在經濟過熱或急速增長時（如 A 點），政府為了防止通脹惡化，通常會實施緊縮性財政政策，例如減少政府支出。因此，政府支出正在增加並非此階段的必然特徵。

Government expenditure is a Fiscal Policy tool. When the economy is overheating or growing rapidly (like at point A), the government typically implements contractionary fiscal policy to prevent worsening inflation, such as decreasing government expenditure. Therefore, an increase in government spending is not an inherent feature of this phase.

正確 / Correct 選項 (4) 公司的商業信心正在增加 / Business confidence of companies is increasing

在經濟增長率高且持續上升的時期，市民收入增加，消費意欲強。企業預期未來的銷售額和利潤會上升，因此商業信心（投資意欲）會自然增加。

During periods of high and rising economic growth, citizens' incomes increase, leading to strong consumer sentiment. Firms expect future sales and profits to rise, so business confidence (and investment willingness) naturally increases.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題的關鍵點 (Pivot Point) 在於分辨圖表的 Y 軸。考評局非常喜歡考驗學生能否分辨「總量 (Level)」與「變動率 (Percentage Change)」。圖中 A 點處於高位，代表「增長極快」，而不是「GDP 最大」。

The Pivot Point of solving this lies in identifying the Y-axis of the graph. The HKEAA loves testing if students can distinguish between "Level" and "Percentage Change". Point A is at a high position, meaning "growing very fast", not "maximum GDP".

拆解步驟 / Steps:

  1. 確認 Y 軸為增長率。A 點在虛線之上，屬經濟擴張/繁榮期。
    Confirm Y-axis is growth rate. Point A is above the dashed line, indicating expansion/boom.
  2. 逐一審視選項是否符合擴張期的特徵。
    Examine each option to see if it fits the features of an expansion.
  3. 運用排除法：看到 (1) 勞力「供應」立即排除，直接刪去 A 和 B 選項。
    Use elimination: Spotting labour "supply" in (1) makes it incorrect, instantly eliminating options A and B.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱一： 混淆「勞力需求」與「勞力供應」。經濟好會令人容易找工作（需求大），但不會令整個社會突然多了一批勞動人口（供應穩定）。

Trap 1: Confusing "Labour Demand" with "Labour Supply". A good economy makes it easier to find jobs (high demand), but it doesn't suddenly create a new batch of working-age population in the society (supply is stable).

陷阱二： 誤以為經濟好＝政府會花更多錢。政府的行為屬於人為干預的政策，而不是市場自動調節的特徵。政府反而可能在經濟差時才增加支出來救市。

Trap 2: Assuming good economy = government spends more money. Government action is a discretionary policy intervention, not an automatic market feature. In fact, governments might increase spending when the economy is bad to stimulate it.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 圖表中曲線下跌的部分是否代表經濟衰退 (Recession)？

A: 不一定！只要曲線仍然在 X 軸（增長率為 0%）之上，即使曲線下跌，也只代表增長放緩，但 GDP 的總量依然在增加。只有當曲線跌穿 X 軸（負增長），才正式進入衰退期。

Q: Does the downward sloping part of the curve represent a recession?
A: Not necessarily! As long as the curve remains above the X-axis (0% growth rate), a downward slope only means the growth is slowing down, but total GDP is still increasing. It only officially enters a recession when the curve falls below the X-axis (negative growth).

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺法 (The One-Second Kill): 看到這類問「經濟特徵」的題目，立刻在腦海中區分哪些是「自然結果」(Natural Outcomes) 哪些是「人為政策」(Discretionary Policies)。(3) 政府支出是政策，(1) 勞力供應是人口結構，兩者都不會隨經濟週期自動發生同向變動。一秒排除了 (1) 和 (3)，答案直接鎖定 D！

Shortcut: When seeing questions asking for "economic features", immediately mentally separate "Natural Outcomes" from "Discretionary Policies". (3) Gov expenditure is a policy, (1) Labour supply is demographics; neither automatically moves in tandem with the business cycle. Eliminating (1) and (3) instantly locks the answer to D!
