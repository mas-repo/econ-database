1. The Question (題目)

假設大豆的國際價格是$20。某小型開放經濟對進口大豆徵收從量關稅$5。下圖顯示該經濟的大豆的供應和需求曲線。下列哪項有關大豆的陳述是正確的？

Suppose the world price of soybeans is $20. A small open economy imposes a per unit tariff of $5 on imported soybeans. The diagram below shows the economy’s supply and demand curves of soybean. Which of the following statements about soybeans is correct?
  1. 在徵收關稅後，本地生產商的總收入是$400。
    The total revenue of domestic producers is $400 after the imposition of tariff.
  2. 包括關稅在內的進口總值是$500。
    The total import value inclusive of tariff is $500.
  3. 若關稅調高至$15，該經濟會變成大豆出口國。
    If the tariff is raised to $15, the economy will become an exporter of soybeans.
  4. 若取消關稅，進口總值會是$400。
    If the tariff is abolished, the total import value will be $400.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

處理這類國際貿易題目時，我們必須清楚區分「世界價格」與「連關稅後的本地價格」。對於一個小型開放經濟體，關稅會直接加在世界價格之上。

When dealing with international trade questions, we must clearly distinguish between the "world price" and the "domestic price inclusive of tariff". For a small open economy, a tariff is directly added on top of the world price.
$$P_t = P_w + t$$

其中 \(P_t\) 是徵收關稅後的價格，\(P_w\) 是世界價格，\(t\) 是從量關稅。

Where \(P_t\) is the price after tariff, \(P_w\) is the world price, and \(t\) is the per unit tariff.

進口量 (Volume of Imports): 本地需求量減去本地供應量。

$$M = Q_d - Q_s$$

進口總值 (Total Import Value): 要特別注意題目問的是「包含關稅」還是「不包含關稅」。

  • 包含關稅 (Inclusive of tariff) = \( P_t \times M \)
  • 不含關稅 (Exclusive of tariff) = \( P_w \times M \)
Total Import Value: Pay close attention to whether the question asks for the value "inclusive" or "exclusive" of the tariff. Inclusive = \( P_t \times M \); Exclusive = \( P_w \times M \).

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是：Option B

A 錯誤 (False)

徵收關稅後，本地市場價格 \(P_t = \$20 + \$5 = \$25\)。從圖表可見，當價格為 $25 時，本地供應量 \(Q_s = 30\) 單位。因此，本地生產商的總收入 = \( \$25 \times 30 = \$750 \)，而不是 $400。($400 是未徵收關稅前，即價格為 $20 時的總收入：$20 × 20)。

After the tariff, the domestic price \(P_t = \$20 + \$5 = \$25\). From the graph, at $25, domestic quantity supplied \(Q_s = 30\) units. Thus, total revenue of domestic producers = \( \$25 \times 30 = \$750 \), not $400. ($400 is the revenue before the tariff at \(P_w = \$20\): $20 × 20).

B 正確 (True)

在徵收關稅後 (\(P_t = \$25\))，本地需求量 \(Q_d = 50\)，本地供應量 \(Q_s = 30\)。進口量 \(M = 50 - 30 = 20\) 單位。
題目詢問的是「包括關稅在內的進口總值」，所以我們要用連稅價格計算：\( \$25 \times 20 = \$500 \)。

At the tariff-inclusive price (\(P_t = \$25\)), domestic quantity demanded \(Q_d = 50\) and quantity supplied \(Q_s = 30\). The import volume \(M = 50 - 30 = 20\) units.
The question specifically asks for the import value inclusive of tariff, so we use the tariff price: \( \$25 \times 20 = \$500 \).

C 錯誤 (False)

若關稅調高至 $15，加上關稅後的價格看似會變成 \( \$20 + \$15 = \$35 \)。然而，本地的封閉均衡價格 (Autarky price) 只是 $30。因為國際市場的收購價依然是世界價格 $20，本地生產商無法以 $35 的高價出口圖利。因此，過高的關稅（懲罰性關稅）只會令進口量跌至 0，經濟體回復到閉關自守的狀態（價格停留在 $30），而不會變成出口國。

If the tariff is raised to $15, the tariff-inclusive price seems to be \( \$20 + \$15 = \$35 \). However, the domestic equilibrium (autarky) price is only $30. Since the global market still only pays the world price of $20, domestic producers cannot export for a profit at $35. Therefore, a prohibitive tariff simply drops imports to zero, returning the economy to autarky (price settles at $30). It will not become an exporter.

D 錯誤 (False)

若取消關稅，價格會跌回世界價格 \(P_w = \$20\)。此時，\(Q_d = 60\)，\(Q_s = 20\)。進口量 \(M = 60 - 20 = 40\) 單位。
進口總值 = \( \$20 \times 40 = \$800 \)，而不是 $400。

If the tariff is abolished, the price falls back to the world price \(P_w = \$20\). At this price, \(Q_d = 60\) and \(Q_s = 20\). The import volume \(M = 60 - 20 = 40\) units.
Total import value = \( \$20 \times 40 = \$800 \), not $400.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類圖表題的核心在於「畫橫線」。解題樞紐 (Pivot Point) 是找出這兩個關鍵價格：

  1. 世界價格 \(P_w = \$20\) （未有徵稅的基準線）
  2. 徵稅後價格 \(P_t = \$20 + \$5 = \$25\) （有徵稅的基準線）

在圖上這兩個價格位置畫兩條水平線，然後分別找出這兩條線與 S 曲線和 D 曲線的交點（即對應的 \(Q_s\) 和 \(Q_d\)）。只要將圖表數據轉換成這四個數字，所有選項都能輕易用公式計算出來驗證。

The core strategy for this type of graphical question is "drawing horizontal lines". The Pivot Point is identifying these two key prices: 1) World price \(P_w = \$20\), and 2) Tariff-inclusive price \(P_t = \$25\). Draw horizontal lines at these prices on your graph to find the corresponding \(Q_s\) and \(Q_d\). Once you extract these four numbers, every option can be easily verified using simple formulas.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • ❌ 陷阱一：混淆「包含關稅」與「不含關稅」的進口價值。
    很多同學計算 Option B 時，會直接用世界價格乘進口量 (\( \$20 \times 20 = \$400 \))，然後誤以為 B 是錯的。必須留意題目指定是「包括關稅在內 (inclusive of tariff)」。
  • ❌ 陷阱二：以為高關稅能催生出口。
    (對應 Option C) 關稅只能用作「擋住進口」，它無法改變世界其他國家願意支付的價格 ($20)。當關稅過高（超過本地均衡價格與世界價格的差額），國家只會停止進口，不會變成出口國。

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

🙋‍♂️ 學生問 (Q): 為什麼計算 Option A (本地生產商的總收入) 時，我們是用 $25 乘 30，而不是用 $20 乘 30？

👨‍🏫 導師答 (A): 因為在徵收關稅後，外國貨的售價被迫提高到 $25，這形成了一個「保護傘」。本地生產商因此也能夠以 $25 的價格在本地市場出售大豆。所以他們賺取的單價是 $25，而非世界價格的 $20。

Q: Why do we use $25 instead of $20 when calculating Option A (total revenue of domestic producers)?
A: The tariff raises the price of imported goods to $25, creating a "protective umbrella." Domestic producers can now legally sell their soybeans at this higher local market price of $25. Therefore, the unit price they receive is $25, not the world price of $20.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

💡 速解技巧：
看到這種題目，不要先急著看選項！第一時間在試卷圖表上寫下兩組數據：

  • @ P = 20 (無稅): \(Q_s = 20\), \(Q_d = 60\), \(M = 40\)
  • @ P = 25 (有稅): \(Q_s = 30\), \(Q_d = 50\), \(M = 20\)

完成這十秒鐘的數據提取後，再逐一核對 A, B, C, D。你會發現計算過程變得無比直觀，極大降低「眼殘」看錯圖表刻度的機會！
