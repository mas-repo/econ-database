HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Unemployment Rate (失業率) | Difficulty: ★★★☆☆

The Question (題目)

假設某經濟的失業率是8%。下列哪些會令失業率上升？
Suppose the unemployment rate of an economy is 8%. Which of the following would increase the unemployment rate?
  1. 15%的新畢業生未能成功找到工作。 (15% of new graduates could not find jobs.)
  2. 一些僱主結束其本地業務並移居外國。 (Some employers closed down businesses and migrated.)
  3. 一些兼職的工人因為生產自動化而失去工作。 (Part-time workers lost jobs due to automation.)

Correct Answer: D (1), (2) & (3)

Concept Review (概念重溫)

$$ u = \frac{U}{U + E} \times 100\% $$
  • \(u\): Unemployment Rate (失業率)
  • \(U\): Unemployed Population (失業人口)
  • \(E\): Employed Population (就業人口)
  • \(U+E\): Labor Force (勞動人口)

Detailed Analysis (詳細分析)

(1) 15% of new graduates could not find jobs

Analogy: Test Scores (考試平均分)
分析： 這是關於「加權平均數」(Weighted Average) 的概念。
想像全班原本的平均分是 8 分（代表原本 8% 失業率）。現在有一批新轉校生加入，這批新學生的平均分是 15 分（代表新畢業生 15% 失業率）。
當你把「高分」的學生加入「低分」的班級，全班的總平均分一定會被拉高。 Analysis: This is about "Weighted Average". Imagine a class with an original average score of 8 (representing the 8% rate). A group of new students joins with an average score of 15 (representing the 15% rate of graduates). Adding a "higher score" group to a "lower score" group must pull the overall average up.

Result: Rate Increases (上升)

(2) Employers closed down and migrated

Logic: Numerator/Denominator Check
分析： 這選項有兩層影響，我們需要分開考慮「僱主」與「僱員」。 Analysis: This option has two layers. We need to consider "Employers" and "Employees" separately.
第一層：僱主本人 (Layer 1: The Employers themselves)
僱主屬於就業人口 (\(E\))。當他們移居外國，他們離開了勞動人口。
公式：\( u = \frac{U}{U + E \downarrow} \)。
分子不變，分母變小 \(\rightarrow\) 數值變大。單單是僱主離開，已令失業率上升。 Employers are part of \(E\). When they migrate, they leave the labor force. Formula: \( u = \frac{U}{U + E \downarrow} \). Numerator constant, denominator shrinks \(\rightarrow\) Rate rises.
第二層：被解僱的員工 (Layer 2: The Laid-off Employees)
題目提到「結束業務」(Closed down)，暗示員工會失業。
如果員工留在本地找工作：\(E \downarrow\) (失去工作) 轉變為 \(U \uparrow\) (正在找工作)。
公式：\( u = \frac{U \uparrow}{U \uparrow + E \downarrow} \)。
注意：分母 (勞動人口) 不變，但分子 (失業人口) 增加了 \(\rightarrow\) 失業率大幅上升。 "Closed down" implies employees lose jobs. If they stay and look for work: \(E\) becomes \(U\). Numerator rises, Denominator stays same \(\rightarrow\) Rate rises significantly.

結論： 無論只看僱主，還是考慮埋員工，兩者都推高失業率。 Conclusion: Whether looking at employers only or including employees, both factors push the rate up.

(3) Part-time workers lost jobs due to automation

Logic: Scenario Testing
分析： 兼職工人失去工作。這裡有兩個可能的情況，我們來驗證是否都會令失業率上升。 Analysis: Part-time workers lose jobs. There are two possible scenarios. Let's verify if both lead to an increase.
情況 (Scenario) 數學變化 (Math Change) 結果 (Result)
A. 繼續找工作
(Continue seeking work) 		\(E \downarrow, U \uparrow\) (勞動人口不變)
\( u = \frac{U \uparrow}{L} \) 		上升 (Increases)
分子增加
B. 放棄尋找/離開勞動市場
(Give up / Leave labor force) 		\(E \downarrow\) (U 不變)
\( u = \frac{U}{U + E \downarrow} \) 		上升 (Increases)
分母變小
注意： 情況 B 中，雖然沒有人變成「失業人士」(U 沒有增加)，但因為「就業人士」(E) 減少了，導致總勞動人口這個分母變小。在數學上，\( \frac{8}{100} = 8\% \)，如果變成 \( \frac{8}{99} \)，數值是大於 8% 的。 Note: In Scenario B, even though U didn't increase, E decreased, shrinking the denominator. Mathematically, \( \frac{8}{99} > \frac{8}{100} \).
