Quantity Theory of Money (貨幣數量論) Growth Rate (增長率) Nominal vs Real Output (名義與實質產出)

題目 The Question

細閱上圖。根據貨幣數量論，下列哪項陳述是正確的？

A. 實質產出正在上升。
B. 名義產出正在下降。
C. 貨幣供應正在下降。
D. 貨幣流動速度正在下降。

Study the diagram above. According to the quantity theory of money, which of the following statements is correct?

A. Real output is increasing.
B. Nominal output is decreasing.
C. Money supply is decreasing.
D. The velocity of circulation of money is decreasing.

📚 概念重溫 Concept Review

要解答這題，我們必須熟悉貨幣數量論 (Quantity Theory of Money, QTM) 的方程式及其增長率版本。

To solve this, we must be familiar with the Quantity Theory of Money (QTM) equation and its growth rate version.
交易方程式 (Equation of Exchange): $$ M \times V = P \times Y $$
  • \( M \): 貨幣供應 (Money Supply)
  • \( V \): 貨幣流動速度 (Velocity of Circulation)
  • \( P \): 一般物價水平 (General Price Level)
  • \( Y \): 實質產出 / 實質 GDP (Real Output / Real GDP)
  • \( P \times Y \): 名義產出 / 名義 GDP (Nominal Output / Nominal GDP)

將上述方程式轉換為百分比增長率 (Percentage Growth Rate) 的形式：

Converting the above equation into Percentage Growth Rate form:
$$ \% \Delta M + \% \Delta V = \% \Delta P + \% \Delta Y $$

核心假設 (Core Assumption): 根據貨幣數量論，貨幣流動速度被假設為常數 (Constant)。因此，其增長率為零：\( \% \Delta V = 0 \)。

Core Assumption: According to QTM, the velocity of circulation is assumed to be constant. Therefore, its growth rate is zero: \( \% \Delta V = 0 \).

✅ 答案與詳細分析 The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案是：Option A

The correct answer is: Option A

A. 實質產出正在上升 (Real output is increasing) - CORRECT

根據圖表，代表「貨幣供應」的線高於「一般物價水平」的線。這意味著貨幣供應的增長率大於一般物價水平的增長率，即 \( \% \Delta M > \% \Delta P \)。
將 \( \% \Delta V = 0 \) 代入增長率方程式，我們得到：
\( \% \Delta M = \% \Delta P + \% \Delta Y \)
因此， \( \% \Delta Y = \% \Delta M - \% \Delta P \)。
因為 \( \% \Delta M > \% \Delta P \)，所以 \( \% \Delta Y > 0 \)。
實質產出的增長率大於零（正數），代表實質產出正在上升。選項 A 正確。

According to the diagram, the line for "Money supply" is above the line for "General price level". This means the growth rate of money supply is greater than the growth rate of the general price level, i.e., \( \% \Delta M > \% \Delta P \).
Substituting \( \% \Delta V = 0 \) into the growth rate equation, we get:
\( \% \Delta M = \% \Delta P + \% \Delta Y \)
Therefore, \( \% \Delta Y = \% \Delta M - \% \Delta P \).
Since \( \% \Delta M > \% \Delta P \), it must be true that \( \% \Delta Y > 0 \).
A positive growth rate (\( > 0 \)) means real output is increasing. Option A is correct.

B. 名義產出正在下降 (Nominal output is decreasing) - INCORRECT

名義產出是 \( P \times Y \)。其增長率為 \( \% \Delta P + \% \Delta Y \)。
從方程式可知，名義產出的增長率 = \( \% \Delta M + \% \Delta V \)。由於 \( \% \Delta V = 0 \)，名義產出增長率 = 貨幣供應增長率 (\( \% \Delta M \))。
圖中顯示 \( \% \Delta M \) 處於 x 軸之上（即正數，\( > 0 \)）。既然增長率是正數，名義產出正在上升，而非下降。

Nominal output is \( P \times Y \). Its growth rate is \( \% \Delta P + \% \Delta Y \).
From the equation, the growth rate of nominal output = \( \% \Delta M + \% \Delta V \). Since \( \% \Delta V = 0 \), the growth rate of nominal output = growth rate of money supply (\( \% \Delta M \)).
The diagram shows \( \% \Delta M \) is above the x-axis (i.e., it is a positive number, \( > 0 \)). Since the growth rate is positive, nominal output is increasing, not decreasing.

C. 貨幣供應正在下降 (Money supply is decreasing) - INCORRECT

圖表的 y 軸是「增長率 (%)」，而不是「數值」。雖然代表貨幣供應的線向下傾斜（表示增長速度放緩），但整條線依然在 x 軸之上（即增長率大於 0）。只要增長率為正數，總量就依然在增加。

The y-axis of the diagram represents "Growth rate (%)", not "absolute value". Although the line for money supply is downward sloping (meaning growth is slowing down), the entire line remains above the x-axis (i.e., growth rate is greater than 0). As long as the growth rate is positive, the total amount is still increasing.

D. 貨幣流動速度正在下降 (The velocity of circulation of money is decreasing) - INCORRECT

根據 DSE 課程中的貨幣數量論，我們必須假設貨幣流動速度是恆定的 (constant)。因此，它的增長率是零，不會上升或下降。

According to the quantity theory of money in the DSE syllabus, we must assume that the velocity of circulation is constant. Therefore, its growth rate is zero; it is neither increasing nor decreasing.

🔍 題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

這類題目的「破題關鍵 (Pivot Point)」有兩個：

  1. 看清 Y 軸 (Y-axis check): Y軸是「增長率」，不是「數值」。任何在 x 軸以上的線，無論是向上斜還是向下斜，都代表該變量正在增加。這直接排除了選項 C。
  2. 線條的上下關係 (Relative Position): 在圖表中，貨幣供應增長率的線一直在一般物價水平的線之上。這代表 \( \% \Delta M > \% \Delta P \)，這是推導出實質產出 (\( Y \)) 正在上升的關鍵證據。
The "Pivot Points" for this type of question are:
  1. Y-axis check: The y-axis is "Growth rate", not absolute level. Any line above the x-axis, whether sloping up or down, means the variable is increasing. This immediately eliminates Option C.
  2. Relative Position: The line for money supply growth rate is consistently above the line for general price level growth rate. This signifies \( \% \Delta M > \% \Delta P \), which is the crucial evidence needed to deduce that real output (\( Y \)) is increasing.

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

陷阱：將「增長率下降」與「數值下降」混淆。
很多學生看到貨幣供應的線「向下斜」，就直覺選擇了 C (貨幣供應下降)。
比喻：想像你駕駛汽車，原本時速 100km/h，現在減速到 50km/h。你的「速度（增長率）」是在下降（線向下斜），但你的車依然在向前走，你的「行駛總距離（數值）」依然在增加！

Trap: Confusing "decreasing growth rate" with "decreasing absolute value".
Many students see the money supply line "sloping downwards" and intuitively choose C (Money supply is decreasing).
Analogy: Imagine driving a car. You decelerate from 100km/h to 50km/h. Your "speed (growth rate)" is decreasing (downward sloping line), but your car is still moving forward, so your "total distance traveled (absolute value)" is still increasing!

🙋‍♂️ 學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

問 (Q): 如果這兩條線在未來的某一年相交（交叉）了，那會怎樣？
(What if these two lines cross each other in a future year?)

答 (A): 如果兩條線交叉，代表在交叉點的那一刻，\( \% \Delta M = \% \Delta P \)。根據公式，那時 \( \% \Delta Y = 0 \)，實質產出沒有改變。如果在交叉點之後，「一般物價水平」的線高於「貨幣供應」的線（即 \( \% \Delta P > \% \Delta M \)），那麼 \( \% \Delta Y \) 就會變成負數，實質產出就會開始下降！

Answer: If the lines cross, it means at that intersection point, \( \% \Delta M = \% \Delta P \). According to the formula, \( \% \Delta Y \) would be \( 0 \) at that exact moment (real output is unchanged). If, after crossing, the "General price level" line goes above the "Money supply" line (i.e., \( \% \Delta P > \% \Delta M \)), then \( \% \Delta Y \) would become negative, and real output would start to decrease!

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

在考場上遇到「增長率 (Growth Rate)」的圖表題：

  1. 立刻在圖表的 X 軸上畫一條粗線，並標記 0。
  2. 只要線條在 0 之上，對應的變數就在上升（立刻排除 C）。
  3. 由於題目提到「貨幣數量論 (QTM)」，立刻在腦中浮現 \( \% \Delta V = 0 \)，這可以直接排除 D。
  4. 剩下 A 和 B 之間，因為線條都在 0 之上，名義變量（如名義產出）通常都會上升，所以 B 也錯了。利用消除法，幾秒鐘就能選出 A。
When encountering "Growth Rate" graphs in the exam:
  1. Immediately draw a thick line on the X-axis and label it 0.
  2. As long as a line is above 0, the corresponding variable is increasing (instantly eliminate C).
  3. Since the question mentions "Quantity Theory of Money", immediately recall \( \% \Delta V = 0 \), which eliminates D.
  4. Between A and B: since lines are above 0, nominal variables (like nominal output) are generally increasing, so B is also wrong. By elimination, you can pick A in seconds.
