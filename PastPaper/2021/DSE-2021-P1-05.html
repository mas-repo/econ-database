HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic (課題): Wage Payment Methods (工資支付方法) / Labour Economics (勞工經濟學)

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐⭐ (Moderate 中等)

Key Concepts (核心概念): Time Rate (計時工資), Piece Rate (計件工資), Quality Control (品質控制)

1. The Question (題目)

工廠A (Factory A) 工廠B (Factory B)
聘用的工人數目
Number of workers employed		 90 78
每位工人的工作時數／每月
Working hours per worker/month		 125 155
總產出／每月
Total output/month		 79 850 82 800
產出質素不達標的百分比
Percentage of output with quality below standard		 37% 11%

參閱上表，下列哪項最能解釋工廠A和工廠B產出質素的差別？

Refer to the above table. Which of the following can best explain the difference in quality of the outputs between Factory A and Factory B?

  • A. 與工廠B相比，工廠A聘請較少勞工服務。
    Factory A employs less labour service than Factory B.
  • B. 與工廠B相比，工廠A進行較高度的分工。
    Factory A practices a higher degree of division of labour than Factory B.
  • C. 與工廠B相比，工廠A機械化的程度較高。
    The degree of mechanisation of Factory A is higher than that of Factory B.
  • D. 工廠A是以件計薪而工廠B是以時計薪。
    Factory A uses piece rate while Factory B uses time rate.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解決這條題目，我們需要明白不同工資支付方法 (Wage Payment Methods) 對工人行為和產品質素的影響。

To solve this question, we must understand how different wage payment methods affect worker behaviour and product quality.

1. 計件工資 (Piece Rate): 工人的收入取決於他們的生產數量。這會鼓勵工人提高生產速度，但往往會犧牲產品質素，因為他們只想盡快完成更多件數。監管質素的成本較高。

1. Piece Rate: Workers' income depends on the quantity they produce. This incentivizes workers to speed up production, often at the expense of quality, as they just want to complete as many pieces as possible. The cost of monitoring quality is high.

2. 計時工資 (Time Rate): 工人的收入取決於他們的工作時間，與產量無直接關係。由於趕工並不會帶來額外收入，工人通常會維持較穩定的產品質素。監管工人偷懶的成本較高。

2. Time Rate: Workers' income depends on their working hours, regardless of output. Since rushing does not bring extra income, workers usually maintain a more stable product quality. The cost of monitoring shirking is high.

計算公式 (Formulas):

$$ \text{Total Wage}_{\text{ (Piece Rate)}} = \text{Wage Rate per Unit} \times \text{Total Quantity Produced} $$ $$ \text{Total Wage}_{\text{ (Time Rate)}} = \text{Hourly Wage Rate} \times \text{Total Hours Worked} $$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 Correct Answer: Option D

A 錯誤 (Incorrect): 我們可以計算勞工服務 \(L\)。勞工服務 = 工人數目 \(\times\) 工作時數。工廠A的勞工服務是 \(90 \times 125 = 11,250\) 小時；工廠B是 \(78 \times 155 = 12,090\) 小時。雖然選項A的陳述是事實（A的勞工服務確實較少），但「勞工服務較少」並不能解釋為什麼「產品質素較差」。這是一個答非所問的選項。

We can calculate labour service \(L\). Labour service = Number of workers \(\times\) Working hours. Factory A is \(90 \times 125 = 11,250\) hours; Factory B is \(78 \times 155 = 12,090\) hours. Although the statement in Option A is factually true (A does have less labour service), "less labour service" cannot explain why "product quality is worse". This option does not answer the core question.

B 錯誤 (Incorrect): 高度分工通常會讓工人透過熟能生巧來提高或穩定產出質素，而不是導致高達 37% 的不達標率。因此這與數據不符。

A high degree of division of labour usually allows workers to improve or stabilize output quality through practice makes perfect, rather than leading to a 37% below-standard rate. Therefore, this contradicts the data.

C 錯誤 (Incorrect): 機械化程度高通常意味著產品會更加標準化，次品率（不達標率）應該較低。工廠A的不達標率高達 37%，這與高機械化的特徵相反。

A higher degree of mechanisation usually means products are more standardized, and the defect rate should be lower. Factory A's defect rate is as high as 37%, which is contrary to the characteristics of high mechanisation.

D 正確 (Correct): 這是最佳解釋。工廠A使用計件工資，工人為了賺取更多工資，會盲目追求產量（求量不求質），導致不達標比率極高（37%）。相反，工廠B使用計時工資，工人沒有誘因去趕工犧牲質素，因此不達標比率較低（11%）。

This is the best explanation. Factory A uses piece rate, so workers blindly pursue quantity to earn higher wages (prioritizing quantity over quality), leading to an extremely high defect rate (37%). Conversely, Factory B uses time rate, so workers have no incentive to rush and sacrifice quality, resulting in a lower defect rate (11%).

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

🎯 關鍵樞紐 (The Pivot Point): 題目的核心並不是要求你計算總產量或勞工時數，而是要求你解釋「質素的差別 (difference in quality)」。數據中最大的亮點是工廠A的不達標率 (37%) 遠高於工廠B (11%)。

🎯 The Pivot Point: The core of the question is not asking you to calculate total output or labour hours, but to explain the "difference in quality". The biggest highlight in the data is that Factory A's below-standard rate (37%) is drastically higher than Factory B's (11%).

🌫️ 擾兵之計 (Distractors): 表格中的前三行（工人數目、工作時數、總產出）主要是用來混淆視聽的。特別是選項A，它引誘你進行計算，當你發現計算結果與選項A吻合時，很容易就會掉進陷阱選了A，忘記了題目問的是「質素」。

🌫️ Distractors: The first three rows in the table (Number of workers, working hours, total output) are mainly smoke and mirrors. Option A in particular tempts you to calculate. When you find the calculation matches Option A, it's easy to fall into the trap and choose A, forgetting the question is actually asking about "quality".

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：事實正確 ≠ 邏輯正確 (Factual truth ≠ Logical explanation)

很多學生會選A，因為他們計算過：
工廠A勞工服務: \(90 \times 125 = 11,250\)
工廠B勞工服務: \(78 \times 155 = 12,090\)
11,250 確實小於 12,090。但請記住，選擇題的答案必須回答題目所問的問題。勞工總時數較少，絕不能解釋為什麼產品會充滿瑕疵。

Trap: Factual truth ≠ Logical explanation

Many students choose A because they calculated it:
Factory A labour service: \(90 \times 125 = 11,250\)
Factory B labour service: \(78 \times 155 = 12,090\)
11,250 is indeed less than 12,090. But remember, the answer to an MC question must answer what the prompt is asking. Having fewer total labour hours cannot explain why the products are full of defects.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

🤔 學生問：「如果題目問的是『為什麼工廠A的總產出較低』，那麼選項A會是正確答案嗎？」

🤔 Student Asks: "If the question asked 'Why is Factory A's total output lower?', would Option A be the correct answer?"

💡 導師答：「是的！如果題目改變了焦點，詢問總產出量 \(Q\) 下降的原因，那麼投入的勞工服務（生產要素）減少，確實可以解釋總產出的下降。所以審題時，找出題目究竟在問『質(Quality)』還是『量(Quantity)』非常重要！」

💡 Tutor Answers: "Yes! If the question shifted focus and asked for the reason behind the lower total output \(Q\), then a decrease in labour service (factor of production) inputted could indeed explain the drop in total output. Therefore, when reading the question, it is crucial to identify whether they are asking about 'Quality' or 'Quantity'!"

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

⚡ 秒殺技巧 (One-Second Kill Trick): 在 HKDSE 經濟科中，只要題目同時出現「產量數據 (Output data)」和「質素/次品/不達標數據 (Quality/Defect data)」，這幾乎是100%在考驗你對「計件 vs 計時」工資制度的理解。看到「質素差」，直接尋找「計件工資」的選項！

⚡ Shortcut: In HKDSE Economics, whenever a question presents both "Output data" and "Quality/Defect data" together, it is almost 100% testing your understanding of "Piece rate vs. Time rate" wage systems. When you see "poor quality", look straight for the "Piece rate" option!
