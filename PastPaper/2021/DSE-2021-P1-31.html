概念重溫 / Concept Review

要解決這類題目，你必須熟記以下貨幣定義與公式：
To solve this type of question, you must memorize the following monetary definitions and formulas:
$$MS = Cp + D$$ $$MB = Cp + AR$$ $$AR = RR + ER$$ $$RR = D \times RRR$$ $$Max\ D = \frac{AR}{RRR}$$
  • $MS$ (Money Supply / 貨幣供應): 經濟體系中的貨幣總量。
    The total amount of money in the economy.
  • $MB$ (Monetary Base / 貨幣基礎): 由中央銀行發行的強大貨幣。
    The high-powered money issued by the central bank.
  • $Cp$ (Cash in public / 公眾持有的法定貨幣): 不在銀行體系內的現金。
    Cash not held within the banking system.
  • $D$ (Deposits / 存款): 銀行內的客戶存款。
    Customer deposits in banks.
  • $AR$ (Actual Reserves / 實際儲備): 銀行實際持有的現金。
    The actual cash held by banks.
  • $RR$ (Required Reserves / 法定儲備): 銀行依法必須保留的最低現金量。
    The minimum cash banks are legally required to keep.
  • $ER$ (Excess Reserves / 超額儲備): 銀行持有的超過法定要求的現金。
    Cash held by banks above the required minimum.

答案與詳細分析 / The Answer & Detailed Analysis

Correct Option: B
第一步：計算存款 ($D$) 及法定儲備 ($RR$)
題目提供了貨幣供應 ($MS=250$) 和公眾持有現金 ($Cp=50$)。我們可先找出銀行存款：
$250 = 50 + D \Rightarrow D = 200$
有了存款，我們就可以利用法定儲備比率 ($RRR=0.2$) 找出法定儲備：
$RR = 200 \times 0.2 = 40$
Step 1: Calculate Deposits ($D$) and Required Reserves ($RR$)
The question provides Money Supply ($MS=250$) and Cash in public ($Cp=50$). We can find bank deposits first:
$250 = 50 + D \Rightarrow D = 200$
With deposits, we can use the required reserve ratio ($RRR=0.2$) to find required reserves:
$RR = 200 \times 0.2 = 40$
第二步：檢驗陳述 (2) - 實際儲備
實際儲備 ($AR$) 等於法定儲備加上超額儲備 ($ER=10$)：
$AR = RR + ER = 40 + 10 = 50$
陳述 (2) 說實際儲備是 40，這是錯誤的（40 只是法定儲備）。
Step 2: Check Statement (2) - Actual Reserves
Actual Reserves ($AR$) equal Required Reserves plus Excess Reserves ($ER=10$):
$AR = RR + ER = 40 + 10 = 50$
Statement (2) claims actual reserves are 40, which is INCORRECT (40 is only the required reserves).
第三步：檢驗陳述 (1) - 貨幣基礎
貨幣基礎 ($MB$) 等於公眾持有現金加上實際儲備：
$MB = Cp + AR = 50 + 50 = 100$
陳述 (1) 是正確的。
Step 3: Check Statement (1) - Monetary Base
Monetary Base ($MB$) equals Cash in public plus Actual Reserves:
$MB = Cp + AR = 50 + 50 = 100$
Statement (1) is CORRECT.
第四步：檢驗陳述 (3) - 最大存款金額
當銀行沒有超額儲備且沒有現金流失時，存款會達到最大值。公式為實際儲備除以法定儲備比率：
$Max\ D = \frac{AR}{RRR} = \frac{50}{0.2} = 250$
陳述 (3) 是正確的。
Step 4: Check Statement (3) - Maximum Deposits
Deposits reach their maximum when banks hold no excess reserves and there is no cash leakage. The formula is Actual Reserves divided by the Required Reserve Ratio:
$Max\ D = \frac{AR}{RRR} = \frac{50}{0.2} = 250$
Statement (3) is CORRECT.

題目拆解 / Problem Decomposition

切入點 (The Pivot Point)： 這類題目的核心往往是隱藏的存款 ($D$)。題目給了 $MS$ 和 $Cp$，但沒有直接給出存款。沒有存款，你就無法計算出儲備的相關數據。因此，解題的第一步永遠是找出 $D$。

無關資訊 (Irrelevant Info)： 在這道題目中，所有提供的數據都是必要的，沒有干擾項。但要注意數據之間的層級關係，$MS$ 是宏觀的總量，而 $ER$ 是微觀的銀行儲備細節，橋接兩者的就是「存款」。

The Pivot Point: The core of these questions is often the hidden Deposits ($D$). The question gives $MS$ and $Cp$, but doesn't explicitly state deposits. Without deposits, you cannot calculate any reserve-related figures. Therefore, step one is always finding $D$.

Irrelevant Info: In this specific question, all provided data is necessary; there are no distractors. However, pay attention to the hierarchy of data. $MS$ is a macro total, while $ER$ is a micro detail of bank reserves. The bridge between them is "Deposits".

常犯錯誤與陷阱 / Common Mistakes & Traps

  • 混淆法定儲備與實際儲備： 很多學生算出了 $RR = 40$ 就直接認為陳述 (2) 是對的，忘記了題目中還給了超額儲備 ($ER = 10$)。謹記 $AR = RR + ER$。
  • 計算最大存款時用錯分子： 學生有時會誤用貨幣基礎 ($MB$) 乘上乘數來計算最大存款。記住，只有在銀行體系內的錢（實際儲備 $AR$）才能創造存款，公眾手上的現金 ($Cp$) 是不能創造存款的。
  • Confusing Required and Actual Reserves: Many students calculate $RR = 40$ and immediately assume Statement (2) is correct, forgetting the Excess Reserves ($ER = 10$) provided in the table. Always remember $AR = RR + ER$.
  • Using the wrong numerator for Maximum Deposits: Students sometimes mistakenly use the Monetary Base ($MB$) multiplied by the multiplier to find max deposits. Remember, only money inside the banking system (Actual Reserves, $AR$) can create deposits. Cash in the public's hands ($Cp$) cannot.

學生常見疑問 / Student Q&A

Q: 為什麼計算「最大存款」時，假設超額儲備會變成零？
A: 因為銀行要創造「最大」的存款，就必須把所有閒置的資金（超額儲備）都借出去。當所有超額儲備都被借出並重新存入銀行體系，直到 $AR = RR$ 時，存款創造過程才會結束，此時存款達到數學上的最大值。

Q: Why do we assume excess reserves become zero when calculating "maximum deposits"?
A: To create the "maximum" possible deposits, banks must lend out all idle funds (excess reserves). When all excess reserves are lent out and redeposited into the banking system until $AR = RR$, the credit creation process ends, and deposits reach their mathematical maximum.
