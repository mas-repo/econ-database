主題 (Topic): 貨幣與銀行 - 香港的三級銀行體制 (Money & Banking - Three-tier Banking System in Hong Kong)

難度 (Difficulty): ★★★☆☆

核心概念 (Key Concepts): 持牌銀行 (Licensed Banks) 接受存款公司 (DTCs) 存款條件 (Deposit Requirements)

📝 題目 (The Question)

杰克想存款 $400,000，為期 5 個月。下列哪些本港的金融機構可以接受他的存款？

(1) 一家有限制牌照銀行
(2) 一家被歸類為持牌銀行的虛擬銀行
(3) 一家接受存款公司

A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

Jack wants to deposit $400,000 for 5 months. Which of the following financial institutions in Hong Kong can accept his deposit?

(1) a restricted licence bank
(2) a virtual bank which is classified as a licensed bank
(3) a deposit-taking company

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

🧠 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解答這道題目，我們必須熟記香港三級銀行體制（Three-tier Banking System）對接受存款的法定限制。香港金融管理局（HKMA）對不同級別的認可機構有嚴格的規定。

To solve this question, we must memorize the statutory restrictions on accepting deposits under Hong Kong's Three-tier Banking System. The Hong Kong Monetary Authority (HKMA) has strict regulations for different tiers of authorized institutions.
  • 持牌銀行 (Licensed Banks, LBs): 無存款金額及期限限制。虛擬銀行（Virtual Banks）只要獲發全牌照，亦屬於此類別。
    No restrictions on deposit amount and maturity. Virtual banks granted a full banking license belong to this tier.
  • 有限制牌照銀行 (Restricted Licence Banks, RLBs): 只可接受 $500,000 或以上的存款，無期限限制。
    Can only accept deposits of $500,000 or more, with no maturity restrictions.
  • 接受存款公司 (Deposit-taking Companies, DTCs): 只可接受 $100,000 或以上，且最初存款期最少為 3 個月的存款。
    Can only accept deposits of $100,000 or more, with an original maturity of at least 3 months.

我們可以用以下的數學邏輯模型來表示金融機構接受存款的條件 ($A$ 代表存款金額，$T$ 代表存款期)：

We can express the conditions for financial institutions to accept deposits using the following mathematical logic model ($A$ represents the deposit amount, $T$ represents the term/maturity):
$$ \text{Accept}(A, T) = \begin{cases} \text{True}, & \text{for LBs: } \forall A, \forall T \\ \text{True}, & \text{for RLBs: } A \ge 500,000 \\ \text{True}, & \text{for DTCs: } A \ge 100,000 \land T \ge 3 \text{ months} \end{cases} $$

✅ 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option)： Option C

分析 (1)：有限制牌照銀行 (Restricted Licence bank) — 錯誤 (False)

有限制牌照銀行最低存款額要求為 $500,000。杰克只有 $400,000，未達到最低門檻，因此不能接受。

The minimum deposit requirement for a restricted licence bank is $500,000. Jack only has $400,000, which falls short of the minimum threshold, so it cannot accept his deposit.

分析 (2)：虛擬銀行 (Virtual bank classified as a licensed bank) — 正確 (True)

由於題目列明該虛擬銀行被歸類為「持牌銀行」，它享有與傳統持牌銀行相同的權利，即對存款金額和期限沒有任何限制。杰克的 $400,000 存款可被接受。

Since the question specifies the virtual bank is classified as a "licensed bank," it enjoys the same rights as traditional licensed banks, meaning there are no restrictions on deposit amount and maturity. Jack's $400,000 deposit can be accepted.

分析 (3)：接受存款公司 (Deposit-taking company) — 正確 (True)

接受存款公司的要求是：金額 $\ge$ $100,000 且 期限 $\ge$ 3個月。杰克的存款金額為 $400,000（大於 $100,000），且為期 5 個月（大於 3 個月），同時滿足兩個條件。因此可被接受。

The requirements for a deposit-taking company are: amount $\ge$ $100,000 AND maturity $\ge$ 3 months. Jack's deposit amount is $400,000 (greater than $100,000) and the term is 5 months (greater than 3 months), satisfying both conditions. Therefore, it can be accepted.

🔍 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

關鍵情報 (Pivot Point): 本題的解題核心在於同時檢視兩個數據：「$400,000」 和 「5個月」。缺一不可。

Pivot Point: The core of solving this problem lies in simultaneously examining two pieces of data: "$400,000" and "5 months". Both are indispensable.

解題步驟 (Approach):

  1. 先看金額 ($400,000)：這筆錢小於 $500,000，立刻可以淘汰 (1) RLB。
  2. 再看期限 (5個月)：因為大於 3 個月，所以 (3) DTC的條件被觸發，加上金額大於 $100,000，DTC過關。
  3. 檢查機構類別：(2) 雖然是虛擬銀行，但重點在後面的「持牌銀行」四字，所以一路綠燈。
Approach:
1. Check the amount ($400,000): It is less than $500,000, immediately eliminating (1) RLB.
2. Check the term (5 months): Since it is greater than 3 months, condition for (3) DTC is triggered. Combined with the amount being > $100,000, DTC passes.
3. Check institution type: Although (2) is a virtual bank, the keyword is "licensed bank", meaning it has a green light for everything.

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

1. 混淆 RLB 和 DTC 的金額限制： 很多同學會把兩者的最低金額記反，誤以為 RLB 才是 10 萬，DTC 是 50 萬。記住，RLB（有限制牌照銀行）多數處理大額企業融資，所以門檻最高 ($500,000)。

1. Confusing the amount limits of RLBs and DTCs: Many students mix up the minimum amounts, mistakenly thinking RLB is $100k and DTC is $500k. Remember, RLBs generally handle large corporate financing, so their threshold is the highest ($500,000).

2. 忽略 DTC 的「雙重條件」： 有些同學只看到金額大於 $100,000 就立刻選 DTC，忘記檢查「最少 3 個月」的期限條件。如果題目改成存款「2 個月」，DTC 就不能選了！

2. Ignoring the "dual conditions" of DTCs: Some students only see the amount is > $100,000 and immediately select DTC, forgetting to check the "minimum 3 months" maturity condition. If the question changed the term to "2 months," the DTC option would be incorrect!

🙋‍♂️ 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

問 (Q): 題目特別強調「虛擬銀行」，虛擬銀行的法例規管跟實體銀行不一樣嗎？

答 (A): 在香港，只要是獲金管局發牌的「持牌銀行」（無論是傳統實體銀行還是虛擬銀行），在接受存款的規定上是完全一致的。考評局在這裡加入「虛擬銀行」只是作為干擾項（Distractor），測試同學是否清楚虛擬銀行其實也屬於持牌銀行級別。

Q: The question specifically mentions a "virtual bank". Are virtual banks regulated differently from physical banks?
A: In Hong Kong, as long as it is a "licensed bank" authorized by the HKMA (whether a traditional physical bank or a virtual bank), the deposit-taking regulations are exactly the same. The HKEAA includes "virtual bank" here merely as a distractor to test if students know that virtual banks actually fall under the licensed bank tier.

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

消去法 (Process of Elimination)： 在多項選擇題中，一旦你確定某個選項是絕對錯誤的，就能快速刪去多個選項。

  • 當你看到金額是 $400,000，而你記得 RLB 的門檻是 $500,000 時，你立刻知道 (1) 必定錯誤。
  • 看看選項：A包含(1)、B包含(1)、D包含(1)。
  • 只需 5 秒鐘，你連 (2) 和 (3) 都不用看，就能直接選出正確答案 C！
Process of Elimination: In MCQs, once you confirm a statement is absolutely false, you can quickly eliminate multiple options.
- When you see the amount is $400,000, and you recall the RLB threshold is $500,000, you immediately know (1) is strictly false.
- Look at the options: A includes (1), B includes (1), D includes (1).
- In just 5 seconds, without even analyzing (2) and (3), you can directly choose the correct answer C!
