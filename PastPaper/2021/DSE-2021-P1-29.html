📊 HKDSE Economics: Macroeconomic Policies

Topic (課題): Macroeconomic Policies & Exchange Rate (宏觀經濟政策與匯率)

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)

Key Concepts (核心概念): Aggregate Demand (總需求), Devaluation (貶值), Export Volume (出口量), Employment (就業)

📝 The Question (題目)

新冠肺炎爆發令某國的就業水平和出口數量顯著下跌。下列哪項政策最能紓緩以上兩個問題？

A. 中央銀行調低貼現率。
B. 政府向18歲或以上的市民派發現金。
C. 政府對受負面影響的行業實行「就業支援計劃」。
D. 該國令其貨幣兌主要貿易伙伴的貨幣官方貶值。

The outbreak of COVID-19 results in a significant decline in employment level and the volume of exports of a country. Which of the following policies can most likely relieve the above two problems?

A. The central bank reduces the discount rate.
B. The government distributes cash to citizens aged 18 years old or above.
C. The government imposes ‘employment support scheme’ to industries that are adversely affected.
D. The country devalues its currency against the currencies of its major trading partners.

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解決這道題目，我們需要理解總需求（Aggregate Demand, AD）的組成部分以及匯率變動對國際貿易的影響。

$$AD = C + I + G + (X - M)$$

1. 總需求與就業 (AD and Employment): 當總需求 \(AD\) 增加時，廠商會增加產出，從而衍生出對勞工的更大需求，提升就業水平。

2. 貨幣貶值 (Devaluation): 當本國貨幣貶值，本國出口商品在國外（以外幣計算）的價格會下降。根據需求定律，出口數量（Volume of Exports）會上升。

To solve this question, we must understand the components of Aggregate Demand (AD) and how exchange rate movements affect international trade.

1. AD and Employment: When Aggregate Demand \(AD\) increases, firms increase output, which leads to a higher derived demand for labor, raising the employment level.

2. Devaluation: When the domestic currency devalues, the price of domestic exports in terms of foreign currency decreases. According to the law of demand, the volume of exports will increase.

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: Option D

A. 中央銀行調低貼現率 (The central bank reduces the discount rate) - ❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect)

調低貼現率屬於擴張性貨幣政策，會降低利率，刺激消費 \(C\) 和投資 \(I\)，從而增加就業。但這並不能直接且有效地解決「出口數量下跌」的問題。

Reducing the discount rate is an expansionary monetary policy. It lowers interest rates, stimulating consumption \(C\) and investment \(I\), thereby increasing employment. However, it does not directly and effectively solve the problem of "decline in the volume of exports".

B. 政府派發現金 (The government distributes cash) - ❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect)

這屬於擴張性財政政策，能增加市民的可支配收入，刺激本地消費 \(C\)，有助提升就業。然而，這對增加出口數量沒有直接幫助，甚至可能因為本地物價上升而削弱出口競爭力。

This is an expansionary fiscal policy that increases citizens' disposable income, stimulating local consumption \(C\) and helping employment. However, it does not directly help increase export volume, and might even weaken export competitiveness if domestic price levels rise.

C. 實行「就業支援計劃」 (Imposes employment support scheme) - ❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect)

這項政策直接針對就業問題，能有效保住打工仔的飯碗（紓緩就業下跌）。但是，它完全無法解決國外對本國出口商品需求減少的問題。

This policy directly targets the employment issue and effectively saves jobs. However, it completely fails to address the problem of reduced foreign demand for domestic exports.

D. 貨幣官方貶值 (Devalues its currency) - ✅ 正確 (Correct)

貨幣貶值會令本國出口貨品在外國變得更便宜，直接刺激外國對本國貨品的需求，令「出口數量」 \(X\) 上升（解決了問題二）。同時，出口 \(X\) 增加會令總需求 \(AD\) 上升，帶動本地生產，從而創造職位，提升「就業水平」（解決了問題一）。一石二鳥！

Devaluation makes domestic exports cheaper in foreign countries, directly stimulating foreign demand and increasing the "volume of exports" \(X\) (solves problem 2). Simultaneously, an increase in exports \(X\) raises Aggregate Demand \(AD\), boosting domestic production and creating jobs, which raises the "employment level" (solves problem 1). Two birds with one stone!

🔍 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (破題關鍵): 題目要求同時解決「兩個」問題：就業下跌 + 出口下跌。這是一個典型的「一石二鳥」題型。

Distractors (干擾資訊): 選項 A、B、C 都是非常好的「救市」政策，但它們都只偏重於刺激內部需求 (Domestic Demand)。學生容易因為看到「紓緩就業問題」就匆忙選擇 A、B 或 C，而忽略了題目後半部分的「出口」條件。

Pivot Point: The question requires solving "TWO" problems simultaneously: declining employment + declining exports.

Distractors: Options A, B, and C are excellent economic rescue policies, but they only focus on stimulating Domestic Demand. Students often rush to pick A, B, or C because they see "relieve employment", completely ignoring the "exports" condition in the latter half of the prompt.

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：混淆「出口價值 (Value)」與「出口數量 (Volume)」
有些學生會猶豫：「貶值一定會令出口增加嗎？如果需求彈性低怎麼辦？」注意！題目問的是「出口數量 (Volume)」。只要符合需求定律（需求曲線向下傾斜），價格下降，數量必定上升。彈性 (Elasticity) 只會影響出口的總值 (Total Value)，並不影響數量的升跌方向。

Trap: Confusing Export "Value" with Export "Volume"
Some students hesitate: "Does devaluation always increase exports? What if demand is inelastic?" Attention! The question asks about the "Volume of exports" (quantity). As long as the law of demand holds (downward-sloping demand curve), a price drop guarantees an increase in quantity. Elasticity only affects the Total Value of exports, not the direction of change in quantity.

🙋‍♂️ Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼選項 A (減息) 不能幫助出口？匯率不是會因為減息而下跌嗎？

A: 在浮動匯率制度下，減息確實可能導致資金外流，令貨幣貶值，從而間接幫助出口。但這是一個「間接」且不確定的效果（取決於資金流動性及匯率制度）。相比之下，選項 D 是「官方貶值」，直接且必然地改變匯率，是針對出口問題最直接、最有效的政策。

Q: Why doesn't Option A (cutting interest rates) help exports? Wouldn't the exchange rate drop due to lower interest rates?

A: Under a floating exchange rate system, cutting interest rates might indeed cause capital outflows, depreciating the currency and indirectly helping exports. However, this is an "indirect" and uncertain effect. In contrast, Option D is an "official devaluation", which directly and inevitably changes the exchange rate, making it the most direct and effective policy for the export problem.

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法 (Shortcut): 看到題目有「出口 (Exports)」這個外部因素 (External factor)，直接在選項中尋找與「對外貿易 / 匯率 (Trade / Exchange Rate)」相關的政策。A、B、C 全是內部政策 (Internal policies)，只有 D 涉及外部匯率，秒殺！

Shortcut: When you see the external factor "Exports" in the question, immediately look for policies related to "Trade / Exchange Rate" in the options. A, B, and C are purely internal policies, while only D involves external exchange rates. Instant kill!
