HKDSE Economics Tutorial

Topic (課題): Market Intervention (市場干預) - Price Controls

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5 Stars)

Key Concepts (核心概念): Price Ceiling (價格上限), Producer Surplus (生產者盈餘), Effective vs. Ineffective Controls (有效與無效干預)

The Question (題目)

參考以下物品 Y 的供需圖。

若政府將物品 Y 的價格上限由 \( P_1 \) 下調至 \( P_2 \)，

A. 生產者盈餘會下降。
B. 消費者盈餘會上升。
C. 總社會盈餘會維持不變。
D. 物品 Y 的超額供應會消失。

Refer to the following demand-supply diagram of Good Y.

If the government lowers the price ceiling of Good Y from \( P_1 \) to \( P_2 \),

A. the producer surplus will decrease.
B. the consumer surplus will increase.
C. the total social surplus will remain unchanged.
D. the excess supply of Good Y will be eliminated.

Concept Review (概念重溫)

解答此題的關鍵在於分辨「有效」與「無效」的價格上限，以及它們對市場均衡和盈餘的影響。

The key to answering this question is distinguishing between "effective" and "ineffective" price ceilings, and their impact on market equilibrium and surplus.
  • Price Ceiling (價格上限): 政府規定的最高合法交易價格。目的是保護消費者。
    A maximum legal trading price set by the government, aimed at protecting consumers.
  • Ineffective Price Ceiling (無效價格上限): 當價格上限設定在均衡價格之上（如圖中的 \( P_1 \)）。因為市場本來就在較低的均衡價格交易，這個上限不會產生任何實際影響。市場價格和交易量維持在均衡點。
    When a price ceiling is set above the equilibrium price (like \( P_1 \) in the diagram). Because the market naturally trades at a lower equilibrium price, this ceiling has no actual effect. Market price and quantity remain at equilibrium.
  • Effective Price Ceiling (有效價格上限): 當價格上限設定在均衡價格之下（如圖中的 \( P_2 \)）。市場價格被迫降至 \( P_2 \)，導致需求量大於供應量，形成超額需求（短缺）。交易量會下降至供應量。
    When a price ceiling is set below the equilibrium price (like \( P_2 \) in the diagram). The market price is forced down to \( P_2 \), causing quantity demanded to exceed quantity supplied, creating excess demand (shortage). The transacted quantity falls to the quantity supplied.
  • Producer Surplus (生產者盈餘): 生產者實際收取的價格與他們願意接受的最低價格（供應曲線）之間的差額。
    The difference between the price producers actually receive and the minimum price they are willing to accept (supply curve).

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option A

Option A: 正確 (Correct)。
當價格上限在 \( P_1 \) 時，它是無效的，市場在原本的均衡點交易（供需曲線相交處）。此時，生產者盈餘是均衡價格線以下、供應曲線以上的三角形面積。當價格上限下調至 \( P_2 \) 時，它變成有效。市場價格被迫降至 \( P_2 \)，且交易量減少（沿著供應曲線下降）。由於價格下跌且交易量減少，生產者盈餘（現在是 \( P_2 \) 線以下、供應曲線以上的較小三角形面積）必定會下降。

When the price ceiling is at \( P_1 \), it is ineffective, and the market trades at the original equilibrium (intersection of S and D). The producer surplus is the triangle below the equilibrium price and above the supply curve. When the ceiling is lowered to \( P_2 \), it becomes effective. The market price is forced down to \( P_2 \), and the transacted quantity decreases (moving down along the supply curve). Because both price and quantity decrease, the producer surplus (now a smaller triangle below \( P_2 \) and above the supply curve) must decrease.

Option B: 錯誤 (Incorrect)。
雖然價格下降對消費者有利，但由於實施有效價格上限後，交易量減少了（出現短缺），部分消費者無法買到物品。因此，消費者盈餘的變化是不確定的（取決於需求曲線的彈性），不能斷言它一定會上升。

Although a lower price benefits consumers, the transacted quantity decreases after an effective price ceiling is imposed (creating a shortage), meaning some consumers can no longer buy the good. Therefore, the change in consumer surplus is uncertain (depending on the elasticity of demand), and we cannot definitively say it will increase.

Option C: 錯誤 (Incorrect)。
由無效價格上限（自由市場均衡）轉變為有效價格上限，會導致交易量低於均衡數量。這會產生效率損失（Deadweight Loss），因此總社會盈餘必定會下降，而非維持不變。

Changing from an ineffective price ceiling (free market equilibrium) to an effective price ceiling reduces the transacted quantity below the equilibrium level. This creates an efficiency loss (Deadweight Loss). Therefore, the total social surplus must decrease, not remain unchanged.

Option D: 錯誤 (Incorrect)。
價格上限在 \( P_1 \) 時，市場處於均衡，沒有超額供應。當價格上限降至 \( P_2 \) 時，需求量大於供應量，會產生超額需求 (Excess Demand / Shortage)，而不是消除超額供應。

At price ceiling \( P_1 \), the market is in equilibrium; there is no excess supply. When the ceiling is lowered to \( P_2 \), quantity demanded exceeds quantity supplied, creating excess demand (shortage), not eliminating excess supply.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (解題樞紐): 辨認 \( P_1 \) 和 \( P_2 \) 的性質。

Identifying the nature of \( P_1 \) and \( P_2 \).

很多學生看到「價格上限下調」，就直接假設原本有超額供應或短缺。正確的步驟是：
1. 觀察 \( P_1 \)：在均衡點之上 ➔ 無效干預 ➔ 市場處於均衡狀態。
2. 觀察 \( P_2 \)：在均衡點之下 ➔ 有效干預 ➔ 價格下降，交易量減少。
3. 結論：從「沒有干預」變成「有干預」，生產者面對更低的價格和更少的銷售量，盈餘必定受損。

Many students see "price ceiling lowered" and assume there was already a shortage or surplus. The correct steps are:
1. Observe \( P_1 \): Above equilibrium ➔ Ineffective intervention ➔ Market is at equilibrium.
2. Observe \( P_2 \): Below equilibrium ➔ Effective intervention ➔ Price drops, transacted quantity drops.
3. Conclusion: Going from "no intervention" to "intervention", producers face lower prices and lower sales volume, so their surplus must suffer.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：混淆價格上限 (Price Ceiling) 和價格下限 (Price Floor)

學生常常記錯：
- 價格上限要設在均衡之下才有效（例如租金管制）。
- 價格下限要設在均衡之上才有效（例如最低工資）。
如果把 \( P_1 \) 誤認為有效的價格干預（例如誤當成價格下限），就會選錯答案。

Trap: Confusing Price Ceiling and Price Floor
Students often misremember:
- A price ceiling must be set below equilibrium to be effective (e.g., rent control).
- A price floor must be set above equilibrium to be effective (e.g., minimum wage).
If you mistakenly treat \( P_1 \) as an effective intervention (e.g., confusing it with a price floor), you will choose the wrong answer.
