Topic: Functions of Money (貨幣的功能) Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆

Key Concepts: Medium of Exchange, Unit of Account, Standard of Deferred Payment

題目 The Question

細閱以下有關比特幣 (Bitcoin) 的新聞撮要。

Study the following news extract about Bitcoin.

2018年，加拿大肯德基家鄉雞推出了「比特幣桶餐」。該公司開設了臉書直播，顯示購買該套餐所需的比特幣數量（如下圖所示）。該「比特幣桶餐」只能在網上購買，並會稍後外送至購買者家中。

In 2018, Kentucky Fried Chicken Canada launched 'Bitcoin Bucket'. The company set up a Facebook live feed, displaying the number of bitcoins needed to pay for the meal as in the picture below. The 'Bitcoin Bucket' could only be purchased online and would be delivered to the buyers' home later.

[Image shows a KFC bucket with a Bitcoin symbol and the number 0.0011204]

在以上個案中，比特幣用作 ______________。

In the above case, Bitcoin performed as a ______________.

  • (1) 交易媒介 (medium of exchange)
  • (2) 記帳單位 (unit of account)
  • (3) 延期支付的標準 (standard of deferred payment)
  • A. 只有 (1)、(2) / (1) and (2) only
  • B. 只有 (1)、(3) / (1) and (3) only
  • C. 只有 (2)、(3) / (2) and (3) only
  • D. (1)、(2) 及 (3) / (1), (2) and (3)

概念重溫 Concept Review

要解答這道題目，我們必須清楚分辨貨幣的三個主要功能：

To answer this question, we must clearly distinguish between the three main functions of money:

  • 交易媒介 (Medium of Exchange): 貨幣被廣泛接受作為購買物品和服務的支付工具，解決了「雙重需求偶合」(double coincidence of wants) 的問題。
    Money is widely accepted as a payment tool to buy goods and services, solving the problem of "double coincidence of wants".
  • 記帳單位 (Unit of Account): 貨幣用作量度及標示物品和服務的價值（即標價）。
    Money is used to measure and quote the value of goods and services (i.e., pricing).
  • 延期支付的標準 (Standard of Deferred Payment): 貨幣用作計算及清還未來的債務。這涉及「先取得物品/借款，後付款」的借貸行為。
    Money is used to calculate and settle future debts. This involves borrowing behavior: "getting the good/loan first, paying later".

答案與詳細分析 The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案是 Correct Option: A. 只有 (1)、(2) / (1) and (2) only

分析 (1) 交易媒介 Medium of Exchange：正確 Correct

題目指出顧客可以使用比特幣「在網上購買」(purchased online) 桶餐。這代表比特幣被肯德基接受作為支付食物的工具，因此它發揮了交易媒介的功能。

The question states that customers can use Bitcoin to "purchase online" the bucket. This means Bitcoin is accepted by KFC as a tool to pay for food, thus performing the function of a medium of exchange.

分析 (2) 記帳單位 Unit of Account：正確 Correct

圖片中清楚顯示了該桶餐的標價為「0.0011204」比特幣。因為比特幣被用作標示該套餐的價格，所以它發揮了記帳單位的功能。

The picture clearly shows the price of the bucket is quoted as "0.0011204" Bitcoin. Because Bitcoin is used to quote the price of the meal, it performs the function of a unit of account.

分析 (3) 延期支付的標準 Standard of Deferred Payment：錯誤 Incorrect

這是本題的陷阱。題目提到桶餐會「稍後外送」(delivered later)。這代表顧客是「先付款，後收貨」（預繳/現貨交易但延遲交貨）。延期支付的標準必須涉及債務，即「先收貨，後付款」（例如使用信用卡或分期付款）。因為這裡沒有涉及借貸或未來償還債務，所以比特幣沒有用作延期支付的標準。

This is the trap of the question. The text mentions the bucket would be "delivered later". This means the customer "pays first, receives goods later" (prepayment/spot transaction with delayed delivery). The standard of deferred payment MUST involve debt, i.e., "receiving goods first, paying later" (like using a credit card or installment). Since there is no borrowing or settling future debt involved here, Bitcoin is not used as a standard of deferred payment.

題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

解答這類「貨幣功能」的題目，關鍵在於在文字中尋找對應的關鍵字 (Keywords)：

To solve this type of "functions of money" question, the key is to look for corresponding keywords in the text:

  • 看到「標價 / 顯示所需的數量」(displaying the number... needed) \(\rightarrow\) 記帳單位 (Unit of Account)
  • 看到「購買 / 支付」(purchased / pay for) \(\rightarrow\) 交易媒介 (Medium of Exchange)
  • 看到「借貸 / 信用卡 / 分期付款 / 償還」(loan / credit card / installment / settle debt) \(\rightarrow\) 延期支付的標準 (Standard of Deferred Payment)

本題的「轉捩點」(Pivot Point)在於準確理解「稍後外送」(delivered later) 的經濟學含義。時間差出現在「交貨」而非「付款」，因此不構成債務。

The Pivot Point of this question lies in accurately understanding the economic meaning of "delivered later". The time lag occurs in "delivery" rather than "payment", so it does not constitute a debt.

常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

陷阱：被「稍後」(Later) 一詞誤導

很多同學一看到題目中有「稍後」(later) 或「未來」(future) 等字眼，就條件反射地選擇「延期支付的標準」。請記住，必須是「支付」(Payment) 被延期（即產生債務），才算數。如果是「交貨」(Delivery) 被延期，那只是預繳 (Prepayment)，並不涉及延期支付的標準。

Trap: Misled by the word "Later"
Many students reflexively choose "standard of deferred payment" as soon as they see words like "later" or "future" in the question. Remember, it MUST be the "Payment" that is deferred (i.e., creating a debt) for it to count. If the "Delivery" is deferred, it is merely a prepayment and does not involve the standard of deferred payment.

學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

Q: 如果我用信用卡在網上購買這個「比特幣桶餐」，這會涉及延期支付的標準嗎？

Q: If I use a credit card to buy this 'Bitcoin Bucket' online, would it involve the standard of deferred payment?

A: 會的！但要注意主體是甚麼。如果你用信用卡，你是向銀行借「法定貨幣」(Fiat money，如港幣/加幣) 來付款，然後未來再還款給銀行。在這種情況下，發揮「延期支付的標準」功能的是法定貨幣，而不是比特幣。在題目的設定中，顧客是直接從電子錢包扣除比特幣來付款，所以沒有涉及債務。

A: Yes! But pay attention to the subject. If you use a credit card, you are borrowing "fiat money" (like HKD/CAD) from the bank to pay, and you will repay the bank in the future. In this case, it is the fiat money that performs the function of a standard of deferred payment, not Bitcoin. In the question's scenario, the customer pays by directly deducting Bitcoin from their crypto wallet, so no debt is involved.

考試技巧與速解 Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

邏輯排除法 (Logical Elimination):

當你看到圖片中明顯有一個價格標籤（0.0011204 BTC），你就可以 100% 確定 (2) 記帳單位是正確的。這時你可以立刻排除選項 A 和 B（如果它們不包含2，但此題選項設定較狡猾）。接著，確認這是一手交錢（雖然是網上）的買賣，確認 (1) 正確。最後，檢查有沒有「借錢/欠債」的字眼，沒有的話果斷排除 (3)。

Logical Elimination:
When you see a clear price tag in the picture (0.0011204 BTC), you can be 100% sure that (2) Unit of Account is correct. Next, confirm this is a direct purchase (even if online) where money changes hands, confirming (1) is correct. Finally, check for words implying "borrowing/debt". Since there are none, decisively eliminate (3).
