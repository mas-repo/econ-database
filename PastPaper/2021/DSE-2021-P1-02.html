📝 HKDSE Economics Analysis (經濟學題目分析)

Topic (課題): Basic Economic Problems & Resource Allocation (基本經濟問題與資源分配)

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐ (2/5 - Basic/基礎)

Key Concepts (核心概念): Basic Economic Problems Market Mechanism Government Command

1. The Question (題目)

為應對口罩市場的短缺，政府向全港市民免費派發可重用的銅芯口罩。
這是以 ____________ 去解決 _______________ 問題的一個例子。

In response to the shortage in face mask market, the Government distributes free reusable CuMask to all Hong Kong citizens.
This is an example of using ____________ to solve the problem of _______________.

A. 政府指令 … 「如何生產」

A. government command … ‘how to produce’

B. 政府指令 … 「為誰生產」

B. government command … ‘for whom to produce’

C. 市場機制 … 「如何生產」

C. market mechanism … ‘how to produce’

D. 市場機制 … 「為誰生產」

D. market mechanism … ‘for whom to produce’

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這道題目，我們必須理解兩個核心經濟學概念：資源分配機制 (Resource Allocation Mechanisms) 以及 基本經濟問題 (Basic Economic Problems)。

To solve this question, we must understand two core economic concepts: Resource Allocation Mechanisms and Basic Economic Problems.

資源分配機制 (Allocation Mechanisms)

  • 市場機制 (Market Mechanism / Price Mechanism): 透過價格（由市場供求決定）來分配資源。例如：價高者得。
  • Market Mechanism (Price Mechanism): Resources are allocated through prices determined by market demand and supply. E.g., The highest bidder wins.
  • 政府指令 / 非價格競爭 (Government Command / Non-price Competition): 透過行政命令、配額或先到先得等非價格方式分配。題目中的「免費派發」就是一種指令機制。
  • Government Command / Non-price Competition): Resources are allocated through administrative orders, quotas, or first-come-first-served basis, rather than prices. The "free distribution" in the question is a command mechanism.

基本經濟問題 (Basic Economic Problems)

  • 生產甚麼 (What to produce): 決定生產哪種物品及數量。
  • What to produce: Deciding which goods to produce and in what quantities.
  • 如何生產 (How to produce): 決定生產的方法（例如：勞工密集 vs 資本密集）。
  • How to produce: Deciding the method of production (e.g., labor-intensive vs. capital-intensive).
  • 為誰生產 (For whom to produce): 決定產品的分配對象（誰能獲得這些產品）。
  • For whom to produce: Deciding who gets to consume the goods produced.

Market Shortage (市場短缺)

題目提到了「短缺」(Shortage)。在經濟學中，當價格低於均衡價格時，需求量 \(Q_d\) 會大於供應量 \(Q_s\)，從而產生短缺。公式如下：

The question mentions a "shortage". In economics, when the price is below the equilibrium price, quantity demanded \(Q_d\) exceeds quantity supplied \(Q_s\), creating a shortage. The formula is:

$$ Shortage = Q_d - Q_s \quad \text{(where } Q_d > Q_s \text{)} $$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: B (正確答案：B)

Option A (錯誤): 雖然「免費派發」確實是「政府指令」，但「如何生產」是指生產的方法（例如用機器還是人手製造口罩）。題目並沒有提及口罩的製造過程。

Option A (Incorrect): Although "free distribution" is indeed a "government command", "how to produce" refers to the method of production (e.g., using machines vs. manual labor to make masks). The question does not mention the manufacturing process.

Option B (正確): 政府「免費派發」不依賴市場價格，屬於「政府指令」(Government command)。「向全港市民」派發，解決了「誰可以獲得口罩」的問題，這正是「為誰生產」(For whom to produce) 的定義。

Option B (Correct): The government "distributing for free" does not rely on market prices, which falls under "government command". Distributing "to all Hong Kong citizens" answers the question of "who gets the masks", which is the exact definition of "for whom to produce".

Option C (錯誤): 「免費派發」沒有動用價格機制，因此不是「市場機制」。同時，題目亦與「如何生產」無關。

Option C (Incorrect): "Free distribution" does not involve the price mechanism, so it is not a "market mechanism". Also, the question is entirely unrelated to "how to produce".

Option D (錯誤): 後半部的「為誰生產」是正確的，但前半部錯誤。因為政府是免費派發，而非讓市民按市價購買，所以這不是「市場機制」。

Option D (Incorrect): The second half "for whom to produce" is correct, but the first half is wrong. Since the government distributes them for free rather than letting citizens buy them at market prices, this is not a "market mechanism".

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解決這類題目的關鍵在於找出題幹中的「關鍵詞 (Pivot Points)」，並過濾掉「干擾資訊 (Distractors)」。

The key to solving this type of question is identifying the "Pivot Points" in the text and filtering out the "Distractors".

  • 🔍 關鍵詞 1: 「免費派發」 (distributes free)
    ➔ 推論: 沒有使用金錢/價格進行交易 = 政府指令 (Government command)。

    🔍 Pivot Point 1: "distributes free"
    ➔ Deduction: No money/price is used for transactions = Government command.

  • 🔍 關鍵詞 2: 「向全港市民」 (to all Hong Kong citizens)
    ➔ 推論: 決定了物品的最終接收者 = 解決「為誰生產」的問題。

    🔍 Pivot Point 2: "to all Hong Kong citizens"
    ➔ Deduction: Determines the final recipients of the goods = Solves the "for whom to produce" problem.

  • ⚠️ 干擾資訊: 「可重用的銅芯」 (reusable CuMask)
    ➔ 分析: 很多學生看到口罩的材質，會誤以為這是在討論「如何生產」(How to produce) 或「生產甚麼」(What to produce)。但這只是口罩的描述，題目的核心動作是「派發給誰」。

    ⚠️ Distractor: "reusable CuMask"
    ➔ Analysis: Many students see the material of the mask and mistakenly think it's discussing "how to produce" or "what to produce". However, this is just a description; the core action of the question is "distributing to whom".

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

❌ 陷阱 1：將「政府參與」等同於「政府指令」
這題雖然「免費派發」是指令，但學生要留意：如果政府是「在市場上以高價購買口罩」（這是市場機制），然後再「免費派發給市民」（這是指令）。必須仔細看清楚題目問的是哪一個階段的動作！

❌ Trap 1: Equating "Government Involvement" with "Government Command"
Although "free distribution" here is a command, students must be careful: if the government "buys masks at high prices in the market" (this is the market mechanism), and then "distributes them for free to citizens" (this is a command). You must carefully read which stage of the action the question is asking about!

❌ 陷阱 2：混淆「如何生產」與「生產甚麼」
「生產甚麼」是指決定做口罩而不是做衣服；「如何生產」是指用全自動機器做還是半人手做。這兩者與「派發」完全無關。

❌ Trap 2: Confusing "How to produce" with "What to produce"
"What to produce" means deciding to make masks instead of clothes; "how to produce" means using fully automated machines vs. semi-manual labor. Neither of these has anything to do with "distribution".

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

🙋‍♂️ 學生問： 為什麼這不是解決「生產甚麼 (What to produce)」的問題？政府不是決定了要生產「銅芯口罩」嗎？

🙋‍♂️ Student Q: Why isn't this solving the "What to produce" problem? Didn't the government decide to produce "CuMasks"?

👨‍🏫 導師答： 這是個很好的問題！政府決定製造銅芯口罩的那一刻，確實解決了「生產甚麼」的問題。但是，題目的重點句子是「向全港市民免費派發」(distributes free... to all citizens)。這個具體的「派發動作」所解決的，是物品最終落入誰手中的問題，所以針對這個動作，答案必然是「為誰生產」。

👨‍🏫 Tutor A: That's a great question! The moment the government decided to make CuMasks, they indeed solved the "what to produce" problem. However, the focal sentence of the question is "distributes free... to all citizens". This specific "action of distributing" solves the problem of whose hands the goods ultimately fall into. Therefore, regarding this specific action, the answer must be "for whom to produce".

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

💡 秒殺技巧 (Speed-Solving Trick): 做這種題目時，使用「連線對應法」：

  • 看到「價格 / 購買 / 拍賣」 ➔ 連結「市場機制」
  • 看到「免費 / 抽籤 / 配額 / 派發」 ➔ 連結「政府指令 / 非價格」
  • 看到「給誰 / 長者 / 學生 / 全港市民」 ➔ 連結「為誰生產」
  • 看到「勞工密集 / 機器 / 生產線」 ➔ 連結「如何生產」

這題只要看到「免費」和「市民」，兩秒鐘就能鎖定「指令」和「為誰」，直接選 B！

💡 Speed-Solving Trick: When doing this type of question, use the "Keyword Matching Method":

  • See "Price / Buy / Auction" ➔ Link to "Market Mechanism"
  • See "Free / Lottery / Quota / Distribute" ➔ Link to "Government Command / Non-price"
  • See "To whom / Elderly / Students / Citizens" ➔ Link to "For whom to produce"
  • See "Labor-intensive / Machinery / Production line" ➔ Link to "How to produce"

In this question, just by spotting "free" and "citizens", you can lock into "command" and "for whom" in two seconds, choosing B instantly!
