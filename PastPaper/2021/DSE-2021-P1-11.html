2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

邊際回報遞減定律 (Law of Diminishing Marginal Returns): 在短期內，當我們將連續數量的可變要素（如勞工）加入固定要素（如機器）時，可變要素的邊際產出（MP）最終一定會下降。

In the short run, as successive units of a variable factor (e.g., labour) are added to fixed factors (e.g., machinery), the marginal product (MP) of the variable factor will eventually decrease.
$$MP = \frac{\Delta TP}{\Delta L}$$

邊際產出 (Marginal Product, MP): 增加一個單位的可變要素所帶來的額外總產出。

The additional total product resulting from the employment of one more unit of a variable factor.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: C

首先，我們需要計算表格中已知數據的邊際產出（MP）。

First, we need to calculate the Marginal Product (MP) from the given data in the table.
$L=1: MP = 10$
$L=2: MP = 25 - 10 = 15$
$L=3: MP = 45 - 25 = 20$

從第1名勞工到第3名勞工，邊際產出（MP）正在上升（10 $\rightarrow$ 15 $\rightarrow$ 20）。由於題目指數據「說明了」邊際回報遞減定律，這意味著邊際產出必須在第4名或第5名勞工時開始下降（即小於20，或持續下降）。

From the 1st to the 3rd labourer, the Marginal Product (MP) is increasing (10 $\rightarrow$ 15 $\rightarrow$ 20). Since the question states the data "illustrates" the law of diminishing marginal returns, the MP must start to decrease at the 4th or 5th labourer (i.e., become less than 20, or show a decreasing trend).

檢驗情況 (1) / Checking Scenario (1): Q1 = 60; Q2 = 70

如果 Q1 = 60，第4名勞工的 $MP = 60 - 45 = 15$。如果 Q2 = 70，第5名勞工的 $MP = 70 - 60 = 10$。邊際產出由20下降至15再降至10，這完美符合定律。

If Q1 = 60, the 4th labourer's $MP = 60 - 45 = 15$. If Q2 = 70, the 5th labourer's $MP = 70 - 60 = 10$. The MP drops from 20 to 15, then to 10. This perfectly illustrates the law. (Consistent)

檢驗情況 (2) / Checking Scenario (2): Q1 = 40; Q2 = 30

如果 Q1 = 40，第4名勞工的 $MP = 40 - 45 = -5$。如果 Q2 = 30，第5名勞工的 $MP = 30 - 40 = -10$。雖然總產出和邊際產出是負數，但邊際產出（由20降至-5再降至-10）確實正在下降。因此，它符合定律。

If Q1 = 40, the 4th labourer's $MP = 40 - 45 = -5$. If Q2 = 30, the 5th labourer's $MP = 30 - 40 = -10$. Although TP is falling and MP is negative, the MP is still continuously decreasing (20 $\rightarrow$ -5 $\rightarrow$ -10). Therefore, it illustrates the law. (Consistent)

檢驗情況 (3) / Checking Scenario (3): Q1 = 65; Q2 = 85

如果 Q1 = 65，第4名勞工的 $MP = 65 - 45 = 20$。如果 Q2 = 85，第5名勞工的 $MP = 85 - 65 = 20$。邊際產出保持在20不變（固定回報）。邊際產出沒有下降，因此無法說明遞減定律。

If Q1 = 65, the 4th labourer's $MP = 65 - 45 = 20$. If Q2 = 85, the 5th labourer's $MP = 85 - 65 = 20$. The MP remains constant at 20 (constant returns). Since MP never decreases, this fails to illustrate the law. (Inconsistent)

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (解題關鍵): 這題的關鍵在於將「總產出 (TP)」轉換為「邊際產出 (MP)」。很多學生只看總產出的增減，而忽略了定律是關於邊際產出的下降。

The pivot point of this question is converting "Total Output (TP)" to "Marginal Product (MP)". Many students only look at whether TP is increasing or decreasing, completely forgetting that the law specifically describes the fall in Marginal Product.

Distractors (干擾資訊): 機器數量一直保持為2。這只是用來證明我們處於「短期」(Short run，存在固定生產要素) 狀態。計算時無需使用數字2。

The Machinery amount is constantly 2. This is purely to prove that production is happening in the "Short Run" (presence of a fixed factor). You do not use the number 2 in any of your calculations.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 混淆「回報遞減」與「總產量下降」: 很多學生看到情況(2)中 Q1=40，比之前的45還要低，就覺得不合理。然而，邊際回報遞減定律絕對容許出現「負邊際產出」(Negative MP)，只要 MP 的數值在下降（例如從 20 跌至 -5），就已經展示了該定律。
  • Confusing "Diminishing Returns" with "Falling Total Output": Many students see that in scenario (2), Q1=40 is lower than the previous 45 and immediately reject it. However, the law absolutely allows for "Negative Marginal Returns". As long as the MP value is falling (e.g., from 20 to -5), it successfully illustrates the law.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q1: 定律不是說邊際產量一定會下降嗎？為什麼前三名勞工的 MP 還在上升 (10 $\rightarrow$ 15 $\rightarrow$ 20)？

Q1: Doesn't the law say MP must decrease? Why is the MP for the first three workers increasing (10 $\rightarrow$ 15 $\rightarrow$ 20)?

A1: 注意定律中的關鍵詞：「最終」(Eventually)。在生產初期，增加勞工可以促進分工合作 (specialization)，令 MP 短暫上升（邊際回報遞增）。但由於固定要素有限，MP 最終一定會下跌。題目問哪組數據「說明」了這定律，我們就要找出 MP 由升轉跌的那一刻。

A1: Note the keyword in the law: "Eventually". In the initial stages of production, adding labour allows for specialization, causing MP to rise temporarily (increasing marginal returns). But because fixed factors are limited, MP must eventually fall. Since the question asks which data "illustrates" this, we need to find the data that shows the turning point where MP begins to fall.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法 (The Quick Elimination Method):

  1. 在考卷表格旁邊直接寫下 MP 的數值：10, 15, 20。
  2. 我們需要 MP 在 L=4 或 L=5 時小於 20。
  3. 快速檢視選項 (3)：Q1 = 65 代表新增了 20 個單位 ($65 - 45 = 20$)。因為 $MP=20$，MP沒有下降（這是常數回報），所以馬上剔除包含 (3) 的選項 (B 和 D)。
  4. 剩下的答案只能是 A 或 C。我們只需再驗證 (2) 即可。
  1. Write down the MP values directly next to the table on your exam paper: 10, 15, 20.
  2. We need MP to be strictly less than 20 at L=4 or L=5.
  3. Quick scan of scenario (3): Q1 = 65 means an addition of 20 units ($65 - 45 = 20$). Since $MP=20$, it hasn't decreased (this is constant returns), so immediately eliminate any option containing (3), which means eliminating B and D.
  4. The remaining answer must be A or C. You only need to verify scenario (2) to get the final answer.
