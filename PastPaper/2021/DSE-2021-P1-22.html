Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答此題，我們需要運用以下勞工市場的核心概念：
To solve this problem, we need to apply the following core concepts of the labour market:

1. 衍生需求 (Derived Demand)

對生產要素（如球員）的需求是源自對其產品（如球賽、轉播）的需求。如果產品的收益上升，對生產要素的需求也會上升。
The demand for factors of production (e.g., players) is derived from the demand for the product (e.g., matches, broadcasts). If product revenue rises, demand for the factor rises.

2. 邊際收入產量 (Marginal Revenue Product, MRP)

這是決定勞工需求的主要因素。廠商願意支付的最高工資等於勞工的邊際收入產量。
This is the main determinant of labour demand. The maximum wage a firm is willing to pay equals the worker's MRP.

$$ MRP = MPP \times MR $$

\(MPP\) = 邊際產量 (Marginal Physical Product)
\(MR\) = 邊際收入 (Marginal Revenue)

3. 名義與實質工資 (Nominal vs. Real Wage)

表格顯示的是名義收入 (Nominal Income)（以貨幣單位 £ 計算）。如果某地的物價水平較高，工人會要求較高的名義工資以維持購買力。
The table shows Nominal Income (in £). If the price level in a region is higher, workers demand higher nominal wages to maintain purchasing power.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案：D (Option D)

分析選項 (1) / Analysis of (1)

陳述：英格蘭的電視轉播收入較高。
Statement: TV broadcast revenue in England is higher.

解釋：這是正確的。較高的轉播收入意味著球會的邊際收入 (\(MR\)) 較高。根據 \(MRP = MPP \times MR\)，球員的邊際收入產量 (\(MRP\)) 會上升。這增加了對球員的需求，從而推高了他們的工資。這解釋了為何英格蘭球員的收入普遍高於意大利球員。
Explanation: This is correct. Higher broadcast revenue implies higher Marginal Revenue (\(MR\)) for the clubs. According to \(MRP = MPP \times MR\), the Marginal Revenue Product (\(MRP\)) of players increases. This raises the demand for players, pushing up their wages. This explains why players in England earn more than in Italy generally.

分析選項 (2) / Analysis of (2)

陳述：英格蘭的物價水平較高。
Statement: The price level in England is higher.

解釋：這是正確的。表格中的數據是名義收入 (£)。如果英格蘭的生活成本（物價）較高，球員會要求更高的名義工資來維持相同的生活水平（勞工供應減少/向左移）。同時，球會也必須支付更高的名義工資才能吸引人才。這也是解釋英格蘭名義工資較高的合理原因。
Explanation: This is correct. The data in the table is nominal income (£). If the cost of living (price level) in England is higher, players will demand higher nominal wages to maintain the same standard of living (Labour supply decreases/shifts left). Also, clubs must pay higher nominal wages to attract talent. This is a plausible reason for the higher nominal wages in England.

分析選項 (3) / Analysis of (3)

陳述：進攻球員較受歡迎，商業價值較高。
Statement: Attacking players are more popular and have higher commercial value.

解釋：這是正確的。這解釋了同一聯賽內不同位置的收入差異。進攻球員受歡迎意味著他們能為球會帶來更多門票或商品銷售收入（較高的 \(MRP\)）。因此，對進攻球員的需求較高，導致他們的工資高於防守球員。
Explanation: This is correct. This explains the income difference between positions within the same league. Popularity implies attacking players generate more revenue from tickets or merchandise (higher \(MRP\)). Therefore, demand for attacking players is higher, leading to higher wages compared to defensive players.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目通常包含兩個維度的比較。要選出正確答案，你必須意識到「收入差異」包含：
This type of question usually involves comparison across two dimensions. To choose the right answer, you must realize "income differences" includes:

  • 維度 A：國家之間的差異 (Country vs. Country)
    為何英格蘭 > 意大利？ → 由選項 (1) 和 (2) 解釋。
  • 維度 B：職位之間的差異 (Position vs. Position)
    為何進攻球員 > 防守球員？ → 由選項 (3) 解釋。
解題關鍵 (Pivot Point):
不要只尋找解釋「為何英格蘭工資較高」的原因，也要尋找解釋「為何進攻球員由資較高」的原因。題目問的是所有觀察到的差異。
Don't just look for reasons explaining "why England pays more". Also look for reasons explaining "why attackers earn more". The question asks about ALL observed differences.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：忽視名義工資 (Ignoring Nominal Wages)

錯誤想法：「經濟學只關心實質變量，物價高只代表通脹，不代表球員真的比較富有，所以 (2) 不是原因。」
Misconception: "Economics only cares about real variables. High prices just mean inflation, not that players are richer, so (2) is not a reason."

糾正：題目問的是表格中數字（名義工資）差異的原因。物價高正是導致名義工資高的直接原因之一（補償性工資差異）。
Correction: The question asks for the reason behind the numbers (nominal wages) in the table. High price levels are a direct cause of high nominal wages (compensating wage differentials).

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果選項 (1) 說「英格蘭的所得稅率較意大利高」，這會是原因嗎？
Q: If option (1) said "Income tax rates in England are higher than in Italy", would that be a reason?

A: 是的，這也是可能的！如果英格蘭稅率較高，球員會要求更高的「稅前名義工資」來維持相同的「稅後收入」。這會使勞工供應曲線向左移，推高均衡工資。
A: Yes, that is also possible! If tax rates are higher in England, players would demand higher "pre-tax nominal wages" to maintain the same "after-tax income". This shifts the labour supply curve to the left, pushing up the equilibrium wage.

Q: 為何防守球員的邊際產量 (MPP) 不一定比進攻球員低？
Q: Why isn't the Marginal Physical Product (MPP) of defensive players necessarily lower than attackers?

A: 防守球員對球隊獲勝的貢獻（MPP）可能很高。但工資取決於 \(MRP = MPP \times MR\)。即使 MPP 相同，如果進攻球員能吸引更多粉絲（較高的 MR），他們的 MRP 仍會較高。
A: Defensive players' contribution to winning (MPP) might be high. But wages depend on \(MRP = MPP \times MR\). Even if MPP is the same, if attackers attract more fans (higher MR), their MRP will still be higher.
