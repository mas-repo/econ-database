HKDSE Economics Analysis

課題 (Topic): 需求與供應 (Demand & Supply) 彈性 (Elasticity)
難度 (Difficulty): ★★★☆☆
關鍵概念 (Key Concepts): 聯合需求 (Joint Demand), 供應價格彈性 (Price Elasticity of Supply)

題目 (The Question)

參考以下一則新聞。
為了減少污染，政府對所有種類的車輛徵收一項生產稅（電動車除外）。
結果，汽油的價格改變了22%及其交易量改變了35%。下列哪項陳述是正確的？

Refer to the following news.
In order to reduce pollution, the government imposes a production tax on all kinds of motor vehicles (except electric vehicles).
As a result, the price of gasoline changes by 22% and its quantity transacted changes by 35%. Which of the following statements is correct?

A. 汽油的需求屬高彈性。
The demand for gasoline is elastic.
B. 汽油的需求屬低彈性。
The demand for gasoline is inelastic.
C. 汽油的供應屬高彈性。
The supply of gasoline is elastic.
D. 汽油的供應屬低彈性。
The supply of gasoline is inelastic.

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

1. 互補品與聯合需求 (Complements & Joint Demand)

汽車與汽油是互補品。當汽車的價格上升（或數量減少），消費者對汽油的需求會下降（需求曲線向左移）。

Cars and gasoline are complements. When the price of cars increases (or quantity decreases), the demand for gasoline decreases (Demand curve shifts left).

2. 沿著曲線移動 vs 曲線移動 (Movement along vs Shift)

計算彈性時，我們必須觀察哪一條曲線沒有移動。如果需求曲線 (D) 移動，我們是在沿著供應曲線 (S) 移動，因此計算出來的是供應彈性。

When calculating elasticity, we must observe which curve did not shift. If the Demand curve (D) shifts, we are moving along the Supply curve (S), so the calculation reflects the Elasticity of Supply.

3. 彈性公式 (Elasticity Formula)

價格彈性是用來量度數量改變對價格改變的反應程度：

Price elasticity measures the responsiveness of quantity change to price change:

$$ E = \frac{\%\Delta Q}{\%\Delta P} $$
  • 若 \( E > 1 \)：高彈性 (Elastic)
  • 若 \( E < 1 \)：低彈性 (Inelastic)

答案與詳細分析 (Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): C

詳細拆解 (Step-by-Step Breakdown):

第一步：分辨受影響的市場 (Identify the Market Impact)
政府向車輛徵稅 \(\rightarrow\) 車輛供應減少 \(\rightarrow\) 車輛價格上升。
由於車輛與汽油是互補品，車輛昂貴了，人們少買車，連帶對汽油的需求減少。

Step 1: The government taxes vehicles \(\rightarrow\) Supply of vehicles decreases \(\rightarrow\) Price of vehicles increases. Since vehicles and gasoline are complements, fewer people buy cars, leading to a decrease in Demand for gasoline.

第二步：確定移動的曲線 (Identify the Shifting Curve)
汽油市場中，需求曲線 (D) 向左移。題目沒有提及任何影響汽油生產成本的因素，所以汽油的供應曲線 (S) 保持不變。

Step 2: In the gasoline market, the Demand curve (D) shifts to the left. The question mentions nothing affecting the production cost of gasoline, so the Supply curve (S) remains unchanged.

第三步：計算彈性 (Calculate Elasticity)
既然我們是沿著供應曲線移動，我們計算的是供應彈性 (Elasticity of Supply)。
利用公式：

Step 3: Since we are moving along the Supply curve, we are calculating the Elasticity of Supply. Using the formula:

$$ E_s = \frac{\%\Delta Q}{\%\Delta P} = \frac{35\%}{22\%} \approx 1.59 $$

因為 \( 1.59 > 1 \)，所以供應屬高彈性。

Since \( 1.59 > 1 \), the supply is elastic.

選項分析 (Options Analysis):

  • A & B (錯誤): 由於需求曲線移動了，我們無法單憑這兩個均衡點計算需求彈性。我們觀察到的是供應曲線的斜率特性。
  • A & B (Incorrect): Since the Demand curve shifted, we cannot calculate demand elasticity using just these two equilibrium points. We are observing the properties of the Supply curve.
  • C (正確): 這是供應彈性，且數值大於 1。
  • C (Correct): This is supply elasticity, and the value is greater than 1.
  • D (錯誤): 雖然是供應彈性，但 \(35\% > 22\%\)，分子大於分母，應為高彈性。
  • D (Incorrect): While it is supply elasticity, since \(35\% > 22\%\), the numerator is larger than the denominator, so it should be elastic.

題目拆解：解題關鍵 (Problem Decomposition)

這類題目的「破題點」在於判斷「誰在動，誰不動」。

The "Pivot Point" of this question is determining "Who moves, and who stays?"

  1. 事件 (Event): 汽車稅 (Tax on cars)。
  2. 傳導 (Transmission): 影響汽車市場 \(\rightarrow\) 影響汽油市場的需求 (Demand)。
  3. 結論 (Conclusion): 汽油需求移動，汽油供應不動。
  4. 計算 (Calculation): 既然供應不動，價格與數量的變化反映供應曲線的形狀（彈性）。

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱 1：搞錯主角 (Confusing the Subject)
學生常誤以為稅是加在汽油上，認為汽油供應減少。請仔細閱讀：稅是加在車輛上。

Trap 1: Students often mistake the tax as being on gasoline, thinking gasoline supply decreases. Read carefully: the tax is on vehicles.

陷阱 2：倒轉公式 (Inverting the Formula)
彈性公式永遠是 \(\frac{\%\Delta Q}{\%\Delta P}\) (數量在分子)。
錯誤計算：\(\frac{22}{35} \approx 0.6\)，誤以為是低彈性。

Trap 2: The elasticity formula is always \(\frac{\%\Delta Q}{\%\Delta P}\) (Quantity on top). Incorrect calculation: \(\frac{22}{35} \approx 0.6\), leading to the wrong conclusion of inelasticity.

學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼需求曲線移動，我們就算不到需求彈性？ (Why can't we calculate demand elasticity if the demand curve shifts?)

A: 彈性是量度同一條曲線上，價格對數量的影響。當需求曲線由 \(D_1\) 移至 \(D_2\)，我們只有兩個點：\(D_1\) 上的一點和 \(D_2\) 上的一點。這兩點不在同一條需求曲線上，所以無法計算需求彈性。相反，這兩點都在同一條供應曲線 \(S\) 上，所以我們算出的是供應彈性。

Elasticity measures the responsiveness along one single curve. When Demand shifts from \(D_1\) to \(D_2\), we have two points on two different demand curves. We cannot calculate demand elasticity. However, both points lie on the same Supply curve \(S\), so we calculate Supply Elasticity.

考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

口訣：動者不計，不計者動 (Calculate the Static Curve)

  • 如果題目說需求變（如：收入變、偏好變、互補品價格變）\(\rightarrow\) 答案一定是關於供應彈性。
  • 如果題目說供應變（如：原材料變、科技變、生產稅）\(\rightarrow\) 答案一定是關於需求彈性。

在本題，因為是互補品導致需求變，直接刪除選項 A 和 B！只需比較 \(35\%\) 和 \(22\%\) 即可得出答案。

Shortcut: If Demand shifts \(\rightarrow\) Answer is about Supply Elasticity. If Supply shifts \(\rightarrow\) Answer is about Demand Elasticity. In this question, Demand shifts, so eliminate A and B immediately!
