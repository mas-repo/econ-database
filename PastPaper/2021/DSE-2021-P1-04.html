課題 Topic: 沉沒成本與決策 / Sunk Cost and Decision Making

難度 Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆

關鍵概念 Key Concepts:

機會成本 Opportunity Cost 沉沒成本 Sunk Cost 理性決策 Rational Decision Making

題目 The Question

由於新冠肺炎，學校的日常教學受到干擾，大部分學校使用視訊軟件（例如Zoom和Google Meet）進行網上授課。假設校園重新開放，並准許面對面授課。

下列哪項是教師選擇網上授課還是面對面授課時最不重要的因素？

  • A. 網上授課會減低教師感染新冠肺炎的風險。
  • B. 教師不知道網上授課時學生正在做什麼，因為學生可能關掉鏡頭。
  • C. 教師已經花費很多時間學習使用視訊軟件進行網上授課。
  • D. 頻密使用視訊軟件進行網上授課可導致教師的視力受損。

Because of COVID-19, normal schooling was disrupted. Most schools used video communication software, such as Zoom and Google Meet, to conduct online lessons. Suppose the school campus was re-opened and face-to-face lessons were allowed.

Which of the following would be the LEAST important factor for teachers to choose between online lessons and face-to-face lesson?

  • A. The risk of contracting COVID-19 for teachers is lower when they conduct online lessons.
  • B. Teachers have no idea what their students are doing during online lessons because students may turn off their cameras.
  • C. Teachers have already spent a lot of time learning how to use the video communication software to conduct online lessons.
  • D. The eyesight of teachers may be worsened by the intensive use of the video communication software in conducting online lessons.

概念重溫 Concept Review

1. 機會成本 (Opportunity Cost):

機會成本是指在作決定時，被放棄的選項中價值最高的那一個。在理性決策中，我們必須考慮未來的得失。

Opportunity cost is the highest-valued option forgone. In rational decision-making, we must consider future costs and benefits.

2. 沉沒成本 (Sunk Cost):

沉沒成本是指已經發生且無法收回的過去成本。因為無論你現在做甚麼決定，這些成本都不會改變，因此不應該影響未來的決策。

A sunk cost is a past cost that has already been incurred and cannot be recovered. Because these costs will not change regardless of your current choice, they should not affect future decision-making.

決策原則 Rule of Decision Making:

$$Rational\ Decision \implies Consider\ Marginal\ Costs,\ Ignore\ Sunk\ Costs$$

答案與詳細分析 The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Option: C

A. 網上授課會減低感染風險

A. Lower risk of contracting COVID-19

這是一個未來的預期好處（降低風險）。如果選擇網上授課，教師就能享受這個好處，因此這會影響決策，是一個重要的考慮因素。

This is an expected future benefit (lowered risk). If teachers choose online teaching, they gain this benefit. Therefore, it affects decision-making and is an important factor.

B. 不知道學生正在做什麼

B. No idea what students are doing

這是網上授課的一個缺點或未來成本。這個缺點會直接影響教學質素，因此教師在選擇授課模式時必定會考慮此因素。

This is a disadvantage or future cost of online teaching. This drawback directly affects teaching quality, so teachers will definitely consider this factor when choosing the teaching mode.

C. 已經花費很多時間學習使用視訊軟件 (正確答案)

C. Already spent a lot of time learning the software (Correct Answer)

留意關鍵詞「已經花費」。教師學習軟件的時間是過去發生的，無論他們現在選擇網上授課還是面對面授課，這些時間都無法收回。這屬於沉沒成本 (Sunk Cost)，在理性決策中是最不重要的因素。

Notice the keyword "already spent". The time teachers spent learning the software happened in the past. Whether they choose online or face-to-face lessons now, that time cannot be recovered. This is a Sunk Cost and is the least important factor in rational decision-making.

D. 視力受損

D. Eyesight may be worsened

這項也是未來的潛在成本。如果持續網上授課，視力就會受損；如果恢復面授，就可以避免這個成本。因此，這是影響決策的重要因素。

This is also a potential future cost. If online teaching continues, eyesight will worsen; if face-to-face resumes, this cost can be avoided. Thus, it is an important factor influencing the decision.

題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

這類題目的核心支點 (Pivot Point) 在於區分「過去」與「未來」的代價。

The Pivot Point of this type of question lies in distinguishing between "past" and "future" costs/benefits.

  • 選項 A、B、D 都是描述如果採取某一行動，未來會發生的好處或壞處。
  • Options A, B, and D all describe the advantages or disadvantages that will occur in the future if a certain action is taken.
  • 選項 C 描述的是過去已經付出的代價，無法逆轉。
  • Option C describes a price that has already been paid in the past and cannot be reversed.

常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

沉沒成本謬誤 (Sunk Cost Fallacy):

學生在日常生活中常有一種錯覺：「我已經花了這麼多時間學 Zoom，如果不用就浪費了！」這種想法在經濟學上是不理性的。我們不應該因為過去的付出而勉強繼續一項選擇，決策應只取決於未來的邊際利益與邊際成本。

Students often have a misconception in daily life: "I've already spent so much time learning Zoom, it's a waste if I don't use it!" This kind of thinking is irrational in economics. We should not force ourselves to continue a choice because of past efforts; decisions should only depend on future marginal benefits and marginal costs.

學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

問：如果選項 C 改為「教師未來還需要花費很多時間學習軟件的進階功能」，那它還會是答案嗎？

Q: What if Option C was changed to "Teachers will need to spend a lot of time learning advanced features of the software in the future"? Would it still be the answer?

答：不會！如果描述的是「未來還需要花費」的時間，那就是未來的成本（Future Cost），而不是沉沒成本。在這種情況下，它就會成為決定是否繼續網上授課的重要因素。

A: No! If it describes time that "will need to be spent in the future", it becomes a Future Cost, not a sunk cost. In that case, it would be an important factor in deciding whether to continue online teaching.

考試技巧與速解 Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

掃描時間關鍵詞 (Scan for Temporal Keywords):

在 DSE 經濟科的選擇題中，當題目詢問「甚麼因素不會影響決策 / 最不重要」時，直接在選項中尋找表示過去式的字眼，例如：「已經」(already)、「曾」(previously)、「不可退回的按金」(non-refundable deposit)。這些字眼通常是沉沒成本的強烈信號，能幫你秒殺題目！

In DSE Economics MCQs, when a question asks "what factor would NOT affect the decision / is LEAST important", directly scan the options for past-tense indicator words such as: "already", "previously", or "non-refundable deposit". These words are usually strong signals for sunk costs and can help you solve the question instantly!
