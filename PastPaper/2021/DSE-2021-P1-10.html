HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Firms & Production (廠商與生產) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Shutdown Condition (結業條件), Sunk Cost (沉沒成本)
The Question (題目)
因為新冠肺炎爆發，某國政府宣佈臨時禁制餐廳提供堂食服務。在該臨時禁制下，下列哪項有關餐廳的陳述是正確的？ Because of the outbreak of COVID-19, the government of a country announced a temporary ban of dine-in services in restaurants. Which of the following statements about restaurants under the temporary ban is correct?
  • A. 若從外賣食品所得的預期銷售收入超過營運餐廳的可變成本，餐廳會繼續經營。 A. If the expected sales revenue generated from takeaway food exceeds the variable cost of running a restaurant, the restaurant will continue to operate.
  • B. 當違反租賃合約的成本極高，固定租金會影響東主是否繼續經營餐廳。 B. With the extremely high cost of breaking the rental contract, the fixed rental will affect whether the owners continue to operate their restaurant.
  • C. 外賣食品的價格下降必然會令餐廳的銷售收入下降。 C. A decrease in prices of takeaway food will necessarily decrease the sales revenue of the restaurants.
  • D. 不受歡迎的餐廳會早於受歡迎的餐廳倒閉。 D. Unpopular restaurants will close down before popular ones.
Concept Review (概念重溫)

Short-run Shutdown Condition (短期結業條件)

在短期內，廠商必須支付固定成本（Fixed Cost, FC），無論是否生產。因此，廠商決定是否繼續營業的關鍵在於能否收回可變成本（Variable Cost, VC）。 In the short run, firms must pay Fixed Costs (FC) regardless of whether they produce. Therefore, the key to deciding whether to continue operating is whether they can cover the Variable Costs (VC).

$$ \text{Total Revenue (TR)} \ge \text{Total Variable Cost (TVC)} $$
OR / 或
$$ \text{Price (P)} \ge \text{Average Variable Cost (AVC)} $$

邏輯：如果收入大於可變成本（\(TR > TVC\)），多出的收入可以用來支付部分固定成本，虧損會比直接結業（虧損全部 TFC）少。 Logic: If revenue exceeds variable costs (\(TR > TVC\)), the excess revenue can contribute to paying off some fixed costs. The loss incurred is smaller than shutting down immediately (where loss equals total TFC).

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)
Correct Option: A
Option A Correct

分析：這是標準的短期結業條件定義。在「臨時」禁制下（短期），租金是固定成本（沉沒成本）。只要外賣收入（TR）能覆蓋食材、水電、員工薪金等可變成本（TVC），繼續經營的虧損會小於直接關門。因此餐廳會繼續經營。

Analysis: This is the standard definition of the short-run shutdown condition. Under a "temporary" ban (short run), rent is a fixed cost (sunk cost). As long as takeaway revenue (TR) covers variable costs (TVC) like ingredients, utilities, and wages, the loss from operating is smaller than closing down immediately. Thus, the restaurant will continue to operate.

Option B Incorrect

分析：題目提到違反合約成本極高，暗示租金是沉沒成本 (Sunk Cost)。在經濟學決策中，沉沒成本不應影響當前的生產決策。無論經營與否，這筆錢都要付，所以它不會影響「是否繼續經營」的決定。

Analysis: The question mentions the high cost of breaking the contract, implying rent is a Sunk Cost. In economic decision-making, sunk costs should not affect current production decisions. Since this cost must be paid whether operating or not, it does not influence the decision of "whether to continue operating."

Option C Incorrect

分析：價格下降對總收入的影響取決於需求價格彈性 (Price Elasticity of Demand)。如果外賣需求是高彈性的（Elastic），價格下降反而會令銷售收入增加。使用「必然 (Necessarily)」一詞使其成為錯誤陳述。

Analysis: The effect of a price decrease on total revenue depends on the Price Elasticity of Demand. If the demand for takeaway is elastic, a decrease in price will actually lead to an increase in sales revenue. The use of the word "necessarily" makes this statement incorrect.

Option D Incorrect

分析：受歡迎程度與結業無直接關係。受歡迎的餐廳可能成本極高（如昂貴食材、大量人手），導致 \(TR < TVC\) 而結業；不受歡迎的餐廳若成本極低，反而可能生存。經濟決策是基於財務數據，而非名氣。

Analysis: Popularity is not directly related to shutting down. A popular restaurant might have extremely high costs (expensive ingredients, large staff), leading to \(TR < TVC\) and closure. An unpopular restaurant with very low costs might survive. Economic decisions are based on financial data, not fame.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

關鍵點 (Pivot Point)：識別題目中的「臨時 (Temporary)」二字。這暗示了我們處於短期 (Short Run)。 Pivot Point: Identify the word "temporary" in the question. This implies we are in the Short Run.

干擾項 (Distractor)：選項 B 中關於「違反租賃合約的成本極高」是一個陷阱。 Distractor: The mention of "extremely high cost of breaking the rental contract" in Option B is a trap.

  • 許多學生會想：「因為很難解約，所以租金很重要。」 Many students think: "Since it's hard to break the contract, rent is important."
  • 經濟學思維：正因為很難解約，這筆錢是「逃不掉的」，所以它是沉沒成本，做決策時應該將其剔除。 Economic Thinking: Precisely because it's hard to break, this cost is "unavoidable," making it a sunk cost. It should be ignored when making the decision.
Common Mistakes (常犯錯誤)

誤區：以為「虧本（Profit < 0）」就一定要結業。 Misconception: Thinking that "Making a loss (Profit < 0)" means you must shut down.

糾正：只要 \(TR \ge TVC\)，即使總利潤是負數，繼續經營也是理性的，因為這樣可以「止蝕」（減少虧損）。 Correction: As long as \(TR \ge TVC\), even if total profit is negative, it is rational to continue operating to "minimize loss."
