Topic (課題): Market Structure (市場結構)

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐ (2/5 Stars)

Key Concepts (核心概念): Oligopoly (寡頭壟斷), Market Share (市場佔有率), Data Interpretation (數據解讀)

The Question (題目)

細閱下圖。
根據上圖的資料，我們可以得出結論：2017年中國網上食品外送行業的市場結構可被視為 ______________，因為 ________________。

A. 壟斷性競爭 … 食品外送服務的市場資訊並不完全
B. 壟斷性競爭 … 服務供應商之間有價格和非價格競爭
C. 寡頭壟斷 … 服務供應商在作決定時會考慮其他主要供應商的反應
D. 寡頭壟斷 … 少數服務供應商的市場佔有率明顯大於其他供應商

Study the following diagram.
Based on the information in the above diagram, we can conclude that the market structure of online food delivery sector in China in 2017 could be regarded as ____________ because __________.

A. monopolistic competition … market information about the food delivery services was imperfect
B. monopolistic competition … there were price and non-price competitions among the service providers
C. oligopoly … service providers considered the reactions of other major providers in making decisions
D. oligopoly … a few service providers had significantly larger market shares than others

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這類題目，我們需要清楚了解不同市場結構的特徵，以及如何從數據中辨識它們。

To answer this type of question, we need a clear understanding of the characteristics of different market structures and how to identify them from data.
  • Oligopoly (寡頭壟斷): 一個由少數大型賣家主導的市場結構。在這種市場中，前幾大企業佔據了絕大部分的市場佔有率 (Market Share)。
    A market structure dominated by a small number of large sellers. In this market, the top few firms account for the vast majority of the market share.
  • Interdependence (相互依存): 寡頭壟斷的一個重要特徵。由於主要競爭者數量少，一間公司的定價或產量決定會直接影響其他公司，因此它們在作決定時必須考慮對手的反應。
    A key feature of oligopoly. Because there are few major competitors, one firm's pricing or output decision directly affects others, so they must consider rivals' reactions when making decisions.
  • Concentration Ratio (集中率): 用來量度市場集中程度的指標。例如，四大企業集中率是指市場中最大的四間企業所佔的總市場份額。
    An indicator used to measure market concentration. For example, the four-firm concentration ratio is the total market share held by the four largest firms in the market.
$$ \text{Concentration Ratio (CR}_n) = \sum_{i=1}^{n} S_i $$

Where \( S_i \) is the market share of the \( i \)-th largest firm. (其中 \( S_i \) 為第 \( i \) 大企業的市場佔有率)

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option D

Option A & B: 錯誤 (Incorrect)。
圖表顯示三間公司（餓了麼、美團點評、百度外賣）合共佔據了 \( 36.5\% + 33\% + 17.3\% = 86.8\% \) 的市場佔有率。這種由少數巨頭主導的特徵屬於寡頭壟斷，而非壟斷性競爭（後者應有大量市場佔有率平均的小型賣家）。此外，圖表並沒有提供關於資訊流通度或競爭方式（價格/非價格）的資料。

The chart shows that three companies combined account for \( 86.8\% \) of the market share. This dominance by a few giants is a characteristic of an oligopoly, not monopolistic competition (which would have many small sellers with relatively equal, small shares). Furthermore, the chart provides no information about information availability or types of competition.

Option C: 錯誤 (Incorrect)。
這是一個典型的陷阱！雖然「考慮其他主要供應商的反應（相互依存）」確實是寡頭壟斷的特徵，但題目的關鍵字是「根據上圖的資料」。圓形圖只顯示了市場佔有率（百分比），並沒有顯示任何關於企業決策過程或相互依存的證據。我們不能從圖表中得出這個結論。

This is a classic trap! While "considering the reactions of other major providers (interdependence)" is indeed a feature of oligopoly, the keyword in the question is "Based on the information in the above diagram". The pie chart ONLY shows market shares (percentages); it provides no evidence of firms' decision-making processes. We cannot draw this conclusion from the diagram.

Option D: 正確 (Correct)。
圖表清晰地顯示，少數幾間供應商（餓了麼、美團點評、百度外賣）的市場佔有率顯著大於其他供應商（其餘所有公司加起來只有 \( 13.2\% \)）。這直接符合寡頭壟斷的定義，而且這個結論是完全建基於圖表所提供的數據。

The chart clearly shows that a few service providers have significantly larger market shares than others (all other companies combined only account for \( 13.2\% \)). This directly fits the definition of an oligopoly, and this conclusion is based entirely on the data provided in the diagram.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (解題樞紐): 題目的限制條件「根據上圖的資料 (Based on the information in the above diagram)」。

The constraint in the question: "Based on the information in the above diagram".

這類題目測試的不是你背誦了多少市場結構的特徵，而是你解讀數據的能力。圖表有什麼，你就只能推論出什麼。圓形圖 = 市場份額分佈 = 集中度。它無法告訴你產品是否同質、資訊是否完全，或者企業之間是否存在勾結。

This type of question tests your data interpretation skills, not just rote memorization of market features. You can only deduce what the chart explicitly shows. Pie chart = market share distribution = concentration. It cannot tell you if products are homogeneous, if information is perfect, or if firms are interdependent.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 既然圖中有「其他 (Others) 13.2%」，這代表市場上其實有很多賣家，為什麼不能是壟斷性競爭 (Monopolistic Competition) 呢？

A: 雖然市場上可能有很多小賣家（組成那 13.2%），但決定市場結構的關鍵在於「主導權」。在壟斷性競爭中，市場份額應該相對平均地分散在大量賣家手中，沒有任何少數賣家能佔據絕對優勢。但在這裡，僅僅三間公司就佔據了近 87% 的市場，這絕對符合寡頭壟斷「由少數大企業主導」的定義。

Q: Since there is an "Others (13.2%)" category, meaning there are many sellers, why can't it be monopolistic competition?
A: While there may be many small sellers (making up the 13.2%), the key to determining market structure is "dominance". In monopolistic competition, market share is relatively evenly distributed among a large number of sellers, with no few sellers holding an absolute advantage. Here, just three companies control nearly 87% of the market, which perfectly fits the oligopoly definition of being "dominated by a few large firms".

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

The "Evidence-Matching" Rule (證據配對法則):

當題目出現「根據圖表/資料 (Based on the source/diagram)」時，請使用配對法：

  1. 圖表顯示 百分比/圓形圖 ➔ 答案必須與 市場佔有率 (Market Share) / 集中度 有關。
  2. 圖表顯示 廣告支出 ➔ 答案必須與 非價格競爭 (Non-price competition) 有關。
  3. 圖表顯示 公司互相觀察對方定價的文字描述 ➔ 答案必須與 相互依存 (Interdependence) 有關。

在此題中，圖表只有百分比，因此直接秒殺選擇包含「市場佔有率」描述的選項 D。

When a question says "Based on the diagram/source", use the matching method:
1. Diagram shows Percentages/Pie Chart ➔ Answer must relate to Market Share / Concentration.
2. Diagram shows Advertising expenditure ➔ Answer must relate to Non-price competition.
3. Source describes firms observing each other's pricing ➔ Answer must relate to Interdependence.
In this question, the chart only has percentages, so you can instantly eliminate options and choose D which mentions "market shares".
