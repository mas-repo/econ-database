經濟學題目解析 | Economics Question Analysis

主題 (Topic): 市場干預：配額 | Market Intervention: Quotas

難度 (Difficulty): ★★★☆☆

關鍵字 (Key Concepts): 均衡價格 (Equilibrium Price) 有效配額 (Effective Quota) 無效配額 (Ineffective Quota)

1. 題目 (The Question)

參閱以下物品X的需求表及供應表。

Refer to the following demand and supply schedules of good X.

單位價格 Unit price ($) 201816141210
需求量 Quantity demanded (units) 500550600650700750
供應量 Quantity supplied (units) 800750700650600550

若政府把配額由700單位減低至550單位，

If the government lowers the quota from 700 units to 550 units,

A. 市價會下跌$2。
B. 市價會上升$2。
C. 市價會上升$4。
D. 市價會上升$6。

A. the market price will drop by $2.
B. the market price will rise by $2.
C. the market price will rise by $4.
D. the market price will rise by $6.

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

市場均衡 (Market Equilibrium)：當需求量 \(Q_d\) 等於供應量 \(Q_s\) 時的狀態，此時的價格為均衡價格 \(P_e\)。

Market Equilibrium: The state where quantity demanded \(Q_d\) equals quantity supplied \(Q_s\). The price at this state is the equilibrium price \(P_e\).

$$ Q_d = Q_s \implies P_e \text{ and } Q_e $$

配額 (Quota)：政府對某物品設定的最高生產或進口數量限制。

Quota: A government-imposed maximum limit on the quantity produced or imported of a specific good.

有效與無效配額 (Effective vs. Ineffective Quota)：

Effective vs. Ineffective Quota:

  • 若 配額量 \(\geq\) 均衡數量 \(Q_e\)，則配額無效 (不會影響市場價格和交易量)。
    If Quota limit \(\geq\) Equilibrium quantity \(Q_e\), the quota is ineffective (does not affect market price or quantity transacted).
  • 若 配額量 \(<\) 均衡數量 \(Q_e\)，則配額有效。消費者會根據新的稀缺數量在需求曲線上競爭，推高市場價格。
    If Quota limit \(<\) Equilibrium quantity \(Q_e\), the quota is effective. Consumers will compete for the restricted quantity along the demand curve, pushing up the market price.

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): C

步驟 1：找出自由市場均衡。 從圖表中可見，當價格為 $14 時，需求量 (650) 等於供應量 (650)。因此，原來的均衡價格 \(P_1 = \$14\)，均衡交易量 \(Q_e = 650\)。

Step 1: Find the free market equilibrium. From the table, when the price is $14, the quantity demanded (650) equals the quantity supplied (650). Therefore, the original equilibrium price \(P_1 = \$14\) and equilibrium quantity \(Q_e = 650\).

步驟 2：分析原本的 700 單位配額。 由於 700 單位大於自由市場的均衡交易量 (650 單位)，這個配額是無效的。市場價格維持在均衡點的 $14。

Step 2: Analyze the original quota of 700 units. Since 700 units is greater than the free market equilibrium quantity (650 units), this quota is ineffective. The market price remains at the equilibrium point of $14.

步驟 3：分析新的 550 單位配額。 新配額 (550) 小於均衡交易量 (650)，因此配額有效。市場實際交易量被限制在 550 單位。消費者願意為 550 單位支付的最高價格 (查看需求表) 是 $18。因此，新市價 \(P_2 = \$18\)。

Step 3: Analyze the new quota of 550 units. The new quota (550) is less than the equilibrium quantity (650), making it effective. The transacted quantity is restricted to 550 units. The maximum price consumers are willing to pay for 550 units (look at the demand schedule) is $18. Thus, the new market price \(P_2 = \$18\).

步驟 4：計算價格變動。 新價格 ($18) 減去 舊價格 ($14) = 上升 $4。故選 C。

Step 4: Calculate the price change. New price ($18) minus Old price ($14) = Rise by $4. Therefore, Option C is correct.

錯誤選項分析 (Analysis of Incorrect Options)

選項 A & B：這兩個選項通常是學生計算錯誤或看錯供求表數值時得出的結果。

Options A & B: These options are typically chosen if a student makes a calculation error or misreads the values in the supply and demand tables.

選項 D (常犯錯誤陷阱)：若學生誤以為原本 700 單位的配額是有效的，並錯誤地查看「當需求量為700時的價格」(\(P = \$12\))，他們會以為原價是 $12。然後用新價 $18 減去 $12，得出錯誤的上升 $6 結論。實際上，原本的配額根本沒有推低市價至 $12，因為在 $12 時供應商只願提供 600 單位。

Option D (Common Trap): If a student mistakenly thinks the original quota of 700 units was effective and incorrectly looks at the "price when quantity demanded is 700" (\(P = \$12\)), they will assume the old price was $12. Subtracting $12 from the new price of $18 gives an incorrect conclusion of a $6 rise. In reality, the original quota never pushed the price down to $12 because suppliers are only willing to supply 600 units at $12.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

拆題關鍵 (Pivot Point)：

Pivot Point:

  • 解決此題的最關鍵一步是判斷初始政策的狀態。不要假設每一個寫出來的政策都必定會改變市場。
    The most crucial step in solving this problem is evaluating the status of the initial policy. Do not assume every policy mentioned automatically changes the market.

干擾資訊 (Distractors)：

Distractors:

  • 供應表在低於均衡價格 (如 $12, $10) 時的數值完全是干擾。一旦配額生效，價格由需求者的最高支付意願 (最高願意支付的價格) 決定，我們只需查看需求表。
    The supply quantities at prices below the equilibrium (e.g., $12, $10) are purely distractors. Once a quota is effective, the price is determined by the demanders' maximum willingness to pay, so we only need to look at the demand schedule.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

誤區：看錯決定新價格的曲線。

Misconception: Looking at the wrong curve to determine the new price.

很多學生知道配額是 550 單位後，會在表中尋找 550 這個數字。他們看到價格 $10 時，供應量也是 550，於是誤以為新價格是 $10。這是完全錯誤的。因為在數量被限制為 550 的情況下，消費者願意以高達 $18 的價格購買這些稀有物品。在自由競爭下，價格會被推高至 $18，而不是停留在 $10。

Many students, knowing the quota is 550 units, search for the number 550 in the table. They see that at a price of $10, quantity supplied is 550, and mistakenly conclude the new price is $10. This is fundamentally wrong. Given the restricted quantity of 550, consumers are willing to pay up to $18. Through free competition, the price will be bid up to $18, not stay at $10.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q1：如果新的配額設定為 600 單位，價格又會變成多少呢？

Q1: What if the new quota was set at 600 units? What would the price be?

A1：如果配額是 600 單位 (依然小於均衡的 650，所以有效)，我們只需查看需求表，找出當需求量為 600 時的對應價格。答案是 $16。

A1: If the quota was 600 units (still less than the equilibrium of 650, so it's effective), we simply check the demand schedule to find the price corresponding to a quantity demanded of 600. The answer would be $16.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

做這類題目時，遵循以下「三步秒殺法」：

When doing these types of questions, follow this "3-Step Quick Kill" method:

  1. 找均衡： 肉眼掃描表格，找出 \(Q_d = Q_s\) 的那一行。(得出 \(P_e=14\), \(Q_e=650\))。
    Find Equilibrium: Visually scan the table to find the row where \(Q_d = Q_s\). (Yields \(P_e=14\), \(Q_e=650\)).
  2. 查舊政策： 原配額 700 > 650，直接劃掉它，當它不存在，舊價即為 \(P_e = 14\)。
    Check Old Policy: Old quota 700 > 650, cross it out immediately, pretend it doesn't exist. Old price is \(P_e = 14\).
  3. 對應新政策： 新配額 550 < 650，有效。直接在需求表找數量 550 對應的價格。(得出新價為 18)。計算差距：18 - 14 = 4。
    Match New Policy: New quota 550 < 650, effective. Look directly at the demand schedule for the price corresponding to quantity 550. (Yields new price of 18). Calculate gap: 18 - 14 = 4.
