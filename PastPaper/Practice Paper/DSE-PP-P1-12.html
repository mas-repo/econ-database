2. 概念重溫 / Concept Review

要解決這道題目，我們必須掌握以下三個核心微觀經濟學概念：
To solve this problem, we must master the following three core microeconomic concepts:
  • 1. 利潤極大化條件 (Profit Maximization Condition)： 受價公司（完全競爭市場）的利潤極大化條件是 邊際收益 (MR) 等於 邊際成本 (MC)。因為市場價格是不變的，所以 \( P = MR = MC \)。
    1. Profit Maximization Condition: For a price-taking firm (perfect competition), profit is maximized where Marginal Revenue (MR) equals Marginal Cost (MC). Since the market price is constant, \( P = MR = MC \).
  • 2. 利潤公式 (Profit Formula)： 利潤等於總收益減去總成本。
    2. Profit Formula: Profit equals Total Revenue minus Total Cost.
    $$ \text{Profit } (\pi) = \text{Total Revenue } (TR) - \text{Total Cost } (TC) $$
  • 3. 生產者盈餘 (Producer Surplus, PS)： 生產者盈餘有兩種常見的計算方法：總收益減去總可變成本，或者利潤加上總固定成本。
    3. Producer Surplus (PS): There are two common ways to calculate producer surplus: Total Revenue minus Total Variable Cost, or Profit plus Total Fixed Cost.
    $$ PS = TR - \text{Total Variable Cost } (TVC) $$ $$ \text{OR} $$ $$ PS = \text{Profit} + \text{Total Fixed Cost } (TFC) $$

選項分析 / Option Breakdown:

  • A. $10 ... $10: 錯誤。計算了正確的利潤 ($10)，但誤以為生產者盈餘等於利潤，沒有加上固定成本。 Incorrect. Calculated the correct profit ($10) but mistakenly assumed Producer Surplus equals Profit, forgetting to add Fixed Costs.
  • B. $10 ... $30: 正確答案。 Correct answer.
  • C. $30 ... $10: 錯誤。將利潤與生產者盈餘的數字調轉了。 Incorrect. Swapped the values for Profit and Producer Surplus.
  • D. $30 ... $30: 錯誤。計算了正確的生產者盈餘，但誤以為利潤也等於 $30 (即計算利潤時忘記扣除固定成本)。 Incorrect. Calculated PS correctly, but assumed profit was also $30 (forgot to deduct fixed costs when calculating profit).

4. 題目拆解 / Problem Decomposition

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point): 本題的關鍵在於找出 \( Q=1 \) 時的邊際成本。很多學生會直接用 \( 40 - 0 = 40 \)，這就錯了！題目清楚標明固定成本等於 $20。因此，當 \( Q=0 \) 時，\( TC=20 \)。第一件產品的 \( MC \) 是 \( 40 - 20 = 20 \)。
Pivot Point: The key to this problem lies in finding the Marginal Cost at \( Q=1 \). Many students mistakenly do \( 40 - 0 = 40 \). This is wrong! The question explicitly states fixed cost is $20. Therefore, at \( Q=0 \), \( TC=20 \). The \( MC \) for the first unit is \( 40 - 20 = 20 \).

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 / Common Mistakes & Traps

⚠️ 陷阱一：混淆利潤與生產者盈餘。
很多學生以為利潤和生產者盈餘是同一樣東西。記住：在短期內，因為有固定成本的存在，生產者盈餘通常大於利潤。(\( PS = \text{Profit} + TFC \))
⚠️ Trap 1: Confusing Profit and Producer Surplus.
Many students assume profit and producer surplus are the same. Remember: in the short run, due to the existence of fixed costs, producer surplus is usually greater than profit. (\( PS = \text{Profit} + TFC \))

6. 學生常見疑問 / Student Q&A

Q: 為什麼我們不在表格裡計算平均成本 (AC)？ / Why don't we calculate Average Cost (AC) in the table?

A: 雖然我們可以用 \( (P - AC) \times Q \) 來計算利潤，但計算每個數量的 AC 會浪費時間且容易出現小數點計算錯誤。直接用 \( TR - TC \) 是最直接且最不易出錯的。
A: While we can calculate profit using \( (P - AC) \times Q \), calculating AC for every quantity is time-consuming and prone to decimal errors. Using \( TR - TC \) directly is the most straightforward and error-free method.

7. 考試技巧與速解 / Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

🚀 秒殺技巧：聯動公式
一旦你在考試中計出 利潤 (Profit) = $10，你完全不需要重新用 \( TR - TVC \) 或者畫圖去計算生產者盈餘。直接使用神級公式：
\( PS = \text{Profit} + \text{Fixed Cost} = 10 + 20 = 30 \)。
兩秒鐘，答案 B 已經浮現！
🚀 Speed-run Trick: Linked Formula
Once you calculate that Profit = $10 in the exam, you do not need to recalculate Producer Surplus using \( TR - TVC \) or by drawing a graph. Simply use the golden formula:
\( PS = \text{Profit} + \text{Fixed Cost} = 10 + 20 = 30 \).
In two seconds, Option B becomes the obvious answer!
