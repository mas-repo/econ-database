  • 關鍵字 (Key Concepts): 價格下限 (Price Floor), 勞力過剩 (Labor Surplus), 非價格競爭 (Non-price Competition)

1. 題目 (The Question)

下列哪項是有效的最低工資法最有可能帶來的影響？

Which of the following is the most likely effect of an effective minimum wage law?

  • A. 收入分布會變得較平均。
  • A. Income distribution will become more even.
  • B. 工人的總工資收入會上升。
  • B. Total wage earnings of workers will rise.
  • C. 就業人口會增加。
  • C. The employed population will increase.
  • D. 非價格競爭會出現。
  • D. Non-price competition will emerge.

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

有效的最低工資 (Effective Minimum Wage)： 政府設定的合法最低工資水平，必須設定在市場均衡工資（Equilibrium Wage）之上才算「有效」。

Effective Minimum Wage: A legally mandated minimum wage level set by the government. To be "effective," it must be set above the market equilibrium wage.

因為工資被人為地推高，勞力供應量 \( Q_s \)（工人願意提供勞力的數量）會大於勞力需求量 \( Q_d \)（僱主願意聘請的數量）。這會導致勞力過剩（失業）。

Because the wage is artificially pushed up, the quantity supplied of labor \( Q_s \) (number of workers willing to work) will exceed the quantity demanded of labor \( Q_d \) (number of workers employers are willing to hire). This leads to a surplus of labor (unemployment).

$$ Q_s > Q_d \implies \text{Surplus (Unemployment)} $$

非價格競爭 (Non-price Competition)： 當市場出現過剩，由於法例規定工人不能透過「減薪」（價格競爭）來爭取工作，他們必須依靠其他非價格手段競爭，例如提高學歷、無薪加班、或依靠人事關係。

Non-price Competition: When a surplus occurs, since the law prevents workers from securing jobs by "lowering their asking wage" (price competition), they must rely on non-price methods to compete, such as acquiring better qualifications, working unpaid overtime, or using personal connections.

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是：選項 D

The correct answer is: Option D

選項 A (Option A) - ❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect)

雖然保住工作的工人能賺取更高工資，但由於勞力需求量下降，部分低技術工人會失業，收入變為零。因此，低收入群體內部的貧富懸殊可能會惡化，收入分布未必變得更平均。

Although workers who keep their jobs earn a higher wage, the decrease in the quantity demanded of labor means some low-skilled workers will become unemployed, reducing their income to zero. Therefore, income disparity among the low-income group may worsen, and income distribution will not necessarily become more even.

選項 B (Option B) - ❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect)

工人的總收入 \( TR = W \times Q_d \)。總收入會否上升，取決於勞力需求的價格彈性（Price elasticity of demand for labor）。如果勞力需求是具彈性的（Elastic），工資上升的百分比會小於就業人數下降的百分比，導致總收入下跌。因此這不是「最有可能」的必然結果。

Total wage earnings \( TR = W \times Q_d \). Whether total earnings rise depends on the price elasticity of demand for labor. If labor demand is elastic, the percentage increase in wage will be smaller than the percentage decrease in employment, leading to a fall in total earnings. Therefore, this is not a guaranteed or "most likely" outcome.

選項 C (Option C) - ❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect)

就業人口等於市場實際交易量。在有效最低工資下，交易量由需求量 \( Q_d \) 決定。由於工資上升，僱主減少聘人，就業人口必然減少，而非增加。

The employed population equals the actual transacted quantity in the market. Under an effective minimum wage, the transacted quantity is determined by the quantity demanded \( Q_d \). As wages rise, employers hire fewer people, so the employed population must decrease, not increase.

選項 D (Option D) - ✅ 正確 (Correct)

由於最低工資導致勞力供應量 \( Q_s \) 大於需求量 \( Q_d \)，市場出現勞力過剩（求職者多於職位空缺）。法例禁止求職者以低於最低工資的價格競爭，因此賣方（工人）之間必然會出現非價格競爭（如：進修考取證書、改善面試表現等）來爭奪有限的職位。

Since the minimum wage causes the quantity supplied of labor \( Q_s \) to exceed the quantity demanded \( Q_d \), a labor surplus occurs (more job seekers than vacancies). The law prohibits job seekers from competing by offering to work for less than the minimum wage, so non-price competition (e.g., getting certificates, improving interview skills) will inevitably emerge among sellers (workers) to secure the limited jobs.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point)： 看到「有效的」(Effective) 這個詞，你必須立刻聯想到：價格被人為推高 \( \implies \) 出現過剩 (Surplus) \( \implies \) 賣方無法減價 \( \implies \) 衍生非價格競爭。

The Pivot Point: Whenever you see the word "Effective" regarding a price floor, you must immediately deduce: Price is artificially raised \( \implies \) Surplus occurs \( \implies \) Sellers cannot lower prices \( \implies \) Non-price competition emerges.

干擾項 (Distractors)： 選項 A 和 B 是利用了學生的「直覺誤區」。一般人認為最低工資是「幫助窮人」，所以直覺會選收入增加（B）或更平均（A）。但經濟學要求我們看見「看不見的代價」——即被解僱的邊緣工人。

Distractors: Options A and B exploit students' "intuitive traps." Laypeople assume minimum wages "help the poor," leading to intuitive guesses of higher income (B) or more even distribution (A). However, Economics requires us to see the "unseen costs"—namely, the marginal workers who lose their jobs.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  1. 混淆「勞力需求」與「勞力供應」： 很多學生忘記了在勞工市場中，工人是供應者 (Suppliers)，而廠商是需求者 (Demanders)。非價格競爭是發生在「過剩」的一方，即工人之間。

    Confusing Labor Demand and Supply: Many students forget that in the labor market, workers are the suppliers, and firms are the demanders. Non-price competition occurs among the party in "surplus," which is the workers.

  2. 忽視彈性對總收入的影響： 以為工資 \( W \) 上升，總收入 \( TR \) 必定上升。忘記了這完全取決於勞力需求彈性 \( E_d \)。

    Ignoring Elasticity's Effect on Total Revenue: Assuming that if wage \( W \) increases, total revenue (earnings) \( TR \) must increase. They forget this entirely depends on the elasticity of labor demand \( E_d \).

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

學生問： 選項 D 說的「非價格競爭」，有沒有可能發生在僱主（買方）身上？

Student asks: Could the "non-price competition" mentioned in Option D happen among employers (buyers)?

導師答： 不會。因為最低工資造成的是「勞力過剩」(Surplus)。市場上有很多工人爭奪少量職位，僱主是佔優勢的一方。僱主不需要競爭來請人，反而是工人需要競爭來獲得僱主青睞。如果今天是「最高工資」或「價格上限」（造成短缺 Shortage），那麼非價格競爭就會發生在買方（僱主）身上了！

Tutor answers: No. This is because a minimum wage creates a "labor surplus." There are many workers competing for a few jobs, so employers have the upper hand. Employers don't need to compete to hire; workers must compete to be chosen. If this were a "maximum wage" or "price ceiling" (causing a Shortage), then non-price competition would occur among the buyers (employers)!

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

秒殺口訣 (One-Second Kill Formula)：

  • 有效價格下限 (Price Floor) = 過剩 (Surplus) = 賣方非價格競爭
  • 有效價格上限 (Price Ceiling) = 短缺 (Shortage) = 買方非價格競爭

Quick Recall Formula:

  • Effective Price Floor = Surplus = Non-price competition among Sellers
  • Effective Price Ceiling = Shortage = Non-price competition among Buyers

看到「最低工資」（下限），直接找「失業/過剩」或「賣方非價格競爭」的選項，直接選D，節省時間！

When you see "minimum wage" (floor), look immediately for options indicating "unemployment/surplus" or "non-price competition among sellers." Choose D directly and save time!
