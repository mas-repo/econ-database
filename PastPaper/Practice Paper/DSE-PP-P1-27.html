Topic: Money & Banking 貨幣與銀行 Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Key Concept: Credit Creation 信用創造 Key Concept: Reserve Ratio 法定儲備比率

1. 題目 (The Question)

假設某銀行體系的資產負債表如下： Suppose the balance sheet of a banking system is as follows:
資產 Assets ($)負債 Liabilities ($)
儲備 Reserves150存款 Deposits500
貸款 Loans350
該銀行體系的超額儲備是 $50。現在一名客戶從他的銀行戶口提取 $50。若該銀行體系將可貸出款項全數借出，該銀行體系的貸款額最大為_______。 The amount of excess reserves in the banking system is $50. Now, a customer withdraws $50 from his bank account. If the banking system is fully loaned up, the maximum amount of loans in the banking system is _______.
A. $350
B. $400
C. $450
D. $500

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

法定儲備比率 (Required Reserve Ratio, RRR):
銀行必須保留在保險箱或中央銀行的存款比例，不能全數借出。 The fraction of deposits that banks are required to keep in their vaults or at the central bank, which cannot be loaned out.

超額儲備 (Excess Reserves, ER):
銀行持有的實際儲備減去法定儲備。只要 $ER > 0$，銀行就可以創造更多貸款。 Actual reserves minus required reserves. As long as $ER > 0$, banks can create more loans.

全數借出 (Fully Loaned Up):
當銀行將所有超額儲備借出，直到超額儲備為 $0$ ($ER = 0$)，此時存款和貸款達到最大值。 When banks lend out all excess reserves until $ER = 0$, reaching maximum deposits and loans.

$$ Actual\ Reserves = Required\ Reserves\ (RR) + Excess\ Reserves\ (ER) $$
$$ Required\ Reserve\ Ratio\ (RRR) = \frac{Required\ Reserves}{Deposits} $$
$$ Maximum\ Deposits = \frac{Actual\ Reserves}{Required\ Reserve\ Ratio} $$
$$ Maximum\ Loans = Maximum\ Deposits - Actual\ Reserves $$

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是 (The correct option is): Option B

第一步：找出法定儲備比率 (Step 1: Find the RRR)

根據題目，實際儲備是 150，超額儲備是 50。由此可知，法定儲備 (RR) 是 $150 - 50 = 100$。因此，法定儲備比率是 $\frac{100}{500} = 20\%$。 Given actual reserves are 150 and excess reserves are 50, the Required Reserves (RR) = $150 - 50 = 100$. Therefore, RRR = $\frac{100}{500} = 20\%$.

第二步：處理提款 (Step 2: Process the withdrawal)

客戶提取 50。這會同時減少銀行的實際儲備和存款。新的實際儲備變成 $150 - 50 = 100$，新的存款變成 $500 - 50 = 450$。 A customer withdraws 50. This reduces both actual reserves and deposits. New Actual Reserves = $150 - 50 = 100$, New Deposits = $500 - 50 = 450$.

第三步：計算「全數借出」後的最大值 (Step 3: Calculate maximums when "fully loaned up")

「全數借出」意味著系統達到極限，超額儲備為 0。此時，最大存款取決於剩下的實際儲備 (100) 和 RRR (20%)。最大存款 = $\frac{100}{0.2} = 500$。最大貸款 = 最大存款 - 實際儲備 = $500 - 100 = 400$。 "Fully loaned up" means the system reaches its limit with 0 excess reserves. Max Deposits depends on the remaining actual reserves (100) and RRR (20%). Max Deposits = $\frac{100}{0.2} = 500$. Max Loans = Max Deposits - Actual Reserves = $500 - 100 = 400$.

選項分析 (Options Breakdown):

A. $350

這是提款前的初始貸款額。這沒有計算提款後重新創造信用帶來的影響。 This is the initial loan amount before the withdrawal. It ignores the credit creation process after the withdrawal.

B. $400

正確計算。最大貸款為 $400。 Correct calculation. Maximum loans are $400.

C. $450

這是提款後的存款餘額 ($500 - 50 = 450$)，並非最大貸款額。 This is the deposit level immediately after withdrawal ($500 - 50 = 450$), not the maximum loans.

D. $500

這是系統全數借出後的「最大存款」(Maximum Deposits)，考生容易將其與「最大貸款」(Maximum Loans) 混淆。 This is the "Maximum Deposits" when fully loaned up. Students often confuse this with Maximum Loans.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題切入點 (The Pivot Point):
做任何銀行乘數題目前，第一件事永遠是尋找法定儲備比率 (RRR)！題目故意不直接給你 RRR，而是給你「實際儲備」和「超額儲備」，這是破題的關鍵鑰匙。 Before doing any banking multiplier question, always find the RRR first! The question doesn't give you RRR directly, but gives you "actual reserves" and "excess reserves", which is the key to unlocking the problem.

干擾資訊 (Distractors):
初始的「貸款 $350」其實是多餘的資訊。只要你有儲備、存款和超額儲備的數據，你就能算出所有未來的最大值。 The initial "Loans $350" is actually redundant information. As long as you have the data for reserves, deposits, and excess reserves, you can calculate all future maximums.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱一：忘記提款是雙重扣減 (Trap 1: Forgetting withdrawal is a double deduction)
當現金流出銀行系統時，必須同時從「實際儲備」和「存款」中扣除。很多學生只扣減存款，忘記扣減儲備。 When cash flows out of the banking system, it must be deducted from BOTH "actual reserves" and "deposits". Many students only deduct from deposits and forget to deduct reserves.

陷阱二：混淆最大存款與最大貸款 (Trap 2: Confusing Max Deposits with Max Loans)
考試時很多學生算出了 $100 / 0.2 = 500$，看到選項 D 有 500 就立刻圈選。緊記公式：$Maximum\ Loans = Maximum\ Deposits - Actual\ Reserves$。 In exams, many calculate $100 / 0.2 = 500$, see Option D, and immediately choose it. Remember the formula: Maximum Loans = Maximum Deposits - Actual Reserves.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 客戶提取了 50，為什麼銀行不需要馬上收回貸款 (Call back loans)？

A: 因為提款後，新的法定儲備要求是 $450 \times 20\% = 90$。而銀行剩下的實際儲備是 100 ($150 - 50$)。這意味著銀行仍然有 10 ($100 - 90$) 的超額儲備，不用收回貸款，甚至還可以繼續借出！
Q: The customer withdrew 50, why didn't the bank need to call back loans immediately?
A: Because after withdrawal, the new required reserves is $450 \times 20\% = 90$. The remaining actual reserves is 100 ($150 - 50$). This means the bank still has 10 ($100 - 90$) in excess reserves. It doesn't need to call back loans and can even lend more!

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

如果你不想算得那麼複雜，你可以使用「最終狀態」速算法：
If you want a shortcut, use the "Final State" method:

  1. 找出最終留在銀行體系內的儲備：$150 - 50 = 100$。 (Find final reserves inside the system)
  2. 找出 RRR：$100 / 500 = 20\%$。 (Find RRR)
  3. 直接使用最大貸款捷徑公式：$Maximum\ Loans = Actual\ Reserves \times (\frac{1}{RRR} - 1)$
    計算：$100 \times (\frac{1}{0.2} - 1) = 100 \times (5 - 1) = 100 \times 4 = 400$。

10 秒鐘就能得出答案！You get the answer in 10 seconds!
