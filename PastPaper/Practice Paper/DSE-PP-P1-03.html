Topic: 利息的概念 (Concepts of Interest)
Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts: Interest, Barter Economy, Present Consumption

1. The Question (題目)

下列哪些有關利息的陳述是正確的？

Which of the following statements about interest are correct?

  1. 利息是就延遲當時消費所收取的一個溢價。
    (1) Interest is a premium received for postponing current consumption.
  2. 在以物易物經濟中，利率並不存在。
    (2) An interest rate does not exist in a barter economy.
  3. 利率下降會增加現時的消費。
    (3) A fall in the interest rate increases present consumption.

A. 只有(1)、(2)
A. (1) and (2) only

B. 只有(1)、(3)
B. (1) and (3) only

C. 只有(2)、(3)
C. (2) and (3) only

D. (1)、(2)及(3)
D. (1), (2) and (3)

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

利息的定義 (Definition of Interest): 根據費雪 (Irving Fisher) 的理論，利息是提早獲取物品的代價，或延遲消費的補償 (Premium for earlier availability of goods / Premium for postponing consumption)。

According to Irving Fisher's theory, interest is the price paid for the earlier availability of goods, or the compensation/premium for postponing consumption.

以物易物與利息 (Barter Economy & Interest): 利息的核心是「時間偏好 (Time Preference)」，與是否使用貨幣無關。只要人們偏好「現在擁有」多於「未來擁有」，利息就會存在。這稱為實質利率 (Real Interest Rate)。

The core of interest is "Time Preference" and has nothing to do with the existence of money. As long as people prefer having goods "now" rather than "later," interest will exist. This is known as the Real Interest Rate.

現時消費的代價 (Price of Present Consumption): 利率可以視為現時消費的價格。當你決定現在消費，你放棄了把資源借出以賺取利息的機會。

The interest rate can be viewed as the price of present consumption. When you choose to consume now, you give up the opportunity to lend the resources out to earn interest.

現時消費的價格 (Price of Present Consumption)
$$ P = 1 + r $$
Where \( P \) is the relative price of present consumption, and \( r \) is the interest rate.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 Correct Option: B

分析陳述 (1) / Analysis of Statement (1): CORRECT ✔️

這是正確的。根據經濟學定義，放棄現時消費（即儲蓄或借出資源）會帶來機會成本。為了鼓勵人們延遲消費，借款人必須支付一個溢價（即利息）作為補償。

This is correct. According to economic definition, giving up current consumption (i.e., saving or lending resources) incurs an opportunity cost. To encourage people to postpone consumption, borrowers must pay a premium (interest) as compensation.

分析陳述 (2) / Analysis of Statement (2): WRONG ❌

這是錯誤的。利息的存在取決於時間偏好，而不是貨幣。即使在以物易物經濟中，如果你今天借給別人10個蘋果，要求對方明年歸還11個蘋果，那額外的1個蘋果就是「實質利息」。因此，利率在以物易物經濟中仍然存在。

This is incorrect. The existence of interest depends on time preference, not money. Even in a barter economy, if you lend someone 10 apples today and ask them to return 11 apples next year, that 1 extra apple is the "real interest." Therefore, an interest rate still exists in a barter economy.

分析陳述 (3) / Analysis of Statement (3): CORRECT ✔️

這是正確的。因為現時消費的價格是 \( 1 + r \)（其中 \( r \) 為利率）。當利率 \( r \) 下降時，現時消費的價格便會下降。根據需求定律 (Law of Demand)，價格下降會導致現時消費的數量增加。

This is correct. Because the price of present consumption is \( 1 + r \) (where \( r \) is the interest rate). When the interest rate \( r \) falls, the price of present consumption falls. According to the Law of Demand, a lower price will lead to an increase in the quantity demanded of present consumption.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (破題關鍵): 這題的核心在於測試學生是否明白「利息不等於金錢」。很多學生誤以為沒有貨幣就沒有利息，但經濟學上的利息概念是建基於實物與時間 (Real resources and time) 的關係。

Pivot Point: The core of this question is testing whether students understand that "interest is not equal to money." Many students mistakenly believe that without fiat money, there is no interest. However, the economic concept of interest is based on the relationship between real resources and time.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ 概念陷阱 (Conceptual Trap): 將「貨幣 (Money)」與「利息 (Interest)」掛鉤。
DSE 經常利用「以物易物經濟 (Barter Economy)」這個情境來設陷阱。請謹記：沒有貨幣（名義變量），仍然會有實質利率（實質變量）。

Conceptual Trap: Equating "Money" exclusively with "Interest". DSE frequently uses the "Barter Economy" scenario to set traps. Remember: Without money (nominal variables), real interest rates (real variables) still exist.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 既然以物易物沒有銀行，利息是怎樣決定的？
Q: Since there are no banks in a barter economy, how is the interest rate determined?

A: 利率是由市場上人們對「現在擁有」與「未來擁有」的偏好（時間偏好）透過借貸行為互動而決定的。例如農夫借出種子給鄰居，約定收成後歸還更多種子，這多出來的部分就是市場決定的實質利息。
A: The interest rate is determined by the interaction of people's time preferences (preference for having things "now" vs. "later") through borrowing and lending. For example, a farmer lending seeds to a neighbor with the agreement to receive more seeds after harvest establishes a market-determined real interest rate.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

💡 消除法 (Elimination Method)

結果：你甚至不需要閱讀陳述 (1) 和 (3)，就能在 5 秒內選出正確答案 B！

In HKDSE Economics, "interest does not exist in a barter economy" is a classic false statement that has appeared countless times. Once you instantly recognize that Statement (2) is false, you can immediately cross out options A, C, and D (since they all include 2).
Result: You don't even need to read statements (1) and (3) to arrive at the correct answer B in under 5 seconds!
