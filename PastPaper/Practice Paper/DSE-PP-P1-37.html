📝 HKDSE Economics Question Analysis

📚 課題 Topic: 公共財政 Public Finance 賦稅原則 Principles of Taxation

⭐ 難度 Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆ (2/5)

🔑 核心概念 Key Concepts: 亞當史密夫 Adam Smith 明確原則 Certainty

1. 題目 The Question

[繁體中文] 稅務局會透過郵件或稅務易戶口通知每位薪俸稅納稅人應付的稅款總額。這項安排符合亞當史密夫所提出的_______賦稅原則。

  • A. 公平
  • B. 明確
  • C. 方便
  • D. 經濟

[English] The Inland Revenue Department will inform every salaries tax payer of the amount of tax payable either by mail or by the etax account. This practice is in line with the _______ principle of taxation put forward by Adam Smith.

  • A. equity
  • B. certainty
  • C. convenience
  • D. economy

2. 概念重溫 Concept Review

要解答這題，我們必須清楚分辨亞當史密夫（Adam Smith）提出的四大賦稅原則：

To answer this question, we must clearly distinguish the four canons of taxation proposed by Adam Smith:

  1. 公平 (Equity): 稅收應按納稅人的負擔能力（Ability-to-pay）來徵收。高收入者應繳納更多的稅款。
    Taxes should be levied according to the taxpayer's ability to pay. Higher-income earners should pay more taxes.
  2. 明確 (Certainty): 繳稅的金額、時間和方式必須清晰明確，不可隨意更改，讓納稅人有預算。
    The amount, time, and manner of payment must be clear and plain to the contributor, avoiding arbitrary changes and allowing for proper budgeting.
  3. 方便 (Convenience): 繳稅的時間和方式應盡量為納稅人提供便利（例如：容許分期付款、提供多種繳費渠道）。
    The time and manner of payment should be convenient to the taxpayer (e.g., allowing installment payments, providing various payment channels).
  4. 經濟 (Economy): 徵稅的行政成本（Administrative cost）應減至最低，以確保大部分稅收能用於公共開支。
    The administrative cost of tax collection should be minimized to ensure most revenue goes to public expenditure.

經濟原則公式表示 (Economy Principle expressed as an equation):

$$ \text{Net Tax Revenue} = \text{Total Tax Revenue collected} - \text{Administrative Costs} $$

稅務局的目標是將行政成本減至最低，令淨稅收最大化。
The IRD's goal is to minimize administrative costs to maximize net tax revenue.

3. 答案與詳細分析 Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Option: B. 明確 (Certainty)

選項 A: 公平 (Equity) - ❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect)

題目沒有提及稅款如何根據納稅人的收入或負擔能力來計算。
The question does not mention how the tax is calculated based on the taxpayer's income or ability to pay.

選項 B: 明確 (Certainty) - ✅ 正確 (Correct)

稅務局主動發出通知，告訴納稅人「應付的稅款總額」(amount of tax payable $T$)，這讓納稅人準確知道自己需要繳交多少錢，消除了不確定性，完美體現了「明確」原則。
The IRD proactively issues notices to inform taxpayers of the "amount of tax payable" $T$. This allows taxpayers to know exactly how much they owe, eliminating uncertainty and perfectly reflecting the principle of "certainty".

選項 C: 方便 (Convenience) - ❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect)

雖然使用「稅務易」聽起來很方便，但題目的重點是「通知稅款總額」這個動作，而不是「提供方便的繳稅途徑」。如果是方便原則，題目通常會寫「市民可透過便利店或稅務易『繳交』稅款」。
Although "eTax" sounds convenient, the focus of the question is the action of "informing the amount", not "providing a convenient payment method". If it were the convenience principle, the question would usually state "citizens can *pay* taxes via convenience stores or eTax".

選項 D: 經濟 (Economy) - ❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect)

題目沒有提供任何關於稅務局徵稅成本（如節省紙張、減少人手等）的資訊。
The question provides no information regarding the tax collection costs (e.g., saving paper, reducing manpower) of the IRD.

4. 題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

這類題目的關鍵在於找出「題眼」（Pivot Point），同時避開考評局設置的「干擾資訊」（Distractor）。

The key to these questions is finding the "Pivot Point" while avoiding the "Distractor" set by the HKEAA.

  • 🎯 題眼 Pivot Point: 「通知...應付的稅款總額」 (inform... the amount of tax payable).
    分析： 知道要交多少錢 $\rightarrow$ 清晰無誤 $\rightarrow$ 明確 (Certainty)。
    Analysis: Knowing exactly how much to pay $\rightarrow$ Clear and plain $\rightarrow$ Certainty.
  • 👻 干擾資訊 Distractor: 「透過郵件或稅務易戶口」 (by mail or by the etax account).
    分析： 很多同學看到「稅務易 (eTax)」就立刻聯想到「方便 (Convenience)」。但請注意，這裡是透過 eTax 收通知，而不是討論付款方式的便利性。
    Analysis: Many students see "eTax" and immediately associate it with "Convenience". However, notice that this is about receiving a notice via eTax, not about the convenience of the payment method.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

⚠️ 混淆「明確」與「方便」 (Confusing Certainty and Convenience)

同學經常在B和C之間猶豫。記住一個簡單的區分方法：
如果是關於「資訊的透明度」（Know What, When, How），這就是明確。
如果是關於「行動的省時省力」（Easy to do），這才是方便。

Students often hesitate between B and C. Remember a simple way to distinguish them:
If it's about "transparency of information" (Knowing What, When, How), it is Certainty.
If it's about "saving time and effort in action" (Easy to do), it is Convenience.

6. 學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

🙋‍♂️ 學生問 (Student): 如果題目改成「稅務局全面推行電子化報稅，大幅減少了處理實體文件的職員人數」，那答案會是什麼？
Q: If the question changed to "The IRD fully implemented electronic tax filing, significantly reducing the number of staff needed to process physical documents", what would the answer be?

👨‍🏫 導師答 (Tutor): 答案會變成「經濟 (Economy)」。因為「減少職員人數」代表大幅降低了徵收稅款的行政成本。
A: The answer would become "Economy". Because "reducing the number of staff" represents a significant decrease in the administrative costs of tax collection.

7. 考試技巧與速解 Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

在 DSE 經濟科考試中，遇到「亞當史密夫四大原則」的題目，可以使用「關鍵字秒殺法」 (Keyword Matching)：

In the DSE Economics exam, when encountering questions on "Adam Smith's 4 Canons", you can use the Keyword Matching Technique:

原則 (Principle) 秒殺關鍵字 (Keywords to Look For)
公平 Equity 負擔能力 (Ability to pay), 收入高低 (Income level), 累進稅 (Progressive tax)
明確 Certainty 通知稅額 (Inform amount), 指引清晰 (Clear guidelines), 避免濫收 (Prevent arbitrary collection)
方便 Convenience 繳交途徑多 (Multiple payment channels), 網上繳費 (Pay online), 分期 (Installment)
經濟 Economy 行政費用 (Admin cost), 節省人手/紙張 (Save manpower/paper), 淨稅收 (Net revenue)
