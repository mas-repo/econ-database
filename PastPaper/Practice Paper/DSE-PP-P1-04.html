Topic: Types of Production & Interdependence
課題：生產種類與相互依賴

Difficulty: ★☆☆☆☆ Primary Production Secondary Production Tertiary Production
下列哪項對三類生產之間的關係的描述是正確的？
Which of the following descriptions about the relationship between the three types of production is correct?
  • A. 第一級生產為其他類型的生產提供原材料。
    Primary production provides raw materials for the other types of production.
  • B. 第二級生產毋須依賴第三級生產去製造完成品。
    Secondary production does not rely on tertiary production to produce finished goods.
  • C. 第三級生產只依賴第一級生產。
    Tertiary production relies only on primary production.
  • D. 三類生產是獨立的，其中第三級生產為最重要。
    The three types of production are independent, and tertiary production is the most important.
Concept Review (概念重溫)
1. 生產的三種類型 (Three Types of Production):
  • 第一級生產 (Primary): 直接從大自然提取天然資源（如：農業、漁業、採礦）。
    Extraction of natural resources directly from nature (e.g., farming, fishing, mining).
  • 第二級生產 (Secondary): 將原材料加工轉化為製成品（如：製造業、建造業）。
    Processing raw materials into finished goods (e.g., manufacturing, construction).
  • 第三級生產 (Tertiary): 提供服務（如：運輸、零售、金融、保險）。
    Providing services (e.g., transport, retail, finance, insurance).
2. 相互依賴 (Interdependence):
在現代經濟中，這三個階段是互相依賴的，沒有任何一個階段可以獨立運作。
In a modern economy, these three stages are interdependent. No single stage can operate in isolation.
Correct Option: A

Detailed Analysis (詳細分析)

Option A: Correct (正確)

分析：第一級生產涉及開採天然資源（如木材、金屬礦石、農作物）。這些資源是第二級生產（如製造傢俬、煉鋼）和第三級生產（如餐廳使用蔬菜）的基礎。
Analysis: Primary production involves extracting natural resources (e.g., timber, metal ores, crops). These resources serve as the foundation for Secondary production (e.g., making furniture, steel) and Tertiary production (e.g., restaurants using vegetables).

Option B: Incorrect (錯誤)

分析：第二級生產（工廠）高度依賴第三級生產。例如，工廠需要電力供應（服務）、運輸原材料和製成品（服務）、以及購買保險（服務）。
Analysis: Secondary production (factories) relies heavily on Tertiary production. For example, factories need electricity supply (service), transportation for materials and goods (service), and insurance (service).

Option C: Incorrect (錯誤)

分析：第三級生產不只依賴第一級生產，它也依賴第二級生產。例如，銀行（第三級）需要電腦和建築物（第二級產品）來運作。
Analysis: Tertiary production does not rely only on Primary production; it also relies on Secondary production. For instance, a bank (Tertiary) needs computers and buildings (Secondary goods) to operate.

Option D: Incorrect (錯誤)

分析：這是經濟學中的大忌。三類生產是相互依賴 (Interdependent) 的，並非獨立。此外，我們不能客觀地說哪一類生產「最重要」，因為缺一不可。
Analysis: This is a major misconception. The three types of production are interdependent, not independent. Furthermore, we cannot objectively say which type is "most important" as all are essential.

🎯 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

1. 關鍵字識別 (Identify Keywords):
題目問的是「關係」(Relationship)。在經濟學中，生產類型的關係關鍵詞永遠是 「相互依賴」(Interdependence)。
The question asks about "Relationship". In Economics, the keyword for the relationship between production types is always "Interdependence".
2. 排除絕對詞 (Eliminate Absolute Words):
選項 B 使用了「毋須依賴」(does not rely)，選項 C 使用了「只」(only)，選項 D 使用了「獨立」(independent)。在描述經濟體系各部門關係時，這些絕對分割的詞彙通常暗示該選項是錯誤的。
Option B uses "does not rely", Option C uses "only", and Option D uses "independent". When describing the relationship between economic sectors, these words suggesting absolute separation usually indicate the option is wrong.
Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)
Q: 為什麼餐廳屬於第三級生產？它不是在「製造」食物嗎？
Q: Why are restaurants classified as Tertiary production? Aren't they "making" food?
A: 雖然廚師涉及烹飪（加工），但在經濟學分類中，餐廳主要提供的是餐飲服務 (Catering Service)，因此歸類為第三級生產。如果是食品加工廠製作罐頭食品，那就是第二級生產。
A: Although chefs cook (process) food, in economic classification, restaurants primarily provide a Catering Service, so they fall under Tertiary production. If it were a food processing factory making canned food, that would be Secondary production.

⚡ Exam Tricks (考試速解)

看到這類題目，直接尋找描述「合作」或「提供支援」的選項。任何暗示「某個行業可以完全獨立存在」的選項，99% 都是錯誤的。
When you see this type of question, look directly for options describing "cooperation" or "providing support". Any option implying that "a sector can exist completely independently" is 99% likely to be incorrect.
