📊 Topic (課題): International Trade - Absolute and Comparative Advantage (國際貿易：絕對優勢與比較優勢)

⭐ Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆ (2/5)

🔑 Key Concepts (核心概念): Absolute Advantage (絕對優勢), Comparative Advantage (比較優勢), Opportunity Cost (機會成本)

1. The Question (題目)

假設甲、乙兩國只生產及消費物品X及物品Y。若甲國比乙國可用較少資源生產一單位的物品X，
Assume both Country A and Country B produce and consume Good X and Good Y only. If Country A can produce a unit of Good X with less resources than Country B,
(1) 甲國生產物品X有絕對優勢。
(2) 甲國生產物品X有相對優勢。
(3) 乙國生產物品Y有絕對優勢。
(4) 乙國生產物品Y有相對優勢。
(1) Country A has an absolute advantage in producing Good X.
(2) Country A has a comparative advantage in producing Good X.
(3) Country B has an absolute advantage in producing Good Y.
(4) Country B has a comparative advantage in producing Good Y.
A. 只有(1)
B. 只有(1)、(2)
C. 只有(2)、(4)
D. (1)、(2)、(3)及(4)
A. (1) only
B. (1) and (2) only
C. (2) and (4) only
D. (1), (2), (3) and (4)

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 絕對優勢 (Absolute Advantage)
指一個國家在生產某物品時，能以較少的資源生產相同數量的物品，或以相同的資源生產較多的物品。這單純是生產力或投入資源的絕對比較。
Refers to a situation where a country can produce the same quantity of a good using fewer resources, or produce more goods using the same amount of resources. This is purely an absolute comparison of productivity or resource input.
2. 比較優勢 / 相對優勢 (Comparative Advantage)
指一個國家在生產某物品時，其機會成本較低。要計算機會成本，我們必須同時知道生產兩種物品（物品X和物品Y）的數據。
Refers to a situation where a country can produce a good at a lower opportunity cost. To calculate opportunity cost, we MUST have data regarding the production of BOTH goods (Good X and Good Y).
機會成本公式 (Opportunity Cost Formula) - Input Approach (投入資源法):
$$ \text{Opportunity Cost of 1 unit of X} = \frac{\text{Resources needed for 1X}}{\text{Resources needed for 1Y}} \text{ units of Y} $$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是 / The correct option is: A
分析 (1)：甲國生產物品X有絕對優勢 (正確)
題目清楚指出「甲國比乙國可用較少資源生產一單位的物品X」。根據定義，投入較少資源代表擁有絕對優勢。因此，(1) 是必定正確的。
Analysis of (1): Country A has an absolute advantage in Good X (TRUE).
The question explicitly states that "Country A can produce a unit of Good X with less resources than Country B." By definition, using fewer resources means having an absolute advantage. Therefore, (1) is definitely true.
分析 (2)：甲國生產物品X有相對優勢 (不確定/錯誤)
相對優勢取決於機會成本。雖然甲國生產X的絕對資源較少，但我們完全不知道甲國生產物品Y需要多少資源。如果甲國生產Y的效率極高（例如只需極微少的資源），那麼甲國生產X的機會成本反而會很高，相對優勢可能在乙國。由於資料不足，不能斷定(2)正確。
Analysis of (2): Country A has a comparative advantage in Good X (UNCERTAIN/FALSE).
Comparative advantage depends on opportunity cost. Although Country A uses fewer absolute resources for X, we do not know how many resources are needed for Good Y. If Country A is exceptionally efficient at producing Y, its opportunity cost for producing X might be very high, giving the comparative advantage to Country B. Due to insufficient data, we cannot conclude (2) is true.
分析 (3) 及 (4)：乙國在物品Y的優勢 (不確定/錯誤)
題目完全沒有提及任何關於生產物品Y的資源數據。因此，我們無法比較兩國在生產物品Y上的絕對資源投入，也無法計算機會成本。所以 (3) 和 (4) 都不能確定。
Analysis of (3) & (4): Country B's advantages in Good Y (UNCERTAIN/FALSE).
The question provides absolutely zero data regarding the resources needed to produce Good Y. Therefore, we can neither compare the absolute resource inputs for Good Y nor calculate the opportunity costs. Thus, both (3) and (4) cannot be determined.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)：
這條題目的核心在於區分「單一物品的資源投入」與「兩種物品的機會成本」。只要留意到題目只提供了物品X的資料，而欠缺物品Y的資料，就能立即判斷任何涉及「機會成本」或「比較優勢」的選項都是無法證實的。
Pivot Point:
The core of this question lies in distinguishing between "resource input for a single good" and "opportunity cost involving two goods". Once you notice the question only provides data for Good X and lacks data for Good Y, you can immediately deduce that any option involving "opportunity cost" or "comparative advantage" cannot be proven.
干擾資訊 (Distractors)：
題目開頭說「生產及消費物品X及物品Y」。這只是設定國際貿易的背景，誘導學生以為可以推算出Y的結果。
Distractors:
The opening phrase "produce and consume Good X and Good Y" simply sets the stage for international trade, attempting to trick students into thinking they can somehow infer the results for Good Y.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

❌ 陷阱：將絕對優勢等同於比較優勢
很多學生誤以為「只要我在這方面比你強（絕對優勢），我就應該專門做這件事（比較優勢）」。這在經濟學上是錯誤的！例如，一個頂級律師打字的速度（絕對優勢）可能比他的秘書快，但他打字的機會成本（放棄處理高薪法律案件的時間）極高。因此，律師打字沒有比較優勢。
❌ Trap: Equating Absolute Advantage with Comparative Advantage
Many students mistakenly believe that "if I am absolutely better at this, I must have the comparative advantage in it." This is economically false! For example, a top lawyer might type faster than their secretary (absolute advantage), but the lawyer's opportunity cost of typing (giving up time for high-paying legal cases) is extremely high. Thus, the lawyer does NOT have a comparative advantage in typing.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 題目需要提供甚麼額外資訊，我們才能判斷選項 (2) 和 (4) 是否正確？
Q: What additional information would the question need to provide for us to determine if statements (2) and (4) are correct?
A: 我們需要知道甲國和乙國生產一單位物品Y所需的資源數量。有了這兩項數據，我們就可以使用公式 \(\frac{X\text{的資源}}{Y\text{的資源}}\) 來計算兩國生產物品X的機會成本，然後比較誰的機會成本較低，從而找出比較優勢。
A: We would need to know the amount of resources required to produce one unit of Good Y in both Country A and Country B. With this data, we could use the formula \(\frac{\text{Resources for X}}{\text{Resources for Y}}\) to calculate the opportunity cost of producing Good X for both countries, compare them, and find out who has the lower opportunity cost (and thus, the comparative advantage).

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

🚀 10秒速解法 (10-Second Shortcut):
在DSE考試中，當你看到題目只給出一種物品的生產數據（或沒有完整列出兩國兩種物品的生產矩陣），你可以立刻斷定：
1. 無法計算機會成本。
2. 無法判斷任何「相對優勢 / 比較優勢」。
馬上刪去包含 (2) 和 (4) 的選項（剔除 C 和 D）。由於沒有Y的資料，(3) 也無從判斷（剔除 D）。答案立刻鎖定為 A。
🚀 10-Second Shortcut:
In the DSE exam, when you see a question that only provides production data for ONE good (or fails to provide the full 2x2 production matrix), you can instantly conclude:
1. You cannot calculate opportunity costs.
2. You cannot determine ANY "comparative advantage".
Immediately eliminate any options containing (2) and (4) (cross out C and D). Since there's no data for Y, (3) is also unknown. You are instantly left with Option A.
