HKDSE Economics: 題目詳解 (2012 Practice Paper)

Topic: 香港稅制 (Taxation in Hong Kong) Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆ Key Concepts: 直接稅, 間接稅, 比例稅, 累進稅

2. 題目 (The Question)

[中文版]
在香港，下列哪種稅項屬直接稅和比例稅？
A. 酒店房租稅
B. 利得稅
C. 薪俸稅
D. 差餉

[English Version]
Which of the following is a direct and proportional tax in Hong Kong?
A. hotel accommodation tax
B. profits tax
C. salaries tax
D. general rates

3. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

稅收分類 (Tax Classifications)

稅收主要可以根據稅務負擔能否轉嫁以及稅率的變化來分類。
Taxes can be classified based on whether the tax burden can be shifted and how the tax rate changes.

  • 直接稅 (Direct Tax): 納稅人 (Taxpayer) 與 負稅人 (Tax bearer) 是同一人。稅務負擔不能轉嫁。例子：薪俸稅、利得稅。
    The taxpayer and the tax bearer are the same person. The tax burden cannot be shifted. Examples: Salaries Tax, Profits Tax.
  • 間接稅 (Indirect Tax): 納稅人可以將部分或全部稅務負擔轉嫁給其他人（通常是消費者）。例子：差餉、酒店房租稅、博彩稅。
    The taxpayer can shift part or all of the tax burden to others (usually consumers). Examples: General Rates, Hotel Accommodation Tax, Betting Duty.
  • 比例稅 (Proportional Tax): 平均稅率 (Average Tax Rate) 在任何入息或稅基水平下都保持不變。
    The Average Tax Rate remains constant at any level of income or tax base.
  • 累進稅 (Progressive Tax): 平均稅率會隨着入息或稅基增加而上升。
    The Average Tax Rate increases as the income or tax base increases.
平均稅率公式 (Average Tax Rate Formula): $$ \text{Average Tax Rate} = \frac{\text{Tax Paid } (T)}{\text{Income / Tax Base } (Y)} \times 100\% $$

對於比例稅而言，無論 \(Y\) 的數值是多少，其平均稅率 \( \frac{T}{Y} \) 皆為常數。
For a proportional tax, regardless of the value of \(Y\), its average tax rate \( \frac{T}{Y} \) is a constant.

4. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是 (Correct Option is): 選項 B (Option B)

A 酒店房租稅 (Hotel Accommodation Tax)

這是一種間接稅。酒店雖然是向政府繳稅的實體，但它們會將稅款加在房租上，將稅務負擔轉嫁給住客。因此不符合題目「直接稅」的要求。
This is an indirect tax. Although hotels pay the tax to the government, they shift the tax burden to the guests by adding it to the room charges. Thus, it does not meet the "direct tax" requirement of the question.

B 利得稅 (Profits Tax)

這是一種直接稅及比例稅。公司必須自行承擔稅款（在DSE經濟學定義中不視為可轉嫁），而且在傳統稅制中，利得稅是以一個固定的百分比（如16.5%）向應評稅利潤徵收，因此稅率是固定的。
This is a direct tax and a proportional tax. Companies must bear the tax burden themselves (not considered shiftable in DSE economics definition), and under the traditional system, profits tax is charged at a flat percentage (e.g., 16.5%) on assessable profits, making the rate constant.

C 薪俸稅 (Salaries Tax)

這是一種直接稅，但它是累進稅（Progressive）。香港的薪俸稅實行階梯式稅率，入息越高，其邊際稅率和平均稅率也相應提高。
This is a direct tax, but it is progressive. Hong Kong's salaries tax uses a tiered rate system; as income increases, both the marginal and average tax rates increase accordingly.

D 差餉 (General Rates)

這是一種間接稅及比例稅。差餉按物業的應課差餉租值一個固定百分比（通常為5%）徵收，但業主可以透過提高租金將差餉的負擔轉嫁給租客。
This is an indirect tax and a proportional tax. Rates are charged at a fixed percentage (usually 5%) of a property's rateable value, but landlords can shift the burden to tenants by charging higher rent.

5. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

這題的「拆局位」(Pivot Point) 在於題目設定了雙重條件：(1) 直接稅 AND (2) 比例稅。解題時應採用淘汰法 (Elimination Method)。
The "Pivot Point" of this question lies in the dual conditions: (1) Direct Tax AND (2) Proportional Tax. You should use the Elimination Method to solve it.

  1. 條件一：直接稅 (Condition 1: Direct Tax)
    淘汰所有可轉嫁稅負的間接稅。選項 A (酒店房租稅) 和 選項 D (差餉) 出局。
    Eliminate all indirect taxes where the burden can be shifted. Options A and D are out.
  2. 條件二：比例稅 (Condition 2: Proportional Tax)
    在剩下的 B (利得稅) 和 C (薪俸稅) 中，只有利得稅的稅率是劃一固定的，薪俸稅是多勞多繳的累進制。因此答案是 B。
    Among the remaining B (Profits tax) and C (Salaries tax), only Profits tax has a flat rate. Salaries tax is progressive. Therefore, the answer is B.

6. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • 混淆概念 (Confusing Concepts): 很多學生會將「直接/間接」與「累進/比例」混為一談。記住，前者看轉嫁能力 (Shiftability)，後者看稅率變化 (Rate variation)。它們是兩個完全獨立的分類系統。
    Many students mix up "direct/indirect" with "progressive/proportional". Remember, the former is about shiftability, and the latter is about rate variation. They are two completely independent classification systems.
  • 誤判薪俸稅 (Misjudging Salaries Tax): 部分同學知道香港薪俸稅有「標準稅率」(Standard Rate) 的上限，就誤以為它是比例稅。在DSE課程中，薪俸稅的整體性質被定性為累進稅。
    Some students know HK Salaries Tax has a "Standard Rate" cap and mistakenly think it's a proportional tax. In the DSE syllabus, Salaries Tax is fundamentally classified as a progressive tax.

7. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 差餉 (Rates) 不是由業主直接交給政府的嗎？為什麼算間接稅？
Q: Aren't General Rates paid directly to the government by the property owner? Why is it considered an indirect tax?

A: 雖然行政上是業主交表交錢（納稅人），但經濟學上我們看的是「誰最終承擔這筆錢」。如果業主把物業出租，他可以調高租金，讓租客變相支付了這筆差餉。因為稅務負擔可以轉嫁，所以歸類為間接稅。
A: While administratively the owner pays the bill (the taxpayer), in economics, we look at "who ultimately bears the cost". If the owner rents out the property, they can raise the rent, effectively making the tenant pay the rates. Because the tax burden can be shifted, it is classified as an indirect tax.

Q: 香港的利得稅最近不是變成了兩級制 (Two-tiered system) 嗎？那它還是比例稅嗎？
Q: Hasn't HK's Profits Tax recently changed to a two-tiered system? Is it still a proportional tax?

A: 很好的問題！現實中，香港近年確實引入了利得稅兩級制（首200萬利潤稅率較低），使其帶有微弱的累進性質。但在 2012年 的背景下，我們將標準的利得稅視為比例稅 (Proportional Tax) 的典型例子。
A: Great question! In reality, HK recently introduced a two-tiered profits tax rates regime, giving it a slight progressive nature. However, in the context of 2012, standard Profits Tax is still treated as the textbook example of a Proportional Tax.
