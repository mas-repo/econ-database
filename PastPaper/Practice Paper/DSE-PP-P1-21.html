Topic / 課題: National Income Accounting / 國民收入會計

Difficulty / 難度: ★★☆☆☆ (2/5)

Key Concepts / 關鍵概念: GDP vs GNP Net Income from Abroad Macroeconomic Identities

The Question (題目)

若某經濟的對外要素收益流動淨值（即海外收入淨值）為負數，這意味着該經濟的

If the value of the net external factor income flow (i.e. the net income from abroad) of an economy is negative, it implies that

  1. 資本流入較資本流出為大。
    the capital inflow to the economy is greater than the capital outflow from it.
  2. 總投資較淨投資為小。
    the gross investment of the economy is smaller than the net investment.
  3. 國民生產總值較本地生產總值為小。
    the gross national product of the economy is less than the GDP.
  4. 進口值較出口值為大。
    the value of imports of the economy is greater than that of exports.

Concept Review (概念重溫)

GDP vs. GNP (本地生產總值 與 國民生產總值)

本地生產總值 (GDP) 是以地域 (Territory) 為界線的生產總值；而國民生產總值 (GNP) 是以常住居民 (Residents) 為界線的生產總值。兩者之間的橋樑就是「對外要素收益流動淨值」 (Net External Factor Income Flow, NFI)。
Gross Domestic Product (GDP) is measured based on territorial boundaries, while Gross National Product (GNP) is measured based on residents. The bridge between the two is the Net External Factor Income Flow (NFI).

$$ \text{GNP} = \text{GDP} + \text{NFI} $$

(國民生產總值 = 本地生產總值 + 海外收入淨值)

其中，NFI = 本地居民從外地賺取的要素收益 \(-\) 非本地居民從本地賺取的要素收益。
Where NFI = Factor income earned by residents from abroad \(-\) Factor income earned by non-residents from the domestic economy.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option / 正確答案: Option C

A 資本流入較資本流出為大 / capital inflow is greater than capital outflow

這描述的是國際收支平衡表中的「資本及金融帳」盈餘，涉及資產的買賣。而海外收入淨值 (NFI) 是「經常帳」中的初級收入。兩者是不同的宏觀經濟概念。
This describes a surplus in the capital and financial account of the Balance of Payments, involving the trading of assets. NFI, however, is primary income in the current account. They are different macroeconomic concepts.

B 總投資較淨投資為小 / gross investment is smaller than net investment

總投資 = 淨投資 + 折舊。因為折舊通常是正數，所以總投資必然大於或等於淨投資。這選項在數學定義上已經錯誤，並且與海外收入完全無關。
Gross Investment = Net Investment + Depreciation. Since depreciation is usually positive, Gross Investment must be greater than or equal to Net Investment. This option is mathematically incorrect by definition and is entirely unrelated to foreign income.

C 國民生產總值較本地生產總值為小 / gross national product is less than GDP

根據公式：\(\text{GNP} = \text{GDP} + \text{NFI}\)。題目指明 NFI 為負數 (\(\text{NFI} < 0\))，因此計算出來的 GNP 必然小於 GDP。完全正確。
According to the formula: \(\text{GNP} = \text{GDP} + \text{NFI}\). The question states that NFI is negative (\(\text{NFI} < 0\)). Therefore, the calculated GNP must be smaller than the GDP. This is perfectly correct.

D 進口值較出口值為大 / value of imports is greater than exports

進口大於出口代表「淨出口」(Net Exports, \(NX\)) 為負數，即貿易赤字。這涉及貨物和服務的流動，而不是要素收益（如薪金、股息）的流動。
Imports exceeding exports implies negative Net Exports (\(NX\)), i.e., a trade deficit. This involves the flow of goods and services, not the flow of factor incomes (like wages and dividends).

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

  • Pivot Point (解題關鍵): 看到「對外要素收益流動淨值」(Net external factor income flow)，腦海必須立刻彈出公式：\(\text{GNP} = \text{GDP} + \text{NFI}\)。
    When you see "Net external factor income flow", the formula \(\text{GNP} = \text{GDP} + \text{NFI}\) must immediately pop into your mind.
  • Distractor Identification (辨別干擾項): 題目故意混入「資本」(Capital)、「投資」(Investment) 和「進出口」(Imports/Exports)。這些都是宏觀經濟學其他章節的概念，用來擾亂概念不清晰的學生。
    The question intentionally mixes in "Capital", "Investment", and "Imports/Exports". These are concepts from other chapters in macroeconomics used to confuse students with unclear foundational knowledge.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ 混淆 Net Exports (\(NX\)) 與 Net Factor Income (\(NFI\))

學生經常將「錢流出/流入香港」一概而論。進口外國貨品會導致金錢流出（屬於 \(NX\)），而外資在香港賺取股息並匯出也會導致金錢流出（屬於 \(NFI\)）。必須分清產品/服務貿易和生產要素收益的分別。
Students often generalize all "money flowing in/out of HK". Importing foreign goods causes money outflow (belongs to \(NX\)), while foreign investors earning and repatriating dividends from HK also causes money outflow (belongs to \(NFI\)). You must distinguish between trade in goods/services and factor income.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 負面的海外收入淨值代表該經濟體表現很差嗎？
(Does a negative NFI mean the economy is performing poorly?)
A: 不一定。很多發展中國家（或像愛爾蘭等吸引大量外資的國家）都有大量外國直接投資。外國企業在當地賺取巨額利潤並匯回母國，會導致該國的 NFI 為負數，但當地的 GDP 和就業可能十分強勁。
Not necessarily. Many developing countries (or countries that attract massive foreign investment, like Ireland) have large amounts of Foreign Direct Investment (FDI). Foreign firms earning huge profits locally and repatriating them causes the host country's NFI to be negative, yet the local GDP and employment might be very strong.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

💡 Direct Elimination Method (直接排除法)

這是一條「純定義題」(Definitional Question)。不用多想，只要看到 NFI，馬上找包含 GDP 和 GNP (或 GNI) 的選項。A、B、D 根本沒有提及 GDP/GNP，可以直接秒殺排除。
This is a pure definitional question. Don't overthink it; the moment you see NFI, look for the option containing GDP and GNP (or GNI). Options A, B, and D don't even mention GDP/GNP, so they can be eliminated instantly in seconds.
