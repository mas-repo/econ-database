1. 題目 (The Question)

下列哪些是完全競爭市場的特徵？

(1) 每間公司的邊際收益等於市價。
(2) 賣家和買家掌握完全市場資訊。
(3) 一間公司的邊際成本表與它的供應表相同。

A. 只有(1)、(2)
B. 只有(1)、(3)
C. 只有(2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2)及(3)

Which of the following are features of a perfectly competitive market?

(1) The marginal revenue of each firm equals the market price.
(2) The sellers and buyers have perfect market information.
(3) The marginal cost schedule of a firm is the same as its supply schedule.

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

完全競爭市場 (Perfectly Competitive Market) 是一個理論上的市場結構，具備以下核心特徵：市場內有無數買家和賣家、產品完全同質 (Homogeneous products)、沒有進入或退出障礙 (Free entry and exit)、以及買賣雙方擁有完全資訊 (Perfect information)。
A Perfectly Competitive Market is a theoretical market structure with the following core features: a large number of buyers and sellers, homogeneous products, free entry and exit, and perfect information for both buyers and sellers.
受價者 (Price Taker) 與邊際收益 (Marginal Revenue, $MR$): 由於每間公司的產量佔市場總量極少，公司無法影響市價。因此，每賣出一件額外產品所帶來的額外收入 (即 $MR$)，必然等於市場價格 ($P$)。
Price Taker and Marginal Revenue ($MR$): Since each firm's output is a tiny fraction of the total market, they cannot influence the market price. Therefore, the extra revenue gained from selling one additional unit (which is $MR$) must equal the market price ($P$).
$$ P = AR = MR $$
利潤極大化與供應表 (Profit Maximization and Supply Schedule): 所有理性的公司都會在邊際收益等於邊際成本 ($MR = MC$) 的產出點上生產。在完全競爭下，$P = MR$，所以公司會在 $P = MC$ 的位置生產。這意味著，市場價格的變動會順着邊際成本曲線 ($MC$) 決定公司的供應量。
Profit Maximization and Supply Schedule: All rational firms produce at the output level where Marginal Revenue equals Marginal Cost ($MR = MC$). Under perfect competition, $P = MR$, so firms produce where $P = MC$. This means changes in market price will trace along the Marginal Cost ($MC$) curve to determine the firm's quantity supplied.
$$ \text{Profit Max: } MR = MC \implies P = MC $$

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 / Correct Option: D
陳述 (1) 解析：正確。 在完全競爭市場中，公司是受價者 (Price Taker)。這意味著他們只能按照市場決定的價格出售產品。既然價格固定，每多賣一件產品，總收入就會增加該市場價格的金額。因此，邊際收益等於市價 ($MR = P$)。
Statement (1) Analysis: TRUE. In a perfectly competitive market, firms are price takers. This means they can only sell products at the market-determined price. Since the price is fixed, selling one more unit increases total revenue by exactly that price amount. Thus, marginal revenue equals market price ($MR = P$).
陳述 (2) 解析：正確。 買賣雙方掌握完全市場資訊 (Perfect Information) 是完全競爭市場的四大基本假設之一。這確保了市場內不會出現「一物二價」的情況，也沒有資訊不對稱的現象。
Statement (2) Analysis: TRUE. Buyers and sellers having perfect market information is one of the four fundamental assumptions of a perfectly competitive market. This ensures the "law of one price" holds and there is no information asymmetry.
陳述 (3) 解析：正確。 為了追求利潤極大化，公司會生產直到邊際收益等於邊際成本 ($MR = MC$)。由於 $MR = P$，公司實際上是在 $P = MC$ 的水平生產。因此，在任何給定的價格下，公司的供應量就是其邊際成本表上的對應數量。邊際成本表也就是該公司的供應表。
Statement (3) Analysis: TRUE. To maximize profit, a firm produces until marginal revenue equals marginal cost ($MR = MC$). Since $MR = P$, the firm effectively produces where $P = MC$. Therefore, at any given price, the quantity supplied by the firm corresponds exactly to its marginal cost schedule. The marginal cost schedule is its supply schedule.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)： 這道題考驗的是對完全競爭市場「定義」與「推論」的熟悉度。(2) 是死記硬背的定義，而 (1) 和 (2) 則是從「受價者」與「利潤極大化」推導出來的結果。看到「完全競爭」，腦海中必須立刻浮現 $P = MR$ 以及 $MC = 供應曲線$ 的等式。
Pivot Point: This question tests your familiarity with the "definitions" and "implications" of perfect competition. (2) is a definition you must memorize, while (1) and (3) are logical derivations from being a "price taker" seeking "profit maximization". Upon seeing "perfect competition", the equations $P = MR$ and $MC = Supply Curve$ should immediately come to mind.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

混淆「公司需求曲線」與「市場需求曲線」： 學生常忘記在完全競爭中，個別公司面對的需求曲線是完全彈性的 (水平線，$P = MR$)，而整個市場的需求曲線仍然是向下傾斜的 (Downward sloping)。這導致學生對陳述 (1) 產生懷疑。
Confusing "Firm's Demand Curve" with "Market Demand Curve": Students often forget that in perfect competition, the demand curve facing an individual firm is perfectly elastic (horizontal line, $P = MR$), while the overall market demand curve is still downward sloping. This confusion causes students to doubt statement (1).

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

問： 大學經濟學提到，只有高於「平均可變成本 (Average Variable Cost, $AVC$)」最低點的邊際成本曲線，才是公司的供應曲線。為甚麼陳述 (3) 說「邊際成本表等於供應表」仍然算完全正確？
Q: University economics states that only the portion of the marginal cost curve that lies above the minimum Average Variable Cost ($AVC$) is the firm's supply curve. Why is statement (3) claiming "marginal cost schedule is the same as supply schedule" considered fully correct here?
答： 這是一個非常好的問題！這是因為 香港中學文憑試 (HKDSE) 經濟科課程作了簡化處理。在 DSE 的教學大綱中，學生不需要學習或理解 $AVC$ 及「停業點 (Shutdown point)」的複雜概念。為了簡化中學階段的學習，考評局默認了公司的邊際成本曲線 ($MC$) 整條就等同於它的供應曲線。因此，在 DSE 考試中，遇到這個說法請毫不猶豫地當作「正確」。
A: This is an excellent question! The reason is a simplification in the HKDSE Economics syllabus. In the DSE curriculum, students are not expected to learn or understand the complexities of $AVC$ and the "shutdown point". To simplify learning at the high school level, the HKEAA assumes the entire Marginal Cost ($MC$) curve equates to the supply curve. Therefore, in a DSE exam, you should unhesitatingly treat this statement as "True".

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

速解法 (Elimination Method)： 當你閱讀題目時，陳述 (2) 是最基礎的教科書定義，必定正確。陳述 (1) 也是完全競爭最標誌性的特徵 ($P=MR$)，必定正確。只要確定 (1) 和 (2) 正確，你就可以直接剔除選項 B 和 C。這時你只需花時間思考陳述 (3) 是否符合邏輯，就能從 A 和 D 中選出正確答案。
Shortcut (Elimination Method): When reading the question, statement (2) is the most basic textbook definition and must be true. Statement (1) is the most iconic mathematical feature ($P=MR$) and must also be true. Once you lock in (1) and (2) as correct, you can immediately eliminate options B and C. Then, you only need to evaluate the logic of statement (3) to decide between A and D.
