主題 Topic: 價格彈性與市場均衡 Price Elasticity & Market Equilibrium

難度 Difficulty: ★★★☆☆

關鍵字 Key Concepts: 需求價格彈性 Price Elasticity of Demand 供應移動 Shift in Supply 市場均衡 Market Equilibrium

2. 題目 The Question

假設原油出口國政局不穩。結果，汽油的價格增加30%，而汽油的交易量改變了15%。我們可以得出結論如下：

Suppose there is political unrest in the countries exporting crude oil. As a result, there is a 30% increase in the price of gasoline and a 15% change in the quantity transacted of gasoline. We can conclude that
A. 汽油的需求屬高彈性。
the demand for gasoline is elastic.
B. 汽油的需求屬低彈性。
the demand for gasoline is inelastic.
C. 汽油的供應屬高彈性。
the supply of gasoline is elastic.
D. 汽油的供應屬低彈性。
the supply of gasoline is inelastic.

3. 概念重溫 Concept Review

需求價格彈性 Price Elasticity of Demand ($E_d$)

量度需求量對價格變動的反應程度。當供應曲線移動時，均衡點會沿著不變的需求曲線移動，此時我們才可以計算需求彈性。

Measures the responsiveness of quantity demanded to a change in price. When the supply curve shifts, the equilibrium moves along the stationary demand curve, allowing us to calculate the elasticity of demand.
$$E_d = \frac{\% \Delta Q_d}{\% \Delta P}$$
  • 高彈性 Elastic ($E_d > 1$): $\% \Delta Q_d > \% \Delta P$
  • 低彈性 Inelastic ($E_d < 1$): $\% \Delta Q_d < \% \Delta P$
  • Elastic ($E_d > 1$): % change in quantity > % change in price
  • Inelastic ($E_d < 1$): % change in quantity < % change in price

4. 答案與詳細分析 The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Option: B

詳細分析 Step-by-Step Analysis:

步驟 1: 判斷哪條曲線移動
原油是生產汽油的生產要素（原料）。原油出口國政局不穩會導致原油供應減少，生產成本上升，從而令汽油的供應減少（供應曲線向左移）。需求曲線保持不變。

Step 1: Identify which curve shifted
Crude oil is a factor of production (raw material) for gasoline. Political unrest reduces the supply of crude oil, raising production costs, which leads to a decrease in the supply of gasoline (supply curve shifts left). The demand curve remains unchanged.

步驟 2: 計算彈性
因為是沿著需求曲線移動，我們可以計算需求價格彈性。已知價格上升30%（$\% \Delta P = 30\%$），交易量改變15%（由於供應減少，交易量必然是下跌15%，即 $\% \Delta Q = 15\%$）。
根據公式：$E_d = \frac{15\%}{30\%} = 0.5$。
因為 $0.5 < 1$，所以需求屬低彈性。

Step 2: Calculate elasticity
Since we are moving along the demand curve, we can calculate the price elasticity of demand. Given price increased by 30% ($\% \Delta P = 30\%$) and quantity changed by 15% (due to a decrease in supply, quantity must have fallen by 15%, so $\% \Delta Q = 15\%$).
Using the formula: $E_d = \frac{15\%}{30\%} = 0.5$.
Since $0.5 < 1$, the demand is inelastic.
  • A 錯誤 (Incorrect): 計算出的彈性是 $0.5$，小於1，屬於低彈性，而非高彈性。
    The calculated elasticity is $0.5$, which is less than 1, indicating inelastic demand, not elastic.
  • B 正確 (Correct): 沿著需求曲線移動，且 $\% \Delta Q < \% \Delta P$，證明需求是低彈性。
    Moving along the demand curve with $\% \Delta Q < \% \Delta P$ proves demand is inelastic.
  • C & D 錯誤 (Incorrect): 供應曲線發生了移動（整條曲線向左平移），新的均衡點位於一條全新的供應曲線上。我們無法透過這兩個均衡點來計算供應彈性。
    The supply curve itself shifted (moved to the left), meaning the new equilibrium is on an entirely new supply curve. We cannot calculate the price elasticity of supply using these two points.

5. 題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

🔑 關鍵轉折點 Pivot Point:

本題的破題關鍵是第一句：「原油出口國政局不穩」。學生必須立刻聯想到這是供應面的衝擊 (Supply-side shock)。確認是供應移動後，就必定是計算「需求彈性」，因為市場是沿著不變的需求曲線尋找新均衡。

The crucial key to solving this problem is the first sentence: "political unrest in the countries exporting crude oil." Students must instantly recognize this as a supply-side shock. Once you confirm the supply shifted, you MUST be calculating "Demand Elasticity", because the market is moving along the stationary demand curve to find the new equilibrium.

干擾資訊 Distractors:

題目說「交易量改變了15%」而沒有明言是增加還是減少。這其實考驗你是否真正理解供應減少會導致價格上升和交易量下跌。不過，計算彈性時只需要變動的百分比絕對值，所以方向不影響數值計算。

The question says "quantity transacted changed by 15%" without specifying an increase or decrease. This tests if you truly understand that a decrease in supply leads to higher prices and lower quantity. However, calculating elasticity only requires the absolute percentage changes, so the direction doesn't affect the numerical calculation.

6. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

  • 陷阱一：死背公式但用錯對象。 很多同學看到價格和數量的改變，就隨便代入公式，得出0.5後，在B和D之間猶豫。忘記了「供應移動量需求，需求移動量供應」的鐵律。
    Trap 1: Applying formulas blindly. Many students see price and quantity changes, plug them in, get 0.5, and then guess between B and D. They forget the golden rule: "A shift in supply measures demand elasticity; a shift in demand measures supply elasticity."
  • 陷阱二：分子分母倒置。 粗心的同學可能會算成 $\frac{30\%}{15\%} = 2$，從而誤選高彈性(選項A)。記住，數量變動（果）永遠在分子，價格變動（因）永遠在分母。
    Trap 2: Inverting numerator and denominator. Careless students might calculate $\frac{30\%}{15\%} = 2$, mistakenly choosing elastic (Option A). Remember, change in quantity (effect) is ALWAYS the numerator, and change in price (cause) is ALWAYS the denominator.

7. 學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

Q: 老師，既然價格上升了，那供應量不是應該增加嗎（供應定律）？為什麼交易量會下跌？

A: 這是一個很好的問題！這裡發生的是「供應減少」（整條供應曲線向左移），而不是「供應量增加」。當供應減少，市場會出現短缺，推高價格。價格上升會令消費者減少購買（沿著需求曲線向上移動，需求量下跌）。最終的均衡交易量，是由新的供應和原來的需求決定的，必然比原來少。

Q: Teacher, since the price increased, shouldn't the quantity supplied increase (Law of Supply)? Why did the transacted quantity fall?

A: Great question! What happened here is a "decrease in supply" (the whole supply curve shifted left), NOT an "increase in quantity supplied". When supply decreases, there is a shortage, which pushes prices up. The higher price causes consumers to buy less (movement up along the demand curve, decrease in quantity demanded). The final equilibrium quantity, determined by the new supply and original demand, must be lower than before.

8. 考試技巧與速解 Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

⚡ 10秒速解法 10-Second Shortcut:

  1. 找源頭 (Find the Source): 判斷是誰移動？「原油政局不穩」= 供應移動。
  2. 刪選項 (Eliminate): 既然供應移動，我們只能找出需求彈性。立刻刪除 C 和 D。
  3. 比大小 (Compare): 數量變動(15%) 小於 價格變動(30%)。分子小於分母，數值必小於1。
  4. 秒殺 (Solve): 小於1 = 低彈性。答案是 B。不用動筆寫算式！
  1. Find the Source: Who shifted? "Unrest in oil countries" = Supply shifted.
  2. Eliminate: Since Supply shifted, we can ONLY find Demand elasticity. Instantly cross out C and D.
  3. Compare: % Quantity (15%) is LESS than % Price (30%). Numerator < Denominator, so value < 1.
  4. Solve: Less than 1 = Inelastic. Answer is B. No writing needed!
