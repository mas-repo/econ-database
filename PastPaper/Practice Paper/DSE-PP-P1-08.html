1. 課題資料 (Topic Information)

課題 (Topic): 需求與供應 - 課稅與市場干預 (Demand & Supply - Taxation and Market Intervention)

難度 (Difficulty): ★★★★☆ (4/5 Stars)

關鍵概念 (Key Concepts): 間接稅 (Indirect Tax), 價格彈性 (Price Elasticity), 總收益與總支出 (Total Revenue & Total Expenditure)

2. 題目 (The Question)

增加煙草稅會導致

An increase in tobacco duty will lead to

A. 政府從香煙所徵收的稅款增加。

a rise in government tax revenue from cigarettes.

B. 吸煙人士對香煙的總支出增加。

a rise in smokers’ total expenditure on cigarettes.

C. 香煙總市值下降。

a fall in the total market value of cigarettes.

D. 香煙零售商除稅後的總收益下降。

a fall in cigarette retailers’ total revenue net of tax.

3. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

當政府徵收從量稅 (per-unit tax) 時，供應曲線會向上平移稅款的幅度。這會導致三個必然結果：

When the government imposes a per-unit tax, the supply curve shifts upwards by the amount of the tax. This leads to three definite outcomes:

  • 消費者支付的價格 \(P_c\) 會上升。
    The price paid by consumers \(P_c\) increases.
  • 生產者/零售商的除稅後實收價格 \(P_p\) 會下降。
    The net price received by producers/retailers \(P_p\) decreases.
  • 市場交易量 \(Q\) 會下降。
    The transacted quantity \(Q\) decreases.

相關公式 (Relevant Formulas):

$$ \text{Total Expenditure (TE)} = P_c \times Q $$ $$ \text{Total Revenue Net of Tax (NR)} = P_p \times Q $$ $$ P_p = P_c - \text{tax} $$

定義 (Definitions):

  • 總開支 (Total Expenditure): 消費者購買商品所支付的總金額。
    The total amount paid by consumers to purchase the goods.
  • 除稅後總收益 (Total Revenue Net of Tax): 賣家在扣除需要上繳給政府的稅款後，實際保留的總金額。
    The total amount actually kept by sellers after deducting the tax paid to the government.

4. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是 (Correct Option): D

分析選項 A (Analyzing Option A): 錯誤 (Incorrect)

稅收總額 = 稅率 \(\times\) 交易量 \(Q\)。雖然每單位的稅款增加了，但交易量 \(Q\) 必然下降。除非我們知道需求彈性，否則無法確定這「一升一跌」對稅收總額的最終影響。

Total Tax Revenue = Tax rate \(\times\) Quantity \(Q\). Although the tax per unit increases, the quantity \(Q\) definitely falls. Without knowing the elasticity of demand, we cannot determine the final effect of this "one rise, one fall" on total tax revenue.

分析選項 B & C (Analyzing Options B & C): 錯誤 (Incorrect)

總支出 (或總市值) = \(P_c \times Q\)。加稅後，消費者面對的價格 \(P_c\) 上升，但數量 \(Q\) 下降。總支出的變化完全取決於需求的價格彈性 (\(E_d\))。如果需求屬低彈性 (inelastic)，總支出會上升；如果是高彈性 (elastic)，總支出會下降。題目並沒有明確給定彈性數據。

Total Expenditure (or Total Market Value) = \(P_c \times Q\). After taxation, the price facing consumers \(P_c\) rises, but quantity \(Q\) falls. The change in total expenditure depends entirely on the Price Elasticity of Demand (\(E_d\)). If demand is inelastic, TE rises; if elastic, TE falls. The question does not explicitly state the elasticity.

分析選項 D (Analyzing Option D): 正確 (Correct)

零售商除稅後的總收益 = 實收價格 \(P_p \times\) 數量 \(Q\)。加稅後，賣家的實收價格 \(P_p\) 必然下降，同時交易量 \(Q\) 也必然下降。由於兩組數值同時下跌，相乘的結果（除稅後總收益）必然下降，這是一個不需依賴任何彈性假設的數學必然結果。

Retailers' total revenue net of tax = Net Price \(P_p \times\) Quantity \(Q\). After taxation, the net price received by sellers \(P_p\) must fall, and the quantity \(Q\) must also fall. Since both variables decrease simultaneously, their product (net total revenue) MUST DECREASE. This is a mathematical certainty that does not rely on any elasticity assumptions.

5. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point):

這類題目的核心在於區分「可能發生」(Might happen) 與「必然發生」(Must happen)。在沒有提供價格彈性數據的情況下，我們必須尋找公式中兩個變數同方向變動的選項。

The core of this question type lies in distinguishing between what "might happen" and what "must happen". Without data on price elasticity, we must look for the option where both variables in the formula move in the same direction.

干擾資訊 (The Distractor):

題目使用了「香煙」(Cigarettes) 作為例子。許多學生被訓練成一看到香煙就自動聯想到「低需求彈性」(Inelastic demand)。這是出卷者刻意設置的心理陷阱！即使香煙通常是低彈性，選項 D 的推論在任何正常供求曲線上都是絕對正確的，而 B 只是「極有可能」，在嚴格的選擇題邏輯中，絕對必然的 D 才是最佳答案。

The question uses "Cigarettes" as the example. Many students are trained to automatically associate cigarettes with "inelastic demand". This is a psychological trap set by the examiners! Even though cigarettes usually have inelastic demand, the deduction in Option D is absolutely true on any normal supply and demand curve, whereas B is only "highly likely". In rigorous MCQ logic, the absolute certainty of D makes it the best answer.

6. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱 1：混淆總支出與除稅後收益

學生常忘記：消費者的總支出 (Total Expenditure) ≠ 賣家的實收收益 (Net Revenue)。中間的差額被政府以稅收形式取走了。

Trap 1: Confusing Total Expenditure with Net Revenue. Students often forget that Consumers' Total Expenditure ≠ Sellers' Net Revenue. The difference is taken by the government as tax.

陷阱 2：過度假設 (Over-assuming)

未見「假設需求為低彈性」等字眼，就自行代入現實世界的認知。HKDSE 經濟科極度重視嚴謹的邏輯推導。

Trap 2: Over-assuming. Applying real-world assumptions without seeing phrases like "assume demand is inelastic". HKDSE Economics heavily emphasizes rigorous logical deduction.

7. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

🙋 學生問 (Student): 現實中香煙的需求彈性確實很低呀（缺乏彈性），那加稅後總支出一定會上升，為何選項 B 不是正確答案呢？

Q: In reality, the demand for cigarettes is indeed inelastic, so total expenditure will definitely rise after a tax increase. Why isn't Option B correct?

👨‍🏫 導師答 (Tutor): 這是一個非常好的問題！在真實世界中你的想法很對。但在 DSE 選擇題的遊戲規則裡，我們追求的是「邏輯上的絕對唯一解」。選項 B 必須建立在一個隱含假設（需求缺乏彈性）上才能成立；而選項 D 則是無論彈性高低（只要不是極端情況），數學上都百分之百必然成立。因此，D 是比 B 更嚴謹、更無懈可擊的答案。

A: This is a great question! In the real world, your reasoning is sound. However, under the rules of DSE MCQs, we are looking for the "absolute logical certainty". Option B relies on an implicit assumption (inelastic demand) to be true; whereas Option D is 100% mathematically guaranteed regardless of the elasticity. Therefore, D is a rigorously tighter and more bulletproof answer than B.

8. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

「雙箭咀同向法」 (The "Double Arrow" Method)

遇到稅收 (Tax) 或津貼 (Subsidy) 導致收益變化的題目時，立刻在腦海中（或草稿紙上）寫出公式並畫出升跌箭咀：

When facing questions about revenue changes due to Tax or Subsidy, immediately write the formula and draw directional arrows in your head (or on rough paper):

  • TE = \(P_c\) (↑) \(\times\) \(Q\) (↓) ➔ 一升一跌，結果打交叉 (Uncertain ❌)
  • Tax Rev = \(t\) (↑) \(\times\) \(Q\) (↓) ➔ 一升一跌，結果打交叉 (Uncertain ❌)
  • Net Rev = \(P_p\) (↓) \(\times\) \(Q\) (↓) ➔ 雙跌，結果必然跌！(Guaranteed Fall ✅)

用這個方法，你可以在 10 秒內秒殺這條題目！

Using this method, you can kill this question in 10 seconds!
