HKDSE Economics Tutorial: Money Supply

Topic: 貨幣供應 (Money Supply) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ (Moderate) Key Concepts: M1, M2, M3, DTC, Remittance

1. The Question (題目)

若陳先生從他在接受存款公司的戶口提取港幣500 000元，並將該筆款項匯給在加拿大的親人，香港的貨幣供應定義二（M2）會_______而定義三（M3）會_______。

If Mr. Chan withdraws HK$500 000 from his account in a deposit-taking company and remits the sum to his relatives in Canada, Hong Kong’s money supply M2 will ______ and M3 will _______.

  1. 減少 … 減少 (decrease … decrease)
  2. 維持不變 … 維持不變 (remain unchanged … remain unchanged)
  3. 減少 … 維持不變 (decrease … remain unchanged)
  4. 維持不變 … 減少 (remain unchanged … decrease)

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這類題目，我們必須清楚了解香港貨幣供應的三個定義（M1, M2, M3）以及香港三級銀行體系的分類。

To solve this type of question, we must clearly understand the three definitions of Hong Kong's money supply (M1, M2, M3) and the classification of Hong Kong's three-tier banking system.

$$ \text{M1} = C_p + D_d $$ $$ \text{M2} = \text{M1} + D_s + D_t + \text{NCD}_{LB} $$ $$ \text{M3} = \text{M2} + D_{RLB} + D_{DTC} + \text{NCD}_{RLB/DTC} $$

  • \( C_p \) (Cash in Public): 公眾持有的法定貨幣。

    Legal tender held by the non-bank public.

  • \( D_d, D_s, D_t \): 分別代表在持牌銀行 (Licensed Banks, LB) 的活期 (Demand)、儲蓄 (Savings) 及定期 (Time) 存款。

    Demand, Savings, and Time deposits with Licensed Banks (LB) respectively.

  • \( D_{RLB}, D_{DTC} \): 在限制牌照銀行 (Restricted Licence Banks, RLB) 及接受存款公司 (Deposit-Taking Companies, DTC) 的客戶存款。

    Customer deposits with Restricted Licence Banks (RLB) and Deposit-Taking Companies (DTC).

  • 匯款 (Remittance): 將資金匯出香港體系外，意味著該筆資金不再屬於香港的任何貨幣供應類別。

    Remitting funds out of the Hong Kong system means the money is no longer part of any Hong Kong money supply category.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是 D (Option D)。 讓我們逐一分析每個選項：

The correct answer is Option D. Let's analyze each option step-by-step:

Incorrect Option A & C: M2 減少 (M2 decreases)

分析： 提取資金的機構是「接受存款公司」(DTC)。請注意，DTC的存款只計算在 \( \text{M3} \) 內，並不包含在 \( \text{M1} \) 或 \( \text{M2} \) 內。因此，這筆資金從DTC流失，對 \( \text{M2} \) 沒有任何直接影響。所以 \( \text{M2} \) 應該是「維持不變」，選項A和C錯誤。

Analysis: The institution from which funds were withdrawn is a Deposit-Taking Company (DTC). Note that DTC deposits are only counted in \( \text{M3} \) and are NOT included in \( \text{M1} \) or \( \text{M2} \). Therefore, the loss of funds from a DTC has no direct impact on \( \text{M2} \). Thus, \( \text{M2} \) should "remain unchanged", making Options A and C incorrect.

Incorrect Option B: M3 維持不變 (M3 remains unchanged)

分析： 資金從DTC被提取，如果陳先生將現金留在身上，\( C_p \)（公眾持有現金）會增加，屆時 \( \text{M3} \) 內部互相抵銷才會不變。但題目指明他將款項「匯給加拿大的親人」，這筆錢完全離開了香港的貨幣體系。由於DTC存款減少，而沒有其他香港貨幣成分增加，\( \text{M3} \) 必然減少。

Analysis: The funds were withdrawn from a DTC. If Mr. Chan had kept the cash, \( C_p \) (cash in public hands) would increase, and \( \text{M3} \) would remain unchanged due to internal offsetting. However, the question specifies he "remits the sum to relatives in Canada," meaning the money completely left HK's monetary system. Since DTC deposits decreased without an increase in any other HK money component, \( \text{M3} \) must decrease.

Correct Option D: M2 維持不變 … M3 減少 (M2 remain unchanged … M3 decrease)

分析： 綜合上述邏輯：因為資金原本在DTC，不屬於 \( \text{M2} \)，所以提取且匯出後，對 \( \text{M2} \) 的總額毫無影響（\( \text{M2} \) 維持不變）。然而，DTC存款是 \( \text{M3} \) 的一部分，資金被匯走導致DTC存款下跌，因此 \( \text{M3} \) 會相應減少。

Analysis: Combining the logic above: Because the funds were originally in a DTC, they were never part of \( \text{M2} \). Withdrawing and remitting them has zero effect on the total amount of \( \text{M2} \) (\( \text{M2} \) remains unchanged). However, DTC deposits are part of \( \text{M3} \). Remitting the money causes a drop in DTC deposits, thus \( \text{M3} \) decreases accordingly.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

遇到這類題目，請遵循以下 「三步走」 策略：

When encountering this type of question, follow this "Three-Step" strategy:

  1. 定位來源 (Pivot Point): 留意資金最初在哪裡？題目關鍵字是「接受存款公司 (DTC)」。腦海要立刻反射：DTC只關乎 \( \text{M3} \)，不入 \( \text{M1} \)/\( \text{M2} \)。

    Locate the Source (Pivot Point): Where were the funds initially? The keyword is "deposit-taking company (DTC)". Your immediate reflex should be: DTC is only relevant to \( \text{M3} \), not \( \text{M1} \)/\( \text{M2} \).

  2. 追蹤去向 (Trace the Destination): 錢去了哪裡？題目說「匯給加拿大」。這是一個關鍵的干擾/資訊點。這代表資金離開了系統，而不是轉變成公眾手中的現金 \( C_p \)。

    Trace the Destination: Where did the money go? The question says "remits to Canada". This is crucial info. It means the funds left the system, rather than turning into cash in public circulation \( C_p \).

  3. 計算淨影響 (Calculate Net Effect):
    \( \Delta \text{M2} = 0 \) (因為原本就沒有計入 \( \text{M2} \))
    \( \Delta \text{M3} = - \$500,000 \) (因為DTC存款減少，且無其他項目增加)

    Calculate Net Effect:
    \( \Delta \text{M2} = 0 \) (since it was never counted in \( \text{M2} \))
    \( \Delta \text{M3} = - \$500,000 \) (since DTC deposits dropped and no other item increased).

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 陷阱一：誤以為所有「提款」都會增加公眾現金 (\( C_p \))。 很多學生看到「提取」就以為 \( C_p \) 增加，帶動 \( \text{M1} \) 和 \( \text{M2} \) 增加。請一定要看完句子：他把錢「匯出」了！

    Trap 1: Assuming all "withdrawals" increase public cash (\( C_p \)). Many students see "withdraw" and assume \( C_p \) rises, pushing up \( \text{M1} \) and \( \text{M2} \). You must read the whole sentence: he "remitted" the money out!

  • 陷阱二：混淆三級銀行體系與貨幣供應的對應關係。 記住：只有「持牌銀行」(Licensed Banks) 的存款才會計入 \( \text{M2} \)。RLB和DTC只屬於 \( \text{M3} \)。

    Trap 2: Confusing the 3-tier banking system with money supply definitions. Remember: Only deposits in "Licensed Banks" are counted in \( \text{M2} \). RLBs and DTCs belong exclusively to \( \text{M3} \).

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果陳先生沒有把錢匯到加拿大，而是把提取的50萬元現金放在家裡的保險箱，答案會有什麼不同？

A: 很好的問題！如果他把現金放在家裡，這筆錢就變成了「公眾持有的法定貨幣」(\( C_p \))。
1. 因為 \( C_p \) 增加了，所以 \( \text{M1} \) 和 \( \text{M2} \) 都會增加。
2. 對於 \( \text{M3} \) 而言，雖然DTC存款減少了50萬，但 \( C_p \)（屬於 \( \text{M3} \) 的一部分）增加了50萬。一加一減之下，\( \text{M3} \) 將會維持不變。

Q: What if Mr. Chan didn't remit the money to Canada, but instead kept the $500,000 cash in his safe at home? How would the answer change?

A: Excellent question! If he kept the cash at home, it becomes "legal tender held by the public" (\( C_p \)).
1. Because \( C_p \) increases, both \( \text{M1} \) and \( \text{M2} \) would increase.
2. As for \( \text{M3} \), although DTC deposits decreased by 500k, \( C_p \) (which is part of \( \text{M3} \)) increased by 500k. The net effect is zero, so \( \text{M3} \) would remain unchanged.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺法 (The 5-Second Kill): 在DSE考試中，看到「匯款」(Remittance) 或「外地」(Overseas)，通常意味著整體貨幣供應的最高層級 (即 \( \text{M3} \)) 必定減少，因為資金離開了香港。知道這一點後，你可以立即排除選項 B 和 C。然後再看資金來源（DTC不屬 \( \text{M2} \)），就能快速選出 D。

The 5-Second Shortcut: In the DSE exam, seeing "Remittance" or "Overseas" usually means the highest level of money supply (\( \text{M3} \)) MUST decrease, as funds have left Hong Kong entirely. Knowing this allows you to instantly eliminate Options B and C. Then, look at the source (DTC is not in \( \text{M2} \)), and you can quickly lock in Option D.
