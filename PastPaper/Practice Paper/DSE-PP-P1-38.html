HKDSE Economics Analysis (經濟學題目分析)

Topic (課題): International Trade - Market Intervention (國際貿易 - 市場干預)

Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆ (3/5 Stars)

Key Concepts (核心概念): Small Open Economy Tariff Consumer/Producer Surplus

1. The Question (題目)

若某小型開放經濟減低一進口物品的關稅，

  1. 該物品的本地生產量和消費量均會增加。
  2. 關稅收益和進口量均會減少。
  3. 消費者盈餘會增加，而生產者盈餘則會減少。
  4. 該物品的全球價格會下跌，而總社會盈餘則會增加。

If a small open economy reduces the tariff on an imported good,

  1. both domestic production and consumption of the good will increase.
  2. both tariff revenue and import volume will decrease.
  3. the consumer surplus will increase while the producer surplus will decrease.
  4. the world price of the good will fall while the total social surplus will increase.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

這道題目考核的是關稅 (Tariff) 對小型開放經濟 (Small Open Economy) 的影響。

This question tests the impact of a tariff on a small open economy.

  • 小型開放經濟 (Small Open Economy): 該國在國際市場中只是「價格接受者」(Price Taker)。其進出口量的改變不會影響世界價格 \(P_w\)。
    The country is a "price taker" in the international market. Changes in its import/export volume will not affect the world price \(P_w\).
  • 關稅的影響 (Impact of Tariff): 徵收關稅會使本地價格 \(P_d\) 高於世界價格。公式如下：
    Imposing a tariff raises the domestic price \(P_d\) above the world price. The formula is:
$$P_d = P_w + t$$

當中 \(P_d\) 為本地價格，\(P_w\) 為世界價格，\(t\) 為單位關稅。
Where \(P_d\) is domestic price, \(P_w\) is world price, and \(t\) is per-unit tariff.

當關稅降低 (When tariff decreases): \(t\) 下降會導致本地價格 \(P_d\) 下降。根據需求及供給定律：

A decrease in \(t\) leads to a lower domestic price \(P_d\). According to the laws of demand and supply:

  • 本地需求量 (Quantity Demanded): 增加 (Increase) — 價格下降，消費者買得更多。
  • 本地供給量 (Quantity Supplied): 減少 (Decrease) — 價格下降，本地生產者減少生產。
  • 進口量 (Import Volume): 增加 (Increase) — 進口量 = 本地需求量 - 本地供給量 (\(Q_d - Q_s\))。

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是 (Correct Option): Option C

Option A: 該物品的本地生產量和消費量均會增加。 (both domestic production and consumption of the good will increase.)

❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect): 關稅降低導致本地價格下跌。雖然本地消費量（需求量）會增加，但本地生產量（供給量）會減少，因為價格較低對生產者不利。

A tariff reduction lowers domestic prices. While domestic consumption (quantity demanded) will increase, domestic production (quantity supplied) will decrease because the lower price is less favorable for producers.

Option B: 關稅收益和進口量均會減少。 (both tariff revenue and import volume will decrease.)

❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect): 由於本地消費量增加且本地生產量減少，國內短缺擴大，必須依賴更多進口，因此進口量會增加，而不是減少。至於關稅收益（每單位關稅 \(t \times\) 進口量），由於 \(t\) 減少但進口量增加，總收益的改變是不確定的（視乎彈性）。

Since domestic consumption increases and production decreases, the domestic shortage widens, requiring more imports. Thus, import volume will increase, not decrease. As for tariff revenue (per-unit tariff \(t \times\) import volume), since \(t\) falls but import volume rises, the change in total revenue is uncertain (depends on elasticity).

Option C: 消費者盈餘會增加，而生產者盈餘則會減少。 (the consumer surplus will increase while the producer surplus will decrease.)

✅ 正確 (Correct): 本地價格 (\(P_d\)) 下降。消費者能以更低價格購買物品，因此消費者盈餘增加。相反，本地生產者只能以更低價格出售物品，因此生產者盈餘減少。

The domestic price (\(P_d\)) falls. Consumers can buy the good at a lower price, so consumer surplus increases. Conversely, domestic producers must sell at a lower price, so producer surplus decreases.

Option D: 該物品的全球價格會下跌，而總社會盈餘則會增加。 (the world price of the good will fall while the total social surplus will increase.)

❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect): 關鍵詞是「小型開放經濟」。這代表該國的政策變動無法影響全球市場。因此，全球價格 (\(P_w\)) 保持不變。雖然總社會盈餘確實會增加（無謂損失減少），但前半句是錯的。

The keyword is "small open economy." This means the country's policy changes cannot affect the global market. Therefore, the world price (\(P_w\)) remains unchanged. Although total social surplus does increase (deadweight loss decreases), the first half of the statement is wrong.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (解題關鍵):

  1. 「小型開放經濟」("Small Open Economy"): 一看到這個詞，立刻知道世界價格 (\(P_w\)) 不受影響。這能讓你瞬間淘汰選項 D。
    Upon seeing this term, you immediately know the world price (\(P_w\)) is unaffected. This allows you to instantly eliminate Option D.
  2. 「減低關稅」("Reduces tariff"): 這意味著本地價格 (\(P_d\)) 必定跟隨下跌。價格下跌的直接後果就是：對消費者有利（CS上升），對生產者不利（PS下降）。
    This means the domestic price (\(P_d\)) must fall. The direct consequence of a price drop is: beneficial to consumers (CS rises), detrimental to producers (PS falls).

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 陷阱一：誤以為減關稅必導致關稅收入下跌。
    很多學生認為 \(t\) 下跌，收入就一定跌。但關稅收入 = \(t \times\) 進口量。由於進口量會大幅上升，如果進口需求彈性極高，總關稅收入甚至可能上升（類似拉弗曲線效應）。因此，選項 B 的前半段是「不確定」而非「必然減少」。
    Trap 1: Assuming lower tariffs always decrease tariff revenue. Tariff revenue = \(t \times\) Import Volume. Because import volume rises, if elasticity is very high, total revenue could actually increase. Thus, the first part of Option B is "uncertain", not "definitely decrease".

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼選項 D 的「總社會盈餘會增加」是對的，但整個選項還是錯？
Why is the statement "total social surplus will increase" in Option D correct, but the overall option is still wrong?

A: 在 DSE 的多項選擇題中，一個選項只要有一半是錯的，整個選項就是錯的。減關稅確實會減少市場扭曲（減少無謂損失 Deadweight Loss），所以總社會盈餘 (Total Social Surplus) 會增加。但是，它聲稱「全球價格會下跌」是致命的錯誤，因為小型經濟體無法改變世界價格。
In DSE MCQs, if any part of an option is wrong, the entire option is wrong. Reducing tariffs does reduce market distortion (reduces deadweight loss), so total social surplus increases. However, the claim that "world price will fall" is fatally flawed because a small economy cannot change world prices.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺法 (The 10-Second Kill Method):

在考場上，你可以用常理直接推斷：

  1. 關稅 = 稅 = 價格上升。減關稅 = 價格下跌。
  2. 價格下跌，必定有人開心（消費者），有人傷心（生產者）。
  3. 馬上找「消費者盈餘增加，生產者盈餘減少」的選項，直接選 C！

In the exam, use direct logic: Tariff = Tax = Higher Price. Reduced tariff = Lower price. A lower price always makes buyers happy (CS up) and sellers sad (PS down). Look immediately for the option that says CS increases and PS decreases. Choose C!
