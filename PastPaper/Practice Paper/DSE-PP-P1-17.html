1. 題目 (The Question)

在下列哪些情況下，會達致經濟效率？

Economic efficiency is attained when

(1) 消費者得到最大的總利益。

(1) total benefits to consumers are maximized.

(2) 所有潛在得益可以完全攫取。

(2) all potential gains are fully captured.

(3) 邊際社會利益等於邊際社會成本。

(3) marginal social benefits are equal to marginal social costs.

  • A. 只有 (1)、(2)     (1) and (2) only
  • B. 只有 (1)、(3)     (1) and (3) only
  • C. 只有 (2)、(3)     (2) and (3) only
  • D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)     (1), (2) and (3)

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

經濟效率 (Economic Efficiency) 指資源分配達到最優化，社會總盈餘（Total Social Surplus）達到最大值的狀態。此時，沒有任何改變可以在不損害他人的情況下使某人獲益（即達到柏利圖最優 Pareto Optimality）。

Economic Efficiency refers to an optimal allocation of resources where Total Social Surplus is maximized. At this point, no one can be made better off without making someone else worse off (Pareto Optimality).

總社會盈餘 (Total Social Surplus, TSS) 是消費者盈餘和生產者盈餘的總和：

Total Social Surplus (TSS) is the sum of Consumer Surplus (CS) and Producer Surplus (PS):

$$TSS = CS + PS$$

邊際條件 (Marginal Condition): 經濟效率達致的條件是社會每多生產一單位物品所帶來的額外利益，剛好等於其帶來的額外成本。

The condition for attaining economic efficiency is when the extra benefit to society from producing one more unit of a good exactly equals the extra cost of producing it.

$$MSB = MSC$$

(MSB: 邊際社會利益 Marginal Social Benefit, MSC: 邊際社會成本 Marginal Social Cost)

3. 答案與詳細分析 (Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): Option C

分析選項 (1) / Analysis of (1)

❌ 錯誤 (False)。 消費者得到最大的總利益並不代表經濟效率。若要將消費者的總利益極大化，物品的價格必須降至零，甚至生產至邊際利益為負數。這會導致生產者蒙受巨大損失，並產生龐大的無謂損失（Deadweight Loss, DWL），因為資源被過度分配（$MSC > MSB$）。經濟效率追求的是「社會」總盈餘最大化，而非單一持份者（消費者）的利益最大化。

Maximizing total benefits to consumers does not equal economic efficiency. To maximize consumer benefit, the price would have to be zero or production would continue until marginal benefit is zero (or negative). This causes huge losses for producers and creates a massive deadweight loss (DWL) because resources are over-allocated ($MSC > MSB$). Efficiency maximizes "total social" surplus, not just one stakeholder's benefit.

分析選項 (2) / Analysis of (2)

✅ 正確 (True)。 「所有潛在得益可以完全攫取」意味著市場上已經沒有任何可以增加總盈餘的交易機會（即無謂損失 DWL = 0）。這正是經濟效率的定義，社會總盈餘達到最大值。

"All potential gains are fully captured" means there are no more mutually beneficial trade opportunities left in the market (i.e., Deadweight Loss = 0). This is exactly the definition of economic efficiency, where total social surplus is maximized.

分析選項 (3) / Analysis of (3)

✅ 正確 (True)。 這是達致經濟效率的標準邊際條件。當邊際社會利益（$MSB$）等於邊際社會成本（$MSC$）時，社會的資源分配達到最優，不會出現生產過剩或生產不足的情況。

This is the standard marginal condition for economic efficiency. When Marginal Social Benefit ($MSB$) equals Marginal Social Cost ($MSC$), resource allocation is optimal, meaning there is neither overproduction nor underproduction.

總結 (Conclusion): 只有 (2) 和 (3) 正確，因此選 C。
Only (2) and (3) are correct, hence Option C.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point):
本題的核心在於區分「局部利益」與「整體社會利益」。選項 (1) 是一個常見的干擾項（Distractor）。經濟學強調平衡，只偏袒消費者（或只偏袒生產者）都會破壞效率。

The Pivot Point: The core of this question lies in distinguishing between "partial benefit" and "total social benefit". Option (1) is a classic distractor. Economics emphasizes balance; favoring only consumers (or only producers) destroys efficiency.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

⚠️ 概念混淆 (Conceptual Confusion):
許多同學誤以為「價格越低越好」或「消費者賺得越多代表經濟越好」。但在微觀經濟學中，如果價格被人為壓低（例如有效價格上限 Price Ceiling），雖然部分消費者可能受惠，但供應量會減少，導致交易量下降，最終社會總盈餘縮減，產生無謂損失（Deadweight Loss）。

Many students mistakenly believe that "the lower the price, the better" or "maximizing consumer gains means a better economy." However, in microeconomics, artificially suppressing prices (e.g., via an effective price ceiling) might benefit some consumers, but it reduces the quantity supplied and transacted. This ultimately shrinks the total social surplus and creates a deadweight loss.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼選項(2)「所有潛在得益可以完全攫取」代表有效率？

Q: Why does Option (2) "all potential gains are fully captured" represent efficiency?

A: 想像一個買家願意出 $100 買一件物品，而賣家的成本是 $60。這中間有 $40 的「潛在得益」（Potential Gain）。只要交易發生，這 $40 就會轉化為實際的社會盈餘。當市場上所有這類 $MSB > MSC$ 的交易都完成了，代表所有潛在得益都被「攫取」了，社會盈餘達到最大，這就是經濟效率。
A: Imagine a buyer is willing to pay $100 for an item, and the seller's cost is $60. There is a "potential gain" of $40. Once the trade happens, this $40 becomes actual social surplus. When all such trades where $MSB > MSC$ have occurred, all potential gains are "captured." Total surplus is maximized, which is economic efficiency.

In multiple-choice questions, once you are absolutely certain that statement (1) is false (because it only considers consumers and ignores producers), immediately look at the options:

Instant Kill! You don't even need to spend time verifying statements (2) and (3). You can confidently choose C within 10 seconds. This is crucial in time-pressured public exams.
