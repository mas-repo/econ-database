2. 題目 The Question

下列哪些圖描繪累進稅？

  1. 只有 (1)、(2)
  2. 只有 (1)、(3)
  3. 只有 (2)、(4)
  4. 只有 (3)、(4)

Which of the following diagrams describe a progressive tax?

  1. (1) and (2) only
  2. (1) and (3) only
  3. (2) and (4) only
  4. (3) and (4) only

3. 概念重溫 Concept Review

在稅制中，我們通常根據**平均稅率 (Average Tax Rate, ATR)** 隨著應課稅收入的變化來分類稅制。理解 Y 軸代表什麼是解題的關鍵！

In taxation, we classify tax systems based on how the **Average Tax Rate (ATR)** changes as taxable income changes. Understanding what the Y-axis represents is the key to solving this problem!

$$ \text{Average Tax Rate (ATR)} = \frac{\text{Tax Payment (T)}}{\text{Taxable Income (Y)}} \times 100\% $$
  • 累進稅 (Progressive Tax): 當應課稅收入 \(Y\) 增加時，平均稅率 \(ATR\) 上升。
    Progressive Tax: As taxable income \(Y\) increases, the Average Tax Rate \(ATR\) increases.
  • 比例稅 (Proportional Tax): 當應課稅收入 \(Y\) 增加時，平均稅率 \(ATR\) 維持不變。稅款圖形會是一條從原點出發的直線。
    Proportional Tax: As taxable income \(Y\) increases, the Average Tax Rate \(ATR\) remains constant. The tax payment diagram will be a straight line from the origin.
  • 累退稅 (Regressive Tax): 當應課稅收入 \(Y\) 增加時，平均稅率 \(ATR\) 下降。
    Regressive Tax: As taxable income \(Y\) increases, the Average Tax Rate \(ATR\) decreases.

4. 答案與詳細分析 The Answer & Detailed Analysis

✅ 正確答案 Correct Option: C. 只有 (2)、(4) / (2) and (4) only

圖 (1) 分析 (錯誤): Y軸是「稅款 (Tax Payment)」。這是一條從原點出發的直線，意味著稅款與收入成正比 \(T = kY\)。因此，平均稅率 \(T/Y = k\) 是一個常數。這是比例稅 (Proportional Tax)。

Diagram (1) Analysis (Incorrect): The Y-axis is "Tax Payment". It is a straight line from the origin, meaning tax payment is directly proportional to income \(T = kY\). Thus, the average tax rate \(T/Y = k\) is constant. This is a Proportional Tax.

圖 (2) 分析 (正確): Y軸是「平均稅率 (Average Tax Rate)」。圖中顯示一條向上傾斜的線，代表當收入增加時，平均稅率也隨之上升。這是標準的累進稅 (Progressive Tax)。

Diagram (2) Analysis (Correct): The Y-axis is "Average Tax Rate". The diagram shows an upward-sloping line, meaning as income increases, the average tax rate also increases. This defines a Progressive Tax.

圖 (3) 分析 (錯誤): Y軸是「稅款」。圖形是向下凹的曲線 (Concave downward)。這表示當收入增加時，稅款雖然增加，但增加的幅度越來越小。我們如果在曲線上畫一條連接原點的割線 (Secant line)，這條線的斜率代表 \(T/Y\) (即平均稅率)。隨著收入增加，割線的斜率越來越小，代表平均稅率下降。這是累退稅 (Regressive Tax)。

Diagram (3) Analysis (Incorrect): The Y-axis is "Tax Payment". The curve is concave downward. This means as income increases, tax payment increases but at a decreasing rate. The slope of a secant line drawn from the origin represents \(T/Y\) (the Average Tax Rate). As income increases, this slope becomes flatter, meaning the ATR is decreasing. This is a Regressive Tax.

圖 (4) 分析 (正確): Y軸是「平均稅率」。這是一條向上傾斜的曲線。只要線是向上升的（無論是直線還是曲線），就代表收入增加時，平均稅率上升。因此這也是累進稅 (Progressive Tax)。

Diagram (4) Analysis (Correct): The Y-axis is "Average Tax Rate". It is an upward-sloping curve. As long as the line is rising (whether straight or curved), it indicates that the ATR increases as income increases. Thus, this is also a Progressive Tax.

5. 題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)：看清楚 Y軸的標籤 (Y-axis Labels)！考評局最喜歡在坐標軸上設下陷阱。左邊的圖 (1) 和 (3) Y軸是「稅款 ($)」，右邊的圖 (2) 和 (4) Y軸是「平均稅率 (%)」。

Pivot Point: Look carefully at the Y-axis Labels! The HKEAA loves setting traps on the axes. Diagrams (1) and (3) on the left have "Tax payment ($)" on the Y-axis, while (2) and (4) on the right have "Average tax rate (%)".

解題策略：如果題目問「累進稅」，我們只需要尋找那些反映「平均稅率隨收入上升」的圖形。對於Y軸已經是平均稅率的圖，只要線條是「向上升的」，就是正確答案！

Approach: If the question asks for a "progressive tax", we only need to look for graphs showing the "average tax rate rising with income". For graphs where the Y-axis is already the ATR, any "upward-sloping" line is the correct answer!

6. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

⚠️ 死背圖形形狀而不看坐標軸 (Memorizing shapes without checking axes): 很多學生看到圖 (1) 和圖 (3) 是向上傾斜的，就誤以為「交更多稅 = 累進稅」。記住，幾乎所有的稅制（無論累進、比例還是累退），總稅款通常都會隨收入增加而上升。關鍵在於比例 (Rate)的變化，而不是總額！

⚠️ Memorizing shapes without checking axes: Many students see that diagrams (1) and (3) slope upward and mistakenly think "paying more tax = progressive tax". Remember, in almost all tax systems (progressive, proportional, or regressive), the total tax payment usually increases with income. The key is the change in the Rate, not the total amount!

7. 學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

Q: 圖 (1) 明明是斜向上的，為什麼不是累進稅？

A: 因為 Y軸是「稅款」。一條穿過原點的直線代表斜率不變。在「稅款-收入」圖中，連接原點的斜率就是平均稅率（\( \text{斜率} = T \div Y \)）。斜率不變，代表平均稅率不變，所以是「比例稅」。如果是累進稅，圖(1)的線必須要像一個開口向上的拋物線（越來越斜）才對。

Q: Diagram (1) is sloping upward, why isn't it a progressive tax?

A: Because the Y-axis is "Tax Payment". A straight line passing through the origin has a constant slope. In a "Tax Payment vs Income" graph, the slope of a line from the origin equals the average tax rate (\( \text{Slope} = T \div Y \)). Since the slope is constant, the ATR is constant, making it a "Proportional Tax". For it to be a progressive tax, the curve in diagram (1) would need to bend upwards (become steeper, convex).

8. 考試技巧與速解 Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

🚀 一秒刪除法 (The 1-Second Elimination): 在這題中，你只要發現圖 (2) 和 (4) 的 Y軸直接就是「平均稅率」，而且它們都在上升，你就能立刻肯定 (2) 和 (4) 都是累進稅。看選項，只有選項 C 包含了這兩個圖，直接秒殺！完全不用浪費時間去深究 (1) 和 (3) 的斜率。

🚀 The 1-Second Elimination: In this question, once you notice that diagrams (2) and (4) explicitly have "Average tax rate" on the Y-axis and both are sloping upward, you can immediately confirm they both represent a progressive tax. Looking at the choices, only Option C includes both (2) and (4). Instant kill! You don't even need to waste time analyzing the slopes of (1) and (3).
