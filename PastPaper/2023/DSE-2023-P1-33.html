📊 HKDSE Economics: Question Analysis

Topic: 貨幣供應與定義 (Money Supply Definitions) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ (3/5)
Key Concepts: M1, M2, M3 Savings deposits (儲蓄存款) Current account (往來戶口)

1. The Question (題目)

嘉雯從一間香港的持牌銀行提取港幣$600 000儲蓄存款。她將三分之一的金額存入她的往來戶口，並將餘額匯至海外。

上述行動對港元貨幣供應量有什麼即時的影響？

(1) 貨幣供應定義一上升港幣$200 000。
(2) 貨幣供應定義二下降港幣$600 000。
(3) 貨幣供應定義三下降港幣$400 000。

A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

Carmen withdraws HK$600 000 from her savings deposits with a licensed bank in Hong Kong. She puts one-third of the amount into her current account and remits the remaining amount overseas.

What will be the immediate effects of the above actions on the Hong Kong dollar money supply?

(1) M1 increases by HK$200 000.
(2) M2 decreases by HK$600 000.
(3) M3 decreases by HK$400 000.

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這類題目，我們必須清楚香港對貨幣供應（Money Supply）的嚴格定義。貨幣供應是層層遞進的，即 \( \text{M2} \) 包含了 \( \text{M1} \)，而 \( \text{M3} \) 包含了 \( \text{M2} \)。

To solve this type of question, we must understand the strict definitions of Money Supply in Hong Kong. Money supply is cumulative, meaning \( \text{M2} \) includes \( \text{M1} \), and \( \text{M3} \) includes \( \text{M2} \).

$$ \text{M1} = \text{C}_p + \text{D} $$ 公眾持有的法定貨幣 (Legal tender held by public) + 往來戶口存款/活期存款 (Demand deposits)
$$ \text{M2} = \text{M1} + \text{S} + \text{T} + \text{NCDs(LB)} $$ M1 + 持牌銀行客戶儲蓄及定期存款 (Savings & Time deposits with Licensed Banks) + 持牌銀行發行的可轉讓存款證 (NCDs issued by LBs)
$$ \text{M3} = \text{M2} + \text{Deposits(RLB & DTC)} + \text{NCDs(RLB & DTC)} $$ M2 + 有限制牌照銀行及接受存款公司的客戶存款 (Deposits with RLBs & DTCs) + 其發行的可轉讓存款證 (NCDs issued by them)

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 / Correct Option: Option B

行動分析：嘉雯提取了HK$600 000儲蓄存款。這導致持牌銀行的儲蓄存款減少HK$600 000。然後，三分之一（HK$200 000）存入往來戶口（活期存款增加HK$200 000）。餘下三分之二（HK$400 000）匯至海外（這筆錢完全離開了香港的貨幣供應系統）。

Action Analysis: Carmen withdrew HK$600,000 from savings deposits. This decreases savings deposits in licensed banks by HK$600,000. Then, 1/3 (HK$200,000) is deposited into a current account (demand deposits increase by HK$200,000). The remaining 2/3 (HK$400,000) is remitted overseas (this money leaves HK's money supply system entirely).

陳述 (1) 分析： 往來戶口屬於活期存款，是 \( \text{M1} \) 的一部分。增加 HK$200 000 會令 \( \text{M1} \) 上升 HK$200 000。此陳述正確。

Statement (1) Analysis: The current account is a demand deposit, which is part of \( \text{M1} \). An increase of HK$200,000 causes \( \text{M1} \) to increase by HK$200,000. This statement is TRUE.

陳述 (2) 分析： 提取儲蓄存款令 \( \text{M2} \) 下降 HK$600 000。但是，存入往來戶口的 HK$200 000 增加了 \( \text{M1} \)，而 \( \text{M1} \) 是包含在 \( \text{M2} \) 內的。因此，\( \text{M2} \) 的淨變動為 \( -600 000 + 200 000 = -400 000 \)。此陳述指下降 600 000 是錯誤的。

Statement (2) Analysis: Withdrawing the savings deposit decreases \( \text{M2} \) by HK$600,000. However, the HK$200,000 deposited into the current account increases \( \text{M1} \), and \( \text{M1} \) is included in \( \text{M2} \). Therefore, the net change in \( \text{M2} \) is \( -600,000 + 200,000 = -400,000 \). The statement claims it decreases by 600,000, so it is FALSE.

陳述 (3) 分析： \( \text{M3} \) 包含 \( \text{M2} \)。由於此交易不涉及有限制牌照銀行 (RLB) 或接受存款公司 (DTC)，\( \text{M3} \) 的變動與 \( \text{M2} \) 相同，即淨下降 HK$400 000（即匯出海外的金額）。此陳述正確。

Statement (3) Analysis: \( \text{M3} \) includes \( \text{M2} \). Since this transaction does not involve Restricted Licence Banks (RLBs) or Deposit-taking Companies (DTCs), the change in \( \text{M3} \) is identical to \( \text{M2} \), which is a net decrease of HK$400,000 (the exact amount remitted overseas). This statement is TRUE.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題關鍵 (The Pivot Point)：這條題目的核心在於明白貨幣供應的「包含性」(Cumulative Nature)。當資金在銀行系統內不同戶口之間轉移時（例如由儲蓄戶口轉至往來戶口），高層次的貨幣供應（如 \( \text{M2} \) 和 \( \text{M3} \)）需要計算「淨變動」(Net change)。

The Pivot Point: The core of this question is understanding the cumulative nature of money supply. When funds are transferred between different accounts within the banking system (e.g., from savings to current accounts), you must calculate the "net change" for higher-level money aggregates like \( \text{M2} \) and \( \text{M3} \).

  • 干擾資訊 (Distractor): "提取" (Withdraws) 這個字眼容易令人誤以為全數金額變成了公眾持有的現金 (\( \text{C}_p \))。但題目寫明「將三分之一...存入...並將餘額匯至海外」，我們只需看最終的資金落腳點，跳過中間短暫的現金狀態。
  • Distractor: The word "withdraws" might trick students into thinking the entire amount becomes cash in public hands (\( \text{C}_p \)). However, the prompt specifies she puts 1/3 into an account and remits the rest. We only need to look at the final destination of the funds, ignoring the brief moment it might have been held as physical cash.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

🚨 陷阱一：忘記 M2 包含 M1
很多學生看到「提取儲蓄存款 600 000」，就直接判定 M2 減少了 600 000（誤以為陳述 2 正確）。他們忘記了那 200 000 進入了 M1，而這 200 000 會「抵銷」部分 M2 的跌幅！

🚨 Trap 1: Forgetting that M2 includes M1
Many students see "withdraws HK$600,000 savings" and immediately conclude M2 decreases by 600,000 (falsely believing statement 2 is correct). They forget that HK$200,000 entered M1, which "offsets" part of the drop in M2!

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

🙋‍♂️ 學生問：為什麼匯至海外的 HK$400 000 不計入任何貨幣供應中？

👨‍🏫 導師答：香港的貨幣供應只計算留在香港本地經濟體系內的貨幣。一旦資金匯出海外（例如匯到了美國的銀行），這筆錢就不再屬於香港的 \( \text{M1} \)、\( \text{M2} \) 或 \( \text{M3} \) 了。

🙋‍♂️ Student Q: Why isn't the HK$400,000 remitted overseas counted in any money supply?

👨‍🏫 Tutor A: Hong Kong's money supply aggregates only measure money kept within the domestic economy of Hong Kong. Once funds are remitted overseas (e.g., to a bank in the US), that money is no longer part of Hong Kong's \( \text{M1} \), \( \text{M2} \), or \( \text{M3} \).

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

⚡ 秒殺技巧 (The "Follow the Money" Shortcut):

不需要逐個選項慢慢算！直接宏觀地看整體資金流出：

  1. 嘉雯原本有 60 萬在銀行系統內 (屬於 \( \text{M2} \) 和 \( \text{M3} \))。
  2. 這 60 萬當中，有 20 萬仍然留在本地銀行 (轉了去 M1)，有 40 萬徹底離開了香港。
  3. 因此，香港銀行系統整體的錢（最闊的定義 \( \text{M3} \)）必定是淨流失了 40 萬！
  4. 這意味著 (3) 必定正確，(2) 必定錯誤。既然 (2) 錯，選項 A、C、D 全部剔除，答案必定是 B！只需 10 秒鐘！

⚡ Speed Trick (The "Follow the Money" Shortcut):

You don't need to calculate each statement step-by-step! Look at the macro-level capital outflow:

  1. Carmen originally had 600k in the banking system (which was part of \( \text{M2} \) and \( \text{M3} \)).
  2. Out of this 600k, 200k remained in a local bank (shifted to M1), while 400k left Hong Kong entirely.
  3. Therefore, the total money in HK's broad banking system (the broadest definition \( \text{M3} \)) must have a net loss of exactly 400k!
  4. This means statement (3) MUST be true, and statement (2) MUST be false. Since (2) is false, eliminate options A, C, and D. The answer is instantly B! This takes 10 seconds!
