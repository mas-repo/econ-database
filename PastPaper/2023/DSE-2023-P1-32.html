HKDSE Economics Tutorial

Topic: Money and Banking 貨幣與銀行 Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Monetary Base, Open Market Operations

1. The Question (題目)

下列哪項會令貨幣基礎上升？
A. 中央銀行調高貼現率。
B. 中央銀行調低法定儲備比率。
C. 中央銀行從公眾購入政府債券。
D. 中央銀行以塑質鈔票取代部分流通的紙質鈔票。
Which of the following will lead to an increase in monetary base?
A. The central bank raises the discount rate.
B. The central bank reduces the required reserve ratio.
C. The central bank buys government bonds from the public.
D. The central bank replaces some of the paper banknotes in circulation with polymer banknotes.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

貨幣基礎 (Monetary Base)，又稱高能貨幣 (High-powered money)，是指中央銀行發行的實體貨幣總量。它由公眾持有的現金 \(C_p\) 和銀行體系的儲備 \(R\) 組成。
Monetary Base, also known as high-powered money, refers to the total amount of physical currency issued by the central bank. It consists of cash held by the public \(C_p\) and reserves in the banking system \(R\).
$$MB = C_p + R$$
貨幣供應 (Money Supply) 則是經濟體系中的購買力總和，包括公眾持有的現金 \(C_p\) 和銀行存款 \(D\)。
Money Supply is the total purchasing power in the economy, including cash held by the public \(C_p\) and bank deposits \(D\).
$$M_s = C_p + D$$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 / Correct Option: C

A. 中央銀行調高貼現率 (Raises the discount rate) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
調高貼現率會增加商業銀行向央行借款的成本，這可能會減少銀行的借款，從而可能令儲備 \(R\) 下降，導致貨幣基礎下跌而非上升。
Raising the discount rate increases the cost for commercial banks to borrow from the central bank. This may reduce bank borrowing, potentially decreasing reserves \(R\), which would lead to a decrease, not an increase, in the monetary base.
B. 中央銀行調低法定儲備比率 (Reduces the required reserve ratio) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
這是一個常見陷阱！調低法定儲備比率會增加銀行的超額儲備，從而創造更多存款 \(D\)，導致貨幣供應 (\(M_s\)) 上升。但是，它並沒有改變央行發行的實體貨幣總量，因此貨幣基礎 (\(MB\)) 保持不變。
This is a classic trap! Reducing the required reserve ratio increases banks' excess reserves, allowing them to create more deposits \(D\), which increases the Money Supply (\(M_s\)). However, it does not change the total amount of physical currency issued by the central bank, so the Monetary Base (\(MB\)) remains unchanged.
C. 中央銀行從公眾購入政府債券 (Buys government bonds from the public) - 正確 (Correct)
當央行在公開市場購入債券時，央行會向公眾支付金錢。公眾可以將這些錢存入銀行（增加儲備 \(R\)）或以現金持有（增加公眾持有的現金 \(C_p\)）。無論哪種情況，貨幣基礎 \(MB\) 都會上升。這被稱為擴張性的公開市場操作。
When the central bank buys bonds in the open market, it pays money to the public. The public can either deposit this money into banks (increasing reserves \(R\)) or hold it as cash (increasing cash in public hands \(C_p\)). In either case, the monetary base \(MB\) increases. This is known as an expansionary open market operation.
D. 中央銀行以塑質鈔票取代部分流通的紙質鈔票 (Replaces paper banknotes with polymer banknotes) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
這只是改變了貨幣的物理材質（一換一），並沒有向市場注入新的資金，因此貨幣基礎不變。
This merely changes the physical material of the currency (a one-to-one swap). It does not inject new funds into the market, so the monetary base remains unchanged.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題關鍵 (Pivot Point): 區分「貨幣基礎 (Monetary Base)」和「貨幣供應 (Money Supply)」。
要改變貨幣基礎，中央銀行必須實質地向市場注入或抽走基礎資金（即改變 \(C_p\) 或 \(R\) 的總和）。只有公開市場操作（買賣外匯或債券）或銀行向央行借貸的改變能直接改變貨幣基礎。
Pivot Point: Distinguish between "Monetary Base" and "Money Supply".
To change the monetary base, the central bank must physically inject or withdraw base money from the market (i.e., change the sum of \(C_p\) and \(R\)). Only open market operations (buying/selling foreign exchange or bonds) or changes in bank borrowing from the central bank can directly change the monetary base.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：混淆法定儲備比率的影響
很多學生看到「調低法定儲備比率」(Option B) 就會聯想到「擴張性貨幣政策」，然後錯誤地認為所有貨幣數據都會上升。記住：改變儲備比率只會影響貨幣乘數 (Money Multiplier) 和存款創造過程，它改變的是貨幣供應 \(M_s\)，而絕對不會改變貨幣基礎 \(MB\)。
Trap: Confusing the effect of the Required Reserve Ratio
Many students see "reduces the required reserve ratio" (Option B) and immediately think of "expansionary monetary policy", wrongly assuming all monetary figures will rise. Remember: changing the reserve ratio only affects the Money Multiplier and the deposit creation process. It changes the Money Supply \(M_s\), but it NEVER changes the Monetary Base \(MB\).

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果選項 C 是「央行向公眾出售政府債券」，結果會怎樣？
A: 如果央行出售債券，公眾需要用現金或銀行存款來支付。這會導致公眾持有的現金 \(C_p\) 或銀行儲備 \(R\) 減少，從而令貨幣基礎 \(MB\) 下降。
Q: What if Option C was "The central bank sells government bonds to the public"?
A: If the central bank sells bonds, the public must pay with cash or bank deposits. This would decrease cash in public hands \(C_p\) or bank reserves \(R\), leading to a decrease in the monetary base \(MB\).

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法：
在DSE考試中，問及「貨幣基礎 (Monetary Base) 改變」的題目，通常答案都離不開「公開市場操作 (Open Market Operations)」（即買賣債券/外匯）。看到「改變儲備比率 (Reserve Ratio)」，立刻將其與「貨幣基礎」脫鉤（排除B）。看到「買入 (Buys)」，代表央行把錢放進市場，基礎上升（選C）。
Shortcut:
In the DSE exam, questions asking about changes in the "Monetary Base" almost always point to "Open Market Operations" (buying/selling bonds or foreign exchange). When you see "change in reserve ratio", immediately decouple it from the monetary base (eliminate B). When you see "buys", it means the central bank is putting money into the market, so the base increases (choose C).
