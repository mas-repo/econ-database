HKDSE Economics Tutor Guide

Topic 市場干預 - 補貼 (Market Intervention - Subsidy)
Difficulty ★★★★☆
Key Concepts Producer Surplus, Total Expenditure, Deadweight Loss, Total Revenue

2. 題目 (The Question)

向某物品提供從量生產補貼會導致 _________ 。

The provision of a per-unit production subsidy on a good will lead to __________.

A. 該物品的生產者盈餘下降
a fall in the producer surplus of the good
B. 消費者對該物品的總支出上升
a rise in consumers’ total expenditure on the good
C. 該物品的總社會盈餘上升
a rise in the total social surplus of the good
D. 該物品包括補貼在內的總收入上升
a rise in the total revenue including the subsidy of the good

3. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

從量補貼 (Per-unit Subsidy)： 政府向生產者提供每單位固定金額的資助，這會降低生產成本，使供應曲線向右（向下）移動。

Per-unit Subsidy: A fixed amount of money given by the government to producers for each unit produced. It lowers production costs and shifts the supply curve to the right (downwards).

$$ P_s = P_d + \text{Subsidy} $$

\(P_s\) = 賣家實收價格 (Price received by sellers)
\(P_d\) = 買家支付價格 (Price paid by buyers)

包括補貼在內的總收入 (Total Revenue including subsidy)： 這是生產者實際獲得的總金額。

Total Revenue including subsidy: The total amount of money actually received by producers.

$$ \text{TR}_{new} = P_s \times Q_1 $$

4. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): D

A. 錯誤 (Incorrect)
補貼會降低生產成本，使交易量 \(Q\) 增加，同時賣家實收價格 \(P_s\) 上升。因此，生產者盈餘（Producer Surplus）必定會上升，而非下降。

A subsidy lowers production costs, increasing the transacted quantity \(Q\) and the price received by sellers \(P_s\). Therefore, producer surplus must rise, not fall.

B. 錯誤 (Incorrect)
消費者總支出 \(TE = P_d \times Q\)。補貼使買家支付價格 \(P_d\) 下降，交易量 \(Q\) 上升。總支出是升是跌，取決於該物品的需求價格彈性 (Price Elasticity of Demand)。由於題目沒有提供彈性資料，我們無法確定總支出會上升。

Consumers' total expenditure \(TE = P_d \times Q\). A subsidy lowers the price paid by buyers \(P_d\) and increases quantity \(Q\). Whether TE rises or falls depends on the price elasticity of demand. Without this information, we cannot be certain that TE will rise.

C. 錯誤 (Incorrect)
補貼會導致生產過剩 (Overproduction)，即邊際成本大於邊際利益 (\(MC > MB\))，從而產生效率損失 (Deadweight Loss)。因此，總社會盈餘必定會下降。

A subsidy leads to overproduction, where marginal cost exceeds marginal benefit (\(MC > MB\)), creating a deadweight loss. Therefore, total social surplus must fall.

D. 正確 (Correct)
包括補貼在內的總收入等於賣家實收價格乘以數量 (\(P_s \times Q\))。由於補貼後賣家實收價格 \(P_s\) 高於原來的均衡價格，且交易量 \(Q\) 也增加了，兩者皆上升，因此包括補貼在內的總收入必定上升。

Total revenue including the subsidy equals the price received by sellers multiplied by quantity (\(P_s \times Q\)). Since the post-subsidy price received by sellers \(P_s\) is higher than the original equilibrium price, and the quantity \(Q\) has also increased, their product must rise.

5. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題關鍵 (Pivot Point)： 分清「消費者總支出」與「生產者總收入（連補貼）」。

  • 消費者面對的是 \(P_d\)，\(P_d\) 下降而 \(Q\) 上升，結果不確定（需看彈性）。
  • 生產者面對的是 \(P_s\)，\(P_s\) 上升且 \(Q\) 上升，結果必定上升。

Pivot Point: Distinguish between "consumers' total expenditure" and "producers' total revenue (including subsidy)". Consumers face \(P_d\) (which falls) and \(Q\) (which rises), making the result uncertain (depends on elasticity). Producers face \(P_s\) (which rises) and \(Q\) (which rises), making the result definitively higher.

6. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱 1： 誤以為「補貼是好事」，所以總社會盈餘 (Total Social Surplus) 會上升。記住，任何偏離自由市場均衡的政府干預（無界外效應下）都會產生效率損失 (Deadweight Loss)。

Trap 1: Assuming "subsidy is good," so Total Social Surplus must rise. Remember, any government intervention that deviates from the free-market equilibrium (without externalities) creates a Deadweight Loss.

7. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 既然消費者和生產者都從補貼中獲益（盈餘增加），為什麼總社會盈餘還會下降？

A: 因為政府支付的補貼總額（納稅人的錢）大於消費者盈餘和生產者盈餘的增加總和。這中間的差額就是效率損失 (Deadweight Loss)。

Q: Since both consumers and producers benefit from the subsidy (surplus increases), why does total social surplus fall?

A: Because the total amount of subsidy paid by the government (taxpayers' money) is greater than the combined increase in consumer and producer surplus. The difference is the deadweight loss.

8. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

速解法： 在草稿紙上畫一個簡單的供需圖。補貼使 \(S\) 向右移。標出新的 \(Q\)、買家價格 \(P_d\) 和賣家價格 \(P_s\)。

你會立刻看到代表「包括補貼在內的總收入」的矩形面積 (\(P_s \times Q\)) 明顯比原來的矩形 (\(P \times Q\)) 大，因為它的長和寬都增加了。秒殺選項 D！

Shortcut: Draw a quick supply-demand diagram. Subsidy shifts \(S\) right. Mark the new \(Q\), buyer price \(P_d\), and seller price \(P_s\). You will immediately see that the rectangle representing "total revenue including subsidy" (\(P_s \times Q\)) is strictly larger than the original rectangle (\(P \times Q\)) because both its height and width have increased. Instant choice D!
