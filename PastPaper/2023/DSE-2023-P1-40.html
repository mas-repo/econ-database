Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆

Key Concepts (核心概念):

短期總供給 (SRAS) 生產成本 (Cost of Production) 滯脹 (Stagflation)

The Question (題目)

下列哪項會令某經濟的物價水平上升，同時令該經濟的就業水平下降？

Which of the following would result in a rise in the price level of an economy and a fall in its employment level at the same time?

  • A. 政府調低利得稅稅率。
    The government reduces the profits tax rate.
  • B. 相對於其貿易夥伴的貨幣，該經濟的貨幣升值。
    The economy’s currency appreciates against the currencies of its trading partners.
  • C. 政府增加研究及發展的開支。
    The government increases its spending on research and development.
  • D. 進口原料的價格上升。
    The prices of imported raw materials increase.

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這道題目，我們需要運用總需求-總供給模型 (AS-AD Model)。宏觀經濟的均衡點取決於總需求 ($AD$) 和短期總供給 ($SRAS$) 的相交點。

To answer this question, we need to apply the Aggregate Demand - Aggregate Supply (AS-AD) Model. The macroeconomic equilibrium is determined by the intersection of Aggregate Demand ($AD$) and Short-Run Aggregate Supply ($SRAS$).

均衡條件 (Equilibrium Condition): $$AD = SRAS$$

關鍵定義 (Key Definitions):

  • 總需求 ($AD$): 經濟體系中對所有最終物品及服務的總需求。方程式為 $$AD = C + I + G + NX$$
  • Aggregate Demand ($AD$): The total demand for all final goods and services in an economy. The formula is $$AD = C + I + G + NX$$
  • 短期總供給 ($SRAS$): 在生產要素價格（如工資、原料）固定的情況下，經濟體系在不同物價水平下願意且能夠生產的總產出。
    移動因素： 生產成本 (Cost of production) 的改變。
  • Short-Run Aggregate Supply ($SRAS$): The total output an economy is willing and able to produce at different price levels, assuming factor prices (e.g., wages, raw materials) are fixed.
    Shift factor: Changes in the cost of production.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是 (Correct Option): Option D

選項 A (Option A): 錯誤 (Incorrect)

政府調低利得稅稅率，會增加企業的預期投資回報，刺激投資 ($I$) 增加。因此，總需求 ($AD$) 會向右移。這會導致物價水平 ($P$) 上升，實質產出 ($Y$) 增加，就業水平隨之上升（不符合題目要求的下降）。

A reduction in the profits tax rate increases the expected return on investment for firms, stimulating investment ($I$). Therefore, Aggregate Demand ($AD$) shifts to the right. This leads to a rise in the price level ($P$) and an increase in real output ($Y$), which causes the employment level to rise (contradicting the question's requirement of a fall).

選項 B (Option B): 錯誤 (Incorrect)

貨幣升值會令出口變得相對昂貴，進口變得相對便宜，導致淨出口 ($NX$) 減少。總需求 ($AD$) 會向左移。這會導致物價水平 ($P$) 下降，就業水平下降（不符合題目要求的物價上升）。

Currency appreciation makes exports relatively more expensive and imports relatively cheaper, leading to a decrease in net exports ($NX$). Aggregate Demand ($AD$) shifts to the left. This causes the price level ($P$) to fall and the employment level to fall (contradicting the question's requirement of a price rise).

選項 C (Option C): 錯誤 (Incorrect)

政府增加研發開支，一方面直接增加政府開支 ($G$)，令總需求 ($AD$) 向右移；長遠亦會提升生產力，令總供給 ($AS$) 向右移。無論如何，實質產出 ($Y$) 和就業水平都會上升。

An increase in government R&D spending directly increases government expenditure ($G$), shifting Aggregate Demand ($AD$) to the right. In the long run, it also improves productivity, shifting Aggregate Supply ($AS$) to the right. In either case, real output ($Y$) and the employment level will rise.

選項 D (Option D): 正確 (Correct)

進口原料的價格上升，會直接增加企業的生產成本。在任何物價水平下，企業的利潤減少，導致短期總供給 ($SRAS$) 向左移。這會造成「滯脹」(Stagflation) 現象：物價水平 ($P$) 上升，同時實質產出 ($Y$) 下降，需要較少勞工，令就業水平下降。完全符合題目描述。

An increase in the prices of imported raw materials directly increases the cost of production for firms. At any given price level, firms' profit margins decrease, causing the Short-Run Aggregate Supply ($SRAS$) to shift to the left. This creates "Stagflation": the price level ($P$) rises while real output ($Y$) falls, requiring fewer workers, which causes the employment level to fall. This perfectly matches the question's description.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point): 留意題目給出的兩個最終結果：「物價上升」($P \uparrow$) 及「就業下降」($Y \downarrow$)。

The Pivot Point: Pay attention to the two final outcomes given in the question: "price level rises" ($P \uparrow$) and "employment level falls" ($Y \downarrow$).

  • 在 AS-AD 模型中，只有短期總供給 (SRAS) 向左移，才會同時導致物價上升和產出（及就業）下降。
  • In the AS-AD model, only a leftward shift of the Short-Run Aggregate Supply (SRAS) curve can simultaneously cause a rise in the price level and a fall in output (and employment).
  • 因此，我們只需要在選項中找出「哪一個因素會導致 SRAS 向左移（生產成本上升）」。選項 D (原料價格上升) 顯然是正確答案。
  • Therefore, we only need to look through the options to find "which factor causes SRAS to shift to the left (increase in production cost)". Option D (increase in raw material prices) is clearly the correct answer.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：誤解貨幣升值的影響 (Misunderstanding Currency Appreciation)

有學生看到選項 B（貨幣升值），會聯想到「進口原料變便宜」，從而推斷生產成本下降，令 SRAS 向右移。然而，即使 SRAS 向右移，結果會是物價下降及就業上升，仍然與題意相反。在 DSE 考試中，除非題目特別強調「該國高度依賴進口原料」，否則匯率變動首要影響的是淨出口 ($NX$) 及總需求 ($AD$)。

Some students seeing Option B (currency appreciation) might think "imported raw materials become cheaper," deducing that production costs fall and SRAS shifts right. However, even if SRAS shifts right, the result would be a fall in price level and a rise in employment, which still contradicts the question. In DSE exams, unless the question specifically emphasizes that "the country relies heavily on imported raw materials," exchange rate changes primarily affect net exports ($NX$) and Aggregate Demand ($AD$).

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 題目只說「就業水平下降」，為甚麼解題時會自動等同於「實質產出 ($Y$) 下降」？
Q: The question only says "employment level falls". Why do we automatically equate this to a "fall in real output ($Y$)" when solving the problem?

A: 在短期宏觀經濟中，勞工是主要的浮動生產要素 (variable factor of production)。當實質本地生產總值（產出 $Y$）減少時，企業需要生產的數量下降，自然會減少僱用勞工，導致就業水平下降（或失業率上升）。因此，產出與就業在短期內是同向變動的。

A: In short-run macroeconomics, labour is the primary variable factor of production. When Real GDP (output $Y$) decreases, firms need to produce less, so they naturally hire fewer workers, leading to a fall in the employment level (or a rise in the unemployment rate). Therefore, output and employment move in the same direction in the short run.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

AS-AD 的「四象限法則」(The Four-Quadrant Rule of AS-AD)

在選擇題中，無需每次都畫圖。你可以直接背誦以下四種移動對 $P$ (物價) 和 $Y$ (產出/就業) 的影響，以達至「秒殺」效果：

In MC questions, you don't need to draw a graph every time. You can memorize the effects of the four shifts on $P$ (price) and $Y$ (output/employment) to solve questions instantly:

Curve Shift (曲線移動) Price Level $P$ (物價) Employment $Y$ (就業)
$AD$ Right (向右移) $\uparrow$ 上升 $\uparrow$ 上升
$AD$ Left (向左移) $\downarrow$ 下降 $\downarrow$ 下降
$SRAS$ Right (向右移) $\downarrow$ 下降 $\uparrow$ 上升
$SRAS$ Left (向左移) [Target!] $\uparrow$ 上升 $\downarrow$ 下降

本題尋找 $P \uparrow$ 和 $Y \downarrow$，對照表格，一眼就能確認答案必須是導致 $SRAS$ 向左移的因素 (Option D)。

This question looks for $P \uparrow$ and $Y \downarrow$. Comparing with the table, you can see at a glance that the answer must be the factor causing $SRAS$ to shift left (Option D).
