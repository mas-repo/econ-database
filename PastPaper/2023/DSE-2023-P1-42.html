國際貿易與比較優勢
International Trade & Comparative Advantage

Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts: Absolute Advantage (絕對優勢), Comparative Advantage (比較優勢), Opportunity Cost (機會成本), Terms of Trade (貿易比率)

題目 / The Question

下表顯示甲國和乙國以一單位的資源可生產的蘋果或桃子數量。

The following table shows the amount of apples or peaches that Country A and Country B can produce with one unit of resources.

蘋果 (Apples) 桃子 (Peaches)
甲國 (Country A) 100 80
乙國 (Country B) 180 90

根據上表，下列哪些陳述是正確的？
(1) 甲國生產1單位蘋果的機會成本是0.8單位桃子。
(2) 乙國生產蘋果有絕對優勢。
(3) 一個互惠互利的貿易比率可以是1單位蘋果 = 0.7單位桃子。

According to the above table, which of the following statements are correct?
(1) The opportunity cost of producing 1 unit of apples is 0.8 units of peaches in Country A.
(2) Country B has an absolute advantage in producing apples.
(3) A mutually beneficial terms of trade can be 1 unit of apples = 0.7 units of peaches.

A. 只有 (1)、(2) / (1) and (2) only

B. 只有 (1)、(3) / (1) and (3) only

C. 只有 (2)、(3) / (2) and (3) only

D. (1)、(2) 及 (3) / (1), (2) and (3)

概念重溫 / Concept Review

1. 產出題 (Output Problem)： 題目給定固定資源（一單位資源），比較各國能生產的最大產量。計算機會成本時，我們使用「放棄的產量除以獲得的產量」。

1. Output Problem: The question provides a fixed amount of resources (one unit) and compares the maximum output. To calculate opportunity cost, we divide the "quantity given up" by the "quantity gained".

$$ \text{Opportunity Cost of X} = \frac{\text{Maximum Output of Y}}{\text{Maximum Output of X}} $$

2. 絕對優勢 (Absolute Advantage)： 使用相同數量的資源，能生產出比另一個國家更多的某種物品。

2. Absolute Advantage: The ability to produce more of a good than another country using the same amount of resources.

3. 互惠互利的貿易比率 (Mutually Beneficial Terms of Trade - TOT)： 貿易比率必須介乎兩國生產該物品的機會成本之間，雙方才會同意進行交易。

3. Mutually Beneficial Terms of Trade (TOT): The terms of trade must fall between the opportunity costs of the two countries for both to agree to trade.

$$ \text{Exporter's Opportunity Cost} < \text{Terms of Trade} < \text{Importer's Opportunity Cost} $$

答案與詳細分析 / The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 / Correct Option: D

陳述 (1) 分析：正確
Analysis of Statement (1): Correct

在甲國，1單位資源可生產100單位蘋果或80單位桃子。這意味著 \( 100\text{A} = 80\text{P} \)。
生產1單位蘋果的機會成本 = \( \frac{80}{100}\text{P} = 0.8\text{P} \)。

In Country A, 1 unit of resources can produce 100 Apples or 80 Peaches. This implies \( 100\text{A} = 80\text{P} \).
Opportunity cost of producing 1 Apple = \( \frac{80}{100}\text{P} = 0.8\text{P} \).

陳述 (2) 分析：正確
Analysis of Statement (2): Correct

絕對優勢比較的是實際產量。以1單位資源計算，乙國能生產180單位蘋果，而甲國只能生產100單位。因為 \( 180 > 100 \)，所以乙國在生產蘋果上擁有絕對優勢。

Absolute advantage compares actual output. With 1 unit of resources, Country B produces 180 Apples, while Country A produces 100. Since \( 180 > 100 \), Country B has an absolute advantage in producing apples.

陳述 (3) 分析：正確
Analysis of Statement (3): Correct

首先，我們需要計算乙國生產蘋果的機會成本：
乙國：\( 180\text{A} = 90\text{P} \Rightarrow 1\text{A} = \frac{90}{180}\text{P} = 0.5\text{P} \)。
甲國生產1蘋果的機會成本是 \( 0.8\text{P} \)。乙國生產1蘋果的機會成本是 \( 0.5\text{P} \)。
因此，互惠互利的貿易比率必須介乎於：\( 0.5\text{P} < 1\text{A} < 0.8\text{P} \)。
因為 0.7 落在這個範圍內，所以這是一個合理的貿易比率。

First, calculate Country B's opportunity cost for producing apples:
Country B: \( 180\text{A} = 90\text{P} \Rightarrow 1\text{A} = \frac{90}{180}\text{P} = 0.5\text{P} \).
Country A's OC for 1 Apple is \( 0.8\text{P} \). Country B's OC for 1 Apple is \( 0.5\text{P} \).
Therefore, a mutually beneficial TOT must be: \( 0.5\text{P} < 1\text{A} < 0.8\text{P} \).
Since 0.7 falls strictly within this range, it is a valid terms of trade.

題目拆解 / Problem Decomposition

樞紐點 (Pivot Point)： 解答這類題目的第一步是判斷這是「產出題 (Output)」還是「投入題 (Input)」。題目列明是「以一單位的資源可生產...數量」，因此這是產出題。在產出題中，數字越大代表生產力越高。

Pivot Point: The very first step is to identify if this is an Output or Input problem. The question says "amount... produced with one unit of resources", confirming it's an Output problem. Here, a higher number means higher productivity.

拆解步驟：
1. 看到表格後，立刻在草稿紙上計算出兩國生產兩種物品的機會成本。
2. 比較絕對數字以找出絕對優勢。
3. 利用機會成本的區間來驗證貿易比率。

Steps:
1. Immediately calculate the opportunity costs for both goods in both countries upon seeing the table.
2. Compare the absolute numbers to determine absolute advantage.
3. Use the range of opportunity costs to verify the given Terms of Trade.

常犯錯誤與陷阱 / Common Mistakes & Traps

🚨 混淆「產出題」與「投入題」的計算法則： 很多學生會不小心把公式倒轉。例如在甲國計算蘋果的機會成本時，錯誤地算成 \( \frac{100}{80} = 1.25\text{P} \)。緊記口訣：「放棄的放上面（分子）」。

🚨 Confusing Output vs. Input Formulas: Many students accidentally invert the fraction. For example, calculating Country A's OC for apples as \( \frac{100}{80} = 1.25\text{P} \). Remember the rule: "What you give up goes on top (numerator)".

🚨 誤以為「絕對優勢 = 比較優勢」： 絕對優勢只看單一物品的產量高低（180 > 100）；而比較優勢看的是「機會成本」誰較低（0.5P < 0.8P）。雖然本題中乙國同時擁有蘋果的絕對及比較優勢，但這並非必然的定規。

🚨 Equating Absolute Advantage with Comparative Advantage: Absolute advantage only looks at total output (180 > 100); comparative advantage looks at who has the lower opportunity cost (0.5P < 0.8P). Although Country B has both for apples here, this isn't always the case.

學生常見疑問 / Student Q&A

Q: 在陳述(3)中，如果題目說貿易比率是「1單位蘋果 = 0.5單位桃子」或者「1單位蘋果 = 0.8單位桃子」，這樣還算是「互惠互利 (Mutually Beneficial)」嗎？

A: 不算。 如果 \( 1\text{A} = 0.5\text{P} \)，這剛好等於乙國的機會成本。對乙國來說，自己生產和貿易的成本一樣，這叫「沒有得益 (No gain)」，雖然不虧本，但不算「互利」。互惠貿易比率必須嚴格介乎於兩國成本之間 (不包含等號)。

A: No. If \( 1\text{A} = 0.5\text{P} \), it exactly equals Country B's internal opportunity cost. For Country B, there is no gain from trade. A mutually beneficial term of trade must be strictly between the two costs (exclusive of the endpoints).

Q: 根據數據，誰應該出口蘋果？誰出口桃子？

A: 比較優勢決定了出口方向。乙國生產蘋果的機會成本較低 (\( 0.5\text{P} < 0.8\text{P} \))，所以乙國擁有蘋果的比較優勢，應該出口蘋果。相反，甲國生產桃子的機會成本較低 (\( 1.25\text{A} < 2\text{A} \))，所以甲國應該出口桃子。

A: Comparative advantage dictates trade direction. Country B has a lower OC for apples (\( 0.5\text{P} < 0.8\text{P} \)), so it has the comparative advantage and should export apples. Conversely, Country A has a lower OC for peaches (\( 1.25\text{A} < 2\text{A} \)) and should export peaches.

考試技巧與速解 / Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

💡 交叉相乘法 (Cross-Multiplication Shortcut)：
要快速找出誰有比較優勢而不需要計算小數，可以把表格數據交叉相乘：
甲國蘋果(100) × 乙國桃子(90) = 9000
甲國桃子(80) × 乙國蘋果(180) = 14400
因為 \( 14400 > 9000 \)，沿著對角線 180 和 80 所代表的物品，即乙國有蘋果優勢，甲國有桃子優勢。這在時間緊迫的MCQ中非常有用！

💡 Cross-Multiplication Shortcut:
To quickly find comparative advantage without calculating decimals, cross-multiply the table data:
Country A Apples (100) × Country B Peaches (90) = 9000
Country A Peaches (80) × Country B Apples (180) = 14400
Since \( 14400 > 9000 \), the goods along the 180 and 80 diagonal hold the advantage: Country B has the advantage in Apples, and Country A in Peaches. Very handy for speedy MCQ solving!
