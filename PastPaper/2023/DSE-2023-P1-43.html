經濟學文憑試 專家解析 (HKDSE Economics Tutor)

Topic: International Trade (國際貿易) - Market Intervention (市場干預) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts: World Price (世界價格), Per-unit Tariff (從量關稅), Domestic Production (本地生產量), Domestic Consumption (本地消費量).

1. 題目 (The Question)

下圖顯示某小型開放經濟中物品X的市場。該經濟對物品X徵收了從量關稅。若物品X的世界價格下降， ____________。

The diagram below shows the market of Good X in a small open economy. The economy has imposed a per unit tariff on Good X. If the world price of Good X decreases, ____________.

  • A. 物品X的關稅收入會維持不變
    the tariff revenue of Good X will remain unchanged
  • B. 物品X的本地價格會上升
    the domestic price of Good X will increase
  • C. 物品X的本地消費量會維持不變
    the quantity of Good X consumed domestically will remain unchanged
  • D. 物品X的本地生產量會下降
    the quantity of Good X produced domestically will decrease

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

小型開放經濟與關稅 (Small Open Economy and Tariffs)

在「小型開放經濟」的假設下，該國是價格接受者 (Price Taker)。這意味著該國不能影響世界價格 \(P_w\)。當政府徵收從量關稅 (Per-unit tariff) \(t\) 時，本地市場的價格將由世界價格加上關稅所決定。

Under the "small open economy" assumption, the country is a price taker. This means the country cannot influence the world price \(P_w\). When the government imposes a per-unit tariff \(t\), the domestic market price is determined by the world price plus the tariff.

$$ \text{本地價格 (Domestic Price)} = P_w + t $$
  • 本地消費量 (Domestic Consumption, \(Q_d\)): 由需求曲線 (D) 與本地價格 \(P_w + t\) 的相交點決定。
    Determined by the intersection of the Demand curve (D) and the domestic price \(P_w + t\).
  • 本地生產量 (Domestic Production, \(Q_s\)): 由供應曲線 (S) 與本地價格 \(P_w + t\) 的相交點決定。
    Determined by the intersection of the Supply curve (S) and the domestic price \(P_w + t\).
  • 進口量 (Quantity of Imports): 等於本地消費量減去本地生產量。
    Equals domestic consumption minus domestic production.
$$ \text{進口量 (Imports)} = Q_d - Q_s $$ $$ \text{關稅收入 (Tariff Revenue)} = t \times (Q_d - Q_s) $$

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是 Option D。

The correct answer is Option D.

逐項分析 (Step-by-Step Breakdown)

當世界價格 \(P_w\) 下降，而從量關稅 \(t\) 維持不變時，整條 \(P_w + t\) 的水平線會向下平移（如上圖紅線所示）。

When the world price \(P_w\) decreases and the per-unit tariff \(t\) remains constant, the entire horizontal line for \(P_w + t\) shifts downwards (as shown by the red line in the diagram above).

  • A. 物品X的關稅收入會維持不變 (False)
    由於本地價格下跌，本地消費量 (\(Q_d\)) 會增加，而本地生產量 (\(Q_s\)) 會減少。因此，進口量 (\(Q_d - Q_s\)) 會大幅增加。由於 \(t\) 不變，關稅收入 \(t \times (Q_d - Q_s)\) 必定會上升。
    Because the domestic price falls, domestic consumption (\(Q_d\)) increases and domestic production (\(Q_s\)) decreases. Consequently, the quantity of imports (\(Q_d - Q_s\)) increases significantly. Since \(t\) is constant, the tariff revenue \(t \times (Q_d - Q_s)\) must increase.
  • B. 物品X的本地價格會上升 (False)
    本地價格為 \(P_w + t\)。既然 \(P_w\) 下降，本地價格必定會下降。
    The domestic price is \(P_w + t\). Since \(P_w\) decreases, the domestic price must decrease.
  • C. 物品X的本地消費量會維持不變 (False)
    根據需求定律，當本地價格下降時，本地消費量（需求量）會增加（沿著需求曲線向右下移動）。
    According to the law of demand, when the domestic price falls, the quantity consumed domestically (quantity demanded) will increase (moving down and to the right along the demand curve).
  • D. 物品X的本地生產量會下降 (True)
    根據供應定律，當本地價格下降時，本地生產商的利潤誘因減少，本地生產量（供應量）會下降（沿著供應曲線向左下移動）。
    According to the law of supply, when the domestic price falls, the profit incentive for domestic producers is reduced, so the quantity produced domestically (quantity supplied) will decrease (moving down and to the left along the supply curve).

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

這類題目的「破題關鍵 (Pivot Point)」在於明白「本地價格的組成」。一旦你寫下方程式 \(\text{Price}_{domestic} = P_w + t\)，這題就變成了非常基礎的供需圖形分析。

The "Pivot Point" of this type of question lies in understanding the "composition of the domestic price". Once you write down the equation \(\text{Price}_{domestic} = P_w + t\), the question simply becomes basic supply and demand graphical analysis.

  1. 找出衝擊 (Identify the shock): \(P_w\) 變小。
  2. 找出直接影響 (Identify the direct effect): \(P_w + t\) 整體變小（價格線下移）。
  3. 推導結果 (Derive the results): 價格低了，消費者買多些 (\(Q_d \uparrow\))，生產者造少些 (\(Q_s \downarrow\))。

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱：誤以為「關稅不變」代表「關稅收入不變」。

題目指出「徵收了從量關稅」，代表每件進口物品的稅率/額 (\(t\)) 是固定的。但關稅收入 (Revenue) 是 \(t \times \text{進口數量}\)。許多同學忘記了當世界價格改變時，進口數量會發生巨大改變，從而導致總關稅收入改變。

Trap: Mistaking "unchanged tariff" for "unchanged tariff revenue".
The question states a "per unit tariff" is imposed, meaning the tax amount per item (\(t\)) is fixed. But the tariff revenue is \(t \times \text{Import Quantity}\). Many students forget that when the world price changes, the import quantity changes dramatically, thereby altering the total tariff revenue.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 如果題目改為「從價關稅 (Ad Valorem Tariff)」，答案會改變嗎？

A: 答案 D 仍然正確！從價關稅是以百分比計算（例如 10%）。本地價格公式會變成 \(P_w \times (1 + \text{稅率})\)。如果 \(P_w\) 下降，本地價格依然會下降，導致本地生產量減少。不過，關稅收入 (Option A) 的變化就會變得不確定 (Ambiguous)，因為即使進口數量上升，但每件物品收取的稅款 (\(P_w \times \text{稅率}\)) 卻減少了，這取決於供需的價格彈性。

Q: Would the answer change if it was an "Ad Valorem Tariff" instead?

A: Option D would still be correct! An ad valorem tariff is a percentage (e.g., 10%). The domestic price becomes \(P_w \times (1 + \text{tax rate})\). If \(P_w\) drops, the domestic price still drops, leading to a decrease in domestic production. However, the change in tariff revenue (Option A) would become ambiguous. Even though import quantity rises, the tax collected per item (\(P_w \times \text{tax rate}\)) falls, so the net effect depends on price elasticities of supply and demand.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

「畫線法」秒殺技巧 (The "Line Shift" Shortcut):

在考場上，不要空想！直接在試卷的圖上，於原本的虛線 \(P_w + t\) 下方畫一條新的水平線。觀察這條新線與 D 曲線和 S 曲線的交點：
1. 與 S 的交點向左移了 ➔ 生產量跌 (選 D)。
2. 與 D 的交點向右移了 ➔ 消費量升。

Shortcut: In the exam room, don't just think—draw! Directly on the question paper, draw a new horizontal line below the original dashed line \(P_w + t\). Look at the new intersections with the D and S curves:
1. Intersection with S moved left ➔ Production drops (Select D).
2. Intersection with D moved right ➔ Consumption rises.
