Topic: Demand & Supply / Elasticity (需求與供應 / 彈性) Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
Key Concepts: Price Elasticity of Demand (需求價格彈性), Supply Shift (供應移動), Determinants of Elasticity (彈性的決定因素)

2. The Question (題目)

半導體晶片是生產智能電話的關鍵組件。由於全球半導體晶片短缺，智能電話的銷售量有10%的變動，而智能電話的價格則上升了20%。下列哪項最能解釋上述智能電話價格與銷售量的轉變？

A. 智能電話沒有接近的替代品。
B. 智能電話生產商有剩餘的產能。
C. 消費者在智能電話的開支佔其總開支的比例高。
D. 生產智能電話的工人不容易招聘。

Semiconductor chips are key components for producing smartphones. Due to a global shortage of semiconductor chips, the quantity of smartphones sold has changed by 10% whereas the price of smartphones has increased by 20%. Which of the following best explains the above changes in the price and quantity sold of smartphones?

A. Smartphones have no close substitutes.
B. Smartphone manufacturers have excess capacity in production.
C. Consumers expenditure on smartphones takes up a large proportion of their total expenditure.
D. The workers for producing smartphones cannot be easily recruited.

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

需求價格彈性 (Price Elasticity of Demand)

當供應曲線移動時，均衡點會沿著需求曲線移動。我們可以透過價格和交易量的百分比變動來推斷需求彈性。如果價格的百分比變動大於需求量的百分比變動，這代表需求是低彈性的 (Inelastic)。

When the supply curve shifts, the equilibrium point moves along the demand curve. We can deduce the price elasticity of demand by looking at the percentage changes in price and quantity. If the percentage change in price is greater than the percentage change in quantity demanded, the demand is inelastic.

$$ E_d = \frac{\%\Delta Q_d}{\%\Delta P} $$

決定彈性的因素 (Determinants of Elasticity)

  • 替代品的數量 (Availability of substitutes): 替代品越少，需求彈性越低。
    Fewer substitutes mean lower elasticity (more inelastic).
  • 佔收入的比例 (Proportion of income): 比例越低，需求彈性越低。
    Smaller proportion of income means lower elasticity.

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): Option A

Option A (Correct):

根據題目，價格上升了 20% (\(\%\Delta P = +20\%\))，而交易量變動了 10% (\(\%\Delta Q = -10\%\))。由於 \(\%\Delta Q < \%\Delta P\)，需求彈性 \(E_d < 1\)，即需求屬低彈性 (Inelastic)。「沒有接近的替代品」正正是導致需求低彈性的原因。

According to the question, price increased by 20% (\(\%\Delta P = +20\%\)) and quantity changed by 10% (\(\%\Delta Q = -10\%\)). Since \(\%\Delta Q < \%\Delta P\), the elasticity of demand \(E_d < 1\), meaning demand is inelastic. "Having no close substitutes" is exactly the reason for an inelastic demand.

Option B (Incorrect):

「有剩餘的產能」會影響供應彈性 (使其更具彈性)，但本題的數據反映的是沿著需求曲線的移動，我們需要解釋的是需求彈性，而非供應彈性。

"Excess capacity" affects the elasticity of supply (making it more elastic). However, the data in this question reflects a movement along the demand curve. We need to explain the elasticity of demand, not supply.

Option C (Incorrect):

「開支佔總開支比例高」會導致需求屬高彈性 (Elastic, \(E_d > 1\))。這與題目中計算出低彈性 (\(E_d < 1\)) 的結果矛盾。

"Taking up a large proportion of total expenditure" would result in an elastic demand (\(E_d > 1\)). This contradicts our finding from the data that demand is inelastic (\(E_d < 1\)).

Option D (Incorrect):

「工人不容易招聘」會導致供應屬低彈性。同樣地，這解釋了供應彈性，但無法解釋為何沿著需求曲線移動時，交易量跌幅小於價格升幅。

"Workers cannot be easily recruited" leads to an inelastic supply. Again, this explains supply elasticity, but fails to explain why the drop in quantity is smaller than the rise in price along the demand curve.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (關鍵點): 辨別出是「供應移動」還是「需求移動」。

1. 「晶片短缺」導致智能電話的供應減少 (Supply decreases)，供應曲線向左移。
2. 供應向左移，會沿著需求曲線 (Demand curve) 向上移動。
3. 題目給出 \(\%\Delta P = 20\%\) 和 \(\%\Delta Q = 10\%\)。這反映了需求曲線的斜率/彈性特徵。計算得出 \(E_d = 10/20 = 0.5 < 1\) (Inelastic)。
4. 尋找導致「需求低彈性」的選項。

Pivot Point: Identify whether it's a supply shift or demand shift.
1. "Chip shortage" leads to a decrease in supply of smartphones (supply curve shifts left).
2. A leftward shift in supply causes a movement along the demand curve.
3. The question gives \(\%\Delta P = 20\%\) and \(\%\Delta Q = 10\%\). This reflects the elasticity of the demand curve. Calculating \(E_d = 10/20 = 0.5 < 1\) (Inelastic).
4. Look for the option that causes "inelastic demand".

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

最大的陷阱是混淆需求彈性與供應彈性。學生看到「晶片短缺」或「生產商」，很容易直覺選擇與「生產/供應」有關的選項 (如 B 或 D)。但記住：供應移動，反映的是需求彈性 (Supply shifts trace out the demand curve)。

The biggest trap is confusing demand elasticity with supply elasticity. Seeing "chip shortage" or "manufacturers", students intuitively pick options related to "production/supply" (like B or D). But remember: A shift in supply reflects the elasticity of demand.

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 題目只說銷售量有「10%的變動」，我怎麼知道是增加還是減少？
Q: The question only says quantity "changed by 10%". How do I know if it increased or decreased?

A: 因為晶片短缺導致供應減少 (向左移)。在需求不變的情況下，供應減少必然導致價格上升和交易量下降。所以這 10% 必然是減少。
A: Because the chip shortage causes a decrease in supply (leftward shift). Assuming demand is constant, a decrease in supply must lead to a higher price and a lower quantity transacted. Therefore, the 10% must be a decrease.

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法 (Shortcut):
看到 \(\%\Delta P = 20\%\) 大過 \(\%\Delta Q = 10\%\)，立刻在腦海中寫下 "Inelastic Demand" (需求低彈性)。掃視選項：
A = Inelastic Demand (Match!)
B = Elastic Supply (Wrong concept)
C = Elastic Demand (Opposite)
D = Inelastic Supply (Wrong concept)
答案秒殺為 A。

Shortcut:
Seeing \(\%\Delta P = 20\%\) is greater than \(\%\Delta Q = 10\%\), immediately write down "Inelastic Demand". Scan the options:
A = Inelastic Demand (Match!)
B = Elastic Supply (Wrong concept)
C = Elastic Demand (Opposite)
