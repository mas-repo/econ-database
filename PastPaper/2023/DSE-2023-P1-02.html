HKDSE Economics 經濟學

Topic (課題): Opportunity Cost 機會成本

Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆

Key Concepts (關鍵概念): Opportunity Cost (機會成本), Explicit/Implicit Cost (顯性/隱性成本), Cost vs Value (成本與價值)

The Question 題目

志傑是一名香港中六學生，他打算到韓國畢業旅行。以下哪項會令他到韓國畢業旅行的成本下降？

A. 港元兌南韓圜升值。
B. 由韓國飛往香港的回程航班延誤。
C. 韓國的新冠肺炎確診宗數大幅上升。
D. 韓國的酒店服務比他預期的好。

Jacky, a Secondary 6 student in Hong Kong, is going to travel to Korea for his graduation trip. Which of the following will decrease his cost of having the graduation trip to Korea?

A. Hong Kong dollar appreciates against South Korean Won.
B. The return flight from Korea to Hong Kong is delayed.
C. The number of confirmed cases of COVID-19 in Korea has increased drastically.
D. The services of the hotel in Korea are better than his expectation.

Concept Review 概念重溫

Opportunity Cost 機會成本

機會成本是指在面臨選擇時，被放棄的選項中價值最高的那一個。旅行的總成本包括顯性成本（如機票、酒店的金錢支出）和隱性成本（如時間、健康風險）。

Opportunity cost is the highest-valued option forgone. The total cost of a trip includes explicit costs (monetary expenses like flights and hotels) and implicit costs (time, health risks).

$$ \text{Total Cost} = \text{Explicit Cost} + \text{Implicit Cost} $$

Cost vs Value 成本與價值

成本是你為了得到某物品而「放棄」的東西；價值是你從該物品中「獲得」的好處。所選擇物品本身的質素改變，只會影響其價值，而不會影響其成本。

Cost is what you "give up" to get something; Value is the benefit you "gain" from it. Changes in the quality of the chosen option itself only affect its value, not its cost.

Answer & Detailed Analysis 答案與詳細分析

Correct Option 正確答案: Option A

Option A (正確 Correct): 港元升值意味著志傑可以用較少的港元兌換相同的南韓圜。這直接減少了他在韓國消費的實際金錢支出（顯性成本下降），因此總成本下降。

An appreciation of the HKD means Jacky needs fewer Hong Kong dollars to exchange for the same amount of Korean Won. This directly reduces his actual monetary expenditure (explicit cost decreases), thus lowering the total cost.

Option B (錯誤 Incorrect): 航班延誤會浪費志傑的時間。時間是有機會成本的（例如他本可以利用這些時間休息或工作），因此這增加了他的隱性成本，令總成本上升。

A flight delay wastes Jacky's time. Time has an opportunity cost (e.g., he could have used the time to rest or work), so this increases his implicit cost, making the total cost higher.

Option C (錯誤 Incorrect): 確診宗數上升增加了他在旅程中感染疾病的風險。承擔更高的健康風險屬於隱性成本的增加，因此總成本上升。

An increase in confirmed cases increases his risk of getting sick during the trip. Bearing a higher health risk is an increase in implicit cost, so the total cost rises.

Option D (錯誤 Incorrect): 酒店服務比預期好，這增加了志傑從旅程中獲得的「享受」或「價值」（Value），但他為了這次旅行所放棄的東西（金錢、時間）並沒有改變。因此，這不影響成本。

Hotel services being better than expected increases the "enjoyment" or "value" Jacky gets from the trip, but what he gave up for this trip (money, time) remains unchanged. Therefore, it does not affect the cost.

Problem Decomposition 題目拆解

解答這類題目的關鍵（Pivot Point）在於區分「成本（Cost）」和「價值（Value）」。當題目問及「成本」的變動時，你必須尋找影響「金錢支出」、「時間消耗」或「風險」的選項。

The Pivot Point to solving this type of question is distinguishing between "Cost" and "Value". When asked about changes in "Cost", you must look for options that affect "monetary expenditure", "time consumption", or "risk".

Common Mistakes & Traps 常犯錯誤與陷阱

陷阱：混淆「價值」與「成本」 (Confusing Value and Cost)

很多學生會誤選 D。他們認為「物超所值」就等於「成本下降」。在經濟學中，如果你花 \( \$1000 \) 買了一晚酒店，你的成本就是那 \( \$1000 \)（以及你放棄的其他選擇）。無論那晚酒店的服務是極好還是極差，你的成本依然是 \( \$1000 \)。服務變好只代表你獲得的價值上升了。

Many students mistakenly choose D. They think "getting more than what you paid for" means "cost decreases". In economics, if you spend \( \$1000 \) on a hotel night, your cost is that \( \$1000 \) (plus other forgone alternatives). Whether the service is excellent or terrible, your cost remains \( \$1000 \). Better service only means the value you received has increased.

Student Q&A 學生常見疑問

Q: 匯率變動真的算作成本變動嗎？

A: 是的。因為志傑的收入/資金是以港元（HKD）計算的。當港元升值，他購買相同的韓國商品所需的港元減少了。這意味著他放棄的港元（即其他消費機會）減少了，因此顯性成本下降。

Q: Do exchange rate changes really count as a change in cost?
A: Yes. Since Jacky's funds are in HKD, when the HKD appreciates, he needs fewer HKD to buy the same Korean goods. This means he gives up fewer HKD (i.e., fewer alternative consumption opportunities), so the explicit cost decreases.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts 考試技巧與速解

「秒殺」技巧 (Shortcut): 看到問「成本（Cost）」的題目，立刻掃描選項中描述「產品質素」、「服務態度」、「個人感受」的字眼（如選項 D 的「服務比預期好」）。這些通常是改變「價值（Value）」的干擾項，可以直接剔除！

Shortcut: When you see a question asking about "Cost", immediately scan the options for words describing "product quality", "service attitude", or "personal feelings" (like "services are better" in Option D). These are usually distractors that change "Value" and can be eliminated immediately!
