📚 課題 (Topic): 宏觀經濟學 - 公共財政與香港稅制 (Macroeconomics - Public Finance & Taxation in HK)

⭐ 難度 (Difficulty): ★★☆☆☆ (2/5)

🔑 關鍵字 (Key Concepts): 物業稅 (Property Tax), 差餉 (Rates), 印花稅 (Stamp Duty), 資本增值稅 (Capital Gains Tax)

題目 (The Question)

博文住在他於灣仔新購置的物業。他在香港的股票市場進行股票交易賺得 \(\$100,000\)。
根據以上資料，博文要向香港政府支付 __________。

(1) 資本增值稅
(2) 物業稅
(3) 差餉
(4) 印花稅

A. 只有 (1)、(3)
B. 只有 (2)、(4)
C. 只有 (3)、(4)
D. 只有 (1)、(2)、(3)

Bobby lives in his newly bought property in Wan Chai. He gains \(\$100,000\) from trading shares in the stock market of Hong Kong.
Based on the above information, Bobby needs to pay __________ to the Hong Kong Government.

(1) capital gains tax
(2) property tax
(3) rates
(4) stamp duty

A. (1) and (3) only
B. (2) and (4) only
C. (3) and (4) only
D. (1), (2) and (3) only

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

香港的主要稅項 (Major Taxes in Hong Kong)

要解答這道題目，我們必須清楚了解香港特定稅種的徵收對象與條件：

To answer this question, we must clearly understand the targets and conditions of specific taxes in Hong Kong:
  • 物業稅 (Property Tax): 只向在香港出租物業並賺取租金收入的業主徵收。如果業主自住或物業空置，則無需繳交物業稅。
    Levied only on owners who rent out their properties and earn rental income in Hong Kong. If the owner occupies the property or it is vacant, no property tax is payable.
  • 差餉 (Rates): 對物業的使用權徵收的間接稅，通常由物業的佔用人（業主或租客）繳交。
    An indirect tax levied on the occupation of property, usually paid by the occupier (owner or tenant).
  • 印花稅 (Stamp Duty): 對特定文件徵收的稅項，主要涉及不動產（物業）轉讓及香港股票交易。
    A tax levied on specific documents, mainly involving the transfer of immovable property (real estate) and Hong Kong stock transactions.
  • 資本增值稅 (Capital Gains Tax): 對出售資產（如股票、物業）所賺取的利潤徵稅。香港並沒有設立資本增值稅。
    A tax on the profit earned from selling assets (e.g., stocks, properties). Hong Kong does NOT have a capital gains tax.

答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): C

不適用 (1) 資本增值稅 (capital gains tax)

博文雖然在股票市場賺了 \(\$100,000\)，但香港的稅制中沒有資本增值稅。因此，他不需要為這筆股票利潤繳交資本增值稅。

Although Bobby gained \(\$100,000\) in the stock market, Hong Kong's tax system does not have a capital gains tax. Therefore, he does not need to pay capital gains tax on this stock profit.

不適用 (2) 物業稅 (property tax)

題目指出博文「住在他於灣仔新購置的物業」(lives in his newly bought property)。因為他是自住，並沒有將物業出租賺取租金，所以無需繳交物業稅。

The question states that Bobby "lives in his newly bought property". Because it is owner-occupied and not rented out for rental income, he does not need to pay property tax.

適用 (3) 差餉 (rates)

只要是物業的佔用人（博文住在該物業內），就需要向政府繳交差餉。

As long as one is the occupier of a property (Bobby lives in the property), they are required to pay rates to the government.

適用 (4) 印花稅 (stamp duty)

博文有兩項活動涉及印花稅：第一，他「新購置」物業（買賣樓宇需要繳交印花稅）；第二，他進行「股票交易」（買賣香港股票同樣需要繳交印花稅）。

Bobby has two activities involving stamp duty: First, he "newly bought" a property (buying/selling real estate requires stamp duty); Second, he engaged in "trading shares" (buying/selling HK stocks also requires stamp duty).

總結：只有 (3) 和 (4) 是博文需要支付的稅項，因此答案是 C。

Conclusion: Only (3) and (4) are taxes Bobby needs to pay, so the answer is C.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解答這類情境題，我們需要逐句拆解題目提供的線索（破題點）：

To answer this type of scenario question, we need to break down the clues (pivot points) provided sentence by sentence:
  • 線索一：「住在他...的物業」("lives in his... property") ➔ 關鍵字是「住」(lives)。這排除了物業稅 (Property Tax)，因為沒有租金收入。同時確認了他需要交差餉 (Rates)。
  • 線索二：「新購置」("newly bought") ➔ 買樓的動作會觸發印花稅 (Stamp Duty)。
  • 線索三：「股票交易賺得 \(\$100,000\)」("gains \(\$100,000\) from trading shares") ➔ 股票交易會觸發印花稅。至於賺得的利潤，由於香港沒有資本增值稅，這是一個干擾項 (Distractor)。

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

❌ 混淆「物業稅」與「差餉」： 很多學生以為只要擁有物業就要交「物業稅」。這是不正確的！在香港，擁有物業但自住，只需交差餉（及地租）；只有把物業租出去收租，才需要交物業稅。
❌ 以為所有利潤都要交稅： 看到「賺得 \(\$100,000\)」就直覺認為要交資本增值稅。必須牢記香港的稅制特色：無資本增值稅、無銷售稅/消費稅、無遺產稅。

❌ Confusing "Property Tax" with "Rates": Many students think owning a property automatically means paying "Property Tax". This is incorrect! In HK, owning and living in a property only requires paying Rates (and Government Rent); Property Tax is only for rental income.
❌ Assuming all profits are taxed: Seeing "gains \(\$100,000\)" makes students instinctively think of Capital Gains Tax. You must memorize HK's tax features: NO capital gains tax, NO sales/consumption tax, NO estate duty.

學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 如果博文把灣仔的物業租給別人，自己去租另一間屋住，答案會有什麼不同？

A: 如果他把物業出租，他就會獲得租金收入，這時他就必須繳交(2) 物業稅。至於差餉，通常可以由業主或租客繳交（視乎租約規定），但法例上該物業仍需繳交差餉。印花稅依然適用（買樓及買股票）。

Q: If Bobby rented out his Wan Chai property to someone else and rented another house to live in himself, how would the answer change?

A: If he rented out the property, he would earn rental income, which means he MUST pay (2) property tax. As for rates, it can usually be paid by the owner or tenant (depending on the lease), but legally the property is still subject to rates. Stamp duty still applies (for buying the property and stocks).

考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

💡 秒殺技巧：絕對排除法 (Elimination Method)

在 DSE 經濟科中，關於香港稅制的題目，只要看到 (1) 資本增值稅 (Capital gains tax) 或 銷售稅 (Sales tax)，你可以立刻判定它為錯誤，因為香港根本沒有這些稅！

1. 排除 (1) ➔ 選項 A 和 D 立刻被淘汰。
2. 剩下 B (2,4) 和 C (3,4)。
3. 看到「自住」(lives in) ➔ 排除 (2) 物業稅。
4. 選項 B 被淘汰，答案必定是 C！連印花稅和差餉都不用詳細分析就能得分。

In DSE Economics questions about HK taxation, whenever you see (1) Capital gains tax or Sales tax, you can immediately mark it as incorrect because HK simply doesn't have them!

1. Eliminate (1) ➔ Options A and D are instantly out.
2. Left with B (2,4) and C (3,4).
3. See "lives in" ➔ Eliminate (2) Property tax.
4. Option B is out, the answer MUST be C! You get the mark without even analyzing rates and stamp duty deeply.
