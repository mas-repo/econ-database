HKDSE Economics Tutor Guide

主題 (Topic): 市場干預 - 價格下限 (Market Intervention - Price Floor)

難度 (Difficulty): ★★★☆☆ (陷阱題 Trick Question!)

關鍵字 (Key Concepts): 價格下限 Price Floor 無效干預 Ineffective Intervention 市場均衡 Market Equilibrium

1. 題目 (The Question)

下圖顯示某物品的供需曲線。假設政府將價格下限由 \(P_1\) 改為 \(P_2\)。下列哪項／哪些陳述是正確的？

The following diagram shows the supply and demand curves of a good. Suppose the government changes the price floor from \(P_1\) to \(P_2\). Which of the following statements is/are correct?

(1) 該物品的最後一個單位的邊際利益會下降。

(1) The marginal benefit of the last unit of the good will decrease.

(2) 該物品的超額需求會上升。

(2) There will be an increase in the excess demand for the good.

(3) 消費者對該物品的總支出會下降。

(3) Consumers’ total expenditure on the good will decrease.

(4) 該物品的交易量會維持不變。

(4) The quantity transacted of the good will remain unchanged.

A. 只有 (4)    A. (4) only

B. 只有 (1)、(4)    B. (1) and (4) only

C. 只有 (2)、(3)    C. (2) and (3) only

D. 只有 (1)、(2)、(3)    D. (1), (2) and (3) only

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

價格下限 (Price Floor)

價格下限是政府法定的最低交易價格。賣方不能以低於此價格出售物品。

A price floor is a legally established minimum price set by the government. Sellers cannot sell the good below this price.

有效與無效 (Effective vs. Ineffective)

有效的價格下限 (Effective Price Floor): 必須設定在均衡價格之上。這會導致價格上升、交易量下降，並出現超額供應 (Excess Supply)。

Effective Price Floor: Must be set above the equilibrium price. This leads to a higher price, lower quantity transacted, and excess supply.

無效的價格下限 (Ineffective/Non-binding Price Floor): 如果設定在均衡價格之下，市場價格本來就已經高於這個法定下限，因此該政策不會對市場產生任何影響。市場將維持在原有的均衡價格和數量。

Ineffective Price Floor: If set below the equilibrium price, the market price is already higher than the legal minimum. Therefore, the policy has no effect on the market. The market will remain at the original equilibrium price and quantity.

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): A

圖表分析 (Graph Analysis):
觀察圖表，供給曲線 (S) 和需求曲線 (D) 的相交點代表均衡價格 (\(P_e\))。圖中顯示的 \(P_1\) 和 \(P_2\) 兩條線，都位於均衡點的下方。因此，無論是 \(P_1\) 還是 \(P_2\)，作為價格下限都是無效的 (Ineffective)。

Graph Analysis:
Observing the diagram, the intersection of the supply curve (S) and demand curve (D) represents the equilibrium price (\(P_e\)). Both lines \(P_1\) and \(P_2\) shown in the diagram are located below the equilibrium point. Therefore, whether it is \(P_1\) or \(P_2\), as a price floor, it is ineffective (non-binding).

❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect) 陳述 (1): 該物品的最後一個單位的邊際利益會下降。
由於價格下限無效，市場交易量維持在均衡數量 (\(Q_e\)) 不變。邊際利益 (Marginal Benefit) 取決於交易量（反映在需求曲線上）。既然交易量不變，邊際利益也維持不變。

Statement (1): The marginal benefit of the last unit of the good will decrease.
Since the price floor is ineffective, the market quantity transacted remains unchanged at the equilibrium quantity (\(Q_e\)). Marginal benefit depends on the quantity transacted (reflected on the demand curve). Since the quantity is unchanged, the marginal benefit remains unchanged.

❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect) 陳述 (2): 該物品的超額需求會上升。
因為價格下限無效，市場價格會自然調整至均衡價格 (\(P_e\))，此時需求量等於供應量。市場上不會出現超額需求 (Excess Demand) 或超額供應。即使法例修改，市場依然在均衡狀態。

Statement (2): There will be an increase in the excess demand for the good.
Because the price floor is ineffective, the market price will naturally adjust to the equilibrium price (\(P_e\)), where quantity demanded equals quantity supplied. There will be no excess demand or excess supply in the market. Even with the legal change, the market remains in equilibrium.

❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect) 陳述 (3): 消費者對該物品的總支出會下降。
總支出 (Total Expenditure) = 價格 \(\times\) 交易量 (\(P \times Q\))。由於市場價格和交易量都維持在均衡水平不變，總支出亦維持不變。

Statement (3): Consumers’ total expenditure on the good will decrease.
Total Expenditure = Price \(\times\) Quantity Transacted (\(P \times Q\)). Since both market price and quantity transacted remain unchanged at the equilibrium level, total expenditure also remains unchanged.

✅ 正確 (Correct) 陳述 (4): 該物品的交易量會維持不變。
正如上述分析，無效的價格干預不會改變市場的實際運作，交易量維持在原來的均衡數量。

Statement (4): The quantity transacted of the good will remain unchanged.
As analyzed above, an ineffective price intervention does not change the actual operation of the market. The quantity transacted remains at the original equilibrium quantity.

4. 題目拆解與考試技巧 (Problem Decomposition & Exam Tricks)

破題關鍵 (The Pivot Point):
做任何市場干預（價格上限/下限、配額）的題目時，第一步永遠是找出「均衡點」(Equilibrium Point)。然後，將政府設定的價格/數量與均衡點進行比較。
這題的陷阱在於考驗同學是否盲目背誦「價格下限下降會導致...」的結果，而忽略了前提：該政策是否有效？

The Pivot Point:
When doing any market intervention question (price ceiling/floor, quota), the first step is ALWAYS to locate the "Equilibrium Point". Then, compare the government-set price/quantity with the equilibrium point.
The trap in this question tests whether students blindly memorize the results of "a decrease in price floor leads to..." while ignoring the prerequisite: is the policy effective in the first place?

速解技巧 (Shortcut):
記住口訣：「下限在下無效，上限在上無效」。
Price Floor (下限) 設在均衡點之下 \(\rightarrow\) 無效 (No effect)。
Price Ceiling (上限) 設在均衡點之上 \(\rightarrow\) 無效 (No effect)。
一看到無效，所有關於價格、數量、總支出改變的選項都可以直接剔除！

Shortcut:
Remember the rule: "Floor below is ineffective, Ceiling above is ineffective".
Price Floor set below equilibrium \(\rightarrow\) No effect.
Price Ceiling set above equilibrium \(\rightarrow\) No effect.
Once you see it's ineffective, you can immediately eliminate all options claiming a change in price, quantity, or total expenditure!

5. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 如果題目將「價格下限 (Price Floor)」改成「價格上限 (Price Ceiling)」，答案會不同嗎？

Q: What if the question changed "Price Floor" to "Price Ceiling"? Would the answer be different?

A: 會完全不同！如果 \(P_1\) 和 \(P_2\) 是價格上限 (Price Ceiling)，因為它們低於均衡價格，所以兩者都會是有效的。將價格上限由 \(P_1\) 降至 \(P_2\)，會導致價格下降、交易量減少（邊際利益上升）、超額需求 (Excess Demand) 擴大。那樣的話，這題的分析方向就會完全反轉。

A: It would be completely different! If \(P_1\) and \(P_2\) were price ceilings, because they are below the equilibrium price, both would be effective. Lowering the price ceiling from \(P_1\) to \(P_2\) would lead to a lower price, a decrease in quantity transacted (marginal benefit increases), and an expansion of excess demand. In that case, the analysis would be entirely reversed.
