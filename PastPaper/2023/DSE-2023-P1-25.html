Topic: National Income Accounting 本地生產總值量度 Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
Key Concepts: GDP at factor cost (按要素成本計算的本地生產總值), Expenditure Approach (開支計算法), Value-added Approach (增值計算法)

📝 The Question (題目)

下圖顯示某經濟中智能電話的生產鏈。若以上生產鏈對該經濟的本地生產總值（以要素成本計算）的貢獻是 $560，X的數值是 _________。

The following diagram shows a production chain of smartphones in an economy. If the contribution of the above production chain to the economy’s GDP at factor cost is $560, the value of X is _____________.

A. 40
B. 80
C. 140
D. 180

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這類生產鏈題目，我們可以使用開支計算法 (Expenditure Approach)。開支計算法透過計算所有最終物品及服務的總開支來量度 GDP。

To solve this type of production chain question, we can use the Expenditure Approach. This approach measures GDP by calculating the total expenditure on all final goods and services.

$$GDP_{mp} = C + I + G + X_{exports} - M_{imports}$$

題目要求的是按要素成本計算的本地生產總值 (GDP at factor cost)，因此我們需要從市價 (Market Price) 中扣除間接稅 (Indirect Taxes) 並加上津貼 (Subsidies)。

The question asks for the GDP at factor cost, so we must deduct indirect taxes and add subsidies to the Market Price.

$$GDP_{fc} = GDP_{mp} - \text{Indirect Taxes} + \text{Subsidies}$$

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: C

第一步：辨別最終開支 (Identify final expenditures)
- 本地消費者開支 (Consumption, $C$) = $640
- 外地消費者開支 (Exports, $X_{exports}$) = $100
- 進口智能電話 (Imports, $M_{imports}$) = $X

Step 1: Identify final expenditures
- Local consumer expenditure ($C$) = $640
- Foreign consumer expenditure ($X_{exports}$) = $100
- Imported smartphones ($M_{imports}$) = $X

第二步：設立等式 (Set up the equation)
根據公式，將數值代入：

Step 2: Set up the equation
Substitute the values into the formula:

$$GDP_{fc} = C + X_{exports} - M_{imports} - \text{Indirect Taxes}$$ $$560 = 640 + 100 - X - 40$$ $$560 = 700 - X$$ $$X = 140$$

Distractor Analysis (選項分析):

  • A. 40 (錯誤): 考生忘記加上出口 (Exports $100)。若錯誤算式為 $640 - X - 40 = 560$，得出 $X = 40$。
    Incorrect. The student forgot to add exports ($100). The wrong equation $640 - X - 40 = 560$ leads to $X = 40$.
  • B. 80 (錯誤): 考生同時忘記加上出口 ($100) 及忘記扣除銷售稅 ($40)。錯誤算式為 $640 - X = 560$，得出 $X = 80$。
    Incorrect. The student forgot both exports ($100) and deducting the sales tax ($40). The wrong equation $640 - X = 560$ leads to $X = 80$.
  • D. 180 (錯誤): 考生忘記將市價轉為要素成本（忘記扣減 $40 銷售稅）。錯誤算式為 $640 + 100 - X = 560$，得出 $X = 180$。
    Incorrect. The student forgot to convert market price to factor cost (forgot to deduct $40 tax). The wrong equation $640 + 100 - X = 560$ leads to $X = 180$.

🧩 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

關鍵支點 (Pivot Point)： 本題的破題關鍵在於理解哪些箭頭代表最終需求。流向「本地消費者」($640) 和「海外消費者」($100) 的是最終物品的價值，而流向零售商的 ($460) 只是中間物品 (Intermediate goods)，在開支計算法中不應重複計算。

Pivot Point: The key to cracking this question is understanding which arrows represent final demand. Flows to "Local consumers" ($640) and "Foreign consumers" ($100) are values of final goods, whereas the flow to retailers ($460) is an intermediate good and should not be double-counted in the expenditure approach.

🙋‍♂️ Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q1: 為什麼我們不把「本地原料 ($60)」計算在內？
A: 在開支計算法中，我們只看「最終物品」。這 $60 的原料價值已經隱含在最終賣給消費者的 $640 及外國消費者的 $100 之中。如果再加 $60，就會犯了「重複計算 (Double Counting)」的錯誤！

Q1: Why didn't we include the "Local materials ($60)" in our calculation?
A: In the expenditure approach, we only look at "final goods". The $60 value of materials is already embedded in the final $640 sold to local consumers and $100 to foreign consumers. Adding it again would cause a "Double Counting" error!


Q2: 我可以用「增值計算法 (Value-Added Approach)」來算嗎？
A: 絕對可以！
原料增值 = $60
製造商增值 = $(100 + 460) - 60 = 500$
零售商增值 (以市價計) = $640 - 460 - X = 180 - X$
零售商增值 (以要素成本計) = $(180 - X) - 40 = 140 - X$
總 GDP (要素成本) = $60 + 500 + 140 - X = 700 - X = 560$
得出 $X = 140$。兩種方法答案一致！

Q2: Can I use the Value-Added Approach to calculate this?
A: Absolutely!
VA of materials = $60
VA of manufacturers = $(100 + 460) - 60 = 500$
VA of retailers (Market Price) = $640 - 460 - X = 180 - X$
VA of retailers (Factor Cost) = $(180 - X) - 40 = 140 - X$
Total GDP (Factor Cost) = $60 + 500 + 140 - X = 700 - X = 560$
Thus $X = 140$. Both methods yield the same answer!

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

畫圈法 (Circling Method): 在考試時，直接在圖表上圈出「流出這個經濟體系」的最終箭頭 (即 C 和 X)，並打叉「流入這個體系」的箭頭 (即 M)。
一眼看出：總產值 = $640 + 100$。總扣除 = $X$ (進口) $+ 40$ (稅)。
$740 - X - 40 = 560 \rightarrow X = 140$。30秒內即可解題！

Circling Method: During the exam, circle the final arrows "flowing out of the economy" (C and X) and cross out arrows "flowing into the economy" (M).
At a glance: Total value = $640 + 100$. Total deductions = $X$ (imports) $+ 40$ (taxes).
$740 - X - 40 = 560 \rightarrow X = 140$. Solved in under 30 seconds!
