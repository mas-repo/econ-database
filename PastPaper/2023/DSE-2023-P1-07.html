HKDSE Economics Tutorial 經濟學導修

Topic (課題): Labour Market - Labour Mobility (勞工市場 - 勞工流動性)

Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆

Key Concepts (核心概念): Occupational Mobility 職業流動性 Geographical Mobility 地域流動性 Specialisation 專門化

1. The Question (題目)

若 __________ ，該經濟中勞工的職業流動性會下降。

A. 居於偏遠地區的工人獲發交通津貼
B. 更多工作需要專門化的技能
C. 法定退休年齡由65歲提高至70歲
D. 政府提供更多免費的再培訓課程

The occupational mobility of labour of an economy will decrease if __________ .

A. transport subsidies are provided to workers who live in remote districts.
B. more jobs require specialised skills.
C. the statutory retirement age is raised from 65 to 70.
D. more job retraining courses are provided by the government free of charge.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

勞工流動性 (Labour Mobility)

勞工流動性是指勞工改變其工作地點或職業的容易程度。主要分為兩類：

  • 職業流動性 (Occupational Mobility): 勞工由一種行業/職位轉換到另一種行業/職位的容易程度。影響因素包括：技能要求、培訓成本、專業資格限制等。
  • 地域流動性 (Geographical Mobility): 勞工由一個工作地點轉換到另一個工作地點的容易程度。影響因素包括：交通網絡、交通成本、住屋成本、家庭因素等。

Labour mobility refers to the ease with which workers can change their job location or occupation. It is divided into two main types:

  • Occupational Mobility: The ease of moving from one occupation/industry to another. Factors include skill requirements, training costs, and professional licensing.
  • Geographical Mobility: The ease of moving from one workplace location to another. Factors include transport networks, transport costs, housing costs, and family ties.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option B

Option A: 錯誤。提供交通津貼會降低勞工跨區工作的交通成本，這會提高地域流動性 (Geographical Mobility)，而不是降低職業流動性。

Incorrect. Providing transport subsidies lowers the transport cost for cross-district work, which increases geographical mobility, rather than decreasing occupational mobility.

Option B: 正確。當更多工作需要專門化的技能時，勞工轉換職業的門檻變高。他們需要花費更多的時間和金錢去獲取新技能，因此職業流動性會下降。

Correct. When more jobs require specialised skills, the barrier to switching occupations becomes higher. Workers need to spend more time and money to acquire new skills, hence occupational mobility decreases.

Option C: 錯誤。提高法定退休年齡會增加勞動人口 (Labour Force) 的數量，即勞工供應增加，但這與勞工轉換職業的難易程度沒有直接關係。

Incorrect. Raising the statutory retirement age increases the size of the labour force (labour supply), but it has no direct relationship with the ease of switching occupations.

Option D: 錯誤。政府提供免費再培訓課程，降低了勞工學習新技能的成本，這會提高職業流動性，與題目要求的「下降」相反。

Incorrect. Providing free retraining courses lowers the cost of acquiring new skills, which increases occupational mobility. This is the exact opposite of what the question asks.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (解題關鍵): 題目有兩個關鍵詞：「職業流動性」(Occupational) 及「下降」(Decrease)。

  • 看到「職業」，就要排除與「地點/交通」相關的選項 (排除 A)。
  • 看到「下降」，就要尋找會增加轉行難度或成本的因素 (排除 D，因為 D 會降低成本，使流動性上升)。
  • 選項 C 屬於勞工供應量 (Quantity of Labour) 的範疇，屬於無關資訊 (Irrelevant info)。

Pivot Point: The question has two keywords: "Occupational" and "decrease".

  • "Occupational" tells us to eliminate options related to location/transport (Eliminate A).
  • "Decrease" tells us to look for factors that increase the difficulty or cost of changing jobs (Eliminate D, as it lowers costs and increases mobility).
  • Option C relates to the quantity of labour supply, which is irrelevant information here.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：混淆「職業流動性」與「地域流動性」

很多學生看到「流動性」就急於作答，沒有看清楚是 Occupational 還是 Geographical。考評局經常將這兩者的影響因素混合在選項中作為干擾項 (Distractors)。例如選項 A 就是標準的地域流動性干擾項。

Trap: Confusing Occupational and Geographical Mobility

Many students rush to answer when they see "mobility" without checking if it's Occupational or Geographical. The HKEAA frequently mixes factors affecting both types in the options as distractors. Option A is a classic geographical mobility distractor.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼選項 C (提高退休年齡) 完全不影響流動性？難道工作時間長了，轉行的機會不是更多嗎？

A: 在經濟學的嚴格定義中，流動性 (Mobility) 衡量的是轉換工作的「容易程度 (Ease)」或「成本 (Cost)」，而不是「次數」。提高退休年齡只是延長了勞工留在勞動市場的總時間，並沒有降低他們轉換職業時所面臨的技能門檻或培訓成本。因此，它不直接影響職業流動性。

Q: Why doesn't Option C (raising retirement age) affect mobility at all? Doesn't a longer working life give people more chances to change careers?

A: In strict economic terms, mobility measures the "ease" or "cost" of changing jobs, not the "frequency". Raising the retirement age simply extends the total time a worker remains in the labour market; it does not lower the skill barriers or training costs they face when trying to switch careers. Therefore, it does not directly affect occupational mobility.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

對立法 (Method of Opposites):

當題目問「下降」時，快速掃描選項，找出明顯會導致「上升」的選項並劃掉。選項 D (免費再培訓) 顯然會讓轉行變容易 (流動性上升)，立刻排除。選項 A (交通津貼) 也是讓移動變容易的正面因素。剩下 B 和 C，B 提到「專門化技能」(增加難度)，自然就是正確答案。

Method of Opposites:

When the question asks for a "decrease", quickly scan the options for things that obviously cause an "increase" and cross them out. Option D (free retraining) clearly makes switching easier (increases mobility), so eliminate it immediately. Option A (subsidies) is also a positive factor making movement easier. Between B and C, B mentions "specialised skills" (which adds difficulty), making it the natural correct answer.
