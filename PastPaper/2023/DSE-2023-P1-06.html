HKDSE Economics: Labour Market Analysis

Topic: 勞工市場 (Labour Market) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Keys: 勞力供應, 勞工生產力

📝 題目 (The Question)

下列哪項會同時提高某經濟的勞力供應和平均勞工生產力？

Which of the following would raise both the labour supply and average labour productivity of an economy?

  1. 政府提高失業救濟金額。
    The government raises the amount of unemployment benefits.
  2. 免費教育延伸至大學課程。
    Free education is extended to university programmes.
  3. 更多廠商安裝先進的生產設備。
    More firms install advanced production equipment.
  4. 一項為高技術人才而設的入境計劃的配額增加。
    The quota of an immigration scheme of highly skilled individuals is increased.

🧠 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

1. 勞力供應 (Labour Supply)
勞力供應是指在特定時間內，勞工願意並能夠提供的總工作時數。它受人口規模、勞動參與率及工作時數影響。

Labour supply refers to the total number of working hours that workers are willing and able to provide in a given period. It is affected by population size, labour force participation rate, and working hours.

$$ \text{Labour Supply} = \text{Number of Workers} \times \text{Working Hours per Worker} $$

2. 平均勞工生產力 (Average Labour Productivity)
平均勞工生產力是指每單位勞力（如每名工人或每工時）所生產的產出。它受人力資本（教育、技術）、資本設備及科技影響。

Average labour productivity refers to the output produced per unit of labour (e.g., per worker or per man-hour). It is affected by human capital (education, skills), capital equipment, and technology.

$$ \text{Average Labour Productivity} = \frac{\text{Total Output}}{\text{Total Labour Input}} $$

✅ 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): D

選項 A (Option A) 錯誤 Wrong

提高失業救濟金會降低失業者尋找工作的意欲，導致勞動參與率下降，從而減少勞力供應。這對平均生產力沒有直接的正面影響。

Raising unemployment benefits reduces the incentive for the unemployed to seek jobs, leading to a lower labour force participation rate and thus decreasing labour supply. It has no direct positive effect on average productivity.

選項 B (Option B) 錯誤 Wrong

將免費教育延伸至大學會鼓勵更多年輕人留在學校，延遲投入勞動市場。因此，在短期內勞力供應會減少（儘管長遠而言會提高生產力）。

Extending free education to universities encourages more young people to stay in school, delaying their entry into the labour market. Therefore, labour supply will decrease in the short run (even though it increases future productivity).

選項 C (Option C) 錯誤 Wrong

安裝先進設備增加了資本 \(K\)，這確實會提高勞工的平均生產力。然而，這並不會改變勞力供應（工人數量或願意工作的時數）。

Installing advanced equipment increases capital \(K\), which indeed raises average labour productivity. However, this does not change the labour supply (number of workers or hours they are willing to work).

選項 D (Option D) 正確 Correct

增加入境配額會引入更多勞工，直接增加勞力供應。同時，因為這些是「高技術人才」，他們的人力資本高於現有平均水平，因此會拉高整體的平均勞工生產力。

Increasing the immigration quota brings in more workers, directly increasing labour supply. Meanwhile, because they are "highly skilled individuals", their human capital is above the current average, which pulls up the overall average labour productivity.

🔍 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題關鍵 (The Pivot Point):
題目要求找出能「同時 (both)」提高兩個變數的選項。這是一個典型的雙重條件題。你必須逐一測試每個選項是否滿足條件一（增加勞力供應）及條件二（增加生產力）。

The question asks for an option that raises "both" variables. This is a typical dual-condition question. You must test each option against Condition 1 (increase labour supply) and Condition 2 (increase productivity).

  • 干擾資訊 (Distractors): 選項 B 和 C 看似是「好」的經濟政策，但它們只滿足了其中一個條件，或者在時間點上不符合（例如選項 B 短期內減少勞力）。
  • Options B and C look like "good" economic policies, but they only satisfy one condition or fail on the timeline (e.g., Option B reduces labour supply in the short run).

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

1. 混淆「勞力供應」與「勞工需求」
很多學生誤以為選項 C（安裝新設備）會增加勞力供應。事實上，廠商安裝設備可能會影響廠商對勞工的「需求 (Demand)」，但不會改變工人提供勞力的「供應 (Supply)」。

Confusing Labour Supply with Labour Demand: Many students mistakenly think Option C (new equipment) increases labour supply. In fact, installing equipment might affect the firms' "Demand" for labour, but it does not change the workers' "Supply" of labour.

2. 忽略教育的短期代價
學生常直覺認為教育 (選項 B) 是萬能的。但讀書需要時間，當學生在大學上課時，他們就退出了勞動市場，導致短期勞力供應下降。

Ignoring the short-term cost of education: Students often intuitively think education (Option B) solves everything. But studying takes time; while students are in university, they exit the labour market, causing a short-term drop in labour supply.

🙋 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 如果選項 D 只是引入「普通非技術勞工」，答案還會成立嗎？

Q: If Option D only brought in "unskilled workers", would it still be the answer?

A: 不會。引入普通非技術勞工確實會增加「勞力供應」，但因為他們的技術水平可能低於或等於現有平均水平，這會拉低或維持不變整體的「平均勞工生產力」。題目特別註明「高技術人才 (highly skilled)」，這才是拉高平均數的關鍵。

A: No. Bringing in unskilled workers would indeed increase "labour supply", but because their skill level is likely below or equal to the current average, it would drag down or maintain the overall "average labour productivity". The question specifically mentions "highly skilled", which is the key to pulling up the mathematical average.

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

消去法 (Elimination Method):
在處理「同時符合A與B」的題目時，只要發現選項違反其中一個條件，即可立即剔除，節省時間。

When dealing with "both A and B" questions, as soon as you find an option violates one condition, eliminate it immediately to save time.

  • 看 A：救濟金增加 \(\rightarrow\) 不想工作 \(\rightarrow\) 供應跌。(剔除)
  • 看 B：去讀大學 \(\rightarrow\) 不出來工作 \(\rightarrow\) 供應跌。(剔除)
  • 看 C：機器增加 \(\rightarrow\) 不影響人口或工時 \(\rightarrow\) 供應不變。(剔除)
  • 只剩下 D，直接選 D！
