The diagram below shows the supply and demand curves of electric vehicles. The original equilibrium point is \(E_0\).

Suppose there is an increase in the cost of producing electric vehicles. At the same time, the decreasing number of car accidents involving electric vehicles increases consumers’ confidence in electric vehicles. As a result, the new equilibrium point will be at __________.

  1. \(E_1\)
  2. \(E_2\)

  4. \(E_4\)

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

供應的變動 (Changes in Supply)

當生產成本上升時，生產者在每一個價格水平下願意且能夠提供的數量會減少，導致供應曲線向左移動。

When the cost of production increases, producers are willing and able to supply less at each price level, causing the supply curve to shift to the left.

需求的變動 (Changes in Demand)

當消費者信心提高（偏好增加）時，消費者在每一個價格水平下願意且能夠購買的數量會增加，導致需求曲線向右移動。

When consumer confidence increases (favorable change in preference), consumers are willing and able to buy more at each price level, causing the demand curve to shift to the right.

$$ \text{New Equilibrium} = \text{New Supply Curve} \cap \text{New Demand Curve} $$

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): B

選項 A (\(E_1\)): 錯誤 (Incorrect)

\(E_1\) 是 \(S_1\) 和 \(D_1\) 的交點。這代表供應增加（向右移）和需求增加。但題目指出生產成本上升，供應應該減少而非增加。

\(E_1\) is the intersection of \(S_1\) and \(D_1\). This represents an increase in supply (rightward shift) and an increase in demand. However, the question states production costs increased, which should decrease supply, not increase it.

選項 B (\(E_2\)): 正確 (Correct)

\(E_2\) 是 \(S_2\) 和 \(D_1\) 的交點。生產成本上升令供應曲線由 \(S_0\) 左移至 \(S_2\)；消費者信心提高令需求曲線由 \(D_0\) 右移至 \(D_1\)。兩條新曲線的交點正是 \(E_2\)。

\(E_2\) is the intersection of \(S_2\) and \(D_1\). The increase in production cost shifts the supply curve left from \(S_0\) to \(S_2\); the increase in consumer confidence shifts the demand curve right from \(D_0\) to \(D_1\). The intersection of these two new curves is exactly \(E_2\).

選項 C (\(E_3\)): 錯誤 (Incorrect)

\(E_3\) 是 \(S_2\) 和 \(D_2\) 的交點。這代表供應減少和需求減少。但題目中消費者信心是提高的，需求應該增加而非減少。

\(E_3\) is the intersection of \(S_2\) and \(D_2\). This represents a decrease in supply and a decrease in demand. However, consumer confidence increased, so demand should increase, not decrease.

選項 D (\(E_4\)): 錯誤 (Incorrect)

\(E_4\) 是 \(S_1\) 和 \(D_2\) 的交點。這代表供應增加和需求減少，與題目描述的兩個事件方向完全相反。

\(E_4\) is the intersection of \(S_1\) and \(D_2\). This represents an increase in supply and a decrease in demand, which is the exact opposite of the events described in the question.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解答這類題目的關鍵在於將複雜的情境拆解為獨立的供需移動：

The key to solving this type of question is to break down the complex scenario into independent supply and demand shifts:

  • 事件一 (Event 1): 生產成本上升 (Cost of production ↑) \(\rightarrow\) 供應減少 (Supply ↓) \(\rightarrow\) 曲線由 \(S_0\) 移至 \(S_2\)。
  • 事件二 (Event 2): 意外減少，信心增加 (Accidents ↓, Confidence ↑) \(\rightarrow\) 需求增加 (Demand ↑) \(\rightarrow\) 曲線由 \(D_0\) 移至 \(D_1\)。
  • 尋找交點 (Find Intersection): 找出 \(S_2\) 與 \(D_1\) 相交的點，即 \(E_2\)。

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱：將供應減少誤認為曲線「向下」移動

很多學生看到「減少」，就直覺認為曲線應該向下移。在圖表中，供應減少（向左移）視覺上看起來像是「向上」移動（因為在同一數量下，價格上升了）。緊記：增加向右，減少向左 (Increase = Right, Decrease = Left)。

Trap: Mistaking a decrease in supply as a "downward" shift.
Many students intuitively think "decrease" means moving down. On a graph, a decrease in supply (leftward shift) visually looks like it moved "up" (because price is higher at the same quantity). Always remember: Increase = Right, Decrease = Left.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 我們可以確定新的均衡價格 (Price) 和數量 (Quantity) 是上升還是下降嗎？

A: 需求增加（推高價格和數量）加上供應減少（推高價格但壓低數量）。兩者都會令價格必定上升。至於數量，則取決於兩條曲線移動的幅度。但在這張特定的圖表中，\(E_2\) 剛好在 \(E_0\) 的正上方，意味著在這個特定例子中，均衡數量維持不變，而價格上升。

Q: Can we be certain if the new equilibrium price and quantity will increase or decrease?

A: An increase in demand (pushes price and quantity up) combined with a decrease in supply (pushes price up but quantity down) means the price will definitely increase. The change in quantity depends on the magnitude of the shifts. However, in this specific diagram, \(E_2\) is directly above \(E_0\), meaning in this specific case, equilibrium quantity remains unchanged while price increases.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

畫箭嘴法 (The Arrow Method):

在考試時，不要單憑想像。直接在試卷上畫箭嘴！

  1. 看到「成本上升」，立即在 \(S_0\) 旁邊畫一個向左的箭嘴，指向 \(S_2\)。
  2. 看到「信心提高」，立即在 \(D_0\) 旁邊畫一個向右的箭嘴，指向 \(D_1\)。
  3. 用筆圈出 \(S_2\) 和 \(D_1\) 的交點。答案一秒找出，絕不粗心！

During the exam, don't just imagine it. Draw arrows directly on the paper! 1. See "cost increase", draw a left arrow from \(S_0\) to \(S_2\). 2. See "confidence increase", draw a right arrow from \(D_0\) to \(D_1\). 3. Circle the intersection. You'll find the answer in seconds without careless mistakes!
