HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic / 課題:
Price Elasticity & Total Expenditure (需求價格彈性與總支出)
Difficulty / 難度:
★★★★☆
Key Concepts / 關鍵概念:
Linear Demand Curve (直線需求曲線), Unitary Elasticity (單一彈性)

The Question (題目)

假設口罩的需求曲線是一條向下傾斜的直線。當口罩價格由$5下降至$4時，消費者對口罩的總支出維持不變。若口罩價格再由$4下降至$3，消費者對口罩的總支出：
Suppose the demand curve of face masks is a downward-sloping straight line. When the price of face masks falls from $5 to $4, consumers' total expenditure on face masks remains unchanged. If the price of face masks falls further from $4 to $3, consumers' total expenditure on face masks:

  • A. 會上升。
    will increase.
  • B. 會下降。
    will decrease.
  • C. 會維持不變。
    will remain unchanged.
  • D. 可能上升、下降或維持不變。
    may increase, decrease or remain unchanged.

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 總支出與價格彈性的關係 (Total Expenditure & PED)

消費者總支出 (Total Expenditure, \(TE\)) 等於價格乘以交易量 (\(TE = P \times Q\))。價格變動對 \(TE\) 的影響取決於需求價格彈性 (\(E_d\))： Total Expenditure (\(TE\)) equals Price times Quantity (\(TE = P \times Q\)). The effect of a price change on \(TE\) depends on the Price Elasticity of Demand (\(E_d\)):

$$ \% \Delta TE \approx \% \Delta P + \% \Delta Q $$
  • 彈性需求 (Elastic, \(E_d > 1\)): 價格下降 \(\rightarrow\) 數量大幅上升 \(\rightarrow\) \(TE\) 上升。
  • 低彈性需求 (Inelastic, \(E_d < 1\)): 價格下降 \(\rightarrow\) 數量微幅上升 \(\rightarrow\) \(TE\) 下降。
  • 單一彈性需求 (Unitary, \(E_d = 1\)): 價格下降 \(\rightarrow\) 數量上升幅度剛好抵銷 \(\rightarrow\) \(TE\) 不變。

2. 直線需求曲線的特性 (Properties of a Linear Demand Curve)

這是本題的關鍵。對於一條向下傾斜的直線需求曲線，彈性並非恆定不變，而是隨價格改變： This is the key to this question. For a downward-sloping straight-line demand curve, elasticity is NOT constant but changes with price:

  • 中點以上 (Top half): 彈性 \( > 1 \) (Elastic)。
  • 中點 (Midpoint): 彈性 \( = 1 \) (Unitary)。此時總支出 (\(TE\)) 達到最大值 (Maximum)。
  • 中點以下 (Bottom half): 彈性 \( < 1 \) (Inelastic)。

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: B

答案：會下降 (will decrease)

Step 1: 定位中點 (Locating the Midpoint)

題目指出當價格由 $5 降至 $4 時，總支出 (\(TE\)) 維持不變。在直線需求曲線上，\(TE\) 呈現倒 U 型拋物線，峰值位於 \(E_d = 1\) 的中點。
若 \(TE\) 在 $5 和 $4 時相等，根據拋物線的對稱性，\(TE\) 的最大值（即 \(E_d=1\) 的點）必然位於這兩個價格的正中間，即 \(P = 4.5\)。
The question states that \(TE\) remains unchanged when price falls from $5 to $4. On a straight-line demand curve, \(TE\) follows an inverted U-shape parabola, peaking at the midpoint where \(E_d = 1\). If \(TE\) is equal at $5 and $4, by symmetry, the maximum \(TE\) (where \(E_d=1\)) must be exactly in the middle, at \(P = 4.5\).

Step 2: 判斷新價格區間的彈性 (Determining Elasticity in the New Range)

現在價格由 $4 下降至 $3。由於中點（單一彈性點）是 $4.5，任何低於 $4.5 的價格都位於需求曲線的下半部 (Bottom half)。
這意味著從 $4 到 $3 的整個區間都屬於低彈性 (Inelastic, \(E_d < 1\)) 區域。
Now the price falls from $4 to $3. Since the midpoint (unitary elastic point) is $4.5, any price below $4.5 lies on the bottom half of the demand curve. This means the entire range from $4 to $3 is in the inelastic region (\(E_d < 1\)).

Step 3: 推論結果 (Conclusion)

在低彈性區域 (\(E_d < 1\))，價格下降會導致總支出下降（因為數量增加的百分比小於價格下降的百分比）。因此，總支出會下降。
In the inelastic region (\(E_d < 1\)), a decrease in price leads to a decrease in total expenditure (because the percentage increase in quantity is smaller than the percentage decrease in price). Therefore, total expenditure will decrease.

Why other options are wrong (為何其他選項錯誤)

A. 會上升 (will increase)

這只會在需求屬「彈性」(Elastic) 時發生。這需要價格位於中點 ($4.5) 之上。但我們現在是在 $4 降至 $3，位於低彈性區。
This would only happen if demand were elastic. That requires the price to be above the midpoint ($4.5). However, we are moving from $4 to $3, which is in the inelastic zone.

C. 會維持不變 (will remain unchanged)

這是一個常見陷阱。學生可能誤以為「既然之前不變，之後也不會變」。這只適用於「長方形雙曲線」(Rectangular Hyperbola) 的需求曲線（即 \(E_d = 1\) 恆定不變）。但題目明確指出是直線 (Straight Line)，直線的彈性是會隨位置改變的。
This is a common trap. Students might assume "if it didn't change before, it won't change now." This is only true for a "Rectangular Hyperbola" demand curve (where \(E_d = 1\) everywhere). But the question specifies a straight line, where elasticity changes along the curve.

D. 可能上升、下降或維持不變

由於我們有足夠的資訊（直線特性 + 兩個價格點的 TE 關係）來確定我們處於低彈性區，因此結果是確定的，並非不確定。
Since we have sufficient information (straight line property + TE relationship at two points) to determine we are in the inelastic zone, the result is definite, not uncertain.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解決此類題目的思維流程 (Mental Flow to solve this):

  1. 識別關鍵字 (Identify Keywords): 看到 "Straight Line" (直線) 和 "Total Expenditure" (總支出) \(\rightarrow\) 立即聯想到中點 (Midpoint) 概念。
  2. 尋找分界點 (Find the Pivot): 題目說 $5 \to $4 支出不變。這鎖定了 \(E_d = 1\) 的位置大約在 $4.5。
    The question says TE is constant from $5 to $4. This locks the \(E_d = 1\) point around $4.5.
  3. 定位新區間 (Locate New Range): 新動作是 $4 \to $3。這完全在 $4.5$ 以下。
    The new move is $4 \to $3. This is entirely below $4.5.
  4. 應用規則 (Apply Rule): 中點以下 = 低彈性 = 價格與支出同向變動 (P \(\downarrow\) \(\rightarrow\) TE \(\downarrow\))。

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

幾何直覺法 (Geometric Intuition):

想像一個倒 U 型曲線代表總支出 (TE)。
1. $5 和 $4 處於同一高度（TE 相等）。
2. 頂峰 (Peak) 必須在它們中間 ($4.5)。
3. 從 $4 往左走到 $3（價格降低），你正在沿著倒 U 型曲線的右坡往下滑。
4. 往下滑 = TE 減少。
Visualize an inverted U-curve representing TE. $5 and $4 are at the same height. The peak is in the middle ($4.5). Moving from $4 down to $3 means you are sliding down the slope away from the peak. Sliding down = TE decreases.
