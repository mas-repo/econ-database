HKDSE Economics Tutorial

1. The Question (題目)

細閱下列有關某國的資料。

Study the following data of a country.

年份 / Year 2020 2021
失業人口（百萬）
Unemployed population (million)		 176.9 175.4
失業率 (%)
Unemployment rate (%)		 3.8 4.0

下列哪項最能解釋上述國家從2020年到2021年的改變？

Which of the following best explains the above changes of the country from 2020 to 2021?

  1. 更多人尋找工作。
    More people look for jobs.
  2. 更多就業人士移民到其他國家。
    More employed persons emigrate to other countries.
  3. 政府提高有效的最低工資率。
    The government raises the effective minimum wage rate.
  4. 政府提高失業救濟金額。
    The government raises the amount of unemployment benefits.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要拆解這條題目，我們必須清楚勞動人口的結構以及失業率的計算公式。

To solve this question, we must understand the structure of the labor force and the formula for the unemployment rate.

1. 勞動人口 (Labor Force, \(LF\)): 包括所有就業人口 (\(E\)) 和失業人口 (\(U\))。

1. Labor Force (\(LF\)): Includes all employed persons (\(E\)) and unemployed persons (\(U\)).

$$ LF = E + U $$

2. 失業率 (Unemployment Rate, \(UR\)): 失業人口佔勞動人口的百分比。

2. Unemployment Rate (\(UR\)): The percentage of the labor force that is unemployed.

$$ UR = \frac{U}{LF} \times 100\% = \frac{U}{E + U} \times 100\% $$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是：選項 B

The correct answer is: Option B

A 更多人尋找工作。這會導致失業人口 \(U\) 上升（因為尋找工作但未找到的人屬於失業）。然而，題目數據顯示失業人口從 176.9 跌至 175.4（\(U\) 下降）。因此與數據矛盾。 More people look for jobs. This would lead to an increase in the unemployed population \(U\) (since people looking for jobs but unable to find one are considered unemployed). However, the data shows the unemployed population dropped from 176.9 to 175.4 (\(U\) decreases). This contradicts the data.
B 更多就業人士移民。這會導致就業人口 \(E\) 大幅減少，從而令整體勞動人口 \(LF\) 下降。根據公式 \(UR = \frac{U}{LF}\)，即使失業人口 \(U\) 輕微下跌，但如果勞動人口 \(LF\)（分母）的跌幅更大，整體的失業率 \(UR\) 仍然會上升。這完美解釋了題目中 \(U\) 跌但 \(UR\) 升的奇特現象。 More employed persons emigrate. This drastically reduces the employed population \(E\), leading to a drop in the total labor force \(LF\). According to the formula \(UR = \frac{U}{LF}\), even if the unemployed population \(U\) drops slightly, if the labor force \(LF\) (the denominator) drops by a larger percentage, the overall unemployment rate \(UR\) will still rise. This perfectly explains the peculiar phenomenon where \(U\) falls but \(UR\) rises.
C 提高有效的最低工資率。這通常會增加企業的生產成本，導致裁員，從而令失業人口 \(U\) 上升。同樣地，這與題目中 \(U\) 下降的數據不符。 Raises the effective minimum wage rate. This generally increases production costs for firms, leading to layoffs, which increases the unemployed population \(U\). Again, this contradicts the data showing a decrease in \(U\).
D 提高失業救濟金額。這會降低人們尋找工作的意欲，或吸引非勞動人口重新加入勞動市場以獲取救濟金，通常會導致失業人口 \(U\) 上升。與數據不符。 Raises the amount of unemployment benefits. This reduces the incentive to find work, or attracts those outside the labor force to enter the market to claim benefits, generally leading to an increase in the unemployed population \(U\). This contradicts the data.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解答這類題目的「關鍵點（Pivot Point）」在於觀察數據的方向性：

The "Pivot Point" to solving this type of question lies in observing the direction of the data:

  • 現象一：失業人口 \(U\) 下降 (\(176.9 \rightarrow 175.4\)) Phenomenon 1: Unemployed population \(U\) decreases (\(176.9 \rightarrow 175.4\))
  • 現象二：失業率 \(UR\) 上升 (\(3.8\% \rightarrow 4.0\%\)) Phenomenon 2: Unemployment rate \(UR\) increases (\(3.8\% \rightarrow 4.0\%\))

唯一能夠讓一個分數（\(\frac{U}{LF}\)）的分子變小，但整體數值變大的情況，就是分母（\(LF\)）變小的幅度比分子更大。因此，這道題目的核心是找出哪一個選項會導致勞動人口（特別是就業人口）大幅萎縮。

The only mathematical way for a fraction (\(\frac{U}{LF}\)) to increase while its numerator decreases is if the denominator (\(LF\)) decreases by a larger proportion than the numerator. Therefore, the core of this question is finding the option that causes a massive shrinkage in the labor force (specifically the employed population).

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

忽略分母的變化： 學生常誤以為「失業率上升」等同於「失業人數增加」。他們忘記了失業率是一個比例（Ratio），會受到整體勞動人口（分母）的影響。當就業人口大量流失（如移民潮），失業率就會被被動拉高。

Ignoring the denominator: Students often mistakenly equate a "rising unemployment rate" directly with "increasing unemployed numbers." They forget that the unemployment rate is a ratio affected by the total labor force (the denominator). When there is a massive exodus of the employed population (e.g., an emigration wave), the unemployment rate is passively pushed up.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

問：我怎麼能從題目提供的數字中，確實證明勞動人口是減少了呢？

Q: How can I mathematically prove from the data provided that the labor force definitely decreased?

答： 我們可以倒推（Reverse-engineer）勞動人口（\(LF\)）的實際數值：
2020年 \(LF\) = \(176.9 \div 0.038 \approx 4655.26\) 百萬
2021年 \(LF\) = \(175.4 \div 0.040 = 4385.00\) 百萬
勞動人口大幅減少了超過2.7億！這證明了有一個會導致人口大規模流失的因素正在發生，而選項 B (移民) 完美吻合。

A: We can reverse-engineer the actual value of the Labor Force (\(LF\)):
2020 \(LF\) = \(176.9 \div 0.038 \approx 4655.26\) million
2021 \(LF\) = \(175.4 \div 0.040 = 4385.00\) million
The labor force decreased by over 270 million! This proves a massive population drain occurred, which Option B (emigration) perfectly explains.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

⚡ 十秒極速解題法 (The 10-Second Elimination Method)：

⚡ The 10-Second Elimination Method:

在多項選擇題中，善用排除法最為快捷。看一眼數據：失業人口 (\(U\)) 是下跌的。直接掃視四個選項：
A (更多人找工作) $\rightarrow$ \(U\) 升
C (加最低工資) $\rightarrow$ \(U\) 升
D (加失業救濟) $\rightarrow$ \(U\) 升
既然 A、C、D 都會導致失業人口上升，完全違背題目數據，就直接剔除。剩下唯一的可能就只有 B，連計算都不需要！

In multiple-choice questions, elimination is your best friend. Look at the data: Unemployed population (\(U\)) fell. Scan the options immediately:
A (More looking for jobs) $\rightarrow$ \(U\) rises
C (Raise minimum wage) $\rightarrow$ \(U\) rises
D (Raise unemployment benefits) $\rightarrow$ \(U\) rises
Since A, C, and D all cause the unemployed population to rise (contradicting the data), eliminate them instantly. The only remaining possibility is B. No math required!
