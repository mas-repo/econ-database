主題 (Topic): 國際收支平衡：經常帳 (Balance of Payments: Current Account)

難度 (Difficulty): ★★★☆☆ (中等 Moderate)

關鍵字 (Key Concepts): 經常帳 (Current Account), 初級收入 (Primary Income), 金融帳 (Financial Account), 國際收支平衡 (BOP)

1. 題目 (The Question)

下列哪項包括在香港經常帳差額的計算之內？

Which of the following is included in the calculation of the balance of Hong Kong’s current account?

  1. 一位内地億萬富翁斥資200億港元在香港興建一座購物中心。
    A Mainland billionaire spends HK$20 billion building a shopping mall in Hong Kong.
  2. 一位日本居民向一家日本航空公司購買前往香港的機票。
    A Japanese resident buys an air ticket to Hong Kong from an airline company in Japan.
  3. 一位香港居民收取美國政府債券所派發的利息。
    A Hong Kong resident receives interest from US Government bonds.
  4. 一位香港投資者購買一家德國上市公司發行的股票。
    A Hong Kong investor buys shares issued by a German listed company.

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

國際收支平衡表 (Balance of Payments, BOP) 記錄了一個經濟體在特定時期內與世界各地的所有經濟交易。它主要分為兩大帳目：

The Balance of Payments (BOP) records all economic transactions between an economy and the rest of the world over a specific period. It is mainly divided into two accounts:

  • 經常帳 (Current Account): 記錄貨物、服務、初級收入（如投資回報、薪酬）及次級收入（單方面轉移）。
    Records goods, services, primary income (e.g., investment returns, compensation of employees), and secondary income (unilateral transfers).
  • 資本及金融帳 (Capital and Financial Account): 記錄資產所有權的變動，包括直接投資（如設廠、買樓）、證券投資（如買賣股票、債券）及儲備資產。
    Records changes in asset ownership, including direct investment (e.g., building factories, buying real estate), portfolio investment (e.g., trading stocks, bonds), and reserve assets.
$$CAB = NX + NPI + NSI$$

其中 $CAB$ 代表經常帳差額，$NX$ 代表淨出口（貨物及服務），$NPI$ 代表淨初級收入，$NSI$ 代表淨次級收入。

Where $CAB$ is the Current Account Balance, $NX$ is Net Exports (goods and services), $NPI$ is Net Primary Income, and $NSI$ is Net Secondary Income.

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案：選項 C

Correct Option: Option C

選項 A 分析 (錯誤): 內地富豪在香港興建購物中心，屬於獲取實質資產的行為。這是外來直接投資 (Foreign Direct Investment)，應記錄在金融帳，而非經常帳。

Analysis for Option A (Incorrect): A mainland billionaire building a mall in HK is an acquisition of physical assets. This is Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) and should be recorded in the Financial Account, not the current account.

選項 B 分析 (錯誤): 日本居民向日本航空公司買機票，這是兩個日本實體之間的交易。由於沒有牽涉香港居民或企業，這項交易根本不會記錄在香港的國際收支平衡表中。

Analysis for Option B (Incorrect): A Japanese resident buying a ticket from a Japanese airline is a transaction between two Japanese entities. Since it does not involve Hong Kong residents or firms, this transaction is not recorded in Hong Kong's BOP at all.

選項 C 分析 (正確): 香港居民收取美國政府債券的「利息」。利息屬於投資回報，是生產要素收入的一種，因此歸類為初級收入 (Primary Income)，必須計算在經常帳之內。

Analysis for Option C (Correct): A Hong Kong resident receives "interest" from US bonds. Interest is a return on investment and a type of factor income. Therefore, it is classified as Primary Income and must be included in the Current Account.

選項 D 分析 (錯誤): 香港投資者「購買股票」屬於金融資產的買賣。這是對外證券投資 (Portfolio Investment)，應記錄在金融帳中。

Analysis for Option D (Incorrect): A HK investor "buying shares" is the trading of financial assets. This is an outward Portfolio Investment and should be recorded in the Financial Account.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

這類題目的拆解關鍵 (Pivot Point) 在於區分「買賣資產本身」還是「獲取資產所產生的收入」。

The Pivot Point of this type of question is distinguishing between "trading the asset itself" versus "receiving income generated by the asset".

  • 資產買賣（如建樓、買股票、買債券）= 金融帳 (Financial Account)
    Trading assets (e.g., building properties, buying shares, buying bonds) = Financial Account
  • 資產收入（如利息、股息、租金）= 經常帳 (Current Account)
    Income from assets (e.g., interest, dividends, rent) = Current Account

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱 1：混淆投資行為與投資收入。 很多學生看到「債券」或「股票」就直覺認為是金融帳。記住：買賣債券是金融帳，但收債券利息是經常帳！

Trap 1: Confusing investment actions with investment income. Many students see "bonds" or "shares" and instinctively think of the Financial Account. Remember: Buying/selling bonds goes to the Financial Account, but receiving bond interest goes to the Current Account!

陷阱 2：無關交易的干擾。 如選項 B 所示，題目刻意加入「前往香港」字眼來混淆考生。但只要付款方（日本居民）和收款方（日本航空）都不是香港實體，就不入香港的帳。

Trap 2: Irrelevant transactions. As seen in Option B, the prompt deliberately adds "to Hong Kong" to confuse candidates. But as long as neither the payer (Japanese resident) nor the payee (Japanese airline) is a HK entity, it doesn't enter HK's accounts.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

學生問 (Q)： 選項 A 中，建購物中心會創造香港人的就業機會，這不是會增加香港的 GDP 和收入嗎？為什麼不放在經常帳？

Student: In Option A, building a mall creates jobs for HK people. Doesn't this increase HK's GDP and income? Why isn't it in the Current Account?

老師答 (A)： 很好的問題！「支付給香港工人的薪水」確實會增加 GDP，但選項 A 描述的是「200億港元興建購物中心」這個整體投資資本流動。外國資金流入香港購買或建立固定資產，在 BOP 的定義中嚴格屬於「直接投資」(金融帳)。

Teacher: Great question! The "wages paid to HK workers" would indeed increase GDP. However, Option A describes the overall capital flow of "HK$20 billion to build a mall". Foreign capital flowing into HK to purchase or establish fixed assets falls strictly under "Direct Investment" (Financial Account) according to BOP definitions.

學生問 (Q)： 如果選項 B 改成「向國泰航空 (香港公司) 購買機票」呢？

Student: What if Option B was changed to "buying a ticket from Cathay Pacific (a HK airline)"?

老師答 (A)： 那樣就會入帳！這代表香港向日本輸出了「運輸服務」(Transport Services)，這會記錄在香港經常帳中的「服務輸出」(Export of Services) 內。

Teacher: Then it would be recorded! This would represent Hong Kong exporting "Transport Services" to Japan, which would be recorded under "Export of Services" in Hong Kong's Current Account.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

秒殺技巧 (Shortcut)：找關鍵動詞！

Shortcut: Look for key verbs!

  • 看到「興建 (Build)」、「購買股票/資產 (Buy shares/assets)」 $\rightarrow$ 立刻聯想 金融帳 (Financial Account)。可以直接刪去選項 A 和 D。
    Seeing "Build" or "Buy shares/assets" $\rightarrow$ Immediately think Financial Account. You can eliminate A and D instantly.
  • 看到「收取利息/股息/薪金 (Receive interest/dividends/wages)」 $\rightarrow$ 立刻聯想 經常帳 (Current Account)。直接鎖定選項 C。
    Seeing "Receive interest/dividends/wages" $\rightarrow$ Immediately think Current Account. Lock in Option C.
