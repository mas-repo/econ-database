HKDSE Economics Expert Guide
經濟學專家解析指南

Topic (課題): Money and Banking - Functions of Central Bank / HKMA (貨幣與銀行 - 中央銀行/金管局的功能)

Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆ (2/5)

Key Concepts (核心概念): HKMA 金管局 Exchange Fund 外匯基金 Central Bank 中央銀行

1. The Question (題目)

下列哪項是香港金融管理局的功能？

A. 向持牌銀行及公眾提供貸款
B. 管理外匯儲備
C. 決定市場利率
D. 為政府收取稅款

Which of the following is a function of the Hong Kong Monetary Authority?

A. providing loans to licensed banks and the public
B. managing foreign exchange reserves
C. determining the market interest rates
D. collecting taxes for the government

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

香港金融管理局 (HKMA) 的角色：
在香港，金管局扮演著「準中央銀行」(de facto central bank) 的角色。它的主要職能包括： 1. 維持貨幣穩定（透過聯繫匯率制度）。 2. 管理外匯基金（即香港的外匯儲備）。 3. 促進銀行體系的安全與穩定。 4. 協助鞏固香港作為國際金融中心的地位。

Role of the Hong Kong Monetary Authority (HKMA):
In Hong Kong, the HKMA acts as the de facto central bank. Its main functions include: 1. Maintaining currency stability (via the Linked Exchange Rate System). 2. Managing the Exchange Fund (Hong Kong's foreign exchange reserves). 3. Promoting the safety and stability of the banking system. 4. Helping to maintain Hong Kong's status as an international financial centre.

注意 (Note): 金管局不會與一般市民大眾進行交易，這與商業銀行不同。

The HKMA does not deal with the general public, unlike commercial banks.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option B

A. 向持牌銀行及公眾提供貸款 (providing loans to licensed banks and the public) - ❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect)

金管局作為「最後貸款人」，的確會在銀行出現流動性危機時向持牌銀行提供貸款（透過貼現窗）。但是，金管局絕對不會向「公眾」提供貸款。向公眾提供貸款是商業銀行的功能。

While the HKMA acts as the "lender of last resort" and can provide liquidity to licensed banks (via the Discount Window), it never provides loans to the "public". Lending to the public is a function of commercial banks.

B. 管理外匯儲備 (managing foreign exchange reserves) - ✅ 正確 (Correct)

金管局負責管理外匯基金（Exchange Fund），這正是香港的外匯儲備，用於維持港元的穩定及捍衛聯繫匯率制度。

The HKMA is responsible for managing the Exchange Fund, which constitutes Hong Kong's foreign exchange reserves, used to maintain the stability of the Hong Kong dollar and defend the Linked Exchange Rate System.

C. 決定市場利率 (determining the market interest rates) - ❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect)

香港的市場利率（如最優惠利率 Prime Rate）是由市場資金供求及商業銀行自行決定的。金管局只會設定「貼現窗基本利率」(Base Rate)，但不能直接決定市場利率。

Market interest rates in Hong Kong (like the Prime Rate) are determined by the market supply and demand of funds and commercial banks. The HKMA only sets the "Base Rate" for the Discount Window, but does not directly determine market interest rates.

D. 為政府收取稅款 (collecting taxes for the government) - ❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect)

為政府收取稅款是稅務局 (Inland Revenue Department) 的職責，並非金管局的功能。

Collecting taxes for the government is the duty of the Inland Revenue Department, not a function of the HKMA.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目測試考生對香港政府不同機構及金融機構職能的區分能力。解題的「關鍵點」(Pivot Point) 在於識別出專屬於中央銀行的功能，並排除屬於商業銀行或政府其他部門的功能。

This type of question tests your ability to distinguish the functions of different government and financial institutions in Hong Kong. The "Pivot Point" is identifying functions exclusive to a central bank and eliminating those belonging to commercial banks or other government departments.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱 1：忽略了「及公眾」三個字 (Ignoring "and the public")
很多同學看到選項 A 前半部分的「向持牌銀行提供貸款」就匆忙選擇，忘記了中央銀行是「銀行的銀行」，不會與普通市民（公眾）有直接業務往來。

Trap 1: Many students rush to choose Option A after reading the first half "providing loans to licensed banks", forgetting that a central bank is the "bankers' bank" and does not deal directly with the general public.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 既然金管局會調整基本利率，為什麼不能說它決定市場利率？
A: 基本利率 \(r_{base}\) 只是銀行向金管局借錢的成本。雖然它會影響銀行的資金成本，從而間接影響市場利率，但最終市民面對的市場利率是由商業銀行根據市場競爭和資金流動性自行訂立的。因此，金管局是「影響」而非「決定」市場利率。

Q: Since the HKMA adjusts the Base Rate, why can't we say it determines market interest rates?
A: The Base Rate \(r_{base}\) is only the cost for banks to borrow from the HKMA. While it affects banks' cost of funds and indirectly influences market rates, the actual market rates faced by the public are set by commercial banks based on market competition and liquidity. Therefore, the HKMA "influences" rather than "determines" market interest rates.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

「公眾排除法」(The "Public" Elimination Method):
在 DSE 經濟科中，只要題目問及「中央銀行 / 金管局」的功能，任何選項中包含「向公眾提供服務 / 接受公眾存款 / 向公眾貸款」的，都可以立即剔除。這能幫你瞬間排除選項 A。

In DSE Economics, whenever a question asks about the functions of a "Central Bank / HKMA", any option that includes "providing services to the public / accepting deposits from the public / lending to the public" can be immediately eliminated. This helps you instantly cross out Option A.
