📝 DSE Economics Tutor: Question Analysis

Topic / 課題 Aggregate Demand & Aggregate Supply (總需求與總供應模型)

Difficulty / 難度 ★★★★☆

Key Concepts / 核心概念 Long-run Adjustment (長期調整), Deflationary Gap (通縮差距), Self-correction Mechanism (自我修正機制)

📌 The Question (題目)

下圖顯示某經濟的總需求(AD)曲線、短期總供應(SRAS)曲線及長期總供應(LRAS)曲線。該經濟原初在E點運作。下列哪項會導致長期均衡點由E改變為F？

The diagram below shows the aggregate demand (AD) curves, the short run aggregate supply (SRAS) curves and the long run aggregate supply (LRAS) curve of an economy. The economy initially operates at point E. Which of the following will result in a change in the long run equilibrium point from E to F?

  • A. 住戶儲蓄意慾上升。 (Households’ desire to save increases.)
  • B. 要素投入的價格下降。 (Prices of factor inputs decrease.)
  • C. 政府為工人提供更多再培訓課程。 (The government offers more retraining programmes to workers.)
  • D. 法定儲備比率下降。 (The required reserve ratio decreases.)

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這類題目，我們必須理解 AD-AS 模型的自我修正機制 (Self-correction mechanism)。當經濟受到外圍衝擊導致總需求 (AD) 改變時，短期內會出現產出缺口。在長期，要素價格（如工資）會向下或向上調整，令短期總供應 (SRAS) 曲線移動，最終令經濟回復到長期總供應 (LRAS) 的全民就業水平。

To answer this type of question, we must understand the self-correction mechanism of the AD-AS model. When the economy is hit by an external shock that changes Aggregate Demand (AD), an output gap occurs in the short run. In the long run, factor prices (such as wages) will adjust downwards or upwards, causing the Short-Run Aggregate Supply (SRAS) curve to shift, ultimately returning the economy to the full-employment level of output at the Long-Run Aggregate Supply (LRAS).

Key Components (核心組成部分):

  • 總需求 (Aggregate Demand, AD):
    $$AD = C + I + G + NX$$

    \(C\) (消費), \(I\) (投資), \(G\) (政府開支), \(NX\) (淨出口)。任何影響這些組成的因素都會令 AD 曲線移動。

    \(C\) (Consumption), \(I\) (Investment), \(G\) (Government spending), \(NX\) (Net exports). Any factor affecting these components will shift the AD curve.

  • 短期總供應 (Short-Run Aggregate Supply, SRAS):

    受生產成本 (Cost of production) 影響。要素價格（如工資、租金）下降，生產成本減低，SRAS 向右移。

    Affected by the cost of production. If factor prices (e.g., wages, rent) decrease, production costs fall, and SRAS shifts to the right.

  • 長期總供應 (Long-Run Aggregate Supply, LRAS):

    代表經濟的潛在產出 (Potential output)。受科技、資源數量（勞動力、資本）及制度影響。價格水平的改變不會影響 LRAS。

    Represents the economy's potential output. Affected by technology, quantity of resources (labor, capital), and institutions. Changes in the price level do not affect LRAS.

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 Correct Option: A

Step-by-Step Breakdown (逐項分析):

Option A: 住戶儲蓄意慾上升 / Households’ desire to save increases

正確 (Correct)。 當住戶儲蓄意慾上升，消費支出 \(C\) 便會減少。這會導致總需求曲線由 \(AD_1\) 向左移至 \(AD_2\)。短期內，經濟出現通縮差距 (Deflationary gap)，產出低於全民就業水平，失業率上升。在長期，由於勞動力供過於求，工資（要素投入價格）會面臨下調壓力。當工資下降，生產成本降低，短期總供應曲線會由 \(SRAS_1\) 向右移至 \(SRAS_2\)。最終，經濟在 F 點重新達到長期均衡，產出回復至全民就業水平，但物價水平進一步下降。這完美吻合圖表中的變化。

When households' desire to save increases, consumption expenditure \(C\) decreases. This causes the aggregate demand curve to shift leftward from \(AD_1\) to \(AD_2\). In the short run, a deflationary gap emerges; output is below full employment, and unemployment rises. In the long run, excess supply of labor puts downward pressure on wages (factor input prices). As wages fall, production costs decrease, shifting the short-run aggregate supply curve rightward from \(SRAS_1\) to \(SRAS_2\). Ultimately, the economy reaches a new long-run equilibrium at point F, where output returns to the full-employment level, but at a lower price level. This perfectly matches the changes in the diagram.

Option B: 要素投入的價格下降 / Prices of factor inputs decrease

錯誤 (Incorrect)。 要素投入價格下降確實會令 \(SRAS_1\) 向右移至 \(SRAS_2\)。但如果僅僅發生這件事，總需求 \(AD\) 曲線是不會移動的。經濟均衡點只會沿著原有的 \(AD_1\) 曲線移動到一個產出更高、物價更低的新短期均衡點，而不會到達 F 點（F 點位處於新的 \(AD_2\) 曲線上）。

A decrease in factor input prices will indeed shift \(SRAS_1\) rightward to \(SRAS_2\). However, if only this happens, the aggregate demand \(AD\) curve will not shift. The economic equilibrium would only move along the original \(AD_1\) curve to a new short-run equilibrium with higher output and a lower price level, and it would not reach point F (which lies on the new \(AD_2\) curve).

Option C: 政府為工人提供更多再培訓課程 / The government offers more retraining programmes to workers

錯誤 (Incorrect)。 再培訓課程能提升勞工生產力 (Labor productivity)。這會增加經濟的生產能力，導致長期總供應 (LRAS) 曲線向右移。然而，在題目提供的圖表中，LRAS 曲線的位置維持不變。因此此選項不符合圖表顯示。

Retraining programs improve labor productivity. This increases the productive capacity of the economy, causing the Long-Run Aggregate Supply (LRAS) curve to shift to the right. However, in the diagram provided, the position of the LRAS curve remains unchanged. Therefore, this option contradicts the diagram.

Option D: 法定儲備比率下降 / The required reserve ratio decreases

錯誤 (Incorrect)。 法定儲備比率下降屬於擴張性貨幣政策 (Expansionary monetary policy)。這會增加貨幣供應，壓低利率，從而刺激投資 \(I\) 和消費 \(C\)。這將導致總需求 (AD) 曲線向右移。但圖表顯示的是 AD 曲線向左移，方向完全相反。

A decrease in the required reserve ratio is an expansionary monetary policy. This increases money supply, lowers interest rates, and thereby stimulates investment \(I\) and consumption \(C\). This would cause the aggregate demand (AD) curve to shift to the right. However, the diagram shows the AD curve shifting to the left, which is the exact opposite.

🛠️ Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

關鍵突破點 (The Pivot Point): 留意題目問的是如何由「長期均衡點 E」變成「長期均衡點 F」。這不是問兩件獨立發生的事件，而是問一個完整的宏觀經濟調整過程 (Macroeconomic adjustment process)。

The Pivot Point: Notice that the question asks how the "long run equilibrium point" changes from E to F. This is not asking for two independent events, but rather a complete macroeconomic adjustment process.

觀察圖表，我們看到三個關鍵變化：

  1. AD 向左移。
  2. SRAS 向右移。
  3. LRAS 不變，產出最終回復到原點 (Yf)。

在 DSE 課程中，AD 左移和 SRAS 右移同時發生，通常只有一個劇本：需求衝擊後的長期自我修正 (Long-run self-correction after a demand shock)。任何導致 AD 左移的因素（如選項 A）就是這個故事的「起因」，而 SRAS 右移只是長期調整的「結果」。

Observing the graph, we see three key changes: 1) AD shifts left, 2) SRAS shifts right, 3) LRAS is unchanged, and output returns to its original level (Yf). In the DSE syllabus, an AD leftward shift accompanied by an SRAS rightward shift usually follows one script: Long-run self-correction after a demand shock. Any factor causing the initial AD leftward shift (like Option A) is the "cause" of the story, while the SRAS rightward shift is merely the "result" of the long-run adjustment.

🙋‍♂️ Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q1: 既然圖中 SRAS 也有向右移，為什麼選項 B (要素投入價格下降) 不對？

Q1: Since the SRAS curve also shifts to the right in the diagram, why is Option B (Prices of factor inputs decrease) incorrect?

A: 這是因果關係的問題！選項 B 只解釋了圖表的一半（SRAS 右移），卻完全無法解釋為什麼 AD 會由 \(AD_1\) 左移至 \(AD_2\)。如果僅僅是要素價格下降，經濟只會沿著 \(AD_1\) 擴張。選項 A 則完美解釋了全部：儲蓄增加（AD左移）導致經濟衰退，衰退引致工資下降（SRAS右移），最終到達 F 點。選項 A 是一個完整的故事，選項 B 只是一個片段。

A: It's a matter of cause and effect! Option B only explains half of the diagram (SRAS shifting right) but completely fails to explain why AD shifts left from \(AD_1\) to \(AD_2\). If only factor prices decreased, the economy would simply expand along \(AD_1\). Option A perfectly explains everything: increased saving (AD shifts left) causes a recession, the recession leads to falling wages (SRAS shifts right), finally arriving at point F. Option A is a complete story; Option B is just a fragment.

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺法 (The Elimination Method): 當你看到這種「由舊長期均衡走到新長期均衡」的圖表題，第一步先看 AD 移向哪邊。

  • 圖中 AD 向左移。
  • 選項 C (再培訓) -> 影響 LRAS，圖中 LRAS 沒動 -> 刪去！
  • 選項 D (降低準備金率) -> AD 向右移 -> 方向反了 -> 刪去！
  • 選項 B (要素價格降) -> 只影響 SRAS，不影響 AD -> 刪去！
  • 只剩下選項 A 能令 AD 左移。答案呼之欲出，只需 10 秒！

The Elimination Method: When you see a diagram question moving from an "old long-run equilibrium to a new long-run equilibrium", look at which way AD shifted first.

  • In the graph, AD shifts left.
  • Option C (retraining) -> affects LRAS, but LRAS didn't move -> Eliminate!
  • Option D (lower reserve ratio) -> AD shifts right -> Wrong direction -> Eliminate!
  • Option B (factor prices fall) -> only affects SRAS, not AD -> Eliminate!
  • Only Option A causes AD to shift left. The answer is obvious in just 10 seconds!
