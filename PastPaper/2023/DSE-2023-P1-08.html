2. 概念重溫 Concept Review

1. 判斷企業類型 (Identifying Business Types)

根據香港的商業法規，不同類型的企業有不同的特徵：

According to Hong Kong commercial laws, different business ownership types have distinct features:

  • 公眾有限公司 (Public Limited Company): 可以向公眾發行股票集資，必須公開財務狀況。股票轉讓自由。
    Can issue shares to the public, must disclose financial status publicly. Shares are freely transferable.
  • 私人有限公司 (Private Limited Company): 股東人數為 1-50 人。不能向公眾發行股票，通常無需公開財務報表。轉讓股份需獲董事局/其他股東同意。是獨立法人。
    1-50 shareholders. Cannot issue shares to the public, usually not required to disclose financial statements. Transfer of shares requires consent. It is an independent legal entity.
  • 合夥 (Partnership): 東主（合夥人）人數通常為 2-20 人。不能發行股票，無需公開財務資料。轉讓擁有權需所有合夥人同意。不是獨立法人。
    Usually 2-20 partners. Cannot issue shares, no need to disclose financial information. Transfer of ownership requires all partners' consent. It is NOT an independent legal entity.

2. 香港利得稅 (Hong Kong Profits Tax)

企業的利潤計算公式如下：

The formula for a firm's profit is:

$$ \pi = TR - TC $$

（其中 \( \pi \) 代表利潤，\( TR \) 代表總收入，\( TC \) 代表總成本。香港政府會對企業的利潤徵稅。）

(Where \( \pi \) is profit, \( TR \) is total revenue, and \( TC \) is total cost. The HK government taxes this profit.)

香港實行兩級制利得稅，並按「法團」與「非法團」區分：

Hong Kong implements a two-tiered profits tax regime, differentiated by incorporated and unincorporated businesses:

  • 法團 (Corporations, 即有限公司): 標準稅率較高（16.5%）。
  • 非法團 (Unincorporated, 即獨資/合夥): 標準稅率較低（15%）。

3. 答案與詳細分析 The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Option: C

Distractor A

選項 A 錯誤： 雖然乙廠商（公眾有限公司）有向公眾集資的「能力」，但這並不代表它「實際上」籌集的資金一定比甲廠商多。甲廠商（例如一家大型私人有限公司或專業合夥）可能擁有多於乙廠商的資本。

Option A is incorrect: Although Firm B (a public limited company) has the "ability" to raise capital from the public, it does not mean it "actually" raises more capital. Firm A (e.g., a massive private limited company or professional partnership) could theoretically have more capital than Firm B.

Distractor B

選項 B 錯誤： 乙廠商是公眾有限公司，其股票可以在股票市場上自由買賣，股東轉讓擁有權時 不需要 獲得其他股東的同意。

Option B is incorrect: Firm B is a public limited company. Its shares can be traded freely on the stock market, so owners do not need consent from other owners to transfer their ownership.

Correct C

選項 C 正確： 甲廠商有3名東主，不能公開發行股票且無需披露財務。它 可能 (may) 是一間「合夥企業」。如果是合夥企業（非法團業務），它適用的利得稅稅率（15%）就會低於乙廠商（作為法團的公眾有限公司，稅率為16.5%）。題目使用了「可能」一詞，因此這是一個合理的推論。

Option C is correct: Firm A has 3 owners, cannot issue public shares, and has no public financial disclosure. It may be a "partnership". If it is a partnership (an unincorporated business), its applicable profits tax rate (15%) would be lower than Firm B's (a public limited company, which is a corporation taxed at 16.5%). Because the option uses the word "may", it is a logically sound conclusion.

Distractor D

選項 D 錯誤： 雖然甲廠商可能是合夥企業（不是獨立法人），但它也 可能 是一間「私人有限公司」（擁有3名股東，符合1-50人的限制，同樣不能公開招股及無需公開財務）。如果甲廠商是私人有限公司，它 就是 獨立法人。因此，我們不能絕對地「得出結論」甲廠商不是獨立法人。

Option D is incorrect: While Firm A could be a partnership (not an independent legal entity), it could also be a "private limited company" (having 3 shareholders fits the 1-50 limit, cannot issue public shares, and does not need to publish financials). If Firm A is a private limited company, it IS an independent legal entity. Therefore, we cannot absolutely "conclude" that Firm A is not a legal entity.

4. 題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

尋找「解題樞紐」(The Pivot Point)：

The key to solving this problem is precisely classifying the firms based on the table:

  • 乙廠商的確定性： 「可能發行股票」+「需要披露財務」 ➜ 100% 肯定是公眾有限公司。
    Certainty of Firm B: "Possible to issue shares" + "Disclosure necessary" ➜ 100% certainty it is a Public Limited Company.
  • 甲廠商的模糊性： 「3名東主」+「不能發行股票」+「無需披露財務」 ➜ 可能是私人有限公司 或 合夥。
    Ambiguity of Firm A: "3 owners" + "Cannot issue shares" + "No disclosure" ➜ Could be a Private Limited Company OR a Partnership.

因為甲廠商的身份不確定，任何帶有絕對語氣的選項（如 D 的「甲廠商不是」）都容易出錯。而帶有「可能」語氣的選項（如 C 的「可能享有」）則是極佳的候選答案。

Because Firm A's identity is ambiguous, any option with absolute phrasing (like D's "Firm A is not") is likely wrong. Options with probabilistic phrasing (like C's "may enjoy") are excellent candidates.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

🚨 陷阱一：忽略了「私人有限公司」的可能性

Trap 1: Ignoring the possibility of a Private Limited Company

很多同學看到「無需向公眾披露財務資料」，就立刻認定甲廠商是合夥企業，從而誤選 D。記住，私人有限公司同樣符合甲廠商的所有條件！

Many students see "disclosure not necessary" and immediately assume Firm A is a partnership, leading them to wrongly choose D. Remember, a Private Limited Company perfectly matches all of Firm A's criteria too!

🚨 陷阱二：混淆「融資能力」與「實際資金」

Trap 2: Confusing "Financing Capability" with "Actual Capital"

公眾有限公司（乙廠商）有最廣的融資渠道，但不代表它現時戶口裡的錢一定比一間富有的私人企業（甲廠商）多。經濟學考題中，能力 (Capability) 不等於 實際規模 (Actual scale)。

Public limited companies (Firm B) have the broadest financing channels, but that doesn't mean their current bank balance is definitely larger than a wealthy private firm (Firm A). In Econ MCQs, Capability ≠ Actual Scale.

6. 學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

Q1: 題目表格裡寫著「東主」(Owners)，這不是代表甲廠商一定是合夥或者獨資嗎？因為有限公司應該叫「股東」(Shareholders) 啊？
Q1: The table uses the word "Owners". Doesn't this imply Firm A must be a partnership/sole proprietorship, since limited companies use the term "Shareholders"?

A1: 這是一個很好的觀察，但在考評局 (HKEAA) 的題目設定中，「東主」有時會被當作一個統稱 (umbrella term) 來概括所有企業的擁有者。你可以看到，表格中乙廠商也被稱為有 50 名「東主」，但乙廠商肯定是公眾有限公司（因為它可以發行股票）。既然乙廠商的股東可以被稱為「東主」，甲廠商的「東主」自然也可能是股東。
This is a sharp observation, but in HKEAA exams, "owners" is sometimes used as an umbrella term for all business proprietors. Notice that Firm B is also listed as having 50 "owners", yet Firm B is definitively a public limited company (as it issues shares). Since Firm B's shareholders can be called "owners", Firm A's "owners" could easily be shareholders too.

7. 考試技巧與速解 Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

💡 語氣排除法 (Tone Elimination Method)

When information is incomplete, watch out for absolute words.

在處理這類「根據資料，我們可得出結論... (Based on the information, we can conclude...)」的題目時，如果題目給的資料不足以 100% 確定某個企業的具體類型（如甲廠商），那麼包含 絕對字眼 的選項通常是錯的。包含 可能性字眼（例如「可能」、「未必」）的選項，往往為了包容未知情況，而成為正確答案。

  • 選項 B：「...均需獲得...」 (required to...) ❌ 太絕對
  • 選項 D：「...是...而...不是。」 (is... while... is not) ❌ 太絕對
  • 選項 C：「甲廠商可能...」 (Firm A may...) ✅ 留有餘地，合乎邏輯
