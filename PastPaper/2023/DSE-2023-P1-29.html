2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

貨幣需求 (Money Demand) 主要分為兩大類：
Money Demand is primarily divided into two main categories:
  • 交易性貨幣需求 \( (M_t) \): 為了日常交易而持有貨幣的需求。它主要取決於名義國民收入 (Nominal Income)。在高中經濟學圖表中，它通常被繪製為垂直線，表示它不受名義利率影響（完全缺乏彈性）。
    Transaction demand for money \( (M_t) \): The demand to hold money for daily transactions. It primarily depends on Nominal Income. In high school economics, it is usually drawn as a vertical line, implying it is perfectly inelastic to the nominal interest rate.
  • 資產性貨幣需求 \( (M_a) \): 作為保值資產而持有貨幣的需求。它與名義利率 (Nominal Interest Rate) 呈反比（因此曲線向下傾斜）。此外，它亦受其他資產的風險回報率影響。
    Asset demand for money \( (M_a) \): The demand to hold money as a store of value. It is inversely related to the Nominal Interest Rate (hence a downward-sloping curve). It is also affected by the risk and return of alternative assets.
$$ M_d = M_t(Y) + M_a(R, \text{Risk}) $$
Where \( M_d \) = Total Money Demand, \( Y \) = Nominal Income, \( R \) = Nominal Interest Rate

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

✅ 正確答案 Correct Option: A

當股票市場日趨波動，代表持有股票的風險 (Risk) 增加。投資者為求避險，會減少持有股票，轉而持有風險最低的資產——貨幣。這會導致在任何給定的名義利率下，人們對持有貨幣作為資產的需求增加，因此資產性貨幣需求曲線 \( (M_a) \) 向右平移。圖 A 正確顯示了這個轉變。
When the stock market becomes more volatile, the risk of holding stocks increases. To avoid risk, investors will reduce their stock holdings and switch to the safest asset: money. This causes an increase in the demand to hold money as an asset at any given nominal interest rate. Therefore, the asset demand for money curve \( (M_a) \) shifts to the right. Diagram A correctly illustrates this shift.

❌ 錯誤選項分析 (Analysis of Incorrect Options)

Option B:
圖 B 顯示的是沿著同一條 \( M_a \) 曲線向下滑動。這代表名義利率下降導致資產性貨幣需求量增加，而不是由外在因素（如股票風險增加）引起的整條曲線移動。
Diagram B shows a downward movement along the same \( M_a \) curve. This represents an increase in the quantity of asset money demanded caused by a decrease in the nominal interest rate, not a shift of the entire curve caused by an exogenous factor like stock market risk.
Option C:
圖 C 顯示交易性貨幣需求 \( (M_t) \) 向右平移。只有當物價水平上升或實質國民收入增加（即名義 GDP 上升）時，才會發生這種情況。股票市場波動不會直接影響日常交易所需的貨幣量。
Diagram C shows a rightward shift of the transaction demand for money \( (M_t) \). This only happens when the price level rises or real national income increases (i.e., nominal GDP increases). Stock market volatility does not directly affect the amount of money needed for daily transactions.
Option D:
圖 D 顯示沿著垂直的 \( M_t \) 曲線向下滑動。這純粹反映名義利率的下跌，並且對交易性貨幣需求量沒有影響（因為它是完全缺乏彈性的）。
Diagram D shows a downward movement along the vertical \( M_t \) curve. This purely reflects a drop in the nominal interest rate and has no effect on the quantity of transaction money demanded (since it is perfectly inelastic).

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解答這類題目的關鍵在於辨清「移動的原因」與「受影響的對象」：
The key to solving this type of question is identifying the "cause of the shift" and the "affected target":
  1. 尋找 Pivot Point (關鍵詞)：「股票市場波動」= 替代資產的風險改變。
    Find the Pivot Point (Keyword): "Stock market volatile" = Change in risk of alternative assets.
  2. 配對變數：風險改變會影響人們保值的選擇，因此對應的是 資產性貨幣需求 \( (M_a) \)，排除 C 和 D。
    Match the Variable: Changes in risk affect people's choices for storing value, which corresponds to the asset demand for money \( (M_a) \). Eliminate C and D.
  3. 區分 Shift (平移) vs Movement (沿線滑動)：因為改變的因素（風險）不是 Y 軸上的變數（名義利率），所以整條曲線會平移 (Shift)。風險上升，對安全資產（貨幣）的需求上升，曲線向右移。答案是 A。
    Distinguish Shift vs. Movement: Because the changing factor (risk) is NOT the variable on the Y-axis (nominal interest rate), the entire curve will shift. As risk rises, demand for safe assets (money) rises, shifting the curve right. The answer is A.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

⚠️ 陷阱一：混淆 \( M_a \) 與 \( M_t \)
許多同學會誤以為「股票交易」也是一種「交易」，因而錯誤選擇了改變交易性貨幣需求 (\( M_t \)) 的選項。在 DSE 經濟學中，\( M_t \) 嚴格用來購買最終物品和服務 (GDP 相關)。購買金融資產（如股票、債券）屬於財富管理，與資產性貨幣需求 (\( M_a \)) 相關。
⚠️ Trap 1: Confusing \( M_a \) and \( M_t \)
Many students mistakenly think "stock trading" is also a "transaction," and thus wrongly select options changing the transaction demand (\( M_t \)). In DSE Economics, \( M_t \) is strictly for purchasing final goods and services (GDP-related). Buying financial assets (stocks, bonds) is wealth management, which relates to the asset demand for money (\( M_a \)).

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

🙋‍♂️ 學生問：如果題目變成「信用卡被廣泛使用」，圖表會怎樣變？
🙋‍♂️ Student: If the question changes to "Credit cards are widely used," how would the diagram change?

👨‍🏫 老師答：廣泛使用信用卡會減少人們日常出街需要攜帶的現金。這影響的是交易性貨幣需求 \( (M_t) \)。因為在相同的收入水平下，需要的貨幣減少了，所以 \( M_t \) 曲線會向左平移（即圖 C 的反方向）。
👨‍🏫 Teacher: Widespread use of credit cards reduces the cash people need to carry for daily outings. This affects the transaction demand for money \( (M_t) \). Since less money is needed at the same income level, the \( M_t \) curve will shift to the left (the opposite of diagram C).

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

  • 秒殺口訣：
    見到「收入/物價/信用卡/發薪頻率」 👉 瞄準 垂直線 \( M_t \) 的左右平移。
    見到「風險/回報/股票/債券/通脹預期」 👉 瞄準 斜線 \( M_a \) 的左右平移。
    見到「利率本身改變」 👉 瞄準 沿線滑動 (Movement)。
    Shortcut Formula:
    See "Income/Prices/Credit cards/Pay frequency" 👉 Target left/right shift of the vertical \( M_t \) line.
    See "Risk/Returns/Stocks/Bonds/Inflation expectations" 👉 Target left/right shift of the sloped \( M_a \) line.
    See "Interest rate itself changes" 👉 Target Movement along the curve.
