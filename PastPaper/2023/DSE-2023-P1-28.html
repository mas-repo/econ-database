📊 經濟學試題分析指南
Economics Question Analysis Guide

主題 (Topic): 總體經濟學：總需求與總供應 (Macroeconomics: AD-AS Model)

難度 (Difficulty): ★★★☆☆ (3/5)

核心概念 (Key Concepts): Aggregate Demand (總需求) Aggregate Output (總產出) Fiscal Policy (財政政策)

📝 題目 (The Question)

假設某國的主要貿易夥伴的國民收入下降。下列哪項會抵銷其對該國總產出的影響？

A. 政府提高薪俸稅退稅金額。
B. 政府對入境旅客實施更嚴緊的隔離要求。
C. 廠商對該國的經濟前景變得悲觀。
D. 業主提高寫字樓及零售鋪位的租金。
Suppose there is a fall in the national income of a country’s major trading partner. Which of the following would offset its effect on the aggregate output of the country?

A. The government increases the amount of tax rebate on salaries tax.
B. The government imposes stricter quarantine requirements for inbound travellers.
C. Firms become pessimistic about the economic prospect of the country.
D. Landlords raise the rent of their office and retail premises.

🧠 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解答這道題目，我們需要明白總需求 (Aggregate Demand, AD) 的組成部分，以及甚麼因素會導致總需求曲線移動。

To solve this question, we need to understand the components of Aggregate Demand (AD) and the factors that shift the AD curve.

$$AD = C + I + G + (X - M)$$
  • $C$ (Consumption / 私人消費開支): 受可支配收入 (Disposable Income) 影響。退稅會增加可支配收入，從而帶動 $C$ 上升。
    Affected by disposable income. Tax rebates increase disposable income, leading to an increase in $C$.
  • $I$ (Investment / 投資開支): 受營商預期 (Business Expectations) 影響。悲觀預期會令 $I$ 下降。
    Affected by business expectations. Pessimism causes $I$ to decrease.
  • $X$ (Exports / 出口): 受貿易夥伴的國民收入影響。夥伴收入下降，他們對我國產品的需求便會減少，$X$ 隨之下降。
    Affected by trading partners' national income. If their income falls, their demand for our goods falls, thus $X$ decreases.

✅ 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): A

📌 初始衝擊分析 (Initial Shock Analysis):
主要貿易夥伴的國民收入下降 $\rightarrow$ 夥伴國減少進口 $\rightarrow$ 該國出口 ($X$) 下降 $\rightarrow$ 總需求 ($AD$) 減少 $\rightarrow$ 總產出 ($Y$) 下跌。因此，我們需要找出一個能令總產出上升的選項來「抵銷」這個跌幅。

📌 Initial Shock Analysis:
Fall in trading partner's income $\rightarrow$ Partner imports less $\rightarrow$ The country's exports ($X$) fall $\rightarrow$ Aggregate Demand ($AD$) decreases $\rightarrow$ Aggregate Output ($Y$) falls. Therefore, we need to find an option that increases aggregate output to "offset" this drop.

選項 A 分析 (正確):
政府提高薪俸稅退稅 $\rightarrow$ 市民的可支配收入增加 $\rightarrow$ 消費 ($C$) 增加 $\rightarrow$ 總需求 ($AD$) 上升 $\rightarrow$ 總產出 ($Y$) 上升。這正好與初始衝擊方向相反，產生抵銷作用。

Option A Analysis (Correct):
Government increases tax rebate $\rightarrow$ Citizens' disposable income increases $\rightarrow$ Consumption ($C$) rises $\rightarrow$ Aggregate Demand ($AD$) increases $\rightarrow$ Aggregate Output ($Y$) rises. This works in the opposite direction of the initial shock, creating an offset effect.

選項 B 分析 (錯誤):
嚴緊的隔離要求會打擊旅遊業 (服務輸出) $\rightarrow$ 出口 ($X$) 進一步下降 $\rightarrow$ $AD$ 再減少，令總產出跌幅加劇，而非抵銷。

Option B Analysis (Incorrect):
Stricter quarantine requirements hit the tourism industry (export of services) $\rightarrow$ Exports ($X$) fall further $\rightarrow$ $AD$ decreases more, exacerbating the fall in aggregate output rather than offsetting it.

選項 C 分析 (錯誤):
廠商前景悲觀 $\rightarrow$ 減少投資 ($I$) $\rightarrow$ $AD$ 下降，同樣會加劇總產出的跌幅。

Option C Analysis (Incorrect):
Firms become pessimistic $\rightarrow$ Investment ($I$) decreases $\rightarrow$ $AD$ falls, which also exacerbates the drop in aggregate output.

選項 D 分析 (錯誤):
租金上升 $\rightarrow$ 生產成本增加 $\rightarrow$ 短期總供應 ($SRAS$) 減少向左移 $\rightarrow$ 總產出下降並推高物價。這亦是加劇了產出下跌的問題。

Option D Analysis (Incorrect):
Rent increases $\rightarrow$ Cost of production rises $\rightarrow$ Short-Run Aggregate Supply ($SRAS$) shifts left $\rightarrow$ Aggregate output falls while price level rises. This worsens the output decline.

🔍 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解答此類題目的關鍵在於理解「抵銷 (Offset)」一詞。這是一個兩步推論題：

  1. 判斷初始事件的影響： 貿易夥伴變窮 = 出口跌 = 總產出跌 (向下箭頭 $\downarrow$)。
  2. 尋找相反的變量 (Pivot Point)： 要「抵銷」向下的影響，正確答案必須是一個帶來正面擴張效應、令總產出上升的事件 (向上箭頭 $\uparrow$)。

The key to solving this type of question lies in understanding the word "Offset". This is a two-step deduction problem:

  1. Determine the impact of the initial event: Trading partner gets poorer = Exports fall = Aggregate output falls (Downward arrow $\downarrow$).
  2. Find the opposite variable (Pivot Point): To "offset" a downward impact, the correct answer must be an event that brings a positive expansionary effect, causing aggregate output to rise (Upward arrow $\uparrow$).

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • 誤解「抵銷」為「加劇」： 很多學生在壓力下看到題目開頭是壞消息 (收入下降)，便慣性地在選項中尋找另一個壞消息 (如 B、C 或 D)，忘記了「抵銷」代表需要找好消息 (擴張性政策)。
    Confusing "Offset" with "Exacerbate": Under pressure, seeing bad news (fall in income) in the prompt, many students instinctively look for another bad news in the options (like B, C, or D), forgetting that "offset" means looking for good news (expansionary policy).
  • 混淆稅收與政府開支 ($G$)： 退稅 (Tax rebate) 改變的是家庭的可支配收入，從而影響消費 ($C$)。它並不直接等同於政府支出 ($G$) 的增加。
    Confusing Taxes with Government Expenditure ($G$): A tax rebate changes households' disposable income, thus affecting consumption ($C$). It is not a direct increase in government expenditure ($G$).

🙋 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

問 (Q)：為什麼選項 D 是影響總供應 (AS) 而不是總需求 (AD)？

Q: Why does Option D affect Aggregate Supply (AS) rather than Aggregate Demand (AD)?

答 (A)： 租金是企業做生意的生產成本 (Cost of production) 之一。在宏觀經濟學中，任何全經濟範圍內的生產成本變動 (如工資、租金、原材料價格)，都會直接導致短期總供應曲線 ($SRAS$) 的移動。成本上升會令生產者在同一物價水平下減少生產，因此 $SRAS$ 向左移。

A: Rent is one of the costs of production for businesses. In macroeconomics, any economy-wide change in production costs (e.g., wages, rent, raw material prices) directly causes a shift in the Short-Run Aggregate Supply ($SRAS$) curve. Higher costs lead producers to supply less at the same price level, thus shifting $SRAS$ to the left.

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

方向排除法 (Directional Elimination Method) 🎯

在選擇題中，這招非常快！題目問「抵銷」一個負面衝擊 (令產出 $\downarrow$)，代表答案必須是一個正面衝擊 (令產出 $\uparrow$)。我們給四個選項標記正負號：

  • A. 退稅 $\rightarrow$ 派錢 $\rightarrow$ 好事 (產出 $\uparrow$)
  • B. 隔離 $\rightarrow$ 無旅客 $\rightarrow$ 壞事 (產出 $\downarrow$)
  • C. 悲觀 $\rightarrow$ 無投資 $\rightarrow$ 壞事 (產出 $\downarrow$)
  • D. 加租 $\rightarrow$ 成本增 $\rightarrow$ 壞事 (產出 $\downarrow$)

考評局的題目設計中，B、C、D 都是導致產出下跌的因素，唯獨 A 的方向不同 (Odd one out)。不用畫圖，5秒就能鎖定答案！

Directional Elimination Method 🎯

This is a very fast trick for MCQs! The question asks to "offset" a negative shock (Output $\downarrow$), meaning the answer must be a positive shock (Output $\uparrow$). Let's tag the options with positive/negative signs:

  • A. Tax rebate $\rightarrow$ More money $\rightarrow$ Good news (Output $\uparrow$)
  • B. Quarantine $\rightarrow$ No tourists $\rightarrow$ Bad news (Output $\downarrow$)
  • C. Pessimistic $\rightarrow$ No investment $\rightarrow$ Bad news (Output $\downarrow$)
  • D. Rent hike $\rightarrow$ Higher costs $\rightarrow$ Bad news (Output $\downarrow$)

In the HKEAA's question design, B, C, and D are all factors causing output to fall. Only A has a different direction (Odd one out). You can lock in the answer in 5 seconds without drawing a single curve!
