Topic (課題): International Trade - Balance of Payments (國際貿易 - 國際收支平衡)

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐ (2/5)

Key Concepts (核心概念): Visible Trade (有形貿易), Trade Surplus (貿易順差), Value of Exports & Imports (出口與進口價值)

The Question (題目)

下圖顯示某國從2018年至2022年間物品出口及進口價值。

從 __________ 年至 __________ 年間，該國的物品進口價值和有形貿易順差同時上升。

A. 2018 …… 2019
B. 2019 …… 2020
C. 2020 …… 2021
D. 2021 …… 2022

The figure below shows the values of a country's exports and imports of goods from 2018 to 2022.

From year __________ to year __________, the country had an increase in the value of imports of goods and an increase in visible trade surplus at the same time.

A. 2018 …… 2019
B. 2019 …… 2020
C. 2020 …… 2021
D. 2021 …… 2022

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這道題目，我們必須清楚兩個核心概念：物品進口價值與有形貿易順差。在圖表中，「物品出口價值」由實線表示，而「物品進口價值」由虛線表示。

To solve this question, we must understand two core concepts: Value of imports of goods and Visible trade surplus. In the graph, "Value of exports of goods" is represented by the solid line, while "Value of imports of goods" is represented by the dashed line.

有形貿易餘額 (Visible Trade Balance) 的定義是：

The definition of Visible Trade Balance is:
$$ \text{Visible Trade Balance} = \text{Value of Exports of Goods} - \text{Value of Imports of Goods} $$

當出口大於進口時，這被稱為貿易順差 (Trade Surplus)。在圖表上，這就是實線（出口）高於虛線（進口）時的垂直距離。

When exports are greater than imports, this is called a Trade Surplus. On a graph, this is the vertical distance between the solid line (exports) and the dashed line (imports) when the solid line is above the dashed line.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是：選項 C (Option C)

The correct answer is: Option C

A. 2018 …… 2019: 錯誤。雖然物品進口價值（虛線）上升了，但兩條線之間的垂直距離（順差）收窄了。出口維持在約 $17$，但進口由約 $14$ 升至 $15$，順差由 $3$ 跌至 $2$。

Incorrect. Although the value of imports (dashed line) increased, the vertical distance between the two lines (the surplus) narrowed. Exports remained at approx. $17$, but imports rose from approx. $14$ to $15$, decreasing the surplus from $3$ to $2$.

B. 2019 …… 2020: 錯誤。物品進口價值（虛線）繼續上升，但兩條線的距離進一步收窄，幾乎相交。這意味著貿易順差正在下降。

Incorrect. The value of imports (dashed line) continued to increase, but the distance between the two lines narrowed further, almost intersecting. This means the trade surplus was decreasing.

C. 2020 …… 2021: 正確。物品進口價值（虛線）從2020年到2021年呈上升趨勢。同時，實線（出口）上升的幅度比虛線（進口）大得多，導致兩者之間的垂直距離顯著擴闊（從約 $0.5$ 增加到 $6$）。因此，進口和順差同時上升。

Correct. The value of imports (dashed line) trended upwards from 2020 to 2021. At the same time, the solid line (exports) rose much faster than the dashed line (imports), causing the vertical distance between them to widen significantly (surplus increased from approx. $0.5$ to $6$). Therefore, both imports and the surplus increased simultaneously.

D. 2021 …… 2022: 錯誤。雖然這段時間內的有形貿易順差（兩條線的距離）確實增加了，但物品進口價值（虛線）是下降的。

Incorrect. Although the visible trade surplus (the distance between the lines) did increase during this period, the value of imports (dashed line) decreased.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

樞紐點 (The Pivot Point): 這是一道「雙條件」題目。你必須同時滿足兩個條件才能得分：
1. 虛線必須呈上升趨勢（進口上升）。
2. 實線與虛線之間的差距必須拉大（順差上升）。

The Pivot Point: This is a "dual-condition" question. You must satisfy both conditions simultaneously to get the mark:
1. The dashed line must slope upwards (imports increase).
2. The gap between the solid and dashed lines must widen (surplus increases).

干擾資訊 (Distractors): 題目從來沒有問過「出口價值」是升還是跌。因此，單單看實線的升跌是沒有意義的，實線的作用僅僅是用來與虛線相減以找出順差。

Distractors: The question never asks whether the "value of exports" increased or decreased. Therefore, looking solely at the trend of the solid line is meaningless; its only purpose is to be subtracted from the dashed line to find the surplus.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 我需要準確讀出圖表上的數字才能計算出順差嗎？
A: 不需要！這是一個常見的誤解。在這種選擇題中，你只需要觀察線條的「趨勢」和「相對距離」。只要實線高於虛線，且兩者距離越來越闊，就代表順差在上升。浪費時間去估算確切的數字反而容易出錯。

Q: Do I need to read the exact numbers on the graph to calculate the surplus?
A: No! This is a common misconception. In this type of multiple-choice question, you only need to observe the "trend" of the lines and their "relative distance." As long as the solid line is above the dashed line, and the gap between them is getting wider, it means the surplus is increasing. Wasting time estimating exact figures can easily lead to mistakes.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

視覺淘汰法 (Visual Elimination Method):

Visual Elimination Method:

不要逐一檢查選項，而是運用邏輯快速排除：

步驟一：題目要求「進口價值上升」。查看圖表的虛線，發現在 2021 至 2022 年間虛線是向下的。因此，立刻秒殺排除 選項 D。

步驟二：題目要求「順差上升」（即兩線差距擴大）。觀察圖表，2018 至 2020 年間，兩條線明顯越靠越近，代表順差正在縮小。因此，立刻排除 選項 A 和 B。

剩下的 選項 C 自然是唯一正確答案。整個過程不需要任何計算，5秒內即可解題！

Do not check the options one by one; use logic to quickly eliminate them:
Step 1: The question requires an "increase in imports." Look at the dashed line on the graph. It goes down from 2021 to 2022. Immediately eliminate Option D.
Step 2: The question requires an "increase in surplus" (the gap widens). Observe the graph from 2018 to 2020; the two lines are clearly getting closer together, meaning the surplus is shrinking. Immediately eliminate Options A and B.
The remaining Option C must be the correct answer. The whole process requires zero calculation and can be solved in under 5 seconds!
