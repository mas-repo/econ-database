Topic / 主題: Market Intervention & Efficiency (市場干預與效率)

Difficulty / 難度: ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4/5 Stars)

Key Concepts / 核心概念: Price Ceiling (價格上限), Deadweight Loss (效率損失), Under-production (生產不足), Marginal Benefit vs. Marginal Cost (邊際利益與邊際成本)

📝 The Question (題目)

下圖顯示物品Z的供需曲線。

假設價格上限設於 \(P_1\)。當物品Z的需求從 \(D_1\) 上升至 \(D_2\)，會出現 __________ ，效率損失為 __________。

  1. 過度生產 …… 面積 abc
  2. 過度生產 …… 面積 acf
  3. 生產不足 …… 面積 abc
  4. 生產不足 …… 面積 acf

The following diagram shows the supply and demand curves of Good Z.

Suppose a price ceiling is set at \(P_1\). When the demand for Good Z increases from \(D_1\) to \(D_2\), there is __________ and the deadweight loss is __________.

  1. over-production …… area abc
  2. over-production …… area acf
  3. under-production …… area abc
  4. under-production …… area acf

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

要拆解這條題目，我們必須掌握兩個核心概念：價格上限 (Price Ceiling) 以及 效率損失 (Deadweight Loss)。

To crack this question, we must master two core concepts: Price Ceiling and Deadweight Loss.
  • 價格上限 (Price Ceiling): 政府設定的最高合法價格。當價格上限低於市場均衡價格時，它便具有約束力 (Binding)，會導致供不應求 (Shortage)。此時，實際交易量由供應量 (Quantity Supplied) 決定，因為買家無法強迫賣家在虧本或不願意的情況下生產。
    The maximum legally allowable price set by the government. When set below the equilibrium price, it is binding and causes a shortage. The actual transacted quantity is determined by the quantity supplied, as buyers cannot force sellers to produce more than they are willing to at that price.
  • 效率 (Efficiency) 與 最優產量 (Optimal Quantity): 當邊際利益等於邊際成本 (\(MB = MC\)) 時，總社會盈餘最大化，這就是最優產量。在圖表中，這通常是需求曲線和供應曲線的相交點。
    Maximum total social surplus occurs when Marginal Benefit equals Marginal Cost (\(MB = MC\)). On a graph, this is the intersection of the demand and supply curves.
  • 效率損失 / 淨損失 (Deadweight Loss): 由於市場未能以最優產量運作而導致的潛在總盈餘損失。它等於未生產單位（或過度生產單位）的邊際利益與邊際成本之間的差額總和。
    The loss of potential total surplus because the market is not producing at the optimal quantity. It equals the sum of the differences between marginal benefit and marginal cost for the unproduced (or over-produced) units.
$$ \text{Deadweight Loss} = \sum (\text{Marginal Benefit} - \text{Marginal Cost}) \quad \text{for units not produced} $$

*紅色高亮區域為效率損失 (Red highlighted area is the Deadweight Loss)

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: C

Step-by-Step Breakdown (逐步拆解):

第一步：確認新的市場狀況與最優產量
當需求由 \(D_1\) 上升至 \(D_2\)，新的市場均衡點（如果沒有價格管制）應該在點 \(c\)。因此，社會最優產量 (Socially optimal quantity) 是點 \(c\) 對應的數量 (\(Q_c\))。

Step 1: Identify new market conditions and optimal quantity.
When demand shifts from \(D_1\) to \(D_2\), the new unregulated market equilibrium would be at point \(c\). Thus, the socially optimal quantity is the quantity corresponding to point \(c\) (\(Q_c\)).

第二步：分析價格上限的影響 (過度生產 vs 生產不足)
政府將價格限制在 \(P_1\)。在這個價格下，生產者只願意提供點 \(a\) 對應的數量 (\(Q_a\))，即使消費者在該價格下需求量達到點 \(f\) (\(Q_f\))。因為實際交易量不能超過生產者的供應量，所以市場只會生產及交易 \(Q_a\)。
因為實際產量 \(Q_a\) 小於 最優產量 \(Q_c\) (\(Q_a < Q_c\))，這代表資源未被充分利用，出現了生產不足 (Under-production)。這立刻排除了選項 A 和 B。

Step 2: Analyze the impact of the price ceiling (Over vs. Under-production).
The price is capped at \(P_1\). At this price, suppliers are only willing to produce the quantity at point \(a\) (\(Q_a\)), even though consumers demand quantity \(f\) (\(Q_f\)). Since the transacted quantity is constrained by supply, only \(Q_a\) is produced.
Because the actual quantity \(Q_a\) is less than the optimal quantity \(Q_c\) (\(Q_a < Q_c\)), there is under-production. This immediately eliminates Options A and B.

第三步：找出效率損失 (Deadweight Loss) 的面積
效率損失是由於沒有生產 \(Q_a\) 到 \(Q_c\) 之間的單位而損失的總社會盈餘。對於這些單位，消費者願意支付的最高價格（由需求曲線 \(D_2\) 反映，即點 \(b\) 到點 \(c\) 的線段）高於生產者的邊際成本（由供應曲線 \(S\) 反映，即點 \(a\) 到點 \(c\) 的線段）。
這部分流失的淨價值，就是在實際產量線 (\(ab\))、需求曲線 (\(D_2\)) 和供應曲線 (\(S\)) 之間所包圍的三角形面積，也就是面積 abc。因此，正確答案是 C（排除了 D）。

Step 3: Determine the area of Deadweight Loss.
Deadweight loss is the total surplus lost because the units between \(Q_a\) and \(Q_c\) are not produced. For these units, the maximum price consumers are willing to pay (Marginal Benefit, shown by \(D_2\) from point \(b\) to \(c\)) is higher than the sellers' marginal cost (shown by \(S\) from point \(a\) to \(c\)).
The net value lost is the triangle bounded by the actual quantity line (\(ab\)), the demand curve (\(D_2\)), and the supply curve (\(S\)). This is area abc. Therefore, the correct answer is C (eliminating D).

🛠️ Problem Decomposition (題目拆解技巧)

The Pivot Point (解題樞紐):
這道題的關鍵在於判斷實際交易量 (Transacted Quantity)。很多同學看到需求上升到 \(D_2\)，就直覺以為數量會跟著跑到點 \(f\)。記住黃金法則：「買賣雙方，短板決定交易量」。在價格上限下，供應量 (\(a\)) 少於需求量 (\(f\))，所以只能成交到點 \(a\) 的數量。

The key to this question is identifying the transacted quantity. Many students see demand shift to \(D_2\) and mistakenly assume the quantity moves to point \(f\). Remember the golden rule: "The lesser of quantity supplied or demanded determines the transacted quantity." Under a binding price ceiling, supply (\(a\)) is less than demand (\(f\)), so only the quantity at point \(a\) is exchanged.

Distractors (干擾資訊):
圖中的點 \(f\) 和面積 \(acf\) 是出題者精心設計的陷阱。面積 \(acf\) 使用了人為的價格線 \(P_1\) 作為邊界，但請緊記：計算社會總盈餘和效率損失時，我們只看真正的邊際利益 (Demand) 和 真正的邊際成本 (Supply)，政府的價格管制線不代表產品的真實價值或成本！

Point \(f\) and area \(acf\) are deliberately designed traps. Area \(acf\) uses the artificial price line \(P_1\) as a boundary. Always remember: when calculating total social surplus and DWL, we only look at true marginal benefit (Demand) and true marginal cost (Supply). The government's price line does not reflect the true value or cost of the product!

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 名詞混淆： 誤以為「需求增加」就等於「過度生產 (Over-production)」。過度生產是指實際產量大於市場自由運作的最優產量（例如政府提供補貼時才會發生）。價格上限通常導致短缺，即生產不足。
  • 劃錯三角形： 錯誤地將效率損失三角形的底部畫在價格線 \(P_1\) 上（選了面積 acf）。記住，效率損失的尖端永遠指向新的自由市場均衡點 (點 \(c\))。
  • Confusing terms: Assuming "increase in demand" equates to "over-production." Over-production means producing more than the efficient free-market quantity (which typically happens with government subsidies). Price ceilings generally cause shortages, hence under-production.
  • Drawing the wrong triangle: Incorrectly placing the base of the DWL triangle on the price line \(P_1\) (choosing area acf). Remember, the tip of the DWL triangle always points toward the new efficient free-market equilibrium (point \(c\)).

🙋 Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

學生問： 點解唔係生產點 \(f\) 嘅數量？需求曲線明明去到點 \(f\) 呀？
解答： 需求曲線去到點 \(f\) 只是代表「消費者在價格 \(P_1\) 時想買這麼多」。但市場是雙向的。在價格 \(P_1\) 時，供應商覺得無利可圖，只願意生產到點 \(a\)。消費者無法強迫供應商生產他們不願意生產的數量。因此，實際產量停在點 \(a\)。

Student: Why isn't the production at point \(f\)? The demand curve clearly reaches point \(f\)!
Tutor: Point \(f\) only represents how much consumers want to buy at price \(P_1\). But a market is two-sided. At price \(P_1\), suppliers find it unprofitable to produce more and are only willing to supply up to point \(a\). Consumers cannot force suppliers to produce what they don't want to. Thus, actual production stops at point \(a\).

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

在考場上想用 10 秒鐘解出這題？記住這個口訣：

  1. 有效價格上限 (Binding Price Ceiling) 必導致短缺 $\rightarrow$ 必定是「生產不足 (Under-production)」。 直接劃走選項 A 和 B。
  2. 效率損失 (DWL) 的三角形，其頂點永遠指向「如果沒有政府干預時的均衡點」。 在這題中，沒有干預的均衡點是點 \(c\)。包含點 \(c\) 且指向點 \(c\) 的三角形只有 \(abc\)。直接選 C！
Want to solve this in 10 seconds during the exam? Remember this cheat code:
  1. A binding price ceiling always causes a shortage $\rightarrow$ It MUST be "under-production". Immediately eliminate A and B.
  2. The tip of the Deadweight Loss (DWL) triangle always points exactly to the "unregulated market equilibrium point". Here, the unregulated equilibrium is point \(c\). The triangle pointing to \(c\) bounded by the curves is \(abc\). Pick C!
