概念重溫 (Concept Review)

寡頭壟斷 (Oligopoly)

寡頭壟斷是指市場由少數幾家大型企業主導的市場結構。這些企業擁有巨大的市場佔有率（Market Share），對價格有顯著的控制力。

An oligopoly is a market structure dominated by a few large firms. These firms hold a massive market share and have significant control over prices.

市場集中度與集中率 (Market Concentration and Concentration Ratio)

經濟學家通常使用集中率 (Concentration Ratio) 來衡量市場結構。最常見的是「四強集中率」，即計算市場上最大的四家企業的市場佔有率總和。

Economists often use the Concentration Ratio to measure market structure. The most common is the "Four-Firm Concentration Ratio", which calculates the sum of the market shares of the four largest firms in the market.
$$ CR_n = \sum_{i=1}^{n} S_i $$

公式中，\( CR_n \) 代表首 \( n \) 家企業的集中率，而 \( S_i \) 代表第 \( i \) 家企業的市場佔有率。如果 \( CR_4 \) 的數值很高（通常大於 50%），則強烈顯示該市場屬於寡頭壟斷。

In the formula, \( CR_n \) represents the concentration ratio of the top \( n \) firms, and \( S_i \) represents the market share of the \( i \)-th firm. If the value of \( CR_4 \) is high (typically greater than 50%), it strongly indicates that the market is an oligopoly.

答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): Option B

❌ Option A 寡頭壟斷 …… 賣家的營銷策略互為影響

分析： 雖然寡頭壟斷市場中的企業確實存在互相牽制（interdependence）的特徵，但題目給出的表格只顯示了市場佔有率的數字，並沒有提供任何關於這些公司如何制定營銷策略或互相影響的資料。我們不能從純粹的數字比例中「得出此結論」。

Analysis: While it is true that firms in an oligopoly are interdependent, the table provided only shows market share figures. It provides absolutely no data on how these companies formulate their marketing strategies or influence each other. We cannot "conclude" interdependence solely from percentage figures.

✅ Option B 寡頭壟斷 …… 市場上有數家具支配力的賣家

分析： 根據表格數據，最大的四家公司（宏利、匯豐、永明、友邦）總共佔據了 63.9% (即 25.8% + 18.6% + 10.4% + 9.1%) 的市場份額。這個極高的集中度客觀地證明了市場由少數幾家大型（具支配力的）賣家主導，這正是寡頭壟斷的定義，且直接受到數據支持。

Analysis: Based on the table's data, the top four firms (Manulife, HSBC, Sun Life, AIA) collectively account for 63.9% (i.e., 25.8% + 18.6% + 10.4% + 9.1%) of the market share. This extremely high concentration ratio objectively proves that the market is dominated by a few large (dominant) sellers. This is the definition of an oligopoly and is directly supported by the provided data.

❌ Option C 壟斷性競爭 …… 市場上有大量賣家

分析： 壟斷性競爭市場的特徵是每個賣家的市場佔有率都很小。然而，表格顯示單是宏利一家就佔了 25.8%，這與「大量且各自佔有率微小」的特徵不符。因此，這不是壟斷性競爭市場。

Analysis: A monopolistic competition market is characterized by each seller having a very small market share. However, the table shows that Manulife alone takes up 25.8%, which contradicts the feature of having "many sellers with insignificant individual market shares." Therefore, this is not monopolistic competition.

❌ Option D 壟斷性競爭 …… 不同賣家所提供的服務是異質的

分析： 雖然強積金服務確實可能有一定程度的異質性（產品差異化），但這與選項 A 犯了相同的錯誤：表格提供的數字無法證明服務是否存在異質性。我們只能根據已知的數據來得出結論。

Analysis: Although MPF services might indeed have some degree of heterogeneity (product differentiation), this option commits the same logical fallacy as Option A: the numbers provided in the table cannot prove whether the services are heterogeneous or not. We must base our conclusion strictly on the given data.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解答這類題目的樞紐點 (Pivot Point) 在於題目的引言：「根據以上資料 (Based on the above information)」。

The Pivot Point for answering this type of question lies in the prompt's instruction: "Based on the above information".
  • 分辨事實與推論： 選項 A 的後半部分（策略互為影響）在經濟學理論上是正確的，但它是一個與表格無關的「干擾項 (Distractor)」。表格只有數字，數字只能反映規模、比例和集中度。
  • Distinguish Fact from Inference: The second half of Option A (interdependent strategies) is theoretically correct in economics, but it acts as a "Distractor" that is unrelated to the table. The table only contains numbers, and numbers can only reflect scale, proportion, and concentration.
  • 計算驗證： 看到市場佔有率，第一時間應該計算龍頭企業的總和。前四名總和達 63.9%，這是一個強烈的寡頭壟斷信號。
  • Verification by Calculation: When you see market shares, your immediate reflex should be to calculate the sum of the top firms. The top four sum up to 63.9%, which is a strong signal of an oligopoly.

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

學生最常犯的錯誤是死背特徵，忽略證據。很多同學看到「寡頭壟斷」，腦海中立刻浮現「互相牽制 (interdependence)」，便毫不猶豫地選擇了 A。他們忘記了 DSE 考試中，如果題目提供了圖表或數據，答案的解釋部分必須能直接從該圖表中觀察出來。

The most common mistake students make is rote-memorizing features while ignoring evidence. Many students see "oligopoly," immediately think of "interdependence," and unhesitatingly choose A. They forget that in DSE exams, if a question provides a chart or data, the explanation part of the answer must be directly observable from that specific chart/data.

學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 表格中明明寫著「其他 36.1%」，這可能包含了一百間小公司。既然有這麼多公司，為什麼不能說是「壟斷性競爭」？

Q: The table clearly says "Others 36.1%", which could include a hundred small companies. Since there are so many companies, why can't we call it "monopolistic competition"?

A: 好問題！判定市場結構的關鍵不在於市場上「總共有多少間公司」，而在於「市場是否被少數公司支配」。即使「其他」包含了 100 間公司，它們平均每間也只有 0.36% 的佔有率。相比之下，宏利一間就佔了 25.8%。當少數幾家公司擁有壓倒性的市場佔有率時，這些小公司無法對市場價格起顯著影響，因此該市場依然是由巨頭主導的寡頭壟斷。

A: Great question! The key to determining market structure isn't "how many companies exist in total," but rather "whether the market is dominated by a few firms." Even if "Others" includes 100 companies, they would average only a 0.36% share each. In contrast, Manulife alone holds 25.8%. When a few firms have an overwhelming market share, the small companies cannot significantly affect market prices. Therefore, the market remains an oligopoly dominated by the giants.

考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

「眼見為實」法則 (The "Seeing is Believing" Rule)：

在應付 MC 題時，如果題目給你的是「數據表 (Data Table)」，答案的後半部（解釋部分）必須是關於「量度 (Measurement)」的描述（例如：數量少、市佔率高）。

如果題目給你的是一段「文字描述 (Text Description)」（例如：A公司推出減價優惠，B公司立即跟隨），那麼答案的解釋部分才會是關於「行為 (Behavior)」（例如：互相牽制、非價格競爭）。善用這個分類法，你可以瞬間排除 A 和 D！

When tackling MC questions, if the prompt gives you a "Data Table", the second half of the answer (the explanation) must be a description of a "Measurement" (e.g., few in number, high market share).

If the prompt gives you a "Text Description" (e.g., Company A launches a discount, and Company B immediately follows), only then will the explanation relate to "Behavior" (e.g., interdependence, non-price competition). By using this categorization trick, you can instantly eliminate options A and D without overthinking!
