HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Opportunity Cost (機會成本) Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆ Keywords: Highest-valued option forgone, Mutually exclusive

一塊土地可用來建造高速鐵路、高速公路、隧道或文化區綜合發展項目。假設以上各項可帶來的預期回報的金錢價值分別為 $800,000、$750,000、$650,000 及 $550,000。

使用該塊土地來建造高速公路的機會成本是__________;而使用該塊土地來建造隧道的機會成本是__________。

A piece of land can be used for the construction of a high speed railway, a highway, a tunnel or a cultural hub. Suppose the money value of the expected return generated by each project is $800,000, $750,000, $650,000 and $550,000 respectively.

The opportunity cost of using this piece of land for the construction of a highway is _________ and the opportunity cost of using this piece of land for the construction of a tunnel is _________.

  • A. $650,000 ..... $550,000
  • B. $750,000 ..... $650,000
  • C. $800,000 ..... $750,000
  • D. $800,000 ..... $800,000

Concept Review (概念重溫)

定義 Definition:

機會成本是指在作出選擇時，被放棄的選項中價值最高的一項。

Opportunity cost is the value of the highest-valued option forgone when a choice is made.
$$ \text{Opportunity Cost} = \text{Value of the best alternative option} $$ $$ \text{機會成本} = \text{被放棄而價值最高的選擇之價值} $$

關鍵要點 Key Points:

  • 機會成本不是所有被放棄選項的總和。
    Opportunity cost is NOT the sum of all forgone options.
  • 機會成本只看「第二好」的選擇 (如果選擇了最好的)。
    It looks at the "next best" alternative.

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: D ($800,000 ... $800,000)

Step 1: 排列價值 Ranking the Values

首先，我們將所有選項按價值由高至低排列：
First, let's rank all options by value from highest to lowest:

  1. 高速鐵路 (High Speed Railway): $800,000 (Highest/最高)
  2. 高速公路 (Highway): $750,000
  3. 隧道 (Tunnel): $650,000
  4. 文化區 (Cultural Hub): $550,000

Step 2: 分析第一個情況 Analyzing Case 1

選擇：建造高速公路 ($750,000)
Choice: Constructing a Highway ($750,000)

被放棄的選項包括：高速鐵路 ($800k)、隧道 ($650k)、文化區 ($550k)。
Options forgone include: HSR ($800k), Tunnel ($650k), Cultural Hub ($550k).

在被放棄的選項中，價值最高的是高速鐵路 ($800,000)。
Among the forgone options, the highest value is the High Speed Railway ($800,000).

➜ 機會成本 = $800,000

Step 3: 分析第二個情況 Analyzing Case 2

選擇：建造隧道 ($650,000)
Choice: Constructing a Tunnel ($650,000)

被放棄的選項包括：高速鐵路 ($800k)、高速公路 ($750k)、文化區 ($550k)。
Options forgone include: HSR ($800k), Highway ($750k), Cultural Hub ($550k).

在被放棄的選項中，價值最高的依然是高速鐵路 ($800,000)。
Among the forgone options, the highest value is still the High Speed Railway ($800,000).

➜ 機會成本 = $800,000

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的核心在於找出「首選」是什麼。
The core of this type of question is to identify the "First Choice".

Pivot Point (關鍵點):

只要你沒有選擇價值最高的項目（高速鐵路 $800,000），無論你選擇了其他哪一個項目（公路、隧道或文化區），你放棄的「最好」選擇永遠都是高速鐵路。

As long as you do not choose the highest-valued project (HSR $800,000), no matter which other project you choose (Highway, Tunnel, or Hub), the "best" option you gave up is ALWAYS the High Speed Railway.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

錯誤觀念 1：誤以為機會成本是「第二好」的價值

有些同學在第二個情況（建隧道）時，會誤以為機會成本是高速公路 ($750,000)，因為它是剩餘選項中比隧道好的那一個。這是錯誤的！機會成本必須與所有被放棄的選項比較，而不僅僅是比自己好的選項。

Misconception 1: Thinking Opportunity Cost is the "Second Best" value relative to the choice.
Some students think in Case 2 (Tunnel), the cost is the Highway ($750,000) because it's the next one up. This is wrong! Opportunity cost compares against ALL forgone options. Since HSR ($800,000) was also forgone, it is the cost.
錯誤觀念 2：計算利潤而非成本

有些同學會嘗試用 $800,000 減去 $750,000。這是在計算「經濟利潤」(Economic Profit) 或損失，而不是機會成本。

Misconception 2: Calculating Profit instead of Cost.
Some students try to subtract $750,000 from $800,000. This calculates Economic Profit/Loss, not Opportunity Cost.

Exam Tricks (考試技巧)

快速檢查法 (The "Best Option" Rule):

  1. 找出價值最高的選項 (Find the Max Value) → $800,000
  2. 問自己：題目中的選擇是這個 $800,000 嗎？ (Is the choice this $800,000?)
  3. 如果不是 (If NO): 機會成本就是 $800,000。
    (因為你放棄了最好的。)
  4. 如果是 (If YES): 機會成本就是第二高的價值。
    (因為你選了最好的，放棄了第二好的。)

在本題中，兩次選擇都不是 $800,000，所以兩次的答案直接就是 $800,000。秒殺！
In this question, neither choice was the $800,000 option. Therefore, the answer for both is simply $800,000. Solved instantly!
