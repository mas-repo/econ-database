2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解決這類題目，我們必須清楚理解「受價者」的特徵以及如何達到「利潤極大化」。

  • 受價者 (Price-taker): 面對完全競爭市場，廠商不能改變價格，只能接受市場價格。因此，邊際收益 \(MR\) 會等於價格 \(P\)。
  • 利潤極大化條件 (Profit-Maximizing Condition): 當邊際成本 \(MC\) 等於邊際收益 \(MR\) 時，利潤達到最高。對於受價者而言，這表示 \(P = MC\)。
$$ \text{Profit Maximization: } MC = MR = P $$

然而，題目只提供了平均成本 \(AC\)。我們需要運用以下公式來找出總成本 \(TC\) 和邊際成本 \(MC\)：

$$ TC = AC \times Q $$ $$ MC = \frac{\Delta TC}{\Delta Q} $$ $$ \text{Profit } (\pi) = TR - TC = (P \times Q) - (AC \times Q) $$

To solve this type of question, we must clearly understand the characteristics of a "price-taker" and how to achieve "profit maximization".

  • Price-taker: In a perfectly competitive market, a firm cannot change the price and must accept the market price. Therefore, Marginal Revenue \(MR\) equals Price \(P\).
  • Profit-Maximizing Condition: Profit is maximized when Marginal Cost \(MC\) equals Marginal Revenue \(MR\). For a price-taker, this means \(P = MC\).

However, the question only provides Average Cost \(AC\). We need to use formulas to find Total Cost \(TC\) and Marginal Cost \(MC\).

3. 答案與詳細分析 (Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是 / The correct option is: B

為了找出答案，我們首先需要擴展原本的表格，計算每個產出水平的總成本 \(TC\) 和邊際成本 \(MC\)。

選項 A (Option A): 4單位及$0

雖然產出量正確 (\(Q=4\))，但利潤計算錯誤。在 \(Q=4\) 時，總收益 \(TR = 4 \times 25 = 100\)，總成本 \(TC = 88\)。利潤應為 \(100 - 88 = 12\)，而不是 $0。

While the output is correct (\(Q=4\)), the profit calculation is wrong. At \(Q=4\), Total Revenue \(TR = 4 \times 25 = 100\), Total Cost \(TC = 88\). Profit should be \(100 - 88 = 12\), not $0.
選項 B (Option B): 4單位及$12

正確。 從我們計算的表格可見，當產出 \(Q=4\) 時，邊際成本 \(MC = 25\)。由於市場價格 \(P = 25\)，符合 \(P = MC\) 的利潤極大化條件。此時，利潤 \(\pi = TR - TC = (25 \times 4) - 88 = 100 - 88 = 12\)。

Correct. From our calculated table, when output \(Q=4\), Marginal Cost \(MC = 25\). Since the market price \(P = 25\), it fulfills the profit-maximizing condition \(P = MC\). At this point, Profit \(\pi = TR - TC = (25 \times 4) - 88 = 100 - 88 = 12\).
選項 C (Option C): 7單位及$0

這是最常見的陷阱選項！在 \(Q=7\) 時，平均成本 \(AC = 25\)，剛好等於價格 \(P=25\)。此時利潤的確是 $0 (\(TR=175, TC=175\))。但這只是「收支平衡點」(Break-even point)，並不是「利潤極大化」。這裏的 \(MC = 31\)，已經大於 \(P\)，代表生產第7件產品會帶來虧損。

This is the most common trap! At \(Q=7\), Average Cost \(AC = 25\), which equals Price \(P=25\). The profit is indeed $0. However, this is only the "Break-even point", not "Profit maximization". Here, \(MC = 31\), which is greater than \(P\), meaning producing the 7th unit brings a loss.
選項 D (Option D): 7單位及$12

產出量錯誤，且在此產出水平下的利潤計算也錯誤。在 \(Q=7\) 時，利潤應為 $0，絕不可能是 $12。

The output is wrong, and the profit calculation for this output level is also wrong. At \(Q=7\), the profit should be $0, it is impossible to be $12.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point):
解決這題的關鍵在於識破「平均成本」的偽裝。題目問的是「利潤極大化」，我們腦中必須立刻彈出公式：\(MC = MR\)。因為表格沒給 \(MC\)，我們必須自己動手把 \(TC\) 和 \(MC\) 算出來。

干擾資訊 (Distractors):
表格中 \(Q=7\) 時 \(AC=25\)，而題目又剛好設定市場價格 \(P=25\)。這是考評局精心設計的陷阱，專門用來欺騙那些把「\(P = MC\)」誤記為「\(P = AC\)」的同學。

The Pivot Point:
The key to solving this question is seeing through the disguise of "Average Cost". The question asks for "profit maximization", so the formula \(MC = MR\) must immediately pop into our heads. Since the table doesn't provide \(MC\), we must manually calculate \(TC\) and \(MC\).

Distractors:
In the table, when \(Q=7\), \(AC=25\), and the market price is exactly set at \(P=25\). This is a carefully designed trap by the HKEAA to trick students who mistakenly remember "\(P = MC\)" as "\(P = AC\)".

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

⚠️ 概念混淆：誤將 P = AC 當作極大化利潤
很多同學看到「市場價格 = $25」，立刻在表格裏找「25」這個數字，結果找到了 \(Q=7\) 時的 \(AC=25\)。請記住：\(P = AC\) 是「收支平衡」(賺取正常利潤) 的條件，而 \(P = MC\) 才是「爭取最高利潤」的條件！

⚠️ Conceptual Confusion: Mistaking P = AC for Profit Maximization
Many students see "Market price = $25" and immediately look for "25" in the table, finding \(AC=25\) at \(Q=7\). Remember: \(P = AC\) is the condition for "breaking even" (earning normal profit), while \(P = MC\) is the condition for "maximizing profit"!

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 在完全競爭市場中，廠商不是只能賺取「零經濟利潤」(正常利潤) 嗎？為什麼這裡的最高利潤是 $12？

A: 很好的問題！「零經濟利潤」是完全競爭市場在長期 (Long-run) 的均衡狀態。但題目問的是在目前的市場價格下（短期），廠商應該生產多少來獲取最高利潤。目前價格夠高（$25 > 最小平均成本），所以廠商可以賺取短期的「超額利潤」(Supernormal profit) $12。如果賺 $12 總好過賺 $0！

Q: In a perfectly competitive market, shouldn't a firm only earn "zero economic profit" (normal profit)? Why is the maximum profit $12 here?

A: Great question! "Zero economic profit" is the long-run equilibrium state for a perfectly competitive market. But this question is asking how much the firm should produce to maximize profit given the current market price (short-run). Currently, the price is high enough ($25 > Minimum AC), so the firm can earn a short-run "supernormal profit" of $12. Making $12 is definitely better than making $0!

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

💡 邏輯秒殺法 (Logic Check Elimination Method)

即使你不填寫完整的 \(MC\) 表格，你也可以用常理推斷來消去選項 C 和 D！
看看選項 C：\(Q=7\)，利潤=$0。我們來測試一下：如果廠商少生產一點，例如只生產 \(Q=6\)，當時的 \(AC\) 是 $24。每賣出一件產品，廠商收 $25 但成本平均只是 $24，所以一定有錢賺（利潤為正數）。既然 \(Q=6\) 時有錢賺，那麼最高利潤絕對不會是 $0！這樣你就能瞬間排除了 C 和 D，大大縮小範圍。

💡 Logic Check Elimination Method

Even without filling out the entire \(MC\) table, you can eliminate options C and D using common sense!
Look at Option C: \(Q=7\), Profit=$0. Let's test it: if the firm produces less, say \(Q=6\), the \(AC\) is $24. For every unit sold, the firm receives $25 but the average cost is only $24. The firm must be making money (profit is positive). Since profit is positive at \(Q=6\), the maximum profit can absolutely NEVER be $0! This allows you to instantly eliminate C and D.
