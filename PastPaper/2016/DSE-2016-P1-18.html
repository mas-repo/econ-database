HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Law of Demand & Relative Price (需求定律與相對價格) Difficulty: ★★★★☆ Key Concepts: Alchian-Allen Effect, Ad Valorem Tax

Question (題目)

下列哪項與需求定律並不相符？

Which of the following is INCONSISTENT with the law of demand?

A. 當政府對紅酒徵收從價稅（即該稅項是價格的某一百分比），售出的高質紅酒和劣質紅酒的比率維持不變。
A. When the government imposes an ad valorem tax (i.e. the tax is a certain percent of the price) on red wine, the ratio of high-quality red wine sold to low-quality sold remains unchanged.
B. 與農場附近的市場比較，距離農場較遠的市場售出較大量的貴價水果。
B. A larger quantity of expensive fruits is sold in a faraway market than a market near the farm.
C. 在經濟繁榮時，住宅樓宇的價格和交易量是正向關係。
C. Price of residential flat and quantity transacted are positively related during an economic boom.
D. 泊車位的月租租金上升會令停泊於這些泊車位的車輛的平均質素下降。
D. An increase in the monthly rental of parking spaces will lead to a decrease in the average quality of vehicles that use the parking spaces.

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 相對價格 (Relative Price)

需求定律指出，當某物品的價格相對於代替品下降時，其需求量會上升。我們必須比較「相對價格」。

The Law of Demand states that when the price of a good falls relative to its substitutes, the quantity demanded increases. We must compare the "Relative Price".

\[ \text{Relative Price (相對價格)} = \frac{P_{\text{High Quality}}}{P_{\text{Low Quality}}} \]

2. 阿爾欽-艾倫效應 (Alchian-Allen Effect)

當在兩個不同質素的代替品上加上一個相同的固定費用（Fixed Cost/Specific Charge）（如運費、固定稅項、租金），高質素物品的相對價格會下降，導致人們傾向購買更多高質素物品。

When a fixed charge (e.g., transportation cost, specific tax, rent) is added to two substitute goods of different quality, the relative price of the high-quality good decreases, leading people to buy relatively more of the high-quality good.

Let \( P_H \) = Price of High Quality, \( P_L \) = Price of Low Quality, \( C \) = Fixed Cost.
\[ \frac{P_H + C}{P_L + C} < \frac{P_H}{P_L} \]

數學上證明：加上常數 \( C \) 後，分數的值會變小（即高質素物品變得相對便宜）。

Mathematically proven: Adding a constant \( C \) makes the fraction smaller (High-quality goods become relatively cheaper).

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: D
Option A: Consistent (相符)

分析：從價稅（Ad Valorem Tax）是按百分比徵收（例如 10%）。假設稅率是 \( t \)。 新的相對價格是 \( \frac{P_H(1+t)}{P_L(1+t)} \)。分子分母的 \( (1+t) \) 會互相抵銷，結果等於 \( \frac{P_H}{P_L} \)。 既然相對價格不變，消費者沒有誘因轉移消費，故比率維持不變。這與需求定律相符。

Analysis: An ad valorem tax is a percentage tax. Let the tax rate be \( t \). The new relative price is \( \frac{P_H(1+t)}{P_L(1+t)} \). The \( (1+t) \) terms cancel out, leaving \( \frac{P_H}{P_L} \). Since the relative price remains unchanged, the ratio of consumption remains unchanged. This is consistent with the Law of Demand.

Option B: Consistent (相符)

分析：這是經典的「把好蘋果運出去」例子。運費是固定成本。加上運費後，貴價水果的相對價格下降（見上述公式），外地人會傾向買更多貴價水果。這與需求定律相符。

Analysis: This is the classic "Shipping the Good Apples Out" example. Transportation cost is a fixed cost. Adding it lowers the relative price of expensive fruit (see formula above), so people in faraway markets buy more expensive fruit. This is consistent.

Option C: Consistent (相符)

分析：這選項描述的是「價格」與「交易量」同方向變動。這並非違反需求定律，而是因為需求曲線向右移動 (Shift in Demand)。經濟繁榮令收入增加，需求增加，導致均衡價格和數量同時上升。需求定律是在「其他因素不變」下才成立，這裡「收入」改變了，所以不屬違反。

Analysis: This option describes Price and Quantity moving in the same direction. This does not violate the Law of Demand; it is due to a Shift in Demand. An economic boom increases income, shifting demand right, causing both equilibrium price and quantity to rise. The Law of Demand holds "ceteris paribus"; here, income changed.

Option D: INCONSISTENT (並不相符) - The Correct Answer

分析：泊車位月租是一個固定成本 (Fixed Cost)。無論你泊的是名貴房車還是廉價汽車，租金都一樣。 根據阿爾欽-艾倫效應，加上固定成本後，使用名貴房車的「總成本」（車價+租金）相對於廉價汽車的「總成本」會下降（相對價格下降）。 需求定律預測人們會轉用較高質素的汽車，令平均質素上升。 但選項 D 聲稱平均質素會下降，這與需求定律的預測相反。

Analysis: The monthly parking rent is a Fixed Cost. Whether you park a luxury car or a cheap car, the rent is the same. According to the Alchian-Allen effect, adding a fixed cost lowers the relative price of the luxury car (Car Price + Rent). The Law of Demand predicts people will switch to higher quality cars, causing the average quality to rise. Option D claims the average quality will decrease, which contradicts the prediction of the Law of Demand.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

關鍵判斷點 (The Pivot Point): 區分「固定成本」與「百分比成本」。

Pivot Point: Distinguish between "Fixed Cost" and "Percentage Cost".

  • 固定金額 (Fixed Amount): 如運費、按量稅 (Specific Tax)、月費。會改變相對價格 \(\rightarrow\) 改變消費比例 (Alchian-Allen Effect)。
  • 百分比 (Percentage): 如從價稅 (Ad Valorem Tax)、通脹。不會改變相對價格 \(\rightarrow\) 消費比例不變。

題目問「不相符」，所以我們要找一個違背上述邏輯的選項。選項 D 描述了固定成本上升，但結果卻與理論相反。

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱 1：誤以為選項 C 違反定律

學生常死記「P升Q跌」。但必須分清是「沿著曲線移動」(Movement along the curve) 還是「曲線移動」(Shift of the curve)。經濟繁榮是外在因素改變，屬於 Shift，故 P 與 Q 同升是正常的市場均衡結果。

Trap 1: Thinking Option C violates the law. Students often memorize "Price up, Quantity down". But you must distinguish between "movement along the curve" and "shift of the curve". An economic boom is an external factor change (Shift), so P and Q rising together is a normal equilibrium outcome.

陷阱 2：數學直覺錯誤

很多人直覺認為「加了租金，大家都變窮了，所以會開平價車」。這是「收入效應」(Income Effect)。但在這類相對價格題目中，「替代效應」(Substitution Effect) 通常是主導。數學上，分子分母加同一個數，大數（高質素）的相對優勢會增加。

Trap 2: Mathematical Intuition Error. Many intuitively think "Rent went up, everyone is poorer, so they will drive cheaper cars." This is the Income Effect. However, in relative price questions, the Substitution Effect is usually dominant. Mathematically, adding the same number to numerator and denominator increases the relative attractiveness of the larger number (high quality).
