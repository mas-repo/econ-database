HKDSE Economics: Question Analysis

Topic: Demand & Supply (需求與供應) Difficulty: ⭐⭐ (2/5) Key Concepts: Derived Demand, Complements

1. 題目 (The Question)

__________ 的需求可被視為 __________的引申需求。

The demand for __________ can be regarded as a derived demand for __________.


A. 咖啡豆 …… 咖啡
B. 自拍桿 …… 數碼相機
C. 雞翼 …… 雞腿
D. 報紙 …… 印刷用紙

A. coffee beans …… coffee
B. selfie sticks …… digital cameras
C. chicken wings …… chicken legs
D. newspapers …… printing paper

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

引申需求 (Derived Demand) 是指對一種物品（通常是生產要素或原材料）的需求，是源於對另一種最終物品的需求。

Derived Demand refers to the demand for a good (usually a factor of production or raw material) that arises from the demand for another final good.

簡單來說，生產者購買要素 \(X\) 不是為了直接消費，而是為了生產最終產品 \(Y\)。如果市場對產品 \(Y\) 的需求上升，生產者便需要投入更多要素 \(X\) 去生產，因此對要素 \(X\) 的需求也會隨之增加。

Simply put, producers buy factor \(X\) not for direct consumption, but to produce final product \(Y\). If the market demand for product \(Y\) increases, producers will need to input more factor \(X\) for production, thus increasing the demand for factor \(X\).

核心邏輯 (Core Logic):

$$ \text{Demand for Final Good } (Y) \uparrow \implies \text{Quantity Produced } \uparrow \implies \text{Demand for Input } (X) \uparrow $$

其他相關概念：

Other related concepts:

  • 輔助需求/聯合需求 (Joint Demand): 兩種物品必須一起使用才能滿足某種慾望（即補充品 Complements），例如相機和記憶卡。
    Joint Demand: Two goods that must be used together to satisfy a specific want (i.e., Complements), like cameras and memory cards.

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是 (Correct Option): A

選項 A (Option A) - 正確 (Correct)

咖啡豆是製造咖啡的原材料（生產要素）。人們對咖啡豆的需求，是源於人們想飲用咖啡。因此，對咖啡豆的需求是對咖啡的引申需求。

Coffee beans are the raw materials (factors of production) used to make coffee. The demand for coffee beans stems from people's desire to drink coffee. Therefore, the demand for coffee beans is a derived demand for coffee.

選項 B (Option B) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

自拍桿和數碼相機通常是一起使用的最終消費品，兩者屬於補充品 (Complements)，它們之間是輔助需求 (Joint Demand) 的關係，而不是引申需求。自拍桿並不是生產數碼相機的原材料。

Selfie sticks and digital cameras are final consumer goods typically used together. They are complements and have a relationship of joint demand, not derived demand. A selfie stick is not a raw material used to produce a digital camera.

選項 C (Option C) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

雞翼和雞腿是從同一隻雞身上得來的，它們在生產上屬於聯生供應 (Joint Supply)。在消費上，它們可能是替代品 (Substitutes)。兩者之間沒有「原材料與最終產品」的關係。

Chicken wings and chicken legs come from the same chicken, so they are in joint supply in terms of production. In consumption, they might act as substitutes. There is no "raw material and final product" relationship between them.

選項 D (Option D) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

這個選項的因果關係反了。印刷用紙是生產報紙的原材料。因此，正確的說法應該是：對「印刷用紙」的需求是對「報紙」的引申需求。選項 D 將位置對調了。

The causal relationship is reversed in this option. Printing paper is the raw material for producing newspapers. Thus, the correct statement should be: The demand for "printing paper" is a derived demand for "newspapers". Option D swapped the positions.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

這類題目的「關鍵點 (Pivot Point)」在於找出「生產要素 (Input)」與「最終產品 (Output)」的關係。句型結構為：
「[要素/原材料] 的需求可被視為 [最終產品] 的引申需求。」

The "Pivot Point" for this type of question is identifying the "Input" (factor of production) and "Output" (final product) relationship. The sentence structure is:
"The demand for [Input/Raw Material] can be regarded as a derived demand for [Final Product]."

解題時，我們只需要將選項代入以下邏輯測試：「左邊的物品是用來製造右邊的物品嗎？」

When solving, simply plug the options into this logic test: "Is the item on the left used to manufacture the item on the right?"

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

⚠️ 陷阱一：因果倒置 (Reversed Order)
考評局非常喜歡出像選項 D 這樣的陷阱。學生一看到「報紙」和「紙」，知道它們有關聯就立刻選擇，卻忽略了是「紙（原材料）衍生自報紙（最終產品）」，而不是反過來。

⚠️ Trap 1: Reversed Order
The examination authority loves to set traps like Option D. Students see "newspaper" and "paper", know they are related, and choose it immediately, ignoring that "paper (raw material) is derived from newspaper (final good)", not the other way around.

⚠️ 陷阱二：混淆引申需求與輔助需求 (Confusing Derived & Joint Demand)
學生常誤以為「兩件物品有連帶關係」就是引申需求（如選項 B 的相機與自拍桿）。記住：引申需求必須跨越「生產階段」（一個是材料，另一個是製成品）。

⚠️ Trap 2: Confusing Derived Demand and Joint Demand
Students often mistake "two related goods" for derived demand (like Option B's camera and selfie stick). Remember: Derived demand must cross a "production stage" (one is the material, the other is the finished product).

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼選項 B 不對？買自拍桿的需求明明是因為買了數碼相機啊！

Q: Why is Option B wrong? The demand for selfie sticks clearly exists because of digital cameras!

A: 雖然你購買自拍桿的動機是因為你有相機，但這在經濟學上稱為「輔助需求 (Joint demand) / 補充品 (Complements)」。我們只會在兩者是「原材料」與「最終產品」的關係時（例如木材與木椅），才使用「引申需求 (Derived demand)」這個詞。

A: Although your motive for buying a selfie stick is because you own a camera, in economics, this is called "Joint demand" (Complements). We only use the term "Derived demand" when the relationship is between a "raw material" and a "final product" (e.g., wood and wooden chairs).

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

三秒必殺技 (The 3-Second Rule):
遇到「引申需求」的題目，直接在兩個空格中間畫一個向右的箭頭「\(\rightarrow\)」，並讀作「製成 (is used to make)」。句子讀得通順的就是正確答案！

The 3-Second Rule:
When you see a "derived demand" question, draw a right-pointing arrow "\(\rightarrow\)" between the two blanks, and read it as "is used to make". The option that makes logical sense is the correct answer!

  • A. 咖啡豆 \(\rightarrow\) 製成 \(\rightarrow\) 咖啡 (合理！)
  • A. Coffee beans \(\rightarrow\) are used to make \(\rightarrow\) coffee (Makes sense!)
  • B. 自拍桿 \(\rightarrow\) 製成 \(\rightarrow\) 數碼相機 (荒謬)
  • B. Selfie sticks \(\rightarrow\) are used to make \(\rightarrow\) digital cameras (Nonsense)
  • C. 雞翼 \(\rightarrow\) 製成 \(\rightarrow\) 雞腿 (荒謬)
  • C. Chicken wings \(\rightarrow\) are used to make \(\rightarrow\) chicken legs (Nonsense)
  • D. 報紙 \(\rightarrow\) 製成 \(\rightarrow\) 印刷用紙 (方向反了)
  • D. Newspapers \(\rightarrow\) are used to make \(\rightarrow\) printing paper (Backwards)
