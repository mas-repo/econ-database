📊 HKDSE Economics Question Analysis

  • Topic (課題): Opportunity Cost & Exchange Rates (機會成本與匯率)
  • Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆
  • Key Concepts (關鍵概念): Opportunity Cost (機會成本), Value vs. Cost (價值與代價), Explicit Cost (顯性成本)

2. The Question (題目)

一名香港市民吳先生準備前往東京旅遊。下列哪項會令他去這次旅遊的成本下降？

Mr Ng, a Hong Kong citizen is going to travel to Tokyo for leisure. Which of the following will decrease his cost of having the trip?

  1. 在他出發前，有一颱風剛剛移近東京。
    A typhoon is approaching Tokyo right before his trip.
  2. 在他放假期間，他的老闆將會請全體同事享用自助餐。
    His boss is going to treat all the staff to a free buffet during his leave.
  3. 東京的風景比他想像中更優美。
    The scenery in Tokyo is better than he expected.
  4. 日圓兌其他貨幣貶值。
    Japanese Yen depreciates against other currencies.

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

機會成本 (Opportunity Cost): 在作出選擇時，所放棄的選項中價值最高的一個。要計算總機會成本，我們需要考慮金錢上的支出（顯性成本）以及放棄了的時間或選項的價值（隱性成本）。

The highest-valued option forgone in making a decision. To calculate the total opportunity cost, we must consider monetary expenses (explicit cost) and the value of time or alternative options given up (implicit cost).

$$ \text{Opportunity Cost} = \text{Explicit Cost} + \text{Implicit Cost} $$

價值 vs. 成本 (Value vs. Cost): 「價值」是你從該選項中獲得的好處或享受；「成本」是你為了得到該選項而需要付出的代價。兩者是完全獨立的概念。選擇本身的質素改變，只會影響其價值，而不會影響其成本。

"Value" is the benefit or enjoyment you derive from an option; "Cost" is what you have to give up to obtain it. They are completely independent concepts. A change in the quality of the chosen option only affects its value, not its cost.

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): Option D

選項 A (Option A) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

颱風移近東京會降低這次旅行的享受程度，這意味著旅遊的「價值」(Value) 下降了，但這並沒有減少他需要支付的機票、酒店等「成本」(Cost)。相反，如果颱風導致滯留，成本可能還會增加。

A typhoon approaching Tokyo lowers the enjoyment of the trip, meaning the "value" of the trip decreases. However, this does not reduce the "cost" (e.g., flights, hotels) he has to pay. Conversely, if the typhoon causes him to be stranded, the cost might even increase.

選項 B (Option B) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

老闆請吃免費自助餐是吳先生因為去旅行而被迫放棄的選項。當放棄的選項（留港上班）變得更吸引時，他的隱性成本會上升，導致去旅行的總機會成本增加，而非減少。

The free buffet treated by his boss is an option Mr. Ng is forced to give up because of the trip. When the forgone option (staying in HK) becomes more attractive, his implicit cost rises, causing the total opportunity cost of the trip to increase, not decrease.

選項 C (Option C) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

風景優美會令吳先生覺得這次旅行更「抵玩」，這代表旅行的「價值」(Value) 上升了。但如概念重溫所述，選擇本身的價值改變不會影響其成本。

Beautiful scenery makes the trip more enjoyable for Mr. Ng, meaning the "value" of the trip has increased. But as stated in the concept review, a change in the value of the chosen option does not affect its cost.

選項 D (Option D) - 正確 (Correct)

當日圓貶值，意味著港元相對升值。吳先生可以用相同數量的港幣兌換到更多的日圓。因此，他在東京購買同等數量的商品和服務時，所需付出的港幣（顯性成本）減少了，所以旅遊的總成本下降。

When the Japanese Yen depreciates, it means the HKD appreciates relatively. Mr. Ng can exchange the same amount of HKD for more Yen. Therefore, when purchasing the same amount of goods and services in Tokyo, the amount of HKD he needs to pay (explicit cost) decreases, thus lowering the total cost of the trip.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的「樞紐點」(Pivot Point) 在於區分三個概念：

  1. 被選選項的價值 (Value of the chosen option) -> 選項 A, C 屬於這類，用來混淆視聽。
  2. 放棄選項的價值 (Value of the forgone option) -> 選項 B 屬於這類，影響隱性成本。
  3. 實際支付的代價 (Explicit Cost) -> 選項 D 屬於這類，直接影響顯性成本。

The "Pivot Point" of such questions lies in distinguishing three concepts: 1) Value of the chosen option (Options A & C are distractors here); 2) Value of the forgone option (Option B, affects implicit cost); 3) Explicit Cost (Option D, directly affects out-of-pocket expenses).

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 混淆「價值」與「成本」： 很多學生看到「颱風」就覺得是壞事，看到「風景優美」就覺得是好事，然後直覺地將好壞與成本的高低掛鉤。記住，成本是你「付出」了什麼，價值是你「得到」了什麼。
  • Confusing "Value" with "Cost": Many students instinctively link "good/bad" events (like typhoons or beautiful scenery) to cost. Remember, cost is what you "give up", value is what you "get".
  • 忽略匯率對顯性成本的影響： 學生容易忘記跨國消費的成本是以自身的本國貨幣（港幣）來衡量的。
  • Ignoring exchange rate effects on explicit cost: Students often forget that the cost of transnational consumption is measured in the consumer's home currency (HKD).

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 假設吳先生已經提早用信用卡全數支付了機票和酒店，日圓貶值（選項 D）還會令他的成本下降嗎？
If Mr. Ng had already paid for his flights and hotels in full, would Yen depreciation (Option D) still lower his cost?

A: 會的！即使機票和酒店已成「沉沒成本」(Sunk Cost)，吳先生在東京當地依然需要花費吃飯、交通和購物。日圓貶值會令這部分的在地消費（以港幣結算）變得更便宜，因此整趟旅程的邊際成本依然會下降。
Yes! Even if the flights and hotels are "Sunk Costs", Mr. Ng still needs to spend money on food, transportation, and shopping in Tokyo. Yen depreciation makes local spending cheaper (in HKD terms), so the marginal cost of the entire trip still decreases.

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺法 (Elimination Shortcut): 當題目問及「成本」(Cost) 改變時，立刻在選項中尋找與「金錢流出」(Money Outflow) 或 「其他選項」(Alternative Options) 相關的字眼。看到描述事物本身質素的選項（如風景、天氣），可以直接劃走，因為它們只影響「價值」(Value)！

Elimination Shortcut: When a question asks about changes in "Cost", immediately scan the options for keywords related to "Money Outflow" or "Alternative Options". If an option describes the quality of the item itself (like scenery or weather), cross it out immediately because it only affects "Value"!
