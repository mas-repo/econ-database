HKDSE Economics Tutor Guide

Topic: 勞工需求 / 引申需求 (Demand for Labor / Derived Demand) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts: 引申需求 (Derived Demand), 需求與需求量 (Demand vs. Quantity Demanded), 勞工供應 (Labor Supply)

題目 (The Question)

在下列哪種情況下，香港建築工人的需求會上升？

A. 市區重建的法律程序簡化了。
B. 建築工人的工資率下降。
C. 政府向建築業的培訓計劃提供更多津貼。
D. 澳門建築業的工作機會減少。

The demand for construction workers in Hong Kong will increase if

A. the legal procedures for urban re-development are simplified.
B. wage rates of construction workers decrease.
C. the government provides more allowances for training programmes for the construction industry.
D. there are fewer job opportunities in the construction industry in Macau.

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

1. 引申需求 (Derived Demand)

對生產要素（如勞工）的需求是一種引申需求。這意味著對建築工人的需求，取決於社會對他們所生產的物品或服務（即建築物/工程）的需求。

The demand for a factor of production (like labor) is a derived demand. This means the demand for construction workers depends on the demand for the goods or services they produce (i.e., buildings/construction projects).

2. 需求 (Demand) vs 需求量 (Quantity Demanded)

這是一個經典的經濟學概念。當該物品本身的價格（在勞工市場中即工資率，Wage Rate \(W\)）改變時，只會導致需求量改變（沿著需求曲線移動）。只有非價格因素改變，才會導致需求改變（整條需求曲線移動）。

This is a classic economics concept. When the price of the good itself (in the labor market, the wage rate \(W\)) changes, it only leads to a change in quantity demanded (movement along the curve). Only changes in non-price factors lead to a change in demand (shift of the entire curve).

$$ D_L = f(D_G, P_S, P_C, \dots) $$

\(D_L\) = 勞工需求 (Demand for Labor)
\(D_G\) = 產品需求 (Demand for Goods produced)

答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): A

Option A (正確 Correct): 市區重建的法律程序簡化，會鼓勵更多市區重建項目。這增加了對建築工程的需求，從而引申出對建築工人更高的需求。整條需求曲線向右移。

Simplifying legal procedures for urban re-development encourages more redevelopment projects. This increases the demand for construction projects, which in turn leads to a higher derived demand for construction workers. The entire demand curve shifts to the right.

Option B (錯誤 Incorrect): 建築工人工資率 \(W\) 下降，只會導致建築工人的需求量 (Quantity Demanded) 上升，而不是需求 (Demand) 上升。這是沿著同一條需求曲線向右下方移動。

A decrease in the wage rate \(W\) of construction workers will only lead to an increase in the quantity demanded for construction workers, not an increase in demand. This is a downward movement along the same demand curve.

Option C (錯誤 Incorrect): 政府提供培訓津貼會吸引更多人入行，這會增加建築工人的供應 (Supply)，而不是需求。需求是由僱主（建築商）決定的。

Government training allowances will attract more people to enter the industry, which increases the supply of construction workers, not the demand. Demand is determined by the employers (construction firms).

Option D (錯誤 Incorrect): 澳門工作機會減少，會導致原本在澳門工作的香港工人回流，或者澳門工人來港找工作。這同樣會增加香港建築工人的供應 (Supply)，與需求無關。

Fewer job opportunities in Macau will cause Hong Kong workers in Macau to return, or Macau workers to seek jobs in HK. This also increases the supply of construction workers in Hong Kong, and is unrelated to demand.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

  • 關鍵點 (Pivot Point): 題目問的是「需求上升 (Increase in Demand)」。這意味著你必須尋找一個能令僱主想聘請更多工人的非工資因素 (non-wage factor)。
  • 干擾項 (Distractors): 選項 B 是最常見的陷阱，利用了學生對「需求」和「需求量」的混淆。選項 C 和 D 則是測試學生能否分辨「供應方 (勞工)」和「需求方 (僱主)」。

Pivot Point: The question asks for an "Increase in Demand". This means you must look for a non-wage factor that makes employers want to hire more workers.
Distractors: Option B is the most common trap, exploiting the confusion between "Demand" and "Quantity Demanded". Options C and D test whether students can distinguish between the "Supply side (workers)" and the "Demand side (employers)".

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱一：混淆「需求」與「需求量」
很多學生看到「工資下降」，直覺認為會「請多啲人」，於是誤選 B。記住，價格/工資改變，永遠只影響「量 (Quantity)」。

Trap 1: Confusing "Demand" with "Quantity Demanded". Many students see "wage decrease" and intuitively think "hire more people", mistakenly choosing B. Remember, a change in price/wage ONLY affects "Quantity".

陷阱二：主客體顛倒 (Supply vs Demand)
勞工市場中，勞工是供應者 (Suppliers)，公司/僱主是需求者 (Demanders)。任何影響工人入行意願的因素（如培訓津貼、其他地區的就業機會），影響的都是「供應」。

Trap 2: Reversing Subject and Object. In the labor market, workers are the suppliers, and firms/employers are the demanders. Any factor affecting workers' willingness to enter the industry affects "Supply".

學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 如果選項 B 改為「建築工人的生產力 (productivity) 上升」，答案會是 B 嗎？

A: 會的！ 如果工人的生產力上升（非工資因素），他們能為僱主帶來更高的邊際回報（Marginal Revenue Product）。在同一工資水平下，僱主會願意聘請更多工人，這會導致勞工需求 (Demand) 上升。

A: Yes! If workers' productivity increases (a non-wage factor), they bring a higher Marginal Revenue Product to employers. At the same wage level, employers will be willing to hire more workers, which leads to an increase in the Demand for labor.

考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

面對這類題目，可以使用「三步排除法 (3-Step Elimination)」：

  1. 圈出關鍵字： 圈出「需求 (Demand)」二字。
  2. 秒殺價格因素： 看到「工資 (Wage) / 價格 (Price)」改變，立刻排除 (刪去 B)，因為它只影響需求量。
  3. 區分買賣雙方： 問自己：「這是影響工人（供應），還是影響老闆（需求）？」C 和 D 明顯影響工人找工作的意願，屬於供應，立刻排除。剩下 A 就是答案。

For these types of questions, use the 3-Step Elimination Method:
1. Circle keywords: Circle the word "Demand".
2. Kill price factors instantly: If you see a change in "Wage/Price", eliminate it immediately (cross out B) because it only affects quantity demanded.
3. Distinguish buyers vs sellers: Ask yourself: "Does this affect workers (Supply) or bosses (Demand)?" C and D clearly affect workers' willingness to find jobs (Supply), so eliminate them. A is the only one left.
