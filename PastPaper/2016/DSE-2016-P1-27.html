HKDSE Economics Tutorial 經濟學導修

Topic (課題): 總體經濟學：總需求與總供應 (Macroeconomics: AD-AS Model)

Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts (核心概念): Aggregate Demand (AD) Long-Run Aggregate Supply (LRAS) Fiscal Policy

1. The Question (題目)

若政府興建新的基建，這會令 __________ 上升。
(1) 建築期間的就業人口
(2) 建築期間的物價水平
(3) 基建完成後的潛在產出
(4) 基建完成後的物價水平

A. 只有(1)、(2)
B. 只有(3)、(4)
C. 只有(1)、(2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2)、(3)及(4)

If the government builds new infrastructures, there will be an increase in __________.
(1) employed population during the construction period.
(2) the price level during the construction period.
(3) potential output after the construction is completed.
(4) the price level after the construction is completed.

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (3) and (4) only
C. (1), (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2), (3) and (4)

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

總需求與總供應模型 (AD-AS Model)

政府興建基建屬於擴張性財政政策 (Expansionary Fiscal Policy)。這會影響總需求 \(AD\)。

Building infrastructure by the government is an expansionary fiscal policy. This affects Aggregate Demand \(AD\).
$$ AD = C + I + G + (X - M) $$

當政府支出 \(G\) 增加，總需求 \(AD\) 會向右移。在短期內，這會導致產出 \(Y\) 和物價水平 \(P\) 上升。
基建完成後，經濟的生產能力提高，這會令長期總供應 (LRAS) 向右移。

When government expenditure \(G\) increases, \(AD\) shifts to the right. In the short run, this leads to an increase in output \(Y\) and price level \(P\).
After completion, the economy's productive capacity increases, shifting the Long-Run Aggregate Supply (LRAS) to the right.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option C

(1) 建築期間的就業人口 (Employed population during construction): 正確 (Correct)
政府增加支出 \(G\) 聘請工人進行建築，直接創造就業機會，令就業人口上升。

The government increases expenditure \(G\) to hire workers for construction, directly creating job opportunities and increasing the employed population.

(2) 建築期間的物價水平 (Price level during construction): 正確 (Correct)
政府支出增加令總需求 \(AD\) 增加（向右移）。在總供應不變的情況下，這會拉高物價水平。

The increase in government expenditure increases \(AD\) (shifts right). Assuming aggregate supply remains unchanged, this pushes up the price level.

(3) 基建完成後的潛在產出 (Potential output after completion): 正確 (Correct)
基建（如公路、鐵路）能減低運輸成本，提高整體經濟的生產力。這代表資本累積，令長期總供應 \(LRAS\) 增加，潛在產出上升。

Infrastructure (like highways, railways) reduces transport costs and improves overall economic productivity. This represents capital accumulation, increasing \(LRAS\) and potential output.

(4) 基建完成後的物價水平 (Price level after completion): 錯誤 (Incorrect)
當長期總供應 \(LRAS\) 向右移，在總需求不變的情況下，物價水平會下降，而非上升。

When \(LRAS\) shifts to the right, assuming \(AD\) remains unchanged, the price level will decrease, not increase.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的關鍵點 (Pivot Point)在於區分「建築期間 (Short-run demand effect)」和「完成後 (Long-run supply effect)」。

The Pivot Point of this type of question is distinguishing between the "construction period (Short-run demand effect)" and "after completion (Long-run supply effect)".
  • 短期 (Short-run): 視為政府支出 \(G\) 增加 \(\rightarrow\) \(AD \uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) \(Y \uparrow, P \uparrow\)。
  • 長期 (Long-run): 視為生產力提升 \(\rightarrow\) \(LRAS \uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) 潛在 \(Y \uparrow, P \downarrow\)。

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：混淆需求與供應的影響時間點
很多學生會以為基建永遠只會帶來通脹（物價上升）。他們忽略了基建完成後，供應面的改善（\(LRAS\) 增加）實際上會對物價造成下調壓力 (downward pressure)。

Trap: Confusing the timing of demand and supply effects
Many students assume infrastructure always causes inflation. They ignore that after completion, the supply-side improvement (increase in \(LRAS\)) actually puts downward pressure on the price level.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼我們不考慮基建完成後，市民收入增加導致的 AD 上升？
A: 在經濟學選擇題中，我們通常採用「其他因素不變 (ceteris paribus)」的原則。基建完成的直接長期經濟效果是提升生產力（LRAS 右移）。雖然 AD 可能因乘數效應保持在較高水平，但單就「基建完成」這個事件本身而言，它帶來的是強烈的供應面擴張，這會壓低物價。

Q: Why don't we consider the increase in AD due to higher income after completion?
A: In MCQs, we use the "ceteris paribus" principle. The direct long-run effect of completed infrastructure is improved productivity (LRAS shifts right). While AD might remain high, the event of "completion" itself is a strong supply-side expansion, which pushes prices down.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

消除法 (Elimination Method):
只要你清楚知道「供應增加會令價格下降」這個基本供求法則，你就能確定 (4) 是錯的。一旦排除了包含 (4) 的選項 (B 和 D)，你只需在 A 和 C 之間選擇。因為基建明顯會提升未來的生產力，(3) 必定正確，所以答案秒選 C。

Elimination Method:
As long as you know the basic rule that "an increase in supply lowers prices", you know (4) is wrong. Eliminating options with (4) removes B and D. Since infrastructure clearly boosts future productivity, (3) must be correct. Thus, the answer is instantly C.
