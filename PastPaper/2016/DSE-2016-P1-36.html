HKDSE Economics Exam Guide
經濟學考試解析

Topic: Public Finance (公共財政) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts: Tax Allowance (免稅額) | Tax Base (稅基) | Income Distribution (收入分布)

1. The Question (題目)

下圖顯示某經濟薪俸稅制的轉變。
與舊稅制相比，新稅制會 ___________。

The following diagram shows the changes in the salaries tax structure of an economy.
Compared to the old tax structure, the new tax structure will ___________.

  1. 令收入分布更平均
    result in a more even income distribution
  2. 減低所有工人的工作意慾
    reduce the work incentive of all workers
  3. 擴闊稅基
    broaden the tax base
  4. 令總稅收上升
    lead to an increase in total tax revenue

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這道題目，我們必須理解薪俸稅圖表中的兩個關鍵元素：免稅額 (Tax Allowance) 與 邊際稅率 (Marginal Tax Rate)。

To solve this question, we must understand two key elements in a salaries tax graph: Tax Allowance and the Marginal Tax Rate.

  • Tax Allowance (免稅額):
    圖表上稅率為 0% 的收入水平（即 \( x \) 軸的截距）。當收入 \( Y \) 小於或等於免稅額 \( Y_0 \) 時，無需繳稅。
    The income level where the tax rate is 0% (i.e., the \( x \)-intercept on the graph). When income \( Y \) is less than or equal to the tax allowance \( Y_0 \), no tax is paid.
  • Tax Base (稅基):
    需要納稅的人口或經濟活動的範圍。免稅額下降會令更多低收入人士跌入稅網，從而擴闊稅基。
    The range of population or economic activities subject to taxation. A decrease in tax allowance pulls more low-income earners into the tax net, thereby broadening the tax base.
$$ \text{Tax Payment (稅款)} = (\text{Income } Y - \text{Allowance } Y_0) \times \text{Tax Rate } t $$

圖解：稅制轉變的兩大關鍵點 / Diagram: Two key changes in the tax structure.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: C

擴闊稅基。 圖中顯示，新稅制（實線）在 \( x \) 軸的起點（即免稅額）比舊稅制（虛線）更接近原點 0。這意味著免稅額被降低了。原本收入介乎新舊免稅額之間而無需交稅的人，現在需要繳納薪俸稅。納稅人數增加，因此稅基被擴闊了。

Broaden the tax base. The diagram shows that the starting point on the \( x \)-axis (the tax allowance) for the new tax structure (solid line) is closer to the origin 0 than the old tax structure (dashed line). This means the tax allowance has been lowered. People whose income falls between the new and old allowance thresholds previously paid no tax, but now they must pay salaries tax. The number of taxpayers increases, thus broadening the tax base.

Option A (Incorrect / 錯誤)

令收入分布更平均。 新稅制降低了免稅額（對低收入人士不利，他們開始要交稅或交更多稅），同時降低了最高稅率（對高收入人士有利，他們交的稅率減少）。這種轉變實際上減輕了富人的稅務負擔，加重了窮人的負擔（相對而言），這會令稅制變得較不累進 (less progressive)，導致稅後收入分布變得更不平均 (more uneven)。

Result in a more even income distribution. The new system lowers the tax allowance (harming low-income earners who now pay tax) and lowers the maximum tax rate (benefiting high-income earners who now face a lower tax rate). This shift actually reduces the tax burden on the rich and increases it for the poor (relatively speaking). This makes the tax system less progressive, resulting in a more uneven after-tax income distribution.

Option B (Incorrect / 錯誤)

減低所有工人的工作意慾。 由於最高邊際稅率下降了，部分高收入人士每多賺一元所需要繳交的稅款減少了。這反而可能增加這批高收入人士的工作意慾。因此，說「所有」工人的工作意慾都被減低是錯誤的。

Reduce the work incentive of all workers. Since the maximum marginal tax rate has decreased, high-income earners will pay less tax for every extra dollar they earn. This might actually increase the work incentive for these high-income individuals. Therefore, it is incorrect to say the work incentive of "all" workers is reduced.

Option D (Incorrect / 錯誤)

令總稅收上升。 這是不確定的 (Uncertain)。雖然向新納入稅網的低收入人士徵稅會增加稅收，但高收入人士的最高稅率下降會導致從他們身上獲得的稅收減少。由於我們不知道經濟體中的收入分布情況（例如：有多少窮人、有多少富人），所以無法確定總稅收的最終增減。

Lead to an increase in total tax revenue. This is uncertain. While taxing the newly included low-income earners increases revenue, the lower maximum tax rate for high-income earners reduces revenue collected from them. Since we do not know the income distribution within the economy (e.g., how many poor vs. rich people there are), the net effect on total tax revenue cannot be determined.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

面對這類圖表題，第一步是尋找「轉捩點」(Pivot Points)：

When facing this type of graphical question, the first step is to identify the Pivot Points:

  1. 觀察 \( X \) 軸截距 (X-intercept)： 截距代表「免稅額」。實線的截距左移，代表免稅額下降。一看到免稅額下降，立刻聯想到「更多人要交稅 = 稅基擴闊」。
    Observe the \( X \)-intercept: The intercept represents the "tax allowance". The intercept of the solid line shifts to the left, meaning a lower tax allowance. Lower tax allowance immediately links to "more people paying tax = broadened tax base".
  2. 觀察最高水平線 (The Plateau)： 水平線代表最高稅率/標準稅率。實線的水平線較低，代表對富人的稅率降低。
    Observe the Plateau: The horizontal line represents the maximum/standard tax rate. The solid line is lower at the top, meaning a lower tax rate for the rich.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 陷阱 1：以為「納稅人增加 = 總稅收必定增加」。 這是同學常選錯 D 的原因。記住，總稅收 (Total Tax Revenue) 受到稅率和稅基的雙重影響。圖中雖然稅基擴闊（低收入人士交多咗稅），但高收入人士交少咗稅。在缺乏實質人口數據下，不能斷言總稅收上升。
    Trap 1: Thinking "More taxpayers = Total tax revenue strictly increases". This is why students wrongly choose D. Remember, total tax revenue depends on both the tax rate and the tax base. Although the tax base broadened (low-income pay more), high-income earners pay less. Without actual population data, we cannot confirm total revenue increases.
  • 陷阱 2：混淆稅率與稅款。 稅率下降不代表不用交稅，只是交的比例減少。同樣，免稅額下降代表起徵點降低，窮人現在也要按比例交稅，這會令貧富懸殊惡化。
    Trap 2: Confusing tax rate with tax payment. A lower tax rate doesn't mean no tax is paid, just a lower proportion. Similarly, a lower tax allowance means the starting point for taxation is lower; the poor now have to pay tax proportionally, which worsens the wealth gap.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果圖中兩條線的最高點（最高稅率）是一樣的，答案 D (總稅收上升) 會變成正確嗎？
If the maximum points (top tax rate) of both lines in the graph were the same, would Option D (increase in total tax revenue) become correct?

A: 是的！如果最高稅率保持不變，而免稅額下降了（整條線向左移），這意味著所有原本有交稅的人都會因為免稅額減少而交更多的稅，同時又有新增的人口被納入稅網。在這種情況下，政府從每一個納稅人身上收取的稅款都增加或維持不變，總稅收必定會上升。
Yes! If the maximum tax rate remained unchanged, but the tax allowance decreased (the whole line shifted left), it would mean all existing taxpayers would pay more tax due to the lower allowance, and new people would enter the tax net. In this scenario, the government collects more or equal tax from everyone, so total tax revenue would definitely increase.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

「極端例子法」 (Extreme Case Elimination)

Extreme Case Elimination

當看到選項包含「所有 (All)」字眼時（如選項 B），通常要響起警號。在經濟學中，一項政策極少會對「所有」人產生單一方向的影響。由於高收入人士的稅率明顯下降了，他們的工作意慾有機會上升，所以一秒鐘就可以剔除包含「所有」的選項 B。

When you see options containing the word "All" (like Option B), red flags should go up. In economics, a policy rarely affects "all" people in a single direction. Since the tax rate for high-income earners has clearly dropped, their work incentive might increase, allowing you to eliminate Option B in one second.
