The Question / 題目

在下列哪些情況下，財政赤字和貿易逆差可以同時被消除？

Under which of the following situations may a fiscal deficit and a trade deficit be eliminated at the same time?

(1)

名義利率下降

a decrease in nominal interest rate

(2)

該經濟的貨幣貶值

a depreciation of the economy’s currency

(3)

入息稅上升

an increase in income tax

A. 只有(1)、(2) / (1) and (2) only
B. 只有(1)、(3) / (1) and (3) only
C. 只有(2)、(3) / (2) and (3) only
D. (1)、(2)及(3) / (1), (2) and (3)

Concept Review / 概念重溫

1. Fiscal Deficit (財政赤字)

Occurs when Government Expenditure exceeds Tax Revenue.

當政府支出超過稅收收入時發生。

\( \text{Deficit} \iff G > T \)

To eliminate: Decrease \(G\) or Increase \(T\).

2. Trade Deficit (貿易逆差)

Occurs when value of Imports exceeds value of Exports.

當進口價值超過出口價值時發生。

\( \text{Deficit} \iff M > X \)

To eliminate: Decrease \(M\) or Increase \(X\).

Key Linkage / 關鍵聯繫:

Income (\(Y\)) affects both:
If Income (\(Y\)) \(\uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) Tax Revenue (\(T\)) \(\uparrow\) (Good for Fiscal)
If Income (\(Y\)) \(\uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) Imports (\(M\)) \(\uparrow\) (Bad for Trade)

收入 (\(Y\)) 影響兩者：
若收入 (\(Y\)) \(\uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) 稅收 (\(T\)) \(\uparrow\) (改善財政)
若收入 (\(Y\)) \(\uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) 進口 (\(M\)) \(\uparrow\) (惡化貿易)

Answer & Detailed Analysis / 答案與詳細分析

Correct Option: C (Only 2 and 3)

正確選項：C (只有 2 和 3)

Option (1): Decrease in nominal interest rate / 名義利率下降

❌ Incorrect / 錯誤

Analysis: A decrease in interest rates stimulates Investment (\(I\)) and Consumption (\(C\)), leading to an increase in Aggregate Demand (\(AD\)) and National Income (\(Y\)).

  • Fiscal: As \(Y \uparrow\), Tax Revenue (\(T\)) increases. Fiscal deficit improves. (OK)
  • Trade: As \(Y \uparrow\), people buy more goods, so Imports (\(M\)) increase. Trade deficit worsens. (Fail)

分析： 利率下降會刺激投資 (\(I\)) 和消費 (\(C\))，導致總需求 (\(AD\)) 和國民收入 (\(Y\)) 上升。
• 財政： 收入上升，稅收增加。財政赤字改善。(可行)
• 貿易： 收入上升，人們購買更多商品，導致進口 (\(M\)) 增加。貿易逆差惡化。(失敗)

Option (2): Depreciation of currency / 貨幣貶值

✅ Correct / 正確

Analysis: Depreciation makes exports cheaper and imports more expensive. Assuming the Marshall-Lerner condition holds, Net Exports (\(NX\)) increase.

  • Trade: Exports (\(X\)) \(\uparrow\) and Imports (\(M\)) \(\downarrow\). Trade deficit improves directly. (OK)
  • Fiscal: The increase in \(NX\) increases Aggregate Demand (\(AD\)), causing National Income (\(Y\)) to rise. As \(Y \uparrow\), Tax Revenue (\(T\)) increases. Fiscal deficit improves. (OK)

分析： 貨幣貶值使出口變便宜，進口變貴。假設馬歇爾-勒納條件成立，淨出口 (\(NX\)) 上升。
• 貿易： 出口 (\(X\)) \(\uparrow\) 且進口 (\(M\)) \(\downarrow\)。貿易逆差直接改善。(可行)
• 財政： \(NX\) 上升帶動總需求 (\(AD\)) 上升，國民收入 (\(Y\)) 增加。收入增加使稅收 (\(T\)) 增加。財政赤字改善。(可行)

Option (3): Increase in income tax / 入息稅上升

✅ Correct / 正確

Analysis: Increasing tax rates directly affects disposable income and government revenue.

  • Fiscal: Tax revenue (\(T\)) increases directly. Fiscal deficit improves. (OK)
  • Trade: Higher tax reduces disposable income (\(Y_d\)). Consumption (\(C\)) falls, so Imports (\(M\)) fall. Trade deficit improves. (OK)

分析： 增加稅率直接影響可支配收入和政府收入。
• 財政： 稅收 (\(T\)) 直接增加。財政赤字改善。(可行)
• 貿易： 稅收增加減少可支配收入 (\(Y_d\))。消費 (\(C\)) 下跌，導致進口 (\(M\)) 下跌。貿易逆差改善。(可行)

Problem Decomposition / 題目拆解

To solve this, you need to find a variable that moves Fiscal Balance and Trade Balance in the same positive direction.
解題關鍵在於找出一個能讓「財政差額」和「貿易差額」向同一個正面方向移動的變量。

Expansionary Policy
(e.g., Interest Rate \(\downarrow\))
Fiscal: Better 😊
Trade: Worse 😢
Conflict / 衝突
Contractionary Policy
(e.g., Tax \(\uparrow\))
Fiscal: Better 😊
Trade: Better 😊
Works / 可行
External Policy
(e.g., Depreciation)
Fiscal: Better 😊
Trade: Better 😊
Works / 可行

Student Q&A / 學生常見疑問

Q: Doesn't a lower interest rate cause capital outflow and depreciation, which helps exports?

問：利率下降不是會導致資金外流和貨幣貶值，從而幫助出口嗎？

A: This is a valid advanced point. However, in standard HKDSE analysis, unless the question explicitly mentions "capital flow" or "flexible exchange rates", the Income Effect (Interest \(\downarrow \rightarrow Y \uparrow \rightarrow \text{Imports} \uparrow\)) is the primary mechanism to consider. Even if depreciation occurs, the surge in domestic income often sucks in enough imports to offset the gain.
答： 這是一個合理的進階觀點。然而，在標準的 DSE 分析中，除非題目明確提及「資金流動」或「浮動匯率」，否則收入效應（利率 \(\downarrow \rightarrow Y \uparrow \rightarrow \text{進口} \uparrow\)）是首要考慮的機制。即使發生貶值，國內收入的激增通常會吸納大量進口，抵銷出口的增益。

Q: Why does depreciation improve the fiscal deficit?

問：為什麼貶值會改善財政赤字？

A: It's indirect. Depreciation \(\rightarrow\) Net Exports \(\uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) Aggregate Demand \(\uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) GDP \(\uparrow\). When GDP (Income) rises, the government collects more income tax and profits tax.
答： 這是間接影響。貶值 \(\rightarrow\) 淨出口 \(\uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) 總需求 \(\uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) GDP \(\uparrow\)。當 GDP（收入）上升時，政府會徵收更多的入息稅和利得稅。

Exam Tricks / 考試技巧

  • The "Twin Deficit" Rule of Thumb: Usually, contractionary fiscal policy (Spending \(\downarrow\) or Tax \(\uparrow\)) cures both deficits. Expansionary policy cures one but worsens the other.
    「雙赤字」速記法： 通常，緊縮性財政政策（支出 \(\downarrow\) 或 稅收 \(\uparrow\)）能同時治療兩個赤字。擴張性政策則會治好一個但惡化另一個。
  • Check Imports: Always ask "Does this policy make people richer?" If yes, they buy more imports, which hurts the trade balance.
    檢查進口： 永遠問自己「這個政策會讓人們變富有嗎？」如果是，他們會買更多進口貨，這會損害貿易差額。
