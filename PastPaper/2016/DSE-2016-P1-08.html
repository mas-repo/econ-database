HKDSE Economics Multiple-Choice Analysis

Topic: Factors of Production (生產要素) Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆
Key Concept: Labour Supply (勞力供應) Key Concept: Labour Productivity (勞工生產力)

1. The Question (題目)

在下列哪種情況下，某國的勞力供應會增加？

The labour supply of a country will increase if

A. 向現有的勞動人口提供更多訓練。

A. more training is provided for the existing labour force.

B. 法定最低的工作年齡由16歲降低至15歲。

B. the legal minimum working age is lowered from 16 to 15.

C. 外國直接投資增加。

C. foreign direct investment increases.

D. 政府提高薪俸稅率。

D. the government increases the salaries tax rate.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

什麼是勞力供應 (Labour Supply)？
在經濟學中，勞力供應是指在特定時間內，勞工願意並能夠提供的**總工作時數 (Man-hours)**。它代表了勞動力的「數量」。

What is Labour Supply?
In economics, labour supply refers to the total number of **man-hours** that workers are willing and able to provide in a given period. It represents the "quantity" of labour.

$$ \text{Labour Supply} = \text{Number of Workers} \times \text{Average Working Hours} $$ $$ \text{勞力供應} = \text{工作人數} \times \text{平均工作時數} $$

勞力供應 (Labour Supply) vs 勞工生產力 (Labour Productivity)
這是一個常見的考試陷阱。勞力供應是指**數量**（工時），而勞工生產力是指勞工的**質素**（例如每工時的產量）。

Labour Supply vs Labour Productivity
This is a classic exam trap. Labour supply is about **quantity** (man-hours), whereas labour productivity is about the **quality** of labour (e.g., output per man-hour).

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案：選項 B

Correct Answer: Option B

❌ 選項 A 分析： 提供更多訓練會提升勞工的技能和知識（人力資本）。這會增加「勞工生產力」(Labour Productivity)，但**不會**增加工人數量或他們的工作時數。因此，它改變的是質素，而非供應量。

❌ Option A Analysis: Providing more training improves workers' skills and knowledge (human capital). This increases "Labour Productivity", but it does **not** increase the number of workers or their working hours. Thus, it changes quality, not supply.

✅ 選項 B 分析： 降低法定最低工作年齡（由16歲降至15歲）意味著15歲的青少年現在可以合法投入勞動市場。這擴大了潛在的工作人口庫，直接增加了公式中的 \(\text{Number of Workers}\)（工作人數），從而增加勞力供應。

✅ Option B Analysis: Lowering the legal minimum working age (from 16 to 15) means 15-year-olds can now legally enter the labour market. This expands the pool of potential workers, directly increasing the \(\text{Number of Workers}\) in our formula, thereby increasing the labour supply.

❌ 選項 C 分析： 外國直接投資（FDI）增加通常會增加國內的資本數量（例如開設新工廠）。這會增加對勞工的「需求」(Labour Demand)，而不是「供應」(Labour Supply)。

❌ Option C Analysis: An increase in Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) generally increases the amount of capital in the country (e.g., opening new factories). This increases the "Demand" for labour, not the "Supply" of labour.

❌ 選項 D 分析： 提高薪俸稅率會降低勞工的稅後實際收入。這會減低人們工作的意欲（休閒的機會成本下降），導致部分人選擇減少工時或退出勞動市場，從而**減少**勞力供應。

❌ Option D Analysis: Increasing the salaries tax rate reduces workers' actual after-tax income. This disincentivizes work (the opportunity cost of leisure decreases), causing some people to work fewer hours or leave the labour market, which **decreases** labour supply.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

面對這類題目，你需要找出題目的**關鍵字**（Pivot Point）：「勞力供應」(Labour Supply)。這意味著你只需要尋找能改變「總工時」或「工作人數」的因素。

When facing this type of question, identify the **Pivot Point**: "Labour Supply". This means you are only looking for factors that change the "total man-hours" or "number of workers".

  • 干擾項 A (Distractor A): 試圖將「供應量」(Quantity) 與「生產力」(Quality) 混淆。
  • Distractor A: Attempts to confuse "Quantity" (Supply) with "Quality" (Productivity).
  • 干擾項 C (Distractor C): 試圖將「供應」(Supply) 與「需求」(Demand) 混淆。
  • Distractor C: Attempts to confuse "Supply" with "Demand".

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ 混淆「勞動力」的質與量
很多學生看到「勞動人口」或「訓練」等字眼，就會直覺認為這是好事情，從而選擇 A。謹記：訓練 = 生產力上升 (質)；人數/工時增加 = 供應上升 (量)。兩者在經濟學上是完全不同的概念。

⚠️ Confusing the Quality and Quantity of Labour
Many students see positive words like "training" and instinctively choose A. Remember: Training = Increased Productivity (Quality); More people/hours = Increased Supply (Quantity). These are entirely different concepts in economics.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果題目把選項 D 改為「政府降低薪俸稅率」，那 D 會是正確答案嗎？

Q: If Option D was changed to "the government decreases the salaries tax rate", would D be the correct answer?

A: 是的！如果政府降低薪俸稅率，市民稅後的實際工資會增加，這會吸引更多人投入勞動市場或願意加班（這被稱為替代效應），從而增加整體的勞力供應。

A: Yes! If the government lowers the salaries tax rate, the after-tax real wage increases. This attracts more people to enter the labour market or work overtime (known as the substitution effect), thereby increasing the overall labour supply.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧 (The Elimination Method)：
當題目問及「勞力供應」(Labour Supply) 時，你的大腦應該立即浮現公式：\( \text{Workers} \times \text{Hours} \)。

Speed Trick (The Elimination Method):
When a question asks about "Labour Supply", the formula \( \text{Workers} \times \text{Hours} \) should immediately pop into your head.

  1. 看到「訓練」(Training) 或「教育」(Education) 👉 立即劃掉 (這是生產力 Productivity)。
  2. See "Training" or "Education" 👉 Cross it out immediately (This is Productivity).
  3. 看到「投資」(Investment) 或「資本」(Capital) 👉 立即劃掉 (這是需求 Demand)。
  4. See "Investment" or "Capital" 👉 Cross it out immediately (This is Demand).
  5. 只剩下 B 和 D，根據稅收的常理推斷即可選出 B。
  6. You are left with B and D, and logical deduction regarding taxes easily leads you to B.
