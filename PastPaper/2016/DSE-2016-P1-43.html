2. 概念重溫 Concept Review

國際收支平衡 (Balance of Payments, BOP) 紀錄了一個國家在特定時間內與世界其他地方的所有經濟交易。在HKDSE中，我們主要關注兩大賬戶：經常賬與資本及金融賬。
Balance of Payments (BOP) records all economic transactions between a country and the rest of the world over a specific period. In HKDSE, we focus on two main accounts: the Current Account and the Capital and Financial Account.
$$ \text{BOP} = \text{Current Account (CA)} + \text{Capital & Financial Account (CFA)} $$
$$ \text{Overall Balance} = \Delta \text{Reserve Assets} $$
  • 經常賬 (CA)： 包含有形貿易（貨物）和無形貿易（服務）。
    公式：\( \text{CA} = \text{有形貿易餘額} + \text{無形貿易餘額} \)
  • 資本及金融賬 (CFA)： 包含資本的流入和流出。
    公式：\( \text{CFA} = \text{資本流入} - \text{資本流出} \)
  • 外匯儲備 (Reserve Assets)： 外匯儲備上升代表國際收支有盈餘（正數）。
  • Current Account (CA): Includes visible trade (goods) and invisible trade (services).
    Formula: \( \text{CA} = \text{Visible Trade Balance} + \text{Invisible Trade Balance} \)
  • Capital and Financial Account (CFA): Includes inflows and outflows of capital.
    Formula: \( \text{CFA} = \text{Capital Inflow} - \text{Capital Outflow} \)
  • Reserve Assets: An increase in foreign exchange reserves indicates a BOP surplus (positive value).

綜合結餘 \( \text{BOP} = \text{外匯儲備變動} = +30 \)

\( (20 + X) + 20 = 30 \)
\( 40 + X = 30 \)
\( X = -10 \)

Option A (-70) is incorrect: This is a result of subtracting all numbers incorrectly or reversing signs. It lacks logical basis.
選項 B (-10) 正確： 完全符合公式推導。
Option B (-10) is correct: This matches our formula derivation perfectly.

Option C (+10) is incorrect: Students might mistakenly think an increase in reserves means a BOP deficit (\( -30 \)), leading to \( 40 + X = -30 \), or they made a careless algebraic error when shifting terms (\( 40 - 30 = 10 \)).

Option D (+70) is incorrect: Students might have mistakenly added capital outflow as a positive number (\( 70 + 50 = 120 \)), causing the calculation to break down completely.

4. 題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

解題切入點 (Pivot Point): 題目的關鍵在於「外匯儲備上升了30百萬元」。在經濟學中，一個國家的外匯儲備增加，必然是因為資金流入大於資金流出，這意味著綜合結餘有盈餘 (Overall Balance = +30)。

將題目拆為三個區塊：
1. 經常賬 = 貨物 + 服務 = \(20 + X\)
2. 資本賬 = 流入 - 流出 = \(70 - 50 = +20\)
3. 總和 = \(+30\)

Pivot Point: The key to solving this lies in the phrase "foreign exchange reserves... increase by $30 million". In economics, an increase in a country's foreign reserves must be due to total inflows exceeding total outflows, meaning there is a BOP surplus (Overall Balance = +30).

Deconstruct the problem into three blocks:
1. Current Account = Goods + Services = \(20 + X\)
2. Capital Account = Inflow - Outflow = \(70 - 50 = +20\)
3. Total Sum = \(+30\)

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

❌ 陷阱一：外匯儲備變動的符號 (Sign of Reserve Assets Change)
很多同學會把「外匯儲備上升」寫成 \(-30\)。記住：在HKDSE的處理中，綜合結餘盈餘（正數）會直接導致外匯儲備增加（正數）。
❌ Trap 1: The Sign of Reserve Assets Change
Many students write "increase in foreign exchange reserves" as \(-30\). Remember: In the HKDSE syllabus, a BOP surplus (positive value) directly leads to an increase in foreign exchange reserves (positive value).
❌ 陷阱二：資本流出是減項 (Capital Outflow is a Deduction)
不要只把表格裡的數字相加。資本「流出」代表資金離開該國，必須是負數。
❌ Trap 2: Capital Outflow is a Deduction
Don't just add up the numbers in the table. Capital "outflow" represents money leaving the country and must be treated as a negative value.

7. 考試技巧與速解 Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

💡 左右天平法 (The Balance Scale Method):
你可以將資金「流入」放左邊，資金「流出」和「儲備增加」放右邊來平衡。
流入總額 = 有形貿易(\(+20\)) + 無形貿易(\(X\)) + 資本流入(\(+70\)) = \(90 + X\)
流出與儲備總額 = 資本流出(\(50\)) + 儲備增加(\(30\)) = \(80\)
所以， \( 90 + X = 80 \implies X = -10 \)。這樣算連負號都不容易弄錯！

💡 The Balance Scale Method:
You can place all "inflows" on the left, and all "outflows" plus "reserve increases" on the right to balance it out.
Total Inflows = Visible (\(+20\)) + Invisible (\(X\)) + Capital Inflow (\(+70\)) = \(90 + X\)
Total Outflows & Reserves = Capital Outflow (\(50\)) + Reserve Increase (\(30\)) = \(80\)
Therefore, \( 90 + X = 80 \implies X = -10 \). This method prevents sign errors completely!
