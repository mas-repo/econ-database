HKDSE Economics Tutor Guide
經濟學導師解析指南

Topic (課題): Nature of Interest (利息的本質) / Time Preference (時間偏好)
Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆ (3/5)
Key Concepts (核心概念): Time Preference (時間偏好) Deferring Consumption (延後消費) Barter Economy (以物易物經濟)

📝 The Question (題目)

下列哪些有關利息的陳述是正確的？
(1) 利息是延後消費的補償。
(2) 因為人們沒有耐性等待，所以利息存在。
(3) 利息在以物易物的經濟中存在。

Which of the following statements about interest are correct?
(1) Interest is the compensation for deferring consumption.
(2) Interest exists because people are impatient to wait.
(3) Interest exists in a barter economy.

A. 只有(1)、(2)
B. 只有(1)、(3)
C. 只有(2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2)及(3)

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 利息的本質 (Nature of Interest)：
在經濟學（特別是費沙 Irving Fisher 的理論）中，利息被定義為「提早獲得物品所支付的代價」或「延後消費所獲得的補償」。

1. Nature of Interest:
In economics (specifically Irving Fisher's theory), interest is defined as the "premium paid for earlier availability of goods" or "compensation for deferring consumption."

2. 正時間偏好 (Positive Time Preference)：
人類天生是缺乏耐性的（impatient）。如果面對相同數量和質量的物品，人們總是偏好「現在」擁有而非「未來」擁有。因為這種「急躁」，借款人願意支付溢價（利息）來提早消費，而貸款人需要獲得補償（利息）才願意延後消費。

2. Positive Time Preference:
Humans are naturally impatient. Given the same quantity and quality of a good, people always prefer to have it "now" rather than in the "future." Because of this impatience, borrowers are willing to pay a premium (interest) to consume earlier, and lenders require compensation (interest) to defer their consumption.

3. 以物易物經濟中的利息 (Interest in a Barter Economy)：
利息是一個實質概念 (real concept)，而非僅限於貨幣概念。只要存在時間偏好，利息就會存在。例如，你今天借入 10 個蘋果，承諾明年歸還 11 個蘋果，那多出來的 1 個蘋果就是實質利息。

3. Interest in a Barter Economy:
Interest is a real concept, not purely a monetary one. As long as time preference exists, interest exists. For example, if you borrow 10 apples today and promise to return 11 apples next year, the 1 extra apple is the real interest.

未來價值與現值的基本關係：

Basic relationship between Future Value and Present Value:

$$ FV = PV \times (1 + r) $$

Where \( r \) is the interest rate (利息率), representing the premium of present over future.
其中 \( r \) 為利率，代表現在相對於未來的溢價。

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option D

陳述 (1)：利息是延後消費的補償。 正確
當貸款人借出資源時，他們放棄了現在的消費機會。為了讓他們願意等待，借款人必須支付利息作為補償。因此，這句陳述是正確的。

Statement (1): Interest is the compensation for deferring consumption. True
When lenders lend out resources, they give up the opportunity to consume now. To make them willing to wait, borrowers must pay interest as compensation. Therefore, this statement is correct.

陳述 (2)：因為人們沒有耐性等待，所以利息存在。 正確
這正是「正時間偏好」的定義。因為人們急於提早享受（impatient to wait），他們願意為「提早獲得」支付代價，這代價就是利息。

Statement (2): Interest exists because people are impatient to wait. True
This is exactly the definition of "positive time preference." Because people are impatient and want to enjoy things earlier, they are willing to pay a price for "earlier availability," which is interest.

陳述 (3)：利息在以物易物的經濟中存在。 正確
利息不依賴貨幣存在。在沒有貨幣的以物易物經濟中，人們依然可以借貸實質物品（如借小麥還小麥）。只要人們有時間偏好，借出物品的一方就會要求未來收回更多的物品作為利息。

Statement (3): Interest exists in a barter economy. True
Interest does not depend on the existence of money. In a barter economy without money, people can still borrow and lend real goods (e.g., borrowing wheat to return wheat). As long as time preference exists, the lender will demand more goods in the future as interest.

結論： 因為 (1)、(2) 和 (3) 皆正確，故選 D。

Conclusion: Since (1), (2), and (3) are all correct, the answer is D.

🔍 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題關鍵 (Pivot Point)：
這類題目的核心在於測試學生是否明白「利息的非貨幣性」。很多學生死記硬背利息與銀行的關係，卻忽略了利息的本質是「時間的價格」。只要看到「以物易物 (Barter economy)」，必須立刻聯想到利息依然存在。

Pivot Point:
The core of this question tests whether students understand the "non-monetary nature of interest." Many students memorize the relationship between interest and banks but forget that the essence of interest is the "price of time." Whenever you see "Barter economy," you must immediately recall that interest still exists.

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：將利息與貨幣掛鈎 (Equating interest with money)
很多同學會誤選 A，因為他們認為「以物易物經濟沒有貨幣，所以沒有利息」。這是一個嚴重的概念錯誤！請記住：利息是提早消費的代價，它可以用任何物品來支付，不一定是貨幣。

Trap: Equating interest with money
Many students mistakenly choose A because they think, "A barter economy has no money, so there is no interest." This is a severe conceptual error! Remember: Interest is the price of earlier consumption; it can be paid in any good, not necessarily money.

🙋 Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果題目說「利息只在有貨幣的經濟中存在」，這句話對嗎？
A: 絕對錯誤。正如我們在陳述(3)中分析的，利息是實質概念。即使沒有貨幣，只要人類有「急躁」的本性（正時間偏好），利息就必然存在。

Q: If a statement says "Interest ONLY exists in a monetary economy", is it correct?
A: Absolutely incorrect. As analyzed in statement (3), interest is a real concept. Even without money, as long as humans have an "impatient" nature (positive time preference), interest must exist.

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧 (Elimination Method)：
在 HKDSE 經濟科中，「利息在以物易物經濟中存在」是一個極高頻的考點（Classic Trap）。如果你熟記這個概念，確認 (3) 是正確的，你就可以立刻排除選項 A。接著，(1) 和 (2) 是教科書上對利息最標準的定義，確認它們正確後，直接秒選 D。

Shortcut (Elimination Method):
In HKDSE Economics, "interest exists in a barter economy" is a highly frequent testing point (Classic Trap). If you memorize this concept and confirm (3) is true, you can immediately eliminate Option A. Next, (1) and (2) are the most standard textbook definitions of interest. Once confirmed, you can instantly choose D.
