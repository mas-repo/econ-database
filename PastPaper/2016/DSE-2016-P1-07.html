📊 經濟學試題解析 | Economics Question Analysis

📝 Topic: 生產與生產要素 (Production & Factors of Production) ⭐ Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ 🔑 Concepts: 長期 (Long Run), 生產要素 (FOP)

📌 題目 / The Question

學校是提供教育服務的生產單位。以下是一些在學校常見的人物和物件：

A school is a production unit which provides educational services. The following are people and objects that are usually found at a school:

(1) 校舍
(2) 教師
(3) 學生
(4) 教師擁有的私家車
(5) 洗手間的洗手液

(1) the school building
(2) teachers
(3) students
(4) private cars owned by teachers
(5) sanitizers in the toilets

長期而言，上述哪些是學校的可變生產要素？

In the long run, which of the above items are variable factors of production of the school?

A. 只有(1)、(5)
B. 只有(1)、(2)、(5)
C. 只有(2)、(3)、(4)
D. (1)、(2)、(3)、(4)及(5)

A. (1) and (5) only
B. (1), (2) and (5) only
C. (2), (3) and (4) only
D. (1), (2), (3), (4) and (5)

💡 概念重溫 / Concept Review

1. 生產要素 (Factors of Production, FOP): 廠商用於生產物品或服務的資源，包括土地 (Land)、勞力 (Labour)、資本 (Capital) 和企業家職能 (Entrepreneurship)。必須是由廠商投入使用的。

1. Factors of Production (FOP): Resources used by a firm to produce goods and services, including Land, Labour, Capital, and Entrepreneurship. They must be inputs utilized by the firm.

2. 短期與長期 (Short Run vs. Long Run):

2. Short Run vs. Long Run:

  • 短期： 至少有一種生產要素的數量是固定不變的（固定生產要素）。
  • Short Run: A period where at least one factor of production is fixed (Fixed Factor).
  • 長期： 所有生產要素的數量都可以改變。長期沒有固定生產要素，全部都是可變生產要素。
  • Long Run: A period where the quantities of ALL factors of production can be varied. In the long run, there are no fixed factors; all are variable factors.

生產函數模型 / Production Function Model

(Where \(Q\) = Output, \(K\) = Capital, \(L\) = Labour)

短期 (Short Run) - 存在固定要素 \(\bar{K}\):

$$ Q = f(\bar{K}, L) $$

長期 (Long Run) - 所有要素皆可變:

$$ Q = f(K, L) $$

✅ 答案與詳細分析 / Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Option: B
(1) 校舍 / School Building: 🟢 正確 (Yes)

校舍是學校生產教育服務的「資本」(Capital) 或「土地」(Land)。在短期內，校舍數量無法輕易改變，屬於固定要素。但題目強調「長期而言」，在長期，所有要素皆可變，因此校舍也變成了可變生產要素。

The school building is "Capital" or "Land" used to produce educational services. In the short run, it's a fixed factor because it cannot be easily changed. However, the question specifies "In the long run", where ALL factors become variable. Thus, it is a variable factor here.

(2) 教師 / Teachers: 🟢 正確 (Yes)

教師提供勞力 (Labour)，是生產教育服務不可或缺的輸入要素。無論長期或短期，勞力通常都是可變生產要素。

Teachers provide "Labour", which is an essential input for producing educational services. Labour is typically a variable factor in both the short and long run.

(3) 學生 / Students: 🔴 錯誤 (No)

學生是享受教育服務的「顧客」(Consumers) 或服務對象，而不是學校為了生產服務而僱用/購買的「生產要素」。

Students are the "Consumers" receiving the educational service. They are not inputs (Factors of Production) employed or purchased by the school to produce the service.

(4) 教師擁有的私家車 / Teachers' Private Cars: 🔴 錯誤 (No)

這些汽車是教師的私人消費品，並不屬於學校，學校也沒有把它們投入到教育服務的生產過程中。

These cars are private consumption goods owned by teachers. They do not belong to the school and are not utilized in the production process of educational services.

(5) 洗手間的洗手液 / Sanitizers: 🟢 正確 (Yes)

洗手液是學校購買並用於維持日常運作的消耗品，屬於「資本」(Capital / Working Capital)。在長期中，它同樣是可變生產要素。

Sanitizers are consumables purchased by the school to maintain daily operations, classified as "Capital" (Working Capital). In the long run, it is also a variable factor.

🔍 題目拆解 / Problem Decomposition

解答這類題目的「關鍵點」(Pivot Point) 有兩個：

There are two "Pivot Points" to solve this type of question:

  1. 時間限制： 留意「長期」(Long Run) 這個字眼。這意味著你不需要區分固定和可變要素，因為只要它是生產要素，在長期就一定是可變的。

    Time Constraint: Notice the keyword "Long Run". This means you don't need to distinguish between fixed and variable factors because as long as it IS a factor of production, it MUST be variable in the long run.

  2. 生產單位的界定： 題目問的是「學校的生產要素」。因此，該物件必須是學校用來生產的。這就排除了(3)消費者和(4)員工的私人物品這兩個干擾項 (Distractors)。

    Defining the Production Unit: The question asks for the factors of production "of the school". Therefore, the item must be used by the school for production. This eliminates the distractors: (3) consumers and (4) employees' private belongings.

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 / Common Mistakes & Traps

  • 忽略「長期」關鍵字： 很多學生一看到「校舍」(1)，就立刻聯想到它是「固定要素」(Fixed Factor)，從而錯誤地排除了包含(1)的選項。記住：長期之下無固定要素！

    Ignoring the "Long Run" Keyword: Many students see "school building" (1) and immediately classify it as a "Fixed Factor", wrongly eliminating options containing (1). Remember: There are NO fixed factors in the long run!

  • 混淆「學校裡的東西」和「生產要素」： 題目故意列出「在學校常見的人物和物件」。學生容易直覺認為學校裡的人(學生)都是生產的一部分，忘記了經濟學對生產要素有嚴格的定義（必須是生產者投入的資源）。

    Confusing "Things in a school" with "Factors of Production": The question trickily lists items "found at a school". Students might intuitively think everyone in a school (like students) contributes to production, forgetting the strict economic definition of FOP (inputs provided by the producer).

🙋 學生常見疑問 / Student Q&A

Q1: 學生不能算是生產教育的「原材料」(Raw materials) 嗎？所以應該是資本？

Q1: Can't students be considered the "raw materials" for producing education? So shouldn't they be Capital?

A1: 不可以。在經濟學的服務業（如教育、醫療）中，學生或病人是接受服務的「消費者」(Consumers)。學校並沒有「買入」學生來製造成品出售，學生是支付學費來獲取服務的人。因此，他們不是學校的生產要素。

A1: No. In service industries (like education or healthcare) in economics, students or patients are the "Consumers" receiving the service. The school does not "purchase" students to manufacture a good for sale; rather, students pay to acquire the service. Thus, they are not FOPs.

Q2: 如果題目把「長期」改成「短期」(Short run)，答案會是什麼？

Q2: If the question changed "Long run" to "Short run", what would the answer be?

A2: 如果是短期，校舍 (1) 就會變成「固定生產要素」(Fixed Factor)，因為學校無法在短時間內輕易擴建或拆除校舍。在這種情況下，學校的「可變生產要素」只會剩下教師 (2) 和洗手液 (5)。

A2: If it were the short run, the school building (1) would become a "Fixed Factor", as the school cannot easily expand or demolish buildings in a short time. In that scenario, the "variable factors" would only be teachers (2) and sanitizers (5).

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 / Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

消去法 (Elimination Method)：

Elimination Method:

在這種多項選擇題中，尋找最明顯錯誤的選項是最快的方法。

In these multiple-item MCQs, finding the most obviously wrong item is the fastest way.

  1. 你很清楚「學生」(3) 絕對不是生產要素（他們是消費者）。
  2. You know for sure that "students" (3) are definitely NOT factors of production (they are consumers).
  3. 因此，任何包含 (3) 的選項都可以立刻刪除 ➔ 剔除選項 C 和 D。
  4. Therefore, immediately cross out any options containing (3) ➔ Eliminate C and D.
  5. 現在只剩下 A (1, 5) 和 B (1, 2, 5)。比較兩者，差異在於 (2) 教師。教師提供勞力，百分之百是生產要素，必須包含在內。
  6. Now you are left with A (1, 5) and B (1, 2, 5). The difference is (2) teachers. Teachers provide labour and are 100% a factor of production, so they MUST be included.
  7. 因此，答案必定是 B。連「長期」和「校舍」的關係都不需要深究就能得分！
  8. Thus, the answer MUST be B. You get the mark without even deeply analyzing the "long run" relationship with the "school building"!
