Note: This question was deleted by the HKEAA. This guide will analyze why the question is controversial and lacks a single correct answer.

1. The Question (題目)

假設政府同時增加支出和入息稅，兩者增加的金額相同。這對總產出的影響屬

A. 收縮性。
B. 中性。
C. 擴張性。
D. 未能確定。

If the government simultaneously increases its expenditure and income tax by the same amount, the effect on the aggregate output is ____________.

A. contractionary
B. neutral
C. expansionary
D. indeterminate

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

平衡預算乘數 (Balanced Budget Multiplier)

當政府支出 \(G\) 和稅收 \(T\) 增加相同金額時（即 \(\Delta G = \Delta T\)），對總需求 (AD) 的淨影響。政府支出的增加會直接並全數增加總需求；但稅收的增加只會減少市民的可支配收入，市民會按其邊際消費傾向 (MPC) 減少消費 \(C\)。因為 \(MPC < 1\)，稅收增加對 AD 的收縮作用，小於政府支出對 AD 的擴張作用。

When government expenditure \(G\) and taxes \(T\) increase by the same amount (\(\Delta G = \Delta T\)), this is the net effect on Aggregate Demand (AD). An increase in \(G\) directly and fully increases AD. However, an increase in \(T\) reduces disposable income, causing consumers to reduce consumption \(C\) based on their Marginal Propensity to Consume (MPC). Since \(MPC < 1\), the contractionary effect of the tax increase is smaller than the expansionary effect of the government spending increase.
$$\Delta AD = \Delta G - (MPC \times \Delta T)$$ $$\text{Since } \Delta G = \Delta T \text{ and } MPC < 1, \text{ then } \Delta AD > 0$$

入息稅的供應面效應 (Supply-side Effect of Income Tax)

在總供應-總需求 (AS-AD) 模型中，入息稅不僅影響總需求，還會影響總供應。入息稅增加會降低工作的金錢回報，減低市民的工作意欲，導致短期及長期總供應 (SRAS & LRAS) 減少（向左移）。

In the AS-AD model, income tax affects not only AD but also Aggregate Supply (AS). An increase in income tax lowers the financial return of working, reducing work incentives, which leads to a decrease (leftward shift) in both Short-Run and Long-Run Aggregate Supply (SRAS & LRAS).

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: None (Deleted Question / 無正確答案 - 遭刪除)

Option A: 收縮性 (Contractionary) - Incorrect

即使考慮到稅收會減少消費，但由於 \(\Delta G\) 對經濟的直接刺激大於 \(\Delta T\) 帶來的消費減少，總需求 (AD) 必定會淨增加。因此，單從需求面來看，它不會是收縮性的。

Even considering that taxes reduce consumption, because the direct stimulus of \(\Delta G\) is greater than the reduction in consumption caused by \(\Delta T\), Aggregate Demand (AD) must have a net increase. Thus, looking solely at the demand side, it is not contractionary.
Option B: 中性 (Neutral) - Incorrect

這是學生最常犯的錯誤，誤以為 \(\Delta G\) 和 \(\Delta T\) 金額相同就會互相抵消。如上所述，由於乘數效應和邊際消費傾向 (\(MPC < 1\))，兩者並不會完全抵消。

This is the most common student mistake, assuming that equal amounts of \(\Delta G\) and \(\Delta T\) will perfectly cancel each other out. As explained above, due to the multiplier effect and the marginal propensity to consume (\(MPC < 1\)), they do not perfectly offset.
Option C: 擴張性 (Expansionary) - Why it could be right, but is flawed

如果我們只考慮傳統的凱恩斯支出模型（只看總需求 AD），淨效應是總需求增加。在物價水平不變或向上傾斜的 SRAS 曲線下，總產出會增加，這屬擴張性。這原本可能是考評局預期的答案。

If we only consider the traditional Keynesian expenditure model (looking only at AD), the net effect is an increase in AD. With a constant price level or an upward-sloping SRAS curve, aggregate output increases, making it expansionary. This was likely the HKEAA's originally intended answer.
Option D: 未能確定 (Indeterminate) - Why it makes the question problematic

題目明確指出是「入息稅」(Income Tax) 而非「定額稅」(Lump-sum Tax)。入息稅增加會打擊工作意欲，令總供應 (AS) 向左移。當 AD 向右移（擴張）而 AS 向左移（收縮）時，物價水平必定上升，但總產出 (Aggregate Output) 的最終變化取決於 AD 和 AS 移動的相對幅度。因此，總產出的變化實際上是「未能確定」的。由於 C 和 D 都有堅實的經濟學理據支持，題目出現歧義，最終被刪除。

The question specifically states "income tax" rather than "lump-sum tax". An increase in income tax disincentivizes work, shifting Aggregate Supply (AS) to the left. When AD shifts right (expansion) and AS shifts left (contraction), the price level definitely rises, but the final change in aggregate output depends on the relative magnitudes of the AD and AS shifts. Therefore, the change in output is actually "indeterminate". Because both C and D have solid economic justifications, the question is ambiguous and was ultimately deleted.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

  • Pivot Point (關鍵字眼): 「同時增加支出和入息稅，金額相同」 (simultaneously increases expenditure and income tax by the same amount).
  • Trap (陷阱): 區分「定額稅」與「入息稅」。如果是定額稅，通常只考慮 AD 效應（答案會是 C）。但「入息稅」在 DSE 課程中明確包含供應面效應 (Supply-side effects)。
    • Distinguish between "lump-sum tax" and "income tax". If it were a lump-sum tax, we usually only consider AD effects (Answer would be C). But "income tax" in the DSE syllabus explicitly includes supply-side effects.

5. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼政府收了市民 $100 稅，再花費 $100，經濟產出還是會增加？這不是左手交右手嗎？

A: 不是完全的左手交右手。當政府收 $100 稅時，市民並不會減少 $100 的消費。假設市民的邊際消費傾向 (MPC) 是 0.8，他們只會減少 $80 的消費（另外 $20 是減少了儲蓄）。但政府卻把那 $100 全數投入市場購買貨品和服務。結果市場上多了 $100 的政府需求，卻只減少了 $80 的私人消費需求，淨需求增加了 $20，帶動經濟擴張。

Q: Why does economic output increase if the government takes $100 in taxes and spends $100? Isn't it just transferring money from one hand to the other?

A: It's not a perfect transfer. When the government taxes $100, citizens don't reduce their consumption by $100. Assuming a Marginal Propensity to Consume (MPC) of 0.8, they only reduce consumption by $80 (the other $20 comes from reduced savings). However, the government spends the full $100 on goods and services. As a result, the market sees +$100 in government demand but only -$80 in private consumption demand. The net demand increases by $20, driving economic expansion.

6. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

在未來的 DSE 考試中，如果題目問及「入息稅」(Income Tax) 或「利得稅」(Profits Tax)，必須同時檢查 AD 和 AS 的變化。如果題目問的是「定額稅」(Lump-sum tax) 或「轉移支付」(Transfer payments)，則通常只需專注於 AD 的變化。

In future DSE exams, if a question mentions "Income Tax" or "Profits Tax", you MUST check for changes in both AD and AS. If the question mentions "Lump-sum tax" or "Transfer payments", you usually only need to focus on AD changes.
