HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic (課題): Demand & Supply / Elasticity (需求與供應 / 彈性)
Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts (核心概念): Price Elasticity of Demand (需求價格彈性), Determinants of Elasticity (彈性的決定因素)

The Question (題目)

物品X的生產成本下降，導致其價格和交易量分別改變了5%及10%。下列哪項最能解釋上述的改變？

A decrease in the cost of producing Good X results in a 5% and 10% change in its price and quantity transacted respectively. Which of the following best explains the above changes?

  • A. 物品X是一種耐用品。
    Good X is a durable good.
  • B. 物品X沒有接近的替代品。
    There are no close substitutes for Good X.
  • C. 生產物品X的廠商有剩餘的產能。
    Firms producing Good X have excess capacity in production.
  • D. 物品X的生產要素並不容易找到。
    The factors of production for Good X are not easily available.

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 需求價格彈性 (Price Elasticity of Demand, \(E_d\))

它量度需求量對價格改變的反應程度。公式如下：

It measures the responsiveness of quantity demanded to a change in price. The formula is:

$$ E_d = \frac{\%\Delta Q_d}{\%\Delta P} $$
  • 如果 \(\%\Delta Q_d > \%\Delta P\)，即 \(E_d > 1\)，需求屬高彈性 (Elastic)。
  • If \(\%\Delta Q_d > \%\Delta P\), i.e., \(E_d > 1\), demand is Elastic.

2. 供應改變與需求曲線 (Change in Supply and Demand Curve)

當供應曲線移動 (Shift in Supply) 時，均衡點會沿著需求曲線移動。因此，價格與交易量的變化比例，反映了需求彈性。

When the Supply curve shifts, the equilibrium point moves along the Demand curve. Therefore, the ratio of changes in price and quantity reflects the Elasticity of Demand.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A (選項 A)

Step 1: Analyze the Data (分析數據)

題目指出生產成本下降，這代表供應增加 (Supply increases)，供應曲線向右移。

The question states production cost decreases, which means Supply increases, shifting the supply curve to the right.

結果：價格改變 5%，交易量改變 10%。

Result: Price changes by 5%, Quantity changes by 10%.

$$ E_d = \frac{10\%}{5\%} = 2 $$

由於 \(E_d > 1\)，這表示物品X的需求屬高彈性 (Demand is elastic)。我們需要在選項中找出導致「高需求彈性」的原因。

Since \(E_d > 1\), this implies the Demand for Good X is elastic. We need to find the option that causes "Elastic Demand".

Option A: Good X is a durable good (物品X是一種耐用品) ✔ CORRECT

分析：耐用品（如汽車、電器）的需求通常屬高彈性。因為當價格變動時，消費者可以選擇延遲更換或維修舊機，對價格較敏感。

Analysis: Durable goods (like cars, appliances) usually have elastic demand. Consumers can postpone replacement or repair existing ones, making them sensitive to price changes.

結論：這解釋了為何 \(E_d > 1\)。

Option B: No close substitutes (沒有接近的替代品) ✘ WRONG

分析：如果沒有替代品，消費者難以轉購其他產品，需求應屬低彈性 (Inelastic)。

Analysis: If there are no substitutes, consumers cannot switch to other products easily, so demand should be Inelastic.

結論：這會導致 \(\%\Delta P > \%\Delta Q\)，與題目數據不符。

Option C: Excess capacity (有剩餘的產能) ✘ WRONG

分析：這是供應彈性 (Elasticity of Supply) 的決定因素，而非需求彈性。這會影響供應曲線移動的幅度或形狀，但不能解釋為何沿著需求曲線移動時，量變大於價變。

Analysis: This is a determinant of Price Elasticity of Supply, not Demand. It affects the magnitude or shape of the supply shift, but does not explain why quantity changes more than price when moving along the demand curve.

Option D: Factors not easily available (生產要素並不容易找到) ✘ WRONG

分析：這同樣是供應彈性 (Elasticity of Supply) 的決定因素（會導致低供應彈性）。這與需求曲線的斜率無關。

Analysis: This is also a determinant of Price Elasticity of Supply (leading to inelastic supply). It is irrelevant to the slope of the demand curve.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的解題邏輯鏈 (Logic Chain) 如下：

  1. 識別衝擊 (Identify the Shock):
    成本下降 \(\rightarrow\) 供應增加 (Supply shifts right)。
  2. 識別移動路徑 (Identify the Movement):
    供應移動，均衡點沿著需求曲線 (Demand Curve) 滑動。
  3. 計算彈性 (Calculate Elasticity):
    \(\%\Delta Q (10\%) > \%\Delta P (5\%)\) \(\rightarrow\) 需求是高彈性 (Elastic)。
  4. 配對因素 (Match Determinant):
    哪個選項會導致高需求彈性？ \(\rightarrow\) 耐用品 (Durable goods)。

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

錯誤觀念：混淆需求彈性與供應彈性

很多學生看到「生產成本」、「廠商」等字眼，就直覺地去選有關供應彈性的選項（如 C 或 D）。

Misconception: Confusing Demand Elasticity with Supply Elasticity. Many students see words like "production cost" or "firms" and intuitively choose options related to Supply Elasticity (like C or D).

破解關鍵：記住「供應移動，測試需求」(Supply shifts, testing Demand)。當供應線移動時，市場結果的變化比例是由需求線的斜率決定的。

Key to solve: Remember "Supply shifts, testing Demand". When the supply curve shifts, the ratio of market changes is determined by the slope of the Demand curve.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼不是供應彈性？題目明明說「生產成本下降」。
Why isn't it about Supply Elasticity? The question clearly says "production cost decreases".

A: 生產成本下降是原因 (Cause)，它導致供應曲線移動。但是，價格和交易量最後的結果 (Result)，是取決於消費者如何反應（即需求曲線）。如果消費者反應很大（高需求彈性），量就會大增，價格只需微跌。所以我們要找的是影響消費者反應的因素。

The decrease in production cost is the Cause, which shifts the Supply curve. However, the final Result of price and quantity depends on how consumers react (i.e., the Demand curve). If consumers react strongly (Elastic Demand), quantity increases significantly with only a small price drop. So we are looking for factors affecting consumer reaction.

Exam Tricks (考試技巧)

口訣：大Q細P是Elastic (Big Q, Small P is Elastic)

看到 \(\%\Delta Q (10\%) > \%\Delta P (5\%)\)，馬上寫下 "Demand Elastic"。然後直接找導致 Demand Elastic 的選項，通常是：

  • 有很多替代品 (Many substitutes)
  • 奢侈品 (Luxury goods)
  • 佔收入比例大 (Large proportion of income)
  • 耐用品 (Durable goods)

這樣可以快速排除干擾項 C 和 D。
