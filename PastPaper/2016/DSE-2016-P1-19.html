HKDSE Economics Tutor Guide

Topic: Market Intervention (Subsidy) 市場干預（補貼） Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Key Concepts: Subsidy, Total Revenue, Elasticity, Complements

1. The Question (題目)

參閱以下某物品的供需表。

Refer to the following demand and supply schedules of a good.

Price ($) / 價格 405060708090
Qd (units) / 需求量 141210864
Qs (units) / 供應量 6810121416

當政府對該物品提供每單位$20的補貼，下列哪項陳述是正確的？

Which of the following statements is correct when the government provides a per unit subsidy of $20 on the good?

  • A. 包括補貼在內的總收益是$840。
    The total revenue including the subsidy is $840.
  • B. 政府所支付的補貼總額是$200。
    The total subsidy granted by the government is $200.
  • C. 在原初市價和新市價的範圍內，需求彈性小於一。
    Within the range of the original and new market prices, the elasticity of demand is smaller than one.
  • D. 它的輔助品的總收益轉變是未能確定的。
    The change in total revenue of its complements is uncertain.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

補貼 (Subsidy): 當政府提供從量補貼時，生產者收到的價格 \(P_p\) 會高於消費者支付的價格 \(P_c\)。兩者的差額等於每單位補貼額。

Subsidy: When the government provides a per-unit subsidy, the price received by producers \(P_p\) is higher than the price paid by consumers \(P_c\). The difference is the per-unit subsidy.

$$ P_p - P_c = \text{Per-unit Subsidy} $$

弧彈性 (Arc Elasticity of Demand): 用於計算兩點之間的價格彈性。

Arc Elasticity of Demand: Used to calculate price elasticity between two points.

$$ E_d = \frac{\Delta Q / ((Q_1 + Q_2)/2)}{\Delta P / ((P_1 + P_2)/2)} $$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A

選項 A (正確): 在補貼前，均衡價格是 $60，數量是 10。補貼 $20 後，我們要在表中尋找一個交易量，使得生產者價格 \(P_p\) 和消費者價格 \(P_c\) 相差 $20。當數量為 12 時，消費者願意支付 $50 (\(P_c\))，而生產者需要 $70 (\(P_p\)) 才願意供應 12 單位。這正好符合 \(70 - 50 = 20\) 的補貼條件。因此，新交易量 \(Q_1 = 12\)。包括補貼在內的總收益 = \(P_p \times Q_1 = 70 \times 12 = 840\)。

Option A (Correct): Before subsidy, equilibrium price is $60 and quantity is 10. After a $20 subsidy, we look for a quantity where the producer price \(P_p\) and consumer price \(P_c\) differ by $20. At a quantity of 12, consumers are willing to pay $50 (\(P_c\)), and producers require $70 (\(P_p\)). This matches the subsidy condition: \(70 - 50 = 20\). Thus, new quantity \(Q_1 = 12\). Total revenue including subsidy = \(P_p \times Q_1 = 70 \times 12 = 840\).

選項 B (錯誤): 補貼總額 = 每單位補貼 \(\times\) 新交易量 = \(20 \times 12 = 240\)，而不是 $200。

Option B (Incorrect): Total subsidy = Per-unit subsidy \(\times\) New quantity = \(20 \times 12 = 240\), not $200.

選項 C (錯誤): 價格由 $60 跌至 $50，需求量由 10 升至 12。代入弧彈性公式：\(E_d = \frac{2 / 11}{10 / 55} = 1\)。彈性等於一（單一彈性），而非小於一。

Option C (Incorrect): Price drops from $60 to $50, quantity demanded rises from 10 to 12. Using arc elasticity: \(E_d = \frac{2 / 11}{10 / 55} = 1\). Elasticity is equal to one (unitary elastic), not smaller than one.

選項 D (錯誤): 該物品的價格下降，會導致其輔助品（Complements）的需求增加（需求曲線向右移）。輔助品的價格和交易量都會上升，因此其總收益必定增加，而非未能確定。

Option D (Incorrect): A drop in the price of this good increases the demand for its complements (rightward shift). Both price and quantity of complements will rise, so their total revenue will definitely increase, not uncertain.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的關鍵突破點 (Pivot Point) 是找出補貼後的新均衡數量。不要嘗試畫圖或移動整條供應曲線，而是直接在表格中尋找「垂直距離」。

因為 供應價格 - 需求價格 = 補貼，你只需要在同一數量下，尋找哪一個數量的 \(P_s - P_d = 20\)。

The Pivot Point of this question is finding the new equilibrium quantity after the subsidy. Don't try to draw or shift the entire supply curve. Instead, look for the "vertical distance" directly in the table.

Since Supply Price - Demand Price = Subsidy, you just need to find the quantity where \(P_s - P_d = 20\).

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱 1： 計算補貼總額時，使用了舊的均衡數量 (10)。學生會誤算為 \(20 \times 10 = 200\)，從而錯誤選擇 B。

Trap 1: Using the old equilibrium quantity (10) to calculate total subsidy. Students might mistakenly calculate \(20 \times 10 = 200\) and wrongly choose B.

陷阱 2： 混淆「包括補貼的總收益」和「不包括補貼的總收益」。包括補貼的總收益是 \(P_p \times Q_1\)，不包括補貼的則是 \(P_c \times Q_1\)。

Trap 2: Confusing "Total revenue including subsidy" with "Total revenue excluding subsidy". TR including subsidy is \(P_p \times Q_1\), while TR excluding subsidy is \(P_c \times Q_1\).

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼計算總收益時要用生產者價格 ($70) 而不是消費者價格 ($50)？

A: 題目問的是「包括補貼在內的總收益」。生產者每賣出一件產品，會從消費者手上收到 $50，同時從政府手上收到 $20 的補貼。因此，他們每單位的實際收入是 $70 (\(P_p\))。

Q: Why do we use the producer price ($70) instead of the consumer price ($50) to calculate total revenue?

A: The question asks for "total revenue including the subsidy". For every unit sold, producers receive $50 from consumers and $20 from the government. Therefore, their actual per-unit revenue is $70 (\(P_p\)).

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法： 在選擇題中遇到稅收或補貼的表格題，直接使用「橫向掃描法」。
補貼 $20 \(\rightarrow\) 找 \(Q_s\) 價格比 \(Q_d\) 價格高 20 的同一數量。
稅收 $20 \(\rightarrow\) 找 \(Q_d\) 價格比 \(Q_s\) 價格高 20 的同一數量。
一眼看出 Q=12 時，70 - 50 = 20，5秒內就能找到新均衡！

Shortcut: For tax/subsidy table questions in MCQs, use the "Horizontal Scan Method".
Subsidy of $20 \(\rightarrow\) Find the quantity where \(Q_s\) price is 20 higher than \(Q_d\) price.
Tax of $20 \(\rightarrow\) Find the quantity where \(Q_d\) price is 20 higher than \(Q_s\) price.
You can instantly see at Q=12, 70 - 50 = 20. You find the new equilibrium in 5 seconds!
