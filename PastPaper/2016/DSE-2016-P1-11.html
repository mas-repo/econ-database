HKDSE Economics: MCQ Analysis

Topic (課題) 企業擴張與整合 (Business Expansion and Integration)

Difficulty (難度) ★★☆☆☆

Key Concepts (關鍵概念) 側向擴張 (Lateral Expansion), 分散風險 (Risk Diversification), 絕對字眼陷阱 (Absolute Word Traps)

1. 題目 (The Question)

香港電視網絡有限公司(HKTV)推出一個網上購物平台「HKTV Mall」。這擴張

(1) 有助分散業務風險。

(2) 確保公司有更高的利潤。

(3) 將其品牌拓展至其他市場。

A. 只有(1)、(2)
B. 只有(1)、(3)
C. 只有(2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2)及(3)

Hong Kong Television Limited (HKTV) has launched an online shopping platform “HKTV Mall”. This expansion

(1) helps spread business risk

(2) ensure a higher profit for the company

(3) extends its brand name to other markets.

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

側向擴張 (Lateral / Conglomerate Expansion)
當一間企業將業務擴展至與原有業務相關但非直接競爭（側向）或完全無關（綜合）的市場時，其主要優勢之一是分散風險 (Spreading Risk)。如果原有業務（如電視廣播）表現不佳，新業務（如網購）的收入可以幫助抵銷損失。
Lateral / Conglomerate Expansion
When a firm expands into related but non-competing markets (lateral) or completely unrelated markets (conglomerate), a primary advantage is spreading risk. If the original business (e.g., TV broadcasting) underperforms, revenue from the new business (e.g., online shopping) can help offset the losses.
利潤的構成 (Composition of Profit)
我們必須記住，任何商業決策都有成本與風險。利潤是由總收入與總成本決定的。
Composition of Profit
We must remember that any business decision involves costs and risks. Profit is determined by Total Revenue and Total Cost.
$$\text{Profit} (\pi) = \text{Total Revenue} (TR) - \text{Total Cost} (TC)$$

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 Correct Option: B
分析陳述 (1) / Analyzing Statement (1): Correct
這項擴張從電視廣播行業跨足到零售/電子商務行業。這種多元化經營意味著公司的收入來源不再單一，因此「有助分散業務風險」是正確的。
This expansion moves from the TV broadcasting industry into the retail/e-commerce industry. This diversification implies the company no longer relies on a single revenue stream, making the statement "helps spread business risk" correct.
分析陳述 (2) / Analyzing Statement (2): Incorrect
經濟學中極少有商業行為能「確保」(ensure) 利潤。建立新平台需要巨大的固定成本和變動成本。如果該平台的總成本 ($TC$) 大於帶來的總收入 ($TR$)，公司將面臨虧損。因此，擴張不能保證更高利潤。
In Economics, very few business actions can "ensure" a profit. Building a new platform requires immense fixed and variable costs. If the Total Cost ($TC$) of this platform is greater than the Total Revenue ($TR$) it brings in, the company will face a loss. Thus, expansion cannot guarantee higher profits.
分析陳述 (3) / Analyzing Statement (3): Correct
網購平台被命名為「HKTV Mall」，直接使用了母公司的名稱「HKTV」。這明顯是將其現有的品牌知名度及商譽拓展至另一個市場（零售市場）。因此這句是正確的。
The shopping platform is named "HKTV Mall", directly utilizing the parent company's name "HKTV". This is a clear move to extend its existing brand awareness and goodwill into another market (the retail market). Therefore, this statement is correct.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題支點 (Pivot Point)：
這題的關鍵在於對「絕對字眼」(Absolute Words)的敏感度。陳述(2)中的「確保」(ensure) 是本題的破綻。只要明白商業世界充滿不確定性，就能立刻判定(2)為錯誤。
Pivot Point:
The key to this question lies in your sensitivity to Absolute Words. The word "ensure" in statement (2) is the flaw. As long as you understand that the business world is full of uncertainties, you can immediately determine that (2) is false.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

迷思：擴大規模 = 利潤上升
很多同學誤以為「擴張」(Expansion) 或「規模經濟」(Economies of Scale) 一定會帶來利潤。請記住，擴大業務規模同時也會增加成本（有時甚至會出現規模負經濟 Diseconomies of Scale）。只有當收入的增長大於成本的增長時，利潤才會上升。
Myth: Expanding Scale = Increased Profit
Many students mistakenly believe that "Expansion" or "Economies of Scale" automatically brings profit. Remember, expanding business scale also increases costs (and sometimes leads to Diseconomies of Scale). Profit only rises if the increase in revenue is greater than the increase in cost.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

問：分散風險不就等於減少了虧損的機會嗎？為什麼不能確保利潤？
答：分散風險（例如：不把雞蛋放在同一個籃子裡）確實能降低「全盤皆輸」的機率。但是，如果新業務（新籃子）本身的營運成本極高，或者不受消費者歡迎，它不但無法帶來利潤，反而會拖累整家公司的財務狀況。降低風險 $\neq$ 確保賺錢。
Q: Doesn't spreading risk mean reducing the chance of loss? Why can't it ensure profit?
A: Spreading risk (e.g., not putting all your eggs in one basket) does reduce the probability of a "total wipeout". However, if the new business (the new basket) has extremely high operating costs or is unpopular with consumers, it won't bring profit and might even drag down the company's overall finances. Reducing risk $\neq$ guaranteeing profit.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

秒殺法：消去法 (Elimination Method)
在DSE經濟科選擇題中，看到如「確保」(ensure)、「必須」(must)、「總是」(always) 等絕對字眼時，該選項通常是錯誤的（除非是必然的數學恆等式）。
當你確定 (2) 是錯誤的，你可以立刻刪除選項 A、C 和 D。唯一剩下的選項就是 B，你甚至不需要花太多時間思考 (1) 和 (3) 就能得分！這能為你節省寶貴的考試時間。
Speedkill Method: Elimination
In DSE Economics MCQs, when you see absolute words like "ensure", "must", or "always", that option is typically wrong (unless it's a mathematical identity).
Once you confirm that (2) is incorrect, you can immediately eliminate options A, C, and D. The only remaining option is B. You don't even need to spend much time analyzing (1) and (3) to score the mark! This saves you precious exam time.
