2. 題目 (The Question)

林先生在香港一間資訊科技公司任職流動程式設計師，收入為$1 000 000。他亦持有一家本地公司所發行的股票，收取$50 000股息。他買了一輛二手跑車，亦經香港國際機場飛往馬爾代夫享受假期。

根據上述資料，林先生要向香港政府支付 __________ 。

Mr Lam earns $1 000 000 as a mobile app designer of an IT firm in Hong Kong. Also, he earns a $50 000 dividend by holding shares issued by a local company. He buys a second-hand racing car and flies to the Maldives to spend his vacation through the Hong Kong International Airport.

Based on the above information, Mr Lam needs to pay __________ to the Hong Kong government.

  • (1) 薪俸稅 / (1) salaries tax
  • (2) 股息稅 / (2) dividend tax
  • (3) 首次登記稅 / (3) first registration tax
  • (4) 飛機乘客離境稅 / (4) air passenger departure tax
  • A. 只有(1)、(4) / (1) and (4) only
  • B. 只有(2)、(3) / (2) and (3) only
  • C. 只有(1)、(2)、(4) / (1), (2) and (4) only
  • D. 只有(1)、(3)、(4) / (1), (3) and (4) only

3. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解答這條題目，我們需要了解香港的基本稅制。香港採用「地域來源原則」(Territorial Source Principle)，並且以稅種簡單、稅率低見稱。香港並沒有對所有類型的收入徵稅。

To answer this question, we need to understand the basic tax system in Hong Kong. Hong Kong adopts the "Territorial Source Principle" and is known for its simple tax structure and low tax rates. Hong Kong does not tax all types of income.

  • 薪俸稅 (Salaries Tax): 對產生於或得自香港的職位、受僱工作或退休金的入息徵收的直接稅。
    A direct tax levied on income arising in or derived from Hong Kong from any office, employment, or pension.
  • 資本增值稅與股息稅 (Capital Gains & Dividend Tax): 香港**沒有**設立股息稅或資本增值稅。
    Hong Kong does **not** have a dividend tax or capital gains tax.
  • 汽車首次登記稅 (First Registration Tax): 對在香港**第一次**登記的汽車徵收的間接稅。如果車輛轉手（二手車），新車主無需再次繳納此稅。
    An indirect tax levied on motor vehicles registered for the **first time** in Hong Kong. If the vehicle is resold (second-hand), the new owner does not pay this tax again.
  • 飛機乘客離境稅 (Air Passenger Departure Tax): 12歲或以上人士乘搭飛機離開香港時需要繳付的稅項（通常已包含在機票價格內）。
    A tax payable by anyone aged 12 or above departing from Hong Kong by air (usually included in the price of the air ticket).

雖然本題不涉及計算，但溫習薪俸稅時，我們常會用到以下公式計算應繳稅款 \(T\)：

Although this question does not involve calculation, when revising salaries tax, we often use the following formula to calculate Tax Payable \(T\):

$$ T = (\text{Total Income} - \text{Deductions} - \text{Allowances}) \times \text{Progressive Tax Rate} $$

或以標準稅率 \(t\) 計算：

Or calculated at the standard rate \(t\):

$$ T = (\text{Total Income} - \text{Deductions}) \times t $$

最終應繳稅款取兩者中較低的一個。

The final tax payable is the lower of the two.

4. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是：The correct option is: Option A

(1) 薪俸稅 (Salaries Tax): 正確 ✔️
林先生在香港的資訊科技公司工作並賺取 $1,000,000。這筆收入源自香港的受僱工作，因此他必須繳納薪俸稅。

(1) Salaries tax: Correct ✔️
Mr. Lam works in an IT firm in Hong Kong and earns $1,000,000. This income is derived from employment in Hong Kong, so he must pay salaries tax.

(2) 股息稅 (Dividend Tax): 錯誤 ❌
他雖然收取了 $50,000 股息，但香港並沒有設立股息稅。因此他不需要為此向政府繳稅。

(2) Dividend tax: Incorrect ❌
Although he receives a $50,000 dividend, Hong Kong does not have a dividend tax. Therefore, he does not need to pay tax on this to the government.

(3) 首次登記稅 (First Registration Tax): 錯誤 ❌
題目清楚指出林先生購買的是「二手跑車」(second-hand racing car)。首次登記稅只會在汽車第一次在香港登記時徵收，買賣二手車無需再次繳交此稅。

(3) First registration tax: Incorrect ❌
The question clearly states that Mr. Lam bought a "second-hand racing car". First registration tax is only levied when a vehicle is registered in Hong Kong for the first time. Buying and selling second-hand cars does not incur this tax again.

(4) 飛機乘客離境稅 (Air Passenger Departure Tax): 正確 ✔️
林先生經香港國際機場飛往馬爾代夫，任何經香港國際機場乘飛機離境的乘客（除非獲豁免，如未滿12歲）均須繳納飛機乘客離境稅。

(4) Air passenger departure tax: Correct ✔️
Mr. Lam flies to the Maldives through the Hong Kong International Airport. Any passenger departing by air from the HKIA (unless exempted, e.g., under 12) must pay the air passenger departure tax.

5. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

做這類情境題，最重要是將題目的長句子拆解成獨立的經濟行為，並逐一配對香港的稅制：

When doing this kind of scenario-based question, the most important thing is to break down the long sentences into independent economic activities and match them one by one with Hong Kong's tax system:

  • 行為 1：「在香港...任職...收入為$1 000 000」 ➔ 觸發：薪俸稅。
    Activity 1: "earns $1 000 000 as a... in Hong Kong" ➔ Triggers: Salaries Tax.
  • 行為 2：「收取$50 000股息」 ➔ 陷阱：香港無股息稅。
    Activity 2: "earns a $50 000 dividend" ➔ Trap: No dividend tax in HK.
  • 行為 3：「買了一輛二手跑車」 ➔ 關鍵字「二手」是解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)。免除了首次登記稅。
    Activity 3: "buys a second-hand racing car" ➔ The keyword "second-hand" is the Pivot Point. It exempts the first registration tax.
  • 行為 4：「經香港國際機場飛往馬爾代夫」 ➔ 觸發：飛機乘客離境稅。
    Activity 4: "flies... through the Hong Kong International Airport" ➔ Triggers: Air Passenger Departure Tax.

6. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱一：對「稅項是否存在」概念模糊
很多學生一看到有「收入」，就直覺認為要交稅。DSE 經常利用股息 (Dividends)、銀行利息 (Bank Interests) 和資本增值 (Capital Gains，例如賣樓賺錢) 來設局。謹記：這三項在香港通常是免稅的！

Trap 1: Confusion over "Existence of Taxes"
Many students intuitively think that all "income" is taxable. The DSE frequently uses dividends, bank interests, and capital gains (e.g., profit from selling property) as distractors. Remember: These three are generally tax-free in Hong Kong!

陷阱二：走漏眼看錯「二手」為「新車」
這是一個經典的粗心大意錯誤 (Careless error)。如果題目只寫「買了一輛跑車」，在現實中可能是一手或二手，但如果是「進口新車」，就必須繳納(3)。考評局刻意加入「二手」二字，就是要淘汰不夠細心的考生。

Trap 2: Overlooking "Second-hand" and assuming "New Car"
This is a classic careless error. If the question just said "bought a racing car", it could be first-hand or second-hand in reality. The HKEAA intentionally added the word "second-hand" to filter out careless candidates.

7. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 如果林先生自己從日本直接進口一輛二手車到香港使用，他需要交首次登記稅嗎？

Q: If Mr. Lam personally imported a used car directly from Japan for use in Hong Kong, does he need to pay the first registration tax?

A: 需要！ 雖然車輛在日本是「二手」，但只要它是第一次在香港運輸署登記出牌，就要繳納汽車首次登記稅。本題的語境暗示這輛車已經在本地市場流通（即之前已經有人在香港登記並交過稅了）。

A: Yes! Even though the vehicle is "used" in Japan, as long as it is being registered with the Hong Kong Transport Department for the first time, the first registration tax applies. The context of this question implies the car was already circulating in the local market (i.e., someone else had already registered it and paid the tax in HK previously).

Q: 題目給出的薪金 $1,000,000 和股息 $50,000 這些數字有用嗎？

Q: Are the figures $1,000,000 for salary and $50,000 for dividend useful?

A: 完全沒有實際計算用途。 這些數字純粹是干擾資訊 (Distractors)。選擇題考驗的是你的定性判斷（交甚麼稅），而不是定量計算（交多少稅）。

A: They have no practical calculation use at all. These numbers are purely distractors. The multiple-choice question tests your qualitative judgment (what tax to pay), not quantitative calculation (how much tax to pay).

8. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

排除法 (Process of Elimination) 是神兵利器！

The Process of Elimination is your best weapon!

  1. 一秒看出 (2) 股息稅 在香港不存在，即時劃走 (2)。
    Spot in one second that (2) Dividend Tax does not exist in HK, immediately cross out (2).
    ➔ 選項 B 和 C 即時出局 (Eliminated)。 / Options B and C are instantly eliminated.
  2. 現在只剩下 A (1, 4) 和 D (1, 3, 4)。兩者的差異在於 (3)。
    Now only A (1, 4) and D (1, 3, 4) remain. The difference between them is (3).
  3. 掃視題目尋找汽車相關字眼，看到「二手」兩字，確認不用交 (3) 首次登記稅。劃走 (3)。
    Scan the text for car-related words, spot "second-hand", confirm that (3) First Registration Tax is not required. Cross out (3).
  4. 答案自然呼之欲出：A。完全不需要花時間去思考 (1) 和 (4)！
    The answer naturally emerges: A. You don't even need to spend time analyzing (1) and (4)!
