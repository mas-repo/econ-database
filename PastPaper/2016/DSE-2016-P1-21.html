Key Concepts 關鍵概念 Allocative Efficiency (配置效率), Marginal Social Benefit (邊際社會得益), Marginal Social Cost (邊際社會成本), Total Social Surplus (總社會盈餘)

2. The Question (題目)

下列哪種情況會達至有效率？

In which of the following situations is efficiency attained?

  • A. 總社會得益等於總社會成本。
    A. Total social benefits is equal to total social cost.
  • B. 生產者盈餘等於消費者盈餘。
    B. Producer surplus is equal to consumer surplus.
  • C. 邊際社會得益和邊際社會成本的差額為零。
    C. The difference between marginal social benefits and marginal social cost is zero.
  • D. 市場上全部消費者的消費者盈餘達至最高。
    D. The consumer surplus of all consumers in the market is maximized.

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

配置效率 (Allocative Efficiency)

在經濟學中，「有效率」通常指**配置效率 (Allocative Efficiency)**。當社會資源被分配到能產生最大總社會盈餘 (Total Social Surplus) 的狀態時，配置效率便會達成。這意味著不可能在不損害其他人的情況下，使任何人變得更好（柏拉圖最適）。

In economics, "efficiency" typically refers to **Allocative Efficiency**. It is attained when society's resources are allocated in a way that maximizes Total Social Surplus. This means it is impossible to make anyone better off without making someone else worse off (Pareto Optimality).

核心條件 (The Core Condition): $$ MSB = MSC $$

這表示額外生產一單位的社會得益 \( MSB \) 剛好等於額外生產該單位的社會成本 \( MSC \)。若將等式移項，即為：

This means the social benefit of producing one additional unit \( MSB \) is exactly equal to the social cost of producing that unit \( MSC \). Rearranging the equation gives:

$$ MSB - MSC = 0 $$
  • 邊際社會得益 (MSB): 社會從消費多一單位物品中所獲得的額外得益（通常由需求曲線 D 反映）。
    The additional benefit society gains from consuming one more unit of a good (usually reflected by the Demand curve D).
  • 邊際社會成本 (MSC): 社會生產多一單位物品所付出的額外成本（通常由供應曲線 S 反映）。
    The additional cost society incurs from producing one more unit of a good (usually reflected by the Supply curve S).
  • 總社會盈餘 (Total Social Surplus): 消費者盈餘 (CS) 與生產者盈餘 (PS) 的總和。當 \( MSB = MSC \) 時，總社會盈餘達到最大值。
    The sum of Consumer Surplus (CS) and Producer Surplus (PS). Total Social Surplus is maximized when \( MSB = MSC \).

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是： 選項 C (Option C)

The correct answer is: Option C

Incorrect 錯誤

選項 A 分析： 總社會得益 (TSB) 等於總社會成本 (TSC) 並非效率的條件。這只代表社會在該物品上的「淨得益為零」 (即盈虧平衡)。要達到效率，我們需要的是**最大化**總社會得益與總社會成本之間的差額（即最大化淨得益/總社會盈餘），而不是讓它們相等。

Option A Analysis: Total social benefit (TSB) equaling total social cost (TSC) is not a condition for efficiency. This merely means society's "net benefit is zero" (break-even). To achieve efficiency, we want to **maximize** the difference between TSB and TSC (i.e., maximize net benefit/total social surplus), not make them equal.

Incorrect 錯誤

選項 B 分析： 生產者盈餘等於消費者盈餘 (\( PS = CS \)) 是一個關於「公平/平均」的概念，與「效率」無關。效率只關心這兩者的「總和」是否最大化，而不在乎兩塊餅是否切得一樣大。

Option B Analysis: Producer surplus equaling consumer surplus (\( PS = CS \)) is a concept about "equity/fairness", unrelated to "efficiency". Efficiency only concerns whether the "sum" of the two is maximized, regardless of whether the pie is divided equally.

Correct 正確

選項 C 分析： 正如概念重溫所述，當市場達到配置效率時，\( MSB = MSC \)。這意味著邊際社會得益和邊際社會成本的差額為零 (\( MSB - MSC = 0 \))。在此產量水平下，社會無法透過增加或減少生產來增加總盈餘，因此達至最高效率。

Option C Analysis: As stated in the concept review, allocative efficiency is achieved when \( MSB = MSC \). This implies that the difference between marginal social benefit and marginal social cost is zero (\( MSB - MSC = 0 \)). At this output level, society cannot increase total surplus by producing more or less, hence efficiency is maximized.

Incorrect 錯誤

選項 D 分析： 效率是要最大化「總社會盈餘」(包含消費者和生產者)。如果單純只追求最大化「消費者盈餘」，通常意味著要把價格壓到極低（甚至為零），這會導致生產者蒙受巨大損失，最終導致整體社會出現淨損失 (Deadweight Loss)，這顯然是無效率的。

Option D Analysis: Efficiency aims to maximize "Total Social Surplus" (including both consumers and producers). Simply maximizing "Consumer Surplus" alone would usually mean pushing prices down to near zero, causing massive losses for producers and creating a Deadweight Loss for society as a whole, which is highly inefficient.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題關鍵點 (The Pivot Point): 釐清「邊際 (Marginal)」與「總量 (Total)」的差別，以及「相等 (Equal)」與「最大化 (Maximize)」的差別。

The Pivot Point: Clarifying the difference between "Marginal" and "Total", as well as the difference between "Equal" and "Maximize".

  • 干擾資訊 (Distractors): 選項 A 故意用「總得益 = 總成本」來迷惑學生。很多學生看到「相等」就覺得是完美狀態，但經濟學中的優化 (Optimization) 總是發生在「邊際」上。
  • 核心概念提取 (Crucial Info): 看到「效率 (Efficiency)」，腦海中應立即浮現公式：最大化總盈餘 (Max Total Surplus) \(\rightarrow\) 條件：\( MSB = MSC \)。
  • Distractors: Option A uses "Total Benefit = Total Cost" to trick students. Many students see "equal" and think it's a perfect state, but optimization in economics always happens at the "margin".
  • Crucial Info: When you see "Efficiency", the formula should immediately pop into your head: Maximize Total Surplus \(\rightarrow\) Condition: \( MSB = MSC \).

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱一：混淆「總量 (Total)」與「邊際 (Marginal)」

學生常誤選 A。請記住，如果 \( TSB = TSC \)，代表社會做這件事完全白做了（利潤為0）。我們做決策的黃金法則是 \( MB = MC \) (邊際得益等於邊際成本)。

Trap 1: Confusing Total vs. Marginal. Students often mistakenly choose A. Remember, if \( TSB = TSC \), it means society gains net zero from this activity (zero profit). The golden rule of decision-making is \( MB = MC \) (Marginal Benefit equals Marginal Cost).

陷阱二：將「公平」與「效率」混為一談

學生常誤選 B。\( CS = PS \) 看起來很公平，但在經濟學中，效率不等於公平。一塊蛋糕如果做到最大（效率），就算一個人拿90%，另一個人拿10%（不公平），依然是有效率的。

Trap 2: Conflating Fairness with Efficiency. Students often choose B. \( CS = PS \) looks fair, but in economics, efficiency \(\neq\) equity. If the pie is maximized (efficiency), even if one person gets 90% and the other 10% (inequitable), it is still efficient.

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果 \( MSB > MSC \)，代表什麼？

A: 這代表社會從多生產一件物品中獲得的得益，大於生產它的成本。此時處於「生產不足 (Underproduction)」的狀態，存在淨損失 (Deadweight Loss)。社會應該**增加生產**，直到 \( MSB = MSC \)。

Q: What does it mean if \( MSB > MSC \)?

A: This means the benefit society gets from producing one more unit is greater than the cost of producing it. This is a state of "Underproduction" and a Deadweight Loss exists. Society should **increase production** until \( MSB = MSC \).

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法 (Shortcut)： 在DSE微觀經濟學中，只要題目問及「效率條件 (Condition for Efficiency)」，你的眼睛應該直接掃描選項尋找包含「**邊際 (Marginal)**」的字眼。效率永遠是在邊際上決定的。選項 A、B、D 都在談論「總 (Total)」或「全部 (All)」，可以直接剔除。

Shortcut: In DSE Microeconomics, whenever a question asks for the "Condition for Efficiency", your eyes should immediately scan the options for the word "**Marginal**". Efficiency is always determined at the margin. Options A, B, and D all talk about "Total" or "All" and can be eliminated almost instantly.
