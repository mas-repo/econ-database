📚 課題 (Topic): 產權與交易費用 (Property Rights and Transaction Costs)

⭐ 難度 (Difficulty): ★★☆☆☆ (2/5)

🔑 關鍵概念 (Key Concepts): 私有產權 (Private Property Rights), 知識產權 (Intellectual Property Rights)

📝 題目 (The Question)

瑪蓮從書店購買了一本烹飪書。她的朋友潔文告訴她：「你擁有這烹飪書的私有產權。」瑪蓮在購入該書後會擁有哪些權利？

(1) 按書中的食譜，為潔文準備晚餐。
(2) 把整本書掃瞄並上載到她的網誌。
(3) 把書放在書架，不去閱讀它。
(4) 把書借給潔文，並要求一頓晚餐作回報。

A. 只有(1)、(2)、(3)
B. 只有(1)、(2)、(4)
C. 只有(1)、(3)、(4)
D. 只有(2)、(3)、(4)

Marlene had bought a cookery book from a bookstore. Her friend, Kitman, said to her, “You have private property rights to this cookery book.” Which of the following rights would Marlene have after the purchase of the book?

(1) To follow the receipts in the book in preparing a diner for Kitman.
(2) To scan and upload the whole book to her blog
(3) To leave the book on the bookshelf without reading it
(4) To lend the book to Kitman and ask for a diner in return

A. (1), (2) and (3) only
B. (1), (2) and (4) only
C. (1), (3) and (4) only
D. (2), (3) and (4) only

🧠 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

私有產權 (Private Property Rights) 包含三個主要部分：

  1. 獨有使用權 (Exclusive Right to Use): 擁有者有權決定如何使用該物品（包括不使用它）。
  2. 獨有收入享受權 (Exclusive Right to Derive Income): 擁有者有權從該物品中獲取收入（收入可以是金錢或非金錢回報）。
  3. 獨有轉讓權 (Exclusive Right to Transfer): 擁有者有權將物品的擁有權或使用權轉讓給他人（如出售、出租、借出或贈送）。

⚠️ 重要區分： 擁有實體物品（如一本書）的產權，不等於擁有該物品內容的版權（知識產權）。

Private Property Rights consist of three main components:

  1. Exclusive Right to Use: The owner has the right to decide how to use the good (including not using it).
  2. Exclusive Right to Derive Income: The owner has the right to receive income generated from the good (income can be monetary or non-monetary).
  3. Exclusive Right to Transfer: The owner has the right to transfer the ownership or usage right to others (e.g., sell, rent, lend, or give away).

⚠️ Crucial Distinction: Owning the physical property right of an item (like a book) does not equal owning the copyright (intellectual property right) of its content.

✅ 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): C

(1) 按書中的食譜，為潔文準備晚餐。 (正確 Correct)

這是行使「獨有使用權」。瑪蓮有權決定如何使用這本實體書。
This is exercising the "Exclusive Right to Use". Marlene has the right to decide how to use the physical book.

(2) 把整本書掃瞄並上載到她的網誌。 (錯誤 Incorrect)

這侵犯了版權（知識產權）。瑪蓮只購買了實體書，並沒有購買該書的版權，因此她沒有權利複製及公開發佈內容。
This violates copyright (intellectual property rights). Marlene only bought the physical book, not its copyright, so she has no right to reproduce and publicly distribute the content.

(3) 把書放在書架，不去閱讀它。 (正確 Correct)

這同樣是行使「獨有使用權」。選擇「不使用」也是一種使用權的體現。
This is also exercising the "Exclusive Right to Use". Choosing "not to use" is a manifestation of the right to use.

(4) 把書借給潔文，並要求一頓晚餐作回報。 (正確 Correct)

這是行使「獨有轉讓權」（借出）及「獨有收入享受權」（獲得一頓晚餐作為非金錢收入）。
This exercises the "Exclusive Right to Transfer" (lending) and the "Exclusive Right to Derive Income" (receiving a dinner as non-monetary income).

🧩 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point): 本題的關鍵在於分辨「實體產權」與「知識產權」。選項 (2) 是一個典型的陷阱，測試學生是否知道買書不等於買版權。

Pivot Point: The key to this question is distinguishing between "physical property rights" and "intellectual property rights". Option (2) is a classic trap testing if students know that buying a book doesn't mean buying the copyright.

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

1. 誤解「收入」的定義： 很多學生以為「收入享受權」必須涉及金錢 \( \$ \)。實際上，選項(4)中的「一頓晚餐」屬於實物收入 (in-kind income)，同樣是行使收入享受權。

2. 以為「不用」等於「沒有權利」： 學生常對選項(3)感到猶豫。記住，你有權決定把你的財產閒置。

1. Misunderstanding "Income": Many students think "derive income" must involve money \( \$ \). In reality, the "dinner" in option (4) is in-kind income, which still counts as deriving income.

2. Thinking "not using" means "no right": Students often hesitate on option (3). Remember, you have the right to leave your property idle.

🙋 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼瑪蓮不能掃描自己的書？書明明是她買的！

A: 因為一本書包含兩種產權：紙張和墨水的「實體產權」（屬於瑪蓮），以及文字內容的「知識產權/版權」（屬於作者或出版社）。瑪蓮只買了前者。

Q: Why can't Marlene scan her own book? She bought it!

A: Because a book contains two types of property rights: the "physical property right" of paper and ink (belongs to Marlene), and the "intellectual property right/copyright" of the text (belongs to the author/publisher). Marlene only bought the former.

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

消去法 (Elimination Method):
在DSE考試中，當你看到選項 (2) 涉及「掃描並上載整本書」，這明顯是侵犯版權的違法行為。確定 (2) 是錯誤的之後，你可以立即刪去包含 (2) 的選項 A、B 和 D。答案瞬間只剩下 C！完全不需要花時間分析 (1)、(3) 和 (4)。

Elimination Method:
In the DSE exam, when you see option (2) involves "scanning and uploading the whole book", it is clearly illegal copyright infringement. Once you determine (2) is wrong, you can immediately eliminate options A, B, and D which contain (2). The answer instantly becomes C! You don't even need to spend time analyzing (1), (3), and (4).
