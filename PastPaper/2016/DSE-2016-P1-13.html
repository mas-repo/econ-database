HKDSE Economics: 需求與供應 (Demand and Supply)

Topic 微觀經濟學：需求與供應 (Microeconomics: Demand and Supply)
Difficulty ★★☆☆☆
Key Concepts Change in Demand, Change in Quantity Demanded, Expectations, Interest Rates

📝 題目 (The Question)

下列哪種情況會令私人房屋的需求增加？
Which of the following situations would lead to an increase in the demand for private housing?
A. 私人房屋的價格下降。
A. The price of private housing decreases.
B. 公共房屋的供應上升。
B. The supply of public housing increases.
C. 人們預期按揭貸款的利率稍後會上升。
C. People expect the interest rate for mortgage loans to increase later.
D. 人們預期私人房屋的價格稍後會上升。
D. People expect the price of private housing to increase later.

🧠 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

需求 (Demand) vs. 需求量 (Quantity Demanded)
Demand vs. Quantity Demanded
  • 需求量改變 (Change in Quantity Demanded)： 僅由物品本身的價格改變引起。在圖表上表現為沿著同一條需求曲線移動 (Movement along the curve)。
  • 需求改變 (Change in Demand)： 由物品本身價格以外的其他因素（如收入、替代品價格、預期等）改變引起。在圖表上表現為整條需求曲線的移動 (Shift of the curve)。
  • Change in Quantity Demanded: Caused ONLY by a change in the price of the good itself. Represented graphically as a movement along the same demand curve.
  • Change in Demand: Caused by factors other than the good's own price (e.g., income, price of substitutes, expectations). Represented graphically as a shift of the entire demand curve.
$$ \text{Price } P \downarrow \implies \text{Quantity Demanded } Q_d \uparrow \text{ (Movement)} $$ $$ \text{Expected Future Price } P_e \uparrow \implies \text{Current Demand } D \uparrow \text{ (Rightward Shift)} $$

✅ 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案：選項 D (Option D)
Correct Answer: Option D
A. 私人房屋的價格下降。
分析：錯誤。私人房屋本身的價格下降，只會導致「需求量」(Quantity Demanded) 增加（沿著需求曲線向下移動），而不會令整條「需求」(Demand) 曲線移動。
Analysis: Incorrect. A decrease in the price of private housing itself will only lead to an increase in "Quantity Demanded" (a downward movement along the demand curve), not a shift in the "Demand" curve.
B. 公共房屋的供應上升。
分析：錯誤。公共房屋是私人房屋的替代品 (Substitute)。當公共房屋供應上升，更多人能獲得公屋，他們對私人房屋的需求便會減少（需求曲線向左移）。
Analysis: Incorrect. Public housing is a substitute for private housing. When the supply of public housing increases, more people can access it, which will decrease their demand for private housing (demand curve shifts to the left).
C. 人們預期按揭貸款的利率稍後會上升。
分析：錯誤。這取決於按揭是「浮動利率」還是「固定利率」。在香港（以及 DSE 的預設語境中），絕大多數按揭都是浮動利率 (Floating Rate)（例如 HIBOR 或 P 按）。這意味著即使你現在買樓，未來的供款也會隨著利率上升而增加。因此，預期未來利率上升代表未來的持有成本增加，這會降低人們現在買樓的意欲，導致現時需求減少。
Analysis: Incorrect. This depends on whether mortgages are "floating rate" or "fixed rate". In Hong Kong (and the implicit context of the DSE), the vast majority of mortgages are floating rate (e.g., HIBOR or Prime-based). This means even if you buy now, your future repayments will increase when the interest rate rises. Therefore, expecting future interest rates to rise means the future cost of holding the property increases, which reduces the incentive to buy now, leading to a decrease in current demand.
D. 人們預期私人房屋的價格稍後會上升。
分析：正確。如果買家預期未來樓價會上升，他們會傾向現在提早購買（以避免將來要付更高價格，或為了將來轉售圖利）。這會導致現時對私人房屋的需求增加（需求曲線向右移）。
Analysis: Correct. If buyers expect housing prices to rise in the future, they will tend to buy now (to avoid paying higher prices later, or to sell for a profit later). This leads to an increase in the current demand for private housing (demand curve shifts to the right).

🔍 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解答這類題目的關鍵在於區分「需求」(Demand) 和 「需求量」(Quantity Demanded)：
The key to solving this type of question is distinguishing between "Demand" and "Quantity Demanded":
  • 樞紐字眼 (Pivot Point)： 題目問的是「需求增加」(Increase in demand)，這意味著你需要尋找一個非價格因素 (Non-price factor)。
    Pivot Point: The question asks for an "increase in demand", which means you must look for a non-price factor.
  • 排除法： 看到選項 A 提及「價格下降」，立刻可以排除，因為這只影響需求量。
    Process of Elimination: Seeing "price decreases" in Option A allows you to eliminate it immediately, as this only affects quantity demanded.

💬 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q1: 關於選項 C，如果按揭是「固定利率 (Fixed Rate)」，答案會不一樣嗎？
Q1: Regarding Option C, if mortgages were "Fixed Rate", would the answer be different?
A: 非常好的問題！是的，如果市場上普遍採用固定利率按揭 (Fixed-rate Mortgages)（即合約期內利率永遠不變），那麼預期未來利率上升，確實會促使買家「趕搭尾班車」——他們會想在利率上升前，現在立刻買樓並鎖定目前的低息。在這種假設下，現時需求確實會增加。

然而，在香港的現實與 DSE 考試語境中： 香港的按揭市場幾乎全是浮動利率 (Floating Rate)。這表示無論你何時買樓，只要未來銀行加息，你每月的供款負擔就會加重。因此，預期加息只會令買家覺得未來供樓更辛苦，從而卻步，導致現時需求減少。在做 DSE 題目時，必須以香港的實際市場運作（浮動利率）為基礎來推論。
A: Excellent question! Yes, if the market predominantly used fixed-rate mortgages (where the rate never changes during the contract term), expecting future interest rates to rise would indeed prompt buyers to "rush to buy"—they would want to buy immediately to lock in the current low interest rate before it goes up. Under this assumption, current demand would increase.

However, in the context of Hong Kong and the DSE exams: Hong Kong's mortgage market is almost entirely based on floating rates. This means no matter when you buy, if banks raise interest rates in the future, your monthly repayment burden will increase. Therefore, expecting a rate hike only makes buyers anticipate heavier future financial burdens, causing them to hold back, which decreases current demand. When doing DSE questions, you must base your reasoning on how the Hong Kong market actually operates (floating rates).

💡 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

秒殺技巧： 圈起題目中的「需求」(Demand) 兩字。只要題目問的是「需求」而不是「需求量」，任何選項只要寫著「該物品的價格改變 (Price of the good changes)」，連看都不用看，直接劃掉 (Cross out)！這樣你就能瞬間排除選項 A，大大提高命中率。
Shortcut: Circle the word "Demand" in the question. As long as the question asks about "Demand" and not "Quantity Demanded", any option that states "the price of the good changes" can be immediately crossed out without a second thought! This allows you to instantly eliminate Option A and significantly increase your chances of getting it right.
