📚 課題 (Topic): 勞工報酬計算方法 (Labour Remuneration Methods)

⭐ 難度 (Difficulty): ★☆☆☆☆ (1/5 Stars)

🔑 關鍵字 (Key Concepts): 件工 (Piece-rate), 計時 (Time-rate), 產出量度 (Measurement of Output)

📝 題目 (The Question)

下列哪位最有可能以件工計酬？

A. 一位報章專欄作家
B. 一位小學教師
C. 一位消防員
D. 一位巴士司機

Which of the following is most likely to be paid on a piece-rate basis?

A. a newspaper columnist
B. a primary school teacher
C. a fireman
D. a bus driver

🧠 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

件工 (Piece-rate): 根據工人完成的產出數量來計算薪酬。適用於產出容易量度、品質容易控制或標準化的工作。
Remuneration based on the quantity of output produced. Suitable for jobs where output is easily measurable and quality is standardized or easily monitored.

$$ \text{Income} = \text{Piece Rate} \times \text{Quantity of Output} $$ $$ \text{收入} = \text{件工工資率} \times \text{產出數量} $$

計時 (Time-rate): 根據工人工作的時間來計算薪酬。適用於產出難以量度、品質比數量更重要、或需要提供持續服務的工作。
Remuneration based on the time spent working. Suitable for jobs where output is hard to measure, quality is more important than quantity, or continuous service is required.

✅ 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): A

A. 一位報章專欄作家 (a newspaper columnist) - 正確 (Correct)
專欄作家的產出（文章數量）非常容易量度。編輯可以輕易根據他們提交的稿件數量（或字數）來計算薪酬，而且文章在出版前會經過編輯審核，容易控制品質。
A columnist's output (number of articles) is highly measurable. Editors can easily pay them based on the number of articles (or words) submitted. Quality is also easily controlled through editorial review before publication.

B. 一位小學教師 (a primary school teacher) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
教師的產出（學生的知識增長或品德培養）難以客觀量度。若以「教導的學生人數」為件工，會忽略教學質素。因此通常以計時（月薪）支付。
A teacher's output (students' knowledge or moral development) is difficult to measure objectively. Paying by "number of students taught" would compromise teaching quality. They are typically paid by time-rate (monthly salary).

C. 一位消防員 (a fireman) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
消防員的工作性質是提供隨時候命的服務。我們不能以「撲滅火災的數量」來計算薪酬，因為火災發生是不可預測的，且這樣會產生道德風險（例如鼓勵縱火）。
A fireman provides a stand-by service. We cannot pay them based on the "number of fires extinguished" because fires are unpredictable, and it would create a moral hazard (e.g., encouraging arson).

D. 一位巴士司機 (a bus driver) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
雖然可以計算司機駕駛的班次，但若採用件工，司機為了增加收入可能會超速駕駛或忽略乘客安全。因為安全（品質）至關重要，所以通常採用計時制。
Although the number of trips could be counted, paying a bus driver by piece-rate might encourage speeding or ignoring passenger safety to complete more trips. Since safety (quality) is paramount, time-rate is preferred.

🔍 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point):
判斷一種職業是否適合件工，只需問兩個問題：
1. 產出是否容易量度？(Is output easily measurable?)
2. 追求數量會否嚴重影響服務質素或安全？(Will pursuing quantity severely compromise quality or safety?)

To determine if a job is suitable for piece-rate, ask two questions: 1. Is the output easily measurable? 2. Will pursuing quantity severely compromise quality or safety?

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

誤區：認為「只要能數得出數量」就可以用件工。
很多學生會誤選 D (巴士司機)，認為巴士班次可以輕易計算。但他們忽略了經濟學中選擇薪酬制度的另一個重要考量：品質控制與安全成本。件工會誘使工人犧牲品質以換取數量。
Trap: Thinking "anything countable" can be piece-rate. Many students wrongly choose D (bus driver) because trips are countable. They ignore a key economic consideration: quality control and safety costs. Piece-rate incentivizes workers to sacrifice quality for quantity.

🙋 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼專欄作家不怕他們為了賺錢而亂寫（犧牲品質）？
Why aren't we worried that columnists will write garbage just to make money (sacrificing quality)?

A: 因為報館有編輯把關（監察成本相對較低）。如果文章質素太差，編輯可以直接退稿不予刊登，作家就收不到錢。但巴士司機如果在路上超速，經理很難實時監察，一旦發生意外成本極高。
Because newspapers have editors acting as gatekeepers (monitoring cost is relatively low). If the quality is poor, editors reject it and the writer doesn't get paid. However, if a bus driver speeds, it's hard for managers to monitor in real-time, and the cost of an accident is catastrophic.

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

排除法 (Elimination Method):
在 DSE 考試中，遇到以下三類職業，通常不會是件工 (Usually NOT piece-rate):
1. 紀律部隊/隨時候命的服務 (Disciplinary forces / Stand-by services): 警察、消防員、保安 (Police, Firemen, Security guards).
2. 注重人身安全的交通運輸 (Safety-critical transport): 巴士司機、飛機師 (Bus drivers, Pilots).
3. 無形服務或知識傳授 (Intangible services / Knowledge transfer): 教師、社工 (Teachers, Social workers).

利用此法則，瞬間可以排除 B、C、D，直接選 A！
Using this rule, you can instantly eliminate B, C, and D, and choose A directly!
