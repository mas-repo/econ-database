1. The Question (題目)

細閱下圖。該圖顯示在2014年10月至2015年7月之間，

Study the diagram below. The diagram shows that during the period from October 2014 to July 2015,

  • A. 名義本地生產總值正在下跌。
    the nominal GDP was falling.
  • B. 貨幣的購買力正在上升。
    the purchasing power of money was increasing.
  • C. 實質本地生產總值的增長率高於名義本地生產總值的增長率。
    the growth rate of real GDP was higher than that of nominal GDP.
  • D. 名義利率高於已實現的實質利率。
    the nominal interest rate was higher than the realised real interest rate.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

GDP Deflator & Inflation (平減指數與通脹)

「本地生產總值平減指數的轉變(%)」實際上代表了**通貨膨脹率 (Inflation Rate)**。只要這個百分比是大於 0（正數），就代表整體物價水平正在上升（即發生通貨膨脹）。

The "Change in GDP deflator (%)" essentially represents the **Inflation Rate**. As long as this percentage is greater than 0 (positive), it means the general price level is rising (i.e., inflation is occurring).

The Fisher Equation (費沙方程式)

實質利率、名義利率與通脹率之間的關係由費沙方程式決定：

The relationship between real interest rate, nominal interest rate, and inflation rate is defined by the Fisher Equation:

$$ \text{Real Interest Rate} (r) = \text{Nominal Interest Rate} (i) - \text{Inflation Rate} (\pi) $$ $$ 實質利率 = 名義利率 - 通貨膨脹率 $$

Nominal vs Real GDP Growth (名義與實質GDP增長)

名義GDP增長率約等於實質GDP增長率加上通脹率：

The growth rate of Nominal GDP is approximately equal to the growth rate of Real GDP plus the inflation rate:

$$ \% \Delta \text{Nominal GDP} \approx \% \Delta \text{Real GDP} + \% \Delta \text{Price Level} (\pi) $$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option D

Option A: Incorrect (錯誤)

圖表只提供了物價（平減指數）的資訊。我們知道物價正在上升（因為轉變率是正數），但我們不知道實質產出（Real GDP）的變化。因此，我們無法確定名義本地生產總值（Nominal GDP）是上升還是下跌。

The graph only provides information about prices (the deflator). We know prices are rising (because the rate of change is positive), but we have no information about real output (Real GDP). Therefore, we cannot determine whether Nominal GDP is falling or rising.

Option B: Incorrect (錯誤)

圖表顯示平減指數的轉變百分比雖然在下降，但**全部都是正數**（大於0%）。這代表物價水平（Price Level, \(P\)）仍然在上升（只是升幅放緩）。貨幣購買力與物價成反比 \( \frac{1}{P} \)。由於物價上升，貨幣的購買力實際上是在**下跌**。

The graph shows that although the percentage change is decreasing, it is **entirely in the positive territory** (> 0%). This means the price level (\(P\)) is still rising (disinflation, not deflation). Purchasing power is inversely related to the price level \( \frac{1}{P} \). Since prices are rising, the purchasing power of money is actually **falling**.

Option C: Incorrect (錯誤)

根據公式：名義GDP增長率 = 實質GDP增長率 + 通脹率。由於通脹率（\(\pi\)）是大於0的正數，所以名義GDP的增長率必然**高於**實質GDP的增長率，選項C的說法剛好相反。

According to the formula: % \(\Delta\) Nominal GDP = % \(\Delta\) Real GDP + Inflation Rate. Since the inflation rate (\(\pi\)) is positive (> 0), the growth rate of nominal GDP must be **higher** than the growth rate of real GDP. Option C states the exact opposite.

Option D: Correct (正確) ✓

根據費沙方程式，已實現的實質利率 = 名義利率 - 實際通脹率。因為這段期間的通脹率是正數（\( \pi > 0 \)），所以實質利率必定小於名義利率（即名義利率高於實質利率）。

Based on the Fisher Equation, realised real interest rate = nominal interest rate - actual inflation rate. Because the inflation rate during this period is positive (\( \pi > 0 \)), the real interest rate must be less than the nominal interest rate (i.e., the nominal interest rate is higher than the realised real interest rate).

$$ r = i - \pi $$ $$ \text{Since } \pi > 0 \implies i - \pi < i \implies r < i $$ $$ \therefore i > r $$

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

The "Pivot Point" (破題關鍵):

這題的關鍵在於看懂 Y 軸。Y 軸是「轉變 (%)」(Change %)，而不是指數的「數值」(Level)。圖表中的線向下斜，很多學生會誤以為物價下跌。但請注意，整條線都在 **0% 之上**。這稱為**通脹放緩 (Disinflation)**，而非通縮 (Deflation)。只要線在 0 以上，物價就是在上升！

The key to solving this is understanding the Y-axis. It shows the "Change (%)", not the index "Level". The line is sloping downwards, misleading students into thinking prices are falling. However, the entire line is **above 0%**. This is called **Disinflation**, not Deflation. As long as the line is above 0, the price level is still increasing!

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

Trap 1: Confusing "Decreasing Rate" with "Falling Level" (混淆「增長率下降」與「數值下跌」)

很多學生看到圖表的線向下跌，就立刻選擇 B（購買力上升，意味著物價下跌）。要記住：通脹率由 3% 跌至 1%，代表物價**依然在上升**，只是升得比較慢。你的100元能買到的東西依然越來越少。

Many students see the downward sloping line and immediately choose B (purchasing power increasing, which implies falling prices). Remember: If inflation drops from 3% to 1%, prices are **still rising**, just at a slower pace. The amount of goods your $100 can buy is still decreasing.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果圖表的線跌穿了 0（變成負數），答案會有什麼不同？
What if the line on the graph dropped below 0 (became negative)? How would the answer change?

A: 如果線跌穿 0，代表通脹率變成負數（\( \pi < 0 \)），這就是**通貨緊縮 (Deflation)**。那時物價水平 (\(P\)) 才會真正下跌。如果發生通縮：
- 選項 B 會變成正確（物價跌，貨幣購買力上升）。
- 選項 C 會變成正確（因為 \( \pi \) 是負數，實質 GDP 增長率會大於名義 GDP 增長率）。
- 選項 D 會變成錯誤（因為 \( r = i - (-\pi) = i + \pi \)，實質利率會高於名義利率）。

If the line drops below 0, the inflation rate is negative (\( \pi < 0 \)), which is **Deflation**. The price level (\(P\)) would actually be falling. If deflation occurs:
- Option B would be correct (prices fall, purchasing power rises).
- Option C would be correct (since \( \pi \) is negative, real GDP growth > nominal GDP growth).
- Option D would be incorrect (since \( r = i - (-\pi) = i + \pi \), real interest rate > nominal interest rate).

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法 (Shortcut): "The Zero-Line Check"

在考場上，遇到「通脹率/平減指數轉變」的圖表題，第一時間畫一條橫線標記 **Y=0** 的位置。

  1. 圖形全部在 0 以上 \(\rightarrow\) 物價升 (\(P \uparrow\)) \(\rightarrow\) 購買力跌，名義利率 > 實質利率。立刻鎖定答案 D！
  2. 選項 A 和 C 通常需要「產出(GDP)」的數據，如果題目沒給產出數據，可以直接判定為無法證實/錯誤。

In the exam, when you see a chart on "Inflation rate/Change in deflator", immediately draw a horizontal line at **Y=0**.

  1. If the graph is entirely above 0 \(\rightarrow\) Prices are rising (\(P \uparrow\)) \(\rightarrow\) Purchasing power falls, Nominal Interest > Real Interest. Lock in D immediately!
  2. Options A and C usually require data on "Output (GDP)". If the question doesn't provide output data, you can often quickly eliminate them as unverified/incorrect.
