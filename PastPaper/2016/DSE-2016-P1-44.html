HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: International Trade & Exchange Rates
課題：國際貿易與匯率
Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
難度：3/5
Key Concepts: Substitutes, Depreciation
關鍵概念：替代品、貶值

The Question (題目)

假設某地區中的國家都是出口物品往美國的競爭對手。除了X國的貨幣外，在該地區中其他國家的貨幣兌美元均貶值。結果，X國出口往美國的物品數量會
Suppose the countries in a certain region are competitors of exporting goods to the U.S.. The currencies of the countries in the region except that of Country X depreciate against the US dollar. As a result, the volume of Country X’s exports to the U.S. will

  • A. 下降。 (decrease.)
  • B. 上升。 (increase.)
  • C. 維持不變。 (remain constant.)
  • D. 未能確定。 (be indeterminate.)

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 匯率與出口價格 (Exchange Rate & Export Price):
當一國貨幣貶值（Depreciation），該國出口貨品以外幣計算的價格會下降。 When a country's currency depreciates, the price of its exports in terms of foreign currency decreases.
$$ \text{Currency} \downarrow \Rightarrow P_{\text{export (in USD)}} \downarrow $$
2. 替代品與需求 (Substitutes & Demand):
題目提到這些國家是「競爭對手」（Competitors），這意味著它們出口的貨品互為「替代品」（Substitutes）。根據需求定律，若替代品的價格下降，對原本物品的需求會減少。 The question states the countries are "competitors," implying their export goods are "substitutes." According to the Law of Demand, if the price of a substitute decreases, the demand for the original good will decrease.
$$ P_{\text{substitute}} \downarrow \Rightarrow D_{\text{original good}} \downarrow $$

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A (下降 / decrease)

邏輯推演 (Logical Deduction):
  1. 競爭對手的貨幣貶值： 除了X國以外，其他地區國家的貨幣兌美元貶值。這表示對於美國進口商來說，購買這些「其他國家」的貨品變得更便宜了。 Competitors' currency depreciates: The currencies of other countries (except X) depreciate against the USD. This means for US importers, buying goods from these "other countries" becomes cheaper.
  2. 相對價格的改變： 雖然X國的貨幣匯率可能沒有變動（題目暗示），但相對於那些貨幣貶值的競爭對手，X國的貨品變得「相對昂貴」。 Change in relative price: Although Country X's currency might be unchanged (implied), compared to the competitors whose currencies depreciated, Country X's goods become "relatively more expensive."
  3. 替代效應： 由於其他國家的貨品是X國貨品的替代品，美國買家會轉而購買價格較低的其他國家貨品。 Substitution Effect: Since goods from other countries are substitutes for Country X's goods, US buyers will switch to buying the cheaper goods from other countries.
  4. 結論： 美國對X國出口物品的需求減少，導致X國出口數量下降。 Conclusion: The demand for Country X's exports by the US decreases, leading to a decrease in the volume of Country X's exports.
選項分析 (Option Breakdown):
  • A (Correct): 競爭對手價格下降 \(\rightarrow\) 替代效應 \(\rightarrow\) X國出口需求下降。 Competitors' prices drop \(\rightarrow\) Substitution effect \(\rightarrow\) Demand for X's exports drops.
  • B (Incorrect): 這只會在競爭對手貨幣升值（變貴）或X國貨幣貶值時發生。 This would only happen if competitors' currencies appreciated (became expensive) or Country X's currency depreciated.
  • C (Incorrect): 由於相對價格改變，貿易量必然受影響，不會維持不變。 Since relative prices have changed, trade volume must be affected and will not remain constant.
  • D (Incorrect): 題目給出的資訊足以判斷方向（替代品價格下降導致需求下降）。 The information provided is sufficient to determine the direction (drop in substitute price leads to drop in demand).

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

關鍵字 (Pivot Point): "競爭對手" (Competitors)
這是解題的核心。如果沒有這個詞，我們就不知道其他國家的貨幣變動跟X國有什麼關係。這個詞建立了「替代品」的關係。 Pivot Point: "Competitors"
This is the core of the solution. Without this word, we wouldn't know how other countries' currency changes relate to Country X. This word establishes the "substitute" relationship.
隱藏假設 (Implicit Assumption):
題目說「除了X國...其他貶值」，這隱含了 X國的貨幣相對於美元是保持穩定，或者至少貶值幅度小於其他國家。在經濟學MC題中，這通常意味著 X國的價格相對於競爭對手變高了。 Implicit Assumption:
The phrase "except that of Country X... depreciate" implies that Country X's currency remains stable against the USD, or at least depreciates less than others. In Economics MCQs, this usually means X's price has become higher relative to competitors.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 只關注 X 國 (Focusing only on Country X):
    學生常誤以為：「X國貨幣沒變，所以出口沒變。」
    錯誤原因： 忽略了「相對價格」。在國際市場上，買家會比較所有賣家的價格。別人的降價等於你的相對漲價。 Students often mistakenly think: "Country X's currency didn't change, so exports won't change."
    Reason for error: Ignoring "relative price." In international markets, buyers compare all sellers. Others lowering prices is equivalent to you relatively raising prices.
  • 混淆計價貨幣 (Confusing Denomination Currency):
    必須從「買家（美國）」的角度看價格。對美國人來說，其他國家的貨變便宜了（用美元買更划算）。 You must view the price from the "buyer's (US)" perspective. To Americans, other countries' goods have become cheaper (cost fewer USD).

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 題目沒有說 X 國的貨幣升值，為什麼當作它變貴了？
Q: The question didn't say Country X's currency appreciated. Why do we treat it as becoming more expensive?
A: 這是「相對」的概念。想像你和對手都賣 $10。對手降價到 $8，雖然你還是賣 $10（沒變），但在顧客眼中，你現在是「比較貴」的那個選擇。經濟學中，相對價格的改變會驅動決策。 A: It's about "relative" concepts. Imagine you and your rival both sell at $10. Your rival drops to $8. Even though you still sell at $10 (unchanged), in the customer's eyes, you are now the "more expensive" option. In economics, changes in relative prices drive decisions.

Q: 題目問的是「數量」(Volume) 還是「總值」(Value)？這有分別嗎？
Q: Does the question ask for "Volume" or "Value"? Is there a difference?
A: 題目問的是「數量」(Volume/Quantity)。如果是問「出口總值」(Total Export Value = \(P \times Q\))，我們就需要知道需求彈性 (Elasticity) 才能計算。但因為這裡只是問數量，根據需求定律，需求減少，數量必然下降，不需要考慮彈性。 A: The question asks for "Volume" (Quantity). If it asked for "Total Export Value" (\(P \times Q\)), we would need to know the price elasticity of demand. But since it only asks for volume, according to the Law of Demand, a decrease in demand inevitably leads to a decrease in quantity, regardless of elasticity.
