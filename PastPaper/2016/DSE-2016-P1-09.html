勞力流動性 (Labour Mobility)

Topic: Factors of Production (生產要素)

Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆
職業流動性 Occupational Mobility 專用性 Specificity 轉職成本 Cost of changing jobs

題目 The Question

就職業流動性而言，下列哪項的流動性最高？

Which of the following is the most mobile in terms of occupational mobility?

A. 一位職業籃球員 (A professional basketball player)
B. 一位持牌水喉匠 (A licensed plumber)
C. 一位律師行的大學畢業實習生 (A graduate trainee in a law firm)
D. 一位會計師行的文員 (A clerk in an accounting firm)

概念重溫 Concept Review

什麼是職業流動性？ (What is Occupational Mobility?)

職業流動性是指勞工**改變職業**（由一份工作轉到另一份工作）的能力和意願。

Occupational mobility refers to the ability and willingness of workers to change occupations (move from one job to another).

影響因素 (Factors Affecting Mobility)

  • 教育與訓練要求： 要求越高，流動性越低。
    (Education & Training requirements: Higher requirements imply lower mobility.)
  • 專業資格/牌照： 需要特定牌照的工作，轉行成本高。
    (Professional qualifications/licenses: Jobs requiring specific licenses have high switching costs.)
  • 技能的專用性： 技能越專門，越難應用於其他行業。
    (Specificity of skills: More specific skills are harder to apply in other industries.)

關鍵公式 (Key Logic)

\[ \text{技能專用性 (Specificity)} \uparrow \implies \text{流動性 (Mobility)} \downarrow \]
\[ \text{轉職成本 (Switching Cost)} \downarrow \implies \text{流動性 (Mobility)} \uparrow \]

答案與詳細分析 Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Option: D

選項 A：一位職業籃球員 (Option A: A professional basketball player)

錯誤。 職業籃球員擁有極高的**專用性技能**（如運球、投籃），這些技能在其他行業（如會計、工程）幾乎沒有價值。此外，他們投入了大量時間進行專項訓練，轉職的機會成本極高。

Incorrect. A professional basketball player possesses highly specific skills (e.g., dribbling, shooting) that have little value in other industries (e.g., accounting, engineering). Also, they have invested significant time in specialized training, making the opportunity cost of switching jobs very high.

選項 B：一位持牌水喉匠 (Option B: A licensed plumber)

錯誤。 水喉匠需要特定的**專業牌照**和技術知識。雖然這是一門手藝，但其技能高度集中於建築和維修領域，難以輕易轉型至完全不同的文職或商業工作。

Incorrect. A plumber requires specific professional licenses and technical knowledge. While it is a skilled trade, the skills are highly concentrated in construction and maintenance, making it difficult to easily switch to completely different clerical or commercial jobs.

選項 C：一位律師行的大學畢業實習生 (Option C: A graduate trainee in a law firm)

錯誤。 雖然實習生資歷較淺，但要進入律師行實習，通常需要完成法律學位（LLB/JD）及相關專業考試（PCLL）。這些**前期投入（Sunk Cost）**和專業知識的積累，限制了他們轉行到非法律領域的意願和能力。

Incorrect. Although a trainee is junior, entering a law firm usually requires completing a law degree and relevant professional exams. These sunk costs and the accumulation of specialized knowledge limit their willingness and ability to switch to non-legal fields.

選項 D：一位會計師行的文員 (Option D: A clerk in an accounting firm)

正確。 文員的工作性質通常涉及**通用技能 (General Skills)**，例如資料輸入、文件歸檔、基本的電腦操作和溝通技巧。這些技能在幾乎所有行業（銀行、學校、零售、政府）都通用。因此，文員轉職到其他行業的障礙最小，職業流動性最高。

Correct. The nature of a clerk's work usually involves general skills, such as data entry, filing, basic computer operations, and communication skills. These skills are transferable to almost all industries (banks, schools, retail, government). Therefore, a clerk faces the lowest barriers to switching industries, resulting in the highest occupational mobility.

題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

解題關鍵 Pivot Point

尋找**「通用技能 (General Skills)」** vs **「專用技能 (Specific Skills)」**。

Look for "General Skills" vs "Specific Skills".

思考流程 (Thinking Process):

  1. 分析技能性質： 該工作的技能是否只能在特定行業使用？
    Analyze skill nature: Can the skills only be used in a specific industry?
  2. 比較轉職障礙： 轉行是否需要重新考牌或讀書？
    Compare barriers: Does switching require new licenses or degrees?
  3. 結論： 技能越通用，轉職障礙越低 \( \rightarrow \) 流動性最高。
    Conclusion: More general skills = lower barriers = highest mobility.

考試技巧與陷阱 Exam Tricks & Traps

常犯錯誤 Common Mistakes

  • 混淆「收入」與「流動性」： 學生常誤以為高薪工作（如律師、球星）流動性高。其實高薪往往伴隨著高專用性，反而難轉行。
    Confusing "Income" with "Mobility": High income often implies high specificity, making it harder to switch.
  • 混淆「地域流動性」與「職業流動性」： 題目問的是換職業（Occupational），不是搬去另一個國家工作（Geographical）。
    Confusing "Geographical" with "Occupational" mobility.

速解技巧 Quick Trick

看到這類題目，直接找「低技術」或「文職/行政」類選項。

這類工作的技能（打字、聽電話）在任何公司都需要，所以最容易轉行。

Look for "Low-skilled" or "Clerical/Admin" options. Their skills are needed everywhere.
