Money & Banking (貨幣與銀行)

Barter System & Functions of Money
實物交易與貨幣功能

難度 Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆

Keywords: Barter (以物易物), Double Coincidence of Wants (需求的雙重巧合), Medium of Exchange (交易媒介)

The Question (題目)

以下哪一項關於「實物交易」的描述是錯誤的？

Which of the following descriptions about "barter" is FALSE?

A. 實物交易指直接以物易物。
Barter means a direct exchange of goods and services.
B. 只有當雙方的需求有雙重巧合時才能出現實物交易。
Barter is possible only when there is double coincidence of wants.
C. 實物交易只存在於沒有交易媒介的社會。
Barter exists only in societies without a medium of exchange.
D. 以實物交易方式進行國際貿易通常較進行本土貿易為困難。
Usually it is more difficult to practise barter in international trade than in domestic trade.
Concept Review (概念重溫)

實物交易 (Barter):
指不使用貨幣，直接以商品或服務交換其他商品或服務的交易制度。
A system of exchange where goods or services are directly exchanged for other goods or services without using money.

需求的雙重巧合 (Double Coincidence of Wants):
交易雙方必須剛好都擁有對方想要的物品，交易才能發生。這是實物交易的主要困難。
Each party must possess the specific good the other party desires for a transaction to occur. This is the main drawback of barter.

Formula Logic:
If Person A has Good \(X\) and wants Good \(Y\),
AND Person B has Good \(Y\) and wants Good \(X\),
\(\rightarrow\) Trade happens.
Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)
Correct Option (正確選項): C
Option A True Statement

分析：這是實物交易的定義。它不涉及任何貨幣媒介。

Analysis: This is the definition of barter. It involves no monetary medium.

Option B True Statement

分析：這是實物交易發生的必要條件。如果沒有「雙重巧合」（我不想要你的東西，或者你不需要我的東西），交易就不會發生。

Analysis: This is a necessary condition for barter. Without "double coincidence" (if I don't want your item, or you don't need mine), the trade fails.

Option C FALSE Statement (The Answer)

分析：這是錯誤的描述。即使在現代有貨幣（港元、美元）的社會，實物交易仍然存在。例如：小朋友交換貼紙、鄰居交換食物、或者國家之間以石油換取基建服務。

Analysis: This statement is incorrect. Barter still exists in modern societies that use money (HKD, USD). Examples include kids trading stickers, neighbors swapping food, or nations trading oil for infrastructure projects.

Option D True Statement

分析：國際貿易涉及更長的距離、運輸成本及資訊成本。要在國際層面尋找「需求的雙重巧合」比在本地（如鄰居之間）更困難。

Analysis: International trade involves greater distances, transport costs, and information costs. Finding a "double coincidence of wants" is usually harder internationally than locally (e.g., between neighbors).

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目要求找出錯誤的選項。解題關鍵在於識別「絕對性」的字眼。

This type of question asks for the FALSE statement. The key is to identify "absolute" qualifiers.

The Pivot Point (關鍵點): Option C's "Only" (只)

選項 C 使用了「只存在於」(exists only) 這樣絕對的字眼。在經濟學中，如果某個制度（如貨幣）出現，並不代表舊的制度（如實物交易）會完全消失。兩者是可以並存的。

Option C uses the absolute phrase "exists only". In Economics, the introduction of a new system (like money) does not mean the old system (barter) disappears completely. They can coexist.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)
Q: 為什麼選項 D 是正確的描述？現在網上交易不是很方便嗎？
Q: Why is Option D true? Isn't online trading very convenient now?
雖然互聯網降低了資訊成本，但選項 D 比較的是「實物交易」。在國際間進行「以物易物」仍然非常困難，因為你需要找到一個外國賣家，他剛好想要你手上的貨物，而且你們還要處理跨國運輸和法律問題。相比之下，本地鄰居交換物品容易得多。
While the internet lowers information costs, Option D compares "barter". Practicing "barter" internationally is still very hard because you need to find a foreign seller who wants exactly what you have, plus deal with cross-border shipping and laws. Swapping items with a local neighbor is much easier.
Q: 現代社會真的還有實物交易嗎？
Q: Does barter really still exist in modern society?
有的。除了小朋友交換玩具外，在惡性通脹（Hyperinflation）的國家（如津巴布韋或委內瑞拉），當貨幣失去價值時，人們會回歸實物交易（例如用雞蛋換麵包）。
Yes. Besides kids trading toys, in countries with hyperinflation (like Zimbabwe or Venezuela), when money loses its value, people revert to barter (e.g., trading eggs for bread).
Exam Tricks (考試技巧)
  • Watch out for "Only" / "Must": 看到「只有」、「一定」等字眼要特別警惕。經濟學中很少有絕對的情況。 (Be alert when you see "Only" or "Must". Absolutes are rare in Economics.)
  • Definition Check: 如果題目問「錯誤」的選項，先快速掃描定義性的選項（如 A 和 B）。如果定義正確，就可以排除它們，專注分析情境題（如 C 和 D）。 (If asked for the FALSE option, quickly scan definition options like A & B. If they are correct definitions, eliminate them and focus on situational options like C & D.)
