Topic 貨幣與銀行 Money & Banking
Difficulty ★★★☆☆
Concepts Nominal Return, Opportunity Cost

1. 題目 (The Question)

若一年期債券的名義利率為10%，而預期通脹率為2%。持有現金的名義回報率和預期成本分別為__________及__________。

A. 0% …. 8%
B. 0% …. 10%
C. –2% …. 8%
D. –10% …. 10%
If the nominal interest rate on a one-year bond is 10% and the expected inflation rate is 2%, the nominal rate of return and the expected cost of holding cash are __________ and __________ respectively.

A. 0% … 8%
B. 0% … 10%
C. −2% … 8%
D. −10% … 10%

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

1. 現金的名義回報率 (Nominal Rate of Return on Cash):
現金不會產生任何利息。因此，無論經濟環境如何，持有現金的名義回報率永遠是 0%。
1. Nominal Rate of Return on Cash:
Cash does not generate any interest. Therefore, regardless of the economic environment, the nominal rate of return on cash is always 0%.

2. 持有現金的成本 (Cost of Holding Cash):
這是一個「機會成本」(Opportunity Cost) 的概念。如果你選擇持有現金，你就放棄了將這筆錢用來購買生息資產（例如債券）所能賺取的名義利息。因此，持有貨幣的成本就是名義利率 ($i$)。
2. Cost of Holding Cash:
This is an "Opportunity Cost" concept. By choosing to hold cash, you forgo the nominal interest you could have earned by investing in an interest-bearing asset (like a bond). Thus, the cost of holding money is exactly the nominal interest rate ($i$).
$$\text{Cost of Holding Money} = \text{Nominal Interest Rate} \ (i)$$
3. 實質回報率 (Real Rate of Return) [補充概念]:
如果題目問的是現金的「實質」回報率，我們就需要扣除通脹的影響。
3. Real Rate of Return [Supplementary]:
If the question asked for the "real" rate of return on cash, we would need to deduct the effect of inflation.
$$\text{Real Rate of Return} = \text{Nominal Rate} - \text{Expected Inflation Rate} \ (\pi^e)$$

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): Option B

選項 A 錯誤： 0% 是正確的現金名義回報率。然而，8% 是債券的「實質利率」(10% - 2%)，而不是持有現金的成本。持有現金的成本應為放棄的全部名義利息。
Option A is Incorrect: 0% is the correct nominal return on cash. However, 8% is the "real interest rate" of the bond (10% - 2%), not the cost of holding cash. The cost of holding cash is the total nominal interest forgone.
選項 B 正確： 現金不會派息，所以名義回報率是 0%。持有現金的預期成本等於你放棄的債券名義利率，即 10%。
Option B is Correct: Cash pays no interest, so its nominal rate of return is 0%. The expected cost of holding cash is equal to the nominal interest rate of the bond you gave up, which is 10%.
選項 C 錯誤： -2% 是持有現金的「實質回報率」(0% - 2% = -2%)，但題目問的是「名義」回報率。第二部分的 8% 也是錯的（見選項 A 解釋）。
Option C is Incorrect: -2% is the "real rate of return" on cash (0% - 2% = -2%), but the question specifically asks for the "nominal" rate. The 8% in the second part is also wrong (see Option A).
選項 D 錯誤： -10% 在這裡沒有任何經濟意義。雖然第二部分的 10% 是正確的成本，但第一部分完全錯誤。
Option D is Incorrect: -10% has no economic meaning in this context. While the second part (10%) is the correct cost, the first part is completely wrong.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

  • 關鍵突破點 (Pivot Point):
    留意「名義回報率」(Nominal rate of return) 這個字眼。只要看到問現金的名義回報，答案必然是 0%。這可以讓你瞬間淘汰選項 C 和 D。
    Pivot Point:
    Notice the keyword "Nominal rate of return". Whenever you are asked for the nominal return on cash, the answer is always 0%. This instantly eliminates options C and D.
  • 干擾資訊 (Distractors):
    題目中的「預期通脹率為 2%」是一個經典的干擾項！它誘使你去做減法 (10% - 2% = 8%)，從而誤選 A。請記住，通脹同時削弱了現金和債券的購買力，兩者之間的「差距」（即機會成本）仍然是名義利率。
    Distractors:
    The "expected inflation rate of 2%" is a classic distractor! It tempts you to subtract (10% - 2% = 8%) and wrongly choose A. Remember, inflation erodes the purchasing power of BOTH cash and bonds equally. The "gap" between them (the opportunity cost) remains the nominal interest rate.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • 混淆「名義」與「實質」： 學生經常將「名義利率」(Nominal) 和「實質利率」(Real) 混為一談。名義利率是你在銀行看到的牌價數字；實質利率才是扣除通脹後的實際購買力增長。
    Confusing "Nominal" and "Real": Students often mix up Nominal and Real rates. Nominal rate is the stated number you see at a bank; Real rate is the actual growth in purchasing power after deducting inflation.
  • 錯誤應用 Fisher Equation： 雖然 Fisher Equation ($r = i - \pi^e$) 在經濟學中很重要，但在計算「持有現金的成本」時，**不需要**扣除通脹率。
    Misapplying the Fisher Equation: While the Fisher Equation ($r = i - \pi^e$) is important, you do **not** need to deduct inflation when calculating the "cost of holding cash".

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 既然通脹率是 2%，為什麼持有現金的成本不是 12% 或者 8%？
A: 想像一下你有兩種選擇：
1. 持有債券：一年後你獲得 10% 的名義利息。
2. 持有現金：一年後你獲得 0% 的名義利息。
無論通脹是多少，你選擇持有現金所「錯失」的那筆利息，實實在在就是那 10%。通脹率只會影響這 10% 利息能買到多少東西（實質購買力），但不會改變你放棄了 10% 名義利息這個事實。
Q: Since inflation is 2%, why isn't the cost of holding cash 12% or 8%?
A: Imagine you have two choices:
1. Hold a bond: You receive 10% nominal interest after one year.
2. Hold cash: You receive 0% nominal interest after one year.
Regardless of inflation, the exact amount of interest you "missed out on" by holding cash is strictly 10%. Inflation only affects how many goods that 10% can buy (real purchasing power), but it doesn't change the fact that you sacrificed a 10% nominal gain.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

「三秒殺題法」:
1. 看到「現金的**名義**回報率」➔ 立即寫下 0%。
2. 看到「持有現金的成本」➔ 立即找題目裡的「名義利率」(Nominal Interest Rate)。
不要理會通脹率！這樣你可以避開所有計算陷阱，5秒內選出答案。
"3-Second Kill Strategy":
1. See "**Nominal** return on cash" ➔ Write down 0% immediately.
2. See "Cost of holding cash" ➔ Look strictly for the "Nominal Interest Rate" in the question.
Ignore the inflation rate! This allows you to bypass all calculation traps and pick the answer in under 5 seconds.
