HKDSE Economics Question Analysis 📝

📘 Topic: 廠商與生產 - 長期成本與規模經濟 (Firms & Production - Long Run Costs) ⭐ Difficulty: 3 / 5 🔑 Key Concepts: Average Cost, Economies of Scale, Long Run

題目 The Question

(中) 某廠商增加全部投入的數量以令產出量增加一倍。下表顯示產出改變後總成本的變化。

(EN) A firm increases all its inputs to double its output. The following table shows the change in total cost after a change in output.

總產出 Total Output (Units) 總成本 Total Cost ($)
10 000 60 000
20 000 140 000

(中) 根據以上資料，我們可得結論如下：

(EN) Based on the above information, we can conclude that

  • A. 該廠商享有規模經濟。 (the firm enjoys economies of scale.)
  • B. 該廠商受損於規模不經濟。 (the firm suffers from diseconomies of scale.)
  • C. 在產出量增加一倍後，該廠商無法歸本。 (the firm cannot cover its cost after doubling the output.)
  • D. 最適規模的產出是10 000單位。 (the optimal scale of production is 10 000 units of output.)

概念重溫 Concept Review

1. 長期與規模經濟 (Long Run & Economies of Scale)

當廠商「增加全部投入」(increases all its inputs) 時，這代表所有生產要素都是可變的，這屬於長期 (Long Run) 的情況。在長期中，我們主要關注平均成本 (Average Cost) 的變化來判斷規模經濟。

When a firm "increases all its inputs", it means all factors of production are variable, which implies a Long Run situation. In the long run, we focus on the change in Average Cost (AC) to determine economies of scale.

  • 規模經濟 (Economies of Scale): 當產量增加，長期平均成本 (LRAC) 下降。
    As output increases, Long Run Average Cost (LRAC) decreases.
  • 規模不經濟 (Diseconomies of Scale): 當產量增加，長期平均成本 (LRAC) 上升。
    As output increases, Long Run Average Cost (LRAC) increases.

2. 平均成本公式 (Average Cost Formula)

$$AC = \frac{TC}{Q}$$

平均成本 (AC) 等於 總成本 (TC) 除以 產出數量 (Q)。

Average Cost (AC) equals Total Cost (TC) divided by Quantity of Output (Q).

答案與詳細分析 The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Option: B

第一步 Step 1：計算原來的平均成本 (Calculate Initial AC)
產量 = 10 000, 總成本 = $60 000
AC = $60 000 / 10 000 = $6

第二步 Step 2：計算擴充後的平均成本 (Calculate New AC)
產量 = 20 000, 總成本 = $140 000
AC = $140 000 / 20 000 = $7

結論 Conclusion： 平均成本由 $6 上升至 $7。(Average cost increased from $6 to $7).

Option A: 錯誤 Incorrect

廠商的平均成本上升了（由$6升至$7），因此這並不是規模經濟（規模經濟要求平均成本下降）。

The firm's average cost increased (from $6 to $7), so this is not economies of scale (which requires average cost to decrease).

Option B: 正確 Correct

隨着產出規模擴大，廠商的平均成本上升了。這是典型的規模不經濟的定義。

As the scale of production expanded, the firm's average cost increased. This is the exact definition of diseconomies of scale.

Option C: 錯誤 Incorrect

歸本（彌補成本）與否取決於總收入 (Total Revenue) 或產品價格 (Price)。由於題目沒有提供產品價格的資料，我們無法計算利潤或虧損，因此不能得出此結論。

Whether the firm can cover its cost depends on Total Revenue or the Price of the good. Since the question provides no information about price, we cannot calculate profit or loss, so we cannot make this conclusion.

Option D: 錯誤 Incorrect

最適規模產出是指長期平均成本 (LRAC) 達到最低點的產出量（最低效率規模 Minimum Efficient Scale）。雖然 10 000 單位的平均成本 ($6) 低於 20 000 單位的平均成本 ($7)，但我們只有兩個數據點，無法證明 $6 就是整條 LRAC 曲線的絕對最低點（可能 5 000 單位的平均成本只需 $5）。

The optimal scale of production refers to the output level where LRAC is at its absolute minimum (Minimum Efficient Scale). Although the AC at 10 000 units ($6) is lower than at 20 000 units ($7), we only have two data points. We cannot prove that $6 is the absolute lowest point on the entire LRAC curve (for instance, AC at 5 000 units could be $5).

題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

  • 解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point): 這類題目的核心在於識破「總成本 (Total Cost)」的掩眼法。總成本一定會隨產量增加而上升，這不能說明任何規模經濟的問題。真正的關鍵是必須自行計算出平均成本 (Average Cost)。
    The core of this question is seeing through the illusion of "Total Cost". Total Cost will always increase as output increases, which tells us nothing about economies of scale. The true pivot is that you must calculate the Average Cost yourself.
  • 干擾資訊 (Distractors): 選項 C 是一個典型的誘餌。學生看到總成本大幅飆升（從 6萬升到 14萬），可能會直覺以為廠商「蝕本 (cannot cover cost)」。但謹記：沒有價格 (Price) 或 總收入 (TR)，絕對無法判斷盈虧！
    Option C is a classic trap. Seeing the total cost surge (from 60k to 140k), students might intuitively guess the firm is making a loss. But remember: without Price or Total Revenue, you can NEVER determine profit or loss!

常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

  • ⚠️ 混淆總成本與平均成本 (Confusing TC and AC): 很多學生看到「規模經濟」，會誤以為是「成本減少」。請記住，擴充生產規模時，總支出 (TC) 必然增加，但我們關心的是「每生產一件產品的成本 (AC)」有沒有變平。
    Many students think "economies of scale" means "costs go down". Remember, when expanding production, total expenditure (TC) inevitably goes up. We only care if the "cost per unit (AC)" gets cheaper.

學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

Q: 題目中「增加全部投入的數量」(increases all its inputs) 這句話有什麼特別作用嗎？如果沒有這句話，答案會改變嗎？
A: 這句話非常關鍵！它定義了這個情境發生在「長期」(Long Run)。在短期 (Short run)，廠商會有固定投入 (Fixed inputs) 和固定成本 (Fixed costs)，這時成本隨產量變動的現象稱為「邊際回報遞減法則 (Law of Diminishing Marginal Returns)」。只有當「全部投入」都可以改變（長期）時，我們才使用「規模經濟 / 規模不經濟 (Economies / Diseconomies of Scale)」這些字眼。
A: This phrase is crucial! It defines the situation as the Long Run. In the short run, firms have fixed inputs and fixed costs, and changes in cost relative to output are governed by the "Law of Diminishing Marginal Returns". We only use the terms "Economies / Diseconomies of Scale" when ALL inputs can be varied (i.e., in the long run).

考試技巧與速解 Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

💡 心算秒殺法 (Mental Math Shortcut):

在 MC 卷中，時間就是分數。不需要寫出完整的算式。看到產量從 1 萬變 2 萬（兩倍），只要在腦海中快速劃掉後面 4 個零：
狀態 1： 6 ÷ 1 = 6
狀態 2： 14 ÷ 2 = 7
因為 7 大過 6，代表每件貨變貴了，立刻秒選 Diseconomies of Scale (規模不經濟)。全程只需 5 秒！

In Paper 1 (MC), time is marks. No need to write out full equations. Seeing output go from 10k to 20k, just cross out the four zeros mentally:
State 1: 6 ÷ 1 = 6
State 2: 14 ÷ 2 = 7
Since 7 is greater than 6, it means each unit got more expensive. Instantly choose Diseconomies of Scale. Takes only 5 seconds!
