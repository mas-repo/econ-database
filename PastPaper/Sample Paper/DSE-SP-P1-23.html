HKDSE Economics Tutorial

Topic: Macroeconomics - Unemployment (總體經濟學 - 失業)
Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆
Key Concepts: Unemployment Rate (失業率) Labor Force (勞動人口) Employed Population (就業人口)

1. 題目 (The Question)

假設某經濟的失業人口是300 000，而失業率是6%。該經濟的就業人口是______________。

Suppose the unemployed population of an economy is 300 000 while the unemployment rate is 6%. The employed population is __________.

  • A. 1 800 000
  • B. 2 100 000
  • C. 4 700 000
  • D. 5 000 000

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解決這條題目，我們必須清楚了解「勞動人口」的組成部分以及「失業率」的計算公式。

To solve this question, we must clearly understand the components of the "Labor Force" and the formula for the "Unemployment Rate".

  • 勞動人口 (Labor Force): 指所有就業人口與失業人口的總和。
    Labor Force: The total sum of the employed population and the unemployed population.
  • 失業率 (Unemployment Rate): 指失業人口佔總勞動人口的百分比。
    Unemployment Rate: The percentage of the unemployed population relative to the total labor force.
$$ \text{Labor Force} (LF) = \text{Employed} (E) + \text{Unemployed} (U) $$
$$ \text{Unemployment Rate} (UR) = \frac{\text{Unemployed} (U)}{\text{Labor Force} (LF)} \times 100\% $$

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案是 選項 C。

The correct answer is Option C.

選項 A (1 800 000) - 錯誤: 這是一個隨機的干擾項。學生可能是盲目地將失業人口乘以上失業率數字 \( (300,000 \times 6 = 1,800,000) \)，這在經濟學上沒有任何意義。

Option A (1 800 000) - Incorrect: This is a random distractor. A student might have blindly multiplied the unemployed population by the unemployment rate figure \( (300,000 \times 6 = 1,800,000) \), which has no economic meaning.

選項 B (2 100 000) - 錯誤: 這也是一個毫無根據的數字干擾項，旨在迷惑沒有掌握公式的學生。

Option B (2 100 000) - Incorrect: This is also a baseless numerical distractor meant to confuse students who have not mastered the formula.

選項 C (4 700 000) - 正確: 根據公式，\( 6\% = \frac{300,000}{LF} \)。首先求出勞動人口 \( LF = \frac{300,000}{0.06} = 5,000,000 \)。因為 \( LF = E + U \)，所以就業人口 \( E = LF - U = 5,000,000 - 300,000 = 4,700,000 \)。

Option C (4 700 000) - Correct: According to the formula, \( 6\% = \frac{300,000}{LF} \). First, find the Labor Force \( LF = \frac{300,000}{0.06} = 5,000,000 \). Since \( LF = E + U \), the employed population \( E = LF - U = 5,000,000 - 300,000 = 4,700,000 \).

選項 D (5 000 000) - 錯誤 (常見陷阱!): 這是該經濟的勞動人口 (Labor Force) 總數，而不是就業人口。學生在計算出這一步後經常忘記減去失業人口而直接選擇此答案。

Option D (5 000 000) - Incorrect (Common Trap!): This is the total Labor Force of the economy, not the employed population. Students often forget to subtract the unemployed population after calculating this step and mistakenly choose this answer.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

這類題目的核心在於「逆向推導」。題目給了你最終的百分比（失業率）和分子（失業人口），要求你找出另一個組成部分（就業人口）。

The core of this type of question lies in "reverse deduction". The question gives you the final percentage (unemployment rate) and the numerator (unemployed population), and asks you to find the other component (employed population).

  • 已知數據: 失業人口 \( U = 300,000 \)，失業率 \( UR = 6\% \)。
    Given Data: Unemployed population \( U = 300,000 \), Unemployment Rate \( UR = 6\% \).
  • 關鍵轉折點 (Pivot Point): 你不能直接從失業人口算出就業人口。你必須先利用這兩個已知數據，作為橋樑算出「勞動人口 (Labor Force)」，然後才能得出最終答案。
    Pivot Point: You cannot calculate the employed population directly from the unemployed population alone. You must first use these two given figures as a bridge to calculate the "Labor Force", and then you can derive the final answer.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

概念混淆：勞動人口 vs. 就業人口

Conceptual Confusion: Labor Force vs. Employed Population

最大的陷阱是算出 5,000,000（選項 D）後就停下來。請緊記：勞動人口包含兩部分。如果你找出了總數（勞動人口），必須減去失業的那一部分，剩下的才是真正在工作的人（就業人口）。

The biggest trap is stopping after calculating 5,000,000 (Option D). Remember: the labor force consists of two parts. If you find the total (labor force), you must subtract the unemployed portion to find those who are actually working (employed population).

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

問：為甚麼我們不能直接把 300,000 乘以 (1 - 6%) 來計算？
Q: Why can't we just multiply 300,000 by (1 - 6%) to calculate the answer?
答：因為失業率 (6%) 的分母是「勞動人口」，而不是「失業人口」。你不能用一個基於勞動人口計算出來的百分比，去直接乘或除「失業人口」。你必須先找出那個作為基數的「勞動人口」。
A: Because the denominator of the unemployment rate (6%) is the "Labor Force", not the "Unemployed Population". You cannot use a percentage that is based on the labor force and directly multiply or divide it by the unemployed population. You must find the base "Labor Force" first.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

速解法：比例法 (Ratio Method)

Shortcut: The Ratio Method

如果在選擇題想節省時間，你可以利用比例。既然失業率是 \( 6\% \)，代表就業人口佔勞動人口的 \( 100\% - 6\% = 94\% \)。

If you want to save time in multiple-choice questions, you can use ratios. Since the unemployment rate is \( 6\% \), it means the employed population makes up \( 100\% - 6\% = 94\% \) of the labor force.

失業與就業的比例是 \( 6 : 94 \)。

The ratio of Unemployed to Employed is \( 6 : 94 \).

$$ \text{Employed} = 300,000 \times \left( \frac{94}{6} \right) = 300,000 \times 15.66... = 4,700,000 $$

這個方法可以讓你跳過計算總勞動人口的步驟，一步得出答案，避免掉入選項 D 的陷阱！

This method allows you to skip the step of calculating the total labor force and get the answer in one step, avoiding the trap of Option D!
