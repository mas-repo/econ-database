📊 HKDSE Economics Topic Analysis

Topic (課題): 價格彈性與市場均衡 (Price Elasticity and Market Equilibrium)

Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts (關鍵概念): 供應價格彈性 (Elasticity of Supply) 需求變動 (Change in Demand) 弧彈性 (Arc Elasticity)

1. The Question (題目)

在冬季，婦女傾向購買更多補濕面霜。結果，某品牌的瓶裝保濕面霜的價格轉變了20 %，成交量則由每月2 000瓶升至2 800瓶。我們可得結論如下︰在上述的價格範圍內，這瓶裝保濕面霜的...
During winter, women tend to buy more hydrating facial cream. As a result, the price of bottled hydrating facial cream of a particular brand changed by 20% and its quantity transacted rose from 2 000 bottles to 2 800 bottles per month. We can conclude that within the above price range this bottled hydrating facial cream had...
  • A. 需求屬高彈性。 (an elastic demand.)
  • B. 需求屬低彈性。 (an inelastic demand.)
  • C. 供應屬高彈性。 (an elastic supply.)
  • D. 供應屬低彈性。 (an inelastic supply.)

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

需求的變動 (Change in Demand): 由於非價格因素（例如：季節性偏好改變），消費者的需求增加。這會導致需求曲線向右移動，沿著同一條向上傾斜的供應曲線移動。因此，新的均衡點反映了供應曲線上的價格與數量的關係。
Change in Demand: Due to non-price factors (e.g., seasonal changes in preference), consumer demand increases. This shifts the demand curve to the right, tracing along the same upward-sloping supply curve. Therefore, the new equilibrium points reflect the price-quantity relationship on the supply curve.
供應的價格彈性 (Price Elasticity of Supply, \(E_s\)): 衡量供應量對價格變動的反應程度。當 \(E_s > 1\) 時，供應屬高彈性；當 \(E_s < 1\) 時，供應屬低彈性。
Price Elasticity of Supply (\(E_s\)): Measures the responsiveness of quantity supplied to a change in price. When \(E_s > 1\), supply is elastic; when \(E_s < 1\), supply is inelastic.
$$ E_s = \frac{\% \Delta Q_s}{\% \Delta P} $$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option C

Options A & B:
錯誤。題目指出婦女傾向購買更多面霜，這代表需求增加（需求曲線向右移）。我們無法透過需求曲線的移動來計算「需求彈性」。市場上的價格和數量變動，實際上是沿著固定的「供應曲線」移動的結果，因此這些數據只能用來計算供應彈性。
Incorrect. The question states women buy more cream, representing an increase in demand (demand curve shifts right). We cannot calculate "elasticity of demand" from a shifting demand curve. The changes in market price and quantity are movements along a stationary "supply curve". Thus, this data can only be used to calculate elasticity of supply.
Option C (Correct):
正確。因為需求增加，導致價格上升和交易量上升。由於這是在同一條供應曲線上移動，我們可以計算供應的價格彈性。無論使用哪種百分比計算方法（詳見下文），數量改變的百分比均大於價格改變的百分比 (20%)，即 \(E_s > 1\)。因此，供應屬高彈性。
Correct. Because demand increased, both price and quantity transacted increased. Since this is a movement along the same supply curve, we can calculate the price elasticity of supply. Regardless of the percentage calculation method used (see below), the percentage change in quantity is greater than the percentage change in price (20%), meaning \(E_s > 1\). Therefore, supply is elastic.
Option D:
錯誤。經過計算，供應量變動的百分比大於 20%，得出 \(E_s > 1\)，而非低於 1。
Incorrect. Calculation shows the percentage change in quantity supplied is greater than 20%, yielding \(E_s > 1\), not less than 1.

4. Analysis: Ordinary Method vs Mid-point Method (普通方法與中點公式分析)

在 HKDSE 經濟科考試中，當計算一段價格範圍內的彈性（弧彈性）時，我們強烈建議使用中點公式 (Mid-point method)。這能避免因基準不同而產生不同的百分比數值。讓我們比較這兩種方法來計算數量的百分比變動 (\(\% \Delta Q\))：
In the HKDSE Economics exam, when calculating elasticity over a price range (arc elasticity), we strongly recommend using the Mid-point method. This avoids different percentage values caused by different bases. Let's compare both methods for calculating the percentage change in quantity (\(\% \Delta Q\)):
Ordinary Method (普通方法):
$$ \% \Delta Q = \frac{2800 - 2000}{2000} \times 100\% = 40\% $$ $$ E_s = \frac{40\%}{20\%} = 2.0 $$
Mid-point / Arc Method (中點 / 弧彈性公式):
$$ \% \Delta Q = \frac{2800 - 2000}{(2800 + 2000) / 2} \times 100\% = \frac{800}{2400} \times 100\% \approx 33.33\% $$ $$ E_s = \frac{33.33\%}{20\%} \approx 1.67 $$
結論： 雖然兩種方法得出的 \(\% \Delta Q\) 不同（40% vs 33.33%），但兩者皆大於題目的 \(\% \Delta P\) (20%)。因此，無論使用哪種方法，\(E_s\) 都大於 1。然而，在 DSE 卷二 (Paper 2) 中，如需列出算式，必須使用弧彈性公式以獲得滿分。
Conclusion: Although the two methods yield different \(\% \Delta Q\) (40% vs 33.33%), both are greater than the \(\% \Delta P\) (20%) given in the question. Therefore, \(E_s > 1\) in both cases. However, in DSE Paper 2, if you need to show working, you must use the arc elasticity formula to score full marks.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

  • Pivot Point (破題關鍵):
    「婦女傾向購買更多」這句話。這是破題的鑰匙。它告訴你「需求增加」了。當需求曲線移動，而供應曲線保持不變時，新的均衡價格和數量會勾勒出供應曲線的形狀。因此，我們只能計算供應彈性。
    The phrase "tend to buy more". This is the key to cracking the question. It tells you "Demand increases". When the demand curve shifts and the supply curve remains constant, the new equilibrium price and quantity trace out the shape of the supply curve. Hence, we can only calculate the elasticity of supply.
  • Crucial Data (關鍵數據):
    \(\% \Delta P = 20\%\)，以及數量由 2000 升至 2800。
    \(\% \Delta P = 20\%\), and quantity rose from 2000 to 2800.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱一：混淆移動的曲線。 同學一看到「購買更多面霜」就直覺認為題目考「需求彈性」，然後盲目猜測 A 或 B。記住這個黃金法則：「需求移動測供應，供應移動測需求」。
Trap 1: Confusing the shifting curve. Students see "buy more cream" and intuitively assume the question tests "demand elasticity", blindly guessing A or B. Remember this golden rule: "A shift in Demand measures Supply; a shift in Supply measures Demand."

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 題目說「價格轉變了20%」，我需要知道價格是上升還是下降嗎？
Q: The question says "price changed by 20%", do I need to know if it increased or decreased?

A: 理論上，需求增加會導致價格上升，所以我們知道是 +20%。但對於計算彈性而言，彈性係數取決於變動的百分比絕對值。只要 \(\% \Delta Q\) 的絕對值大於 \(\% \Delta P\) 的絕對值，就是高彈性。
A: Theoretically, an increase in demand leads to an increase in price, so we know it is +20%. But for calculating elasticity, the coefficient depends on the absolute values of the percentage changes. As long as the absolute value of \(\% \Delta Q\) is greater than \(\% \Delta P\), it is elastic.

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧：
1. 看到「需求增加」（買更多），立刻劃走所有關於「需求彈性」的選項 (刪去 A、B)。
2. 心算百分比：數量由 2000 變 2800，增加了 800。800 相對 2000 是一個很大的比例 (40%)，即使除以中點 (2400) 也有 33%。
3. 比較：33% (或 40%) > 20%。\(\% \Delta Q > \% \Delta P\)。
4. 結論：高彈性 (Elastic)。直接選 C！
Speedkill Technique:
1. See "Demand increases" (buy more), immediately cross out all options regarding "Demand elasticity" (Eliminate A, B).
2. Mental math: Quantity goes from 2000 to 2800, an increase of 800. 800 compared to 2000 is a huge proportion (40%), and even divided by the mid-point (2400) it's 33%.
3. Compare: 33% (or 40%) > 20%. \(\% \Delta Q > \% \Delta P\).
4. Conclusion: Elastic. Choose C instantly!
