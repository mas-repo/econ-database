📊 Topic (課題): Macroeconomics - National Income Accounting (總體經濟學 - 國民收入會計)

⭐ Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆

🔑 Key Concepts (核心概念): Nominal GDP (名義本地生產總值), Real GDP (實質本地生產總值), GDP Deflator (本地生產總值平減物價指數), Per Capita (人均)

The Question (題目)

考慮以下某經濟的資料：
Consider the following information about an economy:
增長率（相對去年）（%）
Growth rate (relative to previous year) (%)		 第一年
Year 1		 第二年
Year 2
-3 0
名義本地生產總值 (Nominal GDP) -3 0
實質本地生產總值 (Real GDP) 2 1
人口 (Population) 2 -1
下列哪項有關該經濟的描述是正確的？
Which of the following about the economy is true?
  • A. 實質產出於第二年減少。
    The real output decreased in year 2.
  • B. 人均名義本地生產總值於第二年減少。
    The per capita nominal GDP decreased in year 2.
  • C. 以上兩年的名義本地生產總值是相同的。
    The nominal GDP in the above two years is the same.
  • D. 一般物價水平於第一年下降，但於第二年上升。
    The general price level decreased in year 1 but increased in year 2.

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這類題目，我們必須熟記三個核心的「增長率（百分比改變）近似值公式」。
To solve this type of question, we must memorize three core "approximation formulas for growth rates (percentage changes)".
1. 一般物價水平 (General Price Level / GDP Deflator) $$ \% \Delta \text{Price Level} \approx \% \Delta \text{Nominal GDP} - \% \Delta \text{Real GDP} $$
2. 人均數值 (Per Capita Variables) $$ \% \Delta \text{Per Capita Variable} \approx \% \Delta \text{Total Variable} - \% \Delta \text{Population} $$
定義 (Definitions):
  • 實質產出 (Real Output): 在這裏即等同於實質本地生產總值 (Real GDP)。
    Here, it is equivalent to Real GDP.
  • 增長率為 0 (Growth Rate = 0%): 代表數值與上一期比較沒有改變。
    Means the value is unchanged compared to the previous period.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

✅ Correct Option (正確答案): C

Option A Analysis (錯誤):

第二年的實質本地生產總值增長率為 \(1\%\)。因為 \(1\% > 0\)，這代表實質產出（Real GDP）仍然在增加，只是增長速度比第一年（\(2\%\)）慢。因此，實質產出並沒有減少。

The growth rate of Real GDP in Year 2 is \(1\%\). Since \(1\% > 0\), this means real output is still increasing, just at a slower pace than in Year 1 (\(2\%\)). Therefore, real output did not decrease.

Option B Analysis (錯誤):

利用人均公式計算第二年的變化：
\( \% \Delta \text{人均名義 GDP} \approx \% \Delta \text{名義 GDP} - \% \Delta \text{人口} \)
第二年：\( 0\% - (-1\%) = +1\% \)
因為得出的結果是正數（\(+1\%\)），所以人均名義本地生產總值實際上是上升了。

Using the per capita formula for Year 2:
\( \% \Delta \text{Per Capita Nominal GDP} \approx \% \Delta \text{Nominal GDP} - \% \Delta \text{Population} \)
Year 2: \( 0\% - (-1\%) = +1\% \)
Since the result is positive (\(+1\%\)), the per capita nominal GDP actually increased.

Option C Analysis (正確):

第二年的名義本地生產總值增長率為 \(0\%\)。當一個變量的增長率是 \(0\%\) 時，代表它的絕對數值（absolute value）與上一年相比維持不變。因此，第一年和第二年的名義本地生產總值是相同的。

The growth rate of Nominal GDP in Year 2 is \(0\%\). When a variable's growth rate is \(0\%\), it means its absolute value remains unchanged compared to the previous year. Thus, the Nominal GDP in Year 1 and Year 2 are exactly the same.

Option D Analysis (錯誤):

利用物價水平公式：
\( \% \Delta \text{物價水平} \approx \% \Delta \text{名義 GDP} - \% \Delta \text{實質 GDP} \)
第一年：\( -3\% - 2\% = -5\% \) (下降)
第二年：\( 0\% - 1\% = -1\% \) (下降)
一般物價水平在兩年內都在下降，而不是第二年上升。

Using the price level formula:
\( \% \Delta \text{Price Level} \approx \% \Delta \text{Nominal GDP} - \% \Delta \text{Real GDP} \)
Year 1: \( -3\% - 2\% = -5\% \) (Decreased)
Year 2: \( 0\% - 1\% = -1\% \) (Decreased)
The general price level decreased in both years, not just Year 1.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的「關鍵點」（Pivot Point）在於理解「增長率（Growth Rate）」與「絕對數值（Absolute Level）」的分別。

The "Pivot Point" of this type of question lies in understanding the difference between the "Growth Rate" and the "Absolute Level".

  • 增長率 \(> 0\)：數值正在上升 (Value is increasing)
  • 增長率 \(< 0\)：數值正在下跌 (Value is decreasing)
  • 增長率 \(= 0\)：數值維持不變 (Value remains unchanged)

題目故意給你第一年的數據（如名義 GDP 的 \(-3\%\)），在判斷選項 C 時，這些資訊其實是干擾項（Distractors）。你只需要看第二年的增長率是 \(0\%\)，就能立即知道它與第一年的數值相同。

The question deliberately gives you data for Year 1 (like \(-3\%\) for Nominal GDP). When evaluating Option C, this information is actually a Distractor. You only need to look at the Year 2 growth rate of \(0\%\) to instantly know its value is identical to Year 1.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ Trap: Confusing a falling growth rate with a falling value (混淆「增長率下跌」與「數值下跌」)

很多學生看到實質 GDP 增長率由第一年的 \(2\%\) 跌至第二年的 \(1\%\)，就誤以為實質產出減少了（錯選 A）。請記住：由賺 \(2\) 蚊變成賺 \(1\) 蚊，你的財富還是增加了，只是增加得比較慢！只有當增長率變成負數時，數值才會減少。

Many students see the Real GDP growth rate drop from \(2\%\) in Year 1 to \(1\%\) in Year 2, and mistakenly think real output decreased (wrongly choosing A). Remember: going from earning $2 to earning $1 means your wealth is still increasing, just more slowly! The value only decreases when the growth rate becomes negative.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼我們可以直接用加減法（例如 \( \% \Delta \) 名義 GDP \( - \) \( \% \Delta \) 人口）來計算人均 GDP 增長率，而不是用除法？
Why can we simply use addition/subtraction (e.g., \( \% \Delta \) Nominal GDP \( - \) \( \% \Delta \) Population) to calculate per capita GDP growth, instead of division?

A: 因為當百分比變化很小（通常小於 \(10\%\)）時，數學上的對數近似值 (logarithmic approximation) 允許我們把乘法變成加法，把除法變成減法。在 DSE 考試中，這種近似計算法是完全接受並且是預期學生會使用的標準技巧。
Because when percentage changes are small (usually less than \(10\%\)), mathematical logarithmic approximations allow us to turn multiplication into addition and division into subtraction. In the DSE exam, this approximation method is fully accepted and is the standard expected technique.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧 (The Elimination Shortcut):

做這類選擇題時，先尋找表格中的 \(0\)。在經濟學表格題中，\(0\%\) 的增長率往往是破題的關鍵。一眼看到第二年名義 GDP 增長為 \(0\%\)，你腦海中應該馬上彈出「兩年數值一樣」這個概念。再看選項 C，完美吻合，直接圈選，節省計算其他選項的時間！

When doing this type of MCQ, scan the table for zeros first. In Economics table questions, a \(0\%\) growth rate is often the key to cracking the problem. The moment you see a \(0\%\) growth in Year 2 Nominal GDP, the concept "same value across both years" should pop into your mind. Look at Option C—it's a perfect match. Select it immediately and save time calculating the other options!
