Firm Behavior & Supply Curve

廠商行為與供應曲線

Difficulty 難度: ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts: Profit Maximization, Marginal Cost

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. Profit Maximization Condition (利潤極大化條件)

廠商為了賺取最大利潤，會生產至邊際收益等於邊際成本的產量水平。

To maximize profit, a firm produces at the output level where Marginal Revenue equals Marginal Cost.

$$ MR = MC $$

2. Price Taker (受價者)

在完全競爭市場(Perfect Competition)中，廠商是受價者，每多賣一單位的收入等於市價，即 \(MR = P\)。

In a perfectly competitive market, the firm is a price taker. The revenue from selling one more unit equals the market price, so \(MR = P\).

3. Derivation (推導)

結合上述兩點，廠商會根據市價 \(P\) 來決定產量 \(Q\)，使得：

Combining the above, the firm decides quantity \(Q\) based on market price \(P\) such that:

$$ P = MC $$

這意味著，給定一個價格 \(P\)，邊際成本曲線告訴了我們廠商願意提供的數量 \(Q_s\)。這正是供應表的定義。

This means that given a price \(P\), the MC curve tells us the quantity \(Q_s\) the firm is willing to supply. This is exactly the definition of a Supply Schedule.

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: B

✕

Option A: 它的產出增加時，它的邊際成本會增加。

Marginal cost will increase when the output of the firm increases.

分析： 這描述了邊際回報遞減定律 (Law of Diminishing Marginal Returns)，解釋了為什麼供應曲線是向上傾斜的，但這不是邊際成本曲線「等同於」供應曲線的原因。

Analysis: This describes the Law of Diminishing Marginal Returns, explaining why the supply curve is upward sloping. However, this explains the shape, not the identity of why the MC curve acts as the supply curve.

✓

Option B: 在已知該物品價格情況下，我們可從邊際成本表得知它計劃生產該物品的數量。

We can tell the quantity of the good the firm plans to produce from the marginal cost schedule given the price of the good.

分析： 這是正確答案。供應表的功能就是「給定價格，找出供應量」。由於廠商根據 \(P = MC\) 來決定產量，只要知道市價 \(P\)，我們就可以在 MC 曲線上找到對應的 \(Q\)。

Analysis: This is correct. The function of a supply schedule is "Given Price, find Quantity Supplied". Since the firm determines output where \(P = MC\), if we know the market price \(P\), we can look at the MC curve to find the corresponding \(Q\).

✕

Option C: 它計劃生產該物品的數量受到生產該物品的邊際成本所影響，兩者關係是正向的。

The quantity of the good the firm plans to produce depends positively on the marginal cost of producing the good.

分析： 邏輯混亂。產量是由價格決定的（在 MC 的約束下）。我們通常說「價格上升導致供應量上升」，而不是產量依賴於邊際成本的正向關係。

Analysis: The logic is confused. Quantity is determined by Price (constrained by MC). We usually say "an increase in price leads to an increase in quantity supplied," not that quantity depends positively on marginal cost itself.

✕

Option D: 在已知該物品數量的情況下，我們可從邊際成本表得知該物品的價格。

We can tell the price of the good from the marginal cost schedule given the quantity of the good.

分析： 雖然數學上 \(P=MC\) 兩邊相等，但經濟學定義上，供應是指「對價格的反應」(Response to Price)。選項 D 描述的是「供應價格」(Supply Price)，即生產特定數量所需的最低價格，這不是供應表的標準定義方向（由 \(P \rightarrow Q\)）。

Analysis: While mathematically \(P=MC\) works both ways, economically, Supply is defined as the "response to price". Option D describes "Supply Price" (the minimum price to induce a quantity), which is reading the curve inversely. The standard definition of a Supply Schedule flows from \(P \rightarrow Q\).

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

The Pivot Point (關鍵點)

這題的關鍵在於理解供應 (Supply) 的定義。

The key is understanding the definition of Supply.

  • 供應 = 在不同價格下，廠商願意出售的數量。
  • Supply = Quantity the firm is willing to sell at different prices.
  • 邏輯流向 Logic Flow: Price (\(P\)) \(\rightarrow\) Quantity (\(Q\))

Why MC is the link? (連接點)

廠商是理性的 (Rational)，只會在 \(P \ge MC\) 時生產。

Firms are rational and only produce when \(P \ge MC\).

因此，MC 曲線實際上就是一張「價格-數量對照表」。

Therefore, the MC curve is effectively a "Price-Quantity lookup table".

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

1. Confusing Slope with Definition (混淆斜率與定義)

很多學生選 Option A。 雖然 MC 上升解釋了為什麼供應曲線向上傾斜（Law of Supply），但它沒有解釋為什麼 MC 曲線本身就是供應曲線。

Many students choose Option A. While rising MC explains why the supply curve slopes upwards (Law of Supply), it doesn't explain why the MC curve is the supply curve itself.

2. Causal Direction (因果方向)

記住：在完全競爭市場模型中，價格是自變量 (Independent Variable)，數量是應變量 (Dependent Variable)。
題目問的是「供應表」(Supply Schedule)，所以必須是 \(P \rightarrow Q\) 的關係。

Remember: In the perfect competition model, Price is the Independent Variable, and Quantity is the Dependent Variable. The question asks about the "Supply Schedule", so it must describe the \(P \rightarrow Q\) relationship.
