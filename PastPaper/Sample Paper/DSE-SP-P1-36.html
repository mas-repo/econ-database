📊 HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic (課題): Macroeconomics - Fiscal Policy & Output Gaps (宏觀經濟學：財政政策與產出差距)

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐☆☆☆ (2/5)

Key Concepts (核心概念): Aggregate Demand (總需求) Inflationary Gap (通貨膨脹差距) Contractionary Fiscal Policy (緊縮性財政政策)

1. The Question (題目)

下列哪項有助解決通貨膨脹差距？

A. 增加稅項
B. 增加財政赤字
C. 增加轉移支付
D. 增加政府支出

Which of the following can help to solve an inflationary gap?

A. an increase in taxes
B. an increase in budget deficit
C. an increase in transfer payments
D. an increase in government expenditure

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

💡 通貨膨脹差距 (Inflationary Gap)

當經濟體的實際產出 (Actual Output) 高於潛在產出 (Potential Output / Full-employment GDP) 時，就會出現通貨膨脹差距。這代表經濟體系內的總需求 ($AD$) 過高，導致物價持續面臨上升壓力。

Occurs when the economy's Actual Output exceeds its Potential Output (Full-employment GDP). This indicates that Aggregate Demand ($AD$) in the economy is too high, leading to continuous upward pressure on prices.

📉 緊縮性財政政策 (Contractionary Fiscal Policy)

為了解決通脹差距，政府必須「降溫」，採取減少總需求的措施。

To close an inflationary gap, the government must "cool down" the economy by taking measures to reduce Aggregate Demand.

總需求公式 (Aggregate Demand Equation): $$AD = C + I + G + (X - M)$$

政府主要透過改變稅收 ($T$)、政府支出 ($G$) 或轉移支付來影響私人消費 ($C$) 和直接影響 $G$。

The government primarily alters Taxes ($T$), Government Expenditure ($G$), or Transfer Payments to influence private consumption ($C$) or directly impact $G$.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option A

A. 增加稅項 (an increase in taxes) - CORRECT

增加稅收會減少市民的可支配收入 (Disposable Income)，從而減少私人消費開支 ($C$)。根據公式，消費下降會導致總需求 ($AD$) 減少，成功為經濟降溫並消除通貨膨脹差距。

Increasing taxes reduces citizens' disposable income, which in turn decreases private consumption expenditure ($C$). According to the equation, a fall in consumption reduces Aggregate Demand ($AD$), successfully cooling the economy and closing the inflationary gap.

B. 增加財政赤字 (an increase in budget deficit) - INCORRECT

財政赤字代表政府支出 ($G$) 大於稅收 ($T$)。增加赤字意味著政府正在實行擴張性政策（例如進一步增加 $G$ 或減少 $T$），這會增加總需求，使通脹問題惡化。

A budget deficit means government expenditure ($G$) exceeds tax revenue ($T$). Increasing the deficit implies the government is implementing expansionary policies (like increasing $G$ or cutting $T$), which would increase $AD$ and worsen inflation.

C. 增加轉移支付 (an increase in transfer payments) - INCORRECT

轉移支付（如失業救濟金、消費券）會增加市民的可支配收入，刺激消費 ($C$)，從而推高總需求 ($AD$)，令通脹更嚴重。

Transfer payments (e.g., unemployment benefits, consumption vouchers) raise disposable income, stimulating consumption ($C$). This pushes up Aggregate Demand ($AD$) and exacerbates inflation.

D. 增加政府支出 (an increase in government expenditure) - INCORRECT

政府支出 ($G$) 是總需求的直接組成部分。增加 $G$ 會直接增加 $AD$，令通貨膨脹差距進一步擴大。

Government expenditure ($G$) is a direct component of Aggregate Demand. Increasing $G$ directly increases $AD$, further widening the inflationary gap.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

  • Pivot Point (解題關鍵): 看到「解決通貨膨脹差距」 (solve an inflationary gap)，大腦必須立刻連繫到 「減少總需求 ($AD \downarrow$)」。
  • Distractors (干擾項分析): 題目測試你是否清楚各項財政工具的方向。選項 B、C、D 全部都是擴張性 (Expansionary) 政策，只有 A 是緊縮性 (Contractionary)。

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ 混淆差距類型 (Confusing Gap Types)

同學常因為粗心，將「通貨膨脹差距」誤看成「通貨緊縮差距 (Deflationary Gap)」。如果在通縮差距下，我們才需要擴張性政策（選項 B、C、D）。考試時請務必圈起 Inflationary (通脹) 一詞以提醒自己需要「降溫」。

Students often carelessly misread "Inflationary Gap" as "Deflationary Gap". Expansionary policies (Options B, C, D) are only needed during a deflationary gap. Always circle the word "Inflationary" during exams to remind yourself the economy needs "cooling down".

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

🙋‍♂️ 學生問： 「增加財政赤字」不是代表政府沒錢嗎？為什麼反而會推高通脹？

Q: Doesn't "budget deficit" mean the government has no money? Why would it push up inflation?

👨‍🏫 導師答： 在宏觀經濟中，赤字 (Deficit) 是指政府「花出去的錢 ($G$)」大於「收回來的錢 ($T$)」。這代表政府正在向市場淨注入資金！市場上的錢變多了，需求自然上升，從而推高物價（通脹）。

A: In macroeconomics, a deficit means the government is "spending more ($G$)" than it is "taxing ($T$)". This means the government is making a net injection of money into the market! More money in the market leads to higher demand, which drives up prices (inflation).

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

🔥 The "Odd One Out" Strategy (「排除法」速解):

在應付這類政策選擇題時，你可以先判斷每個選項的「政策方向」：

  • A. 增加稅項 $\rightarrow$ 收縮經濟
  • B. 增加財政赤字 $\rightarrow$ 擴張經濟
  • C. 增加轉移支付 $\rightarrow$ 擴張經濟
  • D. 增加政府支出 $\rightarrow$ 擴張經濟

在單項選擇題中，方向唯一不同的那個選項（Odd One Out），有 99% 的機率就是正確答案！

When dealing with policy MCQs, evaluate the "direction" of each option. Options B, C, and D are all expansionary. Option A is the only contractionary one. In a single-choice question, the option with the unique direction (the Odd One Out) is almost always the correct answer!
