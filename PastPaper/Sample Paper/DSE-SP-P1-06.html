HKDSE Economics Concept Guide

📍 Topic (課題): Factors of Production (生產要素)
⭐ Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆
🔑 Key Concepts (核心概念): Labour Productivity (勞工生產力) Human Capital (人力資本)

1. The Question (題目)

下列哪項會提高勞工的平均生產力？

Which of the following would raise the average labour productivity?

  1. 輸入外地專才的數目增加
    an increase in the number of imported foreign specialist
  2. 平均工時增加
    an increase in the average working hours
  3. 禁止使用對環境有害的機械
    a ban on the use of environmentally harmful machines
  4. 削減政府持續進修補貼
    a cut in government subsidies for continuing education

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

Average Labour Productivity (平均勞工生產力)

勞工生產力是指每個勞工（或每個工時）所能生產的產量。它反映了勞工的工作效率。要提高生產力，我們需要提升勞工的「質素」（如教育、技術）或增加他們可使用的「資本設備」（如機器）。

Labour productivity refers to the output produced per unit of labour (per worker or per man-hour). It reflects the efficiency of labour. To increase productivity, we need to improve the "quality" of labour (e.g., education, skills) or increase the "capital goods" available to them (e.g., machinery).

$$ \text{Average Labour Productivity (ALP)} = \frac{\text{Total Output (總產出)}}{\text{Total Labour Input (總勞力投入)}} $$

Human Capital (人力資本)

指勞工所擁有的知識、技能和經驗。人力資本增加 \(\uparrow\)，勞工生產力也會相應上升 \(\uparrow\)。

Refers to the knowledge, skills, and experience possessed by workers. An increase in human capital \(\uparrow\) leads to a corresponding rise in labour productivity \(\uparrow\).

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): Option A

選項 A (Option A) - 正確 (Correct)

輸入外地「專才」意味著引進了擁有高技能和豐富知識的勞工。這直接提升了整體勞動人口的人力資本（Human Capital），從而提高平均勞工生產力。

Importing foreign "specialists" means bringing in highly skilled and knowledgeable workers. This directly increases the human capital of the overall labour force, thereby raising the average labour productivity.

選項 B (Option B) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

增加平均工時只是增加了勞力投入的「數量」（總工時）。根據邊際回報遞減法則（Law of Diminishing Marginal Returns）或由於勞工疲勞，每增加一小時所帶來的額外產出可能會下降，因此平均每小時的生產力反而可能下降或不變。

Increasing average working hours only increases the "quantity" of labour input (total man-hours). Due to the law of diminishing marginal returns or worker fatigue, the extra output generated by each additional hour might fall. Thus, the average productivity per hour may actually decrease or remain unchanged.

選項 C (Option C) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

禁止使用某些機械會減少勞工可使用的資本物品（Capital Goods）。當資本與勞工比例（Capital-to-Labour Ratio）下降，勞工的生產效率會降低，導致生產力下降。

Banning the use of certain machines reduces the capital goods available to workers. When the capital-to-labour ratio falls, the efficiency of workers decreases, leading to a drop in productivity.

選項 D (Option D) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

削減持續進修補貼會降低勞工接受培訓和教育的意欲。這會阻礙人力資本的累積，長遠來說會拖低勞工生產力。

A cut in continuing education subsidies reduces workers' incentives to receive training and education. This hinders the accumulation of human capital and will lower labour productivity in the long run.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解答這類題目時，要緊記尋找「影響勞工質素」或「影響資本數量」的因素。

When answering this type of question, always look for factors that "affect labour quality" or "affect the amount of capital".

  • Pivot Point (破題關鍵): 題目問的是「平均生產力 (Average Productivity)」，即效率，而不是「總產量 (Total Output)」。
  • Bilingual: The question asks for "Average Productivity" (efficiency), NOT "Total Output".

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：混淆「總產出 (Total Output)」與「生產力 (Productivity)」

很多學生會誤選 Option B。他們認為工時增加，產量自然增加。這是對的，總產量 \( Q \) 的確可能上升。但是，生產力是 \( \frac{Q}{L} \) （產量除以勞工投入）。如果工時（分母）增加的幅度大於產量（分子）增加的幅度（因為疲勞），平均生產力其實是下降的！

Trap: Confusing Total Output with Productivity. Many students mistakenly choose Option B. They think longer hours naturally mean more output. This is true; total output \( Q \) might rise. However, productivity is \( \frac{Q}{L} \) (Output divided by Labour Input). If hours (the denominator) increase proportionately more than output (the numerator) due to fatigue, average productivity actually falls!

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼 Option A 特別強調「專才 (Specialists)」，如果是普通外地勞工呢？
Q: Why does Option A specifically emphasize "specialists"? What if they were just general foreign workers?

A: 這是個極好的問題！如果題目只寫「輸入外地勞工數目增加」，答案就不一定是 A 了。因為普通勞工只會增加勞動力的「數量」，除非他們的技術比本地勞工高，否則不會提高「平均」生產力。題目刻意加上「專才 (Specialist)」一詞，就是確保這些勞工帶有高水平的人力資本，保證能拉高整體的平均效率。
A: Excellent question! If the option merely said "an increase in foreign workers", it wouldn't necessarily be the correct answer. General workers only increase the "quantity" of labour. Unless they are more skilled than local workers, they won't raise the "average" productivity. The term "specialist" is deliberately used to ensure these workers possess high human capital, guaranteeing a rise in overall average efficiency.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

Elimination Method (淘汰法):

在 MC 選擇題中，你可以利用「正面/負面影響」快速篩選：

In MC questions, you can use "positive/negative impacts" to quickly filter options:

  • Option C (禁止機器 / Ban machines) ➡ 負面影響經濟效率 (Negative) ❌
  • Option D (削減補貼 / Cut subsidies) ➡ 負面影響教育 (Negative) ❌
  • Option B (增加工時 / Increase hours) ➡ 只是「量」的改變，員工會變累 (Just a change in quantity, workers get tired) ❌
  • Option A (輸入專才 / Import specialists) ➡ 「質」的提升 (Upgrade in quality/skills) ✅
