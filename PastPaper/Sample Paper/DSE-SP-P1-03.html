Economics Problem Analysis 經濟學題目分析

Topic / 課題: 基本經濟概念：實證與規範性陳述 (Basic Economic Concepts: Positive vs. Normative) Difficulty / 難度: ⭐⭐ (2/5)
Key Concepts / 關鍵字: 實證陳述 (Positive Statement), 規範性陳述 (Normative Statement), 可推翻性 (Refutability)

2. The Question (題目)

一項實證的陳述
A positive statement
  1. 永遠是對的。
    is always true.
  2. 可以被事實推翻。
    is refutable with facts.
  3. 能包含了價值判斷。
    may contain a value judgment.
  4. 對擁有某些相同價值取向的大眾而言，可能等同於一項規範性陳述。
    may be the same as a normative statement if certain values are shared by many people.

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

實證陳述 (Positive Statement)： 關於「是什麼 (What is)」的陳述。它陳述事實或現象之間的關係。最核心的特徵是它可以被事實驗證（Testable）。如果證據與陳述不符，它可以被證明為錯誤（Refutable）。
Positive Statement: A statement about "what is". It describes facts or relationships between phenomena. Its core characteristic is that it is testable. If evidence contradicts the statement, it can be proven false (refutable).
規範性陳述 (Normative Statement)： 關於「應該如何 (What ought to be)」的陳述。它涉及個人的價值判斷 (Value Judgment)，沒有絕對的對錯之分，因此無法用客觀事實來推翻或驗證。
Normative Statement: A statement about "what ought to be". It involves personal value judgments. There is no absolute right or wrong, and therefore it cannot be refuted or tested by objective facts.
$$ \text{Positive Statement} \implies \text{Testable by Facts} \implies \text{Refutable (Can be False)} $$ $$ \text{Normative Statement} \implies \text{Value Judgment} \implies \text{Not Testable} $$

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option B / 選項 B

選項 A (錯誤): 實證陳述不一定「永遠是對的」。若我們假設一個實證陳述 \(S\)，\(S\) 可以是完全錯誤的（例如：「太陽從西方升起」）。只要它能被事實證明為錯，它就是實證陳述。
Option A (Incorrect): A positive statement is not necessarily "always true". If we assume a positive statement \(S\), \(S\) can be completely wrong (e.g., "The sun rises in the west"). As long as it can be proven false by facts, it is a positive statement.
選項 B (正確): 這是實證陳述的標準定義核心。實證陳述提出了一種假說或對事實的描述，因此我們可以在現實中收集數據或事實來檢驗它。如果事實與陳述不符，該陳述就被推翻（refuted）。
Option B (Correct): This is the core standard definition of a positive statement. It presents a hypothesis or description of facts, so we can collect data in reality to test it. If facts contradict the statement, it is refuted.
選項 C (錯誤): 價值判斷是規範性陳述的專屬特徵。實證陳述關注客觀事實，明確排除主觀的價值判斷。
Option C (Incorrect): Value judgments are the exclusive feature of normative statements. Positive statements focus on objective facts and explicitly exclude subjective value judgments.
選項 D (錯誤): 實證陳述與規範性陳述在邏輯本質上是互斥的。無論有多少人分享同一種價值觀，主觀的規範（「應該」）永遠不會變成客觀的實證（「是」）。
Option D (Incorrect): Positive and normative statements are mutually exclusive in logical nature. No matter how many people share the same value, subjective norms ("ought to be") will never become objective empiricism ("is").

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

樞紐點 (Pivot Point)： 解答此題的關鍵在於打破「實證 = 正確」這個誤區。在經濟學方法論中，「實證」指的是「具備可測試性 (Testability)」。因此，尋找與「事實」、「驗證」或「推翻」相關的字眼是破題關鍵。
Pivot Point: The key to solving this is breaking the misconception that "positive = correct". In economic methodology, "positive" refers to "testability". Therefore, looking for keywords related to "facts", "testing", or "refuting" is the way to solve it.
干擾資訊 (Distractors)： 選項 D 試圖利用「群眾共識 (shared by many people)」來混淆視聽。學生必須堅定記住：科學分類是基於陳述的性質，而非認同該陳述的人數。
Distractors: Option D tries to confuse you using "consensus (shared by many people)". Students must firmly remember: scientific classification is based on the nature of the statement, not the number of people agreeing with it.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ 致命陷阱：將「實證」與「真理」畫上等號
許多學生看到選項 A 覺得很合理，因為他們認為經濟學課本上的實證理論都是「對的」。記住，即使一個陳述 \(P\) 是錯誤的（例如「香港的失業率是 100%」），只要我們能用數據證明它是錯的，它依然是一個實證陳述。
⚠️ Fatal Trap: Equating "Positive" with "Truth"
Many students find Option A reasonable because they think empirical theories in econ textbooks are "true". Remember, even if a statement \(P\) is false (e.g., "Hong Kong's unemployment rate is 100%"), as long as we can use data to prove it wrong, it remains a positive statement.

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果一個實證陳述已經被事實證明為「錯誤」，它還能被稱為實證陳述嗎？
Q: If a positive statement has already been proven "false" by facts, can it still be called a positive statement?
A: 絕對可以！ 它的標籤不會改變。我們稱之為「一個被推翻的實證陳述 (a refuted positive statement)」。它的本質依然是探討客觀現象，只是內容不符合現實罷了。
A: Absolutely! Its label doesn't change. We call it "a refuted positive statement". Its nature is still exploring objective phenomena; its content just doesn't align with reality.

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧 (Shortcut)：尋找關鍵字
在 DSE 考試中區分這兩者，直接掃視句子中的動詞和形容詞：
  • 見到實證 (Positive)： 尋找「是 (is/are)」、「將會 (will)」、「導致 (causes)」、「可推翻 (refutable)」。
  • 見到規範 (Normative)： 尋找「應該 (should/ought to)」、「太 (too... e.g., too high)」、「公平 (fair)」、「最好 (best)」。
Shortcut: Look for Keywords
To distinguish them in DSE exams, scan the verbs and adjectives directly:
  • For Positive: Look for "is/are", "will", "causes", "refutable".
  • For Normative: Look for "should/ought to", "too (e.g., too high)", "fair", "best".
