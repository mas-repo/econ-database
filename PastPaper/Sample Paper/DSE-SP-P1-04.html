2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

私人有限公司 (Private Limited Company)： 股東人數上限為 50 人。不能向公眾發行股票或債券，股份轉讓受到限制。所有股東享有有限債務責任。
Private Limited Company: The maximum number of shareholders is 50. It cannot issue shares or bonds to the general public, and the transfer of shares is restricted. All shareholders enjoy limited liability.
公眾有限公司 (Public Limited Company)： 股東人數沒有上限。可以透過證券交易所向公眾發行股票籌集資金，股份可自由轉讓。所有股東同樣享有有限債務責任。
Public Limited Company: There is no upper limit on the number of shareholders. It can raise capital by issuing shares to the general public through the stock exchange, and shares are freely transferable. All shareholders also enjoy limited liability.
有限債務責任 (Limited Liability)： 東主的損失只限於其投資在公司的金額，其個人財產不會被用來償還公司債務。
Limited Liability: The owners' maximum potential loss is limited to the amount they invested in the company; their personal assets cannot be seized to pay off the company's debts.

Supplementary formula: In the stock market, the market capitalization of a public limited company is calculated as follows:
$$ \text{Market Capitalization} = \text{Share Price} \times \text{Total Number of Outstanding Shares} $$

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): A
為什麼選 A？
Why is A correct?
  • 甲廠商分析： 甲廠商能夠「在股票市場籌集資金」。這是公眾有限公司的獨有特徵。雖然它只有 2 名東主（股東），但公眾有限公司的最低股東要求只需 1 名或以上即可，因此甲是公眾有限公司。
    Firm X Analysis: Firm X is "possible to raise capital in the stock market." This is a unique feature of a public limited company. Although it only has 2 owners (shareholders), a public limited company only requires a minimum of 1 or more shareholders, so X is a public limited company.
  • 乙廠商分析： 乙廠商享有「有限債務責任」，說明它是一間公司。它「不可能在股票市場籌集資金」，且東主數目剛好是 50 人（這正是私人有限公司的人數上限）。因此，乙是私人有限公司。
    Firm Y Analysis: Firm Y enjoys "limited liability," indicating it is an incorporated company. It is "not possible to raise capital in the stock market," and the number of owners is exactly 50 (which is the maximum limit for a private limited company). Therefore, Y is a private limited company.
為何其他選項錯誤？
Why are the other options wrong?
  • 選項 B 及 D (錯誤)： 乙廠商被標籤為「合夥」。普通的合夥企業，東主（合夥人）需要承擔無限債務責任，而且一般合夥人數上限為 20 人。乙廠商擁有 50 人且為有限責任，所以絕不是合夥。
    Options B and D (Incorrect): Firm Y is labeled as a "partnership." In a general partnership, owners (partners) have unlimited liability, and the maximum number of partners is generally 20. Firm Y has 50 owners and limited liability, so it is definitely not a partnership.
  • 選項 C (錯誤)： 這個選項將甲和乙的企業形態完全對調了。
    Option C (Incorrect): This option completely swaps the business forms of Firm X and Firm Y.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

面對這類題目，我們需要尋找「決定性線索」（Pivot Point）。
When facing this type of question, we need to look for the "Pivot Point".
  • 關鍵樞紐 1：「股票市場」(Stock Market)。這是區分私人和公眾有限公司的最強指標。一看到「可能」，秒殺選「公眾有限公司」。
    Pivot Point 1: "Stock Market". This is the strongest indicator to distinguish between private and public limited companies. As soon as you see "possible", instantly choose "Public Limited Company".
  • 關鍵樞紐 2：「有限債務責任」(Limited Liability)。這排除了「獨資」和一般的「合夥」企業。
    Pivot Point 2: "Limited Liability". This immediately rules out "Sole Proprietorship" and general "Partnership".
  • 陷阱資訊 (Distractor)：甲廠商的「2 名東主」。很多同學以為公眾有限公司一定有成千上萬的股東。實際上，法律只規定其沒有上限，下限只需 1 人。這個數字是故意放出來混淆視聽的！
    Distractor: Firm X's "2 owners". Many students think a public limited company must have thousands of shareholders. Legally, it only dictates there is no upper limit, with a minimum of just 1. This number is deliberately placed to confuse you!

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

⚠️ 迷思：私人有限公司必定是小規模，公眾有限公司必定是大規模？
錯誤！雖然常見情況如此，但企業型態的分類是基於法律結構，而非資產或員工人數。例如著名的宜家家居 (IKEA) 和瑪氏食品 (Mars) 都是非上市的私人公司，但規模龐大。
⚠️ Myth: Private companies are always small, and public companies are always large?
False! While this is commonly the case, the classification of business ownership is based on legal structure, not assets or number of employees. For example, famous companies like IKEA and Mars are unlisted private companies but are massive in scale.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q1: 如果乙廠商有 51 名東主會怎樣？ (What if Firm Y had 51 owners?)
A: 根據香港《公司條例》，私人有限公司的最高東主（股東）人數為 50 人（不包括僱員）。如果人數達到 51 人，它便違反了私人公司的定義，必須轉型為公眾有限公司。
A: According to the Hong Kong Companies Ordinance, the maximum number of owners (shareholders) for a private limited company is 50 (excluding employees). If it reaches 51, it violates the definition of a private company and must be converted into a public limited company.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

🚀 5秒速解法 (5-Second Shortcut):
做 MC 題時，養成垂直掃視（Vertical Scanning）的習慣。先看「股票市場」那一欄。甲是「可能」，因此甲必定是公眾有限公司。這一步直接把選項 B 和 C 刪除。接著看乙的「債務責任」，乙是「有限」，所以絕不可能是合夥（排除 D）。答案 A 直接浮現，根本不需要看東主數目！
🚀 5-Second Shortcut:
When doing MC questions, develop the habit of Vertical Scanning. Look at the "Stock Market" row first. Firm X is "possible", so X MUST be a public limited company. This instantly eliminates options B and C. Next, look at Firm Y's "Liability". It is "limited", so it can NEVER be a partnership (eliminating D). Answer A emerges directly; you don't even need to look at the number of owners!
