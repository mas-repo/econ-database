HKDSE Economics Q&A Analysis

Topic (課題): International Trade: Absolute & Comparative Advantage / 國際貿易：絕對優勢與比較優勢
Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts (關鍵概念): Absolute Advantage (絕對優勢), Comparative Advantage (比較優勢), Opportunity Cost (機會成本)

2. The Question (題目)

若甲國生產每一物品的生產力都比乙國高，

If Country X is more productive in producing every good than Country Y,

  1. 甲國在貿易中的賺獲會比乙國多。
    the gain from trade of Country X will be greater than that of Country Y.
  2. 兩國間仍可能有互惠互利的貿易。
    mutually beneficial trade between the two countries is still possible.
  3. 乙國會從甲國入口每一物品，而非由自己生產。
    Country Y will import every good from Country X instead of producing these goods by itself.
  4. 甲國生產每一物品的機會成本可能低於乙國。
    Country X may have a lower opportunity cost in producing every good than Country Y.

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這條題目，我們必須清楚區分「絕對優勢」和「比較優勢」。

To answer this question, we must clearly distinguish between "Absolute Advantage" and "Comparative Advantage".

  • Absolute Advantage (絕對優勢): 指一個國家能以相同的資源生產出比其他國家更多的物品，或以較少資源生產相同數量的物品（即生產力較高）。
    Refers to a country's ability to produce more of a good using the same resources, or the same amount using fewer resources (i.e., higher productivity).

  • Comparative Advantage (比較優勢): 指一個國家生產某物品的機會成本低於其他國家。根據比較優勢定律，只要兩國的機會成本不同，雙方各自專業化生產並進行貿易，便能達致互惠互利。
    Refers to a country's ability to produce a good at a lower opportunity cost than another. According to the Principle of Comparative Advantage, as long as opportunity costs differ, both countries can gain from specialization and trade.
$$ \text{Opportunity Cost of Good A} = \frac{\text{Quantity of Good B Sacrificed}}{\text{Quantity of Good A Gained}} $$

數學必然性：如果甲國生產物品A的機會成本是 \(x\)，那它生產物品B的機會成本必然是 \(\frac{1}{x}\)。因此，一個國家不可能同時在兩種物品上享有比較優勢（即機會成本都比較低）。

Mathematical Certainty: If the opportunity cost of Good A is \(x\), the opportunity cost of Good B must be \(\frac{1}{x}\). Therefore, a country cannot have a comparative advantage (lower opportunity cost) in both goods simultaneously.

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案：選項 B

Correct Option: Option B

A. 錯誤 (Incorrect)

貿易獲益（Gain from trade）的分配取決於「貿易比率」（Terms of Trade），而並非取決於哪個國家的生產力較高。即使甲國擁有絕對優勢，如果貿易比率偏向乙國，乙國的獲益絕對可以多於甲國。

The distribution of the gain from trade depends on the "Terms of Trade", not on which country has higher productivity. Even if Country X has an absolute advantage, if the terms of trade favor Country Y, Country Y can gain more than Country X.

B. 正確 (Correct)

貿易的基礎是「比較優勢」（機會成本的差異），而非「絕對優勢」。即使甲國在所有物品的生產力都較高（享有全部絕對優勢），只要兩國生產物品的機會成本不同，雙方仍可透過專業化和貿易互惠互利。

The basis of trade is "Comparative Advantage" (differences in opportunity costs), not "Absolute Advantage". Even if Country X is more productive in everything, as long as their opportunity costs differ, mutually beneficial trade is still possible through specialization.

C. 錯誤 (Incorrect)

這在現實和理論中皆不可能。如果乙國進口所有物品，它將沒有任何出口來賺取外匯支付進口。根據比較優勢定律，乙國必定會在至少一種物品上享有比較優勢，並會生產和出口該物品。

This is impossible both in theory and reality. If Country Y imports every good, it will have nothing to export to pay for those imports. According to the law of comparative advantage, Country Y must have a comparative advantage in at least one good, which it will produce and export.

D. 錯誤 (Incorrect)

這違反了數學和經濟學的定義。機會成本是倒數關係。如果甲國生產物品A的機會成本低於乙國，甲國生產物品B的機會成本必定高於乙國。沒有國家能在所有物品的機會成本上都較低。

This violates mathematical and economic definitions. Opportunity costs are reciprocal. If Country X has a lower opportunity cost in producing Good A, its opportunity cost for Good B MUST be higher than Country Y's. No country can have lower opportunity costs in every good.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (破題關鍵)： 題目首句「生產每一物品的生產力都比乙國高」等於告訴你：甲國擁有所有絕對優勢 (Absolute Advantage)。

Pivot Point: The phrase "more productive in producing every good" translates directly to: Country X has an Absolute Advantage in all goods.

拆解步驟：

  1. 看到「生產力最高」或「產量最大」，立即聯想這是「絕對優勢」。
    When you see "highest productivity", recognize this refers to Absolute Advantage.
  2. 問自己：貿易發生是因為絕對優勢嗎？不是，是因為比較優勢（機會成本差異）。
    Ask: Is trade based on absolute advantage? No, it's based on Comparative Advantage (differences in opportunity costs).
  3. 掃描選項：尋找描述「比較優勢」或「互惠互利仍可能發生」的陳述。
    Scan options: Look for statements describing comparative advantage or "mutually beneficial trade is possible".

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱一：將「絕對優勢」與「比較優勢」混為一談。 很多同學誤以為一個強大的國家（甚麼都生產得快）就不需要和弱小的國家貿易。事實上，強國的高生產力並不能免除「機會成本」。

Trap 1: Confusing Absolute Advantage with Comparative Advantage. Many students think a strong country (that produces everything faster) doesn't need to trade with a weaker one. In reality, high productivity doesn't eliminate "opportunity costs".

陷阱二：認為比較優勢可以同時存在於所有物品。 這是最致命的錯誤。緊記倒數法則：當 \( \text{A} < \text{B} \)，其倒數必定 \( \frac{1}{\text{A}} > \frac{1}{\text{B}} \)。

Trap 2: Believing a country can have a comparative advantage in ALL goods. This is a fatal error. Remember the reciprocal rule: if \( \text{A} < \text{B} \), then \( \frac{1}{\text{A}} > \frac{1}{\text{B}} \).

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 有沒有可能兩國之間真的無法進行貿易？

Q: Is it possible for the two countries to have NO basis for trade at all?

A: 有可能！如果兩國生產物品的機會成本完全一樣，那就沒有比較優勢的存在。由於沒有機會成本的差異，雙方都無法透過貿易獲取更低的成本，因此就不會有貿易發生。（但留意：選項B是說「仍可能」，這在大多數情況下是正確的）。

A: Yes! If the opportunity costs are exactly the same for both countries, there is no comparative advantage. Without differences in opportunity cost, neither can acquire goods at a lower cost through trade, so no trade occurs. (Note: Option B says trade is "still possible", which is true for almost all scenarios).

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧 (The One-Second Rule):

Shortcut Technique:

  • 在DSE考試中，看到「某國在每一物品上的機會成本都較低」這句話，請立刻將它打交叉！這在數學上是100%不可能的。（即秒殺選項D）
    In the DSE exam, whenever you see "a country has a lower opportunity cost in every good", cross it out immediately! It is 100% mathematically impossible. (Instantly eliminates Option D).

  • 看到「絕對優勢」（生產力最高），直接聯想結論：「這不等於他們有比較優勢，貿易依然會發生。」（直指選項B）。
    When you see "absolute advantage" (highest productivity), jump straight to the conclusion: "This doesn't mean they have comparative advantage in everything; trade will still occur." (Points directly to Option B).
