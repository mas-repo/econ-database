HKDSE Economics Tutorial

Topic (課題): Market Intervention (Subsidies)
市場干預（補貼）
Difficulty (難度): ★★★★☆
Key Concepts (核心概念):
Per-unit Subsidy, Consumer/Producer Surplus, Deadweight Loss

題目 The Question

假設政府向某物品的生產商提供從量補貼，這會導致

Suppose the government provides a per-unit subsidy to the producers of a good. This would lead to

  • A. 消費者盈餘上升及生產者盈餘下降，因此對效率的影響不明確。
    A. an increase in consumer surplus and a fall in producer surplus, so having an ambiguous effect on efficiency.
  • B. 生產者盈餘上升及消費者盈餘下降，因此對效率的影響不明確。
    B. an increase in producer surplus and a fall in consumer surplus, so having an ambiguous effect on efficiency.
  • C. 效率的改善，因賣家及買家均會從補貼得益。
    C. an improvement in efficiency because both sellers and buyers would benefit from the subsidy.
  • D. 淨損失，因政府提供的補貼額大於消費者盈餘及生產者盈餘上升的總和。
    D. a deadweight loss because the amount of subsidy provided by the government is greater than the sum of the increase in consumer and producer surpluses.

概念重溫 Concept Review

當政府提供從量補貼 (Per-unit Subsidy) 時，這等同於降低了生產者的生產成本。供給曲線會垂直向下平移，平移的幅度等於每單位的補貼額。

When the government provides a per-unit subsidy, it effectively lowers the cost of production for producers. The supply curve shifts vertically downwards by the exact amount of the per-unit subsidy.

1. 消費者盈餘 (Consumer Surplus, $CS$)： 消費者願意支付的最高價格與實際支付價格之間的差額。補貼後，市場價格下降，交易量增加，因此 $CS$ 上升。

1. Consumer Surplus ($CS$): The difference between the maximum price consumers are willing to pay and the actual price they pay. After a subsidy, the market price falls and quantity traded increases, so $CS$ increases.

2. 生產者盈餘 (Producer Surplus, $PS$)： 生產者實際收取的價格與其願意接受的最低價格（邊際成本）之間的差額。補貼後，生產者實際收取的價格（市場價格 + 補貼）上升，交易量增加，因此 $總PS$ 上升。

2. Producer Surplus ($PS$): The difference between the actual price producers receive and the minimum price they are willing to accept (marginal cost). After a subsidy, the effective price received by producers (market price + subsidy) rises and quantity increases, so total $PS$ increases.

3. 淨損失 (Deadweight Loss, $DWL$)： 由於市場干預導致的總社會盈餘減少。在補貼下，新的交易量大於市場均衡數量。對於這些額外的單位，邊際成本大於邊際利益 ($MC > MB$)，造成資源錯配及效率損失。

3. Deadweight Loss ($DWL$): The reduction in total social surplus resulting from market intervention. Under a subsidy, the new quantity is greater than the free-market equilibrium quantity. For these extra units, Marginal Cost exceeds Marginal Benefit ($MC > MB$), causing misallocation of resources and a loss of efficiency.

$$ \text{Total Social Surplus} = CS + PS - \text{Government Expenditure} $$ $$ \text{Deadweight Loss (DWL)} = \text{Government Expenditure} - (\Delta CS + \Delta PS) $$

答案與詳細分析 The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案：選項 D

Correct Option: Option D

Option A & B

錯誤。 補貼會令市場價格下降，消費者買得更平更多，故消費者盈餘 ($CS$) 必定上升。同時，生產者扣除補貼後的實際收入增加，賣得更多，故生產者盈餘 ($PS$) 亦會上升。A和B選項分別說其中一方的盈餘下降，這是違反經濟學基本原理的。

Incorrect. A subsidy lowers the market price, allowing consumers to buy more at a lower price, so Consumer Surplus ($CS$) must increase. Simultaneously, the effective price received by producers increases, and they sell more, so Producer Surplus ($PS$) also increases. Options A and B state that one of the surpluses falls, which violates basic economic principles.

Option C

錯誤。 雖然買家和賣家都從補貼中獲益（兩者的盈餘皆上升），但這不等於效率改善。效率 (Efficiency) 看的是「整個社會」，而政府（代表納稅人）為這項補貼支付了龐大開支。因為過度生產 ($MC > MB$)，總社會盈餘實際上是減少了的。

Incorrect. Although both buyers and sellers benefit (both surpluses increase), this does not mean an improvement in efficiency. Efficiency considers the "whole society," and the government (representing taxpayers) pays a massive expense for this subsidy. Because of overproduction ($MC > MB$), total social surplus actually decreases.

Option D

正確。 政府的總補貼開支是一筆巨大的成本（圖表上的長方形面積）。這筆開支的數值，是大於消費者和生產者盈餘增加的總和的。兩者之間的差額，就是由過度生產帶來的淨損失 (Deadweight Loss)。因此，市場效率下降了。

Correct. The government's total subsidy expenditure is a huge cost (a large rectangular area on the diagram). The value of this expenditure is strictly greater than the combined increase in consumer and producer surpluses. The difference between the government's cost and the gains to buyers/sellers is the Deadweight Loss caused by overproduction. Thus, market efficiency drops.

題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)： 這條題目的核心在於區分「個人利益」(Private Benefit) 與「社會效率」(Social Efficiency)。看到「補貼」，學生必須立刻聯想到三件事：1. 買家開心 ($CS\uparrow$)，2. 賣家開心 ($PS\uparrow$)，3. 社會傷心 ($DWL$ 出現，效率下降)。

Pivot Point: The core of this question lies in distinguishing between "Private Benefit" and "Social Efficiency." When you see "Subsidy," you must immediately recall three things: 1. Buyers are happy ($CS\uparrow$), 2. Sellers are happy ($PS\uparrow$), 3. Society is sad ($DWL$ exists, efficiency drops).

干擾項 (Distractors)： 選項 A、B 和 C 利用了學生的直覺誤區——以為「如果兩邊都獲益，效率就一定不明確或改善」。這忽略了「政府支出」這個隱藏的第三者成本。

Distractors: Options A, B, and C exploit a common intuitive trap—assuming that "if both parties benefit, efficiency must be ambiguous or improved." This completely ignores the hidden third-party cost: Government Expenditure.

常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

  • ❌ 誤解： 將「公平/大眾受惠」等同於「經濟效率提升」。
    ❌ Misconception: Equating "fairness / public benefit" with an "improvement in economic efficiency."
    📍 真相： 在沒有外部性 (Externalities) 的自由市場中，均衡點已經是效率最高的點。任何政府干預（無論是稅收還是補貼）都會扭曲價格信號，導致淨損失。
    📍 Truth: In a free market without externalities, the equilibrium is already at maximum efficiency. ANY government intervention (tax or subsidy) distorts price signals and causes a deadweight loss.

學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

🤔 同學問：「既然消費者和生產者都賺了，政府也是拿稅收來補貼，這筆錢只是左手交右手，為什麼會產生『損失』？」

🤔 Student Asks: "Since both consumers and producers gain, and the government just uses tax money, isn't this just transferring money from one pocket to another? Why is there a 'loss'?"

💡 導師答：因為補貼導致了過度生產 (Overproduction)。補貼讓市場生產了原本不該生產的數量（數量由 $Q_0$ 增加到 $Q_1$）。對於這些額外生產的物品，生產它們的成本（$MC$）實際上大於消費者從中獲得的價值（$MB$）。這意味著社會將珍貴的資源浪費在了效益較低的地方，這就是淨損失的來源。

💡 Tutor Answers: Because the subsidy causes overproduction. It forces the market to produce units that shouldn't have been produced (quantity increases from $Q_0$ to $Q_1$). For these extra units, the cost of making them ($MC$) is actually higher than the value consumers get from them ($MB$). This means society is wasting scarce resources on low-value activities. That waste is the Deadweight Loss.

考試技巧與速解 Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

⚡ 一秒刪除法： 在 DSE 經濟科中，只要題目沒有提及「外部性 (Externalities)」，任何形式的政府干預（配額、價格上限、價格下限、稅收、補貼）必定會導致無效率 (Inefficiency) 或淨損失 (Deadweight Loss)。

⚡ One-Second Elimination: In DSE Economics, as long as the question does not mention "Externalities", ANY form of government intervention (quotas, price ceilings, price floors, taxes, subsidies) MUST lead to inefficiency or a deadweight loss.

因此，當你看到選項中出現「效率改善 (improvement in efficiency)」或「對效率影響不明確 (ambiguous effect on efficiency)」時，你可以毫不猶豫地立刻將它們劃掉。這題可以直接秒殺 A、B、C，直接選 D！

Therefore, when you see options containing "improvement in efficiency" or "ambiguous effect on efficiency," you can cross them out immediately without hesitation. For this question, you can instantly eliminate A, B, and C, and choose D in seconds!
