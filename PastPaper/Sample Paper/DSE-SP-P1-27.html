2. The Question (題目)

下列哪項有關貨幣的陳述是正確的？
Which of the following statements about money is / are correct?

A. 通貨收縮減低貨幣的購買力。
Deflation reduces the purchasing power of money.

B. 在一個沒有現金的社會中，所有交易都透過電子繳費系統支付，貨幣在該社會並不存在。
Money does not exist in a cashless society where all transactions are settled through an electronic payment system.

C. 若交易中使用貨幣，縱使交易雙方沒有雙重需求偶合，交易仍是可能的。
With the use of money in exchange, exchange is possible even when trading parties do not have double coincidence of wants.

D. 以上均正確。
All of the above are correct.

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

Definition & Functions of Money (貨幣的定義與功能)

貨幣是指任何在日常交易中普遍被接受為交易媒介（Medium of Exchange）的物品。它解決了以物易物（Barter economy）中必須存在「雙重需求偶合」（Double coincidence of wants）的困難。
Money is anything that is generally accepted as a medium of exchange in daily transactions. It solves the problem in a barter economy where a "double coincidence of wants" is strictly required.

Purchasing Power of Money (貨幣購買力)

貨幣購買力是指一單位貨幣能夠購買到的貨物和服務的數量。它與一般物價水平（General Price Level）呈反比關係。
The purchasing power of money refers to the amount of goods and services one unit of money can buy. It is inversely related to the general price level.
$$\text{Purchasing Power} \propto \frac{1}{\text{Price Level } (P)}$$

當物價水平 $P$ 下降（即通貨收縮 Deflation），購買力會上升。
When the price level $P$ decreases (i.e., deflation), purchasing power increases.

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Answer: Option C (正確答案：C)

這正是貨幣作為「交易媒介」的核心功能。在以物易物的經濟中，你要用蘋果換橙，對方必須剛好有橙且想要蘋果（雙重需求偶合）。貨幣的出現打破了這個限制，你可以賣掉蘋果換取貨幣，再用貨幣買橙。
This illustrates the core function of money as a "medium of exchange". In a barter economy, to trade apples for oranges, the other party must have oranges and want your apples (double coincidence of wants). Money breaks this barrier: you sell apples for money, and use the money to buy oranges.

Why Option A is wrong (為何 A 錯誤)

通貨收縮（Deflation）代表一般物價水平下降。由於東西變便宜了，同一張鈔票能買到更多的貨品，因此貨幣的購買力應該是上升，而非減低。
Deflation means a general decrease in the price level. Because goods are cheaper, the same banknote can buy more goods. Therefore, the purchasing power of money increases, rather than reduces.

Why Option B is wrong (為何 B 錯誤)

現金（紙幣和硬幣）只是貨幣的其中一種形式。電子繳費系統（如八達通、支付寶、銀行轉帳）背後轉移的是銀行存款。由於銀行存款普遍被接受為交易媒介，所以它們也是貨幣（貨幣供應 M1, M2, M3 的一部分）。無現金社會仍然有貨幣。
Cash (notes and coins) is just one form of money. Electronic payment systems transfer bank deposits. Since bank deposits are generally accepted as a medium of exchange, they are considered money (part of money supply M1, M2, M3). A cashless society still uses money.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題關鍵 (Pivot Point)： 此題考驗學生對貨幣本質的理解。貨幣由其「功能」定義，而非其「物理形態」。只要它發揮著交易媒介的功能，無論是貝殼、黃金還是電子數據，它就是貨幣。
Pivot Point: This question tests your understanding of the nature of money. Money is defined by its "function", not its "physical form". As long as it serves as a medium of exchange, whether it's shells, gold, or digital data, it is money.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱 1：混淆「現金」與「貨幣」
許多學生以為沒有實體現金 = 沒有貨幣。記住：銀行存款（Bank Deposits）是現代社會最重要的貨幣形式！
Trap 1: Confusing "Cash" with "Money"
Many students think no physical cash = no money. Remember: Bank deposits are the most important form of money in modern society!

陷阱 2：通縮與購買力的關係
學生經常粗心大意，看到「通縮 (Deflation)」就直覺聯想到負面詞彙如「減少 (Reduces)」。必須冷靜套用公式：物價跌 $\rightarrow$ 購買力升。
Trap 2: Deflation and Purchasing Power
Students often carelessly associate "Deflation" with negative words like "Reduces". You must apply the logic calmly: Price drops $\rightarrow$ Purchasing power rises.

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

學生問： 既然電子繳費系統中的結餘算作貨幣，那麼「信用卡 (Credit Card)」算不算貨幣？

Student Q: Since balances in electronic payment systems count as money, are "Credit Cards" considered money?

導師答： 不算！這是一個極常見的 DSE 陷阱。信用卡本質上是銀行批出的一筆短期貸款（Loan），它只是一種「延期支付（Deferred Payment）」的工具。當你找卡數時，你用來還款的銀行存款才是真正的貨幣。

Tutor A: No! This is a very common DSE trap. A credit card is essentially a short-term loan granted by a bank. It is merely a means of "deferred payment". The bank deposits you use to pay off your credit card bill later are the actual money.

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

排除法 (Elimination Method)：
考試時若想節省時間，看到選項 A 中通縮會「減低」購買力，這是明顯的邏輯錯誤，馬上刪去 A 和 D。剩下 B 和 C，選項 B 說電子支付社會沒有貨幣，這違反常理（我們每天用八達通都在使用貨幣），刪去 B。答案 C 秒殺！
Elimination Method:
To save time in exams: Option A claims deflation "reduces" purchasing power, which is a direct logical error. Eliminate A and D immediately. Between B and C, Option B says cashless societies have no money, which defies common sense (we use Octopus daily, which is money). Eliminate B. You get C instantly!
