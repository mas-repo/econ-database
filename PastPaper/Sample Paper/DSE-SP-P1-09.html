HKDSE Economics MC Guide
文憑試經濟科多項選擇題詳解

Topic (課題): Types of Integration (廠商合併的種類) - Horizontal Integration (橫向合併)
Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)
Key Concepts (核心概念): Horizontal Integration Risk Diversification Economies of Scale

1. The Question (題目)

假設一家中國電腦生產商收購一家美國電腦生產商。下列哪項並不是該中國電腦在該收購中可獲得的好處？
Suppose a Chinese computer manufacturer takes over a US computer manufacturer. Which of the following is NOT an advantage to the Chinese computer manufacturer in the above takeover?
A. 將競爭對手變成夥伴
A. turning a competitor into a partner
B. 分散需求波動的風險
B. diversifying risk of demand fluctuation
C. 為公司建立良好聲譽
C. building up the goodwill of the company
D. 降低平均生產成本
D. lowering the average cost of production

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

橫向合併 (Horizontal Integration): 指兩間生產同類產品或處於同一生產階段的企業合併。在此題目中，兩家公司都是「電腦生產商」，因此這是標準的橫向合併。
Horizontal Integration: This occurs when two firms producing the same type of goods or operating at the same stage of production combine. In this question, both are "computer manufacturers," making it a classic horizontal integration.
橫向合併的常見好處: 包括擴大市場佔有率（減少競爭）、享有規模經濟（降低平均成本），以及透過資源共享建立更好的商譽或品牌效應。
Common Benefits of Horizontal Integration: These include expanding market share (reducing competition), enjoying economies of scale (lowering average cost), and building better goodwill or brand effects through resource sharing.
規模經濟 (Economies of Scale): 當產量 \( Q \) 增加時，由於固定成本被分攤或購買原材料時享有折扣，長遠的平均成本 \( AC \) 會下降。
Economies of Scale: As output \( Q \) increases, the long-run average cost \( AC \) decreases because fixed costs are spread out or bulk purchase discounts are obtained.
$$ AC = \frac{Total\ Cost\ (TC)}{Quantity\ (Q)} $$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option B

A 將競爭對手變成夥伴
分析： 這是橫向合併的經典好處。收購了美國的競爭對手，原本的對手就成為了旗下公司，直接減少了市場競爭。因此，這是一個好處。
A turning a competitor into a partner
Analysis: This is a classic benefit of horizontal integration. By acquiring the US competitor, the rival becomes a subsidiary, directly reducing market competition. Therefore, this IS an advantage.
B 分散需求波動的風險
分析： 這是不正確的（因此是本題答案）。因為兩家公司都是生產「電腦」，產品種類單一。如果全球對電腦的需求整體下跌（例如經濟衰退），這家合併後的大型電腦公司依然會受到嚴重打擊。分散需求風險通常是複合合併 (Conglomerate Integration) 或側向合併 (Lateral Integration) 的特徵（即生產完全不同或相關但非同類的產品）。
B diversifying risk of demand fluctuation
Analysis: This is INCORRECT (and thus the correct answer to the question). Because both companies produce "computers," their product line is highly uniform. If global demand for computers falls (e.g., during a recession), this merged giant will still suffer a heavy blow. Diversifying demand risk is typically a characteristic of Conglomerate Integration or Lateral Integration (producing completely unrelated or related-but-different products).
C 為公司建立良好聲譽
分析： 中國企業收購知名的美國企業（例如現實中的 Lenovo 收購 IBM 電腦業務），可以借助對方原有的國際知名度和客戶網絡，大幅提升自身的品牌商譽。因此，這是一個好處。
C building up the goodwill of the company
Analysis: A Chinese firm acquiring an established US firm (like the real-world example of Lenovo acquiring IBM's PC division) can leverage the acquired firm's international brand recognition and customer network to significantly boost its own goodwill. Therefore, this IS an advantage.
D 降低平均生產成本
分析： 合併後，產量 \( Q \) 大幅增加，公司可以進行大量採購以獲得折扣，或整合研發部門（R&D），從而享有規模經濟 (Economies of Scale)。這導致平均成本下降。因此，這是一個好處。
D lowering the average cost of production
Analysis: After the merger, the output \( Q \) increases significantly. The firm can make bulk purchases to get discounts or consolidate Research & Development (R&D) departments, thereby enjoying economies of scale. This leads to a lower average cost. Therefore, this IS an advantage.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

  • 解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point): 留意兩家公司的業務性質。「電腦生產商」收購「電腦生產商」，代表這是同類產品的合併（橫向）。只要確認了這一點，就能立刻聯想到「無法分散產品單一的市場風險」。
    The Pivot Point: Pay attention to the business nature of the two companies. A "computer manufacturer" acquiring a "computer manufacturer" means this is a merger of the same product type (Horizontal). Once you identify this, you can immediately deduce that "it cannot diversify the market risk of a single product line."
  • 擾亂資訊 (Distractors): 題目特意寫出「中國」與「美國」。這很容易讓同學誤以為「地理上的擴張 = 風險分散」。在經濟學的嚴格定義中，這裡的「需求波動風險」主要針對產品市場（如科技業衰退），單純跨國生產同一產品並不能完全免疫該行業的整體衰退。
    Distractors: The question deliberately specifies "Chinese" and "US". This easily misleads students into thinking "geographical expansion = risk diversification." In strict economic definitions here, "risk of demand fluctuation" primarily refers to the product market (e.g., tech industry slump). Merely producing the same product across different countries does not grant immunity to an industry-wide recession.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

❌ 錯誤觀念：混淆橫向合併與複合合併的好處
許多學生只要看到「收購 / 合併」，就會自動將所有「好處」都選進去。記住，「分散風險」是複合合併 (Conglomerate) 的專利商標！ 賣電腦的去收購賣雪糕的，才能在電腦銷量差時，靠賣雪糕來穩定收入。
❌ Misconception: Confusing the benefits of Horizontal and Conglomerate Integration
Many students automatically select all listed "benefits" whenever they see the word "takeover/merger". Remember, "diversifying risk" is the trademark benefit of Conglomerate Integration! A computer seller acquiring an ice cream seller can stabilize its income by selling ice cream when computer sales drop.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

🙋‍♂️ 學生問： 中國和美國是兩個不同的市場。如果中國對電腦的需求下跌，但美國的需求上升，那不就能夠「分散需求波動的風險」了嗎？
🙋‍♂️ Student: China and the US are two different markets. If demand for computers drops in China but rises in the US, doesn't that "diversify the risk of demand fluctuation"?
👨‍🏫 導師答： 這是個很好的思考！地理上的多樣化確實能稍微緩衝地區性經濟衝擊。然而，在 DSE 經濟科的標準框架中，「分散需求波動的風險」是特定用來區分「不同產品」（側向/複合合併）與「同一產品」（橫向合併）的考點。因為兩者同屬個人電腦行業，若面對全球性科技股災或智能手機取代電腦的大趨勢，兩國的需求會同時下跌。因此，考試時遇到同類產品合併，預設為「不能」分散需求風險。
👨‍🏫 Tutor: That's a great thought! Geographical diversification can indeed slightly cushion regional economic shocks. However, within the standard framework of DSE Economics, "diversifying risk of demand fluctuation" is a specific testing point used to distinguish "different products" (Lateral/Conglomerate) from the "same product" (Horizontal). Because both belong to the PC industry, if there is a global tech crash or a megatrend of smartphones replacing PCs, demand in both countries will drop simultaneously. Therefore, in exams, always assume that merging same-product firms "cannot" diversify demand risk.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

⚡ 秒殺技巧 (Keyword Matching)：
做合併種類的 MC 題，養成圈出產品名稱的習慣。這題圈出「電腦」和「電腦」。
公式： 同產品 (Same Product) ➜ 橫向合併 (Horizontal) ➜ 絕對不能分散需求風險。
看到「分散風險 (Diversifying risk)」，直接找有沒有出現兩種完全不同的產品。沒有？那它就是答案 (NOT an advantage)！
⚡ Shortcut Trick (Keyword Matching):
When doing MCs on integration types, make it a habit to circle the product names. Here, circle "computer" and "computer".
Formula: Same Product ➜ Horizontal Integration ➜ Absolutely CANNOT diversify demand risk.
When you see "Diversifying risk," immediately check if there are two completely different products. No? Then that's your answer for "NOT an advantage"!
