Topic / 課題: 總體經濟學：短期總體供應 (Macroeconomics: Short-Run Aggregate Supply)

Difficulty / 難度: ★★★☆☆ (Moderate / 中等)

Key Concepts / 關鍵概念: Short-Run Aggregate Supply (SRAS) 短期總體供應, Price Expectations 價格預期, Cost of Production 生產成本, Movement vs. Shift 沿著曲線移動與曲線移動

1. The Question (題目)

在下列哪種情況下，短期總體供應曲線會向左移？

The short run aggregate supply curve will shift to the left if:

A. 勞工成本下降
A. the labour cost decreases.
B. 生產力上升
B. the labour productivity increases.
C. 一般物價水平上升
C. the general price level increases. (Note: minor typo fixed from prompt to complete the sentence)
D. 人們預期一般物價水平會上升
D. the general price level is expected to increase.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

短期總體供應 (Short-Run Aggregate Supply, SRAS): 指在一般物價水平改變，但生產要素（如勞工）價格未能完全相應調整的短期內，經濟體系中所有廠商願意且能夠提供的實質產出總量。SRAS 曲線通常向上傾斜。

Short-Run Aggregate Supply (SRAS): Refers to the total real output that all firms in an economy are willing and able to produce in the short run, where the general price level can change but factor prices (like wages) have not fully adjusted. The SRAS curve is generally upward-sloping.

移動 (Shift) vs. 沿曲線移動 (Movement along the curve):

  • 沿曲線移動： 只有當目前的「一般物價水平」 \(P\) 改變時發生。
  • 曲線移動： 當物價水平以外的因素改變時發生，例如：生產成本（工資、原材料價格）、生產力、政府稅收/津貼，以及預期物價水平 \(P^e\)。

Shift vs. Movement along the curve:

  • Movement: Occurs ONLY when the current general price level \(P\) changes.
  • Shift: Occurs when factors other than the current price level change, such as: costs of production (wages, raw material prices), productivity, government taxes/subsidies, and the expected price level \(P^e\).

相關的盧卡斯總體供應函數 (Lucas Aggregate Supply Function) 可簡化表示為：

The relevant Lucas Aggregate Supply Function can be simply represented as:

$$ Y = \bar{Y} + a(P - P^e) $$

這裡 \(Y\) 是實質產出，\(\bar{Y}\) 是潛在產出，\(P\) 是實際物價水平，而 \(P^e\) 是預期物價水平。當預期物價水平 \(P^e\) 上升，在任何給定的實際物價 \(P\) 之下，產出 \(Y\) 都會減少，這在圖像上顯示為 SRAS 向左移。

Here, \(Y\) is real output, \(\bar{Y}\) is potential output, \(P\) is the actual price level, and \(P^e\) is the expected price level. When the expected price level \(P^e\) increases, for any given actual price \(P\), output \(Y\) decreases, which graphically means the SRAS shifts to the left.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案：D

Correct Option: D

選項 A 分析 (錯誤): 勞工成本下降會降低廠商的生產成本，提高利潤空間。在同一個物價水平下，廠商願意供應更多。因此，SRAS 曲線會向右移，而不是向左移。

Option A Analysis (Wrong): A decrease in labor cost reduces firms' production costs, widening profit margins. At the same price level, firms are willing to supply more. Therefore, the SRAS curve shifts to the right, not left.

選項 B 分析 (錯誤): 生產力上升意味著相同的投入能產生更多的產出，這相當於降低了單位生產成本。同樣地，這會導致 SRAS 曲線向右移。

Option B Analysis (Wrong): An increase in productivity means the same input produces more output, effectively lowering unit production costs. Similarly, this causes the SRAS curve to shift to the right.

選項 C 分析 (錯誤): 一般物價水平上升是縱軸變數的改變。這只會導致「總量供應增加」（Increase in quantity supplied），即沿著現有的 SRAS 曲線向上移動，而不是整條曲線移動。

Option C Analysis (Wrong): An increase in the general price level is a change in the y-axis variable. This only leads to an "increase in quantity supplied", which is a movement along the existing SRAS curve upwards, not a shift of the curve.

選項 D 分析 (正確): 當人們（特別是工人）預期未來物價水平會上升時，他們會要求更高的名目工資以維持實質購買力。工資要求增加導致廠商的預期生產成本上升，利潤空間縮小。因此，廠商會減少供應，導致 SRAS 曲線向左移。

Option D Analysis (Correct): When people (especially workers) expect the general price level to increase in the future, they will demand higher nominal wages to maintain their real purchasing power. Increased wage demands lead to higher expected production costs for firms, shrinking profit margins. Thus, firms reduce supply, shifting the SRAS curve to the left.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

第一步：鎖定關鍵字。 題目要求「短期總體供應曲線 (SRAS)」、「向左移 (Shift to the left)」。向左移代表「供應減少」或「成本增加」。

Step 1: Identify Keywords. The question asks for "short run aggregate supply curve (SRAS)", "shift to the left". A leftward shift means "decrease in supply" or "increase in costs".

第二步：過濾選項（移動 vs 沿線移動）。 看到選項 C 提到「一般物價水平」，必須立刻警覺！這是座標軸上的變數。座標軸變數的改變只會導致「沿曲線移動」，絕不會使曲線平移。直接刪除 C。

Step 2: Filter Options (Shift vs Movement). Seeing "general price level" in Option C should trigger an immediate alert! This is a variable on the axes. A change in an axis variable ONLY causes a "movement along the curve", never a shift. Eliminate C immediately.

第三步：判斷方向。 SRAS 向左移 = 供應條件惡化 = 成本上升 / 生產力下降。選項 A 是成本下降 (右移)，選項 B 是生產力上升 (右移)。只剩下選項 D，預期物價上升推高工資要求 (成本上升)，導致左移。完美契合！

Step 3: Determine Direction. SRAS shift left = worsening supply conditions = higher costs / lower productivity. Option A is cost decrease (shift right), Option B is productivity increase (shift right). Only Option D remains: expected inflation pushes up wage demands (higher costs), causing a leftward shift. Perfect match!

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ 最大陷阱：混淆「實際物價水平」與「預期物價水平」

很多學生看到「物價水平上升」，就會直覺認為會影響供應。HKDSE 考評局非常喜歡將「一般物價水平 (The general price level)」和「預期一般物價水平 (Expected general price level)」放在一起考。記住：

  • 實際 \(P\) 改變 \(\rightarrow\) 不移動（沿線移動）
  • 預期 \(P^e\) 改變 \(\rightarrow\) 整條線移動

⚠️ Biggest Trap: Confusing "Actual Price Level" with "Expected Price Level"

Many students see "price level increases" and instinctively think it shifts supply. The HKDSE examining body loves to put "general price level" and "expected general price level" together to trick you. Remember:

  • Change in actual \(P\) \(\rightarrow\) NO SHIFT (Movement)
  • Change in expected \(P^e\) \(\rightarrow\) CURVE SHIFTS

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

問：為什麼「預期」物價上升，現在的 SRAS 就會立刻左移？這不是未來的事嗎？

答： 這是因為工資合約的談判。工人是具有理性的。如果他們「預期」未來物價會變貴（通脹），他們在「現在」簽訂勞資合約時，就會要求更高的工資來補償預期的購買力損失。對僱主而言，這代表「現在」的生產成本即將或已經上升，因此他們會立即減少短期供應，導致 SRAS 左移。


Q: Why does an "expected" future price increase shift the SRAS left immediately? Isn't that something in the future?

A: This is due to wage contract negotiations. Workers are rational. If they "expect" goods to become more expensive in the future (inflation), they will demand higher nominal wages "now" when signing employment contracts to compensate for the expected loss of purchasing power. For employers, this means production costs are rising or about to rise "now", so they immediately reduce short-run supply, shifting SRAS to the left.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

10秒速解法 (10-Second Elimination Method):

  1. 題目問「左移」(供應變差)。
  2. A (成本降) = 好事 \(\rightarrow\) 右移 (淘汰)
  3. B (生產力升) = 好事 \(\rightarrow\) 右移 (淘汰)
  4. C (一般物價) = 座標軸變數 \(\rightarrow\) 不移動 (淘汰)
  5. 答案只剩下 D！即使你不完全明白預期物價的機制，也能用刪去法穩拿分數。

10-Second Elimination Method:

  1. Question asks for "Shift Left" (worsening supply).
  2. A (Cost drops) = Good thing \(\rightarrow\) Shift Right (Eliminate)
  3. B (Productivity up) = Good thing \(\rightarrow\) Shift Right (Eliminate)
  4. C (General price) = Axis variable \(\rightarrow\) No Shift (Eliminate)
  5. Only D is left! Even if you don't fully grasp the expectation mechanism, you can securely score the point through elimination.
