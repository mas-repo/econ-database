古典貨幣量理論
Classical Quantity Theory of Money

難度 / Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ 貨幣流動速度 / Velocity of Money 交易方程式 / Equation of Exchange

題目 / The Question

古典貨幣量理論

(1) 推斷貨幣供應的轉變可能不會影響一般物價水平。
(2) 名義國民收入的轉變與貨幣供應的轉變有正向關係。
(3) 假設貨幣流動速度不變。

A. 只有(1)、(2)
B. 只有(1)、(3)
C. 只有(2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2)、(3)

The classical quantity theory of money

(1) predicts that changes in money supply may have no effect on the general price level.
(2) predicts that changes in nominal national income are positively related to changes in money supply.
(3) assumes that the velocity of circulation of money is constant.

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

概念重溫 / Concept Review

要解答這道題目，我們必須了解交易方程式 (Equation of Exchange) 及其在古典學派中的假設。

To answer this question, we must understand the Equation of Exchange and its assumptions in the classical school of thought.

$$ M \times V = P \times Y $$
  • $M$ = 貨幣供應量 (Money Supply)
  • $V$ = 貨幣流動速度 (Velocity of circulation of money) - 貨幣轉手的平均次數 / the average number of times a unit of money is used
  • $P$ = 一般物價水平 (General Price Level)
  • $Y$ = 實質國民收入 / 實質產出 (Real National Income / Real Output)

名義國民收入 (Nominal National Income) 的定義就是 $P \times Y$。

The definition of Nominal National Income is exactly $P \times Y$.

古典學派的兩大假設 (Two Key Classical Assumptions):

  1. $V$ 是常數 (Constant $V$): 貨幣流動速度在短期內保持不變。
    Velocity of money remains constant in the short run.
  2. $Y$ 處於全民就業水平 (Full-employment $Y$): 實質產出由生產要素決定，短期內不會因貨幣供應改變。
    Real output is determined by real factors of production and is fixed at full employment in the short run.

答案與詳細分析 / Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Option: C

分析 (1) / Analysis (1): 錯誤 False

根據方程式 $MV = PY$，因為古典學派假設 $V$ 和 $Y$ 都是常數，所以當貨幣供應 $M$ 改變時，必定會導致一般物價水平 $P$ 出現相同比例的改變。貨幣供應必定會影響物價水平，而不會「可能不會影響」。

According to the equation $MV = PY$, since classical economists assume $V$ and $Y$ are constant, any change in money supply $M$ must lead to a proportionate change in the general price level $P$. Changes in money supply will definitely affect the price level, not "may have no effect".

分析 (2) / Analysis (2): 正確 True

名義國民收入等於 $PY$。將方程式重寫為 $M \times V = \text{名義國民收入}$。既然 $V$ 被假設為不變 (常數)，當貨幣供應 $M$ 增加時，$PY$ (名義國民收入) 亦必定會增加。因此兩者呈正向關係。

Nominal national income equals $PY$. Rewriting the equation gives $M \times V = \text{Nominal National Income}$. Given that $V$ is assumed to be constant, an increase in $M$ must result in an increase in $PY$. Therefore, they are positively related.

分析 (3) / Analysis (3): 正確 True

這是古典貨幣量理論最核心的基礎假設：人們的交易習慣在短期內不會大幅改變，因此貨幣流動速度 $V$ 是固定不變的。

This is the core foundational assumption of the classical quantity theory: people's transaction habits do not change drastically in the short run, so the velocity of money $V$ is assumed to be constant.

綜合以上，(2) 和 (3) 正確，故答案為 C。
In conclusion, (2) and (3) are correct, so the answer is C.

題目拆解 / Problem Decomposition

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point):
解開這題的關鍵在於清楚區分「名義變量」(Nominal) 與「實質變量」(Real)。名義國民收入是 $PY$，而實質國民收入是 $Y$。只要你在腦海中寫出 $MV = PY$，並標記 $V$ 為常數，(2) 和 (3) 的答案就會顯而易見。

The key to solving this is clearly distinguishing between "Nominal" and "Real" variables. Nominal income is $PY$, while real income is $Y$. If you visualize $MV = PY$ and mark $V$ as a constant, statements (2) and (3) become obvious.

常犯錯誤與陷阱 / Common Mistakes & Traps

  • 混淆名義與實質收入： 很多學生看到 (2) 會以為說的是「實質國民收入」(Real Income)。記住，古典理論中貨幣是「中立的」(Money is neutral)，$M$ 的改變不會影響實質 $Y$，但會影響名義 $PY$！
    Confusing Nominal and Real Income: Many students misread (2) as "Real national income". Remember, in classical theory, money is neutral. A change in $M$ does not affect real $Y$, but it DOES affect nominal $PY$!
  • 將凱恩斯理論與古典理論混淆： 凱恩斯學派認為在「流動性陷阱」(Liquidity Trap) 下，增加貨幣供應可能無法刺激經濟或改變物價。但本題問的是古典理論，千萬不要套用錯學派的假設。
    Mixing Keynesian and Classical theories: Keynesians argue that in a liquidity trap, increasing money supply might not affect prices. But this question specifically asks about Classical theory. Don't mix up the schools of thought.

學生常見疑問 / Student Q&A

Q: 為什麼古典學派認為實質國民收入 ($Y$) 是不變的？
Why do classical economists assume real national income ($Y$) is constant?

古典經濟學家相信市場機制是完美的，價格和工資具備完全彈性(perfectly flexible)。因此，經濟體系會自動調節並長期處於「全民就業」(Full employment) 的狀態。既然所有資源都已充分利用，產出 $Y$ 就達到極限，不會因為你印更多銀紙而增加。
Classical economists believe markets are perfect and wages/prices are perfectly flexible. Thus, the economy naturally adjusts to full employment. Since all resources are fully utilized, output $Y$ is at its maximum and won't increase just because you print more money.

考試技巧與速解 / Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

秒殺法 / The "One-Second" Elimination Method

在應付 DSE MC 題時，我們不一定要逐一分析。看看這題：

只要你溫習過古典理論，你一定知道它的招牌口號就是「貨幣流動速度不變」($V$ is constant)。所以 (3) 肯定是正確的。這無法幫我們排除任何選項，因為 B, C, D 都有 (3)。

接下來看 (1)。古典理論的核心結論是「貨幣只會影響物價 (Money neutrality)」，即 $M$ 決定 $P$。所以說「可能不影響」是百分之百錯誤的，直接違背了該學派的宗旨。

既然 (1) 是錯的，你可以立即劃掉選項 A、B、D（因為它們都包含 1）。
根本不需要看 (2) 就能確定答案是 C！ 這樣可以為你節省寶貴的考試時間。

In DSE MC, you don't always need to analyze every statement.
If you studied Classical theory, you know its hallmark assumption is "$V$ is constant". So (3) is definitely TRUE.
Next, look at (1). The core conclusion of classical theory is that money dictates prices. Saying it "may have no effect" completely contradicts the theory. So (1) is absolutely FALSE.
Since (1) is false, cross out options A, B, and D immediately. You arrive at the correct answer C without even needing to read statement (2)! This saves crucial exam time.
