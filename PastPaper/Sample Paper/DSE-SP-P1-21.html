3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. Total GDP vs. Per Capita GDP (總本地生產總值 vs. 人均本地生產總值)

「人均本地生產總值」是指平均每人的產出。要計算一個國家的「總本地生產總值」，我們必須將人均數值乘以總人口。

"Per capita GDP" refers to the average output per person. To calculate a country's "Total GDP", we must multiply the per capita value by the total population.

$$ \text{Total GDP} = \text{Per Capita GDP} \times \text{Population} $$

2. Gini Coefficient (堅尼系數)

堅尼系數是量度收入不均的指標，數值介乎 \(0\) 至 \(1\) 之間。數值越大，代表收入分佈越不平均（貧富懸殊越嚴重）。

The Gini coefficient measures income inequality, ranging from \(0\) to \(1\). A higher value indicates a more uneven income distribution (greater wealth gap).

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案： Correct Answer: Option A
A (Correct 正確)

分析： 甲國的總本地生產總值為 \( \$10,000 \times 20,000,000 = \$2,000 \) 億。乙國的總本地生產總值為 \( \$12,000 \times 10,000,000 = \$1,200 \) 億。因為 \( \$2,000 \text{ 億} > \$1,200 \text{ 億} \)，所以甲國的本地生產總值較高。

Analysis: The Total GDP of Country A is \( \$10,000 \times 20,000,000 = \$200 \) billion. The Total GDP of Country B is \( \$12,000 \times 10,000,000 = \$120 \) billion. Since \( \$200 \text{ billion} > \$120 \text{ billion} \), Country A's GDP is higher.

B (Incorrect 錯誤)

分析： 甲國的堅尼系數 (\(0.45\)) 大於乙國的堅尼系數 (\(0.3\))。堅尼系數越大，代表收入分配越不平均。因此，甲國的收入分配較乙國不平均。

Analysis: Country A's Gini coefficient (\(0.45\)) is greater than Country B's (\(0.3\)). A larger Gini coefficient means the income distribution is more uneven. Therefore, Country A's income distribution is less even than Country B's.

C (Incorrect 錯誤)

分析： 雖然總GDP較高，但甲國的平均收入較低（人均 \( \$10,000 \) < \( \$12,000 \)），而且貧富懸殊較嚴重（堅尼系數較高）。這意味著甲國的低收入組別分得一個「較小經濟餅」中「較小的份額」，所以他們的生活水平理應較乙國差，而不是較高。

Analysis: Although total GDP is higher, Country A has a lower average income (per capita \( \$10,000 \) < \( \$12,000 \)) AND worse wealth inequality (higher Gini). This means the low-income group in Country A receives a "smaller slice" of a "smaller average pie". Thus, their living standards should logically be lower than in Country B, not higher.

D (Incorrect 錯誤)

分析： 因為選項 B 和 C 是錯誤的，所以選項 D 不成立。

Analysis: Since Options B and C are incorrect, Option D is invalid.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 陷阱一：直接比較人均 GDP 代替總 GDP。 很多學生看到乙國的 \( \$12,000 \) 較高，就誤以為乙國的 GDP 較高，而忘記了甲國人口是乙國的兩倍。
    Trap 1: Comparing Per Capita GDP instead of Total GDP. Many students see Country B's higher \( \$12,000 \) and mistakenly assume its GDP is higher, forgetting that Country A has twice the population.
  • 陷阱二：誤解堅尼系數的數值意義。 學生容易忘記堅尼系數是「越大越不平均」，錯誤地將高數值等同於「較高水平的平均度」。
    Trap 2: Misinterpreting the Gini coefficient value. Students easily forget that a higher Gini means "more uneven", wrongly equating a high number with a "higher level of equality".

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

問：既然甲國總GDP較高，為甚麼低收入人士的生活水平卻可能較低？

Q: Since Country A has a higher total GDP, why might the living standard of its low-income group be lower?

答： 總 GDP 只代表國家整體的經濟規模。生活水平更取決於「平均每人有幾多」(人均GDP) 以及「財富如何分配」(堅尼系數)。甲國人口龐大拉高了總產值，但平均下來每人較少，加上財富集中在富人手中（高堅尼系數），窮人分到的資源就更少了。

A: Total GDP only represents the overall economic size of the nation. Living standards depend more on "how much each person gets on average" (per capita GDP) and "how wealth is distributed" (Gini coefficient). Country A's huge population inflates the total output, but the average per person is lower. Combined with wealth being concentrated among the rich (high Gini), the poor receive fewer resources.
