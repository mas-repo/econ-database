📝 HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Market Structure (市場結構) Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆ Key Concepts: Oligopoly, Market Dominance

1. 題目 (The Question)

在下列哪種情況下，一個市場可被視為寡頭壟斷？

A. 市場訊息不完全
B. 巿場被少數賣家支配
C. 政府限制賣家進入市場
D. 不同賣家出售相似但可區別的產品

A market can be regarded as an oligopoly when

A. market information is not perfect.
B. the market is dominated by a few sellers.
C. the government restricts the entry of sellers into the market.
D. different sellers sell similar but differentiated products.

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

寡頭壟斷 (Oligopoly) 是一種市場結構，其最核心的定義特徵是「市場由少數大型賣家支配」。因為賣家數量少，他們之間存在着高度的相互牽制 (Interdependence)。任何一個賣家的定價或產量決定，都會直接影響其他對手的利潤。

Oligopoly is a market structure fundamentally defined by the characteristic that "the market is dominated by a few large sellers." Because there are only a few sellers, there is a high degree of interdependence among them. Any pricing or output decision by one seller will directly affect the profits of rival firms.

在經濟學中，我們經常使用市場集中率 (Concentration Ratio) 來量度市場被少數企業支配的程度。例如，四行集中率衡量市場中最大的四家企業所佔的市場份額總和：

In economics, we often use the Concentration Ratio to measure the degree to which a market is dominated by a few firms. For example, the four-firm concentration ratio measures the total market share held by the four largest firms in the market:

$$ CR_n = \sum_{i=1}^{n} S_i $$

當中 \( CR_n \) 代表 \( n \) 家最大企業的集中率，而 \( S_i \) 則是第 \( i \) 家企業的市場佔有率。如果少數幾家企業（例如 \( n=4 \)）的 \( CR_n \) 非常高，該市場便可被視為寡頭壟斷。

Where \( CR_n \) represents the concentration ratio of the \( n \) largest firms, and \( S_i \) is the market share of the \( i \)-th firm. If the \( CR_n \) for a small number of firms (e.g., \( n=4 \)) is very high, the market can be regarded as an oligopoly.

3. 答案與詳細分析 (Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): B

❌ 選項 A 分析： 市場訊息不完全 (Market information is not perfect)。
雖然寡頭壟斷市場的確存在不完全訊息，但這並非其獨有特徵。壟斷性競爭 (Monopolistic Competition) 和壟斷 (Monopoly) 市場同樣面臨訊息不完全的情況。因此，這不能作為定義寡頭壟斷的決定性條件。

Analysis for Option A: While it is true that market information is imperfect in an oligopoly, this is not a unique characteristic. Both monopolistic competition and monopoly markets also face imperfect information. Therefore, it cannot serve as the defining condition for an oligopoly.

✅ 選項 B 分析： 巿場被少數賣家支配 (The market is dominated by a few sellers)。
這是寡頭壟斷的最核心定義。「少數支配」導致了寡頭壟斷市場中獨有的「相互牽制」現象。只要市場份額高度集中在少數幾個賣家手中，該市場就是寡頭壟斷。

Analysis for Option B: This is the core definition of an oligopoly. "Domination by a few" leads to the unique phenomenon of "interdependence" in an oligopoly market. As long as market share is highly concentrated in the hands of a few sellers, the market is an oligopoly.

❌ 選項 C 分析： 政府限制賣家進入市場 (The government restricts entry)。
政府限制（如發牌制度、專利權）是形成「極高進入障礙 (High barriers to entry)」的其中一個原因。極高進入障礙可能導致寡頭壟斷，但也可能導致完全壟斷 (Monopoly)（如果只發牌給單一公司）。因此，這只是成因，而非結構定義。

Analysis for Option C: Government restrictions (like licensing or patents) are one of the reasons for "high barriers to entry." High barriers can lead to an oligopoly, but they can also lead to a Monopoly (if a license is only granted to a single firm). Thus, this is a cause, not a structural definition.

❌ 選項 D 分析： 不同賣家出售相似但可區別的產品 (Similar but differentiated products)。
這是壟斷性競爭 (Monopolistic Competition) 的定義特徵。寡頭壟斷的賣家可以出售同質產品 (Homogeneous products，如鋼鐵、水泥) 或異質產品 (Differentiated products，如智能手機、汽車)。

Analysis for Option D: This is the defining characteristic of Monopolistic Competition. Sellers in an oligopoly can sell either homogeneous products (like steel and cement) or differentiated products (like smartphones and cars).

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

應對這類「定義型」題目，關鍵在於尋找樞紐字眼 (Pivot Point)。題目問的是「被視為 (regarded as)」，這要求我們找出該市場結構的獨有且必要的特徵。
很多同學會被選項 C 誤導，因為寡頭壟斷確實有進入障礙，但進入障礙並非定義該市場的「唯一指標」（壟斷也有進入障礙）。只有「少數支配 (Dominated by a few)」是將寡頭與其他市場完全區分的金標準。

For this type of "definition-based" question, the key is to find the Pivot Point. The question asks what a market can be "regarded as", which requires us to find the unique and necessary characteristic of the market structure.
Many students get distracted by Option C because oligopolies do have barriers to entry. However, entry barriers are not the "sole indicator" that defines the market (monopolies have them too). Only "dominated by a few" is the gold standard that completely separates oligopoly from other market structures.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • 混淆「特徵」與「定義」： 訊息不完全和進入障礙都是寡頭壟斷的特徵，但它們並不具有排他性（其他市場也有）。考試時必須選擇最具代表性的核心定義。

    Confusing "characteristics" with "definition": Imperfect information and entry barriers are characteristics of an oligopoly, but they are not mutually exclusive (other markets have them too). In exams, you must choose the most representative core definition.

  • 誤以為寡頭壟斷必須出售異質產品： 受到日常生活中汽車、手機等品牌的影響，同學常以為寡頭一定有產品差異（選項 D）。事實上，寡頭也可以出售完全相同的產品（如石油卡特爾 OPEC）。

    Mistakenly thinking oligopolies must sell differentiated products: Influenced by everyday examples like car and smartphone brands, students often think oligopolies must have product differentiation (Option D). In reality, oligopolies can also sell completely identical products (like the oil cartel OPEC).

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

🙋‍♂️ 學生問：為什麼政府限制賣家進入（選項 C）不能選？如果政府只發三個牌照，那不就是寡頭壟斷嗎？

👨‍🏫 導師答：你說得對，政府發放三個牌照會「導致」寡頭壟斷。但是，選項 C 只是說「政府限制進入」，並沒有說明限制到什麼程度。如果政府發放了一百個牌照，那是壟斷性競爭；如果只發放一個，那就是完全壟斷。因此，單憑「政府限制」這一點，不足以直接將市場「定義」為寡頭壟斷。

🙋‍♂️ Student: Why can't we choose government restriction (Option C)? If the government only issues three licenses, isn't that an oligopoly?

👨‍🏫 Tutor: You are right that issuing exactly three licenses "results in" an oligopoly. However, Option C merely states "government restricts entry" without specifying the extent of the restriction. If the government issues 100 licenses, it might be monopolistic competition; if it issues only one, it's a monopoly. Therefore, "government restriction" alone is not sufficient to directly "define" the market as an oligopoly.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

🔑 關鍵字配對法 (Keyword Matching Method)：

在 HKDSE 經濟科的選擇題中，看到特定的形容詞就可以快速鎖定市場結構：

  • 看到 "Many + Identical" (極多 + 同質) ➔ 完美競爭 (Perfect Competition)
  • 看到 "Many + Differentiated" (多數 + 異質) ➔ 壟斷性競爭 (Monopolistic Competition)
  • 看到 "Few / Interdependence" (少數 / 互相牽制) ➔ 寡頭壟斷 (Oligopoly)
  • 看到 "One / Single" (單一 / 唯一) ➔ 完全壟斷 (Monopoly)

In HKDSE Economics MCQs, seeing specific adjectives allows you to quickly lock onto the market structure. "Few / Interdependence" immediately points to Oligopoly.
