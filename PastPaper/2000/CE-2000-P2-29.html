HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic (課題): Money & Banking (貨幣與銀行)
Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐⭐☆☆ (3/5)
Key Concepts (核心概念): Credit Creation (信用創造), Money Supply (貨幣供應), Multiplier (乘數)

Question (題目)

銀行接受一筆從外國來的存款$1 000。在創造存款的過程完成後，該筆從海外來的匯款使整個銀行系統的存款額增加$4 000。 A bank accepts a deposit of $1 000 from a foreign country. Through the credit creation process, the amount of deposit of the whole banking system increases by $4 000.

根據以上資料，我們可得結論如下︰ Based on the above information, we can conclude that

  • A. 法定儲備率是25%。 the legal reserve ratio is 25%.
  • B. 銀行並不持有超額儲備。 banks do not keep excess reserves.
  • C. 總貨幣供應量最少增加了$4 000。 the total money supply has increased by at least $4 000.
  • D. 在存款創造的過程中，有現金流失的現象。 there is a cash leakage in the credit creation process.

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 銀行乘數 (Banking Multiplier)

銀行乘數反映了初始存款能創造出多少總存款。 The banking multiplier indicates how much total deposit can be created from an initial deposit.

$$ \text{Banking Multiplier} = \frac{\text{Total Change in Deposits}}{\text{Initial Deposit}} $$
$$ \text{Max Multiplier} = \frac{1}{\text{Required Reserve Ratio (RRR)}} $$

2. 貨幣供應定義 (Money Supply Definition)

貨幣供應量 (M1/M2) = 公眾持有的現金 (\(C_p\)) + 存款 (\(D\)) Money Supply (M1/M2) = Cash held by public (\(C_p\)) + Deposits (\(D\))

$$ \Delta M_s = \Delta C_p + \Delta D $$

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: C
Option A: Incorrect (不正確)

雖然 \(1000 \times 4 = 4000\) 吻合 25% 的法定儲備率（\(1/0.25 = 4\)），但這不是唯一的可能性。如果法定儲備率是 20%，而銀行持有 5% 的超額儲備，乘數同樣是 4。題目沒有說明銀行是否持有超額儲備，因此不能斷定法定儲備率必然是 25%。 Although \(1000 \times 4 = 4000\) fits a 25% legal reserve ratio (\(1/0.25 = 4\)), this is not the only possibility. If the legal reserve ratio is 20% and banks hold 5% excess reserves, the multiplier is also 4. The question does not state whether banks hold excess reserves, so we cannot conclude the legal reserve ratio must be 25%.

Option B: Incorrect (不正確)

我們無法得知銀行是否持有超額儲備。實際乘數是 4，這可能是由「法定儲備率 + 超額儲備率 + 現金流失」共同組成的結果。 We do not know if banks hold excess reserves. The actual multiplier is 4, which could be the combined result of "legal reserve ratio + excess reserve ratio + cash leakage".

Option C: Correct (正確)

分析： 貨幣供應改變 (\(\Delta M_s\)) = 公眾持有現金改變 (\(\Delta C_p\)) + 存款改變 (\(\Delta D\))。
已知 \(\Delta D = +4000\)。
由於初始存款來自「外國」（新資金），並非公眾將手上的現金存入，所以 \(\Delta C_p\) 不會是負數。
如果在過程中有現金流失，公眾持有的現金會增加 (\(\Delta C_p > 0\))，那麼 \(\Delta M_s > 4000\)。
如果沒有現金流失，公眾持有的現金不變 (\(\Delta C_p = 0\))，那麼 \(\Delta M_s = 4000\)。
結論：總貨幣供應量最少增加了 $4 000。 Analysis: Change in Money Supply (\(\Delta M_s\)) = Change in Cash held by public (\(\Delta C_p\)) + Change in Deposits (\(\Delta D\)).
We know \(\Delta D = +4000\).
Since the deposit came from "abroad" (new money), it wasn't cash deposited from the public's pockets, so \(\Delta C_p\) is not negative.
If there is cash leakage, public cash holdings increase (\(\Delta C_p > 0\)), so \(\Delta M_s > 4000\).
If there is no cash leakage, public cash holdings remain constant (\(\Delta C_p = 0\)), so \(\Delta M_s = 4000\).
Conclusion: The total money supply has increased by at least $4 000.

Option D: Incorrect (不正確)

我們無法確定是否有現金流失。如果法定儲備率剛好是 25% 且沒有超額儲備，那麼在沒有現金流失的情況下，存款也會增加 $4 000。 We cannot determine if there is cash leakage. If the legal reserve ratio is exactly 25% with no excess reserves, deposits would increase by $4 000 even without any cash leakage.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

關鍵點 (Pivot Point)： 區分「必然性」(Must be true) 與「可能性」(Could be true)。 Pivot Point: Distinguish between what "Must be true" and what "Could be true".

  • 實際乘數 (Actual Multiplier): \( 4000 / 1000 = 4 \)。這是一個確定的事實。
  • 陷阱 (Trap): 看到乘數是 4，學生很容易直接聯想到 \(1/0.25 = 4\)，從而誤選 A。但你要記住，實際乘數的分母包含所有「流失」的因素（法定儲備、超額儲備、現金流失）。
  • 解題邏輯 (Logic): 選項 C 運用了不等式邏輯（最少 / At least）。只要 \(\Delta C_p \ge 0\)，且 \(\Delta D = 4000\)，那麼 \(\Delta M_s \ge 4000\) 必定成立。

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼這筆錢來自「外國」這麼重要？ (Why is it important that the money comes from a "foreign country"?)

A: 如果這筆 $1 000 是你從家裡的夾萬拿去銀行存（本地現金），那麼公眾持有現金會先減少 $1 000 (\(\Delta C_p = -1000\))。雖然存款增加了，但貨幣供應的淨增長會少於 $4 000。
因為錢來自外國，這是一筆新注入的資金，所以初始的 \(\Delta C_p\) 不是負數。 A: If you took this $1 000 from your home safe to deposit (local cash), public cash holding would initially drop by $1 000 (\(\Delta C_p = -1000\)). Although deposits increase, the net increase in money supply would be less than $4 000.
Because the money is from abroad, it is a new injection, so the initial \(\Delta C_p\) is not negative.

Q: 如果題目說「銀行沒有超額儲備」，答案會變嗎？ (If the question said "banks hold no excess reserves", would the answer change?)

A: 如果題目說「沒有超額儲備」且「沒有現金流失」，那麼 A 才是對的。但在目前題目資訊不足的情況下，C 是唯一在任何假設下都安全的結論。 A: If the question stated "no excess reserves" AND "no cash leakage", then A would be correct. But with the limited information currently provided, C is the only conclusion that is safe under any assumption.
