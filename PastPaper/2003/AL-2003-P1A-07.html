Topic: Consumer Theory (Indifference Curves)

課題：消費者理論（等優曲線）

Difficulty: ★★★★☆ Key Concept: Quasi-linear Preferences Income Effect
The Question 題目

Condition / 條件:

假若兩件物品之間的等優曲線是垂直平行的（即是說，這些等優曲線沿任何一條垂直線都有相同的斜率），
If indifference curves are vertically parallel (i.e., they have the same slope along any vertical straight line) in a two-good example,

  • A. 甲物品的需求曲線，在金錢收入維持不變時與在實質收入維持不變時，都是一樣的。 The demand curve for Good A is the same whether we hold money income constant or real income constant.
  • B. 甲物品的收入效應為正數。 The income effect of Good A is positive.
  • C. 甲物品為貧窮物品（又稱劣等物品）。 Good A is an inferior good.
  • D. 以上均不正確。 None of the above.
Concept Review 概念重溫

1. 邊際替代率 (MRS) 與 斜率

等優曲線 (Indifference Curve, IC) 的斜率代表邊際替代率 (\(MRS_{AB}\))。題目指出 IC 是「垂直平行」的，這意味著對於任何固定的甲物品數量 \(x\)，無論乙物品數量 \(y\) 是多少，IC 的斜率都相同。
The slope of the Indifference Curve (IC) represents the Marginal Rate of Substitution (\(MRS_{AB}\)). "Vertically parallel" means that for any fixed quantity of Good A (\(x\)), the slope is constant regardless of the quantity of Good B (\(y\)).

2. 均衡條件 (Equilibrium Condition)

消費者均衡發生在 IC 斜率等於預算線 (Budget Line) 斜率時： $$ MRS_{AB} = \frac{P_A}{P_B} $$ Consumer equilibrium occurs where the slope of the IC equals the slope of the budget line.

3. 收入效應 (Income Effect)

當相對價格不變（預算線斜率不變），但收入增加時，預算線會平行向外移動。如果新的均衡點保持在相同的 \(x\) 軸位置（甲物品數量不變），則甲物品的收入效應為零。
When relative prices are constant but income increases, the budget line shifts outward in parallel. If the new equilibrium stays at the same vertical x-coordinate (quantity of A unchanged), the Income Effect for Good A is zero.

Answer & Detailed Analysis 答案與詳細分析
Correct Answer: Option A (正確答案：A)

Step 1: 理解幾何意義 (Understanding the Geometry)

題目指出「沿任何一條垂直線都有相同的斜率」。這意味著邊際替代率（MRS）只取決於甲物品的數量（X軸），而與乙物品的數量（Y軸）無關。
The question states "slope is the same along any vertical straight line". This implies that the MRS depends only on the quantity of Good A (X-axis), not Good B (Y-axis).

Step 2: 分析收入變動的影響 (Analyzing Income Change)

假設收入增加，但價格不變。預算線會平行向外移動（斜率 \(P_A/P_B\) 保持不變）。
因為消費者會在 \(MRS = P_A/P_B\) 處最大化效用，而 \(MRS\) 只受甲物品數量影響，所以消費者會選擇相同的甲物品數量來維持 \(MRS\) 等於價格比率。
結論：收入改變，甲物品的需求量不變。即收入效應 = 0。
Suppose income increases with prices constant. The budget line shifts out parallel (slope \(P_A/P_B\) constant). Since the consumer maximizes utility where \(MRS = P_A/P_B\), and MRS only depends on the quantity of A, the consumer chooses the SAME quantity of A to keep MRS equal to the price ratio. Conclusion: Income changes, quantity of A stays constant. Income Effect = 0.

Step 3: 檢視選項 (Evaluating Options)

Option A (Correct):

斯盧茨基方程 (Slutsky Equation) 指出：總效應 = 替代效應 + 收入效應。
「金錢收入不變的需求曲線」是指普通需求曲線 (Marshallian Demand)，包含收入效應。
「實質收入不變的需求曲線」是指補償需求曲線 (Hicksian Demand)，只包含替代效應。
由於收入效應為零，總效應等於替代效應。因此，兩條需求曲線是重疊（一樣）的。
Slutsky Equation: Total Effect = Substitution Effect + Income Effect. "Money income constant" refers to Marshallian Demand (includes IE). "Real income constant" refers to Hicksian Demand (SE only). Since Income Effect is zero, Total Effect = Substitution Effect. Thus, the two demand curves are identical.

Option B (Incorrect):

如上所述，收入效應是零，不是正數。
As derived, the income effect is zero, not positive.

Option C (Incorrect):

貧窮物品（Inferior good）意味著收入效應是負數。這裡是零，這被稱為「收入中性物品」(Income neutral good)。
An inferior good implies a negative income effect. Here it is zero (Income neutral).

Problem Decomposition 題目拆解

這類題目是關於準線性效用函數 (Quasi-linear Utility Function) 的圖形化表達。
This question is a graphical representation of a Quasi-linear Utility Function.

$$ U(A, B) = v(A) + B $$

關鍵點 (Pivot Point):
看到「垂直平行」(Vertically Parallel) \(\rightarrow\) 聯想到「X 軸物品的邊際替代率只與 X 有關」 \(\rightarrow\) 聯想到「收入效應為零」(Zero Income Effect)。
See "Vertically Parallel" \(\rightarrow\) Think "MRS depends only on X" \(\rightarrow\) Think "Zero Income Effect".

Exam Tricks 考試技巧
  • 畫圖法 (Draw it out):
    試著在圖上畫一條平行向外移動的預算線（代表收入增加）。要在哪裡切到更高的等優曲線？因為垂直方向斜率一樣，切點必須在垂直上方。這直接告訴你 X 的數量沒變 \(\rightarrow\) 收入效應 = 0。
    Draw a budget line shifting outward parallel (income increase). Where is the tangency? Since slopes are identical vertically, the tangency must be directly above. This tells you quantity of X is unchanged \(\rightarrow\) IE = 0.
  • 記憶口訣 (Memory Aid):
    IC 垂直平行 \(\rightarrow\) X 的收入效應 = 0。
    IC 水平平行 \(\rightarrow\) Y 的收入效應 = 0。
    IC Vertically Parallel \(\rightarrow\) IE of X = 0.
    IC Horizontally Parallel \(\rightarrow\) IE of Y = 0.
