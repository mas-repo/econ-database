Topic: National Income & Labour Market
課題：國民收入與勞工市場
Type: Structured Question
題型：結構式問答
Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts: GDP vs GNP, Wage Systems, Labour Mobility

The Question (題目)

假設香港某球會聘請了一位巴西著名足球員擔任為期兩個月的兒童足球訓練班教練，他收取了八百萬元的薪金，其後返回巴西。不過，足球班的收生欠佳，學費收入只有三百萬元。

Suppose a football club in Hong Kong hired a famous football player from Brazil as the coach for a 2-month course for kids. He received $8 million as his salary and returned to Brazil afterwards. However, the enrollment of the course was poor and the revenue from the course was $3 million only.
(a) 舉辦上述足球班對下列各項有什麼影響？試加以解釋。
(i) 香港的本地生產總值 (GDP) (2分)
(ii) 香港的本地居民生產總值 (GNP) (3分) (a) What is the effect of organising the above course on each of the following items? Explain.
(i) Hong Kong's gross domestic product (GDP) (2 marks)
(ii) Hong Kong's gross national product (GNP) (3 marks)
(b) 下列是兩位職業足球員的合約。
合約甲 (Contract A) 合約乙 (Contract B)
球員甲 (Player A):
每週$10 000
$10 000 per week		 球員乙 (Player B):
每場比賽$8 000 + 每進一球$10 000
$8 000 per match + $10 000 for each goal scored
從僱主的角度，解釋合約乙較合約甲兩項優勝之處。(4分) Explain TWO advantages of Contract B over Contract A from the viewpoint of the employer. (4 marks)
(c) 職業足球員通常在三十多歲退休。雖然他們大多在當球員的時候薪酬可觀，但有些在退休後只能找到低薪的工作。以勞工流動性為依據，解釋上述現象。(3分) Professional football players often retire in their mid-thirties. Although most of them have once been well-paid as footballers, some of them can only find low-paid jobs after retirement. Explain the above phenomenon in terms of labour mobility. (3 marks)

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. GDP vs. GNP (本地生產總值 vs 本地居民生產總值)

  • GDP (本地生產總值): 一個國家或地區的常住生產單位 (Resident Producing Units) 在一段特定時間內，生產的最終產品和服務的市場總值。關鍵在於「地域」(Location)。
    GDP: The total market value of all final goods and services produced by resident producing units within a country or territory in a specified period. Key focus: Location/Boundary.
  • GNP (本地居民生產總值): 一個國家或地區的本地居民 (Residents) 在一段特定時間內，從事生產活動所賺取的總收入。關鍵在於「身分」(Identity)。
    GNP: The total income earned by residents of a country or territory from engaging in production activities in a specified period. Key focus: Identity.
$$ \text{GNP} = \text{GDP} + \text{Net Factor Income from Abroad (NFIA)} $$ $$ \text{GNP} = \text{GDP} + (\text{Factor income inflow} - \text{Factor income outflow}) $$

2. Wage Systems (工資制度)

  • Time Rate (計時制): Pay based on time worked (e.g., Contract A). Low risk for employee, high monitoring cost for employer.
  • Piece Rate (計件制): Pay based on output (e.g., Contract B). High incentive, risk sharing (employer pays less if output is low).

3. Occupational Mobility (職業流動性)

指勞工從一種職業轉到另一種職業的容易程度。主要受技能的可轉移性 (Transferability of skills) 影響。
Refers to the ease with which workers can move from one occupation to another. Mainly affected by the transferability of skills.

Suggested Answer & Detailed Analysis (建議答案與詳細分析)

(a)(i) Effect on GDP (對 GDP 的影響)

Answer: GDP increases by $3 million.
答案：本地生產總值增加三百萬元。
Explanation: 該足球班是由香港的常住生產單位（球會）在香港舉辦（生產）。其生產的服務的市場價值為三百萬元（學費收入）。
解釋： The course was organised (produced) by a resident producing unit (the club) in Hong Kong. The market value of the service produced is $3 million (revenue from tuition).

(a)(ii) Effect on GNP (對 GNP 的影響)

Answer: GNP decreases by $5 million.
答案：本地居民生產總值減少五百萬元。
Explanation:
  • GNP = GDP + 對外要素收益流動淨值 (NFIA)。
  • 巴西教練為非本地居民，其薪金 ($8M) 屬於要素收益流出。
  • 計算：\( \text{GDP變動} (+3M) - \text{要素收益流出} (8M) = -5M \)。
  • GNP = GDP + Net factor income from abroad (NFIA).
  • The Brazilian coach is a non-resident; his salary ($8M) is factor income outflow.
  • Calculation: \( \Delta \text{GDP} (+3M) - \text{Factor Outflow} (8M) = -5M \).

(b) Advantages of Contract B (合約乙的優點)

1. 加強工作誘因 / 提高生產力 (Stronger Incentive / Higher Productivity):
合約乙將薪酬與表現掛鈎（進球獎金），球員會更努力比賽以賺取更高收入，從而提高球隊表現。
Contract B links pay to performance (goal bonus). The player will work harder to earn more, thereby increasing productivity/performance.
2. 風險分擔 / 減低監管成本 (Risk Sharing / Lower Monitoring Cost):
若球員表現欠佳（如沒有進球），僱主支付的薪金較低。僱主與僱員分擔了表現不佳的風險。此外，僱主不需要花費太多資源去監督球員是否盡力，因為球員會自動盡力。
If the player performs poorly (no goals), the employer pays less. The risk of poor performance is shared. Also, monitoring cost is lower as the player is self-motivated to perform.

(c) Labour Mobility Explanation (勞工流動性解釋)

Answer: 職業流動性低 (Low occupational mobility)。
答案： Low occupational mobility.
Explanation: 足球員擁有的技能（如踢球技術）具高度專用性 (highly specific)，難以轉移 (difficult to transfer) 到其他行業。因此，當他們退休離開足球行業後，在其他行業的生產力較低，只能賺取較低工資。
解釋： The skills possessed by football players are highly specific and difficult to transfer to other industries. Therefore, when they retire from football, their productivity in other sectors is low, leading to lower wages.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Part (a) - The Trap of Profit vs. Production:

很多學生看到「虧損」(收入300萬 - 成本800萬 = 虧損500萬)，就以為 GDP 會減少。
關鍵概念： GDP 衡量的是「生產價值」(Value of Production)，而不是「利潤」(Profit)。球會確實生產了價值 300 萬元的服務。至於那 800 萬元的成本，是收入分配的問題 (Distribution of Income)，在 GDP 層面，這是服務的進口 (Import of Service) 或是要素收益流出，視乎教練身分。

Many students see the "loss" ($3M revenue - $8M cost) and think GDP decreases.
Key Concept: GDP measures Value of Production, not Profit. The club produced services worth $3M. The $8M cost relates to income distribution.

Part (b) - Employer's Perspective:

題目指定由「僱主角度」(Employer's viewpoint) 出發。千萬不要寫「多勞多得」(這是對員工的好處)。要寫「提升生產力」或「節省監管成本」。

Common Mistakes (常犯錯誤)

  • GDP 計算錯誤： 誤以為 GDP = $3M - $8M = -$5M。
    糾正： 生產面看，產值是 $3M。那 $8M 是給非本地居民的，不屬於本地居民收入，但在支出面看，若視為服務輸入，淨出口會減少。但在 DSE 簡單邏輯下，通常指該生產單位的產值 ($3M)。
  • GNP 定義不清： 忘記扣除流出的 $8M。
  • 流動性混淆： 混淆「職業流動性」(Occupational Mobility) 與「地域流動性」(Geographical Mobility)。題目是指退休後轉行，所以是職業流動性。

Exam Tricks (考試技巧)

  • Keywords (關鍵字):
    • GNP: 常住居民 (Residents), 要素收益流動 (Factor income flow)
    • Wage System: 監管成本 (Monitoring cost), 風險分擔 (Risk sharing)
    • Mobility: 技能轉移 (Transferability of skills), 專用性 (Specificity)
  • Calculation Layout: 在回答 (a)(ii) 時，列出算式 \( 3 - 8 = -5 \) 能幫助閱卷員清楚看到你的邏輯，即使文字解釋稍弱也能得分。
