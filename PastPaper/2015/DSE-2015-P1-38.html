HKDSE Economics Tutor Analysis

Topic / 課題 總需求與總供應 (AD-AS Model) & 宏觀經濟政策 (Macroeconomic Policies)

Difficulty / 難度 ★★☆☆☆ (2/5 Stars)

Key Concepts / 關鍵概念 通縮差距 (Deflationary Gap), 財政政策 (Fiscal Policy), 貨幣政策 (Monetary Policy), 總需求 (Aggregate Demand)

The Question (題目)

某經濟原初的運作處於它的長期均衡。下列哪項會令該經濟出現通縮(產出)差距？
Initially an economy operates at the long-run equilibrium. Which of the following will lead to a deflationary (output) gap in the economy?
  • A. 政府提高薪俸稅率
    A. The government increases the salaries tax rate.
  • B. 政府減低利得稅率
    B. The government reduces the profits tax rate.
  • C. 政府投資興建跨境高鐵延線
    C. The government invests in the construction of a cross-border express rail link.
  • D. 中央銀行降低法定儲備比率
    D. The central bank reduces the required reserve ratio.

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 長期均衡 (Long-run Equilibrium):
當經濟處於長期均衡時，短期的實質產出 \(Y\) 等於充分就業產出（或潛在產出） \(Y_f\)。此時沒有產出差距。
1. Long-run Equilibrium:
When an economy is at long-run equilibrium, the short-run real output \(Y\) equals the full-employment output (or potential output) \(Y_f\). There is no output gap.
2. 通縮（產出）差距 (Deflationary Output Gap):
當短期均衡產出低於充分就業產出時（即 \(Y < Y_f\)），就會出現通縮差距。這通常是由於總需求 (AD) 減少（左移）或短期總供應 (SRAS) 減少（左移）所引起。
2. Deflationary Output Gap:
A deflationary gap occurs when the short-run equilibrium output is less than the full-employment output (i.e., \(Y < Y_f\)). This is typically caused by a decrease in Aggregate Demand (AD) (leftward shift) or a decrease in Short-Run Aggregate Supply (SRAS) (leftward shift).
$$ \text{Deflationary Gap} \implies Y < Y_f $$ $$ AD = C + I + G + (X - M) $$
3. 宏觀經濟政策 (Macroeconomic Policies):
緊縮性政策（如加稅、減少政府開支、減少貨幣供應）會壓抑總需求，導致 AD 曲線向左移，從而產生通縮差距。擴張性政策則相反。
3. Macroeconomic Policies:
Contractionary policies (e.g., increasing taxes, decreasing government spending, decreasing money supply) depress aggregate demand, shifting the AD curve to the left, which creates a deflationary gap. Expansionary policies do the opposite.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A

✓ 選項 A (正確): 政府提高薪俸稅率（緊縮性財政政策）。這會減少市民的可支配收入 (Disposable Income)。可支配收入下降會導致私人消費開支 \(C\) 減少。由於 \(C\) 是總需求 \(AD\) 的一部分，\(AD\) 會減少（向左移）。在 AD-AS 模型中，從長期均衡開始，AD 左移會導致短期均衡產出 \(Y\) 下降至低於 \(Y_f\)，形成通縮差距。
✓ Option A (Correct): The government increases the salaries tax rate (Contractionary Fiscal Policy). This reduces citizens' disposable income. A fall in disposable income leads to a decrease in private consumption expenditure \(C\). Since \(C\) is a component of Aggregate Demand \(AD\), \(AD\) decreases (shifts left). Starting from long-run equilibrium in the AD-AS model, a leftward shift in AD causes the short-run equilibrium output \(Y\) to fall below \(Y_f\), creating a deflationary gap.
✗ 選項 B (錯誤): 政府減低利得稅率會增加企業的除稅後利潤（或預期回報），從而刺激投資開支 \(I\)。這會令 \(AD\) 增加（向右移），導致產出高於充分就業水平，形成通脹差距 (Inflationary Gap)。
✗ Option B (Incorrect): Reducing the profits tax rate increases firms' after-tax profits (or expected returns), which stimulates investment expenditure \(I\). This increases \(AD\) (shifts right), leading to an output level higher than the full-employment level, creating an inflationary gap.
✗ 選項 C (錯誤): 政府投資基建會直接增加政府開支 \(G\)。這會令 \(AD\) 增加（向右移），同樣會產生通脹差距。
✗ Option C (Incorrect): Government investment in infrastructure directly increases government spending \(G\). This increases \(AD\) (shifts right), which also creates an inflationary gap.
✗ 選項 D (錯誤): 中央銀行降低法定儲備比率（擴張性貨幣政策）會增加貨幣供應，導致利率下降。較低的利率會刺激消費 \(C\) 和投資 \(I\)，令 \(AD\) 增加（向右移），產生通脹差距。
✗ Option D (Incorrect): The central bank reducing the required reserve ratio (Expansionary Monetary Policy) increases the money supply, leading to a lower interest rate. A lower interest rate stimulates consumption \(C\) and investment \(I\), increasing \(AD\) (shifts right) and creating an inflationary gap.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point):
題目的核心在於「通縮差距」(Deflationary gap)。你需要立即聯想到：通縮差距 = 產出下跌 = 總需求 (AD) 或 短期總供應 (SRAS) 減少。因此，你只需要在選項中尋找哪一項政策會導致 AD 或 SRAS 減少。
Pivot Point:
The core of the question is "deflationary gap". You must immediately recall: Deflationary gap = Fall in output = Decrease in Aggregate Demand (AD) or Short-Run Aggregate Supply (SRAS). Therefore, you simply need to find which option causes a decrease in AD or SRAS.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱一：混淆「通縮差距」與「通縮」
學生常以為「通縮差距」單純指物價下跌。雖然 AD 左移通常伴隨物價水平下降（通縮），但「通縮差距」的嚴格定義是產出 \(Y\) 低於潛在產出 \(Y_f\)。
Trap 1: Confusing "Deflationary Gap" with "Deflation"
Students often think a "deflationary gap" simply means a fall in the price level. While a leftward shift in AD is usually accompanied by a falling price level (deflation), the strict definition of a deflationary gap is that output \(Y\) is below potential output \(Y_f\).
陷阱二：錯判政策方向
有時學生會直覺認為「建高鐵是好事，所以不會導致通縮」。在經濟學中，我們只看它對 \(C, I, G, NX\) 的影響。建高鐵增加 \(G\)，屬擴張性政策，會帶來通脹差距而非通縮差距。
Trap 2: Misjudging Policy Direction
Sometimes students intuitively think "building railways is good, so it won't cause deflation". In economics, we only look at its impact on \(C, I, G, NX\). Building railways increases \(G\), which is an expansionary policy and brings an inflationary gap, not a deflationary gap.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果題目問的是「通脹差距」(Inflationary gap)，答案會是什麼？
Q: What if the question asked for an "inflationary gap"?
A: 如果題目改為通脹差距，那麼選項 B、C 和 D 都會是正確的，因為它們都是擴張性政策（增加 AD）。這也是為什麼在單選題中，它們必定是錯誤選項（見下方考試技巧）。
A: If the question asked for an inflationary gap, then options B, C, and D would all be correct because they are all expansionary policies (increase AD). This is also why they must be incorrect in a single-choice question (see Exam Tricks below).

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

「找不同」排除法 (The "Odd One Out" Method):
在多項選擇題中，如果四個選項中有三個屬於同一類型的政策，剩下的一個通常就是答案。
  • B: 減稅 (擴張性 Fiscal)
  • C: 增加政府開支 (擴張性 Fiscal)
  • D: 降低儲備比率 (擴張性 Monetary)
B、C、D 全部都會刺激經濟（增加 AD）。唯獨 A 是加稅（緊縮性 Fiscal，減少 AD）。即使你忘記了「通縮差距」的定義，憑邏輯也能推斷出 A 是唯一與眾不同的選項，極大機率是正確答案！
The "Odd One Out" Method:
In MCQs, if three out of four options belong to the same category of policy, the remaining one is usually the answer.
  • B: Tax cut (Expansionary Fiscal)
  • C: Increase Gov spending (Expansionary Fiscal)
  • D: Lower reserve ratio (Expansionary Monetary)
B, C, and D all stimulate the economy (increase AD). Only A is a tax hike (Contractionary Fiscal, decreases AD). Even if you forgot the definition of a deflationary gap, logic dictates that A is the only odd one out, making it highly likely to be the correct answer!
