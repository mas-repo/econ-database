This involves cross-market effects.

Pivot Point: Supply Shift vs. Demand Shift
The core is to distinguish whether the event affects Supply or Demand.
- Supply Shift: P and Q move in opposite directions. You MUST know Elasticity to determine TR. If elasticity isn't given, this is usually not the answer.
- Demand Shift: P and Q move in the same direction. You DO NOT need elasticity; TR moves in the same direction as P/Q.
