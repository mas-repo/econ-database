1. The Question 題目

下表顯示雪糕的價格和顧客對雪糕的總支出的關係。
The table below shows the relationship between the price of and the total expenditure on ice-cream.
單位價格 Unit price ($) 5 6 7 8
總支出 Total expenditure ($) 25 30 35 40
我們可從上表得出結論：雪糕的需求彈性是___________（不理會其負號）。
From the above table, we can conclude that the elasticity of demand for ice-cream is ________ (without regard to its negative sign).
  • A. 在零和一之間 / between zero and one
  • B. 等於零 / equal to zero
  • C. 大於一 / larger than one
  • D. 等於一 / equal to one

2. Concept Review 概念重溫

總支出 (Total Expenditure, TE)：消費者購買某數量的物品所付出的總金額。公式為價格 $P$ 乘以需求量 $Q_d$。
Total Expenditure (TE): The total amount of money consumers spend on a certain quantity of a good. It is calculated by multiplying the Price $P$ by the Quantity Demanded $Q_d$.
$$TE = P \times Q_d$$
透過重組公式，我們可以從總支出和價格中找出需求量：
By rearranging the formula, we can find the quantity demanded from total expenditure and price:
$$Q_d = \frac{TE}{P}$$
需求價格彈性 (Price Elasticity of Demand, $E_d$)：量度需求量對價格變動的敏感度。
Price Elasticity of Demand ($E_d$): Measures the responsiveness of quantity demanded to a change in price.
$$E_d = \frac{\% \Delta Q_d}{\% \Delta P}$$
完全無彈性需求 (Perfectly Inelastic Demand)：當價格改變時，需求量完全沒有變動。在這種情況下，$E_d = 0$。
Perfectly Inelastic Demand: When the price changes, the quantity demanded does not change at all. In this case, $E_d = 0$.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis 答案與詳細分析

正確答案 Correct Option: B

Step-by-Step Analysis
我們需要利用題目提供的數據計算出每一個價格水平下的需求量 ($Q$)。
We need to calculate the quantity demanded ($Q$) for each price level using the provided data.
  • 當 $P = 5$, $Q = \frac{25}{5} = 5$
  • 當 $P = 6$, $Q = \frac{30}{6} = 5$
  • 當 $P = 7$, $Q = \frac{35}{7} = 5$
  • 當 $P = 8$, $Q = \frac{40}{8} = 5$
我們發現無論價格如何變動，需求量 ($Q$) 始終保持在 5 不變（即 $\Delta Q = 0$）。根據彈性公式，既然需求量變動百分比為零，需求彈性必定等於零。因此正確答案是 B。
We can see that no matter how the price changes, the quantity demanded ($Q$) remains strictly at 5 (i.e., $\Delta Q = 0$). According to the elasticity formula, since the percentage change in quantity demanded is zero, the elasticity of demand must be equal to zero. Thus, the correct option is B.
Why A is wrong
雖然當價格上升時，總支出也隨之上升，這通常是「低彈性 / 缺乏彈性」 ($E_d < 1$) 的特徵。但此題的數據顯示需求量是絕對不變的，這是一個極端情況（完全無彈性）。選項 B（等於零）比選項 A（在零和一之間）更準確地描述了這組數據。
Although total expenditure increases as price increases, which is generally a characteristic of "inelastic demand" ($E_d < 1$), the data here shows that quantity demanded is absolutely constant. This is an extreme case (perfectly inelastic). Option B (equal to zero) is a perfectly precise description of the data, making Option A incorrect.
Why C & D are wrong
若需求彈性大於一 ($E_d > 1$)，價格上升會導致總支出下降。若需求彈性等於一 ($E_d = 1$)，價格變動時總支出會保持不變。題目數據與這兩種情況都不符。
If elasticity is larger than one ($E_d > 1$), an increase in price would lead to a decrease in total expenditure. If elasticity is equal to one ($E_d = 1$), total expenditure would remain constant when price changes. The data contradicts both scenarios.

4. Problem Decomposition 題目拆解

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point)：
考評局出這類題目的用意，是測試學生會否只看表面趨勢（價格升、總支出升），而不去實際計算背後隱藏的需求量 ($Q$)。拆解此題的關鍵是：看到 $TE$ 和 $P$，立刻動筆找出 $Q$！
Pivot Point:
The HKEAA sets this type of question to test whether students merely look at surface trends (Price goes up, TE goes up) without calculating the hidden Quantity Demanded ($Q$). The key to solving this is: Whenever you see $TE$ and $P$, immediately calculate $Q$!

5. Common Mistakes & Traps 常犯錯誤與陷阱

陷阱：死記硬背趨勢關係
很多學生背誦了「價格與總支出同方向變動 = 缺乏彈性 (Inelastic)」，於是看到價格由 $5 升至 $8，總支出由 $25 升至 $40，就立刻選擇 A（在零和一之間）。這是一個陷阱！你必須確保它不是特例（即 $E_d = 0$）。
Trap: Memorizing trend relationships blindly
Many students memorize the rule "Price and Total Expenditure moving in the same direction = Inelastic", so when they see P rise from $5 to $8 and TE rise from $25 to $40, they immediately jump to Option A (between zero and one). This is a trap! You must always verify it's not the extreme case (i.e., $E_d = 0$).

6. Student Q&A 學生常見疑問

問：「完全無彈性」難道不屬於「缺乏彈性」的一種嗎？為甚麼不能選 A？
Q: Isn't "perfectly inelastic" a specific type of "inelastic"? Why can't I choose A?
答：在數學邏輯上，「在零和一之間」(Between 0 and 1) 的定義是 $0 < E_d < 1$，並不包含零。既然我們計算出 $E_d$ 精確地等於 $0$，選項 A 在數學定義上就是錯誤的。
A: In mathematical logic, "between zero and one" defines the range as $0 < E_d < 1$, which strictly excludes zero. Since our calculation shows $E_d$ is exactly $0$, Option A is mathematically incorrect.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts 考試技巧與速解

極速秒殺法 (The 5-Second Rule)：
在選擇題中，時間就是分數。不需要把全部數字算完！只需抽查頭兩組數據：
1. $\frac{25}{5} = 5$
2. $\frac{30}{6} = 5$
既然 $Q$ 是一樣的，證明它是垂直的需求曲線，立刻選 B (等於零) 並跳到下一題！
The 5-Second Rule:
In MC papers, time is marks. You don't need to calculate everything! Just sample the first two pairs of data:
1. $\frac{25}{5} = 5$
2. $\frac{30}{6} = 5$
Since $Q$ is identical, it proves a vertical demand curve. Pick Option B (equal to zero) instantly and move to the next question!
