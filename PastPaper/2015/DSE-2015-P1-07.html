HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic / 課題: Types of Business Ownership (企業擁有權類型)

Difficulty / 難度: ★★☆☆☆

Key Concepts / 關鍵概念: Private Limited Company (私人有限公司) Legal Entity (法人地位) Financing (融資)

1. The Question (題目)

某廠商有獨立的法人地位，但擁有人不得未經其他擁有人同意而轉讓其擁有權。當擁有人想擴展廠商的業務，他們可以_____________。

(1) 運用廠商的保留利潤
(2) 向公眾發行股票以籌集資金
(3) 向朋友借貸以籌集資金

A. 只有(1)、(2)
B. 只有(1)、(3)
C. 只有(2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2)及(3)

A firm has an independent legal entity but the owners cannot transfer their ownership without the consent of the other owners. When the owners want to expand the firm’s business, they can ___________.

(1) use the retained profit of the firm
(2) issue shares to the public to raise capital
(3) borrow from friends to raise capital

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

私人有限公司 (Private Limited Company)

這條問題測試你對企業特徵的認識。根據題目的描述，「獨立的法人地位」排除了獨資和合夥企業；而「未經同意不得轉讓擁有權（股份）」則確認了這是一間私人有限公司。

This question tests your knowledge of the characteristics of business ownership. Based on the description, "independent legal entity" rules out sole proprietorships and partnerships; "cannot transfer ownership (shares) without consent" confirms that this is a Private Limited Company.

保留利潤 (Retained Profit)

企業在支付稅款和股息後，保留在公司內部的盈餘，可用作未來投資或擴張。計算公式如下：

The surplus kept within the company after paying taxes and dividends, used for future investment or expansion. The formula is:

$$Retained\ Profit = Net\ Income - Dividends\ Paid$$ $$(保留利潤 = 淨利潤 - 已派發股息)$$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): B. 只有(1)、(3) / (1) and (3) only

✔ (1) 運用廠商的保留利潤

任何有盈利的企業（包括私人有限公司）都可以使用其過去累積的保留利潤作為內部融資來源，以擴展業務。

Any profitable business (including private limited companies) can use its accumulated retained profits as an internal source of financing to expand its business. This is correct.

✘ (2) 向公眾發行股票以籌集資金

這是公眾有限公司 (Public Limited Company) 的專利。私人有限公司在法律上被明確禁止邀請公眾認購其股份或債券。因此，這個選項是錯誤的。

This is a privilege exclusive to a Public Limited Company. Private limited companies are legally prohibited from inviting the general public to subscribe to their shares or debentures. Therefore, this option is incorrect.

✔ (3) 向朋友借貸以籌集資金

雖然公司有獨立法人地位，但這並不阻止公司的擁有人（股東）向他們的親友借取私人貸款，然後再將資金注資入公司，或者由公司直接向擁有人的親友借貸。

Although the company is a separate legal entity, this does not prevent the owners (shareholders) from taking out personal loans from friends and injecting the capital into the firm, or the firm itself borrowing directly from the owners' friends. This is correct.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解答這類題目的「破題點 (Pivot Point)」在於辨識企業類型：

  • 線索一：「獨立法人地位 (independent legal entity)」$\rightarrow$ 範圍縮小至有限公司 (Limited Companies)。
  • 線索二：「擁有人不得未經同意轉讓擁有權 (owners cannot transfer ownership without consent)」$\rightarrow$ 鎖定為私人有限公司 (Private Limited Company)。

The "Pivot Point" to solving this type of question is identifying the type of business:
- Clue 1: "Independent legal entity" $\rightarrow$ Narrows down to Limited Companies.
- Clue 2: "Owners cannot transfer ownership without consent" $\rightarrow$ Locks it in as a Private Limited Company.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

很多同學會因為「向朋友借貸」通常出現在獨資或合夥企業的題目中，就誤以為有限公司不能向朋友借貸。事實上，任何企業都可以向私人網絡借貸，只是銀行貸款或發行股票是有限公司更常見的集資方式。不要被刻板印象誤導！

Many students mistakenly believe that "borrowing from friends" is strictly for sole proprietorships or partnerships. In reality, any business entity can borrow from personal networks; it's just that bank loans or issuing shares are more *common* for limited companies. Don't be misled by stereotypes!

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 私人有限公司完全不可以發行股票嗎？

A: 不是的。私人有限公司可以發行股票，但只能向私人投資者（例如創辦人的親屬、朋友、現有員工或特定的風險投資者）發行，且股東人數最高限制為50人。它唯一不能做的是「向公眾 (to the public)」公開招股。

Q: Can a private limited company issue shares at all?

A: It can! A private limited company can issue shares, but only to private investors (like founders' relatives, friends, employees, or specific venture capitalists), and the maximum number of shareholders is capped at 50. The only thing it cannot do is issue shares "to the public".

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

🎯 關鍵字秒殺法 (Keyword Elimination Method)

當你從題目第一句確認了這是一間「私人 (Private)」公司後，馬上掃描選項尋找「公眾 (Public)」字眼。第(2)項的「向公眾 (to the public)」絕對互不相容，直接判定(2)為錯誤。在選項中，包含(2)的 A、C、D 可以瞬間被剔除，答案必定是 B！

Once you identify from the first sentence that this is a "Private" company, immediately scan the options for the word "Public". Option (2) explicitly states "to the public", which is mutually exclusive with a private company. Therefore, (2) is strictly false. You can instantly eliminate any options containing (2) – A, C, and D are gone. The answer must be B!
