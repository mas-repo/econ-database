HKDSE Economics Concept Guide

Topic (主題): 經濟體制 Economic Systems 市場經濟 Market Economy
Difficulty (難度): ★☆☆☆☆ (Easy / 簡單)
Key Concepts (核心概念): Private Property Rights (私有產權) Price Mechanism (價格機制)

2. The Question (題目)

下列哪項是市場經濟的一個特點？

A. 該經濟的收入分配不平均。
B. 政府在調控該經濟內的商業活動中沒有角色。
C. 該經濟的生產要素大多是私人擁有的。
D. 該經濟內只有價格競爭。

Which of the following is a characteristic of a market economy?

A. Income distribution is uneven in the economy.
B. The government has no role in regulating business activities in the economy.
C. Most factors of production in the economy are privately owned.
D. There is only price competition in the economy.

3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

市場經濟的定義與特徵 (Definition & Features of a Market Economy)

市場經濟（Market Economy）是一種依賴價格機制（Price Mechanism）來解答「生產什麼」、「如何生產」和「為誰生產」這三大基本經濟問題的經濟體系。

核心特徵 (Core Features):

  1. 私有產權 (Private Property Rights): 大部分生產要素（Factors of Production）由私人擁有。
  2. 追求自利 (Self-interest Motive): 生產者追求利潤極大化，消費者追求效用極大化。
    $$ \text{Profit} (\pi) = \text{Total Revenue} (TR) - \text{Total Cost} (TC) $$
  3. 有限度政府干預 (Limited Government Intervention): 政府扮演「守夜人」角色，主要負責維持法治和保障產權，而不是完全「零」干預。

A Market Economy is an economic system that relies on the price mechanism to answer the three basic economic questions: "what to produce", "how to produce", and "for whom to produce".

Core Features:

  1. Private Property Rights: Most factors of production are privately owned.
  2. Self-interest Motive: Producers aim to maximize profit, while consumers aim to maximize utility.
  3. Limited Government Intervention: The government plays a "night-watchman" role, mainly responsible for maintaining law and order and protecting property rights, rather than having absolutely "zero" intervention.

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Answer: Option C

C. 該經濟的生產要素大多是私人擁有的。

分析： 這是市場經濟最基本及核心的「定義特徵」。在市場經濟中，土地、勞力、資本等生產要素（Factors of Production）均由私人擁有，並由私人決定其用途。

C. Most factors of production in the economy are privately owned.

Analysis: This is the most fundamental and core "defining characteristic" of a market economy. In a market economy, factors of production like land, labor, and capital are privately owned, and private individuals decide how they are used.

Distractor: Option A

A. 該經濟的收入分配不平均。

分析： 收入分配不均是市場經濟下的一個「結果/現象 (Outcome/Phenomenon)」，而不是一個「特點/特徵 (Characteristic)」。此外，計劃經濟中也可能存在嚴重的收入不均（例如因為貪污或特權階級）。

A. Income distribution is uneven in the economy.

Analysis: Uneven income distribution is a "result/outcome" of a market economy, not a defining "characteristic". Furthermore, severe income inequality can also exist in planned economies (e.g., due to corruption or privileged classes).

Distractor: Option B

B. 政府在調控該經濟內的商業活動中沒有角色。

分析： 這句陳述過於極端。即使在自由市場經濟中，政府也絕對有其角色（例如：設立法律框架、執行合約、保障私有產權、防止壟斷等）。「有限度干預」不等於「沒有角色」。

B. The government has no role in regulating business activities in the economy.

Analysis: This statement is too extreme. Even in a free market economy, the government definitely has a role (e.g., establishing a legal framework, enforcing contracts, protecting private property rights, preventing monopolies). "Limited intervention" does not mean "no role".

Distractor: Option D

D. 該經濟內只有價格競爭。

分析： 又是極端字眼惹的禍。現實的市場經濟中，除了價格競爭外，企業還會進行大量的非價格競爭 (Non-price competition)，例如賣廣告、提升產品質量、提供售後服務等。

D. There is only price competition in the economy.

Analysis: Another error caused by an extreme word. In a real-world market economy, besides price competition, firms also engage heavily in non-price competition, such as advertising, improving product quality, and providing after-sales services.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point): 釐清「定義特徵 (Defining Features)」與「經濟結果 (Economic Outcomes)」。

本題考驗同學是否能分辨什麼是構成體制的「規則」（如私有產權），什麼是這些規則運作後產生的「現象」（如貧富懸殊）。

  • 無關資訊/干擾項 (Distractors): 選項A利用了學生對資本主義「貧富懸殊」的刻板印象來混淆視聽。
  • 關鍵資訊 (Crucial Info): 選項C直接擊中市場經濟的基石——沒有私有產權，價格機制就無法運作。

The Pivot Point: Distinguishing between "Defining Features" and "Economic Outcomes".

This question tests whether students can differentiate between the "rules" that constitute the system (like private property rights) and the "phenomena" generated after these rules operate (like wealth disparity).

  • Distractors: Option A uses students' stereotypes about capitalism causing "wealth disparity" to confuse them.
  • Crucial Info: Option C directly hits the cornerstone of a market economy—without private property rights, the price mechanism cannot function.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 陷阱一：混淆「結果」與「特徵」。 很多同學一看到「資本主義/市場經濟」就會聯想到「貧富懸殊」，從而誤選A。記住，特徵是系統的DNA，結果是系統運作後的產物。
  • 陷阱二：忽略極端字眼。 選項B的「沒有 (no)」和選項D的「只有 (only)」是經典的MC題陷阱。在社會科學（如經濟學）中，極少有絕對的「零」或「唯一」。
  • Trap 1: Confusing "Outcomes" with "Characteristics". Many students immediately associate "capitalism/market economy" with "wealth gap", leading them to mistakenly choose A. Remember, characteristics are the DNA of the system; outcomes are the byproducts of its operation.
  • Trap 2: Ignoring extreme words. "No" in Option B and "only" in Option D are classic MC question traps. In social sciences (like Economics), there are very few absolute "zeros" or "onlys".

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 老師，既然政府在市場經濟中有角色，那香港還算不算「自由市場」？
(Q: Teacher, since the government has a role in a market economy, does Hong Kong still count as a "free market"?)
A: 絕對算！世界上沒有完美的「純粹市場經濟 (Pure Market Economy)」，所有現實中的經濟體都是「混合經濟 (Mixed Economy)」。我們稱香港為自由市場，是因為香港的資源分配主要 (primarily) 是由價格機制決定的，而且政府干預相對其他國家來說非常少。但香港政府依然會提供警察、法庭、公共財貨（如街燈），這證明了政府必須存在。
A: Absolutely! There is no perfect "Pure Market Economy" in the world; all real-world economies are "Mixed Economies". We call Hong Kong a free market because its resource allocation is primarily determined by the price mechanism, and government intervention is relatively minimal compared to other countries. However, the HK government still provides police, courts, and public goods (like streetlights), proving that the government must exist.

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺法：極端字眼排除法 (The "Absolute Word" Elimination Method)

做DSE經濟科MC題時，一旦看到「沒有 (no / never)」、「只有 (only / solely)」、「必定 (must)」這類極端的字眼，該選項有 90% 的機率是錯誤的。

  • 看到 B 選項的「沒有角色 (no role)」 \(\rightarrow\) 刪除！
  • 看到 D 選項的「只有 (only)」 \(\rightarrow\) 刪除！
  • 剩下 A 和 C，再利用「特徵 vs 結果」的概念，輕鬆鎖定 C。

Shortcut: The "Absolute Word" Elimination Method

When doing DSE Economics MCs, if you see extreme words like "no / never", "only / solely", or "must", there is a 90% chance that the option is incorrect.

  • See "no role" in Option B \(\rightarrow\) Eliminate!
  • See "only" in Option D \(\rightarrow\) Eliminate!
  • Left with A and C, apply the "Characteristic vs. Outcome" concept to easily lock in C.
