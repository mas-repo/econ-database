HKDSE Economics Tutorial
經濟學試題解析

Topic (課題): Market Structure (市場結構)
Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐ (2/5)
Key Concepts (關鍵概念): Oligopoly (寡頭壟斷) Interdependence (互相牽制)

1. The Question (題目)

下列哪項有關香港主題公園行業的陳述是正確的？
Which of the following statements about the theme park industry in Hong Kong is correct?
  1. 香港迪士尼樂園是一壟斷者，因為當遊客增加，它的平均生產成本下降。
    Hong Kong Disneyland is a monopolist because its average cost of production drops when there are more tourists.
  2. 香港迪士尼樂園保證能獲得利潤，因為它受政府保護。
    Hong Kong Disneyland guarantees a profit because it is protected by the government.
  3. 香港迪士尼樂園和香港海洋公園之間沒有競爭，因為遊客可能兩個主題公園皆到訪。
    Hong Kong Disneyland and Ocean Park Hong Kong have no competition with each other as tourists may go to both theme parks.
  4. 香港迪士尼樂園和香港海洋公園的營銷策略是互相影響的。
    Hong Kong Disneyland and Ocean Park Hong Kong are interdependent in their marketing strategies.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

寡頭壟斷 (Oligopoly)
寡頭壟斷是指市場上只有少數幾個支配性的賣者（Dominant sellers）。由於競爭者數量少，任何一間公司的定價、產量或營銷策略（如推出新機動遊戲、門票優惠）都會直接影響對手的利潤。這種特徵稱為互相牽制 / 相互依存 (Interdependence)。
Oligopoly refers to a market structure dominated by a few large sellers. Because there are only a few competitors, any firm's pricing, output, or marketing strategies (e.g., launching new rides, ticket discounts) will directly affect its rivals' profits. This key characteristic is known as Interdependence.
利潤的定義 (Definition of Profit)
在經濟學中，利潤 \( \pi \) 取決於總收入 (Total Revenue, \(TR\)) 和總成本 (Total Cost, \(TC\)) 的差額。沒有任何市場結構或政府保護能「保證」利潤大於零。
In economics, profit \( \pi \) depends on the difference between Total Revenue (\(TR\)) and Total Cost (\(TC\)). No market structure or government protection can "guarantee" a profit greater than zero.
$$ \pi = TR - TC $$ $$ TR = P \times Q $$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option D

選項 A (Option A) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
香港的主題公園行業並非壟斷（Monopoly），因為市場上至少有兩個主要競爭者（迪士尼與海洋公園）。此外，「平均生產成本下降」是指規模經濟（Economies of scale），這並非定義壟斷的唯一條件。
The theme park industry in Hong Kong is not a monopoly because there are at least two major competitors (Disneyland and Ocean Park). Furthermore, "average cost dropping" refers to economies of scale, which is not the sole defining condition of a monopoly.
選項 B (Option B) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
即使有政府支持或保護，如果總成本 \(TC\) 高於總收入 \(TR\)，企業仍然會虧損。現實中，香港迪士尼樂園在某些年份亦錄得虧損，因此「保證獲利」的說法是錯誤的。
Even with government support or protection, if Total Cost \(TC\) is higher than Total Revenue \(TR\), a firm will still suffer losses. In reality, HK Disneyland has recorded losses in certain years, so the claim of "guaranteed profit" is false.
選項 C (Option C) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
即使遊客可能兩者皆去，但遊客的時間和預算（Budget）是有限的。兩大樂園仍然需要競爭遊客的消費（例如餐飲、紀念品及優先入場券），因此它們之間絕對存在競爭。
Even though tourists might visit both, their time and budget are limited. The two parks still compete for tourists' spending (e.g., dining, souvenirs, and priority passes). Therefore, competition definitely exists between them.
選項 D (Option D) - 正確 (Correct)
香港主題公園市場屬於寡頭壟斷（Oligopoly）。兩大樂園的市場份額龐大，當其中一方（如海洋公園）推出「哈囉喂」等大型活動時，另一方（迪士尼）的入場人數可能會受影響，因此它們的營銷策略是互相牽制（Interdependent）的。
The HK theme park market is an oligopoly. Both parks hold a large market share. When one (e.g., Ocean Park) launches a major event like "Halloween Fest", the other's (Disneyland's) attendance might be affected. Thus, their marketing strategies are strictly interdependent.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point):
辨識市場結構。只要你認出香港主題公園只有「少數幾個大賣家」（Few dominant sellers），就能立刻聯想到「寡頭壟斷」及其核心特徵「互相牽制」。
Identifying the market structure. As soon as you recognize that the HK theme park industry has only a "few dominant sellers", you should immediately link it to "Oligopoly" and its core feature "Interdependence".

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 混淆「規模經濟」與「自然壟斷」: 學生常以為平均成本下降（規模經濟）就等於壟斷。雖然自然壟斷（Natural Monopoly）具有長期平均成本不斷下降的特徵，但這不代表所有享有規模經濟的企業都是壟斷者。
    Confusing Economies of Scale with Natural Monopoly: Students often think falling average costs equal a monopoly. While a natural monopoly features continuously falling long-run average costs, it doesn't mean every firm enjoying economies of scale is a monopolist.
  • 忽略預算限制 (Budget Constraint): 以為「兩者皆去」就沒有競爭。忽視了消費者在財富 \(W\) 和時間上的稀少性（Scarcity）。
    Ignoring Budget Constraints: Thinking "visiting both" means zero competition, ignoring the scarcity of consumers' wealth \(W\) and time.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼這不是「壟斷性競爭」(Monopolistic Competition)？
A: 壟斷性競爭的特徵是「賣者眾多」(Many sellers) 且「進入市場門檻低」(Low barriers to entry)（例如茶餐廳、理髮店）。主題公園需要極龐大的資本和土地，進入門檻極高，且市場上只有兩間主導公司，因此屬於寡頭壟斷。
Q: Why isn't this Monopolistic Competition?
A: Monopolistic competition is characterized by "many sellers" and "low barriers to entry" (e.g., local cafes, hair salons). Theme parks require massive capital and land, meaning entry barriers are extremely high. With only two dominant firms, it is an oligopoly.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

絕對字眼排除法 (Elimination by Absolute Terms):
在經濟學 MC 題中，帶有「保證 (guarantees)」、「必定 (must)」、「完全沒有 (no competition at all)」等極端字眼的選項（如 B 和 C），通常都是錯誤的。利用這技巧可以快速刪去 B 和 C。
Elimination by Absolute Terms:
In Economics MCQs, options containing extreme words like "guarantees", "must", or "no competition at all" (like Options B and C) are usually incorrect. Using this trick helps you quickly eliminate B and C.
