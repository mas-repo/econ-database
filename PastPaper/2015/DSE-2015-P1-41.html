HKDSE Economics Tutorial (經濟學導修)

📚 Topic (課題): International Trade - Absolute & Comparative Advantage (國際貿易 - 絕對優勢與比較優勢)

⭐ Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆

🔑 Key Concepts (核心概念): Absolute Advantage (絕對優勢), Comparative Advantage (比較優勢), Direction of Trade (貿易方向)

📝 The Question (題目)

某國生產物品X擁有絕對優勢。下列哪項有關該國的陳述是正確的？

A country has an absolute advantage in producing good X. Which of the following statements about the country is correct?

A. 運用相同數量的資源，該國可生產的物品X數量較另一種物品多。
A. The country can produce a greater amount of good X than another good with the same amount of resources.

B. 該國生產物品X擁有比較優勢。
B. The country has a comparative advantage in producing good X.

C. 該國生產另一種物品有絕對劣勢。
C. The country has an absolute disadvantage in producing another good.

D. 該國可能從國際市場進口物品X。
D. The country may import good X from the international market.

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這條題目，我們必須清楚區分「絕對優勢」和「比較優勢」。

To answer this question, we must clearly distinguish between "Absolute Advantage" and "Comparative Advantage".

  • 絕對優勢 (Absolute Advantage): 指一個國家在生產某物品時，能以相同資源生產比另一個國家更多的產量，或以較少資源生產相同產量。這比較的是生產力 (Productivity)。
  • Refers to a country's ability to produce more of a good using the same resources, or the same amount using fewer resources, compared to another country. This compares productivity.
  • 比較優勢 (Comparative Advantage): 指一個國家生產某物品的機會成本 (Opportunity Cost) 低於另一個國家。
  • Refers to a country's ability to produce a good at a lower opportunity cost than another country.

貿易方向 (Direction of Trade): 根據比較優勢法則，一個國家會出口其擁有比較優勢的物品，並進口其沒有比較優勢的物品。絕對優勢不能決定貿易方向。

According to the law of comparative advantage, a country exports goods in which it has a comparative advantage and imports goods in which it does not. Absolute advantage cannot determine the direction of trade.

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): Option D

Option A: 錯誤 (Incorrect)

絕對優勢是將該國生產物品X的生產力與另一個國家作比較，而不是將該國生產物品X的數量與另一種物品作比較。

Absolute advantage compares the country's productivity of good X with another country, not comparing the amount of good X with another good within the same country.

Option B: 錯誤 (Incorrect)

擁有絕對優勢（高生產力）不代表擁有比較優勢（低機會成本）。機會成本取決於該國生產另一種物品的生產力。

Having an absolute advantage (high productivity) does not imply having a comparative advantage (low opportunity cost). Opportunity cost depends on the productivity of the other good.

Option C: 錯誤 (Incorrect)

一個國家絕對有可能在生產所有物品上都擁有絕對優勢（即生產力全面高於他國）。因此，該國不一定在另一種物品上有絕對劣勢。

It is entirely possible for a country to have an absolute advantage in producing ALL goods (i.e., higher overall productivity). Therefore, it doesn't necessarily have an absolute disadvantage in another good.

Option D: 正確 (Correct)

因為絕對優勢不等於比較優勢。即使該國生產物品X有絕對優勢，其生產物品X的機會成本仍可能高於他國（即沒有比較優勢）。在這種情況下，該國會選擇進口物品X。因此，該國「可能」進口物品X是正確的。

Because absolute advantage \(\neq\) comparative advantage. Even with an absolute advantage in good X, the country's opportunity cost for X might be higher than another country's (no comparative advantage). In this case, it will import good X. Thus, it "may" import good X.

🔍 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (關鍵點): 題目測試你是否知道「絕對優勢」與「比較優勢/貿易方向」之間沒有必然關係。

The question tests whether you know there is no necessary relationship between "absolute advantage" and "comparative advantage / direction of trade".

當你看到題目給出「絕對優勢」的條件時，你必須立刻聯想到：這只代表生產力高，無法推斷機會成本，也無法推斷出口或進口。因此，任何武斷的結論（如一定出口、一定有比較優勢）都是錯的。

When you see a condition of "absolute advantage", you must immediately recall: this only means high productivity; it cannot infer opportunity cost, nor can it infer export/import. Thus, any absolute conclusions (e.g., must export, must have comparative advantage) are wrong.

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

Trap 1: 混淆比較對象。學生常誤以為絕對優勢是比較同一個國家內兩種產品的產量（如 Option A）。記住，絕對優勢是國與國之間的比較。

Confusing the subject of comparison. Students often mistakenly think absolute advantage compares the output of two goods within the same country (like Option A). Remember, absolute advantage is a comparison between countries.

Trap 2: 誤以為「強國一定出口」。即使一個國家在所有產品上都最強（絕對優勢），它依然會進口其機會成本較高的產品。

Assuming "strong countries always export". Even if a country is the strongest in all products (absolute advantage), it will still import products where its opportunity cost is higher.

🙋 Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼一個國家生產物品X比較厲害（有絕對優勢），最後卻要進口它？

Q: Why would a country that is better at producing good X (has absolute advantage) end up importing it?

A: 想像一位世界頂級的外科醫生，他打字的速度（絕對優勢）也比他的秘書快。但他會自己打字嗎？不會。因為他花一小時打字的「機會成本」是放棄做一場高薪手術。所以他會「進口」秘書的打字服務。國家也是一樣，資源有限，要專注生產機會成本最低的物品。

A: Imagine a top surgeon who types faster than their secretary (absolute advantage). Will they type their own documents? No. The opportunity cost of spending an hour typing is giving up a highly paid surgery. So they "import" the secretary's typing service. Countries are the same; with limited resources, they focus on producing goods with the lowest opportunity cost.

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

字眼提示 (Word Clues): 經濟學 MC 題中，帶有「可能 (may / might)」字眼的選項通常是正確的，因為經濟學中很少有絕對的情況（除非是定義）。而帶有「一定 (must)」或直接下定論的選項，往往需要極嚴格的條件才能成立。看到 Option D 的「可能 (may)」，在無法確定其他選項時，這是一個高勝率的猜題方向。

In Economics MCQs, options with words like "may" or "might" are often correct because absolute situations are rare (unless it's a definition). Options that make definitive claims usually require very strict conditions. Spotting "may" in Option D makes it a high-probability guess if you are unsure.
