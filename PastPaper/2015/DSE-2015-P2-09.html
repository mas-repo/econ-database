🧠 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

邊際回報遞減定律 (Law of Diminishing Marginal Returns)

在短期內，當最少有一種生產要素的數量是固定的（固定要素），若我們不斷增加可變要素（Variable Factor）的數量，邊際產量（Marginal Product, MP）最終必然會下降。

In the short run, when at least one factor of production is fixed (Fixed Factor), if we continuously add more units of a variable factor, the Marginal Product (MP) will eventually decrease.

邊際產量公式 (Marginal Product Formula)

$$ MP = \frac{\Delta TP}{\Delta Q_v} $$

其中 \( \Delta TP \) 是總產量的變動，而 \( \Delta Q_v \) 是可變生產要素數量的變動。

Where \( \Delta TP \) is the change in Total Product, and \( \Delta Q_v \) is the change in the quantity of the variable factor.

Option A (Correct)

如果 \( X = 180 \)，那麼 \( MP = 180 - 160 = 20 \)。因為 \( 20 < 30 \)，邊際產量正在下降，符合定律。所以 X 可以等於 180。

If \( X = 180 \), then \( MP = 180 - 160 = 20 \). Since \( 20 < 30 \), the marginal product is decreasing, which satisfies the law. So X can be equal to 180.
Option B (Incorrect)

如果 \( X = 190 \)，那麼 \( MP = 190 - 160 = 30 \)。邊際產量維持不變（恆常回報），並未遞減，不符合定律。

If \( X = 190 \), then \( MP = 190 - 160 = 30 \). The marginal product remains constant (constant returns), it is not diminishing, so it does not satisfy the law.
Option C (Incorrect)

如果 \( X > 200 \)，那麼 \( MP > 40 \)。邊際產量正在上升（遞增回報），不符合定律。

If \( X > 200 \), then \( MP > 40 \). The marginal product is increasing (increasing returns), which does not satisfy the law.
Option D (Incorrect)

定律適用於短期生產。在此題中，勞力（12單位）是固定要素，資本是可變要素。只要有最少一種固定要素，該定律就適用。

The law applies to short-run production. In this question, Labour (12 units) is the fixed factor, and Capital is the variable factor. As long as there is at least one fixed factor, the law applies.

🧩 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題關鍵 (Pivot Point)：

  • 辨認固定與可變要素： 很多學生習慣勞工是可變要素，資本是固定要素。但這題故意反轉，勞工數量維持在 12（固定要素），資本數量從 2 增加到 5（可變要素）。
    Identify Fixed vs Variable Factors: Many students are used to Labour being variable and Capital being fixed. This question deliberately reverses it: Labour is constant at 12 (Fixed), while Capital increases from 2 to 5 (Variable).
  • 計算邊際產量 (MP)： 不要只看總產出 (TP) 的絕對值，必須計算每次增加一單位要素所帶來的額外產出 (MP)。
    Calculate Marginal Product (MP): Don't just look at the absolute value of Total Product (TP); you must calculate the extra output generated by each additional unit of the factor (MP).

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱一：誤以為邊際回報遞減等於總產量下降

邊際回報遞減只代表「新增的產量」越來越少（即 MP 下降），並不代表總產量 (TP) 一定會下跌。只要 MP 仍然是正數（例如這題的 20），總產量依然會上升（從 160 升至 180）。

Trap 1: Confusing Diminishing Marginal Returns with Falling Total Product
Diminishing marginal returns only means the "additional output" is getting smaller (i.e., MP is falling); it does not mean Total Product (TP) must fall. As long as MP is positive (like 20 in this question), TP will still increase (from 160 to 180).

🙋 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼選項 D 是錯的？資本通常不是固定要素嗎？

Q: Why is Option D wrong? Isn't capital usually a fixed factor?

A: 在現實或常見的教科書例子中，機器的確較難在短期內改變數量，所以常被設為固定要素。但在經濟學定義中，短期的定義是「最少有一種生產要素的數量不能改變」。在這道題目給出的數據中，勞力維持在 12 不變，所以勞力是固定要素；資本數量在改變，所以是可變要素。只要有固定要素存在，這就是短期生產，邊際回報遞減定律就適用。

A: In reality or common textbook examples, machines are indeed harder to change in the short run, so they are often fixed factors. However, the economic definition of the short run is simply "a period where at least one factor of production cannot be changed." In the data provided, Labour remains constant at 12, so Labour is the fixed factor; Capital is changing, so it is the variable factor. As long as a fixed factor exists, it is short-run production, and the law applies.

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)
