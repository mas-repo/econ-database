📚 Topic: Balance of Payments - Current Account
(國際收支平衡表 - 經常帳)

Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts (核心概念): Resident Status (居民身分) Current Account (經常帳) Financial Account (金融帳)

📝 The Question (題目)

一位來自美國的講師在香港工作了10年。下列哪些會包括在香港經常帳差額的計算之內？

(1) 他給予在香港的太太的款項
(2) 他用來購買在美國股市上市的股票的款項
(3) 他捐給非洲慈善機構的款項
(4) 他到美國探望父母所花的款項

A. 只有(1)、(2)
B. 只有(1)、(4)
C. 只有(2)、(3)
D. 只有(3)、(4)

A lecturer from the US has been working in Hong Kong for 10 years. Which of the following would be included in the calculation of Hong Kong’s current account balance?

(1) the money he gives to his wife in Hong Kong
(2) the money he uses to buy shares listed in the US stock market
(3) the money he donates to a charity organization in Africa
(4) the money he spends on visiting his parents in the US

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (4) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (3) and (4) only

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. Resident Status (居民身分)

在國際收支平衡表中，個人若在一個經濟體內居住或工作超過一年，便會被視為該經濟體的居民 (Resident)，其國籍並不重要。因此，這位在香港工作了10年的美國講師，在經濟學上是香港居民。

In the Balance of Payments, if an individual resides or works in an economy for more than one year, they are considered a Resident of that economy, regardless of their nationality. Therefore, the US lecturer who has worked in HK for 10 years is an HK Resident.

2. Balance of Payments Accounts (國際收支平衡表帳戶)

國際收支平衡表主要分為經常帳 (Current Account) 和資本及金融帳 (Capital and Financial Account)。

The Balance of Payments is mainly divided into the Current Account and the Capital and Financial Account.

$$ CAB = NX + NY + NCT $$

經常帳餘額 ($CAB$) 的組成部分包括：
- $NX$: 貨物及服務淨出口 (Net Exports of Goods and Services)
- $NY$: 初級收益淨值 / 淨要素收益 (Net Primary Income)
- $NCT$: 次要收益淨值 / 經常轉移淨值 (Net Secondary Income / Net Current Transfers)

Components of the Current Account Balance ($CAB$) include:
- $NX$: Net Exports of Goods and Services
- $NY$: Net Primary Income
- $NCT$: Net Secondary Income (Net Current Transfers)

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是 (Correct Option): Option D

分析 (1) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

講師（香港居民）將金錢給予在香港的太太（香港居民）。這屬於本地經濟體內兩名居民之間的轉移，並不涉及跨境交易，因此不會記錄在國際收支平衡表中。

The lecturer (HK resident) gives money to his wife in HK (HK resident). This is a domestic transfer between two residents within the local economy. It does not involve cross-border transactions and is therefore NOT recorded in the Balance of Payments.

分析 (2) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)

講師購買美國股票。這是香港居民購買海外金融資產，屬於證券投資 (Portfolio Investment)。這筆交易會記錄在金融帳 (Financial Account) 中，而不是經常帳。

The lecturer buys US shares. This is an HK resident purchasing foreign financial assets, which falls under Portfolio Investment. This transaction is recorded in the Financial Account, not the Current Account.

分析 (3) - 正確 (Correct)

講師捐款給非洲慈善機構。這屬於單向的資金流出，沒有換取任何實質物品或服務，因此歸類為次要收益 / 經常轉移 (Secondary Income / Current Transfers) 的流出，會包括在經常帳內。

The lecturer donates to an African charity. This is a one-way outward flow of funds without receiving any goods or services in return. It is classified as an outflow of Secondary Income / Current Transfers and WILL be included in the Current Account.

分析 (4) - 正確 (Correct)

講師到美國探望父母所花的款項。香港居民在海外的旅遊消費，會被視為香港對外國旅遊服務的購買，屬於服務輸入 (Import of Services)。因此，這會包括在經常帳的貨物及服務貿易中。

The money spent visiting his parents in the US. The travel expenditure of an HK resident abroad is treated as a purchase of foreign travel services, which is classified as an Import of Services. Therefore, this WILL be included in the goods and services trade of the Current Account.

🔍 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

💡 Pivot Point (破題關鍵):
題目第一句「在香港工作了10年」是解題的核心。這確認了該講師是香港居民。一旦確認他是香港居民，我們只需要判斷他的行為是否屬於「香港與外國之間的經常性交易」。

The first sentence, "working in Hong Kong for 10 years," is the core to solving this problem. It establishes that the lecturer is an HK Resident. Once established, we only need to judge whether his actions constitute "current transactions between HK and the rest of the world."

Distractor (干擾資訊): 題目強調他是一位「來自美國的講師」。這是為了誤導你以為他是美國居民，從而錯誤判斷資金流向。在經濟學上，判斷 BoP 交易看的是「居住地 (Residency)」，而非「國籍 (Nationality)」。

Distractor: The question highlights that he is a "lecturer from the US." This is a trap to make you think he is a US resident, which would lead to incorrect judgments about capital flows. In economics, BoP transactions are based on "Residency," not "Nationality."

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 混淆國籍與居民身分 (Confusing Nationality with Residency): 學生常以為美國人匯錢回美國不算「對外交易」，但只要他在港滿一年，他就是香港經濟體系的一部分。
    Students often think an American sending money to the US isn't an "external transaction." But as long as he has been in HK for >1 year, he is part of the HK economy.
  • 混淆金融帳與經常帳 (Confusing Financial Account with Current Account): 看到買賣股票、債券等投資行為（選項2），就直覺以為是經常帳。記住，只有「貨物、服務、收入、無償轉移」才屬於經常帳；「資產、負債」的買賣屬於金融帳。
    Seeing investments like stocks/bonds (Option 2) and assuming they belong to the current account. Remember: Goods, Services, Income, and Unrequited transfers = Current Account; Buying/selling Assets and Liabilities = Financial Account.

🙋 Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果這位講師只在香港工作了 2 個月，答案會改變嗎？

A: 會的！如果只工作了2個月，他依然是「非香港居民」。那麼他把錢給香港的太太，就變成了外國人匯款給香港人，屬於經常轉移的流入（會計入經常帳）。而他去美國消費，則是美國居民在美國消費，與香港的 BoP 無關了。

Q: If this lecturer had only worked in Hong Kong for 2 months, would the answer change?
A: Yes! If he only worked for 2 months, he would still be a "Non-HK Resident." Giving money to his wife in HK would then be an inward current transfer from a foreigner to an HK resident (included in the Current Account). His spending in the US would just be a US resident spending in the US, which has nothing to do with HK's BoP.

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

口訣 (Mnemonic): "Resident Test -> Asset Test"

  1. Resident Test: 看到題目有人物，先圈出他的居住時間。大於一年 = 本地居民。直接把「來自美國」這四個字劃掉，當他是香港人來做題。
  2. Asset Test: 掃視選項，看到「股票 / 債券 / 房地產 (Shares / Bonds / Real Estate)」等資產字眼，直接在旁邊寫上 "FA" (Financial Account)。題目問經常帳 (Current Account)，所以立刻可以剔除包含 (2) 的選項 A 和 C。然後再看 (1) 發現是內部轉移，剔除 B，秒選 D。
  1. Resident Test: Circle the time spent in the economy. >1 year = Local Resident. Cross out "from the US" and treat him purely as a Hong Konger.
  2. Asset Test: Scan the options. If you see assets like "shares/bonds/real estate", write "FA" (Financial Account) next to them. The question asks for the Current Account, so immediately eliminate options A and C which contain (2). Then, seeing (1) is an internal transfer, eliminate B. You can instantly select D.
