HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic (課題): Market Intervention & Elasticity (市場干預與彈性)

Difficulty (難度): ★★★★☆

Key Concepts (核心概念): Per-unit Tax (從量稅), Perfectly Inelastic Supply (完全無彈性供應), Tax Burden (稅項負擔)

1. The Question (題目)

細閱下列有關某物品的供需表。
Refer to the following table for the supply and demand schedules for a good.

單位價格 Unit price ($) 45678
需求量 Quantity demanded (units) 161412108
供應量 Quantity supplied (units) 1414141414

假設政府對該物品徵收$2的從量稅。下列哪項有關該物品的陳述是正確的？
The government imposes a $2 per unit tax on the good. Which of the following statements about the good is correct?

A. 賣方的除稅後收入會是$70。
A. Sellers’ revenue net of tax is $70.

B. 新的市場價格是$6。
B. The new market price is $6.

C. 總稅項收入是$28。
C. The total tax revenue is $28.

D. 上述資料不足以判斷賣方的稅項負擔。
D. The above information is insufficient to determine the tax burden on sellers.

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

完全無彈性供應 (Perfectly Inelastic Supply): 當供應量在任何價格下都保持不變時，供應曲線是一條垂直線。在這種情況下，供應彈性 \(E_s = 0\)。

When the quantity supplied remains constant at all price levels, the supply curve is a vertical line. In this case, the elasticity of supply is \(E_s = 0\).

從量稅與稅項負擔 (Per-unit Tax and Tax Burden): 徵收從量稅會使供應曲線向上移動稅款的幅度。然而，如果供應曲線是垂直的，向上移動後仍然是同一條垂直線。因此，市場均衡價格（買家支付的價格）不會改變，賣方將承擔 100% 的稅項負擔。

Imposing a per-unit tax shifts the supply curve upwards by the amount of the tax. However, if the supply curve is vertical, shifting it upwards results in the exact same vertical line. Therefore, the market equilibrium price (price paid by buyers) remains unchanged, and sellers bear 100% of the tax burden.

$$ \text{Total Tax Revenue} = \text{Tax per unit} \times \text{New Quantity Transacted} $$ $$ \text{總稅收} = \text{每單位稅款} \times \text{新交易量} $$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 Correct Option: C

原來的均衡 (Original Equilibrium):
在未徵稅前，當價格 \(P = \$5\) 時，需求量 = 供應量 = 14 單位。因此，原來的市場價格是 $5，交易量是 14。

Before the tax, at price \(P = \$5\), Quantity Demanded = Quantity Supplied = 14 units. Thus, the original market price is $5 and the transacted quantity is 14.

徵稅後的均衡 (New Equilibrium after Tax):
由於供應量固定在 14 單位（完全無彈性），供應曲線是一條垂直線。徵收 $2 稅款後，供應曲線向上移動，但垂直線向上移動後位置不變。因此，市場均衡數量仍然是 14 單位，買家支付的市場價格維持在 $5。

Since the quantity supplied is fixed at 14 units (perfectly inelastic), the supply curve is vertical. After a $2 tax, the supply curve shifts upwards, but a vertical line shifted upwards remains in the same position. Therefore, the equilibrium quantity remains 14 units, and the market price paid by buyers stays at $5.

分析選項 A (Analysis of A) - 錯誤 Incorrect:
賣方從買家收到 $5，但需繳納 $2 稅款，因此每單位淨收 $3。除稅後總收入 = \(\$3 \times 14 = \$42\)，而非 $70。

Sellers receive $5 from buyers but must pay a $2 tax, leaving them with a net price of $3 per unit. Total revenue net of tax = \(\$3 \times 14 = \$42\), not $70.

分析選項 B (Analysis of B) - 錯誤 Incorrect:
如上所述，新的市場價格（買家支付的價格）維持在 $5，並沒有上升到 $6。

As explained above, the new market price (paid by buyers) remains at $5, it does not rise to $6.

分析選項 C (Analysis of C) - 正確 Correct:
總稅項收入 = 每單位稅款 \(\times\) 交易量 = \(\$2 \times 14 = \$28\)。

Total tax revenue = Tax per unit \(\times\) Transacted quantity = \(\$2 \times 14 = \$28\).

分析選項 D (Analysis of D) - 錯誤 Incorrect:
資料充足。因為供應完全無彈性，賣方承擔了全部 $28 的稅項負擔。

The information is sufficient. Because supply is perfectly inelastic, sellers bear the entire tax burden of $28.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解答這類題目的關鍵點 (Pivot Point) 在於觀察數據表的特徵。當你看到「供應量」在所有價格下都是 14 時，你必須立刻意識到這是一條完全無彈性 (Perfectly Inelastic) 的供應曲線。

The Pivot Point for solving this question is observing the data table's pattern. When you see that the "Quantity supplied" is 14 at all price levels, you must immediately recognize that this is a perfectly inelastic supply curve.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：假設徵稅必定導致價格上升。
很多學生習慣性地將稅款加到原來的價格上（例如 \(5 + 2 = 7\)），或者認為供應減少會推高價格。但在完全無彈性供應的情況下，賣方無法將稅款轉嫁給買家，價格維持不變。

Trap: Assuming a tax always increases the market price.
Many students habitually add the tax to the original price (e.g., \(5 + 2 = 7\)) or assume a decrease in supply will push prices up. However, with perfectly inelastic supply, sellers cannot pass the tax burden onto buyers, so the market price remains unchanged.

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼賣方不提高價格來彌補稅款損失？
A: 因為如果賣方將價格提高到 $6，市場需求量會下降到 12 單位。但賣方「必須」或「只會」供應 14 單位（供應完全無彈性）。為了清出這 14 單位，他們只能接受買家願意為 14 單位支付的價格，也就是 $5。

Q: Why don't sellers raise the price to cover the tax loss?
A: Because if sellers raise the price to $6, the quantity demanded drops to 12 units. But sellers "must" or "will only" supply 14 units (perfectly inelastic supply). To clear all 14 units, they have to accept the price buyers are willing to pay for 14 units, which is $5.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧 (Shortcut): 看到表格中某一行數字完全一樣（例如 Qs 全是 14），直接判定該方（供應方）彈性為零。彈性為零的一方承擔 100% 稅款。交易量必定不變（維持 14）。直接計算稅收：\(14 \times \$2 = \$28\)，選 C！

Shortcut: When you see a row with identical numbers (e.g., Qs is all 14), immediately conclude that side (supply) has zero elasticity. The side with zero elasticity bears 100% of the tax. The transacted quantity must remain unchanged (at 14). Calculate tax revenue directly: \(14 \times \$2 = \$28\). Choose C!
