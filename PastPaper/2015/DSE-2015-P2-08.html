HKDSE Economics Tutor Guide

Topic (課題): 企業擴張與整合 (Business Expansion and Integration)

Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆ (2/5)

Key Concepts (關鍵概念): 側向擴張 (Lateral Expansion) 規模經濟 (Economies of Scale) 水平整合 (Horizontal Integration)

1. The Question (題目)

759阿信屋是本地一家售賣進口零食的零售商。它將業務擴展至售賣化妝品和護膚產品。下列哪項最不可能是該擴張背後的動機？

A. 減少競爭
B. 享有規模經濟
C. 將其品牌推廣至其他產品
D. 以業務多元化分散風險

759Store, a local retailer of imported snacks, expands its business to sell cosmetics and skincare products. Which of the following is the LEAST likely motive behind the expansion?

A. to reduce competition
B. to enjoy economies of scale
C. to extend its brand name to other products
D. to spread risk by business diversification

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

側向擴張 (Lateral Expansion / Diversification)

當企業擴展至生產或售賣相關但不直接競爭，或完全無關的產品時，稱為側向擴張。零食與化妝品屬於不同市場，因此這是側向擴張（業務多元化）。

When a firm expands into producing or selling products that are related but not directly competing, or completely unrelated, it is called lateral expansion. Snacks and cosmetics belong to different markets, so this is lateral expansion (diversification).

規模經濟 (Economies of Scale)

當產量或業務規模增加時，平均成本 \(AC\) 下降。公式為：

$$AC = \frac{TC}{Q}$$

側向擴張可帶來管理規模經濟 (Managerial economies)及市場推廣規模經濟 (Marketing economies)。

When output or business scale increases, average cost \(AC\) falls. Lateral expansion can bring managerial economies and marketing economies.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option A

Option A (正確): 減少競爭 (to reduce competition)

這是最不可能的動機。減少競爭通常是水平整合 (Horizontal Integration) 的動機（即收購同業競爭對手）。售賣化妝品並不會減少零食市場的競爭，反而會令企業面對化妝品市場的新競爭者。

This is the LEAST likely motive. Reducing competition is typically a motive for Horizontal Integration (acquiring a competitor in the same industry). Selling cosmetics does not reduce competition in the snack market; instead, it exposes the firm to new competitors in the cosmetics market.

Option B (錯誤): 享有規模經濟 (to enjoy economies of scale)

企業可以共用行政部門、物流網絡或進行聯合宣傳，從而降低平均成本 \(AC\)。這是合理的動機。

The firm can share administrative departments, logistics networks, or conduct joint promotions, thereby lowering average cost \(AC\). This is a valid motive.

Option C (錯誤): 將其品牌推廣至其他產品 (to extend its brand name to other products)

759阿信屋已建立一定知名度，利用現有品牌效應推廣新產品（化妝品）能節省建立新品牌的成本。這是合理的動機。

759Store has established brand recognition. Utilizing the existing brand effect to promote new products (cosmetics) saves the cost of building a new brand. This is a valid motive.

Option D (錯誤): 以業務多元化分散風險 (to spread risk by business diversification)

這是側向擴張最經典的動機。如果零食市場不景氣，化妝品市場的收入可以補償損失，從而分散風險。

This is the most classic motive for lateral expansion. If the snack market performs poorly, revenue from the cosmetics market can compensate for the loss, thereby spreading risk.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

  • Pivot Point (關鍵點): 留意題目中的「最不可能 (LEAST likely)」字眼，以及認清「零食」與「化妝品」屬於兩個完全不同的市場。
  • Distractors (干擾資訊): 學生可能會誤以為只要公司變大，競爭力變強，就等同於「減少競爭」。但在經濟學定義中，「減少競爭」特指消除同一市場內的對手。
  • Pivot Point: Notice the keyword "LEAST likely" and recognize that "snacks" and "cosmetics" belong to completely different markets.
  • Distractors: Students might mistakenly think that as a company grows larger and more competitive, it equates to "reducing competition." However, in economics, "reducing competition" specifically refers to eliminating rivals within the same market.

5. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 零食和化妝品完全不同，為什麼還能享有規模經濟 (Option B)？

A: 雖然產品不同，但它們都是零售業。公司可以共用同一個收銀系統、同一批會計人員、甚至同一個貨倉。這分攤了固定成本 (Fixed Costs)，令平均成本下降，屬於管理及財務上的規模經濟。

Q: Snacks and cosmetics are completely different. How can they still enjoy economies of scale (Option B)?

A: Although the products are different, both are in the retail industry. The company can share the same POS system, accounting staff, or even warehouses. This spreads out the Fixed Costs, lowering the average cost, which constitutes managerial and financial economies of scale.

6. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

關鍵詞配對法 (Keyword Matching Method):

在HKDSE經濟科中，特定的動機通常對應特定的擴張類型：

  • 看到 「減少競爭 (Reduce competition)」 ➔ 必然是 水平整合 (Horizontal Integration)。
  • 看到 「確保原料供應 / 確保銷售渠道」 ➔ 必然是 垂直整合 (Vertical Integration)。
  • 看到 「分散風險 (Spread risk)」 ➔ 必然是 側向擴張 (Lateral Expansion)。

題目是側向擴張，因此直接剔除屬於水平整合的動機 (Option A)。

In HKDSE Economics, specific motives usually correspond to specific types of expansion:

  • "Reduce competition" ➔ Always Horizontal Integration.
  • "Secure supply/outlets" ➔ Always Vertical Integration.
  • "Spread risk" ➔ Always Lateral Expansion.

The question describes lateral expansion, so immediately eliminate the motive that belongs to horizontal integration (Option A).
