📝 題目 (The Question)

當非預期的通縮出現，下列哪些人士會受損？

When unanticipated deflation occurs, which of the following people will lose?

  • A. 支付定額租金的人
    people paying a fixed amount of rental payment
  • B. 收取儲蓄存款利息的人
    people receiving interest from their savings deposits
  • C. 償還浮息按揭貸款的人
    people repaying a mortgage loan at a floating interest rate
  • D. 在固定名義工資合約下工作的人
    people working under a fixed nominal wage contract

🧠 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解答這類題目，我們必須理解「非預期通縮」(Unanticipated Deflation) 對貨幣購買力的影響。通縮意味著一般物價水平下降，因此貨幣的實質購買力 (Real Purchasing Power) 會上升。

To solve this type of question, we must understand the impact of Unanticipated Deflation on the purchasing power of money. Deflation means the general price level falls, so the real purchasing power of money increases.

當通縮是「非預期」時，實際的通脹率低於預期（甚至變為負數）。我們可以透過費沙方程式 (Fisher Equation) 來理解財富轉移的效應：

When deflation is "unanticipated", the actual inflation rate is lower than expected (and becomes negative). We can understand the wealth redistribution effect through the Fisher Equation:

$$ \text{Real Interest Rate } (r) = \text{Nominal Interest Rate } (R) - \text{Actual Inflation Rate } (\pi) $$ $$ \text{實質利率} = \text{名義利率} - \text{實際通脹率} $$

在非預期通縮下，實際通脹率 \(\pi\) 是負數且低於預期。因此，實質利率 \(r\)（或實質價值）會高於預期。這會導致財富重分配：

Under unanticipated deflation, the actual inflation rate \(\pi\) is negative and lower than expected. Therefore, the real interest rate \(r\) (or real value) will be higher than expected. This leads to wealth redistribution:

  • 收取固定金額的人 (Receivers of fixed nominal amounts)： 獲益 (Gain)。因為他們收到的錢能買到更多東西。
  • 支付固定金額的人 (Payers of fixed nominal amounts)： 受損 (Lose)。因為他們付出的錢實質上更值錢了，實質負擔加重。

✅ 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): A

Option A: 支付定額租金的人 (people paying a fixed amount of rental payment) — CORRECT

這類人士需要支付「固定名義金額」(Fixed nominal amount)。在通縮期間，物價下跌，貨幣的實質購買力上升。這意味著他們支付的每一元租金，其實質價值都比預期高。因此，他們的實質負擔加重了，他們是受損者。

These people pay a "fixed nominal amount". During deflation, the price level falls, and the real purchasing power of money increases. This means every dollar of rent they pay has a higher real value than expected. Therefore, their real burden increases, making them the losers.

Option B: 收取儲蓄存款利息的人 (people receiving interest from their savings deposits) — WRONG

這類人士是收取利息的「收款人」。因為非預期通縮導致實質利率上升，他們收到的利息能購買更多的商品和服務。因此，他們的實質購買力增加，他們是獲益者。

These people are "receivers" of interest. Because unanticipated deflation causes the real interest rate to rise, the interest they receive can buy more goods and services. Thus, their real purchasing power increases, making them gainers.

Option C: 償還浮息按揭貸款的人 (people repaying a mortgage loan at a floating interest rate) — WRONG

「浮息」(Floating rate) 意味著名義利率會隨著市場情況（包括通脹/通縮預期）而調整。在通縮環境下，市場名義利率通常會下調，從而減輕借款人的名義利息負擔。由於利率會自我調整，他們不會像支付固定金額的人那樣承受巨大的實質損失。

"Floating rate" means the nominal interest rate adjusts according to market conditions (including inflation/deflation expectations). In a deflationary environment, market nominal rates typically fall, reducing the borrower's nominal interest burden. Because the rate adjusts, they do not suffer the massive real loss that fixed-rate payers do.

Option D: 在固定名義工資合約下工作的人 (people working under a fixed nominal wage contract) — WRONG

這類人士收取固定的名義工資。利用公式 \( \text{實質工資} = \frac{\text{名義工資}}{\text{物價指數}} \)，當物價下跌時，即使名義工資不變，他們的實質工資（購買力）也會上升。因此，他們是獲益者。

These people receive a fixed nominal wage. Using the formula \( \text{Real Wage} = \frac{\text{Nominal Wage}}{\text{Price Index}} \), when the price level falls, their real wage (purchasing power) increases even if the nominal wage is unchanged. Therefore, they are gainers.

🔍 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解答此題的「關鍵樞紐」(Pivot Point) 在於兩個詞：「通縮」(Deflation) 和 「受損」(Lose)。

The "Pivot Point" to solving this question lies in two words: "Deflation" and "Lose".

  1. 通縮 = 錢變貴了 (Deflation = Money becomes more valuable)。
  2. 誰會因為錢變貴而受損？ (Who loses when money becomes more valuable?) 答案是：必須把錢交給別人的人（支付者）。
  3. 掃視選項，尋找「支付固定金額」的人。A 是支付租金，B 是收取利息，C 是浮動（非固定），D 是收取工資。因此秒殺選 A。
  1. Deflation = Money becomes more valuable.
  2. Who loses when money becomes more valuable? The answer is: the person who has to give money to others (the payer).
  3. Scan the options for the person "paying a fixed amount". A is paying rent, B is receiving interest, C is floating (not fixed), D is receiving wages. Thus, A is the instant answer.

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

學生最常犯的錯誤是混淆通脹與通縮的影響。很多學生死記硬背「固定收入者在通脹中受損」，然後在考試緊張時，看到「固定」就選，忘記了題目問的是「通縮」。

The most common mistake is confusing the effects of inflation and deflation. Many students rote-memorize "fixed income earners lose during inflation", and under exam stress, they pick any option with "fixed" without realizing the question asks about "deflation".

陷阱： 選項 D (固定工資) 正是針對這個盲點設計的。在通脹時 D 會受損，但在通縮時 D 是獲益的！

Trap: Option D (fixed wage) is designed exactly for this blind spot. D would lose during inflation, but D gains during deflation!

🙋 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼選項 C (浮息按揭) 不是答案？通縮時錢變貴了，還貸款不是應該更辛苦嗎？

Q: Why isn't Option C (floating rate mortgage) the answer? Money is more valuable during deflation, shouldn't repaying loans be harder?

A: 如果是「定息按揭」(Fixed-rate mortgage)，借款人確實會受損。但題目特別註明是「浮息」(Floating rate)。在通縮期間，中央銀行通常會減息救市，市場利率（如 HIBOR 或最優惠利率 P）會下降。因此，借款人需要支付的「名義利息」會減少，這抵銷了貨幣購買力上升帶來的負面影響，保護了借款人免受嚴重損失。

A: If it were a "Fixed-rate mortgage", the borrower would indeed lose. But the question specifically says "Floating rate". During deflation, central banks usually cut interest rates to save the economy, and market rates (like HIBOR or Prime rate) fall. Thus, the "nominal interest" the borrower pays decreases, which offsets the negative impact of the increased purchasing power of money, protecting the borrower from severe losses.

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

「收付口訣」(The Pay-Receive Rule):

遇到非預期通脹/通縮的題目，立刻在草稿紙上畫個十字：

  • 通脹 (Inflation): 錢貶值 \(\rightarrow\) 支付固定金額者 (Win) \(\mid\) 收取固定金額者 (Lose)
  • 通縮 (Deflation): 錢升值 \(\rightarrow\) 支付固定金額者 (Lose) \(\mid\) 收取固定金額者 (Win)

題目問「通縮 + 受損」，對應表格就是「支付固定金額者」。直接找帶有「支付」(Paying) 和「固定」(Fixed) 字眼的選項即可！

The Pay-Receive Rule:

When encountering unanticipated inflation/deflation questions, immediately draw a cross on your scratch paper:

  • Inflation: Money depreciates \(\rightarrow\) Fixed Payers (Win) \(\mid\) Fixed Receivers (Lose)
  • Deflation: Money appreciates \(\rightarrow\) Fixed Payers (Lose) \(\mid\) Fixed Receivers (Win)

The question asks for "Deflation + Lose", which corresponds to "Fixed Payers". Just look for the option with the words "Paying" and "Fixed"!
