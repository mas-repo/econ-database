假設租用機器的租金(屬固定成本)上升了$2，而每單位產出的邊際成本減少了$0.5。該廠商利潤極大化的產量因此_________，而利潤 _________。 Suppose the rental of the machine hired, a fixed cost, increases by $2 while the marginal cost for each unit of the output is reduced by $0.5. A. 上升 … 下跌 (increases … decreases)

B. 上升 … 可能上升或下跌 (increases … may increase or decrease)

C. 下跌 … 下跌 (decreases … decreases)

D. 下跌 … 可能上升或下跌 (decreases … may increase or decrease)

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 利潤極大化條件 (Profit Maximization Condition):

廠商會生產至邊際收益等於邊際成本的水平。在完全競爭市場中，價格 (P) 等於邊際收益 (MR)。 Firms produce where Marginal Revenue equals Marginal Cost. In Perfect Competition, Price (P) equals Marginal Revenue (MR).
$$ MR = MC \implies P = MC $$

2. 固定成本 vs 邊際成本 (Fixed Cost vs Marginal Cost):

  • 固定成本 (Fixed Cost, FC): 不隨產量改變的成本（如租金）。FC 的改變不會影響 MC，因此不會影響利潤極大化的產量 (除非導致停業)。 Costs that do not change with output (e.g., rent). Changes in FC do not affect MC, and thus do not affect the profit-maximizing output (unless it causes a shutdown).
  • 邊際成本 (Marginal Cost, MC): 生產多一單位產品的額外成本。MC 的改變會直接影響產量決定。 The additional cost of producing one more unit. Changes in MC directly affect the output decision.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: B

上升 … 可能上升或下跌 (Increases … may increase or decrease)

第一步：分析產量 (Step 1: Analyze Output)

題目指出「每單位產出的邊際成本減少了$0.5」。這代表整條 MC 曲線向下移動 (或向右移動)。 The question states "marginal cost for each unit of the output is reduced by $0.5". This means the entire MC curve shifts downward (or to the right).
由於市場價格 \(P\) (即 \(MR\)) 保持不變，當 \(MC\) 下跌時，原本 \(P = MC\) 的均衡點會改變。現在 \(P > New MC\)，廠商會增加產量以獲取更多利潤，直到 \(P\) 再次等於新的 \(MC\)。因此，產量上升。 Since the market price \(P\) (which is \(MR\)) remains constant, when \(MC\) falls, the original equilibrium where \(P = MC\) is disrupted. Now \(P > New MC\), so the firm will increase output to capture more profit until \(P\) equals the new \(MC\). Therefore, output increases.

注意：租金上升 (固定成本) 不會影響邊際成本，所以不影響產量決定。

第二步：分析利潤 (Step 2: Analyze Profit)

利潤的變化受兩個相反因素影響： The change in profit is affected by two opposing factors:
  1. 成本增加 (Cost Increase): 固定成本 (租金) 上升 $2。這會令總利潤減少 $2。 Fixed cost (rent) rises by $2. This reduces total profit by $2.
  2. 成本節省 (Cost Saving): 邊際成本每單位減少 $0.5。這代表生產每一單位都節省了 $0.5 的可變成本。總節省額 = \(0.5 \times Q\) (產量)。這會令總利潤增加。 Marginal cost falls by $0.5 per unit. This means variable cost is saved by $0.5 for every unit produced. Total saving = \(0.5 \times Q\) (Output). This increases total profit.
$$ \Delta \text{Profit} = (\text{Total Saving}) - (\text{Rent Increase}) $$ $$ \Delta \text{Profit} = 0.5Q - 2 $$
關鍵點：我們不知道原本的市場價格 \(P\)，因此不知道產量 \(Q\) 是多少。 Key Point: We do not know the original market price \(P\), so we do not know what the output quantity \(Q\) is.
  • 如果 \(Q = 3\)，利潤改變 = \(0.5(3) - 2 = -0.5\) (下跌)。
  • 如果 \(Q = 8\)，利潤改變 = \(0.5(8) - 2 = +2.0\) (上升)。
因為結果取決於未知的產量，所以利潤可能上升或下跌。 Since the result depends on the unknown output level, the profit may increase or decrease.

Why A, C, D are wrong (為何 A, C, D 錯誤)

  • Option A: 錯誤地假設利潤必然下跌。如果廠商產量很大 (例如 \(Q=9\))，節省的可變成本 ($4.5) 會超過租金升幅 ($2)。 Incorrectly assumes profit must decrease. If the firm produces a large output (e.g., \(Q=9\)), the variable cost savings ($4.5) would exceed the rent increase ($2).
  • Option C & D: 錯誤地指出產量下跌。當邊際成本下降時，廠商為了極大化利潤，必然會增加 (或維持) 產量，絕不會減少。 Incorrectly state that output decreases. When marginal cost falls, to maximize profit, a firm will always increase (or maintain) output, never decrease it.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

1. 尋找「轉折點」 (Identify the Pivot Point):

題目的核心在於分辨固定成本 (FC) 與 邊際成本 (MC) 的不同影響。
FC 改變 \(\rightarrow\) 只影響利潤，不影響產量。
MC 改變 \(\rightarrow\) 影響產量，同時影響利潤。 The core of the problem is distinguishing the different effects of Fixed Cost (FC) and Marginal Cost (MC).
FC change \(\rightarrow\) Affects Profit only, NOT Output.
MC change \(\rightarrow\) Affects Output AND Profit.

2. 數據陷阱 (Data Trap):

題目給出了一個詳細的數字表格，但沒有給出市場價格 (Price)。沒有價格，你就無法鎖定具體的產量 \(Q\)。這是一個暗示，答案很可能涉及「不確定性」(Uncertainty)。 The question provides a detailed numerical table but does NOT provide the Market Price. Without the price, you cannot pinpoint the specific output \(Q\). This is a hint that the answer likely involves "uncertainty".

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼固定成本上升不會令廠商減少產量來省錢？ (Why doesn't an increase in fixed cost make the firm reduce output to save money?)
A: 因為固定成本（如租金）是「必須支付」的，無論你生產 1 件還是 100 件，租金都一樣。減少產量並不能節省租金，反而可能因為減少了銷售收入而損失更多。只要 \(P \ge AVC\) (價格高於平均可變成本)，廠商就會繼續生產。 A: Because fixed costs (like rent) must be paid regardless of whether you produce 1 unit or 100 units. Reducing output does not save rent; instead, it might cause you to lose more money by reducing sales revenue. As long as \(P \ge AVC\) (Price covers Average Variable Cost), the firm continues to produce.
Q: 考試時如果沒有時間計算，有什麼速解法？ (Is there a shortcut if I'm running out of time?)
A: 有。看到 MC 下降 \(\rightarrow\) 產量一定上升 (刪除 C, D)。看到有「成本上升」($2) 和「成本下降」($0.5Q) 同時出現，且不知道 \(Q\) 是多少 \(\rightarrow\) 淨效果通常是不確定的。直接選 B。 A: Yes. See MC falls \(\rightarrow\) Output must increase (Eliminate C, D). See "Cost Increase" ($2) and "Cost Decrease" ($0.5Q) happening simultaneously, and you don't know \(Q\) \(\rightarrow\) Net effect is usually uncertain. Choose B directly.
