📌 題目 (The Question)

貨幣量理論意味著
The quantity theory of money implies that
A. 當實質產出正在下降，增加貨幣供應會令物價水平上升。
A. an increase in money supply will lead to a rise in price level when real output is falling.
B. 該經濟經常處於全民就業產出水平。
B. the economy is always at the full employment output level.
C. 當實質產出維持不變，增加貨幣供應會令貨幣流通速率下降。
C. an increase in money supply will lead to a decrease in the velocity of circulation of money when real output is kept constant.
D. 增加貨幣供應會令實質產出有相同比例的提升。
D. an increase in money supply will lead to a same-proportional increase in real output.

🧠 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

貨幣量理論 (Quantity Theory of Money) 是建基於交易方程式 (Equation of Exchange) 的一個重要經濟學概念。

The Quantity Theory of Money (QTM) is an important economic concept based on the Equation of Exchange.

$$M \times V = P \times Y$$
  • $M$: 貨幣供應量 (Money Supply)
  • $V$: 貨幣流通速率 (Velocity of circulation of money)
  • $P$: 物價水平 (Price level)
  • $Y$: 實質產出 (Real output)

貨幣量理論的唯一核心假設是：貨幣流通速率 ($V$) 是恆定的 (Constant)。

The sole core assumption of the Quantity Theory of Money is that the velocity of money ($V$) is constant.

為了方便計算，我們通常會將公式轉換為百分比變動 (Percentage Change Form)：

For easier calculation, we usually convert the formula into its percentage change form:

$$\%\Delta M + \%\Delta V = \%\Delta P + \%\Delta Y$$

由於 $V$ 是恆定的，因此 $\%\Delta V = 0$。公式可進一步簡化為：

Since $V$ is constant, $\%\Delta V = 0$. The formula can be further simplified to:

$$\%\Delta M = \%\Delta P + \%\Delta Y$$

✅ 答案與詳細分析 (Answer & Detailed Analysis)

🎉 正確答案 (Correct Option): A

選項 A (Option A) - 正確 Correct
當實質產出正在下降 ($\%\Delta Y < 0$)，而貨幣供應增加 ($\%\Delta M > 0$) 時，根據公式 $\%\Delta P = \%\Delta M - \%\Delta Y$：
正數減去負數，必定會得出一個正數。這意味著物價水平必定會上升 ($\%\Delta P > 0$)。
When real output is falling ($\%\Delta Y < 0$) and money supply increases ($\%\Delta M > 0$), according to the formula $\%\Delta P = \%\Delta M - \%\Delta Y$:
A positive number minus a negative number must result in a positive number. This means the price level must rise ($\%\Delta P > 0$).
選項 B (Option B) - 錯誤 Incorrect
貨幣量理論本身只假設 $V$ 恆定，並沒有假設經濟必定處於全民就業狀態 (即 $Y$ 恆定)。這是「古典學派」的額外假設，而非貨幣量理論本身的必然意味。
The QTM itself only assumes $V$ is constant; it does not assume the economy is always at full employment (i.e., $Y$ is constant). This is an additional assumption of the "Classical School," not an absolute implication of the theory itself.
選項 C (Option C) - 錯誤 Incorrect
貨幣量理論的定義正是假設 $V$ 維持不變，因此 $V$ 絕對不會下降。這直接違背了理論的核心假設。
The very definition of the QTM assumes that $V$ remains constant, so $V$ would absolutely not decrease. This directly contradicts the core assumption of the theory.
選項 D (Option D) - 錯誤 Incorrect
當 $M$ 增加時，通常是物價水平 ($P$) 會受到相同比例的影響（若 $Y$ 恆定）。理論並沒有指出實質產出 ($Y$) 會跟隨 $M$ 以相同比例上升。
When $M$ increases, it is usually the price level ($P$) that is affected proportionally (if $Y$ is constant). The theory does not state that real output ($Y$) will follow $M$ and increase by the same proportion.

🧩 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

這類題目的「破題關鍵」(Pivot Point) 在於你是否清楚知道「貨幣量理論」的唯一定義：$V$ 是恆定不變的。

The "Pivot Point" for this type of question lies in whether you clearly know the sole definition of the "Quantity Theory of Money": $V$ is strictly constant.

題目中的選項 (B, C, D) 嘗試用各種經濟情況來混淆你。你只需要列出百分比變動公式，代入選項的情境，檢查數學邏輯是否成立即可。這是一條考驗邏輯推導而非死記硬背的題目。

The options (B, C, D) attempt to confuse you with various economic scenarios. You simply need to write down the percentage change formula, substitute the scenarios from the options, and check if the mathematical logic holds. This is a question testing logical deduction rather than rote memorization.

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

很多同學會將「貨幣量理論」(Quantity Theory of Money) 與「古典學派假設」(Classical Assumptions) 混淆。同學常誤以為理論意味著 $Y$ 也是恆定的，從而誤選選項 B。

Many students confuse the "Quantity Theory of Money" with "Classical Assumptions." Students often mistakenly believe the theory implies $Y$ is also constant, leading them to wrongly choose Option B.

解毒劑： 記住，只有當題目明確提到「古典貨幣量理論」(Classical QTM) 或「假設充分就業」(assuming full employment) 時，$Y$ 才會不變。

Antidote: Remember, $Y$ is only constant when the question explicitly mentions the "Classical QTM" or "assumes full employment."

🙋 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼計算時要用百分比變動公式（$\%\Delta$）而不是原本的乘法公式？

Why use the percentage change formula ($\%\Delta$) instead of the original multiplication formula for calculations?

A: 因為題目討論的是變數的「上升」或「下降」（即變動率）。使用百分比變動公式，我們可以利用簡單的加減法（正數、負數）來判斷結果，這比處理乘法和除法更直觀、更不容易出現「粗心大意」(careless error) 的錯誤。

Because the question discusses the "increase" or "decrease" of variables (i.e., rates of change). By using the percentage change formula, we can use simple addition and subtraction (positive and negative values) to determine the outcome, which is much more intuitive and less prone to "careless errors" than dealing with multiplication and division.

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

見到「貨幣量理論」，立即在試卷邊緣寫下：

When you see "Quantity Theory of Money," immediately write down on the margin:

M + V = P + Y
  1. 直接把 V 劃掉（當作 0）。公式變成 M = P + Y。
    Directly cross out V (treat it as 0). The formula becomes M = P + Y.
  2. 將選項的條件代入：選項 A 說 $M$ 是 +，$Y$ 是 -。
    Substitute the conditions: Option A says $M$ is + and $Y$ is -.
  3. 公式變成 (+) = P + (-)。將 - 移項到左邊，(+) - (-) = P，得出 $P$ 必定是大於零的正數（上升）。5 秒即可選出正確答案！
    The formula becomes (+) = P + (-). Move the - to the left side, (+) - (-) = P, which means $P$ must be positive (rise). You can pick the correct answer in just 5 seconds!
