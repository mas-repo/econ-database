HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Efficiency & Equity / Externalities (效率與公平 / 外部性) Difficulty: ⭐⭐ (2/5)

Key Concepts: Divergence between Private and Social Benefits, Positive Externality, Non-paying Third Party.
核心概念： 私人利益與社會利益的分歧、正面外部性（外部利益）、非付費第三方。

The Question (題目)

下列哪項是私人利益和社會利益分歧的一個例子？

Which of the following is an example of the divergence between private and social benefits?

A. 一名保安員在演奏廳當值時觀看到現場音樂會。
A security guard watches live concerts when he is working in a concert hall.
B. 一名遊客欣賞街頭歌手的音樂表演而沒有付費。
A tourist enjoys the music performed by a singer on the street and he does not pay for that.
C. 一名住在卡拉OK酒廊隔鄰的居民因噪音不能入睡。
A resident living next to a karaoke lounge cannot fall asleep because of the noise.
D. 機場遷離後，該地區的住宅樓宇的市場價值上升。
After the removal of the airport, the market value of the residential flats in that region increases.

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 外部性 (Externalities)

當一個人的生產或消費行為影響到第三方 (Third Party)，而該第三方沒有因此而付出代價或獲得補償，這就是外部性。 Externalities exist when a person's production or consumption affects a third party without payment or compensation.

2. 私人利益與社會利益的分歧 (Divergence between Private and Social Benefits)

這通常指正面外部性 (Positive Externality) 或稱外部利益 (External Benefit)。 This usually refers to Positive Externality or External Benefit.

$$ \text{Social Benefit (MB}_s\text{)} = \text{Private Benefit (MB}_p\text{)} + \text{External Benefit (MB}_e\text{)} $$
  • 分歧 (Divergence): 當 \(\text{MB}_e > 0\) 時，\(\text{MB}_s > \text{MB}_p\)。 When \(\text{MB}_e > 0\), then \(\text{MB}_s > \text{MB}_p\).
  • 含義 (Implication): 某人的行為令其他人（第三方）得益，但該人無法向受益者收費。 Someone's action benefits others (third parties), but the doer cannot charge the beneficiaries for it.

3. 外部成本 (External Cost)

這是私人成本與社會成本的分歧。 This is the divergence between private and social costs.

$$ \text{Social Cost (MC}_s\text{)} = \text{Private Cost (MC}_p\text{)} + \text{External Cost (MC}_e\text{)} $$

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Answer: Option B (選項 B)

Option A: 錯誤 (Incorrect)

分析： 保安員是受僱於演奏廳的，他是交易中的參與者（僱員），並非第三方。他在工作期間觀看音樂會，這通常被視為工作的附帶利益 (fringe benefit) 或工作環境的一部分，已包含在僱傭合約的考慮中（例如工資水平）。 Analysis: The security guard is employed by the concert hall. He is a participant in the transaction (employee), not a third party. Watching the concert while working is considered a fringe benefit or part of the working conditions, which is internalized in the employment contract (e.g., wage level).

Option B: 正確 (Correct)

分析： 街頭歌手（生產者）表演音樂，遊客（第三方）在沒有付費的情況下享受了音樂（獲得利益）。
這裡存在外部利益 (External Benefit)。歌手無法向路過的遊客收費，但遊客卻獲得了滿足感。因此，社會利益（包括了遊客的利益）大於歌手的私人利益（僅指歌手自己獲得的得益）。這就是利益的分歧。 Analysis: The street singer (producer) performs music, and the tourist (third party) enjoys the music without paying (receives benefit).
There is an External Benefit. The singer cannot charge the passing tourist, yet the tourist gains satisfaction. Thus, Social Benefit (including the tourist's benefit) is greater than Private Benefit (singer's own gain). This is a divergence of benefits.

Option C: 錯誤 (Incorrect)

分析： 這是外部成本 (External Cost) 的例子，即私人成本與社會成本的分歧，而非利益的分歧。卡拉OK酒廊的運作對鄰居造成了滋擾（噪音），鄰居受損卻沒有得到賠償。 Analysis: This is an example of External Cost, which is a divergence between private and social costs, not benefits. The operation of the karaoke lounge causes nuisance (noise) to the neighbor, who suffers without compensation.

Option D: 錯誤 (Incorrect)

分析： 這是市場供需變動的結果。當機場遷離，環境改善，該區住宅的需求增加，導致市場價格上升。這種價值的上升已經透過價格機制 (Price Mechanism) 反映出來，業主透過擁有或出售資產獲得了這部分利益。既然利益已反映在價格中，便不屬於外部性。 Analysis: This is a result of changes in market demand and supply. When the airport moves, the environment improves, increasing demand for housing and raising market prices. This increase in value is reflected through the Price Mechanism. Owners capture this benefit by owning or selling the asset. Since the benefit is reflected in the price, it is not an externality.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (破題關鍵)

題目問的是「利益分歧 (Divergence of Benefits)」。你需要尋找的是：
1. 正面外部性 (Positive Externality)。
2. 涉及第三方 (Third Party)。
3. 沒有付費 (Unpaid) 的得益。

邏輯檢查 (Logic Check):

  • 看到「噪音」、「污染」 \(\rightarrow\) 通常是成本 (Cost) 分歧 \(\rightarrow\) 排除 C。 See "noise", "pollution" \(\rightarrow\) Usually Divergence of Costs \(\rightarrow\) Eliminate C.
  • 看到「市場價值上升」、「價格改變」 \(\rightarrow\) 利益已由價格反映，市場運作正常 \(\rightarrow\) 排除 D。 See "market value increases", "price change" \(\rightarrow\) Benefit is reflected in price, market is functioning \(\rightarrow\) Eliminate D.
  • 比較 A 和 B：A 是僱員（內部人士），B 是路人（外部人士）。外部性必須涉及外部人士。 Compare A and B: A is an employee (insider), B is a passerby (outsider). Externalities must involve outsiders.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

Trap 1: 混淆利益與成本 (Confusing Benefits and Costs)

學生常看到「外部性」就選 C（噪音），卻忽略了題目問的是「利益」(Benefits) 而非「成本」(Costs)。 Students often see "externality" and pick C (noise), ignoring that the question asks for "Benefits", not "Costs".

Trap 2: 誤解價格變動 (Misinterpreting Price Changes)

選項 D 是一個常見陷阱。如果環境改善令樓價上升，這只是市場對新資訊的反應。外部性的定義關鍵在於沒有透過價格機制反映的影響。如果業主能以更高價賣樓，代表他已經收回了環境改善的利益，沒有出現「分歧」。 Option D is a common trap. If environmental improvement raises flat prices, it is just the market reacting to new information. The key definition of externality is an effect not reflected via the price mechanism. If the owner can sell the flat at a higher price, they have captured the benefit, so there is no "divergence".
