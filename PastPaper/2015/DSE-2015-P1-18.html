Topic: 需求定律與艾智仁一般性概念 (Law of Demand & Alchian's Generalization) Difficulty: ★★★★☆ Key Concepts: Relative Price, Fixed Cost, Substitution

1. 題目 (The Question)

下列哪項與需求定律並不相符？

A. 中秋節期間，香港進口較多日本的優質葡萄。
B. 提高啤酒的從量稅引致小瓶啤酒在市場上的銷售數量下降。
C. 對所有旅客徵收固定金額的機場離境稅後，旅客平均飛行旅程縮短了。
D. 當提高入息稅中的子女免稅額，出生率便上升。

Which of the following is INCONSISTENT with the law of demand?

A. More high-quality Japanese grapes are imported to Hong Kong during Mid-Autumn Festival.
B. An increase of the per unit tax on beer results in a fall in the quantity of small-bottle beer sold in the market.
C. After the introduction of the airport departure tax which is a fixed amount for any traveler, the average length of airplane journeys decreases.
D. The birth rate increases when the income tax allowance for children increases.

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

需求定律 (Law of Demand)

在其他因素不變的情況下，物品的價格與其需求量呈反方向變動。
Assuming other factors remain constant (ceteris paribus), the price of a good and its quantity demanded are inversely related.

艾智仁一般性概念 (Alchian's Generalization)

當兩種替代品（如高質素與低質素物品）同時加上一個固定附加費（如運費、從量稅）時，高質素物品的相對價格 (Relative Price) 會下降，導致消費者傾向購買更多高質素物品。
When a fixed charge (e.g., transport cost, per unit tax) is added to two substitute goods (e.g., high-quality and low-quality goods), the relative price of the high-quality good decreases. Consumers will substitute towards the high-quality good.

$$ \text{Relative Price of High Quality} = \frac{P_{high} + T}{P_{low} + T} < \frac{P_{high}}{P_{low}} $$

其中 \(P_{high}\) 是高質素物品原價，\(P_{low}\) 是低質素物品原價，\(T\) 是固定費用。
Where \(P_{high}\) is the price of the high-quality good, \(P_{low}\) is the price of the low-quality good, and \(T\) is the fixed charge.

3. 答案與詳細分析 (Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): C

選項 A: 與需求定律相符 (Consistent)

日本進口葡萄包含固定的運輸成本。根據艾智仁一般性概念，加上固定運輸費後，優質葡萄的相對價格下降，因此香港會進口較多優質葡萄。

Imported grapes from Japan include a fixed transport cost. According to Alchian's generalization, adding a fixed transport cost lowers the relative price of high-quality grapes, so Hong Kong imports proportionally more of them.

選項 B: 與需求定律相符 (Consistent)

從量稅是固定稅款。加上從量稅後，大瓶啤酒（高價）的相對價格下降，小瓶啤酒（低價）的相對價格上升。消費者會以大瓶替代小瓶，令小瓶啤酒銷量下降。

A per unit tax is a fixed charge. Adding it lowers the relative price of large (expensive) bottles and raises the relative price of small (cheap) bottles. Consumers substitute large for small, decreasing the quantity of small bottles sold.

選項 C: 與需求定律不相符 (INCONSISTENT) - 正確答案

機場離境稅對所有旅客是固定金額。長途機票（高價）與短途機票（低價）同時加上固定稅款後，長途機票的相對價格會下降。根據需求定律，旅客應傾向選擇長途飛行，平均飛行旅程應增加，而非縮短。

The departure tax is a fixed amount. When added to both long (expensive) and short (cheap) journeys, the relative price of long journeys decreases. According to the law of demand, travelers should substitute towards longer journeys, meaning the average length should increase, not decrease.

選項 D: 與需求定律相符 (Consistent)

提高子女免稅額變相降低了生兒育女的「價格」（成本）。價格下降，需求量（出生率）上升，完全符合需求定律。

Increasing the tax allowance reduces the "price" (cost) of having children. A decrease in price leads to an increase in quantity demanded (birth rate), which perfectly aligns with the law of demand.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

  • 關鍵點 (Pivot Point): 留意題目中的「固定金額」(Fixed amount) 或「從量稅」(Per unit tax)。這暗示你需要運用「相對價格」(Relative Price) 的概念。
  • 解題邏輯 (Logic): 找出哪一個選項的結果與「相對價格下降導致需求量上升」的推論相反。選項 C 描述相對價格下降，但結果卻是縮短（減少），這就是矛盾所在。

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

混淆絕對價格與相對價格 (Confusing Absolute and Relative Prices)

很多學生以為加稅後所有機票都變貴了，所以人們會飛少一點（旅程縮短）。但這裡考的是相對選擇 (Relative Choice)。既然都要付那筆固定的離境稅，飛長途（本來就很貴）感覺上就「沒那麼虧」了，因此長途的比例會增加。

Many students think that since taxes make all flights more expensive, people will fly shorter distances. However, this tests relative choice. Since you must pay the fixed tax anyway, an expensive long-haul flight becomes relatively "less of a bad deal," increasing the proportion of long journeys.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼選項 B 中，小瓶啤酒的銷量一定會下降？

A: 有兩個原因。第一是「絕對價格效應」：加稅令啤酒整體變貴，總需求量下降。第二是「相對價格效應」（艾智仁一般性概念）：加固定稅令大瓶啤酒相對變便宜，消費者轉買大瓶。雙重打擊下，小瓶啤酒銷量必然下跌。
A: Two reasons. First, the absolute price effect: taxes make all beer more expensive, reducing total quantity demanded. Second, the relative price effect: the fixed tax makes large bottles relatively cheaper, causing substitution. Both effects guarantee a fall in small bottle sales.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

「固定附加費 = 買貴嘢」法則 (Fixed Charge = Buy Premium Rule)

在 MC 題中，只要看到「固定運費」、「從量稅」、「固定離境稅」，立刻聯想結論：人們會傾向選擇較高質素、較大、較長途的選項。掃描選項，如果發現有選項說人們選擇了「較低質素/較短途」，那通常就是違反定律的錯誤陳述（即本題的答案）。

In MCQs, when you see "fixed transport cost", "per unit tax", or "fixed departure tax", immediately recall the conclusion: people will favor the higher-quality, larger, or longer option. If an option states people choose the lower-quality/shorter option, it is the inconsistent statement.
