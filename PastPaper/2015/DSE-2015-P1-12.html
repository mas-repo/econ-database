HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Market Intervention (Subsidy) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Subsidy Incidence, Elasticity

The Question (題目)

政府對生產綠色產品提供$10的從量補貼，市場價格因而下降了$6。下列哪項/些有關綠色產品市場的陳述是正確的？ The government provides a per-unit subsidy of $10 on producing green products. The market price drops by $6 as a result. Which of the following statements about the green product market is/are correct?

(1) 綠色產品供應較其需求更具彈性 (1) The supply of green products is more elastic than their demand.

(2) 消費者所獲得的補貼得益比生產者多 (2) Consumers enjoy more subsidy benefit than producers.

(3) 綠色產品的需求上升 (3) The demand for green products increases.

  • A. 只有(2) (2) only
  • B. 只有(1)、(2) (1) and (2) only
  • C. 只有(1)、(3) (1) and (3) only
  • D. (1)、(2)及(3) (1), (2) and (3)

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答此題，你需要掌握以下關於補貼 (Subsidy) 的核心概念： To solve this problem, you need to master the following core concepts regarding Subsidy:

1. 補貼得益的分配 (Distribution of Subsidy Benefit)

總補貼金額會由消費者和生產者共同分享。 The total subsidy amount is shared between consumers and producers.

$$ \text{Unit Subsidy} = \text{Consumer's Share} + \text{Producer's Share} $$

  • 消費者得益 (Consumer's Share): 市場價格下降的幅度 \( |\Delta P| \)。 Consumer's Share: The magnitude of the drop in market price \( |\Delta P| \).
  • 生產者得益 (Producer's Share): 單位補貼減去消費者得益。 Producer's Share: Unit subsidy minus Consumer's Share.

2. 彈性與得益的關係 (Elasticity and Benefit)

黃金定律：彈性越低 (Inelastic) 的一方，獲得的補貼得益越多。 Golden Rule: The side with lower elasticity (more inelastic) enjoys more subsidy benefit.

\( E_d < E_s \Rightarrow \text{Consumer Share} > \text{Producer Share} \)

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案： Option B
Correct Answer: Option B

Step 1: Calculate the Shares (計算份額)

題目資料：單位補貼 = $10，價格下降 = $6。 Given: Unit Subsidy = $10, Price Drop = $6.

  • 消費者得益 (Consumer Benefit): $6 (因為價格便宜了$6) Consumer Benefit: $6 (Since price is $6 cheaper)
  • 生產者得益 (Producer Benefit): \( \$10 - \$6 = \$4 \) Producer Benefit: \( \$10 - \$6 = \$4 \)

Step 2: Evaluate Statements (評估陳述)

Statement (2): Consumers enjoy more subsidy benefit than producers.

比較兩者得益：\( \$6 > \$4 \)。消費者得益確實比生產者多。 Comparing the benefits: \( \$6 > \$4 \). Consumers indeed enjoy more benefit than producers.

✔ Statement (2) is CORRECT (正確).

Statement (1): The supply of green products is more elastic than their demand.

根據「彈性越低，得益越多」的原則： According to the principle "The lower the elasticity, the greater the benefit":

因為 消費者得益 ($6) > 生產者得益 ($4) Since Consumer Benefit ($6) > Producer Benefit ($4)

所以 需求彈性 < 供應彈性 (需求較低彈性，供應較高彈性)。 Therefore, Demand Elasticity < Supply Elasticity (Demand is less elastic, Supply is more elastic).

陳述句說「供應較需求更具彈性」，這與我們的推論一致。 The statement says "supply is more elastic than demand," which matches our deduction.

✔ Statement (1) is CORRECT (正確).

Statement (3): The demand for green products increases.

這是一個常見陷阱。補貼會降低生產成本，導致供應增加 (供應曲線向右/下移動)。 This is a common trap. A subsidy lowers production costs, causing Supply to Increase (Supply curve shifts right/down).

這會導致沿著需求曲線的移動 (Movement along the curve)，即需求量 (Quantity Demanded) 上升，而不是需求 (Demand) 上升。除非有其他非價格因素改變，否則需求曲線本身不會移動。 This leads to a movement along the demand curve, meaning Quantity Demanded increases, not Demand itself. The Demand curve does not shift unless other non-price factors change.

✘ Statement (3) is INCORRECT (錯誤).

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的關鍵樞紐 (Pivot Point) 在於迅速計算出生產者的得益。 The Pivot Point of this question types lies in quickly calculating the producer's benefit.

  1. 識別數字：看到 $10 (總數) 和 $6 (消費者部分)。 Identify Numbers: See $10 (Total) and $6 (Consumer part).
  2. 做減法：立刻算出剩餘的 $4 是生產者的。 Subtract: Immediately calculate that the remaining $4 belongs to the producer.
  3. 比大小：$6 vs $4。 Compare: $6 vs $4.
  4. 套用定律：贏家 (拿得多的) = 彈性低。 Apply Law: The Winner (who gets more) = Low Elasticity.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

⚠️ Trap: Demand vs. Quantity Demanded (需求 vs 需求量)

這是 DSE 經濟科最致命的陷阱之一。 This is one of the most fatal traps in HKDSE Economics.

  • 補貼/稅項 (Subsidy/Tax)：只會移動供應曲線 (Supply Curve)。 Subsidy/Tax: Only shifts the Supply Curve.
  • 結果：價格改變導致「需求量」改變。 Result: Price change leads to a change in "Quantity Demanded".
  • 錯誤觀念：以為價格跌了，大家多買了，就是「需求上升」。(錯！這只是點在線上移動)。 Misconception: Thinking that because price dropped and people bought more, "Demand Increased". (Wrong! This is just a movement along the line).
