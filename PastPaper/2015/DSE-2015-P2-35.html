Topic: 貨幣的功能 (Functions of Money) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: 價值儲存 (Store of Value), 購買力 (Purchasing Power)

📝 題目 (The Question)

細閱以下新聞撮要。
餘額寶是阿里巴巴新推出的網上投資基金。餘額寶受歡迎的主要原因是它可向客戶提供像活期存款般的方便，即他們可隨時提取其資金，但它的回報遠高於銀行可提供的利息。餘額寶近日宣傳指其每年回報為5.25%，但自該基金成立以來，回報水平有時高於7%。相對而言銀行一年定期存款的利率為3.3%，儲蓄存款則近乎沒有利息。

與銀行存款相比，餘額寶是較佳的_____________，因為_____________。

  1. 記帳單位 …… 它提供同質的付款方法
  2. 記帳單位 …… 它能促進人們買賣物品及服務
  3. 價值儲存 …… 它令顧客在將來有更高的購買力
  4. 價值儲存 …… 顧客可將他們未用的資金存在儲額寶

Refer to the following news extract.
Yu’e Bao (餘額寶) is a new online investment fund introduced by Alibaba. Much of the popularity of Yu’e Bao has come from its ability to give customers the convenience of a demand deposit – meaning they can withdraw their funds whenever they like – but with returns well above what a bank could offer. It recently advertised an annualised return of 5.25%, but that level has on occasion risen to above 7% since the fund was set up. By contrast, banks offer 3.3% as interest for a one-year fixed deposit and close to nothing on savings deposits.

Yu’e Bao is a better ___________ as compared with bank deposits because ________.

  1. unit of account …… it provides a homogeneous means of payment
  2. unit of account …… it can facilitate people’s buying and selling of goods and services
  3. store of value …… it allows customers to have higher purchasing power in the future
  4. store of value …… the customers can keep their unused funds in Yu’e Bao

📚 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

貨幣的功能 (Functions of Money)

要解答這道題目，我們必須清楚區分貨幣的四大功能：

  • 交易媒介 (Medium of Exchange): 普遍被接受作為支付物品和服務的工具。
  • 記帳單位 (Unit of Account): 用作標示價格和計算價值的標準。
  • 價值儲存 (Store of Value): 將目前的購買力保留到將來使用。
  • 延期支付標準 (Standard of Deferred Payment): 用作計算未來還款的單位。

To answer this question, we must clearly distinguish the four functions of money:

  • Medium of Exchange: Generally accepted as a tool for paying for goods and services.
  • Unit of Account: Used as a standard to quote prices and calculate value.
  • Store of Value: Retaining current purchasing power for future use.
  • Standard of Deferred Payment: Used as a unit to calculate future debt repayments.

購買力與回報 (Purchasing Power and Returns)

作為「價值儲存」工具，其優劣取決於它能否維持甚至增加未來的購買力。回報率（利率）越高，未來的購買力就越高。

$$ \text{Future Purchasing Power} \propto (1 + \text{Nominal Return Rate}) $$

As a "store of value", its effectiveness depends on whether it can maintain or increase future purchasing power. The higher the rate of return (interest rate), the higher the future purchasing power.

✅ 答案與詳細分析 (Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): C

選項 A & B (Options A & B): ❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect)

題目中的新聞撮要強調的是「回報率」(5.25% - 7%) 和「利息」，這與標示價格無關。因此，這不是關於「記帳單位」的功能。

The news extract emphasizes "returns" (5.25% - 7%) and "interest", which has nothing to do with quoting prices. Therefore, this is not about the "unit of account" function.

選項 C (Option C): ✅ 正確 (Correct)

餘額寶提供較高的回報率（高達7%），而銀行存款的利率較低（3.3%或接近零）。較高的回報意味著資金的增長速度更快，從而令顧客在將來擁有更高的購買力。這正正解釋了為什麼它是「較佳的」價值儲存工具。

Yu'e Bao offers a higher rate of return (up to 7%), while bank deposits have lower interest rates (3.3% or close to zero). A higher return means funds grow faster, allowing customers to have higher purchasing power in the future. This perfectly explains why it is a "better" store of value.

選項 D (Option D): ❌ 錯誤 (Incorrect)

雖然餘額寶和銀行存款都可以讓顧客存放未用的資金（兩者都是價值儲存工具），但這個選項沒有解釋為什麼餘額寶是「較佳的」(better)。「較佳」的原因在於回報率的差異，而不是單純因為它可以存放資金。

Although both Yu'e Bao and bank deposits allow customers to keep unused funds (both are stores of value), this option fails to explain why Yu'e Bao is "better". The reason it is better lies in the difference in returns, not simply because it can store funds.

🔍 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point):
題目中的關鍵詞是「較佳的」(better) 和「回報遠高於銀行」(returns well above what a bank could offer)。這要求我們找出兩者之間的差異點，並將該差異點與貨幣的特定功能連繫起來。

Pivot Point:
The keywords in the question are "better" and "returns well above what a bank could offer". This requires us to identify the difference between the two and link that difference to a specific function of money.

⚠️ 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

很多學生會誤選 D。選項 D 的陳述本身是正確的（顧客確實可以將未用資金存入），但它犯了「答非所問」的錯誤。在比較題型中，原因必須能解釋「優勢」(advantage)，而選項 D 只是描述了兩者的「共通點」。

Many students mistakenly choose D. The statement in Option D is factually correct (customers can indeed keep unused funds), but it fails to answer the specific question. In comparison questions, the reason must explain the "advantage", whereas Option D only describes a "commonality" between both options.

🙋 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

問 (Q): 題目提到「像活期存款般的方便，即他們可隨時提取其資金」，這不代表它是交易媒介 (Medium of Exchange) 嗎？

答 (A): 隨時提取資金確實增加了流動性，方便日後作交易之用。但請注意，選項中並沒有提供「交易媒介」這個選擇。此外，整段文字的重點對比在於 \( 5.25\% \) 甚至 \( 7\% \) 的回報率與銀行 \( 3.3\% \) 的對比，這明確指向了購買力的增長（價值儲存）。

Q: The text mentions "the convenience of a demand deposit... withdraw their funds whenever they like". Doesn't this mean it's a Medium of Exchange?

A: Being able to withdraw funds anytime does increase liquidity for future transactions. However, notice that "Medium of Exchange" is not even provided in the options. Furthermore, the core contrast in the text is between the \( 5.25\% \) or \( 7\% \) return versus the bank's \( 3.3\% \), which directly points to the growth of purchasing power (Store of Value).

⚡ 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

對比法 (Contrast Method): 當題目出現「較佳」(better)、「相比」(compared with) 等字眼時，立刻在文中尋找對比數據。此題的對比數據是「利率/回報」。利息/回報永遠與「價值儲存」(Store of Value) 和「購買力」(Purchasing Power) 直接掛鈎。看到利息差異，直接秒選包含「購買力」的「價值儲存」選項。

Contrast Method: When you see words like "better" or "compared with", immediately look for contrasting data in the text. Here, the contrasting data is "interest/returns". Interest/returns are ALWAYS directly linked to "Store of Value" and "Purchasing Power". Seeing the interest difference allows you to instantly select the "Store of Value" option that mentions "purchasing power".
