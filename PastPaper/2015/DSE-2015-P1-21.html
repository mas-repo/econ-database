HKDSE Economics: Question Analysis

Topic (課題): Income Inequality & Taxation (收入不均與稅制)

Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆ (3/5)

Key Concepts (核心概念): Gini Coefficient (堅尼系數), Regressive Tax (累退稅), Income Distribution (收入分配)

📝 The Question (題目)

假設某經濟除稅後的堅尼系數比稅前的堅尼系數高，這意味著

  1. 政府增加了給窮人的轉移支付的金額。
  2. 該經濟的稅制很可能是累退性的。
  3. 收入不均的問題比去年差。
  4. 政府應該改革稅制。

Suppose the post-tax Gini coefficient of an economy is larger than its pre-tax Gini coefficient. This implies that

  1. the government has increased the amount of transfer payment to the poor.
  2. the tax system of the economy is likely to be regressive.
  3. the problem of income inequality is worse than that in the previous year.
  4. the government should reform the tax system.

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. Gini Coefficient (堅尼系數)

堅尼系數是量度收入不均的指標，數值介乎 0 至 1 之間。數值越大，代表收入分配越不平均。

The Gini coefficient is a measure of income inequality, ranging from 0 to 1. A higher value indicates greater income inequality.

$$ 0 \le \text{Gini Coefficient} \le 1 $$

\( \text{Gini} \uparrow \implies \text{Inequality} \uparrow \)

2. Types of Taxes & Income Distribution (稅制與收入分配)

  • Progressive Tax (累進稅): 稅率隨收入增加而上升。有助拉近貧富懸殊，令堅尼系數下降。
    Tax rate increases as income increases. Reduces inequality, lowers Gini.
  • Regressive Tax (累退稅): 稅率隨收入增加而下降（或低收入人士的稅務負擔比例較重）。會加劇貧富懸殊，令堅尼系數上升。
    Tax burden falls more heavily on low-income earners. Worsens inequality, raises Gini.

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Answer (正確答案): Option B

A

錯誤： 增加對窮人的轉移支付會令低收入人士的可用收入增加，從而改善收入不均，這會導致堅尼系數下降，而非上升。

Incorrect: Increasing transfer payments to the poor raises their disposable income, which improves income equality. This would cause the Gini coefficient to decrease, not increase.

B

正確： 題目指出「除稅後的堅尼系數 > 稅前的堅尼系數」，代表徵稅後收入不均惡化了。累退稅（Regressive tax）正是會對低收入人士造成較重負擔，導致徵稅後貧富差距擴大的稅制。

Correct: The question states "post-tax Gini > pre-tax Gini", meaning taxation worsened income inequality. A regressive tax places a heavier proportional burden on lower-income earners, thereby widening the wealth gap after taxes.

C

錯誤： 題目只比較了同一年內「稅前」與「稅後」的數據，並沒有提供任何關於「去年」的數據，因此無法作出跨時間的比較。

Incorrect: The question only compares "pre-tax" and "post-tax" data within the same period. It provides no data regarding the "previous year", so a time-series comparison cannot be made.

D

錯誤： 「政府應該改革稅制」屬於規範性陳述（Normative statement），涉及價值判斷。堅尼系數上升只是一個客觀事實（實證性陳述 Positive statement），不能直接推導出政府「應該」做什麼。

Incorrect: "The government should reform the tax system" is a normative statement involving value judgments. An increase in the Gini coefficient is a positive (objective) fact, which cannot logically dictate what the government "should" do.

🔍 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point): 釐清「堅尼系數上升」的含義。

Pivot Point: Clarify the meaning of an "increase in the Gini coefficient".

  • Step 1: Post-tax Gini > Pre-tax Gini \(\implies\) Inequality worsened after paying taxes.
  • Step 2: What causes inequality to worsen? \(\implies\) A tax system that hurts the poor more than the rich (Regressive Tax).

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 誤解堅尼系數的數值意義： 很多學生誤以為堅尼系數像考試分數一樣「越高越好」。緊記：堅尼系數越高，代表社會越不公平。

    Misinterpreting the Gini scale: Students often think a higher Gini is "better". Remember: Higher Gini = Higher Inequality.

  • 混淆實證與規範陳述 (Option D)： 經濟學選擇題中，除非題目特別問及價值觀，否則客觀數據（如堅尼系數）不能推導出「應該 (should)」的結論。

    Confusing Positive and Normative statements: Objective data cannot lead to a "should" conclusion in standard economic logic.

🙋 Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果題目改成「除稅後的堅尼系數比稅前低」，答案會是什麼？

Q: What if the question said "post-tax Gini is lower than pre-tax Gini"?

A: 這代表稅制成功拉近了貧富懸殊，改善了收入不均。這意味著該經濟的稅制很可能是累進性的 (Progressive)。
This means the tax system successfully reduced the wealth gap. It would imply the tax system is likely progressive.

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

秒殺技巧： 看到「堅尼系數上升」立刻聯想「惡化/不公平」。選項中只有 B (累退稅) 是導致不公平惡化的因素。A 是改善，C 是無關時間線，D 是規範性廢話。直接選 B！

Shortcut: "Higher Gini" = "Worse inequality". Look for the option that causes worse inequality. A improves it, C is wrong timeframe, D is normative. Choose B instantly!
