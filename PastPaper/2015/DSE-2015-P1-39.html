📝 The Question (題目)

某經濟出現嚴重通脹和收入不均。政府可以藉_____________以減低物價水平和收窄收入差距。

A. 增加處理住戶污水的環境稅
B. 提高薪俸稅及其累進程度
C. 減少它在社會福利的開支
D. 減低薪俸稅的個人免稅額

In an economy with severe inflation and income inequality, the government can lower the price level and reduce the income gap by

A. increasing the environmental tax on handling household sewage.
B. increasing the salaries tax and its progressivity.
C. reducing its spending on social welfare.
D. reducing the personal allowance of the salaries tax.

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 應對通脹 (Tackling Inflation):
要降低物價水平，政府需要實施緊縮性財政政策 (Contractionary Fiscal Policy)，即減少政府開支 \(G\) 或增加稅收 \(T\)。這會導致總需求 \(AD\) 下降，從而降低物價水平。
To lower the price level, the government needs to implement a contractionary fiscal policy, which means decreasing government spending \(G\) or increasing taxes \(T\). This reduces Aggregate Demand \(AD\), thereby lowering the price level.
$$ AD = C + I + G + (X - M) $$
2. 收窄收入差距 (Reducing Income Gap):
要改善收入不均，政策必須具有財富再分配 (Wealth Redistribution) 的效果，將財富從富人轉移到窮人。例如：增加累進稅 (Progressive Tax) 或增加對低收入人士的社會福利。
To improve income inequality, policies must have a wealth redistribution effect, transferring wealth from the rich to the poor. Examples include increasing progressive taxes or increasing social welfare for low-income earners.

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): Option B

A. 增加處理住戶污水的環境稅 (❌ 錯誤)
雖然加稅能減少可支配收入並降低 \(AD\)（有助控制通脹），但環境稅通常是累退的 (Regressive)。因為低收入家庭將更大比例的收入用於基本生活開支（如排污），這會擴大收入差距。
A. increasing the environmental tax... (❌ Incorrect)
While increasing taxes reduces disposable income and lowers \(AD\) (helping control inflation), environmental taxes are generally regressive. Low-income households spend a larger proportion of their income on basic necessities (like sewage), so this would widen the income gap.
B. 提高薪俸稅及其累進程度 (✅ 正確)
提高薪俸稅會減少市民的可支配收入，導致消費 \(C\) 下降，總需求 \(AD\) 減少，從而降低物價水平。同時，提高「累進程度」意味著高收入人士需要繳交更大比例的稅款，這有效地將財富再分配，收窄收入差距。完全符合題目兩個要求。
B. increasing the salaries tax and its progressivity. (✅ Correct)
Increasing salaries tax reduces disposable income, leading to lower consumption \(C\) and lower \(AD\), which lowers the price level. Meanwhile, increasing "progressivity" means high-income earners pay a larger percentage of their income in taxes. This effectively redistributes wealth and reduces the income gap. It perfectly satisfies both conditions.
C. 減少它在社會福利的開支 (❌ 錯誤)
減少政府開支 \(G\) 確實能降低 \(AD\) 和物價水平。但是，社會福利主要惠及低收入人士。削減福利會對窮人造成更大打擊，從而惡化收入不均。
C. reducing its spending on social welfare. (❌ Incorrect)
Reducing government spending \(G\) does lower \(AD\) and the price level. However, social welfare primarily benefits low-income earners. Cutting it hits the poor harder, thereby worsening income inequality.
D. 減低薪俸稅的個人免稅額 (❌ 錯誤)
減低免稅額會令更多低收入人士跌入稅網（原本不用交稅的現在要交）。這對低收入人士的稅務負擔增加比例大於高收入人士，因此在效果上是累退的，會擴大稅後收入差距。
D. reducing the personal allowance of the salaries tax. (❌ Incorrect)
Reducing the personal allowance brings more low-income earners into the tax net (those previously exempt now have to pay). This increases the tax burden on the poor proportionally more than on the rich, making it regressive in effect and widening the after-tax income gap.

🔍 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (解題關鍵):
這是一條「雙條件」題目。你必須同時滿足兩個目標：
1. 減低物價水平 \(\rightarrow\) 需要減少總需求 (AD)。
2. 收窄收入差距 \(\rightarrow\) 需要劫富濟貧（累進稅或增加基層福利）。
所有選項 A, B, C, D 都能達到目標 1（它們都是緊縮性政策）。因此，真正的決勝點在於目標 2（收入分配）。
Pivot Point:
This is a "dual-condition" question. You must satisfy two goals simultaneously:
1. Lower price level \(\rightarrow\) Requires reducing Aggregate Demand (AD).
2. Reduce income gap \(\rightarrow\) Requires taking from the rich to help the poor (progressive tax or pro-poor welfare).
All options A, B, C, and D achieve Goal 1 (they are all contractionary policies). Therefore, the real deciding factor is Goal 2 (Income Distribution).

🙋‍♂️ Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼減少「個人免稅額」(Option D) 不能收窄收入差距？大家不是都多交了稅嗎？
A: 雖然大家都多交了稅，但我們要看的是「比例」。假設免稅額減少 $10,000。對於一個月入 $15,000 的基層市民，這可能意味著他原本不用交稅，現在突然要交，佔他收入的比例很大。但對於月入 $100,000 的富人來說，這額外的稅款只佔他收入的極小部分。因此，這會拉闊稅後的貧富懸殊。
Q: Why doesn't reducing the "personal allowance" (Option D) narrow the income gap? Doesn't everyone pay more tax?
A: While everyone pays more tax, we must look at the "proportion". Suppose the allowance is reduced by $10,000. For a low-income earner, they might go from paying zero tax to paying some tax, which is a large percentage of their small income. For a rich person, this extra tax is a tiny fraction of their income. Thus, it widens the after-tax wealth gap.

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

雙條件過濾法 (Dual-Condition Filtering):
遇到這類題目，先挑選最容易判斷的條件。判斷「收入分配」通常比判斷「宏觀經濟影響」更直觀。
- A (環境稅) -> 累退 -> 擴大差距 (剔除)
- C (減福利) -> 損害窮人 -> 擴大差距 (剔除)
- D (減免稅額) -> 窮人受害較深 -> 擴大差距 (剔除)
只需 10 秒，你就可以靠第二個條件直接鎖定答案 B！
Dual-Condition Filtering:
For these questions, start with the condition that is easiest to evaluate. Judging "income distribution" is usually more intuitive than "macroeconomic effects".
- A (Environmental tax) -> Regressive -> Widens gap (Eliminate)
- C (Cut welfare) -> Hurts poor -> Widens gap (Eliminate)
- D (Cut allowance) -> Hurts poor proportionally more -> Widens gap (Eliminate)
In just 10 seconds, you can lock in Option B using only the second condition!
