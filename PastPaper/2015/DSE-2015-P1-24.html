1. 題目 (The Question)

下列哪項會包括在2014年香港本地生產總值的計算之內？
Which of the following would be included in the calculation of Hong Kong’s GDP for 2014?
  • A. 2014年政府支付的長者生活津貼
    the old age allowance paid by the government in 2014
  • B. 2013年建成但在2014年出售的樓宇的市場價值
    the market value of a flat built in 2013 but sold in 2014
  • C. 2014年一名在二手手袋店工作的售貨員的薪金
    the salary of a salesperson working in a shop selling second-hand handbags in 2014
  • D. 2014年香港本地常住生產單位所擁有的加拿大物業的租金收入
    the rental income from a property in Canada owned by a Hong Kong resident producing unit in 2014

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

本地生產總值 (Gross Domestic Product, GDP)

本地生產總值是指一個國家或地區在指定期間內（通常為一年），於其經濟領土內所生產的全部最終物品及服務的總市價。
GDP is the total market value of all final goods and services currently produced within the territorial boundary of a country or region in a specified period (usually a year).

四大計算原則 (Four Key Principles):

  1. 現期生產 (Current Production): 必須是當年製造的產品或提供的服務。過去生產的物品（如二手貨）不計入當年的 GDP。
    Must be produced in the current year. Past production (e.g., second-hand goods) is excluded.
  2. 境內生產 (Territorial Boundary): 必須在該地區的地理範圍內生產，無論生產者是本地人還是外國人。
    Must be produced within the geographical boundary, regardless of the producer's nationality.
  3. 涉及生產 (Involves Production): 必須有實質的物品或服務產出。轉移支付（如政府津貼、利是）沒有生產貢獻，因此不計入。
    Must involve actual production of goods/services. Transfer payments (e.g., allowances) are excluded.
  4. 最終物品 (Final Goods): 只計算最終物品的價值，以避免重複計算 (Double Counting)。
    Only the value of final goods is counted to avoid double counting.

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

選項 A (Option A) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
長者生活津貼屬於單向轉移支付 (Transfer Payment)。政府派發津貼時，長者並沒有提供任何相應的物品或服務作為回報，即沒有涉及當年的生產活動，因此不計入 GDP。
The old age allowance is a transfer payment. When the government distributes the allowance, the elderly do not provide any goods or services in return. Since no current production is involved, it is excluded from the GDP.
選項 B (Option B) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
該樓宇是在 2013 年建成的，其市場價值已經計入了 2013 年的 GDP。如果將其市場價值計入 2014 年的 GDP，就會違反「現期生產」的原則。注意：如果在 2014 年出售時涉及地產經紀佣金，該佣金則會計入 2014 年 GDP，但題目問的是整棟樓宇的「市場價值」。
The flat was built in 2013, so its market value was already included in the 2013 GDP. Including its market value in 2014 would violate the "current production" principle. Note: If there was a real estate agent commission during the 2014 sale, that commission would be included in 2014, but the option specifies the "market value" of the flat.
選項 C (Option C) - 正確 (Correct)
雖然手袋是「二手」的（手袋本身的價值不計入 2014 年 GDP），但售貨員在 2014 年提供了現期的銷售服務。售貨員的薪金反映了他在 2014 年提供服務的生產價值，因此這筆薪金必須計入 2014 年的 GDP。
Although the handbags are "second-hand" (the value of the bags themselves is excluded from 2014 GDP), the salesperson is providing a current sales service in 2014. The salary reflects the value of the productive service provided in 2014, so it must be included in the 2014 GDP.
選項 D (Option D) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
該物業位於加拿大，即香港的經濟領土之外。根據 GDP 的「境內生產」原則，這筆租金收入應計入加拿大的 GDP。不過，由於擁有者是香港常住居民，這筆收入會計入香港的本地居民總收入 (GNI / GNP)，而非 GDP。
The property is located in Canada, which is outside Hong Kong's economic territory. According to the territorial principle of GDP, this rental income belongs to Canada's GDP. However, since the owner is a HK resident, it would be included in Hong Kong's Gross National Income (GNI / GNP), not GDP.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point):
這類題目的核心在於測試你對 GDP 定義中三個關鍵詞的敏感度：時間 (2014年)、地點 (香港)、生產 (實質服務/物品)。
The core of this question tests your sensitivity to three keywords in the GDP definition: Time (2014), Location (Hong Kong), and Production (actual goods/services).
干擾資訊 (Distractors):
選項 C 中的「二手 (second-hand)」是一個經典陷阱。考評局故意用這個詞來誤導學生，讓學生以為「二手物品不計入 GDP，所以整個選項都不計」。關鍵是要區分「二手物品的價值」與「買賣二手物品所涉及的現期服務價值」。
The word "second-hand" in Option C is a classic trap. The HKEAA uses this to mislead students into thinking "second-hand goods are excluded, so the whole option is excluded." The key is to distinguish between the "value of the second-hand good" and the "value of current services involved in trading it."

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

  • 混淆 GDP 與 GNI (Confusing GDP and GNI): 學生常忘記 GDP 只看「地理位置 (Territory)」，而 GNI 才看「居民身份 (Resident Status)」。看到選項 D 的「香港常住生產單位」，就很容易誤選。
    Students often forget that GDP is strictly about "Territory", while GNI is about "Resident Status". Seeing "Hong Kong resident" in Option D easily leads to a wrong choice.
  • 忽略服務的生產 (Ignoring the production of services): 以為只有製造出實體物品才算生產。實際上，零售、經紀、運輸等無形服務 \(S\) 同樣對當年的 GDP 有貢獻。
    Assuming only the creation of physical goods counts as production. In reality, intangible services \(S\) like retail, brokerage, and transport also contribute to the current year's GDP.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 如果選項 B 改成「2014年出售該2013年建成的樓宇時，地產經紀所收取的佣金」，答案會改變嗎？
Q: If Option B was changed to "the commission charged by the estate agent when selling the flat built in 2013 during 2014", would the answer change?
A: 會的！如果是「經紀佣金」，就會計入 2014 年的 GDP。因為經紀在 2014 年付出了勞力，提供了配對買賣雙方的「現期服務」。這與選項 C 中售貨員的薪金原理完全相同。
A: Yes! If it were the "agent's commission", it WOULD be included in the 2014 GDP. This is because the agent exerted effort and provided a "current service" of matching the buyer and seller in 2014. The principle is exactly the same as the salesperson's salary in Option C.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

「關鍵字剔除法」 (Keyword Elimination Method):
在做 GDP 計算的 MC 題時，拿著筆圈出致命關鍵字，可以秒殺錯誤選項：
  • 看到「津貼 (allowance)」、「援助金」、「利是」 ➔ 轉移支付 ➔ ❌ 剔除
  • 看到「[往年] 建成 / 生產」 ➔ 非現期生產 ➔ ❌ 剔除
  • 看到「外國 / 境外 (如加拿大)」 ➔ 非境內生產 ➔ ❌ 剔除
剩下的 C 選項，即使有「二手」這個字，但主體是「薪金 (salary)」，代表勞工服務，自然是正確答案！
Keyword Elimination Method:
When doing GDP MC questions, circle the fatal keywords to instantly eliminate wrong options:
  • See "allowance", "aid", "red packet" ➔ Transfer payment ➔ ❌ Eliminate
  • See "built/produced in [past year]" ➔ Not current production ➔ ❌ Eliminate
  • See "foreign country (e.g., Canada)" ➔ Not domestic production ➔ ❌ Eliminate
The remaining Option C, despite having the word "second-hand", focuses on "salary", which represents labor service. Thus, it must be the correct answer!
