Topic / 課題 Labour Market - Labour Mobility (勞工市場 - 勞工流動性)

Difficulty / 難度 ★★☆☆☆ (2/5 Stars)

Key Concepts / 核心概念 Occupational Mobility (職業流動性), Geographical Mobility (地域流動性)

1. The Question (題目)

若_________，勞工的_________流動性會下降。

  1. 政府在所有火車站推行「城市單車租賃計劃」 …… 地域
  2. 數家大公司在其辦公室設立幼兒託管中心 …… 地域
  3. 政府所提供的交通津貼因財政赤字而減少 …… 職業
  4. 政府在更多的行業規定從業員須具備執業牌照 …… 職業

The _________ mobility of labour will decrease if _________.

  1. geographical … the government introduces “city-bike rental scheme” in all train stations
  2. geographical … several big companies set up child care centres in their offices
  3. occupational … the travelling subsidy provided by the government is reduced due to a budget deficit
  4. occupational … licences are required by the government for more professions

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這條題目，我們必須清楚區分兩種勞工流動性：

To answer this question, we must clearly distinguish between the two types of labour mobility:

  • 職業流動性 (Occupational Mobility): 勞工改變其職業或行業的容易程度。影響因素包括：技能要求、教育水平、執業牌照規定等。若轉行成本 \(C_{occ}\) 增加，流動性便會下降。
    The ease with which workers can change their jobs or professions. Factors include: skill requirements, education level, licensing requirements, etc. If the cost of changing jobs \(C_{occ}\) increases, mobility decreases.
  • 地域流動性 (Geographical Mobility): 勞工改變其工作地點的容易程度。影響因素包括：交通時間 \(T\)、交通費用、房屋成本、家庭束縛等。若交通成本上升，流動性便會下降。
    The ease with which workers can change their work locations. Factors include: transportation time \(T\), travel costs, housing costs, family ties, etc. If transport costs rise, mobility decreases.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): D

Option A (錯誤 Incorrect)

推行單車租賃計劃會降低勞工往返工作地點的交通時間與成本。因此，地域流動性會上升，而非下降。

Introducing a bike rental scheme reduces the commuting time and cost for workers. Therefore, geographical mobility will increase, not decrease.

Option B (錯誤 Incorrect)

在辦公室設立幼兒託管中心減輕了父母的家庭束縛，使他們更容易接受該地點的工作。這會令地域流動性上升。

Setting up child care centres in offices reduces family ties/constraints for parents, making it easier for them to work in that location. This increases geographical mobility.

Option C (錯誤 Incorrect)

減少交通津貼會增加跨區工作的交通成本，這確實會導致流動性下降，但這是地域流動性下降，而不是職業流動性。

Reducing travelling subsidies increases the transport cost of working across districts. This indeed decreases mobility, but it is a decrease in geographical mobility, not occupational mobility.

Option D (正確 Correct)

政府規定更多行業需要執業牌照，這增加了進入新行業的門檻（如需要額外考試或進修）。轉行的難度增加，因此職業流動性下降。完全符合題目要求。

Requiring licences for more professions increases the barrier to entry for new industries (e.g., needing extra exams or study). The difficulty of changing jobs increases, hence occupational mobility decreases. This perfectly matches the question.

4. Problem Decomposition & Exam Tricks (題目拆解與考試技巧)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point): 處理這類「配對題」時，最佳策略是「先看事件，後配種類與方向」。

Strategy: For this type of matching question, the best strategy is to "look at the event first, then match the type and direction".

  1. 閱讀後半部的「事件」。(Read the "event" in the second half.)
  2. 判斷該事件影響的是「轉行」(職業) 還是「轉地區」(地域)。(Determine if it affects changing jobs or changing locations.)
  3. 判斷該事件是令轉變「更容易」(上升) 還是「更困難」(下降)。(Determine if it makes the change easier/increase or harder/decrease.)

題目要求尋找「流動性下降」的選項。A和B明顯令流動性上升，可即時剔除 (Eliminate A and B immediately as they increase mobility)。C的事件影響地域，但選項寫職業，所以錯。D的事件影響職業，且增加難度，所以正確。

5. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼選項 C 的交通津貼減少不會影響職業流動性？
Why doesn't the reduction in travel subsidy in Option C affect occupational mobility?

A: 職業流動性關注的是「從事另一種工作的技能或資格門檻」。交通津貼只影響你「去哪裡上班」的成本，並不影響你「有沒有能力做另一份工作」。例如，沒有交通津貼不會令一個會計師突然失去轉做教師的資格，它只會令他不願意去較遠的學校教書。
Occupational mobility focuses on the "skill or qualification barriers to taking up a different job". Travel subsidies only affect the cost of "where you go to work", not "whether you have the ability to do another job". For example, a lack of travel subsidy won't make an accountant lose the qualifications to become a teacher; it only makes them less willing to travel to a distant school to teach.
