3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解決這類題目，我們必須熟記宏觀經濟學中關於貨幣的幾個核心定義與公式：

To solve this type of question, we must memorize several core definitions and formulas regarding money in macroeconomics:
  • 貨幣供應 (Money Supply, \(M_s\)): 公眾持有的現金 (\(C_p\)) 加上 銀行存款 (\(D\))。
    Cash held by public (\(C_p\)) plus Bank Deposits (\(D\)).
  • 貨幣基礎 (Monetary Base, \(MB\)): 公眾持有的現金 (\(C_p\)) 加上 銀行儲備 (\(R\))。
    Cash held by public (\(C_p\)) plus Bank Reserves (\(R\)).
  • 超額儲備 (Excess Reserves, \(ER\)): 實際儲備 (\(R\)) 減去 法定儲備 (\(RR\))。
    Actual Reserves (\(R\)) minus Required Reserves (\(RR\)).
$$ M_s = C_p + D $$ $$ MB = C_p + R $$ $$ ER = R - (D \times rrr) $$

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案是 Option B。

The correct answer is Option B.

Step-by-Step Analysis (逐步分析):

步驟 1: 找出新的存款總額 (\(D_{new}\))
已知貨幣供應增加500 (\(\Delta M_s = 500\))，公眾現金增加100 (\(\Delta C_p = 100\))。根據 \( \Delta M_s = \Delta C_p + \Delta D \)，我們得出 \( 500 = 100 + \Delta D \)，即存款增加了400。原本存款為1000，所以新存款 \( D_{new} = 1000 + 400 = 1400 \)。

Step 1: Find the new total deposits (\(D_{new}\))
Given the money supply increases by 500 (\(\Delta M_s = 500\)) and cash held by public increases by 100 (\(\Delta C_p = 100\)). From \( \Delta M_s = \Delta C_p + \Delta D \), we get \( 500 = 100 + \Delta D \), meaning deposits increased by 400. Original deposits were 1000, so new deposits \( D_{new} = 1000 + 400 = 1400 \).

步驟 2: 找出新的儲備總額 (\(R_{new}\))
央行發行200新鈔票，代表貨幣基礎增加200 (\(\Delta MB = 200\))。根據 \( \Delta MB = \Delta C_p + \Delta R \)，我們得出 \( 200 = 100 + \Delta R \)，即儲備增加了100。原本儲備為300，所以新儲備 \( R_{new} = 300 + 100 = 400 \)。

Step 2: Find the new total reserves (\(R_{new}\))
The central bank issuing 200 in new banknotes means the monetary base increases by 200 (\(\Delta MB = 200\)). From \( \Delta MB = \Delta C_p + \Delta R \), we get \( 200 = 100 + \Delta R \), meaning reserves increased by 100. Original reserves were 300, so new reserves \( R_{new} = 300 + 100 = 400 \).

步驟 3: 計算超額儲備 (\(ER\))
新的法定儲備 \( RR_{new} = D_{new} \times 20\% = 1400 \times 0.2 = 280 \)。
新的超額儲備 \( ER_{new} = R_{new} - RR_{new} = 400 - 280 = 120 \)。

Step 3: Calculate Excess Reserves (\(ER\))
New required reserves \( RR_{new} = D_{new} \times 20\% = 1400 \times 0.2 = 280 \).
New excess reserves \( ER_{new} = R_{new} - RR_{new} = 400 - 280 = 120 \).

5. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

你可以直接利用「變量 (\(\Delta\))」來計算超額儲備的變化，再加上原本的超額儲備：
原本的 \( ER = 300 - (1000 \times 0.2) = 100 \)。
儲備變化 \(\Delta R = \Delta MB - \Delta C_p = 200 - 100 = 100 \)。
存款變化 \(\Delta D = \Delta M_s - \Delta C_p = 500 - 100 = 400 \)。
法定儲備變化 \(\Delta RR = 400 \times 0.2 = 80 \)。
超額儲備變化 \(\Delta ER = \Delta R - \Delta RR = 100 - 80 = 20 \)。
最終 \( ER = 100 + 20 = 120 \)。

You can directly use the changes (\(\Delta\)) to calculate the change in excess reserves, then add it to the original excess reserves. Original \( ER = 100 \). \(\Delta R = 100\), \(\Delta D = 400\). \(\Delta RR = 80\). \(\Delta ER = 100 - 80 = 20\). Final \( ER = 100 + 20 = 120 \).
