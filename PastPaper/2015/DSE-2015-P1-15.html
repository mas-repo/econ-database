DSE 經濟學題解分析

DSE Economics Question Analysis

Topic: 需求與供應 (Demand & Supply) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Keywords: 需求移動 (Shift in Demand), 替代品 (Substitutes)

1. 題目 (The Question)

[中文]
智能電話的供應曲線是SS。下列哪項原因最能解釋智能電話市場的均衡位置由A改變至B？
A. 零售商向購買智能電話的顧客提供更多贈品。
B. 具備電話功能的平板電腦的體積縮小了。
C. 市場上有更多智能電話生產商。
D. 主要的流動網絡服務供應商同時提高他們的服務收費。
[English]
SS is the supply curve for smartphones. Which of the following reasons can best explain the change of the equilibrium position in the smartphone market from A to B?
A. The retailers offer more free gifts for buying smartphones.
B. The size of a tablet computer with a phone function is reduced.
C. There are more smartphone producers in the market.
D. The major network service providers increase the service charge at the same time.

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

沿著曲線的移動 vs. 曲線的移動
Movement along a curve vs. Shift of a curve

在解答這條題目之前，我們必須區分兩個重要概念：

  • 沿著供應曲線的移動 (Movement along the supply curve): 這是由於物品本身的價格 (Price) 改變所引起的供應量 (Quantity supplied) 改變。整條供應曲線沒有移動。
  • 供應曲線的移動 (Shift of the supply curve): 這是由價格以外的因素 (Non-price factors) 引起的，例如生產成本改變或生產者數目改變。

Before answering this question, we must distinguish between two important concepts:

  • Movement along the supply curve: This is caused by a change in the price of the good itself, leading to a change in quantity supplied. The curve itself does not shift.
  • Shift of the supply curve: This is caused by non-price factors, such as changes in production costs or the number of producers.

圖表分析 (Graphical Analysis)

觀察圖表，點 A 移動到點 B 是沿著同一條供應曲線 (SS) 向上移動。這意味著：

均衡價格 \( (P) \) 上升，同時 均衡交易量 \( (Q) \) 增加。
$$ P \uparrow, Q \uparrow $$

要導致這種現象，唯一的可能是需求增加 (Increase in Demand)，即需求曲線向右移動，而供應曲線保持不變。

Observing the graph, the movement from point A to point B is an upward movement along the same supply curve (SS). This means that both the equilibrium price \( (P) \) and equilibrium quantity \( (Q) \) have increased. The only way to cause this outcome is an increase in demand (the demand curve shifts to the right) while the supply curve remains constant.

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): A

A. 零售商向購買智能電話的顧客提供更多贈品。
The retailers offer more free gifts for buying smartphones.

提供更多贈品等同於增加了購買智能電話的邊際利益 (Marginal Benefit) 或變相減價。這會吸引更多消費者購買，導致對智能電話的需求增加（需求曲線向右移）。在供應不變的情況下，這會令價格上升，交易量增加，這完美吻合圖中沿著供應曲線由 A 移至 B 的現象。

Offering more free gifts effectively increases the marginal benefit of buying a smartphone (or acts as an implicit price discount). This attracts more consumers, leading to an increase in demand (the demand curve shifts to the right). With supply remaining constant, this leads to a higher price and higher quantity, which perfectly matches the movement from A to B along the supply curve shown in the graph.

B. 具備電話功能的平板電腦的體積縮小了。
The size of a tablet computer with a phone function is reduced.

這類平板電腦是智能電話的替代品 (Substitute)。體積縮小令平板電腦變得更方便、更具吸引力，因此對平板電腦的需求會增加。當消費者轉向購買平板電腦時，對智能電話的需求會減少。這會導致價格和交易量下降（沿供應曲線向下移），與圖表不符。

These tablet computers are substitutes for smartphones. A reduction in size makes them more convenient and attractive, thus increasing the demand for such tablets. As consumers switch to tablets, the demand for smartphones will decrease. This would lead to a lower price and lower quantity (a downward movement along the supply curve), which contradicts the graph.

C. 市場上有更多智能電話生產商。
There are more smartphone producers in the market.

更多生產商進入市場，會導致智能電話的供應增加（供應曲線向右移）。如果供應曲線移動了，我們會在圖表上看到一條新的供應曲線 (例如 S1)，而不是在同一條曲線 (SS) 上移動。供應增加會導致價格下降，交易量上升。

More producers entering the market would cause an increase in supply for smartphones (the supply curve shifts to the right). If the supply curve shifted, we would see a new supply curve on the graph (e.g., S1) rather than a movement along the same curve (SS). An increase in supply would cause the price to drop and quantity to increase.

D. 主要的流動網絡服務供應商同時提高他們的服務收費。
The major network service providers increase the service charge at the same time.

流動網絡服務是智能電話的輔助品 (Complement)。輔助品價格上升，會導致主產品（智能電話）的需求減少。這會令價格及交易量雙雙下降，與圖表顯示的上升趨勢相反。

Mobile network services are complements to smartphones. An increase in the price of a complement will lead to a decrease in demand for the main good (smartphones). This would cause both price and quantity to fall, which is the opposite of the upward trend shown in the graph.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

🔑 解題樞紐 (The Pivot Point)

這題的關鍵在於「看圖辨向」。一看到點在同一條供應曲線上向上移動，你腦海中應該立刻浮現唯一的因果關係：
需求增加 \(\rightarrow\) 價格上升 \(\rightarrow\) 供應量增加 (沿曲線移動)。
因此，你的任務只是在四個選項中，找出哪一個會導致「智能電話需求增加」。

The key to this question is "reading the graph correctly". The moment you see a point moving upward along the same supply curve, only one causal relationship should pop into your mind:
Increase in Demand \(\rightarrow\) Price increases \(\rightarrow\) Quantity supplied increases (movement along the curve).
Therefore, your only task is to find which of the four options causes an "increase in demand for smartphones".

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

很多同學會因為看到圖表中「交易量 (Quantity) 增加了」，就誤以為是「供應增加 (Increase in Supply)」，從而錯選 C。請緊記：
供應增加是指在任何價格下，生產者都願意提供更多數量，這必須用一條全新的供應曲線 (New Supply Curve) 來表示。只要點還在同一條線 $SS$ 上，供應 (Supply) 就是沒有改變的，改變的只是供應量 (Quantity Supplied)。

Many students see that the "Quantity has increased" in the graph and mistakenly think it's an "Increase in Supply", leading them to wrongly choose C. Remember:
An Increase in Supply means producers are willing to offer more at any given price, which must be represented by a completely new supply curve. As long as the points are on the exact same line $SS$, the Supply itself hasn't changed; only the Quantity Supplied has changed due to a price change.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 既然零售商送贈品，等於成本上升，為什麼供應曲線沒有向左移？
Q: Since retailers are giving away free gifts, which means higher costs, why doesn't the supply curve shift to the left?

A: 這是一個很好的問題！在 DSE 經濟學中，我們通常將「買機送贈品」視為針對消費者的非價格競爭 (推廣策略)，它直接改變了消費者眼中的「產品價值」，因此被歸類為影響需求的因素。除非題目明確指出贈品導致生產商的整體生產成本大幅上升以致影響生產意欲，否則我們優先考慮它對需求的刺激作用。

A: This is a great question! In DSE Economics, "giving free gifts with purchase" is generally treated as non-price competition (a promotional strategy) targeted at consumers. It directly changes the "value of the product" in the eyes of the consumer, so it is classified as a factor affecting Demand. Unless the question explicitly states that the gifts caused a significant increase in overall production costs that affects production willingness, we primarily consider its stimulating effect on demand.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

秒殺法 (The Elimination Method):

  1. 看圖：發現是沿著 $SS$ 向上移 \(\rightarrow\) 需求 \(\uparrow\)。
  2. 看選項找「需求 \(\uparrow\)」：
    • A. 送贈品 \(\rightarrow\) 更吸引 \(\rightarrow\) 需求 \(\uparrow\) (留住 Keep)
    • B. 替代品變好 \(\rightarrow\) 轉買替代品 \(\rightarrow\) 需求 \(\downarrow\) (交叉 Cross)
    • C. 關乎生產商 \(\rightarrow\) 影響供應 \(\rightarrow\) 錯！我們需要找影響需求的因素。 (交叉 Cross)
    • D. 輔助品加價 \(\rightarrow\) 一併使用的成本上升 \(\rightarrow\) 需求 \(\downarrow\) (交叉 Cross)
  3. 答案秒選 A。

Shortcut:
1. Read graph: Upward movement along $SS$ \(\rightarrow\) Demand \(\uparrow\).
2. Scan options for "Demand \(\uparrow\)": A (gifts = more attractive $\rightarrow$ D $\uparrow$), B (substitute better $\rightarrow$ D $\downarrow$), C (affects supply, not demand), D (complement more expensive $\rightarrow$ D $\downarrow$).
3. Instantly pick A.
