HKDSE Economics Tutor 經濟學導師解析

主題 (Topic): 宏觀經濟學 - 貨幣供應定義 (Macroeconomics - Definitions of Money Supply)
難度 (Difficulty): ★★★☆☆
關鍵字 (Key Concepts): M1, M2, M3 持牌銀行 (Licensed Banks) 接受存款機構 (Deposit-taking Institutions)

1. 題目 (The Question)

下列哪項交易會增加香港貨幣供應定義二(M2)？
A. 陳女士從一家有限制牌照銀行的定期存款戶口轉賬了$500 000至一家持牌銀行的定期存款戶口。
B. 李先生從一家持牌銀行的往來戶口轉賬了$200 000至該銀行的儲蓄戶口。
C. 文先生將$100 000現金存入一家持牌銀行的儲蓄戶口。
D. 鍾女士將$600 000現金存入一家接受存款公司的定期存款戶口。
Which of the following transactions would increase the money supply (M2) of Hong Kong?
A. Ms Chan transfers $500 000 from her time deposit account in a restricted licence bank to her time deposit account in a licensed bank.
B. Mr Lee transfers $200 000 from his current account to his saving account in a licensed bank.
C. Mr Man deposits $100 000 cash into his savings account in a licensed bank.
D. Ms Chung deposits $600 000 cash into her time deposit account in a deposit-taking company.

2. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

要解答這類題目，我們必須清楚記得香港貨幣供應的三個定義（M1, M2, M3）包含甚麼元素。香港的三級銀行體制決定了不同機構的存款歸屬於不同的貨幣供應定義。
To solve this type of question, we must clearly remember the components of the three definitions of money supply in Hong Kong (M1, M2, M3). Hong Kong's three-tier banking system determines which money supply definition different institutions' deposits belong to.
$$ M1 = C_p + D_d $$
\( C_p \) = 公眾持有的法定貨幣 (Legal tender notes and coins held by the non-bank public)
\( D_d \) = 持牌銀行的活期存款 (Demand deposits at Licensed Banks)
$$ M2 = M1 + D_s + D_t + NCD_{LB} $$
\( D_s \) = 持牌銀行的儲蓄存款 (Savings deposits at Licensed Banks)
\( D_t \) = 持牌銀行的定期存款 (Time deposits at Licensed Banks)
\( NCD_{LB} \) = 持牌銀行發行並由非銀行公眾持有的可轉讓存款證 (Negotiable certificates of deposit issued by Licensed Banks)
$$ M3 = M2 + D_{RLB/DTC} + NCD_{RLB/DTC} $$
\( D_{RLB/DTC} \) = 有限制牌照銀行及接受存款公司的所有存款 (All deposits at Restricted Licence Banks and Deposit-taking Companies)

3. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): A

選項 A (Option A) - 正確 (Correct)
有限制牌照銀行 (RLB) 的存款只屬於 M3，不屬於 M2。持牌銀行 (LB) 的定期存款屬於 M2。當資金從 RLB 轉移到 LB 時，一筆原本不在 M2 內的資金進入了 M2 體系。因此，M2 會增加。
Deposits in a restricted licence bank (RLB) belong to M3 only, not M2. Time deposits in a licensed bank (LB) belong to M2. When funds are transferred from an RLB to an LB, money that was previously outside M2 enters the M2 system. Therefore, M2 increases.
選項 B (Option B) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
持牌銀行的往來戶口 (活期存款) 屬於 M1 (因此也屬於 M2)。持牌銀行的儲蓄戶口屬於 M2。這只是 M2 體系內部的資金轉移，M1 減少但 M2 的非 M1 部分增加，兩者抵銷。因此，M2 不變。
Current accounts (demand deposits) in an LB belong to M1 (and thus M2). Savings accounts in an LB belong to M2. This is merely an internal transfer within the M2 system. M1 decreases but the non-M1 component of M2 increases, offsetting each other. Therefore, M2 remains unchanged.
選項 C (Option C) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
公眾持有的現金 \( C_p \) 屬於 M1 (因此也屬於 M2)。存入持牌銀行的儲蓄戶口後，現金減少，但儲蓄存款增加。這同樣是 M2 內部的轉移。因此，M2 不變。
Cash held by the public \( C_p \) belongs to M1 (and thus M2). After depositing it into an LB savings account, cash decreases, but savings deposits increase. This is also an internal transfer within M2. Therefore, M2 remains unchanged.
選項 D (Option D) - 錯誤 (Incorrect)
公眾持有的現金屬於 M2。接受存款公司 (DTC) 的存款只屬於 M3，不屬於 M2。將現金存入 DTC，代表資金流出了 M2 體系。因此，M2 會減少。
Cash held by the public belongs to M2. Deposits in a deposit-taking company (DTC) belong to M3 only, not M2. Depositing cash into a DTC means funds flow out of the M2 system. Therefore, M2 decreases.

4. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

面對這類題目，我們的核心解題步驟 (Pivot Point) 是判斷資金的「起點」和「終點」分別屬於哪個貨幣供應定義。
  • 如果 起點在 M2 內，終點在 M2 內 \(\rightarrow\) M2 不變 (Internal transfer)
  • 如果 起點在 M2 外，終點在 M2 內 \(\rightarrow\) M2 增加 (Inflow)
  • 如果 起點在 M2 內，終點在 M2 外 \(\rightarrow\) M2 減少 (Outflow)
題目中的具體金額（如 $500,000 或 $100,000）其實是干擾資訊 (Distractors)，我們只需要關注資金的流向。
When facing this type of question, our Pivot Point is to determine which money supply definition the "starting point" and "ending point" of the funds belong to.
  • If Start is inside M2, End is inside M2 \(\rightarrow\) M2 unchanged (Internal transfer)
  • If Start is outside M2, End is inside M2 \(\rightarrow\) M2 increases (Inflow)
  • If Start is inside M2, End is outside M2 \(\rightarrow\) M2 decreases (Outflow)
The specific amounts in the question (like $500,000 or $100,000) are actually Distractors; we only need to focus on the direction of the money flow.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱一：忘記現金已經計算在貨幣供應內
很多學生誤以為「將現金存入銀行」會創造新的貨幣供應。事實上，公眾手上的現金 \( C_p \) 已經是 M1, M2 和 M3 的一部分。存入持牌銀行只是改變了貨幣的形態（從現金變成存款），總量不變。
Trap 1: Forgetting that cash is already counted in the money supply
Many students mistakenly believe that "depositing cash into a bank" creates new money supply. In fact, cash in public hands \( C_p \) is already part of M1, M2, and M3. Depositing it into a licensed bank merely changes the form of money (from cash to deposits), leaving the total amount unchanged.

6. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼選項 D 會令 M2 減少？錢不是還在香港的金融體系內嗎？
A: 錢確實還在香港的金融體系內，所以最廣義的貨幣供應 M3 是不變的。但是，M2 的定義比較狹窄，它不包括接受存款公司 (DTC) 的存款。當你把原本屬於 M2 的現金，存入一個不屬於 M2 的機構時，這筆錢就退出了 M2 的計算範圍，因此 M2 減少了。
Q: Why does Option D decrease M2? Isn't the money still in Hong Kong's financial system?
A: The money is indeed still in the financial system, so the broadest money supply M3 remains unchanged. However, the definition of M2 is narrower and does not include deposits in deposit-taking companies (DTCs). When you take cash (which belongs to M2) and deposit it into an institution outside M2, that money exits the M2 calculation scope, causing M2 to decrease.

7. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

速解法：畫同心圓 (Concentric Circles Method)
在草稿紙上畫三個同心圓：最內圈是 M1，中圈是 M2，外圈是 M3。
  • 持牌銀行 (LB) 的所有存款都在 M2 圈內（活期在 M1，儲蓄/定期在 M2 邊緣）。
  • RLB 和 DTC 的存款在 M3 圈內，但在 M2 圈外。
只要看到資金從「M2 圈外」箭頭指向「M2 圈內」，那就是 M2 增加。選項 A 正是從 RLB (外圈) 指向 LB (中圈)，秒殺！
Shortcut: Concentric Circles Method
Draw three concentric circles on your scratch paper: the innermost is M1, the middle is M2, and the outermost is M3.
  • All deposits of Licensed Banks (LB) are inside the M2 circle (current in M1, savings/time on the M2 edge).
  • Deposits of RLBs and DTCs are inside the M3 circle but outside the M2 circle.
Whenever you see an arrow pointing from "outside the M2 circle" to "inside the M2 circle", M2 increases. Option A is exactly pointing from RLB (outer circle) to LB (middle circle). Instant solve!
