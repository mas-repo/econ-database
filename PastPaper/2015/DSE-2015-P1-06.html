3. 概念重溫 (Concept Review)

勞工生產力 (Labour Productivity) 是指平均每個工人的產出，或者平均每工時的產出。當比較不同工作時數的工人（例如全職與兼職）時，我們必須使用「每工時產出」來進行公平的比較。

Labour Productivity refers to the output per worker or output per man-hour. When comparing workers with different working hours (e.g., full-time vs. part-time), we must use "output per man-hour" for a fair comparison.
$$Labour\ Productivity = \frac{Total\ Output}{Total\ Man\text{-}hours}$$

總工時 (Total Man-hours) 的計算方法如下：

The calculation for Total Man-hours is as follows:
$$Total\ Man\text{-}hours = Number\ of\ Workers \times Average\ Working\ Hours$$

4. 答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): Option B

首先，我們必須計算兩間工廠的實際勞工生產力：

First, we must calculate the actual labour productivity of both factories:
  • 工廠甲 (Factory A):
    總工時 = 25 × 8 = 200 小時。
    生產力 = 12000 ÷ 200 = 60 單位/每工時。
    Total man-hours = 25 × 8 = 200 hours. Productivity = 12000 ÷ 200 = 60 units/man-hour.
  • 工廠乙 (Factory B):
    總工時 = 50 × 6 = 300 小時。
    生產力 = 15000 ÷ 300 = 50 單位/每工時。
    Total man-hours = 50 × 6 = 300 hours. Productivity = 15000 ÷ 300 = 50 units/man-hour.

結論：工廠甲的勞工生產力高於工廠乙。題目實際上是問：「哪個選項能解釋工廠甲的生產力較高？」

Conclusion: Factory A's labour productivity is higher than Factory B's. The question is actually asking: "Which option explains why Factory A has higher productivity?"

選項 A (Option A): 一般而言，件工 (piece rate) 能提供較大誘因，令勞工生產力高於時薪 (time rate)。如果乙用件工，乙的生產力應該較高，但計算結果顯示乙的生產力較低。因此不合理。

Generally, a piece rate provides a stronger incentive, making labour productivity higher than a time rate. If Factory B uses a piece rate, its productivity should be higher. However, the calculation shows B's productivity is lower. Therefore, it is incorrect.

選項 B (Option B): 提供較多訓練能提升工人的技術和效率，從而提高勞工生產力。這正好解釋了為何工廠甲的生產力（60）會高於工廠乙（50）。這是一個合理的原因。

Providing more training enhances workers' skills and efficiency, thereby increasing labour productivity. This perfectly explains why Factory A's productivity (60) is higher than Factory B's (50). This is a logical reason.

選項 C (Option C): 如果工廠乙的工人技術較高，工廠乙的勞工生產力理應較高。但這與我們的計算結果（甲 > 乙）相違背。

If Factory B's workers are more skilled, Factory B's labour productivity should logically be higher. But this contradicts our calculated results (A > B).

選項 D (Option D): 運用較多資本物品（如機器）有助提高勞工生產力。如果工廠乙運用較多資本，其生產力應該較高，但同樣與計算結果不符。

Using more capital goods (like machinery) helps increase labour productivity. If Factory B uses more capital, its productivity should be higher, which again contradicts the calculation.

5. 題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

拆題樞紐 (The Pivot Point):
很多學生在處理這類題目時，會直接跳去看選項。解題的關鍵是先不要看選項，必須先用數據計算出哪間工廠的生產力較高。確定了「甲 > 乙」這個事實後，選項就變成了一條簡單的邏輯題。

The Pivot Point: Many students jump straight to the options when handling this type of question. The key is to ignore the options first and calculate which factory has higher productivity using the data. Once you establish the fact that "A > B", evaluating the options becomes a simple logic puzzle.

干擾資訊 (Distractors):
總產出（工廠乙的 15 000 單位 > 工廠甲的 12 000 單位）。考評局故意讓工廠乙的總產出較高，試圖誘導沒有計算工時的學生誤以為工廠乙的生產力較高。

Distractors: Total output (Factory B's 15,000 units > Factory A's 12,000 units). The HKEAA intentionally makes Factory B's total output higher to trick students who don't calculate man-hours into thinking Factory B is more productive.

6. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱一：混淆「總產出」與「生產力」
學生常以為總產出高，生產力就一定高。生產力是相對的概念（產出與投入的比例）。工廠乙產出高純粹是因為它投入了極大量的總工時（300小時），而不是因為效率高。

Trap 1: Confusing "Total Output" with "Productivity"
Students often assume that higher total output means higher productivity. Productivity is a relative concept (the ratio of output to input). Factory B's higher output is simply because it utilized a massive amount of total man-hours (300 hours), not because of high efficiency.

陷阱二：忘記統一比較單位 (工時 vs 人數)
由於一邊是全職，一邊是兼職，他們的工作時數不同。如果不計算 $Man\text{-}hours$，直接用產出除以人數（工廠甲：12000 ÷ 25 = 480；工廠乙：15000 ÷ 50 = 300），雖然這題剛好也得出甲大於乙的結果，但概念上是不嚴謹的，在其他題目可能會導致算錯。

Trap 2: Forgetting to standardize the unit of comparison (Man-hours vs. Number of workers)
Since one side is full-time and the other part-time, their working hours differ. If you don't calculate $Man\text{-}hours$ and directly divide output by the number of workers (Factory A: 12000 ÷ 25 = 480; Factory B: 15000 ÷ 50 = 300), although it coincidentally yields A > B in this specific question, it is conceptually flawed and will lead to wrong answers in other scenarios.

7. 學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 既然兼職工人的工資通常較低，這會影響生產力的計算嗎？

A: 不會。在經濟學中，我們計算「勞工生產力」(Physical Productivity) 時，只看實質的數量 (單位/工時)，不看工資成本的金額。工資高低會影響「生產成本」(Cost of production)，但不會直接改變這條實質生產力的算式。

Q: Since part-time workers usually have lower wages, does this affect the calculation of productivity?

A: No. In economics, when calculating "Labour Productivity" (Physical Productivity), we only look at physical quantities (units/man-hour), not the monetary value of wage costs. The level of wages affects the "Cost of Production", but it does not directly alter this physical productivity formula.

8. 考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

速解法：符號標記法 (The Arrow Method)
在選擇題中，一旦算出甲的生產力較高 (甲 > 乙)，立即在題目旁邊寫下大大的 「找：利好甲的因素」。然後掃視 A, B, C, D，在每個選項旁邊畫向上箭嘴（↑）：
A. 乙用件工 (乙 ↑) ❌
B. 甲有訓練 (甲 ↑) ✅
C. 乙技術高 (乙 ↑) ❌
D. 乙多資本 (乙 ↑) ❌
一眼就能看出只有 B 符合方向！不需要在腦內把每個選項長篇大論翻譯一次。

Shortcut: The Arrow Method
In MCQs, once you calculate that A has higher productivity (A > B), immediately write down "Find: Factors favoring A" next to the question. Then scan options A, B, C, D and draw an upward arrow (↑) indicating which factory benefits:
A. B uses piece rate (B ↑) ❌
B. A gets training (A ↑) ✅
C. B is highly skilled (B ↑) ❌
D. B has more capital (B ↑) ❌
You can instantly see that only B matches the direction! You don't need to overthink or internally translate each option into a long paragraph.
