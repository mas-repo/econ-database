HKDSE Economics Tutor

Topic (課題): Market Structure 市場結構
Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)
Key Concepts (關鍵概念): Monopoly 壟斷 Natural Monopoly 天然獨佔 Economies of Scale 規模經濟

1. The Question 題目

下列哪一項關於中華電力有限公司的描述是正確的？

A. 它是寡頭壟斷者，因為香港有兩間互相競爭的電力公司。
B. 它是寡頭壟斷者，因為在大亞灣將會有一所新核電站。
C. 它是天然獨佔者，因為它享有規模經濟。
D. 它是專利獨佔者，因為政府給予它特許專營權。

Which of the following statements about the China Light and Power Company Limited is correct?

A. It is an oligopoly because there are two competing electricity companies in Hong Kong.
B. It is an oligopoly because there will be a new nuclear power station in Daya Bay.
C. It is a natural monopoly because it enjoys economies of scale.
D. It is a franchised monopoly because the government grants an exclusive right for its running.

2. Concept Review 概念重溫

Natural Monopoly (天然獨佔)

當一間企業在生產過程中享有龐大的規模經濟 (Economies of Scale)，其平均成本 \(AC\) 會隨着產量 \(Q\) 增加而不斷下降。在這種情況下，由單一企業供應整個市場的成本，會比由兩間或以上企業供應的成本更低。公用事業（如電力、煤氣、食水供應）通常需要鋪設龐大的地下管網，固定成本極高，因此屬於天然獨佔。

When a firm enjoys massive economies of scale, its average cost \(AC\) continuously decreases as output \(Q\) increases. In this situation, it is cheaper for a single firm to supply the entire market than for two or more firms to do so. Public utilities (like electricity, gas, and water supply) require extensive underground networks, leading to huge fixed costs, making them natural monopolies.

$$ AC = \frac{Total\ Cost\ (TC)}{Quantity\ (Q)} = \frac{Fixed\ Cost\ (FC) + Variable\ Cost\ (VC)}{Q} $$

由於 \(FC\) 極大，當 \(Q\) 增加，\(\frac{FC}{Q}\) 會大幅下降，導致 \(AC\) 持續下降。
Since \(FC\) is huge, as \(Q\) increases, \(\frac{FC}{Q}\) drops significantly, causing \(AC\) to fall continuously.

Franchised Monopoly (專利獨佔 / 特許專營)

企業透過政府賦予的法律權利（如專利權、版權、特許專營權）成為市場的唯一供應商。其他企業在法律上被禁止進入市場。

A firm becomes the sole supplier in the market through legal rights granted by the government (e.g., patents, copyrights, exclusive franchises). Other firms are legally prohibited from entering the market.

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis 答案與詳細分析

Correct Option 正確答案: Option C

Option A: Incorrect (錯誤)

雖然香港有兩間電力公司（中電和港燈），但它們在各自的供電區域（中電負責九龍及新界，港燈負責香港島）內是唯一的供應商，兩者並沒有在同一地區直接競爭。因此，在各自的地區市場內，它們是獨佔者而非寡頭壟斷者。

Although there are two electricity companies in Hong Kong (CLP and HK Electric), they are the sole suppliers in their respective geographical areas (CLP for Kowloon/NT, HK Electric for HK Island). They do not compete directly in the same area. Therefore, within their respective regional markets, they are monopolies, not oligopolies.

Option B: Incorrect (錯誤)

大亞灣核電站只是中電的電力來源之一，這並不會改變其在香港供電市場的獨佔結構。

The Daya Bay nuclear power station is merely one of CLP's sources of electricity. This does not change its monopolistic market structure in the Hong Kong electricity supply market.

Option C: Correct (正確)

電力供應需要建立龐大的發電廠和鋪設廣泛的電網，這涉及極高的固定成本。隨着供電量增加，平均成本會大幅下降（享有規模經濟）。由一間公司供應整個區域是最具成本效益的，這正是天然獨佔的定義。

Electricity supply requires building massive power plants and laying extensive power grids, which involves extremely high fixed costs. As the quantity of electricity supplied increases, the average cost falls significantly (enjoying economies of scale). It is most cost-effective for a single company to supply an entire region, which is the exact definition of a natural monopoly.

Option D: Incorrect (錯誤)

這是一個常見的陷阱！香港政府並沒有給予中電或港燈「特許專營權」(Exclusive right)。它們只是受到《管制計劃協議》(Scheme of Control Agreement) 規管，法律上並沒有禁止其他公司進入市場（只是因為天然獨佔的經濟壁壘太高，其他公司難以進入）。

This is a common trap! The Hong Kong government does NOT grant CLP or HK Electric an "exclusive right" or franchise. They are only regulated under the Scheme of Control Agreement. There is no legal barrier preventing other companies from entering the market (it's just that the economic barriers of a natural monopoly are too high for others to enter).

4. Problem Decomposition 題目拆解

Pivot Point (解題關鍵): 區分「天然獨佔」與「專利獨佔」，以及了解香港電力市場的實際背景。

  • 陷阱資訊： 題目提到「香港有兩間電力公司」，試圖引導學生選擇「寡頭壟斷」(Oligopoly)。但必須考慮地理市場界線 (Geographical market boundaries)。
  • 關鍵知識： 必須清楚知道香港政府沒有發出電力供應的專營權。有專營權的是巴士、渡輪等（例如專營巴士 Franchised buses）。

Pivot Point: Distinguishing between "Natural Monopoly" and "Franchised Monopoly", and understanding the actual context of the HK electricity market.

  • Distractor: The mention of "two electricity companies" tries to mislead students into choosing Oligopoly. You must consider geographical market boundaries.
  • Key Knowledge: You must know that the HK government does not issue exclusive franchises for electricity. Franchises are given to buses or ferries (e.g., Franchised buses).

5. Common Mistakes & Traps 常犯錯誤與陷阱

  • 誤解香港電力市場結構： 很多學生以為全香港是一個單一市場，有兩間公司就是寡頭壟斷。實際上，九龍/新界和香港島是兩個獨立的地區市場。
  • 混淆法例與經濟壁壘： 學生常以為沒有競爭者就一定是因為政府發了專營權。事實上，巨大的固定成本（經濟壁壘）足以形成天然獨佔。
  • Misunderstanding the HK electricity market structure: Many students think HK is a single market, so two firms mean oligopoly. In reality, Kowloon/NT and HK Island are separate regional markets.
  • Confusing legal and economic barriers: Students often assume a lack of competitors must be due to a government franchise. In fact, huge fixed costs (economic barriers) are enough to create a natural monopoly.

6. Student Q&A 學生常見疑問

Q: 如果政府開放電網，讓其他公司租用中電的電網供電，中電還是獨佔者嗎？

A: 如果政府開放電網，供電市場（零售層面）可能會變成寡頭壟斷甚至壟斷性競爭，因為其他公司可以進入市場賣電。但「電網擁有權」本身仍然是一個天然獨佔，因為重新鋪設一套新電網是不符合成本效益的。

Q: If the government opens up the power grid, allowing other companies to rent CLP's grid to supply electricity, is CLP still a monopoly?

A: If the grid is opened, the electricity supply market (retail level) might become an oligopoly or monopolistic competition, as other firms can enter to sell electricity. However, the "grid ownership" itself remains a natural monopoly, because laying a completely new grid is not cost-effective.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts 考試技巧與速解

常識秒殺法 (Common Sense Elimination):

在 DSE 經濟科中，關於香港的公用事業有幾個「死背」的經典例子：

  1. 電力 (中電/港燈)、煤氣 (中華煤氣)： 天然獨佔 (Natural Monopoly)。沒有專營權！
  2. 專營巴士 (九巴/城巴)、天星小輪： 專利獨佔 / 特許專營 (Franchised Monopoly)。政府有發出專營權。

只要記住「電力 = 天然獨佔」，這題可以直接秒殺選 C，連看 A、B、D 都可以省下時間！

Common Sense Elimination:

In DSE Economics, there are classic examples of HK public utilities you should just memorize:

  1. Electricity (CLP/HKE), Towngas: Natural Monopoly. NO exclusive franchise!
  2. Franchised Buses (KMB/Citybus), Star Ferry: Franchised Monopoly. Government grants franchises.

By simply remembering "Electricity = Natural Monopoly", you can instantly choose C and save time reading A, B, and D!
