3. Concept Review (概念重溫)

緊縮性裂口 (Deflationary Gap): 當經濟的短期均衡產出（或收入）低於充分就業產出水平時，便會出現緊縮性裂口。這通常是因為總需求 (AD) 不足所致。

A deflationary gap occurs when the short-run equilibrium output (or income) is below the full-employment output level. This is typically caused by insufficient Aggregate Demand (AD).
$$ Y_e < Y_f $$

其中 \( Y_e \) 是均衡收入 (Equilibrium Income)，\( Y_f \) 是充分就業收入 (Full Employment Income)。

Where \( Y_e \) is Equilibrium Income and \( Y_f \) is Full Employment Income.

財政政策 (Fiscal Policy): 為了消除緊縮性裂口，政府應採取擴張性財政政策（即赤字預算 Deficit Budget），例如增加政府開支或減稅，以刺激總需求。

To eliminate a deflationary gap, the government should adopt an expansionary fiscal policy (i.e., a Deficit Budget), such as increasing government spending or cutting taxes, to stimulate aggregate demand.

4. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): A

(1) 均衡收入低於充分就業收入。 (正確)
這是緊縮性裂口的定義。當總需求不足時，均衡產出 \( Y_e \) 會小於潛在產出 \( Y_f \)。

(1) the equilibrium income is below the full employment income. (Correct)
This is the exact definition of a deflationary gap. When AD is insufficient, equilibrium output \( Y_e \) is less than potential output \( Y_f \).

(2) 應使用盈餘預算來消除該裂口。 (錯誤)
盈餘預算（減少開支或增加稅收）會進一步壓抑總需求，使裂口惡化。政府應使用「赤字預算」來刺激經濟。

(2) a surplus budget should be used to eliminate the gap. (Incorrect)
A surplus budget (cutting spending or raising taxes) would further depress AD and worsen the gap. The government should use a "deficit budget" to stimulate the economy.

(3) 經濟體系缺乏足夠的總需求來達致充分就業。 (正確)
緊縮性裂口的成因正是總需求 (AD) 曲線與短期總供應 (SRAS) 曲線的相交點位於長期總供應 (LRAS) 曲線的左方。

(3) the economy has insufficient aggregate demand to achieve full employment. (Correct)
The root cause of a deflationary gap is that the intersection of the AD and SRAS curves lies to the left of the LRAS curve.

(4) 實質收入小於名義收入。 (錯誤)
這取決於物價指數（通脹或通縮），與是否存在緊縮性裂口沒有必然的直接關係。緊縮性裂口是指產出水平，而不是名義與實質數值的比較。

(4) the real income is smaller than the nominal income. (Incorrect)
This depends on the price index (inflation or deflation) and has no direct, necessary relationship with the existence of a deflationary gap. The gap refers to output levels, not the comparison between nominal and real values.

5. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point): 釐清「緊縮性裂口」的定義及其對應的政策。
看到「緊縮性裂口」，腦海中必須立刻浮現兩個概念：
1. 狀態：產出不足 (\( Y_e < Y_f \))。
2. 對策：需要擴張性政策（赤字預算）來推高 AD。

Pivot Point: Clarifying the definition of a "deflationary gap" and its corresponding policy.
When you see "deflationary gap", two concepts must immediately come to mind:
1. State: Insufficient output (\( Y_e < Y_f \)).
2. Solution: Expansionary policy (deficit budget) is needed to boost AD.

6. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：混淆「緊縮性裂口」與「通貨緊縮」
學生常以為緊縮性裂口必然代表物價正在下跌（通縮）。實際上，緊縮性裂口只代表產出低於潛在水平，物價可能只是上升得較慢，不一定出現通縮。選項 (4) 就是利用這個誤解來設計的干擾項。

Trap: Confusing "Deflationary Gap" with "Deflation"
Students often assume a deflationary gap means prices are currently falling (deflation). In reality, it only means output is below potential; prices might just be rising more slowly. Option (4) is a distractor designed around this misconception.

7. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼選項 (2) 說盈餘預算是錯的？政府不是應該保持財政盈餘嗎？
A: 在經濟衰退或有緊縮性裂口時，民間消費和投資疲弱。如果政府此時實行盈餘預算（即政府收的稅多於它的開支），會進一步抽走市場資金，令總需求 (AD) 跌得更低。因此，這時候需要的是「赤字預算」來救市。

Q: Why is Option (2) wrong about a surplus budget? Shouldn't the government maintain a surplus?
A: During an economic downturn or when a deflationary gap exists, private consumption and investment are weak. If the government runs a surplus budget (taxing more than it spends), it withdraws funds from the market, causing AD to fall further. Therefore, a "deficit budget" is needed to rescue the economy.

Q: 題目選項 (3) 的「總需求不足」可以改為「總供應不足」嗎？
A: 不可以。雖然短期總供應 (SRAS) 下降也會導致均衡產出低於充分就業水平（這種情況稱為滯脹 Stagflation），但在宏觀經濟學的嚴格定義中，「緊縮性裂口」(Deflationary Gap) 一詞源於凱恩斯學派，專指在充分就業的物價水平下，總需求 (AD) 不足以吸納所有潛在產出的情況。因此，這個裂口的核心概念在於「需求不足」，而非供應不足。如果改為「總供應不足」，這句陳述在經濟學定義上就會變得不準確。

Q: Can "insufficient aggregate demand" in Option (3) be changed to "insufficient aggregate supply"?
A: No. Although a decrease in Short-Run Aggregate Supply (SRAS) also causes equilibrium output to fall below the full-employment level (a situation known as stagflation), in strict macroeconomic definitions, the term "Deflationary Gap" originates from Keynesian economics. It specifically refers to a situation where Aggregate Demand (AD) is insufficient to absorb all potential output at the full-employment price level. Therefore, the core concept of this gap is "insufficient demand," not insufficient supply. Changing it to "insufficient aggregate supply" would make the statement economically inaccurate.

8. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

消去法 (Elimination Method):
在DSE考試中，如果你非常確定某個陳述是錯的，可以立刻刪去包含該陳述的選項。例如，只要你知道緊縮性裂口需要「擴張性政策 / 赤字預算」來解決，就能確定 (2) 是錯的。
刪去包含 (2) 的選項 C 和 D。
剩下 A 和 B。再看 (3) 是標準定義，必定正確，因此答案秒選 A。

Elimination Method:
In the DSE exam, if you are absolutely sure a statement is wrong, immediately eliminate options containing it. For example, knowing a deflationary gap requires "expansionary policy / deficit budget" makes (2) definitely wrong.
Eliminate options C and D which contain (2).
You are left with A and B. Since (3) is the standard definition and must be correct, you can instantly choose A.
