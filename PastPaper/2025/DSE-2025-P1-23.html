Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 洛倫茨曲線 (Lorenz Curve)

洛倫茨曲線顯示了家庭累積百分比與收入累積百分比之間的關係。45度線代表絕對平均 (Perfect Equality)。
The Lorenz curve shows the relationship between the cumulative percentage of households and the cumulative percentage of income. The 45-degree line represents Perfect Equality.

曲線越接近45度線，收入分佈越平均；曲線越彎曲（離45度線越遠），收入分佈越不平均。
The closer the curve is to the 45-degree line, the more equal the income distribution. The more bowed the curve (further from the line), the more unequal the distribution.

2. 堅尼系數 (Gini Coefficient)

堅尼系數是用來量度收入不均的指標，數值介乎 0 至 1 之間。
The Gini coefficient is a measure of income inequality, ranging from 0 to 1.

$$ \text{Gini} = \frac{\text{Area between diagonal and curve}}{\text{Total area under diagonal}} $$
  • 數值越大 \(\rightarrow\) 越不平均 (Higher value \(\rightarrow\) More unequal)
  • 數值越小 \(\rightarrow\) 越平均 (Lower value \(\rightarrow\) More equal)

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確選項): D

Statement (1): True (正確)

分析：觀察圖表，甲國的曲線較乙國的曲線更接近45度對角線（較平直）。這意味著甲國的收入分佈較平均。因此，甲國的堅尼系數較低。
Analysis: Observing the graphs, Country A's curve is closer to the 45-degree diagonal (less bowed) than Country B's. This implies Country A has a more equal income distribution. Therefore, Country A has a lower Gini coefficient.

Statement (2): True (正確)

分析：我們需要比較最低端20%住戶的收入。看橫軸 (X軸) 的 20%：
Analysis: We need to compare the income of the bottom 20% of households. Look at 20% on the horizontal axis (X-axis):

  • 甲國 (Country A): 曲線在 X=20 時，Y軸數值明顯高於 0 (目測約 5-8%)。
    At X=20, the Y-value is visibly above 0 (approx. 5-8%).
  • 乙國 (Country B): 曲線在 X=20 時，Y軸數值非常低 (目測約 1-2%)。
    At X=20, the Y-value is very low (approx. 1-2%).

題目指明「兩國總收入相等」。由於甲國最低端20%住戶佔總收入的百分比較高，且總收入基數相同，所以他們的實際總收入也較高。
The question states "total incomes in the two countries are equal". Since the bottom 20% in Country A command a larger percentage share of the total income, and the base total income is the same, their actual total income is also higher.

Statement (3): True (正確)

分析：「最高端20%」是指由第80%到第100%的住戶。我們需要計算這部分的收入佔比。
Analysis: The "top 20%" refers to households from the 80th percentile to the 100th percentile. We need to calculate the income share for this segment.

$$ \text{Top 20\% Share} = 100\% - \text{Cumulative Income at 80\%} $$
  • 甲國 (Country A): 在 X=80 時，Y=40。
    最高端 20% 收入佔比 = \(100\% - 40\% = 60\%\)。
    At X=80, Y=40. Top 20% income share = \(100\% - 40\% = 60\%\).
  • 乙國 (Country B): 在 X=80 時，Y=20。
    最高端 20% 收入佔比 = \(100\% - 20\% = 80\%\)。
    At X=80, Y=20. Top 20% income share = \(100\% - 20\% = 80\%\).

\(60\% < 80\%\)，所以甲國最高端20%住戶的收入佔比確實較小。
\(60\% < 80\%\), so the proportion of income earned by the top 20% households in Country A is indeed smaller.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

關鍵點 (Pivot Point): 比較曲線的彎曲程度以及讀取特定座標點 (X=20 和 X=80)。
Pivot Point: Comparing the curvature and reading specific coordinates (X=20 and X=80).

重要提示 (Crucial Info): 「假設兩國的總收入相等」這句話至關重要。如果沒有這句話，我們只能比較百分比 (Statement 1 & 3)，而無法比較實際金額 (Statement 2)。
Crucial Info: The phrase "Suppose the total incomes in the two countries are equal" is vital. Without this, we could only compare percentages (Statements 1 & 3), but not absolute amounts (Statement 2).

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 誤解累積概念 (Misinterpreting Cumulative Data):
    學生常誤以為 Y軸上的數值直接代表該組別的收入。例如看到 X=80, Y=20，誤以為那 20% 的人賺取 20% 收入。事實上，Y=20 代表最底層 80% 的人總共只賺取 20% 收入。
    Students often mistake the Y-axis value for the income of that specific group. E.g., seeing X=80, Y=20, they might think that 20% group earns 20%. In reality, Y=20 means the bottom 80% combined earn only 20%.
  • 計算最高層收入時忘記減法 (Forgetting Subtraction for Top Group):
    計算最高端收入時，必須用 \(100\% - \text{累積百分比}\)。
    When calculating the top group's income, you MUST use \(100\% - \text{Cumulative Percentage}\).
