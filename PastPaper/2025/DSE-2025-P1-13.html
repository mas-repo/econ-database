HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic (課題): Market Equilibrium (市場均衡)
Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆
Key Concepts (關鍵概念): Vertical Supply Curve Shifts

The Question (題目)

物品X的供應曲線是垂直的，而其需求曲線是向下傾斜的。
假設物品X的供應下降，同時物品X的需求上升。結果，物品X的均衡價格 ___________，而其均衡數量 ___________。

The supply curve of Good X is vertical and its demand curve is downward-sloping.
Suppose the supply of Good X decreases while the demand for Good X increases. As a result, the equilibrium price of Good X _______ and its equilibrium quantity _________.
  • A. 會上升 …… 會下降 (will increase …… will decrease)
  • B. 會上升 …… 可能上升、下降或維持不變 (will increase …… may increase, decrease or remain unchanged)
  • C. 會下降 …… 會下降 (will decrease …… will decrease)
  • D. 會下降 …… 可能上升、下降或維持不變 (will decrease …… may increase, decrease or remain unchanged)

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 垂直供應曲線 (Vertical Supply Curve):
當供應曲線是垂直時，這代表供應是完全無彈性的（Perfectly Inelastic Supply, \( E_s = 0 \)）。無論價格如何變動，供應量（Quantity Supplied）在該時刻都是固定的。 When the supply curve is vertical, it represents perfectly inelastic supply (\( E_s = 0 \)). The quantity supplied is fixed at that moment, regardless of price changes.

2. 曲線的移動 (Shifts in Curves):

  • 供應下降 (Supply Decreases): 供應曲線向左移動。對於垂直供應曲線，這意味著固定的數量減少了（例如由 \(Q=10\) 變為 \(Q=5\)）。 Supply curve shifts to the left. For a vertical curve, this means the fixed quantity decreases (e.g., from \(Q=10\) to \(Q=5\)).
  • 需求上升 (Demand Increases): 需求曲線向右移動。代表在任何價格下，消費者都想購買更多。 Demand curve shifts to the right. Consumers want to buy more at any given price.

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A

第一步：分析供應變動 (Step 1: Analyze Supply Change)
題目指出供應曲線是垂直的，且供應下降。這表示整條垂直線向左移動。 由於供應線決定了市場上可用的最大數量，當垂直線左移，均衡數量 (Equilibrium Quantity) 必然下降。 The question states the supply curve is vertical and supply decreases. This means the entire vertical line shifts to the left. Since the vertical line determines the quantity available, Equilibrium Quantity must decrease.
第二步：分析需求變動 (Step 2: Analyze Demand Change)
題目指出需求上升，即需求曲線向右移動。這會對價格造成上升的壓力。 The question states demand increases, meaning the demand curve shifts to the right. This puts upward pressure on the price.
第三步：綜合分析 (Step 3: Combined Analysis)
1. 價格 (Price): 供應減少（推高價格）加上需求增加（推高價格）。兩股力量方向相同，所以均衡價格必定上升。
2. 數量 (Quantity): 雖然通常「供應減、需求增」會令數量不確定，但因為供應曲線是垂直的，均衡數量完全由供應曲線的位置決定。供應線左移，數量就一定減少。需求線的移動只會令價格在新的數量水平上進一步上升。 1. Price: Supply decrease (pushes price up) plus Demand increase (pushes price up). Both forces work in the same direction, so Equilibrium Price must increase.
2. Quantity: Although usually "Supply down, Demand up" makes quantity indeterminate, because the Supply curve is vertical, the quantity is determined solely by the position of the supply curve. As the supply line shifts left, quantity must decrease. The demand shift only pushes the price higher at this new quantity level.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

The Pivot Point (解題關鍵點): "Vertical Supply" (垂直供應)

這題的關鍵在於「垂直」二字。如果沒有這兩個字，答案會是 B（數量不確定）。
The crucial keyword is "Vertical". Without this word, the answer would be B (quantity indeterminate).

邏輯鏈 (Logic Chain):
垂直供應 \( \rightarrow \) 數量由供應線獨自決定 (Quantity determined by Supply line alone) \( \rightarrow \) 供應減少 (Supply decreases) \( \rightarrow \) 數量必然下降 (Quantity MUST decrease)。

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：死記硬背「一增一減，數量不確定」

很多學生背誦口訣：「當供應下降且需求上升時，價格上升，但數量不確定（Indeterminate）。」
這個口訣只適用於兩條曲線都是傾斜的一般情況！
當其中一條曲線是垂直（或水平）時，這種不確定性就會消失。

Trap: Rote memorization of "One up one down, quantity indeterminate"
Many students memorize the rule: "When Supply decreases and Demand increases, Price rises, but Quantity is Indeterminate."
This rule ONLY applies when both curves are sloping! When one curve is vertical (or horizontal), this ambiguity disappears.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼需求上升不會令數量增加？(Why doesn't the increase in demand cause quantity to rise?)

A: 因為供應是垂直的（完全無彈性）。想像一下拍賣會上只有 5 個古董花瓶（垂直供應）。就算現在有 1000 個人想買（需求大增），成交量最多也只能是 5 個。需求增加只會把價格搶高，但變不出更多的花瓶來。 Because supply is vertical (perfectly inelastic). Imagine an auction with only 5 antique vases (vertical supply). Even if 1000 people want to buy them (demand increases), the quantity traded can only be 5. The increase in demand only bids up the price, it cannot create more vases.

Exam Tricks (考試技巧)

畫圖法 (Draw the Graph):
不要只在腦海中想像。在試卷邊緣快速畫一個十字坐標：

  1. 畫一條垂直線 \(S_1\) 和一條向下斜線 \(D_1\)。
  2. 將 \(S_1\) 向左移變 \(S_2\)。
  3. 將 \(D_1\) 向右移變 \(D_2\)。
  4. 找出新的交叉點。你會發現它明顯在左上方（價格更高，數量更少）。
Draw it out: Don't just visualize. Quickly sketch axes: 1. Draw vertical \(S_1\) and downward \(D_1\). 2. Shift \(S_1\) left to \(S_2\). 3. Shift \(D_1\) right to \(D_2\). 4. Find the new intersection. It is clearly to the top-left (Higher Price, Lower Quantity).
