HKDSE Economics Tutorial (經濟學導修)

Topic (課題): Functions of Money & Hyperinflation (貨幣的功能與惡性通脹)

Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts (關鍵概念): Medium of Exchange (交易媒介), Store of Value (價值儲藏), Standard of Deferred Payment (延期支付的標準), Hyperinflation (惡性通脹)

The Question (題目)

參考以下假設個案。

P國經歷長時間的惡性通脹。當工人收到以P元（P國的法定貨幣）支付的薪金時，他們會趕快地以之購買黃金，或將之兌換成美元。一些工人更只接受以美元計算薪金的工作。放貸者貸款時一般不接受以P元歸還欠款。

根據上述資料，P元的哪些貨幣功能被削弱了？

(1) 交易媒介
(2) 價值儲藏
(3) 延期支付的標準

A. 只有 (1)、(2)
B. 只有 (1)、(3)
C. 只有 (2)、(3)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)

Refer to the following hypothetical case.

Country P experiences a long period of hyperinflation. When workers receive wages in P dollar (the legal tender of Country P), they would quickly use the P dollar to buy gold or convert it into US dollar. Some workers even only accept jobs with wages denominated in US dollar. Lenders generally do not accept repayment of loans in P dollar when lending money.

Based on the above information, which functions of money of the P dollar were weakened?

(1) medium of exchange
(2) store of value
(3) standard of deferred payment

A. (1) and (2) only
B. (1) and (3) only
C. (2) and (3) only
D. (1), (2) and (3)

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這道題目，我們必須清楚了解貨幣的三個主要功能，以及惡性通脹如何影響貨幣的價值。貨幣的實質價值（購買力）與物價水平成反比：

To answer this question, we must clearly understand the three main functions of money and how hyperinflation affects the value of money. The real value (purchasing power) of money is inversely related to the price level:

$$ \text{Value of Money} = \frac{1}{P} $$

(Where \( P \) is the general price level / 其中 \( P \) 為一般物價水平)

  • Medium of Exchange (交易媒介):
    貨幣被普遍接受作為購買物品和服務的媒介。如果人們拒絕接受某種貨幣進行交易，這個功能就被削弱。
    Money is widely accepted as a medium to buy goods and services. If people refuse to accept a currency for transactions, this function is weakened.
  • Store of Value (價值儲藏):
    貨幣能在一段時間內保持其購買力。在惡性通脹下，物價 \( P \) 急升，貨幣購買力急跌，人們會盡快將貨幣換成實物或其他保值資產。
    Money retains its purchasing power over time. Under hyperinflation, price level \( P \) surges, and purchasing power plummets, so people quickly convert money into real assets or other stable currencies to preserve value.
  • Standard of Deferred Payment (延期支付的標準):
    貨幣被用作計算和結清未來債務的單位。如果債權人拒絕以該貨幣收回貸款，這個功能就被削弱。
    Money is used as a unit to calculate and settle future debts. If creditors refuse to accept the currency for loan repayments, this function is weakened.

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option (正確答案): D

(1) 交易媒介 (Medium of Exchange): 被削弱 (Weakened)
題目指出「一些工人更只接受以美元計算薪金的工作」。薪金是勞工服務的交易代價。既然工人拒絕接受P元作為提供勞工服務的報酬，這代表P元作為交易媒介的功能已經失效。

The question states "Some workers even only accept jobs with wages denominated in US dollar." Wages are the transaction payment for labor services. Since workers refuse to accept P dollar as payment for their labor, its function as a medium of exchange is weakened.

(2) 價值儲藏 (Store of Value): 被削弱 (Weakened)
題目指出「工人收到P元...會趕快地以之購買黃金，或將之兌換成美元」。這行為是因為P元在惡性通脹下迅速貶值，無法保存購買力。因此，人們不願意持有P元，其價值儲藏功能被嚴重削弱。

The question states "workers... quickly use the P dollar to buy gold or convert it into US dollar." This behavior occurs because the P dollar depreciates rapidly during hyperinflation and cannot retain purchasing power. Thus, people are unwilling to hold it, severely weakening its store of value function.

(3) 延期支付的標準 (Standard of Deferred Payment): 被削弱 (Weakened)
題目指出「放貸者貸款時一般不接受以P元歸還欠款」。貸款涉及未來的債務結算。既然放貸者拒絕在未來以P元收回欠款，這直接說明P元失去了作為延期支付標準的功能。

The question states "Lenders generally do not accept repayment of loans in P dollar." Loans involve the settlement of future debts. Since lenders refuse to accept P dollar for future repayments, it directly shows that the P dollar has lost its function as a standard of deferred payment.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

做這類題目時，我們需要進行「關鍵字配對」(Keyword Matching)。將題目中的行為特徵，逐一對應到貨幣的功能上：

When tackling this type of question, we need to do "Keyword Matching". Map the behavioral characteristics in the text to the specific functions of money:

  • 趕快購買黃金 (Quickly buy gold) ➔ 怕貨幣貶值 ➔ 價值儲藏 (Store of Value)
  • 只接受美元薪金 (Only accept USD wages) ➔ 拒絕用P元進行勞務交易 ➔ 交易媒介 (Medium of Exchange)
  • 不接受歸還欠款 (Do not accept repayment) ➔ 涉及未來債務 ➔ 延期支付的標準 (Standard of Deferred Payment)

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

陷阱：法定貨幣 (Legal Tender) 的迷思
很多學生誤以為只要是「法定貨幣」，其「交易媒介」功能就不會被削弱。事實上，即使政府規定P元是法定貨幣，但在極端惡性通脹下，如果公眾普遍失去信心並拒絕接受（例如工人拒收P元薪金），其交易媒介功能依然會崩潰。

Trap: The Myth of Legal Tender
Many students mistakenly believe that as long as something is "legal tender", its "medium of exchange" function cannot be weakened. In reality, even if the government decrees the P dollar as legal tender, under extreme hyperinflation, if the public loses confidence and refuses to accept it (e.g., workers refusing P dollar wages), its medium of exchange function will still collapse.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 題目沒有提到「計價單位 (Unit of Account)」，這個功能有被削弱嗎？
A: 實際上，在惡性通脹下，物價一日多變，商店可能放棄用P元標價而改用美元，這代表「計價單位」功能也被削弱了。但由於選擇題的選項中沒有提供這個選項，我們只需專注分析題目給出的三個功能即可。

Q: The question didn't mention "Unit of Account". Was this function weakened too?
A: In reality, under hyperinflation, prices change multiple times a day. Shops might abandon pricing in P dollars and use USD instead, meaning the "unit of account" function is also weakened. However, since it's not provided in the options, we only need to focus on analyzing the three functions given.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

速解法： 看到「惡性通脹 (Hyperinflation)」一詞，通常貨幣的所有功能都會受到嚴重打擊。如果題目描述了人們全面放棄使用該貨幣（拒收、急放、拒還），你可以大膽推斷所有功能皆被削弱，直接選擇包含所有選項的答案 (D)。

Shortcut: When you see the word "Hyperinflation", typically ALL functions of money are severely hit. If the description shows a complete abandonment of the currency (refusing to accept, quickly dumping, refusing for repayment), you can confidently deduce that all functions are weakened and directly choose the option containing all statements (D).
