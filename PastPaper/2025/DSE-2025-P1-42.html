📊 HKDSE Economics Topic Analysis

Topic (課題): 國際貿易 International Trade

Difficulty (難度): ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)

Key Concepts (核心概念): Absolute Advantage 絕對優勢 Comparative Advantage 比較優勢 Transportation Cost 運輸成本

📝 The Question (題目)

假設X國和Y國都是分配一半資源生產成衣，而另一半資源則生產玩具。他們的產出總量如下：

Suppose Country X and Country Y both allocate half of their resources in producing clothing and half in producing toys. Their total output is shown below.

成衣 / Clothing (units) 玩具 / Toys (units)
X國 / Country X 5 25
Y國 / Country Y 20 10

根據以上資料，下列哪項／些是正確的？
(1) Y國在生產成衣方面享有絕對優勢。
(2) Y國在生產成衣方面享有比較優勢。
(3) 若進口每單位成衣的運輸成本是4單位玩具，兩國之間仍可能出現互利貿易。

Based on the above information, which of the following is/are correct?
(1) Country Y enjoys an absolute advantage in producing clothing.
(2) Country Y enjoys a comparative advantage in producing clothing.
(3) If the transportation cost for importing each unit of clothing is 4 units of toys, mutually beneficial trade is still possible between the two countries.

A. 只有 (1) / (1) only
B. 只有 (2) / (2) only
C. 只有 (1)、(3) / (1) and (3) only
D. 只有 (2)、(3) / (2) and (3) only

🧠 Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 絕對優勢 (Absolute Advantage): 指一個國家能使用相同數量的資源，生產出比其他國家更多的某種物品。

Refers to a country's ability to produce more of a good using the same amount of resources compared to another country.

2. 比較優勢 (Comparative Advantage): 指一個國家生產某物品的機會成本較其他國家低。

Refers to a country's ability to produce a good at a lower opportunity cost than another country.

$$ \text{Opportunity Cost of Good A} = \frac{\text{Output of Good B}}{\text{Output of Good A}} $$

3. 運輸成本與貿易 (Transportation Cost and Trade): 即使存在運輸成本，只要出口國的機會成本加上運輸成本，仍然低於進口國的機會成本，互利貿易便可能發生。

Even with transportation costs, mutually beneficial trade is possible if the exporter's opportunity cost plus the transportation cost is strictly less than the importer's opportunity cost.

$$ \text{Cost}_{\text{Exporter}} + \text{Cost}_{\text{Transport}} < \text{Cost}_{\text{Importer}} $$

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: D

分析 (1) - 錯誤 (Incorrect):
題目只說明兩國各自投入「一半資源」，但沒有說明兩國擁有的總資源數量是否相同。由於缺乏資源數量的比較基準，我們無法判斷哪一國享有絕對優勢。這是DSE極常見的陷阱！

The question only states that both countries allocate "half of their resources", but it does NOT state whether their total resources are identical. Without knowing if the resource base is the same, we cannot determine absolute advantage. This is a very common DSE trap!

分析 (2) - 正確 (Correct):
我們需要計算生產1單位成衣的機會成本：
X國：生產5單位成衣 = 放棄25單位玩具 \(\rightarrow\) 1單位成衣 = 5單位玩具 (5T)
Y國：生產20單位成衣 = 放棄10單位玩具 \(\rightarrow\) 1單位成衣 = 0.5單位玩具 (0.5T)
因為Y國生產成衣的機會成本 (0.5T) 低於X國 (5T)，所以Y國享有比較優勢。

We need to calculate the opportunity cost of producing 1 unit of clothing:
Country X: 5 Clothing = 25 Toys \(\rightarrow\) 1 Clothing = 5 Toys (5T)
Country Y: 20 Clothing = 10 Toys \(\rightarrow\) 1 Clothing = 0.5 Toys (0.5T)
Since Country Y's opportunity cost (0.5T) is lower than Country X's (5T), Country Y has a comparative advantage in clothing.

分析 (3) - 正確 (Correct):
Y國出口成衣，X國進口成衣。若要發生貿易，總成本必須低於進口國自己生產的成本。
總成本 = Y國生產成本 + 運輸成本 = \(0.5T + 4T = 4.5T\)
因為 \(4.5T < 5T\) (X國自己生產的成本)，兩國仍有互利貿易的空間。

Country Y exports clothing, and Country X imports it. For trade to occur, the total cost must be lower than the importer's domestic production cost.
Total Cost = Y's production cost + Transport cost = \(0.5T + 4T = 4.5T\)
Since \(4.5T < 5T\) (X's domestic cost), mutually beneficial trade is still possible.

🛠️ Problem Decomposition & Exam Tricks (題目拆解與考試技巧)

Pivot Point (解題關鍵): 留意題目字眼「他們的一半資源 (half of their resources)」。這並不等於「相同數量的資源 (same amount of resources)」。

Pay close attention to the wording "half of their resources". This does not equate to "same amount of resources".

Exam Shortcut (速解技巧): 當你看到題目沒有明確指出兩國規模或資源相同時，立刻排除所有關於「絕對優勢 (Absolute Advantage)」的選項。這樣你可以瞬間排除選項 A 和 C！

When you see that the question doesn't explicitly state the countries have identical size or resources, immediately eliminate any options claiming "Absolute Advantage". This instantly eliminates Options A and C!

🙋 Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 如果運輸成本是 5 單位玩具，還會有貿易嗎？ (What if the transport cost was 5 units of toys?)

A: 不會 (No)。如果運輸成本是 5T，總成本會變成 \(0.5T + 5T = 5.5T\)。這高於X國自己生產的成本 (5T)。X國會選擇自己生產，貿易不會發生。
If the transport cost is 5T, the total cost becomes \(0.5T + 5T = 5.5T\). This is higher than Country X's domestic cost (5T). Country X would just produce it domestically, and no trade would occur.
