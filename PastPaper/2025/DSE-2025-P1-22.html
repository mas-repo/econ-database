📚 Topic: Market Intervention (Taxation) & Efficiency
課題：市場干預（徵稅）與效率
⭐ Difficulty: ★★★☆☆
🔑 Key Concepts: Tax Burden, Deadweight Loss, Perfectly Elastic Demand
關鍵概念：稅項負擔、效率損失、完全彈性需求

The Question (題目)

下圖顯示某物品的供需曲線。假設政府對該物品的生產徵收一項從量稅。
The following diagram shows the supply and demand curves of a good. Suppose the government imposes a per-unit tax on the production of the good.

注意：需求曲線 (D) 是水平的。
Note: The Demand curve (D) is horizontal.

下列哪些關於徵收上述從量稅的陳述是正確的？
Which of the following statements about the imposition of the per-unit tax are correct?

  1. 消費者沒有任何稅項負擔。
    The consumers bear no tax burden.
  2. 總社會盈餘的下降是面積 0abc。
    The decrease in total social surplus is area 0abc.
  3. 徵收該稅項後出現效率損失。
    There is deadweight loss after the imposition of the tax.

A. 只有 (1)、(2) ((1) and (2) only)
B. 只有 (1)、(3) ((1) and (3) only)
C. 只有 (2)、(3) ((2) and (3) only)
D. (1)、(2) 及 (3) ((1), (2) and (3))

💡 Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 稅項分擔 (Tax Incidence):
稅項負擔取決於需求彈性 (\(E_d\)) 與供應彈性 (\(E_s\)) 的相對大小。原則是「彈性較低者，負擔較重」。
Tax Incidence: The burden of tax depends on the relative price elasticities of demand (\(E_d\)) and supply (\(E_s\)). The principle is "the side with lower elasticity bears a heavier burden."

  • 若 \(E_d = \infty\) (完全彈性需求)，消費者對價格極度敏感，價格不能上升，否則需求量跌至零。因此，賣家承擔所有稅項。
    If \(E_d = \infty\) (perfectly elastic demand), consumers are extremely sensitive to price. Price cannot rise; otherwise, quantity demanded drops to zero. Thus, sellers bear the entire tax burden.

2. 總社會盈餘 (Total Social Surplus, TSS):
$$ TSS = \text{消費者盈餘 (CS)} + \text{生產者盈餘 (PS)} $$ 徵稅後，部分盈餘轉移給政府成為稅收，部分消失成為效率損失。
After taxation, part of the surplus is transferred to the government as tax revenue, and part disappears as deadweight loss.

3. 效率損失 (Deadweight Loss, DWL):
這是由於市場扭曲（如徵稅）導致交易量低於效率水平而損失的社會福利。只要交易量減少（\(Q_1 < Q_0\)），通常就會出現效率損失。
This is the loss of social welfare due to market distortions (like taxes) causing the quantity traded to be below the efficient level. As long as quantity decreases (\(Q_1 < Q_0\)), deadweight loss usually occurs.

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

✅ Correct Answer: B (Option 1 & 3 only)

陳述 (1)：消費者沒有任何稅項負擔 (正確)

Statement (1): The consumers bear no tax burden (Correct)

分析： 圖中的需求曲線 (D) 是水平線，代表完全彈性需求 (Perfectly Elastic Demand, \(E_d = \infty\))。這意味著市場價格由需求決定，賣家無法將任何稅項轉嫁給消費者。價格維持不變，消費者支付的價格沒有上升，所以消費者不承擔稅項負擔。
Analysis: The demand curve (D) in the diagram is a horizontal line, representing Perfectly Elastic Demand (\(E_d = \infty\)). This means the market price is determined by demand, and sellers cannot pass any tax burden to consumers. The price remains unchanged, so consumers do not pay a higher price and thus bear no tax burden.

陳述 (2)：總社會盈餘的下降是面積 0abc (錯誤)

Statement (2): The decrease in total social surplus is area 0abc (Incorrect)

分析： 這是一個概念混淆的陷阱。面積 0abc 實際上代表的是生產者盈餘的減少 (Decrease in Producer Surplus)，而非總社會盈餘的減少。

  • 原本的生產者盈餘 (Original PS): 價格線以下、\(S_0\) 以上的面積 (即三角形 \(P\)-\(c\)-\(0\))。
  • 新的生產者盈餘 (New PS): 價格線以下、\(S_1\) 以上的面積 (即三角形 \(P\)-\(b\)-\(a\))。
  • 生產者盈餘的減少: 原本 PS 減去 新 PS = 面積 \(0abc\)。
然而，題目問的是總社會盈餘 (Total Social Surplus) 的下降（即效率損失）。總社會盈餘的下降僅僅是三角形 abc。由於面積 \(0abc\) 包含了稅收收入（轉移給政府的部分）和效率損失，所以它不等於總社會盈餘的淨損失。

Analysis: This is a conceptual trap. Area 0abc actually represents the decrease in Producer Surplus, not the decrease in Total Social Surplus.
  • Original Producer Surplus (PS): The area below the Price line and above \(S_0\) (Triangle \(P\)-\(c\)-\(0\)).
  • New Producer Surplus (PS): The area below the Price line and above \(S_1\) (Triangle \(P\)-\(b\)-\(a\)).
  • Decrease in PS: Original PS minus New PS = Area \(0abc\).
However, the question asks for the decrease in Total Social Surplus (which is the Deadweight Loss). The decrease in Total Social Surplus is specifically the triangle abc. Since area \(0abc\) includes both the tax revenue (transferred to government) and the deadweight loss, it is incorrect to say it represents the loss in social surplus.

陳述 (3)：徵收該稅項後出現效率損失 (正確)

Statement (3): There is deadweight loss after the imposition of the tax (Correct)

分析： 徵稅導致供應曲線由 \(S_0\) 向左移至 \(S_1\)。均衡數量由 c 點對應的數量減少至 b 點對應的數量。由於交易數量低於效率水平（邊際利益 > 邊際成本），市場出現了效率損失 (Deadweight Loss)。這部分損失由三角形 abc 表示。
Analysis: The tax shifts the supply curve from \(S_0\) to \(S_1\). The equilibrium quantity decreases from point c to point b. Since the quantity traded is below the efficient level (Marginal Benefit > Marginal Cost), Deadweight Loss occurs. This efficiency loss is represented by the triangle abc.

🔍 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

  • 圖表類型 (Chart Type): 供需模型 (Supply and Demand Model)。
    • D (需求曲線): 水平線 (Horizontal)，代表完全彈性需求 (Perfectly Elastic Demand)。這意味著消費者對價格極度敏感，價格固定在 \(P\)。
    • S (供給曲線): 從 \(S_0\) 向左上移動到 \(S_1\)，反映從量稅 (Per-unit Tax) 的徵收。
  • 關鍵點與區域 (Key Points & Areas):
    • 均衡點 (Equilibrium): 由點 \(c\) 移動到點 \(b\)。
    • 數量 (Quantity): 交易量由 \(c\) 對應的數量減少至 \(b\) 對應的數量。
    • 面積 abc: 這是效率損失 (Deadweight Loss)，也就是總社會盈餘的下降 (Decrease in Total Social Surplus)。這是由於交易量低於效率水平造成的。
    • 面積 0abc: 這是生產者盈餘的減少 (Decrease in Producer Surplus)。它包含了轉移給政府的稅收收入（矩形部分）以及效率損失（三角形部分），因此不等於總社會盈餘的淨損失。
  • 關鍵破題點 (The Pivot Point):
    • 看到水平需求曲線，立即鎖定：消費者不付稅 (Consumer Burden = 0) 且 價格不變。
  • Chart Type: Supply and Demand Model.
    • D (Demand Curve): Horizontal line, representing Perfectly Elastic Demand. This implies consumers are infinitely sensitive to price, and price is fixed at \(P\).
    • S (Supply Curve): Shifts from \(S_0\) to \(S_1\), reflecting the imposition of a per-unit tax.
  • Key Points & Areas:
    • Equilibrium: Moves from point \(c\) to point \(b\).
    • Quantity: The quantity traded decreases.
    • Area abc: This is the Deadweight Loss, which equals the Decrease in Total Social Surplus. It occurs because the quantity traded is below the efficient level.
    • Area 0abc: This is the Decrease in Producer Surplus. It includes the tax revenue transferred to the government (the rectangular part) and the deadweight loss (the triangular part), so it is NOT the net loss to society.
  • The Pivot Point:
    • Seeing a Horizontal Demand Curve immediately implies: Consumers bear no tax burden and Price remains unchanged.
⚡ Exam Tricks (考試技巧)

「誰線平，誰不付；誰線直，誰全付」
這是一個關於彈性的口訣。
- 線越平 (彈性大) -> 越有能力逃避加價 -> 稅負越少。
- 線越直 (彈性小) -> 越依賴該產品 -> 稅負越重。
此題 D 線完全平 (水平)，所以消費者稅負為 0。
"Flat line pays nothing; Vertical line pays all"
- Flatter curve (High Elasticity) -> Can easily switch/quit -> Low tax burden.
- Steeper curve (Low Elasticity) -> Dependent on good -> High tax burden.
Here, D is perfectly flat, so consumer burden is 0.
