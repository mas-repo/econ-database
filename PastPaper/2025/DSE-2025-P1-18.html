HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Demand & Supply (需求與供應) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Total Revenue, Substitutes
📝 The Question (題目)

由於橙的收成欠佳，很多果汁生產商改以柑橘等其他水果作為生產果汁的材料。

Due to poor harvests of oranges, many juice producers use other fruits such as mandarins as an alternative ingredient to produce juice.

根據上述資料，下列哪項是正確的？

Based on the above information, which of the following is correct?

  • A. 橙的市場上出現效率損失。
    There is a deadweight loss in the market of oranges.
  • B. 橙的賣家的總收入可能上升。
    The total revenue of orange sellers may increase.
  • C. 柑橘市場上的總社會盈餘下降。
    The total social surplus in the market of mandarins decreases.
  • D. 柑橘的總交易價值可能下降。
    The total exchange value of mandarins may decrease.
📚 Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 供應震盪與總收入 (Supply Shock & Total Revenue)

當供應減少（\(S \downarrow\)）時，均衡價格上升（\(P \uparrow\)），均衡數量下降（\(Q \downarrow\)）。總收入（\(TR = P \times Q\)）的變動取決於需求價格彈性（\(E_d\)）。

When supply decreases (\(S \downarrow\)), equilibrium price rises (\(P \uparrow\)) and quantity falls (\(Q \downarrow\)). The change in Total Revenue (\(TR = P \times Q\)) depends on the Price Elasticity of Demand (\(E_d\)).
$$ \text{If } E_d < 1 \text{ (Inelastic/低彈性)}: \% \Delta P > \% \Delta Q \Rightarrow TR \uparrow $$ $$ \text{If } E_d > 1 \text{ (Elastic/高彈性)}: \% \Delta P < \% \Delta Q \Rightarrow TR \downarrow $$

2. 替代品與相關市場 (Substitutes & Related Markets)

橙與柑橘在生產上是替代品。當橙的供應減少導致價格上升，生產商會轉用柑橘，導致柑橘的需求增加（\(D \uparrow\)）。

Oranges and mandarins are substitutes in production. When the supply of oranges decreases and price rises, producers switch to mandarins, causing an increase in demand (\(D \uparrow\)) for mandarins.
✅ Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)
Correct Option: B
Option A: Incorrect (錯誤)

分析：橙的收成欠佳屬於自然因素導致的供應減少（\(S\) 左移）。市場會自動調節至新的均衡點。在新的均衡點上，邊際利益等於新的邊際成本（\(MB = MC'\)），因此達致配置效率，沒有效率損失（效率損失）。

Analysis: Poor harvest is a natural supply decrease (shift of \(S\) to the left). The market adjusts to a new equilibrium. At this new point, Marginal Benefit equals the new Marginal Cost (\(MB = MC'\)), so allocative efficiency is achieved. There is no deadweight loss.
Option B: Correct (正確)

分析：橙的供應減少導致價格上升（\(P \uparrow\)）及交易量下降（\(Q \downarrow\)）。由於題目沒有提供橙的需求彈性（Elasticity），如果橙的需求是低彈性（Inelastic），價格上升的百分比會大於數量下降的百分比，總收入（\(TR\)）便會上升。因此，總收入「可能」上升是正確的。

Analysis: The decrease in supply of oranges leads to a higher price (\(P \uparrow\)) and lower quantity (\(Q \downarrow\)). Since the question does not specify the elasticity of demand for oranges, if the demand is inelastic, the percentage increase in price will exceed the percentage decrease in quantity, causing Total Revenue (\(TR\)) to rise. Thus, stating TR "may" increase is correct.
Option C: Incorrect (錯誤)

分析：果汁生產商轉用柑橘，使柑橘的需求增加（\(D\) 右移）。需求曲線向右移會增加需求曲線與價格線之間的面積（消費者盈餘）以及價格線與供應曲線之間的面積（生產者盈餘）。因此，總社會盈餘（TSS）應該是上升。

Analysis: Juice producers switching to mandarins increases the demand for mandarins (\(D\) shifts right). A rightward shift of the demand curve increases the area between the demand curve and supply curve. Therefore, Total Social Surplus (TSS) should increase.
Option D: Incorrect (錯誤)

分析：柑橘的需求增加（\(D \uparrow\)），而供應不變（假設是一般向上傾斜的供應曲線）。這會導致均衡價格上升（\(P \uparrow\)）及均衡數量上升（\(Q \uparrow\)）。由於 \(P\) 和 \(Q\) 都上升，總交易價值（\(P \times Q\)）必定上升，不可能下降。

Analysis: The demand for mandarins increases (\(D \uparrow\)) while supply remains unchanged (assuming an upward-sloping supply curve). This leads to both higher equilibrium price (\(P \uparrow\)) and higher equilibrium quantity (\(Q \uparrow\)). Since both \(P\) and \(Q\) rise, the total exchange value (\(P \times Q\)) must increase. It cannot decrease.
🔍 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)
Pivot Point (關鍵點)

1. 橙市場 (Orange Market):
收成欠佳 = 供應減少 (\(S \downarrow\))。

2. 柑橘市場 (Mandarin Market):
替代材料 = 需求增加 (\(D \uparrow\))。

1. Orange Market: Poor harvest = Supply decreases (\(S \downarrow\)).
2. Mandarin Market: Alternative ingredient = Demand increases (\(D \uparrow\)).
Logic Flow (邏輯流向)

橙 \(S \downarrow\) \(\rightarrow\) 橙 \(P \uparrow, Q \downarrow\) \(\rightarrow\) 橙 TR ? (視乎彈性)。

柑橘 \(D \uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) 柑橘 \(P \uparrow, Q \uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) 柑橘 TR \(\uparrow\) (必定)。

Orange \(S \downarrow\) \(\rightarrow\) Orange \(P \uparrow, Q \downarrow\) \(\rightarrow\) Orange TR ? (Depends on elasticity).
Mandarin \(D \uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) Mandarin \(P \uparrow, Q \uparrow\) \(\rightarrow\) Mandarin TR \(\uparrow\) (Must increase).
⚠️ Common Mistakes & Exam Tricks (常犯錯誤與考試技巧)
Trap: "Quantity drops, so Revenue must drop?" (陷阱：數量跌，收入一定跌？)

很多學生看到橙的交易量下降，就直覺認為總收入會下降。切記：當價格和數量呈反方向變動時（例如供應改變），必須考慮彈性 (Elasticity)。

Many students intuitively think Total Revenue falls because quantity falls. Remember: When Price and Quantity move in opposite directions (supply shift), you MUST consider Elasticity.
Trick: The word "May" (技巧：留意「可能」二字)

在經濟科 MC 中，如果題目沒有給予足夠數據（如彈性數值），帶有「可能」(May) 的選項通常是正確答案的強烈候選者，因為它包含了不確定性。

In Economics MC, if the question lacks specific data (like elasticity values), options containing "may" are often strong candidates for the correct answer because they account for uncertainty.
