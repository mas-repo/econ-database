📍 Topic: 貨幣供應 Money Supply (Macroeconomics)
⭐ Difficulty: ★★★★☆
🔑 Key Concepts: M1, M2, M3, Cash held by public (公眾持有的法定貨幣), Remittance (匯款)

1. 題目 The Question

何小姐以港幣$500 000現金向黎先生購買某接受存款公司發行的可轉讓存款證。其後，黎先生將港幣$200 000現金存入其港元儲蓄戶口，並將餘款匯出予居於澳洲的父母。
Miss Ho uses HK$500 000 cash to buy from Mr Lai a negotiable certificate of deposit issued by a deposit-taking company. Mr Lai then deposits HK$200 000 cash to his Hong Kong dollar savings account and remits the remaining amount to his parents in Australia.
上述行動對港元貨幣供應會有什麼即時的影響？
What will be the immediate effect of the above actions on the Hong Kong dollar money supply?
A. 貨幣供應M1會上升，而貨幣供應M3會維持不變。
M1 will increase while M3 will remain unchanged.
B. 貨幣供應M1會維持不變，而貨幣供應M3會下降。
M1 will remain unchanged while M3 will decrease.
C. 貨幣供應M1和M3皆會下降。
Both M1 and M3 will decrease.
D. 貨幣供應M1和M3皆會維持不變。
Both M1 and M3 will remain unchanged.

2. 概念重溫 Concept Review

在香港，貨幣供應（Money Supply）分為三個層次。我們必須清楚各層次的定義：
In Hong Kong, the money supply is classified into three tiers. We must clearly understand the definition of each tier:
$$M1 = C_p + D_d$$ $$M2 = M1 + D_s + D_t + NCDs(LB)$$ $$M3 = M2 + Deposits(RLB, DTC) + NCDs(RLB, DTC)$$
  • \(C_p\) = 公眾持有的法定貨幣 (Cash held by the public)
    \(C_p\) = Cash held by the public
  • \(D_d\) = 活期存款 (Demand deposits)
    \(D_d\) = Demand deposits
  • \(D_s, D_t\) = 儲蓄及定期存款 (Savings and Time deposits)
    \(D_s, D_t\) = Savings and Time deposits

3. 答案與詳細分析 The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案是 C。
The correct option is C.
第一步：何小姐以現金向黎先生購買可轉讓存款證 (NCD)
這只是二手市場交易。現金 \(C_p\) 由何小姐轉移到黎先生手上，總 \(C_p\) 不變。NCD 的擁有權改變，但總量不變。因此，M1、M2 和 M3 均沒有變化。
Step 1: Miss Ho buys an NCD from Mr Lai with cash
This is merely a secondary market transaction. Cash \(C_p\) is transferred from Miss Ho to Mr Lai, so total \(C_p\) remains unchanged. The ownership of the NCD changes, but the total amount remains unchanged. Therefore, M1, M2, and M3 remain unchanged.
第二步：黎先生將 $200,000 現金存入儲蓄戶口
現金存入銀行後，不再屬於公眾持有，因此 \(C_p\) 減少 $200,000。同時，儲蓄存款 \(D_s\) 增加 $200,000。
對 M1 的影響：因為 \(C_p\) 下降而 \(D_d\) 不變，M1 下降。
對 M2/M3 的影響：M1 下降了 $200,000，但 \(D_s\) 增加了 $200,000，兩者互相抵消，因此 M2 和 M3 維持不變。
Step 2: Mr Lai deposits $200,000 cash into his savings account
Once cash is deposited into a bank, it is no longer held by the public, so \(C_p\) decreases by $200,000. Meanwhile, savings deposits \(D_s\) increase by $200,000.
Effect on M1: Since \(C_p\) decreases and \(D_d\) is unchanged, M1 decreases.
Effect on M2/M3: M1 decreases by $200,000, but \(D_s\) increases by $200,000. The two offset each other, so M2 and M3 remain unchanged.
第三步：黎先生將餘款 ($300,000) 匯出澳洲
將現金匯出香港，意味著這筆資金完全離開了香港的貨幣體系。因此，公眾持有的法定貨幣 \(C_p\) 減少 $300,000。
因為 \(C_p\) 下降，M1 下降。由於這筆錢沒有轉化為香港銀行體系內的任何存款，M2 和 M3 也會跟隨 M1 一同下降。
Step 3: Mr Lai remits the remaining $300,000 to Australia
Remitting cash out of Hong Kong means this money completely leaves the HK monetary system. Therefore, cash held by the public \(C_p\) decreases by $300,000.
Because \(C_p\) decreases, M1 decreases. Since this money is not converted into any deposits within the HK banking system, M2 and M3 will also decrease along with M1.
總結： M1 總共下降了 $500,000，而 M3 總共下降了 $300,000。兩者皆下降，故選 C。
Conclusion: M1 decreases by a total of $500,000, and M3 decreases by a total of $300,000. Both decrease, hence Option C is correct.

4. 題目拆解 Problem Decomposition

解答這類題目的關鍵在於「逐個動作分析」 (Step-by-step analysis)。
The key to solving this type of question is "step-by-step analysis".
  • 干擾資訊 (Distractor)：「接受存款公司發行的可轉讓存款證」。這只是用來擾亂你的，因為這是個人之間的二手交易，不涉及新發行或贖回，對貨幣供應毫無影響。
    Distractor: "negotiable certificate of deposit issued by a deposit-taking company". This is just to confuse you, as it's a secondary transaction between individuals. It involves no new issuance or redemption, having zero effect on money supply.
  • 轉捩點 (Pivot Point)：「匯出 (Remits)」。只要資金匯出香港，\(C_p\) 就會永久性減少（直到資金回流），這會直接拖低 M1、M2 和 M3。
    Pivot Point: "Remits". Whenever funds are remitted out of Hong Kong, \(C_p\) decreases permanently (until funds return), which directly drags down M1, M2, and M3.

5. 常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

⚠️ 陷阱一： 誤以為存入「儲蓄戶口」會增加 M1。請記住，M1 只包含「活期存款」\(D_d\)，不包含「儲蓄存款」\(D_s\)。
⚠️ Trap 1: Mistakenly thinking that depositing into a "savings account" increases M1. Remember, M1 only includes "demand deposits" \(D_d\), not "savings deposits" \(D_s\).
⚠️ 陷阱二： 忘記 M2 和 M3 是包含 M1 的。如果 M1 下降，而沒有其他存款成分上升來抵消，M2 和 M3 必然會跟著下降。
⚠️ Trap 2: Forgetting that M2 and M3 include M1. If M1 decreases and no other deposit components increase to offset it, M2 and M3 must inevitably decrease as well.

6. 學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

Q: 為什麼何小姐買 NCD 不會令 M3 增加？NCD 不是 M3 的一部分嗎？
Q: Why doesn't Miss Ho buying the NCD increase M3? Isn't NCD part of M3?
A: 是的，接受存款公司發行的 NCD 屬於 M3。但是，這張 NCD 早已存在於市場中（原本由黎先生持有）。何小姐只是從黎先生手上買過來，這屬於二手市場的擁有權轉移。市場上的 NCD 總額並沒有增加，所以 M3 不變。
A: Yes, NCDs issued by DTCs belong to M3. However, this NCD already existed in the market (originally held by Mr Lai). Miss Ho simply bought it from Mr Lai, which is a transfer of ownership in the secondary market. The total amount of NCDs in the market did not increase, so M3 remains unchanged.

7. 考試技巧與速解 Exam Tricks & Shortcuts

💡 速解法：追蹤現金流失！
在考場上，看到「匯出 (Remit)」或「繳交外地稅款」等字眼，代表有資金流出香港體系。只要有現金流出，\(C_p\) 必定下跌。由於這筆錢沒有變成香港的任何存款，它對 M1, M2, M3 的影響是全線下跌。因此，只要看到「餘款匯出」，就可以立刻判斷 M3 必定下降，直接排除 A 和 D！
💡 Shortcut: Track the cash leakage!
In the exam, when you see words like "Remit" or "paying foreign taxes", it means funds are leaking out of the HK system. Whenever cash flows out, \(C_p\) must drop. Since this money doesn't become any HK deposit, its effect on M1, M2, and M3 is an across-the-board decrease. Therefore, once you see "remits the remaining amount", you can immediately conclude M3 must decrease, instantly eliminating options A and D!
