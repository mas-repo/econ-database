Topic: Basic Economic Problems (基本經濟問題)

Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆ Key Concepts: Resource Allocation, Price Mechanism DSE Economics
The Question (題目)

[Chinese] 面對電動車需求上升，一間製造商決定生產更多電動車，並提高電動車的售價。
上述製造商的決定與經濟學中的 ___________ 問題相關，因為該決定與 ___________ 相關。

[English] In response to a rise in the demand for electric vehicles, a manufacturer decided to produce more electric vehicles and sell them at a higher price.
The above decision of the manufacturer is related to the ___________ question(s) in Economics because it is related _____________.

(1) 「生產什麼」 …… 產量的選擇
      ‘what to produce’...... the choice on the quantity of outputs
(2) 「怎樣生產」 …… 要素投入的選擇
      ‘how to produce’...... the choice on factor inputs
(3) 「為誰生產」 …… 產出的分配
      ‘for whom to produce’...... the distribution of outputs
  • A. 只有 (1) / (1) only
  • B. 只有 (2) / (2) only
  • C. 只有 (3) / (3) only
  • D. 只有 (1)、(3) / (1) and (3) only
Concept Review (概念重溫)

To solve this problem, we must understand the Three Basic Economic Problems faced by every society due to scarcity.

1. What to produce? (生產什麼？)
涉及選擇生產貨品的種類及數量。 Concerns the choice of the types and quantities of goods to be produced.
2. How to produce? (怎樣生產？)
涉及生產方法及要素投入的選擇（例如：勞力密集 vs 資本密集）。 Concerns the choice of production methods and factor inputs (e.g., labor-intensive vs. capital-intensive).
3. For whom to produce? (為誰生產？)
涉及產品的分配。在市場經濟中，通常由價格機制決定（誰付得起錢，誰就獲得產品）。 Concerns the distribution of products. In a market economy, this is usually determined by the price mechanism (whoever is willing and able to pay the price gets the product).
Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)
Correct Option: D
Statement (1): "Produce more electric vehicles" (生產更多電動車)
正確。這句話明確提到了「更多」（More），這是一個關於數量 (Quantity) 的決定。決定生產多少數量屬於「生產什麼」的問題。 Correct. The phrase "produce more" explicitly refers to quantity. Deciding on the quantity of output falls under the problem of "What to produce".
Statement (2): "How to produce" (怎樣生產)
錯誤。題目中完全沒有提及製造商使用什麼技術、機器或勞工來生產電動車。沒有涉及生產方法的選擇。 Incorrect. The question does not mention anything about the technology, machinery, or labor used to make the vehicles. There is no choice regarding production methods involved here.
Statement (3): "Sell them at a higher price" (提高電動車的售價)
正確。這是最關鍵的一點。價格決定了產品的分配。提高價格意味著只有那些願意並有能力支付較高價格的人才能獲得電動車。這是關於產品分配 (Distribution of outputs)，即「為誰生產」。 Correct. This is the critical point. Price determines the allocation of goods. Raising the price means only those willing and able to pay the higher price will obtain the vehicle. This concerns the distribution of outputs, which is "For whom to produce".

Therefore, statements (1) and (3) are correct.
\( \therefore \) Option D is the answer.

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)
⚠️ The "Price" Trap (價格陷阱)
很多學生誤以為「價格」只與供求有關，而忘記了價格在資源分配中的角色。在基本經濟問題中，價格設定 (Pricing) 直接影響誰能買到產品，因此它屬於「為誰生產」。 Many students mistakenly think "Price" is only about supply and demand, forgetting its role in resource allocation. In the context of basic economic problems, pricing directly affects who can buy the product, so it belongs to "For whom to produce".
