Topic: International Trade (國際貿易) Sub-topic: Trade Barriers (貿易壁壘) Difficulty: ★★★★☆

Import Quota & World Price Changes

進口配額與國際價格變動

Key Concepts: Import Quota (進口配額), Small Open Economy (小型開放經濟), Excess Demand (超額需求), Quota Rent (配額租值)

The Question (題目)

某小型開放經濟一直對進口紅酒實施有效配額。若紅酒的國際價格下降，___________。

A small open economy has imposed an effective quota on imported red wine. If the world price of red wine decreases, ___________.

A. 該經濟的紅酒進口量會上升
the volume of red wine imported by the economy will increase
B. 該經濟的本地紅酒生產量會上升
the quantity of red wine domestically produced in the economy will increase
C. 該經濟的紅酒消費量會維持不變
the quantity of red wine consumed in the economy will remain unchanged
D. 該經濟中銷售的紅酒的價格會下降
the price of red wine sold in the economy will decrease

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. Effective Import Quota (有效進口配額)

配額是對進口商品數量的限制。當配額數量小於自由貿易下的進口量時，配額才算「有效」。
A quota is a limit on the quantity of a good that can be imported. It is "effective" only if the quota limit is set below the import volume under free trade.

$$ Total\ Supply = Domestic\ Supply (S_d) + Quota $$ $$ 總供應 = 本地供應 (S_d) + 配額 $$

2. Market Equilibrium with Quota (配額下的市場均衡)

在有效配額下，本地價格 \(P_{local}\) 會高於國際價格 \(P_{world}\)。總供應曲線會在配額限制處變成垂直（或向右平移）。
Under an effective quota, the local price \(P_{local}\) is higher than the world price \(P_{world}\). The total supply curve becomes vertical (or shifts to the right) at the quota limit.

關鍵公式 (Key Formula): $$ Q_d = Q_s + Quota $$ (Quantity Demanded = Domestic Production + Import Quota)

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: C

Option A: Incorrect (錯誤)

分析：題目指出配額是「有效」的 (effective)。這意味著進口商已經用盡了所有配額。即使國際價格下降，進口商想進口更多，但受法律限制（配額），進口量不能增加。進口量固定在配額水平。

Analysis: The question states the quota is "effective". This means importers are already utilizing the full quota limit. Even if the world price drops and importers want to buy more, they are legally restricted by the quota. The import volume is fixed at the quota level.

Option B: Incorrect (錯誤)

分析：本地生產量取決於本地價格 \(P_{local}\)。既然進口量被配額鎖死，而本地需求和本地供應曲線沒有移動，市場均衡點不會改變。因此，本地價格 \(P_{local}\) 不變，本地生產量也不變。

Analysis: Domestic production depends on the local price \(P_{local}\). Since the import volume is locked by the quota, and neither the domestic demand nor domestic supply curves have shifted, the market equilibrium remains unchanged. Therefore, \(P_{local}\) stays the same, and domestic production remains constant.

Option C: Correct (正確)

分析：這是一個「數量限制」的特徵。
1. 進口量 = 固定（受配額限制）。
2. 本地價格 \(P_{local}\) = 由本地需求與（本地供應 + 配額）決定。
國際價格 \(P_{world}\) 的變動只會影響進口商的成本，不會影響本地市場的供需平衡。既然 \(P_{local}\) 不變，消費量（需求量）自然維持不變。

Analysis: This is a characteristic of a "quantity restriction".
1. Import volume = Fixed (limited by quota).
2. Local Price \(P_{local}\) = Determined by domestic demand and (domestic supply + quota).
A change in world price \(P_{world}\) only affects the importers' cost, not the supply-demand balance in the local market. Since \(P_{local}\) remains unchanged, the quantity consumed (quantity demanded) remains unchanged.

Option D: Incorrect (錯誤)

分析：這是學生最常犯的錯誤。學生常以為成本下降（國際價格跌），售價就會跌。但在配額制度下，本地售價是由「本地稀缺性」決定的。總供應量沒有增加（因為配額沒變），所以本地價格不會下降。結果只是進口商賺取了更高的配額租值 (Quota Rent)。

Analysis: This is the most common student error. Students often assume that if cost drops (world price falls), the selling price must fall. However, under a quota system, the local selling price is determined by "local scarcity". The total supply has not increased (because the quota is unchanged), so the local price will not decrease. The result is simply that importers earn a higher Quota Rent.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

The Pivot Point (解題關鍵點)

配額切斷了國際價格與本地價格的聯繫。
The quota severs the link between world price and local price.

在關稅 (Tariff) 下，\(P_{local} = P_{world} + Tariff\)，所以 \(P_{world}\) 跌，\(P_{local}\) 會跌。
但在配額 (Quota) 下，只要配額有效，\(P_{local}\) 只受本地供需影響。

Logic Flow (邏輯流程)

  1. Input: World Price \(P_{world}\) decreases ($\downarrow$).
  2. Constraint: Quota is fixed quantity ($\bar{Q}$).
  3. Mechanism: Importers buy cheaper but cannot sell more than $\bar{Q}$.
  4. Result: Total Supply in local market is unchanged.
  5. Conclusion: Local Price and Quantity remain unchanged.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

  • The "Disconnect" Rule (脫鉤定律): 看到「配額 (Quota)」且是「有效 (Effective)」的，請記住：國際價格變動不影響本地市場均衡（除非國際價格升得太高導致配額失效）。
    When you see "Effective Quota", remember: World price changes DO NOT affect local market equilibrium (unless world price rises so much the quota becomes ineffective).
  • Quota Rent Calculation (配額租值): 雖然題目沒問，但要記住：配額租值 = \((P_{local} - P_{world}) \times Quota\)。當 \(P_{world}\) 下降，\(P_{local}\) 不變，租值會上升。
    Quota Rent = \((P_{local} - P_{world}) \times Quota\). If \(P_{world}\) drops and \(P_{local}\) stays same, Quota Rent increases.
