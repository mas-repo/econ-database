Topic: Market Intervention 市場干預 Difficulty: ★★★★☆ Concepts: Quota 配額, Per-unit Tax 從量稅, Deadweight Loss 淨損失

題目 The Question

參考下列物品X的供需表。

Refer to the following supply and demand schedules of Good X.

價格 Price ($) 181614121086
需求量 Qd 100150200250300350400
供應量 Qs 400350300250200150100

假設政府對物品X實施100單位的配額。下列哪項陳述是正確的？

Suppose the government imposes a quota on Good X at 100 units. Which of the following statements is correct?

  1. 若政府增加配額，物品X的總收入可能下降。
    If the government increases the quota, the total revenue of Good X may decrease.
  2. 若政府增加配額，效率損失可能上升。
    If the government increases the quota, the deadweight loss may increase.
  3. 若政府以徵收$6從量稅取代配額，兩項措施對價格的影響是相等的。
    Instead of imposing the quota, the imposition of a per-unit tax of $6 would have equivalent effect on the price.
  4. 若政府以徵收$12從量稅取代配額，兩項措施對交易量的影響是相等的。
    Instead of imposing the quota, the imposition of a per-unit tax of $12 would have equivalent effect on the quantity transacted.

概念重溫 Concept Review

要解答這條題目，我們需要理解市場均衡 (Market Equilibrium)、配額 (Quota) 及 從量稅 (Per-unit Tax) 的運作機制。

To solve this question, we must understand the mechanisms of Market Equilibrium, Quota, and Per-unit Tax.

  • 配額 (Quota): 政府設定的最高交易量限制。當配額生效時，市場價格由需求者願意支付的最高價格決定。 Quota: A legal limit on the quantity transacted. When effective, the market price is determined by the maximum price consumers are willing to pay for that quantity.
  • 從量稅 (Per-unit Tax): 政府對每單位交易物品徵收的固定稅款。這會導致買家支付的價格 \(P_d\) 與賣家實收的價格 \(P_s\) 之間出現差距。 Per-unit Tax: A fixed tax levied on each unit transacted. It creates a wedge between the price buyers pay \(P_d\) and the price sellers receive \(P_s\).
$$ \text{Tax} = P_d - P_s $$

答案與詳細分析 The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Option: D

步驟一：找出自由市場均衡
在沒有干預的情況下，當價格為 $12 時，需求量等於供應量（250單位）。

Step 1: Find the free market equilibrium
Without intervention, at a price of $12, quantity demanded equals quantity supplied (250 units).

步驟二：分析配額為100單位的影響
當配額為100單位時，交易量被限制在100。根據需求表，消費者願意為100單位支付的最高價格是 $18。因此，市場價格為 $18。

Step 2: Analyze the effect of a 100-unit quota
With a quota of 100 units, the transacted quantity is restricted to 100. From the demand schedule, the maximum price consumers are willing to pay for 100 units is $18. Thus, the market price is $18.

選項分析 Option Breakdown:

A 錯誤: 當配額從100增加（例如增至150），價格會降至$16，總收入從 \(18 \times 100 = 1800\) 升至 \(16 \times 150 = 2400\)。只要配額未超過均衡數量(250)，增加配額會使交易量上升，總收入增加。

A is Incorrect: If the quota increases (e.g., to 150), price falls to $16, and total revenue rises from \(18 \times 100 = 1800\) to \(16 \times 150 = 2400\). As long as the quota is below equilibrium (250), increasing it raises total revenue.

B 錯誤: 增加配額會使交易量更接近自由市場均衡數量(250)，因此會減少效率損失（無謂損失）。

B is Incorrect: Increasing the quota moves the transacted quantity closer to the free market equilibrium (250), which decreases the deadweight loss.

C 錯誤: 如果徵收$6從量稅，我們需要尋找 \(P_d - P_s = 6\) 的情況。這不會發生在交易量為100的時候（此時差距為 \(18 - 6 = 12\)）。因此價格不會與配額時相同。

C is Incorrect: If a $6 tax is imposed, we look for a point where \(P_d - P_s = 6\). This does not happen at Q=100 (where the gap is \(18 - 6 = 12\)). Thus, the price effect is not equivalent.

D 正確: 在交易量為100單位時，買家願意支付的價格 \(P_d = \$18\)，賣家願意接受的最低價格 \(P_s = \$6\)。兩者差距為 \(18 - 6 = \$12\)。因此，徵收$12的從量稅會剛好令交易量減少至100單位，與配額的影響完全相同。

D is Correct: At a quantity of 100 units, the buyers' price \(P_d = \$18\) and the sellers' price \(P_s = \$6\). The difference is \(18 - 6 = \$12\). Therefore, a per-unit tax of $12 would reduce the transacted quantity to exactly 100 units, having an equivalent effect on quantity as the quota.

題目拆解 Problem Decomposition & Exam Tricks

關鍵點 (Pivot Point)： 解決此類「配額與稅收等效性」題目的核心在於找出目標交易量（此題為100）對應的需求價格 \(P_d\) 和供應價格 \(P_s\)。

Pivot Point: The core to solving "quota-tax equivalence" questions is finding the demand price \(P_d\) and supply price \(P_s\) at the target quantity (100 in this case).

速解法 (Shortcut)：
1. 看到配額 = 100，立刻在表格找 Qd = 100 及 Qs = 100。
2. Qd = 100 時，P = 18。
3. Qs = 100 時，P = 6。
4. 等效稅款 (Equivalent Tax) = \(18 - 6 = 12\)。直接選 D！

Shortcut:
1. See Quota = 100, immediately find Qd = 100 and Qs = 100 in the table.
2. At Qd = 100, P = 18.
3. At Qs = 100, P = 6.
4. Equivalent Tax = \(18 - 6 = 12\). Choose D directly!

常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

混淆配額下的定價機制： 很多學生誤以為配額為100時，價格會是供應量為100時的價格（$6）。記住，在供不應求的情況下，買家會互相競爭，推高價格直到等於他們願意支付的最高價格（需求曲線上的價格，$18）。

Confusing the pricing mechanism under a quota: Many students mistakenly think the price under a 100-unit quota will be the supply price ($6). Remember, with excess demand, buyers compete and bid up the price to their maximum willingness to pay (the price on the demand curve, $18).
