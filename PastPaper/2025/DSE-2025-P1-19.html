The table below shows the market shares of top four subscription video-on-demand (SVoD) service providers worldwide in March 2024.

概念重溫 Concept Review

要解答這條題目，我們需要了解不同市場結構 (Market Structure) 的特徵，以及如何從數據中辨別它們。

To solve this question, we need to understand the characteristics of different Market Structures and how to identify them from data.

  • 寡頭壟斷 (Oligopoly): 市場由少數幾個大型賣家主導（具支配力）。這通常可以通過計算集中度 (Concentration Ratio) 來證明，即最大的幾間公司佔據了市場的大部分份額。 Oligopoly: A market dominated by a few large sellers (dominant firms). This is usually evidenced by calculating the Concentration Ratio, where the largest firms hold a significant majority of the market share.
  • 壟斷性競爭 (Monopolistic Competition): 市場上有許多賣家，每間公司的市場佔有率都很小，且提供異質（有差異）的產品。 Monopolistic Competition: A market with many sellers, where each firm has a very small market share and provides heterogeneous (differentiated) products.
$$ CR_4 = \sum_{i=1}^{4} \text{Market Share}_i = 18.8\% + 17.7\% + 11.7\% + 10.6\% = 58.8\% $$

四大公司的總市場佔有率超過一半，顯示市場高度集中。

The total market share of the top four firms is over half, indicating a highly concentrated market.

答案與詳細分析 The Answer & Detailed Analysis

正確答案 Correct Option: C

選項 A 及 B 錯誤: 數據顯示四大供應商佔據了 \(58.8\%\) 的市場份額，這意味著市場由少數幾家大公司主導，這不符合「壟斷性競爭」（由大量小公司組成）的特徵。此外，表格中沒有提供任何關於「資訊不完全」或「服務異質性」的資料。

Options A & B are Incorrect: The data shows the top four providers hold \(58.8\%\) of the market share, meaning the market is dominated by a few large firms. This contradicts "monopolistic competition" (which consists of many small firms). Furthermore, the table does not provide any information about "imperfect information" or "heterogeneous services".

選項 C 正確: 表格清楚顯示四家公司（網飛、亞馬遜等）各自擁有龐大的市場佔有率（總和超過一半）。這直接證明了市場上存在「數個具支配力的服務供應商」，符合寡頭壟斷的定義，且結論完全建基於提供的數據。

Option C is Correct: The table clearly shows four companies (Netflix, Amazon, etc.) each holding a massive market share (totaling over half). This directly proves there are "several dominant service providers" in the market, fitting the definition of an oligopoly. This conclusion is entirely based on the provided data.

選項 D 錯誤: 雖然該市場的確是寡頭壟斷，但題目要求「根據以上資料 (Based on the above information)」。表格中完全沒有提及政府管控或進入市場的障礙。因此，我們無法從這份資料中得出選項 D 的結論。

Option D is Incorrect: Although the market is indeed an oligopoly, the question specifies "Based on the above information". The table mentions absolutely nothing about government control or barriers to entry. Therefore, we cannot draw the conclusion in Option D from this data.

題目拆解 Problem Decomposition & Exam Tricks

關鍵字 (Pivot Point)： 題目的核心在於「根據以上資料 (Based on the above information)」這八個字。

Pivot Point: The core of the question lies in the phrase "Based on the above information".

速解法 (Shortcut)：
1. 看到「根據以上資料」，立即掃視表格。表格只有「公司名稱」和「市場佔有率 (%)」。
2. 任何選項提及表格以外的資訊（如：資訊不完全、異質產品、政府管控），即使在現實中是正確的，在考試中也必須直接剔除。
3. 剔除 A、B、D 後，只剩下 C。C 提到的「具支配力 (dominant)」完美對應了表格中高達兩位數的市場佔有率數據。

Shortcut:
1. Seeing "Based on the above information", immediately scan the table. It only contains "Company Names" and "Market Share (%)".
2. Any option mentioning information outside the table (e.g., imperfect info, heterogeneous products, government control) must be eliminated immediately, even if it's true in reality.
3. After eliminating A, B, and D, only C remains. "Dominant" in C perfectly corresponds to the double-digit market share data in the table.

常犯錯誤與陷阱 Common Mistakes & Traps

過度思考 (Overthinking) / 引入外部知識： 學生常犯的錯誤是利用自己的常識作答。例如，學生知道 Netflix 和 Disney+ 的節目不同（異質性），或者知道某些國家會限制外國串流平台（政府管控），於是錯誤地選擇了 B 或 D。記住，經濟學數據題必須嚴格遵守「只看給定數據」的原則。

Overthinking / Bringing in outside knowledge: A common mistake is using common sense to answer. For example, a student knows Netflix and Disney+ have different shows (heterogeneity) or that some countries restrict foreign streaming platforms (government control), and thus wrongly chooses B or D. Remember, data-based economics questions require strict adherence to "only looking at the given data".

學生常見疑問 Student Q&A

Q: 為什麼這不是「獨佔 / 壟斷 (Monopoly)」？最大那間公司有 18.8% 呢！

Q: Why isn't this a "Monopoly"? The largest company has 18.8%!

A: 獨佔 (Monopoly) 是指市場上只有一個賣家（或一個賣家佔據了壓倒性的市場份額，通常接近 100%）。在這裡，最大的公司只有 18.8%，而且有其他強大的競爭對手（17.7%, 11.7% 等）瓜分市場。這種「少數巨頭互相競爭」的狀態正是寡頭壟斷 (Oligopoly) 的特徵。

A: A Monopoly means there is only one seller in the market (or one seller holding an overwhelming share, usually near 100%). Here, the largest firm only has 18.8%, and there are other strong competitors (17.7%, 11.7%, etc.) sharing the market. This state of "a few giants competing" is the exact characteristic of an Oligopoly.
