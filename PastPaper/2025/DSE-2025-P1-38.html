HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: International Trade & Public Finance 主題：國際貿易與公共財政 Difficulty: ★★★★☆

Key Concepts: Twin Deficits (雙赤字), Devaluation (貶值), Aggregate Demand (總需求), Tax Revenue (稅收)

題目：假設某經濟中出現財政赤字和貿易赤字。下列哪些可能同時消除其財政赤字和貿易赤字？

Question: Suppose both a fiscal deficit and a trade deficit exist in an economy. Which of the following may eliminate both the fiscal deficit and the trade deficit at the same time?

(1) 政府對廣泛的進口物品徵收關稅。
The government imposes tariffs on a wide range of imported goods.
(2) 政府提高薪俸稅稅率。
The government raises the rate of the salaries tax.
(3) 政府令其貨幣兌主要貿易伙伴的貨幣官方貶值。
The government devaluates its currency against that of its major trading partners.
  • A. 只有 (1)、(2)
  • B. 只有 (1)、(3)
  • C. 只有 (2)、(3)
  • D. (1)、(2) 及 (3)
Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 雙赤字 (Twin Deficits)

$$ \text{Fiscal Deficit: } G > T \quad | \quad \text{Trade Deficit: } M > X $$

消除赤字需要：增加稅收 \((T\uparrow)\) 或 減少支出 \((G\downarrow)\)；增加出口 \((X\uparrow)\) 或 減少進口 \((M\downarrow)\)。
Elimination requires: Increasing Tax \((T\uparrow)\) or Decreasing Spending \((G\downarrow)\); Increasing Exports \((X\uparrow)\) or Decreasing Imports \((M\downarrow)\).

2. 總需求與稅收 (Aggregate Demand & Tax Revenue)

稅收通常與收入掛鉤（例如薪俸稅、利得稅）。當實質產出 \((Y)\) 上升，政府稅收也會隨之上升。
Tax revenue is usually linked to income (e.g., Salaries Tax, Profits Tax). When Real Output \((Y)\) rises, government tax revenue also rises.

$$ AD \uparrow \rightarrow Y \uparrow \rightarrow \text{Tax Revenue } (T) \uparrow $$
正確答案：D (Option D)

詳細分析 (Detailed Analysis)

(1) 徵收關稅 (Tariffs)

貿易方面：關稅令進口品價格上升，進口量下降。假設需求彈性適中，進口總值 \((M)\) 下降，改善貿易赤字。

Trade: Tariffs raise import prices, reducing import quantity. Assuming moderate elasticity, total import value \((M)\) falls, improving the trade deficit.

財政方面：關稅本身就是一種稅項。徵收關稅直接增加政府收入 \((T)\)，有助改善財政赤字。

Fiscal: Tariffs are a tax. Imposing them directly increases government revenue \((T)\), helping to improve the fiscal deficit.

✔ 有效 (Effective)

(2) 提高薪俸稅 (Increase Salaries Tax)

財政方面：提高稅率直接增加政府收入 \((T)\)，改善財政赤字。

Fiscal: Raising tax rates directly increases government revenue \((T)\), improving the fiscal deficit.

貿易方面：稅稅上升令可支配收入 \((Y_d)\) 下降，導致消費 \((C)\) 減少。由於進口是收入的函數，對進口品的需求亦會減少 \((M \downarrow)\)，改善貿易赤字。

Trade: Higher taxes reduce Disposable Income \((Y_d)\), leading to lower Consumption \((C)\). Since imports are a function of income, demand for imports falls \((M \downarrow)\), improving the trade deficit.

✔ 有效 (Effective)

(3) 貨幣貶值 (Currency Devaluation)

貿易方面：貶值令出口價格 \((P_x)\) 下降，進口價格 \((P_m)\) 上升。若符合馬歇爾-勒納條件 (Marshall-Lerner Condition)，淨出口 \((X-M)\) 會上升，消除貿易赤字。

Trade: Devaluation lowers export prices \((P_x)\) and raises import prices \((P_m)\). If the Marshall-Lerner Condition holds, Net Exports \((X-M)\) rise, eliminating the trade deficit.

財政方面（關鍵分析）：這是一個間接但強力的宏觀經濟效應。
1. 淨出口上升 \((X-M)\uparrow\) 會增加總需求 \((AD\uparrow)\)。
2. 透過乘數效應，實質產出和國民收入上升 \((Y\uparrow)\)。
3. 當企業利潤和個人收入增加，政府的直接稅收入（如利得稅、薪俸稅）也會隨之增加 \((T\uparrow)\)。
因此，這可能消除財政赤字。

Fiscal (Key Analysis): This is an indirect but powerful macroeconomic effect.
1. An increase in Net Exports \((X-M)\uparrow\) increases Aggregate Demand \((AD\uparrow)\).
2. Through the multiplier effect, Real Output and National Income rise \((Y\uparrow)\).
3. As business profits and personal incomes rise, the government's direct tax revenue (e.g., Profits Tax, Salaries Tax) increases \((T\uparrow)\).
Thus, this may eliminate the fiscal deficit.

✔ 有效 (Effective)

題目拆解與考試技巧 (Problem Decomposition & Exam Tricks)

1. 關鍵字「可能」(The Keyword "May"):

題目問的是「可能」(May) 而非「必然」(Must)。這允許我們考慮較為正面的經濟情境。雖然貶值可能增加政府償還外債的成本（負面影響），但它帶來的經濟增長亦能增加稅收（正面影響）。只要正面影響大於負面影響，它就「可能」消除赤字。

The question asks "may", not "must". This allows us to consider positive economic scenarios. While devaluation might increase foreign debt servicing costs (negative), the economic growth it triggers increases tax revenue (positive). As long as the positive outweighs the negative, it "may" eliminate the deficit.

2. 直接效應 vs 間接效應 (Direct vs. Indirect Effects):

  • 選項 (1) 和 (2) 是直接增加收入或減少支出。
  • 選項 (3) 是透過經濟增長 (Economic Growth) 來間接增加稅收。在處理這類題目時，不要忽略 AD 上升對政府收入的帶動作用。

Options (1) and (2) directly increase revenue. Option (3) indirectly increases tax revenue via Economic Growth. Do not overlook the boost to government revenue caused by rising AD.

3. 傳導機制圖 (Transmission Mechanism):

$$ \text{Devaluation} \rightarrow (X-M)\uparrow \rightarrow AD\uparrow \rightarrow Y\uparrow \rightarrow \text{Tax Revenue}\uparrow \rightarrow \text{Fiscal Deficit}\downarrow $$
