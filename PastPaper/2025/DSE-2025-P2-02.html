HKDSE Economics Exam Guide

Topic: Market Structure & Business Ownership
Type: Short Question (短題目)
Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆
Keywords: Perfect Competition, Limited Liability, Private Ltd Co.

The Question (題目)

Input Question:

香港有很多瑜伽中心提供瑜伽課堂。

(a) 舉出兩個原因，解釋為什麼香港的瑜伽課堂市場不屬於完全競爭。 (4分)

(b) 陳小姐和她的朋友以私人有限公司的形式在香港共同營運一所瑜伽中心。

(i) 寫出「有限債務責任」的定義。 (2分)

(ii) 舉出她們選擇成立私人有限公司而非公眾有限公司的兩個可能原因。 (2分)

In Hong Kong, there are many yoga studios providing yoga classes.

(a) Give TWO reasons to explain why the market of yoga classes in Hong Kong is NOT perfectly competitive. (4 marks)

(b) Miss Chan and her friends jointly operate a yoga studio in the form of a private limited company in Hong Kong.

(i) Define ‘limited liability’. (2 marks)

(ii) Give TWO possible reasons why they chose to set up a private limited company instead of a public limited company. (2 marks)

Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 完全競爭 (Perfect Competition) vs. 壟斷性競爭 (Monopolistic Competition)

要判斷一個市場是否屬於「完全競爭」，必須檢查它是否違反了以下四個嚴格條件：

To determine if a market is "Perfectly Competitive", check if it violates any of these four strict conditions:
  • 同質產品 (Homogeneous Products): 產品完全相同，消費者無法區分。
    Products are identical; consumers cannot distinguish between them.
  • 完全資訊 (Perfect Information): 買賣雙方掌握所有價格和質量資訊。
    Buyers and sellers have full knowledge of prices and quality.
  • 價格接受者 (Price Takers): 買賣雙方都不能影響價格。
    Neither buyers nor sellers can influence the price.
  • 自由進出 (Free Entry and Exit): 沒有任何進入障礙。
    No barriers to entry or exit.

2. 有限公司 (Limited Company)

有限債務責任 (Limited Liability): 股東的損失上限僅限於其投資金額（或未繳付的股本）。私人財產受到保障。

Shareholders' maximum loss is limited to the amount of their investment (or unpaid shares). Personal assets are protected.

Suggested Answer & Detailed Analysis (建議答案與詳細分析)

Part (a): Why NOT Perfect Competition? (4 marks)

Marking Scheme Logic: Identify a feature of the Yoga market that contradicts the assumptions of Perfect Competition.

Point 1: 異質產品 (Heterogeneous Products)

瑜伽中心提供的服務並非同質。不同的中心有不同的導師質素、教學風格、環境裝修或地點便利程度。

The services provided by yoga studios are not homogeneous. Different studios have different teacher quality, teaching styles, environments, or locations.

Point 2: 資訊不完全 (Imperfect Information)

消費者無法完全掌握所有瑜伽中心的收費及服務質素資訊（例如：消費者在報名前難以完全知道導師的教學質素）。

Consumers do not have perfect information regarding the prices and service quality of all yoga studios (e.g., it is hard to know the teaching quality before signing up).

(Alternative Point): 價格制定者 (Price Searchers)

瑜伽中心並非價格接受者，它們有能力自行設定價格。

Yoga studios are not price takers; they have the ability to set their own prices.

Part (b)(i): Define 'Limited Liability' (2 marks)

Definition:

股東的最高財務損失只限於其投資總額（或持有的股份價值）。
股東無須動用私人財產來償還公司的債務。

The maximum financial loss of shareholders is limited to the amount of their investment (or the value of shares held).
Shareholders are not liable to use their personal assets to repay the company's debts.

Part (b)(ii): Why Private Ltd instead of Public Ltd? (2 marks)

Reason 1: 保持控制權 (Retain Control)

私人有限公司的股份轉讓受限制，且股東人數通常較少（通常是親友），這能防止外人收購，讓陳小姐和朋友保持對公司的控制權。

Transfer of shares in a private limited company is restricted, and the number of shareholders is usually small (often friends/family). This prevents takeovers by outsiders, allowing Miss Chan and her friends to retain control.

Reason 2: 私隱度較高 / 不需要向公眾披露財務狀況 (Higher Privacy / No need to disclose to public)

私人有限公司不需要向公眾公開詳細的財務報告，商業機密較易保存。

Private limited companies are not required to disclose detailed financial reports to the public, allowing for better protection of business secrets.

Reason 3: 成立成本較低 / 手續較簡單 (Lower setup cost / Simpler procedures)

相比公眾有限公司（特別是上市），成立私人有限公司的法律程序較簡單，合規成本較低。

Compared to a public limited company (especially listed ones), the legal procedures for setting up a private limited company are simpler, and compliance costs are lower.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Part (a) 拆解:

  • Command Word: "Explain why... NOT" (解釋為什麼...不屬於)。這要求你找出市場現實與理論模型之間的矛盾 (Contradiction)。
  • 思考路徑:
    1. 回想完全競爭的特徵：同質、完全資訊、價格接受者、自由進出。
    2. 觀察「瑜伽」這個產品：每一間都一樣嗎？(不一樣 → 異質)。你知道全香港每一間的價格嗎？(不知道 → 資訊不完全)。

Part (b) 拆解:

  • Context (情境): "Miss Chan and her friends" (陳小姐和朋友)。這暗示了規模較小，且關係緊密。
  • Comparison (比較): 私人 vs 公眾。必須針對「為何選擇前者」作答。通常涉及「控制權」、「成本」或「私隱」。
  • Trap (陷阱): 不要回答「因為容易集資」。這是公眾有限公司的優點，不是私人有限公司的優點。

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 錯誤觀念 (Misconception): 以為「很多賣家」就等於完全競爭。
    Thinking that "many sellers" automatically means Perfect Competition.
    更正: 壟斷性競爭 (Monopolistic Competition) 也有很多賣家。區別在於產品是否同質。
  • 定義不清 (Vague Definition): 在定義有限債務責任時，只說「責任有限」。
    Defining limited liability simply as "liability is limited".
    更正: 必須具體指出「損失只限於投資額」及「私人財產受保障」。
  • 混淆優缺點 (Confusing Pros/Cons): 在 (b)(ii) 中回答「可以發行股票集資」。
    Answering "can issue shares to raise capital" in (b)(ii).
    更正: 兩者皆可發行股票，但只有公眾有限公司可以向公眾發行。此點不能解釋為何選擇私人公司。

Exam Tricks (考試技巧)

  • 關鍵字得分 (Keywords): 在 (a) 部份，必須準確使用經濟學術語，如「異質產品 (Heterogeneous products)」或「產品差異 (Product differentiation)」。不要只用日常用語如「不同的服務」。
  • 配對比較 (Comparison): 在回答 (b)(ii) 這類選擇題時，嘗試在腦海中畫一個比較表。選擇私人公司的原因，通常就是公眾公司的缺點 (例如：公眾公司容易被收購、缺乏私隱)。
  • 時間管理: 這是一條 8 分的題目，建議作答時間不超過 10-12 分鐘。
