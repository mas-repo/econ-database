市場干預：補貼 (Market Intervention: Subsidy)

難度 (Difficulty): ★★★★☆
關鍵字 (Key Concepts): 從量補貼 (Per-unit Subsidy) 補貼得益 (Subsidy Benefit) 價格彈性 (Price Elasticity) 總收入 (Total Revenue)

題目 (The Question)

下圖顯示物品X的市場。物品X的生產者有$30從量補貼。
下列哪項關於物品X的市場的陳述是正確的？

The diagram below shows the market of Good X. A per-unit subsidy of $30 is provided to the producers of Good X.
Which of the following statements about the market of Good X is correct?
  • A. 消費者享有的補貼得益是$1,400。
    The subsidy benefit enjoyed by consumers is $1,400.
  • B. 包含補貼在內的總收入是$11,700。
    The total revenue inclusive of the subsidy is $11,700.
  • C. 在介乎原初的均衡價格和新的均衡價格之間的範圍內，需求價格彈性高於供應價格彈性。
    Within the range of the original and the new equilibrium prices, the price elasticity of demand is higher than the price elasticity of supply.
  • D. 在介乎原初的均衡價格和新的均衡價格之間的範圍內，需求價格彈性小於一。
    Within the range of the original and the new equilibrium prices, the price elasticity of demand is smaller than one.

概念重溫 (Concept Review)

1. 從量補貼 (Per-unit Subsidy)

當政府向生產者提供從量補貼時，供應曲線會向下/向右平移，垂直距離等於每單位補貼額。

When the government provides a per-unit subsidy to producers, the supply curve shifts downward/rightward by a vertical distance equal to the per-unit subsidy.

2. 補貼得益分配 (Distribution of Subsidy Benefits)

消費者得益 = 價格下降幅度 \(\times\) 新交易量
生產者得益 = (補貼額 - 價格下降幅度) \(\times\) 新交易量

Consumer Benefit = Decrease in Price \(\times\) New Quantity
Producer Benefit = (Subsidy - Decrease in Price) \(\times\) New Quantity

3. 總收入與彈性 (Total Revenue and Elasticity)

包含補貼在內的總收入 = (新價格 + 每單位補貼) \(\times\) 新交易量
如果價格下降導致總收入（不計補貼，即 \(P \times Q\)）增加，則需求屬彈性 (\(E_d > 1\))。如果總收入減少，則需求屬缺乏彈性 (\(E_d < 1\))。

Total revenue inclusive of subsidy = (New Price + Per-unit Subsidy) \(\times\) New Quantity
If a price decrease leads to an increase in total revenue (excluding subsidy, i.e., \(P \times Q\)), demand is elastic (\(E_d > 1\)). If total revenue decreases, demand is inelastic (\(E_d < 1\)).

答案與詳細分析 (The Answer & Detailed Analysis)

正確答案 (Correct Option): B

A (錯誤 / Incorrect)

原初均衡價格 \(P_0 = 120\)，原初數量 \(Q_0 = 70\)。新均衡價格 \(P_1 = 100\)，新數量 \(Q_1 = 90\)。
消費者得益 = \((P_0 - P_1) \times Q_1 = (120 - 100) \times 90 = \$1,800\)。
因此，得益不是$1,400。

Original equilibrium price \(P_0 = 120\), original quantity \(Q_0 = 70\). New equilibrium price \(P_1 = 100\), new quantity \(Q_1 = 90\).
Consumer benefit = \((P_0 - P_1) \times Q_1 = (120 - 100) \times 90 = \$1,800\).
Therefore, the benefit is not $1,400.

B (正確 / Correct)

包含補貼在內的總收入 = (消費者支付的價格 + 政府補貼) \(\times\) 新數量。
由於補貼是每單位$30，生產者實際收取的每單位金額是 \(100 + 30 = 130\)。
總收入 = \(130 \times 90 = \$11,700\)。

Total revenue inclusive of the subsidy = (Price paid by consumers + Government subsidy) \(\times\) New quantity.
Since the subsidy is $30 per unit, the actual amount received by producers per unit is \(100 + 30 = 130\).
Total revenue = \(130 \times 90 = \$11,700\).

C (錯誤 / Incorrect)

消費者享受的價格下降幅度是 \(120 - 100 = 20\)。生產者享受的價格上升幅度（連補貼）是 \(130 - 120 = 10\)。
因為消費者承擔了較大份額的價格改變（得益較多），這意味著需求比供應更缺乏彈性（需求曲線較陡峭）。因此，需求彈性低於供應彈性。

The price drop enjoyed by consumers is \(120 - 100 = 20\). The price increase enjoyed by producers (with subsidy) is \(130 - 120 = 10\).
Since consumers bear a larger share of the price change (larger benefit), it means demand is more inelastic (steeper) than supply. Therefore, price elasticity of demand is lower than price elasticity of supply.

D (錯誤 / Incorrect)

我們可以使用總收入測試（Total Revenue Test）。
原初消費者總支出（即生產者不計補貼的總收入）= \(120 \times 70 = 8,400\)。
新消費者總支出 = \(100 \times 90 = 9,000\)。
當價格由120下降至100時，總支出由8,400增加至9,000。價格下降導致總支出增加，這表示需求是富有彈性 (Elastic, \(E_d > 1\))。因此，說它小於一是錯誤的。

We can use the Total Revenue Test.
Original total consumer expenditure (producer revenue without subsidy) = \(120 \times 70 = 8,400\).
New total consumer expenditure = \(100 \times 90 = 9,000\).
When price falls from 120 to 100, total expenditure increases from 8,400 to 9,000. A price decrease leading to an increase in total expenditure means demand is elastic (\(E_d > 1\)). Therefore, stating it is smaller than one is incorrect.

題目拆解 (Problem Decomposition)

解題樞紐 (Pivot Point): 找出圖表中隱藏的數據。題目給了補貼額是$30，這代表在任何數量下，\(S_0\) 和 \(S_1\) 的垂直距離都是30。

Pivot Point: Identify the hidden data in the graph. The question states the subsidy is $30, which means the vertical distance between \(S_0\) and \(S_1\) at any quantity is 30.
  • 在新均衡點 (\(Q=90\))，消費者支付 \(P=100\)。
  • 生產者實際收到的金額 = 消費者支付 + 政府補貼 = \(100 + 30 = 130\)。
  • 這就是計算「包含補貼在內的總收入」的關鍵：\(130 \times 90 = 11,700\)。

常犯錯誤與陷阱 (Common Mistakes & Traps)

陷阱 1：混淆「總收入」與「包含補貼的總收入」

很多同學在計算B選項時，只計算了消費者支付的總額 (\(100 \times 90 = 9,000\))，忘記了生產者還會從政府那裡收到每單位$30的補貼。包含補貼的總收入 = 消費者總支出 + 政府總補貼額。

Trap 1: Confusing "Total Revenue" with "Total Revenue Inclusive of Subsidy"
Many students calculating Option B only consider the total amount paid by consumers (\(100 \times 90 = 9,000\)), forgetting that producers also receive a $30 per-unit subsidy from the government. Total Revenue Inclusive of Subsidy = Total Consumer Expenditure + Total Government Subsidy.

陷阱 2：錯誤計算消費者得益

在計算A選項時，有些同學可能會用舊的交易量來計算得益 (\(20 \times 70 = 1,400\))，這剛好是選項A的數字！緊記，補貼得益必須以新的交易量 (\(Q_1 = 90\)) 來計算。

Trap 2: Incorrectly calculating Consumer Benefit
When calculating Option A, some students might use the old quantity (\(20 \times 70 = 1,400\)), which matches the number in Option A! Remember, subsidy benefits must be calculated using the new quantity (\(Q_1 = 90\)).

學生常見疑問 (Student Q&A)

Q: 為什麼選項C中，消費者得益較多就代表需求較缺乏彈性？

A: 想像一下，如果需求極度缺乏彈性（垂直線），消費者必須購買該物品，政府的補貼會完全轉化為價格下降，消費者獲得100%得益。相反，如果需求極有彈性（水平線），價格不會下降，生產者獲得所有得益。因此，誰的彈性較低（較不靈活），誰就能在補貼中獲得較大份額的得益（或在稅收中承擔較大負擔）。

Q: Why does consumers enjoying a larger benefit in Option C mean demand is more inelastic?
A: Imagine if demand is perfectly inelastic (vertical line), consumers must buy the good, so the government subsidy fully translates into a price drop, giving consumers 100% of the benefit. Conversely, if demand is perfectly elastic (horizontal line), the price won't drop, and producers get all the benefit. Therefore, the party with the lower elasticity (less flexible) captures a larger share of the subsidy benefit (or bears a larger burden in a tax).

考試技巧與速解 (Exam Tricks & Shortcuts)

速解法 (Shortcut):

做這類題目時，第一步永遠是在圖上標示所有隱藏數字。題目說補貼是$30，馬上在 \(Q=90\) 的位置，往上推算 \(S_0\) 的價格是 \(100 + 30 = 130\)。

一旦你寫下了 \(130\)，你掃視選項B：「包含補貼在內的總收入」。這就是 \(130 \times 90\)。心算 \(13 \times 9 = 117\)，馬上得出 \(11,700\)，直接鎖定正確答案為B，節省計算其他選項的時間！

Shortcut:
For this type of question, the first step is always to label all hidden numbers on the graph. The question says the subsidy is $30, so immediately go to \(Q=90\) and calculate upwards to find the price on \(S_0\) is \(100 + 30 = 130\).
Once you write down \(130\), you scan Option B: "Total revenue inclusive of subsidy". This is just \(130 \times 90\). Mental math \(13 \times 9 = 117\), giving you \(11,700\) instantly. You can lock in B as the correct answer and save time calculating the other options!
