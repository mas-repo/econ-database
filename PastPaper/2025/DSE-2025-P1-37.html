HKDSE Economics Tutorial

Topic (課題): 總需求與總供應模型 (Aggregate Demand and Aggregate Supply Model)

Difficulty (難度): ★★★☆☆

Key Concepts (核心概念): 總需求 (Aggregate Demand), 短期總供應 (Short-Run Aggregate Supply), 人口變動 (Population Changes)

1. The Question (題目)

在某經濟中，大量工人攜同他們的家人移民海外。在短期，該經濟的物價水平 ___________，而該經濟的實質產出 ___________。

In an economy, a large number of workers migrate overseas together with their family members. In the short run, the price level of the economy ___________ and its real output ___________.

A. 會上升 …… 會下降 (will rise …… will fall)
B. 會下降 …… 可能上升或下降 (will fall …… may rise or fall)
C. 可能上升或下降 …… 會下降 (may rise or fall …… will fall)
D. 可能上升或下降 …… 可能上升或下降 (may rise or fall …… may rise or fall)

2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這條題目，我們需要運用總需求—總供應 (AD-AS) 模型。我們必須分析事件對總需求 \(AD\) 和短期總供應 \(SRAS\) 的雙重影響。

To solve this question, we need to use the Aggregate Demand - Aggregate Supply (AD-AS) model. We must analyze the dual impact of the event on both Aggregate Demand \(AD\) and Short-Run Aggregate Supply \(SRAS\).

$$AD = C + I + G + (X - M)$$
  • 總需求 (Aggregate Demand, AD): 受人口數量影響。人口減少會導致私人消費開支 \(C\) 下降，從而令 \(AD\) 減少（向左移）。
    Affected by population size. A decrease in population leads to a drop in private consumption expenditure \(C\), causing \(AD\) to decrease (shift left).
  • 短期總供應 (Short-Run Aggregate Supply, SRAS): 受勞動人口及生產成本影響。工人流失代表勞動力減少，生產力下降，導致 \(SRAS\) 減少（向左移）。
    Affected by labor force and production costs. A loss of workers means a smaller labor force and lower productive capacity, causing \(SRAS\) to decrease (shift left).

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

正確答案 (Correct Option): C

Step-by-Step Analysis (逐步分析):

  • 事件一：大量工人移民 (Event 1: Workers migrate)
    這會導致勞動人口減少，短期總供應 \(SRAS\) 向左移。結果：物價水平 \(P\) 上升，實質產出 \(Y\) 下降。
    This causes a decrease in the labor force, shifting Short-Run Aggregate Supply \(SRAS\) to the left. Result: Price level \(P\) rises, real output \(Y\) falls.
  • 事件二：攜同家人移民 (Event 2: Family members migrate)
    這會導致總人口減少，總消費下降，總需求 \(AD\) 向左移。結果：物價水平 \(P\) 下降，實質產出 \(Y\) 下降。
    This causes a decrease in the total population, lowering total consumption, shifting Aggregate Demand \(AD\) to the left. Result: Price level \(P\) falls, real output \(Y\) falls.
  • 綜合影響 (Combined Effect):
    由於 \(SRAS\) 和 \(AD\) 同時向左移，兩者都會導致實質產出 \(Y\) 下降，因此實質產出必定會下降。然而，\(SRAS\) 左移令物價上升，而 \(AD\) 左移令物價下降，兩股力量方向相反。由於我們不知道哪條曲線的移動幅度較大，因此物價水平 \(P\) 可能上升或下降。
    Since both \(SRAS\) and \(AD\) shift left, both cause real output \(Y\) to fall, so real output will definitely fall. However, the leftward shift in \(SRAS\) pushes prices up, while the leftward shift in \(AD\) pushes prices down. Because we do not know the relative magnitude of the shifts, the price level \(P\) may rise or fall.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的關鍵點 (Pivot Point) 在於仔細閱讀題目中的每一個群體：

  • 「工人 (Workers)」對應的是生產端 (Supply side)。
  • 「家人 (Family members)」對應的是消費端 (Demand side)。

The Pivot Point of such questions lies in reading every group mentioned carefully: "Workers" corresponds to the Supply side, while "Family members" corresponds to the Demand side.

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

很多學生只看到「工人移民」，便立刻得出 \(SRAS\) 減少的結論，從而錯誤選擇選項 A。考評局經常在題目中加入「及其家屬」這類字眼，就是為了測試學生能否同時考慮對 \(AD\) 的影響。

Many students only see "workers migrate" and immediately conclude that \(SRAS\) decreases, wrongly choosing Option A. The HKEAA often includes phrases like "and their families" to test if students can simultaneously consider the impact on \(AD\).

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 長期總供應 (LRAS) 會受影響嗎？
Q: Will Long-Run Aggregate Supply (LRAS) be affected?

A: 會的。勞動人口永久流失會令 \(LRAS\) 向左移。但請注意，題目特別強調了「在短期 (In the short run)」，因此我們主要分析 \(AD\) 和 \(SRAS\) 的變動即可得出答案。
A: Yes. A permanent loss of the labor force shifts \(LRAS\) to the left. However, the question specifically asks about "In the short run", so analyzing the shifts in \(AD\) and \(SRAS\) is sufficient to find the answer.

Q: 如果只有工人離開，沒有提及家人亦會移民海外，AD會減少嗎？
Q: If only workers leave, and there is no mention of family members migrating overseas, will AD still decrease?

A: 會的，即使題目沒有提及家人，總需求（AD）依然會減少。在經濟學中，工人具有「雙重身份」：他們既是生產者（提供勞力），也是消費者（在本地購買物品和服務）。因此，當大量工人離開本國，本地的總人口和有收入的消費者數量依然會減少。這會導致私人消費開支 \(C\) 下降，從而令總需求 \(AD\) 向左移。
考評局特意加入「攜同家人」的字眼，通常是為了給予強烈提示 (Strong Hint)，確保考生不會只看見「工人」就單純思考總供應 \(SRAS\)，提醒學生必須同時考慮對需求面（消費）的影響。同時，這也放大了 \(AD\) 的跌幅，使題目邏輯更嚴密。
A: Yes, even if the question does not mention family members, Aggregate Demand (AD) will still decrease. In economics, workers have a "dual identity": they are both producers (providing labor) and consumers (buying goods and services locally). Therefore, when a large number of workers leave the country, the total population and the number of income-earning consumers locally will still decrease. This leads to a fall in private consumption expenditure \(C\), causing Aggregate Demand \(AD\) to shift to the left.
The HKEAA specifically adds the phrase "with their family members" usually as a Strong Hint to ensure candidates don't just see "workers" and only think about Aggregate Supply \(SRAS\), reminding them to simultaneously consider the impact on the demand side. It also magnifies the drop in \(AD\), making the question's logic more rigorous.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

雙重移動法則 (Double Shift Rule):

當 \(AD\) 和 \(AS\) 同時移動時，必定有一個變數（\(P\) 或 \(Y\)）是確定的，另一個變數是「不確定 (may rise or fall)」。

在這題中，既然兩者都減少（向左移），產出 \(Y\) 必定減少。只要你確認了 \(Y\) 必定下降，就可以立刻排除 B 和 D。再確認 \(P\) 的方向衝突，就能秒殺選出 C！

Double Shift Rule: When both \(AD\) and \(AS\) shift simultaneously, one variable (\(P\) or \(Y\)) will have a definite direction, and the other will be ambiguous ("may rise or fall"). Since both curves shift left here, \(Y\) must fall. Once you know \(Y\) falls, eliminate B and D. Recognizing the conflicting price effects leaves C as the only possible answer!
