Topic / 課題: 本地生產總值的計算 (Calculation of GDP)

Difficulty / 難度: ⭐⭐⭐ (Medium 中等)

Key Concepts / 關鍵概念: Expenditure Approach (支出計算法), Value-Added Approach (價值附加法), Market Price (市價), Factor Cost (要素成本)

📝 The Question (題目)

下表顯示某經濟中咖啡豆研磨機的生產鏈。

The following diagram shows a production chain of coffee grinders in an economy.

以上生產鏈對該經濟本地生產總值（以要素成本計算）的貢獻是多少？

What is the contribution of the above production chain to the economy’s GDP at factor cost?

  • A. $370
  • B. $430
  • C. $460
  • D. $490

💡 Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解答這題，我們需要掌握兩個核心概念：計算 GDP 的方法 及 市價與要素成本的轉換。

To solve this, we need to grasp two core concepts: Methods of calculating GDP and Converting Market Price to Factor Cost.

1. 支出計算法 (Expenditure Approach)

從消費者和買家的角度計算本地生產總值。公式為：

Calculates GDP from the perspective of buyers. The formula is:

$$GDP = C + I + G + X - M$$
  • $C$: Private Consumption Expenditure (私人消費開支)
  • $X$: Exports (出口)
  • $M$: Imports (進口)

2. 市價 vs. 要素成本 (Market Price vs. Factor Cost)

消費者支付的價格（市價）往往包含了政府徵收的間接稅（如銷售稅）。為了反映生產要素的實際收入（要素成本），我們必須從市價中扣除間接稅，並加上政府津貼。

The price paid by consumers (Market Price) often includes indirect taxes (like sales tax) levied by the government. To reflect the actual income earned by factors of production (Factor Cost), we must deduct indirect taxes and add subsidies.

$$GDP_{fc} = GDP_{mp} - \text{Indirect Taxes} + \text{Subsidies}$$

（註：$GDP_{fc}$ = 要素成本計算的 GDP；$GDP_{mp}$ = 市價計算的 GDP）

✅ The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A

Option A ($370) - Correct (正確)

我們可以使用支出計算法找出以市價計算的本地生產總值 ($GDP_{mp}$)：
本地消費者購買 = $C = \$440$
外國零售商購買 (出口) = $X = \$80$
向外國出口商購買 (進口) = $M = \$90$
$GDP_{mp} = C + X - M = \$440 + \$80 - \$90 = \$430$

題目要求計算要素成本 ($GDP_{fc}$)，因此必須扣除銷售稅（間接稅）：
$GDP_{fc} = \$430 - \$60 (\text{Sales Tax}) = \$370$。答案為 A。

We can use the Expenditure Approach to find GDP at market price ($GDP_{mp}$):
Purchases by local consumers = $C = \$440$
Purchases by foreign retailers (Exports) = $X = \$80$
Purchases from foreign exporters (Imports) = $M = \$90$
$GDP_{mp} = C + X - M = \$440 + \$80 - \$90 = \$430$
The question asks for GDP at factor cost ($GDP_{fc}$), so we must deduct the sales tax (indirect tax):
$GDP_{fc} = \$430 - \$60 (\text{Sales Tax}) = \$370$. The correct answer is A.

Option B ($430) - Incorrect (錯誤)

這是以市價 (Market Price) 計算的 GDP。考生成功計算出 $C + X - M = \$430$，但忘記了題目要求的是要素成本，未有減去 $\$60$ 的銷售稅。

This is the GDP at Market Price. The student correctly calculated $C + X - M = \$430$ but forgot the question asked for Factor Cost, failing to subtract the $\$60$ sales tax.

Option C ($460) - Incorrect (錯誤)

這可能是計算 $C + X - \text{Tax}$ ($440 + 80 - 60 = 460$) 得出的結果。考生忘記了扣除價值 $\$90$ 的進口商品 ($M$)。進口商品的價值並非本地生產，必須從 GDP 中剔除。

This likely comes from calculating $C + X - \text{Tax}$ ($440 + 80 - 60 = 460$). The student forgot to deduct the $\$90$ value of imports ($M$). Imported goods are not locally produced and must be excluded from GDP.

Option D ($490) - Incorrect (錯誤)

考生可能將要素成本的公式記錯，錯誤地將銷售稅加上而非減去 ($430 + 60 = 490$)。

The student likely misremembered the factor cost formula and mistakenly added the sales tax instead of subtracting it ($430 + 60 = 490$).

🔍 Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類跨越多個生產階段的題目，考驗你過濾資訊的能力。

Questions involving multiple production stages test your ability to filter information.

  • Pivot Point (破題關鍵): 辨識最終產品去向與資金流向。不要被生產鏈中間的交易（如「本地生產商賣給本地零售商 $\$140$」）混淆。這 $\$140$ 屬於中間物品交易 (Intermediate goods)，如果用支出法計算，已經包含在最終售價 ($\$440$) 內，不需要重複計算。
    Identify the final destinations of products and money flows. Don't be distracted by intermediate transactions (like the $\$140$ sale between local manufacturers and retailers). This $\$140$ is for intermediate goods; under the expenditure approach, it's already embedded in the final price ($\$440$) and shouldn't be double-counted.
  • Distractors (干擾資訊): 「本地材料 $\$70$」及「本地生產商 $\rightarrow$ 本地零售商 $\$140$」在運用「支出計算法」時是完全不需要理會的資訊。
    "Local materials $\$70$" and "Local manufacturers $\rightarrow$ Local retailers $\$140$" are completely irrelevant pieces of information when using the Expenditure Approach.

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  1. Double Counting (重複計算): 很多學生會嘗試把圖表中所有數字加起來，或者錯誤地把中間物品 (Intermediate goods) 的交易額也算進 GDP 中。
  2. Confusing Market Price and Factor Cost (混淆市價與要素成本): 看到最終售價就急於作答（選 B），忽略了括號內的「(包含 $\$60$ 銷售稅)」。銷售稅歸政府所有，並不屬於生產要素的收入。
  1. Double Counting: Many students try to add all the numbers in the diagram or mistakenly include intermediate goods transactions in the GDP.
  2. Confusing Market Price and Factor Cost: Jumping to the final price to answer (Option B) while ignoring "(includes $\$60$ sales tax)". Sales tax goes to the government and is not income for factors of production.

🙋‍♂️ Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 既然這是一個生產鏈，我可以用「價值附加法」(Value-Added Approach) 來計算嗎？
Q: Since this is a production chain, can I use the Value-Added Approach to calculate it?

A: 絕對可以！而且會得出一樣的答案。(Absolutely! And you will get the exact same answer.)
讓我們驗證一下 (Let's verify):
1. 本地材料生產商的附加價值 = $\$70 - \$0 = \$70$
2. 本地生產商的附加價值 = 總產出 ($\$140 + \$80$) - 中間投入 ($\$70$) = $\$150$
3. 本地零售商的附加價值 = 總產出 ($\$440$) - 中間投入 ($\$140 + \$90$) = $\$210$
$GDP_{mp}$ (市價計算) = $\$70 + \$150 + \$210 = \$430$
最後減去間接稅以求得要素成本：$GDP_{fc} = \$430 - \$60 = \$370$。

⚡ Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

在 DSE 考試中，時間就是分數。雖然「價值附加法」能算出答案，但這題的最佳解題策略是直接使用「支出計算法」(Expenditure Approach)。

In the DSE exam, time is marks. While the Value-Added Approach works, the best strategy here is to use the Expenditure Approach directly.

速解步驟 (Speed-run steps):

  1. 找「最終出路」: 消費者買了多少？ $C = 440$
  2. 找「跨國交易」: 出口多少？進口多少？ $X = 80$, $M = 90$
  3. 算市價 GDP: $440 + 80 - 90 = 430$
  4. 看清楚題目要求：要素成本 (Factor cost) $\rightarrow$ 扣稅！ $430 - 60 = 370$。完成！

By focusing only on the final boundaries (what goes to consumers, what leaves the country, and what enters), you bypass all the confusing intermediate steps ($70, $140) and solve the problem in 15 seconds.
