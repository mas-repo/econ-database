HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic (課題): Economic Systems (經濟制度)
Difficulty (難度): ★★☆☆☆
Key Concepts (核心概念): Market Mechanism (市場機制), Price Competition (價格競爭), Private Property (私有產權)

The Question (題目)

與計劃經濟相比，___________。

Compared to a planned economy, ___________.

(1) 個人在市場經濟中決定資源分配時傾向有較高自由度
individuals in a market economy tend to have higher freedom when deciding the allocation of resources

(2) 利率在市場經濟中傾向較高
interest rate in a market economy tends to be higher

(3) 資源分配在市場經濟中傾向較少依賴非價格競爭
resource allocation in a market economy tends to rely less on non-price competition

  • A. 只有 (1)、(2) (1) and (2) only
  • B. 只有 (1)、(3) (1) and (3) only
  • C. 只有 (2)、(3) (2) and (3) only
  • D. (1)、(2) 及 (3) (1), (2) and (3)
Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 市場經濟 vs 計劃經濟 (Market vs Planned Economy)

  • 市場經濟 (Market Economy): 資源由私有產權 (Private Property Rights) 持有。價格機制 (Price Mechanism) 指導資源分配。 Resources are held by private property rights. The price mechanism guides resource allocation.
  • 計劃經濟 (Planned Economy): 資源由國家擁有 (State-owned)。政府指令 (Government Command) 決定生產什麼、如何生產及為誰生產。 Resources are state-owned. Government commands decide what, how, and for whom to produce.

2. 競爭的形式 (Forms of Competition)

只要資源稀缺 (Scarcity exists)，競爭就無可避免。區別在於競爭的準則 (Criteria)。
As long as scarcity exists, competition is inevitable. The difference lies in the criteria of competition.

  • 價格競爭 (Price Competition): 誰願意付出的價格 \(P\) 最高，誰就獲得資源。這是市場經濟的主要特徵。
  • 非價格競爭 (Non-price Competition): 排隊 (Queuing)、暴力 (Violence)、特權 (Privilege)、抽籤 (Rationing)。這是計劃經濟的主要特徵（因為價格通常被固定在均衡點之下，導致 \(Q_d > Q_s\)）。

The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: B

Statement (1): CORRECT (正確)

個人在市場經濟中決定資源分配時傾向有較高自由度。
在市場經濟中，私有產權容許個人擁有及處置資源。生產者可自由決定生產甚麼，消費者可自由決定購買甚麼（消費者主權）。相反，在計劃經濟中，資源分配由中央政府決定，個人自由極受限制。

Individuals in a market economy tend to have higher freedom when deciding the allocation of resources.
In a market economy, private property rights allow individuals to own and dispose of resources. Producers are free to decide what to produce, and consumers are free to decide what to buy (Consumer Sovereignty). Conversely, in a planned economy, resource allocation is determined by the central government, severely restricting individual freedom.

Statement (2): INCORRECT (錯誤)

利率在市場經濟中傾向較高。
這是一個常見的誤導。雖然計劃經濟中的利率通常由政府人為壓低（甚至不重視利率作為分配資本的工具），但經濟學上並沒有定律指出市場經濟的均衡利率必然「高於」計劃經濟的官方利率。區別在於利率是由市場供求決定還是由政府指令固定，而不是數值的高低。此外，高利率通常反映高通脹或資本短缺，這不是市場經濟的必然特徵。

Interest rate in a market economy tends to be higher.
This is a common distractor. While interest rates in planned economies are often artificially suppressed by the government (or not used effectively to allocate capital), there is no economic law stating that the equilibrium interest rate in a market economy is necessarily "higher" than the official rate in a planned economy. The distinction lies in whether the rate is determined by market supply and demand versus fixed by government command, not the numerical level. Furthermore, high interest rates often reflect high inflation or capital shortage, which are not inherent features of a market economy.

Statement (3): CORRECT (正確)

資源分配在市場經濟中傾向較少依賴非價格競爭。
市場經濟主要依賴價格機制（出價高者得）。雖然市場中也存在非價格競爭（如廣告、服務質素），但相比之下，計劃經濟由於缺乏有效的價格機制，往往出現短缺（Shortage），因此必須極度依賴排隊、配給券或政治關係等非價格手段來分配資源。因此，市場經濟對非價格競爭的依賴相對「較少」。

Resource allocation in a market economy tends to rely less on non-price competition.
Market economies rely primarily on the price mechanism (highest bidder wins). While non-price competition (like advertising, service quality) exists in markets, planned economies often face shortages due to the lack of an effective price mechanism. Consequently, they must rely heavily on non-price methods like queuing, rationing coupons, or political connections to allocate resources. Therefore, market economies rely "less" on non-price competition relatively.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (破題關鍵):

這類題目要求比較兩種制度的本質特徵 (Inherent Characteristics)，而非特定歷史時刻的數據。 This type of question requires comparing the inherent characteristics of the two systems, not data from a specific historical moment.

  • 關鍵詞 (Keywords): "自由度 (Freedom)" 和 "非價格競爭 (Non-price competition)" 是區分兩種制度的標準定義特徵。
  • 干擾項 (Distractor): "利率較高 (Higher interest rate)" 是關於變量數值 (Level of variable) 的陳述，而非制度特徵。凡是涉及「市場經濟的價格/工資/利率必然較高」的選項，通常都是錯的，因為價格由供求決定，可高可低。

Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • 誤解：市場經濟只有價格競爭。
    Misconception: Market economies only have price competition.
    事實：市場也有非價格競爭（例如產品質素、推銷），只是計劃經濟更依賴非價格競爭來解決基本的分配問題。
  • 誤解：計劃經濟沒有競爭。
    Misconception: There is no competition in a planned economy.
    事實：只要有稀缺性，就有競爭。計劃經濟的人們競爭配給券或向官員討好關係（Rent-seeking）。

Exam Tricks (考試技巧)

排除法 (Elimination Method):
當你看到選項 (2) 提及「利率較高」時，應立即警覺。經濟制度比較通常涉及「機制」(如何決定) 而非「水平」(高低)。一旦確定 (2) 是錯的，選項 A、C、D 立即被排除，答案只能是 B。這能讓你在 10 秒內解題。
When you see statement (2) mentioning "higher interest rate," be alert immediately. Comparisons of economic systems usually involve "mechanisms" (how it's decided) rather than "levels" (high/low). Once you determine (2) is false, options A, C, and D are eliminated instantly. The answer must be B. This allows you to solve the question in 10 seconds.
