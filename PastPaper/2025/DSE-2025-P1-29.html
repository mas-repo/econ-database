Macroeconomics (宏觀經濟)

Aggregate Supply (總供應)

Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: LRAS (長期總供應), Flexible Prices (價格彈性), Potential Output (潛在產出)
Question (題目)
下列哪項/些能夠解釋為什麼長期總供應曲線是垂直的？
Which of the following can explain why a long run aggregate supply curve is vertical?
(1) 投入的價格在長期可被完全調整。
Input prices can be perfectly adjusted in the long run.
(2) 廠商在長期無法調整其產出水平。
Firms are unable to adjust their output levels in the long run.
(3) 科技水平在長期不是固定的。
Technological level is not constant in the long run.
  • A. 只有 (1) (1) only
  • B. 只有 (3) (3) only
  • C. 只有 (1)、(2) (1) and (2) only
  • D. 只有 (2)、(3) (2) and (3) only
Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 長期總供應曲線 (LRAS) / Long Run Aggregate Supply

LRAS 是一條垂直線，位於潛在產出水平 \(Y_f\) (Full Employment Output)。
The LRAS is a vertical line positioned at the potential output level \(Y_f\).

2. 為什麼是垂直的？ / Why is it vertical?

這代表在長期，總產出 \(Y\) 獨立於 物價水平 \(P\)。
This implies that in the long run, aggregate output \(Y\) is independent of the price level \(P\).

這是因為在長期，所有名義變數（如工資、原料價格）都是完全彈性 (Fully Flexible) 的。當物價 \(P\) 上升時，成本也會同比例上升，廠商的實質利潤不變，因此沒有誘因增加產量。
This is because, in the long run, all nominal variables (like wages, input prices) are fully flexible. When price \(P\) rises, costs rise proportionally. Real profit remains unchanged, so there is no incentive to increase output.

Formula Logic:
$$ \text{Real Profit} = \frac{\text{Nominal Profit}}{P} $$ If \(P \uparrow\) and Input Prices \(\uparrow\) by the same \%, Real Profit is constant.
Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: A

Statement (1) Correct (正確)

投入的價格在長期可被完全調整。
這是 LRAS 垂直的核心原因。在短期 (Short Run)，由於工資僵固 (Sticky Wages)，物價上升會導致實質工資下降，廠商有利可圖而增加產出（SRAS 向右上方傾斜）。但在長期，投入價格（成本）會隨物價水平完全調整，消除了這種「利潤錯覺」，使產出回歸到 \(Y_f\)。

Input prices can be perfectly adjusted in the long run.
This is the core reason for the vertical LRAS. In the Short Run, due to sticky wages, a price rise lowers real wages, incentivizing firms to increase output (upward sloping SRAS). However, in the Long Run, input prices (costs) fully adjust to the price level, eliminating this "profit illusion" and returning output to \(Y_f\).

Statement (2) Incorrect (錯誤)

廠商在長期無法調整其產出水平。
這是一個概念陷阱。第一，在微觀經濟學中，「長期」定義為廠商可以調整所有生產要素（包括設廠規模），所以廠商絕對「可以」調整產出。第二，LRAS 垂直並非因為廠商「無能為力」，而是因為在充分就業下，資源已被充分利用，且價格變動不再影響實質利潤。

Firms are unable to adjust their output levels in the long run.
This is a conceptual trap. First, in Microeconomics, the "Long Run" is defined as a period where firms can adjust all factors of production (including plant size), so firms definitely "can" adjust output. Second, LRAS is vertical not because firms are "physically unable" to change, but because at full employment, resources are fully utilized, and price changes no longer affect real profits.

Statement (3) Incorrect (錯誤)

科技水平在長期不是固定的。
這解釋了為什麼 LRAS 曲線會移動 (Shift)，而不是解釋為什麼它是垂直 (Vertical) 的。如果科技進步，\(Y_f\) 會增加，整條垂直線會向右移動。這與曲線的「斜率」無關。

Technological level is not constant in the long run.
This explains why the LRAS curve shifts, not why it is vertical. If technology improves, \(Y_f\) increases, and the entire vertical line shifts to the right. This is irrelevant to the "slope" of the curve.

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

Pivot Point (關鍵點):
區分 "Movement along / Slope" (沿線移動/斜率) 與 "Shift" (移動)。

  • 題目問的是「垂直」(Vertical)，即關於斜率 (Slope) 的問題。
  • 斜率取決於 \(Y\) 對 \(P\) 的敏感度。
  • (1) 解釋了為何 \(Y\) 對 \(P\) 不敏感（垂直）。
  • (3) 解釋了 \(Y\) 隨時間的增長（曲線本身的移動）。

Key Distinction: Distinguish between the Slope (why it stands straight) and the Shift (why it moves left/right). The question asks about the vertical shape (Slope). Statement (1) explains the lack of sensitivity to Price. Statement (3) explains economic growth (Shift).

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼選項 (2) 是錯的？既然資源有限，產出不是應該固定嗎？

Q: Why is option (2) wrong? Since resources are limited, shouldn't output be fixed?

A: 小心字眼。「固定」(Fixed) 和「垂直」(Vertical) 是不同的概念。 LRAS 垂直是指產出不隨物價改變。選項 (2) 說廠商「無法調整」，這暗示了一種物理上的不可能。 事實上，如果人口增加或發現新資源，廠商會在長期增加產出。所以說廠商「無法調整」是錯誤的描述。我們只是說，單純的物價上升不會導致產出增加。

A: Be careful with the wording. "Fixed" and "Vertical" are different concepts. Vertical LRAS means output doesn't change with Price. Option (2) says firms are "unable to adjust," implying a physical impossibility. In reality, if population grows or new resources are found, firms will increase output in the long run. So saying they are "unable" is factually wrong. We only mean that a mere Price Level increase won't cause output to increase.

Exam Trick: The "Shift vs. Slope" Trap

看到關於「科技」、「資源增長」的選項時，問自己：
這會改變 \(Y\) 的數值（移動曲線），還是解釋 \(Y\) 與 \(P\) 的關係（曲線形狀）？

速記口訣：
- Input Prices Flexible (投入價格彈性) \(\rightarrow\) 解釋垂直 (Vertical)。
- Technology/Resources (科技/資源) \(\rightarrow\) 解釋移動 (Shift)。
