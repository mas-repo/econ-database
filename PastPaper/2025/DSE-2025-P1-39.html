2. Concept Review (概念重溫)

交易方程式與貨幣數量論 (Equation of Exchange & Quantity Theory of Money)

這條題目考驗你對「交易方程式」(Equation of Exchange) 轉換為「增長率形式」(Growth rate form) 的理解。根據貨幣數量論，貨幣流通速度 $V$ 在短期內是穩定的。
This question tests your understanding of converting the Equation of Exchange into its growth rate form. According to the Quantity Theory of Money, the velocity of money $V$ is relatively stable.

標準公式 (Standard Formula):
$$M \times V = P \times Y$$

當中 (Where):
$M$ = 貨幣供應 (Money Supply)
$V$ = 貨幣流通速度 (Velocity of Money)
$P$ = 物價水平 (Price Level)
$Y$ = 實質產出 (Real Output)
$P \times Y$ = 名義產出 (Nominal Output)

增長率公式 (Growth Rate Formula):
$$g_M + g_V = g_P + g_Y$$

假設 $g_V = 0$ (因為 $V$ 穩定不變 / assumed constant)，我們可以得出通脹率 $g_P$ 的算式：
Assuming $g_V = 0$ (since $V$ is stable), we can derive the formula for inflation rate $g_P$:

$$g_P = g_M - g_Y$$

3. The Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

✅ Correct Answer (正確答案): B

從原圖提取的數據 (Data extracted from the initial graph):

  • $g_M$ (貨幣供應增長率 / Money supply growth rate) 是正數常數 (Constant positive value)。
  • $g_Y$ (實質產出增長率 / Real output growth rate) 是正數常數 (Constant positive value)。
  • 圖中顯示 $g_M > g_Y$。

Option A & B 解析 (Analysis for Options A & B):

根據公式 $g_P = g_M - g_Y$，由於 $g_M$ 大於 $g_Y$，通脹率 $g_P$ 必定是一個大於零的正數常數。因此，圖表 B 顯示通脹率維持在零以上的水平線是正確的。
According to the formula $g_P = g_M - g_Y$, since $g_M$ is strictly greater than $g_Y$, the inflation rate $g_P$ must be a constant positive number. Therefore, Graph B, showing inflation as a horizontal line above zero, is correct. Graph A is wrong because inflation is not zero.

Option C & D 解析 (Analysis for Options C & D):

「名義產出」的增長率等於 $g_P + g_Y$。從公式可知，$g_P + g_Y = g_M$ (假設 $g_V = 0$)。因為圖中的貨幣供應增長率 $g_M$ 是一個大於零的水平線，名義產出增長率也應該是大於零的水平線。選項 C (等於 0) 和 D (小於 0) 皆錯誤。
The growth rate of "Nominal Output" is equal to $g_P + g_Y$. From the formula, $g_P + g_Y = g_M$ (assuming $g_V = 0$). Since the money supply growth rate $g_M$ is a horizontal line above zero, the nominal output growth rate should also be a horizontal line above zero. Options C (equals 0) and D (less than 0) are incorrect.

4. Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目的「破題點」(Pivot Point) 在於將圖表視覺資訊轉化為代數關係式。
The "Pivot Point" of this question lies in translating visual graph information into an algebraic relationship.

  1. Identify variables: 認清圖中的 Y 軸是「增長率」(Growth Rate)，而非「水平」(Level)。
  2. Recall the assumption: 題目提及「貨幣數量論」(Quantity theory of money)，這是一個關鍵詞，暗示你要假設 $g_V = 0$。
  3. Substitute & Compare: 將圖表觀察到的 $g_M > g_Y > 0$ 代入 $g_M = g_P + g_Y$，立刻推導出 $g_P > 0$。

5. Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)

  • ❌ 陷阱 1：混淆「水平」與「增長率」(Confusing "Levels" with "Growth Rates")
    同學見到水平線，可能會誤以為貨幣供應量沒有改變 (Change = 0)。留意 Y 軸是「增長率 (%)」，水平線代表每年以固定百分比上升，而非沒有增長。
  • ❌ 陷阱 2：忘記名義產出的定義 (Forgetting Nominal Output Definition)
    部分同學不知道名義產出增長率 = 實質產出增長率 + 通脹率 ($g_Y + g_P$)，從而無法判斷 C 和 D 是否正確。

6. Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

🤔 Q: 為什麼我們可以假設貨幣流通速度的增長率 ($g_V$) 是零？
Why can we assume the growth rate of the velocity of money ($g_V$) is zero?

💡 A: 這是古典貨幣數量論 (Classical Quantity Theory of Money) 的核心假設。該理論認為，人們的交易習慣和支付科技在短期內不會發生劇烈變化，因此貨幣在經濟體內轉手的速度 ($V$) 是一個常數。常數的增長率就是零。
This is the core assumption of the Classical Quantity Theory of Money. The theory posits that people's transaction habits and payment technologies do not change drastically in the short term, so the speed at which money changes hands ($V$) is constant. The growth rate of a constant is zero.

7. Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

⚡ 視覺速解法 (Visual Shortcut):

在增長率圖表中，根據 $g_P = g_M - g_Y$，通脹率其實就是兩條線之間的「垂直距離」(Vertical Distance)。
既然圖中實線 ($M$) 永遠在虛線 ($Y$) 的上方，這段距離是一個大於零的定值。因此，只需尋找一個 Y 值大於 0 的水平線圖表，即可秒殺選出 B！
In the growth rate graph, based on $g_P = g_M - g_Y$, the inflation rate is visually just the "vertical distance" between the two lines. Since the solid line ($M$) is strictly above the dashed line ($Y$), this distance is a constant value greater than zero. Therefore, just look for a graph with a horizontal line where Y > 0. You can instantly select B!
