HKDSE Economics Analysis

Topic: Money & Banking (貨幣與銀行) Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Balance Sheet, Credit Creation, Reserve Ratio

The Question (題目)

細閱下列某銀行系統的資產負債表。 Study the following balance sheet of a banking system.

資產 Assets ($) 負債 Liabilities ($)
儲備 Reserves: 250 存款 Deposits: 1000
貸款 Loans: 750

原初，該銀行系統有$50的超額儲備。假設公眾決定將$100現金存入該銀行系統以減持現金。當該銀行系統達至最大存款時，下列哪項是其新的資產負債表？ Initially, the banking system has an excess reserve of $50. Suppose the public decides to reduce cash holdings by depositing $100 cash into the banking system. When the maximum deposit of the banking system is reached, which of the following will be its new balance sheet?

A. R:300, D:1200
B. R:350, D:1400
C. R:300, D:1500
D. R:350, D:1750

Concept Review (概念重溫)

要解決這道題目，我們需要掌握以下核心概念： To solve this problem, we need to master the following core concepts:

  1. 法定儲備比率 (Required Reserve Ratio, \(rrr\)): 銀行必須持有的儲備佔存款的百分比。 The percentage of deposits that banks must hold as reserves.
    $$ rrr = \frac{\text{Required Reserves (法定儲備)}}{\text{Deposits (存款)}} $$
  2. 儲備的組成 (Composition of Reserves):
    $$ \text{Actual Reserves} = \text{Required Reserves} + \text{Excess Reserves} $$ $$ \text{實際儲備} = \text{法定儲備} + \text{超額儲備} $$
  3. 最高存款額 (Maximum Deposits): 當銀行借出所有超額儲備（即超額儲備 = 0）時，存款達到最大值。 When banks lend out all excess reserves (i.e., Excess Reserves = 0), deposits reach their maximum.
    $$ \text{Max Deposits} = \frac{\text{Total Reserves (總儲備)}}{rrr} $$

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

這類題目通常分為三個步驟來解決。找到「隱藏的數字」是關鍵。 This type of question is usually solved in three steps. Finding the "hidden number" is the key.

  • Step 1: 找出法定儲備比率 (Find the \(rrr\))
    題目沒有直接給出 \(rrr\)，但給了「超額儲備」。這是解題的樞紐點 (Pivot Point)。 The question doesn't give \(rrr\) directly but gives "excess reserves". This is the Pivot Point.
  • Step 2: 計算新儲備 (Calculate New Reserves)
    公眾存入現金，會直接增加銀行的儲備基礎。 The public depositing cash directly increases the bank's reserve base.
  • Step 3: 應用乘數效應 (Apply Multiplier)
    利用新的儲備和第一步算出的 \(rrr\) 計算最大存款。 Use the new reserves and the \(rrr\) from Step 1 to calculate maximum deposits.

Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)

Correct Option: D ✔ CORRECT

Step 1: Find the Required Reserve Ratio (\(rrr\))

原初實際儲備 = $250，超額儲備 = $50。
Initial Actual Reserves = $250, Excess Reserves = $50.
\( \text{Required Reserves} = 250 - 50 = 200 \)

原初存款 = $1000。
Initial Deposits = $1000.
$$ rrr = \frac{200}{1000} = 0.2 \text{ (or 20\%)} $$

Step 2: Determine New Total Reserves

公眾存入 $100 現金。這 $100 成為銀行的新儲備。
The public deposits $100 cash. This $100 becomes new reserves for the bank.
\( \text{New Reserves} = 250 + 100 = 350 \)

Step 3: Calculate Maximum Deposits

題目要求「最大存款」，即沒有超額儲備。
The question asks for "maximum deposit", meaning no excess reserves.
$$ \text{New Max Deposits} = \frac{\text{New Reserves}}{rrr} = \frac{350}{0.2} = 1750 $$

Step 4: Balance the Sheet (Find Loans)

資產 = 負債。
Assets = Liabilities.
\( \text{Loans} = \text{Deposits} - \text{Reserves} = 1750 - 350 = 1400 \)

結論：儲備=350, 存款=1750, 貸款=1400。 Conclusion: Reserves=350, Deposits=1750, Loans=1400.

Option A

錯誤。 這裡的儲備是300，意味著學生可能忘記將新存入的$100加到原有的儲備中，或者誤以為原有儲備減少了。此外，存款1200並未反映完全的貨幣創造。 Incorrect. The reserves here are 300, implying the student might have failed to add the full $100 new deposit to the reserves. Also, deposits of 1200 do not reflect full money creation.

Option B

錯誤。 雖然儲備(350)正確，但存款(1400)計算錯誤。如果存款是1400，法定儲備應為 \(1400 \times 0.2 = 280\)，那麼超額儲備就是 \(350 - 280 = 70\)，這不是「最大存款」。 Incorrect. Although reserves (350) are correct, deposits (1400) are wrong. If deposits were 1400, required reserves would be \(1400 \times 0.2 = 280\), leaving excess reserves of \(350 - 280 = 70\), which is not the "maximum deposit".

Option C

錯誤。 儲備數值錯誤(300)。必須先將新現金($100)加到舊儲備($250)上得到$350，才能開始計算乘數效應。 Incorrect. The reserve figure is wrong (300). You must add the new cash ($100) to the old reserves ($250) to get $350 before calculating the multiplier effect.

Student Q&A (學生常見疑問)

Q: 為什麼原來的貸款額 ($750) 在計算中沒有用到？

Why was the initial loan amount ($750) not used in the calculation?

A: 原來的貸款額是過去的結果。當我們計算「新的最大存款」時，我們關注的是新的儲備基礎和法定儲備比率。新的貸款額是最後通過資產負債表平衡（存款 - 儲備）推算出來的剩餘項 (Balancing figure)。 A: The initial loan amount is a past result. When calculating "new maximum deposits", we focus on the new reserve base and the required reserve ratio. The new loan amount is a balancing figure derived at the end (Deposits - Reserves).

Q: 為什麼新的儲備是 $350 而不是 $250？存入的錢不是變成存款了嗎？

Why are the new reserves $350 and not $250? Didn't the deposited money become deposits?

A: 當公眾存入現金時，這筆錢同時增加了銀行的負債（存款）和資產（現金儲備）。這 $100 現金進入銀行金庫後，就變成了儲備的一部分。所以總儲備從 $250 增加到 $350。 A: When the public deposits cash, it increases both the bank's Liabilities (Deposits) and Assets (Cash Reserves) simultaneously. Once that $100 cash enters the bank vault, it becomes part of the reserves. So total reserves increase from $250 to $350.

Exam Tricks & Shortcuts (考試技巧與速解)

快速排除法 (Elimination Method): Quick Elimination:

  1. 先看儲備 (Check Reserves first): 現金流入 $100，舊儲備 $250。新儲備一定是 $350。 Check Reserves first: Cash inflow $100, old reserves $250. New reserves MUST be $350.
    → 排除 A 和 C (Eliminate A and C).
  2. 邏輯檢查 (Logic Check): 既然有新錢進來且要達到「最大存款」，存款增幅一定大於現金增幅（因為有乘數效應）。 Logic Check: Since new money came in and we want "maximum deposits", the increase in deposits must be larger than the cash increase (due to the multiplier).
  3. 最後計算 (Final Calculation): 剩下 B 和 D。計算 $350 / 0.2 = 1750$。選 D。 Final Calculation: Left with B and D. Calculate $350 / 0.2 = 1750$. Choose D.
