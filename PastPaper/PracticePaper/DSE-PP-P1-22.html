Topic: National Income Accounting & Inflation
課題：國民收入會計與通脹

Difficulty: ★★★☆☆ Key Concepts: Nominal vs Real GDP, Inflation Rate
The Question (題目)

[Traditional Chinese]
在一段時間內，某經濟以當時市價計算的本地生產總值增加15%，而通脹率則由10%減至5%。
根據以上資料，實質本地生產總值_______，而一般物價水平_______。

[English]
Over a given period, the GDP at current market prices of an economy increases by 15% and the rate of inflation decreases from 10% to 5%.
Based on the above information, the real GDP _______ and the general price level _______.

A. 增加 increases … 上升 rises
B. 增加 increases … 下降 falls
C. 減少 decreases … 上升 rises
D. 減少 decreases … 下降 falls
Concept Review (概念重溫)

1. 名義與實質 GDP 的關係 (Relation between Nominal and Real GDP)

實質變動百分比大約等於名義變動百分比減去通脹率。

The percentage change in Real GDP is approximately equal to the percentage change in Nominal GDP minus the Inflation Rate.

$$ \%\Delta \text{Real GDP} \approx \%\Delta \text{Nominal GDP} - \text{Inflation Rate} $$

2. 通脹率與物價水平 (Inflation Rate vs. Price Level)

通脹率 (Inflation Rate) 是物價水平變動的百分比。只要通脹率是正數 (Positive)，物價水平就在上升。

Inflation Rate is the percentage change in the price level. As long as the inflation rate is positive, the price level is rising.

  • 通脹率上升 (Rising inflation rate) \(\rightarrow\) 物價升得更快 (Prices rise faster).
  • 通脹率下降但仍是正數 (Falling positive inflation rate) \(\rightarrow\) 物價升得較慢 (Prices rise slower), 但仍在上升 (but still rising).
Answer & Detailed Analysis (答案與詳細分析)
Correct Option: A (增加 increases … 上升 rises)

Step 1: 分析一般物價水平 (Analyze General Price Level)

題目指出通脹率由 10% 減至 5%。雖然通脹率下降了，但 5% 仍然是一個正數。

The question states the inflation rate decreased from 10% to 5%. Although the rate fell, 5% is still a positive number.

正的通脹率意味著物價水平正在上升（只是上升速度放緩）。因此，物價水平是上升的。

A positive inflation rate means the price level is rising (just at a slower pace). Therefore, the general price level rises.

\(\rightarrow\) 排除選項 (Eliminate options) B, D.

Step 2: 分析實質本地生產總值 (Analyze Real GDP)

題目給出：名義 GDP (以當時市價計算) 增加 15%，通脹率 (物價升幅) 為 5%。

Given: Nominal GDP (at current market prices) increases by 15%, Inflation Rate (price increase) is 5%.

利用公式：

$$ \%\Delta \text{Real GDP} \approx 15\% - 5\% = +10\% $$

由於結果是正數 (+10%)，實質 GDP 增加。

Since the result is positive (+10%), Real GDP increases.

\(\rightarrow\) 選項 A 是正確答案 (Option A is correct).

Problem Decomposition (題目拆解)

關鍵轉折點 (The Pivot Point):

這題最關鍵的陷阱在於理解「通脹率下降」(Inflation rate decreases) 的含義。

The crucial trap is understanding the meaning of "Inflation rate decreases".

  • 資訊 (Info): 通脹率 10% \(\rightarrow\) 5%。
  • 解讀 (Interpretation): 這是「通脹放緩」(Disinflation)，即物價上升的速度減慢，而不是「通縮」(Deflation)。物價沒有下跌。
  • 干擾項 (Distractor): 數字 "10%" 其實不影響計算，重要的是最後的通脹率 "5%" 用於調整名義 GDP。
Common Mistakes & Traps (常犯錯誤與陷阱)
⚠️ 致命錯誤：混淆「變動率」與「水平」 (Confusing Rate of Change vs. Level)

很多學生看到「通脹率下降」(decreases)，就直覺以為「物價水平下降」(price level falls)。

Many students see "inflation rate decreases" and intuitively think "price level falls".

糾正 (Correction): 就像開車一樣，車速由 100km/h 減至 50km/h (速度下降)，車子仍然在向前行 (距離增加)。只有速度變成負數 (倒車)，距離才會減少。

It's like driving: if speed drops from 100km/h to 50km/h, the car is still moving forward. Only if speed becomes negative (reversing) does the distance decrease.

Exam Tricks (考試技巧)
⚡ 速解法 (Quick Solve Method)
  1. 先看物價：通脹率 = 5% (>0)。正數 = 物價升。刪去 B、D。
  2. Check Prices first: Inflation = 5% (>0). Positive = Prices Rise. Eliminate B, D.
  3. 再看 GDP：名義升 15%，物價只升 5%。名義升幅跑贏通脹 (15 > 5)，實質必定升。選 A。
  4. Check GDP: Nominal up 15%, Prices up only 5%. Nominal growth beats inflation (15 > 5), so Real must rise. Choose A.
